## Setup Guide #12: CRM Implementation (Salesforce/HubSpot)

### Overview
**Service:** Full CRM setup, data migration, integrations, training  
**Market Rate:** $10,000-$50,000/project  
**Your Cost:** $3,000-$15,000/project  
**Profit Margin:** 60-70%

### Key Setup Steps
1. **Get Certified (Days 1-14):**
   - HubSpot Academy: Free CRM certification (8 hours)
   - Salesforce Trailhead: Free Admin certification (40 hours)
   - Choose one to start (HubSpot easier, Salesforce more lucrative)

2. **Set Up Agency (Day 15):**
   - Google Workspace
   - Partner account (HubSpot or Salesforce)
   - Demo environments for practice

3. **Hire Team (Days 16-25):**
   - Certified CRM consultant ($3,000-$10,000/project)
   - Data migration specialist ($1,500-$3,000/project)
   - Trainer ($500-$1,500/project)

4. **Service Packages:**
   - Starter ($10K): Basic setup, data import, 2 integrations, training
   - Professional ($25K): Custom fields, workflows, 5 integrations, advanced training
   - Enterprise ($50K): Full customization, API integrations, ongoing support

5. **Find Clients:**
   - Target: Companies with 20-200 employees
   - Channels: LinkedIn, partner agencies, Salesforce/HubSpot directories

### Timeline
- Setup: 4-5 weeks
- Project delivery: 4-8 weeks per client
- Ongoing: Optional retainer ($1,000-$5,000/month)

## Setup Guide #13: Shopify Store Setup & Customization

### Overview
**Service:** Full store setup, theme customization, app integration  
**Market Rate:** $7,000-$20,000/project  
**Your Cost:** $2,000-$6,000/project  
**Profit Margin:** 65-75%

### Key Setup Steps

   - Learn Liquid (Shopify's templating language)

2. **Become Shopify Partner (Day 8):**
   - Free at [partners.shopify.com](https://partners.shopify.com)
   - Access to partner dashboard, revenue share

3. **Hire Team (Days 9-16):**
   - Shopify developer ($2,000-$5,000/project)
   - Designer ($1,000-$2,500/project)
   - Copywriter ($500-$1,500/project)

4. **Service Packages:**
   - Basic ($7K): Theme setup, 10 products, payment/shipping config
   - Standard ($12K): Custom design, 50 products, 5 apps, SEO setup
   - Premium ($20K): Full custom, unlimited products, 10 apps, marketing integration

5. **Find Clients:**
   - E-commerce Facebook groups
   - Instagram (approach brands with poor stores)
   - Shopify community forums
   - Partner with marketing agencies

### Timeline
- Setup: 2-3 weeks
- Project delivery: 3-6 weeks per client
- Ongoing: Maintenance retainer ($500-$2,000/month)

## Setup Guide #16: Podcast Production (Full-Service)

**Service:** Recording, editing, show notes, distribution, clips (4 episodes/month)  
**Market Rate:** $3,000-$10,000/month  
**Your Cost:** $1,000-$3,000/month  
**Profit Margin:** 60-70%

### Key Setup Steps
1. **Learn Podcast Production (Days 1-7):**
   - Audio editing (Audacity free, or Adobe Audition $23/month)
   - Podcast hosting platforms (Buzzsprout, Libsyn, Anchor)
   - YouTube: "Podcast Editing Tutorial" (5-6 hours)

2. **Set Up Studio (Days 8-10):**
   - Home studio: USB mic ($100), audio interface ($150), headphones ($100)
   - Software: Audacity (free) or Descript ($15/month)
   - Remote recording: Riverside.fm ($15/month) or SquadCast

3. **Hire Team (Days 11-18):**
   - Audio editor ($800-$2,000/month for 4 episodes)
   - Show notes writer ($300-$600/month)
   - Social media clipper ($400-$800/month)

4. **Service Packages:**
   - Starter ($3K): 4 episodes, editing, show notes, distribution
   - Growth ($6K): 4 episodes, editing, show notes, clips, social promotion
   - Premium ($10K): 8 episodes, full production, guest booking, marketing

5. **Find Clients:**
   - Business coaches, consultants
   - B2B companies
   - Authors, speakers
   - LinkedIn outreach, podcast guest matching services

### Timeline
- Setup: 2-3 weeks
- Ongoing per client: 10-15 hours/month
- Retainer model: 3-month minimum
