## 2️⃣ Step-by-Step Setup (Click-by-Click)

### Step 1: Get Bookkeeping Certified (Days 1-7)
**Time: 15-20 hours**

1. **Choose Your Platform**
   - **QuickBooks Online** (most popular in US) - Recommended
   - **Xero** (popular internationally)
   - Focus on one initially (QBO has 80%+ SMB market share)

2. **Complete QuickBooks Certification**
   - Go to [quickbooks.intuit.com](https://quickbooks.intuit.com)
   - Click "ProAdvisor" → "Become a ProAdvisor"
   - Complete "QuickBooks Online Certification" (free, 8-10 hours)
   - Pass exam (80% to pass, unlimited retakes)
   - You become a certified QuickBooks ProAdvisor (free software included!)

3. **Learn Bookkeeping Basics**
   - **Coursera:** "Bookkeeping Basics" (free audit, 10 hours)
   - **YouTube:** "Bookkeeping for Beginners" (5-6 hours)
   - Key concepts: Debits/credits, chart of accounts, reconciliations, financial statements

4. **Practice**
   - Use QuickBooks sandbox (free with ProAdvisor)
   - Create sample companies
   - Practice: Invoices, bills, bank feeds, reconciliations, reports

### Step 2: Set Up Your Agency (Day 8)
**Time: 2 hours**

1. **Create Google Workspace**
   - Go to [workspace.google.com](https://workspace.google.com)
   - Sign up with business name
   - Create email: `hello@youragency.com`

2. **Set Up QuickBooks ProAdvisor Account**
   - Complete certification (see Step 1)
   - Access free QBO subscription (included with ProAdvisor)
   - Get listed in "Find a ProAdvisor" directory (free leads!)

3. **Set Up Practice Management**
   - **Canopy** ($50/month) - Client portal, document management
   - **OR** Use Google Drive (free) for document sharing
   - **Loom** (free) - Video updates to clients

### Step 3: Hire Your Team (Days 9-14)
**Time: 8-10 hours**

1. **Post Job: Bookkeeper**
   - Platform: Upwork or OnlineJobs.ph
   - Title: "QuickBooks Online Certified Bookkeeper"
   - Description:
     ```
     Looking for QuickBooks Online certified bookkeeper for SMB clients.
     
     Requirements:
     - QuickBooks Online certification (required)
     - 2+ years bookkeeping experience
     - Experience with bank reconciliations, AP/AR
     - Detail-oriented, reliable
     - Excellent English communication
     
     Budget: $400-$1,000/month per 10-15 clients (part-time to full-time)
     Long-term opportunity
     ```

2. **Screen Candidates**
   - Verify QBO certification (ask for certificate ID)
   - Test: Give sample bank statement, have them categorize transactions
   - Check references (previous clients or employers)
   - Paid test: One month of bookkeeping for sample company ($50-100)

3. **Hire 1-2 Bookkeepers**
   - Start with one, add backup as you grow
   - Consider hiring senior bookkeeper ($800-1,200/month) to manage quality

### Step 4: Create Service Packages (Day 15)
**Time: 3 hours**

1. **Define Your Offerings**

   **Starter - $500/month**
   - Up to 50 transactions/month
   - Bank/credit card reconciliations
   - Monthly financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet)
   - Email support
   - Monthly review call (15 min)

   **Growth - $1,500/month**
   - Up to 200 transactions/month
   - Everything in Starter
   - Accounts Payable management
   - Accounts Receivable tracking
   - Sales tax preparation
   - Bi-weekly support
   - Monthly review call (30 min)

   **Business - $3,000/month**
   - Up to 500 transactions/month
   - Everything in Growth
   - Payroll processing
   - Budget vs. actual reporting
   - Cash flow forecasting
   - Weekly support
   - Bi-weekly review calls

   **Enterprise - $5,000/month**
   - Unlimited transactions
   - Everything in Business
   - Multiple entities
   - Custom reporting
   - Dedicated bookkeeper
   - Weekly strategy calls
   - CFO advisory (basic)

2. **Create Contract Template**
   - Month-to-month or 3-month minimum
   - Payment due net-7 (first of month for upcoming month)
   - Client provides access (bank feeds, QBO)
   - Scope based on transaction volume
   - 30-day cancellation notice

3. **Build Proposal Template**
   - Use Canva
   - Include: Current state assessment, recommended package, pricing, onboarding timeline

### Step 5: Set Up Workflow (Days 16-18)
**Time: 5 hours**

1. **Client Onboarding Checklist**
   - Signed engagement letter
   - QuickBooks access (invite as accountant)
   - Bank/credit card connections (client connects, you access)
   - Previous year's tax return
   - Chart of accounts review
   - Key contacts (owner, CPA)
   - Any special requirements

2. **Monthly Bookkeeping Workflow**
   ```
   Week 1 (1st-7th):
   - Download bank/credit card statements
   - Categorize transactions
   - Review uncategorized items
   - Reconcile all accounts
   
   Week 2 (8th-14th):
   - Review AP/AR aging
   - Process outstanding items
   - Run preliminary reports
   
   Week 3 (15th-21st):
   - Client review period
   - Answer questions
   - Make adjustments
   
   Week 4 (22nd-end):
   - Finalize books
   - Generate financial statements
   - Send to client + CPA
   - Monthly review call
   ```

3. **Create Quality Control Checklist**
   - All accounts reconciled
   - No uncategorized transactions
   - Bank balances match statements
   - AP/AR aging reviewed
   - Financial statements reviewed for anomalies
   - Prior month compared (significant variances?)

4. **Create SOPs**
   - Bank reconciliation process
   - Transaction categorization guidelines
   - Month-end close checklist
   - Client communication templates

### Step 6: Find Clients (Days 19-30)
**Time: 2-3 hours/day**

1. **Target Small Businesses**
   - E-commerce stores (Shopify, Amazon sellers)
   - Service businesses (consultants, agencies, contractors)
   - Retail stores, restaurants
   - Professional services (lawyers, doctors, real estate)
   - Look for: 10-50 employees, $500K-$10M revenue

2. **Outreach Message Template**
   ```
   Hi [Name],
   
   I help [industry] business owners get their books done accurately and on time—without the hassle.
   
   Many owners I talk to are spending 10+ hours/month on bookkeeping or worried their books aren't right for tax time.
   
   We handle everything: reconciliations, categorization, financial statements. You get peace of mind and clear reports every month.
   
   Would you be open to a quick chat about your current bookkeeping setup?
   
   Best,
   [Your Name]
   QuickBooks ProAdvisor
   ```

3. **Alternative Channels**
   - **QuickBooks Find a ProAdvisor** - Get listed (free leads)
   - **CPA Referrals** - Partner with tax preparers (they hate doing bookkeeping)
   - **Facebook Groups:** Small business, e-commerce groups
   - **Local Chamber of Commerce** - Network with business owners
   - **LinkedIn:** Search "Small Business Owner" + your city

## 5️⃣ Troubleshooting Guide

### Problem: Client's Books Are a Disaster
**Solutions:**
- Charge cleanup fee ($500-$2,000 one-time)
- Set expectations: "This will take 2-3 months to get current"
- Prioritize: Get current month right first, then backfill
- Document everything for CPA

### Problem: Missing Receipts/Documentation
**Solutions:**
- Use Dext or Hubdoc (client snaps photos of receipts)
- Set policy: "No receipt = categorize as 'Owner Draw' or ask"
- Educate client on tax requirements
- Create simple system (Google Drive folder, email to specific address)

### Problem: Client Wants Tax Advice
**Solutions:**
- Clarify: "We do bookkeeping, not tax preparation"
- Partner with CPA (refer clients, get referrals back)
- Provide clean books to CPA (that's your value)
- Never give tax advice without proper licensing

### Problem: Bank Feeds Not Connecting
**Solutions:**
- Try manual upload (CSV from bank website)
- Contact bank (some require business account setup)
- Use intermediary (Plaid, Yodlee)
- Manual entry as last resort (bill extra time)

### Problem: Client Misses Payments
**Solutions:**
- Require auto-pay (Stripe, credit card on file)
- Pause work if 30+ days overdue
- Clear contract terms: "Services suspended after 30 days"
- Get payment upfront (first month + deposit)

### Problem: Bookkeeper Makes Mistakes
**Solutions:**
- Implement QC checklist (review before sending to client)
- Retrain on specific issues
- If chronic: Replace bookkeeper
- Always have backup bookkeeper on bench
