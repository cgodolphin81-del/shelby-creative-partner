## 💡 Key Principles for Success

### 1. Sell First, Fulfill Later
- Get client commitment before building full team
- Use client payment to fund contractor costs
- Bootstrap = minimal upfront investment

### 2. Niche Down Fast
- Start broad to learn
- After 3-5 clients, pick an industry vertical
- Specialists charge 2-3x generalists

### 3. Systematize Everything
- Document every process (SOPs)
- Create templates for everything
- Train team to run without you
- Your goal: Work ON business, not IN it

### 4. Price for Profit, Not Competition
- Don't race to the bottom
- 60-70% margins are achievable
- Charge based on value, not hours

### 5. Fire Bad Clients
- Bottom 20% cause 80% of headaches
- Replace with better clients
- Life's too short for difficult clients

### 6. Build Recurring Revenue
- Retainers > one-time projects
- Aim for 80%+ revenue from recurring
- Predictable income = less stress

### 7. Invest in Team
- Pay above market rates
- Treat contractors well (they stay longer)
- Cross-train for backup
- Your team = your business value

- Monthly revenue $30K+

- Quality is slipping
- You're the bottleneck
- Can afford $3K-$5K/month salary
