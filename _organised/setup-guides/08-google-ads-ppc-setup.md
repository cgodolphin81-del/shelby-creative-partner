## 2️⃣ Step-by-Step Setup (Click-by-Click)

### Step 1: Get Google Ads Certified (Days 1-3)
**Time: 8-10 hours**

1. **Create Google Skillshop Account**
   - Go to [skillshop.exceedlms.com](https://skillshop.exceedlms.com)
   - Sign up with Google account
   - This is Google's official certification platform

2. **Complete Google Ads Certifications**
   - **Google Ads Search Certification** (required) - 2 hours
   - **Google Ads Display Certification** (recommended) - 1.5 hours
   - **Google Analytics Individual Qualification** (recommended) - 2 hours
   - All certifications are free and never expire (but refresh annually)

3. **Practice with Demo Account**
   - Google Ads provides a demo account for practice
   - Create test campaigns (won't go live)
   - Learn interface, bidding, targeting

### Step 2: Set Up Your Agency (Day 4)
**Time: 2 hours**

1. **Create Google Workspace**
   - Go to [workspace.google.com](https://workspace.google.com)
   - Sign up with business name
   - Create email: `hello@youragency.com`

2. **Create Google Ads Manager Account (MCC)**
   - Go to [ads.google.com](https://ads.google.com)
   - Click tools → Setup → Manager accounts
   - Create new manager account
   - This allows you to manage multiple client accounts

3. **Set Up Analytics & Tag Manager**
   - **Analytics:** [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com) → Create account
   - **Tag Manager:** [tagmanager.google.com](https://tagmanager.google.com) → Create account
   - Learn to set up conversion tracking

### Step 3: Hire Your Team (Days 5-7)
**Time: 5-7 hours**

1. **Post Job: PPC Specialist**
   - Platform: Upwork
   - Title: "Google Ads Certified PPC Specialist"
   - Description:
     ```
     Looking for Google Ads certified specialist to manage client campaigns.
     
     Requirements:
     - Google Ads Search certification (required)
     - 2+ years managing Google Ads
     - Experience with e-commerce or lead gen
     - Portfolio with results (ROAS, CPA improvements)
     - Can write ad copy and optimize landing pages
     
     Budget: $500-$1,000/month per client (part-time)
     Long-term opportunity
     ```

2. **Screen Candidates**
   - Verify certification (ask for certificate ID)
   - Review case studies with specific metrics
   - Ask: "What's a good Quality Score?" (Answer: 7-10)
   - Ask: "How do you structure campaigns?" (Look for organized approach)
   - Paid test: Audit a Google Ads account ($50-100)

3. **Hire 1-2 Specialists**
   - One for search campaigns
   - One for display/shopping (as you grow)

### Step 4: Create Service Packages (Day 8)
**Time: 3 hours**

1. **Define Your Offerings**

   **Starter - $1,500/month + 15% of ad spend**
   - 1 campaign (Search)
   - Up to $5,000/month ad spend
   - Keyword research
   - Ad copy writing
   - Basic optimization
   - Monthly report
   - 30-min monthly call

   **Growth - $3,000/month + 12% of ad spend**
   - Up to 3 campaigns (Search + Display)
   - Up to $20,000/month ad spend
   - Advanced keyword research
   - A/B testing (ads + landing pages)
   - Conversion tracking setup
   - Bi-weekly optimization
   - Bi-weekly report
   - Bi-weekly calls

   **Scale - $5,000/month + 10% of ad spend**
   - Unlimited campaigns (all types)
   - Unlimited ad spend
   - Full-funnel strategy
   - Landing page optimization
   - Call tracking included
   - Weekly optimization
   - Weekly report
   - Weekly calls
   - Dedicated account manager

2. **Create Contract Template**
   - 3-month minimum
   - Management fee + % of ad spend
   - Ad spend billed directly to client (they enter card in Google)
   - 30-day cancellation notice after 3 months
   - No guaranteed results (legally required)

3. **Build Proposal Template**
   - Use Canva
   - Include: Account audit, keyword opportunities, projected results, pricing, timeline

### Step 5: Set Up Workflow (Days 9-11)
**Time: 5 hours**

1. **Client Onboarding Checklist**
   - Google Ads access (request via manager account)
   - Google Analytics access
   - Website access (for tracking)
   - Business goals (leads, sales, calls)
   - Target locations
   - Target CPA/ROAS
   - Budget
   - Brand guidelines

2. **Campaign Setup Workflow**
   ```
   Week 1:
   - Complete account audit (or setup from scratch)
   - Keyword research (SEMrush, Google Keyword Planner)
   - Campaign structure (ad groups, keywords)
   - Ad copy writing (3-5 ads per group)
   - Conversion tracking setup
   - Launch campaigns
   
   Week 2-4:
   - Monitor daily (first week)
   - Optimize bids, pause losers
   - A/B test ads
   - Add negative keywords
   - Weekly optimization
   
   Month 2+:
   - Ongoing optimization
   - Scale winners
   - Test new keywords/ads
   - Monthly reporting
   ```

3. **Create Reporting Template**
   - Google Data Studio (free) or AgencyAnalytics
   - Track: Impressions, Clicks, CTR, CPC, Conversions, CPA, ROAS, Quality Score
   - Include insights and recommendations

4. **Create SOPs**
   - Campaign setup checklist
   - Daily/weekly optimization tasks
   - Negative keyword process
   - Ad testing framework
   - Monthly reporting process

### Step 6: Find Clients (Days 12-28)
**Time: 2-3 hours/day**

1. **Target Businesses**
   - E-commerce (Shopping campaigns)
   - Local services (plumbers, lawyers, dentists)
   - SaaS companies
   - Home services (HVAC, roofing, landscaping)
   - Look for businesses already running ads (poorly)

2. **Outreach Message Template**
   ```
   Hi [Name],
   
   I noticed [Company] is running Google Ads for [keyword]. I took a quick look and spotted 3 opportunities to lower your cost-per-lead by 30-50%.
   
   We specialize in Google Ads management and recently helped [Similar Company] reduce CPA from $85 to $42 while doubling conversions.
   
   Would you be open to a free Google Ads audit? I'll show you exactly what's working, what's not, and how to fix it.
   
   Best,
   [Your Name]
   Google Ads Certified
   ```

3. **Alternative Channels**
   - Google Ads: Search for businesses running ads, then outreach
   - LinkedIn: Search "Marketing Manager" + industry
   - Facebook Groups: E-commerce, local business
   - Partner with web design agencies (they need PPC partners)
   - Clutch.co: Find businesses looking for agencies

## 5️⃣ Troubleshooting Guide

### Problem: Ads Aren't Showing
**Solutions:**
- Check: Is budget too low? (Increase or narrow targeting)
- Check: Are bids competitive? (Use bid simulator)
- Check: Is Quality Score low? (Improve ad relevance, landing page)
- Check: Are there approval issues? (Fix policy violations)

### Problem: High Cost-Per-Click (CPC)
**Solutions:**
- Improve Quality Score (better ads, relevant keywords, optimized landing pages)
- Use long-tail keywords (less competition)
- Adjust bid strategy (target CPA or ROAS)
- Pause expensive, low-converting keywords
- Improve ad rank (better CTR)

### Problem: Low Conversion Rate
**Solutions:**
- Check landing page (is it relevant to ad?)
- Add negative keywords (filter bad traffic)
- Improve ad copy (set accurate expectations)
- A/B test landing pages
- Check tracking (are conversions being recorded?)

### Problem: Client Wants Guaranteed Results
**Solutions:**
- Explain: "We can't control auctions, competition, or market conditions"
- Promise: Best practices, transparent reporting, continuous optimization
- Share case studies: "Typical clients see X improvement in Y months"
- Set realistic expectations upfront

### Problem: Account Gets Suspended
**Solutions:**
- Common causes: Policy violations, suspicious payment, circumventing systems
- Review Google's email for specific reason
- Fix the issue, then submit appeal
- Be honest and thorough in appeal
- This is why you follow policies from the start

### Problem: Client Wants to Change Everything
**Solutions:**
- Educate: "Data-driven decisions beat opinions"
- Show the data: "This keyword has 5.2 ROAS, let's not pause it"
- Compromise: Test their idea with small budget
- Set boundaries: "We recommend based on data; final decisions are yours"
