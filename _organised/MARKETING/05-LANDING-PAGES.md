## 🎯 CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Above the Fold
- Clear headline (desired outcome)
- Subheadline (how it works)
- Hero image/video (show, don't tell)
- Primary CTA (contrasting color)

### Trust Signals
- Beta tester testimonials (with photos)
- Results screenshots (analytics, revenue)
- Security badges (SSL, secure checkout)
- Guarantee badge (14-day refund)

### Urgency Elements
- Countdown timer (early access deadline)
- "First 100 buyers" badge
- "Launch pricing ends [date]" banner
- Limited bonus availability

### Reduce Friction
- Guest checkout (no account required)
- Multiple payment options (card, PayPal, Apple Pay)
- Clear pricing (no hidden fees)
- Instant delivery (email + download page)
