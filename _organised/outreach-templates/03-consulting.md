## Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2-3 minutes)

**You:** "Thanks for making time, [First Name]. I know you're busy, so I want to make sure this is valuable for you.

I'd love to start by understanding where [Company Name] is at and what you're grappling with. Can I ask a few questions?"

**[Wait for yes]**

**You:** "Great. Two things upfront:
1. This call is for you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll be honest about that too. Fair?"

---

### Qualifying Questions (10-12 minutes)

**Current State:**
1. "Walk me through where [Company Name] is today - revenue, team, where you're at."
2. "What got you to this point? What's working well?"
3. "What's your biggest frustration right now?"

**Pain Points:**
4. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"
5. "Where do you feel stuck or plateaued?"
6. "What happens if nothing changes in the next 12 months?"

**Goals:**
7. "Where do you want to be in 18 months?"
8. "What's stopping you from getting there today?"

**Decision Process:**
9. "Have you worked with consultants or coaches before? What was that experience like?"
10. "Who else is involved in decisions like this?"
11. "What would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer?"

---

### Presentation (5-8 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], thank you for sharing all that. Based on what you've told me, here's what I'm seeing..."

**[Present findings]**

1. **The Pattern:** "What you're experiencing is incredibly common at this stage. Companies hit $[X]M and the things that got you here won't get you there."

2. **The Root Cause:** "The core issue isn't [surface problem] - it's [deeper issue: strategy clarity, operating system, team alignment, etc.]."

3. **The Opportunity:** "Companies that solve this typically 2-3x revenue in 18 months while reducing chaos. For you, that could mean [specific impact]."

4. **Our Approach:** "We work in 3 phases: Diagnose → Design → Deploy. Here's what each looks like..."

**[Share screen with simple framework]**

---

### Handling Objections

**"We need to think about it"**
"I understand. Can I ask - what specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timing, or do you have questions about the approach?"

**"This is a significant investment"**
"It is. What number are you thinking? ... [Listen] ... I hear you. Here's the framing: our clients typically see [ROI]. At that rate, this pays for itself in [X] months. Does that change the calculus?"

**"We've tried consultants before"**
"I appreciate you sharing that. What happened? ... [Listen] ... That's frustrating. Here's what we do differently: [specific differentiation]. We're partners, not advisors who disappear."

**"Now's not a good time"**
"When would be better? ... [Listen] ... The thing is, every month you wait is a month of [cost of inaction]. What if we started with a focused 90-day engagement on just the highest priority?"

---

### Close (3-5 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

**[Present specific recommendation]**

**Direct Close:**
"Does this feel like the right next step for [Company Name]?"

**Trial Close:**
"How does this align with what you were hoping to accomplish?"

**Next Steps:**
"What would you need to see to move forward? Let's make a plan to get you there."

**[Confirm next steps, schedule follow-up, send proposal]**

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objections

**"This is more than we can afford"**
"I hear you. What range were you expecting? ... [Listen] ... That makes sense. Here's the thing - our clients typically see [ROI]. At that rate, this pays for itself in [X] months. Would it help if we structured it differently - maybe a 90-day sprint first?"

**"We need to see more ROI certainty"**
"Fair. What would give you more confidence? ... [Listen] ... Here's what we can do: milestone-based pricing, performance guarantees, or a pilot program. What would feel right?"

---

### Time Objections

**"We don't have time for this right now"**
"I understand. When would be better? ... [Listen] ... The thing is, you're already spending time on [symptoms]. This is about solving the root cause so you HAVE time. What if we started small - just the diagnostic?"

**"We're in the middle of [major initiative]"**
"That makes sense. When does that wrap? ... [Listen] ... Actually, this could accelerate that initiative. Our approach helps you execute faster. Can I show you how?"

---

### Trust Objections

**"We've been burned by consultants"**
"I appreciate you sharing that. What happened? ... [Listen] ... That's frustrating. Here's what we do differently: we implement with you, not just advise. We're accountable for results, not just recommendations. Would a pilot program ease the concern?"

**"How do we know you can deliver?"**
"Great question. Here are 3 case studies from similar companies... [Share]. We also offer a satisfaction guarantee - if you don't feel it's valuable in the first 90 days, we'll part ways amicably."

---

### Authority Objections

**"I need to discuss with my partner/board"**
"Absolutely - this is a significant decision. What do you think they'll need to see to feel confident? Let me make sure the proposal addresses those concerns. Can I join that conversation?"

**"Let me think about it"**
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timing, or something about the approach? I want to make sure you have what you need."
