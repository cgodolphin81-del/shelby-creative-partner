## Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2-3 minutes)

**You:** "Thanks for making time, [First Name]. I know you're busy, so I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you.

Can I start by asking a few questions about [Company Name]'s current operations and where you're feeling the most pressure?"

**[Wait for yes]**

**You:** "Perfect. Two things upfront:
1. This is about you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll tell you that too. Fair?"

---

### Qualifying Questions (7-10 minutes)

**Current Operations:**
1. "Walk me through a typical week - what processes consume the most of your team's time?"
2. "What's the one thing your team complains about most?"
3. "How are you currently handling [specific process relevant to their industry]?"

**Pain Points:**
4. "If you could eliminate one operational headache overnight, what would it be?"
5. "Where are you losing money due to inefficiency or errors?"
6. "What's preventing you from scaling faster?"

**AI Awareness:**
7. "What's your current approach to AI and automation?"
8. "Have you tried any AI tools before? What worked, what didn't?"

**Decision Process:**
9. "Who else would be involved in a decision like this?"
10. "What would success look like 90 days from now?"

---

### Presentation (5-8 minutes)

**You:** "Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm seeing..."

**[Present findings]**

1. **The Problem:** "You're spending [X] hours/week on [process]. At your team's cost, that's $[X]/month on work that doesn't require human judgment."

2. **The Opportunity:** "AI can automate 70-80% of this. That's [X] hours/week back, [specific business impact]."

3. **Our Approach:** "We use a 3-phase methodology: Discover → Deploy → Scale. Here's what each phase looks like..."

4. **Proof:** "We've done this for [X] companies in [industry]. Here are the typical results..."

**[Share screen with case studies or simple deck]**

---

### Handling Objections

**"We need to think about it"**
"I understand. Can I ask - what specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timeline, or something else?"

**"This sounds expensive"**
"What number are you thinking? ... [Listen] ... I hear you. Here's the framing: our clients typically see payback in [X] weeks. After that, it's pure margin. Does that change the equation?"

**"We don't have time for implementation"**
"That's the beauty of our approach - we handle everything. Your team spends maybe 3-4 hours total for onboarding and training. We do the heavy lifting. What part concerns you most?"

**"Send me information"**
"Absolutely. To make it relevant, what's the one thing you'd need to see to feel confident moving forward?"

---

### Close (3-5 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

**[Present specific recommendation]**

**Direct Close:**
"Does this feel like the right next step?"

**Trial Close:**
"How does this align with what you were hoping to accomplish?"

**Next Steps:**
"What would you need to see to move forward? Let's make a plan."

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objections

**"This is more than we budgeted"**
"I hear you. What range were you expecting? ... [Listen] ... That makes sense. Here's the thing - our clients see payback in [X] weeks. After that, it's pure savings. Would it help if we phased the rollout to spread the investment?"

**"We can't justify this right now"**
"Totally understand. What if we started with just ONE high-impact automation? Lower investment, faster ROI, and you can evaluate before scaling. Which process would you prioritize?"

---

### Time Objections

**"We don't have bandwidth for this"**
"That's why our approach works - we handle everything. Your team spends 3-4 hours total: kickoff, training, and go-live. We do all the building and integration. What part of the timeline concerns you?"

**"Now's not a good time"**
"When would be better? ... [Listen] ... The thing is, every month you wait is [X] hours of wasted time and $[X] in unnecessary costs. What if we started with just the audit and you decide after?"

---

### Trust Objections

**"We've been burned by consultants before"**
"I appreciate you sharing that. What happened? ... [Listen] ... Here's what we do differently: [specific differentiation]. Plus, we offer milestone-based pricing and a satisfaction guarantee. Would that help?"

**"How do we know this will work?"**
"Great question. Here are 3 case studies from similar companies... [Share]. We also offer a 30-day guarantee - if you don't see [specific metric], we'll work for free until you do."

---

### Technical Objections

**"Our systems are too complex"**
"I hear that often. The good news: we've integrated with [list of common tools]. Our approach is API-first and we build custom connectors when needed. What systems are you most concerned about?"

**"We need this to be secure/compliant"**
"Absolutely. We build with security first: [list security measures]. We've worked with [regulated industry clients] and understand compliance requirements. What specific concerns do you have?"
