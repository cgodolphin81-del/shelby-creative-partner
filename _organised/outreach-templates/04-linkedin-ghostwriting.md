## Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2-3 minutes)

**You:** "Thanks for making time, [First Name]. I know you're busy, so I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you.

Can I start by asking a few questions about your current LinkedIn presence and what you're hoping to achieve?"

**[Wait for yes]**

**You:** "Perfect. Two things upfront:
1. This is about you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll be honest. Fair?"

---

### Qualifying Questions (7-10 minutes)

**Current State:**
1. "How often are you currently posting on LinkedIn?"
2. "What's working? What's not?"
3. "What results have you seen from LinkedIn so far?"

**Goals:**
4. "What would success look like for you on LinkedIn?"
5. "What's driving this interest now vs. 6 months ago?"
6. "How important is inbound lead generation vs. brand building?"

**Obstacles:**
7. "What's stopped you from posting more consistently?"
8. "How much time could you realistically spend on this weekly?"

**Decision:**
9. "Have you worked with ghostwriters or content agencies before?"
10. "Who else is involved in decisions like this?"
11. "What would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer?"

---

### Presentation (5-8 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], thank you for sharing all that. Based on what you've told me, here's what I'm seeing..."

**[Present findings]**

1. **The Opportunity:** "You have incredible insights and experience. But you're invisible on LinkedIn while competitors are building authority."

2. **The Problem:** "You don't have time to write and post consistently. That's not a you problem - that's a system problem."

3. **The Solution:** "We ghostwrite everything for you. 30 min/week call, we handle the rest. You approve, we post and engage."

4. **The Results:** "Our clients typically see 1M+ impressions/month, 30-50 inbound leads/quarter, $200k-$500k pipeline."

**[Share screen with case studies]**

---

### Handling Objections

**"This is expensive"**
"I hear you. What number were you expecting? ... [Listen] ... That makes sense. Here's the framing: our clients typically see [ROI]. One closed deal often covers the entire investment. Does that change the equation?"

**"I need to think about it"**
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else?"

**"Can't I just hire a cheaper ghostwriter?"**
"You could. But here's the difference: we specialize in executive LinkedIn content. We know what drives engagement, what converts, and how to write in YOUR voice. Cheap ghostwriters produce generic content that doesn't perform. What's the cost of 6 months of content that doesn't work?"

**"I'm not sure about the time commitment"**
"Totally get that. Our clients spend 30 min/week on the call and maybe 10 minutes approving posts. That's it. We handle everything else. What part concerns you most?"

---

### Close (3-5 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

**[Present specific recommendation]**

**Direct Close:**
"Does this feel like the right next step?"

**Trial Close:**
"How does this align with what you were hoping to accomplish?"

**Next Steps:**
"What would you need to see to move forward? Let's make a plan."

**[Confirm next steps, schedule onboarding, send agreement]**

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objections

**"This is more than I budgeted"**
"I hear you. What range were you expecting? ... [Listen] ... That makes sense. Here's the thing - our clients typically see [ROI]. One closed deal often covers the entire investment. Would it help if we started with a lighter package and scaled up?"

**"I can get this cheaper elsewhere"**
"You could. But here's the question: what's the cost of 6 months of content that doesn't perform? Cheap ghostwriters produce generic content. We write in YOUR voice, with strategy, focused on results. What's 6 months of your time worth if the content doesn't work?"

---

### Time Objections

**"I don't have 30 minutes/week"**
"I get that. What if we did bi-weekly 45-minute calls instead? Or we could do async voice notes. We're flexible. The point is we need YOUR insights - but we'll work with whatever schedule you have. What would be realistic?"

**"Now's not a good time"**
"When would be better? ... [Listen] ... The thing about LinkedIn is it compounds. The people who start now will dominate in 12 months. Every month you wait is a month of building you're not doing. What if we started with just the profile optimization and strategy?"

---

### Trust Objections

**"How do I know you can write in my voice?"**
"Great question. We do a voice calibration session upfront - we interview you, analyze your past writing, and create a voice guide. Then we write sample posts and iterate until it sounds exactly like you. We don't publish anything you don't approve. Want to see examples of client content?"

**"What if this doesn't work?"**
"Fair concern. Here's what we guarantee: if you don't see [specific metric] by day 60, we'll work for free until you do. We've never had to honor that guarantee because the system works. But we stand behind it."

---

### Skepticism Objections

**"LinkedIn is full of noise. Will this actually work?"**
"You're right - most LinkedIn content IS noise. That's why most people don't get results. We don't create noise. We create strategic content that: (1) showcases your unique insights, (2) speaks to your ideal audience, (3) drives real conversations. The results speak for themselves - $340k pipeline for [Client] in 90 days."

**"I've tried posting before and nothing happened"**
"That's common. Most people post randomly without strategy. They write about what THEY want to say, not what their audience wants to hear. We flip that - we research your audience, create content they care about, and engage authentically. That's the difference."
