## Discovery Call Script

### Opening (2-3 minutes)

**You:** "Thanks for making time, [First Name]. I know you're busy, especially during [season]. I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you.

Can I start by asking a few questions about how you're currently getting leads and what's working (or not)?"

**[Wait for yes]**

**You:** "Perfect. Two things upfront:
1. This is about you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll be honest. Fair?"

---

### Qualifying Questions (7-10 minutes)

**Current Lead Sources:**
1. "Walk me through where your leads come from today - what's the mix?"
2. "How much are you spending on Angi/HomeAdvisor monthly?"
3. "What's your close rate on those leads?"

**Pain Points:**
4. "What's your biggest frustration with your current lead flow?"
5. "How's the quality of leads you're getting?"
6. "Are you able to keep your team busy year-round, or do you have slow seasons?"

**Goals:**
7. "If you could wave a magic wand, how many quality leads per month would you want?"
8. "What would consistent lead flow mean for your business?"

**Decision:**
9. "Have you tried other lead generation before? What happened?"
10. "Who else is involved in decisions like this?"
11. "What would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer?"

---

### Presentation (5-8 minutes)

**You:** "[First Name], thank you for sharing all that. Based on what you've told me, here's what I'm seeing..."

**[Present findings]**

1. **The Problem:** "You're spending $[X]/month on Angi, getting shared leads, closing only [Y]%. That's $[Z] per closed job before you even do the work."

2. **The Opportunity:** "There's significant demand in [City] for [services]. Your competitors are capturing most of it. But there's room for you to dominate."

3. **The Solution:** "We build exclusive lead generation systems. You get pre-qualified, exclusive leads. Our clients close 60-70% and know exactly what they're paying."

4. **The Results:** "[Client example] went from 22% to 68% close rate, added $28k/month revenue, and fired Angi."

**[Share screen with case studies or simple deck]**

---

### Handling Objections

**"This sounds expensive"**
"I hear you. What number are you thinking? ... [Listen] ... That makes sense. Here's the framing: if you close [X] jobs at $[Y] average, that's $[Z] in revenue. Our fee is a fraction of that. And you're currently spending $[W] on Angi with worse results. Does that change the equation?"

**"We've tried lead gen before and it didn't work"**
"I appreciate you sharing that. What did you try? ... [Listen] ... That's frustrating. Here's what we do differently: [specific differentiation - exclusive leads, pre-qualification, etc.]. We also offer a performance guarantee. Would that give you more confidence?"

**"I need to think about it"**
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timing, or do you have questions about how it works?"

**"We're happy with our current referral business"**
"That's great - referrals are the best leads. But referrals are unpredictable. What happens if they slow down? Our system gives you predictable, controllable lead flow on top of referrals. It's insurance, not replacement. Would starting small make sense?"

**[Present specific recommendation]**

**Direct Close:**
"Does this feel like the right next step for [Company Name]?"

"What would you need to see to move forward? Let's make a plan."

## Objection Handlers

### Price Objections

**"This is more than we're spending on Angi"**
"I hear you. You're probably spending $[X]/month on Angi, right? ... [Listen] ... And you're closing maybe 20-25% of those leads? So your cost per closed job is around $[Y]? With us, you'll close 60-70%, bringing your cost per closed job to $[Z]. You're actually saving money while getting more jobs. Does that make sense?"

**"We can't afford this right now"**
"I understand cash flow is tight. What if we started with the Starter package for 60 days? Lower investment, you see results, then we scale up. Or we could do performance-based pricing - you pay per closed job instead of monthly. What would work better?"

---

### Trust Objections

**"We've tried lead gen companies before"**
"I appreciate you sharing that. What happened? ... [Listen] ... That's frustrating. Here's what we do differently: we specialize in HVAC only, we guarantee results, and we provide exclusive leads. We're not a general marketing agency. Want to talk to one of our current clients?"

**"How do we know you can deliver?"**
"Great question. Here are 3 case studies from HVAC companies just like yours... [Share]. We also offer a performance guarantee - if we don't deliver [X] leads at [Y]% close rate, you don't pay. We're confident because we've done this [X] times."

---

### Timing Objections

**"We're too busy right now"**
"That's actually the best time to start - you can handle the leads you're getting now. But what about when slow season hits? If we start now, you'll have a system running when you NEED leads. When would be better?"

**"Let me think about it"**
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timing, or do you have questions about how it works? I want to make sure you have what you need."

---

### Competition Objections

**"We're happy with our referrals"**
"That's great - referrals are the best. But they're unpredictable. What happens if they slow down? Our system gives you predictable, controllable leads ON TOP of referrals. It's insurance, not replacement. Would starting with a small package make sense?"

**"We already have someone doing our marketing"**
"That's great. Are they guaranteeing exclusive, pre-qualified HVAC leads with a performance guarantee? ... [Listen] ... If not, we might be able to supplement or improve what you're getting. Would you be open to a comparison?"
