## Social Proof Placeholders

All templates include bracketed placeholders for:
- `[Client Name]` - Replace with actual client
- `[Industry]` - Prospect's industry
- `[X]%` - Specific metrics (close rates, growth, etc.)
- `$[X,XXX]` - Revenue/production numbers
- `[Time Period]` - Days/weeks/months for results
- `[Competitor Name]` - Local competitor for FOMO

**Best Practice:** Keep a running list of your best case studies with specific numbers. Update templates quarterly with fresh results.

## Usage Guidelines

### Before Sending
1. **Research the prospect** - Never send generic templates
2. **Personalize placeholders** - Replace ALL bracketed text
3. **Verify pain points** - Make sure the problem resonates
4. **Check timing** - Avoid holidays, end of quarter chaos

### Best Practices
- **Subject lines:** Test 2-3 variants per campaign
- **Send times:** Tue-Thu, 10am-2pm prospect timezone
- **Follow-up:** 80% of responses come from follow-ups, not initial email
- **Tracking:** Use a CRM or spreadsheet to track open/reply rates
- **Iteration:** Update templates based on what works

### A/B Testing
Test one variable at a time:
- Subject lines
- Opening hooks
- CTA phrasing
- Social proof placement
- Email length
