### Risk 1: Low Conversion Rates
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**

- Offer 14-day money-back guarantee
- Build social proof (testimonials) fast

### Risk 2: API Costs Spike
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Most APIs are free (Edge TTS, LipSync free tier)
- Build relationships with providers
- Self-host alternatives (Ollama, Stable Diffusion)

### Risk 3: Competition Copies Us
**Probability:** High
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage
- Community building (Discord, monthly calls)
- Continuous innovation (new workflows monthly)
- Brand building (you = trust)

### Risk 4: Delivery Bottlenecks
**Probability:** High
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Hire freelancers for DFY work
- Automate with n8n
- Raise DFY prices to control demand

### Risk 5: Platform Changes (TikTok/Instagram bans AI content)
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify across platforms
- Build email list (owned audience)
- Focus on evergreen content (recipes, books, courses)
