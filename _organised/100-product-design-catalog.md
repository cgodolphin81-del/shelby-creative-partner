## 🎨 DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Color Palettes (Organized by Theme)

**ADHD Designs:**
- Tech blues, terminal green
- Vibrant, high-energy colors
- Rainbow (representing scattered focus)
- Earth tones (grounding)

**Autism Designs:**
- Gold/yellow (autism pride)
- Rainbow infinity (neurodiversity)
- Calming blues and greens
- Bold, high contrast

**Sensory Designs:**
- Soft pastels (sensory-friendly)
- Calming palette (blues, greens)
- Warning colors (yellow/black for overload)
- Natural, earthy tones

**Pride Designs:**
- Rainbow infinity flag colors
- Iridescent, sparkly
- Deep space + rainbow
- Vibrant, celebratory

**Mental Health Designs:**
- Gentle pastels
- Growth greens
- Warm, nurturing colors
- Soft, comforting palette

**Community Designs:**
- Unity rainbow
- Warm, inclusive colors
- Bold, energetic
- Celebratory metallics

### Typography Guidelines

**DO USE:**
- Bold, readable fonts (accessibility first)
- Clear sans-serifs for main text
- Hand-drawn/organic for warmth
- High contrast for readability

**AVOID:**
- Script fonts that are hard to read
- Low contrast combinations
- Decorative fonts that reduce accessibility
- Crowded, cramped text

### Product Type Strategy

**High-Demand Products (all 100 designs):**
- Adult T-Shirt (Bella+Canvas 3001 or Comfort Colors 1717)
- Sticker (3x4" vinyl, pack of 3)
- Mug (11oz ceramic)

**Secondary Products (70% of designs):**
- Hoodie (Comfort Colors 1715)
- Tote Bag (canvas)
- Phone Case (silicone)

**Specialty Products (50% of designs):**
- Kids T-Shirt
- Laptop Sticker
- Poster (18x24")
- Tote Bag (if not in secondary)
