## 📊 PRICING TIER PSYCHOLOGY

### Why 3 Tiers Works Best

**Tier 1: Decoy/Low Anchor**
- Purpose: Make Tier 2 look reasonable
- Price: 30-40% of Tier 2
- Features: Limited, but functional
- Expected Conversion: 20-30%

**Tier 2: Target/Most Popular**
- Purpose: Main revenue driver
- Price: Your actual target price
- Features: Best value (highlight "Most Popular")
- Expected Conversion: 50-60%

**Tier 3: High Anchor/Premium**
- Purpose: Make Tier 2 feel affordable, capture high-end
- Price: 2-3x Tier 2
- Features: Everything + exclusivity
- Expected Conversion: 10-20%

**Example:**
```
Starter:     $5,000  (20% choose)
Professional: $15,000 ← "MOST POPULAR" (60% choose)
Enterprise:  $50,000 (20% choose)

Average Order Value: $18,000
```

## 📊 PRICE ELASTICITY GUIDE

### When to Raise Prices

| Signal | Action | Expected Impact |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| >50% conversion rate | Raise 20-30% | Minimal volume drop, +revenue |
| Consistently sold out | Raise 10-20% | Balance supply/demand |
| High LTV customers | Raise 15-25% | Better quality customers |
| Waitlist forming | Raise 20-40% | Capture maximum value |
| Competitors charging more | Raise 10-15% | Position as premium |

### When to Lower Prices

| Signal | Action | Expected Impact |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| <2% conversion rate | Lower 10-20% OR improve offer | Test price vs. value |
| High cart abandonment | Lower 10% OR add payment plans | Reduce friction |
| Competitors undercutting | Add lower tier (don't race to bottom) | Capture price-sensitive |
| Economic downturn | Offer payment plans (not price cuts) | Maintain value perception |

## 📊 A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

### Price Point Testing

**Test 1: Charm Pricing vs. Round Numbers**
- Variant A: $697
- Variant B: $700
- Hypothesis: Charm pricing converts better for consumer products
- Metric: Conversion rate, revenue per visitor

**Test 2: Anchor Positioning**
- Variant A: Show highest price first
- Variant B: Show lowest price first
- Hypothesis: High anchor increases average order value
- Metric: Average order value, tier distribution

**Test 3: Payment Plan Display**
- Variant A: Show monthly price ($167/mo)
- Variant B: Show total price ($5,000)
- Hypothesis: Monthly price reduces sticker shock
- Metric: Conversion rate, refund rate

**Test 4: Guarantee Wording**
- Variant A: "30-day money-back guarantee"
- Variant B: "Try risk-free for 30 days. If you don't [result], full refund + keep bonus"
- Hypothesis: Specific guarantee converts better
- Metric: Conversion rate, refund rate
