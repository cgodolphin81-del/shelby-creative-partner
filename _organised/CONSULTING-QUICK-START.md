## 📝 SALES SCRIPTS

### Discovery Call Framework (30 min)

**1. Rapport (3 min)**
- "Thanks for making time. How's your day going?"
- Brief personal connection

**2. Situation (7 min)**
- "Tell me about your business. What do you do?"
- "What's working well right now?"
- "What's your biggest challenge?"

**3. Problem (5 min)**
- "How is that affecting you?"
- "What happens if you don't solve this?"
- "How long has this been going on?"

**4. Vision (5 min)**
- "If you could wave a magic wand, what would change?"
- "What would that mean for your business? Your life?"

**5. Solution (5 min)**
- "Based on what you've told me, here's what I recommend..."
- Present Accelerator package
- Explain guarantee

**6. Close (5 min)**
- "The investment is $5,000. We have [X] spots left this month."
- "Does this feel like the right fit?"
- Handle objections
- Take payment

### Common Objections & Responses

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timing, or whether this will work for you?"

**"It's too expensive."**
> "I understand. Can I ask: compared to what? If this saves you 20 hours/week (that's $X at your rate) and helps you 2x revenue, what's that worth? The ROI is usually 10x within 90 days."

**"I've been burned by consultants before."**
> "I hear you. That's why we guarantee results. If we don't deliver, we work free until you get results. We only win when you win. Does that help?"

**"I need to talk to my [spouse/partner]."**
> "Absolutely. Let's schedule a 3-way call for [tomorrow]. I can answer their questions too. Does 2pm work?"
