# 📞 Discovery Call Script - AI Agency

**Purpose:** Qualify prospects, understand their pain, determine fit, book proposal meeting  
**Duration:** 30-45 minutes  
**Goal:** Move to proposal stage (not close on this call)

---

## Pre-Call Prep (5 minutes before)

### Research Checklist
- [ ] Review their company website
- [ ] Check their LinkedIn profile
- [ ] Google recent news/press releases
- [ ] Note 2-3 specific observations
- [ ] Have calendar ready for next meeting

### Tech Setup
- [ ] Test audio/video
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs
- [ ] Have CRM/note-taking ready
- [ ] Water/coffee nearby

---

## Call Structure

| Phase | Time | Purpose |
|-------|------|---------|
| Rapport Building | 3-5 min | Build trust, set agenda |
| Discovery | 15-20 min | Understand pain, impact, goals |
| Solution Preview | 5-10 min | Show how AI solves their problem |
| Next Steps | 5 min | Book proposal meeting |

---

## Phase 1: Rapport Building (3-5 min)

### Opening Script
```
"Hey [FIRST NAME], thanks for making time today! How's your [day/week] going?"

[Listen and respond naturally]

"Before we dive in, let me quickly set the agenda for our call:

1. I want to understand what's going on at [COMPANY] and what challenges you're facing
2. I'll share a bit about how we help companies like yours with AI automation
3. If it makes sense, we'll schedule a follow-up to discuss specifics

Sound good?

Great! So tell me, what prompted you to book this call?"
```

### Key Questions
- "How's your [day/week] going?"
- "What prompted you to book this call?"
- "What's top of mind for you right now at [COMPANY]?"

---

## Phase 2: Discovery (15-20 min)

### Current State Questions

```
"Walk me through how you currently handle [SPECIFIC PROCESS]."

"What tools/systems are you using for this today?"

"How many people are involved in this process?"

"How much time does this typically take?"
```

### Pain Point Questions

```
"What's the most frustrating part of this process?"

"If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"

"How is this impacting your business? (revenue, costs, time, customer satisfaction)"

"What happens when this doesn't work well?"
```

### Impact Questions

```
"How much time/money do you think this is costing you monthly?"

"What would it mean for your business if this was solved?"

"If you don't solve this, what happens in 6-12 months?"

"Who else is impacted by this problem?"
```

### Decision Process Questions

```
"Have you explored solutions for this before? What happened?"

"What's your ideal timeline for solving this?"

"Who else is involved in decisions like this?"

"What would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer?"
```

### Budget Questions (Subtle)

```
"What kind of investment were you expecting for a solution like this?"

"Have you allocated budget for this initiative?"

"For context, our clients typically invest $10K-$50K depending on scope. Does that align with what you were thinking?"
```

---

## Phase 3: Solution Preview (5-10 min)

### Transition Script
```
"Based on what you've shared, I think we can definitely help. Let me give you a quick overview of how we'd approach this..."
```

### Solution Framework

```
"Here's how we'd tackle this:

**Phase 1: Discovery & Design (Week 1-2)**
- Deep dive into your current workflows
- Map out automation opportunities
- Design the AI agent architecture
- Get your team's input and approval

**Phase 2: Build & Train (Week 3-5)**
- Build the AI agents
- Train on your data, processes, brand voice
- Integrate with your existing tools
- Test internally

**Phase 3: Launch & Optimize (Week 6-8)**
- Soft launch with small user group
- Gather feedback, make adjustments
- Full rollout to your team/customers
- Ongoing optimization and support

**Expected Outcomes:**
- [SPECIFIC OUTCOME 1 based on their pain]
- [SPECIFIC OUTCOME 2]
- [SPECIFIC OUTCOME 3]

Does this approach resonate with you?"
```

### Social Proof (Tailored to Their Industry)

```
"This is similar to what we did for [SIMILAR COMPANY]. They were [THEIR PAIN POINT], and after implementing our AI automation:

- [METRIC 1]: X → Y
- [METRIC 2]: Saved Z hours/week
- [METRIC 3]: $RESULT impact

Happy to connect you with them if you'd like a reference."
```

---

## Phase 4: Next Steps (5 min)

### Transition Script
```
"I think there's a strong fit here. Here's what I'd recommend..."
```

### Next Steps Script

```
"Here's what I'd suggest:

1. I'll put together a detailed proposal with:
   - Specific scope based on what we discussed
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Investment breakdown
   - Expected ROI

2. We'll schedule a 30-minute follow-up to:
   - Walk through the proposal
   - Answer any questions
   - Discuss next steps if you want to move forward

3. If you decide to move forward, we can kick off within [TIMEFRAME].

Does that work for you?

Great! Let me grab my calendar..."
```

### Booking the Follow-Up

```
"I have [OPTIONS] available. Which works better for you?"

[Book the meeting, send calendar invite]

"Perfect! I'll send over the proposal by [DATE/TIME]. You'll get a calendar invite for our follow-up shortly.

Anything else you need from me before then?"
```

### Closing Script

```
"Awesome. Really enjoyed our conversation, [FIRST NAME]. I'm excited about the potential here.

I'll send the proposal by [DATE], and we'll chat on [FOLLOW-UP DATE].

In the meantime, if any questions come up, just shoot me an email.

Talk soon!"
```

## Objection Handlers

### "This is too expensive"
```
"I totally get that. This is a significant investment. Can I ask—compared to what?

Because if we're talking about cost, sure, there are cheaper options. But if we're talking about investment and ROI...

Based on what you shared, you're currently losing $[X] per month to [PAIN POINT]. This solution pays for itself in [Y] months.

Does that math make sense?"
```

### "We need to think about it"
```
"Of course! This is an important decision. Can I ask—what specifically do you need to think through?

Is it the investment, the timeline, or whether this is the right fit?

[Listen to their concern]

Totally fair. Here's what I'd suggest: Let's [ADDRESS THEIR CONCERN]. And if after [TIMEFRAME] you're still not sure, no pressure at all.

But I don't want you to miss out on [URGENCY ELEMENT].

What do you think?"
```

### "We need to talk to [OTHER PERSON]"
```
"Absolutely, that makes sense. Who else needs to be involved in this decision?

[Listen]

Great. Here's what I'd suggest: Let's include them in our follow-up call. That way, we can answer their questions directly and make sure everyone's aligned.

When would work for all of you?"
```

### "Send me info first"
```
"Happy to send over some info! Just so I send the most relevant stuff, what specifically would be most helpful?

- Case studies from your industry?
- Technical documentation?
- Pricing breakdown?
- Something else?

[Send what they ask for]

And would it make sense to schedule a quick follow-up after you've had a chance to review? That way I can answer any questions that come up."
```

### "We're already working with [COMPETITOR]"
```
"That's great! How's that going for you?

[Listen—often they'll share frustrations]

Interesting. What made you decide to explore other options?

[Listen]

Got it. Well, I won't badmouth the competition, but I will say this: Here's what makes us different...

[SHARE DIFFERENTIATORS]

Would it be worth a conversation to compare approaches? No pressure to switch, but at least you'll know your options."
```

## Red Flags (When to Disqualify)

🚩 **No clear pain point** - "Just curious"  
🚩 **No budget** - "We have no money but..."  
🚩 **No decision-maker** - "I need to run this by 12 people"  
🚩 **Unrealistic expectations** - "Can you do this for $500?"  
🚩 **Not a priority** - "Maybe in 6 months"  
🚩 **Already have a solution** - "We're happy with [X]"  

**Polite Exit Script:**
```
"Honestly, [FIRST NAME], based on what you've shared, I'm not sure we're the right fit right now.

[REASON: no budget / not a priority / already have solution]

I don't want to waste your time. But if things change in the future, feel free to reach out.

Wishing you continued success with [COMPANY]!"
```

---

## Green Flags (When to Move Forward)

✅ **Clear pain point** - "This is costing us $X/month"  
✅ **Decision-maker** - "I can make this decision"  
✅ **Budget aligned** - "We have $X allocated"  
✅ **Timeline urgent** - "Need this in 30 days"  
✅ **Previous attempts failed** - "We tried X, Y, Z—didn't work"  
✅ **Trust built** - "I like your approach"
