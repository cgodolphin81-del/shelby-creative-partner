## 💡 Pro Tips

### Cold Email
- **Personalize the first line** - Reference something specific
- **Keep it under 150 words** - Brevity is respect
- **Follow up 4-5 times** - Most replies come on email 3-4
- **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm** - Best open rates

### Discovery Calls
- **Listen 70%, talk 30%** - Let them sell themselves
- **Ask about impact** - "What's this costing you?"
- **Book the next meeting** - Don't end without a clear next step
- **Send proposal within 24 hours** - Strike while iron is hot

### Proposals
- **Customize based on call** - Use their language, their numbers
- **Show ROI clearly** - Make it undeniable
- **Include case studies** - Social proof matters
- **Follow up within 48 hours** - Don't let it go cold

### Delivery
- **Under-promise, over-deliver** - Set realistic expectations
- **Communicate weekly** - No surprises
- **Get quick wins early** - Build momentum
- **Document everything** - For scaling and handoff

## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Send generic, unpersonalized emails
- Skip the discovery call (always qualify first)
- Underprice your services (you're delivering 10x ROI)
- Over-promise on timeline or outcomes
- Skip the contract (always have one)
- Start build without client approval on design
- Ghost prospects who don't respond (follow up!)

### ✅ Do:
- Personalize every outreach message
- Use the discovery call script (it works)
- Price based on value, not hours
- Set realistic expectations
- Use the contract template
- Get design approval before building
- Follow up persistently but professionally
