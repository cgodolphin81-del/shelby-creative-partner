## Design Tips for One-Pager

### Layout
- **Keep it clean:** Lots of white space, easy to scan
- **Use visuals:** Icons, simple diagrams, charts
- **Highlight key numbers:** ROI, time savings, costs
- **Make CTA prominent:** "Book Discovery Call" button

### Branding
- **Use your colors:** Consistent with website
- **Include logo:** Top of page
- **Professional fonts:** Easy to read
- **High-quality graphics:** No pixelated images

### Format Options
- **PDF:** Easy to email, print-friendly
- **Landing page:** SEO benefits, trackable
- **Print:** For in-person meetings
- **Interactive:** Clickable links, embedded video

### Distribution
- **Email:** After discovery call
- **Website:** Downloadable resource
- **LinkedIn:** Share as carousel
- **Proposals:** Include as appendix
- **Print:** Leave-behind for meetings
