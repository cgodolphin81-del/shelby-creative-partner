# LinkedIn Outreach Templates - AI Agency

**5 Outreach Variants for Different Scenarios**

Each variant includes connection request + 3 follow-up messages.

---

## How to Use

1. **Personalize every message** - Reference something specific from their profile
2. **Send connection requests with notes** - 300 character limit
3. **Follow up 2-3 times** - Most responses come on follow-up 2-3
4. **Engage with their content first** - Like/comment before reaching out

---

## Variant 1: The "Noticed Something Specific" Approach

**Best for:** When you can find a specific pain point or recent post

### Connection Request (300 chars max)
```
[FIRST NAME], noticed your post about [TOPIC] and thought you'd find value in connecting. We help [INDUSTRY] companies automate [SPECIFIC PAIN] with AI. Would love to share what we're seeing in the space.
```

### Follow-up 1 (After they accept)
```
Thanks for connecting, [FIRST NAME]!

Saw your post about [SPECIFIC TOPIC]—really resonated. Curious: how is [COMPANY] currently handling [SPECIFIC PAIN POINT]?

We've been helping similar companies automate this with AI and seeing 40-60% time savings. Happy to share what's working if useful.

No pitch, just insights from the field.
```

### Follow-up 2 (3-4 days later)
```
[FIRST NAME], quick thought:

Most [INDUSTRY] leaders we talk to are struggling with [PAIN POINT]. They're spending 20+ hours/week on what could be automated.

We helped [SIMILAR COMPANY] reduce this by 73% in 30 days.

Worth a 15-min chat to see if there's a fit for [COMPANY]?
```

### Follow-up 3 (7 days later)
```
[FIRST NAME], last ping on this.

If automating [PAIN POINT] isn't a priority right now, no worries at all. I'll stop reaching out.

But if it is, happy to show you how we helped [SIMILAR COMPANY] save 25 hours/week with AI.

Either way, wish you continued success!
```

## Best Practices

### ✅ DO:
- **Personalize every message** - Reference their profile, posts, or company
- **Keep it conversational** - Write like you're talking to a human
- **Provide value first** - Share insights, not just pitches
- **Follow up 2-3 times** - Most responses come on follow-up 2-3
- **Engage with their content** - Like/comment before reaching out
- **Use their name** - But don't overdo it (once per message max)

### ❌ DON'T:
- **Send generic templates** - Personalize or don't send
- **Pitch in the connection request** - Build rapport first
- **Write novels** - Keep messages under 150 words
- **Sound like a bot** - Be conversational, human
- **Give up after 1 message** - Persistence pays off
- **Be pushy** - Respect their time and decision
