AI AGENCY - DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT
45 minutes. Follow this exactly.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRE-CALL PREP (15 min before call)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Research their company (website, LinkedIn, news)
□ Identify 3-5 automatable processes in their industry
□ Have proposal template open
□ Have Calendly ready for booking follow-up
□ Prepare 2-3 specific automation ideas for them

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPENING (Minutes 0-3)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "Thanks for making time, [FIRST NAME]. I know you're busy.

Before I jump into anything, I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you. Can I start by asking a few questions about [COMPANY NAME]'s current operations and where you're feeling the most pressure?"

[WAIT FOR YES]

YOU: "Perfect. Two things upfront:
1. This is about you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll be honest. Fair?"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
QUALIFYING QUESTIONS (Minutes 3-15)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CURRENT OPERATIONS:

1. "Walk me through a typical week - what processes consume the most of your team's time?"

2. "What's the one thing your team complains about most?"

3. "How are you currently handling [SPECIFIC PROCESS RELEVANT TO THEIR INDUSTRY]?"

PAIN POINTS:

4. "If you could eliminate one operational headache overnight, what would it be?"

5. "Where are you losing money due to inefficiency or errors?"

6. "What's preventing you from scaling faster?"

AI AWARENESS:

7. "What's your current approach to AI and automation?"

8. "Have you tried any AI tools before? What worked, what didn't?"

DECISION PROCESS:

9. "Who else would be involved in a decision like this?"

10. "What would success look like 90 days from now?"

[TAKE NOTES. Reference these later.]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRESENTATION (Minutes 15-30)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "[FIRST NAME], thank you for sharing all that. Based on what you've told me, here's what I'm seeing..."

[SHARE SCREEN - SHOW SIMPLE DECK OR DOCUMENT]

1. THE PROBLEM (3 min)

"You're spending [X] hours/week on [PROCESS]. At your team's cost, that's $[X]/month on work that doesn't require human judgment.

And I bet your team would rather be doing [HIGHER-VALUE WORK] than [REPETITIVE TASK]."

2. THE OPPORTUNITY (3 min)

"AI can automate 70-80% of this. That's [X] hours/week back, [SPECIFIC BUSINESS IMPACT].

Your team gets to focus on high-value work. You reduce costs. Everyone wins."

3. OUR APPROACH (5 min)

"We use a 3-phase methodology:

PHASE 1 - DISCOVER (Week 1-2)
• Process mapping and documentation
• Automation opportunity assessment
• ROI modeling for each opportunity
• Technology stack selection

PHASE 2 - DEPLOY (Week 3-6)
• AI system development
• Integration with existing tools
• Testing and quality assurance
• Team training and onboarding

PHASE 3 - SCALE (Week 7-12)
• Performance monitoring
• Iterative improvements
• Additional automation deployment
• Advanced feature rollout"

4. PROOF (4 min)

[SHOW CASE STUDY SLIDE]

"We've done this for [X] companies. Here's a recent example:

[CLIENT] was drowning in [PROCESS]. 45 days after our automation:
• 87% automated
• 35 hours/week reclaimed
• Zero errors
• 340% ROI in first quarter"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HANDLING OBJECTIONS (Minutes 30-38)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BE READY FOR THESE:

OBJECTION 1: "We need to think about it"

YOU: "I understand. Can I ask - what specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timeline, or something else?"

OBJECTION 2: "This sounds expensive"

YOU: "What number are you thinking? ... [LISTEN] ... I hear you. Here's the framing: our clients typically see payback in [X] weeks. After that, it's pure margin. Does that change the equation?"

OBJECTION 3: "We don't have time for implementation"

YOU: "That's the beauty of our approach - we handle everything. Your team spends maybe 3-4 hours total for onboarding and training. We do the heavy lifting. What part concerns you most?"

OBJECTION 4: "Send me information"

YOU: "Absolutely. To make it relevant, what's the one thing you'd need to see to feel confident moving forward?"

[This reveals the REAL objection.]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CLOSE (Minutes 38-45)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "[FIRST NAME], I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

[PRESENT SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATION]

"For a company at your stage, I'd recommend our [PACKAGE NAME] package. This includes:

• Full automation audit
• [X] processes automated
• Full build, deploy, and train
• 30-day post-launch support
• Ongoing optimization

The investment is $[XX,XXX] one-time (or $[X,XXX]/month for 3 months). Most clients see payback within [X] weeks.

[PAUSE. Let them respond.]

Does this feel like the right next step for [COMPANY NAME]?"

[IF YES]

YOU: "Great. Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send over the proposal today
2. You review it - any questions, just ask
3. We sign and schedule kickoff
4. We start the audit within 48 hours

I can have the proposal to you within 2 hours. What's the best email?"

[IF THEY NEED TO THINK]

YOU: "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timeline, or something about the approach?"

[ADDRESS THE CONCERN. Then:]

YOU: "Tell you what - let me send the proposal so you have something concrete to review. No obligation. We can schedule a follow-up call for [DAY] to answer any questions. Does that work?"

[BOOK THE FOLLOW-UP BEFORE HANGING UP]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
POST-CALL ACTIONS (Within 1 hour)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Send personalized email recapping the call
□ Attach proposal
□ Include Calendly for follow-up
□ Log all notes in CRM
□ Set reminder for follow-up date
