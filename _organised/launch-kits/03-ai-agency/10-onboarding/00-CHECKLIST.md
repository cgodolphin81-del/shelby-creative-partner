## Kickoff Call (Week 1)

### ✅ Agenda (60 minutes)

**Introductions (10 min)**
- [ ] All team members introduced
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities clarified
- [ ] Communication preferences confirmed

**Project Overview (15 min)**
- [ ] Review project goals and success metrics
- [ ] Confirm scope from Statement of Work
- [ ] Review timeline and milestones
- [ ] Discuss any concerns or questions

**Current State Deep-Dive (20 min)**
- [ ] Walk through current workflows
- [ ] Identify pain points and bottlenecks
- [ ] Review existing tools and systems
- [ ] Discuss data availability and access

**Next Steps (10 min)**
- [ ] Confirm immediate action items
- [ ] Assign owners to each task
- [ ] Set expectations for Week 1-2
- [ ] Schedule follow-up meetings

**Q&A (5 min)**
- [ ] Address any remaining questions
- [ ] Confirm communication cadence
- [ ] Share contact information

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Client is slow to provide access
**Solution:**
- Send reminder email with clear list of what's needed
- Offer to schedule a 15-min call to walk through it
- Escalate to decision-maker if still delayed
- Document delay and adjust timeline accordingly

### Issue: Stakeholders unavailable for interviews
**Solution:**
- Request alternative times
- Offer asynchronous options (written Q&A)
- Proceed with available stakeholders and fill gaps later
- Document as a risk

### Issue: Scope creep during discovery
**Solution:**
- Refer back to Statement of Work
- Document new requests as change orders
- Explain impact on timeline and budget
- Get written approval before proceeding

### Issue: Client has unrealistic expectations
**Solution:**
- Revisit success metrics and timeline
- Provide examples from similar projects
- Explain technical constraints clearly
- Adjust expectations or scope as needed
