## Tips for Gathering Case Study Data

### Before Implementation
- [ ] Document baseline metrics
- [ ] Get client buy-in on measurement plan
- [ ] Set up tracking/analytics
- [ ] Schedule check-in calls

### During Implementation
- [ ] Capture screenshots/photos
- [ ] Record team feedback
- [ ] Document challenges and solutions
- [ ] Track quick wins

### After Implementation
- [ ] Measure all metrics (same as baseline)
- [ ] Collect testimonials (video + written)
- [ ] Calculate ROI
- [ ] Get client approval on case study

### testimonial Questions to Ask
1. "What was the biggest challenge before working with us?"
2. "What was your experience working with our team?"
3. "What results have you seen since implementation?"
4. "What would you tell someone considering this?"
5. "What's been the most surprising benefit?"
