# 📧 Cold Email Templates - AI Agency

**10 Industry-Specific Cold Email Sequences**

Each sequence includes 5 emails (initial + 4 follow-ups) tailored to specific industries.

---

## How to Use

1. **Customize placeholders** before sending:
   - `[FIRST NAME]` → Prospect's first name
   - `[COMPANY]` → Company name
   - `[SPECIFIC PAIN]` → Industry-specific pain point
   - `[YOUR NAME]` → Your name
   - `[CALENDLY LINK]` → Your scheduling link

2. **Send timing:**
   - Email 1: Day 1 (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm)
   - Email 2: Day 3 (if no response)
   - Email 3: Day 7
   - Email 4: Day 14
   - Email 5: Day 21 (breakup email)

3. **Personalization is key:**
   - Reference something specific about their company
   - Mention a recent news item or achievement
   - Show you've done your homework

## Email Structure

Each email follows this framework:

**Subject Line:** Intriguing, specific, under 50 characters

**Opening:** Personalized hook (not "I hope this finds you well")

**Problem:** Specific pain point they experience

**Solution:** How AI solves it (brief, benefit-focused)

**Social Proof:** One relevant case study or stat

**CTA:** Single, clear call-to-action

**Signature:** Simple, human

---

## Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Keep emails under 150 words
- Send from a personal email (not info@)
- Use plain text (no HTML templates)
- Follow up persistently (most replies come on email 3-4)
- Track opens/clicks

❌ **Don't:**
- Send attachments on first email
- Use salesy language ("game-changing", "revolutionary")
- Ask for too much (just a 15-min call)
- Sound like a bot (be conversational)
- Give up after 1-2 emails
