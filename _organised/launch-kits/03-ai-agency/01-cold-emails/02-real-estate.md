- Focus on tenant inquiries, maintenance requests
- Mention screening, lease signing automation

- Focus on investor qualification, property tours

- Focus on white-glove experience, qualified buyers only

## Objection Handlers

**"Our agents prefer to handle leads personally"**
→ "Totally get that. The AI handles the initial response and qualification, then warm, qualified leads go to your agents. They still close every deal—just less time chasing tire-kickers."

**"We already use a CRM"**
→ "Great! Most CRMs have basic automation, but our AI agents actually have conversations, answer questions, and book showings. It's the difference between a form and a conversation."

**"We're worried about leads feeling like they're talking to a bot"**
→ "Our AI is trained on your brand voice and sounds completely natural. Most leads don't realize they're talking to AI—and honestly, they just want fast answers."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, implementation, or whether this fits your workflow?"
