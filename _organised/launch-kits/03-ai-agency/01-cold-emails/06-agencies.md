## Objection Handlers

**"We already use reporting tools (AgencyAnalytics, DashThis, etc.)"**
→ "Great! Those tools pull data, but they don't communicate with clients. Our AI sends personalized updates, answers questions about the data, and schedules follow-ups. It's the difference between a dashboard and a conversation."

**"Our clients want to talk to humans"**
→ "For strategy and big-picture stuff, absolutely. But for 'what's my ROAS this week?' or 'when's our next call?', clients want instant answers. The AI handles routine comms, humans handle strategy."

**"We're worried about clients feeling like they're getting less attention"**
→ "It's the opposite. Clients get MORE communication (proactive updates, instant answers), and your team has MORE time for strategic work. Everyone wins."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, integration with your tools, or something else?"
