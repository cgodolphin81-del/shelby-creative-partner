## Customization Notes

**For Early-Stage Startups:**
- Focus on rapid iteration, limited resources
- Mention doing more with less, scaling efficiently

**For Growth-Stage SaaS:**
- Focus on optimization, unit economics
- Mention LTV:CAC, conversion rates, efficiency

**For Enterprise SaaS:**
- Focus on security, compliance, integrations
- Mention SOC 2, SSO, enterprise features

**For PLG (Product-Led Growth):**
- Focus on in-product experience, self-serve
- Mention activation, expansion, retention

## Objection Handlers

**"We already have in-app messaging (Intercom, Drift, etc.)"**
→ "Great! Those tools send messages, but they don't have intelligent conversations. Our AI understands context, answers complex questions, and takes action (books demos, triggers workflows). It's the difference between a chatbot and a conversation."

**"We're focused on enterprise sales, not self-serve"**
→ "Perfect. The AI qualifies and nurtures inbound leads, so your sales team only talks to enterprise-ready prospects. Less time on unqualified demos, more time on big deals."

**"We need to integrate with our product"**
→ "Absolutely. We integrate with your stack (Segment, Mixpanel, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.) and can do in-app, email, or SMS. Happy to discuss technical requirements."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Integration complexity, ROI timeline, or something else?"
