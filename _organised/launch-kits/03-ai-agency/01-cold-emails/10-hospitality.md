## Objection Handlers

**"We already have a chatbot on our website"**
→ "Most hotels do! The difference is our AI actually understands context and can handle complex requests (booking spa treatments, special requests, local recommendations), not just FAQ keywords."

**"Our guests prefer talking to humans"**
→ "For complex issues, absolutely. But for 'what time is check-in?' or 'do you have vegan options?', most guests want instant answers. The AI handles routine stuff, staff focuses on high-touch service."

**"We're worried about losing the personal touch"**
→ "It's the opposite. By automating routine questions, your staff has MORE time for genuine human interactions—welcoming guests, handling special requests, creating memorable experiences."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, implementation timeline, or something specific to your property?"
