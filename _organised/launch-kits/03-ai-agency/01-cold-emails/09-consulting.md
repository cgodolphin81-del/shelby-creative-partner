## Objection Handlers

**"Our work is too customized for automation"**
→ "The AI handles the standardized parts (intake, scheduling, proposal generation), so your consultants have MORE time for the customized, high-value work. It amplifies your expertise, not replaces it."

**"Our clients expect high-touch service"**
→ "Absolutely—and they get it. The AI handles routine coordination, so your consultants have MORE time for strategic thinking and client relationships. It enhances the experience."

**"We already have a CRM"**
→ "Great! CRMs store data, but they don't have conversations or automate workflows. Our AI integrates with your CRM and adds intelligent automation on top."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, implementation timeline, or something specific to your practice?"
