- Mention compliance, lot tracking, expiration dates

**For Distribution/Wholesale:**
- Focus on inventory management, order fulfillment

## Objection Handlers

**"We already have an ERP system"**
→ "Great! ERPs store data, but they don't make decisions or coordinate workflows. Our AI integrates with your ERP and adds intelligent automation on top. It's the difference between a database and a decision-maker."

**"Our processes are too complex for AI"**
→ "We've worked with manufacturers across all complexities. The AI learns your specific workflows, constraints, and rules. It doesn't replace your expertise—it amplifies it."

**"We're concerned about downtime during implementation"**
→ "Implementation is phased and non-disruptive. We start with one workflow (usually scheduling or inventory), prove value, then expand. Your operations continue normally throughout."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, integration with your systems, or something specific to your operations?"
