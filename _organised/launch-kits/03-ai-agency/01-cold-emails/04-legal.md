## Objection Handlers

**"We're concerned about giving legal advice via AI"**
→ "Completely agree—that's not what this does. The AI handles intake, scheduling, and general process questions. All legal advice comes from your attorneys. We're very clear about this in the conversation."

**"Our clients want to talk to humans"**
→ "For actual legal matters, absolutely. But for initial intake, scheduling, and process questions, most clients want instant answers. The AI qualifies and schedules—attorneys still handle every consultation."

**"We already have a website form"**
→ "Forms have 20-30% completion rates. Conversational AI has 80%+ completion. Plus, you get qualified leads with context, not just a name and email."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Is it the investment, implementation, or something specific to your practice?"
