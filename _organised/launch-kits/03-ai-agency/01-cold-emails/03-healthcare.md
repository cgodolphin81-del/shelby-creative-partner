## Objection Handlers

**"We're concerned about HIPAA compliance"**
→ "Completely understandable. Our platform is fully HIPAA-compliant, we sign BAAs, and all data is encrypted. Happy to walk through our compliance documentation."

**"Our patients prefer talking to humans"**
→ "For complex issues, absolutely. But for scheduling, reminders, and basic questions, most patients prefer instant self-service. The AI handles routine stuff, humans handle complex care."

**"We already have a patient portal"**
→ "Great! Most portals are clunky and patients don't use them. Our AI meets patients where they are (SMS, email) and makes it conversational. Much higher engagement."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What's the main concern? Compliance, implementation timeline, or something else?"
