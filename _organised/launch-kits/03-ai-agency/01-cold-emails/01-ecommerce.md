## Customization Notes

**For Fashion/Apparel Brands:**
- Mention size guides, styling questions, return rates
- Reference seasonal collections, launch cycles

**For Electronics/Tech:**
- Mention technical specs, compatibility questions, warranty info
- Reference product complexity, troubleshooting

**For Home & Garden:**
- Mention assembly questions, care instructions, shipping (large items)
- Reference seasonal demand spikes

**For Health & Beauty:**
- Mention ingredient questions, usage instructions, subscription management
- Reference compliance considerations

## Objection Handlers

**"We already have a chatbot"**
→ "Most brands do! The difference is our AI agents actually understand context and can handle complex queries, not just keyword matching. Happy to show you the difference."

**"We're worried about losing the human touch"**
→ "Totally get that. Our AI handles the routine stuff (80% of tickets), so your team can focus on the complex, high-touch issues that actually need humans."

**"We don't have budget for this"**
→ "What's the cost of your current setup? Most brands find the ROI is immediate—reduced support costs + increased conversions from faster responses."

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the implementation timeline, or whether this is the right fit?"
