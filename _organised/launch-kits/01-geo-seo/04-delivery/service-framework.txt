GEO SEO SERVICE FRAMEWORK
Version 1.0 | [YOUR COMPANY]

This document outlines exactly how we deliver GEO SEO services.
Follow this framework for every client.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 1: AUDIT & STRATEGY (Weeks 1-2)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DELIVERABLES:
□ Comprehensive GEO Audit Report
□ Competitor Citation Analysis
□ Strategic Roadmap Document
□ Keyword/Query Map

TOOLS WE USE:
• Manual AI queries (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI)
• [Any GEO tools you use]
• Ahrefs/SEMrush for baseline SEO data
• Google Analytics/Search Console

STEP-BY-STEP:

DAY 1-2: Baseline Audit
1. Query each AI platform with client's top 20 keywords
2. Document whether client appears in responses
3. Note position and context of any citations
4. Screenshot key findings
5. Calculate AI Visibility Score (% of queries with citation)

DAY 3-4: Competitor Analysis
1. Identify 3-5 main competitors
2. Run same 20 keywords for each competitor
3. Document where competitors win citations
4. Identify patterns in competitor content
5. Note authority signals competitors have

DAY 5-7: Content Gap Analysis
1. Review client's existing content
2. Identify content not optimized for AI extraction
3. Note missing structured data
4. Identify answer-format opportunities
5. List pages needing optimization

DAY 8-10: Strategy Development
1. Prioritize opportunities by impact/effort
2. Create 90-day roadmap
3. Define success metrics
4. Set up tracking dashboard
5. Prepare client presentation

DAY 11-14: Client Review
1. Present findings on strategy call
2. Get approval on roadmap
3. Begin Phase 2

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 2: OPTIMIZATION (Weeks 3-8)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DELIVERABLES:
□ Optimized Pages (10-20 pages)
□ Structured Data Implementation
□ Content Restructuring
□ Technical GEO Fixes

ON-PAGE OPTIMIZATION CHECKLIST:

□ Add FAQ schema to relevant pages
□ Implement Organization schema
□ Add Article schema to blog posts
□ Ensure clear H1/H2 hierarchy
□ Create direct answer paragraphs (40-60 words)
□ Add definition sections for key terms
□ Include statistics and data points
□ Optimize meta descriptions for AI extraction
□ Add internal links with descriptive anchor text
□ Ensure mobile-friendliness

CONTENT RESTRUCTURING:

For each priority page:
1. Add "What is [X]" section at top
2. Create FAQ section with 5-10 questions
3. Add statistics/data section
4. Include "How to" or step-by-step sections
5. Add comparison tables where relevant
6. Include expert quotes/citations
7. End with clear summary/conclusion

AUTHORITY SIGNAL AMPLIFICATION:

□ Identify 10 target publications for PR
□ Pitch client for expert quotes (HARO, Qwoted)
□ Submit to industry directories
□ Optimize Google Business Profile
□ Ensure NAP consistency across web
□ Build/uncover existing partnerships
□ Identify podcast opportunities

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 3: AMPLIFICATION (Weeks 5-12)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DELIVERABLES:
□ 4-8 Authority Placements
□ Content Distribution
□ Thought Leadership Assets

AUTHORITY BUILDING TACTICS:

1. Guest Posts
   • Target: Industry publications with high AI citation rates
   • Volume: 2-4 per month
   • Focus: Educational content with data/insights

2. Expert Quotes
   • Platforms: HARO, Qwoted, Terkel
   • Goal: 5-10 quotes per month
   • Result: Backlinks + brand mentions

3. Original Research
   • Create industry survey or data study
   • Publish as report
   • Pitch to media
   • AI engines cite original data heavily

4. Podcast Appearances
   • Target: Industry podcasts
   • Pitch client executives as guests
   • Show notes create citations

5. Community Engagement
   • Industry forums (Reddit, specialized forums)
   • Quora answers
   • LinkedIn thought leadership
   • All create citation opportunities

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PHASE 4: MONITORING & ITERATION (Ongoing)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DELIVERABLES:
□ Weekly Performance Reports
□ Monthly Strategy Reviews
□ Continuous Optimization

WEEKLY TRACKING:

Every Monday:
1. Run 20 keywords through each AI platform
2. Document citation changes
3. Note new competitors in results
4. Check Google Analytics for AI-referred traffic
5. Update dashboard

WEEKLY REPORT INCLUDES:
• AI Visibility Score (trend)
• Citations gained/lost
• Top performing queries
• Competitor movements
• Traffic from AI sources (if trackable)
• Wins and highlights
• Next week's priorities

MONTHLY STRATEGY CALL:

Agenda (45 min):
1. Review monthly performance (10 min)
2. Discuss wins and challenges (10 min)
3. Approve next month's priorities (15 min)
4. Q&A (10 min)

QUARTERLY BUSINESS REVIEW:

Every 90 days:
1. Full performance review
2. ROI analysis
3. Strategy adjustment
4. Renewal discussion
