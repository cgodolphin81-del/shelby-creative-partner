GEO SEO - DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT
45 minutes. Follow this exactly.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRE-CALL PREP (Do this 15 min before call)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Pull their GEO audit
□ Check their website for current SEO/content
□ Identify 2-3 competitors in AI search
□ Note their top 3 services from website
□ Have proposal template open
□ Have Calendly ready for booking follow-up

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPENING (Minutes 0-3)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "Thanks for making time, [FIRST NAME]. I know your calendar is packed.

Before I jump into anything, I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you. Can I ask a few quick questions about where [COMPANY NAME] is at with AI search visibility?"

[WAIT FOR YES]

YOU: "Perfect. Two quick things:

1. This call is about you - I'll spend most of the time listening
2. If it makes sense to work together, I'll tell you. If not, I'll tell you that too. Fair?"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
QUALIFYING QUESTIONS (Minutes 3-12)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CURRENT STATE:

1. "How is [COMPANY NAME] currently thinking about AI search - ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews?"

2. "Have you noticed any changes in organic traffic or lead quality over the past 6 months?"

3. "What's your current approach to SEO and content marketing?"

PAIN POINTS:

4. "What's your biggest frustration with your current marketing results?"

5. "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about your lead generation, what would it be?"

DECISION PROCESS:

6. "Have you explored any solutions for AI search optimization before? What happened?"

7. "Who else is involved in decisions like this at [COMPANY NAME]?"

8. "What would success look like 90 days from now?"

[TAKE NOTES. Reference these later in the call.]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRESENTATION (Minutes 12-25)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm seeing..."

[SHARE SCREEN - SHOW GEO AUDIT]

1. CURRENT STATE (2 min)

"Right now, you appear in [X]% of relevant AI responses. Your competitors are at [Y]%.

The gap isn't your content quality - I reviewed your site and it's solid. It's optimization for how AI engines actually work. They use different signals than Google."

2. THE OPPORTUNITY (3 min)

"Companies that optimize for GEO typically see 3-5x more AI citations within 90 days.

For you specifically, based on your [AVERAGE DEAL VALUE] deal size and [CURRENT LEAD VOLUME], capturing AI citations could mean [X] additional qualified leads per month.

That's approximately $[X,XXX] in monthly pipeline."

3. OUR APPROACH (5 min)

[SHOW SIMPLE SLIDE OR DOCUMENT]

"We use a 4-phase methodology:

PHASE 1 - AUDIT (Weeks 1-2)
• Comprehensive GEO audit across 5 AI platforms
• Competitor citation analysis
• Content gap analysis

PHASE 2 - OPTIMIZE (Weeks 3-8)
• On-page GEO optimization
• Content restructuring for AI extraction
• Authority signal amplification

PHASE 3 - AMPLIFY (Weeks 5-12)
• Strategic content distribution
• Industry publication placements
• Thought leadership positioning

PHASE 4 - MONITOR (Ongoing)
• Weekly AI visibility tracking
• Citation monitoring
• Continuous optimization"

4. PROOF (3 min)

[SHOW CASE STUDY SLIDE]

"We've done this for [X] companies. Here's a recent example:

[CLIENT NAME] was invisible in AI search. 90 days later:
• 94% citation rate (up from 12%)
• 67% of demo requests from AI-referred traffic
• CEO called it their highest-ROI marketing investment"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HANDLING OBJECTIONS (Minutes 25-35)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BE READY FOR THESE:

OBJECTION 1: "We need to think about it"

YOU: "I completely understand. Can I ask - what would need to change for this to become a priority? Is it budget timing, or do you need to see something specific?"

[LISTEN. Address the real concern.]

OBJECTION 2: "This sounds expensive"

YOU: "Fair question. What number are you thinking of?"

[LET THEM SAY A NUMBER]

YOU: "I hear you. Can I frame it differently? Our clients typically see [ROI METRIC]. At that rate, the investment pays for itself in [X] months. Does that change the calculus at all?"

OBJECTION 3: "We're already working with an SEO agency"

YOU: "That's great - and most SEO agencies aren't doing GEO yet. Traditional SEO and GEO are complementary, not replacements. We often partner with existing agencies.

Would you be open to us doing a quick audit to show the gaps they might be missing?"

OBJECTION 4: "Send me information"

YOU: "Absolutely - I'll send over a detailed proposal. To make it relevant, can I ask: what's the one thing you'd need to see in that proposal to feel confident moving forward?"

[This reveals the REAL objection.]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CLOSE (Minutes 35-45)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "[FIRST NAME], based on everything we've discussed, I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

[PRESENT SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATION]

"For a company at your stage, I'd recommend our [PACKAGE NAME] package. This includes:

• Full GEO audit and strategy
• On-page optimization for [X] pages
• Authority building campaign
• Weekly tracking and reporting
• Dedicated account manager

The investment is $[X,XXX]/month for 3 months. Most clients see ROI within the first 60 days.

[PAUSE. Let them respond.]

Does this feel like the right next step for [COMPANY NAME]?"

[IF YES]

YOU: "Great. Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send over the proposal today
2. You review it - any questions, just ask
3. We sign and schedule kickoff
4. We start the audit within 48 hours

I can have the proposal to you within 2 hours. What's the best email?"

[IF THEY NEED TO THINK]

YOU: "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the timeline, or something about the approach?"

[ADDRESS THE CONCERN. Then:]

YOU: "Tell you what - let me send the proposal so you have something concrete to review. No obligation. We can schedule a follow-up call for [DAY] to answer any questions. Does that work?"

[BOOK THE FOLLOW-UP BEFORE HANGING UP]

□ Include Calendly for follow-up
□ Log all notes in CRM
