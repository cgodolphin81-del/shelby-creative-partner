LINKEDIN GHOSTWRITING - DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT
30-45 minutes. Follow this exactly.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRE-CALL PREP (15 min before call)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Review their LinkedIn profile thoroughly
□ Note their follower count, post frequency, engagement
□ Identify 2-3 competitors on LinkedIn
□ Have proposal template open
□ Have Calendly ready for booking follow-up
□ Prepare 2-3 content ideas specific to them

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
OPENING (Minutes 0-3)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "Thanks for making time, [FIRST NAME]. I know you're busy.

Before I jump into anything, I want to make sure this is actually valuable for you. Can I ask a few questions about your current LinkedIn presence and what you're hoping to achieve?"

[WAIT FOR YES]

YOU: "Perfect. Two things upfront:
1. This is about you - I'll listen more than I talk
2. If I think we can help, I'll tell you. If not, I'll be honest. Fair?"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
QUALIFYING QUESTIONS (Minutes 3-12)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

CURRENT STATE:

1. "How often are you currently posting on LinkedIn?"

2. "What's working? What's not?"

3. "What results have you seen from LinkedIn so far?"

GOALS:

4. "What would success look like for you on LinkedIn?"

5. "What's driving this interest now vs. 6 months ago?"

6. "How important is inbound lead generation vs. brand building?"

OBSTACLES:

7. "What's stopped you from posting more consistently?"

8. "How much time could you realistically spend on this weekly?"

DECISION:

9. "Have you worked with ghostwriters or content agencies before?"

10. "Who else is involved in decisions like this?"

11. "What would need to be true for this to be a no-brainer?"

[TAKE NOTES. Reference these later.]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PRESENTATION (Minutes 12-25)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "[FIRST NAME], thank you for sharing all that. Based on what you've told me, here's what I'm seeing..."

[SHARE SCREEN - SHOW THEIR PROFILE + CASE STUDIES]

1. THE OPPORTUNITY (3 min)

"You have incredible insights and experience. But you're invisible on LinkedIn while competitors are building authority.

I took a quick look at your profile - [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION]. You've built [COMPANY] to [ACHIEVEMENT]. That's a story worth telling."

2. THE PROBLEM (2 min)

"You don't have time to write and post consistently. That's not a you problem - that's a system problem.

Most executives try to do it themselves, burn out after a few weeks, and give up."

3. THE SOLUTION (5 min)

"We ghostwrite everything for you. Here's how it works:

WEEKLY RHYTHM:
• 30-min call with us (we extract your insights)
• We write 5-7 posts in YOUR voice
• You approve via Google Doc (10 minutes)
• We post and engage for you

Total time from you: 45 minutes/week.

That's it. We handle everything else."

4. THE RESULTS (3 min)

[SHOW CASE STUDY SLIDE]

"Our clients typically see:
• 1M+ impressions/month
• 30-50 inbound leads/quarter
• $200k-$500k pipeline from LinkedIn

Here's a recent example:
[CLIENT] went from 0 posts to 2.3M impressions in 90 days. Generated $340k pipeline, closed 2 deals ($180k).

All while spending 35 hours TOTAL over 90 days."

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HANDLING OBJECTIONS (Minutes 25-35)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

BE READY FOR THESE:

OBJECTION 1: "This is expensive"

YOU: "I hear you. What number were you expecting?"

[LET THEM SAY A NUMBER]

YOU: "That makes sense. Here's the framing: our clients typically see [ROI]. One closed deal often covers the entire investment. Would it help if we started with a lighter package and scaled up?"

OBJECTION 2: "I need to think about it"

YOU: "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else?"

OBJECTION 3: "Can't I just hire a cheaper ghostwriter?"

YOU: "You could. But here's the question: what's the cost of 6 months of content that doesn't perform?

Cheap ghostwriters produce generic content. We write in YOUR voice, with strategy, focused on results.

What's 6 months of your time worth if the content doesn't work?"

OBJECTION 4: "I'm not sure about the time commitment"

YOU: "Totally get that. Our clients spend 30 min/week on the call and maybe 10 minutes approving posts. That's it.

We handle everything else. What part concerns you most?"

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CLOSE (Minutes 35-45)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

YOU: "[FIRST NAME], I think there's a real opportunity here. Here's what I'd recommend..."

[PRESENT SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATION]

"For an executive at your level, I'd recommend our [PACKAGE NAME] package. This includes:

• 25 posts/month (5-6 per week)
• Weekly 30-min strategy calls
• Full engagement management
• Inbound lead tracking
• Dedicated writer who learns your voice

The investment is $[X,XXX]/month for 3 months. Most clients see ROI within the first 60 days.

[PAUSE. Let them respond.]

Does this feel like the right next step?"

[IF YES]

YOU: "Great. Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send over the proposal today
2. You review it - any questions, just ask
3. We sign and schedule onboarding
4. We start with voice calibration within 48 hours

I can have the proposal to you within 2 hours. What's the best email?"

[IF THEY NEED TO THINK]

YOU: "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something about the approach?"

[ADDRESS THE CONCERN. Then:]

YOU: "Tell you what - let me send the proposal so you have something concrete to review. No obligation. We can schedule a follow-up call for [DAY] to answer any questions. Does that work?"

[BOOK THE FOLLOW-UP BEFORE HANGING UP]

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
POST-CALL ACTIONS (Within 1 hour)
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

□ Send personalized email recapping the call
□ Attach proposal
□ Include Calendly for follow-up
□ Log all notes in CRM
□ Set reminder for follow-up date
