## 🆘 IF YOU GET STUCK

**No responses after 50 emails?**
→ Review subject lines. Add more personalization. Lead with stronger pain point.

**Getting responses but no calls?**
→ Your CTA might be weak. Make it easier to say yes. Offer specific times.

**Getting calls but no deals?**
→ Review your call script. Are you qualifying properly? Handling objections?

**Overwhelmed?**
→ Just do the next thing. One email. One call. One proposal.
