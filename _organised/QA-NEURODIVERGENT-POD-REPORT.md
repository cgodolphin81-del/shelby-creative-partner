## 🎯 QUALITY STANDARDS (Ready for When Products Exist)

### Design Quality Standards

**✅ Community-Approved Language:**
- Uses identity-first language ("autistic person" not "person with autism")
- Uses community terms ("neurospicy," "stimming," "masking")
- AVOIDS deficit language ("suffers from," "high functioning")
- Celebratory, not pity-based tone

**✅ Professional Quality:**
- Clean, readable typography (accessibility matters!)
- High-resolution, print-ready files
- Color contrast appropriate for all users
- No pixelation, blurriness, or low-quality artifacts
- Consistent brand voice across all 100 designs

**✅ Unique & Authentic:**
- No copied designs from competitors
- Original artwork for each design
- No stereotypical or offensive imagery
- Represents diversity within neurodivergent community
- Joyful, not somber messaging

---

### Copy Quality Standards

**✅ SEO-Optimized Titles:**
- Primary keyword near beginning
- Clear, descriptive, benefit-focused
- Under 140 characters (Etsy limit)
- Includes product type (t-shirt, hoodie, etc.)
- Example: "Neurodivergent University Student T-Shirt - ADHD Pride Shirt"

**✅ Benefit-Driven Descriptions:**
- Focus on how product makes customer FEEL
- Not just features ("cotton shirt") but benefits ("comfortable all day while you stim")
- Uses community-approved language
- Includes sizing, care instructions
- 5-7 paragraphs with emotional connection
- Example: "Wear your neurodivergent pride with this comfortable, affirming shirt that celebrates your unique brain. Perfect for students, professionals, or anyone who needs to unmask and be themselves."

**✅ Platform Optimization:**
- Etsy: 13 tags, 20+ character keywords
- Redbubble: SEO-optimized, category-specific
- Amazon Merch: Keyword-rich titles
- Each platform has specific requirements
