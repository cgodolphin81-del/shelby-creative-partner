## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### **What is GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)?**

GEO is the practice of optimizing your content to appear in AI-generated search results (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews). Unlike traditional SEO, GEO focuses on how AI models retrieve and cite information.

### **How is this different from SEO?**

SEO optimizes for Google's algorithm. GEO optimizes for AI language models. You need both. Traditional SEO gets you to page 1. GEO gets you cited in the AI answer.

### **How long until I see results?**

Most clients see improved AI visibility within 30-60 days. Full results (traffic/revenue impact) typically show in 90-180 days, depending on your niche and competition.

### **Do I need to hire you for implementation?**

No! The audit gives you a complete action plan. You can implement yourself or with your team. We also offer implementation services if you want us to handle it (contact us for pricing).

### **What if I'm already doing SEO?**

Great! GEO complements SEO. Many optimizations overlap (content quality, schema, technical SEO). We'll identify what's already working and what needs GEO-specific tuning.

### **Can you guarantee #1 ranking in AI search?**

No one can guarantee specific rankings (anyone who says they can is lying). But we guarantee you'll see measurable improvement in AI visibility, or we work free until you do.
