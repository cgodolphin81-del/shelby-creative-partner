## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### **How do you capture my voice?**

We start with a 30-minute interview where we discuss your background, opinions, stories, and style. We also review your past posts (if any) and content you admire. Most clients say our writing sounds exactly like them.

### **Do I approve posts before they go live?**

Yes! You'll review all posts in advance (via Notion or Google Docs). You can request revisions (1-2 rounds depending on plan) before we schedule anything.

### **What topics do you write about?**

Whatever you want! Common topics: Industry insights, lessons learned, client wins (anonymized), behind-the-scenes, hot takes, original research, book summaries, conference takeaways. We'll create a content strategy based on your goals.

### **Do you handle comments and engagement?**

Professional and Enterprise plans include daily engagement (we respond to comments on your behalf). Starter plan does not (but we can add it for +$500/month).

### **Can I post on other platforms too?**

Yes! We can adapt content for Twitter/X, Instagram, or your newsletter (add-on service: +$2,000/month per platform).

### **What if I need to pause or cancel?**

Monthly plans: Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. Quarterly/Annual: Prepaid, but we can pause for emergencies (medical, family, etc.).

### **Do you guarantee results?**

We guarantee increased engagement (50-100%+ in 90 days). Inbound leads depend on your offer, market, and close rate—but we've never had a client NOT get results when they follow our strategy.
