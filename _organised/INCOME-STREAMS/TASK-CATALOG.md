## 🛠️ SOLUTION ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

**For Each Pain Point:**

### 1. Current Solutions (What Exists)
- [ ] Product A (price, features, reviews)
- [ ] Product B (price, features, reviews)
- [ ] Product C (price, features, reviews)
- [ ] DIY approach (time, complexity, failure rate)

### 2. What Sucks (The Gap)
- [ ] Too expensive ($X vs. what they can afford)
- [ ] Too complex (requires Y skill they don't have)
- [ ] Too slow (takes Z time, they need instant)
- [ ] Poor support (no help when stuck)
- [ ] Missing feature (they need ABC, product has XY)
- [ ] Outdated (built for 2020, not 2026)

### 3. Willingness to Pay
- [ ] What have they already spent? ($X on failed solutions)
- [ ] What do they say they'd pay? (forum quotes)
- [ ] What do competitors charge? (pricing analysis)
- [ ] What's the cost of NOT solving? (lost revenue, time, stress)

### 4. Our Better Product
- [ ] How we solve it better (specific advantage)
- [ ] How we charge (one-time, recurring, tiered)
- [ ] How we deliver (digital, service, SaaS, hybrid)
- [ ] How we differentiate (unique angle)

### 5. Validation Test
- [ ] Landing page test (signups before building)
- [ ] Pre-sale test (sell before building)
- [ ] Interview test (talk to 10 target customers)
- [ ] MVP test (build minimal, iterate)

## 💰 DECISION CRITERIA

**Green Light (Build This):**
- ✅ Pain point is EXPENSIVE (costing them $1K+/mo or 10+ hours/week)
- ✅ Existing solutions SUCK (reviews <4 stars, complaints on forums)
- ✅ Willing to pay (already spent $500+ trying to solve)
- ✅ We can do it BETTER (clear advantage: faster, cheaper, easier, more complete)
- ✅ Market is BIG ENOUGH (10K+ potential customers)

**Red Light (Skip):**
- ❌ Nice to have (not urgent, not expensive)
- ❌ Free alternatives exist (and they work fine)
- ❌ Market is tiny (<1K potential customers)
- ❌ We have no advantage (competitors are 10x better)
- ❌ Regulatory nightmare (compliance risk > reward)
