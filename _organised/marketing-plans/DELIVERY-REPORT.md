## 🔑 KEY INSIGHTS

### What Works Across All Plans:
1. **3-Tier Pricing** - Always offer Good/Better/Best
2. **Urgency + Scarcity** - Limited spots, cohort deadlines
3. **Case Studies** - Specific numbers build trust
4. **Email Sequences** - 5 emails optimal for conversion
5. **Social Proof** - Testimonials in every sequence
6. **Clear CTA** - One action per email/post
7. **Follow-Up** - 80% of sales happen after contact 5+

### Common Objections & Responses:
- **"Too expensive"** → Reframe as investment, show ROI
- **"No time"** → Emphasize time-saving/DFY nature
- **"Need to think"** → Create urgency, offer payment plans
- **"Tried before"** → Differentiate with specific results
