## 🔬 INTRODUCTION & BEST PRACTICES

### **Prompt Engineering Fundamentals**

Every professional image prompt should include:

✅ **Subject** - What's in the image (be specific)
✅ **Style** - Artistic style (photorealistic, 3D render, minimalist, etc.)
✅ **Setting/Background** - Where it takes place
✅ **Camera Angle** - Perspective (wide, close-up, macro, aerial, etc.)
✅ **Lighting** - Quality and direction (soft, dramatic, golden hour, studio, etc.)
✅ **Quality Modifiers** - Resolution and detail (8K, ultra-detailed, professional, etc.)
✅ **Color Palette** - Specific colors or mood (warm tones, cool blues, vibrant, etc.)
✅ **Mood/Atmosphere** - Emotional feel (energetic, calm, professional, etc.)
✅ **Technical Specs** - Aspect ratio, model version, etc.

### **Platform-Specific Tips**

**Midjourney v6:**
- Use `--ar [ratio]` for aspect ratio
- Use `--v 6.0` for version
- Use `--style raw` for less artistic interpretation
- Use `--q 2` for double quality
- Excellent for: Artistic renders, stylized images, concept art

**DALL-E 3:**
- Natural language prompts work best
- More literal interpretation than Midjourney
- Excellent for: Text rendering, complex scenes, marketing graphics
- Use `high` resolution when available

**Stable Diffusion XL:**
- Needs more technical specificity
- Works best with negative prompts
- Excellent for: Product mockups, consistent styles, batch generation
- Use CFG scale 7-12 for adherence

### **Prompt Format Template**

```
[SUBJECT DESCRIPTION] in [STYLE] style, featuring [SPECIFIC ELEMENTS], 
set in [SETTING/BACKGROUND], captured with [CAMERA ANGLE], 
lit by [LIGHTING TYPE], [COLOR PALETTE], [MOOD/ATMOSPHERE], 
[QUALITY MODIFIERS], aspect ratio [ASPECT RATIO] --ar [VALUE]
```

### **Aspect Ratio Guide**

- **Instagram Square:** 1:1 (1080x1080)
- **Instagram Portrait:** 4:5 (1080x1350)
- **Instagram Story/Reel/TikTok:** 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Pinterest:** 2:3 (1000x1500)
- **YouTube Thumbnail:** 16:9 (1280x720)
- **Website Hero:** 16:9 or 2.35:1 (2560x1080)
- **Twitter Header:** 3:1 (1500x500)
- **LinkedIn Post:** 1:1 or 4:5
- **Blog Feature Image:** 16:9 or 2:1

### **Quality Settings by Platform**

**Midjourney:**
- Standard: `--ar [ratio] --v 6.0`
- High Quality: `--ar [ratio] --v 6.0 --q 2`
- Less Artistic: `--ar [ratio] --v 6.0 --style raw`
- Stylize: `--ar [ratio] --v 6.0 --s 250` (default 100)

**DALL-E 3:**
- Always requests high resolution when available
- Natural language works best
- More literal interpretation

**Stable Diffusion XL:**
- Steps: 30-50
- CFG Scale: 7-12
- Sampler: DPM++ 2M Karras or Euler a
- Resolution: 1024x1024 (SDXL native)

### **Prompt Customization Tips**

1. **Be Specific:** Instead of "business person," use "woman in her 30s wearing blazer and holding tablet"
2. **Color Control:** Specify exact colors: "sage green and terracotta" not "earthy tones"
3. **Lighting Matters:** "golden hour window light" vs "dramatic studio lighting"
4. **Mood Words:** "professional and trustworthy" vs "exciting and energetic"
5. **Technical Terms:** Use photography terms (bokeh, depth of field, wide-angle) for realism
6. **Platform Optimization:** Adjust aspect ratio and text placement for each platform
7. **Iterate:** Try variations, keep what works, build a personal library
