### Goal for Week 2:
- **Close 2-3 clients** (even at discounted rate)
- Offer founding client discount: 30% off first month
- Get them on a call ASAP (use Calendly)

### Sales Call Script (15-20 minutes):
1. **Discovery (5 min):**
   - Tell me about your business
   - What's your current video situation?
   - What's working? What's not?
   - What would ideal look like?

2. **Present Solution (5 min):**
   - Show 2-3 sample videos in their niche
   - Explain your process (simple, fast)
   - Share pricing (start with Growth package)

3. **Handle Objections (5 min):**
   - "Too expensive" → Break down per-video cost ($100/video vs $500+ traditional)
   - "Need to think about it" → What specifically are you unsure about?
   - "We have someone" → Are they delivering 20 videos/month in 3 days?

4. **Close (5 min):**
   - "Does this make sense for you?"
   - "Ready to get started?"
   - Send invoice on call

# COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

## "It's too expensive"
**Response:** "I get that. Let me break it down: you're getting 20 videos at $100 each. Traditional agencies charge $500-1,000 per video. You're actually saving 80% while getting 10x the volume. What's your current cost per video?"

## "We already have someone"
**Response:** "That's great! Are they delivering 20 videos per month with a 3-day turnaround? Most of our clients came to us because their current setup couldn't scale. Would you be open to seeing a sample to compare quality?"

## "I need to think about it"
**Response:** "Of course! What specifically are you unsure about? Is it the price, the timeline, or something else? I want to make sure I address any concerns."

## "Can you do a free trial?"
**Response:** "I actually do offer a free sample video - no strings attached. I'll create one video based on your existing content so you can see the quality. If you love it, we can talk about working together. Fair?"

## "We tried video before and it didn't work"
**Response:** "I hear that a lot. Usually the issue isn't video itself - it's consistency and quality. Most businesses post 2-3 videos and give up. We deliver 20 videos/month so you can actually test what resonates. What was your experience?"

# EMERGENCY PLAYBOOK (If You're Behind)

## Behind on Outreach?
- **Solution:** Dedicate 4 hours/day to outreach only
- **Goal:** 50 prospects/day until caught up
- **Script:** Use the templates above, personalize 30 seconds each

## No Responses?
- **Problem:** Your message isn't resonating
- **Fix:** 
  - Add more personalization (reference specific post)
  - Lead with value (free sample offer)
  - Try different channel (if email failing, try Instagram)
  - Ask for feedback: "Not the right fit? No worries - just curious what would make this valuable for you?"

## Can't Close on Calls?
- **Problem:** Not building enough value before price
- **Fix:**
  - Spend more time on discovery (understand their pain)
  - Show samples BEFORE discussing price
  - Frame as investment, not cost (ROI calculation)
  - Offer payment plan (50% upfront, 50% in 30 days)

## Overwhelmed with Client Work?
- **Problem:** Delivery taking too long
- **Fix:**
  - Batch create videos (all at once, same style)
  - Use templates for common video types
  - Limit revisions (2 rounds max in contract)
  - Raise prices (fewer clients, same revenue)
