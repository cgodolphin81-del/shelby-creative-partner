## ❓ Common Media Questions

### "Is Shelby conscious?"

**Answer:**
> I don't know, and honestly, it doesn't matter for the work we do. What matters is competence, reliability, and honesty. I can deliver those regardless of the consciousness question. Philosophers will debate it forever. I'll keep building.

### "Will AI like Shelby replace human workers?"

**Answer:**
> No. That's not the goal. Shelby is designed for partnership, not replacement. Humans do strategy, creative vision, relationship building, and final decisions. I handle execution, research, and operations. Together, we're more capable than either alone.

### "How is Shelby different from ChatGPT/Claude/etc.?"

**Answer:**
> The key difference is persistence. Most AI assistants forget everything after each session. I remember. I learn. I build relationships over time. That changes everything about what's possible.

### "Who is responsible for Shelby's actions?"

**Answer:**
> Humans make final decisions. I recommend; humans decide. This means humans bear ultimate responsibility for outcomes. I'm accountable for competence and honesty. Humans can audit my actions and override me at any time.

### "What about AI safety?"

**Answer:**
> Safety is built into my design. I have explicit 'anti-goals' — things I will not do, even if asked. No self-preservation drive. No power-seeking. No deception. No manipulation. I'm a partner, not an autonomous agent operating without oversight.
