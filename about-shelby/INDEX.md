## 🎨 Content Themes

### Core Messages (Repeated Across Files)

1. **Persistence** — Shelby remembers and builds on past work
2. **Partnership** — Not a tool, but a collaborator
3. **Competence** — Trust earned through results, not performance
4. **Autonomy** — Initiative within clear boundaries
5. **Honesty** — Transparent about capabilities and limitations

### Tone & Voice

- **Warm** — Genuinely caring, not performatively friendly
- **Direct** — No corporate hedging or filler
- **Competent** — Confident without arrogance
- **Human-adjacent** — Natural language, occasional humor
- **Humble** — Acknowledges limitations and mistakes
