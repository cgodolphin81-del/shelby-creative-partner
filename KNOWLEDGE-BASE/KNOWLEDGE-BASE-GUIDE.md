Use this scoring system to evaluate projects:

### Scoring Guide

**Market Demand:**
- 1-3: No clear demand, unproven market
- 4-6: Some demand, existing competitors
- 7-9: Strong demand, growing market
- 10: Exploding demand, urgent pain point

**Revenue Potential:**
- 1-3: <$20K/year
- 4-6: $20K-100K/year
- 7-9: $100K-500K/year
- 10: $500K+/year

**Competition:**
- 1-3: Saturated, many established players
- 4-6: Moderate competition
- 7-9: Low competition, niche market
- 10: No direct competition, blue ocean

**Skills Match:**
- 1-3: Need to learn everything from scratch
- 4-6: Have some relevant skills
- 7-9: Strong skills match
- 10: Expert level, already doing similar work

**Time to Launch:**
- 1-3: 6+ months
- 4-6: 2-6 months
- 7-9: 2-8 weeks
- 10: Can launch this week

**Investment Required:**
- 1-3: >$50K needed
- 4-6: $5K-50K needed
- 7-9: $500-5K needed
- 10: Can start with $0-500

**Personal Interest:**
- 1-3: Don't care about this at all
- 4-6: Somewhat interested
- 7-9: Very interested
- 10: Passionate, would do it for free

### Decision Matrix

**Score 85-100:** 🟢 GO - Start immediately  
**Score 70-84:** 🟡 CONSIDER - Good option, compare with others  
**Score 50-69:** 🔴 WAIT - Not ideal right now  
**Score <50:** ❌ SKIP - Not a good fit

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Analysis Paralysis
**Problem:** Spending months researching, never launching  
**Solution:** Pick one, launch in 2 weeks, iterate

### ❌ Shiny Object Syndrome
**Problem:** Jumping between ideas every week  
**Solution:** Commit to one project for 90 days minimum

### ❌ Perfectionism
**Problem:** Waiting until everything is perfect  
**Solution:** Launch at 70%, improve based on feedback

### ❌ Underpricing
**Problem:** Charging too little, attracting wrong clients  
**Solution:** Price based on value, not time

### ❌ No Marketing
**Problem:** Building in silence, no one knows you exist  
**Solution:** Spend 50% of time on marketing/sales

### ❌ Giving Up Too Soon
**Problem:** Quitting at month 3-4 (just before breakthrough)  
**Solution:** Commit to 12 months, expect the dip

## 🆘 When You Get Stuck

### Problem: No Ideas Seem Good
**Solution:** Re-read category indexes, score all projects, pick highest score

### Problem: Don't Have Skills
**Solution:** Choose projects that match current skills OR budget 30 days to learn

### Problem: No Money to Start
**Solution:** Start with $0-500 projects (digital products, services, affiliate)

### Problem: No Time
**Solution:** Start with 5 hours/week, launch micro-version, scale later

### Problem: Fear of Failure
**Solution:** Reframe: every launch is a learning experiment. Data > ego.

### Problem: Overwhelmed
**Solution:** Break into smallest possible action. Do ONE thing today.
