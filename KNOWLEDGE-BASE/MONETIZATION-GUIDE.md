## 📊 Pricing Psychology

### Tiered Pricing Strategy

**Good-Better-Best Model:**
```
Basic:     $97  (anchor price)
Pro:      $197  (most popular - 50% of sales)
Premium:  $297  (high-end - 10% of sales)
```

**Why it works:**
- Basic makes Pro look reasonable
- Most people choose middle option
- Premium captures high-value customers

### Discount Strategies

**Launch Discount:** 20-30% off first week  
**Seasonal:** Black Friday, New Year (30-50% off)  
**Bundle:** Buy 3 reports, get 1 free  
**Payment Plan:** 3 monthly payments (add 10%)  
**Early Bird:** First 100 customers (40% off)

### Price Testing

**Test these variables:**
- Price points ($97 vs $147 vs $197)
- Payment frequency (one-time vs. annual)
- Guarantee length (30 vs. 60 vs. 90 days)
- Bonuses included

**Expected uplift:** 10-30% revenue increase from optimization

## ⚠️ Legal & Compliance

### Business Structure
- **LLC** (recommended) - Liability protection
- **S-Corp** (if >$100K profit) - Tax optimization
- **Sole Proprietorship** (simplest) - No protection

### Contracts Needed
- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- License Agreement (for licensing)
- Client Contract (for consulting)

### Disclaimers
- "Results not typical"
- "For educational purposes"
- "Not financial/legal advice"
- "Do your own research"

### Taxes
- Track all income/expenses
- Set aside 25-30% for taxes
- Hire accountant ($100-300/month)
- Consider sales tax (digital products)
