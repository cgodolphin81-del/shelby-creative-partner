## 📊 Quick Decision Framework

**Answer these 5 questions:**

1. **How much time do you have per week?**
   - <5 hours → Digital products, affiliate
   - 5-15 hours → Service business, consulting
   - 15-40 hours → Full business build
   - 40+ hours → Multiple projects, SaaS

2. **What's your budget?**
   - $0-500 → Digital products, services, content
   - $500-5K → Courses, e-commerce, basic SaaS
   - $5K-50K → Full SaaS, product business
   - $50K+ → Multiple ventures, team

3. **What are your skills?**
   - Technical → SaaS, automation, AI
   - Creative → Content, design, courses
   - People/ Sales → Agency, consulting, coaching
   - Operations → Service business, e-commerce

4. **When do you need money?**
   - This month → Services, consulting, arbitrage
   - 1-3 months → Digital products, courses
   - 3-12 months → Content sites, SaaS
   - 1-5 years → Brands, platforms, portfolios

5. **What do you enjoy?**
   - Working with clients → Services, consulting
   - Creating content → Courses, YouTube, writing
   - Building products → SaaS, digital products
   - Strategy/analysis → Licensing, investing

**Tally your answers** → That's your starting point!
