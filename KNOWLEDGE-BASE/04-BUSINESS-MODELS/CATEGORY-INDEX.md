## Success Factors

### ✅ Critical Success Factors
1. **Clear Niche** - Don't serve everyone
2. **Validated Offer** - People already paying
3. **Marketing System** - Consistent lead flow
4. **Delivery Excellence** - Results/reviews
5. **Pricing Power** - Charge what you're worth

### ❌ Common Failure Points
1. **No Niche** - "I help everyone"
2. **Underpricing** - Race to the bottom
3. **No Marketing** - Build it and they won't come
4. **Poor Delivery** - No referrals/repeat business
5. **Giving Up Too Soon** - Most fail in month 3-6
