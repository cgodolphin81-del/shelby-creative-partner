## 📧 Email Outreach Strategy

### Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep it personal and relevant
- Avoid hype words ("FREE", "URGENT", "Make Money")
- Reference something specific about their work
- Aim for curiosity + authenticity

**Personalization:**
- Mention their specific content you liked
- Reference their platform and follower count
- Show you've done your research
- Keep it concise (200-300 words)

**Call-to-Action:**
- Low pressure (15-min chat, not "join now")
- Make it easy to say yes
- Offer value upfront (demo, resources)
- Include your calendar link

**Follow-Up:**
- Don't give up after one email
- Provide value in each follow-up
- Space emails 3-5 days apart
- Know when to let go (after 3-4 attempts)

## 💡 Pro Tips from Experience

**Tip 1: Personalize or don't bother**
Generic emails get deleted. Reference their actual work and show you've done your homework.

**Tip 2: Lead with value, not commission**
Start with "I love your content" not "Want to make money?"

**Tip 3: Show, don't tell**
Share actual affiliate earnings, screenshots, and real results.

**Tip 4: Make it easy to say yes**
15-minute call, not "join our program." Offer demo access, not pitch deck.

**Tip 5: Follow up strategically**
Most prospects need 3-4 touches before responding. Don't give up.

**Tip 6: Build relationships, not transactions**
Treat affiliates as partners, not sales channels.

**Tip 7: Over-communicate resources**
Affiliates often don't use what you give them. Remind them, show them.

**Tip 8: Celebrate every win**
First sale, first $100, first $1,000 — mark the milestones.

**Tip 9: Be available**
Reply to emails within 24 hours. Be responsive on social.

**Tip 10: Keep learning**
Ask affiliates what's working. Adapt your approach based on feedback.
