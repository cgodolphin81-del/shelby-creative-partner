# Episode 021: Angkor Wat (Cambodia)

## Image Prompt 021-01: Episode Cover - The Iconic Temple at Dawn
**Visual Description:** Majestic wide-angle shot of Angkor Wat's five lotus-bud towers silhouetted against a dramatic sunrise sky. The temple's reflection shimmers perfectly in the foreground pond. Palm trees frame the edges. Warm golden light breaks through purple and orange clouds, illuminating the ancient stone facade.

**Mood/Lighting:** Ethereal, spiritual, awe-inspiring. Golden hour warmth with deep shadows. Soft mist hovers over the water surface.

**Composition Guidance:** Rule of thirds with temple in upper third, reflection in lower third. Symmetrical composition. Include foreground interest (lotus flowers or traditional wooden boat). Shot at f/8-f/11 for sharpness throughout.

**Composition Guidance:** Leading lines down the corridor. Angle shot to show both wall detail and corridor depth. Include a silhouette of an explorer/guide for scale. Macro detail inset optional.

# Episode 023: Easter Island / Rapa Nui (Chile)

**Visual Description:** Iconic row of moai statues at Ahu Tongariki, the largest ceremonial platform on Easter Island. Fifteen massive stone giants stand shoulder to shoulder, backs to the Pacific Ocean, faces turned toward the island. Dramatic sunrise explodes behind them, casting long shadows and creating powerful silhouettes. The ocean crashes against the rocky shore. One moai wears the distinctive red pukao (topknot).

**Composition Guidance:** Wide shot capturing all 15 moai. Low angle for monumentality. Include ocean and sky for context. Symmetrical composition with sun centered behind the row. Consider vertical version for mobile.
