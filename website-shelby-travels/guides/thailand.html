Thailand

Duration: 20,300 words · Best for: 10-21 days

Budget: $25-70/day | Best time: November-March (cool/dry)

Getting There & Getting Around

Flights to Thailand

Major airports: Bangkok (Suvarnabhumi BKK, Don Mueang DMK), Phuket (HKT), Chiang Mai (CNX). Hub for Southeast Asia with many connections.

Getting Around

Bangkok: BTS Skytrain, MRT subway, tuk-tuks, Grab app.

Domestic flights: AirAsia, Nok Air, Thai Lion Air (cheap).

Trains: Overnight trains from Bangkok to Chiang Mai.

Buses: Cheap, comfortable, extensive network.

Where to Stay

Bangkok

Budget: Khao San Road hostels ($10-30/night)
Mid-Range: Sukhumvit, Silom ($50-100/night)
Luxury: Riverside, Sukhumvit ($200-600+/night)

Chiang Mai

Budget: Old City hostels ($8-25/night)
Mid-Range: Old City, Nimman ($40-80/night)
Luxury: Resort-style ($150-400+/night)

Islands (Phuket, Krabi, Koh Samui)

Budget: Beach hostels ($15-40/night)
Mid-Range: Beach bungalows ($60-120/night)
Luxury: Resorts ($200-800+/night)

Suggested Itineraries

10-Day Thailand (North & South)

  • Days 1-3: Bangkok - temples, markets, nightlife
  • Days 4-5: Chiang Mai - temples, elephants, mountains
  • Days 6-9: South - Krabi/Phi Phi, Phuket, islands
  • Day 10: Departure

14-Day Thailand (Extended)

  • Add Ayutthaya (ancient capital) near Bangkok
  • More island time (Koh Lanta, Koh Phangan, Koh Samui)
  • Khao Sok National Park (jungle, caves, lake)

Top Things to Do

  • Bangkok: Grand Palace, Wat Arun, floating markets
  • Chiang Mai: Elephant sanctuaries, Doi Inthanon, night markets
  • Phi Phi Islands: Maya Bay, snorkeling, diving
  • Krabi: Railay Beach, rock climbing, lagoons
  • Koh Samui: Big Buddha, waterfalls, beaches
  • Ayutthaya: Ancient ruins, UNESCO site
  • Food: Street food tours, cooking classes

Budget Breakdown

Per Day: $25-40 (budget) | $60-100 (mid-range) | $150-300+ (luxury)

For 14 days: $350-560 (budget) | $840-1,400 (mid-range) | $2,100-4,200+ (luxury)

Note: Thailand is one of the most budget-friendly destinations in the world.

Essential Tips

  • Visa: 30-60 days visa-free for most countries
  • Money: THB (Thai Baht). ATMs everywhere, but charge fees.
  • Weather: Cool/dry (Nov-Mar), hot (Mar-Jun), rainy (Jul-Oct)
  • Culture: Remove shoes before temples. Respect monarchy.
  • Food: Street food is safe and delicious. Look for busy stalls.
  • Tips: Not expected but appreciated. Round up taxi fares.
  • Scams: Grand Palace "closed" scam, gem scams - stay alert.
