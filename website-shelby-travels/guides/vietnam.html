Vietnam

Duration: 18,300 words · Best for: 10-21 days

Budget: $25-80/day | Best time: February-April, August-November

Getting There & Getting Around

Flights to Vietnam

Major airports: Hanoi (Noi Bai), Ho Chi Minh City (Tan Son Nhat), Da Nang. Many budget airlines (VietJet, Bamboo Airways) for domestic travel.

Getting Around

Trains: Reunification Express runs Hanoi to HCMC (17 hours). Book sleeper berths.

Buses: Overnight buses are common and affordable.

Moto taxis: Grab app recommended for safety and price transparency.

Where to Stay

Hanoi

Budget: Old Quarter hostels ($10-25/night)
Mid-Range: French Quarter ($40-80/night)
Luxury: Sofitel, Park Hyatt ($150-400+/night)

Hoi An

Budget: River-side hostels ($8-20/night)
Mid-Range: Ancient Town ($30-60/night)
Luxury: La Siesta, Anantara ($100-300+/night)

Ho Chi Minh City

Budget: District 1 hostels ($10-25/night)
Mid-Range: District 1, 3 ($40-80/night)
Luxury: Park Hyatt, Caravelle ($150-500+/night)

Suggested Itineraries

10-Day Vietnam (North-South)

  • Days 1-3: Hanoi - Old Quarter, food tour, day trip to Ha Long Bay
  • Days 4-5: Ha Long Bay - overnight cruise
  • Days 6-7: Hoi An - ancient town, beaches
  • Days 8-9: Ho Chi Minh City - war museums, Mekong Delta
  • Day 10: Departure

14-Day Vietnam (Extended)

  • Add Sapa (mountains, trekking) or Da Nang/Ba Na Hills
  • Extend Hoi An for beach time
  • Mekong Delta multi-day tour

Top Things to Do

  • Hanoi: Water puppet show, street food tour, Temple of Literature
  • Ha Long Bay: Overnight cruise, kayaking, cave exploration
  • Hoi An: Lantern festival, tailoring, cooking class
  • Hue: Imperial Citadel, tombs
  • Ho Chi Minh City: Cu Chi Tunnels, War Remnants Museum
  • Mekong Delta: Boat tours, floating markets

Essential Tips

  • Visa: E-visa available for most countries (up to 90 days)
  • Money: VND (Vietnamese Dong). Cash everywhere. ATMs common.
  • Language: Vietnamese. English widely spoken in tourist areas.
  • Tipping: Not expected but appreciated (5-10%)
  • Food: Street food is safe and delicious. Look for busy stalls.
  • Transport: Use Grab app. Haggle for non-app taxis.
