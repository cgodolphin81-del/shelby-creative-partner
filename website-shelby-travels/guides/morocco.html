Getting Around

Trains: ONCF connects major cities (Casablanca, Rabat, Fes, Marrakech).

Grand taxis: Shared taxis for shorter routes.

Private driver: Recommended for Sahara tours.

Where to Stay

Mid-Range: Central riads ($80-150/night)
Luxury: La Mamounia, Royal Mansour ($400-2000+/night)

Sahara Desert

Camp: Luxury camps ($100-300/night), basic camps ($50-100/night)

Fes

Budget: Medina riads ($25-50/night)

Suggested Itineraries

7-Day Morocco (Classic)

  • Days 1-2: Marrakech - souks, palaces, gardens
  • Days 3-4: Sahara Desert - Atlas Mountains, Merzouga, camel trek
  • Days 5-6: Fes - medina, crafts, history
  • Day 7: Chefchaouen or return to Marrakech

10-Day Morocco (Extended)

  • Add Essaouira (coastal town)
  • More time in Sahara
  • Ait Ben Haddou, Dades Valley

Top Things to Do

  • Marrakech: Jemaa el-Fnaa, Bahia Palace, Majorelle Garden
  • Sahara: Camel trek, overnight camp, stargazing
  • Fes: Medina, tanneries, Al Quaraouiyine University
  • Atlas Mountains: Day trip from Marrakech, Berber villages
  • Essaouira: Beach, ramparts, fresh seafood
  • Chefchaouen: Blue city, Rif Mountains

Essential Tips

  • Visa: 90 days visa-free for most countries
  • Money: MAD (Moroccan Dirham). Cash preferred in medinas.
  • Culture: Dress modestly. Ask before photographing people.
  • Haggling: Expected in souks. Start at 50% of asking price.
  • Food: Try tagine, couscous, pastilla. Drink bottled water.
  • Safety: Generally safe. Watch for pickpockets in crowded areas.
