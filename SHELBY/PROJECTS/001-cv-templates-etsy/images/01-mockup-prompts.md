# CV Template Mockup Image Prompts

**Project:** 001 CV Templates Etsy  
**Total Images Needed:** ~55 (5-7 per listing × 10 listings)  
**Format:** 2000px+ width, PNG, Etsy-optimized  
**Style:** Professional, clean, aspirational

---

## 🎨 GLOBAL STYLE GUIDELINES

**All images should:**
- Be 2000px minimum width (Etsy requirement)
- Use natural, professional lighting
- Show CV in realistic context (desk, device, printed)
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds
- Include variety: flat lays, device mockups, lifestyle shots
- No text overlay on hero images (Etsy prefers clean)

**Color Palette:**
- Neutral tones (white, gray, wood, black)
- Occasional color accents for creative templates
- Consistent aesthetic across all listings
