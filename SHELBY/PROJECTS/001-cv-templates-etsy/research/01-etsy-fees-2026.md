## 💰 FEE BREAKDOWN

### Mandatory Fees

| Fee Type | Rate | When Charged |
|----------|------|--------------|
| Listing Fee | $0.20 | Per listing, every 4 months (or on sale) |
| Transaction Fee | 6.5% | On total sale (item + shipping) |
| Payment Processing (US) | 3% + $0.25 | Per transaction |

**Total Base Fees:** ~10-11% of sale price

### Optional Fees

| Fee Type | Rate | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Offsite Ads | 15% (&lt;$10K/yr) or 12% ($10K+/yr) | Only on ad-generated sales, can opt out if &lt;$10K |
| Etsy Plus | $10/month | Listing credits, ads credits, customization |
| Pattern | $15/month | Standalone website builder |

**With Offsite Ads:** ~25-26% total fees

## 📋 SHOP REQUIREMENTS

### To Open Shop

- [ ] Email address
- [ ] Credit/debit card (for fees)
- [ ] Bank account (for payouts)
- [ ] Shop name (unique, 4-20 characters)
- [ ] Shop language, country, currency

### To Start Selling

- [ ] At least 1 listing ($0.20)
- [ ] Payment method set up (Etsy Payments)
- [ ] Payout account configured

### Payout Schedule

- **New Sellers:** Funds may be held 3-7 days
- **Established:** Daily/weekly/monthly options
- **Reserve Possible:** 25-75% held for 45-90 days (new accounts, high case rates)

## 🎯 RECOMMENDATIONS

### For CV Template Shop

1. **Start with 10 listings** ($2.00 in listing fees)
2. **Price at $9-12** per template (competitive, profitable)
3. **Opt out of Offsite Ads** initially (under $10K threshold)
4. **Create bundles** to increase average order value
5. **Factor 12% fees** into all pricing decisions

### Fee Mitigation

- ✅ Opt out of offsite ads (eligible under $10K)
- ✅ Batch listings to manage $0.20 fees
- ✅ Price to include fees (not absorb them)
- ✅ Use Etsy shipping labels (discounted rates)
- ❌ Skip Etsy Plus initially (not worth $10/month at start)
- ❌ Skip Pattern (use GitHub Pages or similar for external site)
