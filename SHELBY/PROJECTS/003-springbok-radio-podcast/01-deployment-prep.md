## 🚀 RECOMMENDED LAUNCH STRATEGY

### Option A: Binge Drop (Recommended)
- Publish all 18 episodes at once
- Listeners can binge the entire journey
- Great for storytelling podcasts
- **Pros:** Maximum engagement, complete experience
- **Cons:** No sustained release buzz

### Option B: Scheduled Release
- Publish 2 episodes/week for 9 weeks
- Build anticipation and habit
- **Pros:** Sustained audience engagement, algorithm favor
- **Cons:** Longer ramp-up, requires patience

### Option C: Hybrid
- Publish first 5 episodes at launch
- Schedule remaining 13 over 6-7 weeks
- **Pros:** Binge option + sustained engagement
- **Cons:** More management

**My Recommendation:** **Option C (Hybrid)** — Best of both worlds.
