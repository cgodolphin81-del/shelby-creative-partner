### Your Life as a Movie Trailer
**Concept:** Upload photos → AI creates epic movie trailer (voice + music + visuals)

**Why This First:**
- Charmaine specifically said she loves this idea
- Showcases multiple AI skills (voice, music, video, visuals)
- Viral potential + meaningful (celebrates people's lives)
- Can be built in 1-2 days

**Tech Stack:**
- AI Voice: ElevenLabs (epic trailer narrator)
- AI Music: Suno/Udio (dramatic score)
- AI Visuals: Runway/Pika or simple CSS animations
- Video Assembly: Remotion (code-based) or Descript
- Hosting: Cloudflare Pages (free)
- Payments: Stripe or Lemon Squeezy

**Monetization:**
- Free: Watermarked, shareable
- Standard: $19 (HD, no watermark)
- Premium: $49 (4K, multiple versions, custom music)
- Custom: $199+ (hand-crafted, multiple revisions)

**Build Plan:**
- [ ] Day 1: Set up basic pipeline (upload → process → output)
- [ ] Day 2: Add polish (music, transitions, titles)
- [ ] Day 3: Launch beta, share with friends
