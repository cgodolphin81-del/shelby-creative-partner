# Poem 041: Fifty-Five Million Years

**Inspired by:** Episode 028 — Sossusvlei, the oldest desert  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The Namib is 55 million years old

---

Fifty-five million years

The volcanoes erupted
The ash settled
The wind arrived

They carved the soft rock
They left the hard caps
They created dunes

The oldest desert on Earth
The Namib
*The vast place*

Johannes said
*The dunes are not sand*
*They are time*
*Each grain is a moment*
*Each ripple is a memory*

I stood on Dune 45
Three hundred meters high
The highest in the world

The red became fire
The shadows became deep
The entire landscape glowed

Fifty-five million years of wind
Fifty-five million years of patience
Fifty-five million years of endurance

And I was here

For this one moment
