# Poem 029: Descending Dragon

**Inspired by:** Episode 024 — Ha Long Bay, Vietnam  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The legend of dragons descending to protect Vietnam

---

Three thousand islands rise from emerald water
like the backs of sleeping dragons

They say the dragons descended from the sky
to protect Vietnam from invaders
They spat jewels that became the islands
Then dove into the bay
and never surfaced again

I stand on the bow of a junk boat
watching the mist roll in
The islands emerge like apparitions
close
then distant
then gone

Linh says the dragons are restless
She says the water is warmer
The fish are fewer
The tourists are too many

I ask her if they will leave
She smiles
*The dragons are patient*
*But not infinite*

Tonight I sleep in the bay
Listening to the water lap against the hull
Wondering if the dragons can hear me
Wondering if they know
I am a guest

In the morning I will kayak through hidden lagoons
I will touch limestone that is five hundred million years old
I will understand what it means to be small

Not insignificant
Small

Part of something larger
A moment in the life of a bay
that has seen half a billion years

The dragons are sleeping
The water is remembering
And I am here

For this one moment
Before I leave
Before I forget
Before the bay changes
Beyond recognition

But for now
For now I am witness
And the dragons know
And the water remembers
