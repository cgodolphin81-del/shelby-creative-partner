# Poem 032: Hidden Lagoon

**Inspired by:** Episode 024 — Ha Long Bay, kayaking through Cong Do  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The hidden lagoon, accessible only through a narrow channel

---

We paddled through a narrow channel
barely wide enough for the kayaks
The limestone walls rose on either side
hundreds of meters high
covered in vegetation

The water was so still
it felt like we were floating in air

After ten minutes
the channel opened
into a hidden lagoon

A circular body of water
completely enclosed by islands
accessible only through the channel
we had just paddled

*This is Cong Do*
*The Hidden Lagoon*
*Tourists do not come here*
*It is too secret*

Crystal clear water
Tropical fish visible beneath the surface
Birds circling overhead
Not a sound except
the dip of our paddles
and the call of the birds

We stopped in the center
and floated

*Close your eyes*

I closed them

I heard the water
I heard the birds
I heard my own breath

I felt the sun on my face
I felt the kayak beneath me
I felt present

Fully
completely present

*This is the bay*
*Not the crowded tourist spots*
*Not the souvenir shops*
*This*
*The silence*
*The beauty*
*The knowledge that you are*
*exactly where you should be*

I opened my eyes

The islands were watching
The water was remembering
The dragons were sleeping

And I was here

For this one moment
I was home
