# Poem 030: The Water Remembers

**Inspired by:** Episode 024 — Ha Long Bay, the floating villages  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The water has seen everything—invaders, fishermen, tourists

---

The water is older than the islands
Five hundred million years
The islands are limestone
pushed up by tectonic forces
But the water
The water has been here since the beginning

It has seen everything

Kublai Khan's fleet
defeated in 1288
The French
The Americans
The Vietnamese
All of them passing through
All of them temporary

Linh's grandfather was a fisherman
Her father was a fisherman
She is a guide
But the water is still in her blood

*The dragons are restless*
*They feel the weight of so many eyes*

I ask her what the water has seen
She says
*Dragons, according to the legend*
*Invaders, according to history*
*All of them temporary*
*All of them gone*

*But the water remembers*

I think about my own footprint
The photograph I took
The souvenir I bought
The waste I left behind

The water knows
The dragons know

And they will respond accordingly
