# Poem 033: Fairy Chimneys

**Inspired by:** Episode 025 — Cappadocia, the fairy chimneys  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** Three million years of wind sculpting volcanic rock

---

Three million years ago
the volcanoes erupted
Erciyes Dağ, Hasan Dağ, Güllü Dağ
Three giants spewing ash and lava
Across the central Anatolian plateau

The ash settled
Layer upon layer
Hard basalt on top
Soft tuff below

Then the wind arrived
And the rain
And the rivers

They carved the soft rock
They left the hard caps
They created chimneys

Fairy chimneys
The locals call them
*Peribacaları*

Tall spires of rock
Topped with stone hats
Rising from the valley floor
Like the fingers of buried giants

Mehmet's grandfather said
The land has spirits
That the chimneys are not rock
They are beings
Watching. Waiting.

I believe him

The wind is still sculpting
The rain is still carving
In a thousand years
They will be different

In a million years
They will be gone

But for now
For this moment
They are here

And they are beautiful
