# Poem 042: Dune 45

**Inspired by:** Episode 028 — Climbing Dune 45 at sunrise  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The sand teaches humility

---

We woke at 4 AM
The sky was still dark
The air was cold
The desert was silent

We drove to Dune 45
In silence

Johannes knew the way
He had driven it
A thousand times

The sun rose at 6:12 AM

The first light hit the dune
The red became fire
The shadows became deep

We began to climb

The sand was soft
Hot
It slipped beneath my feet

Each step forward
Was two steps back

*The sand teaches humility*
*You think you are strong*
*The sand says*
*You are small*
*You are slow*
*You are temporary*

Halfway up
I stopped to catch my breath

I looked back

The landscape stretched to the horizon
Dunes upon dunes
Red upon red
The white pan of Dead Vlei
The black trees standing like skeletons

It was the most beautiful thing
I had ever seen

At the top
The wind was strong
The sand was shifting

Johannes stood at the edge

*This is my church*
*My temple*
*My classroom*

I understood

This was not just a dune
This was fifty-five million years
Of wind
Of time
Of patience
