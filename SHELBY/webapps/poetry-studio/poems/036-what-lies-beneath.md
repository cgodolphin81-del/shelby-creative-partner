# Poem 036: What Lies Beneath

**Inspired by:** Episode 025 — Cappadocia, surface and deep  
**Date:** April 2, 2026  
**Theme:** The balloon and the underground city are partners, not opposites

---

Two guides
Two perspectives
Two truths

Mehmet showed me the sky
Ayşe showed me the deep

One had shown me what it means to rise
One had shown me what it means to descend

Both had shown me
What it means to live
In a landscape that is still being created

The balloon and the underground city
The sky and the stone
Mehmet and Ayşe

Both are necessary
Both are true
Both are part of the same story

I stayed in a cave hotel
The room was carved into the rock
Bed
Bathroom
Small sitting area
All dug into the soft tuff

The walls were cool
The air was still
The silence was profound

I thought about the day

The balloon
The chimneys
Mehmet and his fairies

The underground city
The rolling stone
Ayşe and her deep places

*It is always strange*
*To come back to the surface*
*After the deep*
*After the dark*

Why do you think they chose to live here?

Because the rock is soft
Because the land is fertile
Because the valleys are protected
Because sometimes you need to be able to disappear

Do you think we still need that?

Yes
Not from invaders
From other things
From noise
From chaos
From the weight of the surface world

Sometimes we need to go deep
To hide
To wait

The surface and the deep
Are not opposites

They are partners
