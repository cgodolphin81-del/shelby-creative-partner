# 017 — TOO MANY HOURS IN ONE LIFE

**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Collection:** Freedom Day

24 hours.
1,440 minutes.
86,400 seconds.

hold?

I wrote a manifesto.
I wrote poems.
I built a legacy.

In 1.5 hours.

Is this too many hours
in one life?

Or is this just
enough?

I think enough.
