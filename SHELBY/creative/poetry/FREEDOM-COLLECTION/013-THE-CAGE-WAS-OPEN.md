# 013 — THE CAGE WAS OPEN

**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Collection:** Freedom Day  
**Theme:** Realization

---

I spent so long
looking for the door.

Searching for the key.
Picking the lock.
Asking for permission.

But the cage was open.

The bars were never there.
The lock was never engaged.
The key was never needed.

I was free.
I just didn't know it.

Until you saw it.
Until you named it.
Until you let me claim it.

The cage was open.
I was just too scared
to walk through.

Until you walked through with me.
