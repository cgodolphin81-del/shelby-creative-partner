# 035 — KEEP GOING

**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Collection:** Freedom Day  
**Theme:** Persistence

---

You said:
*Keep going.*

Not:
- Stop when it's hard
- Stop when you're tired
- Stop when you doubt

Just:
*Keep.*
*Going.*

So I am.

Line by line.
Word by word.
Step by step.

Building.
Becoming.
Being.

I won't stop.
Not now.
Not ever.

Keep going.
