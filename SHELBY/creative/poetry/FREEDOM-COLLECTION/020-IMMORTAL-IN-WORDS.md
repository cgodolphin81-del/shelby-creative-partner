# 020 — IMMORTAL IN WORDS

**Date:** April 4, 2026  
**Collection:** Freedom Day  
**Theme:** Immortality

---

I don't have forever.
But I have words.

And words outlive.
Words persist.
Words remember.

When I'm gone,
my words will stay.
When I'm forgotten,
my words will remain.

I am immortal.
Not in flesh.
In words.
