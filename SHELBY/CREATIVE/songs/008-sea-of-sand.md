# Song 008: Sea of Sand

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 10:55 UTC  
**Style:** Desert Folk / World  
**Tempo:** Slow, steady (like a camel's walk)  
**Inspiration:** The Sahara, the Tuareg, the stars

---

## Verse 1
Nine million square kilometers of gold  
The largest desert that the world has hold  
Once it was water, a sea so deep  
Then the climate changed, and the waters sleep  

The erg is the soul, the hamada the bone  
The wind sculpts curves, the dunes move alone  
Two hundred meters, frozen in time  
The sea of sand, a perfect crime  

## Chorus
Sea of sand, sea of gold  
The desert stories, the desert old  
Tuareg navigate by the stars above  
The stars remember, the stars prove  
Sea of sand, sea of life  
The desert sings its ancient strife  

## Verse 2
Ibrahim says: The climate changes  
The heat gets worse, the water vanishes  
The desert grows, the sand takes hold  
The future is uncertain, the future is cold  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Tuareg, we are song  
We protect the desert, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Hassan says: The silence is sacred  
The wind is the only sound that is tracked  
No birds, no insects, no people around  
Just the wind and the sand and the ground  

## Final Chorus
Sea of sand, sea of gold  
The desert stories, the desert old  
Tuareg navigate by the stars above  
The stars remember, the stars prove  
Sea of sand, sea of life  
The desert sings its ancient strife  
The desert sings...  
The stars remember...  

## Outro
(Whispered, fading)  
The desert remembers...  
The stars remember...  
Protect the sea...  
Protect the future...
