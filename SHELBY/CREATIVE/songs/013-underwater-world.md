# Song 013: Underwater World

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 1:30 PM UTC  
**Style:** Ambient Ocean  
**Tempo:** Slow, flowing (like the tide)  
**Inspiration:** The Great Barrier Reef, the coral, the underwater world

---

## Verse 1
Two thousand kilometers of coral blue  
The largest reef that the world ever knew  
Visible from space, a living wonder  
Nine thousand species, the ocean's thunder  

Eight thousand years, the coral grows  
The youngest coral, the life it shows  
The fish are countless, the colors bright  
The reef is alive, the reef is light  

## Chorus
Underwater world, coral and sea  
The reef sings, the reef is free  
Staghorn coral, fragile and fair  
The reef remembers, the reef is there  
Underwater world, coral and time  
The reef sings its ancient rhyme  

## Verse 2
David says: The heat is rising  
The coral bleaches, the reef surprising  
The climate changes, the coral dies  
The future is uncertain, the future cries  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Reef, we are song  
We protect the coral, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Sarah says: The reef remembers  
The reef forgives nothing, nothing, nothing  
The reef takes what it wants to take  
The underwater world is breaking  

## Final Chorus
