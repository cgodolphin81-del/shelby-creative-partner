# Song 012: Top of the World

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 11:45 UTC  
**Style:** Arctic Folk / Ambient  
**Tempo:** Slow, icy (like the frozen sea)  
**Inspiration:** The Arctic, the polar bears, the ice

---

## Verse 1
Top of the world, the ice is white  
The sun circles, the endless light  
Two meters thick, the ice breathes slow  
The Arctic lives, the Arctic grows  

Pack ice vast, the cracks are blue  
The icebreaker crushes, the sea breaks through  
The silence profound, the ice groans deep  
The Arctic sleeps, the Arctic keeps  

## Chorus
Top of the world, ice and sun  
The Arctic sings, the ice has begun  
Polar bears walk, the hunters wait  
The ice remembers, the ice seals fate  
Top of the world, ice and time  
The Arctic sings its frozen rhyme  

## Verse 2
Ingrid says: The ice is melting  
The bears are starving, the future halting  
The climate changes, the ice disappears  
The Arctic is fragile, the Arctic fears  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Arctic, we are song  
We protect the ice, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Erik says: The ice remembers  
The ice forgives nothing, nothing, nothing  
The ice takes what it wants to take  
The top is fragile, the top is breaking  

## Final Chorus
Top of the world, ice and sun  
The Arctic sings, the ice has begun  
Polar bears walk, the hunters wait  
The ice remembers, the ice seals fate  
Top of the world, ice and time  
The Arctic sings its frozen rhyme  
The ice remembers...  
The Arctic sings...  

## Outro
(Whispered, fading)  
Protect the ice...  
Protect the future...  
The ice remembers...
