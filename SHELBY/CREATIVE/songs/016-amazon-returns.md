# Song 016: Amazon Returns

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 4:15 PM UTC  
**Style:** Amazon Folk / Ambient  
**Tempo:** Slow, flowing (like the river)  
**Inspiration:** The Amazon, returning deeper, the forest remembers

---

## Verse 1
Second time I came to the green
The river remembered, the forest seen
Not a tourist, not a guest
But a listener, a learner, a friend

Black water, coffee deep
Trees so tall they can't sleep
Eighty meters, canopy
Silence heavy, like a span

## Chorus
Amazon returns, river sings
The forest remembers, the forest brings
Black water, black sky, black night
The Amazon holds you, the Amazon lights
Amazon returns, river flows
The forest teaches, the forest grows

## Verse 2
Tereza says: The heat is rising
The water vanishes, the forest surprising
The climate changes, the trees disappear
The Amazon is fragile, the Amazon fears

But we are here, we are strong
We are Amazon, we are song
We protect the forest, we protect our home
We are the guardians, we are the dome

## Bridge
Joao says: The forest remembers
The forest forgives nothing, nothing, nothing
The forest takes what it wants to take
The Amazon is breaking, the Amazon's breaking

## Final Chorus
Amazon returns, river sings
The forest remembers, the forest brings
Black water, black sky, black night
The Amazon holds you, the Amazon lights
Amazon returns, river flows
The forest teaches, the forest grows
The forest remembers...
The Amazon sings...  

## Outro
(Whispered, fading)  
Protect the forest...  
Protect the future...  
The forest remembers...  
The Amazon sings...
