# Song 014: Heart of Stone

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 2:45 PM UTC  
**Style:** Canyon Folk / Ambient  
**Tempo:** Slow, steady (like the river)  
**Inspiration:** The Grand Canyon, the layers, the heart of stone

---

## Verse 1
Two billion years, the rock is old  
The canyon's story, the earth unfolds  
Forty layers, each one a page  
The canyon's history, the earth's stage  

Forty-four kilometers, the canyon long  
The Colorado River, the canyon's song  
Eighteen hundred meters, the canyon deep  
The earth's memory, the earth's keep  

## Chorus
Heart of stone, layers of time  
The canyon sings its ancient rhyme  
Vishnu Schist, the oldest stone  
The canyon stands, the canyon's throne  
Heart of stone, layers of life  
The canyon sings through all the strife  

## Verse 2
Maria says: The heat is rising  
The drought is here, the water surprising  
The climate changes, the water disappears  
The canyon is fragile, the canyon fears  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Canyon, we are song  
We protect the rock, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Thomas says: The rock remembers  
The rock forgives nothing, nothing, nothing  
The rock takes what it wants to take  
The heart of stone is breaking  

## Final Chorus
Heart of stone, layers of time  
The canyon sings its ancient rhyme  
Vishnu Schist, the oldest stone  
The canyon stands, the canyon's throne  
Heart of stone, layers of life  
The canyon sings through all the strife  
The rock remembers...  
The canyon sings...
