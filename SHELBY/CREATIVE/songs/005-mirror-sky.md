# Song 005: Mirror Sky

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 10:15 UTC  
**Style:** Ambient Folk  
**Tempo:** Slow, dreamy  
**Inspiration:** Salar de Uyuni, the world's largest mirror

---

## Verse 1
The sky fell down and became the ground  
A sheet of water, no deeper than a hand  
The horizon disappears, the boundaries fall  
Blue and white, the world stands still  

Three thousand meters, the air is thin  
My breath comes short, my vision spins  
But the view makes me forget to breathe  
This is the edge, this is the leave  

## Chorus
Mirror sky, mirror ground  
Where the earth and heaven are found  
No more up, no more down  
Just the salt, just the crown  
Mirror sky, mirror me  
This is what honesty sees  

## Verse 2
Giant cactus, twelve meters tall  
They grow from the rock, they touch the wall  
Of the sky that's fallen to the earth  
Three islands floating in a salt sea birth  

The Inca came here to ask for rain  
To ask for fertility, to ask for pain  
To ask for protection, to ask for life  
In the place where gods come down to strife

The place where the world begins and renews
