# Song 006: Seven Colors

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 10:30 UTC  
**Style:** Andean Folk / World  
**Tempo:** Moderate, rhythmic (like a climb)  
**Inspiration:** Rainbow Mountain, the apus, the earth's story

---

## Verse 1
Five thousand meters, the air is thin  
My heart beats fast, my breath comes in  
The mountain rises, a painting falls  
Colors bleeding, a story calls  

Red from iron, green from copper  
White from aluminum, a mountain supper  
Purple manganese, yellow sulfur  
The earth's chemistry, a mountain flower  

## Chorus
Seven colors, seven stories  
Seven colors, seven glories  
The mountain speaks, the earth sings  
The colors rise, the spirit rings  
Seven colors, seven truths  
Seven colors, seven roots  

## Verse 2
Doña Rosa, ninety-two  
Her face a map, her eyes so true  
The mountain is alive, she says  
The spirits live, they watch and gaze  

We make an offering, coca leaves  
Chicha poured, we believe  
The mountain knows, the mountain sees  
The mountain remembers, the mountain pleads
