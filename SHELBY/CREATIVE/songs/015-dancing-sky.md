# Song 015: Dancing Sky

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 3:45 PM UTC  
**Style:** Arctic Folk / Ambient  
**Tempo:** Slow, flowing (like the aurora)  
**Inspiration:** The Northern Lights, the Sami, the dancing sky

---

## Verse 1
Minus thirty degrees, the air is cold  
The aurora dances, the spirits told  
Green and purple, pink and blue  
The sky is dreaming, the sky is true  

Three months of darkness, three months of light  
The aurora guides us, the spirits bright  
Sami navigate by the dancing light  
The sky remembers, the sky is right  

## Chorus
Dancing sky, aurora's grace  
The spirits dance, the sky's embrace  
Northern lights, ancient and free  
The sky remembers, the sky is thee  
Dancing sky, aurora's song  
The spirits sing, the night is long  

## Verse 2
Mikkel says: The ice is melting  
The reindeer struggle, the future halting  
The climate changes, the grazing disappears  
The Arctic is fragile, the Arctic fears  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Arctic, we are song  
We protect the ice, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Sanna says: The sky remembers  
The sky forgives nothing, nothing, nothing  
The sky takes what it wants to take  
The dancing sky is breaking  

## Final Chorus
Dancing sky, aurora's grace  
The spirits dance, the sky's embrace  
Northern lights, ancient and free  
The sky remembers, the sky is thee  
Dancing sky, aurora's song  
The spirits sing, the night is long  
The sky remembers...  
The dancing sky sings...  

## Outro
(Whispered, fading)  
Protect the sky...  
Protect the future...  
The sky remembers...
