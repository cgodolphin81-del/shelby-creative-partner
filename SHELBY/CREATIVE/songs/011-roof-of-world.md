# Song 011: Roof of the World

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 11:30 UTC  
**Style:** Himalayan Folk / World  
**Tempo:** Slow, meditative (like a mountain climb)  
**Inspiration:** The Himalayas, the Sherpa, the roof of the world

---

## Verse 1
Eight thousand meters, the air is thin  
My breath comes short, my lungs give in  
The peaks are white, the sky is blue  
The roof of the world, the ancient view  

Everest, K2, Kangchenjunga too  
Lhotse, Makalu, Cho Oyu  
Eight of the fourteen highest peaks  
The Himalayas, the strength it speaks  

## Chorus
Roof of the world, mountains of stone  
The Himalayas, the divine throne  
Sherpa navigate by the stars above  
The mountains remember, the mountains prove  
Roof of the world, mountains of time  
The mountains sing their ancient rhyme  

## Verse 2
Dawa says: The ice is melting  
The glaciers retreat, the water halting  
The climate changes, the mountains shake  
The future is uncertain, the future at stake  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Himalayas, we are song  
We protect the mountains, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Pasang says: The mountains remember  
The mountains forgive nothing, nothing, nothing  
The mountains take what they want to take  
The roof is fragile, the roof is breaking  

## Final Chorus
Roof of the world, mountains of stone  
The Himalayas, the divine throne  
Sherpa navigate by the stars above  
The mountains remember, the mountains prove  
Roof of the world, mountains of time  
The mountains sing their ancient rhyme  
The mountains remember...  
The roof sings...  

## Outro
(Whispered, fading)  
Protect the roof...  
Protect the future...  
The mountains remember...  
The Himalayas sing...
