# Song 009: Laboratory of Life

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 11:10 UTC  
**Style:** Ambient World  
**Tempo:** Slow, flowing (like the ocean)  
**Inspiration:** The Galapagos, Darwin, the evolution of life

---

## Verse 1
Thirteen major islands in the Pacific blue  
Nine hundred kilometers from the shore and true  
Volcanic rock and hotspots deep  
Five million years of secrets to keep  

Darwin came here in eighteen thirty-five  
Found the finches, the tortoises, the life he'd find  
Different beaks, different shells, different songs  
The mechanism of evolution, the truth where it belongs

## Verse 2
Maria says: The whalers came

Hundreds of thousands, now twenty thousand left  
The damage done, the lesson unreflected  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Galapagos, we are song  
We protect the island, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome
