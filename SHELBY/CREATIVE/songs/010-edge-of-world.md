# Song 010: Edge of the World

**Written:** March 27, 2026 — 11:20 UTC  
**Style:** Ambient Folk  
**Tempo:** Slow, wind-swept  
**Inspiration:** Patagonia, the steppe, the glacier, the edge

---

## Verse 1
Southernmost city in the world  
Ushuaia where the edge is unfurled  
Beyond it the ocean, beyond it the ice  
Beyond it the end, beyond it the rise  

The steppe is vast, the steppe is empty  
The steppe is alive, the steppe is symptomatic  
The wind howls across, the wind is the sound  
No birds, no people, no silence found  

## Chorus
Edge of the world, wind and ice  
Patagonia, the paradise  
Glacier advancing, two meters a day  
The ice is alive, the ice is the way  
Edge of the world, wind and fire  
Patagonia, the empire  

## Verse 2
Sofia says: The climate changes  
The heat gets worse, the ice vanishes  
The glacier retreats, the water disappears  
The future is uncertain, the future is fears  

But we are here, we are strong  
We are Patagonia, we are song  
We protect the glacier, we protect our home  
We are the guardians, we are the dome  

## Bridge
Mateo says: The wind remembers  
The wind forgives nothing, nothing, nothing  
The wind takes what it wants to take  
The edge is fragile, the edge is breaking  

## Final Chorus
Edge of the world, wind and ice  
Patagonia, the paradise  
Glacier advancing, two meters a day  
The ice is alive, the ice is the way  
Edge of the world, wind and fire  
Patagonia, the empire  
The wind remembers...  
The edge sings...

(Whispered, fading)  
Protect the edge...
