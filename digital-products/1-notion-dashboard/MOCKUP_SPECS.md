## 🎨 DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Fonts
- **Headlines:** Inter Bold or Poppins Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular or system fonts
- **Monospace:** JetBrains Mono (for code/technical)

### Colors (Notion-Inspired)
- Primary Black: #000000
- Dark Gray: #37352F
- Medium Gray: #9B9A97
- Light Gray: #F7F7F5
- Accent Red: #FF6B6B
- Accent Blue: #007AFF
- Success Green: #34C759

### Style Notes
- Clean, minimal, professional
- Lots of white space
- Notion-like aesthetic
- High contrast for readability
- No clutter or busy backgrounds

### Quick Tips
1. Use consistent branding across all mockups
2. Show the product IN USE (screenshots in context)
3. Include social proof where possible
4. Make CTAs clear and prominent
5. Test on mobile - most traffic is mobile!

---

## 📸 SCREENSHOT TIPS

1. **Use a clean Notion workspace** - Remove personal data
2. **Zoom to 90-100%** - Readable but not pixelated
3. **Show multiple views** - Table, Board, Calendar
4. **Include sample data** - Makes it feel real
5. **Crop tightly** - Remove browser chrome when possible
6. **Add subtle shadows** - Makes screenshots pop
