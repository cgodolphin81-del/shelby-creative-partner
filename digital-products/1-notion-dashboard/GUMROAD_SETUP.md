# 🛒 Gumroad Setup Guide - Notion Business Dashboard

## Step-by-Step Setup (30 Minutes)

---

## 📝 STEP 1: Create Your Gumroad Account (5 min)

1. Go to **gumroad.com**
2. Click **"Start Selling"**
3. Sign up with email or Google account
4. Complete your profile:
   - Profile picture (professional headshot or logo)
   - Bio (2-3 sentences about you/your brand)
   - Social media links

### Profile Tips
- Use a professional photo or clean logo
- Write in first person ("I help business owners...")
- Link to your website or social media

---

## 📦 STEP 2: Create Your Product (10 min)

### Product Details

1. Click **"Products"** → **"New Product"**

2. **Product Name:**
   ```
   Ultimate Business Dashboard for Notion
   ```

3. **Product Type:** Select **"Digital Product"**

4. **Price:** 
   - Set to **$47**
   - Enable "Allow customers to pay what they want" → NO
   - Enable "Show 'On Sale' badge" → YES (for promotions)

5. **Description:** Copy from SALES_PAGE.md
   - Use the full sales page copy
   - Gumroad supports Markdown formatting
   - Add line breaks for readability

6. **Preview Image:**
   - Upload your hero mockup (1200x630)
   - This shows in search results and social shares

7. **Product Gallery:**
   - Upload 4-6 images (feature shots, testimonials)
   - Drag to reorder (best image first)
   - Recommended: 1200x630 or 800x800

8. **File Upload:**
   - Click **"Add File"**
   - Upload: NOTION_TEMPLATE.md (or a PDF with the template link)
   - **Better option:** Create a PDF with:
     - Thank you message
     - Template duplicate link
     - Setup instructions
     - Support contact info
   - Name it: `Notion_Business_Dashboard_Instructions.pdf`

### Creating the Template Link

1. In Notion, open your template
2. Click **"Share"** (top right)
3. Click **"Publish"** tab
4. Enable **"Publish to web"**
5. Click **"Copy link"**
6. In your PDF instructions, include:
   ```
   👉 CLICK HERE TO DUPLICATE THE TEMPLATE
   [Paste your Notion link]
   
   Then click "Duplicate" in the top right to add to your workspace.
   ```

### Call-to-Action Button
- Default: "I want this!"
- Recommended: **"Get Instant Access"** or **"Buy Now"**

### Product URL

## 💰 STEP 4: Set Up Payment & Payouts (5 min)

### Connect Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** → **"Payouts"**
2. Choose payout method:
   - **Instant** (debit card): 10% fee, instant access
   - **Weekly** (bank account): 10% fee, weekly deposits
   - **Monthly** (bank account): Lower fees, monthly deposits

3. Enter your details:
   - Legal name (must match ID)
   - Address
   - Tax ID / SSN (required for US sellers)

### Tax Settings

1. Go to **Settings** → **"Tax"**
2. For US sellers:
   - Gumroad automatically collects & remits sales tax
   - Enter your SSN/EIN for 1099 reporting
3. For international:
   - Gumroad handles VAT for EU customers
   - Check your local requirements

---

## 📧 STEP 5: Set Up Email Automation (5 min)

### Welcome Email (Automatic)

Gumroad sends a default receipt email. Customize it:

1. Go to **Settings** → **"Emails"**
2. Edit **"Purchase Receipt"**
3. Add a personal message:
   ```
   Hi [Name],
   
   Thank you for purchasing the Ultimate Business Dashboard!
   
   Your template is attached to this email. Here's how to get started:
   
   1. Click the link in the attached PDF
   2. Click "Duplicate" in Notion
   3. Follow the setup guide (included)
   
   Questions? Reply to this email - I read every message!
   
   Cheers,
   [Your Name]
   
   P.S. Leave a review after you've used it - your feedback helps!
   ```

### Follow-Up Sequence (Optional)

Use Gumroad's **"Posts"** feature:

1. **Day 3:** "How's the setup going?" + quick tips
2. **Day 7:** "Pro tip: Try the client follow-up system"
3. **Day 14:** "Leave a review + check out our other products"

---

## 🎁 STEP 6: Set Up Variants (Optional - Tiers)

If offering multiple tiers:

1. In product editor, scroll to **"Variants"**
2. Click **"Add Variant"**
3. Create:
   - **Basic** - $27 (template only)
   - **Pro** - $47 (template + bonuses) ← Pre-select this
   - **Premium** - $97 (template + bonuses + call)

4. For each variant, specify what's included in description

**Alternative:** Create separate products for each tier and bundle them.

---

## 📊 STEP 7: Enable Analytics

1. Go to **Analytics** dashboard
2. Track:
   - **Views:** How many see your product
   - **Sales:** Conversion rate (aim for 2-5%)
   - **Revenue:** Total and by product
   - **Refunds:** Keep under 5%

3. Set up **Gumroad Pixel** (optional):
   - For Facebook/Google retargeting
   - Found in Settings → Integrations

## 📣 STEP 9: Post-Launch Promotion

### Gumroad Built-In Features

1. **Gumroad Discover:**
   - Opt-in to be featured in Gumroad's marketplace
   - Settings → Discover → Enable

2. **Affiliate Program:**
   - Settings → Affiliates → Enable
   - Set commission: 30-40%
   - Share affiliate link with influencers

3. **Discount Codes:**
   - Create: WELCOME10 (10% off)
   - Create: LAUNCH50 (50% off, first 100 customers)

### External Promotion

1. **Social Media:**
   - Twitter/X: Thread about building the template
   - LinkedIn: Post about productivity systems
   - Instagram: Carousel of features
   - Pinterest: Pin your mockups

2. **Email List:**
   - Launch announcement
   - Behind-the-scenes of creation
   - Customer testimonials (once you have them)

3. **Communities:**
   - Notion Facebook groups
   - Reddit: r/Notion, r/productivity
   - Indie Hacker communities
   - Freelancer forums

**Important:** Always provide value first, then mention your product. No spam!

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### Issue: Template link not working
**Fix:** Make sure Notion link is set to "Public" and test in incognito window.

### Issue: Low conversion rate
**Fix:** Improve product images, add testimonials, clarify value proposition.

### Issue: Refund requests
**Fix:** Improve product description to set clear expectations. Offer help before refunding.

### Issue: Not getting views
**Fix:** Promote on social media, join Gumroad Discover, run a launch promotion.
