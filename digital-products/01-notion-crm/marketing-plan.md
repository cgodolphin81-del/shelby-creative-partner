# Notion CRM Template - Marketing Plan

## Target Audience

### Primary
- Freelancers (designers, developers, writers, consultants)
- Solopreneurs
- Small business owners (1-10 employees)
- Coaches and service providers

### Secondary
- Real estate agents
- Insurance agents
- Sales professionals
- Agency owners

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Content Marketing (Organic)

**Blog Posts** (write on Medium/your site):
- "How I Track 100+ Clients in Notion"
- "Notion vs. Traditional CRM: Why I Switched"
- "5 CRM Mistakes Freelancers Make"
- "My $10K/Month Freelance Setup in Notion"

**Posting Schedule:** 2 posts/week for first month

### 2. Social Media

**Twitter/X:**
- Daily tips on CRM best practices
- Screenshots of template features
- Thread: "How to build a CRM in Notion"
- Engage with #Notion, #freelance, #productivity

**LinkedIn:**
- Weekly posts about sales productivity
- Share case studies
- Connect with freelancers and consultants

**Instagram:**
- Template screenshots with tips
- Reels showing template in action
- Stories with Q&A

**Posting Schedule:** 1 post/day per platform

### 3. Notion Communities

- Join Notion Facebook groups (50K+ members)
- Post in r/Notion subreddit
- Share in Notion Discord servers
- Answer questions, mention template when relevant

**Strategy:** Be helpful first, sell second

### 4. Email Marketing

**Lead Magnet:** Free "CRM Setup Checklist" PDF
- Collect emails via Gumroad or ConvertKit
- 5-email welcome sequence
- Weekly newsletter with productivity tips

**Email Sequence:**
1. Welcome + free checklist
2. CRM best practices
3. Template features spotlight
4. Customer testimonial
5. Limited-time discount (20% off)

### 5. Paid Advertising (Optional)

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Target: Freelancers, small business owners
- Budget: $10-20/day to start
- Creative: Template screenshots + benefit headlines

**Google Ads:**
- Keywords: "notion crm template", "freelance crm"
- Budget: $5-10/day

### 6. Partnerships

- Partner with Notion consultants
- Affiliate program (30% commission)
- Bundle with complementary products
- Guest posts on productivity blogs
