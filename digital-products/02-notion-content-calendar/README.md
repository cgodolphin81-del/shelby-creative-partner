# Notion Content Calendar Template

## 📅 What's Included

Complete content planning system for social media, blogs, and marketing.

## 📦 Template Features

### Core Databases
- **Content Calendar** - Master calendar with all content
- **Content Ideas** - Brainstorming and backlog
- **Platforms** - Social media channel tracking
- **Campaigns** - Marketing campaign organization
- **Assets** - Media library and resources

### Content Types Supported
- Social media posts (Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Facebook)
- Blog posts and articles
- Email newsletters
- YouTube videos
- Podcasts
- Pinterest pins

### Views Included
- Calendar View (monthly, weekly, daily)
- Kanban by Status (Idea → Draft → Scheduled → Published)
- Platform Filter (view by channel)
- Campaign Timeline
- Content Backlog

### Properties Configured
- Publish date and time
- Platform selection (multi-select)
- Content status
- Campaign association
- Content type
- Priority level
- Performance metrics (views, engagement)
- Asset attachments

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Duplicate template to your workspace
2. Set up your content platforms
3. Brainstorm ideas in the Ideas database
4. Schedule content on the calendar
5. Track performance after publishing

## 📋 Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Define Your Platforms
Add your social channels in the Platforms database:
- Instagram
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Blog

### Step 2: Set Content Statuses
Customize the status workflow:
- 💡 Idea
- 📝 Draft
- 🎨 In Design
- ✅ Ready
- 📅 Scheduled
- ✓ Published

### Step 3: Plan Your Campaigns
Create campaigns for:
- Product launches
- Seasonal content
- Content series
- Promotional periods

### Step 4: Start Creating
- Add content ideas daily
- Schedule posts weekly
- Review analytics monthly

## 💡 Pro Tips

- Batch create content on one day per week
- Use templates for recurring post types
- Set up recurring content (weekly tips, monthly roundups)
- Track best-performing content types
- Repurpose top content across platforms
