## 🎉 WELCOME {#welcome}

### Congratulations on Taking Control of Your Time!

This planner is designed to help you:
- ✅ Clarify what matters most
- ✅ Break big goals into actionable steps
- ✅ Track progress consistently
- ✅ Reflect and improve continuously
- ✅ Achieve more with less stress

### How to Use This Planner

**Option 1: Digital (Recommended)**
- Open in any PDF annotation app (GoodNotes, Notability, Adobe Acrobat)
- Fill out digitally with stylus or keyboard
- Save and access anywhere

**Option 2: Print**
- Print the pages you need
- Use a binder or clip system
- Write by hand (proven to improve retention!)

**Option 3: Hybrid**
- Keep digital copies for planning
- Print weekly/daily pages
- Best of both worlds

### The System

This planner uses a **top-down approach**:
1. **Year** → Set your vision and big goals
2. **Quarter** → Break into 90-day sprints
3. **Month** → Plan key projects and milestones
4. **Week** → Schedule priorities and tasks
5. **Day** → Execute with intention

**Review rhythm:**
- Daily: Evening review (5 min)
- Weekly: Weekly planning (30 min)
- Monthly: Monthly review (1 hour)
- Quarterly: Quarterly planning (2-3 hours)
- Yearly: Annual review (half day)
