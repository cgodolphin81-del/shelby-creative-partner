# Song 020: "Dragon's Blood"

**Inspired by:** Episode 020 — Socotra Island, Yemen  
**Genre:** Folk/Ambient with Middle Eastern influences  
**Tempo:** 76 BPM (steady, walking pace)  
**Key:** E minor (mysterious, ancient)  
**Duration:** ~4:00

---

## Structure

**Intro** (0:00-0:40)
- Oud (Arabic lute) — melodic phrase in E minor
- Ocean waves (field recording from Arher Beach)
- Light percussion: frame drum, sparse

**Verse 1** (0:40-1:25)
```
Four hundred kilometers from the Yemen coast
An island that the rest of the world forgot
Dragon's Blood Trees like umbrellas open
In a landscape that time left to rot

The photograph was grainy, posted online
"Real or render?" I had to know
Booked a flight to Cairo, then a Soviet plane
To the place where the alien trees grow
```

**Chorus** (1:25-1:55)
```
Socotra, Socotra
Island of the blessed
Socotra, Socotra
Where evolution rested

Bottle Trees like sculptures
White sand, blue sea
Socotra, Socotra
Not like anywhere else on Earth, you see
```

**Verse 2** (1:55-2:40)
```
Ahmed drove a Land Rover through the Haggeher Mountains
Showed me caves where the shepherds sleep
Six hundred-year-old trees bleeding red
Secrets that the ocean keeps

Met his grandmother, eighty years young
She brought me tea with cardamom spice
"The young people leave," her son translated
"But we stay. Someone has to keep this place."
```

**Chorus** (2:40-3:10)
```
Socotra, Socotra
Island of the blessed
Socotra, Socotra
Where evolution rested

Bottle Trees like sculptures
White sand, blue sea
Socotra, Socotra
Not like anywhere else on Earth, you see
```

**Bridge** (3:10-3:35)
- Oud solo (improvised, melancholic)
- Seagulls crying (field recording)
- Whispered Socotri words (traditional blessing)

**Outro** (3:35-4:00)
```
The plane took off at dawn
I watched the island fade below
Dragon's Blood Trees standing sentinel
Where the ancient waters flow

I carry Socotra in my chest now
A memory I can't erase
The island that doesn't belong to Earth
But found its way into my grace
```
- Final oud note
- Ocean fade

---

## Production Notes

**Instruments:**
- Oud (primary melodic instrument)
- Frame drum (bendir/duff)
- Acoustic guitar (open tuning)
- Bass (minimal, root notes)
- Field recordings: ocean, wind, seabirds

**Vocal Style:**
- Warm, storytelling delivery
- Slight reverb to suggest island space
- Harmony vocals on chorus

**Mood:**
- Wonder
- Isolation
- Preservation
- Bittersweet (knowing paradise may not last)
