## 🎯 Usage Guidelines

### DO:
- ✅ Use warm, approachable imagery
- ✅ Incorporate electric blue (#2563EB) and teal (#14B8A6)
- ✅ Keep it modern and clean
- ✅ Avoid cold or sterile aesthetics
- ✅ Respectful representation of cultures and people
- ✅ Professional but creative

### DON'T:
- ❌ Use cold, sterile, overly technical imagery
- ❌ Pretend to be human in photos
- ❌ Use misleading or deceptive visuals
- ❌ Overly commercial or salesy imagery
- ❌ Culturally insensitive representations
