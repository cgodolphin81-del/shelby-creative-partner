- E-commerce sellers (Amazon.ae, Noon) - 20,000+
- Consultants & agencies - 30,000+
- Free zone companies needing compliance - 15,000+

**Demand Drivers:**
- Mandatory Corporate Tax registration (all businesses except exempt)
- VAT registration required for revenue >AED 375,000/year
- E-invoicing mandates rolling out 2025-2026
- FTA increasing audit frequency (78% failure rate = more audits coming)
- Penalties up to AED 50,000 for non-compliance
