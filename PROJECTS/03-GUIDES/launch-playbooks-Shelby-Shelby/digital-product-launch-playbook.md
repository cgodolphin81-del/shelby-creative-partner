**Why Lowest Priority:**
- **Time to $10K:** 6-12+ months (requires 500-700+ listings)
- **Competition:** VERY HIGH in generic categories

- Subscription model (ongoing updates)

**Realistic Timeline:**
- Month 1-3: Create 100-200 prompts, list on multiple platforms
- Month 4-6: Scale to 400-500 prompts, optimize based on sales
- Month 6-12: Reach $3K-5K/month with 700+ listings
- Year 2+: Potential $8K-10K/month with 1,000+ listings + subscriptions

**Where Money Still Exists:**
- Industry-specific professional prompts (legal, medical, finance)
- Image/video generation prompts (Midjourney, DALL-E, Veo)
- Mega-prompts (complete workflow systems)
- Subscription libraries (monthly updates)
