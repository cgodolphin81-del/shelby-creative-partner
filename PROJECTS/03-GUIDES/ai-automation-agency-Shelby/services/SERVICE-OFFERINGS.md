- Employee onboarding → system access → training assignments

**What We Do:** Design and deploy intelligent conversational AI agents that handle customer inquiries, support tickets, and internal queries 24/7.

**Types of Chatbots:**
- **Customer Support Bots:** Handle FAQs, order status, returns, basic troubleshooting
- **Sales Qualification Bots:** Capture leads, qualify prospects, schedule demos
- **Internal Knowledge Bots:** Answer employee questions about policies, procedures, benefits
- **E-commerce Assistants:** Product recommendations, cart recovery, order tracking
- **Appointment Scheduling Bots:** Book, reschedule, confirm appointments

**Capabilities:**
- Natural language understanding (multiple languages)
- Integration with knowledge bases and documentation
- Handoff to human agents when needed
- Multi-channel deployment (website, WhatsApp, SMS, Slack, Teams)
- Analytics and conversation optimization
- Continuous learning from interactions

**Deliverables:**
- Conversation flow design
- Bot training on your data
- Integration with existing systems
- Analytics dashboard
- Ongoing optimization recommendations

**Timeline:** 3-8 weeks
**Starting Investment:** $8,000 - $40,000
