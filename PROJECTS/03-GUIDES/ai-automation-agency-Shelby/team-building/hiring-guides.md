## Job Descriptions

### Automation Engineer (All Levels)

```
JOB TITLE: Automation Engineer
LOCATION: Remote (US/Canada preferred)
TYPE: Full-time

ABOUT US
--------
We're an AI automation agency helping businesses save 20+ hours/week 
through intelligent workflow automation. We work with companies from 
startup to enterprise, building solutions that deliver real ROI.

We're growing fast and looking for talented engineers to join our 
delivery team.

THE ROLE
--------
As an Automation Engineer, you'll design, build, and deploy workflow 
automations for our clients. You'll work with tools like n8n, Zapier, 
Make, and various AI platforms to solve real business problems.

RESPONSIBILITIES
----------------
• Design and build workflow automations
• Integrate APIs and third-party services
• Test and QA all deliverables
• Document workflows and processes
• Support clients post-launch
• Continuously learn new tools and technologies

REQUIREMENTS
------------
[Junior Level]
• 0-2 years experience with automation tools
• Basic understanding of APIs and webhooks
• Familiarity with JavaScript or Python (plus)
• Strong problem-solving skills
• Excellent communication
• Self-motivated and eager to learn

[Mid-Level]
• 2-4 years experience with automation tools
• Proficiency with n8n, Zapier, or Make
• Strong API integration experience
• JavaScript or Python proficiency
• Experience with AI/LLM integrations (plus)
• Client communication experience

[Senior Level]
• 5+ years experience with automation/integration
• Expert-level knowledge of automation platforms
• Architecture and design experience
• Mentoring experience
• Client-facing experience
• AI/ML integration experience (plus)

NICE TO HAVE
------------
• Experience with OpenAI, Claude, or other LLM APIs
• Background in software development
• Experience in consulting or agency environment
• Certifications in n8n, Zapier, or Make

COMPENSATION
------------
• Junior: $45K-65K base + bonus
• Mid: $65K-100K base + bonus
• Senior: $90K-140K base + bonus
• Equity (for senior roles)
• Health, dental, vision insurance
• 401(k) / RRSP match
• 15-20 days PTO + holidays
• Professional development budget
• Remote work flexibility

TO APPLY
--------
Send your resume and a brief note about why you're interested to 
[jobs@yourcompany.com]. Include links to any automation projects 
you've built (GitHub, Loom demos, etc.).
```

## Interview Guides

### Automation Engineer Interview Guide

**Phone Screen (30 minutes)**

*Background & Experience (10 min)*
- Walk me through your background and how you got into automation.
- What automation tools have you worked with?
- What's the most complex automation you've built?

*Technical Knowledge (10 min)*
- Explain how webhooks work.
- How do you handle API rate limits?
- What's your approach to error handling in workflows?

*Motivation & Fit (10 min)*
- Why are you interested in this role?
- What are you looking for in your next position?
- How do you stay current with new technologies?

---

**Technical Interview (60 minutes)**

*Live Build Exercise (40 min)*
```
Scenario: Build a workflow that:
1. Captures a lead from a webhook (form submission)
2. Enriches the lead data using an API (e.g., Clearbit)
3. Creates a contact in a CRM (e.g., HubSpot)
4. Sends a personalized email
5. Creates a task for the sales team

Tools: Use n8n, Make, or Zapier (candidate's choice)

Evaluation Criteria:
- Workflow logic and structure
- Error handling
- Data mapping accuracy
- Efficiency and best practices
- Problem-solving approach
```

*Technical Discussion (20 min)*
- Walk me through your approach to the exercise.
- How would you scale this for high volume?
- How would you secure API credentials?
- What monitoring would you put in place?

---

**Final Interview (45 minutes)**

*With Delivery Director/CTO*
- Deep dive on technical approach
- Architecture and design thinking
- Client communication scenarios
- Career goals and growth

*With Founder/CEO*
- Culture fit
- Company vision alignment
- Final questions
- Compensation discussion

## Skills Assessments

### Automation Engineer Skills Test

**Take-Home Assignment (2-3 hours)**

```
BUILD AN AUTOMATION WORKFLOW

Scenario:
A real estate company needs to automate their lead management 
process. Build a workflow that:

1. Captures leads from a webhook (form submission with: 
   name, email, phone, property interest, budget)

2. Enriches the lead data:
   - Validate email (use any email validation API)
   - Add lead score based on budget (>$500K = high, 
     $250K-500K = medium, <$250K = low)

3. Creates/updates contact in CRM (use mock CRM or 
   HubSpot free tier)

4. Sends personalized email based on property interest:
   - If "residential": Send residential properties guide
   - If "commercial": Send commercial properties guide
   - If "investment": Send investment opportunities guide

5. Creates task for agent to follow up within 24 hours

6. Adds lead to appropriate nurture sequence based on 
   lead score

7. Logs all activity to a Google Sheet for reporting

Requirements:
- Use n8n, Make, or Zapier
- Include error handling at each step
- Include comments/documentation
- Test with sample data
- Submit workflow export + Loom video walkthrough

Evaluation Criteria:
□ Workflow logic and structure (25%)
□ Error handling (20%)
□ Data mapping accuracy (15%)
□ Efficiency and best practices (15%)
□ Documentation (15%)
□ Creativity and problem-solving (10%)
```

### Account Executive Skills Test

**Take-Home Assignment (1-2 hours)**

```
SALES EXERCISE

Part 1: Discovery Call Plan (30 min)
You're speaking with a VP Operations at a $30M professional 
services company. They responded to our LinkedIn outreach 
about automation.

Prepare:
- 5-7 discovery questions you'd ask
- Your opening pitch (2-3 sentences)
- How you'd handle "we don't have budget" objection
- Your close for the next meeting

Part 2: Proposal Outline (45 min)
Based on this scenario:
- Company spends 40 hours/week on manual data entry
- Average employee cost: $40/hour fully loaded
- They want to automate within 6 weeks
- Budget range: $20K-40K

Create a 1-page proposal outline including:
- Executive summary
- Proposed solution (high-level)
- ROI calculation
- Investment
- Next steps

Part 3: Email Sequence (45 min)
Write a 3-email sequence for a cold prospect who:
- Is a COO at a $50M e-commerce company
- Downloaded our "Automation ROI Calculator"
- Hasn't responded to initial outreach

Evaluation Criteria:
□ Discovery skills (25%)
□ Objection handling (20%)
□ Proposal quality (25%)
□ Written communication (20%)
□ Creativity and personalization (10%)
```

## Onboarding Checklist

### Week 1: Orientation
- [ ] Welcome email and equipment shipped
- [ ] HR paperwork completed
- [ ] Benefits enrollment
- [ ] IT setup (email, Slack, tools access)
- [ ] Meet the team (1:1s scheduled)
- [ ] Company overview and culture deck
- [ ] Review role expectations and goals

### Week 2-3: Training
- [ ] Product/service training
- [ ] Tools training (CRM, automation platforms, etc.)
- [ ] Process documentation review
- [ ] Shadow team members
- [ ] Complete any required certifications
- [ ] First assignment/project

### Week 4: Ramp-Up
- [ ] Begin independent work (with support)
- [ ] Weekly check-ins with manager
- [ ] 30-day review meeting
- [ ] Adjust training plan as needed

### Days 30-60-90: Milestones

**30-Day Goals:**
- Complete all training
- Understand products/services
- Begin contributing to team goals

**60-Day Goals:**
- Working independently
- Meeting baseline expectations
- Building relationships

**90-Day Goals:**
- Fully productive
- Meeting full expectations
- Ready for regular performance reviews
