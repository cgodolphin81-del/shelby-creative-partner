## Common Questions

### "How much should I charge?"
Start with the pricing in `pricing/PRICING-MODELS.md`. For your first few deals, you can discount 20-30% to build case studies, but don't go too low—you attract bad clients.

### "What if I don't have technical skills?"
Partner with a freelance automation engineer. You handle sales and client relationships; they handle delivery. Split revenue 50/50 or pay them hourly.

### "How do I find my first clients?"
Start with your network. Post on LinkedIn about what you're doing. Reach out to former colleagues. Offer free automation audits. See `client-acquisition/ACQUISITION-STRATEGY.md`.

### "What tools do I need to start?"
Minimal stack:
- n8n or Zapier ($0-50/month)
- OpenAI API (pay as you go)
- CRM (HubSpot free)
- Email (Google Workspace, $6/month)
- Project management (Notion free)

Total: <$100/month to start.

### "How do I deliver my first project?"
Follow `build-process/BUILD-PROCESS.md`. Start with discovery, map the workflow, build in stages, test thoroughly, train the client, provide support.

### "When should I hire?"
When you're turning down work or quality is slipping. First hire is usually a delivery engineer (freelance or part-time to start). See `team-building/TEAM-STRUCTURE.md`.

## Success Mindset

### Do's
✅ Start before you feel ready
✅ Charge what you're worth
✅ Over-communicate with clients
✅ Document everything
✅ Ask for testimonials
✅ Raise prices as you get better
✅ Focus on ROI, not features
✅ Build relationships, not just transactions

### Don'ts
❌ Wait for the "perfect" website
❌ Undercut on price
❌ Over-promise and under-deliver
❌ Work without a contract
❌ Skip discovery
❌ Be afraid to say no to bad fits
❌ Try to do everything yourself
❌ Forget to celebrate wins
