## Best Practices

### Do's
✅ Personalize every message (name, company, specific detail)
✅ Keep it short and scannable
✅ Lead with value, not pitch
✅ Include clear, low-pressure CTA
✅ Follow up 3-5 times (most responses come on follow-up 3+)
✅ Test and iterate based on response rates
✅ Track everything in CRM

### Don'ts
❌ Send generic, templated messages
❌ Write walls of text
❌ Lead with your company/features
❌ Use high-pressure tactics
❌ Give up after 1-2 follow-ups
❌ Forget to track and measure
❌ Forget to personalize at scale

---

*The best template is the one that gets tested, measured, and improved. Start with these, then make them your own.*
