# Pricing Models

## Comprehensive Pricing Strategy for AI Automation Services

---

## Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based, Not Hourly:** We price based on the value delivered to the client, not the hours we work. A $50K automation that saves $500K/year is a bargain.

**Transparent & Fair:** Clear pricing with no hidden fees. Clients should understand exactly what they're paying for.

**Aligned Incentives:** Our pricing models align our success with client success. When they win, we win.

**Flexible Options:** Multiple pricing models to fit different client needs, budgets, and risk tolerance.

## Project-Based Pricing

### Overview
Fixed-price engagements for well-defined projects with clear scope and deliverables.

### When to Use
- Clearly defined scope
- Fixed requirements
- Short to medium duration (< 12 weeks)
- Client prefers budget certainty

### Pricing Tiers

#### Tier 1: Starter Automation ($5,000 - $15,000)
**Scope:**
- 1-2 workflow automations
- Up to 5 integration points
- Basic chatbot (FAQ-based)
- Standard documentation
- 30-day support

**Typical Projects:**
- Lead capture to CRM automation
- Basic customer onboarding workflow
- Simple invoice processing
- FAQ chatbot for website

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks

## Performance-Based Pricing

### Overview
Pricing tied to measurable outcomes and ROI delivered.

### When to Use
- Clear, measurable outcomes
- Client open to risk-sharing
- High-confidence projections
- Long-term partnership focus

### Models

#### 1. ROI Share Model
```
Structure:
- Base fee: 50% of standard project price
- Performance fee: 20% of verified savings/revenue for Year 1

Example:
- Standard project price: $50,000
- Base fee: $25,000
- Projected annual savings: $200,000
- Performance fee (20%): $40,000
- Total potential: $65,000

Payment Schedule:
- 50% of base on signing: $12,500
- 50% of base on delivery: $12,500
- Performance fee: Quarterly based on verified metrics
```

---

#### 2. Gainshare Model
```
Structure:
- No or minimal upfront fee
- Monthly share of verified gains for 24-36 months

Example:
- Projected monthly savings: $15,000
- Gainshare rate: 30%
- Monthly payment: $4,500
- Contract term: 36 months
- Total potential: $162,000

Best for: High-confidence, easily measurable outcomes
```

---

#### 3. Hybrid Model
```
Structure:
- Reduced base fee: 70% of standard price
- Success bonus: Fixed amount on achieving milestones
- Ongoing optimization: Retainer for continuous improvement

Example:
- Standard project price: $50,000
- Base fee (70%): $35,000
- Success bonus (on ROI target): $15,000
- Optimization retainer: $3,000/month

Best for: Balanced risk/reward, long-term partnership
```

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- **50% upfront, 50% on delivery** (projects under $25K)
- **40/40/20** (projects $25K-75K): 40% on signing, 40% at 50% completion, 20% on delivery
- **30/30/30/10** (projects over $75K): 30% on signing, 30% at 33%, 30% at 66%, 10% on delivery

### Retainer Terms
- Monthly in advance
- Auto-renew monthly or quarterly
- 30-day cancellation notice

### Performance-Based Terms
- Base fee: Per standard terms
- Performance payments: Quarterly based on verified metrics
- Audit rights: Both parties can request metric verification

### Late Payment
- Invoices due net 15
- 1.5% monthly interest on overdue balances
- Work pause after 30 days overdue
- Legal collection after 60 days overdue

## Discount Guidelines

### Volume Discounts
- 3+ projects in 12 months: 10% discount
- 5+ projects in 12 months: 15% discount
- Enterprise agreement: Custom pricing

### Nonprofit/Startup Discount
- Qualified nonprofits: 20% discount
- Early-stage startups (<$1M revenue): 15% discount
- Must apply and qualify

### Prompt Payment Discount
- Payment within 5 days: 2% discount
- Must be specified in contract

### No Discount Scenarios
- Rush projects (already premium priced)
- Performance-based pricing (already risk-adjusted)
- Retainer contracts (value in predictability)

- Change requests outside original scope

## Pricing Conversation Scripts

### When Asked About Price Early
*"Great question. Our projects typically range from $15,000 to $100,000+ depending on scope and complexity. Before I can give you an accurate quote, I need to understand your specific needs. Can we schedule a discovery call to discuss what you're looking to automate?"*

### When Asked "Why So Much?"
*"I understand it's a significant investment. Let's look at the ROI. Based on what you've shared, this automation will save your team about 40 hours per week. At an average of $40/hour fully loaded, that's $83,000 per year in labor savings alone, plus reduced errors and faster turnaround. The investment pays for itself in about 8 months, and then it's pure value. Does that math make sense?"*

### When They Want to Go Cheaper
*"We can absolutely adjust the scope to fit your budget. Here's what we could do in Phase 1 for $X, and here's how it sets up Phase 2. Or, we could focus on the highest-ROI workflow first. What's most important to you: staying within budget or solving the full problem?"*
