# Pricing Guide

## Pricing Philosophy

We use **value-based pricing** combined with **cost-plus floors**. Our prices reflect:
1. The value delivered to the client (ROI)
2. Our costs (contractors, tools, overhead)
3. Market positioning (premium but accessible)

---

## Pricing Calculation Formula

```
Step 1: Calculate Base Cost
  Base Cost = (Estimated Hours × Blended Contractor Rate)

Step 2: Apply Margin
  Base Price = Base Cost × 2.0 (100% margin target)

Step 3: Add Value Component
  Value Add = (Client's Annual Savings or Revenue Gain) × 0.20
  (Capped at 50% of Base Price)

Step 4: Final Price
  Final Price = Base Price + Value Add
  (Adjusted to tier boundaries: $5K, $10K, $15K, $20K, $25K, etc.)
```

### Example Calculation

**Project:** Customer Service Agent for E-commerce Store

```
Estimated Hours: 80 hours
Contractor Rate: $75/hr (average)
Base Cost: 80 × $75 = $6,000

Base Price: $6,000 × 2.0 = $12,000

Client's Expected Savings: $80,000/year (2 support FTEs reduced)
Value Add: $80,000 × 0.20 = $16,000
(Capped at 50% of Base Price = $6,000)

Final Price: $12,000 + $6,000 = $18,000
(Adjusted to tier: $18,000)
```

## Pricing FAQ

**Q: Why are your prices higher than freelancers?**
A: We provide reliability, quality assurance, ongoing support, and a full team. Freelancers may be cheaper upfront but often lack the depth and backup we provide.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: Yes, for projects over $25K we can structure milestone payments. Retainers must be paid monthly.

**Q: What if the project takes longer than estimated?**
A: Fixed-price projects absorb overruns on our side. Only approved change orders affect the final price.

**Q: Can we start small and scale?**
A: Absolutely! Many clients start with a $5K workflow and scale to larger projects as they see ROI.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If we don't deliver agreed-upon outcomes, we'll work until you're satisfied or refund the portion not delivered.
