## 2. Key Metrics Definitions

### 2.1 Traffic Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Tells You | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Sessions** | Count | Total visits | Varies by size |
| **Page Views** | Count | Total pages viewed | Varies |
| **Unique Visitors** | Count | Individual people | Varies |
| **Traffic Sources** | % by channel | Where visitors come from | Diversified |
| **Organic Traffic** | Sessions from search | SEO performance | Growing MoM |
| **Direct Traffic** | Sessions typed URL | Brand awareness | Growing |
| **Referral Traffic** | Sessions from links | Backlink effectiveness | Growing |
| **Social Traffic** | Sessions from social | Social media ROI | Varies |

### 2.2 Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Tells You | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Avg. Time on Page** | Total time / Sessions | Content quality | 2+ minutes |
| **Bounce Rate** | Single-page sessions / Total | Relevance/engagement | <50% (blog), <30% (landing) |
| **Pages/Session** | Page views / Sessions | Site engagement | 2-4 pages |
| **Scroll Depth** | % of page scrolled | Content consumption | 50%+ |
| **Social Shares** | Count | Content virality | Varies |
| **Comments** | Count | Audience engagement | Varies |
| **Email Click Rate** | Clicks / Opens | Email engagement | 2-5% |

### 2.3 Conversion Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Tells You | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Conversion Rate** | Conversions / Sessions | Overall effectiveness | 2-5% (varies) |
| **Lead Conversion Rate** | Leads / Sessions | Lead gen effectiveness | 3-10% |
| **Customer Conversion** | Customers / Sessions | Sales effectiveness | 1-3% |
| **Email Signup Rate** | Signups / Visitors | List building | 2-5% |
| **Cost Per Lead** | Ad spend / Leads | Efficiency | Varies by industry |
| **Cost Per Acquisition** | Total cost / Customers | Overall efficiency | <30% of LTV |

### 2.4 Revenue & ROI Metrics

| Metric | Formula | What It Tells You | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| **Revenue** | Sum of sales | Total income | Growing |
| **Revenue by Channel** | Attribution | Channel effectiveness | Diversified |
| **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)** | Avg. purchase × Frequency × Lifespan | Long-term value | 3x+ CAC |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | Total marketing / New customers | Acquisition efficiency | <30% of LTV |
| **ROI** | (Revenue - Cost) / Cost | Overall return | 300%+ |
| **ROAS** | Revenue / Ad spend | Ad efficiency | 4:1+ |
| **Marketing % of Revenue** | Marketing spend / Revenue | Investment level | 10-20% |

## 4. Attribution Models

### 4.1 Model Comparison

| Model | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Last Click** | 100% credit to last touchpoint | Simple reporting |
| **First Click** | 100% credit to first touchpoint | Awareness campaigns |
| **Linear** | Equal credit to all touchpoints | Full funnel view |
| **Time Decay** | More credit to recent touchpoints | Short sales cycles |
| **Position-Based** | 40% first, 40% last, 20% middle | Balanced approach |
| **Data-Driven** | Algorithm-based on actual data | Advanced analytics |

### 4.2 Attribution Example

```
Customer Journey:
Day 1: Organic blog post (First Click)
Day 3: Social media ad
Day 5: Email newsletter
Day 7: Google search ad (Last Click)
Day 7: Purchase ($1000)

Attribution by Model:
┌──────────────────┬─────────┬─────────┬─────────┬─────────┐
│ Touchpoint       │ Last    │ First   │ Linear  │ Position│
├──────────────────┼─────────┼─────────┼─────────┼─────────┤
│ Organic Blog     │ $0      │ $1000   │ $250    │ $200    │
│ Social Ad        │ $0      │ $0      │ $250    │ $200    │
```

## 5. ROI Calculation Framework

### 5.1 Content ROI Formula

```
Content ROI = (Revenue Attributed - Content Cost) / Content Cost × 100

Where:
- Revenue Attributed = Conversions × Avg. Customer Value × Attribution %
- Content Cost = Creation + Distribution + Tools + Labor
```

### 5.2 ROI by Content Type

| Content Type | Avg. Creation Cost | Avg. Lifespan | Expected ROI |
|--------------|-------------------|---------------|--------------|
| Blog Post | $200-500 | 12-24 months | 300-500% |
| Video | $500-2000 | 6-12 months | 200-400% |
| Ebook | $1000-3000 | 24+ months | 400-600% |
| Webinar | $500-1500 | 6-12 months | 300-500% |
| Case Study | $500-1000 | 12-24 months | 400-700% |
| Infographic | $300-800 | 12-18 months | 200-400% |

### 5.3 Full Marketing ROI

```
Marketing ROI = (Revenue Attributable to Marketing - Marketing Investment) / Marketing Investment × 100

Example:
- Marketing Investment: $50,000
- Revenue Attributed: $200,000
- ROI = ($200,000 - $50,000) / $50,000 × 100 = 300%
```
