## 1. Case Study Framework

### 1.1 The Case Study Formula

```
COMPELLING CASE STUDY = 
    Relatable Hero (Client)
  + Clear Villain (Problem)
  + Guide (You/Your Solution)
  + Plan (Process)
  + Call to Action (Result)
  + Proof (Data/Testimonials)
```

### 1.2 Case Study Types

| Type | Best For | Length | Format |
|------|----------|--------|--------|
| **Success Story** | General marketing | 1,000-2,000 words | Blog, PDF |
| **Deep Dive** | Complex solutions | 3,000-5,000 words | Report, Microsite |
| **Video** | Social, landing pages | 2-5 minutes | YouTube, Website |
| **One-Pager** | Sales enablement | 1 page | PDF, Print |
| **Before/After** | Visual transformations | 500-800 words | Social, Blog |
| **ROI Focused** | Enterprise sales | 1,500-2,500 words | Sales deck, PDF |

## 7. Case Study Interview Questionnaire

### 7.1 Pre-Interview Questions (Send Before)

```markdown
# Case Study Interview Prep

## Background
1. What's your role at [Company]?

2. How long have you been with the company?

3. Can you describe your company in 1-2 sentences?

## The Challenge
4. What was the main problem you were trying to solve?

5. What impact was this problem having on your business?

6. What had you tried before working with us?

## The Solution
7. Why did you choose [Your Company]?

8. What was your experience working with our team?

9. What surprised you (positively) about the process?

## The Results
10. What results have you seen? (Please share specific numbers if possible)

11. How have these results impacted your business?

12. What's been the most valuable outcome?

## Looking Forward
13. What's next for [Company]?

14. How will [Your Company] continue to support you?

## Permission
15. Are you comfortable with us publishing this as a case study?

16. Can we use your name, title, and company logo?

17. Is there anything we should NOT include?
```

### 7.2 Interview Guide (During Call)

```markdown
# Case Study Interview Guide

**Duration:** 30-45 minutes
**Format:** Video call (recorded with permission)

## Opening (5 min)
- Thank them for their time
- Explain the purpose
- Confirm recording permission
- Review how the case study will be used

## Background (5 min)
- Tell me about your role
- Tell me about [Company]
- What were your goals when we started?

## Challenge (10 min)
- Walk me through the situation before we worked together
- What was the biggest pain point?
- What was at stake?
- How did this affect your team/business?
- What had you tried before?

## Solution (10 min)
- Why did you choose us?
- What was your experience like?
- What did our team do that made a difference?
- Were there any surprises (good or bad)?
- How was the collaboration?

## Results (10 min)
- What results have you seen?
- Can you share specific numbers?
- How do these results compare to your goals?
- What's been the business impact?
- What would you say to someone considering us?

## Closing (5 min)
- What's next for your company?
- Any final thoughts?
- Confirm next steps (review draft, approve, publish)
- Thank them again
```
