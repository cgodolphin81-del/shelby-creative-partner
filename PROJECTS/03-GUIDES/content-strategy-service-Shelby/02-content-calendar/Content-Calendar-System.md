## 9. Calendar Management Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Plan Quarterly, Review Weekly**
   - Set high-level themes quarterly
   - Review and adjust weekly in team meetings

2. **Buffer Content**
   - Always have 2 weeks of content ready to go
   - Prepare evergreen content for emergencies

3. **Batch Production**
   - Group similar content types for efficiency
   - Record multiple videos in one session
   - Write multiple posts in focused blocks

4. **Flexibility**
   - Leave 20% of calendar open for timely/trending content
   - Have backup content ready for breaking news

5. **Repurpose Everything**
   - One pillar piece → 10+ derivative pieces
   - See Repurposing System for details
