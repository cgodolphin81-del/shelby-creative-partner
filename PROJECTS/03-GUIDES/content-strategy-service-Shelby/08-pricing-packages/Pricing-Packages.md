## 1. Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Pricing Framework

```
PRICE = (Value Delivered + Expertise + Results) × Confidence Factor

```

**Our Pricing Principles:**
- Price based on value, not time
- Offer clear tiers with increasing value
- Include performance incentives where possible

- Build in upsell paths
