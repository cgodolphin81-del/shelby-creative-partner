Building a world-class AI training services team requires careful attention to roles, skills, culture, and growth. This document outlines our organizational structure, hiring strategy, and team development approach.

## Key Roles & Responsibilities

### Technical/Delivery Team

#### ML Engineer

**Levels:** Junior, Mid, Senior, Staff, Principal

**Responsibilities:**
- Design and implement ML models
- Fine-tune LLMs for specific use cases
- Build training pipelines
- Optimize model performance
- Deploy models to production
- Monitor and maintain models

**Skills Required:**
- Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow
- Transformer architectures
- Distributed training
- MLOps best practices
- Cloud platforms (AWS/GCP/Azure)

**Experience:**
- Junior: 1-3 years
- Mid: 3-5 years
- Senior: 5-8 years
- Staff: 8+ years

**Compensation (US, 2024):**
- Junior: $120K-$160K
- Mid: $160K-$220K
- Senior: $220K-$300K
- Staff: $300K-$400K+

## Hiring Strategy

### Sourcing Channels

| Channel | Use For | Effectiveness | Cost |
|---------|---------|---------------|------|
| Referrals | All roles | High | Low (bonus) |
| LinkedIn | All roles | High | Medium |
| Indeed/Glassdoor | Junior-Mid | Medium | Medium |
| Specialized Job Boards | Technical | Medium | Low |
| Recruiters | Senior/Executive | High | High (20-25%) |
| Universities | Junior/Intern | Medium | Low |
| Conferences/Events | Senior | Medium | Medium |
| GitHub/Technical | ML Engineers | High | Low |

### Hiring Process

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    HIRING PROCESS                            │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                                             │
│  1. APPLICATION REVIEW (1-2 days)                           │
│     • Resume screening                                      │
│     • Portfolio/GitHub review                               │
│     • Initial qualification                                 │
│                                                             │
│  2. RECRUITER SCREEN (30 min)                               │
│     • Background and experience                             │
│     • Motivation and fit                                    │
│     • Salary expectations                                   │
│     • Logistics (location, timing)                          │
│                                                             │
│  3. TECHNICAL ASSESSMENT                                    │
│     • Technical roles: coding challenge or take-home        │
│     • Sales roles: role play or case study                  │
│     • Time: 2-4 hours                                       │
│                                                             │
│  4. HIRING MANAGER INTERVIEW (60 min)                       │
│     • Deep dive on experience                               │
│     • Role-specific questions                               │
│     • Team fit assessment                                   │
│                                                             │
│  5. TEAM INTERVIEWS (2-3 × 45 min)                          │
│     • Peer interviews                                       │
│     • Cross-functional fit                                  │
│     • Culture assessment                                    │
│                                                             │
│  6. EXECUTIVE INTERVIEW (30-45 min)                         │
│     • For senior roles                                      │
│     • Strategic alignment                                   │
│     • Final assessment                                      │
│                                                             │
│  7. OFFER & CLOSE (3-5 days)                                │
│     • Reference checks                                      │
│     • Offer extended                                        │
│     • Negotiation                                           │
│     • Acceptance                                            │
│                                                             │
│  Total Timeline: 2-4 weeks                                  │
│                                                             │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Compensation & Benefits

- **Performance-Based:** Significant variable comp for sales/leadership
- **Equity Participation:** All employees receive equity
- **Transparent:** Clear leveling and compensation bands

| Level | Equity Range (4-year vest) |

## Retention Strategy

### Retention Goals

- First-year retention: >90%
- Overall annual retention: >85%
- High-performer retention: >95%

### Retention Tactics

1. **Competitive Compensation**
   - Annual market review
   - Proactive adjustments
   - Retention grants for key employees

2. **Growth Opportunities**
   - Clear career paths
   - Internal mobility
   - Stretch assignments
   - Promotion from within

3. **Meaningful Work**
   - Interesting projects
   - Customer impact visibility
   - Innovation time (10%)
   - Publication opportunities

4. **Great Management**
   - Manager training
   - Regular 1:1s
   - Clear expectations
   - Supportive environment

5. **Culture & Community**
   - Strong values
   - Inclusive environment
   - Social connections
   - Work-life balance

### Stay Interviews

```
Conducted: Annually (outside of review cycle)
By: Manager or HR

Questions:
• What do you enjoy most about your role?
• What would make your role even better?
• What are your career goals?
• What might tempt you to leave?
• How can we support you better?

Action:
• Document insights
• Create action plan
• Follow up quarterly
```
