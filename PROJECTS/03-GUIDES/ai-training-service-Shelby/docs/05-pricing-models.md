# Pricing Models & Commercial Terms

## Overview

Our pricing is structured to align with client value, project complexity, and ongoing partnership needs. We offer flexible engagement models from project-based to long-term strategic partnerships.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

**Guiding Principles:**

1. **Value-Based:** Pricing reflects business value delivered, not just hours worked
2. **Transparent:** Clear breakdown of costs with no hidden fees
3. **Flexible:** Multiple engagement models to fit different needs
4. **Aligned:** Our success tied to client success
5. **Scalable:** Pricing scales with usage and value

| Metric | Target | Credit if Missed |
|--------|--------|------------------|

| Response Time (Critical) | <1 hour | 10% of monthly fee |

**For Convenience:**
- Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice
- Client pays for work completed through termination date
- Transition assistance available (additional fee)

**For Cause:**
- Material breach with 30 days to cure
- Immediate termination for non-payment
- Immediate termination for confidentiality breach

## Pricing FAQs

**Q: Can we start small and scale?**
A: Absolutely. We recommend starting with a focused pilot ($50K-100K) and expanding based on results.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits/education?**
A: Yes, we offer 20-30% discounts for qualified nonprofits and educational institutions.

**Q: What if the project takes longer than expected?**
A: Fixed-price projects include 15% buffer. Significant scope changes are handled via change order.

**Q: Can we bring work in-house later?**
A: Yes. All deliverables include documentation and knowledge transfer. We offer transition support.

**Q: Do you work with our existing vendors?**
A: Yes. We integrate with your existing cloud, tools, and vendors whenever possible.

**Q: What about ongoing costs after deployment?**
A: Ongoing costs include infrastructure ($5K-50K/month depending on scale) and optional support retainers.
