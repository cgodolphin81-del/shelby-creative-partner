# Client Acquisition Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines our comprehensive go-to-market strategy for acquiring AI training service clients across enterprise, startup, and government segments.

---

## Target Market Segments

### Primary Segments

| Segment | Company Size | Deal Size | Sales Cycle | Priority |
|---------|--------------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| Enterprise | 1,000+ employees | $200K-$1M+ | 3-9 months | High |
| Mid-Market | 100-1,000 employees | $50K-$300K | 2-4 months | High |
| Growth Startups | 50-500 employees, funded | $75K-$250K | 1-3 months | Medium |
| Government | Federal, State, Local | $100K-$500K | 6-18 months | Medium |

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Enterprise ICP:**
```
✓ Revenue: $500M+
✓ Industry: Financial services, healthcare, retail, technology, manufacturing
✓ Pain Points: High support costs, knowledge silos, document processing burden
✓ Technical Maturity: Has data infrastructure, cloud adoption
✓ Decision Makers: VP/Director of Engineering, IT, Operations, or Digital
✓ Budget: Has AI/innovation budget or proven ROI path
✓ Urgency: Competitive pressure, cost reduction goals
```

**Mid-Market ICP:**
```
✓ Revenue: $50M-$500M
✓ Industry: Technology, professional services, e-commerce
✓ Pain Points: Scaling challenges, efficiency needs
✓ Technical Maturity: Some AI/ML experience or strong tech team
✓ Decision Makers: CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Operations
✓ Budget: $100K+ available for strategic initiatives
```

**Startup ICP:**
```
✓ Funding: Series A or later ($10M+ raised)
✓ Industry: AI-native, SaaS, fintech, healthtech
✓ Use Case: AI is core to product or operations
✓ Decision Makers: CTO, CEO, Head of AI
✓ Budget: Can allocate 10-20% of raise to AI infrastructure
```

Need:

+ Technical Fit:
