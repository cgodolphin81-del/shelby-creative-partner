## Unit Economics

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

```
LTV Calculation:

Average Annual Revenue per Client:
• Year 1: $140K (initial project + partial retainer)
• Year 2: $190K (expansion + full year)
• Year 3: $220K (mature relationship)

Average Client Lifespan: 4 years
Gross Margin: 65% (mature)

LTV = (Annual Revenue × Gross Margin × Lifespan)
LTV = ($200K × 65% × 4) = $520K
```

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

```
CAC Calculation (Year 2 steady state):

Annual S&M Spend: $3.8M
New Clients per Year: 50

CAC = S&M Spend / New Clients
CAC = $3.8M / 50 = $76K

LTV:CAC Ratio:
LTV:CAC = $520K / $76K = 6.8:1

(Target: 3:1 or higher ✓)
```

### CAC Payback Period

```
Payback Calculation:

Average Monthly Gross Profit per Client:
$200K annual revenue × 65% margin / 12 months = $10.8K/month

CAC Payback = CAC / Monthly Gross Profit
CAC Payback = $76K / $10.8K = 7 months

(Target: < 12 months ✓)
```

| Metric | Value | Target | Status |
