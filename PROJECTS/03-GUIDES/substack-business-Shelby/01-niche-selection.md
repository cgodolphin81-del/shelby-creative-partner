# 01 — Niche Selection: 20 Profitable Substack Niches

Choosing the right niche is the single most important decision for your Substack success. This guide provides 20 proven, profitable niches with reader willingness-to-pay data.

---

## 🎯 Niche Selection Framework

Before diving into the list, evaluate niches using this framework:

### The ICE Score
- **Interest (1-10):** How much do you know/care about this?
- **Competition (1-10):** How saturated is the market? (Lower = better)
- **Economics (1-10):** How much will readers pay?

**Target ICE Score: 20+**

### The Expertise Test
Ask yourself:
1. Could I write 50 emails on this without research?
2. Do people already ask me for advice on this?
3. Have I achieved results others want?

If yes to 2+: You're qualified.

## 📈 20 Profitable Substack Niches

### TIER 1: Highest Willingness-to-Pay ($10-50/mo)

#### 1. **AI & Automation for Business**
- **Audience:** Small business owners, solopreneurs, consultants
- **WTP:** $15-30/mo
- **Why it works:** Direct ROI—readers make money from your tips
- **Content angles:** AI tools, workflow automation, prompt libraries
- **Competition:** Medium (but demand is exploding)
- **Monetization:** Paid tier, courses, consulting, tool affiliates
- **Example potential:** 5K subs × 8% paid × $20 = $8K/mo

#### 2. **Investment Research (Retail Focus)**
- **Audience:** Individual investors, FIRE seekers
- **WTP:** $20-50/mo
- **Why it works:** One good stock tip pays for years of subs
- **Content angles:** Stock analysis, portfolio reviews, market timing
- **Competition:** High (but room for unique angles)
- **Monetization:** Paid tier, sponsorships (brokerages), courses
- **Note:** Requires credentials or proven track record

#### 3. **B2B SaaS Growth**
- **Audience:** SaaS founders, growth marketers
- **WTP:** $25-50/mo
- **Why it works:** Readers have company budgets, not personal
- **Content angles:** Growth tactics, case studies, teardown analyses
- **Competition:** Medium
- **Monetization:** Paid tier, sponsorships, consulting, job board
- **Example potential:** 2K subs × 10% paid × $35 = $7K/mo

#### 4. **Real Estate Investing**
- **Audience:** Aspiring and active real estate investors
- **WTP:** $20-40/mo
- **Why it works:** One deal = years of subscription value
- **Content angles:** Market analysis, deal breakdowns, financing strategies
- **Competition:** Medium-High
- **Monetization:** Paid tier, courses, coaching, affiliate (tools/lenders)

#### 5. **Executive Career Strategy**
- **Audience:** Senior professionals, VP/C-suite aspirants
- **WTP:** $15-35/mo
- **Why it works:** Career advancement = $100K+ value
- **Content angles:** Negotiation, positioning, executive presence
- **Competition:** Low-Medium
- **Monetization:** Paid tier, coaching, resume services, courses

## 📊 Niche Validation Checklist

Before committing, validate your niche:

- [ ] **Search Volume:** 10K+ monthly searches for related terms
- [ ] **Competition Check:** 3-10 existing Substacks (not 0, not 100)
- [ ] **Willingness to Pay:** Existing paid newsletters in space?
- [ ] **Your Edge:** What unique perspective/experience do you bring?
- [ ] **Content Pipeline:** Can you brainstorm 50+ post ideas?
- [ ] **Audience Access:** Do you have a way to reach initial readers?
