# 06 — Monetization Models: Paid Subs, Sponsors, Courses, Community, Events

Subscriptions are just the beginning. This guide covers all revenue streams available to Substack publishers and how to layer them for maximum income.

### Subscription Revenue Optimization

#### Levers to Pull:

1. **Increase subscriber count** — Growth tactics (see `04-growth-playbook.md`)
2. **Improve conversion rate** — Better paywall, pricing, positioning
3. **Raise prices** — As value increases
4. **Reduce churn** — Better content, engagement, community
5. **Push annual** — Higher LTV, lower churn

---

### Subscription Math

**Key metrics:**

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **MRR** | Paid subs × Monthly price | Track monthly |
| **ARR** | MRR × 12 | Track annually |
| **LTV** | Avg subscription length × Monthly price | $100+ |
| **Churn** | Cancelled subs ÷ Total paid subs | <5%/month |
| **Conversion** | Paid subs ÷ Total subs | 5-10% |

## 📢 Model 2: Sponsorships

### When to Start

**Minimum requirements:**
- 1,000+ subscribers (absolute minimum)
- 40%+ open rate (proves engagement)
- Consistent publishing (sponsors want reliability)
- Professional presentation

**Ideal starting point:**
- 5,000+ subscribers
- Niche audience (higher CPM)
- Business/professional audience (higher budgets)

---

### Sponsorship Pricing

#### CPM Model (Cost Per Mille/Thousand)

| Niche | CPM Range | Example: 10K Subs |
|-------|-----------|-------------------|
| General interest | $15-25 | $150-250/email |
| Business/Career | $25-40 | $250-400/email |
| Tech/SaaS | $30-50 | $300-500/email |
| Finance/Investing | $40-75 | $400-750/email |
| Specialized B2B | $50-100+ | $500-1,000+/email |

**Calculation:**
```
Opens = Subscribers × Open Rate
Revenue = (Opens ÷ 1,000) × CPM

Example: 10K subs, 45% open rate, $40 CPM
Opens = 10,000 × 0.45 = 4,500
Revenue = (4,500 ÷ 1,000) × $40 = $180/email
```

---

#### Flat Rate Model

Simpler than CPM, based on audience value:

| Subscribers | Rate Per Email | Monthly (4 emails) |
|-------------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1K-5K | $100-300 | $400-1,200 |
| 5K-10K | $300-750 | $1,200-3,000 |
| 10K-25K | $750-2,000 | $3,000-8,000 |
| 25K-50K | $2,000-5,000 | $8,000-20,000 |
| 50K+ | $5,000-15,000+ | $20,000-60,000+ |

---

### Sponsorship Formats

#### 1. **Dedicated Email**
- Entire email from sponsor
- Highest rate (2-3x standard)
- Best for: Product launches, webinars

**Rate:** $500-5,000+ depending on audience

---

#### 2. **Primary Sponsor**
- Top of email, 100-200 words
- Most visible placement
- Best for: Ongoing partnerships

**Rate:** Standard CPM or flat rate

---

#### 3. **Secondary Sponsor**
- Middle or bottom of email
- 50-100 words
- Best for: Budget-conscious sponsors

**Rate:** 50-70% of primary rate

---

#### 4. **Classifieds**
- 1-2 sentences
- Multiple per email
- Best for: Small businesses, job postings

**Rate:** $50-200 per slot

---

### Sponsorship Best Practices

**Do:**
- Vet sponsors (protect your reputation)
- Only promote products you believe in
- Be transparent ("This email is sponsored by...")
- Provide performance data to sponsors
- Build long-term relationships

**Don't:**
- Accept every sponsor
- Over-saturate (max 1-2 sponsors per email)
- Hide sponsorship disclosure
- Promise results you can't guarantee
- Work with sketchy companies

---

### Sponsor Outreach Template

```
Subject: Sponsorship opportunity: [Publication Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], writer of [Publication Name], a newsletter 
about [topic] with [X] subscribers.

My audience is [describe: role, industry, pain points]. They're 
[engaged metric: e.g., 45% open rate, 5% click rate].

I'm reaching out because [their company] serves a similar 
audience, and I think my readers would love [their product].

Sponsorship options:
- Primary sponsor: $X/email (100-200 words, top placement)
- Secondary sponsor: $Y/email (50-100 words, mid-email)
- Dedicated email: $Z (entire email)

All sponsorships include [bonus: e.g., social promo, Notes mention].

Interested in exploring this? I can share more details and 
audience demographics.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Substack URL]
[Media kit link, if available]
```

---

### Media Kit Essentials

Create a one-pager with:

- Publication name and tagline
- Subscriber count and growth rate
- Open rate and click rate
- Audience demographics (role, industry, location)
- Sponsorship options and pricing
- Testimonials from sponsors
- Contact information

**Tools:** Canva, Google Slides, Notion

## 🎓 Model 3: Courses

### When to Launch a Course

**Prerequisites:**
- 1,000+ engaged subscribers
- Proven expertise (results, credentials)
- Topic people ask about repeatedly
- Time to create (20-50 hours)
- Willingness to market it

**Course-fit signals:**
- Readers ask "How do I...?" questions
- You've created frameworks/systems
- People offer to pay for your advice
- You've gotten results you can teach

---

### Course Pricing

| Course Type | Length | Price Range |
|-------------|--------|-------------|
| **Mini-course** | 1-3 hours | $49-97 |
| **Standard course** | 5-10 hours | $197-497 |
| **Premium course** | 10-20 hours | $497-997 |
| **Cohort-based** | 4-8 weeks | $997-2,997 |
| **Mastermind** | Ongoing | $2,000-10,000/yr |

---

### Course Creation Framework

#### Phase 1: Validation (1-2 weeks)
- Survey audience on interest
- Pre-sell to gauge demand
- Define learning outcomes

#### Phase 2: Outline (1 week)
- Module structure
- Lesson breakdown
- Bonuses and resources

#### Phase 3: Creation (2-4 weeks)
- Record videos (or write content)
- Create worksheets/templates
- Build quizzes/assessments

#### Phase 4: Platform Setup (1 week)
- Choose platform (Teachable, Kajabi, Podia, Gumroad)
- Upload content
- Set up payment

#### Phase 5: Launch (2 weeks)
- Email sequence to list
- Webinar or workshop
- Limited-time offer

---

### Course Launch Email Sequence

**Email 1 (7 days before):** Teaser
> "Something big is coming..."

**Email 2 (5 days before):** Problem agitation
> "Struggling with [problem]? You're not alone."

**Email 3 (3 days before):** Solution preview
> "Here's what I've built to help..."

**Email 4 (Launch day):** Open cart
> "[Course Name] is now open! [Link]"

**Email 5 (Day 3):** Social proof
> "Here's what early students are saying..."

**Email 6 (Day 5):** Objection handling
> "Questions about [Course]? Let me answer them."

**Email 7 (Last day):** Urgency
> "Closes tonight at midnight. [Link]"

---

### Course Revenue Potential

| Subscribers | Conversion | Price | Revenue |
|-------------|------------|-------|---------|
| 1,000 | 2% | $297 | $5,940 |
| 5,000 | 2% | $297 | $29,700 |
| 10,000 | 2% | $297 | $59,400 |
| 10,000 | 3% | $497 | $149,100 |

**Note:** Course launches are episodic, not recurring. Plan 1-2 launches per year.

## 👥 Model 4: Community

### Community Models

#### 1. **Included with Paid Subscription**
- Private comments or chat
- No additional charge
- Increases subscription value

**Best for:** Most newsletters, building engagement

---

#### 2. **Separate Paid Community**
- Standalone membership
- $20-100/month
- Dedicated platform (Circle, Discord, Slack)

**Best for:** Highly engaged audiences, networking focus

---

#### 3. **Tiered Community**
- Basic: Free or included
- Premium: $50-200/month
- VIP: $500+/month

**Best for:** Maximizing revenue from superfans

---

### Community Platform Options

| Platform | Monthly Cost | Best For |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| **Substack Chat** | Free | Simple, integrated |
| **Discord** | Free-100/mo | Tech audiences, real-time |
| **Slack** | $8-15/user | Professional, organized |
| **Circle** | $49-199/mo | All-in-one, polished |
| **Mighty Networks** | $39-119/mo | Courses + community |
| **Skool** | $99/mo | Simple, gamified |

---

### Community Monetization

| Model | Price Point | Revenue Potential (1K members) |
|-------|-------------|-------------------------------|
| **Free (value-add)** | $0 | $0 (increases sub retention) |
| **Included with paid** | Bundled | Part of subscription revenue |
| **Standalone** | $20-50/mo | $20,000-50,000/mo |
| **Premium tier** | $100-200/mo | $100,000-200,000/mo |
| **Lifetime access** | $500-2,000 one-time | $500,000-2M (capped) |

---

### Community Success Factors

**Critical elements:**
1. **Clear purpose** — Why does this community exist?
2. **Active moderation** — Remove bad actors quickly
3. **Regular engagement** — You must participate
4. **Member spotlights** — Highlight active members
5. **Exclusive value** — Content/events only for members
6. **Facilitated connections** — Help members meet each other

**Common pitfalls:**
- Building it and abandoning it
- No clear rules or enforcement
- Letting it become a ghost town
- Over-promising and under-delivering

## 🎪 Model 5: Events

### Event Types

#### 1. **Virtual Meetup**
- Cost: $0-500 (Zoom, StreamYard)
- Ticket price: Free-50
- Capacity: 50-500
- Time: 2-4 hours

**Revenue potential:** $2,500-25,000 per event

---

#### 2. **Virtual Summit/Conference**
- Cost: $1,000-5,000 (platform, speakers)
- Ticket price: $97-497
- Capacity: 200-2,000
- Time: 1-3 days

**Revenue potential:** $20,000-500,000 per event

---

#### 3. **In-Person Meetup**
- Cost: $500-2,000 (venue, food)
- Ticket price: $50-150
- Capacity: 20-100
- Time: 2-4 hours

**Revenue potential:** $1,000-15,000 per event

---

#### 4. **In-Person Conference/Retreat**
- Cost: $10,000-100,000+ (venue, catering, staff)
- Ticket price: $500-5,000
- Capacity: 50-500
- Time: 1-3 days

**Revenue potential:** $25,000-1,000,000+ per event

---

#### 5. **Workshop/Training**
- Cost: $0-500 (platform)
- Ticket price: $97-497
- Capacity: 20-100
- Time: 2-8 hours

**Revenue potential:** $2,000-50,000 per workshop

---

### Event Planning Timeline

**8 weeks before:**
- Define event concept and goals
- Set date and budget
- Book venue/platform
- Create landing page

**6 weeks before:**
- Open ticket sales
- Announce to email list
- Start social promotion
- Recruit speakers (if applicable)

**4 weeks before:**
- Email reminder #1
- Social media push
- Finalize logistics
- Prepare materials

**2 weeks before:**
- Email reminder #2
- Send attendee instructions
- Confirm speakers/logistics
- Prepare follow-up sequence

**1 week before:**
- Email reminder #3 (final details)
- Test technology
- Prepare day-of materials
- Send calendar invites

**Day of:**
- Early tech check
- Welcome attendees
- Run event
- Collect feedback

**After:**
- Send replay (if applicable)
- Thank you email
- Feedback survey
- Plan next event

## 🔄 Model 6: Affiliate Marketing

### How It Works

1. Sign up for affiliate programs
2. Get unique tracking links
3. Share links with audience
4. Earn commission on sales

---

### Affiliate Commission Rates

| Category | Commission Range |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Software/SaaS** | 20-40% recurring |
| **Online courses** | 30-50% |
| **Books (Amazon)** | 3-8% |
| **Physical products** | 5-20% |
| **Financial services** | $50-500 per lead |
| **Hosting/tools** | 50-100% first month |

---

### Best Affiliate Practices

**Do:**
- Only promote products you use and love
- Disclose affiliate relationships
- Provide genuine reviews
- Negotiate higher rates as you grow
- Track performance and optimize

**Don't:**
- Promote everything (loses trust)
- Hide affiliate disclosures
- Recommend products you haven't tried
- Spam your list with promos
- Work with disreputable companies

---

| 10,000 | 5% | 3% | $50 | $750 |
| 10,000 | 5% | 5% | $100 | $2,500 |

**Note:** Highly variable based on niche and product fit.

- You have proven expertise/results
- You enjoy 1:1 work

- It distracts from content creation
