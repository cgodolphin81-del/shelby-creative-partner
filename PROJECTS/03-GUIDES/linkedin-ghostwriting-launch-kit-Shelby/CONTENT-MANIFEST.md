## 🚀 HOW TO USE

### For Immediate Deployment:
1. Open `LINKEDIN-CONTENT-LIBRARY.md`
2. Copy any post to Google Docs/Notion
3. Customize bracketed items ([Industry], [Company], etc.)
4. Add client-specific metrics and details
5. Schedule via LinkedIn native or Buffer

### For Client Customization:
1. Match post type to client's goals
2. Insert their actual stories/metrics
3. Adjust tone to their voice
4. Add their specific CTAs
5. Review and approve before scheduling

### For Carousels:
1. Use Canva or Figma for design
2. Follow slide-by-slide content exactly
3. Keep text minimal per slide
4. Export as PDF for LinkedIn
5. Post with brief caption + CTA
