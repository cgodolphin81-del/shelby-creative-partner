## 🚨 Contingency Plans

**If sales are slow (under 20 in first 2 weeks):**
- Increase paid ad budget
- Run flash sale (48 hours, 50% off)
- Partner with bigger influencer for giveaway
- Add urgency: "Price increases Friday"

**If refund rate is high (over 10%):**
- Review product page for overpromising
- Add more detailed preview/samples
- Improve onboarding email sequence
- Offer 1-on-1 support for unhappy buyers

**If affiliate signups are low:**
- Increase commission to 50% temporarily
- Create better affiliate resources
- Run affiliate contest with prizes
- Direct outreach to top performers
