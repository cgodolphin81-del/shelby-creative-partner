## 🚀 HOW TO USE THIS KIT

### Phase 1: Preparation (Before Monday)

1. **Customize the one-pager** with your name, company, and specific credentials
2. **Review the pricing tiers** and adjust to your market/positioning
3. **Have a lawyer review the contract** (don't skip this!)
4. **Set up your Calendly** for discovery calls
5. **Create a simple landing page** using the one-pager content

### Phase 2: Outreach (Monday Morning)

1. **Pick 3-5 sample posts** that best represent your style
2. **Choose 2 outreach DMs** that fit your personality
3. **Identify 20-30 target executives** on LinkedIn
4. **Start sending personalized DMs** (10-15 per day)
5. **Track responses** in a simple spreadsheet

### Phase 3: Sales Calls (Throughout the Week)

1. **Use the one-pager** as your sales deck
2. **Reference the pricing page** when discussing investment
3. **Share sample posts** to demonstrate your capability
4. **Send the contract** immediately after they say yes
5. **Collect payment** before starting work

### Phase 4: Delivery (After Signing)

1. **Send the onboarding questionnaire** immediately
2. **Schedule the 90-minute discovery call**
3. **Create their first 8 posts** based on questionnaire + call
4. **Get approval and start posting**
5. **Request testimonial at 30 days**
6. **Ask for referrals at 90 days**

## 📈 SCALING BEYOND THE FIRST CLIENT

**Once you have 1-2 clients:**

1. **Document your process** — create SOPs for everything
2. **Build a portfolio** — collect case studies and testimonials
3. **Raise your prices** — every 2-3 clients, increase by 10-20%

5. **Productize** — create courses, templates, or group programs
6. **Speak/write** — use your results to get on podcasts and publications
