## Usage Guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Navy Blue (#1a365d)
- Secondary: Gold (#d69e2e)
- Accent: White (#ffffff)
- Text: Dark Gray (#2d3748)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Montserrat Bold
- Body: Open Sans Regular
- Accents: Playfair Display Italic

**Style Notes:**
- Keep designs clean and professional
- Avoid cluttered compositions
- Use plenty of white space
- Maintain brand consistency
- Ensure text is readable on mobile

**Tools:**
- Midjourney v6 for AI generation
- Canva for text overlays
- Photoshop for final adjustments

---

**Delivery Formats:**
- PNG (transparent background for overlays)
- JPG (for photos and complex images)
- SVG (for icons and simple graphics)

**Resolution:**
- Social Media: 1080x1080 minimum
- Ads: 1200x628 minimum
- Landing Page: 1920x1080 minimum
