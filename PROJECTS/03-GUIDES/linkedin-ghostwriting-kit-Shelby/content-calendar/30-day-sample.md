# 30-DAY CONTENT CALENDAR (Sample)

---

## HOW TO USE THIS CALENDAR

This is a sample 30-day calendar showing the rhythm, variety, and strategic flow of a successful LinkedIn content strategy.

**Customize for each client based on:**
- Their industry and audience
- Their goals (leads, recruiting, authority, etc.)
- Their voice and comfort level
- Current events and industry news

**Posting Schedule:** Monday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday (4x/week)
**Best Times:** 8-9am or 12-1pm in their audience's timezone

---

## WEEK 1: FOUNDATION & CREDIBILITY

### Day 1 (Monday) - THE ORIGIN STORY
**Type:** Personal/Vulnerable
**Goal:** Humanize the leader, build connection

**Hook:** "I wasn't supposed to be here."

**Content Direction:**
- Share the unexpected path to their current role
- Include a specific moment of doubt or challenge
- End with what they learned about themselves

**CTA:** "What's a detour that turned out to be the right path?"

**Hashtags:** #Leadership #CareerJourney #OriginStory

---

### Day 2 (Wednesday) - THE INDUSTRY TAKE
**Type:** Thought Leadership
**Goal:** Establish expertise, spark conversation

**Hook:** "Everyone in [industry] is lying about [common topic]."

**Content Direction:**
- Call out a common misconception or bad advice
- Explain why it's wrong (with evidence)
- Share the truth (your contrarian take)
- Back it up with experience/data

**CTA:** "Agree or am I completely off?"

**Hashtags:** #Industry #ThoughtLeadership #TruthBomb

---

### Day 3 (Thursday) - THE CLIENT WIN
**Type:** Social Proof
**Goal:** Show results without bragging

**Hook:** "She said one sentence that made my week: 'This changed everything.'"

**Content Direction:**
- Share a specific client/customer success story
- Focus on THEIR achievement, not your service
- Include specific metrics if possible
- Acknowledge their hard work

**CTA:** "What's a win you're celebrating this week?"

**Hashtags:** #ClientWin #Results #BusinessGrowth

---

### Day 4 (Friday) - THE WEEKLY REFLECTION
**Type:** Personal/Relatable
**Goal:** End week on human note, encourage engagement

**Hook:** "This week taught me something I needed to hear."

**Content Direction:**
- Share one lesson from the week
- Keep it concise and universal
- Show humility and growth mindset

**CTA:** "What did this week teach you?"

**Hashtags:** #FridayReflection #Leadership #Growth

---

## WEEK 2: DEEPENING CONNECTION

### Day 5 (Monday) - THE BEHIND-THE-SCENES
**Type:** Transparency
**Goal:** Show the real work, build authenticity

**Hook:** "My calendar looks nothing like what you see on LinkedIn."

**Content Direction:**
- Share what a real day/week looks like
- Include the messy parts (meetings that run over, fires to put out)
- Contrast with the "highlight reel" people post

**CTA:** "What's your reality vs. your LinkedIn?"

**Hashtags:** #BehindTheScenes #RealTalk #ExecutiveLife

---

### Day 6 (Wednesday) - THE HOW-TO
**Type:** Educational
**Goal:** Provide value, establish helpfulness

**Hook:** "The exact framework I use for [specific task]."

**Content Direction:**
- Share a step-by-step process
- Use numbers/bullets for clarity
- Include a specific example
- Make it immediately actionable

**CTA:** "What framework should I break down next?"

**Hashtags:** #HowTo #Framework #Productivity

---

### Day 7 (Thursday) - THE TEAM SPOTLIGHT
**Type:** Culture/Leadership
**Goal:** Show you value your team, attract talent

**Hook:** "The best hire I made this year almost didn't happen."

**Content Direction:**
- Share a story about a team member
- Highlight what makes them great
- Include what you learned from them
- Show genuine appreciation

**CTA:** "Tag someone who makes your team better."

**Hashtags:** #TeamWork #Hiring #CompanyCulture

---

### Day 8 (Friday) - THE HOT TAKE
**Type:** Contrarian
**Goal:** Spark debate, increase engagement

**Hook:** "Unpopular opinion: [controversial but defensible statement]."

**Content Direction:**
- State a view that goes against conventional wisdom
- Explain your reasoning clearly
- Acknowledge valid counterarguments
- Stand firm but stay respectful

**CTA:** "Change my mind or tell me I'm right."

**Hashtags:** #HotTake #UnpopularOpinion #Debate

---

## WEEK 3: AUTHORITY BUILDING

### Day 9 (Monday) - THE LESSON FROM FAILURE
**Type:** Vulnerable/Teaching
**Goal:** Show growth, make mistakes relatable

**Hook:** "I lost $[X] because of one bad decision."

**Content Direction:**
- Share a specific failure
- Explain what went wrong (take ownership)
- Detail what you learned
- Share how you apply it now

**CTA:** "What's a failure that taught you the most?"

**Hashtags:** #Failure #LessonsLearned #Growth

---

### Day 10 (Wednesday) - THE INDUSTRY PREDICTION
**Type:** Thought Leadership
**Goal:** Position as forward-thinker

**Hook:** "Here's what's going to happen in [industry] in the next 12 months."

**Content Direction:**
- Make 3-5 specific predictions
- Back each with reasoning/trends
- Include one bold prediction
- Invite others to share theirs

**CTA:** "What's your prediction? Let's revisit this in a year."

**Hashtags:** #FutureOfWork #Predictions #Industry

---

### Day 11 (Thursday) - THE BOOK/RESOURCE RECOMMENDATION
**Type:** Value-Add
**Goal:** Show you're a learner, provide value

**Hook:** "The book that changed how I think about [topic]."

**Content Direction:**
- Recommend a specific book/podcast/resource
- Share 3 key takeaways
- Explain how you applied them
- Include who should read it

**CTA:** "What's the best book you've read this year?"

**Hashtags:** #BookRecommendation #Learning #PersonalDevelopment

---

### Day 12 (Friday) - THE QUESTION POST
**Type:** Engagement
**Goal:** Boost comments, learn from audience

**Hook:** "I'm genuinely curious: [thoughtful question about your industry]."

**Content Direction:**
- Ask a real question you want answered
- Explain why you're asking
- Share your current thinking
- Commit to reading all responses

**CTA:** "Drop your thoughts below. I'm reading every comment."

**Hashtags:** #Question #Community #Conversation

---

## WEEK 4: MOMENTUM & CONVERSION

### Day 13 (Monday) - THE VISION POST
**Type:** Inspirational
**Goal:** Rally audience around mission

**Hook:** "Five years from now, I want to be able to say..."

**Content Direction:**
- Share your vision for the future
- Make it specific and ambitious
- Explain why it matters
- Invite others to join the mission

**CTA:** "What's your 5-year vision?"

**Hashtags:** #Vision #Future #Mission

---

### Day 14 (Wednesday) - THE MYTH-BUSTER
**Type:** Educational/Contrarian
**Goal:** Challenge assumptions, show expertise

**Hook:** "[Common belief] is keeping you stuck."

**Content Direction:**
- Identify a common myth in your space
- Explain why it's wrong
- Share the truth
- Provide alternative approach

**CTA:** "What myth did you used to believe?"

**Hashtags:** #MythBuster #Truth #Advice

---

### Day 15 (Thursday) - THE GRATITUDE POST
**Type:** Personal/Connection
**Goal:** Build warmth, acknowledge supporters

**Hook:** "I don't say this enough, but..."

**Content Direction:**
- Express genuine gratitude
- Be specific about who/what you're thankful for
- Share why it matters to you
- Keep it sincere, not performative

**CTA:** "Who are you grateful for today?"

**Hashtags:** #Gratitude #Thankful #Appreciation

---

### Day 16 (Friday) - THE SOFT OFFER
**Type:** Business/CTA
**Goal:** Convert followers to leads (without being salesy)

**Hook:** "I get asked this question at least once a week..."

**Content Direction:**
- Share a common question you receive
- Provide a helpful answer
- Mention you help people with this (subtly)
- Include a soft CTA (link in comments, DM open, etc.)

**CTA:** "If you want to go deeper on this, my DMs are open."

**Hashtags:** #Business #Help #Offer

## CONTENT MIX BREAKDOWN

| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Personal/Vulnerable | 4 posts/month | Build connection |
| Thought Leadership | 4 posts/month | Establish expertise |
| Educational/How-To | 3 posts/month | Provide value |
| Social Proof | 2 posts/month | Build credibility |
| Engagement/Question | 2 posts/month | Boost algorithm |
| Business/CTA | 1 post/month | Generate leads |

---

## ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

**Daily (15-20 minutes):**
- Respond to all comments on your posts (within 2 hours if possible)
- Comment on 5-10 posts from your target audience
- Reply to DMs

**Weekly (30 minutes):**
- Review analytics: What performed well? Why?
- Adjust next week's content based on learnings
- Identify trending topics in your industry

**Monthly (1 hour):**
- Full content review and planning
- Update content pillars based on performance
- Plan any campaigns or special content

## PERFORMANCE TRACKING

**Metrics to Track Weekly:**
- Impressions per post
- Engagement rate (reactions + comments / impressions)
- Follower growth
- Profile views
- DMs received
- Link clicks (if applicable)

**Metrics to Track Monthly:**
- Top 3 performing posts (and why)
- Bottom 3 performing posts (and why)
- Follower demographics changes
- Inbound opportunities generated

## ADAPTATION GUIDE

**If engagement is low:**
- Try more controversial takes
- Increase personal/vulnerable content
- Post at different times
- Use stronger hooks

**If engagement is high but no leads:**
- Add more soft CTAs
- Create content that speaks to pain points
- Include more social proof
- Be clearer about what you offer

**If growth is stagnant:**
- Increase posting frequency
- Engage more with others' content
- Collaborate with other creators
- Try viral post formats (lists, carousels, stories)

---

**Remember:** This calendar is a starting point. The best content comes from staying attuned to your audience, your industry, and your own authentic voice. Adapt, experiment, and double down on what works.
