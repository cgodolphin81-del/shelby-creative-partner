# Marketing Plan: LinkedIn Ghostwriting Kit

**Product:** LinkedIn Ghostwriting Kit
**Launch Date:** Target Q2 2026
**Price Point:** $297-497

---

## 30-60-90 Day Strategy

### Days 1-30: Foundation & Soft Launch

**Week 1-2: Content Creation**
- [ ] Create 15 social media posts
- [ ] Record 3 promo videos
- [ ] Design 10+ graphics
- [ ] Write 5-email launch sequence
- [ ] Set up Gumroad page

**Week 3-4: Audience Building**
- [ ] Post daily on LinkedIn
- [ ] Engage with target audience (30 min/day)
- [ ] Build email list (target: 100 subscribers)
- [ ] Reach out to 10 micro-influencers

**Week 5-6: Soft Launch**
- [ ] Launch to email list first
- [ ] Offer early-bird pricing ($197)
- [ ] Collect testimonials
- [ ] Refine based on feedback

### Days 31-60: Public Launch & Promotion

**Week 7-8: Public Launch**
- [ ] Official public launch
- [ ] Run LinkedIn ads ($500 budget)
- [ ] Partner with 3-5 affiliates
- [ ] Host live Q&A webinar

**Week 9-12: Momentum Building**
- [ ] Daily social media posts
- [ ] Guest podcast appearances (target: 3)
- [ ] Write guest articles (target: 2)
- [ ] Run limited-time bonus offer

### Days 61-90: Optimization & Scale

**Week 13-16: Analysis & Optimization**
- [ ] Analyze sales data
- [ ] Optimize conversion funnel
- [ ] A/B test pricing
- [ ] Collect and showcase case studies

**Week 17-20: Scale**
- [ ] Increase ad budget (if ROI positive)
- [ ] Expand to new channels (Twitter, Instagram)
- [ ] Create upsell offers
- [ ] Build affiliate program

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** Low initial sales
**Mitigation:** Offer money-back guarantee, collect testimonials, refine messaging

**Risk:** Ad spend not profitable
**Mitigation:** Start small, test extensively, focus on organic first

**Risk:** Copycats
**Mitigation:** Build personal brand, continuous updates, community building
