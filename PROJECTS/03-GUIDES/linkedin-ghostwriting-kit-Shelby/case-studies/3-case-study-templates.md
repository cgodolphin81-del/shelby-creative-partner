## HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

1. **Gather data** from client analytics (LinkedIn native analytics or third-party tools)
2. **Interview the client** for quotes and specific outcomes
3. **Screenshot key posts** to include as visuals
4. **Get client approval** before publishing
5. **Create multiple formats:** Blog post, PDF download, carousel, video script
6. **Update quarterly** with fresh results

**Pro Tip:** Create a "Case Study Bank" with 5-10 detailed case studies. Use them in:
- Sales conversations
- Website testimonials page
- Proposal attachments
- Social proof in outreach
- Speaking pitch materials
