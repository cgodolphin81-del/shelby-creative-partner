## 🎯 Content Gap Analysis (What Competitors Miss)

**Underserved Topics:**
1. **DIY eco-friendly pet product tutorials** - Most sites only review products
2. **Cost comparison: eco vs. conventional** - Price transparency lacking
3. **Pet-specific sustainability guides** (by breed, size, age)
4. **Ingredient deep-dives** - What "natural" really means
5. **Local/Small brand spotlights** - Focus on major retailers only
6. **Seasonal eco pet care** - Summer/winter specific guides
7. **Multi-pet household sustainability** - Scaling eco practices
8. **Pet product lifecycle analysis** - True environmental impact
9. **Transition guides** - Switching from conventional to eco products
10. **Veterinarian perspectives** - Medical professionals on eco products

**Content Formats Missing:**
- Video comparisons and unboxings
- Interactive product finders (quiz-based)
- Downloadable sustainability checklists
- Community-driven reviews and forums
- Before/after case studies (pet health improvements)

- Create profiles on pet directories
- Submit to eco-friendly business listings

- Guest post on 2-3 small pet blogs

**Month 3-4: Growth (20-30 links)**
- Target pet blogs with DA 20-40
- Create linkable assets (infographics, studies)

- Partner with vet clinics for resource pages

**Month 5-6: Authority (30-50 links)**
- Pitch to major pet publications
