## ⚠️ Critical Success Factors

1. **Don't cheap out on hosting** - Free hosting is for testing ONLY. Move to paid ($3-10/month) for any monetized site.
2. **Humanize ALL AI content** - Google's 2026 algorithms detect scaled AI content. Add personal experience, case studies, original data.
3. **Build topic clusters, not random posts** - 1 pillar page + 10-20 supporting articles per cluster outperforms 50 scattered posts.
4. **Cross-link carefully** - Never link all sites to each other. Use 3-way linking, vary anchor text, keep it under 20% of total links.
5. **Diversify monetization from day 1** - Don't rely on AdSense alone. Stack affiliate + display + lead gen + digital products.
6. **Content velocity matters** - Publish 3-5 articles/week consistently. Drip-feed content, don't dump 50 posts at once.
7. **E-E-A-T is non-negotiable** - Author bios, About pages, contact info, publication dates, citations to authoritative sources.
