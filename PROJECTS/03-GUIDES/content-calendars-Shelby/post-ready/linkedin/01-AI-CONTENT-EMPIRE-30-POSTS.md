## POST 9 (Thursday - Framework)
**Hook:** The H.V.C. framework for viral content (steal this).

**Body:**
I've analyzed 1,000+ viral posts.

They all share 3 elements:

H - HOOK (first 2 lines)
- Pattern interrupt
- Curiosity gap
- Clear promise
Example: "Stop doing X. Do this instead:"

V - VALUE (main body)
- Actionable insights
- Specific examples
- Easy to scan
Example: Numbered lists, bullet points

C - CALL-TO-ACTION (ending)
- Clear next step
- Low friction
- Relevant to content
Example: "Save this for later" or "Comment your thoughts"
