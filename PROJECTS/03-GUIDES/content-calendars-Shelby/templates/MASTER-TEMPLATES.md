## 💬 ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY (Universal)

### Daily Engagement Routine (30 min/day)

**Morning (15 min):**
- Respond to all comments on your posts (first 2 hours critical)
- Reply to DMs with value (not just "thanks")
- Comment on 5 top accounts in your niche (meaningful comments, not "great post")

**Afternoon (10 min):**
- Share 1 relevant post from your network (add your take)
- Engage with 3 new accounts in your target audience
- Check trending topics in your niche, join conversations

**Evening (5 min):**
- Quick reply to any new comments
- Schedule tomorrow's engagement targets
- Note top performers for repurposing

### Weekly Engagement Goals
- 50+ meaningful comments on others' content
- 10+ DMs with potential leads/collaborators
- 5+ shares of community content
- 1+ collaboration post or takeover
- Respond to 100% of comments on your posts (first 24 hours)

### Comment Strategy (The Value-Add Method)
Instead of: "Great post!"
Try: "This resonates because [personal experience]. I'd add [additional insight]. Thanks for sharing!"

Instead of: "Interesting"
Try: "This challenges my thinking on [topic]. Have you considered [angle]? Would love to hear more."

### Building Relationships
- Tag relevant people when sharing their ideas (with credit)
- Create "community spotlight" posts weekly
- Host monthly Twitter Spaces/LinkedIn Live/Q&A
- Collaborate with 2-3 creators per month

### Handling Negative Comments
- Never argue publicly
- Take heated discussions to DM
- Delete spam, ignore trolls
- Use criticism as content ideas ("Someone asked...")

## 🔄 REPURPOSING STRATEGY (1 Piece → 10 Posts)

### The Content Waterfall Method

**Original Piece:** Long-form content (blog post, YouTube video, podcast episode, LinkedIn article)

**Repurposed Into:**

1. **Twitter Thread** (8-12 tweets)
   - Hook tweet
   - Key points as individual tweets
   - Examples/case studies
   - CTA tweet

2. **LinkedIn Post** (long-form text)
   - Story-driven adaptation
   - Professional angle
   - Industry insights
   - CTA

3. **Instagram/TikTok Carousel** (5-10 slides)
   - Hook slide
   - Key points (1 per slide)
   - Visual examples
   - CTA slide

4. **Short-Form Video** (30-60 sec)
   - Hook (first 3 seconds)
   - Main value prop
   - Quick tips
   - CTA

5. **Email Newsletter**
   - Personal intro
   - Curated content
   - Main piece summary
   - Exclusive bonus

6. **Quote Graphics** (3-5 images)
   - Best quotes from content
   - Branded templates
   - Shareable format

7. **Podcast Clip** (60 sec)
   - Best soundbite
   - Captioned
   - Platform-optimized

8. **Blog Snippet** (300 words)
   - Teaser post
   - Link to full piece
   - SEO-optimized

9. **Community Post** (Discord/Slack/FB Group)
   - Discussion prompt
   - Exclusive insight
   - Engagement question

10. **Story/Behind-the-Scenes**
    - Creation process
    - Personal reflection
    - Raw/unfiltered take

### Weekly Repurposing Workflow

**Monday:** Create long-form piece (blog/video/podcast)
**Tuesday:** Extract Twitter thread + LinkedIn post
**Wednesday:** Create short-form video + carousel
**Thursday:** Email newsletter + quote graphics
**Friday:** Community posts + engagement
**Weekend:** Batch create next week's variations

### Content Pillars (Rotate Weekly)
1. Educational (how-to, tutorials, frameworks)
2. Inspirational (stories, transformations, wins)
3. Promotional (offers, launches, testimonials)
4. Engaging (questions, polls, hot takes)

```
- Introduction with hook and promise
- [Number] main sections with subheadings
- Real examples and case studies
- Actionable takeaways
- Strong CTA

SEO Keywords: [keywords]
Target Reader: [describe ideal reader]
Goal: [educate/convert/entertain]
```

### Twitter Thread Generation
```
Create a [number]-tweet thread about [topic].

Structure:
- Tweet 1: Hook (pattern interrupt or curiosity)
- Tweets 2-[n-1]: Value points (1 idea per tweet)
- Final tweet: Summary + CTA

Requirements:
- Each tweet under 280 characters
- Use emojis strategically (2-3 per tweet max)
- Include line breaks for readability
- Add relevant hashtags (2-3 in final tweet)
- Thread should tell a complete story

Topic: [topic]
Audience: [audience]
Goal: [goal]
```

### LinkedIn Post Generation
```
Write a LinkedIn post about [topic] for [industry professionals].

Structure:
- Hook (first 2 lines must grab attention)
- Personal story or observation
- 3-5 key insights (use line breaks)
- Lesson learned or takeaway
- Question to spark comments
- Relevant hashtags (3-5)

Tone: Professional but personable
Length: 1000-1300 characters
Include: White space for readability
CTA: [specific call-to-action]
```

### Video Script Generation (Short-Form)
```
Write a [30/60/90]-second video script about [topic].

Structure:
- 0-3 sec: Hook (must stop the scroll)
- 3-15 sec: Problem/context
- 15-45 sec: Solution/value
- 45-60 sec: CTA

Requirements:
- Conversational tone
- Include visual cues [in brackets]
- Natural speaking pace (~150 words/min)
- Clear CTA at end

Platform: [TikTok/Reels/Shorts/LinkedIn Video]
Audience: [describe]
Goal: [awareness/engagement/conversion]
```

### Email Newsletter Generation
```
Write a newsletter email about [topic].

Structure:
- Subject line (under 50 chars, create curiosity)
- Preview text (complement subject line)
- Personal greeting
- Opening story/observation (2-3 sentences)
- Main content (3-5 key points)
- Exclusive bonus/insight
- CTA (clear and specific)
- P.S. (often most-read part)

Tone: [friendly/professional/casual]
Length: [short/medium/long]
Audience: [describe subscribers]
```

### Carousel/Slide Content
```
Create content for a [number]-slide carousel about [topic].

For each slide provide:
- Slide number
- Headline (5-7 words max)
- Body text (1-2 sentences)
- Visual suggestion

Structure:
- Slide 1: Hook/Title
- Slides 2-[n-1]: Key points
- Final slide: CTA + handle

Platform: [Instagram/LinkedIn/TikTok]
Style: [minimal/bold/playful]
```

### Engagement Question Generation
```
Generate 10 engagement questions about [topic] for [platform].

Requirements:
- Open-ended (not yes/no)
- Spark discussion and stories
- Relevant to target audience's challenges
- Some controversial/thought-provoking
- Some easy to answer (low friction)

Goal: Drive comments and community building
```

### Testimonial/Case Study Generation
```
Write a case study about [client/result] using this structure:

- Headline: [Result] in [Timeframe]
- The Challenge: [Before situation]
- The Solution: [What was implemented]
- The Process: [Key steps taken]
- The Results: [Specific metrics/outcomes]
- The Quote: [Client testimonial]
- The Lesson: [Key takeaway for reader]

Tone: Credible and specific
Include: Numbers and metrics where possible
```
