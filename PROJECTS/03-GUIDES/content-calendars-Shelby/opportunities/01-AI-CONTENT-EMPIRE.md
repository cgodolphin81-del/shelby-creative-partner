## 📊 PLATFORM STRATEGY

### LinkedIn (5x/week)
- Long-form posts (1000-1300 chars)
- Professional tone with personal stories
- Focus: B2B, agency owners, consultants
- CTA: Newsletter signup, consultation calls

### Twitter/X (7x/week + threads)
- Quick insights, threads, engagement
- Focus: Builders, indie hackers, creators
- CTA: Free resources, community join

### TikTok/Reels (4x/week)
- 30-60 second educational videos
- Behind-the-scenes of AI systems
- Focus: Younger entrepreneurs, creators
- CTA: Link in bio, follow for more

### YouTube (2x/week)
- 1 long-form tutorial (10-20 min)
- 3-5 Shorts (repurposed from TikTok)
- Focus: Deep dives, case studies
- CTA: Subscribe, free guide, course

### Blog (2x/week)
- SEO-optimized long-form content
- Tutorials, frameworks, case studies
- Focus: Organic traffic, evergreen
- CTA: Email capture, related resources
