# Revenue Projections

3-year financial model for a content marketing agency with retainers and project revenue.

---

## Overview

This projection model assumes:
- **Starting Point**: Solo founder or small team
- **Growth Strategy**: Retainer-focused with project upsells
- **Target Market**: B2B SaaS and professional services
- **Pricing**: Mid-to-premium positioning

**Key Assumptions:**
- Average retainer: $10,000/month
- Average project: $15,000
- Client retention: 85% annually
- Gross margin: 60-70%
- Operating margin target: 25-35%

## Exit Scenarios

### Acquisition Multiples

| Buyer Type | Multiple | Year 3 Valuation |
|------------|----------|------------------|
| Strategic (Agency) | 1.0-1.5x revenue | $6.3M - $9.5M |
| Strategic (Tech) | 1.5-2.5x revenue | $9.5M - $15.9M |
| Private Equity | 0.8-1.2x revenue | $5.1M - $7.6M |
| Financial (SDE multiple) | 3-4x SDE | $4.5M - $6.0M |

### Build to Sell vs. Build to Hold

**Build to Sell:**
- Prioritize growth over profitability
- Document all processes
- Reduce founder dependency
- Target: 3-5 year exit

**Build to Hold:**
- Prioritize profitability
- Build recurring revenue
- Develop management team
- Target: Sustainable dividends
