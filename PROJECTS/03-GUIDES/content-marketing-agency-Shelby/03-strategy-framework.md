# Strategy Framework

The complete methodology for developing content strategy that drives business results.

---

## Overview

Our strategy framework follows a four-phase approach:

```
PHASE 1: DISCOVERY → PHASE 2: RESEARCH → PHASE 3: PLANNING → PHASE 4: EXECUTION
     ↓                    ↓                    ↓                    ↓
  Understand           Analyze             Design               Implement
```

**Timeline**: 2-3 weeks for complete strategy  
**Investment**: $5,000 - $15,000

---

## Phase 1: Discovery

**Goal**: Understand the business, goals, and current state.

### Discovery Activities

| Activity | Duration | Output |
|----------|----------|--------|
| Stakeholder Interviews | 2-3 hours | Recorded insights, pain points |
| Business Goal Review | 1 hour | Prioritized objective list |
| Current Content Audit | 1-2 days | Inventory + gap analysis |
| Analytics Review | 2-3 hours | Performance baseline |
| Sales Team Input | 1 hour | Objection handling, FAQs |

### Key Questions to Answer

**Business Objectives**
- What are the top 3 business goals for this year?
- How does content marketing support these goals?
- What does success look like in 6 months? 12 months?

**Audience**
- Who are we trying to reach?
- What problems do they need solved?
- Where do they consume content?

**Current State**
- What content exists today?
- What's working? What's not?
- What resources are available (budget, team, tools)?

**Constraints**
- What can't we talk about?
- Are there compliance/legal considerations?
- What's the approval process?

## Phase 2: Research

**Goal**: Gather data to inform strategy decisions.

### 2.1 Audience Research

**Methods:**
- Customer interviews (5-10 customers)
- Sales team feedback
- Social listening
- Survey data
- Analytics analysis

**Deliverable: Buyer Personas**

Create 3-5 detailed personas including:

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Name/Title | Fictional name, real job title |
| Demographics | Age, location, company size |
| Goals | What they're trying to achieve |
| Challenges | Pain points, obstacles |
| Content Preferences | Formats, channels, frequency |
| Buying Stage | Awareness, consideration, decision |
| Objections | Why they might not buy |
| Trigger Events | What prompts them to seek solutions |

**Persona Template:**
```
NAME: Marketing Mary
TITLE: VP of Marketing
COMPANY: 50-200 employee B2B SaaS

GOALS:
- Generate more qualified leads
- Prove marketing ROI
- Build brand awareness

CHALLENGES:
- Limited budget
- Small team
- Long sales cycles

CONTENT CONSUMPTION:
- LinkedIn (daily)
- Industry newsletters (weekly)
- Podcasts during commute
- Webinars for deep dives

BUYING TRIGGERS:
- New funding round
- Leadership change
- Missing quarterly targets
```

### 2.2 Competitive Analysis

**What to Analyze:**

| Competitor | Content Types | Publishing Frequency | Top Topics | Engagement | Gaps |
|------------|---------------|---------------------|------------|------------|------|
| Competitor A | | | | | |
| Competitor B | | | | | |

**Research Areas:**
- Blog topics and frequency

### 2.3 Keyword Research

**Process:**

1. **Seed Keywords** - Start with 10-20 core terms
2. **Expansion** - Use tools to find related terms
3. **Categorization** - Group by topic/intent
4. **Prioritization** - Score by opportunity

**Keyword Categories:**

| Category | Intent | Example | Priority |
|----------|--------|---------|----------|
| Informational | Learning | "what is content marketing" | High |
| Commercial | Researching | "best content marketing tools" | High |
| Transactional | Buying | "hire content marketing agency" | Highest |
| Navigational | Finding | "openclaw content agency" | Medium |

**Keyword Prioritization Matrix:**

```

              │ (too niche│ (volume   │

    P         │ (low      │ (sweet    │
    E    │    │ competition)│ spot)   │
    T         └───────────┴───────────┘
    ION
```

**Deliverable: Keyword Strategy Document**
- 50-100 target keywords
- Organized by topic cluster
- Priority scoring
- Recommended content for each

### 2.4 Content Audit

**Inventory Existing Content:**

| URL | Type | Publish Date | Traffic | Leads | Status |
|-----|------|--------------|---------|-------|--------|
| | Blog | | | | Keep/Update/Remove |

**Audit Criteria:**
- Traffic (last 12 months)
- Engagement (time on page, bounce rate)
- Conversions (leads, signups)
- SEO performance (rankings, backlinks)
- Accuracy (is info still current?)
- Brand alignment (does it match current positioning?)

**Actions:**
- **Keep** - Performing well, accurate
- **Update** - Good topic, needs refresh
- **Consolidate** - Multiple similar posts → one comprehensive
- **Remove** - Outdated, off-brand, no traffic

**Deliverable: Content Audit Report**
- Full inventory spreadsheet
- Recommendations per piece
- Quick wins (easy updates with high impact)
- Content gaps identified

## Phase 3: Planning

**Goal**: Create actionable plan for content creation and distribution.

### 3.1 Content Pillars

**Definition**: 3-5 core themes that support business objectives and resonate with audience.

**Criteria for Strong Pillars:**
- Aligned to business goals
- Relevant to target audience
- Sustainable (can create 50+ pieces per pillar)
- Differentiating (not what everyone else covers)

**Example Content Pillars:**

```
PILLAR 1: Strategy
- Content planning
- Team building
- Budget allocation
- Measurement

PILLAR 2: Creation
- Writing techniques
- Video production
- Design principles
- AI tools

PILLAR 3: Distribution
- SEO
- Social media
- Email marketing
- Paid promotion

PILLAR 4: Optimization
- Analytics
- A/B testing
- Conversion rate
- ROI
```

**Pillar Validation:**
For each pillar, confirm:
- [ ] We can create 50+ content pieces
- [ ] It aligns to our expertise
- [ ] Our audience cares about this
- [ ] It differentiates us from competitors
- [ ] It supports business goals

### 3.2 Content Mix

**By Funnel Stage:**

| Stage | Percentage | Content Types |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| Top (Awareness) | 50% | Blog posts, social, videos, podcasts |
| Middle (Consideration) | 30% | Case studies, whitepapers, webinars |
| Bottom (Decision) | 20% | Demos, consultations, comparisons |

**By Format:**

| Format | Percentage | Rationale |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| Written (blog, articles) | 40% | SEO, scalable |
| Video | 25% | Engagement, social |
| Audio (podcast) | 15% | Authority, repurposing |
| Visual (infographics, decks) | 10% | Shareability |
| Interactive | 10% | Engagement, leads |

**By Topic Pillar:**
Distribute content evenly across pillars (adjust based on performance).

## Phase 4: Execution

**Goal**: Implement strategy with clear ownership and measurement.

### 4.1 Roles & Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|------------------|
| Strategy Lead | Overall direction, pillar ownership |
| Content Manager | Calendar management, workflow |
| Writers | Content creation |
| Editors | Quality control, brand voice |
| Designer | Visual assets |
| SEO Specialist | Optimization, keyword research |
| Distribution Manager | Social, email, promotion |
| Analyst | Performance tracking, reporting |

### 4.2 Workflow

```
Brief → Create → Edit → SEO Review → Approve → Publish → Distribute → Measure
  ↓       ↓        ↓        ↓          ↓        ↓         ↓          ↓
1 day   2-3 days  1 day    0.5 day    1 day    0.5 day   Ongoing    Weekly
```

### 4.3 KPI Framework

**By Objective:**

| Objective | Primary Metric | Secondary Metrics |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| Brand Awareness | Traffic, reach | Social shares, backlinks |
| Lead Generation | MQLs, signups | Conversion rate, CPL |
| Thought Leadership | Engagement, mentions | Time on page, citations |
| Sales Enablement | Pipeline influence | Sales cycle length |
| Customer Retention | Engagement, NPS | Renewal rate, expansion |

**Reporting Cadence:**

| Report | Frequency | Audience |
|--------|-----------|----------|
| Performance Dashboard | Weekly | Content team |
| Executive Summary | Monthly | Leadership |
| Deep Dive | Quarterly | Strategy review |

**Dashboard Metrics:**

```
TRAFFIC
- Total sessions
- Organic traffic
- Top pages

ENGAGEMENT
- Avg. time on page
- Bounce rate
- Social shares

LEADS
- Form submissions
- Content downloads
- Demo requests

SEO
- Keyword rankings
- Domain authority

CONVERSIONS

- Revenue attributed
```
