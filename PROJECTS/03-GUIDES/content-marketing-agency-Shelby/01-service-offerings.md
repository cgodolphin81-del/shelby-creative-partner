## 1. Content Strategy

**What It Is**: Comprehensive planning for all content activities aligned to business goals.

### Deliverables

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Audience Personas | 3-5 detailed buyer personas with pain points, motivations, content preferences |
| Content Audit | Analysis of existing content (gaps, opportunities, repurposing candidates) |
| Competitive Analysis | What competitors publish, where they win, white space opportunities |
| Content Pillars | 3-5 core themes that support business objectives |
| Channel Strategy | Which channels matter for your audience + why |
| Editorial Calendar | 90-day content calendar with topics, formats, publish dates |
| KPI Framework | Metrics that matter: traffic, engagement, leads, revenue attribution |

### Process

1. **Discovery Call** (1 hr) - Business goals, current state, challenges
2. **Stakeholder Interviews** (2-3 hrs) - Sales, product, customer success input
3. **Research Phase** (3-5 days) - Audience, competitors, keyword research
4. **Strategy Development** (3-5 days) - Synthesis and recommendations
5. **Presentation** (1 hr) - Walkthrough + Q&A
6. **Revisions** (1 week) - Incorporate feedback

### Timeline: 2-3 weeks
### Investment: $5,000 - $15,000

### Ideal For
- Companies launching content marketing for the first time
- Businesses with scattered content needing direction
- Organizations pivoting positioning or entering new markets
