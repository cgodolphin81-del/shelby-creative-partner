### Traffic Requirements for Premium Ad Networks

| Network | Minimum Traffic | Revenue Share | Typical RPM |
|---------|----------------|---------------|-------------|
| Google AdSense | None | 68% | $2-15 |
| Ezoic | None | Varies | $5-15 |
| Mediavine Journey | 1,000 sessions/mo | 70% | $8-15 |
| Mediavine Main | 50,000 sessions/mo | 75-80% | $15-30 |
| Raptive (AdThrive) | 100,000 pageviews/mo | 75% | $20-40 |

| Authority | Months 13-24 | 50,000-200,000+ visits | $3,000-15,000+ | Raptive, diversified revenue |

## Philosophy

**Build for humans, optimize for search, monetize strategically.**

The most successful authority sites in 2025 share these traits:
- Comprehensive topic coverage (topical authority)
- Genuine expertise and experience (E-E-A-T)
- User-first content that solves real problems
- Diversified revenue (not dependent on single income stream)
- Sustainable, ethical growth practices
