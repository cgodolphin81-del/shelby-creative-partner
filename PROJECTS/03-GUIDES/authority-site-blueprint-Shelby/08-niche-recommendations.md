## Niche Selection Criteria

### How We Evaluated These Niches

**Scoring Factors:**

| Factor | Weight | What We Looked For |
|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Profitability** | 25% | RPM rates, affiliate commissions, product prices |
| **Competition** | 20% | Number of established competitors, barrier to entry |
| **Search Demand** | 20% | Total addressable search volume |
| **Content Viability** | 15% | Ability to create 200+ articles |
| **Growth Trend** | 10% | Growing, stable, or declining interest |
| **Monetization Options** | 10% | Diversity of revenue streams |

### Ideal Niche Characteristics

✅ **Profitable:** High RPM ($15+) or high-ticket affiliate opportunities
✅ **Growing:** Stable or increasing search demand
✅ **Achievable:** Can compete without millions in funding
✅ **Evergreen:** Not a passing trend
✅ **Passion Potential:** You can sustain interest for 2-5 years
✅ **Multiple Revenue Streams:** Ads, affiliate, products, sponsorships

**Why This Niche:**
- Remote work is permanent for millions
- Health concerns drive purchases
