# Google Business Profile Optimization

Complete guide to optimizing Google Business Profile (GBP) for local search dominance.

---

## Why GBP Matters

- **76%** of people who search on their smartphone for something nearby visit a business within 24 hours
- **28%** of those searches result in a purchase
- GBP profiles appear in **Google Maps** and **Local Pack** (top 3 local results)
- Free and one of the highest ROI local marketing activities

---

## Initial Setup Checklist

### Claim & Verify
- [ ] Claim your business listing (search Google for your business)
- [ ] Verify ownership (postcard, phone, email, or instant if eligible)
- [ ] Complete verification within 14 days

### Business Information
- [ ] **Business Name**: Use exact legal name (no keyword stuffing)
- [ ] **Address**: Accurate, consistent with other listings
- [ ] **Phone Number**: Local number preferred (not 800)
- [ ] **Website**: Primary website URL with UTM tracking
- [ ] **Hours**: Accurate, including holiday hours
- [ ] **Category**: Primary category + up to 9 additional categories

### Category Selection (Critical)
```
Primary Category: Choose the MOST specific category that describes your core business

Examples:
- "Plumber" not "Contractor"
- "Italian Restaurant" not just "Restaurant"
- "Dental Clinic" not just "Doctor"

Additional Categories: Add all that genuinely apply
- Service businesses: Add each service type
- Retail: Add product categories
- Restaurants: Add cuisine types, meal types
```

## Profile Optimization

### Business Description
```
Character Limit: 750 characters (use all of it!)

Structure:
- First 250 characters are most important (show in search)
- Include primary keywords naturally
- Highlight unique value proposition
- Mention service areas
- Include call-to-action

Example (Plumber):
"ABC Plumbing has been serving [City] homeowners and businesses with expert plumbing 
services since 2005. We specialize in emergency plumbing, drain cleaning, water heater 
installation, and bathroom remodeling. Our licensed, insured plumbers provide 24/7 
emergency service with upfront pricing and a 100% satisfaction guarantee. Serving [City], 
[Neighborhood 1], [Neighborhood 2], and surrounding areas. Call today for same-day service!"
```

### Photos & Videos
```
Photo Requirements:
- Minimum: 10 photos (Google prefers 100+)
- Resolution: 720px minimum, 1080px recommended
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Size: 10KB - 5MB

Photo Types to Upload:
✓ Exterior (front of building, signage)
✓ Interior (waiting area, workspace)
✓ Team photos (uniformed, professional)
✓ Work in progress
✓ Completed projects (before/after)
✓ Products/Services
✓ Equipment/Technology
✓ Customer interactions (with permission)

Upload Schedule:
- Initial: 20-30 photos
- Ongoing: 5-10 new photos monthly
- Always add photos after completing jobs

Video Guidelines:
- Length: 30 seconds maximum
- Format: MP4
- Size: Up to 100MB
- Content: Virtual tours, team intros, work showcases
```

### Services Section
```
Add All Services:
- List every service you offer
- Use customer-friendly language
- Include descriptions (up to 300 characters each)
- Add starting prices if competitive

Service Categories:
Organize services logically:
- Emergency Services
- Residential Services
- Commercial Services
- Maintenance Plans
- Specialized Services
```

### Products Section (If Applicable)
```
For Retail/Restaurant:
- Add product photos
- Include prices
- Write descriptions
- Organize into categories
- Keep updated (remove out of stock)
```

## Reviews Management

### Getting Reviews
```
Best Practices:
- Ask every satisfied customer
- Make it easy (send direct link)
- Ask soon after service (within 24-48 hours)
- Train team to request reviews
- Use multiple channels (email, text, in-person)

Review Request Templates:

Email Template:
Subject: How did we do?

Hi [Name],

Thank you for choosing [Business Name]! We hope you're happy with our [service].

Would you take a moment to share your experience on Google? It helps us serve you 
better and helps others find quality [service] in [City].

[Direct Review Link]

Thank you for your support!

[Name]
[Business Name]

Text Message Template:
"Hi [Name]! Thanks for choosing [Business Name]. If you're happy with our service, 
would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It means the world to us! 
[Short Link]"

In-Person Script:
"We're so glad we could help! If you have a moment, we'd really appreciate if you 
could share your experience on Google. It helps other folks in [City] find us when 
they need [service]. I can text you the link if that's easy?"

QR Code:
- Create QR code linking to review page
- Display at checkout/reception
- Include on business cards
- Add to invoices
```

### Responding to Reviews
```
Positive Reviews (Respond to ALL):
- Thank them by name
- Mention specific details from their review
- Reinforce your value
- Invite them back
- Keep it brief (2-3 sentences)

Example:
"Thank you so much, [Name]! We're thrilled we could [specific service they mentioned]. 
Our team takes pride in [value they praised]. We look forward to serving you again soon! 
- [Owner Name], [Business Name]"

Negative Reviews (Respond to ALL):
- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Stay calm and professional
- Acknowledge their concern
- Apologize sincerely (even if you disagree)
- Take it offline (provide contact info)
- Don't be defensive

Example:
"Hi [Name], thank you for your feedback. We're sorry to hear your experience didn't 
meet our usual standards. This isn't typical of our service, and we'd like to make 
it right. Please contact me directly at [phone/email] so we can discuss this further. 
- [Owner Name], [Business Name]"

Never:
❌ Argue with reviewers
❌ Make excuses
❌ Blame the customer
❌ Share private information
❌ Copy/paste generic responses
```

### Review Goals
```
Metrics to Track:
- Total review count (aim for 100+)
- Average rating (maintain 4.5+)
- Review velocity (3-5 new reviews per week)
- Response rate (100% - respond to all)
- Response time (under 48 hours)

Milestones:
- 10 reviews: Minimum credibility
- 50 reviews: Strong local presence
- 100+ reviews: Market leader
- 4.8+ average: Top-tier reputation
```

## Q&A Section

### Setup
```
- Claim your Q&A section
- Add common questions yourself (yes, you can!)
- Provide helpful, detailed answers
- Include keywords naturally
```

### Common Questions to Pre-Add
```
- Do you offer free estimates?
- What are your hours?
- Do you serve [area]?
- Are you licensed and insured?
- Do you offer [specific service]?
- What payment methods do you accept?
- Do you provide emergency service?
- How do I schedule an appointment?
```

### Monitoring
```
- Check Q&A weekly
- Answer new questions within 24 hours
- Flag inappropriate questions
- Update answers as needed
```

## Messaging

### Setup
```
- Enable messaging in GBP dashboard
- Set up mobile notifications
- Define response hours
- Create quick responses for common questions
- Aim to respond within minutes (not hours)
```

### Best Practices
```
- Response time affects ranking
- Set expectations (auto-reply with hours)
- Use for quick questions only
- Move complex conversations to phone/email
- Track message-to-conversion rate
```

## Insights & Analytics

### Key Metrics to Track
```
Monthly Review:
- Profile views (trend over time)
- Search queries (what people searched for)
- Customer actions (calls, direction requests, website clicks)
- Photo views
- Booking requests (if applicable)

Compare:
- Month-over-month
- Year-over-year
- Vs. similar businesses (Google provides this)
```

### Optimization Based on Data
```
If searches are low:
- Improve categories
- Add more keywords to description
- Get more reviews
- Post more frequently

If actions are low:
- Improve photos
- Add stronger CTAs
- Update business hours
- Add posts with offers
```

## Advanced Tactics

### Service Area Business (No Physical Location)
```
- Hide your address
- Define service areas (cities/regions)
- Use a professional PO Box or virtual office if needed
- Ensure NAP consistency across web
```

### Multiple Locations
```
- Create separate GBP for each location
- Unique phone numbers (local preferred)
- Unique photos for each location
- Location-specific posts
- Manage through GBP dashboard or API
```

### Seasonal Updates
```
- Update hours for holidays
- Post seasonal offers
- Add seasonal photos
- Update services (seasonal offerings)
- Modify description for peak seasons
```

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Keyword stuffing in business name
❌ Using a PO Box as address (unless service area business)
❌ Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across web
❌ Ignoring reviews (positive or negative)
❌ Posting infrequently or not at all
❌ Low-quality or stock photos
❌ Not responding to Q&A
❌ Slow response to messages
❌ Inaccurate business hours
❌ Wrong categories
❌ Not tracking insights
