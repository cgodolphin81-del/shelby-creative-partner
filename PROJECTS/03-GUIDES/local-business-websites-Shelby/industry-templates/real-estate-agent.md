# Real Estate Agent Website Template

## Industry Overview
- **Target Audience:** Home buyers, sellers, investors
- **Key Services:** Buying, selling, property management, consultations
- **Average Commission:** $10,000 - $50,000+ per transaction
- **Decision Factors:** Track record, local expertise, marketing, reviews
