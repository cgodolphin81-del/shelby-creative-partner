# Veterinary Clinic Website Template

## Industry Overview
- **Target Audience:** Pet owners (dogs, cats, exotic pets)
- **Key Services:** Wellness exams, vaccinations, surgery, dental, emergency
- **Average Visit Value:** $100 - $5,000+
- **Decision Factors:** Care quality, emergency services, reviews, location
