# Restaurant Website Template

## Industry Overview
- **Target Audience:** Local diners, tourists, event planners
- **Key Services:** Dine-in, takeout, delivery, catering, private events
- **Average Check:** $15 - $150+ per person
- **Peak Times:** Weekends, holidays, special occasions

## Homepage Structure

### Hero Section
```
HEADLINE: Authentic [Cuisine] in the Heart of [City]
SUBHEAD: Farm-to-Table • Craft Cocktails • Private Dining Available
PRIMARY CTA: Reserve a Table
SECONDARY CTA: Order Online
TRUST BADGES: Locally Owned • Award Winning • Open 7 Days
```

### Key Sections
1. Menu (with prices, dietary indicators)
2. Online Ordering
3. Reservations
4. Private Events & Catering
5. About Our Story
6. Location & Hours
7. Gallery (food photos, ambiance)
8. Reviews & Press

### Menu Highlights
- Featured dishes with photos
- Seasonal specials
- Dietary options (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
- Happy hour menu
- Kids menu
- Wine/cocktail list

### Online Ordering Integration
- Direct ordering (preferred)
- Third-party links (UberEats, DoorDash, GrubHub)
- Pickup and delivery options
- Catering orders

### Reservation System
- OpenTable integration or similar
- Party size selector
- Date/time picker
- Special requests field
- Confirmation email/SMS

### Private Events Section
- Private dining room info
- Catering services
- Event packages
- Contact for bookings
- Capacity information

### About/Story Section
- Chef/owner story
- Restaurant philosophy
- Sourcing practices
- Team photos
- Awards and recognition

### Location & Hours
- Address with map
- Hours of operation
- Parking information
- Public transit directions
- Phone number
- Email for inquiries

### Footer
- Social media links
- Newsletter signup
- Gift cards
- Careers
- Press inquiries
- Allergy disclaimer

## Color Scheme
- Varies by cuisine/concept
- Italian: Red, Green, Cream
- Modern: Black, White, Gold
- Casual: Warm earth tones
- Seafood: Blue, White, Natural wood

- Email List (exclusive offers)
- Birthday Club (free dessert)
- VIP Rewards Program

- Happy Hour Specials

## Required Pages
1. Homepage
2. Menu (with sections)
3. Order Online

5. About Us
6. Private Events
7. Catering

10. Contact

## Conversion Elements
- Prominent reservation button
- Online ordering on every page
- Click-to-call
- Social media integration
- Email capture
- Google Maps integration
- Instagram feed

## Technical Requirements
- Mobile-first design (70%+ mobile traffic)
- Fast loading (menu images optimized)
- ADA compliant
- Menu easily updatable
- Integration with POS system
- Review platform integration

## Special Considerations
- Menu must be easy to update
- High-quality food photography essential
- Hours must be accurate and prominent
- Allergy/dietary information required
- Health inspection scores (some markets)
