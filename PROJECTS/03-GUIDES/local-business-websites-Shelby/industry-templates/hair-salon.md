# Hair Salon/Beauty Salon Website Template

## Industry Overview
- **Target Audience:** Women, men, children seeking hair and beauty services
- **Key Services:** Haircuts, color, styling, treatments, extensions
- **Average Visit Value:** $50 - $500+
- **Peak Times:** Weekends, evenings, holidays
