## Copy Tips

### Do's
- ✅ Speak directly to the reader ("you," "your")
- ✅ Focus on benefits, not features
- ✅ Use specific numbers and details
- ✅ Include social proof everywhere
- ✅ Make CTAs action-oriented and clear
- ✅ Address objections proactively
- ✅ Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- ✅ Use subheadings to break up text

### Don'ts
- ❌ Use generic corporate speak
- ❌ Make claims without proof
- ❌ Write long walls of text
- ❌ Hide contact information
- ❌ Use passive voice excessively
- ❌ Forget mobile readability
- ❌ Overpromise or guarantee the impossible
