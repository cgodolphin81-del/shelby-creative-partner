## Service Description Writing Tips

### Structure
1. Start with the customer's problem or goal
2. Explain how your service solves it
3. List specific deliverables
4. Highlight benefits (not just features)
5. Address common concerns
6. Include clear next steps

### Language
- Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to the reader
- Focus on outcomes, not just activities
- Be specific and concrete
- Avoid jargon unless your audience expects it
- Use active voice

### SEO Tips
- Include primary keyword in H1
- Use related keywords naturally throughout
- Include location (city/region)
- Answer common questions (featured snippets)
- Link to related service pages
- Include schema markup for services

*Adapt these templates to each service's unique value proposition and customer concerns.*
