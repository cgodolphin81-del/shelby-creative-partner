## About Page Best Practices

### Do's
- ✅ Tell a genuine story (people connect with stories)
- ✅ Show real photos of real people (no stock photos)
- ✅ Include specific numbers and credentials
- ✅ Explain WHY you do what you do
- ✅ Address the visitor's needs ("what's in it for me?")
- ✅ Show personality and brand voice
- ✅ Include clear next steps

### Don'ts
- ❌ Make it all about you (connect to customer benefits)
- ❌ Use generic corporate speak
- ❌ Hide behind stock photography
- ❌ Forget to include contact information
- ❌ Make it too long (aim for scannable)
- ❌ Sound like every other business
- ❌ Forget mobile optimization

### SEO Tips
- Include "about [business type] [city]" naturally
- Link to key service pages
- Include schema markup for Organization
- Add team member schema if applicable
- Include location information
- Answer "who is [business name]" queries

---

*The best about pages make the visitor feel like they know you and trust you. Be authentic, be specific, and always connect back to how you can help them.*
