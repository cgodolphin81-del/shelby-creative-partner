## FAQ Best Practices

### Content Guidelines
- ✅ Write questions in your customers' words (use real inquiries)
- ✅ Keep answers clear and concise (2-4 sentences typically)
- ✅ Be honest and transparent
- ✅ Address objections proactively
- ✅ Include relevant internal links
- ✅ Update regularly based on new questions
- ✅ Use plain language (avoid jargon)

### SEO Optimization
- ✅ Use question format (matches search queries)
- ✅ Include relevant keywords naturally
- ✅ Add FAQ schema markup (for rich snippets)
- ✅ Link to relevant service pages
- ✅ Create category pages for large FAQ sections
- ✅ Answer "People Also Ask" questions from Google

### User Experience
- ✅ Organize by category
- ✅ Add search functionality (for large FAQ sections)
- ✅ Use accordion/collapse for easy scanning
- ✅ Include "Was this helpful?" feedback
- ✅ Mobile-friendly formatting
- ✅ Clear contact options if question isn't answered

### Maintenance
- Review and update quarterly
- Add new questions from customer inquiries
- Remove outdated information
- Track which FAQs are viewed most
- Use feedback to improve answers

---

## Common FAQ Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Making up questions (use real customer inquiries)
- ❌ Writing overly long answers
- ❌ Using industry jargon
- ❌ Hiding important information
- ❌ Being vague about pricing
- ❌ Forgetting to update regularly
- ❌ No way to contact if question isn't answered
- ❌ Not optimizing for search

---

*A great FAQ page reduces support inquiries, builds trust, and helps convert visitors into customers by addressing concerns before they become objections.*
