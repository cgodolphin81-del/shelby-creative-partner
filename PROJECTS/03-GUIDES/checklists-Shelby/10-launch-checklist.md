# Complete Launch Checklist 2026

**Deploy 5-10 authority sites in one week**

---

## ⚠️ Reality Check

**Can you launch 5-10 sites in one week?** Yes.

**Should you?** Only if:
- ✅ You have $2,000-5,000 budget for domains/hosting
- ✅ You have AI tools and workflows already set up
- ✅ You have a team (or can work 12-14 hour days)
- ✅ These are MVP sites (not fully optimized)
- ✅ You plan to improve them over following months

**Better Approach:** Launch 2-3 sites properly in week 1, then add 2-3 more per month.

**This checklist assumes:** You're committed to the 7-day sprint. Let's do it.

## 📅 Day 3: Content Batch Creation (AI-Assisted)

### All Day (9 AM - 10 PM): Content Production

**Goal:** Create 15-20 articles per site (150-200 total articles)

**Daily Target:** 150-200 articles ÷ 10 sites = 15-20 articles/site

**Content Mix (Per Site):**
- 5 pillar pages (3,000-5,000 words each)
- 10 cluster articles (1,500-2,500 words each)
- 5 product reviews/buyer intent (2,000-3,000 words each)

**AI Workflow (Per Article):**

**Step 1: Research & Outline (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Research keyword and competitor content
- [ ] Generate outline with AI
- [ ] Add personal insights/experience notes
- [ ] Identify data points to verify

**Step 2: AI Draft (20 minutes)**
- [ ] Generate article section-by-section
- [ ] Provide detailed prompts
- [ ] Include specific examples in prompts

**Step 3: Humanization (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Add personal experience/anecdotes
- [ ] Inject opinions and personality
- [ ] Vary sentence structure
- [ ] Add specific data and citations
- [ ] Include industry jargon
- [ ] Run through AI detector (target <20%)

**Step 4: SEO Optimization (15 minutes)**
- [ ] Add internal links (3-5)
- [ ] Add external authoritative links (2-3)
- [ ] Optimize meta title/description
- [ ] Add schema markup
- [ ] Optimize images (alt text, compression)
- [ ] Set focus keyword in RankMath

**Total Time Per Article:** 80 minutes
**Articles Per Person Per Day:** 8-10 articles (with breaks)

**Team Requirements:**
- 1 person: 8-10 articles/day (not enough for 10 sites)
- 2 people: 16-20 articles/day (bare minimum)
- 3 people: 24-30 articles/day (comfortable)
- 5 people: 40-50 articles/day (ideal)

**Alternative:** Hire freelance writers on Upwork/Fiverr
- Cost: $20-50 per 2,000-word article
- 200 articles × $30 = $6,000
- Faster but expensive

**Content Checklist (Per Article):**
- [ ] AI detection score <20%
- [ ] Personal experience included
- [ ] Data/statistics verified
- [ ] Internal links added (3-5)
- [ ] External links added (2-3)
- [ ] Meta title/description optimized
- [ ] Images optimized
- [ ] Readability checked
- [ ] Grammar checked

**Time:** 10-13 hours of focused work

## ⚠️ Common Launch Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Publishing Too Much Too Fast
- **Problem:** 50 articles on day 1 triggers spam filters
- **Solution:** Drip-feed content (3-5 articles/day/site)

### ❌ Mistake 2: No E-E-A-T Signals
- **Problem:** Anonymous sites don't rank in 2026
- **Solution:** Add author bios, about pages, contact info

### ❌ Mistake 3: Ignoring Mobile
- **Problem:** 60%+ traffic is mobile
- **Solution:** Test every page on mobile before launch

### ❌ Mistake 4: No Internal Linking
- **Problem:** Orphan pages don't rank
- **Solution:** Map and implement internal links before launch

### ❌ Mistake 5: Monetizing Too Early
- **Problem:** Ads before trust = high bounce rates
- **Solution:** Wait until 1,000+ monthly visitors for ads
