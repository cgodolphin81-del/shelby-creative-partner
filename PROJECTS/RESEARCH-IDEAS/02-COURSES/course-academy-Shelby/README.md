## Key Principles

### 1. Validate Before You Build
Don't create a course nobody wants. Test demand first with pre-sales or waitlist.

### 2. Price for Transformation
People don't buy information—they buy outcomes. Price based on value delivered, not hours created.

### 3. Launch > Perfect
Your first launch won't be perfect. Launch anyway. Improve based on feedback.

### 4. Students First
Student success = your success. Invest in completion, not just enrollment.

### 5. Build Assets
Your email list, testimonials, and systems are your most valuable assets.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't:
- Build a course without validating demand
- Underprice from fear
- Skip the email list building
- Ignore student completion rates
- Try to do everything yourself
- Launch without a plan
- Give up after one failed launch

### ✅ Do:
- Validate before you build
- Price confidently based on value
- Build your email list from day one
- Invest in student success
- Build a team as you scale
- Follow a proven launch process
- Learn and iterate from each launch
