# Pricing Strategy Guide
## $97-997 Tiers, Payment Plans & Bundles

---

## The Pricing Mindset

**Price is not about cost. Price is about perceived value.**

Your customers don't buy based on what it costs you to create. They buy based on:
1. The transformation they expect
2. The perceived value of what they're getting
3. Their belief in your ability to deliver
4. Their own financial situation

**Your job:** Position your course so the price feels like a no-brainer investment, not an expense.

---

## Part 1: Pricing Tiers Explained

### The Four Main Price Points

#### 🟢 TIER 1: $97-197 (Entry Level)
**Best For:**
- First-time course creators
- Simple, focused courses (1-3 hours of content)
- Building your student base
- Lead generation for higher-ticket offers
- Impulse-buy territory

**What to Include:**
- 3-5 modules
- Self-paced (no live support)
- Basic worksheets/templates
- Email support (48-72 hour response)
- 30-day guarantee

**Expected Conversion:** 1-3% of warm list

**Revenue Math:**
```
1,000 email subscribers
2% conversion = 20 students
$147 average price
= $2,940 per launch
```

**Example Courses:**
- "Instagram Growth in 30 Days"
- "Meal Prep Mastery"
- "Introduction to Investing"
- "Public Speaking Fundamentals"

---

#### 🟡 TIER 2: $297-497 (Mid-Market)
**Best For:**
- Established creators with testimonials
- Comprehensive courses (4-8 hours of content)
- Students expecting real transformation
- Your "flagship" course at this level

**What to Include:**
- 6-10 modules
- Self-paced + community access
- Worksheets, templates, swipe files
- 1-2 group coaching calls/month
- Priority email support
- 60-day guarantee

**Expected Conversion:** 1-2% of warm list

**Revenue Math:**
```
1,000 email subscribers
1.5% conversion = 15 students
$397 average price
= $5,955 per launch
```

**Example Courses:**
- "Complete Freelance Writing Course"

- "Real Estate Investing Bootcamp"
- "UX Design Career Switch"

---

- Transformational programs (10-20 hours of content)

- Students making business/career investments

**What to Include:**
- 8-12 modules

- Templates, tools, resources
- Monthly group coaching
- Peer accountability groups

**Expected Conversion:** 0.5-1.5% of warm list

**Revenue Math:**

## Part 2: Pricing Psychology

### The Anchoring Effect

**Principle:** People judge price relative to other prices they see.

**Application:**
```
Total Value Breakdown:
✓ Course Modules (10 hours) — Value: $2,000
✓ Workbook & Templates — Value: $500
✓ Community Access (1 year) — Value: $600
✓ Monthly Coaching Calls — Value: $1,200
✓ Private Feedback — Value: $800

Total Value: $5,100
Your Investment: $997
You Save: $4,103
```

**Why it works:** The anchor ($5,100) makes $997 feel like a steal.

---

### The Decoy Effect

**Principle:** Adding a third option makes one of the first two more attractive.

**Example:**
```
OPTION 1: Course Only — $497
OPTION 2: Course + Coaching — $997 ← YOU WANT THIS ONE
OPTION 3: Course + Coaching + 1:1 — $1,497
```

**Why it works:** Option 2 looks reasonable compared to Option 3, making it more attractive than if it stood alone.

---

### The Charm Pricing Effect

**Principle:** Prices ending in 7 or 9 feel significantly lower than rounded numbers.

**Examples:**
- $97 feels cheaper than $100
- $497 feels cheaper than $500
- $997 feels cheaper than $1,000

**Why it works:** We read left-to-right. $997 registers as "900-something" not "almost 1,000."

**Exception:** High-ticket B2B ($5,000, $10,000) often uses round numbers for professionalism.

---

### The Payment Plan Illusion

**Principle:** Breaking price into smaller payments reduces pain of payment.

**Example:**
```
Instead of: "$997 one-time"
Try: "$397/month for 3 months" or "Just $33/day"
```

**Best Practices:**
- Offer both options (some prefer pay-in-full)
- Make payment plan total slightly higher (10-15%)
- Clearly show both options side-by-side
- Highlight monthly payment in marketing

**Revenue Impact:**
```
Pay-in-full: $997
Payment plan: $397 x 3 = $1,191 (20% more revenue)

But more importantly: Higher conversion rate
```

## Part 3: Payment Plan Structures

### Standard Payment Plans

#### 2-Payment Plan
```
Total: $997
Option 1: $997 one-time
Option 2: $547 x 2 payments (30 days apart)
```

**Best for:** $500-1,000 price points

---

#### 3-Payment Plan
```
Total: $997
Option 1: $997 one-time
Option 2: $397 x 3 payments (30 days apart)
```

**Best for:** $800-1,500 price points
**Most common and effective**

---

#### 6-Payment Plan
```
Total: $1,497
Option 1: $1,497 one-time
Option 2: $297 x 6 payments (30 days apart)
```

**Best for:** $1,500-3,000 price points

---

#### 12-Payment Plan
```
Total: $2,997
Option 1: $2,997 one-time
Option 2: $297 x 12 payments (30 days apart)
```

**Best for:** $3,000+ programs
**Note:** Higher risk of chargebacks/cancellations

---

### Payment Plan Best Practices

**✅ DO:**
- Charge first payment immediately
- Use automatic billing (Stripe, PayPal)
- Clearly state payment schedule
- Include what happens if payment fails
- Offer pay-in-full discount (10-15%)

**❌ DON'T:**
- Wait 30 days for first payment (high dropout risk)
- Make payment terms confusing
- Hide the total cost
- Forget to follow up on failed payments

---

### Pay-in-Full Incentive

**Why offer it:** Improves cash flow, reduces admin, rewards committed students

**Examples:**
```
OPTION 1: Pay in Full — $997 (Save $200)
OPTION 2: Payment Plan — $397 x 3 = $1,191
```

**Or:**
```
OPTION 1: Pay in Full — $997 + Bonus [Name]
OPTION 2: Payment Plan — $397 x 3 (no bonus)
```

## Part 4: Bundle Strategies

### The Upsell Bundle

**Structure:** Course + Related Product = Discounted Bundle

**Example:**
```
Course: $497
Template Pack: $97
Coaching Call: $297

Total if bought separately: $891
Bundle Price: $597
Customer Saves: $294
```

**When to use:** Checkout page, post-purchase upsell

---

### The Tiered Bundle

**Structure:** Good / Better / Best options

**Example:**
```
BASIC — $497
✓ Course access
✓ Worksheets
✓ Community

PRO — $797 (MOST POPULAR)
✓ Everything in Basic
✓ Monthly group coaching
✓ Template library
✓ Priority support

ELITE — $1,497
✓ Everything in Pro
✓ 2x 1:1 coaching calls
✓ Custom feedback
✓ VIP community access
```

**Why it works:** Most people choose middle option (Goldilocks effect)

---

### The Evergreen Bundle

**Structure:** Multiple courses packaged together

**Example:**
```
Course 1: Email Marketing Mastery — $497
Course 2: Social Media Strategy — $497
Course 3: Content Creation System — $497

Total Value: $1,491
Bundle Price: $797
Save: $694
```

**When to use:** When you have multiple complementary courses

---

### The Membership Bundle

**Structure:** Course + Ongoing Membership

**Example:**
```
Course: $997 one-time
Membership: $97/month (optional)

Membership includes:
✓ Monthly coaching calls
✓ Community access
✓ New content monthly
✓ Office hours
```

**Why it works:** Recurring revenue, ongoing relationship

## Part 5: Discount Strategy

### When to Discount

**✅ GOOD Reasons:**
- Early bird (reward fast action)
- Loyalty (returning students)
- Financial hardship (case-by-case)
- Launch bonus (limited time)
- Bundle discount (buying more)

**❌ BAD Reasons:**
- "Not enough sales" (fix marketing, not price)
- "Everyone else is discounting"
- "I feel bad charging full price"
- Random discounts without reason

---

### Discount Types

#### Early Bird Discount
```
Enroll by [Date] and save $200

Regular Price: $997
Early Bird: $797
```

**Best for:** Launch urgency, rewarding early adopters

---

#### Loyalty Discount
```
Returning student? Use code WELCOME20 for 20% off.
```

**Best for:** Alumni, previous customers

---

#### Launch Bonus (Better Than Discount)
```
Enroll during launch and get:
✓ Bonus 1: [Name] — Value: $300
✓ Bonus 2: [Name] — Value: $200

Total Bonus Value: $500
```

**Why better:** Doesn't devalue your course, feels like added value

---

#### Payment Plan "Discount"
```
Pay in Full: $997
Payment Plan: $397 x 3 = $1,191

Pay in full and save $194
```

**Why it works:** Incentivizes pay-in-full without discounting list price

---

### Discount Psychology

**Frame discounts as:**
- "Save $X" not "X% off"
- Time-limited (creates urgency)
- Exclusive (for specific audience)
- Reward-based (for taking action)

**Never:**
- Discount constantly (trains people to wait)
- Discount more than 30% (devalues product)
- Apologize for your prices
- Make discounts permanent

## Part 6: Price Testing & Optimization

### When to Raise Prices

**Signs it's time:**
- Consistently selling out
- Students getting amazing results
- Testimonials pouring in
- You're booked solid (if coaching included)
- Inflation/cost increases
- You've added more value

**How to raise:**
- Increase by 10-20% at a time
- Announce in advance ("Prices increasing on [Date]")
- Grandfather existing students
- Add value with the increase

---

### When to Lower Prices

**Rarely should you lower prices.** Instead:

- Add a lower-tier option (payment plans, basic tier)
- Offer scholarships (limited, application-based)
- Create a smaller "intro" course at lower price
- Improve marketing (problem is awareness, not price)

**Only lower if:**
- Market research shows price is truly wrong
- You're consistently getting zero sales at current price
- Economic conditions dramatically changed

---

### A/B Testing Prices

**Method:**
1. Split your email list in half
2. Send same launch to both halves
3. Test different price points ($497 vs. $597)
4. Measure: Conversion rate, total revenue, refund rate

**Caution:** Don't test on same audience twice (they'll notice)

## Part 7: Pricing by Audience Type

### B2C (Consumer) Courses

**Price Range:** $97-997
**Decision Factor:** Personal budget, perceived value
**Payment Plans:** Very important (3-payment standard)

**Examples:**
- Fitness/Health: $197-497
- Hobbies/Creative: $97-297
- Personal Development: $297-697
- Career Skills: $397-997

---

### B2B (Business) Courses

**Price Range:** $497-5,000+
**Decision Factor:** ROI, business budget, team size
**Payment Plans:** Less important (businesses have budgets)

**Examples:**
- Team Training: $997-2,500
- Professional Certification: $1,500-5,000
- Software/Business Skills: $497-1,500
- Leadership/Management: $1,000-3,000

**Note:** B2B buyers often need invoices, POs, and may have longer sales cycles.

---

### High-Income Professionals

**Price Range:** $997-5,000+
**Decision Factor:** Time value, career impact, exclusivity
**Payment Plans:** Optional (they can afford it)

**Examples:**
- Doctors/Lawyers: $2,000-10,000
- Executives: $3,000-15,000
- Entrepreneurs: $1,500-7,500

**Key:** Emphasize time-saving, ROI, and exclusivity.

---

### Students/Entry-Level

**Price Range:** $47-297
**Decision Factor:** Limited budget, high price sensitivity
**Payment Plans:** Essential (or very low price)

**Examples:**
- Study Skills: $47-97
- Entry-Level Certifications: $197-297
- Basic Skills: $97-197

**Strategy:** Low price, high volume, upsell to higher tiers later.

## Part 8: Guarantee Strategies

### Standard Guarantee
```
30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Try [Course Name] risk-free for 30 days. If you're not 
completely satisfied, email me and I'll refund your investment. 
No questions asked.
```

**Best for:** Most courses, standard expectation

---

### Extended Guarantee
```
60-Day Money-Back Guarantee

I'm so confident you'll love [Course Name], I'm giving you 
60 full days to try it. If it doesn't deliver, email me for a 
full refund.
```

**Best for:** Higher-ticket courses ($500+), builds trust

---

### Action-Based Guarantee
```
Do the Work Guarantee

Complete all modules, submit your assignments, and implement 
what you learn. If you don't [achieve specific outcome] within 
90 days, email me your completed work and I'll refund you 100%.

This guarantee shows I stand behind this course—and I know it 
works when you work it.
```

**Best for:** Transformation-focused courses, reduces tire-kickers

---

### Double Guarantee
```
My Double Guarantee:

1. Try it for 60 days. If you don't love it, full refund.

2. Implement for 90 days. If you don't get results, show me 
   your work and I'll refund you AND give you [bonus/extra 
   support].

You literally cannot lose.
```

**Best for:** High-ticket programs, maximum risk reversal

---

### Guarantee Best Practices

**✅ DO:**
- Make it prominent on sales page
- Use clear, simple language
- Honor it without hassle (your reputation matters)
- Use it as a selling point

**❌ DON'T:**
- Hide it in fine print
- Add hoops/conditions (unless action-based)
- Make it sound reluctant
- Forget to mention it in emails

│  └─ Price: $297-497

## Part 11: Common Pricing Mistakes

### ❌ Mistake 1: Pricing Based on Hours Worked
**Wrong:** "I spent 50 hours creating this, so..."
**Right:** Price based on value delivered, not time spent

---

### ❌ Mistake 2: Underpricing from Fear
**Wrong:** "I'll charge less to get more students"
**Right:** Price confidently, attract serious students

**Truth:** Low prices attract demanding customers. High prices attract committed customers.

---

### ❌ Mistake 3: Not Offering Payment Plans
**Wrong:** "$997 one-time only"
**Right:** "$997 or $397/month for 3 months"

**Impact:** Payment plans can double or triple conversions.

---

### ❌ Mistake 4: Discounting Too Often
**Wrong:** Constant sales, always discounting
**Right:** Strategic, limited-time offers

**Impact:** Constant discounts train customers to never pay full price.

---

### ❌ Mistake 5: Not Raising Prices
**Wrong:** Same price for 3+ years
**Right:** Raise prices as you add value and get results

**Impact:** You leave money on the table and attract less-committed students.
