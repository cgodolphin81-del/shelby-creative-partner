# Email Launch Sequences
## 21-Day Pre-Launch + 5-Day Cart Open Sequences

---

## Overview

This document contains complete, copy-paste email sequences for your course launch.

**Sequence 1: 21-Day Pre-Launch (12 emails)**
- Builds anticipation
- Provides value
- Creates desire before cart opens

**Sequence 2: 5-Day Cart Open (7 emails)**
- Converts interest to sales
- Handles objections
- Creates urgency

**Sequence 3: Post-Launch (5 emails)**
- Onboards students
- Drives engagement
- Collects testimonials

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips

**High-Performing Formats:**
- Personal: "[Name], quick question"
- Curiosity: "The truth about [topic]"
- Specific: "How to [result] in [timeframe]"
- Urgent: "⏰ [X] hours left"
- Benefit: "Your [result] is waiting"

**Avoid:**
- ALL CAPS (looks spammy)
- Excessive punctuation!!!
- Spam trigger words (FREE, $$$$, etc.)
- Misleading claims

### Email Design

**Keep it simple:**
- Plain text performs better than HTML
- One clear CTA per email
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Mobile-friendly (most opens are on mobile)
- Personal sign-off

### Send Times

**Best performing:**
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 9-11 AM (recipient's timezone)
- Or 2-4 PM (after lunch)

**Avoid:**
- Monday mornings (inbox overload)
- Friday afternoons (checking out)
- Weekends (lower engagement)

### List Segmentation

**Segment by:**
- Engagement (openers vs. non-openers)
- Past purchasers vs. non-buyers
- VIP list vs. general list
- Industry/role (if B2B)

**Tailor messaging:**
- Past buyers: "Welcome back" + loyalty discount
- Non-openers: Re-engagement campaign
- VIPs: Exclusive early access
