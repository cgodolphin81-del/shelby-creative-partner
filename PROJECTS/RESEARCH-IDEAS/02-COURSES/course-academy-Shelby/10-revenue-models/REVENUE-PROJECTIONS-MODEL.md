# Revenue Projections Model
## 3-Year Forecast: 100 → 1,000 → 10,000 Students

---

## How to Use This Model

This document provides realistic revenue projections for your course business across three growth stages:

**Year 1: Foundation (100 students)**
- Validate your course
- Build testimonials
- Establish your launch system

**Year 2: Growth (1,000 students)**
- Scale your marketing
- Add additional offers
- Build your team

**Year 3: Scale (10,000 students)**
- Multiple revenue streams
- Evergreen systems
- Team-powered operations

**Note:** These are projections, not guarantees. Your results depend on your market, offer, execution, and many other factors.

## Part 5: The Path to 10,000 Students

### How to Get 10,000 Paying Students

**The Math:**
```
10,000 students over 3 years = 3,333 students/year
3,333 / 12 = 278 students/month
278 / 30 = 9 students/day
```

**You need to enroll ~9 students per day.**

---

### Traffic Requirements

**Assuming 2% conversion rate:**
```
9 students/day ÷ 2% = 450 visitors/day
450 × 30 = 13,500 visitors/month
```

**You need ~13,500 targeted visitors per month to your sales page.**

---

### Traffic Sources

| Source | Monthly Visitors | Cost |
|--------|-----------------|------|
| Email List (50K, 5% click) | 2,500 | $0 (owned) |
| Organic Social | 3,000 | $0 (time) |
| SEO/Blog | 2,000 | $2,000 (content) |
| YouTube | 2,000 | $0 (time) |
| Podcast | 1,000 | $0 (time) |
| Paid Ads | 3,000 | $10,000-15,000 |
| **TOTAL** | **13,500** | **$12,000-17,000** |

---

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Year 1:**
```
Ad spend: $6,000
Students: 100
CAC: $60 per student
```

**Year 2:**
```
Ad spend: $36,000
Students: 1,000
CAC: $36 per student
```

**Year 3:**
```
Ad spend: $300,000
Students: 10,000
CAC: $30 per student
```

**Note:** CAC decreases as you optimize and build organic channels.

---

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Average customer across 3 years:**
```
Initial purchase: $597
Upsell to premium: 20% × $3,500 = $700
Mastermind: 5% × $15,000 = $750
Additional courses: 30% × $497 = $149

LTV: $597 + $700 + $750 + $149 = $2,196
```

**LTV:CAC Ratio:**
```
$2,196 LTV : $30 CAC = 73:1
```

**Industry standard:** 3:1 is good. 73:1 is exceptional (but achievable with multiple offers).

## Part 8: Reality Checks

### What Could Go Wrong

**Risk 1: Market Saturation**
- **Mitigation:** Differentiate your offer, find underserved niche

**Risk 2: Platform Changes**
- **Mitigation:** Own your email list, diversify traffic sources

**Risk 3: Burnout**
- **Mitigation:** Build team early, set boundaries, automate

**Risk 4: Economic Downturn**
- **Mitigation:** Offer payment plans, emphasize ROI, build cash reserves

**Risk 5: Algorithm Changes**
- **Mitigation:** Don't rely on one traffic source, build owned audience

---

### What Could Go Right

**Opportunity 1: Viral Moment**
- One piece of content explodes, 10x growth in weeks

**Opportunity 2: Strategic Partnership**
- Partner with established player, instant audience access

**Opportunity 3: Acquisition Offer**
- Larger company buys your business (3-5x annual profit)

**Opportunity 4: Book Deal/Media**
- Traditional publishing or media appearances boost credibility

**Opportunity 5: Product Expansion**
- Software, physical products, or licensing deals
