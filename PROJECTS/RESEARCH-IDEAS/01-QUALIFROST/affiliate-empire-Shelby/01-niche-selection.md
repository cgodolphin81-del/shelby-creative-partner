## 🔍 How to Choose Your Niche

### The 4-Factor Niche Score

Rate each niche 1-10 on:
1. **Passion/Knowledge** - Can you create 100+ pieces of content?
2. **Profitability** - Average commission × conversion rate
3. **Competition** - Lower = easier entry (reverse score)
4. **Traffic Potential** - Search volume + social media reach

**Winning Formula:** (Passion + Profitability) × (Traffic / Competition)

## 🎯 MY RECOMMENDATION FOR BEGINNERS

### Best Starter Niches (Low Competition, High Profit):

1. **AI & Software Tools** - Exploding market, recurring commissions
2. **B2B Services** - High commissions, less saturated
3. **Pet Products** - Evergreen, passionate audience
4. **Smart Home** - Growing rapidly, good margins
5. **Sustainability** - Emerging niche, first-mover advantage

### Avoid as Beginner:
- Health/fitness (oversaturated, compliance issues)
- Finance (heavy regulation)
- Make money online (trust issues, high competition)
- Fashion/beauty (requires heavy visual content investment)

## ✅ NICHE VALIDATION CHECKLIST

Before committing, answer YES to at least 4:

- [ ] Can I create 100+ content pieces without running out of ideas?
- [ ] Are there affiliate programs with 20%+ commissions?
- [ ] Is there proven search volume (10K+ monthly searches)?
- [ ] Can I identify 3+ successful affiliate sites in this niche?
- [ ] Do I have personal experience or strong interest?
- [ ] Are there products at multiple price points ($50-$500+)?
- [ ] Is the market growing (not declining)?
- [ ] Can I differentiate from existing competitors?
