## 💰 Pricing Levers for Profitability

### Increase Revenue
1. **Raise rates 10-15% annually**
2. **Shift mix to retainers** (more predictable, higher lifetime value)
3. **Upsell existing clients** (additional services)
4. **Move upmarket** (enterprise = larger deals)
5. **Productize services** (scalable offerings)

### Reduce Costs
1. **Improve utilization** (target 75%+ for designers)
2. **Reduce scope creep** (better scoping, change orders)
3. **Optimize tool stack** (consolidate, negotiate)
4. **Remote-first** (reduce office costs)
5. **Contractor flexibility** (scale up/down with demand)

### Improve Efficiency
1. **Design systems** (reuse, faster delivery)
2. **Templates** (proposals, documentation)
3. **Automation** (invoicing, reporting)
4. **Process optimization** (reduce rework)
5. **Training** (faster ramp, better quality)

## 🎯 Break-Even Analysis

### Monthly Break-Even (Year 1)
| Fixed Costs | Monthly |
|-------------|---------|
| Salaries + Benefits | $47,000 |
| Office/Remote | $3,000 |
| Software & Tools | $1,500 |
| Marketing | $2,500 |
| Professional Services | $1,500 |
| Other | $5,000 |
| **Total Fixed** | **$60,500** |

**Variable Costs:** 35% of revenue (contractor labor, project expenses)

**Break-Even Revenue:**
```
Break-Even = Fixed Costs / (1 - Variable Cost %)
Break-Even = $60,500 / (1 - 0.35)
Break-Even = $60,500 / 0.65
Break-Even = $93,077/month
```

**Annual Break-Even: $1,116,924**

*Year 1 target ($750K) is below break-even - need to either:*
1. *Increase revenue to $1.1M+*
2. *Reduce fixed costs to ~$40K/month*
3. *Accept Year 1 loss for growth*

## 📝 Financial Management Best Practices

### Monthly Financial Review
1. **Revenue vs. Plan** - Variance analysis
2. **Cash Flow** - 13-week forecast
3. **Utilization** - By person and team
4. **Pipeline** - Coverage and conversion
5. **Expenses** - Budget vs. actual
6. **Profitability** - By project and client

### Key Dashboards
- **Revenue Dashboard:** MTD, QTD, YTD vs. plan
- **Cash Dashboard:** Balance, burn, runway
- **Pipeline Dashboard:** Value, stage, close probability
- **Utilization Dashboard:** Billable %, capacity
- **Project Margin Dashboard:** Budget vs. actual by project

### Financial Controls
- Weekly cash review
- Monthly P&L review
- Quarterly board/advisor review
- Annual audit (Year 3+)
- Expense approval workflow
- Client credit checks (for Net 30+ terms)
