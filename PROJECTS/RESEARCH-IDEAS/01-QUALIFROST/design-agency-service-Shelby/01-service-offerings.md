## 4. Usability Testing

**Validate designs with real users before development.**

### Deliverables
- Test plan & scripts
- Session recordings
- Findings report with severity ratings
- Prioritized recommendations
- Before/after comparison (if iterative)

### Methods
- **Moderated Testing:** $5,000-10,000
  - Live facilitation
  - 5-8 participants
  - Deep qualitative insights

- **Unmoderated Testing:** $3,000-6,000
  - Self-guided sessions
  - 10-20 participants
  - Faster turnaround

- **A/B Testing Support:** $8,000-15,000
  - Test design & hypothesis
  - Variant creation
  - Analysis & recommendations

### Timeline: 1-2 weeks
