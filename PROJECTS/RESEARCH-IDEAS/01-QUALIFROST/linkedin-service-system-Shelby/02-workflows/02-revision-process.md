# Revision Process & Feedback System
## How Client Feedback Is Collected and Incorporated

**Purpose:** Clear, efficient process for handling post revisions while maintaining quality and timelines.

---

## OVERVIEW

```
Client Reviews Draft → Provides Feedback → Ghostwriter Revises → Client Approves → Post Scheduled
     (48h)              (Clear format)        (24h)            (Final)         (Within 48h)
```

**Standard Turnaround:**
- Client review window: **48 hours**
- Revision turnaround: **24 hours**
- Total revision cycle: **72 hours max**

---

## STEP 1: HOW CLIENTS PROVIDE FEEDBACK

### Preferred Method: Notion/Trello Comments

**Where:** Directly on the post card in your content board

**Format Template for Clients:**
```
✅ APPROVED - Ready to schedule

OR

📝 REVISIONS NEEDED:

1. [Section/Paragraph] - Change "[original]" to "[suggested]"
   Reason: [Optional - helps us learn your voice]

2. [Tone adjustment] - "This feels too formal. Can we make it more conversational?"

3. [Fact check] - "Actually, this happened in 2023, not 2022"

4. [Add/remove] - "Add a line about [topic]" or "Remove the second paragraph"

Questions:
- [Any clarifying questions]
```

### Alternative Methods (if needed):

**Email:**
- Subject line: "REVISIONS: [Post Title/ID] - [Client Name]"
- Use the same format as above
- We'll copy feedback into Notion/Trello for tracking

**Loom Video (for complex feedback):**
- Record a 2-5 min video walking through the draft
- Share link in Notion/Trello comments
- We'll transcribe key points and confirm understanding

**NOT Recommended:**
- ❌ Voice messages (hard to reference later)
- ❌ Inline edits on screenshots (easy to miss changes)
- ❌ Vague feedback like "make it better" (not actionable)

---

## STEP 2: FEEDBACK CATEGORIES

We categorize feedback to improve faster:

| Category | Description | Action |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Tone/Voice** | "Too formal," "More punchy," "Sound more like me" | Adjust word choice, sentence rhythm |
| **Fact/Accuracy** | Wrong dates, numbers, names, details | Correct immediately, verify with client |
| **Content Addition** | "Add a point about X," "Include this story" | Incorporate requested content |
| **Content Removal** | "Cut this paragraph," "Too long" | Trim or remove as requested |
| **Structure** | "Move this to the top," "Reorder these points" | Reorganize flow |
| **CTA/Ask** | "Change the call-to-action," "Add a question" | Modify closing |
| **Approval** | "Looks good," "Approved" | Move to scheduling |

## STEP 3: REVISION TURNAROUND

### Standard Timeline:

```
Day 0: Client submits feedback
Day 1: Ghostwriter completes revisions (24h)
Day 1: Revised draft sent back to client
Day 2-3: Client reviews and approves (24-48h)
Day 3-4: Post scheduled
```

### Rush Revisions:

**When needed:** Time-sensitive posts (news, announcements, trending topics)

**Process:**
1. Client marks feedback as "🔴 URGENT" in subject/comment
2. Ghostwriter prioritizes within 4 hours
3. Client confirms receipt and provides rapid approval
4. Post scheduled same-day if needed

**Limit:** 2 rush revisions per month (to maintain quality for all clients)

---

## STEP 4: REVISION LIMITS

### Included in Standard Package:

| Package | Revisions Per Post | Rush Revisions/Month |
|---------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Starter (2 posts/week) | 2 rounds | 1 |
| Pro (3 posts/week) | 2 rounds | 2 |
| Enterprise (5 posts/week) | 3 rounds | 4 |

### What Counts as One "Round":

✅ One round = All feedback submitted at once, then we revise

❌ NOT one round = Sending feedback piecemeal over multiple days

**Example of Good Process:**
```
Client: "Here are my notes: 1) Change X to Y, 2) Add Z, 3) Tone is off"
Ghostwriter: Makes all 3 changes, sends back
= 1 round complete
```

**Example of Problematic Process:**
```
Client: "Change X to Y"
Ghostwriter: Revises, sends back
Client: "Also, can you add Z?"
Ghostwriter: Revises, sends back
Client: "Actually, tone feels off too"
= 3 rounds (inefficient for both parties)
```

### Beyond Included Revisions:

**If a post requires 3+ rounds repeatedly:**
1. Ghostwriter flags to account manager
2. Schedule a 15-min voice call to align on voice/expectations
3. Update voice profile and content guidelines
4. Goal: Reduce future revisions

## STEP 5: COMMON FEEDBACK PATTERNS & SOLUTIONS

### Pattern 1: "This Doesn't Sound Like Me"

**Root Cause:** Voice capture wasn't detailed enough

**Solution:**
1. Ask client to share 2-3 examples of their own writing
2. Schedule 20-min voice calibration call
3. Ghostwriter records key phrases, expressions, rhythm
4. Update voice profile document
5. Apply to next 3 posts, then re-check

---

### Pattern 2: Endless Tweaking (Perfectionism)

**Root Cause:** Client anxiety about public perception

**Solution:**
1. Remind client: "Done is better than perfect"
2. Share data: Posts with 2+ revisions don't outperform 1-revision posts
3. Set hard deadline: "We need to schedule by EOD Thursday"
4. Offer A/B test: "Let's try both versions and see what resonates"

---

### Pattern 3: Vague Feedback

**Examples:**
- "Make it pop"
- "Not quite there"
- "Feels off"

**Solution:**
1. Politely ask for specifics:
   > "Thanks for the feedback! To make sure I nail this, can you help me understand what specifically feels off? Is it the tone, the length, the opening, or something else?"
2. Offer options:
   > "Would it help if I sent 2-3 different opening hooks to choose from?"
3. Schedule quick call if pattern continues

---

### Pattern 4: Ghosting (No Feedback)

**When:** Draft sits in "Ready for Review" for 5+ days

**Solution:**
**Day 3:** Gentle nudge
> "Hey [Name]! Just checking in on the 3 drafts in 'Ready for Review.' Want to make sure we stay on schedule for next week's posts. Any feedback?"

**Day 5:** Phone call
> Call client directly. May reveal:
> - They're traveling/busy
> - They're unsure how to give feedback
> - They've lost confidence in the content

**Day 7:** Escalate
> Account manager reaches out to discuss partnership health

## STEP 6: FEEDBACK TRACKING

### Monthly Feedback Report (Internal)

Track these metrics per client:

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Avg Revisions/Post | <2 | Indicates voice alignment |
| Feedback Turnaround Time | <48h | Keeps content calendar on track |
| Vague Feedback Incidents | <2/month | Shows client understands process |
| Approval Rate (First Draft) | >60% | High = strong voice match |

### Quarterly Review:

**Questions to Ask:**
1. Are revisions decreasing over time? (Should be)
2. Is the client happier with first drafts? (Should be)
3. Are we learning their voice better? (Should be)
4. Is the process feeling smoother? (Should be)

**If No to Any:**
- Schedule voice recalibration session
- Review and update voice profile
- Consider client fit (some people are harder to ghostwrite for)

## STEP 8: ESCALATION PROCESS

### When Revisions Become a Problem:

**Signs to Watch:**
- 4+ revision rounds on multiple posts
- Client expresses frustration repeatedly
- Ghostwriter feels unable to match voice
- Timeline consistently slipping due to revisions

**Escalation Path:**

**Level 1: Ghostwriter → Account Manager**
- Ghostwriter flags concern in weekly check-in
- Account manager reviews feedback history
- Schedule calibration call with client

**Level 2: Account Manager → Client**
- Honest conversation: "We want to serve you better"
- Identify root cause (voice? expectations? process?)
- Agree on action plan (more examples? different ghostwriter? adjusted process?)

**Level 3: Leadership Involvement**
- If still not resolved after 30 days
- Consider: Is this a good fit?
- Options: Reassign ghostwriter, adjust scope, or part ways amicably

## QUALITY ASSURANCE CHECKLIST

### Before Sending Drafts to Client:

- [ ] Post aligns with client's voice profile
- [ ] Facts/stats verified
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Hook is strong (first 2 lines)
- [ ] Clear flow and structure
- [ ] CTA included (if appropriate)
- [ ] Character count within LinkedIn limits (3,000)
- [ ] Hashtags relevant (3-5 max)
- [ ] Visual assets attached (if needed)

### Before Scheduling Approved Posts:

- [ ] Client approval confirmed
- [ ] All revisions incorporated
- [ ] Final proofread complete
- [ ] Optimal posting time selected
- [ ] Post URL tracked for analytics
- [ ] Client notified of schedule
