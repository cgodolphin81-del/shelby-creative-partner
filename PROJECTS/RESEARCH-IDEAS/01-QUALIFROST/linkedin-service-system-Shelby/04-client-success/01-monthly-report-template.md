# Monthly Results Report Template
## Google Slides Structure + Script

**Purpose:** Show clients clear ROI with before/after metrics, insights, and strategic recommendations.

**Format:** 12-slide Google Slides deck (client-facing)

---

## SLIDE 1: TITLE SLIDE

**Layout:**
- Client logo (top left)
- Your agency logo (top right)
- Title: "[Client Name] - LinkedIn Performance Report"
- Subtitle: "[Month Year]"
- Date: [Report Date]
- Prepared by: [Your Name/Agency]

**Design Notes:**
- Clean, professional template
- Use client brand colors if available
- Include report period prominently

---

## SLIDE 2: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Title:** "This Month at a Glance"

**Layout:** 4 metric cards in a 2x2 grid

**Content:**

| Metric | This Month | Last Month | Change |
|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| **Followers** | [X,XXX] | [X,XXX] | [+XX%] |
| **Total Impressions** | [XX,XXX] | [XX,XXX] | [+XX%] |
| **Avg Engagement Rate** | [X.X%] | [X.X%] | [+X.X%] |

**Key Highlight Box (bottom):**
> "🎯 Top Win: [Specific achievement, e.g., 'Post on leadership reached 25K impressions—3x your average']"

**Speaker Notes:**

## GOOGLE SLIDES TEMPLATE SETUP

**To Create Master Template:**

1. Go to slides.new
2. Create 12 slides with layouts above
3. Save as "LinkedIn Monthly Report - MASTER"
4. For each client: File > Make a Copy > Rename with client name
5. Update branding/colors per client

**Pro Tips:**
- Use "Theme Builder" for consistent branding
- Create custom layouts for metric cards
- Use animations sparingly (fade-in for reveals)
- Export as PDF for client delivery
