# Client Success Checklist
## Weekly Account Management Tasks

**Purpose:** Ensure every client receives consistent, excellent service that drives results and retention.

---

## WEEKLY CHECKLIST (Every Monday)

### ☐ CONTENT CALENDAR REVIEW (15 min)

**Check:**
- [ ] This week's posts are scheduled and published
- [ ] Next week's drafts are in "Ready for Review" or "Approved"
- [ ] No gaps in posting schedule
- [ ] Content mix is balanced (not all same pillar/type)

**If Issues:**
- Missing posts → Ghostwriter prioritizes drafting
- No drafts for review → Send client reminder
- Imbalanced content → Adjust upcoming drafts

---

### ☐ PERFORMANCE CHECK (20 min)

**Review Last Week's Posts:**
- [ ] Update impressions, reactions, comments, shares for each post
- [ ] Note any posts exceeding 5K impressions (flag for analysis)
- [ ] Note any posts under 500 impressions (flag for learning)
- [ ] Calculate weekly engagement rate

**Quick Health Check:**
| Metric | Green | Yellow | Red |
|--------|-------|--------|-----|
| Avg Impressions/Post | >2,000 | 1,000-2,000 | <1,000 |
| Engagement Rate | >3% | 1.5-3% | <1.5% |
| Posting Consistency | 100% | 75-99% | <75% |

**If Red Flags:**
- Document in client notes
- Prepare talking points for check-in call
- Consider content strategy adjustment

---

### ☐ ENGAGEMENT MONITORING (15 min)

**Check Client's LinkedIn Notifications:**
- [ ] Review all comments on recent posts
- [ ] Draft reply suggestions for client (if they want help)
- [ ] Note any high-profile commenters (executives, prospects)
- [ ] Check for any negative comments (rare, but monitor)

**Engagement Best Practices:**
- Respond to comments within 2 hours (boosts algorithm)
- Ask follow-up questions to extend conversations
- Thank people for thoughtful comments
- Tag relevant people when appropriate

**If Client Handles Their Own Engagement:**
- [ ] Send weekly reminder: "Don't forget to engage with comments!"
- [ ] Share top comments that deserve responses
- [ ] Provide suggested replies for important conversations

---

### ☐ INBOUND LEAD TRACKING (10 min)

**Check Client's LinkedIn Messages:**
- [ ] Ask client about any inbound messages from posts
- [ ] Update LEADS sheet with new opportunities
- [ ] Note which post generated each lead
- [ ] Track lead status (new, contacted, proposal, won)

**Lead Attribution:**
```
Lead Name | Company | Source Post | Date | Status | Value
----------|---------|-------------|------|--------|-------
John Smith | Acme Co | "Leadership lessons" | 3/15 | Discovery | $25K
```

**If Leads Coming In:**
- Celebrate with client!
- Document what content is driving leads
- Create more of that content type

**If No Leads (After 60+ Days):**
- Review content strategy
- Consider adding stronger CTAs
- Discuss profile optimization
- May need to adjust targeting/messaging

---

### ☐ CLIENT COMMUNICATION (15 min)

**Weekly Touchpoint (Choose One):**

**Option A: Quick Email Update**
```
Hi [Client Name],

Quick weekly update:

✅ This Week:
- 3 posts scheduled (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- Last week's posts: 12K total impressions, 3.2% engagement
- Top post: "[Title]" with 5K impressions

📈 Notable:
- [Any wins, milestones, or observations]

🎯 Next Week:
- Drafts will be ready for review by [Day]
- Planning content around [theme/topic]

Anything on your mind? I'm here if you need me!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Option B: Loom Video (More Personal)**
- 2-3 min video walking through analytics
- Show dashboard/screenshare
- Highlight wins and upcoming content
- Send via email or Slack

**Option C: Quick Call (15 min)**
- For clients who prefer voice
- Same content as email, but conversational
- Good for building relationship

**When to Escalate to Call:**
- Client seems disengaged (not reviewing drafts)
- Performance concerns to discuss
- Strategic pivots needed
- Client requests it

---

### ☐ CONTENT IDEA CAPTURE (Ongoing)

**Sources to Monitor:**
- [ ] Client's industry news (Google Alerts, newsletters)
- [ ] Trending topics in their niche (LinkedIn feed)
- [ ] Client's own observations (they may DM you ideas)
- [ ] Comments/questions on their posts (future content gold)
- [ ] Competitor content (what's working for others)

**Idea Capture System:**
- Add to Notion/Trello "Ideas" column
- Tag with content pillar
- Note any relevant hooks or angles
- Include links to inspiration

**Minimum:** 10 ideas in pipeline at all times

---

### ☐ VOICE & QUALITY CHECK (10 min)

**Review Recent Approved Posts:**
- [ ] Does the voice still sound like the client?
- [ ] Any drift in tone or style?
- [ ] Client happy with recent content?
- [ ] Ghostwriter feeling confident or stuck?

**If Voice Drift Detected:**
- Re-read original voice questionnaire
- Review client's own writing samples
- Consider recalibration call
- Update voice profile document

---

### ☐ ANALYTICS UPDATE (15 min)

**Update Dashboard:**
- [ ] Add new post metrics to POSTS sheet
- [ ] Refresh DASHBOARD charts
- [ ] Check goal progress
- [ ] Note any anomalies or patterns

**Monthly Prep (Last Week of Month):**
- [ ] Start aggregating monthly data
- [ ] Draft monthly report slides
- [ ] Schedule client review call

---

## MONTHLY CHECKLIST (First Week of Month)

### ☐ MONTHLY REPORT DELIVERY

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Complete monthly analytics deck (12 slides)
- [ ] Export as PDF
- [ ] Write summary email
- [ ] Send to client
- [ ] Schedule monthly strategy call

**Timeline:**
- Days 1-2: Compile data, build deck
- Day 3: Internal review
- Day 4: Send to client
- Day 5-7: Strategy call

---

### ☐ MONTHLY STRATEGY CALL (30-45 min)

**Agenda:**
1. Review last month's results (10 min)
2. Discuss what worked/didn't (10 min)
3. Approve next month's content pillars (10 min)
4. Address any client questions/concerns (10 min)
5. Discuss upsell opportunities if appropriate (5 min)

**Prep:**
- [ ] Deck ready and reviewed
- [ ] Know client's current goals/challenges
- [ ] Have content recommendations ready
- [ ] Prepare upsell pitch if relevant

**Post-Call:**
- [ ] Send recording (if applicable)
- [ ] Document decisions in client notes
- [ ] Update content calendar based on call
- [ ] Schedule next month's call

---

### ☐ GOAL REVIEW & ADJUSTMENT

**Check Progress:**
- [ ] Follower growth vs. target
- [ ] Engagement rate vs. target
- [ ] Lead generation vs. target
- [ ] Overall satisfaction (informal check-in)

**If Behind on Goals:**
- Diagnose root cause (content? frequency? profile?)
- Propose adjustments
- Reset expectations if needed
- Document in client notes

**If Ahead on Goals:**
- Celebrate with client!
- Discuss stretching goals
- Consider case study opportunity

---

### ☐ CLIENT SATISFACTION PULSE

**Ask (Informally):**
- "How are you feeling about the content lately?"
- "Anything you'd like to see more or less of?"
- "Is there anything I could be doing better?"
- "How's LinkedIn contributing to your business goals?"

**Formal Check-In (Quarterly):**
- Send NPS survey: "How likely are you to recommend this service? (0-10)"
- Ask: "What's the #1 value you're getting?"
- Ask: "What's one thing we could improve?"

**If Score <8:**
- Schedule call to understand concerns
- Create action plan to address issues
- Follow up within 2 weeks

## QUARTERLY CHECKLIST

**Purpose:** Strategic alignment, not just metrics

**Agenda (60 min):**

3. Content strategy evolution (15 min)
4. Upsell/expansion conversation (15 min)

**Prep:**
- [ ] 3-month trend analysis
- [ ] Before/after comparison (start of quarter vs. now)
- [ ] Industry benchmarking
- [ ] Recommendations for next quarter
- [ ] Upsell options prepared

**Deliverables:**
- [ ] QBR deck (15-20 slides)
- [ ] Written summary with recommendations
- [ ] Updated goals for next quarter
- [ ] Any contract/scope changes documented

## RETENTION RISK INDICATORS

### 🚩 Early Warning Signs

Watch for these red flags:

| Signal | What It Means | Action |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Slow draft reviews (>72h) | Losing priority for client | Gentle nudge, then call |
| Vague or negative feedback | Unhappy with quality | Schedule calibration call |
| Missing scheduled calls | Disengaged or busy | Reschedule, check in personally |
| No inbound leads after 90 days | Not seeing ROI | Review strategy, adjust |
| "Taking a break" requests | May be considering canceling | Understand why, offer pause option |
| Asking about contract end | Shopping around or budget concerns | Discuss value, offer retention incentive |

### 🛡️ Retention Interventions

**If You See Red Flags:**

**Week 1:**
- Account manager personal check-in call
- Ask: "How's everything going? Anything we should adjust?"
- Listen more than talk

**Week 2-3:**
- Implement any requested changes immediately
- Over-communicate on progress
- Share extra value (industry insights, profile tips)

**Week 4:**
- If still concerned, offer:
  - Free profile optimization audit
  - Bonus content piece (article, carousel)
  - Extended strategy session
  - Temporary pause option (better than cancellation)

**Goal:** Address concerns before they become cancellation requests.

### Retention Call Script
```
Opening:
"Hey [Name], I wanted to check in personally. We value you as a client 
and I want to make sure you're getting everything you need from this 
partnership. How are you feeling about things?"

Listen:
[Let them talk. Don't defend or explain yet.]

Acknowledge:
"I hear you. That makes sense. Thank you for being honest with me."

Problem-Solve:
"Here's what I'd like to do to address this..."

Confirm:
"Does that feel right? Anything else on your mind?"

Close:
"Thanks again for the candor. I'm committed to making this work for you. 
I'll follow up on [specific action] by [date]."
```
