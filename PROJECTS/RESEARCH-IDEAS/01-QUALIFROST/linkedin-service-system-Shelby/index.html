Frequently Asked Questions

How do you capture my voice?

We use a comprehensive voice capture process including a 20-question written questionnaire and a 60-minute onboarding call. We analyze your past content, communication style, and key messages to create a detailed voice profile that guides all content creation.

How long does it take to see results?

Most clients see increased engagement within the first 30 days. Follower growth and inbound leads typically accelerate in months 2-3 as the algorithm recognizes your consistent, high-quality content. Our 90-day target is 500-1,000 new followers and 2-5 inbound leads per month.

What if I don't like the content?

We have an unlimited revision policy. Every post goes through our collaborative approval workflow where you can request changes. We don't stop until you're 100% satisfied. Our goal is to make you look good, not to push content you're uncomfortable with.

Do I need to be active on LinkedIn myself?

We recommend engaging with comments on your posts and occasionally commenting on others' content, but we can handle 100% of the content creation and posting. Some clients choose our Engagement Management add-on where we handle all community interaction.

What's the minimum commitment?

We require a 3-month minimum commitment. This gives us enough time to implement strategy, see results, and optimize. After 3 months, you can continue month-to-month or cancel with 30 days notice.

Can you work with my existing content?

Absolutely! We love repurposing your existing content (podcasts, blogs, speeches, etc.) into LinkedIn posts. This is included in our Pro and Enterprise packages, or available as an add-on for Starter.
