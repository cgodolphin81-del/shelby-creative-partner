## 📋 COMPONENT SUMMARY

### 1. Voice Capture Questionnaire (20 Questions)

**Purpose:** Extract the client's unique voice, stories, and perspective before writing a single word.

**Sections:**
- Tone & Personality (5 questions)
- Stories & Experiences (5 questions)
- Opinions & Perspectives (5 questions)
- Values & Philosophy (5 questions)

**Output:** Voice profile document that guides all content creation.

**Key Questions Include:**
- "How would your closest colleagues describe your communication style in 3 words?"
- "What's the biggest failure in your career that taught you something valuable?"
- "What's something everyone in your industry celebrates that you think is overrated?"
- "If you could only teach people ONE thing for the rest of your career, what would it be?"

---

**Purpose:** Deep-dive conversation to clarify voice, goals, and expectations.

**Minute-by-Minute Breakdown:**

### 10. Churn Prevention System

**Purpose:** Identify at-risk clients before they leave.

**Early Warning Signs:**
- 🔴 Critical: "Taking a break," contract end questions, stopped reviewing drafts
- ⚠️ Yellow: Slower responses, skipping calls, budget mentions

**Intervention Levels:**
- Level 1 (Medium Risk): Enhanced communication
- Level 2 (High Risk): Retention call with account manager
- Level 3 (Critical): Leadership intervention + retention offer

**Includes:**
- Complete intervention scripts
- Retention offer menu
- Exit interview process
- Post-churn analysis framework

## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Onboarding:
- ❌ Skipping the questionnaire (you'll write generic content)
- ❌ Rushing the onboarding call (you'll miss key insights)
- ❌ Not setting expectations (they'll expect overnight virality)

### Content:
- ❌ Writing in your voice, not theirs (they'll feel disconnected)
- ❌ Too many revisions per post (inefficient, frustrating)
- ❌ Not tracking what works (you can't optimize)

### Client Management:
- ❌ Only communicating when there's a problem (feels transactional)
- ❌ Not showing ROI clearly (they'll question the investment)
- ❌ Waiting until they're unhappy to check in (too late)

### Business:
- ❌ Not having a churn prevention system (you'll lose clients silently)
- ❌ Never asking for testimonials (missing marketing gold)
- ❌ Not offering upsells (leaving money on the table)
