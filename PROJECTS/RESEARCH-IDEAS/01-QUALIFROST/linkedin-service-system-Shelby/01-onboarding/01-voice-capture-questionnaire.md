# Voice Capture Questionnaire
## Extracting Your Unique LinkedIn Voice

**Purpose:** This questionnaire captures the essence of how you think, speak, and show up—so every post sounds unmistakably like YOU.

**Instructions:** Answer honestly and in detail. There are no wrong answers. The more specific you are, the more authentic your content will be.

---

## SECTION 1: TONE & PERSONALITY

### 1. How would your closest colleagues describe your communication style in 3 words?
_(e.g., "direct, warm, no-BS" or "thoughtful, analytical, encouraging")_

### 2. What's a phrase or expression you find yourself saying repeatedly in meetings?
_(e.g., "Here's what I know for sure..." or "Let me be blunt...")_

### 3. When you're explaining something complex, do you tend to:
- [ ] Use analogies and metaphors
- [ ] Break it into step-by-step lists
- [ ] Tell a story that illustrates the point
- [ ] Lead with data and evidence
- [ ] Other: _______________

### 4. What's your default emotional temperature when posting?
- [ ] Enthusiastic/energetic
- [ ] Calm/measured
- [ ] Provocative/challenging
- [ ] Empathetic/supportive
- [ ] Humorous/witty
- [ ] Mix (describe): _______________

### 5. Complete this sentence: "I get frustrated when people in my industry..."
_(This reveals your contrarian edges—gold for engagement)_

---

## SECTION 2: STORIES & EXPERIENCES

### 6. What's the biggest failure or mistake in your career that taught you something valuable?
_(Describe what happened, what you learned, and how you changed)_

### 7. Tell me about a moment when you felt genuinely proud of something you accomplished.
_(Specific details matter—where were you, who was there, what exactly happened?)_

### 8. What's a belief you held strongly 5 years ago that you've since changed your mind about?
_(What changed it? This shows intellectual honesty)_

### 9. Describe a time when you had to make a difficult decision with incomplete information.
_(What was the situation? How did you decide? What was the outcome?)_

### 10. Who has been the most influential person in your career and why?
_(What did they teach you? Do you still apply those lessons?)_

---

## SECTION 3: OPINIONS & PERSPECTIVES

### 11. What's something everyone in your industry celebrates that you think is overrated?
_(Controversial opinions drive engagement—be honest)_

### 12. What's something everyone in your industry ignores that you think is underrated?
_(This positions you as a thought leader)_

### 13. Fill in the blank: "Most people think ______ is the key to success, but actually it's ______."

### 14. What's a piece of conventional career/business advice that you think is dead wrong?
_(e.g., "Follow your passion" or "Never give up")_

### 15. What question do you wish people would ask you more often?
_(And what's your answer?)_

---

## SECTION 4: VALUES & PHILOSOPHY

### 16. What are your non-negotiable values when it comes to work/business?
_(List 3-5 and briefly explain each)_

### 17. What does "success" mean to you personally—not the generic definition, YOUR definition?

### 18. What legacy do you want to leave in your industry or community?

### 19. What's something you're willing to be misunderstood about?
_(Principles you'll stand by even if unpopular)_

### 20. If you could only teach people ONE thing for the rest of your career, what would it be?
_(This is your core message—your North Star)_

---

## BONUS: VOICE SAMPLES

**Please share 2-3 examples of:**
- An email you've written that felt "very you"
- A presentation slide or speech excerpt
- Any past social posts that got great response
- A LinkedIn message or comment you're proud of

_(These help us match your natural rhythm and word choice)_

---

## FOR THE GHOSTWRITER: Analysis Framework

After receiving responses, analyze for:

| Element | What to Extract | Example Tags |
|---------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Sentence rhythm** | Short punchy vs. long flowing | `#punchy` `#narrative` |
| **Vocabulary level** | Simple vs. technical jargon | `#accessible` `#expert` |
| **Humor style** | Self-deprecating, dry, witty, none | `#selfdeprecating` `#dry` |
| **Vulnerability level** | How personal they go | `#openbook` `#selective` |
| **Authority markers** | How they establish credibility | `#data` `#story` `#credentials` |
| **Call-to-action style** | Direct vs. subtle | `#direct` `#invitational` |
| **Contrarian ratio** | How often they challenge norms | `#challenger` `#consensus` |
