# Follow-Up System: 5-Day Close Sequence + Objection Handling

## Overview

Most webinar sales happen AFTER the live event. This follow-up system maximizes conversions with a strategic 5-day sequence and comprehensive objection handling.

---

## 📊 FOLLOW-UP PHILOSOPHY

### Why Follow-Up Matters
- **70% of sales** happen after the webinar ends
- **80% of prospects** say "no" 4 times before saying "yes"
- **Only 20%** buy during the live presentation
- **Follow-up can 2-3x** your total revenue

### The 5-Day Framework
```
Day 0: Webinar ends → Thank you + Replay
Day 1: Value reinforcement + Bonus reminder
Day 2: Social proof + Objection handling
Day 3: Scarcity activation + Personal outreach
Day 4: Last call + Cart close
```

---

## 🛡️ OBJECTION HANDLING SCRIPTS

### Objection 1: "I Can't Afford It"

**Empathize**:
"I totally get it. [Amount] is a real decision, and I respect 
that you're thoughtful about your finances."

**Reframe**:
"Can I ask: What's the cost of NOT solving [problem]? 

If you're losing [X] per month / staying stuck for [timeframe] / 
continuing to [negative consequence], the cost of inaction is 
actually higher than the investment."

**Offer Solution**:
"We have payment plans: [Details]. That brings it down to 
[amount]/month. Does that feel more manageable?"

**Close**:
"Would a payment plan make this possible for you?"

---

### Objection 2: "I Need to Think About It"

**Validate**:
"Absolutely. This is a big decision, and I'd never want you to 
rush into something."

**Probe**:
"Totally fair. Can I ask—what specifically do you need to think 
through? Is it about [the program fit / the timing / the 
investment / something else]?"

[Listen to their answer]

**Address**:
[Address their specific concern]

**Create Gentle Urgency**:
"I get it. Just want to make sure you know the bonuses expire 
[timeframe]. If timing is the issue, we can [solution]. If it's 
about fit, I'm happy to answer more questions."

**Close**:
"What would need to be true for you to feel confident moving 
forward?"

---

### Objection 3: "I Don't Have Time"

**Empathize**:
"I hear you. Everyone's stretched thin these days."

**Reframe**:
"Quick question: How much time are you currently spending on 
[problem] each week? [Let them answer]"

"Most of our clients were saying the same thing—until they 
realized this system actually SAVES them [X] hours per week by 
eliminating [time-waster]."

**Specifics**:
"The program requires about [time commitment] per week. That's 
less than [comparison: one Netflix episode, one lunch out, etc.]."

**Close**:
"If I could show you how to save [X] hours per week while 
achieving [result], would that be worth [time commitment]?"

---

### Objection 4: "I've Tried Things Before and They Didn't Work"

**Validate**:
"I appreciate your honesty. And I'm not surprised you feel that 
way. There's so much out there that overpromises and underdelivers."

**Differentiate**:
"What makes [Program] different is [key differentiator]. 

Unlike [what they tried], we [specific difference]."

**Proof**:
"Here's what [Client Name] said: They'd tried [X, Y, Z] with no 
luck. Then they [specific action from program] and [result]."

**Risk Reversal**:
"And here's the thing: We have a [X]-day guarantee. If it doesn't 
work for you, you get your money back. Zero risk."

**Close**:
"What would it look like if this actually worked for you?"

---

### Objection 5: "I Need to Talk to My [Spouse/Partner/Boss]"

**Support**:
"That makes complete sense. This affects [them/your team], so 
I'd want to discuss it too."

**Equip**:
"Can I send you some information to share with them? Maybe a 
link to the program page, some testimonials, or even hop on a 
quick call together?"

**Timeline**:
"When do you think you'll be able to talk with them?"

[Get specific date]

**Follow-Up**:
"Great. Let me follow up with you on [date] to see how it went. 
Does that work?"

**Close**:
"In the meantime, here's the link with all the details: [LINK]. 
The bonuses expire [date], so if you can talk with them before 
then, that would be ideal."

---

### Objection 6: "It's Too Expensive"

**Acknowledge**:
"I understand. It's an investment, and I want to make sure it's 
the right one for you."

**Value Stack**:
"Let me break down the value:

• [Component 1]: $[X] value
• [Component 2]: $[X] value
• [Component 3]: $[X] value
• Bonus 1: $[X] value
• Bonus 2: $[X] value
• Bonus 3: $[X] value

Total value: $[Total]
Investment: $[Price]

You're getting $[X] in value for $[Y]."

**Comparison**:
"Compared to [alternative: hiring a consultant ($X), going to 
school ($X), cost of inaction ($X)], this is [comparison]."

**Payment Option**:
"And we have payment plans: [Details]."

**Close**:
"Knowing all that, does the investment make more sense?"

---

### Objection 7: "I'm Not Sure It'll Work for Me"

**Normalize**:
"That's a really common concern, and honestly, a smart one to 
have."

**Evidence**:
"Here's what I'd say: We've worked with [number] clients in 
[similar situations]. Here are a few results:

• [Client 1]: [Result]
• [Client 2]: [Result]
• [Client 3]: [Result]"

**Similarity**:
"Specifically, [Client Name] was in a similar situation to you—
[describe similarity]. They were worried it wouldn't work for 
them either. Here's what happened: [Result]."

**Risk Reversal**:
"And remember, we have a [X]-day guarantee. If it doesn't work, 
you get your money back."

**Close**:
"What would need to happen for you to feel confident this would 
work for you?"

## 📱 FOLLOW-UP CHANNELS

### Email
- **Primary channel** for most audiences
- **Open rates**: 25-45%
- **Best for**: Detailed information, links, automation

### SMS
- **High open rates**: 90%+
- **Best for**: Urgent reminders, short messages
- **Frequency**: 1-2 per webinar max (don't spam)
- **Compliance**: Ensure TCPA compliance, opt-in required

### Phone Calls
- **Best for**: High-ticket offers ($2K+)
- **Conversion**: 3-5x higher than email alone
- **Timing**: Day 2-3 of follow-up
- **Script**: Use objection handling above

### Social Media DM
- **Best for**: Warm leads who engaged publicly
- **Approach**: "Hey, saw you commented on the webinar post. 
  Any questions I can answer?"
- **Don't**: Pitch immediately, build rapport first

### Retargeting Ads
- **Platform**: Facebook, Instagram, Google
- **Audience**: Webinar registrants who didn't buy
- **Message**: "Missed the bonuses? Last chance" or testimonial-based
- **Budget**: $10-50/day for 5 days post-webinar
