# Webinar Topic Research: High-Converting Topics by Niche

- **Hook Angle**: The emotional/curiosity trigger
- **Target Audience**: Who this resonates with
- **Conversion Potential**: Expected registration-to-attendance rate
- **Offer Alignment**: What you can naturally sell

## 🎯 TOPIC SELECTION FRAMEWORK

### The 4-Point Topic Scorecard

Rate your topic ideas 1-10 on each:

1. **Urgency**: Does this solve a painful, immediate problem?
2. **Specificity**: Can you promise a clear, measurable outcome?
3. **Credibility**: Do you have proof/results to back it up?
4. **Offer Alignment**: Does this naturally lead to your paid offer?

**Topics scoring 32+ are webinar gold.**

### Topic Testing Formula

Before committing to a topic, validate with:
- Poll your email list (which topic interests you most?)
- Run a small ad test ($50-100 to gauge CTR)
- Check Google Trends for search volume
- Review competitor webinars in your niche

## 📊 BENCHMARK METRICS

### Industry Standards for Webinar Performance

| Metric | Good | Great | Exceptional |
|--------|------|-------|-------------|
| Registration Rate (ad click → reg) | 25% | 35% | 45%+ |
| Attendance Rate (registered → attended) | 30% | 40% | 50%+ |
| Stay Rate (attended → stayed to offer) | 60% | 75% | 85%+ |
| Conversion Rate (stayed → bought) | 5% | 10% | 15%+ |
| Revenue Per Registrant | $15 | $40 | $75+ |

### Topic Impact on Metrics

- **High-urgency topics** (pain-based): Higher attendance, higher conversion
- **Aspirational topics** (dream-based): Higher registration, lower attendance
- **Educational topics** (skill-based): Moderate across all, loyal audience
- **Contrarian topics** (against grain): Viral potential, polarizing

## 🔄 TOPIC ROTATION STRATEGY

Don't run the same webinar forever. Rotate based on:

1. **Seasonality**: New Year = goals, Summer = lifestyle, Fall = business
2. **Audience Fatigue**: Change every 8-12 weeks
3. **Offer Updates**: New product = new angle
4. **Market Shifts**: Trends, news, platform changes

### Quarterly Topic Planning

- **Q1 (Jan-Mar)**: Fresh start, goals, transformation
- **Q2 (Apr-Jun)**: Growth, scaling, summer prep
- **Q3 (Jul-Sep)**: Back to business, preparation, systems
- **Q4 (Oct-Dec)**: Year-end push, planning, bonuses
