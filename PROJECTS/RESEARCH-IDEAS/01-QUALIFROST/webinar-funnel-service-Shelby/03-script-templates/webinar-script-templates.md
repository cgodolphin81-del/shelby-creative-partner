# Script Templates: Hook, Story, Offer, Close

## Overview

These are word-for-word script templates for the four most critical parts of your webinar. Customize the bracketed sections for your specific topic and offer.

---

## 🎣 HOOK SCRIPTS

The first 5 minutes determine if people stay. Use these hooks to grab attention immediately.

### Hook Template 1: The "Shocking Statistic"

```
"Did you know that [shocking statistic related to problem]? 

That's right—[repeat statistic in different words]. 

And if you're like most people in [audience], you're probably part of that [percentage].

But here's what nobody's telling you: [counterintuitive truth].

Today, I'm going to show you exactly how to [desired outcome]—and why everything 
you've been told about [topic] is wrong.

Stay with me for the next [duration] minutes, and I promise you'll walk away with 
[at least one specific takeaway]."
```

**Example (Weight Loss Niche):**
```
"Did you know that 95% of people who lose weight gain it all back within 2 years?

That's right—only 5% keep it off. And if you're like most people watching, 
you've probably been part of that 95%.

But here's what nobody's telling you: It's not about willpower. It's about biology.

Today, I'm going to show you exactly how to lose 20 pounds and keep it off—
and why everything you've been told about calories in, calories out is wrong.

Stay with me for the next 45 minutes, and I promise you'll walk away with 
the exact 3-step system that's worked for 2,300+ people."
```

## 📖 STORY SCRIPTS

Your story builds connection and credibility. Use this structure.

### Story Arc Template: The "Hero's Journey" (Condensed)

```
THE BEFORE (Relatability)
"[Timeframe] ago, I was exactly where you are right now.

[Describe specific struggle in detail]. 

I [action you took]. I [another action]. I even [extreme action].

But nothing worked. In fact, [things got worse/specific low point].

I remember [specific moment with sensory details]. 

I felt [emotion]. I thought [limiting belief]."

THE TURNING POINT (Discovery)
"Then, everything changed.

[Describe the moment of discovery]. 

I [what happened]. I met [who helped]. I discovered [insight].

And I realized [key realization that changed everything].

It wasn't [what they thought]. It was [truth you discovered]."

THE AFTER (Transformation)
"So I tried [new approach]. 

[Describe implementation]. 

And [specific result 1]. Then [specific result 2]. 

Within [timeframe], I went from [before] to [after].

[Current success metrics]."

THE WHY (Purpose)
"Now, you might be wondering: 'Why are you sharing this?'

[Authentic reason]. 

I [personal mission]. I [why you care]. 

Because [belief about helping others].

And that's why I'm here today—to show you exactly how to 
[what you're teaching]."
```

**Example (Health Niche):**
```
THE BEFORE (Relatability)
"Five years ago, I was exactly where you are right now.

I was 45 pounds overweight, exhausted all the time, and hiding in photos.

I tried keto. I tried intermittent fasting. I even did that juice cleanse 
where you drink nothing but celery juice for 14 days.

But nothing worked. In fact, I kept gaining weight.

I remember looking in the mirror one morning, not recognizing the person 
staring back at me. I felt defeated. I thought maybe this was just my 
genetics—maybe I was destined to be overweight forever."

THE TURNING POINT (Discovery)
"Then, everything changed.

My doctor ran a full hormone panel and found the real problem: 
My insulin was through the roof.

I discovered that it wasn't about calories—it was about hormones.

And I realized everything I'd been told about weight loss was backwards."

THE AFTER (Transformation)
"So I tried a hormone-first approach.

I [specific dietary changes]. I [specific lifestyle changes].

And I lost 12 pounds in the first month. Then 15 more.

Within 8 months, I was down 47 pounds—and I've kept it off for 4 years.

Today, I have more energy than I did in my 20s."

THE WHY (Purpose)
"Now, you might be wondering: 'Why are you sharing this?'

Because nobody should have to struggle like I did.

I became a certified nutritionist specifically to help people 
avoid the decade of frustration I went through.

And that's why I'm here today—to show you exactly how to 
lose weight by working with your hormones, not against them."
```

## 💰 OFFER SCRIPTS

The offer presentation makes or breaks your conversion rate.

```

"Introducing: [Program Name]

[main transformation].

Here's exactly what you get:"

"✓ Module 1: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
✓ Module 2: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
✓ Module 3: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
✓ Module 4: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
✓ Module 5: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)

But that's not all. When you join today, you also get:"

BONUSES
"🎁 BONUS #1: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
🎁 BONUS #2: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
🎁 BONUS #3: [Name] – [Description] (Value: $XXX)
```

## 🎯 CLOSE SCRIPTS

The close drives final action and handles objections.

### Close Script Template 1: The "Direct Close"

```
RECAP VALUE
"Let me recap what you're getting:

✓ [Key benefit 1]
✓ [Key benefit 2]
✓ [Key benefit 3]
✓ [Bonus 1]
✓ [Bonus 2]
✓ [Guarantee]

[Time commitment] per week. That's it.

'I'm not sure it'll work for me.' → That's exactly why we have the 
[guarantee]. Zero risk.

[urgency reason]. The bonuses expire [timeframe]."

FINAL CALL TO ACTION
"Here's what I want you to do:

Click the link below. Secure your spot. Take action on what you learned today.

Future you will thank present you for making this decision.

The link is: [URL]

I'll be here for the next [timeframe] to answer any final questions.

Let's do this."
```

## 🎤 DELIVERY TIPS

### Pacing
- **Hook**: Fast, energetic
- **Story**: Slower, emotional
- **Teaching**: Clear, methodical
- **Offer**: Confident, direct
- **Close**: Urgent, warm

### Tone
- Speak like you're talking to ONE person
- Use conversational language (contractions, casual phrasing)
- Vary your volume and speed for emphasis
- Pause before key points

### Engagement
- Ask for chat interaction every 5-7 minutes
- Use polls when available
- Call out attendees by name when possible
- Respond to questions in real-time
