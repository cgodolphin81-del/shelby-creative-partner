## 🎯 EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile optimization)
- Use personalization (first name, location)
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Test emojis (can increase open rates 10-15%)
- Avoid spam words (free, guarantee, act now)

### Content Tips
- Write like you're talking to ONE person
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Use bold for skimmability
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-optimize (50%+ opens on mobile)

### Timing Tips
- **Promotion emails**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm
- **Reminder emails**: Day/time of webinar
- **Replay emails**: Evening (people watch after work)
- **Offer emails**: Tuesday-Thursday, avoid weekends

### List Segmentation
- **Registered + Attended**: Full sequence
- **Registered + No-Show**: Emphasize replay
- **Not Registered**: Different angle, more value
- **Clicked + Didn't Register**: Address objections
- **Buyers**: Skip offer emails, onboarding sequence

## 🧪 A/B TESTING PLAN

### Test These Elements:
1. **Subject Lines**: Curiosity vs. Direct
2. **Send Time**: Morning vs. Afternoon
3. **CTA Button**: Text, color, placement
4. **Email Length**: Short vs. Long
5. **Personalization**: First name vs. None
6. **Images**: With vs. Without
7. **Preview Text**: Optimized vs. Default

### Testing Schedule:
- Test one element per email
- Minimum 1,000 recipients per variant
- Run for at least 4 webinars
- Implement winner, test next element
