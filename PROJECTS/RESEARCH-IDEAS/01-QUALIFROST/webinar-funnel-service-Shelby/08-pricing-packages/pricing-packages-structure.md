## 💳 PAYMENT STRUCTURES

### Option 1: Pay in Full
- 10% discount for upfront payment
- Best for: Budget-conscious clients
- Example: $5,000 package → $4,500 if paid upfront

### Option 2: 50/50 Split
- 50% deposit to start
- 50% due at webinar delivery
- Best for: Standard arrangements

### Option 3: Payment Plan
- 3-6 monthly payments
- 5-10% premium for payment plans
- Best for: Higher-ticket packages

**Example Payment Plan (DFY $7,500)**:
```
Deposit: $2,500 (to start)
Month 2: $2,500
Month 3: $2,500
Total: $7,500
```

### Option 4: Performance-Based (Advanced)
- Lower upfront fee
- Percentage of webinar revenue
- Requires trust and tracking
- Example: $3,000 + 10% of sales

## 🎯 PACKAGE POSITIONING

### How to Present the Tiers

**Always present all three tiers** (anchoring effect):

```
"I have three ways we can work together:

Option 1 is Done-With-You at $[X]. This is great if you want 
to be hands-on and have a solid framework to follow.

Option 2 is Done-For-You at $[X]. This is our most popular 
package—you focus on delivering, we handle everything else.

Option 3 is Premium at $[X]. This is for executives who want 
a fully managed, broadcast-quality production with a full team.

Most clients find Option 2 is the sweet spot. Which feels like 
the best fit for your situation?"
```

### Why Three Tiers Work

1. **Anchoring**: Premium makes DFY look reasonable
2. **Choice**: Clients feel in control
3. **Upsell Path**: DWY clients can upgrade later
4. **Qualification**: Price self-selects ideal clients

## 📊 PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

### Price Points That Work

| Package | Psychological Price | Avoid |
|---------|-------------------|-------|
| DWY | $2,500, $3,500, $4,997 | $3,000, $4,000 |
| DFY | $5,000, $7,500, $9,997 | $6,000, $8,000 |
| Premium | $10,000, $12,500, $15,000 | $11,000, $14,000 |

### Why These Prices Work

- **End in 7 or 97**: Perceived as discounted
- **Round numbers for premium**: Signals quality
- **Clear gaps between tiers**: Easy differentiation
- **No prices too close**: Avoids decision paralysis

### Value Communication

**Don't say**: "It costs $7,500"  
**Do say**: "The investment is $7,500"

**Don't say**: "You get 10 emails"  
**Do say**: "You get a complete 10-email sequence that's been proven to convert 35% of attendees"

**Don't say**: "I'll design your slides"  
**Do say**: "You'll have a professional slide deck that positions you as the authority and keeps your audience engaged throughout"

## 📝 CONTRACT ESSENTIALS

### Key Clauses to Include

1. **Scope of Work**: Exactly what's included
2. **Timeline**: Key milestones and dates
3. **Payment Terms**: Due dates, late fees
4. **Revisions**: Number included, cost of extras
5. **Client Responsibilities**: What they must provide
6. **Cancellation Policy**: Refund terms, kill fees
7. **Intellectual Property**: Who owns what
8. **Confidentiality**: NDA if needed
9. **Liability**: Limitation of liability
10. **Force Majeure**: What if webinar can't happen

### Cancellation Policy Example

```
If Client cancels:
• More than 30 days before webinar: 50% refund
• 15-30 days before webinar: 25% refund
• Less than 15 days: No refund

If Provider cancels:
• Full refund plus [compensation if applicable]
```
