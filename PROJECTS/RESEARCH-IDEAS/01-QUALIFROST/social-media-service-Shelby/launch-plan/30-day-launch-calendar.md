# 30-Day Launch Calendar

## Social Media Management Service Launch Plan

---

## Overview

This 30-day launch calendar is designed to help you successfully launch your social media management service. Each day includes specific actions across marketing, sales, operations, and content.

**Launch Goal:** Secure 3-5 paying clients within 30 days
**Target Audience:** Small to medium businesses, startups, solopreneurs
**Primary Channels:** LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, Email

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist (Before Day 1)

- [ ] Service packages finalized + priced
- [ ] Website/landing page live
- [ ] Portfolio/samples ready (even if spec work)
- [ ] Contracts + agreements prepared
- [ ] Payment system set up (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
- [ ] Social profiles optimized
- [ ] Content bank created (2 weeks of content)
- [ ] Email templates prepared
- [ ] CRM/tracking system ready
- [ ] Onboarding process documented

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### If You're Behind on Leads

**Day 10 Check:**
- Increase outreach by 50%
- Add paid ads ($100-200 test budget)
- Leverage personal network more aggressively
- Consider limited-time bonus offer

### If You Have Leads But No Conversions

**Diagnosis:**
- Is pricing the issue? → Offer payment plan
- Is trust the issue? → More social proof
- Is timing the issue? → Nurture sequence
- Is fit the issue? → Better qualification

### If You're Overwhelmed

**Solutions:**
- Focus on highest-converting activities only
- Batch similar tasks
- Use templates for everything
- Consider a VA for admin tasks
