## 📅 OVERVIEW

This 30-day calendar provides a structured timeline for implementing CRO audit recommendations. It's designed for the **Audit + Implementation** package but can be adapted for other service tiers.

### Week-by-Week Focus

| Week | Theme | Key Activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **Week 1** | Foundation & Quick Wins | Audit kickoff, analytics setup, quick win implementation |
| **Week 2** | Core Optimization | High-impact changes, A/B test setup |
| **Week 3** | Testing & Refinement | Test monitoring, additional optimizations |
| **Week 4** | Analysis & Scale | Results analysis, documentation, next phase planning |
