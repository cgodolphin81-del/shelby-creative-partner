## Scoring System

- ✅ **Pass** (2 points) - Meets best practice standards
- ⚠️ **Partial** (1 point) - Needs improvement
- ❌ **Fail** (0 points) - Critical issue requiring attention

**Total Possible Score**: 100 points  
**Benchmark Scores**:
- 80-100: Excellent conversion foundation
- 60-79: Good, with optimization opportunities
- 40-59: Significant improvements needed
- Below 40: Critical conversion barriers present
