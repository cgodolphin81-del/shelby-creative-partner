**Full impact**: 8-12 weeks for comprehensive optimization

### What if my traffic is low?

We can still optimize! With lower traffic:
- Focus on high-impact, low-risk changes
- Use qualitative data (recordings, surveys)
- Run longer tests for statistical significance
- Consider qualitative research

We guarantee thorough, expert work and data-driven recommendations. However, conversion rates depend on many factors (product, market, competition). Typical clients see 10-50% improvement, but results vary.
