## Revenue Share Option

### Alternative or Add-On to Monthly Retainer

**Structure:**
- Base retainer: 50-75% of standard rate
- Revenue share: 3-5% of monthly gross revenue
- Minimum: Standard monthly rate
- Term: 6-12 month minimum

**Example (Growth Package):**
```
Standard: $10K setup + $5K/month

Revenue Share Option:
- Setup: $10,000
- Monthly: $2,500 base
- Revenue share: 4% of gross revenue
- Minimum: $5,000/month

At $100K/month revenue:
$2,500 + (4% × $100,000) = $6,500/month

At $200K/month revenue:
$2,500 + (4% × $200,000) = $10,500/month
```

**Best For:**
- Confident in ability to scale
- Aligned incentives
- Higher upside potential
- Established products with proven demand
