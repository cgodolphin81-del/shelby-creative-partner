## Pricing Your Services

### Factors to Consider

**Your Experience:**
- New to e-commerce: Start at lower end
- Proven track record: Premium pricing
- Agency with team: Highest tier

**Client Factors:**
- Product margin (higher = can pay more)
- Current revenue (more = bigger budget)
- Growth goals (aggressive = more investment)
- Decision speed (fast = premium)

**Market Factors:**
- Local market rates
- Competition
- Demand for services
- Economic conditions

### Pricing Psychology

**Anchoring:**
- Present Scale package first ($15K)
- Growth seems reasonable ($10K)
- Launch seems accessible ($5K)

**Value-Based:**
- Tie pricing to outcomes, not hours
- Show ROI projections
- Emphasize revenue share option

**Payment Terms:**
- 50% upfront for setup
- Monthly retainers due 1st
- Revenue share calculated monthly
- Discounts for quarterly/annual prepay
