## SMS Marketing

### SMS Best Practices

**Compliance:**
- Get explicit consent (opt-in)
- Include opt-out in every message
- Don't message before 8am or after 9pm
- Frequency: 2-4 messages per week max

**Character Limit:** 160 characters (including spaces)

**Best Practices:**
- Keep it conversational
- Include clear CTA
- Use short links
- Personalize when possible
- Don't overuse emojis (1-2 max)

### SMS Campaign Types

#### 1. Flash Sales

```
[Brand]: FLASH SALE! 40% off everything for the next 
4 hours only! Shop now: [link] Text STOP to opt out.
```

**Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 12-3pm
**Frequency:** 1-2x per month

#### 2. New Product Launches

```
[Brand]: It's here! 🎉 The [Product] you've been 
waiting for is finally available. Shop before it 
sells out: [link] Text STOP to opt out.
```

**Timing:** Product launch day, 10am
**Frequency:** As needed

#### 3. Cart Abandonment

```
[Brand]: Hey [Name], you left items in your cart! 
Complete your order: [link] Questions? Reply here. 
Text STOP to opt out.
```

**Timing:** 2 hours after abandonment
**Frequency:** Automated

#### 4. Back in Stock

```
[Brand]: Good news! [Product] is back in stock. 
Grab yours before it's gone: [link] Text STOP to 
opt out.
```

**Timing:** Immediately when restocked
**Frequency:** Automated

#### 5. VIP Exclusive

```
[Brand]: VIP EXCLUSIVE: Early access to our sale 
starts NOW! 24 hours before everyone else. Shop: 
[link] Text STOP to opt out.
```

**Timing:** Before public sale
**Frequency:** 1x per quarter

### SMS List Building

**Opt-In Methods:**
- Website popup
- Checkout opt-in
- Keyword text-to-join
- In-store signup
- Social media promotion

**Popup Copy Example:**

**Incentive Ideas:**

- Early access to sales
- Exclusive flash deals
- Free shipping code

## Segmentation Strategies

### By Customer Behavior

| Segment | Criteria | Strategy |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **VIP** | 3+ purchases or $500+ LTV | Exclusive offers, early access |
| **Active** | Purchased in last 90 days | Regular campaigns, cross-sell |
| **At-Risk** | 90-180 days since purchase | Win-back campaigns |
| **Lapsed** | 180+ days since purchase | Aggressive win-back |
| **Subscribers** | No purchases yet | Welcome series, first purchase offer |

### By Product Interest

**Segment Based On:**
- Products viewed
- Products purchased
- Categories browsed
- Cart contents

**Use For:**
- Relevant product recommendations
- Cross-sell complementary products
- Replenishment reminders (consumables)

### By Engagement

| Segment | Criteria | Strategy |
|---------|----------|----------|
| **Highly Engaged** | Opens 50%+ emails | Send more frequently |
| **Moderately Engaged** | Opens 20-50% | Normal frequency |
| **Low Engagement** | Opens <20% | Re-engagement or suppress |
| **Inactive** | No opens in 90 days | Win-back or remove |

## Compliance & Best Practices

### Email Compliance

**CAN-SPAM (US):**
- Include physical address
- Clear unsubscribe link
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days
- Accurate subject lines
- Identify as advertisement if applicable

**GDPR (EU):**
- Explicit consent required
- Right to access data
- Right to be forgotten
- Data protection measures

### SMS Compliance

**TCPA (US):**
- Written consent required
- Include opt-out in every message
- Honor opt-outs immediately
- No messages before 8am or after 9pm

**Best Practices:**
- Double opt-in recommended
- Clear frequency disclosure
- Easy opt-out (reply STOP)
- Keep consent records

### List Hygiene

**Monthly Tasks:**
- Remove hard bounces
- Remove serial complainers
- Suppress inactive subscribers (6+ months)
- Clean invalid emails

**Quarterly Tasks:**
- Re-engagement campaign
- Remove non-responders
- Review segment performance
- Update preferences
