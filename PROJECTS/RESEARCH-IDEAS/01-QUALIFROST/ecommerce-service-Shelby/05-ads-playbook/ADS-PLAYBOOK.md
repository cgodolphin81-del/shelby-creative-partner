## Platform Overview

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
**Best For:** Discovery, impulse purchases, visual products

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Intent** | Low (interruption marketing) |
| **Best Products** | Visual, wow factor, under $100 |
| **Learning Curve** | Medium |
| **Avg CPC** | $0.50 - $3.00 |
| **Avg CVR** | 1-3% |
| **Target ROAS** | 2.0 - 3.0 |

**Best For:** High intent, search-driven purchases

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Intent** | High (active searching) |

| **Avg CPC** | $0.50 - $5.00+ |
| **Avg CVR** | 2-5% |
| **Target ROAS** | 3.0 - 5.0 |

## Facebook Ads Strategy

### Campaign Structure

```
CAMPAIGN (Objective)
├── Ad Set 1 (Audience A)
│   ├── Ad A (Creative 1)
│   ├── Ad B (Creative 2)
│   └── Ad C (Creative 3)
├── Ad Set 2 (Audience B)
│   ├── Ad A (Creative 1)
│   ├── Ad B (Creative 2)
│   └── Ad C (Creative 3)
└── Ad Set 3 (Audience C)
    ├── Ad A (Creative 1)
    ├── Ad B (Creative 2)
    └── Ad C (Creative 3)
```

### Campaign Objectives

**For E-Commerce:**
1. **Sales (Conversions)** - Primary objective
2. **Traffic** - For new store, building pixel data
3. **Engagement** - For content, not direct sales

**Always use "Sales" objective for product ads.**

### Budget Guidelines

**Testing Phase:**
- Daily budget: $20-50 per ad set
- Test 3-5 ad sets
- Test 3-5 creatives per ad set
- Total testing budget: $300-500

**Scaling Phase:**
- Increase budget 20-30% every 2-3 days
- Don't exceed 50% increase at once
- Monitor ROAS closely

### Audience Targeting

#### 1. Interest-Based Audiences

**How to Find Interests:**
- Facebook Audience Insights
- Competitor pages
- Related brands
- Industry publications

**Example (Fitness Product):**
- Interest: Gymshark
- Interest: Myprotein
- Interest: CrossFit
- Interest: Yoga
- Behavior: Engaged shoppers

**Audience Size:** 1M - 10M (broad enough to scale)

#### 2. Lookalike Audiences

**Create Lookalikes From:**
- Purchasers (best)
- Add to cart
- Initiate checkout
- Video viewers (95%+)
- Email list

**Lookalike Percentages:**
- 1% - Most similar, smaller
- 3% - Good balance
- 5% - Larger, less similar

**Strategy:** Test 1%, 3%, and 5% separately

#### 3. Retargeting Audiences

**Create These Audiences:**

| Audience | Window | Purpose |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Website visitors | 30 days | General retargeting |
| Product viewers | 14 days | Specific product interest |
| Add to cart | 14 days | High intent |
| Initiate checkout | 7 days | Very high intent |
| Purchasers | 180 days | Exclude or upsell |

**Retargeting Budget:** 20-30% of total ad spend

### Ad Creative Best Practices

#### Video Ads (Highest Performing)

**Video Structure (15-30 seconds):**
```
0-3s:   HOOK - Stop the scroll
        - Show problem dramatically
        - Surprising visual
        - Bold statement

3-10s:  SOLUTION - Introduce product
        - Show product in action
        - Demonstrate key benefit

10-20s: PROOF - Build credibility
        - Show results
        - Customer testimonials
        - Before/after

20-30s: CTA - Tell them what to do
        - Clear call to action
        - Urgency/scarcity
        - Offer (if applicable)
```

**Video Specs:**
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Ratio: 1:1 (feed) or 9:16 (stories/reels)
- Length: 15-30 seconds (test up to 60s)
- Caption: Always include (85% watch without sound)
- First 3 seconds: Most critical

**Video Types That Convert:**
1. **Product Demo** - Show it working
2. **Problem/Solution** - Agitate pain point
3. **Unboxing** - Build anticipation
4. **Testimonial** - Social proof
5. **Before/After** - Transformation
6. **UGC Style** - Authentic, phone-shot

#### Image Ads

**Best Practices:**
- High quality, well-lit
- Show product in use (lifestyle)
- Minimal text on image (<20%)
- Clear focal point
- Mobile-optimized (vertical works well)

**Image Types:**
- Product on white background
- Lifestyle shot
- User-generated content
- Before/after comparison
- Infographic (features/benefits)
- Customer testimonial overlay

#### Carousel Ads

**Use When:**
- Multiple products
- Show features step-by-step
- Tell a story across cards
- Before/after sequence

**Best Practices:**
- 3-5 cards (not maximum 10)
- Consistent visual style
- Each card should make sense alone
- Strong CTA on last card

### Ad Copy Framework

#### Primary Text (Above Image/Video)

**Formula:**
```
[HOOK - Call out audience or problem]

[BODY - Explain solution, benefits]

[SOCIAL PROOF - Reviews, customers, results]

[CTA - Clear next step]

[OFFER - If applicable]
```

**Example:**
```
Tired of tangled earbuds? 😤

SoundPro Wireless Earbuds give you crystal-clear 
sound with ZERO wires. Connect instantly, last 30 
hours, and never worry about tangles again.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Best earbuds I've ever owned!" - Sarah K.
Join 50,000+ happy customers!

Tap Shop Now for 40% OFF today only! 🎧

[Shop Now Button]
```

#### Headlines (Below Image/Video)

**Keep it short and benefit-driven:**
- "Free Shipping Today Only"
- "50% Off - Limited Time"
- "Rated 4.9/5 Stars"
- "As Seen on TikTok"
- "Buy 1, Get 1 50% Off"

#### Descriptions (Optional)

**Use for:**
- Additional offer details
- Shipping info
- Guarantee
- Urgency

**Example:** "Free 30-day returns • Ships in 24 hours"

### Testing Framework

#### Creative Testing (Week 1-2)

**Test 3-5 Different Creatives:**
- Different hooks
- Different formats (video vs image)
- Different value propositions
- Different visual styles

**Budget:** $20-30/day per creative
**Kill Criteria:** No purchases after $100 spend
**Scale Criteria:** ROAS 2.0+ after $200 spend

#### Audience Testing (Week 3-4)

**Test 3-5 Different Audiences:**
- Interest-based (different angles)
- Lookalike (1%, 3%, 5%)
- Broad (no targeting, let algorithm work)

**Keep Winning Creative, Test Audiences**

#### Offer Testing (Week 5-6)

**Test Different Offers:**
- Percentage discount (20% vs 30%)
- Dollar amount ($20 off vs 20%)
- Free shipping
- BOGO
- Bundle deals

## Google Ads Strategy

### Campaign Types for E-Commerce

#### 1. Performance Max (Recommended Start)

**What It Is:**
- Automated campaign across all Google properties
- Uses your product feed
- AI-optimized for conversions

**Best For:**
- Most e-commerce stores
- Scaling existing products
- Limited ad management time

**Setup:**
1. Create Google Merchant Center account
2. Upload product feed
3. Link to Google Ads
4. Create Performance Max campaign
5. Set budget and target ROAS

**Budget:** Start $30-50/day
**Target ROAS:** 300-400%

#### 2. Search Campaigns

**What It Is:**
- Text ads on Google search results
- Triggered by keyword searches

**Best For:**
- High-intent keywords
- Branded searches
- Specific product searches

**Keyword Types:**

| Type | Example | Intent |
|------|---------|--------|
| **Branded** | "[Brand] earbuds" | High |
| **Product** | "wireless earbuds" | Medium-High |
| **Problem** | "earbuds keep falling out" | Medium |
| **Competitor** | "AirPods alternative" | Medium |

**Match Types:**

| Match | Syntax | When to Use |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Broad** | wireless earbuds | Discovery (use cautiously) |
| **Phrase** | "wireless earbuds" | Balanced approach |
| **Exact** | [wireless earbuds] | High intent, precise |
| **Negative** | -free -cheap | Exclude irrelevant |

**Ad Copy Structure:**

```
Headline 1: [Primary Keyword + Benefit]
Headline 2: [Unique Value Proposition]
Headline 3: [Call to Action]

Description 1: [Expand on benefits, include keyword]
Description 2: [Social proof + CTA]

Extensions:
- Sitelinks (additional pages)
- Callouts (free shipping, returns)
- Structured snippets (product categories)
- Price extensions
```

**Example Search Ad:**
```
H1: Wireless Earbuds - 30hr Battery
H2: Free Shipping & Returns
H3: Shop Now - 40% Off Today

D1: Crystal-clear sound with noise cancellation. 
    30-hour battery life. 50,000+ happy customers.
D2: Try risk-free for 30 days. Order today and 
    get free express shipping. Limited offer!
```

#### 3. Shopping Campaigns

**What It Is:**
- Product listing ads with images
- Shows in Google Shopping tab and search

**Requirements:**
- Google Merchant Center account
- Product feed (automated via Shopify/app)
- Competitive pricing

**Best For:**
- Physical products
- Competitive pricing
- Visual products

**Optimization Tips:**
- High-quality product images
- Competitive pricing
- Complete product titles
- Accurate product categories
- Regular feed updates

#### 4. Display Campaigns

**What It Is:**
- Visual ads on websites in Google Display Network
- Retargeting and prospecting

**Best For:**
- Retargeting website visitors
- Brand awareness
- Visual products

**Retargeting Lists:**
- All visitors (30 days)
- Product viewers (14 days)
- Cart abandoners (14 days)
- Past purchasers (180 days - for upsell)

## Ad Creative Production

### In-House Creative Setup

**Minimum Equipment:**
- Smartphone (iPhone 13+ or equivalent)
- Ring light ($30-50)
- Tripod ($20-30)
- Basic editing app (CapCut, free)

**Total Cost:** < $150

### Creative Types to Produce

**Monthly Creative Production:**
- 4-8 videos (15-30 seconds each)
- 10-20 images (various formats)
- 2-4 carousels

### Video Production Template

**Script Template:**
```
SCENE 1 (0-3s): [HOOK]
Visual: [Dramatic problem or surprising visual]
Text: [Attention-grabbing text]
Audio: [Trending sound or voiceover]

SCENE 2 (3-10s): [SOLUTION]
Visual: [Product solving the problem]
Text: [Key benefit]
Audio: [Explain solution]

SCENE 3 (10-20s): [PROOF]
Visual: [Results, testimonials, features]
Text: [Social proof or features]
Audio: [Build credibility]

SCENE 4 (20-30s): [CTA]
Visual: [Product + offer]
Text: [Clear CTA + urgency]
Audio: [Tell them what to do]
```

### UGC-Style Content

**Why It Works:**
- Feels authentic, not ad-like
- Higher engagement
- Lower production cost
- Relatable

**UGC Brief Template:**
```
PRODUCT: [Product name and description]

KEY MESSAGES:
- [Main benefit 1]
- [Main benefit 2]
- [Main benefit 3]

MUST INCLUDE:
- [Specific feature to show]
- [Specific use case]
- [Specific result]

AVOID:
- [Things not to say]
- [Competitor mentions]

FORMAT:
- Vertical video (9:16)
- 15-30 seconds
- Natural lighting
- Speak to camera

COMPENSATION: $[Amount] + free product
```

## Scaling Strategy

### When to Scale

**Green Lights:**
- ROAS 2.5+ for 7+ days
- 15+ purchases in past week
- CPA below target
- Consistent daily performance

**Red Lights:**
- ROAS declining trend
- CPA increasing
- Frequency > 3.0 (audience fatigue)
- CTR dropping

### How to Scale

#### Vertical Scaling (Increase Budget)

**Safe Approach:**
- Increase 20-30% every 2-3 days
- Monitor ROAS closely
- If ROAS drops, pause increase

**Aggressive Approach:**
- Double budget (creates new learning phase)
- Accept temporary ROAS dip
- Wait 3-7 days for stabilization

#### Horizontal Scaling (New Audiences)

**Expand To:**
- New interest audiences
- New lookalike percentages
- New geographic markets
- New placements (Stories, Reels, Audience Network)

#### Creative Scaling

**Refresh Creatives:**
- Every 2-4 weeks for winning ad sets
- When frequency > 3.0
- When CTR drops below 1%

**Produce:**
- 2-4 new videos monthly
- 5-10 new images monthly
- Test new hooks and angles

## Tracking & Analytics

### Facebook Pixel Setup

**Required Events:**
- PageView (automatic)
- ViewContent (product pages)
- AddToCart
- InitiateCheckout
- Purchase (with value)

**Setup Options:**
- Shopify: Built-in, enable in settings
- WooCommerce: Facebook for WooCommerce plugin
- Manual: Install pixel code + events

### Google Analytics 4

**Required Setup:**
- GA4 property
- Enhanced e-commerce enabled
- Link to Google Ads
- Set up conversion events

**Key Reports:**
- Acquisition > Traffic acquisition
- Engagement > Events
- Monetization > E-commerce purchases

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **ROAS** | Revenue / Ad Spend | 2.5-4.0 |
| **CPA** | Ad Spend / Purchases | < 30% of product price |
| **CTR** | Clicks / Impressions | 1-2%+ |
| **CPC** | Ad Spend / Clicks | $0.50-$2.50 |
| **CVR** | Purchases / Clicks | 2-4% |
| **Frequency** | Impressions / Reach | < 3.0 |
| **CPM** | (Ad Spend / Impressions) × 1000 | $10-30 |

### Daily Monitoring Checklist

- [ ] Check spend vs budget
- [ ] Review ROAS by campaign
- [ ] Check for disapproved ads
- [ ] Monitor frequency
- [ ] Review top/worst performers
- [ ] Check for technical issues

### Weekly Optimization

- [ ] Pause underperformers (ROAS < 1.5 after $200)
- [ ] Scale winners (increase budget 20-30%)
- [ ] Launch new creatives
- [ ] Review audience performance
- [ ] Adjust bids/target ROAS
- [ ] Plan next week's tests

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Giving up too early** - Need $300-500 to properly test
❌ **Testing too many variables** - Isolate one at a time
❌ **Ignoring creative** - Creative is 70% of success
❌ **Over-segmenting audiences** - Let algorithm work
❌ **Not using retargeting** - Leave money on table
❌ **Sending to homepage** - Send to specific product page
❌ **No tracking** - Can't optimize what you don't measure
❌ **Scaling too fast** - Kill momentum with big changes
