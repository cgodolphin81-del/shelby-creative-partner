# 30-60-90 Day Marketing Plan: Ecommerce Service

---

## 📋 Executive Summary

This marketing plan outlines the strategy to launch and scale the Ecommerce Service service over 90 days.

**Goal:** Generate $50K in revenue within 90 days
**Target:** 5-10 paying clients
**Budget:** $5K-10K for marketing activities

---

## 📅 Phase 1: Days 1-30 (Foundation & Launch)

### Week 1-2: Infrastructure Setup
- [ ] Build landing page with clear value proposition
- [ ] Set up analytics (Google Analytics, heatmaps)
- [ ] Create lead magnet (free audit, checklist, or guide)
- [ ] Set up email automation (welcome sequence)
- [ ] Create social media profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter)

### Week 3-4: Content & Outreach
- [ ] Publish 4 blog posts / LinkedIn articles
- [ ] Create 20 social media posts (scheduled)
- [ ] Build prospect list (100-200 targets)
- [ ] Start cold email outreach (20-30/day)
- [ ] Join 5-10 relevant communities/groups

**KPIs:**
- 500 website visitors
- 50 email subscribers
- 10 discovery calls booked
- 2-3 clients closed

---

## 📅 Phase 2: Days 31-60 (Traction & Optimization)

### Week 5-6: Paid Advertising
- [ ] Launch LinkedIn Ads ($2K test budget)
- [ ] Launch Google Ads (search campaigns)
- [ ] A/B test landing pages
- [ ] Optimize email sequences based on open rates

### Week 7-8: Content Marketing
- [ ] Publish 2 case studies (even if hypothetical initially)
- [ ] Start podcast guest outreach
- [ ] Create video content (YouTube, TikTok, Reels)
- [ ] Host first webinar

**KPIs:**
- 2,000 website visitors
- 200 email subscribers
- 25 discovery calls booked
- 5-7 clients closed
- $25K revenue

---

## 📅 Phase 3: Days 61-90 (Scale & Systematize)

### Week 9-10: Scale What Works
- [ ] Double down on best-performing channels
- [ ] Increase ad budget on winning campaigns
- [ ] Hire VA for outreach and admin
- [ ] Create referral program

### Week 11-12: Systematize & Document
- [ ] Document all processes
- [ ] Create client onboarding system
- [ ] Build testimonial/review system
- [ ] Plan Q2 strategy

**KPIs:**
- 5,000 website visitors
- 500 email subscribers
- 50 discovery calls booked
- 10+ clients closed
- $50K+ revenue
