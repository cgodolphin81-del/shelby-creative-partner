## 📧 Cold Outreach System

### Where to Find Clients

| Platform | Best For | Search Strategy |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| **YouTube** | Creators with budgets | Search niche, filter by upload date, check channels with 10K-500K subs |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, brands, executives | Search "founder", "CMO", "marketing director" + industry |
| **Twitter/X** | Creators, tech, SaaS | Search "looking for editor", "hiring video" |
| **Instagram** | Lifestyle, fitness, coaches | Hashtags in niche, check engagement |
| **Podcast guest lists** | Experts with audiences | Find podcasts in niche, note guests |
| **Course platforms** | Course creators | Teachable, Kajabi, Thinkific course directories |
| **Facebook Groups** | Local businesses, coaches | Industry-specific groups |

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

**Minimum qualifications:**
- [ ] Revenue: $20K+/month (can afford $3K+/month)
- [ ] Already spending on marketing ($2K+/month)
- [ ] Has a product/service to monetize
- [ ] Understands long-term investment
- [ ] Decision maker is accessible

**Green flags:**
- ✅ Currently posting inconsistently
- ✅ Good content, poor production
- ✅ Growing but plateaued
- ✅ Has team/VA (can delegate)
- ✅ Active on other platforms

**Red flags:**
- ❌ No clear monetization
- ❌ Expects viral overnight
- ❌ Unwilling to show face/voice
- ❌ History of firing agencies
- ❌ Micromanagement tendencies

### Outreach Volume Math

```
To get 10 clients:

1,000 outreaches
    ↓ 10% response rate
100 conversations
    ↓ 30% call booking rate
30 sales calls
    ↓ 33% close rate
10 clients
```

**Daily targets:**
- 20-50 outreaches/day
- 2-5 calls booked/week
- 1-2 clients closed/month

**Time investment:** 2-3 hours/day for outreach

### Outreach Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Personalize every message (mention specific videos)
- Lead with value, not pitch
- Keep it short (under 150 words)
- Include social proof (case studies)
- Follow up 3-5 times (most say yes on follow-up 3+)
- Track everything in CRM/spreadsheet

❌ **Don't:**
- Send generic copy-paste messages
- Lead with your pricing
- Write novels (they won't read)
- Be pushy or desperate
- Give up after one "no"
- Forget to follow up

## 📞 Sales Call Framework

### Call Structure (30-45 minutes)

| Time | Section | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| 0-5 min | Rapport building | Establish connection |
| 5-15 min | Discovery | Understand their situation |
| 15-25 min | Presentation | Show your solution |
| 25-35 min | Q&A | Address objections |
| 35-45 min | Close | Get commitment |

---

### Discovery Questions

**Current situation:**
1. "Tell me about your channel - what's working well?"
2. "What's your biggest frustration with YouTube right now?"
3. "How much time are you currently spending on YouTube?"
4. "Have you worked with an agency/editor before? What happened?"

**Goals:**
5. "What would success look like in 90 days?"
6. "What would 10x growth mean for your business?"
7. "Where do you want your channel to be in a year?"

**Budget/Authority:**
8. "What budget have you allocated for YouTube growth?"
9. "Who else is involved in this decision?"
10. "If we move forward, what's your ideal timeline?"

**Qualifying:**
11. "What's your current monthly revenue from YouTube/business?"
12. "Are you currently investing in content/marketing?"
13. "What happens if you don't solve this problem?"

---

### Presentation Framework

**1. Recap their situation (2 min)**
> "So if I understand correctly, you're [situation], you want [goal], 
> and the main challenge is [obstacle]. Is that right?"

**2. Show the gap (3 min)**
> "Here's what I'm seeing after reviewing your channel..."
> [Share 2-3 specific observations]

**3. Present your solution (5 min)**
> "Here's how we'd approach this..."
> [Walk through your process]

**4. Show proof (3 min)**
> "We've done this before. Here are the results..."
> [Share 1-2 relevant case studies]

**5. Present investment (2 min)**
> "Our [package name] is $X/month..."
> [Be direct, don't apologize]

---

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| **"It's too expensive"** | "I understand. Can I ask - compared to what? And what would it cost you to NOT solve this problem?" |
| **"I need to think about it"** | "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is there something I didn't address?" |
| **"I'll do it myself"** | "That's totally valid. Can I ask - what's stopped you from getting the results you want so far?" |
| **"I need to talk to my partner"** | "Makes sense. Would it help to include them in our next call? I'm happy to walk through everything again." |
| **"Can we start smaller?"** | "We can, but honestly, our minimum engagement is designed to get you results. Starting smaller often means you won't see ROI and that's not good for either of us." |

---

### Closing Techniques

**1. Assumptive Close**
> "Great! So we'll get started on Monday. I'll send over the agreement 
> and invoice. Do you prefer credit card or bank transfer?"

**2. Option Close**
> "Based on what you've told me, I'd recommend the Growth package. 
> We also have Premium which includes [extra]. Which feels like the 
> better fit?"

**3. Urgency Close**
> "Just so you know, we can only onboard 2 clients this month and we 
> have one spot left. If you want to start in [timeframe], we'd need 
> to get the agreement signed by [date]."

**4. Trial Close**
> "Does this feel like the right solution for you?"
> [If yes] "Great! Let's get you set up."

## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Psychology

**Anchoring:**
- Show Premium package first ($10K/month)
- Growth package seems reasonable ($5K/month)
- Starter feels accessible ($2.5K/month)

**Decoy pricing:**
- Starter: $2,500 (limited value)
- Growth: $5,000 (best value - most popular)
- Premium: $8,500 (full service)

**Result:** Most choose Growth (your target)

---

### Payment Terms

**Standard:**
- Monthly retainer, due 1st of month
- 3-month minimum commitment
- Auto-renew after initial term
- 30-day cancellation notice

**Incentives:**
- 5% discount for quarterly prepay
- 10% discount for annual prepay
- Referral credit: $500 per referral

**Late payment:**
- 5% fee after 7 days
- Service pause after 15 days
- Termination after 30 days

## 📄 Contract Essentials

### Key Contract Clauses

**1. Scope of Work**
- Exactly what's included
- Number of videos/deliverables
- Revision policy
- Turnaround times

**2. Payment Terms**
- Amount and due date
- Payment method
- Late fees
- Refund policy (typically none)

**3. Term & Termination**
- Initial term (3-12 months)
- Auto-renewal terms
- Cancellation notice (30 days)
- What happens on termination

**4. Intellectual Property**
- Client owns final videos
- You retain right to use in portfolio
- Music/licensing responsibilities

**5. Confidentiality**
- Don't share their business info
- They don't share your pricing/process

**6. Liability**
- Limit your liability to fees paid
- No guarantees on specific results
- Force majeure clause

---

### Weekly Pipeline Review

**Every Monday:**
1. Review all active leads
2. Identify stalled opportunities
3. Plan outreach for the week
4. Set call booking goals
5. Review close rate from last week

**Metrics to track:**
- Outreach sent (weekly)
- Response rate (%)
- Calls booked (weekly)
- Close rate (%)
- New clients (monthly)
- Revenue added (monthly)
