**Growth → Premium:**
- "You're ready for full production. Let's handle everything"

- Low CTR → Thumbnail upgrade

## 📞 Discovery Call Script

**Qualifying questions:**
1. "What's your current monthly revenue from YouTube?"
2. "What's your biggest frustration with your channel?"
3. "What would 10x growth mean for your business?"
4. "What's your budget for growth investment?"
5. "Have you worked with an agency before? What happened?"

**Red flags:**
- Budget under $1,500/month (not sustainable)
- Expects viral overnight
- Unwilling to show face/voice
- No clear monetization path
- Micromanagement tendencies

**Green flags:**
- Clear business model
- Investment mindset
- Trusts experts
- Long-term thinking
- Good communication
