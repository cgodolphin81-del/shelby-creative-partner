## ⚠️ CITATION BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ✅ Use EXACT same NAP across all directories
- ✅ Use local phone number (not 800 number as primary)
- ✅ Choose most specific categories available
- ✅ Complete every field in each profile
- ✅ Add high-quality photos regularly
- ✅ Monitor and respond to reviews
- ✅ Track submission progress

### DON'T:
- ❌ Keyword stuff business name
- ❌ Use PO Boxes (use physical address)
- ❌ Create duplicate listings
- ❌ Use call tracking as primary number
- ❌ Leave profiles incomplete
- ❌ Ignore negative reviews
- ❌ Use virtual offices for local SEO
