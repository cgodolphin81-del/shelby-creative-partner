### 2. Local Chamber of Commerce
**Difficulty:** Easy | **Value:** High

**Action:**
- Join local chamber ($100-500/year)

- Participate in chamber events
- Get listed in member directory

**Target:** Every city has one - search "[city] chamber of commerce"

**Expected Result:** High-authority local link, networking

## 📝 CONTENT-DRIVEN LINKS

### 21. Local Resource Page Link
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Value:** High

**Action:**
```
Search queries:
- "[city] resources"
- "[city] links"
- "[city] helpful links"
- "inurl:resources [city]"
- "inurl:links [city]"

Email template:
Subject: Resource for [Site Name] visitors

Hi [Name],

I found your [page name] page while researching [city] resources. 
Great list!

I noticed you include [similar resource]. I wanted to suggest 
[your resource] as a potentially helpful addition for [city] residents.

[Your resource] provides [unique value].

Either way, keep up the great work with the site!

Best,
[Name]
[Business]
```

## 🚀 ADVANCED TACTICS

### 41. Unlinked Brand Mentions
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Value:** High

**Action:**
```
Search queries:
- "[Business Name]" -yoursite.com
- "[Business Name] [city]" -yoursite.com
- "[Owner Name]" -yoursite.com

Email template:
Subject: Quick question about [Site Name] mention

Hi [Name],

Thanks for mentioning [Business Name] in your article about 
[topic]! We appreciate the coverage.

I noticed the mention isn't linked. Would you consider adding 
a link to [URL]? It would help readers find us easily.

Either way, thanks for the mention!

Best,
[Name]
```

### 42. Competitor Backlink Analysis
**Difficulty:** Medium | **Value:** High

**Action:**
```
1. Use Ahrefs/Semrush to analyze competitor backlinks
2. Filter for local/industry links
3. Identify link opportunities
4. Replicate successful links

Search:
- Competitor domain in Ahrefs
- Filter by referring domains
- Sort by domain rating
- Identify achievable targets
```

### 43. Broken Link Building (Local)
**Difficulty:** Hard | **Value:** High

**Action:**
```
1. Find local resource pages
2. Check for broken links (Check My Links extension)
3. Identify your content as replacement
4. Reach out to webmaster

Email template:
Subject: Broken link on [Page Name]

Hi [Name],

Was browsing your [page name] page and noticed a broken link:
[Broken URL]

Just wanted to let you know!

If you're looking for a replacement, we have a resource on 
[topic] that might work:
[Your URL]

Either way, hope it helps!

Best,
[Name]
```

## ⚠️ LINK BUILDING BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ✅ Focus on relevance over quantity
- ✅ Build relationships, not just links
- ✅ Create linkable assets
- ✅ Diversify anchor text
- ✅ Earn links naturally when possible
- ✅ Track all outreach
- ✅ Follow up politely
- ✅ Provide value first

### DON'T:
- ❌ Buy links (against Google guidelines)
- ❌ Use link farms/PBNs
- ❌ Spam blog comments
- ❌ Exchange excessive reciprocal links
- ❌ Use exact-match anchor text excessively
- ❌ Send generic outreach
- ❌ Give up after one email
- ❌ Build irrelevant links
