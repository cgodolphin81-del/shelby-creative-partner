## 💰 PRICING PHILOSOPHY

### Value-Based Pricing Principles:
- Price based on VALUE delivered, not hours worked
- Multi-location = compounded value (more revenue impact)
- Tier by business size/revenue, not just location count
- Include clear ROI expectations
- Offer monthly and annual (discount) options

### Pricing Factors:
| Factor | Impact on Price |
|--------|-----------------|
| Number of locations | +$200-500/location |
| Market competitiveness | +10-30% for competitive markets |
| Current SEO state | +20-50% for major rebuilds |
| Industry value per lead | Higher value = higher price |
| Service scope | More services = higher price |
| Contract length | Annual = 10-20% discount |
