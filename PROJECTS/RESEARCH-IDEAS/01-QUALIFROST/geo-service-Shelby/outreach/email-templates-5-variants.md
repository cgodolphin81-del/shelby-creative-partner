### Personalization Tips

1. **Always include:**
   - Their first name
   - Company name
   - Specific competitor name
   - One specific finding from their site

2. **Research before sending:**
   - Check their current AI visibility (quick ChatGPT search)
   - Find a recent company milestone to reference
   - Identify the actual decision maker

3. **A/B test subject lines:**
   - Question vs. statement
   - Specific number vs. vague benefit
   - Urgency vs. curiosity
