# 10. Revenue Projections

3-year financial model with scenarios for 5, 20, and 50 apps built.

---

## Executive Summary

### Revenue Scenarios

| Scenario | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | Total (3yr) |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|-------------|
| Conservative (5 apps/yr) | $375K | $450K | $540K | $1.37M |
| Target (20 apps/yr) | $1.5M | $2.4M | $3.6M | $7.5M |
| Aggressive (50 apps/yr) | $3.75M | $6.0M | $9.0M | $18.75M |

**Key Assumptions:**
- Average project value: $75K (conservative), $75K (target), $75K (aggressive)
- Maintenance revenue: 15-25% of project value annually
- Gross margin: 50-60%
- Net margin: 15-25% (at scale)

## Scenario 1: Conservative Growth (5 Apps/Year)

### Year 1: Foundation

**Projects:** 5 apps
**Average Project Value:** $75,000
**Project Revenue:** $375,000

**Maintenance Revenue:**
- End of Year 1: 5 clients on maintenance
- Average maintenance: $3,000/month
- Monthly recurring: $15,000
- Annual recurring (Year 2): $180,000

**Additional Services:** $25,000
- 2 app audits @ $10K = $20K
- Consultation hours = $5K

**Total Year 1 Revenue:** $400,000

### Year 2: Steady Growth

**New Projects:** 5 apps × $75K = $375,000
**Maintenance Revenue:** $180,000 (from Year 1 clients)
**Additional Services:** $35,000

**Total Year 2 Revenue:** $590,000

**End of Year 2 Maintenance:**
- 10 clients × $3,000/month = $30,000/month
- Annual recurring (Year 3): $360,000

### Year 3: Maturity

**New Projects:** 5 apps × $75K = $375,000
**Maintenance Revenue:** $360,000
**Additional Services:** $50,000

**Total Year 3 Revenue:** $785,000

### 3-Year Summary (Conservative)

| Category | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 | Total |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|-------|
| New Projects | $375K | $375K | $375K | $1.125M |

## Cash Flow Considerations

### Payment Terms Impact

**Standard Terms:**
- 30% upfront
- 30% at design approval (Week 4-6)
- 25% at beta (Week 10-14)
- 15% at launch (Week 14-18)

**Cash Flow Example ($100K project, 16 weeks):**

| Week | Milestone | Payment | Cumulative |
|------|-----------|---------|------------|
| 0 | Contract signed | $30,000 | $30,000 |
| 6 | Design approval | $30,000 | $60,000 |
| 12 | Beta delivery | $25,000 | $85,000 |
| 16 | Launch | $15,000 | $100,000 |

**Weekly Burn (assuming $15K/week team cost):**
- Weeks 1-6: -$60K + $30K = -$30K
- Weeks 7-12: -$90K + $30K = -$60K (cumulative: -$90K)
- Weeks 13-16: -$60K + $40K = -$20K (cumulative: -$110K)
- Post-launch: Profit realized

**Working Capital Needed:** ~$100K per concurrent project

### Recommended Cash Reserves

| Scenario | Monthly Burn | Reserve (3 months) |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Conservative | $30K | $90K |
| Target | $140K | $420K |
| Aggressive | $350K | $1.05M |

## Risk Factors

### Downside Scenarios

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Economic downturn | Medium | High | Diversify industries, focus on essential apps |
| Key client loss | Medium | Medium | Maintain pipeline, diversify client base |
| Talent shortage | High | High | Competitive comp, culture, remote hiring |
| Project failures | Low | High | Rigorous PM, early warning systems |
| Price compression | Medium | Medium | Differentiate on quality, not price |
| Technology shifts | Low | Medium | Continuous learning, tech radar |

### Contingency Planning

**If revenue is 50% of target:**
- Reduce contractor spend immediately
- Freeze hiring
- Extend runway to 6+ months
- Focus on maintenance revenue
- Founder takes reduced salary

**If revenue is 25% of target:**
- Pivot to smaller projects
- Increase sales efforts
- Consider agency partnerships
- Reduce team size
- Reassess business model
