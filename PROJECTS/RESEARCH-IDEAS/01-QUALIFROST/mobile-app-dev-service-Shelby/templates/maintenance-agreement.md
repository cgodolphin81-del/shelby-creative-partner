## 2. Term and Termination

### 2.1 Initial Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of **12 months** ("Initial Term").

### 2.2 Renewal
After the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days prior to the end of the current term.

### 2.3 Termination for Convenience
Either party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days** written notice after the Initial Term.

### 2.4 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party:
- Materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 14 days of notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

### 2.5 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Client shall pay all outstanding fees through the termination date
- Provider shall deliver all work product completed through termination date
- Provider shall assist with transition to new provider (up to 10 hours at standard rates)

## 4. Service Levels

### 4.1 Response Times

Provider will respond to support requests within the following timeframes:

| Severity | Basic | Standard | Premium |
|----------|-------|----------|---------|
| Critical | 24 hours | 4 hours | 2 hours |
| High | 48 hours | 24 hours | 8 hours |
| Medium | 5 days | 3 days | 24 hours |
| Low | Next sprint | Next sprint | 3 days |

### 4.2 Severity Definitions

**Critical:** Application is unusable, data loss, or security breach
**High:** Major feature broken, significant functionality impaired
**Medium:** Feature impaired but workaround exists
**Low:** Cosmetic issues, minor bugs, enhancement requests

### 4.3 Resolution Targets

Provider will use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve issues within:
- Critical: 24 hours
- High: 3 days
- Medium: 2 weeks
- Low: Next scheduled release
