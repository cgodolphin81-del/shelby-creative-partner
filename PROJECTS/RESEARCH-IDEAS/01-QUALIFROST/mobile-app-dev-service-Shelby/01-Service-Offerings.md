## Engagement Models

### Fixed Price
- Defined scope
- Fixed timeline
- Fixed cost
- Best for: Well-defined projects

### Time & Materials
- Flexible scope
- Weekly billing
- Hourly rates: $100-200/hr
- Best for: Evolving requirements

### Dedicated Team
- Monthly retainer
- Full-time resources
- Scalable team size
- Best for: Long-term partnerships

### Equity Deals
- Reduced cash component
- Equity stake (2-10%)
- Vesting schedule
- Best for: High-potential startups
