## 📝 Case Study Writing Tips

### Before You Write:

1. **Get Client Permission**
   - Written approval to share results
   - Clarify what can be public vs. private
   - Decide if company name can be used

2. **Gather Data**
   - Export LinkedIn analytics
   - Pull CRM data (leads, deals)
   - Screenshot key metrics
   - Get exact revenue numbers (if possible)

3. **Interview the Client**
   - 30-minute call
   - Record (with permission)
   - Ask about challenges, experience, results
   - Get testimonial on the call

### Interview Questions:

1. "What was your situation before working with us?"
2. "What were you skeptical about?"
3. "What surprised you about working with us?"
4. "What results have you seen?"
5. "How has this impacted your business?"
6. "What would you tell someone considering this?"
7. "Anything else you'd like to share?"

### Writing Best Practices:

- **Be specific:** Numbers beat adjectives
- **Be honest:** Acknowledge challenges
- **Be client-focused:** This is their story, not yours
- **Be scannable:** Use headers, bullets, tables
- **Be visual:** Include screenshots, graphs
- **Be credible:** Include testimonial with photo/title
