## 📞 Call Scripts

### Discovery Call Opening (First 5 Minutes)
```
"Hey [Name], thanks so much for making the time. I know you're busy.

Before we dive in, let me just confirm—does 30 minutes still work, or do you need to hop off at a hard stop?

[Wait for response]

Perfect. Here's what I'm thinking for our time:

First, I'd love to understand what's going on with your LinkedIn right now—what's working, what's not, what your goals are.

Then, if it makes sense, I can share how we've helped similar companies and see if there's a fit.

And if there's not, no worries at all—I'm happy to point you in the right direction.

Sound good?

[Wait for confirmation]

Awesome. Let me start by asking..."
```

---

### Discovery Questions Script
```
CURRENT SITUATION:
"Walk me through your current LinkedIn strategy. What's happening today?"

"Who's managing it? Is it you, someone on your team, an agency?"

"How much time would you say you/they spend on it weekly?"

"What's working well? What's frustrating?"

PAIN POINTS:
"If nothing changes, where do you think your LinkedIn will be in 6 months?"

"What's the biggest obstacle preventing you from getting the results you want?"

"Have you worked with agencies or contractors before? How did that go?"

GOALS:
"Ideal scenario—if we're sitting here 90 days from now, what would need to have happened for you to feel like this was a home run?"

"What would that mean for your business? How would it impact revenue, pipeline, hiring, etc.?"

"Why is this important now vs. 6 months from now?"

BUDGET/AUTHORITY:
"Have you set aside budget for this? What range are you thinking?"

"Who else is involved in making this decision?"

"What does your decision process look like?"
```

---

### Handling Common Objections

**"It's too expensive."**
```
"I understand budget is a consideration. Can I ask—compared to what?

If you're comparing this to hiring in-house, a full-time content marketer is $60K-100K/year plus benefits, and they don't have our expertise.

If you're comparing to the revenue potential—one client at $10K ACV pays for 2-3 months of our service. Most clients see 5-10x ROI.

What's the cost of NOT solving this problem?"
```

---

**"We need to think about it."**
```
"Of course. This is a big decision.

Can I ask—what specifically do you need to think through?

Is it:
- The investment?
- Whether we're the right fit?
- Timing?
- Something else?

[Listen to their answer]

If it's [their concern], here's what I'd suggest...

[Address specific concern]

Would it help if we [specific solution]?"
```

---

**"We've had bad experiences with agencies."**
```
"I'm sorry to hear that. That's unfortunately common in our industry.

Can I ask what went wrong?

[Listen—this is gold.]

I totally understand why you'd be hesitant. Here's how we're different:

[Address their specific pain point]

Plus:
- We work month-to-month after 3 months (we have to earn your business)
- You work directly with me/founder (not passed to junior staff)
- Transparent reporting (you see exactly what we're doing)
- We only work with 10-15 clients (we're not a factory)

Would it help to talk to a current client? Happy to make an intro."
```

## ✅ Outreach Best Practices

### Do's:
✓ Personalize every message (reference their content/company)
✓ Keep it short (under 150 words for DMs)
✓ Lead with value, not your pitch
✓ Ask thoughtful questions
✓ Follow up 3-5 times (most don't)
✓ Track everything (CRM or spreadsheet)
✓ Test and iterate (A/B test subject lines, hooks)

### Don'ts:
✗ Generic copy-paste messages
✗ Long paragraphs (hard to read on mobile)
✗ Pitch in the first message
✗ Give up after 1 follow-up
✗ Be pushy or aggressive
✗ Forget to track responses
✗ Send at bad times (weekends, late nights)
