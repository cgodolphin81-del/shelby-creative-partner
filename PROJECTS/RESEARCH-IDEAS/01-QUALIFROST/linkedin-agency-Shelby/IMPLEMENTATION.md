## 🚨 Common Roadblocks & Solutions

### Roadblock 1: "No One Is Responding to Outreach"

**Diagnosis:**
- Generic messages
- Wrong targeting
- Weak profile
- Not enough volume

**Solution:**
- Personalize every message (reference their content)
- Refine your ICP (who really needs this?)
- Optimize your profile (social proof)
- Increase volume (100+ touches/day)

---

### Roadblock 2: "I'm Getting Calls But Not Closing"

**Diagnosis:**
- Wrong prospects (no budget/need)
- Weak value proposition
- Poor discovery skills
- Pricing objections not handled

**Solution:**
- Qualify better before the call (budget, authority, need, timeline)
- Strengthen your positioning (specific results)
- Practice discovery calls (record yourself)
- Prepare objection responses (see `operations/client-acquisition.md`)

---

### Roadblock 3: "I Closed a Client But Don't Know What to Do"

**Diagnosis:**
- Sold before you were ready
- No delivery system

**Solution:**
- Follow `templates/onboarding-checklist.md`
- Use `operations/ghostwriting-framework.md`
- Start with what you know (basic posting + engagement)
- Be honest: "This is my first client, I'm going to over-deliver"
- Learn fast, iterate

---

### Roadblock 4: "I'm Overwhelmed with Delivery"

**Diagnosis:**
- Too many clients
- No systems
- Not delegating

**Solution:**
- Pause outreach (temporarily)
- Document your processes
- Hire first contractor (writer)
- Raise prices (reduce demand)
- Fire difficult clients (if needed)

---

### Roadblock 5: "Results Aren't What I Promised"

**Diagnosis:**
- Overpromised
- Underdelivered
- Wrong expectations
- Algorithm changes

**Solution:**
- Set realistic expectations upfront (3-6 months)
- Communicate proactively (weekly updates)
- Show leading indicators (engagement, not just followers)
- Adjust strategy based on data
- Be honest if something's not working
