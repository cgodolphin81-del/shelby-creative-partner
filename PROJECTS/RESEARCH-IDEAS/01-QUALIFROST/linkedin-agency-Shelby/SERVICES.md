## 💳 Pricing Principles

1. **Value-based, not hourly**: Charge for outcomes
2. **Retainer model**: Predictable revenue, client commitment
3. **Tiered offerings**: Entry point + upsell paths
4. **Minimum 3-month contracts**: Time to show results
5. **50% upfront for projects**: Cash flow protection
6. **Annual discount (15%)**: Incentivize commitment
