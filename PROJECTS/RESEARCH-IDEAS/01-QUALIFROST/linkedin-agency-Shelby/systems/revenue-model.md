**Year 1:**
- Starter: $3,000

- Starter: $3,500 (+17%)

- Starter: $4,000 (+14%)

- Never discount (offer payment terms instead)

## 🎯 Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

### CAC Calculation:

**Monthly Sales & Marketing Costs:**
- Your time (sales): 20 hrs/week @ $100/hr = $8,000
- Outreach tools: $500
- Content creation (your time): $4,000
- Events/networking: $2,000
- Total: $14,500/mo

**New Clients/Month:** 3-5

**CAC:** $14,500 / 4 = $3,625 per client

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** $90,000 / $3,625 = 24.8:1

**Benchmark:** 3:1 is good. You want 5:1+. This is exceptional.

---

### CAC by Channel:

| Channel | Cost/Mo | Clients/Mo | CAC |
|---------|---------|------------|-----|
| LinkedIn Outreach | $500 (tools) | 2 | $250 |
| Email Outreach | $500 (tools) | 1 | $500 |
| Referrals | $0 | 1 | $0 |
| Content (Inbound) | $4,000 (your time) | 1 | $4,000 |
| Events | $2,000 | 0.5 | $4,000 |
| Paid Ads | $5,000 | 1 | $5,000 |

**Best ROI:** Referrals and LinkedIn outreach
**Worst ROI:** Paid ads (early stage)

### Cash Reserves:

**Goal:** 3-6 months of operating expenses

**Example:**
- Monthly expenses: $50,000
- Target reserves: $150,000-300,000

**Build Reserves By:**
- Retaining 50% of profit in first year
- Collecting payments upfront (beginning of month)
- Paying contractors Net 15 or Net 30
- Offering annual prepay discount (10-15%)

## 💼 Exit Options

### Valuation Multiples:

| Business Type | Multiple | Example |
|--------------|----------|---------|
| Service-only (you-dependent) | 1-2x | $400K revenue = $400K-800K valuation |
| Service + systems (team-dependent) | 2-3x | $1M revenue = $2M-3M valuation |
| Service + products (scalable) | 3-5x | $2M revenue = $6M-10M valuation |
| Product-heavy (courses, SaaS) | 5-10x | $1M revenue = $5M-10M valuation |

---

### Exit Paths:

**1. Acquisition by Larger Agency**
- Typical buyer: Marketing agency, consulting firm
- Multiple: 2-3x revenue
- Earn-out: 1-2 years (stay on post-acquisition)

**2. Acquisition by Competitor**
- Typical buyer: Similar agency wanting scale
- Multiple: 2-4x revenue
- Synergy: Client overlap, team integration

**3. Management Buyout**
- Buyer: Your team
- Structure: Seller financing over 3-5 years
- Multiple: 2-3x profit

**4. Private Equity**
- Typical: $5M+ revenue, strong systems
- Multiple: 4-6x EBITDA
- You may stay on as CEO

**5. Continue Operating**
- Cash cow business
- No exit needed
- Lifestyle freedom

## 📈 Scaling Levers

### Lever 1: Raise Prices
- 10-20% annual increases
- Fire bottom 10% of clients
- Attract better clients

**Impact:** +20% revenue, same costs

---

### Lever 2: Add Clients
- Increase sales activity
- Build referral system
- Inbound content engine

**Impact:** +$5K-15K/mo per client

---

### Lever 3: Reduce Costs
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Outsource to lower-cost regions
- Negotiate vendor rates

**Impact:** +5-10% margin

---

### Lever 4: Productize
- Create courses from methodology
- Template packs
- Cohort programs

**Impact:** High margin, scalable revenue

---

### Lever 5: Team Efficiency
- Better hiring
- Training & SOPs
- Tools & automation

**Impact:** Serve 20-30% more clients with same team

## ✅ Financial Health Checklist

### Monthly:
- [ ] Review P&L (profit & loss)
- [ ] Check cash flow
- [ ] Review accounts receivable (who hasn't paid)
- [ ] Review accounts payable (who you owe)
- [ ] Calculate MRR and growth rate
- [ ] Review client profitability

### Quarterly:
- [ ] Update financial projections
- [ ] Review pricing (raise if needed)
- [ ] Analyze CAC by channel
- [ ] Review LTV by client segment
- [ ] Plan tax payments
- [ ] Assess cash reserves

### Annually:
- [ ] Full financial audit
- [ ] Tax planning with CPA
- [ ] Set next year's budget
- [ ] Review compensation (yours and team)
- [ ] Assess exit readiness (if applicable)
- [ ] Plan strategic initiatives
