## 🎯 Path 1: Monthly Retainers (Core Business)

### The Retainer Ladder:

**Level 1: Starter Retainer ($3,000/mo)**
- 12 posts/month (3/week)
- Basic engagement (30 min/day)
- Monthly strategy call
- Monthly report
- **Goal:** Get results, build case studies

**Level 2: Growth Retainer ($5,500/mo)**
- 20 posts/month (5/week)
- Active engagement (1 hr/day)
- Bi-weekly calls
- Bi-weekly reports
- 1 carousel/month
- **Goal:** Scale results, increase retention

**Level 3: Premium Retainer ($8,000/mo)**
- 22 posts/month (daily)
- Full engagement (2 hr/day)
- Weekly calls
- Weekly reports
- 2 carousels/month
- 1 article/month
- DM management
- **Goal:** Maximum results, referrals

**Level 4: Enterprise Retainer ($12,000-20,000/mo)**
- Multiple profiles (3-5 executives)
- Full team support
- Custom reporting
- Dedicated account manager
- Ads management included
- **Goal:** Anchor clients, stable revenue

---

### Retainer Economics:

**Scenario: 20 Clients at Average $7,500/mo**

```
Annual Revenue: $1,800,000

Cost Structure:
- Writers (10 @ $5K/mo): $50,000

- VA/Support (2 @ $3K/mo): $6,000

Monthly Profit: $52,000

```

---

### Retainer Best Practices:

**1. Minimum 3-Month Commitment**
- Time needed to show results
- Reduces churn
- Improves cash flow predictability

**2. Auto-Renewal Contracts**
- 3-month initial term
- Auto-renews monthly after
- 30-day cancellation notice
- Reduces friction to continue

**3. Price Increases Built-In**
- 10% increase at 6 months
- 10% increase at 12 months
- Or re-negotiate at those points
- Captures value as you deliver

**4. Clear Scope Boundaries**
- Exactly what's included
- What costs extra
- Revision limits
- Response time SLAs

**5. Results Tracking**
- Monthly reports mandatory
- Tie to business outcomes
- Celebrate wins publicly (with permission)
- Use results for upsells

## 💡 Monetization Principles

### 1. Start with Services
- Cash flow immediate
- Learn what clients need
- Build case studies
- Fund product development

### 2. Productize What Works
- Turn repeatable services into products
- Courses from your methodology
- Templates from your systems
- Cohorts from your training

### 3. Diversify Over Time
- Don't rely on one revenue stream
- Aim for 3+ streams at 20%+ each
- Services fund products
- Products create freedom

### 4. Raise Prices Annually
- 10-20% increases for existing clients
- New pricing for new clients
- Fire bottom 10% (worst clients)
- Attract better clients with higher prices

### 5. Build Recurring Revenue
- Retainers > projects
- Subscriptions > one-time
- Membership > courses
- Predictable = valuable
