## Engagement Models

### Project-Based
Fixed scope, fixed timeline, fixed price.

**Best for:** Implementations, one-time projects  
**Payment:** 30% upfront, 40% mid-project, 30% at completion

### Retainer
Ongoing support and optimization.

**Best for:** Continuous support, evolving needs  
**Payment:** Monthly in advance

### Time & Materials
Flexible scope with hourly or daily rates.

**Best for:** Exploratory work, ongoing consulting  
**Rates:**
- Senior Consultant: $250/hour
- Principal Consultant: $350/hour
- Project Manager: $200/hour
