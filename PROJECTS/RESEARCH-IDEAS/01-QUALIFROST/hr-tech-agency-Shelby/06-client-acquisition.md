# Client Acquisition

## Strategies for Building a Thriving HR Tech & Recruiting Practice

---

## Overview

This document outlines comprehensive client acquisition strategies across different market segments. Our approach combines inbound marketing, outbound sales, partnerships, and referrals.

### Target Market Segments

| Segment | Employee Count | Annual Revenue | Decision Maker | Sales Cycle |
|---------|----------------|----------------|----------------|-------------|
| **Startup** | 20-100 | $2M-$20M | Founder/Head of People | 2-4 weeks |
| **SMB** | 100-500 | $20M-$100M | HR Director/VP | 4-8 weeks |
| **Mid-Market** | 500-2,000 | $100M-$500M | VP HR/CHRO | 8-12 weeks |
| **Enterprise** | 2,000+ | $500M+ | CHRO/CIO/CTO | 12-24 weeks |

## 1. Startup Segment Strategy

### Profile
- **Company Age:** 1-5 years
- **Funding:** Seed to Series B
- **Hiring Velocity:** 10-50 hires/year
- **Tech Stack:** Limited or no HR tech
- **Budget:** $15K-$50K

### Pain Points
- First-time HR tech buyers
- Limited HR expertise internally
- Need to scale hiring quickly
- Budget constraints
- Want simple, fast solutions

### Acquisition Channels

#### 🎯 Venture Capital Partnerships
**Strategy:** Partner with VC firms to serve their portfolio companies.

**Tactics:**
- Pitch to VC platform teams
- Offer portfolio discounts (15-20%)
- Host workshops at VC offices
- Co-create content (hiring guides, templates)
- Attend portfolio CEO summits

**Target VCs:**
- Andreessen Horowitz (a16z)
- Sequoia Capital
- Accel
- Benchmark
- First Round Capital
- Local/regional VCs

**Investment:** $10K-$30K/year (events, content, relationship building)  
**Expected ROI:** 5-10 clients/year

---

#### 🎯 Startup Accelerators & Incubators
**Strategy:** Become the preferred HR tech partner for accelerators.

**Tactics:**
- Sponsor demo days
- Offer free workshops for cohorts
- Provide template packages
- Mentor startups on hiring
- Offer accelerator-exclusive pricing

**Target Programs:**
- Y Combinator
- Techstars
- 500 Global
- Local university accelerators
- Industry-specific accelerators

**Investment:** $5K-$20K/year  
**Expected ROI:** 3-8 clients/year

---

#### 🎯 Content Marketing
**Strategy:** Attract startups through valuable, SEO-optimized content.

**Content Types:**
- "First 50 Hires" playbook
- ATS comparison guides
- Job description templates
- Interview question banks
- Salary benchmark reports
- Hiring process templates

**Distribution:**
- Company blog
- LinkedIn articles
- Medium publications
- Guest posts on startup blogs
- HR tech communities (Slack, Discord)

**Investment:** $5K-$15K/month (content creation + distribution)  
**Expected ROI:** 10-20 inbound leads/month

---

#### 🎯 Product Hunt & Launch Platforms
**Strategy:** Launch on platforms where startups discover tools.

**Tactics:**
- Product Hunt launch
- BetaList feature
- Hacker News Show HN
- LinkedIn launch post
- Twitter/X launch thread

**Investment:** $2K-$5K (preparation + promotion)  
**Expected ROI:** 500-2,000 visitors, 20-50 leads

---

### Messaging for Startups

**Value Proposition:**
> "Get enterprise-grade hiring infrastructure without the enterprise complexity. We help startups hire their first 50 employees with the right tech, processes, and support—so you can focus on building your product."

**Key Messages:**

## 2. SMB Segment Strategy

### Profile
- **Company Age:** 5-15 years
- **Revenue:** $20M-$100M
- **Hiring Velocity:** 20-100 hires/year
- **Tech Stack:** Basic HRIS, spreadsheets
- **Budget:** $30K-$100K

### Pain Points
- Outgrowing manual processes
- Inefficient hiring slowing growth
- Compliance concerns
- Need for better reporting
- Competition for talent

### Acquisition Channels

#### 🎯 LinkedIn Sales Navigator
**Strategy:** Targeted outbound to HR leaders.

**Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
- Title: HR Director, VP People, Head of Talent
- Company Size: 100-500 employees
- Industries: Tech, Healthcare, Professional Services
- Geography: US, Canada, UK

**Outbound Cadence:**
```
Day 1:   Connection request (personalized)
Day 3:   Thank you + value add (article/resource)
Day 7:   Problem-focused message
Day 14:  Case study or social proof
Day 21:  Breakup message
Day 30:  Re-engagement (new insight)
```

**Messaging Framework:**
```
Subject: Quick question about your hiring process

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] has been growing [specific observation - 
funding, hiring, expansion]. 

Many HR leaders at similar-stage companies are struggling with 
[pain point: inefficient ATS, compliance, scaling hiring].

We recently helped [Similar Company] reduce time-to-hire by 
40% while improving candidate quality.

Would you be open to a 15-minute chat to discuss how we might 
help [Company]?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Investment:** $10K-$20K/month (Sales Navigator + SDR time)  
**Expected ROI:** 50-100 conversations/month, 5-10 opportunities

---

#### 🎯 Industry Events & Conferences
**Strategy:** Attend and sponsor HR/shopping conferences.

**Target Events:**
- HR Technology Conference
- SHRM Annual Conference
- Talent Acquisition Innovators Summit
- Industry-specific events (Healthcare HR, TechHR)
- Regional HR association events

**Tactics:**
- Booth sponsorship
- Speaking opportunities
- Happy hour hosting
- Executive roundtables
- Content distribution

**Investment:** $50K-$150K/year (3-5 events)  
**Expected ROI:** 30-50 qualified leads, 5-10 clients

---

#### 🎯 Partner Referrals
**Strategy:** Build referral partnerships with complementary services.

**Partner Types:**
- HR consultants/fractional CHROs
- Employment lawyers
- Payroll providers (ADP, Paychex partners)
- Benefits brokers
- Business consultants
- Executive coaches

**Referral Agreement:**
- 10-15% referral fee on first-year revenue
- Or reciprocal referrals
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Investment:** $10K-$30K/year (partner events, materials)  
**Expected ROI:** 10-20 referrals/year, 3-7 clients

---

#### 🎯 Case Studies & Social Proof
**Strategy:** Build credibility through documented success.

**Case Study Structure:**
1. Client background
2. Challenge/pain points
3. Solution implemented
4. Results (quantified)
5. Client quote
6. Call to action

**Distribution:**
- Website landing page
- LinkedIn posts
- Sales decks
- Email signatures
- Proposal attachments

**Goal:** 1 new case study per quarter  
**Investment:** $3K-$8K per case study  
**Expected ROI:** Increased conversion rates (20-30%)

---

### Messaging for SMBs

**Value Proposition:**
> "Scale your hiring without the growing pains. We help SMBs implement the right HR technology and recruiting processes to attract top talent, reduce time-to-hire, and build a foundation for sustained growth."

**Key Messages:**
- Proven methodology (200+ implementations)
- ROI-focused (payback in 6-12 months)
- Minimal disruption to operations
- Ongoing support included
- Compliance built-in

## 3. Mid-Market Segment Strategy

### Profile
- **Company Age:** 10-30 years
- **Revenue:** $100M-$500M
- **Hiring Velocity:** 100-500 hires/year
- **Tech Stack:** Legacy systems, multiple point solutions
- **Budget:** $75K-$250K

### Pain Points
- System consolidation needed
- Integration complexity
- Change management challenges
- Data quality issues
- Need for advanced analytics

### Acquisition Channels

#### 🎯 Account-Based Marketing (ABM)
**Strategy:** Targeted campaigns for high-value accounts.

**Target Account List:** 50-100 companies

**ABM Tactics:**
- Personalized direct mail
- Executive briefing invitations
- Custom content (industry reports)
- Targeted LinkedIn ads
- Personalized video messages
- Executive roundtables

**Example Campaign:**
```
Week 1: Personalized direct mail (book + handwritten note)
Week 2: LinkedIn connection to CHRO/VP HR
Week 3: Custom industry insight report
Week 4: Executive briefing invitation
Week 5: Follow-up call
Week 6: Personalized video message
Week 8: Breakup/next quarter touch
```

**Investment:** $50K-$100K/year  
**Expected ROI:** 5-15 opportunities, 2-5 clients

---

#### 🎯 Thought Leadership
**Strategy:** Establish executives as industry experts.

**Tactics:**
- Bylined articles in HR publications
- Podcast guest appearances
- Conference speaking
- Research reports
- Webinars with industry associations

**Target Publications:**
- HR Executive
- HR Dive
- SHRM Online
- Talent Management
- Industry-specific publications

**Investment:** $30K-$60K/year (PR, content, events)  
**Expected ROI:** Brand awareness, 10-20 inbound leads/year

## 7. Sales Process

### Sales Stages

```
1. Lead Generation → 2. Qualification → 3. Discovery → 4. Proposal → 5. Close
```

### Stage Definitions

| Stage | Criteria | Probability |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Lead** | Inbound inquiry or outbound connection | 10% |
| **Qualified** | BANT confirmed (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) | 25% |
| **Discovery** | Requirements gathered, stakeholders identified | 50% |
| **Proposal** | Proposal submitted, negotiation underway | 75% |
| **Closed** | Contract signed, deposit received | 100% |

### Sales Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Leads per Month | 100-200 |
| Qualified Opportunities | 30-50 |
| Proposals Submitted | 15-25 |
| Win Rate | 40-50% |
| Average Deal Size | $50K-$100K |
| Sales Cycle | 6-12 weeks |
