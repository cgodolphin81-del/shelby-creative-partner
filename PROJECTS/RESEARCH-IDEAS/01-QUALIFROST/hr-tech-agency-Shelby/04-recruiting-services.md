## Metrics & Reporting

### Standard Recruiting Metrics

| Metric | Description | Target |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Time to Fill | Days from job open to offer accepted | <45 days |
| Time to Hire | Days from first interview to offer accepted | <21 days |
| Quality of Hire | Performance ratings at 6/12 months | >4.0/5.0 |
| Source Effectiveness | Hires by source channel | Track all |
| Candidate Satisfaction | Candidate NPS/survey scores | >40 NPS |
| Offer Acceptance Rate | % of offers accepted | >85% |
| Diversity Slate | % diverse candidates in pipeline | >40% |
| Cost per Hire | Total recruiting cost / hires | Varies by role |

### Reporting Cadence

- **Weekly:** Pipeline updates, activity metrics
- **Monthly:** Full metrics dashboard, insights
- **Quarterly:** Business review, strategy adjustment
- **Annually:** Year-end summary, planning
