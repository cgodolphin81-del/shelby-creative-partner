## 1. Employment Law Compliance

### Federal Regulations (United States)

#### Title VII of the Civil Rights Act (1964)
**Applies to:** Employers with 15+ employees

**Requirements:**
- Prohibits discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin
- Requires equal opportunity in hiring, promotion, compensation
- Mandates harassment prevention

**Our Implementation:**
- Bias-free job descriptions
- Structured interview processes
- Diverse candidate slates
- Harassment prevention training

---

#### Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
**Applies to:** Employers with 15+ employees

**Requirements:**
- Prohibits discrimination against qualified individuals with disabilities
- Requires reasonable accommodations
- Accessible application processes

**Our Implementation:**
- Accessible career sites (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Accommodation request workflows
- Accessible document formats
- Interview accommodation processes

---

#### Age Discrimination in Employment Act (ADEA)
**Applies to:** Employers with 20+ employees

**Requirements:**
- Prohibits discrimination against workers 40+
- No age preferences in job postings
- Equal benefits for older workers

**Our Implementation:**
- Age-neutral job descriptions
- Diverse age representation in hiring
- Benefits compliance review

---

#### Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
**Applies to:** All employers using background checks

**Requirements:**
- Written consent before background check
- Pre-adverse action notice
- Adverse action notice with copy of report
- Dispute process

**Our Implementation:**
- FCRA-compliant background check workflows
- Automated notice generation
- Consent management
- Dispute tracking

---

#### Immigration Reform and Control Act (IRCA)
**Applies to:** All employers

**Requirements:**
- I-9 verification for all hires
- No discrimination based on citizenship
- Document retention (3 years from hire or 1 year from termination)

**Our Implementation:**
- I-9 workflow automation
- E-Verify integration
- Document retention policies
- Compliance training

---

#### Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA)
**Applies to:** Employers with 50+ employees

**Requirements:**
- 12 weeks unpaid leave for eligible employees
- Job protection
- Health benefit continuation

**Our Implementation:**
- FMLA eligibility tracking
- Leave request workflows
- Return-to-work processes
- Manager training

---

#### Equal Pay Act
**Applies to:** All employers

**Requirements:**
- Equal pay for equal work regardless of gender
- Pay transparency (in many states)
- No retaliation for pay discussions

**Our Implementation:**
- Pay equity analysis
- Salary range transparency
- Compensation review processes
- Negotiation training

#### Adverse Impact Analysis
**Required for:** Federal contractors, recommended for all

**Four-Fifths Rule:**
- Selection rate for any group < 80% of highest group = potential adverse impact
- Requires investigation and remediation

**Our Implementation:**
- Automated adverse impact calculations
- Regular monitoring (quarterly)
- Alert system for potential issues
- Remediation guidance

---

#### Unconscious Bias Training
**Recommended for:** All hiring managers

**Content:**
- Understanding unconscious bias
- Impact on hiring decisions
- Mitigation strategies
- Inclusive interviewing

**Our Implementation:**
- Mandatory training for hiring managers
- Annual refreshers
- Scenario-based learning
- Effectiveness measurement

## 3. Data Privacy Compliance

### General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
**Applies to:** Companies processing EU resident data

#### Key Requirements

| Requirement | Description | Our Implementation |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Lawful Basis** | Legal reason for processing | Consent, legitimate interest documentation |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Use data only for stated purpose | Clear privacy notices |
| **Data Minimization** | Collect only necessary data | Data field audits |
| **Accuracy** | Keep data accurate | Regular updates, correction workflows |
| **Storage Limitation** | Delete when no longer needed | Retention policies, automated deletion |
| **Security** | Protect personal data | Encryption, access controls |
| **Accountability** | Demonstrate compliance | Documentation, audits |

---

#### Individual Rights (GDPR)

| Right | Description | Implementation |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Right to Access** | Obtain copy of personal data | Self-service portal, 30-day response |
| **Right to Rectification** | Correct inaccurate data | Update workflows |
| **Right to Erasure** | Delete data ("right to be forgotten") | Deletion workflows |
| **Right to Restrict** | Limit processing | Processing flags |
| **Right to Portability** | Receive data in portable format | Export functionality |
| **Right to Object** | Object to processing | Objection handling |
| **Rights re: Automated Decision-Making** | Human review of algorithmic decisions | Human-in-the-loop |

---

#### GDPR Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with vendors
- [ ] Privacy notices updated
- [ ] Consent management implemented
- [ ] Data mapping completed
- [ ] Privacy Impact Assessments (PIAs) conducted
- [ ] Data Protection Officer (DPO) appointed (if required)
- [ ] Breach notification procedures in place
- [ ] Individual rights workflows established
- [ ] Staff training completed
- [ ] Documentation maintained

---

### California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) / CPRA
**Applies to:** Companies meeting threshold (revenue, data volume)

#### Key Requirements

| Requirement | Description | Our Implementation |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Right to Know** | Disclose data collected, used, shared | Privacy notice, disclosure portal |
| **Right to Delete** | Delete personal information | Deletion workflows |
| **Right to Opt-Out** | Stop sale/sharing of data | Opt-out mechanisms |
| **Right to Correct** | Correct inaccurate data | Update workflows |
| **Right to Limit** | Limit use of sensitive data | Usage restrictions |
| **Non-Discrimination** | No penalty for exercising rights | Policy enforcement |

---

#### CCPA Compliance Checklist

- [ ] Privacy notice updated with CCPA disclosures
- [ ] "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link
- [ ] Opt-out mechanisms implemented
- [ ] Service provider agreements updated
- [ ] Data inventory completed
- [ ] Request handling procedures established
- [ ] Staff training completed
- [ ] Annual compliance review scheduled

---

### Breach Response

#### Incident Response Plan

**Phase 1: Detection (0-2 hours)**
- Identify and confirm breach
- Contain incident
- Assemble response team

**Phase 2: Assessment (2-24 hours)**
- Determine scope and impact
- Identify affected individuals
- Legal/regulatory analysis

**Phase 3: Notification (24-72 hours)**
- Regulatory notifications (as required)
- Individual notifications (as required)
- Public disclosure (if required)

**Phase 4: Remediation (1-4 weeks)**
- Fix vulnerabilities
- Restore systems
- Implement additional controls

**Phase 5: Review (4-8 weeks)**
- Post-incident analysis
- Lessons learned
- Plan updates
