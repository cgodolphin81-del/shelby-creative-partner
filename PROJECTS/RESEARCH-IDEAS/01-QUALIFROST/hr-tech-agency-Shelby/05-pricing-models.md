# Pricing Models

## Comprehensive Pricing Structure for HR Tech & Recruiting Services

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Our pricing reflects the value we deliver, not just the time we spend. We offer flexible engagement models to match different client needs and budgets.

### Guiding Principles

1. **Transparent:** Clear pricing with no hidden fees
2. **Flexible:** Multiple engagement models
3. **Value-Based:** Priced on outcomes, not just hours
4. **Scalable:** Grow with your business
5. **Fair:** Competitive rates for both parties

## 9. Payment Terms & Policies

### Standard Payment Terms

| Service Type | Terms |
|--------------|-------|
| Projects under $25K | 50% upfront, 50% at completion |
| Projects $25K-$100K | 30% upfront, 40% mid-point, 30% at completion |
| Projects over $100K | 25% upfront, 25% at design, 25% at UAT, 25% at go-live |
| Retainers | Monthly in advance |
| Hourly Consulting | Net 15, invoiced bi-weekly |
| Recruiting (Per-Hire) | 50% at offer, 50% at start |

### Payment Methods

- ACH/Bank Transfer (preferred)
- Wire Transfer
- Check
- Credit Card (3% processing fee)

### Late Payment Policy

- Invoices due net 15 unless otherwise specified
- 1.5% monthly interest on overdue balances
- Work pause after 30 days overdue
- Collection after 60 days overdue

### Cancellation Policy

**Projects:**
- Cancel before kickoff: Full refund minus $2,500 admin fee
- Cancel during discovery: 50% refund
- Cancel after discovery: No refund, pay for work completed

**Retainers:**
- 30-day notice required
- No refund for unused hours
- Pro-rated refund for annual prepay

**Recruiting:**
- No refund after candidate introduction
- Guarantee replacement or 50% refund within guarantee period
