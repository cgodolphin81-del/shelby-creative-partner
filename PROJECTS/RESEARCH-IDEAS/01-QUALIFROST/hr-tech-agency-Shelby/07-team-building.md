# Team Building

## Organizational Structure, Roles, and Hiring Guide

---

## Overview

This document outlines the team structure, roles, responsibilities, and hiring guidelines for building a successful HR tech and recruiting services agency.

### Team Philosophy

- **T-shaped professionals:** Deep expertise + broad collaboration
- **Client-first mindset:** Success = client success
- **Continuous learning:** Stay ahead of HR tech trends
- **Remote-friendly:** Talent over location
- **Diversity & inclusion:** Build a team that reflects our values

## Role Definitions

### Leadership Roles

#### Chief Executive Officer (CEO) / Founder
**Reports to:** Board  
**Direct Reports:** C-Suite

**Responsibilities:**
- Company vision and strategy
- Fundraising and investor relations
- Key client relationships
- Executive team building
- Company culture
- P&L ownership

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years in HR tech, consulting, or services
- Proven track record of scaling businesses
- Strong network in HR/tech ecosystem
- Excellent communication and leadership skills

**Compensation:** $200K-$300K base + significant equity

---

#### Chief Operating Officer (COO)
**Reports to:** CEO  
**Direct Reports:** Delivery Team, Operations, Customer Success

**Responsibilities:**

- Resource planning and utilization
- Customer success and retention
- Process optimization
- Vendor management

**Qualifications:**
- 8+ years in consulting/services delivery

- Project/program management expertise
- Team leadership experience

**Compensation:** $150K-$225K base + 0.5-2% equity

---

## Hiring Guidelines

### Hiring Principles

1. **Hire for potential, not just experience**
2. **Culture add, not culture fit**
3. **Diverse slates for every role**
4. **Structured interviews to reduce bias**
5. **Reference checks are mandatory**

### Interview Process

**For Individual Contributors:**
1. Phone screen (30 min) - Recruiter
2. Hiring manager interview (45 min)
3. Skills assessment / case study
4. Team interview (60 min)
5. Final interview with leadership (30 min)

**For Leadership Roles:**
1. Phone screen (30 min) - Recruiter or CEO
2. Case study presentation (60 min)
3. Panel interview with peers (90 min)
4. Executive interview (60 min)
5. Reference checks (3-5 references)

### Compensation Philosophy

- **Market competitive:** 50th-75th percentile
- **Equity for early employees:** Meaningful ownership
- **Performance-based bonuses:** Clear metrics
- **Transparent ranges:** Posted in job descriptions
- **Annual reviews:** Market adjustments + merit

### Benefits Package

**Standard Benefits:**
- Health, dental, vision insurance
- 401(k) with company match
- Unlimited PTO (with minimum 3 weeks encouraged)
- Paid holidays
- Parental leave (12 weeks paid)
- Professional development budget ($2K-$5K/year)
- Home office stipend
- Mental health support

**Perks:**
- Quarterly team offsites
- Annual company retreat
- Wellness stipend
- Commuter benefits
- Employee referral bonuses ($2K-$5K)

## Team Development

### Onboarding Program

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Company overview and culture
- Team introductions
- Systems and tools setup
- Role-specific training

**Week 2-4: Immersion**
- Shadow senior team members
- First client/project assignment
- Weekly check-ins with manager
- 30-day review

**Month 2-3: Independence**
- Own client/project responsibilities
- Continue skill development
- 60-day and 90-day reviews
- Development plan creation

### Professional Development

**Training Programs:**
- Platform certifications (company-paid)
- Leadership development (for managers)
- Sales training (for revenue team)
- Industry conferences (2/year)
- Internal knowledge sharing (monthly)

**Career Paths:**
- Individual Contributor track
- Management track
- Both tracks have equal prestige and compensation potential

### Performance Management

**Review Cycle:**
- Quarterly check-ins (informal)
- Annual formal review
- Continuous feedback encouraged

**Evaluation Criteria:**
- Results delivery
- Client satisfaction
- Team contribution
- Skill development
- Company values

## Culture & Values

### Core Values

1. **Client Success First** - We win when our clients win
2. **Own It** - Take responsibility, drive results
3. **Learn & Share** - Continuous improvement, knowledge sharing
4. **One Team** - Collaborate across functions
5. **Do the Right Thing** - Integrity in all actions

### Culture Practices

- **All-hands meetings:** Weekly
- **Transparent metrics:** Shared dashboards
- **Recognition:** Peer-to-peer kudos program
- **Feedback:** Regular surveys, open door policy
- **Fun:** Team events, celebrations, traditions

### Remote Work Guidelines

- **Flexible hours:** Core hours 10am-3pm local time
- **Meeting hygiene:** Agendas, recordings, action items
- **Communication:** Slack for quick, email for formal, docs for decisions
- **In-person:** Quarterly team gatherings, annual retreat
