## 2. Support Services Detail

### Technical Support

#### Issue Resolution

| Severity | Definition | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|----------|------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Critical** | System down, business critical function unavailable | 1 hour | 4 hours |
| **High** | Major functionality impaired, workaround available | 4 hours | 24 hours |
| **Medium** | Minor functionality issue, minimal business impact | 24 hours | 3-5 days |
| **Low** | Questions, enhancements, cosmetic issues | 48 hours | 1-2 weeks |

#### Common Support Requests

| Request Type | Examples | Typical Resolution |
|--------------|----------|-------------------|
| **User Management** | Add/remove users, permission changes | Same day |

| **Training** | How-to questions, refresher training | 1-3 days |

### Configuration Changes

#### Change Request Process

```
1. Request Submission → 2. Impact Assessment → 3. Approval → 4. Implementation → 5. Testing → 6. Deployment
```

**Request Submission:**
- Support ticket or email
- Clear description of change
- Business justification
- Priority level

**Impact Assessment:**
- Technical complexity
- Affected users/processes
- Testing requirements
- Timeline estimate

**Approval:**
- Client approval required for significant changes
- Change advisory board (CAB) for major changes
- Emergency changes fast-tracked

**Implementation:**
- Sandbox testing first
- Client UAT (if applicable)
- Scheduled deployment
- Documentation update

Level 1: Support Specialist (0-24 hours)

Level 3: Delivery Manager (48-72 hours)

## 12. Support Best Practices

### For Clients

**Maximize Your Support Investment:**
1. **Use the portal** - Submit tickets through the proper channels
2. **Be specific** - Provide detailed descriptions and screenshots
3. **Prioritize** - Use appropriate priority levels
4. **Train your team** - Ensure users know how to get help
5. **Attend QBRs** - Participate in quarterly reviews
6. **Provide feedback** - Help us improve
7. **Plan changes** - Give advance notice for major initiatives

### For Our Team

**Deliver Excellent Support:**
1. **Respond quickly** - Meet or beat SLAs
2. **Communicate clearly** - No jargon, regular updates
3. **Own the issue** - Follow through to resolution
4. **Document everything** - Create knowledge base articles
5. **Be proactive** - Identify issues before clients do
6. **Continuous learning** - Stay current on platforms
7. **Escalate appropriately** - Don't let issues stall
