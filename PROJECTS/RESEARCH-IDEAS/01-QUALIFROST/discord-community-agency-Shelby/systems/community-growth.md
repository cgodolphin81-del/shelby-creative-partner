## 🚪 Onboarding System

### The First 24 Hours (Critical Window)

**Goal:** Transform lurkers into participants

#### Hour 0: Welcome Sequence

```
Member Joins
    ↓
1. Welcome DM (automated)
   - Welcome message
   - Server rules
   - Quick start guide
   - Key channels to visit

2. Role Assignment
   - Base "Member" role
   - Interest-based roles (optional)
   - Verification complete

3. Public Welcome
   - #introductions post
   - @mention by welcome bot
   - Community greeted
```

**Template: Welcome DM**
```
Hey {name}! 👋

Welcome to {community}! We're stoked to have you here.

📋 Quick Start:
1. Check out #server-guide for the lay of the land
2. Introduce yourself in #introductions
3. Grab some roles in #role-selection
4. Jump into the conversation!

📚 Rules: {link}
❓ Questions? Open a ticket or ask in #questions-help

See you around!
- The {community} Team
```

---

#### Hour 1-4: First Engagement

**Automated Triggers:**
- If no introduction after 1 hour → Gentle nudge DM
- If introduction posted → CM welcomes personally
- Suggest 2-3 relevant channels based on interests

**CM Action Items:**
- Personally welcome new members (first 100/day)
- Answer any questions within 15 minutes
- Make introductions to similar members

---

#### Hour 4-24: Activation

**Activation Goals:**
- ✅ Send first message
- ✅ React to 3+ messages
- ✅ Visit 5+ channels
- ✅ Meet 2+ community members
- ✅ Attend 1 event or voice chat

**Activation Tactics:**
- "New Member" scavenger hunt
- First message celebration (auto-role: "First Steps")
- Pair with community buddy (optional)
- Invite to next scheduled event

---

### 7-Day Onboarding Journey

| Day | Goal | Tactic |
|-----|------|--------|
| 1 | Welcome & orient | DM + public welcome |
| 2 | First contribution | Prompt in #general |
| 3 | Discover value | Share relevant resource |
| 4 | Social connection | Introduce to 1-2 members |
| 5 | Deeper engagement | Invite to event/voice |
| 6 | Role exploration | Suggest interest roles |
| 7 | Check-in | "How's it going?" DM |

**Day 7 Check-in Template:**
```
Hey {name}! 

It's been a week since you joined - hope you're settling in! 🎉

Quick questions:
- Found what you're looking for?
- Any questions I can answer?
- Anything we could improve?

We're here to help make this community awesome for you!

- {CM name}
```

## 🔄 Retention System

### Retention Drivers

**1. Value Delivery**
- Exclusive content/information
- Problem-solving support
- Networking opportunities
- Entertainment/enjoyment

**2. Social Bonds**
- Friendships formed
- Recognition from peers
- Sense of belonging
- Shared identity

**3. Investment**
- Time spent (sunk cost)
- Content contributed
- Relationships built
- Status/roles earned

---

### Retention Tactics by Segment

#### New Members (0-30 days)
**Focus:** Activation & early wins

- Daily check-ins (automated + personal)
- Quick wins (easy contributions)
- Buddy system pairing
- New member events (weekly)
- Progress celebrations

#### Active Members (30-90 days)
**Focus:** Deepen engagement

- Leadership opportunities
- Special projects
- Recognition programs
- Exclusive access
- Feedback requests

#### Veterans (90+ days)
**Focus:** Prevent burnout & churn

- Ambassador roles
- Mentorship opportunities
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Co-creation opportunities
- Appreciation events

#### At-Risk Members (declining activity)
**Focus:** Re-engagement

- Personal check-in DM
- "We miss you" message
- Highlight what they're missing
- Special invitation back
- Survey: "What changed?"

---

### Churn Prevention

**Warning Signs:**
- No messages for 7+ days (was daily)
- Stopped attending events
- Negative sentiment in messages
- Complaints about community
- Competitor community joined

**Intervention Sequence:**
```
Day 7 inactive → Automated "miss you" DM
Day 14 inactive → Personal CM check-in
Day 21 inactive → "What can we improve?" survey
Day 30 inactive → Archive (re-engagement campaign later)
```

**Re-engagement Campaign:**
```
Subject: We've got something new for you! 👀

Hey {name},

It's been a minute! A lot has changed since you've been around:

✨ New: {feature1}
🎉 Launched: {program2}
🚀 Growing: {metric3}

We'd love to have you back. Here's what's happening this week:
- {event1}
- {event2}

No pressure - just wanted to say hi and keep the door open!

- The Team
```

## 🎮 Engagement Loops

### Daily Loops

#### 1. Question of the Day
**Time:** 9 AM local
**Channel:** #general or #qotd
**Format:**
```
🤔 QUESTION OF THE DAY

[Engaging question related to community topic]

Drop your answers below! 👇
```
**Examples:**
- "What's one tool you can't live without?"
- "Biggest win this week?"
- "Unpopular opinion time: Go!"

**Engagement Rate:** 10-20% of DAU

---

#### 2. Daily Wins
**Time:** 5 PM local
**Channel:** #wins-or #general
**Format:**
```
🎉 DAILY WINS THREAD

What's one win you had today? Big or small, celebrate it!

I'll start: [CM shares win]
```

---

#### 3. Content Shares
**Time:** 2 PM local
**Channel:** #showcase or #resources
**Format:**
```
💡 RESOURCE SHARE

[Curated article/tool/resource]

Why it matters: [2-3 sentences]

Check it out: [link]
```

---

### Event Types & Frequency

| Event Type | Frequency | Duration | Avg Attendance |
|------------|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Community Call | Weekly | 45-60 min | 20-50 |
| AMA | Monthly | 60 min | 50-200 |
| Workshop | Bi-weekly | 60-90 min | 30-100 |

| Tournament | Monthly | 2-4 hrs | 30-100 |

| Town Hall | Quarterly | 60 min | 50-200 |

---

### Event Production Checklist

#### 2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Confirm topic/speaker
- [ ] Set date/time (consider timezones)
- [ ] Create event page
- [ ] Design promotional graphics
- [ ] Draft announcement copy
- [ ] Schedule social promotion

#### 1 Week Before
- [ ] First announcement post
- [ ] Email to members (if applicable)
- [ ] Social media posts
- [ ] Speaker prep call (if guest)
- [ ] Test tech (voice, screen share, bots)

#### 2 Days Before
- [ ] Reminder post
- [ ] Final speaker confirmation
- [ ] Prepare moderation plan
- [ ] Set up recording
- [ ] Prepare discussion questions

#### Day Of
- [ ] Morning reminder
- [ ] Tech check (1 hour before)
- [ ] Open voice channel early
- [ ] Welcome attendees
- [ ] Run event
- [ ] Record session

#### Post-Event (24-48 hrs)
- [ ] Thank you post
- [ ] Share recording/transcript
- [ ] Collect feedback
- [ ] Analyze metrics
- [ ] Follow up with attendees
- [ ] Share highlights on social

## 🎯 Growth Strategies by Stage

### Stage 1: Launch (0-500 members)
**Focus:** Foundation & early adopters

**Tactics:**
- Personal invitations (founder network)
- Cross-post from existing audiences
- Launch event with incentives
- Founding member perks
- Content marketing (SEO, social)

**Goals:**
- 100 members in first 30 days
- 50%+ activation rate
- Core group of 10-20 active members

---

### Stage 2: Growth (500-5,000 members)
**Focus:** Systems & scalability

**Tactics:**
- Referral program launch
- Partnership collaborations
- Paid acquisition testing
- Content engine (YouTube, podcast, blog)
- Community-led initiatives

**Goals:**
- 20%+ monthly growth
- 30%+ D30 retention
- Self-sustaining engagement

---

### Stage 3: Scale (5,000-50,000 members)
**Focus:** Optimization & monetization

**Tactics:**
- Premium tier launch
- Ambassador program
- User-generated content
- Strategic partnerships
- Event series (virtual/IRL)

**Goals:**
- Sustainable growth (10-15%/mo)
- Revenue from community
- Industry recognition

---

### Stage 4: Maturity (50,000+ members)
**Focus:** Culture & innovation

**Tactics:**
- Sub-communities (chapters)
- Annual conference
- Certification programs
- Research & insights
- Ecosystem development

**Goals:**
- Maintain culture at scale
- Industry leadership
- Sustainable business model

## 🚀 Growth Hacks

### Viral Loops

**1. Referral Rewards**
```
Member refers friend → Both get exclusive role
3 referrals → Premium feature unlock
5 referrals → 1:1 with founder
10 referrals → VIP status
```

**2. Content Sharing**
```
Member shares achievement → Auto-generated graphic
Graphic includes community link → New signups
New signup mentions referrer → Recognition
```

**3. Event FOMO**
```
Exclusive event announced → Limited spots
Members invite friends → Priority access
Event highlights shared → Next event hype
```

---

### Engagement Hacks

**1. Streaks**
- Daily login streaks (via bot)
- Milestone rewards (7, 30, 100 days)
- Leaderboard for top streaks

**2. Scavenger Hunts**
- Hide Easter eggs in channels
- First to find wins prize
- Encourages channel exploration

**3. Reaction Roles**
- Easy way to customize experience
- Creates investment
- Reveals interests for targeting

**4. Leveling System**
- XP for messages, voice time, events
- Visible progression
- Role unlocks at milestones

---

### Retention Hacks

**1. Milestone Celebrations**
- 1 month anniversary DM
- 100th message celebration
- Level up announcements

**2. Exclusive Access**
- Early access to features
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Direct founder access (premium)

**3. Social Bonds**
- Buddy system for new members
- Mastermind groups
- Accountability partners

**4. Sunk Cost**
- Custom profiles
- Contribution history
- Earned badges/roles
