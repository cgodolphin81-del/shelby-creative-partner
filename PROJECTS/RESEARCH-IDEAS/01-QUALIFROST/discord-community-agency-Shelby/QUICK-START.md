## 💡 Quick Reference

### Pricing Tiers
| Tier | Monthly | Setup | Best For |
|------|---------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $2K-3.5K | $1.5K | Small creators, early-stage |
| Growth | $4K-7K | $3K | Established brands, mid-size |
| Enterprise | $8K-15K | $5K+ | Large communities, Web3, gaming |

### Service Highlights
- **Server Setup:** Custom architecture, roles, permissions
- **Bots:** MEE6, Carl-bot, Ticket Tool, analytics
- **Moderation:** 12-24/7 coverage depending on tier
- **Engagement:** Daily posts, weekly events, monthly strategy
- **Analytics:** Monthly reports, quarterly business reviews

### Target Clients
1. **SaaS Companies:** $4K-15K/mo (support, feedback, retention)
2. **Content Creators:** $2K-8K/mo (fan engagement, monetization)
3. **Web3 Projects:** $8K-25K/mo (security, engagement, alpha)
4. **Gaming Studios:** $5K-20K/mo (events, retention, feedback)

## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

1. **Overpromising metrics** - Be realistic about what you can deliver
2. **Underpricing setup** - Always charge setup fees for initial work
3. **Neglecting documentation** - Document everything for scale
4. **Ignoring churn** - Track and address member churn proactively
5. **Skipping contracts** - Always use signed agreements
6. **Working without boundaries** - Set clear scope and hours
7. **Not tracking ROI** - Always tie community work to business outcomes
