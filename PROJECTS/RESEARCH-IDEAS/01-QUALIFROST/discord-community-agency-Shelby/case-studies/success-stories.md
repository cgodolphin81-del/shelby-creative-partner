## Case Study 1: SaaS Platform

### Client Overview

**Industry:** B2B SaaS (Project Management)  
**Founded:** 2022  
**Employees:** 25  
**ARR:** $3.5M  
**Community Size:** 1,200 members (at start)

---

### Starting Situation

**Metrics:**
- Discord members: 1,200
- DAU/MAU ratio: 8%
- Support tickets/month: 450
- NPS score: 32
- Churn rate: 12%/month

**Challenges:**
- Low user engagement in Discord
- High support ticket volume
- Users not adopting new features
- Competitor had much more active community
- No structured feedback loop

---

### Interventions Implemented

#### Month 1: Foundation
- Complete server audit & reorganization
- Implemented MEE6 + Carl-bot automation
- Created role hierarchy (6 roles)
- Set up Ticket Tool for support
- Wrote comprehensive community guidelines
- Launched onboarding sequence

#### Month 2: Engagement
- Started daily QOTD (Question of the Day)
- Launched weekly community calls
- Created #showcase channel for user wins
- Implemented leveling system
- Began user-generated content campaigns

#### Month 3: Growth
- Launched referral program
- Partnered with 5 micro-influencers
- Started monthly workshops
- Implemented feedback collection system
- Created beta tester program

#### Month 4-6: Optimization
- Launched premium tier ($19/mo)
- Created mastermind group for power users
- Started ambassador program
- Integrated with product analytics
- Implemented A/B testing on engagement tactics

---

### Results (6 Months)

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Discord Members | 1,200 | 4,800 | +300% |
| DAU/MAU Ratio | 8% | 22% | +175% |
| Support Tickets | 450/mo | 280/mo | -38% |
| NPS Score | 32 | 68 | +112% |
| Feature Adoption | 15% | 42% | +180% |
| Churn Rate | 12%/mo | 7%/mo | -42% |
| Premium Revenue | $0 | $18,000/mo | +$216K/yr |

---

### Key Wins

**1. Support Cost Reduction**
- Community-sourced answers: 150 tickets/month
- Saved ~$45,000/year in support costs
- Faster resolution time (avg 2 hours vs 24 hours)

**2. Product Feedback**
- 200+ feature requests collected
- 15 features shipped from user feedback
- Beta program: 300 active testers

**3. Revenue Impact**
- Premium tier: 450 members @ $19/mo
- Referral program: 120 new customers
- Estimated revenue impact: $500K+

---

### Client Testimonial

> "The transformation has been incredible. Our Discord went from a ghost town to our most active community channel. We're seeing real business impact - support costs down, retention up, and a thriving ecosystem around our product. The team is exceptional to work with."
>
> **— CEO, SaaS Company**

## Case Study 3: Web3 Project

**Type:** NFT Project + DeFi Protocol  
**Launch:** Month 6 (community managed)  
**Token Holders:** 4,500  
**Community Size:** 8,000 Discord members

---

**Metrics:**
- Discord members: 8,000
- Active members: 400 (5%)
- Daily spam messages: 500+

- Launched weekly community calls
- Started "Alpha Hour" for token holders
- Created partner showcase channel
- Implemented stakeholder AMAs
- Started transparency reports

#### Month 3: Value Creation
- Launched premium alpha channel ($99/mo)
- Created learning hub (tutorials, guides)
- Started beta testing program
- Implemented governance participation
- Launched community treasury proposal system

#### Month 4-6: Growth
- Partnered with 8 projects
- Hosted virtual summit (500+ attendees)
- Created referral rewards program
- Launched community grants program
- Implemented on-chain engagement tracking

## 💡 Lessons Learned

### What We've Learned

**1. Timing Matters**
- Best to engage before launch (pre-heat)
- Post-launch hype is fleeting
- Long-term engagement requires consistent work

**2. Security is Foundation**
- Scams destroy trust instantly
- Anti-raid protocols essential
- Token-gating adds value

**3. Premium Works When**
- Clear value proposition
- Exclusive content available
- Community already engaged
- Not forced on free members

**4. Automation Scale**
- Bots handle 80% of routine tasks
- Humans handle 20% of relationship work
- Balance is key

**5. Metrics Matter**
- Track what matters (engagement, not just members)
- Report to clients regularly
- Tie community to business outcomes
