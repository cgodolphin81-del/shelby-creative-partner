## 💎 Paid Membership Models

### Tier Structure Template

#### Three-Tier Model (Recommended)

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Everyone | Basic access, public channels |
| **Premium** | $9.99/mo | Super fans | Exclusive channels, early access |
| **VIP** | $49.99/mo | Top supporters | 1:1 access, exclusive events |

---

### Tier Breakdown

#### 🆓 FREE TIER
**Purpose:** Acquisition & community building

**Includes:**
- Access to public channels
- Community events (general)
- Basic support
- Newsletter subscription
- Public resources

**Goal:** Convert 5-10% to paid

---

#### ⭐ PREMIUM TIER ($9.99-19.99/mo)
**Purpose:** Main revenue driver

**Includes:**
- Everything in Free +
- Exclusive premium channels
- Early access to content/products
- Premium events (monthly)
- Direct CM access
- Custom premium role
- Exclusive emojis/stickers
- Ad-free experience
- Monthly Q&A calls

**Target:** 3-7% of free members

---

#### 💎 VIP TIER ($49.99-99.99/mo)
**Purpose:** High-value supporters

**Includes:**
- Everything in Premium +
- Monthly 1:1 call with founder/CM
- Exclusive VIP events
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Input on community direction
- Priority support
- Custom VIP badge
- Physical merch (quarterly)
- Lifetime founder status

**Target:** 0.5-1% of free members

---

### Pricing Psychology

**Anchoring:**
- Show VIP tier first ($99/mo)
- Premium seems reasonable at $19/mo
- Free tier feels like a steal

**Charm Pricing:**
- $9.99 not $10
- $49.99 not $50
- Perceived as significantly less

**Annual Discount:**
- "Get 2 months free!"
- $99/year instead of $119
- Improves cash flow, reduces churn

## 🚀 Membership Platform Options

### Option 1: Whop ($0 + 3% fee)
**Best For:** Web3, digital products

**Pros:**
- Built for Discord
- Instant role assignment
- Marketplace discovery
- Multiple payment methods
- Crypto support

**Cons:**
- 3% transaction fee
- Less customizable

**Setup Time:** 1-2 hours

---

### Option 2: LaunchPass ($0 + 5% fee)
**Best For:** Creator communities

**Pros:**
- Simple setup
- Stripe integration
- Subscription management
- Member analytics
- Free trial support

**Cons:**
- Higher fees
- Limited customization

**Setup Time:** 30 minutes

---

### Option 3: Patreon Integration
**Best For:** Existing Patreon creators

**Pros:**
- Leverages existing audience
- Familiar to members
- Tier management included
- Multiple reward types

**Cons:**
- 5-12% fees
- Patreon brand, not yours
- Limited Discord features

**Setup Time:** 2-4 hours

---

### Option 4: Stripe + Custom Bot
**Best For:** Full control, scale

**Pros:**
- Lowest fees (2.9% + 30¢)
- Full customization
- Own customer data
- No platform dependency

**Cons:**
- Development required ($3-8K)
- Ongoing maintenance
- Payment compliance

**Setup Time:** 2-4 weeks

---

### Option 5: Circle/Skool Alternative
**Best For:** Course + community combo

**Pros:**
- All-in-one platform
- Course hosting
- Better UX than Discord
- Built-in monetization

**Cons:**
- Not Discord (migration required)
- $49-99/mo platform fee
- Less flexible

**Setup Time:** 1 week

### Event Pricing Psychology

**Early Bird:**
- 30% off for first 48 hours
- Creates urgency
- Rewards quick decision-makers

**Tiered Pricing:**
```
Early Bird: $199 (first 50 tickets)
Regular: $299 (next 100 tickets)
Late: $399 (remaining tickets)
```

**Group Discounts:**
- 3+ tickets: 15% off
- 5+ tickets: 25% off
- 10+ tickets: 40% off

**Payment Plans:**
- Pay in full: $999
- 3 payments: $375/month
- Increases accessibility

## 💰 Revenue Optimization

### Pricing Tests

**A/B Test:**
- Price points ($9.99 vs $14.99)
- Billing frequency (monthly vs annual)
- Feature bundles
- Trial lengths (7 vs 14 days)

**Price Increase Strategy:**
1. Add value first (new features)
2. Grandfather existing members (6-12 months)
3. 60-day notice
4. Offer annual lock-in at old price

---

### Churn Reduction

**Common Churn Reasons:**
- Didn't get expected value
- Too expensive
- Lost interest
- Life circumstances

**Intervention Tactics:**
- Exit survey (learn why)
- Win-back campaign (30 days later)
- Downgrade option (not cancel)
- Pause membership (life happens)

**Retention Metrics:**
- Monthly churn rate: Target <5%
- Lifetime value: Target 6+ months
- Reactivation rate: Target 10-15%
