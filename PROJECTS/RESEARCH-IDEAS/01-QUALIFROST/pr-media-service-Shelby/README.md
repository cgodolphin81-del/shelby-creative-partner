## Quick Start Guide

### For New PR Agencies

**Week 1: Setup**
1. Read all guides for strategic foundation
2. Customize templates with your branding
3. Set up tracking systems (coverage tracker, media database)
4. Define your pricing packages based on the templates

**Week 2: First Client**
1. Use the onboarding process with your first client
2. Build media list using the Media List Building guide
3. Develop message house using the template
4. Launch using the 30-Day Plan

**Week 3-4: Execute & Optimize**
1. Follow the 30-Day Launch Plan
2. Track all coverage using the Coverage Tracking System
3. Refine pitches based on response rates
4. Prepare Month 2 plan

## Usage Guidelines

### Customization

All templates and guides should be customized for:
- Your agency branding and voice
- Specific client industries
- Regional media landscapes
- Client budget and stage

### Best Practices

1. **Read the guides first** – Understand the strategy before using templates
2. **Customize everything** – Never send generic, unpersonalized pitches
3. **Track everything** – Use the coverage tracking system from day one
4. **Review quarterly** – Update templates and pricing based on results
5. **Train your team** – Use the media training guide internally

### What's Not Included

These services typically require external partners:
- Wire service distribution (PR Newswire, Business Wire)
- Media monitoring tools (Meltwater, Cision)
- Design/production services
- Legal review
- Translation services
- Event production

## Pricing Your Services

### Factors to Consider

- **Your experience level** – Junior agencies charge less
- **Market/region** – NYC/SF rates higher than regional
- **Client industry** – Regulated industries (healthcare, finance) command premium
- **Service complexity** – Technical products require more expertise
- **Team size** – Factor in your overhead

### Recommended Starting Points

| Experience Level | Retainer Starting Price |
|-----------------|------------------------|
| New agency (0-2 years) | $3,000-5,000/mo |
| Established (3-5 years) | $6,000-10,000/mo |
| Senior team (5+ years) | $10,000-15,000+/mo |

## Measuring Success

### Client Success Metrics

- Media placements (volume and quality)
- Share of voice vs. competitors
- Sentiment trends
- Message pull-through

- Client retention rate (target: 80%+)

- Revenue per client
- Profit margin (target: 40-60%)
