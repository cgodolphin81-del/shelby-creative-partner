# Client Onboarding Process

## Overview

This onboarding process ensures we thoroughly understand our clients' goals, identify the right spokespeople, develop compelling messaging, and set the foundation for successful PR campaigns.

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks from contract signing to campaign launch

---

## Phase 1: Discovery & Goal Setting (Week 1)

### Kickoff Meeting Agenda (90 minutes)

**Attendees:**
- Client: CEO, Marketing Lead, any key stakeholders
- Agency: Account Team Lead, Strategy Director

**Agenda:**

1. **Introductions (10 min)**
   - Team introductions
   - Roles and responsibilities
   - Communication preferences

2. **Company Background (15 min)**
   - Company history and mission
   - Product/service overview
   - Target customers
   - Business model

3. **Current State Assessment (15 min)**
   - Existing media coverage
   - Current messaging
   - Brand perception
   - Previous PR efforts (what worked/didn't)

4. **Goal Setting (30 min)**
   - Business objectives
   - PR-specific goals
   - Success metrics
   - Timeline expectations

5. **Logistics (20 min)**
   - Approval workflows
   - Key contacts
   - Tools and access
   - Next steps

---

### Client Questionnaire

**Send this before the kickoff meeting:**

```markdown
# PR Client Questionnaire

## Company Information
1. Company name and URL:
2. Year founded:
3. Headquarters location:
4. Number of employees:
5. Current funding stage / revenue range:

## Business Overview
6. In one sentence, what does your company do?
7. What problem do you solve?
8. Who is your target customer?
9. What are your top 3 differentiators vs. competitors?
10. What are your company values?

## Current PR Status
11. Have you worked with PR agencies before? If yes, what worked/didn't?
12. Do you have existing media coverage? Please share links.
13. Do you have existing press materials (press kit, bios, etc.)?
14. Are there any journalists/outlets you have existing relationships with?
15. Are there any journalists/outlets you particularly want to reach?

## Goals & Objectives
16. What are your top 3 business goals for the next 12 months?
17. What do you hope PR will accomplish for you?
18. What does success look like in 3 months? 6 months? 12 months?
19. Are there any specific announcements coming up?
20. What's your biggest concern about doing PR?

## Competitors
21. Who are your top 3 competitors?
22. Which competitors are getting the most media attention?
23. What do you admire about their PR/communications?
24. What do you do better than them?

## Spokespeople
25. Who will be the primary spokesperson(s)?
26. Have they done media interviews before?
27. Are they available for interviews on short notice?
28. Do they need media training?

## Logistics
29. Who needs to approve press releases and pitches?
30. What's your typical turnaround time for approvals?
31. Are there any topics that are off-limits?
32. Are there any legal/compliance considerations we should know about?
33. What's your preferred communication method (email, Slack, calls)?
34. What timezone are you in?

## Additional Context
35. Is there anything else we should know about your company, industry, or goals?
```

### Goal-Setting Framework

**SMART PR Goals Template:**

```markdown
# PR Goals - [Client Name]
## Period: [Date Range]

### Goal 1: [Specific Objective]
**Specific:** [What exactly will be accomplished]
**Measurable:** [How will we measure success]
**Achievable:** [Why this is realistic]
**Relevant:** [How this supports business goals]
**Time-bound:** [By when]

**Metrics:**
- Primary: [Metric 1]
- Secondary: [Metric 2]
- Target: [Specific number]

**Strategies:**
- [Strategy 1]
- [Strategy 2]
```

**Example Goals:**

**Goal 1: Increase Brand Awareness**
- Specific: Secure coverage in top-tier tech publications
- Measurable: 15 Tier 1 placements
- Achievable: Based on newsworthy product launches
- Relevant: Supports customer acquisition goals
- Time-bound: By end of Q2 2026
- Metrics: Tier 1 placements, reach, share of voice
- Target: 15 placements, 5M reach, 25% SOV

**Goal 2: Establish Thought Leadership**
- Specific: Position CEO as industry expert
- Measurable: 8 bylined articles, 5 podcast interviews
- Achievable: CEO has unique insights on [topic]
- Relevant: Builds trust with enterprise customers
- Time-bound: Over 6 months
- Metrics: Bylines, speaking opportunities, social followers
- Target: 8 articles, 5 podcasts, 1 speaking event

**Goal 3: Support Fundraising**
- Specific: Generate investor interest through media
- Measurable: 10 investor-focused placements
- Achievable: Strong traction metrics to share
- Relevant: Series B raising in Q3
- Time-bound: Before Series B launch
- Metrics: Investor inquiries, coverage in investor media
- Target: 10 placements, 5 investor meetings sourced from PR
