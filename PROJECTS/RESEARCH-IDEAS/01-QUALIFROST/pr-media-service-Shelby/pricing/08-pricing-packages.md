# PR Service Pricing Packages

## Overview

This document outlines three pricing models for PR & Media Relations services: Retainer, Project-Based, and Crisis Communications. All pricing is in USD.

---

## Package 1: Retainer Services

### Structure

Retainer engagements are ongoing monthly relationships focused on sustained media presence, relationship building, and strategic communications.

### Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Investment | Best For |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| **Essentials** | $3,000 - $5,000/mo | Early-stage startups, limited budget |
| **Growth** | $6,000 - $10,000/mo | Series A/B companies, scaling |
| **Enterprise** | $11,000 - $15,000/mo | Series C+, established brands |

## Package 3: Crisis Communications

### Structure

Crisis services are available as standalone projects or as part of an ongoing retainer. Crisis work is time-sensitive and may require 24/7 availability.

### Crisis Offerings

| Service | Investment | Timeline |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Crisis Preparedness Plan | $5,000 - $10,000 | 3-4 weeks |
| Active Crisis Management | $10,000 - $50,000+ | As needed |
| Crisis Retainer ( standby) | $2,500 - $5,000/mo | Ongoing |

---

### Crisis Preparedness Plan: $5,000 - $10,000

**Timeline:** 3-4 weeks

**Scope:**
- Risk assessment (identify potential crises)
- Crisis classification framework
- Crisis response team definition
- Contact list development
- Holding statement templates (8 scenarios)
- Q&A development
- Social media response guidelines
- Stakeholder communication templates
- Team training workshop (2 hours)

**Deliverables:**
- Crisis Communications Plan (document)
- Holding statement templates
- Q&A templates
- Contact list
- Training session

**Includes:**
- 2 strategy sessions
- Plan document
- Templates
- Team training

## Payment Terms

### Retainer Services
- **Billing:** Monthly, in advance
- **Payment Terms:** Net 15
- **Contract:** 6-12 month minimum (depending on tier)
- **Termination:** 30-day notice after minimum term
- **Overage:** Additional hours at $250-450/hour (by tier)

### Project Services
- **Billing:** 50% upfront, 50% on completion
- **Payment Terms:** Net 15
- **Changes:** Scope changes require written approval
- **Cancellation:** 50% fee if cancelled after work begins

### Crisis Services
- **Billing:** Retainer upfront, hours billed weekly
- **Payment Terms:** Net 7 (crisis), Net 15 (preparedness)
- **Activation:** Immediate upon verbal authorization
- **Expenses:** Billed at cost (travel, wire services, etc.)

## What's Not Included

**Unless explicitly stated:**
- Wire service distribution fees

- Third-party tools/subscriptions
- Printing/production costs
- Legal review fees
- Translation services
- Social media ad spend

## Pricing Factors

**Pricing varies based on:**

1. **Company Stage:** Earlier stage = lower complexity
2. **Industry:** Regulated industries (healthcare, finance) = higher
3. **Geography:** National/International = higher than regional
4. **Complexity:** Technical products = more education required
5. **Executive Availability:** More prep time = higher investment
6. **Crisis Risk:** Higher risk = more preparedness needed
7. **Timeline:** Rush projects = premium pricing
