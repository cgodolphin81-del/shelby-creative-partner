# Media Pitch Templates (20+ Pitch Emails)

## Pitch Framework

**Subject Line Rules:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Lead with news value, not company name
- Personalize when possible
- Avoid spam triggers: "free," "opportunity," "partnership"

**Email Structure:**
1. **Hook (1 sentence):** Why this matters to THEIR audience
2. **Context (2-3 sentences):** What's happening, why now
3. **Proof (1-2 sentences):** Data, traction, credibility
4. **Ask (1 sentence):** What you're requesting
5. **Offer:** Exclusive angle, interview availability, assets

## Pitch Best Practices

### DO:
✓ Personalize every pitch (reference their work)
✓ Keep emails under 200 words
✓ Lead with why it matters to THEIR audience
✓ Include clear call-to-action
✓ Follow up max 2 times
✓ Respect "no" and opt-outs

### DON'T:
✗ Send mass BCC emails
✗ Use generic greetings ("Dear Journalist")
✗ Attach press releases without asking
✗ Pitch stories outside their beat
✗ Follow up more than twice
✗ Pitch on Monday morning or Friday afternoon

### Subject Line Formulas That Work:
- "Exclusive: [News] for [Publication] readers"
- "Story idea: [Trend] affecting [audience]"
- "[Company] [action] to [outcome]"
- "New data: [Statistic] of [group] [behavior]"
- "Former [Notable Company] exec joins [Company]"
