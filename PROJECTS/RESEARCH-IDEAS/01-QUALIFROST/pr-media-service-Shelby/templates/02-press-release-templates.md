## Press Release Best Practices

### Formatting Standards
- **Length:** 400-800 words (1-2 pages max)
- **Dateline:** CITY, State — Month Day, Year
- **Quotes:** Use for opinion/color, not facts
- **Boilerplate:** 2-3 sentences, consistent across releases
- **End mark:** ### or -END- centered at bottom

### Distribution Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 9-11 AM local time of target media
- **Avoid:** Mondays (inbox overload), Fridays (weekend news dump), weekends
- **Embargoes:** Use for major announcements, give 24-48 hours minimum

### SEO Optimization
- Include target keywords naturally in headline and first paragraph
- Link to relevant pages on your website
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Add schema markup when publishing on your site

### Media Contact Best Practices
- Always include named media contact (not just "Media Team")
- Provide direct email and phone
- Respond to inquiries within 2 hours during business hours
- Have high-res logos and executive headshots ready
