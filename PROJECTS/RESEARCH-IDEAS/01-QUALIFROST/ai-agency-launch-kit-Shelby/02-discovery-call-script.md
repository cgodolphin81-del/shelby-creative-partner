# Discovery Call Script & Questionnaire

## 📋 Pre-Call Preparation

**Review before the call:**
- [ ] Client's website and social media
- [ ] Industry and competitors
- [ ] Any previous communications
- [ ] Prepare customized questions

**Tech check:**
- [ ] Video conferencing link tested
- [ ] Recording permission requested
- [ ] Note-taking system ready
- [ ] Calendar open for scheduling follow-up

---

## 🎯 Call Opening (5 minutes)

### Introduction
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for taking the time to chat today! I'm [Your Name] from [Agency Name]. How are you doing?"

*[Small talk - 1-2 minutes max]*

### Set Expectations
"Here's what I'd like to do today:
1. Learn about your business and what you're looking to accomplish
2. Understand the challenges you're facing
3. See if and how we might be able to help
4. Answer any questions you have

Does that sound good? And just so you know, this call is about [30 minutes]. I'll be taking notes - is that okay?"

### Permission to Record (if applicable)
"I'd like to record this call so I can focus on our conversation rather than frantic note-taking. The recording is just for internal use and won't be shared. Is that alright with you?"

---

## 🔍 Discovery Questions (20 minutes)

### Business Overview
1. "Tell me about your business - what do you do and who do you serve?"
2. "How long have you been in business?"
3. "What does your team look like? How many people?"
4. "What's your typical customer/profile look like?"

### Current Challenges
5. "What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now in your business?"
6. "Are there any tasks or processes that take up more time than they should?"
7. "What does your customer communication look like today?"
8. "How do you currently handle [relevant process: customer service, lead qualification, scheduling, etc.]?"

### Pain Points (Dig Deeper)
9. "When you say [repeat their pain point], can you tell me more about that?"
10. "How is that impacting your business? Revenue, time, customer satisfaction?"
11. "What have you tried so far to solve this?"
12. "Why haven't those solutions worked?"

### Goals & Vision
13. "If we could wave a magic wand, what would success look like 6 months from now?"
14. "What would solving this problem mean for your business?"
15. "Are there any specific metrics you're trying to hit?"
16. "What's driving this initiative now vs. 6 months ago?"

### Budget & Decision-Making
17. "Have you worked with agencies or consultants on projects like this before?"
18. "What kind of investment range are you considering for this?"
19. "Besides yourself, who else is involved in making this decision?"
20. "What does your decision-making timeline look like?"

### Technical Environment
21. "What tools and software are you currently using? [CRM, website platform, communication tools]"
22. "Are there any systems we absolutely need to integrate with?"
23. "Do you have anyone technical on your team, or would you need us to handle everything?"

## 📊 Qualification Checklist

**Green Flags ✅:**
- [ ] Clear pain point we can solve
- [ ] Budget aligns with our pricing ($2,500+)
- [ ] Decision-maker is on the call or engaged
- [ ] Timeline is reasonable (not "yesterday")
- [ ] They value expertise (not just shopping price)
- [ ] Good communication and responsiveness

**Red Flags 🚩:**
- [ ] Budget way below our minimum
- [ ] Unrealistic expectations or timeline
- [ ] Not the decision-maker and can't get access
- [ ] Vague about goals or challenges
- [ ] Previous bad agency experiences (trust issues)
- [ ] Wants to skip discovery/planning phase

## 💡 Solution Framing (5 minutes)

### Connect Their Pain to Our Solution

"Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm hearing:

**Your main challenges are:**
- [Repeat their top 2-3 pain points]
- [Mention impact they described]

**Here's how we typically help businesses like yours:**
- [Brief overview of relevant service]
- [Specific outcome they can expect]
- [Timeline expectation]

**For example, we recently worked with [similar company] who had [similar challenge]. We [what we did], and they saw [specific result] within [timeframe]."

### Next Steps

"Here's what I recommend:
1. I'll put together a custom proposal based on what we discussed
2. We'll schedule a follow-up call to walk through it
3. If it's a good fit, we can get started with [timeline]

I can have that proposal to you within [48 hours]. Does [specific day/time] work for a follow-up call?"

## ❓ Common Questions & Responses

### "How much does this cost?"
"That's a great question. Our projects typically range from $2,500 to $20,000 depending on scope and complexity. Based on what you've shared today, I think you'd be in the [range] range. The proposal I send over will have exact pricing based on your specific needs."

### "How long does it take?"
"Most projects take between 2-8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Chatbots are typically on the shorter end (2-4 weeks), while complex workflow automations can take 6-8 weeks. We'll outline the specific timeline in the proposal."

### "Do you offer ongoing support?"
"Absolutely. Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. We also offer monthly maintenance and optimization packages if you want ongoing partnership. I'll include those options in the proposal."

### "What if it doesn't work?"
"We don't launch anything until it's tested and working properly. We build in checkpoints throughout the project so you can review progress. And if there are any issues after launch, that's what our support period is for. We're invested in your success."

### "Can you guarantee results?"
"I can guarantee our work and our commitment to your success. What I can't guarantee is specific business outcomes because those depend on many factors outside our control. What I can tell you is that our clients typically see [relevant metric] within [timeframe]."

## 📝 Call Closing

### Confirm Next Steps
"Perfect. So to confirm:
- I'll send you a custom proposal by [day/time]
- We'll meet again on [date/time] to review it
- In the meantime, feel free to reach out if you have any questions

Does that work for you?"

### Final Question
"Is there anything else you'd like to know before we wrap up?"

### Thank You
"Great! Thanks again for your time today, [Name]. I'm excited about the possibility of working together. I'll be in touch soon with that proposal. Have a great rest of your day!"
