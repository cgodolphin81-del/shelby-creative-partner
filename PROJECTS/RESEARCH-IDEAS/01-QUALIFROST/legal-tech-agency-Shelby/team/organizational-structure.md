# Organizational Structure & Team Building

## Overview

This document outlines the organizational structure, roles, hiring plan, and team development strategy for LexTech Solutions. The structure is designed to scale from a founding team to a 50+ person organization over three years.

---

## Organizational Philosophy

**Guiding Principles:**
1. **Client-first:** Every role exists to serve clients better
2. **Expertise matters:** Deep legal + technology knowledge is our differentiator
3. **Lean and agile:** Stay nimble as we scale
4. **Remote-friendly:** Hire the best talent, regardless of location
5. **Career growth:** Clear paths for individual contributors and managers

---

## Compensation Philosophy

### Guiding Principles
1. **Market competitive:** Pay at or above 75th percentile for key roles
2. **Performance-based:** Significant variable comp for revenue-facing roles
3. **Equity participation:** All employees share in company success
4. **Transparent:** Clear bands and progression paths
5. **Holistic:** Competitive benefits and work-life balance

### Compensation Bands (Year 3)

| Level | Base Range | Bonus Target | Equity Range |
|-------|------------|--------------|--------------|
| Executive (C-level) | $200K-$350K | 30-50% | 1.0-5.0% |
| Principal | $160K-$230K | 20-30% | 0.5-1.5% |
| Senior | $130K-$180K | 15-20% | 0.25-0.75% |
| Mid-Level | $90K-$130K | 10-15% | 0.1-0.25% |
| Junior | $60K-$90K | 5-10% | 0.05-0.1% |

### Benefits Package

#### Standard Benefits (All Employees)
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision) - 80% company paid
- 401(k) with 4% company match
- Life insurance and disability
- Flexible PTO (minimum 3 weeks encouraged)
- 12 paid holidays
- Home office stipend ($1,000 one-time)
- Professional development budget ($3,000/year)

#### Additional Benefits (Senior Level+)
- Enhanced equity grants
- Executive health benefits
- Additional PTO (4+ weeks)
- Sabbatical program (after 5 years)

- 3+ years experience

## Recruiting Strategy

### Sourcing Channels

| Channel | Target Roles | Investment | Expected Yield |
|---------|--------------|------------|----------------|
| LinkedIn Recruiter | All roles | $15K/year | 40% of hires |
| Employee Referrals | All roles | $5K bonus/hire | 30% of hires |
| Industry Events | Senior roles | $20K/year | 15% of hires |
| Legal Tech Communities | Technical roles | $5K/year | 10% of hires |
| Recruiters | Executive roles | 20% fee | 5% of hires |

### Interview Process

#### Standard Process (Consulting Roles)
1. **Phone Screen** (30 min) - Recruiter/HR
2. **Hiring Manager Interview** (45 min) - Role fit, experience
3. **Case Study** (Take-home, 2-3 hours) - Practical skills
4. **Case Presentation** (60 min) - Present to team
5. **Final Interview** (45 min) - Culture fit, executive
6. **Reference Checks** - 2-3 references
7. **Offer**

**Timeline:** 2-3 weeks from application to offer

#### Sales Roles
1. **Phone Screen** (30 min) - Recruiter
2. **Sales Manager Interview** (45 min) - Sales experience
3. **Role Play** (60 min) - Mock sales call
4. **Panel Interview** (60 min) - Team fit
5. **Final Interview** (45 min) - Executive
6. **Reference Checks**
7. **Offer**

### Onboarding Program

#### Week 1: Orientation
- Company overview and culture
- Tools and systems training
- Meet the team
- Assign onboarding buddy
- Review role expectations

#### Weeks 2-4: Training
- Product/service training
- Methodology training
- Shadow client calls
- Complete certifications
- First client assignment

#### Month 2-3: Ramp-Up
- Independent client work
- Weekly check-ins
- 30/60/90 day reviews
- Full productivity expected by day 90

## Retention Strategy

### Risk Factors & Mitigation

| Risk | Indicators | Mitigation |
|------|------------|------------|
| Burnout | Long hours, decreased quality | Workload monitoring, PTO enforcement |
| Stagnation | No growth, repetitive work | Career pathing, new challenges |
| Compensation | Market below, equity concerns | Annual reviews, market adjustments |
| Culture fit | Disengagement, conflicts | Regular check-ins, culture surveys |

### Retention Programs

- **Stay Interviews:** Annual conversations about satisfaction
- **Career Development Plans:** Individualized growth plans
- **Equity Refresh:** Additional grants for top performers
- **Sabbatical Program:** 4-week paid sabbatical after 5 years
- **Alumni Network:** Maintain relationships with departing employees
