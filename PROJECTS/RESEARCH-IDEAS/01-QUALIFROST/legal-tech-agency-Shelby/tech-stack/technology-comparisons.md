# Technology Stack Comparisons

## Overview

This document provides detailed comparisons of leading legal technology solutions across key categories. Use this to make informed vendor recommendations based on client size, budget, and requirements.

---

## 1. Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)

### Enterprise Tier

| Vendor | Starting Price | Best For | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|--------|---------------|----------|-----------|------------|
| **Icertis** | $75K+/year | Large enterprises, complex contracts | AI-powered, extensive integrations, global scale | Expensive, complex implementation |
| **Agiloft** | $50K+/year | Mid-large enterprises | Highly customizable, no-code platform, strong workflow | Steeper learning curve |
| **Ironclad** | $40K+/year | Tech companies, fast-growing | User-friendly, strong collaboration, good UX | Less customizable than competitors |
| **Conga** | $60K+/year | Salesforce shops | Deep Salesforce integration, mature platform | Can be complex, pricing opaque |
| **DocuSign CLM** | $35K+/year | DocuSign e-signature users | Seamless e-sign integration, easy adoption | Less robust than pure-play CLM |

### Mid-Market Tier

| Vendor | Starting Price | Best For | Strengths | Weaknesses |
|--------|---------------|----------|-----------|------------|
| **PandaDoc** | $15K/year | SMB, sales contracts | Easy to use, good templates, affordable | Limited enterprise features |
| **ContractWorks** | $12K/year | Small-mid legal teams | Simple, affordable, quick deployment | Basic features, limited customization |
| **Evisort** | $30K+/year | AI-powered contract intelligence | Strong AI extraction, good analytics | Newer platform, less mature |
| **LinkSquares** | $25K+/year | Contract analytics focus | Excellent search/analytics, AI-powered | More analytics than workflow |

### Recommendation Framework

```
Company Size → Recommended Tier
─────────────────────────────────
< 50 employees → ContractWorks, PandaDoc
50-200 employees → Ironclad, LinkSquares
200-1000 employees → Agiloft, Evisort
1000+ employees → Icertis, Conga, Ironclad Enterprise
```

## 2. E-Signature Solutions

| Vendor | Pricing (per user/month) | Best For | Key Features | Limitations |
|--------|-------------------------|----------|--------------|-------------|
| **DocuSign** | $25-$60 | Enterprise, all industries | Most integrations, globally recognized, advanced features | Premium pricing, can be overkill for small teams |
| **Adobe Sign** | $15-$50 | Adobe ecosystem users | Deep Adobe integration, included in some Creative Cloud | Fewer integrations than DocuSign |
| **HelloSign (Dropbox)** | $15-$45 | Dropbox users, SMB | Simple, clean interface, good value | Limited enterprise features |
| **PandaDoc** | $19-$49 | Sales teams, proposals | Built-in templates, tracking, payments | E-sign is secondary to document creation |
| **SignNow** | $8-$30 | Budget-conscious | Affordable, decent features | Less brand recognition |
| **OneSpan Sign** | Custom | Highly regulated industries | Strong security, compliance focus | Complex, expensive |

### Recommendation

- **Enterprise:** DocuSign or Adobe Sign
- **Mid-Market:** DocuSign, HelloSign, or PandaDoc
- **SMB:** HelloSign, SignNow, or PandaDoc
- **Highly Regulated:** OneSpan or DocuSign Advanced

## Technology Stack Recommendations by Client Profile

### Solo Practitioner / Small Firm (1-10 attorneys)
```
- Practice Management: Clio ($69/user/month)
- E-Signature: HelloSign or DocuSign ($15-25/user/month)
- Legal Research: Fastcase or Casetext ($500-800/attorney/year)
- Document Management: NetDocuments or Worldox ($40-50/user/month)
- Total Tech Stack: ~$15K-25K/year
```

### Mid-Size Firm (11-50 attorneys)
```
- Practice Management: Clio Manage or Litify ($100-150/user/month)
- CLM: Ironclad or Agiloft ($40K-60K/year)
- E-Signature: DocuSign ($40-60/user/month)
- Legal Research: Bloomberg Law or Westlaw ($3K-5K/attorney/year)
- Document Management: NetDocuments or iManage ($50-70/user/month)
- E-Discovery: Everlaw or Logikcull (as needed)
- Total Tech Stack: ~$150K-300K/year
```

### Large Firm (51-200 attorneys)
```
- Practice Management: Litify or Elite 3E (custom pricing)
- CLM: Icertis or Agiloft ($75K-150K/year)
- E-Signature: DocuSign Enterprise ($60/user/month)
- Legal Research: Westlaw + Lexis ($5K-7K/attorney/year)
- Document Management: iManage Work ($75-100/user/month)
- E-Discovery: RelativityOne (custom pricing)
- Compliance: Navex or LogicGate ($50K-100K/year)
- Total Tech Stack: ~$500K-1M+/year
```

### Corporate Legal Department (10-50 attorneys)
```
- Matter Management: SimpleLegal or LegalTracker ($50K-100K/year)
- CLM: Ironclad or Evisort ($40K-80K/year)
- E-Signature: DocuSign or Adobe Sign ($40-50/user/month)
- Legal Research: Bloomberg Law ($4K-6K/attorney/year)
- E-Billing: Brightflag or LegalTracker ($40K-80K/year)
- Document Management: SharePoint or NetDocuments
- Total Tech Stack: ~$200K-400K/year
```

## Vendor Selection Criteria

When recommending vendors, evaluate against these criteria:

1. **Functionality** (30% weight)
   - Feature completeness
   - Ease of use
   - Customization options

2. **Integration** (20% weight)
   - Existing system compatibility
   - API availability
   - Pre-built connectors

3. **Cost** (20% weight)
   - Total cost of ownership
   - Implementation costs
   - Ongoing maintenance

4. **Vendor Stability** (15% weight)
   - Company financials
   - Market presence
   - Customer references

5. **Support & Training** (15% weight)
   - Implementation support
   - Ongoing support quality
   - Training resources
