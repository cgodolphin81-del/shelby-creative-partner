## Sales Strategy

### Sales Process

#### Stage 1: Lead Generation
**Sources:**
- Inbound (website, content, SEO)
- Outbound (LinkedIn, email campaigns)
- Referrals (clients, vendors, partners)
- Events (conferences, webinars)

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- **Budget:** $25K+ available for legal tech
- **Authority:** Access to decision makers
- **Need:** Clear pain point identified
- **Timeline:** Looking to implement within 12 months

#### Stage 2: Discovery Call (30-45 minutes)
**Agenda:**
1. Understand current situation (10 min)
2. Identify pain points (10 min)
3. Discuss desired outcomes (10 min)
4. Explain our approach (10 min)
5. Next steps (5 min)

**Qualifying Questions:**
- "What prompted you to look at technology solutions now?"
- "What have you tried before? What worked/didn't work?"
- "Who else is involved in this decision?"
- "What's your ideal timeline?"
- "Do you have budget allocated for this initiative?"

#### Stage 3: Needs Assessment (Paid or Complimentary)
**Complimentary Assessment:** For deals >$100K potential
**Paid Assessment:** $5K-$15K (credited toward project if won)

**Deliverables:**
- Current state summary
- Preliminary recommendations
- High-level implementation approach
- Budget range estimate

#### Stage 4: Proposal & Presentation
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks after assessment

**Presentation Agenda:**
1. Understanding of current state (5 min)
2. Proposed solution (15 min)
3. Implementation approach (10 min)
4. Investment and timeline (10 min)
5. Q&A (15 min)

#### Stage 5: Negotiation & Closing
**Typical Timeline:** 2-8 weeks depending on deal size

**Common Objections & Responses:**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Let's discuss ROI. Most clients see payback in 12-18 months through efficiency gains." |
| "We tried this before and it failed" | "Understandable. Let's discuss what went wrong and how our methodology addresses those risks." |
| "We don't have bandwidth" | "That's exactly why you need this. We handle the heavy lifting; your team focuses on legal work." |
| "Need to think about it" | "Of course. What specific concerns do you need to address? Let's schedule a follow-up." |
| "Going with [competitor]" | "Respect that decision. What factors drove that choice? We'd love to understand for future opportunities." |

#### Stage 6: Onboarding
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks after contract signing

**Activities:**
- Kickoff meeting scheduling
- Stakeholder introduction
- Project management tool setup
- Discovery phase planning

## Partnership Strategy

### Technology Vendor Partnerships

#### Partnership Tiers

**Referral Partner**
- Commitment: Minimal
- Benefits: Referral fees (10-15% of first-year revenue)
- Requirements: Basic product training

**Implementation Partner**
- Commitment: Certified consultants, minimum implementations/year
- Benefits: Higher referral fees (15-20%), co-marketing, lead sharing
- Requirements: 2+ certified consultants, 5+ implementations/year

**Strategic Partner**
- Commitment: Exclusive focus in category, joint GTM
- Benefits: Highest fees (20-25%), joint marketing fund, executive alignment
- Requirements: 5+ certified consultants, 15+ implementations/year

#### Target Vendor Partnerships
| Vendor | Partnership Type | Priority |
|--------|-----------------|----------|
| Ironclad | Implementation | High |
| Icertis | Implementation | High |
| DocuSign | Implementation | High |
| Clio | Implementation | Medium |
| Relativity | Implementation | Medium |
| SimpleLegal | Implementation | Medium |
| Brightflag | Implementation | Medium |
| LinkSquares | Implementation | Low |

### Channel Partners

#### Management Consulting Firms
- **Target:** Regional consulting firms without legal tech practices
- **Value Prop:** Extend service offerings without hiring
- **Model:** Subcontracting or referral fees

#### Law Firm Consultants
- **Target:** Consultants serving law firms on non-tech matters
- **Value Prop:** Technology expertise for their clients
- **Model:** Referral fees or joint engagements

#### Legal Recruiters
- **Target:** Recruiters placing legal ops roles
- **Value Prop:** Technology assessments for their candidates' employers
- **Model:** Referral fees
