# Pricing Models

## Overview

LexTech Solutions offers flexible pricing models designed to align with client needs, project complexity, and engagement duration. This document outlines our pricing structure, packages, and guidelines for proposal development.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-Based:** Price reflects value delivered, not just hours worked
2. **Transparent:** Clear breakdown of costs with no hidden fees
3. **Flexible:** Multiple models to suit different client preferences
4. **Fair:** Profitable for us, valuable for clients

## ROI Framework

### Implementation ROI Calculator

#### Cost Savings
```
1. Time Savings
   - Hours saved per user per week × Users × Weeks × Hourly Rate
   - Example: 5 hours × 50 users × 52 weeks × $200/hr = $2.6M/year

2. Reduced Outside Counsel Spend
   - % reduction × Annual outside counsel spend
   - Example: 10% × $5M = $500K/year

3. Technology Consolidation
   - Retired systems annual cost
   - Example: 3 systems × $50K = $150K/year
```

#### Revenue Enablement
```
1. Faster Contract Cycle Time
   - Days reduced × Deals per year × Average deal value / 365
   - Example: 5 days × 200 deals × $100K / 365 = $274K/year

2. Improved Compliance
   - Reduced penalty risk (qualitative, but can estimate)
```

#### Typical ROI Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Payback Period | < 12 months |
| 3-Year ROI | 200-400% |
| Annual Cost Savings | 2-5x implementation cost |
| User Productivity Gain | 20-40% |
