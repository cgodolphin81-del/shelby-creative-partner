## Critical Success Factors

### 1. Niche Focus
Don't try to serve everyone. Start with:
- **Industry focus:** Technology, Healthcare, or Financial Services
- **Service focus:** CLM implementation OR Legal Ops OR Contract Automation
- **Client size:** Mid-market (50-500 employees) initially

### 2. Credibility Building
- Publish thought leadership content weekly
- Speak at industry events
- Get certified on key platforms
- Build relationships with vendor partners

### 3. Delivery Excellence
- Follow the implementation methodology religiously
- Over-communicate with clients
- Document everything
- Create reusable assets from each engagement

### 4. Cash Management
- Bill milestones promptly
- Follow up on receivables aggressively
- Maintain 6 months cash reserve
- Don't over-hire before revenue supports it

### 5. Client Retention
- Deliver measurable value
- Transition to retainer model where possible
- Conduct regular check-ins
- Ask for referrals from happy clients

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

| Pitfall | Consequence | Prevention |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Underpricing | Margin compression, burnout | Use pricing guide, know your value |
| Over-customization | Scope creep, delays | Stick to methodology, change orders |
| Hiring too fast | Cash burn, culture issues | Hire on revenue triggers |
| Ignoring marketing | Feast/famine pipeline | Consistent marketing investment |
| Poor scope definition | Disputes, margin erosion | Detailed SOW, clear assumptions |
| No retainer focus | Unpredictable revenue | Always pitch ongoing support |
| Skipping discovery | Implementation failures | Never skip paid assessment |
