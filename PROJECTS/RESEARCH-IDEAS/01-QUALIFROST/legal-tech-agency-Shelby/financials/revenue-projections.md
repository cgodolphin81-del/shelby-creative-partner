## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Financial Risks

| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-------------|------------|
| Slower sales ramp | High | Medium | Reduce hiring, extend runway |
| Client concentration | High | Medium | Diversify client base, retainer focus |
| Margin compression | Medium | Medium | Pricing discipline, efficiency improvements |
| Cash flow gaps | High | Medium | Line of credit, milestone billing |
| Key person risk | High | Low | Documentation, cross-training |

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Maintain 6 months cash reserve**
2. **Diversify client base (no client >20% of revenue)**
3. **Focus on retainer revenue for predictability**
4. **Milestone billing for projects (30/40/30)**
5. **Regular financial reviews (monthly)**
6. **Scenario planning (quarterly)**
