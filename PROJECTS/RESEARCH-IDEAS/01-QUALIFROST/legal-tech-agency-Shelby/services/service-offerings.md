# Service Offerings

## Overview

LexTech Solutions provides comprehensive legal technology consulting services across five core practice areas. Each service is designed to address specific pain points faced by modern legal organizations.

---

## 1. Legal Tech Implementation

### Description
End-to-end implementation of legal technology solutions, from vendor selection through go-live and adoption.

### Service Tiers

#### **Tier 1: Assessment & Selection** ($15K - $35K)
- Technology needs assessment
- Vendor market analysis
- RFP development and management
- Vendor demonstrations coordination
- Recommendation report with ROI analysis
- **Deliverables:** Assessment report, vendor scorecard, implementation roadmap
- **Timeline:** 4-6 weeks

#### **Tier 2: Standard Implementation** ($40K - $75K)
- Everything in Tier 1
- System configuration
- Data migration (up to 50,000 records)
- Integration with 2-3 existing systems
- User training (up to 50 users)
- Go-live support
- **Deliverables:** Configured system, migration report, training materials, user guides
- **Timeline:** 8-12 weeks

#### **Tier 3: Enterprise Implementation** ($80K - $150K)
- Everything in Tier 2
- Complex data migration (500,000+ records)
- Custom integrations (5+ systems)
- Workflow automation design
- Change management program
- Super-user training program
- Post-go-live optimization (30 days)
- **Deliverables:** Full implementation package, custom workflows, optimization report
- **Timeline:** 12-20 weeks

### Common Technologies Implemented
- Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM)
- Matter/Case Management Systems
- Legal Billing & e-Billing
- Document Management Systems
- Client Intake Portals
