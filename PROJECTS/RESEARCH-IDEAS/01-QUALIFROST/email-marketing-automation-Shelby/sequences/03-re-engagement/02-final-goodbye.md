## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Final re-engagement attempt before list removal  
**Tone:** Respectful, gracious, clear  
**Elements:** Clear consequence, strong incentive, social alternatives  
**CTA:** Stay subscribed with best offer + unsubscribe option  
**Timing:** 7 days after first re-engagement email  
**Follow-up:** Automatically remove non-responders after 48 hours  
**Compliance:** Required for list hygiene and sender reputation  
**Segmentation:** Tag those who re-engage for special nurture track

---

## Post-Campaign Actions

**For those who re-engage:**
1. Tag as "Re-engaged" in ESP
2. Add to special nurture sequence (3-5 emails over 2 weeks)
3. Monitor engagement closely for next 30 days
4. If they disengage again, move to shorter re-engagement cycle (30 days)

**For those who don't respond:**
1. Remove from main list after 48 hours
2. Move to "Suppressed" list (don't re-import)
3. Optionally: Add to custom audience for social retargeting
4. Document removal date for compliance records
