# Re-engagement Email #1 - "We Miss You"

**Trigger:** No opens/clicks in 60-90 days  
**Send Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm recipient timezone  
**Goal:** Re-engage inactive subscribers with value + ask for feedback

---

## Subject Line Options (A/B Test)

A. [First Name], we miss you!  
B. Is this goodbye?  
C. Did we do something wrong?  
D. [First Name], come back!

## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Re-engage or cleanly remove inactive subscribers  
**Tone:** Warm, understanding, not guilt-tripping  
**Elements:** Feedback mechanism, value recap, incentive, easy out  
**CTA:** Comeback offer + preference center options  
**Timing:** After 60-90 days of inactivity  
**Segmentation:** Tag responders for re-engagement tracking  
**Compliance:** Clear unsubscribe (required for re-engagement campaigns)
