## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Overcome hesitation with financial incentive  
**Tone:** Empathetic, helpful, value-focused  
**Elements:** Discount code, social proof, FAQ  
**CTA:** Discount-focused CTA with urgency  
**Timing:** 24 hours (enough time to consider, not enough to forget)  
**Incentive:** 10% is standard; adjust based on margins
