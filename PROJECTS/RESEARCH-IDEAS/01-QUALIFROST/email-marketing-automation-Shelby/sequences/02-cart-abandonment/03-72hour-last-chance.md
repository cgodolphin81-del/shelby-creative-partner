## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Final conversion attempt with maximum urgency  
**Tone:** Urgent but not aggressive, scarcity-driven  
**Elements:** Expiration timeline, social proof, stronger incentive  
**CTA:** Urgency-focused with clear deadline  
**Timing:** 72 hours (last reasonable attempt before list fatigue)  
**Incentive:** 15% + free shipping (adjust based on margins)  
**Compliance:** Include clear unsubscribe option for cart emails
