# Cart Abandonment Email #1 - 1 Hour After Abandonment

**Trigger:** Cart abandoned (no purchase within 1 hour)  
**Send Time:** 1 hour after abandonment  
**Goal:** Gentle reminder + remove friction

---

## Subject Line Options (A/B Test)

A. Forgot something, [First Name]?  
B. Your cart is waiting...  
C. [Product Name] is still in your cart  
D. Oops! You left something behind

## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Gentle nudge, remove objections  
**Tone:** Helpful, not pushy  
**Elements:** Cart summary, objection handling, social proof  
**CTA:** Clear, prominent button  
**Personalization:** Dynamic product images, names, prices  
**Timing:** 1 hour after abandonment (while intent is still high)
