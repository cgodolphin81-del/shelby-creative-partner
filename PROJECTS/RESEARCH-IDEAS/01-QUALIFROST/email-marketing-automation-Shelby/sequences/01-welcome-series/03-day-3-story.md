# Welcome Email #3 - Day 3 (Story/Connection)

**Trigger:** 3 days after signup  
**Send Time:** Same time as previous emails  
**Goal:** Build emotional connection through storytelling

---

B. The truth about [topic] nobody tells you  
C. [First Name], let me tell you something...

## Email Copy

**Preheader:** A personal story about [topic] and what it taught me

---

Hi [First Name],

I want to tell you a quick story.

Back in [Year], I was [describe struggle/situation relevant to audience].

I tried everything:
- [Thing #1 that didn't work]
- [Thing #2 that didn't work]
- [Thing #3 that wasted time/money]

Nothing worked. I was frustrated and ready to give up.

### Then Something Changed

[Describe the turning point - a discovery, mentor, insight, or breakthrough moment]

That's when I realized: **[Key insight or lesson]**

### Fast Forward to Today

That lesson became the foundation of [Company Name].

Since then, we've helped [number] of people/clients:
- [Achievement #1]
- [Achievement #2]
- [Achievement #3]

But here's what I'm most proud of:

**[Emotional outcome or impact on customers]**

Because I know [First Name], you're probably dealing with [specific challenge your audience faces].

And I want you to know: **It's possible to get to the other side.**

You don't need to figure it all out alone. That's exactly why I send these emails.

### Here's How I Can Help Right Now

Depending on where you are in your journey:

**Just getting started?**
→ [Link: Free resource/beginner guide]

→ [Link: Intermediate resource/case study]

**Want personalized help?**

Hit reply and tell me. I genuinely want to know.

Rooting for you,

## Technical Notes

**Goal:** Humanize the brand, build trust through vulnerability  
**Tone:** Personal, authentic, conversational  
**Structure:** Classic story arc (struggle → turning point → resolution)  
**Length:** Medium-long (400-500 words)  
**CTA:** Soft, multiple options based on readiness level
