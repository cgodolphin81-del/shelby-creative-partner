## Report Delivery Options

### Option 1: PDF Report
- Format: Professional PDF with charts
- Delivery: Email attachment
- Best for: Executive summaries, client presentations

### Option 2: Google Slides Deck
- Format: Interactive presentation
- Delivery: Shared Google Slides link
- Best for: Review meetings, collaborative discussions

### Option 3: Live Dashboard
- Format: Google Data Studio / Looker
- Delivery: Shared dashboard link
- Best for: Real-time access, self-serve reporting

### Option 4: Email Summary
- Format: Condensed email with key highlights
- Delivery: Direct email
- Best for: Busy stakeholders, quick updates
