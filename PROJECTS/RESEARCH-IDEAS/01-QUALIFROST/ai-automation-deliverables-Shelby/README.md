## 🛠️ TECH STACK RECOMMENDATIONS

### Core Tools
- **Automation:** Zapier ($20-50/mo) or Make ($29-99/mo)
- **Chatbots:** ManyChat ($15/mo) or Botpress (Free-$$)
- **CRM:** HubSpot (Free), Pipedrive ($15/user/mo), or Airtable ($20/mo)
- **Project Management:** Notion (Free), Asana ($11/user/mo), or ClickUp ($7/user/mo)
- **Documentation:** Google Workspace ($6/user/mo)
- **Video:** Loom (Free-$$$)

### Optional Add-ons
- **Calendar:** Calendly ($12/mo) or Cal.com (Free)
- **Forms:** Typeform ($25/mo) or Google Forms (Free)
- **Email:** Gmail (Free) or ActiveCampaign ($15/mo)
- **Support:** Help Scout ($25/mo) or Zendesk ($19/agent/mo)
