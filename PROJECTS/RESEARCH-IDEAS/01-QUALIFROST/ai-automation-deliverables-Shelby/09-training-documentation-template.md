## PART 3: TROUBLESHOOTING GUIDE

### Quick Fixes

**Problem:** Zap not running
**Check:**
- [ ] Is the Zap turned on?
- [ ] Has the trigger event occurred?
- [ ] Are all apps connected?

**Problem:** Data not appearing correctly
**Check:**
- [ ] Field mappings are correct
- [ ] Data format matches expectations
- [ ] No filters blocking the data

**Problem:** Too many errors
**Check:**
- [ ] API rate limits
- [ ] Authentication expired
- [ ] Source data has changed

### When to Contact Support

**Contact us immediately if:**
- Multiple Zaps failing at once
- Data loss or corruption
- Critical business process broken
- Error message you don't understand

**Contact us during business hours for:**
- Single Zap failures (after 3 retries)
- Questions about functionality
- Requests for changes

**Don't contact us for:**
- Single failed task (just replay it)
- Questions answered in this guide
- Feature requests (use our feedback form)

## PART 4: FAQ

**Q: How do I turn a Zap on/off?**
A: In your Zapier dashboard, click the toggle switch next to the Zap name.

**Q: Can I edit a Zap myself?**
A: You can, but we recommend contacting us first. Small changes are fine, but structural changes can break things.

**Q: How many tasks do I have?**
A: Check your Zapier plan limits in Settings > Usage.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my task limit?**
A: Zaps will pause until the next billing cycle or until you upgrade.

**Q: Can I add new Zaps later?**
A: Absolutely! Contact us for a quote on additional automations.

**Q: How long do you keep task history?**
A: Zapier keeps history for 90 days on most plans.

**Q: What if Zapier goes down?**
A: It's rare, but if it happens, your automations will pause. We monitor uptime and will notify you of any issues.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, each Zap can send data to Google Sheets for backup and export.
