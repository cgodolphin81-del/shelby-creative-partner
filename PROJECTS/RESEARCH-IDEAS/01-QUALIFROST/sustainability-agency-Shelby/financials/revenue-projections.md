# 3-Year Revenue Projections & Financial Model

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the 3-year financial projections for a sustainability consulting agency, including revenue forecasts, expense projections, profitability analysis, and key assumptions.

---

## Key Assumptions

### Market Conditions

- **Market Growth:** Sustainability consulting market growing 15-20% annually
- **Regulatory Drivers:** CSRD, SEC rules, California laws driving demand
- **Competitive Landscape:** Fragmented, room for specialized players
- **Pricing Pressure:** Moderate (value-based pricing sustainable)

### Business Model

- **Revenue Mix:**
  - Year 1: 70% project, 30% retainer
  - Year 2: 55% project, 45% retainer
  - Year 3: 45% project, 55% retainer

- **Average Deal Sizes:**
  - Small projects: $25K-50K
  - Medium projects: $75K-150K
  - Large projects: $200K-500K
  - Retainers: $5K-30K/month

- **Sales Cycle:**
  - Small: 30-60 days
  - Medium: 60-90 days
  - Large: 90-180 days

## Risk Factors

### Market Risks

- **Regulatory Changes:** Delays or weakening of sustainability regulations
- **Economic Downturn:** Sustainability budgets cut first
- **Competition:** Increased competition from large firms
- **Technology Disruption:** AI/automation reducing demand for consulting

### Execution Risks

- **Talent:** Difficulty hiring qualified sustainability professionals
- **Delivery:** Quality issues as scaling
- **Client Concentration:** Over-reliance on few large clients
- **Cash Flow:** Working capital constraints

### Mitigation Strategies

- Diversify client base and service offerings
- Build strong retainer base for stability
- Invest in talent development and retention
- Maintain conservative cash management
- Develop proprietary methodologies and IP
- Build strategic partnerships

---

## Conclusion

The 3-year financial model demonstrates a viable path to building a profitable sustainability consulting agency:

**Year 1:** Prove the model, build case studies, achieve break-even
**Year 2:** Scale delivery, expand team, build retainer base, 12% net margin
**Year 3:** Enterprise clients, premium positioning, 13%+ net margin, $10M+ revenue

**Key Success Factors:**
1. Deliver exceptional client outcomes (drive referrals and retention)
2. Build recurring revenue base (retainers provide stability)
3. Maintain pricing discipline (value-based, not commoditized)
4. Invest in talent (delivery quality is the business)
5. Develop proprietary IP (differentiation and margin protection)

With $500K in seed funding and disciplined execution, the agency can achieve $10M+ revenue within 3 years with strong profitability and multiple exit options.
