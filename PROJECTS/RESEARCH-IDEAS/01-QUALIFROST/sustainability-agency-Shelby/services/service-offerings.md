## Overview

Comprehensive sustainability and carbon management services for organizations committed to environmental responsibility and regulatory compliance.

## 1. Carbon Accounting Services

### What We Deliver
Complete greenhouse gas (GHG) inventory and accounting aligned with international standards.

### Service Components

#### 1.1 Baseline Carbon Footprint Assessment
- **Scope**: Organization-wide GHG inventory
- **Standards**: GHG Protocol Corporate Standard, ISO 14064-1
- **Deliverables**:
  - Emissions baseline report (tCO2e)
  - Scope 1, 2, and 3 breakdown
  - Data quality assessment
  - Uncertainty analysis
  - Benchmarking against industry peers
- **Timeline**: 8-12 weeks
- **Ideal For**: First-time carbon accounting, baseline establishment

#### 1.2 Annual Carbon Inventory
- **Scope**: Yearly emissions tracking and reporting
- **Standards**: GHG Protocol, CDP, TCFD alignment
- **Deliverables**:
  - Annual emissions report
  - Year-over-year comparison
  - Intensity metrics (per revenue, per employee, per unit)
  - Data management system setup
  - Staff training on data collection
- **Timeline**: 6-8 weeks (recurring annually)
- **Ideal For**: Ongoing carbon management, regulatory compliance

#### 1.3 Product Carbon Footprinting
- **Scope**: Lifecycle emissions of specific products/services
- **Standards**: GHG Protocol Product Standard, ISO 14067, PAS 2050
- **Deliverables**:
  - Cradle-to-gate or cradle-to-grave analysis
  - Hotspot identification
  - Reduction opportunity assessment
  - EPD (Environmental Product Declaration) support
  - Carbon labeling guidance
- **Timeline**: 10-16 weeks per product category
- **Ideal For**: Product differentiation, supply chain engagement, eco-labeling

#### 1.4 Scope 3 Deep Dive
- **Scope**: Comprehensive value chain emissions analysis
- **Standards**: GHG Protocol Scope 3 Standard, CDP Scope 3 Guidance
- **Deliverables**:
  - All 15 Scope 3 categories assessment
  - Supplier engagement strategy
  - Spend-based vs. activity-based analysis
  - Priority category identification
  - Supplier data collection framework
- **Timeline**: 12-20 weeks
- **Ideal For**: Mature carbon programs, supply chain decarbonization
