## Role Definitions

### Partner/Principal

**Level:** Executive
**Reports To:** CEO
**Direct Reports:** Managers, Senior Managers

**Responsibilities:**
- Practice area leadership
- Business development (originating $500K+/year)
- Client relationship management (strategic accounts)
- Thought leadership (speaking, publishing)
- Team development and mentoring
- Quality assurance
- Methodology development
- P&L responsibility

**Requirements:**
- 10+ years sustainability/consulting experience
- Proven business development track record
- Deep expertise in one or more areas
- Strong network
- Leadership experience
- Advanced degree preferred (MBA, MS)

**Compensation:**
- Base: $200K-300K
- Bonus: 50-100% of base
- Equity: 1-5%
- **Total:** $300K-600K+

---

### Director

**Level:** Senior Leadership
**Reports To:** Partner/CEO
**Direct Reports:** Managers, Senior Consultants

**Responsibilities:**
- Project delivery oversight (multiple concurrent)
- Client management (key accounts)
- Business development support ($200K+ deals)
- Team management and development
- Quality review
- Methodology contribution
- Proposal leadership

**Requirements:**
- 7-10 years sustainability/consulting experience
- Project management expertise
- Client management experience
- Team leadership
- Strong technical knowledge
- MBA or relevant Master's preferred

**Compensation:**
- Base: $150K-200K
- Bonus: 30-50% of base
- Equity: 0.5-2%
- **Total:** $200K-300K

---

---

### Secondary Channels

**Universities:**
- Target programs (sustainability, environmental science, business)
- Internship programs
- Career fairs
- **Cost:** $5K-20K/year
- **Effectiveness:** High for Analyst/Consultant roles

**Referrals:**
- Employee referral program ($2K-5K bonus)
- Network referrals
- Client referrals
- **Cost:** Variable (bonus-based)
- **Effectiveness:** Very high (quality and retention)

- For senior/hard-to-fill roles

- Work on Climate Summit
- GreenBiz
- Sustainability conferences
- **Cost:** $5K-15K/event
- **Effectiveness:** Medium (networking + hiring)

## Interview Process

### Standard Process (Consultant and Above)

**Round 1: Recruiter Screen (30 min)**
- Background and experience
- Motivation and interest
- Salary expectations
- Logistics (location, availability)

**Round 2: Hiring Manager (45 min)**
- Deep dive on experience
- Technical knowledge
- Case discussion
- Cultural fit

**Round 3: Case Study (Take-home or Live)**
- Relevant sustainability problem
- Analysis and recommendations
- Presentation (30 min)
- Q&A (30 min)

**Round 4: Team Interviews (2-3 sessions, 45 min each)**
- Peer interview
- Cross-functional interview
- Culture add assessment

**Round 5: Final Interview (Partner/Director, 45 min)**
- Strategic thinking
- Career aspirations
- Final assessment
- Offer discussion

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
**Decision:** Consensus-based

---

### Assessment Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | Evaluation Method |
|----------|--------|-------------------|
| Technical Knowledge | 25% | Interview, case study |
| Problem Solving | 20% | Case study, scenarios |
| Communication | 20% | All interviews, presentation |
| Cultural Fit | 15% | Team interviews |
| Client Skills | 10% | Role-play, references |
| Growth Potential | 10% | Career discussion |

### Performance Improvement

**For Developing/Below Performers:**
1. Clear feedback and expectations
2. Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)
3. 30-60-90 day timeline
4. Support and resources
5. Decision point (improvement or exit)

**Principles:**
- Fair and transparent
- Supportive but clear
- Timely decisions
- Dignified exits when needed
