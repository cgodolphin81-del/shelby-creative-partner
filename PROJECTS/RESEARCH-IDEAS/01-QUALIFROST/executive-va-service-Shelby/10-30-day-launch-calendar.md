# 30-Day Launch Calendar

## Executive VA Service Launch Plan

A comprehensive day-by-day guide to launching your executive virtual assistant service from zero to fully operational.

---

## 📋 Overview

### Launch Phases

| Phase | Days | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| **Foundation** | 1-7 | Business setup, positioning, pricing |
| **Assets** | 8-14 | Marketing materials, systems, processes |
| **Outreach** | 15-21 | Lead generation, networking, content |
| **Launch** | 22-30 | Sales calls, onboarding, delivery |

### Success Metrics

By Day 30, you should have:
- [ ] Complete service offerings documented
- [ ] All systems and tools set up
- [ ] Marketing materials ready
- [ ] 10+ qualified leads in pipeline
- [ ] 3+ discovery calls booked
- [ ] 1-2 paying clients onboarded
- [ ] Delivery systems tested and working

## WEEK 1: Foundation (Days 1-7)

### Day 1: Vision & Positioning

**Morning (3 hours)**
- [ ] Define your ideal client profile
  - Industry
  - Company size/revenue
  - Role/title
  - Pain points
  - Budget range
- [ ] Clarify your unique value proposition
  - What makes you different?
  - Why should clients choose you?
  - What's your specialty/niche?
- [ ] Set 90-day goals
  - Revenue target
  - Number of clients
  - Hours per week
  - Income goals

**Afternoon (2 hours)**
- [ ] Choose your business name (if needed)
- [ ] Secure domain name
- [ ] Create professional email address
- [ ] Set up basic business structure
  - Sole proprietorship, LLC, etc.
  - Check local requirements

**Deliverables:**
- Ideal client profile document
- Value proposition statement
- 90-day goal document
- Business email active

## 🆘 When You Get Stuck

### No Responses to Outreach?
- Review your messaging (is it about them?)
- Increase volume (more outreach)
- Improve targeting (better ICP)
- Add more value upfront
- Try different channels

### Calls Not Converting?
- Are you qualifying properly?
- Are you listening enough?
- Is your pricing clear?
- Do you have social proof?
- Practice and refine your script

### Overwhelmed with Delivery?
- Review and optimize systems
- Automate where possible
- Set better boundaries
- Consider raising prices
- Don't take on too much too fast

### Imposter Syndrome?
- Remember: done is better than perfect
- You don't need to know everything
- You can learn as you go
- Your clients need help NOW
- You have value to offer
