## ❓ FAQ

**Q: What happens if I don't use all my hours?**
A: Starter hours don't rollover. Professional allows 5 hours rollover (60 days). Executive allows 10 hours rollover (90 days). We'll proactively communicate if you're underutilizing.

**Q: Can I change packages mid-month?**
A: Package changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. We can discuss urgent needs on a case-by-case basis.

**Q: What if I need more hours than my package includes?**
A: Overage hours are available at the package rate. We'll always get approval before exceeding your package. Consistent overages suggest an upgrade might be more economical.

**Q: Do you work with multiple clients?**
A: Yes, but each client gets a dedicated VA. Your VA's capacity is managed to ensure your needs are met. Executive clients get priority scheduling.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied?**
A: We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If we're not the right fit, we'll help transition you or refund the first month.

**Q: Can I meet my VA before committing?**
A: Absolutely! We offer a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to ensure good fit before any commitment.

**Q: Do you provide equipment and software?**
A: Yes, all standard tools and software are included. Specialized software requested by client may be billed separately.

**Q: What time zones do you support?**
A: Our standard hours are ET, but we have VAs in multiple time zones. International and after-hours coverage available as add-ons.
