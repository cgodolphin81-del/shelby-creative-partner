Professional virtual assistant services designed for executives, entrepreneurs, and busy professionals. All services delivered remotely with white-glove attention to detail.

## 📬 Inbox Management

### What's Included
- **Daily Inbox Triage**: Process all incoming emails, flag urgent items, archive/delete clutter
- **Priority Response Drafting**: Draft responses for your review on high-priority messages
- **Newsletter & Subscription Management**: Unsubscribe from noise, organize valuable content
- **Email Filing System**: Maintain organized folder/label structure for easy retrieval
- **Follow-up Tracking**: Never lose track of pending responses or action items

### Deliverables
- Daily inbox summary report (urgent items, pending responses, action required)
- Organized email folders with consistent naming conventions
- Draft responses ready for your one-click send
- Weekly newsletter digest of must-read content

### Time Commitment
- Light: 2-3 hrs/week (50-100 emails/day)
- Standard: 5-7 hrs/week (100-200 emails/day)
- Heavy: 10+ hrs/week (200+ emails/day)
