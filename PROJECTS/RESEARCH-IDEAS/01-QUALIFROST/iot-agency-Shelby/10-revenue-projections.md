## Scenario Analysis

### Conservative Scenario

**Assumptions:**
- Slower sales ramp (70% of moderate)
- Lower average project size (-15%)
- Lower attachment rate for recurring (-20%)
- Higher customer churn (15% vs 10%)

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Revenue | $1.2M | $2.0M | $2.5M |
| Gross Margin | 40% | 45% | 55% |
| Operating Expenses | $1.5M | $2.2M | $2.8M |
| Operating Income | -$1.0M | -$1.3M | -$1.4M |
| Cash Needed | $1.5M | $2.0M | $2.5M |

**Outcome:** Not profitable by Year 3. Requires pivot or additional funding.

---

## Conclusion

The IoT Solutions Agency has significant revenue potential with a clear path to profitability:

**Year 1:** Foundation building, expected losses as we invest in team and capabilities
**Year 2:** Growth phase, approaching break-even with scale
**Year 3:** Profitability achieved with strong recurring revenue base

**Key Success Factors:**
1. Consistent project delivery (drives referrals)
2. Recurring revenue attachment (60%+ of projects)
3. Margin discipline (30%+ gross margin)
4. Cash flow management (project financing)
5. Team building (hire A-players)

**Capital Required:** $2-4M depending on growth pace
**Expected Return:** 5-10x in 5-7 years (exit or dividends)
