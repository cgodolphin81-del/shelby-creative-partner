# Pricing Models

## Overview

This document outlines comprehensive pricing strategies for IoT solutions, including project-based pricing, recurring revenue models, and value-based pricing approaches.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Principles
1. **Value-Based:** Price based on value delivered, not just costs
2. **Transparent:** Clear breakdown of what's included
3. **Flexible:** Options for different budget levels
4. **Profitable:** Minimum 30% gross margin on all projects
5. **Competitive:** Market-aware but not race-to-bottom

### Pricing Strategy
- **Entry-level:** Competitive pricing to build portfolio
- **Mid-market:** Value-based pricing with clear ROI
- **Enterprise:** Premium pricing with full service

## Value-Based Pricing

### ROI-Based Pricing

For projects with clear ROI, consider value-based pricing:

**Formula:**
```
Price = (Annual Value Created × 0.5) + Costs
```

**Example:**
- Predictive maintenance saves $500K/year in downtime
- Value-based price: ($500K × 0.5) + $100K costs = $350K
- Client still sees $150K net benefit Year 1

### Risk-Based Pricing

| Risk Level | Premium | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| Low | Standard | Standard smart home |
| Medium | +10-20% | Complex integration |
| High | +25-50% | Mission-critical, tight timeline |
| Extreme | +50-100% | Regulatory, safety-critical |

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- **Deposit:** 30-50% upon contract signing
- **Milestone 1:** 25% upon design approval
- **Milestone 2:** 25% upon installation completion
- **Final:** 25% upon project closeout

### Large Projects ($100K+)
- **Deposit:** 30%
- **Monthly Progress:** Based on % complete
- **Retention:** 10% held for 30 days post-completion

### Retainer Services
- **Payment:** Monthly in advance
- **Term:** Minimum 12 months preferred
- **Auto-renew:** Unless cancelled with 60-day notice

### Payment Methods
- ACH/Wire (preferred)
- Check
- Credit card (3% fee for balances >$10K)

### Late Payment
- **Net 30:** Standard terms
- **Late Fee:** 1.5% per month (18% APR)
- **Suspension:** Services suspended after 60 days
- **Collections:** After 90 days

## Change Orders

### Change Order Pricing

| Change Type | Pricing |
|-------------|---------|
| Additional Devices | Cost + 30% + installation |
| Additional Features | Estimated hours × rate |
| Timeline Acceleration | +25-50% premium |
| Scope Reduction | Credit at 80% of original |
| Rework (client-caused) | Time + materials |
| Rework (our error) | No charge |

### Change Order Process
1. Change request documented
2. Impact analysis (cost, timeline)
3. Client approval required
4. Work begins after approval
5. Invoice per standard terms

## Competitive Positioning

### Price Positioning

| Position | Strategy | Target Margin |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| Premium | Best service, highest quality | 35-45% |
| Value | Good balance of price/quality | 25-35% |
| Economy | Price-focused, basic service | 15-25% |

**Recommended:** Value to Premium positioning
- Compete on value, not price
- Clear differentiation
- Justify premium with service quality

### Competitive Response

| Competitor Action | Our Response |
|-------------------|--------------|
| Undercutting price | Emphasize value, quality, support |
| Matching features | Highlight expertise, track record |
| New market entrant | Leverage experience, references |
