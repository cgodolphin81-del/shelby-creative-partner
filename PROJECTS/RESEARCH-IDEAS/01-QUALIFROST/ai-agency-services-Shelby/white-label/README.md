## Program Overview

### Value Proposition

**For Partners:**
- Expand service offerings without building AI expertise
- Leverage proven methodologies and tools
- Focus on client relationships while we deliver
- Competitive margins and recurring revenue
- Differentiate in crowded market

**For End Clients:**
- Access to AI expertise through trusted advisor
- Faster time to value
- Lower risk with proven solutions
- Single point of accountability

### Program Benefits

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Ready-to-Sell** | Pre-packaged services with pricing |

# Pricing Guidelines

## Pricing Principles

1. **Value-Based:** Price based on value delivered, not hours
2. **Transparent:** Clear pricing in partner portal
3. **Protected:** Minimum margins guaranteed by tier
4. **Flexible:** Custom pricing for enterprise deals
5. **Recurring:** Focus on ongoing revenue, not one-time

## Discount Authority

| Role | Max Discount | Approval Required |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| Partner | 10% | None |
| Partner Manager | 15% | None |
| Director | 25% | VP Sales |
| VP Sales | 35% | CEO (if >$500K) |
| CEO | Unlimited | Board (if >$2M) |

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Net 30 for established partners
- Credit check for terms > Net 30

### Milestone Billing (Large Projects)
- 30% on signing
- 30% on midpoint delivery
- 30% on final delivery
- 10% after 30-day acceptance

### Managed Services
- Monthly in advance
- Annual prepay discount: 10%
- Auto-renew unless cancelled

## Price Protection

### Deal Registration
- Register opportunity in partner portal
- 90-day protection period
- Exclusive during protection
- Must show active engagement

### Price Changes
- 90-day notice for price increases
- Existing deals grandfathered
- Annual price review

## Competitive Situations

### Competitive Discounting
- Match competitor pricing (with proof)
- Maintain minimum 20% margin
- Executive approval required

### Loss Leader Strategy
- Strategic deals for market entry
- Requires CEO approval
- Clear path to profitability

## Margin Protection

### Minimum Margins
- Registered: 30%
- Silver: 35%
- Gold: 40%
- Platinum: 45%

### Margin Erosion Prevention
- Regular price reviews
- Cost tracking
- Efficiency improvements
- Value communication
