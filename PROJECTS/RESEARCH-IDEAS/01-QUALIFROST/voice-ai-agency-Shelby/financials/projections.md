## 5. Unit Economics

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

```
Average Customer Profile:
- Implementation: $80K (one-time)
- Monthly recurring: $8K
- Gross margin: 65%
- Average lifespan: 36 months

LTV Calculation:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
Implementation margin: $80K × 55% = $44K
Monthly margin: $8K × 65% = $5.2K/month
Annual recurring margin: $5.2K × 12 = $62.4K
3-year recurring margin: $62.4K × 3 = $187.2K

Total LTV (gross margin): $44K + $187.2K = $231.2K
```

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

```
Annual Sales & Marketing: $300K (Year 1)
New Customers (Year 1): 25

CAC = $300K / 25 = $12K per customer

LTV:CAC Ratio = $231.2K : $12K = 19:1
(Excellent - target is 3:1 or better)
```

### Payback Period

```
First-year margin per customer:
- Implementation: $44K
- Recurring (Year 1): $62.4K
- Total Year 1 margin: $106.4K

CAC: $12K

Payback Period = $12K / ($106.4K/12 months) = 1.4 months

(Excellent - target is <12 months)
```
