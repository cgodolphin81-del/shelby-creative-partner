## 📧 Sequence 1: Cold Outreach (5 Emails)

Worth a 15-minute chat to see if there's a fit?

Best,
[Your Name]

## 🎯 Best Practices

1. **Personalize** - Reference something specific about their business
2. **Keep it short** - Under 100 words for cold emails
3. **One CTA** - One clear ask per email
4. **Follow up** - Most replies come on email 3-5
5. **Test subject lines** - A/B test different approaches
