## 🎯 Usage Rights by Asset Type

### Logos
- **Personal:** Use for your single brand only
- **Commercial:** Use for up to 10 client brands
- **Extended:** Unlimited client use, trademark allowed*

*Note: Base logo templates cannot be trademarked. Significant modifications required.

### Color Palettes
- **All Licenses:** Unlimited use
- Colors cannot be copyrighted
- Free to use in any project

### Typography
- **All Licenses:** Follow individual font licenses
- Google Fonts: Free for all uses
- Premium Fonts: Purchase separately if required

### Templates (Social Media, Print, etc.)
- **Personal:** Your brand only
- **Commercial:** Your brand + 10 clients
- **Extended:** Unlimited clients

### Brand Guidelines
- **Personal:** Your brand documentation
- **Commercial:** Create for up to 10 clients
- **Extended:** Unlimited client documentation

### Mockups
- **All Licenses:** Use in presentations, portfolios, marketing
- Cannot resell mockup files themselves
