## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Your name/agency] helps families find their perfect home in [area].
With [X] years of experience and [X] homes sold, we're your 
trusted local experts.

Mission: Help every client achieve their real estate goals
Vision: Be the most trusted agency in [area]
Values: Integrity, Excellence, Communication, Results
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 0.75 inch width (print), 80px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of house icon on all sides
- **Don'ts:** Don't recolor, stretch, add effects, or rotate

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Use for logo, main headings (60%)
- **Secondary:** Use for buttons, accents (30%)
- **Accent:** Use for CTAs, important info (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Montserrat Bold, 42px
- **H2:** Montserrat SemiBold, 32px
- **H3:** Montserrat, 24px
- **Body:** Open Sans, 16px
- **Caption:** Open Sans Light, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Professional, friendly, knowledgeable
- **Language:** Clear, local-focused, action-oriented
- **Avoid:** Overpromising, jargon, negativity about competitors

### 6. Photo Style
- Professional headshots
- High-quality property photos
- Consistent editing/filters
- Local landmarks/neighborhoods
- Happy clients (with permission)
