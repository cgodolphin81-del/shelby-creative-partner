## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Restaurant name] serves [cuisine type] made with [unique quality].
We bring people together through [value proposition].

Mission: Create memorable dining experiences
Vision: Be the community's favorite gathering place
Values: Quality, Hospitality, Authenticity, Community
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 0.75 inch width (print), 80px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of logo
- **Don'ts:** Don't recolor, stretch, distort

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Main brand (60%)
- **Secondary:** Supporting (30%)
- **Accent:** CTAs, specials (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Playfair Display Bold, 48px
- **H2:** Playfair Display, 36px
- **H3:** Lato Bold, 24px
- **Body:** Lato, 16px
- **Caption:** Lato Light, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Welcoming, appetizing, authentic
- **Language:** Descriptive, warm, inviting
- **Avoid:** Overpromising, generic descriptions

### 6. Photography Style
- Natural lighting
- Appetizing angles
- Steam/freshness
- Lifestyle shots
- Consistent filters
