## 📄 Brand Guidelines Highlights

### Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Confident, aspirational, on-trend
- **Language:** Fashion-forward, descriptive, engaging
- **Avoid:** Outdated terms, overly casual, generic descriptions

### Imagery Style
- High-fashion photography
- Consistent filters
- Diverse models
- Lifestyle context
- Clean backgrounds
