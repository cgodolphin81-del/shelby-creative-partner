# Revenue Projections (3-Year Model)

## Executive Summary

HealthAI Consulting Group projects strong growth over the first three years, reaching profitability in Year 1 and achieving $25M+ in annual recurring revenue by Year 3. This document outlines our financial projections, assumptions, and key metrics.

---

## Key Assumptions

### Market Assumptions
- Healthcare AI market growing at 45% CAGR
- Increasing regulatory pressure driving compliance needs
- Physician burnout creating demand for automation
- Value-based care expansion driving analytics needs
- Hospital margins under pressure requiring efficiency

### Sales Assumptions
- Average sales cycle: 6 months (enterprise), 3 months (SMB)
- Win rate: 30% (qualified opportunities)
- Pipeline coverage: 3x quota
- Customer acquisition cost: $50K - $150K
- Customer lifetime: 3-5 years
- Net revenue retention: 120%

### Delivery Assumptions
- Utilization rate: 75% (billable time)
- Average project margin: 35-45%
- Managed services margin: 60-70%
- Implementation timeline: 3-6 months average
- Client satisfaction: >4.5/5
