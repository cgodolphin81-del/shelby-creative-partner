# Client Acquisition Strategy

## Overview

HealthAI Consulting Group employs a multi-channel client acquisition strategy focused on building trust, demonstrating expertise, and creating long-term partnerships with healthcare organizations.

---

## Target Market Segments

### Primary Segments (Year 1-2)

#### 1. Regional Health Systems (50-300 beds)
**Characteristics:**
- Revenue: $200M - $1B
- IT Budget: $10M - $50M
- Multiple service lines
- Value-based care contracts
- Competitive market pressure

**Pain Points:**
- Margin pressure
- Physician burnout
- Administrative burden
- Quality measure performance
- Digital transformation needs

**Decision Makers:**
- CEO, CMO, CIO, CFO
- VP Medical Affairs
- VP Quality
- Service Line Leaders

**Average Deal Size:** $300,000 - $750,000
**Sales Cycle:** 6-9 months

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Tier 1 (Best Fit)
- Revenue: $100M - $500M
- IT maturity: Moderate to high
- Innovation culture: Yes
- Decision speed: Moderate
- Budget availability: Yes
- Competitive pressure: High
- **Close Rate Target:** 40%+

### Tier 2 (Good Fit)
- Revenue: $50M - $100M or $500M - $1B
- IT maturity: Any
- Innovation culture: Emerging
- Decision speed: Slow to moderate
- Budget availability: Moderate
- Competitive pressure: Moderate
- **Close Rate Target:** 25-35%

### Tier 3 (Develop)
- Revenue: <$50M or >$1B
- IT maturity: Low or very high
- Innovation culture: Low
- Decision speed: Very slow
- Budget availability: Limited
- Competitive pressure: Low
- **Close Rate Target:** 10-20%

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Prospecting (Week 1-2)
**Activities:**
- Identify target accounts
- Research decision makers
- Initial outreach
- Qualification

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- **Budget:** $100K+ available
- **Authority:** Access to decision makers
- **Need:** Clear pain point identified
- **Timeline:** Decision within 6 months

**Exit Criteria:** Qualified meeting scheduled

---

### Stage 2: Discovery (Week 3-4)
**Activities:**
- Initial discovery call (60 min)
- Stakeholder mapping
- Pain point validation
- Preliminary solution discussion
- Technical discovery (if needed)

**Deliverables:**
- Discovery summary
- Preliminary ROI estimate
- Next steps proposal

**Exit Criteria:** Mutual interest confirmed, proposal requested

---

### Stage 3: Proposal (Week 5-8)
**Activities:**
- Solution design
- Pricing development
- Proposal creation
- Internal review
- Client presentation

**Deliverables:**
- Formal proposal
- ROI analysis
- Implementation plan
- Reference contacts

**Exit Criteria:** Proposal presented, questions addressed

---

### Stage 4: Evaluation (Week 9-16)
**Activities:**
- Client internal review
- Additional stakeholder meetings
- Technical deep dives
- Reference calls
- Security/compliance review
- Contract negotiation

**Deliverables:**
- Answers to RFP/RFI (if applicable)
- Security documentation
- Draft contracts
- Final pricing

**Exit Criteria:** Verbal commitment or decision

---

### Stage 5: Close (Week 17-24)
**Activities:**
- Final contract negotiation
- Legal review
- Executive alignment
- Signature collection
- Kickoff scheduling

**Deliverables:**
- Executed contracts
- Project charter
- Kickoff presentation

**Exit Criteria:** Deal closed, project initiated

## Compensation Structure

### Account Executive
- Base Salary: $120,000 - $150,000
- Commission: 10% of quota attainment
- Accelerator: 15% above 100% quota
- OTE at 100%: $240,000 - $300,000
- Quota: $2M - $3M/year

### Sales Development Representative
- Base Salary: $60,000 - $75,000
- Commission: $500-1,000 per qualified meeting
- Bonus: $2,000 per closed deal (from their meetings)
- OTE: $90,000 - $120,000
- Target: 8-10 qualified meetings/month

### Solutions Architect
- Base Salary: $140,000 - $180,000

### VP Sales

## Competitive Positioning

### Key Competitors

|------------|-----------|------------|---------------|
| Big 4 Consulting | Brand, resources | Expensive, less specialized | Healthcare AI focus, agility |

| AI Boutiques | Technical excellence | Limited healthcare compliance | HIPAA expertise built-in |

### Differentiation Strategy

1. **Healthcare + AI Expertise**
   - Not just AI experts who learned healthcare
   - Not just healthcare experts who learned AI
   - True hybrid expertise

2. **Compliance First**
   - HIPAA built into everything
   - Not an afterthought
   - Reduced risk for clients

3. **Proven Methodology**
   - HEALTH-AI Framework
   - 100+ implementations
   - Documented best practices

4. **Outcome Focused**
   - ROI guarantees (select engagements)
   - Performance-based pricing options
   - Success metrics defined upfront

5. **Partnership Model**
   - Not vendor-client relationship
   - Long-term partnership
   - Shared success
