# Growth Marketing Playbook

Comprehensive guide to SaaS growth across all channels.

---

## Growth Framework

### The Growth Equation
```
Revenue = Traffic × Conversion Rate × Average Deal Size × Retention

Growth levers:
1. Increase traffic (more visitors)
2. Improve conversion (more signups)
3. Raise prices/deal size (more revenue per customer)
4. Reduce churn (longer lifetime)
```

### Growth Stage Framework

| Stage | Focus | Budget | Team |
|-------|-------|--------|------|
| **Pre-PMF** | Customer discovery | $0-5K | Founder-led |
| **Early PMF** | Channel testing | $5-20K/mo | 1-2 marketers |
| **Growth** | Scale winners | $20-100K/mo | 3-10 marketers |
| **Scale** | Optimize + expand | $100K+/mo | 10+ marketers |

## 1. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

### SEO for SaaS: The Fundamentals

#### Keyword Strategy
| Keyword Type | Intent | Difficulty | Priority |
|--------------|--------|------------|----------|
| **Branded** | "[Your Product]" | Easy | High |
| **Category** | "[Category] software" | Medium | High |
| **Problem** | "How to [solve problem]" | Medium | High |
| **Comparison** | "[A] vs [B]" | Medium | High |
| **Feature** | "[Feature] tool" | Low-Medium | Medium |
| **Long-tail** | Specific queries | Low | Medium |

#### Content Pillars
```
Pillar Page: "Complete Guide to [Category]"
├── Cluster: "Best [Category] Tools"
├── Cluster: "[Category] Pricing Guide"
├── Cluster: "How to Choose [Category]"

## 2. Content Marketing

### Content Distribution Framework

```
Create Once, Distribute Everywhere (CODE)

1 Long-form Piece
├── 5-10 Social Posts
├── 1 Newsletter
├── 2-3 LinkedIn Articles
├── 1 YouTube Video
├── 5-10 Short Videos (TikTok/Reels)
├── 1 Podcast Episode
├── 3-5 Community Posts
└── 1 Press Release (if newsworthy)
```

### Content Types by Funnel Stage

#### Top of Funnel (Awareness)
- Blog posts (educational)
- Social media content
- YouTube videos
- Podcasts
- Infographics
- Industry reports

#### Middle of Funnel (Consideration)
- Case studies
- Comparison guides
- Webinars
- Email courses
- Templates/tools
- Product demos

#### Bottom of Funnel (Decision)
- Free trials
- Demos
- Customer testimonials
- ROI calculators
- Implementation guides
- Security/compliance docs

### Content Production Workflow

```
1. Research (2-3 hrs)
   ├── Keyword research
   ├── Competitor analysis
   └── Expert interviews

2. Outline (1 hr)
   ├── Structure
   ├── Key points
   └── CTA placement

3. Write (3-4 hrs)
   ├── First draft
   ├── Add visuals
   └── Internal links

4. Edit (1-2 hrs)
   ├── Proofread
   ├── SEO optimization
   └── Fact-check

5. Publish & Distribute (2 hrs)
   ├── Upload to CMS
   ├── Social promotion
   └── Email newsletter
```

### Content Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Blog traffic growth | 15-25%/mo |
| Time on page | 2+ minutes |
| Bounce rate | < 60% |
| Social shares | 50+ per post |
| Lead generation | 2-5% conversion |

### Channel Comparison

| Channel | Best For | CAC Range | Ramp Time |
|---------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Google Search** | High intent | $50-500 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Google Display** | Retargeting | $20-100 | 2-4 weeks |
| **Facebook/IG** | B2C, SMB | $30-200 | 4-8 weeks |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, Enterprise | $100-1000 | 4-8 weeks |
| **Twitter/X** | Tech, developers | $50-300 | 4-6 weeks |
| **Reddit** | Niche communities | $30-150 | 4-6 weeks |
| **YouTube** | Visual products | $40-250 | 6-10 weeks |

### Google Ads Strategy

#### Campaign Structure
```
Account
├── Campaign: Brand
│   └── Ad Group: [Company Name]
├── Campaign: Competitor
│   ├── Ad Group: [Competitor 1]
│   └── Ad Group: [Competitor 2]
├── Campaign: Category
│   ├── Ad Group: [Primary Keyword]
│   └── Ad Group: [Secondary Keyword]
└── Campaign: Retargeting
    └── Ad Group: Website Visitors
```

#### Quality Score Optimization
- **Expected CTR:** Write compelling ad copy
- **Ad Relevance:** Match keywords to ad groups
- **Landing Page:** Relevant, fast, mobile-friendly

#### Ad Copy Template
```
Headline 1: [Primary Keyword/Benefit]
Headline 2: [Unique Value Proposition]
Headline 3: [Call to Action]

Description 1: [Problem they have]
Description 2: [How you solve it + CTA]

Extensions:
- Sitelinks (4+)
- Callouts (4+)
- Structured snippets
- Price extensions
```

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

#### Audience Strategy
| Audience | Size | Purpose |
|----------|------|---------|
| Lookalike 1% | 1-2M | Best performers |
| Lookalike 3% | 3-5M | Scale |
| Interest-based | 1-5M | Testing |

#### Creative Best Practices
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Text overlay for sound-off
- Show product in action
- Social proof (logos, testimonials)
- Clear CTA
- A/B test 3-5 creatives minimum

### Paid Media Budget Allocation

#### Scale Stage ($50K+/mo)
- 35% Google Search
- 25% LinkedIn/Facebook
- 20% Retargeting
- 20% Testing/Expansion

## 4. Referral Marketing

### Referral Program Design

#### Incentive Structures
| Type | Example | Best For |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Single-sided** | Give $20, get $20 | General |
| **Double-sided** | Both get $25 | High value |
| **Tiered** | More referrals = better rewards | Power users |
| **Non-monetary** | Features, status, swag | Community-driven |

#### Referral Program Checklist
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Easy sharing (1-2 clicks)
- [ ] Multiple channels (email, social, link)
- [ ] Real-time tracking
- [ ] Automatic reward delivery
- [ ] Clear terms and conditions
- [ ] Fraud prevention

### Referral Email Templates

#### Initial Ask
```
Subject: Love [Product]? Share with a friend!

Hi [Name],

Thanks for being a [Product] user! If you're enjoying 
[specific benefit], we'd love if you shared us with 
a friend or colleague.

When they sign up, you BOTH get [reward]:
🎁 You: [Reward]
🎁 Them: [Reward]

[Share Button] [Copy Link]

Thanks for spreading the word!
```

#### Follow-Up (After Value Delivered)
```
Subject: Quick favor?

Hi [Name],

I noticed you've [achieved specific outcome] with 
[Product] – that's awesome!

Know anyone else who could use [benefit]? A quick 
intro would mean a lot, and you'll both get [reward].

[Share Button]

Cheers!
```

### Referral Metrics
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Participation rate | 5-15% |
| Referral conversion | 10-25% |
| K-factor | 0.5-1.5 |
| CAC (referral) | 50-70% lower than paid |
| LTV (referral) | 20-30% higher |

- Technical integration
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Example:** Slack + Zoom integration

#### Channel Partners
- Resellers
- Agencies
- Consultants

**Example:** Shopify Experts program

#### Co-Marketing Partners
- Similar audience, non-competing
- Joint content
- Webinar swaps
- Bundle offers

**Example:** HubSpot + LinkedIn

### Partnership Outreach Template
```
Subject: Partnership opportunity: [Their Company] + [Your Company]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Your Company]. We help [target customer] 
[achieve outcome] – similar to what you do for [their value prop].

## 6. Community Marketing

#### Platform Selection
| Platform | Best For | Effort |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Slack** | Active engagement | High |
| **Discord** | Tech, gaming, Web3 | High |
| **Facebook Groups** | Broad audiences | Medium |
