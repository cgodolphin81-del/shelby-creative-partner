# Client Onboarding Template

Complete onboarding process for SaaS service clients.

---

## Onboarding Overview

**Goal:** Set clients up for success, gather critical information, and establish working rhythms.

**Timeline:** Week 1-2 of engagement

**Owner:** Account Manager / Project Lead

### Pre-Kickoff Questionnaire

```
CLIENT ONBOARDING QUESTIONNAIRE

SECTION 1: Business Overview
1. What does your company do? (2-3 sentences)
2. What problem do you solve for customers?
3. Who is your target customer?
4. What's your current MRR/revenue?
5. What are your growth goals for the next 6-12 months?

SECTION 2: Product/Service
6. Describe your product/service
7. What are your key features?
8. What makes you different from competitors?
9. What's your pricing model?
10. What's your current conversion rate?

SECTION 3: Current State
11. What's working well right now?
12. What are your biggest challenges?
13. What have you tried before? What worked/didn't?
14. What tools/platforms are you currently using?
15. Do you have existing brand guidelines?

SECTION 4: Goals & Success
16. What does success look like for this project?
17. What are your top 3 priorities?
18. What metrics will you use to measure success?
19. What's your timeline/deadline?
20. What's your budget range?

SECTION 5: Team & Process
21. Who are the key stakeholders?
22. Who has final decision-making authority?
23. How do you prefer to communicate? (email, Slack, calls)
24. What's your preferred meeting cadence?
25. Any important dates/deadlines we should know?
```

## Phase 7: Client Success

### Health Score Framework

```
CLIENT HEALTH SCORE

ENGAGEMENT (30%)
□ Meeting attendance (>90% = 10, <50% = 0)
□ Response time (<24hr = 10, >72hr = 0)
□ Stakeholder involvement (High = 10, Low = 0)

DELIVERY (30%)
□ On-time delivery (>95% = 10, <80% = 0)
□ Quality score (>9/10 = 10, <6/10 = 0)
□ Scope adherence (On track = 10, Over = 0)

RESULTS (30%)
□ Goal progress (>100% = 10, <50% = 0)
□ ROI achieved (Positive = 10, Negative = 0)
□ Satisfaction score (>9/10 = 10, <6/10 = 0)

RELATIONSHIP (10%)
□ NPS score (10 = 10, 0 = 0)
□ Reference willingness (Yes = 10, No = 0)

TOTAL SCORE: __/100

HEALTH STATUS:
90-100: 🟢 Excellent
70-89: 🟡 Good
50-69: 🟠 At Risk
<50: 🔴 Critical
```

### Check-In Cadence

```
HEALTHY CLIENT (80-100)
- Regular cadence
- Standard reporting
- Quarterly business reviews

AT RISK (50-79)
- Increased check-ins
- Bi-weekly reporting
- Action plan required

CRITICAL (<50)
- Weekly check-ins
- Executive involvement
- Recovery plan
- Consider escalation
```

### Escalation Path

```
LEVEL 1: Account Manager
- First point of contact
- Day-to-day issues
- Resolution within 48 hours

LEVEL 2: Department Head
- Unresolved Level 1 issues
- Strategic concerns
- Resolution within 1 week

LEVEL 3: Leadership
- Critical issues
- Relationship at risk
- Immediate attention
```

## Appendices

### A: Communication Guidelines

```
COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

RESPONSE TIMES
- Email: Within 24 hours (business days)
- Slack: Within 4 hours (business hours)
- Urgent: Call/text immediately

MEETING ETIQUETTE
- Send agenda 24 hours in advance
- Start and end on time
- Assign note-taker
- Send notes within 24 hours

DECISION MAKING
- Document all decisions
- Include context and rationale
- Share with all stakeholders
- Track in decision log
```
