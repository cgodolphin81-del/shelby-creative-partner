## 📊 PART 3: REVIEW MANAGEMENT WORKFLOW

### Daily Tasks (10-15 minutes)
- [ ] Check for new reviews on all platforms
- [ ] Respond to any new reviews (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Flag suspicious/fake reviews
- [ ] Log reviews in tracking spreadsheet

### Weekly Tasks (30 minutes)
- [ ] Send review requests to recent customers (10-20)
- [ ] Review response templates for quality
- [ ] Identify customers for testimonial requests
- [ ] Share positive reviews on social media

### Monthly Tasks (1 hour)
- [ ] Analyze review trends and patterns
- [ ] Update review request templates if needed
- [ ] Train team on review generation best practices
- [ ] Set monthly review goals (e.g., 10 new reviews)
- [ ] Report review metrics to stakeholders

## ⚠️ COMPLIANCE & ETHICS

### DO:
✅ Ask all customers for reviews
✅ Make it easy to leave reviews
✅ Respond to all reviews professionally
✅ Report fake/spam reviews
✅ Incentivize reviews generally (not positive-only)

### DON'T:
❌ Offer incentives for positive reviews only
❌ Write fake reviews (even for yourself)
❌ Ask employees to write reviews
❌ Review bomb competitors
❌ Ignore negative reviews
❌ Argue with reviewers publicly

### FTC Guidelines:
- Disclose any material connections
- Don't incentivize only positive reviews
- Genuine opinions only
- No misleading claims
