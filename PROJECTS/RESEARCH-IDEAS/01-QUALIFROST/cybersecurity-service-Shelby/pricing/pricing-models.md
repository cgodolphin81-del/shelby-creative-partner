## Pricing Philosophy

Our pricing reflects the value we deliver, the expertise required, and the risk we assume on behalf of our clients. We offer transparent, predictable pricing with flexible engagement models.

### Pricing Principles
- **Value-based:** Prices reflect business impact, not just hours worked
- **Transparent:** No hidden fees or surprise charges
- **Flexible:** Multiple engagement models to fit client needs
- **Scalable:** Pricing grows with client requirements
- **Competitive:** Market-aligned with premium service justification

## Pricing Models

### 1. Fixed-Price Projects

**Best For:** Well-defined scopes, audits, assessments, implementations

**Structure:**
- Detailed scope of work
- Fixed deliverables
- Defined timeline
- Change order process for scope changes

**Payment Terms:**
- 30% upfront
- 40% at midpoint
- 30% at completion

**Advantages:**
- Predictable cost for client
- Clear expectations
- Easy budgeting

**Disadvantages:**
- Requires detailed scoping
- Change orders for scope creep
- Risk borne by provider

---

### 2. Time & Materials

**Best For:** Ongoing work, undefined scopes, advisory services

**Structure:**
- Hourly or daily rates
- Not-to-exceed caps (optional)
- Weekly or monthly billing

**Rates:**
| Role | Hourly Rate | Daily Rate |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Security Analyst | $175 - $250 | $1,400 - $2,000 |
| Senior Consultant | $250 - $350 | $2,000 - $2,800 |
| Principal Consultant | $350 - $450 | $2,800 - $3,600 |
| Managing Consultant | $450 - $600 | $3,600 - $4,800 |

**Payment Terms:**
- Net 15 or Net 30
- Weekly or monthly invoicing
- Detailed time tracking

**Advantages:**
- Flexible scope
- Pay for actual work
- Easy to adjust priorities

**Disadvantages:**
- Less predictable cost
- Requires trust
- More oversight needed

---

### 3. Retainer Agreements

**Best For:** Ongoing advisory, compliance support, priority access

**Structure:**
- Monthly recurring fee
- Pre-purchased hours or services
- Rollover options (partial or none)
- Priority scheduling

**Tiers:**

| Tier | Monthly Price | Hours | Rate | Rollover |
|------|---------------|-------|------|----------|
| **Silver** | $5,000 | 20 hours | $250/hour | 50% |
| **Gold** | $10,000 | 45 hours | $222/hour | 50% |
| **Platinum** | $20,000 | 100 hours | $200/hour | 25% |
| **Enterprise** | $50,000 | 300 hours | $167/hour | None |

**Payment Terms:**
- Monthly in advance
- Quarterly or annual discounts (5-10%)
- Unused hours expire per rollover policy

**Advantages:**
- Predictable monthly cost
- Priority access
- Discounted rates
- Flexible usage

**Disadvantages:**
- Use-it-or-lose-it pressure
- Requires planning
- May not use full allocation

---

### 4. Subscription/Managed Services

**Best For:** Ongoing monitoring, continuous services

**Structure:**
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Tiered service levels
- Annual contracts (typically)
- Automatic renewal

**Payment Terms:**
- Monthly or annual billing
- Annual prepay discount (10-15%)
- 30-day cancellation notice
- 12-month minimum typical

**Advantages:**
- Predictable revenue
- Long-term client relationships
- Scalable pricing
- Easy to budget

**Disadvantages:**
- Higher commitment
- Service delivery must be consistent
- Churn risk

## ROI Calculator for Clients

### Cost of Breach Avoidance

```
Expected Annual Loss = (Annual Breach Probability × Average Breach Cost)

Industry Average Breach Cost (2025):
- Small Business: $150,000
- Mid-Market: $500,000
- Enterprise: $4,000,000

Security Investment ROI = (Avoided Loss - Security Investment) / Security Investment × 100

Example:
Mid-market company with 20% annual breach probability
- Expected annual loss: $500,000 × 20% = $100,000
- Security investment: $50,000/year
- Risk reduction: 70%
- Avoided loss: $100,000 × 70% = $70,000
- ROI: ($70,000 - $50,000) / $50,000 × 100 = 40%
```

### Compliance Value

```
Compliance Investment Value = (Avoided Fines + Business Enablement + Risk Reduction)

Example SOC 2 Investment:
- Investment: $75,000
- Avoided potential fines: $200,000+
- New revenue enabled: $500,000 (enterprise customers)
- Risk reduction value: $100,000
- Total value: $800,000
- ROI: 967%
```
