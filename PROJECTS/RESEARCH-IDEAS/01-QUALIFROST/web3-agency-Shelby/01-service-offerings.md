## Pricing Notes

1. **Payment Structure:**
   - 30% upfront
   - 40% at milestone (50% completion)
   - 30% at delivery

2. **Crypto Payments:**
   - Accept USDC, USDT, ETH, SOL
   - Price in USD, settle in crypto
   - Include 5-10% buffer for volatility

3. **Equity/Token Options:**
   - Consider 5-15% token allocation for high-potential projects
   - Negotiate advisory tokens for ongoing involvement
   - Always include vesting (1-2 years)

4. ** Rush Fees:**
   - 25-50% premium for 50%+ timeline reduction
