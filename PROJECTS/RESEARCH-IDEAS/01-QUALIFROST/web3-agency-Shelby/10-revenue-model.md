# Revenue Model

**Pricing Strategies & Revenue Streams for Web3 Agencies**

---

## Overview

This guide covers all revenue models for Web3 agencies, from project fees to ongoing retainers and equity participation.

**Revenue Goal:** $1M-$10M+ annually
**Profit Margin Target:** 20-40%
**Revenue Mix:** Diversified across streams

---

## 1. Project-Based Fees

### Fixed-Price Projects

**Best For:**
- Well-defined scope
- Experienced team
- Standard deliverables
- Clear timelines

**Pricing Method:**
```
1. Estimate hours for each deliverable
2. Multiply by hourly rate ($100-$200/hr)
3. Add 20-30% buffer
4. Round to clean number
```

**Example Project Pricing:**

| Project Type | Low | Standard | Premium |
|--------------|-----|----------|---------|
| NFT Collection | $50K | $100K | $200K |
| Token Launch | $100K | $200K | $500K |
| Smart Contract | $25K | $50K | $150K |
| Website | $20K | $50K | $100K |
| Marketing Campaign | $50K | $100K | $250K |
| Community Setup | $10K | $25K | $50K |

**Pros:**
- Predictable revenue
- Clear client expectations
- Easier to sell

**Cons:**
- Scope creep risk
- capped upside
- Requires accurate scoping

### Payment Terms

**Standard Structure:**
```
30% - Upon signing (non-refundable)
40% - At 50% completion milestone
30% - Upon delivery/launch
```

**Alternative Structures:**

**Option A (Conservative):**
```
50% - Upfront
50% - On delivery
```

**Option B (Enterprise):**
```
25% - Upfront
25% - At 33% completion
25% - At 66% completion
25% - On delivery
```

**Option C (Milestone-Based):**
```
30% - Upfront
20% - After design approval
20% - After development complete
20% - After testing/QA
10% - After launch
```

### Change Orders

**Process:**
1. Client requests change
2. Assess impact (hours, timeline)
3. Provide written estimate
4. Client approves
5. Work begins
6. Invoice with next payment

**Template:**
```
Change Order #001

Original Scope: [description]
New Request: [description]

Impact:
- Additional Hours: X
- Additional Cost: $X,XXX
- Timeline Impact: X days

Approval Required: Yes
```

## 2. Percentage of Raise

### Token Sale Commission

**Standard Rates:**

| Raise Size | Commission Rate |
|------------|-----------------|
| <$1M | 5-8% |
| $1M-$5M | 3-5% |
| $5M-$10M | 2-3% |
| $10M+ | 1-2% |

**Example:**
```
Client raises $3M
Your commission: 4%
Your fee: $120,000

Can be:
- All cash
- 50% cash + 50% tokens
- All tokens (at discount)
```

**When to Use:**
- Confident in ability to help raise
- Client has strong fundamentals
- You're providing full marketing + advisory
- Aligned incentives desired

**Risks:**
- Raise might not happen
- Market conditions change
- Client might fail to close
- Payment timing uncertain

**Mitigation:**
- Minimum fee guarantee ($50K-$100K)
- Clear definition of "raise"
- Payment triggers defined
- Vesting schedule for tokens

### NFT Mint Commission

**Standard Rates:**

| Mint Price | Commission |
|------------|------------|
| <0.05 ETH | 10-15% |
| 0.05-0.1 ETH | 7-10% |
| 0.1-0.2 ETH | 5-7% |
| 0.2+ ETH | 3-5% |

**Example:**
```
10,000 NFTs @ 0.08 ETH each
Total mint: 800 ETH
Your commission: 8%
Your fee: 64 ETH (~$100K at $1,600/ETH)

Payment: At mint (ETH or USDC)
```

**Structure Options:**

**Option 1: Percentage of Mint**
- X% of total mint proceeds
- Paid immediately after mint
- Simple, aligned incentives

**Option 2: Tiered Structure**
```
First 50% sold: 5%
Next 30% sold: 8%
Final 20% sold: 12%
Incentivizes full sellout
```

**Option 3: Base + Bonus**
```
Base fee: $50K
Bonus: 5% of mint if >80% sold
Bonus: 10% of mint if 100% sold
```

### Retainer Best Practices

**Contract Terms:**
- Minimum 3-month commitment
- 30-day termination notice
- Monthly payment in advance
- Clear scope of work
- Overage rates defined

- Monthly scope review
- Change orders for additional work
- Quarterly strategy reviews

**Pricing Psychology:**

- Annual contracts: 20% discount
- Include annual increase (3-5%)

## 4. Token/Equity Participation

### When to Accept Tokens

**Green Flags ✅:**
- Strong team with track record
- Clear utility and roadmap
- Reasonable valuation
- You believe in the project
- Can afford the risk
- Diversified portfolio approach

**Red Flags 🚩:**
- Anonymous team
- No clear utility
- Unrealistic promises
- Pressure to take tokens
- Unclear vesting
- Poor tokenomics

### Token Deal Structures

**Structure 1: Fee + Tokens**
```
Cash: 70% of normal fee
Tokens: 30% of fee value
Vesting: 1 year, 6-month cliff
Example: $100K fee → $70K cash + $30K tokens
```

**Structure 2: Reduced Fee + More Tokens**
```
Cash: 50% of normal fee
Tokens: 2-5% of supply
Vesting: 2 years, 1-year cliff
Example: $100K fee → $50K cash + 3% tokens
```

**Structure 3: Advisory Tokens**
```
Cash: Minimal ($10K-$50K retainer)
Tokens: 1-3% advisory allocation
Vesting: 2-4 years, 1-year cliff
Commitment: 5-10 hours/month
```

**Structure 4: Success-Based**
```
Cash: Below market ($50K-$100K)
Tokens: Performance-based
- X% at TGE
- Y% if price doubles
- Z% if market cap hits target
```

### Token Valuation

**Methods:**

**1. FDV Method:**
```
Fully Diluted Valuation: $100M
Your allocation: 2%
Your stake value: $2M (on paper)
Reality check: Apply 50-90% discount
```

**2. Comparable Method:**
```
Similar projects' valuations
Your project's metrics
Reasonable valuation range
```

**3. Cash Equivalent:**
```
"What cash fee would I accept instead?"
Use that as token value basis
Negotiate allocation accordingly
```

### Vesting Terms

**Standard Advisor Vesting:**
```
Duration: 2-4 years
Cliff: 6-12 months
Frequency: Monthly after cliff
Acceleration: On acquisition (optional)
```

**Example:**
```
Grant: 100,000 tokens
Vesting: 4 years
Cliff: 1 year
Month 13: 25,000 tokens
Months 14-48: 2,083 tokens/month
```

### Token Portfolio Strategy

**Diversification:**
- Don't take tokens from >30% of clients
- Diversify across sectors (DeFi, NFT, Gaming, Infra)
- Diversify across chains
- Set aside 50%+ for cash needs

**Allocation Guidelines:**
```
Conservative: 10% of revenue in tokens
Moderate: 20-30% of revenue in tokens
Aggressive: 40-50% of revenue in tokens
```

**Exit Strategy:**
- Have clear sell plan
- Consider tax implications
- Don't be greedy
- Rebalance periodically

## 5. Additional Revenue Streams

### Referral Fees

**Sources:**
- Audit firms: 10-20% of audit fee
- Law firms: 10-15% of legal fees
- Exchanges: Listing fee percentage
- Launchpads: 10-20% of their fee
- Other agencies: 10-20% of project

**Example:**
```
Refer client for audit ($30K)
Your referral: 15% = $4,500
```

**Best Practices:**
- Disclose to client
- Don't inflate prices
- Only refer quality partners
- Get agreements in writing

### White-Label Services

**Model:**
- Other agencies resell your services
- You deliver work
- They markup and manage client
- You get wholesale rate

**Pricing:**
```
Direct client rate: $100K
White-label rate: $70K-$80K
Agency margin: $20K-$30K
```

**Pros:**
- Steady pipeline
- Less sales effort
- Scalable

**Cons:**
- Lower margins
- Less client relationship
- Dependent on partners

### Products & IP

**Examples:**
- Smart contract templates ($5K-$50K/license)
- Tokenomics models ($2K-$10K)
- Course/education ($500-$5K/student)
- SaaS tools ($100-$1K/month)
- Research reports ($1K-$10K)

**Potential:**
- Passive income
- Scalable
- Brand building

**Reality:**
- Takes time to build
- Marketing required
- Not immediate revenue

## Financial Management

### Cash Flow Management

**Challenges:**
- Crypto payments volatile
- Client payment delays
- Irregular revenue (project-based)
- Token illiquidity

**Solutions:**
- Maintain 6 months operating cash
- Convert crypto to stablecoins quickly
- Require upfront payments
- Diversify revenue streams
- Line of credit for gaps

### Pricing Reviews

**Frequency:**
- Quarterly: Review rates
- Annually: Increase 10-20%
- Per project: Adjust based on demand

**Triggers for Increase:**
- Consistently booked out
- Turning away work
- Team costs increased
- Delivering exceptional results
- Market rates increased

### Profitability Targets

**By Revenue Stream:**

| Stream | Target Margin |
|--------|---------------|
| Project Fees | 30-50% |
| Retainers | 25-40% |
| Token Deals | Variable (high risk) |
| Referrals | 90%+ (pure margin) |

**Overall Business:**
- Gross Margin: 50-70%
- Operating Margin: 20-40%
- Net Margin: 15-30%

## Contracts & Legal

### Essential Contract Clauses

**Payment Terms:**
- Amount and currency
- Payment schedule
- Late payment penalties
- Currency conversion (for crypto)

**Scope of Work:**
- Detailed deliverables
- Timeline
- Revision limits
- Change order process

**IP Rights:**
- Who owns what
- License grants
- Portfolio usage rights
- Confidentiality

**Termination:**
- Notice period
- Payment on termination
- Work product ownership
- Transition assistance

**Liability:**
- Limitation of liability
- No guarantees
- Force majeure
- Dispute resolution

### Crypto Payment Considerations

**Best Practices:**
- Price in USD, settle in crypto
- Use stablecoins when possible
- Define conversion rate timing
- Include volatility buffer (5-10%)
- Clear tax documentation

**Accepted Currencies:**
- USDC (recommended)
- USDT
- ETH
- SOL
- Others (case by case)
