## Common Questions

### "How much should I charge?"

**Answer:** See [Revenue Model](./10-revenue-model.md)

**Quick guide:**
- Junior agency: $50K-$100K/project
- Mid-level: $100K-$250K/project
- Premium: $250K-$500K+/project

**Rule:** Charge based on value, not hours. If you're helping raise $5M, charging $150K is reasonable (3%).

---

### "What if I don't have a portfolio?"

**Answer:** See [Client Acquisition](./08-client-acquisition.md)

**Options:**
1. Do 1-2 pro bono projects
2. Document your own projects
3. Partner with established agency
4. Create detailed case studies from any work

---

### "How do I find clients?"

**Answer:** See [Client Acquisition](./08-client-acquisition.md)

**Quick start:**
- Twitter: Post daily, engage heavily
- Outreach: 50-100/week
- Networking: Events, Discords, AMAs
- Partnerships: VCs, other agencies

**Expect:** 2-3 months to first clients, 6 months to consistent pipeline

---

### "Should I accept tokens?"

**Answer:** See [Revenue Model](./10-revenue-model.md)

**Guideline:**
- Early stage: Yes (diversify risk)
- Established: Selective (20-30% of deals)
- Always: Get some cash

**Structure:** 50-70% cash, 30-50% tokens with vesting

---

### "When should I hire?"

**Answer:** See [Team Building](./09-team-building.md)

**Signals:**
- Consistently booked out
- Turning away work
- Quality suffering
- Founder is bottleneck
- Can afford 6+ months salary

**First hires:**
1. Project Manager / Head of Delivery
2. Senior Smart Contract Dev
3. Community Manager
4. Marketing Lead
