## Quick Start

1. Review **Service Offerings** to understand what you can sell
2. Study **Blockchain Selection** to advise clients properly
3. Use **NFT Collection Build** or **Token Launch Framework** based on client needs
4. Implement **Community Building** and **Marketing Strategy** in parallel
5. Ensure **Legal & Compliance** before any launch
6. Follow **Client Acquisition** to land deals
7. Build your **Team** as you scale
8. Apply the **Revenue Model** to price and structure deals
