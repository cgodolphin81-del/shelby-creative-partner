# Marketing Strategy

**Complete Guide to Web3 Marketing & Growth**

---

## Overview

This framework covers all aspects of Web3 marketing from influencer partnerships to airdrop campaigns, designed to maximize reach and conversion.

**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks (campaign)
**Budget Range:** $50K - $200K+ (excluding ad spend)
**Key Metric:** CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) & ROAS

---

## Marketing Funnel

```
AWARENESS → CONSIDERATION → CONVERSION → RETENTION → ADVOCACY
   ↓              ↓              ↓            ↓           ↓
Social Media   Whitepaper     Mint/Buy    Engagement   Referrals
PR/Press       AMAs           Website     Community    UGC
Influencers    Reviews        Presale     Staking      Ambassadors
Ads            Comparisons    Token Sale  Utility      Governance
```

## 1. Influencer & KOL Marketing

### Influencer Tiers

**Tier 1: Mega Influencers (500K+ followers)**
- Reach: 500K - 5M+
- Cost: $20K - $100K+ per post
- Best for: Brand awareness, major announcements
- Examples: Crypto Twitter whales, YouTube stars

**Tier 2: Macro Influencers (100K-500K followers)**
- Reach: 100K - 500K
- Cost: $5K - $20K per post
- Best for: Credible endorsements, deep dives
- Examples: Established crypto analysts, educators

**Tier 3: Micro Influencers (10K-100K followers)**
- Reach: 10K - 100K
- Cost: $500 - $5K per post
- Best for: Targeted reach, high engagement
- Examples: Niche experts, community leaders

**Tier 4: Nano Influencers (1K-10K followers)**
- Reach: 1K - 10K
- Cost: $100 - $500 or token payment
- Best for: Grassroots, authentic advocacy
- Examples: Active community members

### Influencer Deliverables

**Twitter/X:**
- Dedicated tweet: $500-$50K
- Thread: $1K-$100K
- Retweet + comment: $200-$5K
- Profile banner (week): $1K-$10K
- Long-term ambassador: $5K-$50K/month

**YouTube:**
- Dedicated video (10-20 min): $2K-$100K
- Integration in existing video: $1K-$30K
- Shorts: $500-$10K

**Telegram/Discord:**
- Announcement post: $500-$10K
- AMA hosting: $1K-$20K
- Community partnership: $2K-$30K/month

**Multi-Platform Packages:**
- Twitter + Telegram: 1.5x single rate
- Twitter + YouTube: 2x single rate
- Full package (all platforms): 3x single rate

### Vetting Influencers

**Red Flags:**
- Bought followers (check engagement rate)
- History of rug pull promotions
- No genuine crypto knowledge
- Only positive content (no critical analysis)
- Multiple promotions per day

**Green Flags:**
- 3%+ engagement rate
- Genuine community interaction
- Track record of quality projects
- Transparent about partnerships
- Long-term presence in crypto

### Outreach Template

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - [Project Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Project], a [brief description].

We've been following your content on [platform] and love your 
approach to [specific content]. Your recent post about [topic] 
really resonated with our team.

We're launching [brief description] on [date] and think your 
audience would genuinely benefit from learning about it.

We'd love to discuss:
- Sponsored content opportunities
- Long-term ambassador role
- Early access for you and your community

Are you open to a quick call this week?

Best,
[Name]
```

### Influencer Budget Allocation

**Recommended Split:**
- Tier 1: 20% (1-2 influencers)
- Tier 2: 40% (3-5 influencers)
- Tier 3: 30% (10-20 influencers)
- Tier 4: 10% (20-50 influencers)

**Sample $100K Budget:**
- 2 Tier 1: $40K
- 4 Tier 2: $40K
- 10 Tier 3: $15K
- 30 Tier 4: $5K

## 2. AMA (Ask Me Anything) Campaigns

### AMA Formats

**Twitter Spaces:**
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Reach: 100-10,000+ listeners
- Cost: $500-$10K (host fee)
- Best for: General awareness

**Discord AMA:**
- Duration: 45-90 minutes
- Reach: 500-10,000+ members
- Cost: $1K-$20K (community fee)
- Best for: Deep engagement

**Telegram AMA:**
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Reach: 1,000-50,000+ members
- Cost: $500-$15K (community fee)
- Best for: International audiences

**Reddit AMA:**
- Duration: 2-4 hours (async)
- Reach: 1,000-100,000+ viewers
- Cost: Free-$5K (mod fee)
- Best for: Technical audiences

**YouTube Live:**
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Reach: 100-10,000+ viewers
- Cost: $1K-$20K (host fee)
- Best for: Visual presentations

### AMA Preparation

**2 Weeks Before:**
- Confirm date/time (consider timezones)
- Prepare key talking points
- Create promotional assets
- Submit questions (seed questions)
- Tech check

**1 Week Before:**
- Promotional push
- Question collection
- Team briefing
- Backup plans

**Day Of:**
- Final tech check
- Team roles assigned
- Monitoring setup
- Recording enabled

**Follow-Up:**
- Thank you message
- Recording/timestamps
- Key highlights thread
- Unanswered questions addressed

### AMA Talking Points Template

```
1. Introduction (2 min)
   - Who you are
   - Project overview

2. Problem & Solution (5 min)
   - What problem you're solving
   - Your unique approach

3. Product/Technology (10 min)
   - Key features
   - Technical advantages

4. Tokenomics (5 min)
   - Token utility
   - Distribution

5. Roadmap (5 min)
   - Recent milestones
   - Upcoming plans

6. Q&A (30+ min)
   - Pre-submitted questions
   - Live questions
```

### AMA Budget

| Tier | Communities | Budget |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Basic | 5-10 small | $5K-$15K |
| Standard | 10-20 medium | $20K-$50K |
| Premium | 20-40 large | $50K-$150K |

## 3. Airdrop Campaigns

### Airdrop Types

**Type 1: Holder Airdrop**
- Target: Existing token/NFT holders
- Criteria: Snapshot at specific block
- Cost: Token allocation only
- Best for: Rewarding loyalty

**Type 2: Task-Based Airdrop**
- Target: New users
- Criteria: Complete social tasks
- Cost: Tokens + platform fees
- Best for: Growth & awareness

**Type 3: Community Airdrop**
- Target: Specific communities
- Criteria: Discord/Telegram members
- Cost: Tokens + community fees
- Best for: Strategic partnerships

**Type 4: Retroactive Airdrop**
- Target: Early users
- Criteria: Historical usage
- Cost: Significant token allocation
- Best for: Decentralization, goodwill

### Airdrop Platforms

| Platform | Type | Cost | Reach |
|----------|------|------|-------|
| Galxe | Task-based | $5K-$50K | 100K-1M+ |
| Zealy | Task-based | $3K-$30K | 50K-500K |
| QuestN | Task-based | $5K-$40K | 100K-500K |
| Layer3 | Task-based | $10K-$100K | 200K-1M+ |
| Guild | Holder-gating | Free-$10K | Variable |

### Airdrop Structure

**Example Task-Based Campaign:**

```
Total Pool: 1,000,000 tokens
Participants: 10,000 expected
Avg per user: 100 tokens

Tasks:
- Follow Twitter: 10 tokens
- Join Discord: 10 tokens
- Join Telegram: 10 tokens
- Retweet announcement: 20 tokens
- Tag 3 friends: 20 tokens
- Complete quiz: 30 tokens

Maximum per user: 100 tokens
```

### Airdrop Best Practices

**Do:**
- Clear eligibility criteria
- Reasonable task requirements
- Transparent timeline
- Sybil protection
- Fair distribution

**Don't:**
- Over-promise
- Delay without communication
- Ignore bot problem
- Exclude genuine users
- Forget tax implications

### Airdrop Budget

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Platform fees | $5K-$50K |
| Token allocation | Variable |
| Marketing promotion | $10K-$50K |
| Legal/compliance | $5K-$20K |
| **Total (excl. tokens)** | **$20K-$120K** |

## 4. Partnership Strategy

### Partnership Types

**Type 1: Cross-Promotion**
- Mutual marketing
- No money exchanged
- Social swaps, newsletter mentions
- Effort: Low
- Value: Medium

**Type 2: Integration Partnership**
- Technical integration
- Shared users
- Revenue share possible
- Effort: High
- Value: High

**Type 3: Ecosystem Partnership**
- Long-term collaboration
- Multiple initiatives
- Strategic alignment
- Effort: High
- Value: Very High

**Type 4: Launch Partnership**
- Co-launch initiatives
- Shared marketing
- Joint AMAs/events
- Effort: Medium
- Value: High

### Target Partners

**For NFT Projects:**
- Other NFT collections
- Marketplaces (OpenSea, Magic Eden)
- Analytics platforms (IcyTools, NFTGo)
- Wallets (MetaMask, Phantom)
- Tools (Genie, Gem)

**For Token Projects:**
- DeFi protocols
- Exchanges (CEX/DEX)
- Launchpads
- Analytics (CoinGecko, CMC)
- Infrastructure (RPC, oracles)

### Partnership Outreach

```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity - [Your Project] x [Their Project]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Name] from [Project]. We're building [brief description] 
and have been following [Their Project]'s work in [area].

We see strong alignment between our communities and believe 
a partnership could benefit both sides.

Potential collaboration ideas:
- Cross-promotion on social channels
- Joint AMA/event
- [Specific integration opportunity]
- Shared community benefits

Would you be open to exploring this? Happy to hop on a call.

Best,
[Name]
```

### Partnership Budget

| Type | Typical Investment |
|------|-------------------|
| Cross-Promotion | $0 (time only) |
| Integration | $10K-$100K (dev time) |
| Ecosystem | $25K-$200K (various) |
| Launch | $20K-$150K (marketing) |

## 5. Content Marketing

### Content Pillars

**Educational (40%):**
- How-to guides
- Explainer threads
- Industry insights
- Tutorials

**Product (25%):**
- Feature announcements
- Updates
- Behind-the-scenes
- Development progress

**Community (20%):**
- Member spotlights
- UGC features
- Contests
- Celebrations

**Promotional (15%):**
- Launch announcements
- Partnership news
- Milestone celebrations
- Call-to-actions

### Content Calendar Template

```
Weekly Structure:
Monday:    Educational thread
Tuesday:   Product update
Wednesday: Community spotlight
Thursday:  Industry insight
Friday:    Week recap + weekend plans
Saturday:  Light engagement/memes
Sunday:    AMA or Spaces

Daily:
- 3-5 tweets
- 10+ engagements
- 1 Discord announcement
- 1 Telegram update
```

### Content Production

**In-House:**
- Community Manager: Daily posts
- Content Writer: 2-3 threads/week
- Designer: Visual assets

**Outsourced:**
- Thread writers: $200-$1K/thread
- Graphic design: $50-$500/asset
- Video editing: $100-$1K/video

### Content Budget

| Component | Monthly Cost |
|-----------|--------------|
| Content Writer | $3K-$8K |
| Graphic Designer | $2K-$6K |
| Video Editor | $2K-$5K |
| Tools (Canva, etc.) | $100-$500 |
| **Total** | **$7K-$20K** |

## 6. Paid Advertising

### Ad Platforms

**Twitter Ads:**
- CPC: $0.50-$3
- CPM: $10-$30
- Best for: Awareness, followers
- Budget: $5K-$50K/month

**Google Ads:**
- CPC: $1-$10
- Best for: Search intent
- Budget: $5K-$50K/month
- Note: Crypto restrictions apply

**Reddit Ads:**
- CPC: $0.50-$2
- Best for: Community targeting
- Budget: $3K-$30K/month

**Discord Ads:**
- CPM: $5-$20
- Best for: Community growth
- Budget: $2K-$20K/month

**YouTube Ads:**
- CPV: $0.01-$0.10
- Best for: Video content
- Budget: $5K-$50K/month

### Ad Creative Best Practices

**Do:**
- Clear value proposition
- Strong visual
- Single call-to-action
- Mobile-optimized
- A/B test everything

**Don't:**
- Overpromise
- Use crypto jargon
- Ignore platform rules
- Set and forget
- Use low-quality assets

### Ad Budget Allocation

**Sample $50K Monthly Budget:**
- Twitter: $20K (40%)
- YouTube: $15K (30%)
- Reddit: $10K (20%)
- Testing: $5K (10%)
