## Cash Flow Considerations

### Payment Timing

```
Typical Project Cash Flow:
Month 1: +30% (deposit)
Month 2-3: 0%
Month 4: +40% (milestone)
Month 5-6: 0%
Month 7: +30% (completion)

Average collection period: 4-5 months
```

### Working Capital Requirements

```
Year 1:
- Monthly burn: ~$67K
- Reserve needed (6 months): $400K

Year 2:
- Monthly burn: ~$208K
- Reserve needed (6 months): $1.25M

Year 3:
- Monthly burn: ~$459K
- Reserve needed (6 months): $2.75M
```

### Crypto Payment Considerations

```
Best Practices:
1. Convert 70% to stablecoins immediately
2. Keep 20% in major crypto (ETH, SOL)
3. Keep 10% in project tokens (if bullish)
4. Rebalance quarterly
5. Track for tax purposes
```

## Exit Scenarios

### Acquisition

**Potential Acquirers:**
- Traditional marketing agencies
- Web3 infrastructure companies
- Consulting firms
- Competitors

**Valuation Multiples:**
- 1-2x revenue (typical)
- 2-4x revenue (high growth)
- 3-5x EBITDA (profitable)

**Example:**
```
Year 5 Revenue: $35M
Multiple: 2x
Exit Value: $70M

Founder ownership: 60%
Proceeds: $42M (pre-tax)
```

### Continue Operating

**Benefits:**
- Ongoing cash flow
- Asset building
- Legacy creation
- Flexibility

**Considerations:**
- Market cycles
- Team dependency
- Succession planning
- Diversification
