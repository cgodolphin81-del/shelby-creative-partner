## 🎯 Target Client Profiles

### Enterprise
- 500+ employees
- Legacy infrastructure (on-prem or early cloud)
- Compliance requirements (SOC2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
- Budget: $250K-$500K projects

### Mid-Market
- 50-500 employees
- Growing cloud footprint
- Need scalability and reliability
- Budget: $75K-$200K projects

### Startups
- 10-50 employees
- Cloud-native or early migration
- Need speed and cost efficiency
- Budget: $25K-$75K projects
