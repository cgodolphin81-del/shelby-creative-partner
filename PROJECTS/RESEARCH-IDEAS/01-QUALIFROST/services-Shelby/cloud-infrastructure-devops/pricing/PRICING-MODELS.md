# Pricing Models

**Comprehensive pricing strategies for cloud infrastructure and DevOps services**

---

## Executive Summary

This document provides pricing frameworks for all service offerings, including project-based pricing ($25K-$500K) and retainer models ($10K-$50K/month).

### Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-based, not cost-plus** - Price based on client value, not just hours
2. **Transparent but not commoditized** - Clear pricing without race-to-bottom
3. **Flexible engagement models** - Match pricing to client needs
4. **Profitable for both parties** - Ensure margin while delivering ROI

## 6. Discounting Strategy

### When to Discount

| Scenario | Discount Range | Approval Required |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Strategic reference client | 10-15% | Partner |
| Multi-project commitment | 10-20% | Partner |
| Annual retainer prepay | 10% | Partner |
| Non-profit / Education | 15-25% | Partner |
| Competitive situation | 5-10% | Sales Director |
| Existing client (repeat) | 5-10% | Sales Director |
| Quick close (<2 weeks) | 5% | Sales Director |

### When NOT to Discount

- First engagement with no strategic value
- Price-shopping clients
- Scope is unclear
- Client has payment issues
- Margins already thin

### Value-Adds Instead of Discounts

Instead of reducing price, consider:
- Extended support period
- Additional training sessions
- Enhanced documentation
- Priority support tier
- Free assessment for next phase
- Complimentary security review

## 7. Payment Terms & Policies

### Standard Payment Terms

| Engagement Type | Terms |
|-----------------|-------|
| Projects < $50K | 50% upfront, 50% on completion, Net 15 |
| Projects $50K-$200K | 30/30/30/10 milestone-based, Net 30 |
| Projects > $200K | 25/25/25/15/10 milestone-based, Net 45 |
| Retainers < $20K/month | Monthly in advance, Net 15 |
| Retainers > $20K/month | Quarterly in advance, Net 30 |

### Late Payment Policy

| Days Late | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 1-15 days | Reminder email |
| 16-30 days | Phone call, late fee (1.5%/month) |
| 31-45 days | Work pause notice |
| 46-60 days | Work suspended |
| 60+ days | Collections, legal action |

### Change Order Policy

- Changes < 10% of scope: Absorbed
- Changes 10-25%: Change order at standard rates
- Changes > 25%: Re-baseline project
- All changes require written approval
