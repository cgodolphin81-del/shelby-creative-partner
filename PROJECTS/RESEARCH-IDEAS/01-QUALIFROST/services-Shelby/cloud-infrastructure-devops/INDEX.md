## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

### For New Agency Owners

1. **Start with Assessment Services** (lowest risk entry point)
   - Read: `assessment/ASSESSMENT-PROCESS.md`
   - Use templates to deliver $25K-$50K engagements
   - Convert 60-75% to larger projects

2. **Build Core Delivery Team**
   - Read: `team-building/TEAM-BUILDING.md`
   - Hire: 2 Solutions Architects, 2-4 DevOps Engineers
   - Get certified on primary cloud platform

3. **Establish Service Offerings**
   - Read: `service-offerings/SERVICES.md`
   - Pick 2-3 core services to start
   - Create proposals using `pricing/PRICING-MODELS.md`

4. **Generate Pipeline**
   - Read: `client-acquisition/CLIENT-ACQUISITION.md`
   - Target: 50 leads/month
   - Goal: 2-3 deals/month by Month 6

5. **Deliver Excellence**
   - Read: `project-management/PROJECT-MANAGEMENT.md`
   - Read: `migration/MIGRATION-PROCESS.md`
   - Use templates and checklists

6. **Convert to Retainers**
   - Read: `post-migration/POST-MIGRATION-SUPPORT.md`
   - Offer managed services to all project clients
   - Target: 50%+ revenue from recurring by Year 3

7. **Track Financials**
   - Read: `revenue-projections/REVENUE-PROJECTIONS.md`
   - Monitor: Revenue, margins, utilization, CAC
   - Adjust: Pricing, hiring, marketing based on metrics
