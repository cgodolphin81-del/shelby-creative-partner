## 🎓 Key Concepts

### Service-Led Growth
Start with services (projects + retainers) to generate cash flow and learn client needs. Use insights to productize and scale.

### Value-Based Pricing
Price based on business impact delivered, not hours worked. Typical client ROI: 3-10x investment.

### Discovery-Driven Sales
Invest in thorough discovery (1-2 weeks) to:
- Understand real problems
- Build trust
- Create accurate proposals
- Increase close rates (>40%)

### Agile Delivery
Use 2-week sprints with:
- Regular client demos
- Transparent progress
- Flexible scope management
- Quality built-in

### Retainer Economics
Convert project clients to retainers for:
- Predictable revenue
- Higher margins (60-70%)
- Deeper relationships
- Expansion opportunities
