### Interview Process

**For Technical Roles (Data Engineer, BI Developer):**
```
Stage 1: Recruiter Screen (30 min)
в”ңв”Җ Background and experience
в”ңв”Җ Role expectations
в”ңв”Җ Compensation alignment
в””в”Җ Culture fit initial

Stage 2: Hiring Manager Screen (45 min)
в”ңв”Җ Deep dive on experience
в”ңв”Җ Technical discussion
в”ңв”Җ Problem-solving approach
в””в”Җ Team fit

Stage 3: Technical Assessment (Take-home, 3-5 hours)
в”ңв”Җ Real-world scenario
в”ңв”Җ Code/query submission
в”ңв”Җ Documentation
в””в”Җ Presentation prep

Stage 4: Technical Interview (90 min)
в”ңв”Җ Review take-home
в”ңв”Җ Live problem-solving
в”ңв”Җ Technical depth questions
в””в”Җ Architecture discussion

Stage 5: Team Fit Interview (60 min)
в”ңв”Җ Meet potential teammates
в”ңв”Җ Collaboration scenarios
в”ңв”Җ Values alignment
в””в”Җ Q&A

Stage 6: Final Interview with Leadership (45 min)
в”ңв”Җ Career goals
в”ңв”Җ Company vision
в”ңв”Җ Compensation discussion
в””в”Җ Close

Timeline: 2-3 weeks
Decision: Within 48 hours of final interview
```

**For Client-Facing Roles (Consultant, AE, PM):**
```
Stage 1: Recruiter Screen (30 min)
Stage 2: Hiring Manager Screen (45 min)
Stage 3: Case Study/Presentation (Take-home)
Stage 4: Role-Play Interview (60 min)
в”ңв”Җ Client scenario
в”ңв”Җ Objection handling
в””в”Җ Presentation skills
Stage 5: Team Fit Interview (60 min)
Stage 6: Leadership Interview (45 min)

Timeline: 2-3 weeks
```
