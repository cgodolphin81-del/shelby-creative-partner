## Target Customer Profiles

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) #1: Growth-Stage SaaS
```
Company Profile:
├─ Revenue: $10M - $100M ARR
├─ Employees: 50 - 500
├─ Funding: Series B - D
├─ Industry: B2B SaaS
└─ Tech Stack: Modern (Snowflake, dbt, etc.)

Decision Makers:
├─ VP/Head of Revenue Operations
├─ VP/Head of Product
├─ Chief of Staff
└─ CFO (for larger deals)

Pain Points:
├─ Data scattered across tools
├─ No single source of truth
├─ Manual reporting consuming time
├─ Can't measure product usage effectively
└─ Need better unit economics visibility

Budget Range: $50K - $150K
Sales Cycle: 4-8 weeks
```

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) #2: Mid-Market E-commerce
```
Company Profile:
├─ Revenue: $20M - $200M
├─ Employees: 100 - 1,000
├─ Channels: DTC + Marketplace
├─ Platforms: Shopify Plus, Magento
└─ Order Volume: 10K+ monthly

Decision Makers:
├─ VP/Director of E-commerce
├─ Head of Marketing
├─ VP of Operations
└─ CFO

Pain Points:
├─ Can't attribute revenue accurately
├─ Inventory optimization challenges
├─ Customer LTV unknown
├─ Marketing ROI unclear
└─ Manual reporting across channels

Budget Range: $40K - $100K
Sales Cycle: 6-10 weeks
```

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) #3: Traditional Enterprise (Digital Transformation)
```
Company Profile:
├─ Revenue: $100M - $1B+
├─ Employees: 500 - 5,000
├─ Industry: Manufacturing, Retail, Healthcare, Finance
├─ Legacy: On-premise systems, Excel-heavy
└─ Initiative: Digital transformation

Decision Makers:
├─ VP/Director of Business Intelligence
├─ VP/Director of Analytics

└─ CFO

├─ Slow time-to-insight
├─ Limited self-service capabilities
├─ Data quality issues
└─ Competitive pressure to modernize

Budget Range: $100K - $300K+
Sales Cycle: 3-6 months
```

## Acquisition Channels

### Channel 1: Outbound Prospecting

#### LinkedIn Outreach Strategy
**Volume:** 50-100 prospects/week
**Response Rate Target:** 10-15%
**Meeting Rate:** 3-5%

**Connection Request Template:**
```
Hi {First Name},

I noticed {Company} is scaling rapidly in the {industry} space. 
We've helped similar companies like {Similar Company} reduce 
reporting time by 80% and uncover $2M+ in revenue opportunities 
through better data visibility.

Would you be open to a brief conversation about how you're 
handling analytics as you grow?

Best,
{Your Name}
```

**Follow-Up Sequence:**
```
Day 1: Connection request
Day 3: Thank you + value proposition
Day 7: Case study share
Day 14: Insight/observation about their business
Day 21: Break-up message
```

#### Email Outreach
**Volume:** 200-500 emails/week
**Open Rate Target:** 25-35%
**Reply Rate:** 5-10%

**Cold Email Template:**
```
Subject: Quick question about {Company}'s analytics

Hi {First Name},

I'm reaching out because we recently helped {Similar Company} 
solve a challenge that sounds familiar:

They were spending 20+ hours/week on manual reporting, had data 
scattered across 10+ tools, and couldn't get real visibility into 
{relevant metric}.

We built them a unified analytics platform that:
✓ Automated 95% of reporting
✓ Reduced time-to-insight from days to minutes
✓ Uncovered $1.8M in optimization opportunities

Are you facing similar challenges with {Company}'s data and 
analytics?

If it makes sense, I'd love to share how we approached this. 
15 minutes next week?

Best,
{Your Name}
{Title}
{Company}
{Calendar Link}

P.S. Happy to share the {Similar Company} case study if helpful.
```

#### Cold Calling
**Volume:** 20-30 calls/day
**Connect Rate:** 15-20%
**Meeting Rate:** 5-8%

**Opening Script:**
```
"Hi {First Name}, this is {Your Name} from {Company}. 

I know I'm calling you out of the blue, so I'll be brief. 

We work with {industry} companies like {Similar Company} to help 
them get better visibility into their data and make faster 
decisions.

The reason I'm calling is we just helped them {specific result}, 
and I noticed {observation about their company}.

Is analytics and reporting something you're actively working to 
improve right now?"
```

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Lead Qualification (1-2 days)
**Criteria:**
- Budget: $25K+
- Authority: Decision maker or influencer
- Need: Clear pain point
- Timeline: Within 6 months

**Tools:**
- CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- Lead scoring
- Automated qualification

---

### Stage 2: Discovery Call (30-60 min)
**Objectives:**
- Understand business context
- Identify pain points
- Assess fit
- Set expectations

**Outcome:**
- Qualified → Proposal
- Not qualified → Nurture or refer

---

### Stage 3: Proposal & Presentation (3-5 days)
**Deliverables:**
- Written proposal
- Presentation deck
- ROI analysis
- References

**Presentation:**
- 60-90 minutes
- All stakeholders
- Address concerns
- Clear next steps

---

### Stage 4: Negotiation & Closing (1-4 weeks)
**Activities:**
- Address objections
- Adjust scope/pricing
- Legal review
- Procurement process

**Closing:**
- Contract signed
- Invoice sent
- Kickoff scheduled
