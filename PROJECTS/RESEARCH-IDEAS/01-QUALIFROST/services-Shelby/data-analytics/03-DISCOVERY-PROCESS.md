# Discovery Process

## Client Engagement Framework

---

## Phase 1: Initial Contact & Qualification

### Timeline: 1-3 days

### Objectives
- Understand client's situation
- Assess fit and alignment
- Set expectations
- Schedule discovery workshop

### Activities

#### 1. Initial Inquiry Response (Same Day)
```
Response Template:
- Acknowledge inquiry
- Brief introduction to our approach
- Request 30-minute discovery call
- Share relevant case studies
```

#### 2. Qualification Call (30 minutes)
**Discovery Questions:**

**Business Context:**
- What industry are you in?
- Company size (revenue, employees)?
- What triggered this initiative now?
- What's the business problem you're trying to solve?

**Current State:**
- What data sources do you currently have?
- What tools are you using today?
- Do you have an analytics team?
- What's working well? What's not?

**Desired Outcomes:**
- What does success look like?
- What decisions need better data?
- What metrics matter most?
- Any specific deadlines or events?

**Budget & Timeline:**
- What's your investment range?
- What's your ideal timeline?
- Who are the decision makers?
- What's your procurement process?

#### 3. Qualification Criteria
| Criteria | Green Light | Yellow Light | Red Light |
|----------|-------------|--------------|-----------|
| Budget | $25K+ | $10K-25K | <$10K |
| Timeline | 4+ weeks | 2-4 weeks | <2 weeks |
| Data Access | Ready | Partial | Blocked |
| Stakeholder Buy-in | Strong | Moderate | Weak |
| Clear Use Case | Yes | Developing | No |

### Deliverables
- Qualification summary
- Proposed next steps
- Discovery workshop agenda
- NDA (if needed)

## Phase 2: Data Audit & Assessment

### Timeline: 1-2 weeks

### Objectives
- Understand current data landscape
- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Assess technical feasibility
- Build foundation for proposal

### Activities

#### 1. Discovery Workshop (4-8 hours)
**Attendees:**
- Executive sponsor
- Business stakeholders
- IT/data team leads
- Our discovery team (2-3 people)

**Agenda:**
```
Morning Session (3-4 hours):
├─ Introductions & Context (30 min)
├─ Business Goals & Objectives (60 min)
├─ Current State Assessment (60 min)
├─ Pain Points & Challenges (45 min)
└─ Success Criteria Definition (45 min)

Afternoon Session (2-4 hours):
├─ Data Source Mapping (60 min)
├─ Technical Deep Dive (60 min)
├─ User Needs & Personas (45 min)
├─ Prioritization Exercise (45 min)
└─ Next Steps & Timeline (30 min)
```

#### 2. Technical Assessment (3-5 days)
**Data Source Inventory:**
```
For Each Source:
├─ Source name and description
├─ Data type (transactional, log, external, etc.)
├─ Volume (rows, GB/TB)
├─ Update frequency
├─ Access method (API, database, file, etc.)
├─ Data quality notes
├─ Owner/contact
└─ Integration complexity (1-5)
```

**Current Tools Assessment:**
- BI platforms in use
- Data infrastructure
- Analytics capabilities
- Security & compliance requirements

**Team Capability Assessment:**
- Current team skills
- Training needs
- Change readiness
- Support requirements

#### 3. Data Quality Analysis
**Dimensions Assessed:**
1. **Completeness** - Missing values, gaps
2. **Accuracy** - Correctness, validity
3. **Consistency** - Across sources, over time
4. **Timeliness** - Latency, freshness
5. **Uniqueness** - Duplicates, keys
6. **Conformity** - Standards, formats

**Scoring:**
- Green (80-100%): Ready for analytics
- Yellow (60-79%): Needs improvement
- Red (<60%): Significant issues

### Deliverables
- Data source inventory
- Current state architecture diagram
- Data quality report
- Gap analysis
- Preliminary recommendations

## Phase 3: KPI Identification & ROI Calculation

### Timeline: 3-5 days

### Objectives
- Define success metrics
- Calculate potential ROI
- Align on business outcomes
- Build business case

### Activities

#### 1. KPI Workshop (2-3 hours)
**Attendees:** Business stakeholders, analytics lead

**Framework:**
```
Business Objective → KPI → Target → Data Source
```

**KPI Categories:**

**Financial:**
- Revenue growth
- Profit margins
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)

**Operational:**
- Efficiency metrics
- Cycle time
- Quality indicators
- Resource utilization

**Customer:**
- Satisfaction scores (NPS, CSAT)
- Retention/churn rates
- Engagement metrics
- Conversion rates

**Growth:**
- User acquisition
- Activation rates
- Expansion revenue
- Market share

#### 2. KPI Prioritization Matrix
| | High Impact | Low Impact |
|---|---|---|
| **Easy to Measure** | Priority 1 (Do First) | Priority 3 (Nice to Have) |
| **Hard to Measure** | Priority 2 (Strategic) | Priority 4 (Defer) |

#### 3. ROI Calculation
**Formula:**
```
ROI = (Benefits - Costs) / Costs × 100
```

**Benefit Categories:**

**Revenue Impact:**
- Increased sales conversion
- Upsell/cross-sell improvement
- Reduced churn
- Price optimization

**Cost Reduction:**
- Labor savings (automation)
- Reduced tool costs (consolidation)
- Lower error rates
- Faster decision cycles

**Risk Mitigation:**
- Compliance avoidance
- Fraud prevention
- Better forecasting

**Example ROI Calculation:**
```
Current State:
- Monthly reporting: 40 hours × $75/hr = $3,000/month
- Missed opportunities: $50,000/year (estimated)
- Tool costs: $5,000/month

Future State (with our solution):
- Automated reporting: 4 hours × $75/hr = $300/month
- Captured opportunities: $150,000/year
- Consolidated tools: $2,000/month

Annual Benefits:
- Labor savings: $32,400
- Additional revenue: $100,000
- Tool savings: $36,000
Total Benefits: $168,400

Investment: $75,000 (one-time) + $10,000/year (support)

Year 1 ROI: ($168,400 - $85,000) / $85,000 = 98%
Year 2+ ROI: ($168,400 - $10,000) / $10,000 = 1,584%
```

### Deliverables
- KPI definition document
- KPI prioritization matrix
- ROI analysis
- Business case document

## Phase 4: Proposal Development

### Timeline: 3-5 days

### Objectives
- Present comprehensive solution
- Demonstrate value
- Address concerns
- Close the deal

### Proposal Structure

#### 1. Executive Summary (1-2 pages)
- Client situation
- Proposed solution
- Investment & timeline
- Expected outcomes

#### 2. Current State Assessment (2-3 pages)
- Discovery findings
- Pain points
- Opportunities
- Gap analysis

#### 3. Proposed Solution (5-10 pages)
- Architecture overview
- Scope of work
- Deliverables
- Technology stack
- Implementation approach

#### 4. Project Plan (2-3 pages)
- Phases & milestones
- Timeline (Gantt chart)
- Resource requirements
- Client responsibilities

#### 5. Investment (1-2 pages)
- Pricing breakdown
- Payment terms
- Options (if applicable)
- ROI summary

#### 6. About Us (2-3 pages)
- Company overview
- Relevant experience
- Case studies
- Team bios

#### 7. Terms & Conditions
- Scope boundaries
- Change order process
- Intellectual property
- Confidentiality
- Termination clauses

### Proposal Presentation
**Format:** 60-90 minute presentation
**Attendees:** Decision makers, stakeholders
**Agenda:**
```
├─ Recap of Discovery (15 min)
├─ Proposed Solution (30 min)
├─ Implementation Plan (15 min)
├─ Investment & ROI (15 min)
├─ Q&A (15-30 min)
└─ Next Steps (5 min)
```

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 1: Send proposal, schedule review
- Day 3: Check-in call
- Day 7: Address questions/concerns
- Day 14: Final follow-up
- Day 21: Last attempt (then archive)

### Deliverables
- Written proposal (PDF)
- Presentation deck
- ROI calculator (Excel)
- Reference contacts
- Contract/MSA
