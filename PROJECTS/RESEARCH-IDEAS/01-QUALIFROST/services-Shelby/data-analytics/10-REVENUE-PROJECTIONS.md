# Revenue Projections

## 3-Year Financial Model

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the 3-year revenue projections for the Data Analytics & BI Agency, based on three growth scenarios: Conservative (10 clients), Moderate (50 clients), and Aggressive (150 clients).

### Key Assumptions

| Assumption | Value |
|------------|-------|
| Average Project Value | $65,000 |
| Average Monthly Retainer | $8,500 |
| Project Margin | 45-55% |
| Retainer Margin | 60-70% |
| Client Retention Rate | 85-90% |
| Sales Cycle | 6-10 weeks |
| Delivery Capacity | 3-4 projects/team |

---

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Project Revenue** - One-time implementation projects
2. **Retainer Revenue** - Ongoing support and maintenance
3. **Training Revenue** - Workshops and enablement programs
4. **Add-on Services** - Additional features, integrations, analytics

---

