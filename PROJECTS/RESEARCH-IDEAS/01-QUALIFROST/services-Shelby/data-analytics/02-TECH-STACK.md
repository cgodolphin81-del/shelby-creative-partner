- Executive dashboards

- Large user bases

## Data Warehousing

### Snowflake
**Best For:** Multi-cloud, variable workloads, separation of compute/storage

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Pricing | Pay-per-use (compute + storage) |
| Strengths | Zero management, auto-scaling, multi-cloud |
| Weaknesses | Can be expensive, vendor lock-in concerns |
| Typical Cost | $5,000-50,000/month |

---

### Google BigQuery
**Best For:** Large-scale analytics, serverless, Google Cloud users

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Pricing | On-demand or flat-rate |
| Strengths | Serverless, fast queries, ML integration |
| Weaknesses | Cost unpredictability, Google Cloud dependent |
| Typical Cost | $3,000-40,000/month |

## Technology Selection Framework

### Decision Criteria

| Factor | Weight | Questions |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Business Requirements | 30% | Does it solve the problem? |
| Total Cost of Ownership | 25% | What are all costs over 3 years? |
| Team Expertise | 20% | Can our team support it? |
| Scalability | 15% | Will it grow with us? |
| Vendor Stability | 10% | Will the vendor be around? |

### Recommended Stacks by Company Size

#### Startup (<50 employees)
- Looker Studio (free) or Power BI
- BigQuery or Snowflake (small)
- Fivetran or Stitch
- dbt Core (free)

#### Mid-Market (50-500 employees)
- Tableau or Power BI Premium
- Snowflake or BigQuery
- Fivetran + dbt Cloud
- Airflow (managed)

#### Enterprise (500+ employees)
- Tableau + Power BI (hybrid)
- Snowflake Enterprise or multi-cloud
- Custom ETL + Fivetran
- Full DevOps pipeline
