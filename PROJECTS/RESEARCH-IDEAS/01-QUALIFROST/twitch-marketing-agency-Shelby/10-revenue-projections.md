## Revenue Model Components

### 1. Streamer Service Revenue

**Packages**:
- Starter ($2,000/month): 30% of streamer clients
- Growth ($5,000/month): 50% of streamer clients
- Elite ($10,000/month): 20% of streamer clients

**Weighted Average Revenue Per Client**:
```
($2,000 × 0.30) + ($5,000 × 0.50) + ($10,000 × 0.20)
= $600 + $2,500 + $2,000
= $5,100 average per streamer client
```

**Year 1 Adjusted**: $4,200 (mix weighted toward Starter)
**Year 2 Adjusted**: $4,800 (mix improves)
**Year 3 Adjusted**: $5,400 (mix weighted toward Growth/Elite)

---

### 2. Brand Retainer Revenue

**Packages**:
- Basic ($5,000/month): 40% of brand clients
- Standard ($10,000/month): 40% of brand clients
- Premium ($25,000/month): 20% of brand clients

**Weighted Average Revenue Per Client**:
```
($5,000 × 0.40) + ($10,000 × 0.40) + ($25,000 × 0.20)
= $2,000 + $4,000 + $5,000
= $11,000 average per brand client
```

**Year 1 Adjusted**: $8,500 (more Basic clients)
**Year 2 Adjusted**: $10,500 (mix improves)
**Year 3 Adjusted**: $12,500 (more Premium clients)

---

### 3. Sponsorship Commission Revenue

**Commission Rates by Year**:
- Year 1: 25% (standard rate)
- Year 2: 22% (volume discounts begin)
- Year 3: 20% (premium volume rates)

**Sponsorship Volume Drivers**:
- More streamer clients = more sponsorship inventory
- Higher-tier streamers = higher sponsorship rates
- Better brand relationships = larger deals

---

### 4. One-Time Service Revenue

**Services**:
- Channel audits: $750 each
- Media kit creation: $500-1,000
- Setup fees: $1,000-5,000
- Campaign activation: $500-2,000

**Projected as % of recurring revenue**:
- Year 1: 8%
- Year 2: 6%
- Year 3: 5% (recurring scales faster)

|----------|--------------|

| Contractor Costs | 5% |
| Payment Processing | 1% |

### Break-Even Calculation

```
Fixed Costs = $12,700/month
Contribution Margin = 100% - 31% = 69%

Break-Even Revenue = Fixed Costs / Contribution Margin
Break-Even Revenue = $12,700 / 0.69 = $18,406/month

Break-Even Clients:
- 3 Streamer clients @ $4,200 avg = $12,600
- 1 Brand client @ $8,500 = $8,500
- Total = $21,100 (above break-even)

Break-Even Point: Month 2-3 (achievable with 2-3 clients)
```
