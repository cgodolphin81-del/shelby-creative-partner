The EU AI Act is a comprehensive regulation that:

• Establishes enforcement mechanisms

Key Dates:

## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Colors
- **Primary:** EU Blue (#003399)
- **Secondary:** EU Gold (#FFCC00)
- **Accent:** [Your brand color]
- **Neutral:** Grays for text
- **Background:** White or light gray

### Typography
- **Headings:** Montserrat or similar sans-serif
- **Body:** Open Sans, Lato, or similar
- **Monospace:** For code/technical content
- **Sizes:** 
  - Title: 36-48pt
  - Headings: 24-32pt (H1), 18-22pt (H2), 14-16pt (H3)
  - Body: 11-12pt
  - Captions: 9-10pt

### Layout
- **Margins:** 0.75" - 1" all sides
- **Columns:** Single or two-column for dense content
- **Line Spacing:** 1.15 - 1.5
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 6-12pt after

### Visual Elements
- **Icons:** Consistent icon set (Feather, Font Awesome, or custom)
- **Images:** High-quality, professional
- **Diagrams:** Clean, labeled, color-coded
- **Tables:** Alternating row colors, clear headers
- **Callouts:** Colored boxes for tips, warnings, notes

### Interactive Elements (if interactive PDF)
- Clickable table of contents
- Hyperlinked URLs
- Fillable form fields in templates
- Embedded videos (optional)
- Navigation buttons
