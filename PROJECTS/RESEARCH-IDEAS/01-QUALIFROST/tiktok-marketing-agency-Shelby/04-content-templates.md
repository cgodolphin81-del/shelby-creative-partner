## 1. E-COMMERCE & RETAIL

**Problem/Solution:**
- "Stop struggling with [problem] - here's the fix"
- "I wish I found this sooner..."
- "The mistake everyone makes when buying [product]"
- "Why you're paying too much for [product category]"

**Social Proof:**
- "10K people bought this last month - here's why"
- "Our bestseller for a reason..."
- "See why customers keep coming back"
- "The product that changed everything"

**Urgency/Scarcity:**
- "Only 47 left at this price!"
- "Sale ends in 3 hours"
- "You've never seen [product] like this"
- "First 100 get free shipping"

**Curiosity:**
- "What's inside our mystery box?"
- "You won't believe what this does"
- "The secret behind our viral product"
- "3 reasons you need this NOW"

### Script Templates

**Product Demo Script:**
```
0-3s:  [Hook] "Stop wasting money on [problem solution]"
3-10s: [Problem] Show common struggle with current solution
10-25s: [Solution] Show your product solving it
25-40s: [Benefits] 3 key features/benefits
40-50s: [Social Proof] "Join 10K+ happy customers"
50-60s: [CTA] "Link in bio - 50% off today only"
```

**Unboxing Script:**
```
0-3s:  [Hook] "Opening our most requested product"
3-10s: [First impression] Show packaging, first reactions
10-25s: [Reveal] Full product reveal, close-ups
25-40s: [Features] Highlight key features
40-50s: [Usage] Show it in action
50-60s: [CTA] "Available now - link in bio"
```

**Customer Review Script:**
```
0-3s:  [Hook] "This customer sent us THIS..."
3-10s: [Review highlight] Show customer testimonial
10-25s: [Context] Show product they reviewed
25-40s: [Results] Show their transformation/results
40-50s: [Proof] More reviews, ratings
```

## Implementation Guide

### How to Use These Templates

**1. Customize for Your Brand:**
- Add your voice and personality
- Insert specific details (numbers, names, products)
- Match your audience's language

**2. Test & Iterate:**
- Try 3-5 variations of each hook
- Track which performs best
- Double down on winners
- Kill what doesn't work

**3. Batch Create:**
- Use templates to speed up scripting
- Create 10-20 videos in one session
- Maintain consistency in quality

**4. A/B Test:**
- Post similar content with different hooks
- Test different CTAs
- Measure what drives results

### Tracking Template Performance

Create a simple spreadsheet:
```
| Date | Hook Type | Video Topic | Views | Engagement | Follows | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------|------------|---------|-------|
| 1/1  | Problem   | Product X   | 50K   | 8%         | 500     | Strong hook |
| 1/3  | Social    | Product Y   | 12K   | 4%         | 80      | Weak hook |
```

**Review weekly:**
- Which hooks drive most views?
- Which generate most follows?
- Which get most engagement?
- Adjust based on data
