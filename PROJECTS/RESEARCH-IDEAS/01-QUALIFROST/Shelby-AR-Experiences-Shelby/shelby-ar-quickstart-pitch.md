## Part 1: Quick Start Guide

### 🚀 Getting Started in 5 Steps

**Step 1: Understand the Opportunity**
Augmented Reality is no longer futuristic—it's mainstream:
- 2.4 billion AR users worldwide (2025)
- $88 billion AR market by 2027
- 71% of consumers would shop more with AR
- 40% higher conversion rates with AR try-on

**Step 2: Know Our Sweet Spot**
We excel at:
- ✅ Social media filters & lenses (Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat)
- ✅ E-commerce product visualization (try-on, placement)
- ✅ Marketing campaign AR (brand activations)
- ✅ Educational experiences (museums, training)
- ✅ Enterprise solutions (custom apps, internal tools)

**Step 3: Qualify Leads Fast**
**Ideal Client Profile:**
- Marketing budget: $50K+/year
- Tech-savvy decision makers
- Previous digital campaign experience
- Clear business objective (not just "we want AR")
- Timeline: 4-12 weeks out

**Red Flags:**
- ❌ Budget under $5K (refer to DIY tools)
- ❌ "Need it next week" (unless rush fee accepted)
- ❌ No clear objective ("just want to be cool")
- ❌ Unwilling to provide brand assets

**Step 4: Run a Discovery Call**
**Key Questions:**
1. "What business problem are you trying to solve?"
2. "Who is your target audience?"
3. "What does success look like?" (metrics)
4. "What's your budget range?"
5. "What's your ideal launch date?"

**Step 5: Send a Winning Proposal**
**Proposal Must-Haves:**
- Clear understanding of their objective
- Relevant case studies (2-3)
- Specific deliverables (not vague promises)
- Timeline with milestones
- Investment (with payment terms)
- Next steps & call to action

## Part 4: Common Objections & Responses

### Objection: "AR is too expensive"
**Response:**
> "I understand budget is a concern. Let me reframe: a traditional video campaign might cost $50K and get 500K views with 2% engagement. Our AR experiences typically get 15-30% engagement and continue generating value for months or years. Plus, we have starter packages from $5K if you want to test the waters."

---

### Objection: "We tried AR before and it didn't work"
**Response:**
> "I'd love to hear more about that experience. Often, AR 'failures' come from poor execution—clunky UX, no distribution plan, or misaligned objectives. We start with strategy, not technology. What was the goal of your previous campaign, and how did you measure success?"

---

### Objection: "Our audience isn't tech-savvy"
**Response:**
> "That's a common concern, but the data shows otherwise. 2.4 billion people used AR last year—that's nearly a third of the global population. Modern AR requires zero learning curve: point your camera and go. The technology has matured significantly. Can you tell me more about your specific audience?"

---

### Objection: "We need this in 2 weeks"
**Response:**
> "We can absolutely accelerate timelines with our express service. For a 50% premium, we can deliver in half the standard time by dedicating a full team and prioritizing your project. That said, quality AR takes iteration. Would you be open to a phased approach—launch something simple in 2 weeks, then enhance it?"

---

### Objection: "Can't we just use a template?"
**Response:**
> "Template tools like Spark AR's pre-mades are great for testing, but they have limitations. You're limited to their assets, you can't customize deeply, and your experience looks like everyone else's. If AR is strategic for your brand, custom is the way to go. That said, we can start with a template-based proof of concept and scale from there."

---

### Objection: "How do we measure ROI?"
**Response:**
> "Great question—we're obsessive about measurement. We track:
> - Usage metrics (views, interactions, shares)
> - Engagement time
> - Click-through to purchase
> - Conversion lift (with proper tracking setup)
> - Social sentiment
> 
> We'll work with your analytics team to ensure we're measuring what matters to YOUR business."

## Part 7: Frequently Asked Questions

### Technical FAQs

**Q: Do users need to download an app?**
A: For social media filters (Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat), users just need those apps—which they likely already have. For WebAR, no app is needed at all; it works in the browser.

**Q: How long do AR experiences last?**
A: Social filters can live indefinitely. We've seen filters from 2020 still getting daily uses. WebAR experiences last as long as you host them.

**Q: Can we update the experience after launch?**
A: Yes! Social platforms allow updates (may require re-review). WebAR can be updated instantly.

**Q: What devices are supported?**
A: We target iPhone 6s+ and Android devices from 2018+. We test on 10+ devices to ensure broad compatibility.

---

### Business FAQs

**Q: Who owns the IP?**
A: You own the final deliverables upon full payment. We retain rights to our underlying tools and frameworks, and we ask to display the work in our portfolio.

**Q: What if the platform rejects our filter?**
A: We handle all submissions and revisions at no extra cost. Rejections are rare (<5%) when we follow guidelines.

**Q: Can you work with our existing agency?**
A: Absolutely! We often white-label for agencies who don't have in-house AR capabilities.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**
A: For projects over $25K, we can split into 3 payments (50% deposit, 25% at midpoint, 25% on delivery).
