# Client Audit Process

**Comprehensive discovery methodology for e-commerce brands**

---

## Overview

Our audit process is designed to:
1. Understand the current state of the business
2. Identify growth opportunities
3. Quantify potential impact
4. Build trust through insight
5. Create a clear path forward

**Timeline:** 5-7 business days  
**Deliverable:** 30-50 page audit deck + 60-minute presentation

---

## Phase 3: Opportunity Identification (Day 5-6)

### Opportunity Framework

Categorize each opportunity by:
- **Impact:** Low / Medium / High
- **Effort:** Low / Medium / High
- **Timeline:** Immediate (0-30 days) / Short-term (30-90 days) / Long-term (90+ days)

### Common Opportunities by Category

#### Quick Wins (0-30 days, High Impact, Low Effort)
- Email flow optimization
- Checkout improvements
- Ad account restructuring
- Landing page tweaks
- Offer testing

#### Strategic Projects (30-90 days, High Impact, Medium Effort)
- Creative production system
- CRO testing program
- Audience expansion
- SMS program launch
- Loyalty program

#### Long-term Initiatives (90+ days, High Impact, High Effort)
- Platform migration
- Brand repositioning
- Product line expansion
- International expansion
- Omnichannel strategy

### Quantifying Impact

For each opportunity, estimate:
1. **Current baseline** (e.g., 2.0% conversion rate)
2. **Potential improvement** (e.g., 2.5% conversion rate)
3. **Revenue impact** (e.g., +$50K/month at current traffic)
4. **Investment required** (e.g., $10K one-time + $3K/month)
5. **Payback period** (e.g., 2 months)

5. Email/SMS Audit (5-7 slides)

6. Retention & LTV (4-6 slides)

## Phase 5: Presentation & Close (Day 7+)

### Presentation Agenda (60 minutes)

| Time | Topic |
|------|-------|
| 0-5 min | Introductions & agenda |
| 5-15 min | Executive summary |
| 15-35 min | Key findings (website, ads, email) |
| 35-50 min | Opportunities & roadmap |
| 50-60 min | Partnership proposal & Q&A |

### Handling Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We've tried agencies before" | "Totally get that. Here's what makes us different..." |
| "The investment is high" | "Let's talk about ROI. Here's what we expect to deliver..." |
| "We need to think about it" | "Of course. What specific questions do you have?" |
| "We want to start smaller" | "We can phase the engagement. Here's a starter package..." |

### Close Process

1. **Present proposal** during audit call
2. **Send follow-up** within 24 hours (proposal + deck)
3. **Check-in** at 48 hours if no response
4. **Final call** at 1 week to address remaining questions
5. **Close or disqualify** by day 10
