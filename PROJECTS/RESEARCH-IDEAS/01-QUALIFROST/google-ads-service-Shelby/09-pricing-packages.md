## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: Is there a minimum contract length?
**A:** We require a 90-day initial commitment to allow enough time for optimization and results. After that, it's month-to-month.

### Q: What happens if I want to pause campaigns?
**A:** You can pause campaigns anytime. Management fee is adjusted based on active campaigns. We recommend at least 2 weeks notice.

### Q: Do you guarantee results?
**A:** We guarantee professional management and best practices. Specific results depend on many factors (budget, competition, website, offer). We're confident in our ability to deliver ROI.

### Q: Can I change my budget?
**A:** Yes, with 48-hour notice. We recommend changes in increments of 20% or less to avoid disrupting performance.

### Q: What if I'm not happy with results?
**A:** Communication is key. We'll work with you to adjust strategy. If we can't meet your goals after 90 days of optimization, we'll help transition your account.

### Q: Do you work with competitors?
**A:** We limit one client per industry per geographic area to avoid conflicts. Ask about our exclusivity policy.

### Q: What access do you need?
**A:** We need access to Google Ads, Google Analytics, and your website (for tracking). We can also create accounts for you if needed.
