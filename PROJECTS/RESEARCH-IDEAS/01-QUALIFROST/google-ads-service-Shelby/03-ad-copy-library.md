## 📝 AD COPY BEST PRACTICES

### Headline Tips
- Include primary keyword
- Highlight unique value proposition
- Use numbers when possible
- Create urgency or scarcity
- Match search intent
- Stay under 30 characters

### Description Tips
- Expand on headline promise
- Include clear CTA
- Add social proof
- Mention offers or guarantees
- Address pain points
- Stay under 90 characters

### Testing Framework
1. Test 2-3 ads per ad group minimum
2. Rotate ads evenly for 2 weeks
3. Pause lowest CTR ad
4. Create new variant
5. Repeat monthly
