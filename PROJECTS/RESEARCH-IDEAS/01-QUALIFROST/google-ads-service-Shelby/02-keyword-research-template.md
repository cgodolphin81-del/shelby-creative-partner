## 📋 SHEET 3: MATCH TYPE GUIDE

### Exact Match [keyword]
- Triggers on searches that mean the same as your keyword
- Highest intent, highest conversion rate
- Use for: High-value, proven converters
- Example: [plumber near me]

### Phrase Match "keyword"
- Triggers on searches that include the meaning of your keyword
- Good balance of reach and relevance
- Use for: Discovery and expansion
- Example: "emergency plumber"

### Broad Match +keyword (BMM)
- Triggers on searches related to your keyword
- Widest reach, requires negative keyword management
- Use for: Research and new keyword discovery
- Example: +plumber +emergency

### Broad Match keyword
- Triggers on searches related to your keyword (widest)
- Use with Smart Bidding only
- Use for: Maximum reach with conversion tracking
- Example: plumber

## 📋 SHEET 4: SEARCH INTENT FRAMEWORK

### Informational Intent
**User is:** Researching, learning, comparing
**Keywords:** how to, what is, guide, tips, best way to
**Strategy:** Educational content, blog posts, awareness campaigns
**Conversion Expectation:** Low (focus on email capture)

### Commercial Intent
**User is:** Comparing options, ready to buy soon
**Keywords:** best, reviews, vs, comparison, top 10
**Strategy:** Comparison pages, testimonials, case studies
**Conversion Expectation:** Medium (demo requests, consultations)

### Transactional Intent
**User is:** Ready to purchase
**Keywords:** buy, price, cost, order, near me, hire
**Strategy:** Product pages, direct CTAs, strong offers
**Conversion Expectation:** High (purchases, quotes, calls)

### Navigational Intent
**User is:** Looking for specific brand/website
**Keywords:** [brand name], [brand] login, [brand] contact
**Strategy:** Brand protection, ensure top position
**Conversion Expectation:** Very High (existing customers)
