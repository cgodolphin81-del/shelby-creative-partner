# 30-Day Launch Calendar
## Google Ads Campaign Launch Timeline

---

## 📅 OVERVIEW

This calendar guides you through a complete Google Ads campaign launch from Day 0 to Day 30. Follow this timeline to ensure nothing is missed and campaigns launch successfully.

### Key Milestones
- **Day 0-5:** Discovery & Strategy
- **Day 6-10:** Campaign Build
- **Day 11-14:** Testing & QA
- **Day 15:** LAUNCH
- **Day 16-30:** Optimization & Scaling

---

## 🗓️ WEEK 1: DISCOVERY & STRATEGY (Days 0-5)

### Day 0: KICKOFF

**Goals:** Align on goals, gather information, set expectations

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Send welcome email with onboarding form
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call (30-45 minutes)
- [ ] Request account access (Google Ads, Analytics, website)

**Kickoff Call Agenda:**
1. Business overview (10 min)
2. Goals and KPIs (10 min)
3. Target audience and market (10 min)
4. Timeline and next steps (10 min)
5. Q&A (5 min)

**Deliverables:**
- ✓ Kickoff call completed

### Day 3: KEYWORD RESEARCH

**Goals:** Build comprehensive keyword list

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Brainstorm seed keywords
- [ ] Use Google Keyword Planner
- [ ] Expand with SEMrush/Ahrefs
- [ ] Analyze search intent
- [ ] Group keywords by theme
- [ ] Identify negative keywords
- [ ] Prioritize by volume and intent

**Keyword Research Process:**
1. Seed keywords from onboarding form
2. Expand with tools (target 200+ keywords)
3. Categorize by intent (informational, commercial, transactional)
4. Group into ad group themes
5. Assign match types
6. Create negative keyword lists

**Deliverables:**
- ✓ Master keyword list (200+ keywords)
- ✓ Keyword grouping by ad group
- ✓ Negative keyword lists

## 📋 DAILY CHECKLIST (Post-Launch)

### Quick Daily Checks (5-10 minutes)

**Every Morning:**
- [ ] Check account for disapproved ads
- [ ] Review spend vs. budget
- [ ] Check for significant changes
- [ ] Review conversions from previous day

**Red Flags to Watch:**
- ⚠️ Spend 50%+ above daily average
- ⚠️ CTR dropped below 1%
- ⚠️ No conversions in 3+ days
- ⚠️ Impressions dropped 50%+
- ⚠️ CPC increased 50%+
- ⚠️ Disapproved ads
