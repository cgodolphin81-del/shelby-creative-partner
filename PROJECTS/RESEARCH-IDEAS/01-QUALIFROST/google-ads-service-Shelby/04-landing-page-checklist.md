## 🧪 A/B TESTING IDEAS

### Headline Tests
- [ ] Benefit-driven vs. Feature-driven
- [ ] Question vs. Statement
- [ ] Short vs. Long headlines
- [ ] Including numbers vs. No numbers
- [ ] Including location vs. Generic
- [ ] Emotional vs. Logical appeal

### CTA Button Tests
- [ ] Button color (red, green, orange, blue)
- [ ] Button text ("Get Started" vs. "Learn More" vs. "Buy Now")
- [ ] Button size (large vs. medium vs. small)
- [ ] Button placement (above fold vs. below)
- [ ] Single CTA vs. Multiple CTAs
- [ ] With icon vs. Without icon

### Form Tests
- [ ] Number of fields (short vs. long)
- [ ] Single column vs. Multi-column
- [ ] Inline labels vs. Top labels
- [ ] Required fields indicated vs. Not indicated
- [ ] Progress bar (for multi-step) vs. No progress
- [ ] Submit button text variations

### Social Proof Tests
- [ ] Testimonials with photos vs. Without
- [ ] Video testimonials vs. Text testimonials
- [ ] Number of reviews displayed (3 vs. 10 vs. 50+)
- [ ] Star ratings visible vs. Hidden
- [ ] Customer logos vs. No logos
- [ ] Case studies vs. Simple testimonials

### Trust Signal Tests
- [ ] Security badges visible vs. Hidden
- [ ] Guarantee badge placement
- [ ] "As seen in" media logos vs. None
- [ ] Certification badges vs. None
- [ ] Years in business displayed vs. Not
- [ ] Team photos vs. Stock photos

### Pricing Tests
- [ ] Price shown vs. "Contact for pricing"
- [ ] Monthly vs. Annual pricing emphasis
- [ ] Strikethrough original price vs. No strikethrough
- [ ] Payment plan options shown vs. Not shown
- [ ] Free trial vs. Money-back guarantee
- [ ] Tiered pricing (3 tiers) vs. Simple pricing

### Image/Video Tests
- [ ] Hero image vs. Hero video
- [ ] Product in use vs. Product alone
- [ ] People facing camera vs. Looking at product
- [ ] Lifestyle images vs. Product-focused
- [ ] Real photos vs. Stock photos
- [ ] Before/After vs. After only

### Layout Tests
- [ ] Single column vs. Two column
- [ ] CTA above fold vs. Below fold
- [ ] Long-form vs. Short-form content
- [ ] Video at top vs. Video in middle
- [ ] Testimonials at top vs. Bottom
- [ ] Left-aligned vs. Centered content

### Urgency Tests
- [ ] Countdown timer vs. No timer
- [ ] "Limited spots" vs. No scarcity
- [ ] Offer expiration date vs. No date
- [ ] Live visitor count vs. None
- [ ] Recent purchase notifications vs. None
- [ ] Stock level indicators vs. None

### Navigation Tests
- [ ] No navigation (focused) vs. Full navigation
- [ ] Minimal navigation vs. Complete menu
- [ ] Logo links to home vs. Stays on page
- [ ] External links removed vs. Included
- [ ] Footer links minimal vs. Complete
- [ ] Sticky header vs. Standard header

## 🔍 HEATMAP & BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS

### What to Look For

**Click Heatmaps:**
- Are people clicking on non-clickable elements?
- Are CTAs getting clicks?
- Are navigation items distracting?

**Scroll Heatmaps:**
- Where do most people stop scrolling?
- Is key content above the fold?
- Are you losing people at a specific point?

**Move Heatmaps:**
- Where does attention focus?
- Are people reading your copy?
- What captures attention first?

**Session Recordings:**
- Watch 10-20 recordings per week
- Note where people hesitate
- Identify friction points
- See where people drop off

## 📝 LANDING PAGE COPY FRAMEWORK

### AIDA Model
1. **Attention** - Headline that stops the scroll
2. **Interest** - Subheadline that builds curiosity
3. **Desire** - Benefits, social proof, features
4. **Action** - Clear, compelling CTA

### PAS Model
1. **Problem** - Identify their pain point
2. **Agitate** - Make it hurt (emphasize consequences)
3. **Solution** - Present your offer as the answer

### FAB Model
1. **Features** - What your product/service does
2. **Advantages** - What that means for them
3. **Benefits** - How it improves their life

## 🚀 QUICK WINS (Implement Today)

1. **Add urgency** - "Limited time" or "X spots left"
2. **Strengthen CTA** - Use action verbs, be specific
3. **Add social proof** - Reviews, testimonials, logos
4. **Reduce form fields** - Every field reduces conversion
5. **Improve page speed** - Compress images, minimize code
6. **Add trust badges** - Security, guarantees, certifications
7. **Clarify value prop** - What do they get, specifically?
8. **Mobile check** - Test on actual mobile devices
9. **Remove distractions** - Links, navigation, pop-ups
10. **Add risk reversal** - Guarantee, free trial, refund

## 📋 LANDING PAGE TYPES

### Lead Generation Page
- Goal: Capture contact information
- Key elements: Form, value prop, social proof
- CTA: "Get Quote", "Download", "Sign Up"

### Click-Through Page
- Goal: Warm up visitors for next step
- Key elements: Benefits, details, testimonials
- CTA: "Learn More", "See Pricing", "Continue"

### Sales Page
- Goal: Direct purchase
- Key elements: Full pitch, objections, guarantee
- CTA: "Buy Now", "Add to Cart", "Subscribe"

### Squeeze Page
- Goal: Email capture only
- Key elements: One offer, minimal copy
- CTA: "Get Free [X]", "Send Me [X]"

### Thank You Page
- Goal: Confirm + next step
- Key elements: Confirmation, what's next, upsell
- CTA: "Check Email", "Schedule Call", "Download"
