## High-Converting Copy Frameworks

### The PAS Framework (Problem-Agitation-Solution)

**Structure:**
```
PROBLEM: Identify the pain point your customer feels
AGITATION: Make it hurt (describe the consequences)
SOLUTION: Present your product as the answer
```

**Example (Posture Corrector):**
```
PROBLEM: Your back hurts after hours at your desk.

AGITATION: That nagging pain between your shoulders won't go away. 
You've tried stretching, expensive chairs, even chiropractors. But 
nothing fixes the root cause: poor posture. Over time, it gets worse. 
Headaches. Lower back pain. That hunched-over feeling that never goes 
away.

SOLUTION: The PosturePro™ corrects your alignment instantly. Wear it 
under your clothes for 2 hours a day. Within 2 weeks, your body 
remembers proper posture naturally. No more pain. No more slouching. 
Just stand tall with confidence.
```

### The FAB Framework (Features-Advantages-Benefits)

**Structure:**
```
FEATURE: What your product IS or HAS
ADVANTAGE: What that feature DOES
BENEFIT: What that means for the CUSTOMER
```

**Example (Wireless Earbuds):**
```
FEATURE: Active Noise Cancellation technology
ADVANTAGE: Blocks out 95% of ambient noise
BENEFIT: Focus on your music, calls, or podcasts without distractions

FEATURE: 30-hour battery life with charging case
ADVANTAGE: Charge once per week with daily use
BENEFIT: Never worry about dead batteries during your commute

FEATURE: IPX7 waterproof rating
ADVANTAGE: Survives sweat, rain, and accidental drops in water
BENEFIT: Work out hard without worrying about damaging your earbuds
```

### The Before-After-Bridge Framework

**Structure:**
```
BEFORE: Describe their current struggling state
AFTER: Paint a picture of their ideal outcome
BRIDGE: Explain how your product gets them there
```

**Example (Teeth Whitening):**
```
BEFORE: You hide your smile in photos. Coffee stains won't budge no 
matter how much you brush. Whitening strips cause sensitivity. The 
dentist wants $600.

AFTER: Flash a confident smile in every photo. Enjoy your morning 
coffee without worry. Feel proud when you laugh.

BRIDGE: Our LED whitening kit gives you professional results at home. 
15 minutes a day for 7 days. Zero sensitivity. 94% of customers see 
results in week one.
```

## Image & Video Requirements

### Product Photography Checklist

**Must-Have Shots (Minimum 5-8 images):**

1. **Hero Shot** (Main image)
   - Clean white or lifestyle background
   - Product at 3/4 angle
   - High resolution (2048x2048 minimum)
   - Well-lit, no shadows

2. **Front View**
   - Straight-on, product facing camera
   - Shows full product clearly

3. **Back View**
   - Reverse angle
   - Shows any back features

4. **Side Views** (Left & Right)
   - Profile angles
   - Shows depth and dimensions

5. **Top/Bottom View**
   - Overhead or underside
   - Shows any unique features

6. **In-Use/Lifestyle Shot**
   - Product being used by person
   - Shows scale and context
   - Emotional connection

7. **Detail Shots** (2-3)
   - Close-ups of key features
   - Texture, materials, craftsmanship
   - Unique selling points

8. **Size/Scale Reference**
   - Product next to common object
   - Or being held by person
   - Helps customers visualize size

9. **Packaging Shot**
   - Shows what arrives
   - Unboxing experience
   - Gift-worthiness

10. **Comparison Shot** (Optional)
    - Your product vs. competitors
    - Before/after
    - Size comparison

### Image Specifications

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| Resolution | 2048x2048 pixels minimum |
| Format | JPG or PNG |
| File Size | Under 500KB each |
| Aspect Ratio | 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait) |
| Background | White or consistent lifestyle |
| Lighting | Bright, even, no harsh shadows |

### Image Optimization Tips

- **Compress images:** Use TinyPNG or Squoosh
- **Use WebP format:** Better compression than JPG
- **Consistent styling:** All images should look cohesive
- **Show variety:** Different angles, contexts, use cases
- **Include people:** Helps with scale and emotional connection
- **Zoom capability:** Enable zoom on product pages

### Video Requirements

**Types of Product Videos:**

1. **Product Demo (30-60 seconds)**
   - Show product in action
   - Highlight key features
   - Quick, engaging cuts

2. **How-To/ Tutorial (1-3 minutes)**
   - Step-by-step usage
   - Educational value
   - Address common questions

3. **Lifestyle Video (15-30 seconds)**
   - Emotional connection
   - Brand vibe
   - User-generated style

4. **Unboxing Video (30-60 seconds)**
   - Show packaging experience
   - Build anticipation
   - Show what's included

### Video Specifications

| Requirement | Specification |
|-------------|---------------|
| Length | 15-90 seconds (shorter is better) |
| Format | MP4 (H.264 codec) |
| Resolution | 1080p minimum |
| Aspect Ratio | 1:1 (feed) or 9:16 (stories/reels) |
| File Size | Under 100MB |
| Sound | Include captions (85% watch without sound) |

### Video Best Practices

- **Hook in first 3 seconds:** Grab attention immediately
- **Show, don't tell:** Demonstrate, don't just describe
- **Add captions:** Most watch without sound
- **Include CTA:** Tell viewers what to do next
- **Optimize for mobile:** Vertical or square format
- **Keep it authentic:** UGC-style often outperforms polished ads

## Review Generation System

### The Review Funnel

```
Purchase → Delivery → Usage → Request → Review → Incentive
   ↓          ↓          ↓         ↓         ↓         ↓
 Order     Shipping   Follow-up   Email     Submit    Reward
 Placed    Notice     Email     Request   Review    (Optional)
```

### Email Sequence for Reviews

**Email 1: Delivery Confirmation (Day 0)**
```
Subject: Your order has arrived! 📦

Hey [Name],

Great news — your [Product] has been delivered!

We hope you love it as much as we loved making it. 

Give it a try and let us know what you think. We read every piece of 
feedback.

Questions? Just reply to this email.

Cheers,
The [Brand] Team
```

**Email 2: First Impressions (Day 3-5)**
```
Subject: How's it going with your [Product]?

Hey [Name],

You've had your [Product] for a few days now. We'd love to hear your 
first impressions!

→ How's it working for you?
→ Anything we could improve?
→ Would you recommend it to a friend?

Hit reply and let us know. We're always listening.

Best,
[Founder Name]
Founder, [Brand]
```

**Email 3: Review Request (Day 7-14)**
```
Subject: Quick favor? 🙏

Hey [Name],

Loving your [Product]? We'd be so grateful if you could share your 
experience with a quick review.

[Leave a Review Button]

It takes less than 2 minutes and helps other customers make informed 
decisions.

As a thank you, here's 15% off your next order: THANKYOU15

Thanks for being part of the [Brand] family!

[Your Name]
Customer Success Team
```

**Email 4: Photo Review Request (Day 21)**
```
Subject: Show us your [Product]! 📸

Hey [Name],

We're featuring customer photos this week and would love to include 
yours!

Share a photo of your [Product] in action and get:
✓ Featured on our Instagram
✓ $20 credit toward your next purchase
✓ Bragging rights as a [Brand] VIP

[Upload Photo Button]

Can't wait to see it!

The [Brand] Team
```

### Review Incentive Strategies

**Ethical Incentives:**
- Discount on next purchase (10-20%)
- Entry into monthly giveaway
- Loyalty points
- Free shipping on next order
- Early access to new products

**What NOT to Do:**
- ❌ Don't offer incentives for 5-star reviews only
- ❌ Don't ask customers to change negative reviews
- ❌ Don't fake reviews (illegal and destroys trust)
- ❌ Don't review your own products

### Review Display Best Practices

- **Show all reviews:** Including 3-4 star (looks more authentic)
- **Sort by most recent:** Shows active customer base
- **Enable photo reviews:** Visual proof increases conversions
- **Respond to reviews:** Especially negative ones
- **Highlight helpful reviews:** Pin the most useful ones
- **Show review count:** "4.8 stars from 2,347 reviews"

### Handling Negative Reviews

**Response Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your honest feedback. We're sorry to hear [specific 
issue] wasn't the experience we want for our customers.

We'd love to make this right. Could you please email us at 
support@[brand].com with your order number? We'll get this sorted 
for you ASAP.

We appreciate you giving us a chance to fix this.

Best,
[Name]
Customer Care Team
[Brand]
```

## Urgency & Scarcity Tactics (Ethical)

### Types of Urgency

**1. Quantity Scarcity (Only use if TRUE)**
```
"Only 7 left in stock"
"Low inventory - order soon"
"Selling fast - 847 sold this week"
```

**2. Time Scarcity (Only use if TRUE)**
```
"Sale ends tonight at midnight"
"Free shipping ends in 2:34:17"
"Last chance - 50% off ends Sunday"
```

**3. Seasonal Urgency**
```
"Order by Dec 18 for Christmas delivery"
"Summer sale - while supplies last"
"Back to school - limited stock"
```

**4. Event-Based Urgency**
```
"Black Friday prices - 48 hours only"
"Launch special - first 100 customers"
"Anniversary sale - this weekend only"
```

### Ethical Guidelines

**DO:**
- ✅ Only show real inventory levels
- ✅ Only run sales for actual time periods
- ✅ Honor advertised deadlines
- ✅ Be transparent about limitations
- ✅ Use urgency sparingly (not on every page)

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Fake countdown timers that reset
- ❌ Show false "low stock" warnings
- ❌ Extend sales repeatedly
- ❌ Create fake scarcity
- ❌ Use urgency on every product

## A/B Testing Framework

### What to Test (Priority Order)

**High Impact (Test First):**
1. Headline/product title
2. Main product image
3. Price point
4. Call-to-action button (color, text, placement)
5. Product description (long vs. short)

**Medium Impact:**
6. Review placement
7. Trust badges
8. Shipping information
9. Guarantee messaging
10. Video vs. no video

**Lower Impact:**
11. Color scheme
12. Font choices
13. Secondary images
14. Social proof placement
15. Cross-sell offers

### A/B Test Template

```
Test Name: _______________
Date Started: _______________
Hypothesis: _______________

VARIATION A (Control):
- Current version
- Elements: _______________

VARIATION B (Test):
- What's different: _______________
- Expected impact: _______________

Success Metric: _______________
(e.g., Add to Cart Rate, Conversion Rate, AOV)

Minimum Sample Size: _______________
(use abtestcalculator.com)

Test Duration: _______________
(minimum 2 weeks or 1000+ visitors per variant)

Results:
- Variant A: ___% conversion (___ visitors)
- Variant B: ___% conversion (___ visitors)
- Winner: _______________
- Confidence Level: ___%

Learnings:
_______________
_______________

Next Test:
_______________
```

### Statistical Significance

**Before declaring a winner:**
- Minimum 1000 visitors per variant
- Minimum 2 weeks (accounts for weekly fluctuations)
- 95%+ confidence level
- Consistent results over time

**Free Tools:**
- AB Test Calculator: abtestcalculator.com
- Google Optimize (being sunset, consider alternatives)
- Shopify's built-in A/B testing (plus plans)

### Testing Best Practices

- **Test one variable at a time:** Isolate what's causing change
- **Run tests to completion:** Don't stop early
- **Don't peek:** Wait for statistical significance
- **Document everything:** Build institutional knowledge
- **Implement winners:** Don't let tests sit idle
- **Keep testing:** Optimization is ongoing

## Product Page Teardown Checklist

### Above the Fold (What visitors see first)

- [ ] Clear, benefit-driven headline
- [ ] High-quality hero image
- [ ] Visible price
- [ ] Variant selector (if applicable)
- [ ] Prominent Add to Cart button
- [ ] Trust signals near CTA
- [ ] No distractions or popups

### Product Information

- [ ] Compelling product description
- [ ] Benefit-focused copy (not just features)
- [ ] Bullet points for easy scanning
- [ ] Technical specs available
- [ ] Size guide (if applicable)
- [ ] Care/maintenance instructions

### Social Proof

- [ ] Star rating visible
- [ ] Review count shown
- [ ] Reviews easily accessible
- [ ] Photo reviews featured
- [ ] User-generated content
- [ ] Press mentions (if any)

### Trust & Risk Reversal

- [ ] Clear shipping info
- [ ] Return policy visible
- [ ] Guarantee/warranty stated
- [ ] Secure payment badges
- [ ] Contact information
- [ ] FAQ section

### Conversion Elements

- [ ] Stock level (if low)
- [ ] Shipping deadline/urgency
- [ ] Free shipping threshold
- [ ] Related products
- [ ] Recently viewed
- [ ] Email capture (exit intent)

### Mobile Optimization

- [ ] Fast loading on mobile
- [ ] Easy to navigate
- [ ] Thumb-friendly buttons
- [ ] Readable text (16px+)
- [ ] Images load quickly
- [ ] Checkout works smoothly
