## Welcome Series (5 Emails)

### Overview

**Goal:** Convert new subscribers into first-time buyers
**Timeline:** Days 0-10
**Expected Revenue:** 15-25% of total email revenue

### Email 1: Welcome + Offer (Day 0)

**Timing:** Immediately after signup
**Goal:** Deliver offer, set expectations, build connection

**Subject Lines:**
- Welcome to [Brand]! Here's 15% off 🎁
- You're in! Your discount is inside
- Welcome! Let's get you [desired outcome]
- Hi [Name], your 15% off is waiting

**Preview Text:**
- Your exclusive discount code is inside + here's what to expect
- Start your journey to [outcome] today
- Here's everything you need to know

**Template:**
```
Hey [First Name],

Welcome to the [Brand] family! 🎉

We're so glad you're here. As a welcome gift, here's 15% off your 
first order:

CODE: WELCOME15

[Shop Now Button]

Here's what you can expect from us:
✓ Weekly tips on [topic]
✓ Exclusive deals for subscribers
✓ Early access to new products
✓ No spam, ever. Unsubscribe anytime.

Questions? Just reply to this email. We read every message.

Cheers,
[Founder Name]
Founder, [Brand]

P.S. Free shipping on orders over $[X]!
```

Best,
[Founder Name]
Founder, [Brand]

[Product image or lifestyle photo]
[Shop Collection Button]

## Abandon Cart Flow (3 Emails + SMS)

### Overview

**Goal:** Recover lost sales from cart abandoners
**Timeline:** 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours
**Expected Recovery Rate:** 10-20%

### Email 1: Gentle Reminder (1 Hour)

**Timing:** 1 hour after abandonment
**Goal:** Quick reminder, assume good intent

**Subject Lines:**
- Did you forget something?
- Your cart is waiting...
- You left something behind
- Complete your order, [Name]!

**Preview Text:**
- Your items are still in your cart
- We saved your cart for you
- Ready to checkout?

**Template:**
```
Hey [First Name],

We noticed you left some items in your cart. No worries — we saved 
them for you!

[Cart Items with Images]

[Product 1] - $[Price]
[Product 2] - $[Price]

[Return to Cart Button]

Questions about your order? Reply to this email.

See you soon,
The [Brand] Team

P.S. Free shipping on orders over $[X]!
```

### SMS 1: Quick Nudge (2 Hours)

**Timing:** 2 hours after abandonment
**Goal:** Immediate attention, short and sweet

**Template:**
```
[Brand]: Hey [Name]! Your cart is waiting 🛒 Complete your order 
now: [Short Link]

Reply STOP to opt out
```

### Email 2: Social Proof + Urgency (24 Hours)

**Timing:** 24 hours after abandonment
**Goal:** Build trust, add urgency

**Subject Lines:**
- Your cart is expiring soon
- Still thinking about it?
- Popular items sell out fast
- [Name], your cart won't last

**Preview Text:**
- Items in your cart are in high demand
- Complete your order before it's gone
- See what others are saying

**Template:**
```
Hey [First Name],

Just a heads up — items in your cart are selling fast!

[Cart Items with Images]

[Product 1] - $[Price]
[Product 2] - $[Price]

**Total: $[Total]**

[Complete Your Order Button]

**Why customers love us:**

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Best purchase I've made this year!" - Sarah M.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Exactly what I needed. Fast shipping too!" - Mike R.
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ "Game changer. Highly recommend!" - Jessica L.

Still have questions? We're here to help.

[Complete Your Order Button]

Best,
The [Brand] Team

P.S. Free shipping + [X]-day returns on all orders.
```

### Email 3: Incentive (72 Hours)

**Timing:** 72 hours after abandonment
**Goal:** Remove final barriers with discount

**Subject Lines:**
- Here's 10% off to finish your order
- A little something for you...
- Complete your order & save 10%
- Last chance + special offer

**Preview Text:**
- Your exclusive discount inside
- Save 10% on your cart
- Final reminder + bonus

**Template:**
```
Hey [First Name],

We'd love to help you complete your order. Here's 10% off as a 
little nudge:

CODE: COMEBACK10

[Cart Items with Images]

[Product 1] - $[Price]
[Product 2] - $[Price]

**Original Total: $[Total]**
**With 10% Off: $[Discounted Total]**

[Complete Your Order Button]

This code expires in 48 hours. Don't miss out!

[Complete Your Order Button]

Questions? Just reply. We're here for you.

Cheers,
The [Brand] Team

P.S. Free shipping + risk-free [X]-day trial. You've got nothing to 
lose!
```

### SMS 2: Final Offer (72 Hours)

**Timing:** 72 hours after abandonment
**Goal:** Deliver discount code, create urgency

**Template:**
```
[Brand]: [Name], here's 10% off your cart! Use code COMEBACK10 at 
checkout. Expires in 48hrs: [Short Link]

Reply STOP to opt out
```

## Post-Purchase Sequence (4 Emails)

### Overview

**Goal:** Delight customers, encourage reviews, drive repeat purchases
**Timeline:** Day 0, 3, 7, 21
**Expected Impact:** 20-30% increase in LTV

### Email 1: Order Confirmation + Excitement (Day 0)

**Timing:** Immediately after purchase
**Goal:** Confirm order, build excitement

**Subject Lines:**
- Order confirmed! 🎉
- You're all set, [Name]!
- Great choice! Your order is confirmed
- Welcome to the family! Order #[Number]

**Preview Text:**
- Here's what happens next
- Your order details inside
- Get ready for [outcome]

**Template:**
```
Hey [First Name],

Great news — your order is confirmed! 🎉

**Order #[Order Number]**
[Date]

[Order Items]
[Product 1] - $[Price]
[Product 2] - $[Price]

**Subtotal: $[Subtotal]**
**Shipping: $[Shipping]**
**Total: $[Total]**

**Shipping To:**
[Customer Address]

**What's Next:**
1. We'll pack your order with care
2. You'll get a tracking number within [X] hours
3. Delivery in [X-X] business days

[Track Your Order Button]

Questions? Reply to this email. We're here for you!

Thanks for choosing [Brand],
The [Brand] Team

P.S. Follow us on Instagram @[handle] for tips, updates, and customer 
features!
```

### Email 2: Shipping Notification (Day 3-5)

**Timing:** When order ships
**Goal:** Provide tracking, maintain excitement

**Subject Lines:**
- Your order is on the way! 📦
- Good news! Shipped & on its way

- It's coming! Your [Brand] order shipped

**Preview Text:**
- Track your package inside

- Delivery estimate inside

**Template:**
```

**Order #[Order Number]**

[Track Your Package Button]

[Customer Address]

[Tip 3]

The [Brand] Team

**Goal:** Generate reviews, gather feedback

**Subject Lines:**

CODE: THANKYOU15

The [Brand] Team

## SMS Message Templates

### SMS Best Practices

**Guidelines:**
- Keep it under 160 characters
- Include clear CTA
- Use short links
- Always include opt-out
- Don't over-message (2-4/month max)

### SMS Templates

**Welcome SMS:**
```
[Brand]: Welcome to [Brand]! Here's 15% off: WELCOME15. Shop now: 
[Link]. Reply STOP to opt out.
```

**Flash Sale:**
```
[Brand]: FLASH SALE! 30% off everything for 24hrs only! Use code: 
FLASH30. Shop: [Link]. Reply STOP to opt out.
```

**Back in Stock:**
```
[Brand]: Good news! [Product] is back in stock. Grab yours before 
it's gone: [Link]. Reply STOP to opt out.
```

**Cart Abandonment:**
```
[Brand]: Hey [Name]! Your cart is waiting 🛒 Complete your order: 
[Link]. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Klaviyo Setup Guide

### Initial Setup

**Step 1: Create Account**
- Visit: klaviyo.com
- Sign up for free account (up to 250 contacts)
- Connect your Shopify store

**Step 2: Install Klaviyo on Site**
- Install Klaviyo app from Shopify App Store
- Or add Klaviyo snippet to theme code
- Verify installation

**Step 3: Configure Settings**
- Set up sender profile (name, email)
- Configure SMS settings (if using)
- Set up tracking (UTM parameters)

**Step 4: Create Segments**

**Essential Segments:**
```
1. Engaged Subscribers (opened/clicked in 90 days)
2. VIP Customers (3+ purchases or $500+ LTV)
3. At-Risk Customers (60+ days since last purchase)
4. Cart Abandoners (added to cart, no purchase)
5. Browse Abandoners (viewed product, no cart)
6. First-Time Buyers (exactly 1 purchase)
7. Repeat Customers (2+ purchases)
8. Email List (all subscribers)
```

**Step 5: Set Up Flows**
- Welcome Series
- Abandon Cart
- Browse Abandonment
- Post-Purchase
- Win-Back
- Review Request

**Step 6: Create Campaigns**
- Weekly newsletter
- Promotional campaigns
- Product launches
- Seasonal campaigns

### Flow Setup Checklist

**Welcome Series:**
- [ ] Trigger: Subscribed to list
- [ ] Filter: Not yet purchased
- [ ] Emails: 5 emails over 10 days
- [ ] Include discount code
- [ ] Test all links

**Abandon Cart:**
- [ ] Trigger: Started checkout
- [ ] Filter: Not purchased
- [ ] Time delay: 1 hour, 24 hours, 72 hours
- [ ] Include cart items dynamic
- [ ] Add discount in final email

**Post-Purchase:**
- [ ] Trigger: Placed order
- [ ] Emails: Confirmation, shipping, review, cross-sell
- [ ] Timing: 0, 3, 14, 21 days
- [ ] Personalize with order details

**Win-Back:**
- [ ] Trigger: Placed order (last)
- [ ] Filter: No orders since
- [ ] Time delay: 60, 70, 90 days
- [ ] Include reactivation offer

## Segmentation Strategies

### Behavioral Segments

**By Purchase Behavior:**
```
- First-time buyers
- Repeat customers (2-5 purchases)
- VIP customers (6+ purchases)
- High AOV customers
- Discount shoppers
- Full-price buyers
```

**By Engagement:**
```
- Highly engaged (opened/clicked in 7 days)
- Engaged (opened/clicked in 30 days)
- At-risk (no engagement in 60 days)
- Inactive (no engagement in 90+ days)
```

**By Product Interest:**
```
- Category A buyers
- Category B buyers
- Cross-category buyers
- Single-product buyers
```

### RFM Segmentation

**Recency (Days since last purchase):**
- R5: 0-30 days
- R4: 31-60 days
- R3: 61-90 days
- R2: 91-180 days
- R1: 180+ days

**Frequency (Number of purchases):**
- F5: 10+ purchases
- F4: 5-9 purchases
- F3: 3-4 purchases
- F2: 2 purchases
- F1: 1 purchase

**Monetary (Total spend):**
- M5: $1000+
- M4: $500-999
- M3: $250-499
- M2: $100-249
- M1: Under $100

**Best Segments to Target:**
- R5F5M5: Champions (reward, exclusive access)
- R5F4M4: Loyal customers (upsell, referral program)
- R4F3M3: Potential loyalists (nurture, special offers)
- R2F2M2: At-risk (win-back campaigns)
- R1F1M1: Lost (aggressive win-back or sunset)
