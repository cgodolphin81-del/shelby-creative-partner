## 2. Break-Even Calculator

### Formula:
```
Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost per Unit)
Break-Even Revenue = Break-Even Units × Price
```

### Calculator:

```

===================

Salaries: $_______________
Software/Tools: $_______________
Marketing (Base): $_______________
Other: $_______________
TOTAL FIXED COSTS: $_______________

VARIABLE COSTS (Per Unit):
Product Cost: $_______________
Shipping to Customer: $_______________
Packaging: $_______________
Payment Processing (%): _____% = $_______________
Other: $_______________
TOTAL VARIABLE COST/UNIT: $_______________

PRICING:
Selling Price per Unit: $_______________

CALCULATIONS:

Contribution Margin per Unit:
= Price - Variable Cost
= $_______ - $_______ = $_______________

Break-Even Units (Monthly):
= Fixed Costs / Contribution Margin
= $_______ / $_______ = _______________ units/month

Break-Even Revenue (Monthly):
= Break-Even Units × Price
= _______ × $_______ = $_______________/month

Break-Even Daily:
= Break-Even Units / 30
= _______ / 30 = _______________ units/day

ANALYSIS:
□ Easily achievable (<10 orders/day)
□ Challenging but doable (10-50 orders/day)
□ Difficult (50+ orders/day) - reconsider pricing or costs

SCENARIOS:
```

## 3. Unit Economics Calculator

### How to Use:
Fill in your numbers to calculate profitability per order

```
UNIT ECONOMICS CALCULATOR
=========================

AVERAGE ORDER VALUE (AOV):
Product Revenue: $_______________
Shipping Charged: $_______________
Other: $_______________
TOTAL AOV: $_______________

COST OF GOODS SOLD (COGS):
Product Cost: $_______________
Inbound Shipping: $_______________
Duties/Tariffs: $_______________
Packaging: $_______________
TOTAL COGS: $_______________

GROSS PROFIT:
= AOV - COGS
= $_______ - $_______ = $_______________

GROSS MARGIN %:
= (Gross Profit / AOV) × 100
= ($_______ / $_______) × 100 = _______%

OPERATING COSTS (Per Order):
Marketing/CAC: $_______________
Fulfillment (3PL): $_______________
Payment Processing (3%): $_______________
Returns Reserve (3%): $_______________
Other: $_______________
TOTAL OPERATING COST/ORDER: $_______________

CONTRIBUTION MARGIN:
= Gross Profit - Operating Costs
= $_______ - $_______ = $_______________

CONTRIBUTION MARGIN %:
= (Contribution Margin / AOV) × 100
= _______%

PROFITABILITY:
□ Profitable (positive contribution margin)
□ Break-even (near zero)
□ Unprofitable (negative) - need to optimize

LEVERS TO IMPROVE:
□ Increase AOV (bundles, upsells, free shipping threshold)
□ Reduce COGS (negotiate with suppliers, order more)
□ Reduce CAC (better ad performance, organic channels)
□ Reduce fulfillment costs (negotiate with 3PL)
```

## 4. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) Calculator

### Formula:
```
LTV = AOV × Purchase Frequency × Gross Margin % × Customer Lifespan
```

### Calculator:

```
LTV CALCULATOR
==============

AVERAGE ORDER VALUE (AOV): $_______________

PURCHASE FREQUENCY:
Orders per year: _______________
(Calculate: Total orders / Unique customers / Years)

GROSS MARGIN %: _______%
(From unit economics above)

CUSTOMER LIFESPAN:
Years as active customer: _______________
(Months / 12, e.g., 24 months = 2 years)

LTV CALCULATION:
LTV = $_______ × _______ × _______% × _______
LTV = $_______________

CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COST (CAC): $_______________
(Calculate: Total ad spend / New customers)

LTV:CAC RATIO:
= LTV / CAC
= $_______ / $_______ = _______:1

HEALTH CHECK:
□ Excellent (5:1+) - Can invest more in growth
□ Good (3:1 to 5:1) - Healthy, sustainable
□ Okay (2:1 to 3:1) - Room for improvement
□ Poor (<2:1) - Need to improve LTV or reduce CAC

CAC PAYBACK PERIOD:
= CAC / (AOV × Gross Margin % × Purchase Frequency / 12)
= $_______ / $_______ = _______ months

Target: <3 months is excellent, <6 months is good
```

## 6. Inventory Reorder Calculator

### When to Reorder:
```
Reorder Point = (Daily Sales × Lead Time) + Safety Stock
```

### Calculator:

```

=============================

PRODUCT: _______________________
SKU: _______________________

SALES DATA:
Average Daily Sales: _______ units
Max Daily Sales: _______ units

SUPPLIER LEAD TIME:
Average Lead Time: _______ days
Max Lead Time: _______ days

SAFETY STOCK CALCULATION:
= (Max Daily Sales × Max Lead Time) - (Avg Daily Sales × Avg Lead Time)
= (_______ × _______) - (_______ × _______)
= _______ - _______ = _______ units

REORDER POINT CALCULATION:
= (Avg Daily Sales × Avg Lead Time) + Safety Stock
= (_______ × _______) + _______
= _______ + _______ = _______ units

CURRENT INVENTORY: _______ units

STATUS:
□ REORDER NOW (at or below reorder point)
□ Monitor closely (within 20% of reorder point)
□ Well stocked (above reorder point)

REORDER QUANTITY:
= (Daily Sales × Days of inventory desired) - Current Stock + Safety Stock
= (_______ × _______) - _______ + _______
= _______ units

SUGGESTED ORDER: _______ units @ $_______/unit = $_______________
```
