## Phase 1: Content Strategy Development

### Content Audit (Existing Content)

**Step 1: Inventory**
- Export all URLs (Screaming Frog)
- Pull performance data (GSC, GA4)
- Categorize by content type

**Step 2: Performance Analysis**

| Metric | Tool | Action |
|--------|------|--------|
| Organic traffic | GA4 | Identify winners/losers |
| Rankings | GSC | Track keyword positions |
| Conversions | GA4 | Find high-converting content |
| Bounce rate | GA4 | Identify engagement issues |
| Backlinks | Ahrefs | Find link-worthy content |

**Step 3: Content Scoring**

Rate each piece (1-5):
- Traffic performance
- Conversion performance
- Content quality
- SEO optimization
- Freshness/accuracy

**Step 4: Action Matrix**

| Score | Traffic | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 5 | High | Maintain, update annually |
| 4 | High | Minor optimization |
| 3 | Medium | Significant refresh |
| 2 | Low | Rewrite or consolidate |
| 1 | Low | Delete or redirect |

---

### Content Gap Analysis

**Process:**
1. Identify 3-5 top competitors
2. Export their top content (Ahrefs Top Pages)
3. Compare with client's content
4. Identify missing topics
5. Prioritize by opportunity

**Gap Categories:**
- Topics they cover, you don't
- Keywords they rank for, you don't
- Content formats they use, you don't
- SERP features they have, you don't

### Content Pillar Strategy

**Pillar Types:**

#### 1. Guide Pillars
Comprehensive resources (3,000-5,000+ words)
- "Ultimate Guide to X"
- "Complete Beginner's Guide to Y"
- "Everything You Need to Know About Z"

**Purpose:** Establish authority, attract links, rank for broad terms

---

#### 2. Product/Service Pillars
Commercial intent pages
- Product category pages
- Service pages
- Comparison pages

**Purpose:** Drive conversions, target commercial keywords

---

#### 3. Hub Pillars
Topic cluster centers
- Resource hubs
- Learning centers
- Knowledge bases

**Purpose:** Organize content, improve internal linking

### Topic Cluster Planning

**Template:**

```
PILLAR: [Broad Topic]
Target Keyword: [main keyword]
URL: /pillar-url/

CLUSTER CONTENT:
├── [Subtopic 1] → Keyword: [keyword] → URL: /cluster-1/
├── [Subtopic 2] → Keyword: [keyword] → URL: /cluster-2/
├── [Subtopic 3] → Keyword: [keyword] → URL: /cluster-3/
└── [Subtopic 4] → Keyword: [keyword] → URL: /cluster-4/

INTERNAL LINKING:
- All clusters → Pillar (contextual)
- Pillar → All clusters (navigation)
- Clusters → Related clusters (where relevant)
```

---

### Brief Creation Process

**Step 1: Keyword Analysis**
- Pull keyword data (volume, KD, intent)
- Analyze SERP (top 10 results)
- Identify content gaps

**Step 2: Competitor Research**
- Review top 3-5 ranking pages
- Note word count, structure, depth
- Identify what's missing

**Step 3: Outline Creation**
- Create H2/H3 structure
- Map keywords to sections
- Include unique angles

**Step 4: Requirements**
- Set word count target
- Define internal linking
- Specify CTAs

**Step 5: Review**
- SEO manager approval
- Send to writer

## Phase 3: Content Creation

### Writer Guidelines

**Quality Standards:**
- Original research/insights (not just rehashed)
- Data-backed claims (cite sources)
- Clear, scannable structure
- Conversational but authoritative tone
- No fluff or filler
- Actionable takeaways

**SEO Requirements:**
- Primary keyword in H1, first 100 words, conclusion
- Secondary keywords naturally integrated
- Internal links (3-5 per 1,000 words)
- External links to authoritative sources (2-3)
- Optimized images with alt text
- Schema markup where applicable

**Readability:**
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)
- Subheadings every 200-300 words
- Bullet points and lists
- Bold key phrases
- Active voice

---

### Content Types & Specifications

#### Blog Posts
- **Length:** 1,500-3,000 words
- **Purpose:** Traffic, awareness
- **Frequency:** 2-4x/week
- **KPIs:** Traffic, time on page, shares

#### Pillar Pages
- **Length:** 3,000-5,000+ words
- **Purpose:** Authority, rankings
- **Frequency:** 1-2x/month
- **KPIs:** Rankings, backlinks, traffic

#### Product/Service Pages
- **Length:** 800-2,000 words
- **Purpose:** Conversions
- **Frequency:** As needed
- **KPIs:** Conversion rate, revenue

#### Case Studies
- **Length:** 1,500-3,000 words
- **Purpose:** Trust, conversions
- **Frequency:** 1-2x/month
- **KPIs:** Demo requests, sales calls

#### Guides/Ebooks
- **Length:** 5,000-10,000+ words
- **Purpose:** Lead generation
- **Frequency:** 1x/quarter
- **KPIs:** Downloads, leads

---

### Content Creation Workflow

```
Brief Created → Writer Assigned → First Draft → SEO Review → 
Client Review (if needed) → Revisions → Final Approval → 
Publication → Promotion
```

**Timeline:**
- Brief: 1 day
- First draft: 3-5 days
- Review/revisions: 2-3 days
- Publication: 1 day
- **Total: 7-10 days per piece**

### Content Refresh Strategy

**When to Refresh:**
- Traffic declining (check GSC)
- Rankings dropped 5+ positions
- Content outdated (12+ months)
- New information available
- Competitors outranking you

**Refresh Process:**
1. Audit current performance
2. Identify gaps vs. competitors
3. Update statistics/data
4. Add new sections
5. Improve internal linking
6. Re-optimize for keywords
7. Republish with new date
8. Promote as "updated"

**Impact:** Refreshed content can see 50-200% traffic increase

## Phase 5: Internal Linking

### Internal Link Strategy

**Principles:**
1. Link from high-authority pages to new/important pages
2. Use descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
3. Link deep (not just homepage)
4. Create topic clusters with hub pages
5. Update old content with new links

**Link Types:**
- **Navigational:** Menu, footer, sidebar
- **Contextual:** Within content (most valuable)
- **Image links:** With optimized alt text
- **CTA links:** Conversion-focused

---

### Internal Link Audit

**Monthly Check:**
- [ ] Orphan pages (no internal links)
- [ ] Pages with only 1-2 internal links
- [ ] Broken internal links
- [ ] Over-optimized anchor text
- [ ] Link equity distribution

**Tools:**
- Screaming Frog (internal link report)
- Ahrefs (Internal Link Opportunities)
- Sitebulb (visualization)

---

### Link Building Within Content

**Best Practices:**
- 3-5 internal links per 1,000 words
- Link to relevant cluster content
- Link to pillar pages from clusters
- Update old posts with new links
- Use varied anchor text

### Content ROI Calculation

```
Content ROI = (Revenue from Content - Content Cost) / Content Cost × 100

Example:
- Content cost: $5,000/month
- Revenue attributed: $50,000/month
- ROI: ($50,000 - $5,000) / $5,000 × 100 = 900%
```

**Attribution Methods:**
- First-touch (organic landing page)
- Last-touch (before conversion)
- Multi-touch (assisted conversions)
- Time-decay (recent interactions weighted higher)

4. **Promote everything** - Publishing is only 50% of the work
5. **Measure everything** - Data drives decisions
6. **Repurpose smartly** - Maximize content ROI
7. **Build clusters** - Topical authority wins
8. **Internal link strategically** - Distribute link equity
