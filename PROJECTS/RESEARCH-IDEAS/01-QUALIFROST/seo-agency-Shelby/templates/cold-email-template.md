# Cold Email Templates

Proven templates for SEO agency outreach.

---

## Template 1: The Observation

**Subject:** Quick SEO observation for {{company}}

```
Hi {{first_name}},

I was searching for "{{target_keyword}}" and noticed {{company}} 
ranks on page {{page_number}}, just behind {{competitor}}.

You're actually outranking them on {{strength}}, but they're 
winning on {{opportunity}}.

We recently helped {{similar_company}} jump from page {{page}} 
to position {{position}} for similar terms, resulting in 
{{result}}.

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call to discuss what 
I'm seeing? No pitch, just insights.

Best,
{{your_name}}
{{your_title}}
{{your_company}}
```

**Best For:** Companies ranking on page 1-2
**Response Rate:** 8-15%

## Best Practices

### Personalization

**Minimum:**
- First name
- Company name
- One specific observation

**Better:**
- Recent company news
- Specific keyword/opportunity
- Competitor name
- Industry-specific insight

**Best:**
- All of the above
- Personal connection (if any)
- Customized case study
- Video message (Loom)

---

### Subject Lines That Work

**Observation-based:**
- "Quick SEO observation for {{company}}"
- "Noticed something on {{company}}'s site"
- "{{number}} SEO opportunities for {{company}}"

**Competitor-based:**
- "{{competitor}} is outranking you"
- "Your competitors are winning on Google"
- "Traffic going to {{competitor}}"

**Result-based:**
- "How we helped {{similar_company}} grow {{x}}%"
- "{{x}}% more organic traffic in {{months}} months"
- "Adding {{revenue}}/mo in organic revenue"

**Question-based:**
- "Quick question about {{company}}'s SEO"
- "Are you happy with {{company}}'s rankings?"
- "Can I share an SEO insight?"

---

### Timing

**Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
**Best Times:** 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (recipient's timezone)
**Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

---

### A/B Testing

**Test:**
- Subject lines
- Email length
- CTA type
- Personalization level
- Send time
- Follow-up timing

**Metrics to Track:**
- Open rate (target: 30-50%)
- Reply rate (target: 5-15%)
- Call booked rate (target: 2-5%)
- Close rate (target: 20-40% of calls)

### Compliance

**GDPR (Europe):**
- Legitimate interest basis
- Opt-out required
- Data privacy compliance

**CAN-SPAM (US):**
- Accurate subject lines
- Physical address required
- Opt-out mechanism
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days

**Best Practice:** Always include opt-out, even when not strictly required.
