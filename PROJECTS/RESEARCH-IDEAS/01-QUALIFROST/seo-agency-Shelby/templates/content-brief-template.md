## Best Practices

1. **Be specific** - Vague briefs = vague content
2. **Include examples** - Show, don't just tell
3. **Provide context** - Help writer understand the "why"
4. **Set clear expectations** - Word count, deadline, quality
5. **Include keywords naturally** - Don't keyword stuff
6. **Review before sending** - Ensure brief is complete
7. **Be available for questions** - Writers will have them
8. **Iterate** - Improve briefs based on output quality
