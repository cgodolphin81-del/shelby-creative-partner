## Team Structure Overview

### Agency Stages & Team Size

| Stage | Revenue | Team Size | Structure |
|-------|---------|-----------|-----------|
| Solo | $0-$10K/mo | 1 | Founder does everything |
| Boutique | $10K-$50K/mo | 2-5 | Founder + specialists |

### Interview Process

**Stage 1: Screening Call (15-20 min)**
- Verify resume details
- Assess communication
- Salary expectations
- Availability
- Culture fit initial read

**Questions:**
- "Tell me about your background."
- "What interests you about this role?"
- "What are you looking for in your next role?"
- "What's your salary expectation?"

---

**Stage 2: Skills Assessment (Take-home)**

**For SEO Specialists:**
```
Audit this website and provide:
1. Top 5 technical issues
2. Top 5 content opportunities
3. 3-month priority roadmap
4. Expected outcomes

[Provide access to a real or sample site]
```

**For Content Writers:**
```
Write a 1,500-word article on [topic].
Target keyword: [keyword]
Include: H2s, H3s, internal links, meta tags
```

**For Link Builders:**
```
Find 20 link opportunities for [client type].
Include: URL, DR, contact info, opportunity type.
Draft an outreach email.
```

**Time Limit:** 2-3 hours max
**Compensation:** Pay for their time ($50-$200)

---

**Stage 3: Deep Dive Interview (45-60 min)**
- Review assessment
- Scenario questions
- Problem-solving
- Team fit
- Career goals

**Questions:**
- "Walk me through your assessment."
- "How would you handle [common scenario]?"
- "Tell me about a time you [specific situation]."
- "What's your approach to [key skill]?"
- "Where do you see yourself in 2-3 years?"

---

**Stage 4: Final Interview (30 min)**
- Meet founder/leadership
- Culture fit
- Final questions
- Close

## Onboarding Process

### Week 1: Orientation

**Day 1:**
- [ ] Welcome email sent
- [ ] Equipment shipped/ready
- [ ] Accounts created (email, Slack, tools)
- [ ] Intro call with manager
- [ ] Company overview presentation

**Day 2-3:**
- [ ] Tool training (Ahrefs, GSC, etc.)
- [ ] Process documentation review
- [ ] Shadow team members
- [ ] Meet the team (virtual coffee)

**Day 4-5:**
- [ ] First assignment (low stakes)
- [ ] Review and feedback
- [ ] Week 1 check-in

---

### Week 2-3: Training

**SEO Fundamentals (if needed):**
- Our SEO methodology
- Audit process
- Reporting standards
- Client communication guidelines

**Tool Training:**
- Ahrefs/SEMrush
- Google Analytics
- Google Search Console
- Project management tool
- Reporting tool

**Shadowing:**
- Sit in on client calls
- Review past work examples
- Learn from senior team members

---

### Week 4: First Real Work

- [ ] Assigned first clients/tasks
- [ ] Clear expectations set
- [ ] Regular check-ins scheduled
- [ ] Feedback loop established

---

### 30-60-90 Day Plan

**30 Days:**
- Complete onboarding
- Handle basic tasks independently
- Understand processes
- Build team relationships

**60 Days:**
- Manage own workload
- Handle client communication
- Contribute ideas
- Meet productivity targets

**90 Days:**
- Fully independent
- Consistent quality
- Take on more responsibility
- Performance review

## Freelancer vs. In-House

### When to Hire Freelancers

**Good For:**
- Overflow work
- Specialized skills (one-off)
- Testing before hiring
- Cost savings
- Flexibility

**Roles:**
- Content writers
- Link builders
- Graphic designers
- Developers (project-based)

**Management:**
- Clear briefs
- Regular check-ins
- Quality control
- Build relationships

---

### When to Hire In-House

**Good For:**
- Core competencies
- Client-facing roles
- Strategic work
- Culture building
- Long-term growth

**Roles:**
- SEO specialists
- Account managers
- Strategists
- Leadership

## Scaling the Team

### Hiring Triggers

| Metric | Trigger | Hire |
|--------|---------|------|
| Revenue/Employee | >$150K/year | Add capacity |
| Utilization | >80% for 4 weeks | Add specialist |
| Client Satisfaction | Dropping | Add support |
| Pipeline | 3+ months full | Add sales |
| Burnout Signs | Multiple team members | Add capacity |

---

### Scaling Phases

**Phase 1 ($0-$50K/mo): Founder + 1-3 Generalists**
- Founder handles sales + strategy
- Generalists handle execution
- Heavy use of freelancers

**Phase 2 ($50K-$150K/mo): Department Heads**
- Hire SEO Manager
- Hire Content Manager
- Founder focuses on sales/strategy
- Specialists under managers

**Phase 3 ($150K-$500K/mo): Full Departments**
- Director of SEO
- Director of Content
- Account Management team
- Operations Manager
- Sales team

**Phase 4 ($500K+/mo): Executive Layer**
- VP of SEO
- VP of Sales
- VP of Operations
- Multiple directors
- Specialized teams

## Best Practices

1. **Hire slow, fire fast** - Bad hires are expensive
2. **Culture eats strategy** - Hire for fit
3. **Document everything** - Scale requires systems
4. **Invest in training** - Better skills = better results
5. **Pay well** - Top talent costs more but delivers more
6. **Promote from within** - When possible
7. **Get feedback** - Regular surveys, 1:1s
8. **Lead by example** - Culture starts at top
