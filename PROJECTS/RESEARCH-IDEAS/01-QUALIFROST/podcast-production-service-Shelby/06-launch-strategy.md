# Launch Strategy

**How to launch your podcast with maximum impact: trailer, first 5 episodes, and promotion**

---

## 🎯 Why Your Launch Matters

**First impressions are everything.** Podcast algorithms favor shows with strong early momentum. A strategic launch can:

- Get you featured in "New & Noteworthy" sections
- Build initial audience momentum
- Generate reviews that build social proof
- Create buzz in your niche
- Set the tone for long-term growth

**The goal:** Launch with enough episodes and promotion that you look established from day one.

## 🎬 Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks -4 to -2)

### Week -4: Foundation

#### Define Your Launch Goals
```
□ Download targets: ___ downloads in first 30 days
□ Review targets: ___ reviews in first week
□ Email list: ___ subscribers from launch
□ Social following: ___ new followers
□ Guest bookings: ___ podcast appearances
```

#### Finalize Podcast Identity
- [ ] Podcast name (trademark search completed)
- [ ] Cover art (3000x3000px, professional)
- [ ] Show description (short and long versions)
- [ ] Episode format and length finalized
- [ ] Release schedule determined (weekly? biweekly?)
- [ ] Release day chosen (Tuesday-Thursday perform best)

#### Build Your Launch Assets
- [ ] Website or landing page
- [ ] Email signup form
- [ ] Social media profiles (minimum: Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram)
- [ ] Media kit (one-pager about your show)
- [ ] Launch announcement graphics

### Week -3: Content Creation

#### Record Your Launch Episodes
**Minimum for launch:**
- 1 Trailer (Episode 0)
- 3-5 Full Episodes

**Why multiple episodes?**
- Binge-worthy for early adopters
- Shows commitment (not a "podfader")
- More content to promote
- Better algorithm performance

#### Episode 0: The Trailer (2-3 minutes)

**Trailer Script Template:**
```
[INTRO MUSIC - 5 seconds, then fades under]

HOST: "Welcome to [Podcast Name], the show that [unique value 
proposition in one sentence]."

[PAUSE]

HOST: "I'm [Your Name], and every [release day], I'll be bringing 
you [what listeners can expect]."

[PAUSE]

HOST: "Whether you're [target audience 1] or [target audience 2], 
this show is for you if you want to [desired outcome]."

[PAUSE]

HOST: "We're launching on [launch date] with [number] episodes to 
get you started. Make sure you subscribe now so you don't miss 
Episode 1."

[PAUSE]

HOST: "[Podcast Name] launches [launch date] on Apple Podcasts, 
Spotify, and everywhere you listen to podcasts. See you there."

[OUTRO MUSIC - 5 seconds, fade out]
```

**Trailer Checklist:**
- [ ] Under 3 minutes
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Release date mentioned
- [ ] Call-to-action to subscribe
- [ ] Professional audio quality
- [ ] Music licensed or royalty-free

#### Full Episodes (Episodes 1-5)

**Launch Episode Strategy:**

| Episode | Type | Purpose |
|---------|------|---------|
| 001 | High-value solo or big guest | Make a statement, show what's possible |
| 002 | Practical/how-to | Give immediate value, actionable takeaways |
| 003 | Interview with known name | Borrow their audience, add credibility |
| 004 | Controversial/unique angle | Generate discussion, stand out |
| 005 | Story-driven or emotional | Build connection, memorable |

**Quality Standards:**
- Professional audio (no background noise, consistent levels)
- Tight editing (no long pauses, filler words minimized)
- Clear structure (intro, segments, outro)
- Show notes completed for each
- Cover art consistent

## 📈 Phase 3: Post-Launch (Weeks +2 to +4)

### Week +2: Sustain Momentum

#### Review Collection Campaign

**Why reviews matter:**
- Apple Podcasts algorithm favors reviewed shows
- Social proof for new listeners
- Helps you get featured

**How to ask for reviews:**

**In your episode outro:**
```
"If you're enjoying [Podcast Name], please leave us a review on 
Apple Podcasts. It takes less than a minute and really helps 
other people find the show. Here's how:

1. Open Apple Podcasts
2. Search for '[Podcast Name]'
3. Tap the show
4. Scroll down and tap 'Write a Review'

Thank you so much for your support!"
```

**In show notes:**
```
🌟 Love the show? Leave us a review on Apple Podcasts!
[Direct link to review page]
```

**In email:**
```
Subject: Quick favor? 🙏

Hey [Name],

If you've been enjoying [Podcast Name], would you mind leaving 
us a review on Apple Podcasts?

It takes less than 60 seconds and makes a huge difference:
[Direct link]

Thank you!
[Your Name]
```

#### Guest Promotion Coordination

If you have guests on your launch episodes:

**Provide them with:**
- Pre-written social posts
- Graphics with their photo
- Direct links to their episode
- Suggested posting times

**Guest Posting Template:**
```
Hey [Guest Name],

Your episode of [Podcast Name] goes live on [date]!

To help maximize reach, here are some assets:

📝 Suggested post copy:
"Just had a great conversation with [Your Name] on the [Podcast 
Name] podcast about [topic]. Give it a listen! [link]"

🎨 Graphics: [Link to folder with quote cards, cover art]

🎧 Direct links:
- Apple Podcasts: [link]
- Spotify: [link]
- Your episode page: [link]

Feel free to post whenever works best for you. Tag us 
@[handle] and we'll reshare!

Thanks again for being on the show!

[Your Name]
```

### Week +3: Expand Reach

#### Podcast Guest Appearances

**Goal:** Appear on 2-3 other podcasts in your niche

**Find podcasts to guest on:**
- Listen Notes (search your topic)
- Podchaser (browse categories)
- Matchmaker.fm (podcast guest matching)
- Your network (ask for introductions)

**Pitch Template:**
```
Subject: Guest idea for [Their Podcast Name]

Hey [Host Name],

Love your show - especially the episode with [Guest Name] about 
[topic]. Great conversation!

I'm the host of [Your Podcast Name], where I [brief description]. 
I'd love to come on your show and talk about [specific topic idea 
that fits their show].

Some things we could discuss:
- [Topic idea 1]
- [Topic idea 2]
- [Topic idea 3]

I think your audience would enjoy this because [reason].

Here's where you can find me:
- [Website]
- [LinkedIn/Twitter]
- [My podcast]

Let me know if this sounds interesting!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### Cross-Promotion

**Podcast swap:**
- Find shows at similar size in adjacent niches
- Record a "trailer swap" – you promote them, they promote you
- Or do guest exchanges

**Example:**
- Business podcast partners with productivity podcast
- Health podcast partners with fitness podcast
- Tech podcast partners with startup podcast

## 🎯 Launch Goals & Benchmarks

### Realistic First 30-Day Goals

**By show size:**

| Show Type | Downloads | Reviews | Followers |
|-----------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| **Brand new, no audience** | 100-500 | 5-15 | 50-200 |
| **Existing audience (email/social)** | 500-2,000 | 15-50 | 200-1,000 |
| **Well-known host/guests** | 2,000-10,000+ | 50-200+ | 1,000-5,000+ |

**Don't compare yourself to others.** Focus on your own growth trajectory.

### New & Noteworthy (Apple Podcasts)

**Requirements:**
- 8 episodes published within first 8 weeks
- Strong subscription and review velocity
- Apple's algorithm is secret, but momentum matters

**Strategy:**
- Launch with 5 episodes
- Publish 1 episode/week for 3 more weeks
- Drive reviews in first 2 weeks
- Promote heavily during this window

## 🚨 Common Launch Mistakes

| Mistake | Consequence | Solution |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Launching with only 1 episode | Looks amateur, no binge potential | Launch with 3-5 episodes minimum |
| No trailer | Confusing for new listeners | Record a 2-3 minute trailer |
| Inconsistent release schedule | Loses audience trust | Commit to schedule you can maintain |
| No promotion plan | "If you build it, they will come" doesn't work | Create detailed promotion plan |
| Asking for reviews too late | Miss New & Noteworthy window | Ask from episode 1 |
| Ignoring email list | Leaving easiest wins on table | Email list converts best |
| No guest coordination | Missing amplification opportunities | Provide guests with share assets |
| Giving up too soon | Most podcasts quit before 10 episodes | Commit to at least 10 episodes |

## 💡 Launch Hacks

### The "Review Party"
Host a virtual launch party. Offer to do a live Q&A for anyone who leaves a review and sends you a screenshot.

### The "Episode Challenge"
Challenge listeners to complete an action from your episode and share results on social media. Best submission wins a prize.

### The "Founding Listener" Program
First 100 subscribers get:
- Special "Founding Listener" badge
- Access to private community
- Input on future episodes
- Exclusive bonus content

### The "Review Swap"
Partner with 5-10 other podcasters launching around the same time. Everyone agrees to review each other's shows.

### The "LinkedIn Launch"
Write a LinkedIn article about your podcast journey. Tag people who helped. LinkedIn's algorithm loves personal stories.
