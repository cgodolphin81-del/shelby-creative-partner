# Pre-Production Checklist

**Complete guide to planning your podcast before recording**

---

## 📋 Phase 1: Topic Research & Validation

### Market Research
- [ ] Identify your niche (be specific: "business" → "SaaS founders raising Series A")
- [ ] Search Apple Podcasts/Spotify for competing shows in your niche
- [ ] Note top 5 competitors: format, episode length, release frequency
- [ ] Read competitor reviews – what do listeners love/complain about?
- [ ] Identify content gaps your show can fill

### Audience Definition
- [ ] Create listener avatar (age, job, pain points, goals)
- [ ] Where does your audience hang out online? (Reddit, LinkedIn, Twitter, FB groups)
- [ ] What questions are they asking? (Search Quora, Reddit, Amazon reviews)
- [ ] What transformation do they want? (Current state → Desired state)

### Topic Validation
- [ ] List 20 potential episode topics
- [ ] Score each topic: 1-5 on audience interest, 1-5 on your expertise
- [ ] Pick top 10 topics for your first season
- [ ] Create working titles for each episode
- [ ] Validate with potential listeners (poll your network, social media)

### Unique Value Proposition
- [ ] Complete this sentence: "My podcast helps [audience] achieve [outcome] by [unique approach]"
- [ ] What makes YOUR perspective different?
- [ ] Why should someone listen to YOU specifically?

## 👥 Phase 2: Guest Booking (If Applicable)

### Guest List Creation
- [ ] Brainstorm 30 potential guests (aim for 3x your needed episodes)
- [ ] Categorize: Dream guests (10%), Realistic targets (40%), Easy wins (50%)
- [ ] Research each guest: recent appearances, topics they discuss, contact info

### Outreach Template
```
Subject: Podcast Invitation: [Their Topic] on [Your Show Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name], host of [Podcast Name] – a show that helps [audience] 
[achieve outcome]. We've reached [X downloads/listeners] and I think 
your perspective on [specific topic] would be invaluable.

I loved your [recent article/podcast/talk] about [specific detail]. 
Would you be open to a 30-45 minute conversation about [topic]?

No pressure at all – either way, keep up the great work!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Contact Info]
[Podcast Website/Links]
```

### Booking Workflow
- [ ] Send initial outreach (10 guests/week)
- [ ] Track responses in spreadsheet (Name, Contact, Date Sent, Response, Status)
- [ ] Send follow-up at day 5 if no response
- [ ] Send final follow-up at day 12
- [ ] Schedule confirmed guests (use Calendly or similar)
- [ ] Send calendar invite with Zoom/recording link
- [ ] Send prep email 48 hours before recording

### Guest Prep Package
- [ ] Send guests: episode topic, sample questions, tech check instructions
- [ ] Request headshot and bio for show notes
- [ ] Ask about topics they want to avoid
- [ ] Confirm pronunciation of their name

## 📝 Phase 3: Script & Outline Development

### Episode Structure Template
```
COLD OPEN (0:00-0:30)
- Hook: compelling quote, surprising stat, or teaser
- No intro music yet – grab attention first

INTRO (0:30-1:30)
- Show intro music
- Host introduction
- Show premise (one sentence)
- What listener will learn today
- Guest intro (if applicable)

MAIN CONTENT (1:30-XX:XX)
- Segment 1: [Topic/Question]
- Segment 2: [Topic/Question]
- Segment 3: [Topic/Question]
- [Adjust segments based on format]

OUTRO (Last 2 minutes)
- Key takeaways (3 bullet points)
- Guest/social plugs (if applicable)
- Call-to-action (subscribe, review, visit website)
- Tease next episode
- Outro music
```

### Outline Template (Solo Episodes)
```markdown
# Episode #[Number]: [Title]

## Hook Options (Pick 1)
- [ ] Surprising statistic: _______
- [ ] Provocative question: _______
- [ ] Brief story/anecdote: _______
- [ ] Quote from expert: _______

## Main Points
1. [Point 1]
   - Supporting detail
   - Example/story
   - Transition to next point

2. [Point 2]
   - Supporting detail
   - Example/story
   - Transition to next point

3. [Point 3]
   - Supporting detail
   - Example/story

## Call-to-Action
- [ ] What do you want listeners to do?
- [ ] Where should they go?

## Show Notes Keywords
- [Primary keyword]
- [Secondary keywords]
```

```markdown

## Questions

### Warm-Up (2-3 questions)
1. [Easy, biographical question]
```

## ✅ Pre-Recording Checklist

### 48 Hours Before
- [ ] Tech check email sent to guest (if applicable)
- [ ] Outline/questions finalized
- [ ] Recording space reserved
- [ ] Equipment charged/tested

### 24 Hours Before
- [ ] Calendar reminder set
- [ ] Water/snacks prepared
- [ ] Phone on Do Not Disturb
- [ ] Recording software updated

### 1 Hour Before
- [ ] Room acoustics check (close windows, turn off fans/AC)
- [ ] Mic test recording (listen for background noise)
- [ ] Levels check (aim for -12dB to -6dB, never hitting 0)
- [ ] Backup recording method ready (phone voice memo, second device)
- [ ] Notes/outline printed or on second screen

### 5 Minutes Before
- [ ] Deep breath
- [ ] Smile (it comes through in your voice)
- [ ] Hit record
- [ ] Record 10 seconds of room tone (for noise reduction later)
