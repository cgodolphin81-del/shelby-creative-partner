# Distribution Guide

**Getting your podcast on Spotify, Apple, Google, and all major platforms**

---

## 🗺️ Distribution Overview

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    YOUR PODCAST FILE                        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                            │
                            ▼
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│              PODCAST HOSTING PLATFORM                       │
│         (Buzzsprout, Libsyn, Podbean, etc.)                │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
                            │
                            ▼
              RSS FEED GENERATED
                            │
        ┌───────────────────┼───────────────────┐
        ▼                   ▼                   ▼
┌───────────────┐  ┌───────────────┐  ┌───────────────┐
│ Apple         │  │ Spotify       │  │ Google        │
│ Podcasts      │  │               │  │ Podcasts      │
└───────────────┘  └───────────────┘  └───────────────┘
        │                   │                   │
        └───────────────────┴───────────────────┘
                            │
                            ▼
              15+ OTHER PLATFORMS
        (Amazon, iHeartRadio, Stitcher, etc.)
```

**Key Concept:** You upload ONCE to your hosting platform. They distribute everywhere via RSS feed.

## 📝 Step 2: Set Up Your Podcast

### Before You Start
Gather these assets:

- [ ] Podcast name (final, checked for trademarks)
- [ ] Cover art (3000x3000 pixels, JPG or PNG)
- [ ] Podcast description (short: 150 chars, long: 2000+ chars)
- [ ] Category and subcategory
- [ ] Language
- [ ] Country
- [ ] Episode 0 (trailer) ready to upload
- [ ] Website URL (even if just a landing page)
- [ ] Social media handles
- [ ] Contact email
- [ ] RSS feed (from your hosting platform)

### Cover Art Requirements

**Technical Specs:**
- Size: 3000 x 3000 pixels (square)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Color space: RGB
- File size: Under 500KB (Apple), under 1MB (most platforms)

**Design Best Practices:**
- ✅ Clear, readable at small sizes (thumbnail view)
- ✅ Show name is prominent
- ✅ Reflects your podcast's tone/topic
- ✅ Professional quality (hire designer if needed)
- ✅ No URLs or social handles (waste of space)
- ❌ Don't use Apple/Spotify logos
- ❌ Don't say "Top 10 Podcast" or make false claims
- ❌ Avoid tiny text

**Tools:**
- Canva (free templates)
- Adobe Express (free)
- Fiverr ($50-150 for professional design)
- 99designs (contest-based, $200+)

### Podcast Description

**Short Description (150 characters):**
Appears in search results and some apps.

```
Example: "Weekly interviews with startup founders who share their 
biggest failures and the lessons that made them successful."
```

**Long Description (2000+ characters):**
Full show description for your podcast page.

```
Structure:
1. Hook (1-2 sentences) – What's the show about?
2. Format – What can listeners expect?
3. Host bio – Why are you qualified?
4. Value prop – Why should someone listen?
5. Call-to-action – Subscribe, visit website, etc.

Example:
"Welcome to [Podcast Name], the show that [unique value proposition].

Every [day/week], host [Your Name] sits down with [guest type] to 
explore [topics]. Whether you're [target audience 1] or [target 
audience 2], you'll walk away with [specific benefit].

[Your Name] is [credentials/experience]. They've [achievements] and 
now dedicate their time to [mission].

New episodes drop every [day]. Subscribe now and never miss an episode!

Visit us at [website] for show notes, transcripts, and exclusive content.
Follow us on [social media] to join the conversation.

Got a topic suggestion or want to be a guest? Email us at [email].

© [Year] [Podcast Name]. All rights reserved."
```

## 🍎 Step 3: Submit to Apple Podcasts

**Apple Podcasts is the most important directory.** Many other platforms pull from Apple's directory.

### Submission Process

1. **Get an Apple ID** (if you don't have one)
2. **Go to:** podcastconnect.apple.com
3. **Sign in** with your Apple ID
4. **Add your show:**
   - Click "+" to add new show
   - Enter your RSS feed URL (from your hosting platform)
   - Apple will fetch your show information
5. **Verify ownership:**
   - Apple will email the address in your RSS feed
   - Click the verification link
6. **Review and submit:**
   - Check all information is correct
   - Submit for review
7. **Wait for approval:**
   - Typically 24-72 hours
   - You'll receive email when approved

### Apple Podcasts Best Practices

- **Verify your website:** Add meta tag to your website for faster verification
- **Episode numbers:** Use consistent numbering (001, 002, 003)
- **Seasons:** Use seasons for organized content (optional)
- **Explicit content:** Mark appropriately
- **Episode types:** Use Full, Trailer, or Bonus tags

### Common Rejection Reasons

- ❌ Cover art doesn't meet specs
- ❌ Copyrighted music in episodes
- ❌ Inappropriate content without explicit tag
- ❌ RSS feed has errors
- ❌ Show description has URLs (not allowed in some cases)
- ❌ Placeholder/empty episodes

## 🟢 Step 4: Submit to Spotify

Spotify is now the largest podcast platform. Essential for discovery.

### Submission Process

1. **Go to:** podcasters.spotify.com
2. **Sign up or log in** with Spotify account
3. **Add your podcast:**
   - Click "Add Your Podcast"
   - Enter RSS feed URL
   - Spotify will fetch your show info
4. **Verify email:**
   - Click verification link sent to your email
5. **Submit for review:**
   - Review all information
   - Submit
6. **Wait for approval:**
   - Usually within 24 hours
   - Email notification when live

### Spotify-Specific Features

- **Spotify Audience Network:** Monetization option
- **Video podcasts:** Upload video versions
- **Q&A:** Listener questions feature
- **Polls:** Engage your audience
- **Clips:** Share short clips on social media

## 🔄 Step 7: Ongoing Distribution Management

### When You Publish New Episodes

Your hosting platform automatically:
1. Updates your RSS feed
2. Notifies all connected platforms
3. Pushes the episode live

**Timeline:**
- Most platforms: Within 1-2 hours
- Some platforms: Up to 24 hours
- Apple: Can take 24-48 hours initially

### Best Practices for Publishing

- **Schedule in advance:** Upload episodes 1-2 weeks early
- **Consistent day/time:** Train your audience when to expect episodes
- **Time zones:** Consider your primary audience's time zone
- **Batch upload:** Record and schedule multiple episodes at once

### Monitoring Your Distribution

**Check regularly:**
- [ ] All platforms have latest episode
- [ ] Episode titles/descriptions display correctly
- [ ] Cover art shows properly everywhere
- [ ] Audio plays without errors
- [ ] Show notes/links work

**Tools:**
- Your hosting platform's dashboard
- Podchaser (shows where your show is listed)
- Listen Notes (search for your show)
- Manual checks on key platforms

## 📊 Analytics & Tracking

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | What It Means | Good Benchmark |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **Downloads per episode (first 30 days)** | True measure of audience size | 100-500 (new), 1000-5000 (growing), 10000+ (established) |
| **Download growth rate** | Are you growing? | 10-20% month-over-month is healthy |
| **Listener retention** | How much of each episode is heard | 60-70% average is good |
| **Platform breakdown** | Where your audience listens | Varies by audience |
| **Geographic data** | Where listeners are located | Helps with targeting |
| **Device types** | Mobile vs. desktop vs. smart speakers | 70%+ mobile is typical |

### Analytics by Platform

**Hosting Platform Analytics:**
- Most accurate (counts actual downloads)
- Real-time data
- IAB-certified if host is IAB member

**Apple Podcasts Analytics:**
- Requires claiming your show
- Shows plays, not downloads
- Demographic data
- Engagement metrics

**Spotify for Podcasters Analytics:**
- Detailed listener data
- Demographics
- Listening behavior
- Clip performance

**Third-Party Tools:**
- **Chartable:** Advanced analytics, attribution tracking
- **Podsights:** Podcast advertising attribution
- **Magellan AI:** Growth analytics

## 🚨 Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Episode Not Showing Up

**Checklist:**
1. Is episode published (not just scheduled)?
2. Has it been 24-48 hours?
3. Is your RSS feed valid? (Use castfeedvalidator.com)
4. Is there an error in the episode metadata?
5. Contact platform support if still missing

### Cover Art Not Displaying

**Solutions:**
1. Verify specs (3000x3000, RGB, under 500KB)
2. Re-upload to hosting platform
3. Wait 24-48 hours for propagation
4. Some platforms cache – may need to contact support

### RSS Feed Errors

**Common issues:**
- Invalid characters in title/description
- Missing required fields
- Malformed XML
- Server errors

**Fix:**
1. Use castfeedvalidator.com to identify errors
2. Contact your hosting platform support
3. Most hosts will fix RSS issues for you

### Downloads Not Counting

**Understanding download counting:**
- Most platforms count a download after 60+ seconds of playback
- Some count immediately on click
- IAB has standards for what counts as a download
- Your hosting platform's numbers are most accurate
