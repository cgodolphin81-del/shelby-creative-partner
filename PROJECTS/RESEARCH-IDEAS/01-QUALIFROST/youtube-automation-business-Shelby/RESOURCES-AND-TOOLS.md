## 💡 Bonus Tips & Shortcuts

### SPEED UP WORKFLOW:
1. **Batch everything:** Script all videos in one day, record all VO in another
2. **Create templates:** Script templates, thumbnail templates, editing templates
3. **Use shortcuts:** Keyboard shortcuts in editing software
4. **Automate:** Use Zapier to connect tools
5. **Delegate:** Outsource low-value tasks early

### SAVE MONEY:
1. **Start free:** Use free tiers, upgrade when needed
2. **Annual billing:** Save 15-20% on most tools
3. **Free alternatives:** DaVinci Resolve vs Premiere, Pexels vs Storyblocks
4. **Bundle tools:** Some suites offer discounts

### SCALE FASTER:
1. **Hire before you need to:** Have talent ready when you're ready to scale
2. **Document everything:** SOPs allow delegation
3. **Build email list from day 1:** Your safety net
4. **Diversify revenue:** Don't rely on AdSense
5. **Test early:** A/B test thumbnails, titles, CTAs
