### Profile Image Guidelines

**DO:**
✓ Use logo mark or simplified logo
✓ Ensure it's readable at small sizes
✓ Keep important elements centered (circular crop)
✓ Use consistent image across platforms
✓ Update when logo changes

**DON'T:**
✗ Use full logo with tagline (too small)
✗ Include text (won't be readable)
✗ Use busy backgrounds
✗ Let important elements get cropped

**Body:**
- Provide value
- Tell a story
- Share insights
- Keep it scannable

**CTA:**

**Hashtags:**

- Mix of volumes

## 7. Engagement Guidelines

### Response Time

**Goal:** Respond within 2 hours during business hours

**Priority Order:**
1. Customer service issues
2. Direct questions
3. Comments on posts
4. General engagement

---

### Response Tone

**Match Platform:**
- Instagram: Friendly, casual, emoji-friendly
- LinkedIn: Professional, helpful, thoughtful
- Twitter: Conversational, quick, witty
- Facebook: Warm, community-focused
- TikTok: Fun, authentic, trendy

---

### Handling Negative Comments

**DO:**
✓ Respond quickly
✓ Acknowledge the issue
✓ Take it offline when needed
✓ Show you're solving it
✓ Stay professional

**DON'T:**
✗ Delete (unless spam/abuse)
✗ Get defensive
✗ Argue publicly
✗ Ignore legitimate complaints

**Template Response:**
> "Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. Please DM us with more details so we can make this right. 🙏"

## 11. Crisis Management

### Social Media Crisis Plan

**Level 1: Minor Issue**
- Single negative comment
- Response: Address individually

**Level 2: Growing Concern**
- Multiple similar complaints
- Response: Public statement + individual responses

**Level 3: Full Crisis**
- Widespread negative attention
- Response: Formal statement, pause scheduled posts, all-hands response

---

### Crisis Response Checklist

- [ ] Acknowledge the issue quickly
- [ ] Pause scheduled posts
- [ ] Prepare holding statement
- [ ] Designate spokesperson
- [ ] Monitor mentions constantly
- [ ] Update stakeholders
- [ ] Document everything
- [ ] Follow up with resolution
