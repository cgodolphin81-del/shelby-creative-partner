# 30-Day Branding Calendar

## Overview

This calendar provides a structured 30-day plan for building or refreshing your brand identity. Follow this timeline to stay on track and ensure all brand elements are developed systematically.

---

## Week 1: Discovery & Strategy

**Morning:**

- [ ] Review client onboarding form
- [ ] Set up project management board

## Risk Management

### Potential Delays

**Client Feedback Delays:**
- Mitigation: Set clear deadlines, send reminders
- Buffer: Built into timeline

**Revision Scope Creep:**
- Mitigation: Clear revision policy, track rounds
- Buffer: Additional rounds available (paid)

**File Issues:**
- Mitigation: Quality check before delivery
- Buffer: Day 25 internal review

**Stakeholder Changes:**
- Mitigation: Confirm decision-makers upfront
- Buffer: Extra presentation if needed
