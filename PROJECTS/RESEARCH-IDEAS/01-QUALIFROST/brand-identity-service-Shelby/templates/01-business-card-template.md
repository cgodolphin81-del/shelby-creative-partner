# Business Card Templates

## Standard Business Card

### Specifications

**Size:** 3.5" x 2" (88.9mm x 50.8mm)

**Bleed:** 0.125" (3mm) on all sides

**Safe Zone:** 0.125" (3mm) from edges

**Finish Options:**
- Matte
- Gloss
- Soft-touch
- Spot UV
- Foil stamping

**Paper Weight:**
- Standard: 14pt
- Premium: 16pt
- Luxury: 18pt+

## Special Finishes

### Spot UV
**What:** Glossy coating on specific areas
**Best For:** Logo, name, key elements
**Cost:** +$50-100 per 100 cards

### Foil Stamping
**What:** Metallic or colored foil
**Colors:** Gold, Silver, Rose Gold, Brand Color
**Best For:** Logo, borders, accents
**Cost:** +$100-200 per 100 cards

### Embossing/Debossing
**What:** Raised or recessed design
**Best For:** Logo, monogram
**Cost:** +$75-150 per 100 cards

### Soft-Touch Coating
**What:** Velvety matte finish
**Best For:** Premium feel
**Cost:** +$25-50 per 100 cards

### Colored Edges
**What:** Dyed paper edges
**Best For:** Thick cards (16pt+)
**Cost:** +$50-100 per 100 cards

## Proof Checklist

Before ordering, verify:

- [ ] Spelling (name, title, email, website)
- [ ] Phone number format
- [ ] Email address accuracy
- [ ] Website URL (test it!)
- [ ] Logo is high resolution
- [ ] Colors match brand
- [ ] Bleed is included
- [ ] Safe zone respected
- [ ] Fonts are outlined/embedded
- [ ] QR code scans correctly
- [ ] Proof approved by stakeholder

---

## Distribution Tips

**Always Carry:**
- Keep 50+ cards with you
- Use a card holder (prevents damage)
- Store in cool, dry place

**When to Give:**
- Networking events
- Meetings (end, not beginning)
- Conferences
- Casual introductions
- When asked

**Follow-Up:**
- Connect on LinkedIn within 24 hours
- Reference where you met
- Add value in follow-up message
