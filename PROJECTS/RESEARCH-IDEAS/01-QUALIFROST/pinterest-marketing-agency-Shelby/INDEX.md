## 💡 Quick Reference Guides

### Pinterest SEO Quick Reference
- **Pin Title:** Include primary keyword, under 60 characters
- **Pin Description:** 100-500 characters with 2-3 keyword variations
- **Hashtags:** 3-8 relevant hashtags at end
- **Board Titles:** Clear, keyword-rich, 2-5 words
- **Pin Ratio:** 2:3 (1000x1500px minimum)

### Pinning Schedule Quick Reference
- **New Accounts:** 5-10 pins/day
- **Growing Accounts:** 10-20 pins/day
- **Established Accounts:** 15-30 pins/day
- **Best Times:** 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 8-11 PM (audience time zone)

### Pricing Quick Reference
- **Starter Package:** $1,500-2,500/month
- **Growth Package:** $3,000-5,000/month
- **Scale Package:** $7,000-10,000/month
- **Enterprise Package:** $10,000-20,000+/month

### Client Acquisition Quick Reference
- **Daily Outreach:** 50-75 touchpoints
- **Conversion Rate:** 1-3% of outreach
- **Discovery Calls:** Close 20-30%
- **Time to Close:** 2-6 weeks average
