## 4. GAP IDENTIFICATION FRAMEWORK

### Gap Categories

#### Category 1: Missing Flows
**Definition:** Essential flows not yet implemented

**Identification:**
- Review Flow Inventory Checklist
- Compare against industry standards
- Identify "❌ Missing" items

**Priority:** HIGH
**Action:** Build missing flows

---

#### Category 2: Underperforming Flows
**Definition:** Flows below benchmark performance

**Identification:**
- Review Flow Performance Analysis
- Flag flows with "Average" or "Poor" assessment
- Identify metrics 20%+ below benchmark

**Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH
**Action:** Optimize content, timing, segmentation

---

#### Category 3: Technical Issues
**Definition:** Flows with broken links, triggers, or tracking

**Identification:**
- Test each flow end-to-end
- Check trigger conditions
- Verify link functionality
- Review analytics tracking

**Priority:** CRITICAL
**Action:** Fix immediately

---

#### Category 4: Content Gaps
**Definition:** Flows with weak copy, design, or offers

**Identification:**
- Review content quality
- Check personalization level
- Assess mobile optimization
- Evaluate offer strength

**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Action:** Refresh content

---

#### Category 5: Segmentation Gaps
**Definition:** Flows not properly segmented

**Identification:**
- Check if flows send to all subscribers equally
- Identify lack of VIP treatment
- Look for missing behavioral triggers

**Priority:** MEDIUM-HIGH
**Action:** Implement segmentation

---

#### Category 6: Channel Gaps
**Definition:** Missing SMS integration or multi-channel approach

**Identification:**
- Review SMS flow implementation
- Check email + SMS coordination
- Identify single-channel-only flows

**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Action:** Add SMS where appropriate

## 10. PRIORITIZATION MATRIX

### Priority Framework

**Priority 1: Critical (Do Immediately)**
- Technical issues breaking flows
- Compliance violations
- Tracking/data loss
- Revenue impact >$10K/month

**Priority 2: High (Do This Month)**
- Missing essential flows
- Underperforming flows (50%+ below benchmark)
- Segmentation gaps
- Revenue impact $5-10K/month

**Priority 3: Medium (Do This Quarter)**
- Content optimization
- Design refresh
- Additional segmentation
- Revenue impact $1-5K/month

**Priority 4: Low (Do When Possible)**
- Nice-to-have flows
- Minor optimizations
- Revenue impact <$1K/month
