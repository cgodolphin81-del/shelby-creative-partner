## Best Practices

### General Guidelines

1. **Start with flows** - Automation drives 40-60% of email revenue
2. **Segment everything** - Relevance drives engagement
3. **Test constantly** - Always be optimizing
4. **Track attribution** - Know what's working
5. **Maintain deliverability** - Protect sender reputation

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Sending too frequently without value
2. ❌ Ignoring mobile optimization
3. ❌ Not testing before sending
4. ❌ Skipping authentication setup
5. ❌ Buying email lists
6. ❌ Ignoring unsubscribe requests
7. ❌ Not segmenting audiences
8. ❌ Focusing only on promotions
