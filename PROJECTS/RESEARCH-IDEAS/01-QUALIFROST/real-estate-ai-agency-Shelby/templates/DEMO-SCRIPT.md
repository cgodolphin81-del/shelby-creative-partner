# 🎬 Product Demo Script

**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Audience:** Broker/Owner, Operations Manager, Key Agents  
**Goal:** Demonstrate value, address objections, advance to proposal

---

## Pre-Demo Checklist

□ Research client (website, recent listings, news)  
□ Review discovery call notes  
□ Prepare customized demo environment  
□ Test all integrations and features  
□ Have case studies ready (similar client type)  
□ Send calendar invite with demo link  
□ Send reminder email 24 hours before  

---

### 3. Lead Management Demo (15 minutes)

#### 3.1 Lead Capture & Scoring

**Show:** Lead inbox with scored leads

**Script:**
```
"Here's where the magic happens. Every lead that comes in - whether 
from Zillow, your website, Facebook, or anywhere else - gets instantly 
scored from 0-100.

The AI analyzes 50+ signals:
• Behavior on your website (which pages, how long)
• Response time to messages
• Financial indicators (if available)
• Timeline urgency
• Property preferences
• And many more

Let me show you a real example...

[Click on a high-score lead]

This lead has a score of 92. Why? They viewed 8 listings, spent 12 
minutes on your mortgage calculator page, responded to your text 
within 3 minutes, and their browsing pattern matches recent buyers.

Compare that to this lead with a score of 34...

[Click on low-score lead]

They filled out a form but haven't engaged since. Not a bad lead - 
just not ready yet. The system will nurture them automatically.

The result? Your agents focus on the 92s first. No more calling 
through tire-kickers while hot leads go cold."
```

#### 3.2 Automated Nurture

**Show:** Nurture sequence builder

**Script:**
```
"Now, not every lead is ready to talk today. That's where automated 
nurture comes in.

Let me show you a typical sequence...

[Walk through email + SMS sequence]

Day 1: Welcome email + intro text
Day 3: Market update for their area
Day 7: New listings matching their criteria
Day 14: 'Still looking?' check-in
Day 30: Monthly newsletter

But here's the key - it's smart. If the lead engages at any point - 
clicks a link, replies to a text, visits your site - the system 
immediately notifies an agent to jump in.

So you get the best of both worlds: automation for efficiency, 
human touch when it matters.

One of our clients in Austin told us this alone increased their 
lead-to-appointment rate from 12% to 31%. That's 2.5x more 
appointments from the same leads."
```

**Handle Objections:**

**"It's too expensive."**
```
"I understand budget is a consideration. Can I ask - compared to 
what? Because at ${ROI}%, this isn't a cost - it's an investment 
that pays for itself in {X} months.

What if we started with a 90-day pilot? Just lead management and 
valuations. If you don't see results, we part ways. Fair enough?"
```

**"We need to think about it."**
```
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? Is it 
about fit, timing, budget, or something else?

[Listen carefully]

I ask because I want to make sure I address your concerns. If it's 
about {their concern}, here's what I'd suggest..."
```

**"Our agents won't use it."**
```
"That's a valid concern. Change management is real. Here's how we 
handle it:

1. We identify champions on your team - the tech-forward agents
2. We train them first, get them excited
3. They become advocates
4. We show quick wins - 'Agent X closed a deal from an AI-scored 
   lead'
5. Adoption follows

We've onboarded 200+ brokerages. The ones who succeed do three 
things: executive sponsorship, proper training, and celebrate 
early wins. We'll help with all three."
```

## Demo Best Practices

### Do's:
✅ Customize demo to their pain points  
✅ Use their market data when possible  
✅ Show real examples, not just features  
✅ Quantify value throughout  
✅ Handle objections with empathy  
✅ Always have a clear next step  

### Don'ts:
❌ Don't show every feature (focus on what matters)  
❌ Don't talk more than you listen  
❌ Don't oversell or overpromise  
❌ Don't skip the ROI discussion  
❌ Don't end without clear next steps  
❌ Don't forget to follow up
