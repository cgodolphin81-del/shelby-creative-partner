# TikTok Agency Revenue Model

## Building a Profitable, Scalable TikTok Agency

From solo operator to 7-figure agency. Complete financial modeling and projections.

---

## 💰 REVENUE STREAMS

### Primary Revenue: Client Retainers

**Tier 1: Starter ($2,000-4,000/month)**
- Target: Small businesses, solopreneurs
- Services: 15-20 videos, basic strategy, community management
- Margin: 50-60%
- Time investment: 5-8 hours/week per client

**Tier 2: Growth ($4,000-7,000/month)**
- Target: Established businesses, funded startups
- Services: 20-30 videos, advanced strategy, bi-weekly calls
- Margin: 55-65%
- Time investment: 8-12 hours/week per client

**Tier 3: Enterprise ($7,000-10,000+/month)**
- Target: Brands, larger companies
- Services: 30+ videos, full-service, dedicated team, ads
- Margin: 60-70%
- Time investment: 12-20 hours/week per client

---

### Secondary Revenue: Project-Based

| Service | Price | Margin | Time |
|---------|-------|--------|------|
| Account Audit | $500 | 90% | 2 hours |
| Strategy Deck | $1,500 | 85% | 4 hours |
| Video Editing (per video) | $75-150 | 70% | 1-2 hours |
| Hook Writing (10 hooks) | $300 | 95% | 1 hour |
| Ads Setup | $1,000 | 80% | 3 hours |
| Training Workshop (2 hrs) | $1,200 | 90% | 2 hours prep + 2 hours delivery |

---

### Tertiary Revenue: Performance-Based

**Ad Spend Management:**
- 10-20% of ad spend
- Example: $10K ad spend = $1,000-2,000/month
- Margin: 80%+ (mostly passive)

**Revenue Share Deals:**
- 5-10% of attributed revenue
- Higher risk, higher reward
- Best for: E-commerce, direct response

**Affiliate/Commission:**
- 10-30% on products sold
- For: Courses, coaching, physical products
- Margin: 90%+ (pure profit)

## 💵 PRICING STRATEGY

### Value-Based Pricing Framework

**Don't price based on hours. Price based on value.**

**Question to Ask:**
- What is this worth to the client?
- What's the ROI they can expect?
- What would this cost them to do in-house?

**Example Calculation:**

Client: E-commerce brand doing $50K/month
- TikTok could add 20% revenue = $10K/month
- Your fee: $5K/month
- Client ROI: 2:1 (they make $2 for every $1 spent)
- This is a NO-BRAINER for them

**Pricing Psychology:**

**1. Anchor High**
- Present Enterprise package first ($10K)
- Makes Growth package ($5K) seem reasonable
- Starter package ($2.5K) seems like a bargain

**2. Show ROI, Not Cost**
- "Investment: $5,000/month"
- "Expected return: $15,000-30,000/month"
- Frame as investment, not expense

**3. Payment Terms**
- Monthly: Standard
- Quarterly: 5% discount
- Annual: 15% discount (improves cash flow)

**4. Price Increases**
- Raise prices every 6-12 months
- Grandfather existing clients (or give 60-day notice)
- New clients always get current pricing

## 💰 CASH FLOW MANAGEMENT

### Payment Terms

**Standard Terms:**
- Monthly retainers: Due 1st of month
- Project work: 50% upfront, 50% on delivery
- Net-15 or Net-30 for enterprise clients

**Late Payment Policy:**
- 5% fee after 7 days
- 10% fee after 14 days
- Work pauses after 30 days
- Contract termination after 60 days

**Auto-Charge:**
- Use Stripe, HoneyBook, or similar
- Auto-charge on 1st of month
- Reduces late payments significantly

---

### Cash Reserves

**Target:** 3-6 months of operating expenses

**Why:**
- Client churn happens
- Economic downturns
- Unexpected expenses
- Peace of mind

**Example:**
- Monthly expenses: $40,000
- Target reserve: $120,000-240,000
- Build this in Year 2

---

### Managing Cash Flow

**Tips:**
1. **Get paid upfront** - Monthly retainers due on 1st
2. **Offer annual discounts** - 15% off for annual prepay
3. **Invoice immediately** - Don't wait to bill
4. **Follow up on late payments** - Day 1, 7, 14, 30
5. **Keep expenses variable** - Contractors vs. employees early on
6. **Reinvest profits** - Build reserves before taking big distributions

## 🎯 CLIENT LIFETIME VALUE (LTV)

### Calculating LTV

**Formula:**
```
LTV = Average Monthly Retainer × Average Client Lifespan (months)
```

**Example:**
- Average retainer: $4,000/month
- Average lifespan: 12 months
- LTV: $48,000

### Improving LTV

**1. Deliver Results**
- Clients who see ROI stay longer
- Focus on their success, not just your deliverables

**2. Build Relationships**
- Regular check-ins
- Be proactive, not reactive
- Become a trusted advisor

**3. Upsell Services**
- Start with content, add ads
- Add strategy, then management
- Expand scope as trust grows

**4. Create Switching Costs**
- Deep integration with their business
- Proprietary systems
- Strong relationships with their team

**5. Consistent Communication**
- Weekly updates
- Monthly strategy calls
- Quarterly business reviews

### Target LTV Metrics

**Good:** 9-12 months average lifespan
**Great:** 12-18 months average lifespan
**Excellent:** 18-24+ months average lifespan

**Churn Rate:**
- Acceptable: 5-8% monthly
- Good: 3-5% monthly
- Excellent: <3% monthly

## 📊 UNIT ECONOMICS

### Economics Per Client

**Starter Client ($3,000/month):**
- Revenue: $3,000
- Editor time: 8 hours × $30/hour = $240
- Strategist time: 2 hours × $50/hour = $100
- Account manager: 1 hour × $40/hour = $40
- Tools/allocation: $50
- **Total COGS: $430**
- **Gross Profit: $2,570 (86% margin)**

Wait, this seems too high. Let me recalculate more realistically:

**Starter Client ($3,000/month) - Realistic:**
- Revenue: $3,000
- Editor (15 videos @ $75): $1,125
- Strategist (5 hours @ $50): $250
- Account Manager (3 hours @ $40): $120
- Tools/allocation: $50
- **Total COGS: $1,545**
- **Gross Profit: $1,455 (48% margin)**

**Growth Client ($5,000/month):**
- Revenue: $5,000
- Editor (25 videos @ $75): $1,875
- Strategist (8 hours @ $50): $400
- Account Manager (4 hours @ $40): $160
- Tools/allocation: $75
- **Total COGS: $2,510**
- **Gross Profit: $2,490 (50% margin)**

**Enterprise Client ($8,000/month):**
- Revenue: $8,000
- Editor (35 videos @ $75): $2,625
- Strategist (12 hours @ $50): $600
- Account Manager (6 hours @ $40): $240
- Ads specialist (5 hours @ $60): $300
- Tools/allocation: $100
- **Total COGS: $3,865**
- **Gross Profit: $4,135 (52% margin)**

---

### Break-Even Analysis

**Fixed Costs (Monthly):**
- Your salary: $8,000
- Software/tools: $500
- Rent/utilities: $0 (home office) or $2,000
- Insurance: $300
- Professional services: $500
- **Total Fixed: $9,300** (without office) or $11,300 (with office)

**Variable Costs:** ~50% of revenue

**Break-Even Formula:**
```
Break-Even Revenue = Fixed Costs ÷ (1 - Variable Cost %)
Break-Even Revenue = $9,300 ÷ (1 - 0.50) = $18,600/month
```

**You need ~$19K/month to break even.**

At $4K average retainer: **5 clients to break even**

## 🚀 SCALING MODELS

### Model 1: Boutique Agency (Lifestyle)

**Goal:** $30K-50K/month, high margins, small team

**Structure:**
- You (owner-operator)
- 2-3 contractors (editors)
- 1 VA (part-time)
- 8-12 clients

**Pros:**
- High profit margins (50%+)
- Flexible lifestyle
- Low overhead
- Less stress

**Cons:**
- Capped revenue
- You're still involved in delivery
- Hard to sell/exit

---

### Model 2: Scaled Agency (Growth)

**Goal:** $100K-300K/month, systematic growth

**Structure:**
- Full leadership team
- Departments (creative, strategy, AM, ads)
- 30-50 clients
- 20-40 team members

**Pros:**
- Significant revenue
- Can step back from delivery
- Valuable, sellable asset
- Impact at scale

**Cons:**
- More complex
- Lower margins (30-40%)
- More stress/responsibility
- Harder to manage

---

### Model 3: Hybrid (Best of Both)

**Goal:** $50K-100K/month, balanced approach

**Structure:**
- You focus on strategy/sales
- Small core team (5-10)
- Contractors for flexibility
- 15-25 clients

**Pros:**
- Good revenue
- Maintain quality
- Some lifestyle flexibility
- Option to scale further

**Cons:**
- Still requires significant involvement
- Can get stuck in middle

## 💼 EXIT STRATEGIES

### Option 1: Continue Operating

- Keep collecting profits
- Pay yourself salary + distributions
- No exit needed if lifestyle is good

### Option 2: Sell the Agency

**Valuation Multiples:**
- Small agencies (<$50K/month): 2-3x annual profit
- Medium agencies ($50K-200K/month): 3-4x annual profit
- Large agencies ($200K+/month): 4-6x annual profit

**Example:**
- Annual profit: $500,000
- Multiple: 3.5x
- **Sale price: $1.75M**

**What Buyers Want:**
- Recurring revenue (retainers)
- Low churn (<5% monthly)
- Strong team (not dependent on founder)
- Systems and processes
- Growth trajectory

### Option 3: Merge/Partner

- Merge with complementary agency
- Bring on equity partner
- Roll up smaller agencies

### Option 4: Productize

- Turn services into products
- Create courses, templates, software
- Scale beyond time-for-money
- Potential acquisition by larger company

## 🎯 KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Start with 3-5 clients** at $2K-4K each = $6K-20K/month
2. **Raise prices** every 6 months as you get results
3. **Fire bad clients** to make room for good ones
4. **Build systems** before you need them
5. **Track everything** - you can't improve what you don't measure
6. **Reinvest profits** in Year 1-2, take distributions in Year 3+
7. **Focus on retention** - LTV is everything
8. **Build cash reserves** - 3-6 months minimum
9. **Know your numbers** - margins, break-even, LTV
10. **Have an exit strategy** - even if you don't use it
