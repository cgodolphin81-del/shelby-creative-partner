## 📊 KEY METRICS TO TRACK

### Awareness Metrics (Top of Funnel)

**1. Views**
- What it is: Total number of times video was watched
- Why it matters: Indicates reach and algorithm distribution
- Good benchmark: 500+ views for new accounts, 10K+ for established
- Track: Per video, weekly, monthly

**2. Impressions**
- What it is: Times video thumbnail was shown
- Why it matters: Shows potential reach before views
- Good benchmark: 2-5x your follower count
- Track: Per video, overall account

**3. Reach**
- What it is: Unique accounts that saw your video
- Why it matters: True audience size (vs. repeat views)
- Good benchmark: 60-80% of impressions
- Track: Per video

---

### Engagement Metrics (Middle of Funnel)

**4. Watch Time**
- What it is: Total time spent watching
- Why it matters: Algorithm's #1 ranking factor
- Good benchmark: 50%+ of video length
- Track: Per video, average across account

**5. Average View Duration**
- What it is: Average seconds watched per view
- Why it matters: Indicates content quality and retention
- Good benchmark: 15+ seconds for 30-second videos
- Track: Per video

**6. Completion Rate**
- What it is: % of viewers who watch to the end
- Why it matters: Shows content holds attention
- Good benchmark: 40%+ for 60-second videos
- Track: Per video

**7. Re-watches**
- What it is: Times video was watched more than once
- Why it matters: Indicates highly engaging content
- Good benchmark: 5-10% of total views
- Track: Per video

**8. Likes**
- What it is: Total likes on video
- Why it matters: Basic engagement signal
- Good benchmark: 3-5% of views
- Track: Per video, weekly, monthly

**9. Comments**
- What it is: Total comments on video
- Why it matters: High-value engagement, algorithm boost
- Good benchmark: 0.5-2% of views
- Track: Per video, response rate

**10. Shares**
- What it is: Times video was shared
- Why it matters: Expands reach beyond your audience
- Good benchmark: 1-3% of views
- Track: Per video

**11. Saves**
- What it is: Times video was bookmarked
- Why it matters: Indicates high-value content
- Good benchmark: 2-5% of views
- Track: Per video

---

### Conversion Metrics (Bottom of Funnel)

**12. Profile Visits**
- What it is: Times viewers visited your profile
- Why it matters: Indicates interest in more content
- Good benchmark: 1-3% of views
- Track: Per video, overall

**13. Follows**
- What it is: New followers from video
- Why it matters: Long-term audience building
- Good benchmark: 0.5-2% of views
- Track: Per video, daily, weekly

**14. Link Clicks**
- What it is: Clicks on bio link
- Why it matters: Drives traffic off-platform
- Good benchmark: 0.5-2% of profile visits
- Track: Daily, per campaign

**15. Website Conversions**
- What it is: Actions taken on your website
- Why it matters: Actual business results
- Good benchmark: Varies by offer
- Track: Using UTM parameters

## 🧪 A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

### What to Test

**Test One Variable at a Time:**

**Week 1: Hook Testing**
- Video A: Hook type 1
- Video B: Hook type 2
- Same content, different hooks
- Measure: 3-second retention

**Week 2: Length Testing**
- Video A: 30 seconds
- Video B: 60 seconds
- Same content, different lengths
- Measure: Completion rate

**Week 3: CTA Testing**
- Video A: Comment CTA
- Video B: Follow CTA
- Same content, different CTAs
- Measure: Engagement type

**Week 4: Posting Time Testing**
- Video A: Morning post
- Video B: Evening post
- Same content, different times
- Measure: Initial velocity (first 2 hours)

## 📉 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Views Suddenly Drop

**Possible Causes:**
- Algorithm shift (platform-wide)
- Account shadowban (check analytics access)
- Content quality decline
- Posting inconsistency
- Niche saturation

**Solutions:**
- Audit last 10 videos for patterns
- Check if competitors also dropped
- Review analytics for shadowban signs
- Return to basics (stronger hooks)
- Post more consistently for 2 weeks

---

### Engagement Rate Declining

**Possible Causes:**
- Wrong audience (bought followers?)
- Content not resonating
- Posting at wrong times
- Not engaging with comments
- Algorithm deprioritizing your content type

**Solutions:**
- Review audience demographics
- Survey your audience (ask what they want)
- Test different posting times
- Spend 30 min/day engaging with comments
- Try different content formats

---

### Followers Not Growing

**Possible Causes:**
- No clear value proposition
- Inconsistent posting
- Weak CTAs
- Content not shareable
- Wrong niche

**Solutions:**
- Clarify your bio and niche
- Commit to posting schedule
- Add clear follow CTAs
- Create more shareable content
- Consider niche pivot if needed

---

### Videos Stuck at 200-500 Views

**Possible Causes:**
- Hook isn't working (low 3-second retention)
- Content quality issues
- Account is new (building trust)
- Niche is very competitive

**Solutions:**
- Focus entirely on hook improvement
- Study viral videos in your niche
- Be patient (takes 20-50 videos to gain traction)
- Find less competitive angle

**The "200-View Jail" Reality:**
- This is normal for new accounts
- TikTok is testing your content
- Keep posting consistently
- One viral video breaks you out
- Typically takes 30-90 days
