## 🔄 DUETS STRATEGY

### What Are Duets?
Duets let you create a video alongside another video. Your video appears next to the original.

### Why Duets Work
- Tap into existing viral video's audience
- Algorithm pushes duets of trending content
- Low effort, high reward
- Great for commentary and reactions

### Duet Strategies

**1. The Expert Commentary**
- Find viral video in your niche
- Duet with your expert take
- Add value the original missed
- Example: Fitness coach dueting workout videos with form corrections

**2. The Reaction**
- React to shocking/surprising content
- Genuine reactions perform well
- Add your personality
- Example: "Reacting to crazy transformation stories"

**3. The Addition**
- "This is great, but here's what they missed..."
- Build on the original content
- Position as helpful, not critical
- Example: "5 more tips to add to this"

**4. The Challenge**
- Duet with a challenge video
- Show your attempt
- Add your twist
- Example: Fitness challenges, cooking challenges

**5. The Collaboration (Remote)**
- Duet with creators in your niche
- Creates pseudo-collaboration
- Cross-pollinates audiences
- Example: "Adding my verse to this story"

### Finding Videos to Duet

**Method 1: Trending Page**
- Scroll For You Page
- Look for videos with 100K+ views
- Check if duets are enabled
- Prioritize videos from last 48 hours

**Method 2: Niche Search**
- Search your niche keywords
- Filter by "Most Liked" this week
- Find high-performing content
- Duet with your angle

**Method 3: Competitor Analysis**
- Check top 10 accounts in niche
- See their top videos
- Duet their best performers
- Add your unique perspective

**Method 4: Trending Sounds**
- Click on trending sound
- Find top videos using it
- Duet the best ones
- Ride the sound's momentum

### Duet Best Practices
- ✅ Add genuine value (don't just exist next to video)
- ✅ Use good lighting and audio
- ✅ Hook in first 2 seconds (even though it's a duet)
- ✅ Credit original creator in caption
- ✅ Post within 48 hours of original going viral
- ❌ Don't be mean or overly critical
- ❌ Don't duet without adding value
- ❌ Don't duet your own videos (looks desperate)

### Duet Posting Schedule
- 2-3 duets per week
- Mix with original content
- Best posted during peak hours
- Monitor which duets perform best

## ✂️ STITCHES STRATEGY

### What Are Stitches?
Stitches let you clip and integrate scenes from another video into your own. You can use up to 5 seconds of their video.

### Why Stitches Work
- Use viral moments as setup
- Create "part 2" content
- Respond to questions/takes
- Algorithm favors stitches of trending content

### Stitch Strategies

**1. The Answer**
- Find video ending with question
- Stitch the question
- Provide your answer
- Example: "What's the best [thing]?" → Your answer

**2. The Correction**
- Find video with misinformation
- Stitch the incorrect part
- Politely correct with evidence
- Example: "Actually, here's what the research says..."

**3. The Continuation**
- "Part 2 because this is important..."
- Stitch key moment from Part 1
- Continue the story/lesson
- Example: "Here's what happened next..."

**4. The Comparison**
- Stitch someone's take
- Show your alternative
- "They did X, I'm doing Y"
- Example: Different approaches to same problem

**5. The Evidence**
- Stitch a claim
- Show proof it works (or doesn't)
- Data-driven response
- Example: "They said this works. I tested it."

### Finding Videos to Stitch

**Best Candidates:**
- Videos ending with unanswered questions
- Controversial takes in your niche
- Incomplete stories ("Part 1" with no Part 2)
- Claims you can verify/debunk
- Trending topics you have expertise on

**Where to Look:**
- Comments on viral videos (people asking questions)
- Niche hashtag feeds
- Competitor videos with high engagement
- Trending sounds with question formats

### Stitch Best Practices
- ✅ Use the full 5 seconds strategically
- ✅ Make your addition longer than the stitch
- ✅ Add clear value beyond the original
- ✅ Be respectful (even when disagreeing)
- ✅ Tag original creator
- ❌ Don't be mean-spirited
- ❌ Don't steal content without adding value
- ❌ Don't stitch without context

## 🤝 COLLABORATION STRATEGY

### Why Collaborations Work
- Access to another creator's audience
- Algorithm boost for collab content
- Fresh content for both audiences
- Relationship building in niche

### Types of Collaborations

**1. The Duet Chain**
- Creator A posts
- Creator B duets
- Creator C duets both
- Creates viral chain

**2. The Remote Collab**
- Both create content separately
- Post same day, tag each other
- "Part 1" and "Part 2" format
- Example: "I asked [creator] about..."

**3. The Takeover**
- You post on their account (or vice versa)
- Cross-pollinates audiences
- Requires trust and planning
- Best for similar-sized accounts

**4. The Live Collab**
- Go live together
- Q&A, interview, challenge
- Real-time engagement
- Both audiences notified

**5. The Series Collab**
- Multi-part series together
- Builds anticipation
- Both post episodes
- Example: "7-day challenge with [creator]"

### Finding Collaboration Partners

**Ideal Partner Profile:**
- Similar niche (or adjacent)
- Similar or slightly larger following
- Similar engagement rate
- Compatible audience
- Professional and reliable

**Where to Find Them:**
- TikTok creator Discord servers
- Instagram creator communities
- Twitter/X creator circles
- Niche-specific Facebook groups
- Comment sections of top creators
- Creator marketplace platforms

### Outreach Template

```
Subject: Collab Idea - [Your Handle] x [Their Handle]

Hey [Name]!

I'm [Your Name], I create content about [niche] on TikTok 
([your handle], [follower count]).

[specific thing about their content]. Your video on 
[specific video] was incredible.

I have a collab idea I think both our audiences would love:
[1-2 sentence pitch]

Would you be open to hopping on a quick call to discuss?
No pressure at all if you're swamped!

Either way, keep killing it.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Handle]
[Link to your best video]
```

### Collaboration Content Ideas

**For Similar Niches:**
- Debate/discussion on hot topic
- Challenge together (same challenge, different approaches)
- "Day in the life" swap
- React to each other's content
- Share tips/tricks with each other's audience

**For Adjacent Niches:**
- How your niches overlap
- "I tried [their niche] for a day"
- Cross-education (teach each other's audiences)
- Combined expertise on broader topic

### Collab Posting Strategy
- Coordinate posting times (same day, within hours)
- Tag each other in captions and on-screen
- Engage with each other's comments
- Share to stories/other platforms
- Plan 2-3 collabs per month minimum

## #️⃣ HASHTAG STRATEGY

### How Hashtags Work on TikTok
- Categorize your content for the algorithm
- Help TikTok understand who to show your video to
- Can drive discovery from hashtag pages
- Less important than content quality, but still matter

### The Hashtag Formula

**Use 3-5 hashtags per video:**

1. **1 Broad Niche Hashtag** (1M+ posts)
   - #fitness, #business, #cooking
   
2. **2 Medium Niche Hashtags** (100K-1M posts)
   - #homeworkouts, #smallbusinesstips, #easyrecipes
   
3. **1-2 Specific Hashtags** (10K-100K posts)
   - #10minuteworkout, #solopreneur, #30minutemeals
   
4. **Optional: 1 Branded Hashtag**
   - Your personal or campaign hashtag

### Hashtag Research Process

**Step 1: Competitor Analysis**
- Check top 10 creators in your niche
- Note hashtags on their top videos
- Identify patterns

**Step 2: TikTok Search**
- Search your niche keywords
- See what hashtags auto-populate
- Note view counts on hashtag pages

**Step 3: Creative Center**
- Go to tiktok.com/creativecenter
- Check "Trending Hashtags"
- Filter by your industry

**Step 4: Test & Track**
- Try different hashtag combinations
- Track which perform best
- Double down on winners

### Hashtag Categories by Niche

**Fitness:**
- Broad: #fitness, #workout, #gym
- Medium: #homeworkout, #fitnessmotivation, #weightloss
- Specific: #10minuteworkout, #absworkout, #beginnerfitness

**Business:**
- Broad: #business, #entrepreneur, #money
- Medium: #smallbusiness, #entrepreneurship, #sidehustle
- Specific: #solopreneur, #onlinebusiness, #passiveincome

**Beauty:**
- Broad: #beauty, #makeup, #skincare
- Medium: #makeuptutorial, #skincareroutine, #beautyhacks
- Specific: #drugstorebeauty, #kbeauty, #maturebeauty

**Food:**
- Broad: #food, #cooking, #recipe
- Medium: #easyrecipes, #foodtiktok, #homecooking
- Specific: #30minutemeals, #budgetmeals, #mealprep

### Hashtag Don'ts
- ❌ Don't use irrelevant hashtags (shadowban risk)
- ❌ Don't use banned hashtags
- ❌ Don't use only huge hashtags (#fyp, #foryou - too competitive)
- ❌ Don't use 20+ hashtags (looks spammy)
- ❌ Don't use the same hashtags on every video

### Hashtag Placement
- Put hashtags in caption (not comments)
- Put them at the END of caption
- Keep caption readable before hashtags
- Example:
  ```
  This changed everything for me. Try it and let me know!
  
  #homeworkout #fitnessjourney #beginnerfitness #quickworkout
  ```

## 🧠 ALGORITHM HACKS

### How the TikTok Algorithm Works (2026)

**The algorithm prioritizes:**
1. **Watch Time** - How long people watch
2. **Completion Rate** - % who watch to the end
3. **Re-watches** - People watching multiple times
4. **Engagement** - Comments, shares, saves (in that order)
5. **Follower Engagement** - How your followers respond
6. **Session Time** - Does your video keep people on TikTok?

### Algorithm Hack #1: The First Hour

**The first 60 minutes are critical:**
- Algorithm tests your video with small batch (200-500 views)
- Based on performance, decides whether to push wider
- Strong early performance = viral potential

**How to maximize first hour:**
- Post when your audience is most active
- Engage with every comment in first hour
- Share to your other platforms immediately
- Have followers ready to engage (build community)

### Algorithm Hack #2: The Comment Strategy

**Comments are weighted heavily:**
- More comments = more distribution
- Longer comments = better than short ones
- Comment threads = even better

**How to drive comments:**
- Ask specific questions in video
- Ask questions in caption
- Pin a question as first comment
- Reply to comments with questions
- Create controversy (respectfully)
- Make relatable content people want to discuss

### Algorithm Hack #3: The Save Bait

**Saves are the new likes:**
- Saves signal high-value content
- Algorithm pushes save-worthy videos
- Saves = people want to reference later

**How to increase saves:**
- Create educational/tutorial content
- Use "Save this for later" in video
- Make listicles and roundups
- Share tips/tricks/hacks
- Create templates people can use
- Post content with long-term value

### Algorithm Hack #4: The Share Trigger

**Shares expand your reach exponentially:**
- Shares put your content in front of new audiences
- Shares signal highly relatable/valuable content
- Shares can trigger viral loops

**How to increase shares:**
- Create highly relatable content
- Make people say "this is SO you"
- Share controversial (but true) takes
- Create content people want to be associated with
- Make inspirational/motivational content
- Share surprising statistics/facts

### Algorithm Hack #5: The Session Keeper

**TikTok rewards videos that keep people on the platform:**
- If people watch your video, then scroll and leave = bad
- If people watch your video, then watch more videos = good
- If people watch your video multiple times = best

**How to keep people on TikTok:**
- Create series content ( Part 1, Part 2, etc.)
- Reference your other videos
- Create binge-worthy content
- End with "watch next" energy
- Make content that rewards rewatching

### Algorithm Hack #6: The Niche Down

**The algorithm needs to understand who you are:**
- Accounts with clear niche get pushed to right audience
- Mixed content confuses the algorithm
- Consistency = clarity = distribution

**How to niche down:**
- Pick one primary topic
- Post about that topic 80%+ of time
- Use consistent hashtags
- Engage with same niche community
- Don't pivot randomly

### Algorithm Hack #7: The Engagement Pod (Ethical Version)

**Build a genuine engagement community:**
- Connect with 10-20 creators in your niche
- Genuinely engage with their content
- They do the same for you
- NOT a "like for like" scheme - genuine community

**How to build:**
- Join creator Discords
- Engage genuinely with niche creators daily
- Build real relationships
- Support each other authentically
- Quality over quantity

### Algorithm Hack #8: The Trend Jacking

**Riding trends = algorithm boost:**
- Trends are already being pushed by TikTok
- Using trends = free distribution
- Early adopters win biggest

**How to jack trends:**
- Monitor trending sounds daily
- Check trending hashtags
- Put your niche spin on trends
- Move fast (trends die in 3-7 days)
- Don't force it - must fit your brand

## 📈 GROWTH SPRINTS

### The 30-Day Growth Sprint

**Goal:** Gain 10K-50K followers in 30 days

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Optimize profile (bio, photo, link)
- Create content buffer (10 videos)
- Research top 20 creators in niche
- Identify winning content patterns

**Week 2-3: Volume + Testing**
- Post 2-3x per day
- Test different hooks, formats, lengths
- Engage 1 hour/day (comments, duets, stitches)
- Track what works

**Week 4: Double Down**
- Create more of what worked
- Collaborate with 2-3 creators
- Go live 2-3 times
- Push best performers with ads (optional)

### The Viral Ladder Strategy

**Step 1: Get one video to 1K views**
- Focus on hook and retention
- Post consistently
- Engage heavily

**Step 2: Get one video to 10K views**
- Replicate what worked in Step 1
- Improve production quality
- Use trending sounds

**Step 3: Get one video to 100K views**
- Study your 10K video
- Make better version
- Post at optimal time
- Engage heavily first hour

**Step 4: Get one video to 1M+ views**
- You now know what works
- Invest more in production
- Collaborate with bigger creators
- Consider ads to boost

**Each level unlocks:**
- More followers
- More credibility
- More opportunities
- Easier to hit next level
