## 📋 DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

### Opening (5 minutes)

```
"Hey {{first_name}}, thanks for hopping on the call! 

Before we dive in, let me quickly explain how I like to use 
these calls:

First, I want to learn about {{company_name}} and what you're 
trying to achieve with TikTok.

Then, if it makes sense, I'll share how we've helped similar 
companies and what that could look like for you.

And if it's not a good fit, I'll be the first to tell you - 
I'd rather point you in the right direction than waste your time.

Sound good?

Awesome. Let me start by asking..."
```

---

### Discovery Questions (15 minutes)

**Current State:**
- "What's your current TikTok presence like?"
- "Have you worked with agencies before? What was that experience like?"
- "What's working well right now? What's not?"

**Goals:**
- "What are you hoping to achieve with TikTok?"
- "What does success look like in 90 days? 6 months?"
- "How does TikTok fit into your broader marketing strategy?"

**Audience:**
- "Who's your ideal customer?"
- "Where are they spending time online?"
- "What problems do you solve for them?"

**Budget & Timeline:**
- "What budget have you allocated for TikTok marketing?"
- "What's your ideal timeline for getting started?"
- "Are there any key dates or launches we should know about?"

**Decision Process:**
- "Who else is involved in this decision?"
- "What would need to happen for you to move forward?"
- "What's your ideal timeline for making a decision?"

### Handling Objections

**"It's too expensive"**
```
"I totally get that. Can I ask - compared to what?

If you're comparing this to doing it yourself or hiring a cheap 
freelancer, I get it. But we're not just posting videos - we're 
building a revenue channel.

Our clients typically see {{ROI}}. At that rate, the investment 
pays for itself in {{timeframe}}.

What's your target ROI on marketing spend?"
```

**"We need to think about it"**
```
"Of course! What specifically do you need to think through?

Is it the investment? The timeline? Whether we're the right fit?

I ask because I want to make sure I address any concerns before 
you make a decision."
```

**"We've tried agencies before"**
```
"I hear that a lot, and honestly, I don't blame you for being 
cautious. The agency space is full of people who overpromise 
and underdeliver.

Can I ask what specifically didn't work with the last agency?

[Listen carefully]

Here's how we're different: {{differentiation}}.

Plus, we {{guarantee/risk_reversal}}.

Would it help if we started with a smaller pilot project to 
prove ourselves first?"
```

### Closing (5 minutes)

```
   signed and schedule our kickoff

Does this timeline work for you?"
```

## 💰 PRICING CALCULATOR

### Quick Pricing Formula

```
Base Price = (Hours per Month × Hourly Rate) + Overhead

Example:
- 20 hours/month × $75/hour = $1,500
- Overhead (tools, software) = $200
- Base Cost = $1,700

Markup for Profit (2-3x):
- 2x = $3,400/month
- 3x = $5,100/month

Recommended Price: $4,000-5,000/month
```

### ROI-Based Pricing

```
Client's Expected Revenue from TikTok: $50,000/month
Your Fee as % of Revenue: 10%
Your Price: $5,000/month

Client ROI: 10:1 ($10 earned for every $1 spent)
```
