## 🎯 NICHE SELECTION FRAMEWORK

### Step 1: The Venn Diagram
Find the intersection of:
1. **What you know** (expertise/experience)
2. **What you enjoy** (won't burn out)
3. **What pays** (monetization potential)
4. **What's viral** (proven demand on TikTok)

### Step 2: Validate Demand
- Search your niche on TikTok
- Are there accounts with 100K+ followers?
- Do videos regularly hit 100K+ views?
- Are brands advertising in this space?

### Step 3: Check Monetization
- Are there products to sell?
- Are there affiliate programs?
- Do brands sponsor creators?
- Can you create a course/coaching?

### Step 4: Assess Competition
- **Too little competition:** May not be profitable
- **Too much competition:** Hard to stand out
- **Sweet spot:** Active niche with room for new voices

## 🏆 BEST NICHES FOR BEGINNERS

If you're starting from zero, these niches have the lowest barrier to entry:

1. **Relationships/Dating** - Just share opinions and stories
2. **Self-Improvement** - Document your own journey
3. **Food/Cooking** - Show what you already make
4. **Comedy** - If you're naturally funny
5. **Home Organization** - Clean and organize on camera

---

## 💎 UNDERSERVED NICHES (OPPORTUNITIES)

These niches have demand but less competition:

1. **Parenting (specific angles)** - Dad jokes, single parenting, special needs
2. **Career Advice** - Industry-specific tips, negotiation, promotions
3. **Sustainable Living** - Zero waste, eco swaps, minimalism
4. **Pet Training** - Specific breeds, behavior issues
5. **Hobby Deep-Dives** - Woodworking, pottery, gardening
6. **Local Content** - City-specific guides, hidden gems
7. **Nostalgia** - 90s/2000s content, retro reviews
8. **Disability/Limitation Hacks** - Adaptive living tips

---

## 🚫 NICHES TO AVOID

- **Oversaturated without differentiation:** Generic fitness, basic beauty
- **Too narrow:** Hyper-specific topics with no monetization
- **Controversial without expertise:** Medical advice, legal advice
- **Banned/restricted:** Certain supplements, get-rich-quick schemes
- **No clear path to money:** Pure entertainment without audience loyalty
