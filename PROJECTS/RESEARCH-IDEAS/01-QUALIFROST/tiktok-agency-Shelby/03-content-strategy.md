## 🎵 TRENDING SOUNDS STRATEGY

### How to Find Trending Sounds

**Method 1: TikTok Creative Center**
- Go to: tiktok.com/creativecenter
- Check "Trending Songs" updated daily
- Filter by region and industry

**Method 2: In-App Discovery**
- Scroll FYP for 15 minutes
- Note sounds used 3+ times
- Click sound → check video count
- Ideal: 10K-500K videos (rising, not saturated)

**Method 3: Competitor Research**
- Check top 10 accounts in your niche
- See what sounds they're using
- Note which videos performed best

**Method 4: Sound Libraries**
- Follow @tiktoktips, @creators
- Join TikTok creator Discords
- Use tools: Tokboard, TrendTok, SoundRaw

### Sound Strategy Framework

| Video Type | Sound Choice |
|------------|--------------|
| Educational | Low-volume trending or original voice |
| Entertainment | High-energy trending sound |
| Storytime | Minimal background or emotional track |
| Transformation | Build-up sound with drop |
| Comedy | Meme sounds or trending audio |
| Tutorials | Upbeat, non-distracting |

### Sound Timing
- **Early adopter:** Sound has 1K-10K videos → Highest viral potential
- **Growth phase:** Sound has 10K-100K videos → Still good
- **Peak:** Sound has 100K-1M videos → Use with unique angle
- **Saturated:** Sound has 1M+ videos → Avoid unless perfect fit

### Minimum Viable Posting
- **3-4 times per week** = Slow but steady growth
- **5-7 times per week** = Optimal for most accounts
- **2-3 times per day** = Aggressive growth (for serious creators)

## 📺 SERIES PLANNING

### Why Series Work
- Creates anticipation ("What happens next?")
- Increases return viewers
- Higher completion rates
- Easier content planning
- Build-in narrative arc

### Series Formats That Work

**1. The Journey Series**
- "Day 1 of [goal]"
- "Week 1, 2, 3, 4 of [transformation]"
- Example: "Day 1 of building a 6-figure business"

**2. The List Series**
- "30 days of [topic]"
- "50 ways to [action]"
- Example: "30 days of posting TikTok tips"

**3. The Story Arc**
- Part 1, 2, 3 of a story
- Cliffhangers between parts
- Example: "The time I [crazy story] - Part 1"

**4. The Challenge Series**
- "Trying [thing] for 30 days"
- "Can I [achievement] in [timeframe]?"
- Example: "Can I gain 10K followers in 30 days?"

**5. The Educational Series**
- "[Topic] 101: Lesson 1, 2, 3..."
- "Master [skill] in 10 videos"
- Example: "TikTok growth masterclass: Episode 1"

**6. The Reaction Series**
- "Reacting to [thing] - Part 1"
- "Rating [things] 1-10"
- Example: "Rating viral TikTok hacks"

### Series Best Practices
- **Number your episodes** in caption and on-screen
- **End with cliffhanger** or teaser for next episode
- **Use consistent branding** (same intro, colors, music)
- **Post on schedule** (same day/time each episode)
- **Create playlist** on your profile

## 🎬 CONTENT PILLARS

### The 3-5-2 Framework
Post content in this ratio:

**3 Educational Videos** (value, tips, tutorials)
**5 Entertainment Videos** (trends, comedy, stories)
**2 Engagement Videos** (Q&A, polls, behind-the-scenes)

Per week (10 videos):
- Monday: Educational
- Tuesday: Entertainment (trend)
- Wednesday: Educational
- Thursday: Entertainment (story)
- Friday: Engagement
- Saturday: Entertainment (trend)
- Sunday: Educational
- Monday: Entertainment
- Tuesday: Engagement
- Wednesday: Educational

### Alternative: The 4-Pillar System

**Pillar 1: Educate** (40%)
- How-tos, tips, tutorials, explainers

**Pillar 2: Entertain** (30%)
- Trends, comedy, stories, reactions

**Pillar 3: Engage** (20%)
- Q&As, polls, behind-the-scenes, day-in-life

**Pillar 4: Empower** (10%)
- Motivation, inspiration, transformations

## 🔄 CONTENT REPURPOSING SYSTEM

### One Video → Multiple Platforms

**Original:** TikTok (9:16 vertical)

**Repurpose to:**
- Instagram Reels (same file)
- YouTube Shorts (same file)
- Pinterest Idea Pins (same file)
- Twitter/X (crop to square, post as GIF)
- LinkedIn (if professional content)
- Email newsletter (embed or screenshot)
- Blog post (transcript + video)

### Batch Creation Workflow

**Day 1: Planning (1 hour)**
- Research trends
- Write 10 scripts/hooks
- Plan outfits/locations

**Day 2: Filming (2-3 hours)**
- Film all 10 videos
- Multiple takes per video
- Capture B-roll

**Day 3: Editing (3-4 hours)**
- Edit all videos
- Add captions, effects
- Export and schedule

**Result:** 2 weeks of content in 3 days
