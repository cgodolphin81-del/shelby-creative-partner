## ROI Framework

### Value Calculation

```
ROI = (Value Created - Investment) / Investment × 100

Value Created = 
  + Funds raised (token sale)
  + Revenue generated (protocol fees)
  + Cost savings (automation)
  + Market cap increase
  + Strategic value
```

## FAQ

### Q: Why is blockchain development so expensive?
**A:** Blockchain development requires specialized expertise, extensive security measures, and carries significant financial risk. A single vulnerability can result in millions in losses. Our pricing reflects the expertise and rigor required.

### Q: Do you offer discounts for startups?
**A:** Yes, we offer 15-25% discounts for pre-seed and seed-stage startups with credible backing. We also offer equity arrangements for exceptional opportunities.

### Q: Can you work with our budget?
**A:** We can often phase projects to fit budgets. We'll work with you to prioritize features and create a roadmap that matches your financial constraints.

### Q: What if the project takes longer than estimated?
**A:** Fixed-price projects absorb our overruns. For time-and-materials projects, we provide regular updates and seek approval before exceeding estimates.

### Q: Do you accept cryptocurrency?
**A:** Yes, we accept USDC, USDT, ETH, and BTC. Crypto payments receive a 5% discount.
