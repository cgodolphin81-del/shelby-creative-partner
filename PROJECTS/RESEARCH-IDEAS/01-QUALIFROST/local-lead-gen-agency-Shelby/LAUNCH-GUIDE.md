## 🚀 Quick Launch Checklist

### Option A: Build Your Own Lead Gen Business (Recommended)

This system is designed for YOU to build and operate a local lead generation business. Here's how to get started:

#### Week 1: Foundation (10-15 hours)
- [ ] **Select Your First Niche** (2 hours)
  - Review `guides/01-niche-selection.md`
  - Choose from top 20 niches (roofing, plumbing, HVAC, etc.)
  - Consider your local market knowledge

- [ ] **Choose Target City** (1 hour)
  - Population 100K-500K ideal for first site
  - Not too competitive, not too small
  - Research local competition

- [ ] **Register Domain & Hosting** (1 hour)
  - Domain: CityService.com (e.g., DenverRoofPros.com)
  - Hosting: SiteGround, Bluehost, or Cloudways ($5-15/month)
  - SSL certificate (usually free with hosting)

- [ ] **Build WordPress Site** (4-6 hours)
  - Install WordPress
  - Use template from `templates/wordpress-theme/`
  - Create 5-7 core pages (Home, Services, About, Contact, Service Areas)
  - Add phone number and contact forms

- [ ] **Set Up Call Tracking** (1 hour)
  - Sign up for CallRail, CallFire, or similar ($20-50/month)
  - Get local phone number
  - Set up call forwarding to voicemail initially
  - Install tracking code on website

#### Week 2: SEO Foundation (15-20 hours)
- [ ] **On-Page SEO** (4-6 hours)
  - Follow `guides/03-ranking-strategy.md`
  - Optimize title tags, meta descriptions
  - Add schema markup (LocalBusiness)
  - Create location-specific service pages

- [ ] **Google Business Profile** (2 hours)
  - Create GBP for the business
  - Verify with postcard (takes 5-7 days)
  - Complete all fields thoroughly
  - Add photos and services

- [ ] **Local Citations** (4-6 hours)
  - Submit to top 50 citation sites
  - Ensure NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistency
  - Use tools like BrightLocal or manual submission

- [ ] **Initial Content** (4-6 hours)
  - Write 5-10 blog posts targeting local keywords
  - Create city-specific landing pages
  - Add FAQs, service guides, how-to content

#### Week 3-4: Link Building & Optimization (10-15 hours)
- [ ] **Initial Backlinks** (5-8 hours)
  - Local chamber of commerce
  - Industry associations
  - Local sponsorships
  - Guest posts on local blogs

- [ ] **Content Expansion** (3-4 hours)
  - Add 2-3 new blog posts per week
  - Create service-specific landing pages
  - Build out city pages for surrounding areas

- [ ] **Monitor & Adjust** (2-3 hours)
  - Track rankings (use free tools initially)
  - Monitor Google Search Console
  - Adjust based on performance

#### Month 2-3: Ranking Phase
- [ ] Continue building content (2-3 posts/week)
- [ ] Build 5-10 quality backlinks per month
- [ ] Optimize based on ranking data
- [ ] Engage in local community (online)
- [ ] Wait for rankings to improve (typically 3-6 months)

#### Month 4-6: Monetization
- [ ] **Start Outreach to Local Businesses** (5-10 hours/week)
  - Use scripts from `scripts/outreach/`
  - Contact 20-30 businesses per week
  - Present lead volume and pricing

- [ ] **Close First Rental Partner** 
  - Use contracts from `contracts/`
  - Monthly rental: $500-2,000/month
  - Or pay-per-lead: $30-150/lead

- [ ] **Set Up Lead Delivery**
  - Forward calls to partner
  - Set up lead form email notifications
  - Provide monthly lead reports

## ⚠️ Critical Success Factors

### Do's:
✅ Pick niches with high lead value ($50+ per lead)
✅ Build quality sites (not spammy)
✅ Focus on ONE niche/city for your first site
✅ Be patient (SEO takes 3-6 months)
✅ Track everything (calls, forms, rankings)
✅ Communicate regularly with rental partners

### Don'ts:
❌ Don't build in oversaturated markets (NYC, LA) for first site
❌ Don't use black-hat SEO tactics
❌ Don't overpromise to rental partners
❌ Don't neglect mobile optimization
❌ Don't skip call tracking
❌ Don't give up before month 6
