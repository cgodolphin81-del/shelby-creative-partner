## 2. LEAD DEFINITION AND PRICING

### 2.1 Qualified Lead Definition
A "Qualified Lead" is defined as:

**(a) Phone Leads:**
- Incoming phone call to the Property's tracking number
- Call duration of **60 seconds or more**
- Caller is seeking [Service Type] services
- Caller is located within the Service Area
- Call occurs during stated business hours: **[Days/Times]**

**(b) Form Leads:**
- Online form submission through the Property
- Includes valid name, phone number, and email address
- Request is for [Service Type] services
- Requester is located within the Service Area
- Submission is not spam or duplicate

### 2.2 Lead Exclusions
The following are NOT considered Qualified Leads and will NOT be billed:
- Calls lasting less than **30 seconds**
- Spam, prank, or test calls/submissions
- Wrong numbers
- Competitors calling for research
- Job seekers or employment inquiries
- Calls/submissions from outside the Service Area
- Sales solicitations or vendor calls
- Duplicate leads (same person within 30 days)
- Calls with no answer (voicemail only)

### 2.3 Lead Pricing

**Option A: Flat Rate Per Lead**
```
All Qualified Leads: $[Amount] per lead
```

**Option B: Tiered Pricing**
```

[X+1] - [Y] leads/month: $[Amount] per lead
[Y+] leads/month:        $[Amount] per lead
```

**Option C: By Lead Type**
```
Phone Leads (60+ sec):   $[Amount] per lead
Form Submissions:        $[Amount] per lead
Emergency/After-Hours:   $[Amount] per lead
```

- Minimum Monthly Charge: **$[Amount]**

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of **[3/6/12] months** ("Initial Term").

### 4.2 Renewal
After the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew on a [month-to-month] basis unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience
Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time with **30 days written notice** to the other Party.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:
- Materially breaches any term of this Agreement
- Fails to cure a breach within 10 days of written notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy
- Engages in fraudulent or illegal activities

### 4.5 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Client's right to receive leads ceases immediately
- Provider shall deliver any Qualified Leads received within 24 hours of termination
- Client shall pay all outstanding invoices within 7 days
- Each Party returns any confidential information of the other Party
