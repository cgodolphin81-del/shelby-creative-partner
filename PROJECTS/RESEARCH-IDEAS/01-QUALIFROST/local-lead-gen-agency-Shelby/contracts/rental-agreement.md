## 2. LEAD DEFINITION

### 2.1 Qualified Lead
A "Qualified Lead" is defined as:

**(a) Phone Leads:**
- Incoming phone call to the Property's tracking number
- Call duration of 60 seconds or more
- Caller is seeking [Service Type] services
- Caller is located within the Service Area
- Call occurs during Provider's stated business hours (or after-hours if Client offers after-hours service)

**(b) Form Leads:**
- Online form submission through the Property
- Includes valid name, phone number, and email address
- Request is for [Service Type] services
- Requester is located within the Service Area

### 2.2 Exclusions
The following are NOT considered Qualified Leads:
- Calls lasting less than 30 seconds
- Spam, prank, or test calls/submissions
- Wrong numbers
- Competitors calling for research
- Job seekers or employment inquiries
- Calls/submissions from outside the Service Area
- Sales solicitations

### 2.3 Lead Verification
Provider shall maintain call recordings and form submission records for verification purposes. Client may request lead verification with reasonable notice.

## 3. COMPENSATION

### 3.1 Monthly Fee
Client agrees to pay Provider a flat monthly fee of **$[Amount]** ("Monthly Fee") for exclusive rights to all Qualified Leads.

### 3.2 Payment Terms
- **Due Date:** Monthly Fee is due on the 1st day of each month
- **Payment Method:** Automatic credit card or ACH charge
- **Late Fees:** Payments more than 7 days late incur a 1.5% monthly late fee
- **First Payment:** First month's fee is due upon signing this Agreement

### 3.3 Price Adjustments
- **Annual Increase:** Monthly Fee may increase by up to 10% annually upon renewal
- **Market Adjustment:** Provider may adjust fees at renewal based on market conditions and lead volume
- **Notice:** Provider shall give 30 days written notice of any fee changes

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of **[6/12] months** ("Initial Term").

### 4.2 Renewal
After the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew on a month-to-month basis unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience
Either Party may terminate this Agreement at any time with **30 days written notice** to the other Party.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:
- Materially breaches any term of this Agreement
- Fails to cure a breach within 10 days of written notice
- Becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

### 4.5 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
- Client's right to receive leads ceases immediately
- Provider shall forward any pending leads received within 24 hours of termination
- Client shall pay all outstanding fees through the termination date
- Each Party returns any confidential information of the other Party
