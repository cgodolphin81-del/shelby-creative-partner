# 09 - Team Structure Guide
## Roles, Hiring & Management

Building the right team is the difference between a side hustle and a scalable business. This guide covers every role you'll need and when to hire.

## 🎯 Hiring Process

### Step 1: Define the Role

**Create a Job Description Including:**
- Role title
- Responsibilities (detailed)
- Required skills
- Nice-to-have skills
- Compensation range
- Work schedule
- Reporting structure
- Growth opportunities

### Step 2: Source Candidates

**Best Channels by Role:**

| Role | Best Channels |
|------|---------------|
| VA | OnlineJobs.ph, Upwork |
| Writer | ProBlogger, Upwork |
| Link Builder | SEO groups, Upwork |
| SEO Specialist | LinkedIn, SEO communities |
| Account Manager | LinkedIn, Indeed |
| Sales Rep | LinkedIn, commission networks |
| Developer | Upwork, Toptal |
| Manager | LinkedIn, executive search |

### Step 3: Screen Candidates

**Initial Screening:**
- Review resume/portfolio
- Check references
- Assess communication skills
- Verify experience claims

**Skills Assessment:**
- Writers: Writing test (paid)
- Link builders: Outreach test
- SEO: Audit exercise
- Sales: Role-play call
- Developers: Test project

### Step 4: Interview

**Interview Structure:**
1. **Introduction** (5 min) - Build rapport
2. **Experience Review** (10 min) - Discuss background
3. **Skills Discussion** (10 min) - Role-specific questions
4. **Culture Fit** (5 min) - Values alignment
5. **Candidate Questions** (5 min) - Their turn
6. **Next Steps** (5 min) - Timeline and process

**Key Questions to Ask:**
- "Tell me about a time you [relevant situation]"
- "What's your approach to [key responsibility]?"
- "What are you looking for in your next role?"
- "What's your biggest strength/weakness?"
- "Why do you want to work here?"

### Step 5: Trial Period

**30-Day Trial Structure:**
- Week 1: Training and onboarding
- Week 2-3: Supervised work
- Week 4: Independent work
- Day 30: Performance review

**Trial Compensation:**
- Pay fair market rate (don't exploit)
- Clear performance expectations
- Written agreement on conversion terms

### Step 6: Onboarding

**First Week:**
- Welcome email and introduction
- Access to all tools/systems
- Review SOPs and documentation
- Meet the team
- First assignments

**First 30 Days:**
- Daily check-ins (Week 1)
- Weekly 1-on-1s (Weeks 2-4)
- Clear goals and expectations
- Regular feedback
- Training as needed

**First 90 Days:**
- 30/60/90 day goals
- Monthly performance reviews
- Integration into team culture
- Increased responsibility
- Long-term goal setting

## 📊 Management Best Practices

### Communication

**Daily:**
- Team chat (Slack)
- Quick standup (async or 15 min call)

**Weekly:**
- Team meeting (30-60 min)
- 1-on-1s with direct reports
- Goal review and planning

**Monthly:**
- All-hands meeting
- Performance reviews
- Strategy updates

**Quarterly:**
- Quarterly planning
- Team offsite (virtual or in-person)
- Annual goal progress review

### Performance Management

**Set Clear Expectations:**
- Written job description
- 30/60/90 day goals
- Quarterly objectives
- Annual goals

**Regular Feedback:**
- Weekly check-ins
- Monthly formal review
- Quarterly performance review
- Annual comprehensive review

**Address Issues Early:**
- Don't wait for quarterly review
- Address problems within 48 hours
- Document conversations
- Create improvement plans when needed

### Culture Building

**For Remote Teams:**
- Virtual coffee chats
- Team building activities
- Recognition programs
- Clear values and mission
- Transparent communication

**Team Bonding Ideas:**
- Virtual game nights
- Online trivia
- Show-and-tell sessions
- Birthday celebrations
- Milestone recognition

## ⚠️ Common Hiring Mistakes

### ❌ Hiring Too Fast
**Problem:** Desperate hiring leads to bad fits
**Solution:** Have a pipeline, don't rush

### ❌ Hiring Too Slow
**Problem:** Miss opportunities, burn out team
**Solution:** Always be recruiting, even when not急需

### ❌ Unclear Expectations
**Problem:** Employee doesn't know what success looks like
**Solution:** Document everything, set clear goals

### ❌ Underpaying
**Problem:** High turnover, low quality
**Solution:** Pay fair market rate or above

### ❌ No Training
**Problem:** Employee struggles, frustrated on both sides
**Solution:** Invest in proper onboarding

### ❌ Micromanaging
**Problem:** Kills autonomy, creates dependency
**Solution:** Set expectations, then trust

### ❌ Ignoring Culture
**Problem:** Toxic environment, high turnover
**Solution:** Hire for culture fit, build intentionally
