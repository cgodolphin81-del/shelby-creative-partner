# Cold Email Outreach Scripts
## Proven Templates for Lead Gen Client Acquisition

---

## 📧 Email Best Practices

**Before You Send:**
- ✅ Research the business (website, reviews, services)
- ✅ Find the owner/decision maker's name
- ✅ Personalize every email (no mass blasting)
- ✅ Use a professional email address
- ✅ Keep it under 150 words
- ✅ One clear CTA

**Technical Setup:**
- Use a separate domain (e.g., yourbrand.co vs yourbrand.com)
- Warm up your email (send 10-20/day initially)
- Use email tracking (HubSpot, Mailtrack, etc.)
- Follow CAN-SPAM laws (include unsubscribe)
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm local time

## 🎯 Email Template 1: The "I Have Leads" Approach

**Subject Line Options:**
- Quick question about [their business name]
- [FirstName], I have customers looking for [service] in [City]
- Are you taking on new [service] jobs in [City]?
- [Mutual Connection/Local Reference] recommended I reach out

**Email Body:**

```
Hi [FirstName],

I'm reaching out because I run a local website that connects 
[City] residents with [service] companies, and I'm getting 
more inquiries than I can handle.

Last month alone, we generated [X] qualified leads for 
[service] in [City]. Instead of turning them away, I'd prefer 
to partner with a reputable local company.

I came across [Business Name] and was impressed by [specific 
compliment - reviews, years in business, something specific].

Are you currently accepting new customers for [service] work?

If so, I'd love to send some leads your way and discuss a 
simple partnership.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Phone]
[Your Website]

P.S. No upfront costs - we only succeed when you get leads.
```

**Why This Works:**
- Leads with value (you have something they want)
- Social proof (mentions lead volume)
- Specific compliment (shows you researched)
- Low-pressure ask (just asking if they're accepting)
- Risk reversal (no upfront costs)

## 🎯 Email Template 2: The "Missing Opportunity" Approach

**Subject Line Options:**
- [Business Name] isn't showing up for "[keyword]" in [City]
- Your competitors are getting these customers
- Missing out on [X] monthly searches in [City]
- Quick SEO observation about [Business Name]

**Email Body:**

```
Hi [FirstName],

I was searching for "[service] in [City]" this morning and 
noticed [Business Name] isn't appearing on the first page of 
Google.

Here's the thing: there are [X,XXX]+ monthly searches for 
"[primary keyword]" in [City]. Most of those clicks go to 
the top 3 results.

Your competitors ([Competitor 1], [Competitor 2]) are 
capturing those leads right now.

I specialize in building local websites that rank #1 in 
[City] for [service] keywords. I'd be happy to:

1. Show you exactly where you're losing visibility
2. Share what your top competitors are doing
3. Discuss how we could capture those leads together

Got 10 minutes for a quick call this week?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Phone]
[Your Website]

P.S. I've already done the keyword research for [City] - 
happy to share it even if we don't work together.
```

**Why This Works:**
- Creates urgency (missing opportunity)
- Specific data (shows you did homework)
- Names competitors (triggers competitive instinct)
- Offers value upfront (free research)
- Clear, low-commitment CTA

[Your Website]

## 📧 Follow-Up Email Sequences

### Follow-Up #1 (3 days after initial email)

```
Subject: Re: [Original Subject]

Hi [FirstName],

Just floating this to the top of your inbox in case it got buried.

Still happy to send some [service] leads your way if you're 
interested.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up #2 (5 days after Follow-Up #1)

```
Subject: Should I close your file?

Hi [FirstName],

I haven't heard back, so I'm assuming you're either:

1. Not interested (totally fine)
2. Too busy to respond (I get it)
3. Waiting to see if I follow up (here I am!)

If you're still open to it, I'd love to send some leads 
your way. If not, no hard feelings - just let me know so 
I can reach out to other companies in [City].

Either way, wish you the best!

[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up #3 (7 days after Follow-Up #2 - Breakup Email)

```
Subject: Last try

Hi [FirstName],

This is my last attempt to reach you.

If I don't hear back, I'll assume [Business Name] isn't 
interested in additional leads right now, and I'll move on.

If you DO want to talk, just reply with "yes" and I'll 
send over my calendar.

No pressure either way.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## 📊 Email Metrics to Track

| Metric | Good | Great |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Open Rate** | 25-35% | 40%+ |
| **Reply Rate** | 5-10% | 15%+ |
| **Meeting Booked** | 2-5% | 8%+ |
| **Conversion to Client** | 20-30% of meetings | 40%+ |

**If Your Metrics Are Low:**

- **Low opens**: Improve subject lines, test send times
- **Low replies**: Better personalization, clearer CTA
- **Low meetings**: Reduce friction, offer more value upfront
- **Low conversions**: Better qualification, improve pitch

## ⚠️ Legal Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Requirements (USA)

✅ **Must Include:**
- Accurate "From" and "Subject" lines
- Your physical mailing address
- Clear unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days

❌ **Don't:**
- Use deceptive subject lines
- Hide your identity
- Continue emailing after opt-out
- Buy email lists (use at your own risk)

### GDPR Considerations (EU)

If emailing EU residents:
- Need explicit consent OR legitimate interest
- Must provide privacy policy
- Must honor deletion requests
- Stricter penalties for violations

**Best Practice**: Focus on US/Canada/Australia initially. EU requires more compliance work.

## 🎯 Email Outreach Checklist

### Before Sending

- [ ] Research the business (website, reviews, services)
- [ ] Find owner/decision maker name
- [ ] Verify email address
- [ ] Personalize email (name, business, specific detail)
- [ ] Proofread for typos
- [ ] Test all links
- [ ] Set up email tracking
- [ ] Add to CRM/spreadsheet

### After Sending

- [ ] Track opens and replies
- [ ] Send follow-up #1 (Day 3)
- [ ] Send follow-up #2 (Day 8)
- [ ] Send follow-up #3 (Day 15)
- [ ] Mark as "Not Interested" if no response
- [ ] Book call if they respond positively
- [ ] Send calendar invite after booking

### Weekly Goals

| Activity | Target |
|----------|--------|
| **Emails Sent** | 50-100 |
| **Replies** | 5-15 |
| **Calls Booked** | 2-5 |
| **New Clients** | 1-2 |

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Personalization beats volume** - 50 personalized emails > 500 generic ones
2. **Follow up 3-5 times** - Most replies come on follow-up #2 or #3
3. **Test subject lines** - A/B test to find what works
4. **Send at optimal times** - Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm local time
5. **Keep it short** - Busy owners won't read long emails
6. **Lead with value** - What's in it for THEM?
7. **Be human** - Write like you talk
8. **Track everything** - Know your conversion rates
9. **Warm up new domains** - Don't blast from day 1
10. **Always provide value** - Even if they say no
