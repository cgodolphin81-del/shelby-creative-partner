## 💰 Business Model Summary

### Revenue Models Covered
1. **Flat Rental** - $500-5,000/month per site
2. **Pay-Per-Lead** - $20-500/lead
3. **Hybrid** - Base + overage
4. **Revenue Share** - 10-30% of closed business
5. **Site Sale** - 25-40x monthly revenue

### Financial Projections
| Timeline | Sites | Revenue/Mo | Profit/Mo |
|----------|-------|------------|-----------|
| 6 months | 10-12 | $12-18K | $7-12K |
| 12 months | 20-25 | $30-40K | $18-25K |
| 24 months | 50-55 | $70-85K | $40-55K |
| 36 months | 80+ | $100-150K | $60-100K |

### Unit Economics
- **Avg. Revenue/Site:** $1,500/month
- **Direct Costs/Site:** $340/month
- **Gross Margin:** 77%
- **Net Margin:** 30-40%
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 40:1+

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

### What Makes This Model Work
1. **Asset Ownership** - You own the digital real estate
2. **Recurring Revenue** - Monthly rentals create predictability
3. **High Margins** - 75%+ gross, 30%+ net
4. **Scalable** - Systems allow 100+ sites
5. **Exit Options** - Sell individual sites or entire portfolio

### Critical Success Factors
1. **Niche Selection** - Pick profitable, manageable niches
2. **Quality Sites** - Professional, fast, mobile-optimized
3. **Local SEO** - Citations, backlinks, content
4. **Lead Tracking** - Prove value with data
5. **Client Retention** - Communication + results

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
1. Scaling before proving the model
2. Underpricing services
3. Poor lead tracking
4. No written contracts
5. Ignoring client communication
