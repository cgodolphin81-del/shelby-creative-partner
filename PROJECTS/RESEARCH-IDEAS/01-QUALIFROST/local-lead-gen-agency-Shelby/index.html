Two Ways to Monetize

Choose the revenue model that fits your goals and market.

🏠 Monthly Rental Model

$500 - $3,000/month per site

Business pays flat monthly fee for exclusive rights to all leads from your site.

  • Predictable recurring revenue
  • Easy to manage (set and forget)
  • Higher lifetime value per partner
  • Best for: Established sites with consistent leads

📊 Pay-Per-Lead Model

$20 - $200/per lead

Business pays only for qualified leads (calls, form submissions, etc.).

  • Lower barrier to entry for partners
  • Higher revenue potential in high-value niches
  • More tracking required
  • Best for: High-value niches (roofing, legal, etc.)
