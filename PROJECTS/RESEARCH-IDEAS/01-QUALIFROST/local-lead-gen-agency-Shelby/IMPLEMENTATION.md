## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Read the Overview (30 minutes)
Start with [README.md](./README.md) to understand the business model.

### Step 2: Choose Your Niche (1-2 hours)
Read [01-niche-selection.md](./guides/01-niche-selection.md) and pick your first niche.

**Recommended for beginners:**
- Garage Door Repair
- Tree Service
- Pressure Washing
- Pool Cleaning
- Pest Control

### Step 3: Select Your City (30 minutes)
Choose a city with:
- Population: 100K-500K (ideal for beginners)
- Growing economy
- Not overly saturated
- At least 10-20 potential rental clients

### Step 4: Follow the Quick Start Checklist (90 days)
Use [templates/quick-start-checklist.md](./templates/quick-start-checklist.md) as your day-to-day guide.

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Strategic Mistakes
❌ Picking overly competitive niches (personal injury as first niche)
❌ Targeting cities that are too small or too large
❌ Scaling before proving the model
❌ Not tracking lead sources
❌ Underpricing your services

### Tactical Mistakes
❌ Building ugly/unprofessional sites
❌ Skipping call tracking
❌ No contract (verbal agreements only)
❌ Not following up with prospects
❌ Ignoring client communication

### Operational Mistakes
❌ Not documenting processes
❌ Hiring too fast or too slow
❌ Mixing personal and business finances
❌ Not reinvesting profits
❌ Trying to do everything yourself

## 🆘 When You Get Stuck

### Problem: "My site isn't ranking"
**Check:**
- Is content high quality and original?
- Do you have 50+ citations?
- Do you have 20+ backlinks?
- Is technical SEO correct?
- Have you waited 3-6 months?

### Problem: "I can't get clients"
**Check:**
- Are you reaching out to 10+ businesses/day?
- Are you following up 3-5 times?
- Is your pitch clear and compelling?
- Are you offering fair pricing?
- Do you have lead data to show?

### Problem: "Leads aren't converting"
**Check:**
- Are leads high quality?
- Is client responding quickly?
- Is client's pricing competitive?
- Is client's sales process effective?
- Are leads in client's service area?

### Problem: "I'm overwhelmed"
**Solution:**
- Focus on ONE niche
- Build ONE site
- Sign ONE client
- Follow the checklist
- Ask for help (forums, mentors, communities)
