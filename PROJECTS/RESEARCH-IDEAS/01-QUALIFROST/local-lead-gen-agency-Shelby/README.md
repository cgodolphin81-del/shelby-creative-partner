## 🎯 Business Model Overview

**Rank and Rent** = Build websites → Rank in Google → Rent to businesses

- **Asset You Own**: The website, phone number, and Google Business Profile
- **Revenue Model**: Monthly rental ($500-$3,000/site) OR pay-per-lead ($20-$200/lead)
- **Scalability**: Each site is independent; build once, rent for years
- **Exit Potential**: Sell aged sites for 25-40x monthly revenue

### Why This Model Works

✅ **Recurring Revenue** - Monthly rentals create predictable income
✅ **Asset Ownership** - You control the asset, not the client
✅ **Location Arbitrage** - Rank in multiple cities simultaneously
✅ **Low Overhead** - No employees needed to start
✅ **High Margins** - 80-95% profit margins after initial build
✅ **Recession Resistant** - Local services always needed
