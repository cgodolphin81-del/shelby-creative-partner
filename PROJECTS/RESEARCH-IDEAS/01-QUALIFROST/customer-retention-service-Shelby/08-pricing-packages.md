### 7.2 ROI Calculation Framework

```
Retention ROI = (Revenue Retained + Expansion Revenue - Investment) / Investment

Example:
- Current monthly churn: 5%
- Current MRR: $100,000
- Monthly churned revenue: $5,000
- Annual churned revenue: $60,000

With 20% churn reduction:
- New monthly churn: 4%
- Monthly churned revenue: $4,000
- Annual churned revenue: $48,000
- Revenue retained: $12,000

Plus expansion revenue (5% increase):
- Additional MRR: $5,000
- Annual expansion: $60,000

Total benefit: $72,000
Investment (Professional Audit): $5,500
ROI: ($72,000 - $5,500) / $5,500 = 1,209%
Payback: <1 month
```
