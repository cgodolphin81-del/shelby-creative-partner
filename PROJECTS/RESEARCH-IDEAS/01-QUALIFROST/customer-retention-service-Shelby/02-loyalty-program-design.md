### 2.2 Bonus Multipliers

| Condition | Multiplier | Duration |
|-----------|------------|----------|
| Tier bonus (Gold) | 1.25x | Ongoing |
| Tier bonus (Platinum) | 1.5x | Ongoing |
| Birthday month | 2x | 30 days |
| Flash promotion | 3x | 24-72 hours |
| Double points day | 2x | Selected days |
| Category bonus | 2-5x | Specific products |

### 2.3 Points Valuation

```
Points Currency:
- 100 points = $1 USD (1% return rate)
- Minimum redemption: 500 points ($5 value)
- Maximum redemption per transaction: 50% of order value
- Points expiry: 24 months from earn date
- Points forfeiture: Upon account closure
```

## 3. Tier Structure

### 3.1 Tier Overview

| Tier | Name | Qualification | Benefits | % of Members |
|------|------|---------------|----------|--------------|
| 1 | Member | Sign up | Base benefits | ~60% |
| 2 | Silver | $500/year OR 5 purchases | Enhanced benefits | ~25% |
| 3 | Gold | $1,500/year OR 15 purchases | Premium benefits | ~12% |
| 4 | Platinum | $5,000/year OR 50 purchases | Elite benefits | ~3% |

### 3.2 Tier Benefits Matrix

| Benefit | Member | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|---------|--------|--------|------|----------|
| Points earning rate | 1x | 1.25x | 1.5x | 2x |
| Free shipping threshold | $75 | $50 | $35 | Always |
| Birthday reward | $5 | $10 | $20 | $50 |
| Early access to sales | ❌ | 24hr | 48hr | 72hr |
| Exclusive products | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Dedicated support | ❌ | ❌ | Email | Phone |
| Free returns | 30 days | 45 days | 60 days | 90 days |
| Gift with purchase | ❌ | $50+ | $30+ | Always |
| Surprise rewards | ❌ | Quarterly | Monthly | Weekly |
| VIP events access | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ + Guest |
| Personal shopper | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Beta product testing | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

### 3.3 Tier Progression & Status

```
Status Tracking:
- Real-time progress bar in account dashboard
- "X points until next tier" notifications
- Tier review: Annual (rolling 12-month period)
- Grace period: 60 days to maintain tier
- Tier downgrade: Soft landing (one tier max per year)
```

### 3.4 Fast-Track Opportunities

| Opportunity | Requirement | Result |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| Big spend month | $2,000 in 30 days | Skip to Gold |
| Referral champion | 10 successful referrals | Skip to Silver |
| Brand advocate | 20 reviews + 50 social shares | Custom tier review |
| Challenge completion | Complete all quarterly challenges | Tier boost |

### 4.2 Reward Rotation Strategy

```
Monthly Rotation:
- 70% evergreen rewards (always available)
- 20% seasonal rewards (quarterly rotation)
- 10% exclusive/limited rewards (flash availability)

Psychological Tactics:
- Scarcity: "Only X remaining"
- Urgency: "Ends in 48 hours"
- Exclusivity: "Gold+ members only"
- Social proof: "X members redeemed this week"
```

**Seasonal Quests:**
```
Example: "Summer Adventure Quest"
- Week 1: Make any purchase (200 pts)
- Week 2: Write a review (300 pts)
- Week 3: Refer a friend (500 pts)
- Week 4: Reach Gold tier status (1,000 pts)
- Completion Bonus: 2,000 pts + Exclusive Badge
```
