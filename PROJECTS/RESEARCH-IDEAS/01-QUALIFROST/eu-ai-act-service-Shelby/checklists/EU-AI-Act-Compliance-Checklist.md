# EU AI Act Compliance Checklist

## SECTION 1: GOVERNANCE & OVERSIGHT (Points 1-8)

| # | Requirement | Pass/Fail | Evidence Required | Notes |
|---|-------------|-----------|-------------------|-------|
| 1.1 | AI governance framework established and documented | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Governance policy document | Must include roles, responsibilities, escalation procedures |
| 1.2 | Senior management accountability assigned for AI systems | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Organizational chart, appointment letters | C-level or equivalent must be named |
| 1.3 | AI compliance officer or team designated | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Job description, team charter | Can be existing role with added responsibilities |
| 1.4 | Regular AI risk assessments scheduled (minimum annually) | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Assessment calendar, past reports | High-risk systems require more frequent review |
| 1.5 | Board/executive oversight of AI strategy and risks | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Board minutes, AI agenda items | Evidence of AI discussed at leadership level |
| 1.6 | AI policy communicated to all relevant employees | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Training records, acknowledgment logs | Include contractors and third parties |
| 1.7 | Whistleblower mechanism for AI-related concerns | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Policy document, reporting channel | Must allow anonymous reporting |
| 1.8 | Documentation retention policy (10+ years for high-risk AI) | ☐ Pass ☐ Fail | Retention schedule, storage system | Aligns with Article 24 requirements |
