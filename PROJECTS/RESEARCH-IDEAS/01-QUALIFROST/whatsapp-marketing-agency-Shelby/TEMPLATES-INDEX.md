## 🚀 How to Use These Templates

### For New Clients:
1. Use CLIENT-ONBOARDING-CHECKLIST.md
2. Fill out CAMPAIGN-BRIEF-TEMPLATE.md for each campaign
3. Track performance with PERFORMANCE-REPORT-TEMPLATE.md

### For Planning:
1. Use REVENUE-PROJECTION-MODEL.md for financial planning
2. Create ROI-CALCULATOR.md for client proposals
3. Use PRICING-CALCULATOR.md for internal analysis

### For Compliance:
1. Use COMPLIANCE-CHECKLIST.md before each campaign
2. Track opt-in documentation
3. Monthly audit of messaging practices
