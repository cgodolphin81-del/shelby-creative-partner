# Pricing Models - Revenue Structure & Profitability

## 💰 AGENCY PRICING STRATEGY

### Core Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based Pricing (Recommended):**
- Price based on value delivered, not hours worked
- Align pricing with client ROI
- Scale with client success

**Cost-Plus Pricing (Alternative):**
- Calculate your costs
- Add 30-50% margin
- Good for service-heavy work

**Tiered Pricing (Hybrid):**
- Multiple packages at different price points
- Captures different budget levels
- Anchors higher tiers as "best value"

## 💵 PRICING STRUCTURE OPTIONS

### Option 1: Retainer-Only (Recommended)

**Structure:**
- Monthly retainer for all services
- Minimum 3-month commitment
- Month-to-month after 3 months
- No setup fees (included in first month)

**Pros:**
- Predictable revenue
- Client commitment
- Better unit economics
- Easier to sell

**Cons:**
- Higher initial barrier
- May lose price-sensitive clients

**Best For:** Established agencies, quality-focused positioning

---

### Option 2: Hybrid (Setup + Retainer)

**Structure:**
- One-time setup fee
- Lower monthly retainer
- Minimum 3-month commitment

**Example:**
- Setup: $5,000 (covers onboarding, templates, training)
- Monthly: $4,000 (reduced from $6,500 retainer-only)

**Pros:**
- Lower monthly commitment
- Covers upfront costs
- Flexible

**Cons:**
- One-time revenue (less predictable)
- Client may leave after setup
- More administrative work

**Best For:** New agencies, service-heavy work

---

### Option 3: Performance-Based

**Structure:**
- Lower base fee
- Performance bonus
- Revenue share option

**Example:**
- Base: $3,000/mo
- Performance: 10% of incremental revenue generated
- Minimum guarantee: $5,000/mo

**Pros:**
- Aligned with client success
- Higher upside potential
- Builds trust

**Cons:**
- Risky (revenue attribution challenges)
- Payment delays
- Complex tracking

**Best For:** Confident agencies, proven track record

**⚠️ Warning:** Only use performance pricing with clients you trust. Always have a minimum guarantee.

## 📊 PROFITABILITY ANALYSIS

### Unit Economics (Per Client)

**E-commerce Growth Bundle Example:**

```
REVENUE:
Monthly Retainer: $6,500
Message Overages (avg): $500
Add-ons (avg): $300
TOTAL REVENUE: $7,300/mo

COSTS:
WhatsApp BSP Costs: $2,000/mo
Team Costs (allocated): $3,500/mo
Software/Tools: $300/mo
Overhead (allocated): $500/mo
TOTAL COSTS: $6,300/mo

GROSS PROFIT: $1,000/mo
GROSS MARGIN: 13.7%

Wait... that's too low. Let's optimize:

OPTIMIZED MODEL:
Monthly Retainer: $6,500
Message Overages: $500
Add-ons: $300
TOTAL REVENUE: $7,300/mo

COSTS:
WhatsApp BSP Costs: $1,500/mo (better rate)
Team Costs (optimized): $2,500/mo
Software/Tools: $200/mo
Overhead: $300/mo
TOTAL COSTS: $4,500/mo

GROSS PROFIT: $2,800/mo
GROSS MARGIN: 38.4% ✅
```

### Break-Even Analysis

```
FIXED COSTS (Agency):
Rent/Office: $2,000/mo
Software/Tools: $1,000/mo
Marketing: $3,000/mo
Admin/Overhead: $4,000/mo
TOTAL FIXED: $10,000/mo

CONTRIBUTION MARGIN PER CLIENT: $2,800/mo

BREAK-EVEN POINT:
$10,000 / $2,800 = 3.6 clients

So you need 4 clients to break even.

After break-even, every new client = $2,800 profit
```

## 💡 PRICING STRATEGY TIPS

### 1. Start Higher Than You Think

**Common Mistake:** Underpricing to win clients

**Better Approach:**
- Price at 20-30% above market average
- Justify with value, not hours
- Focus on ROI, not cost

**Example:**
```
Market Average: $5,000/mo
Your Price: $6,500/mo
Justification: "We typically generate $50K+/month in 
incremental revenue. At $6,500, that's 7.7x ROI."
```

### 2. Always Show ROI

**Before discussing price, show value:**

```
CURRENT STATE:
- Monthly Revenue: $200K
- Cart Abandonment: 72%
- Email Recovery: 8%
- Recovered Revenue: $18K/month

WITH OUR SOLUTION:
- WhatsApp Recovery: +10%
- New Recovery Rate: 18%
- Recovered Revenue: $40.5K/month
- INCREMENTAL REVENUE: $22.5K/month

INVESTMENT:
- Our Fee: $6.5K/month
- ROI: 246%

Question: If we can add $22.5K for $6.5K, 
does the investment make sense?"
```

### 3. Anchor Your Pricing

**Always present 3 options:**

```
BASIC: $4,000/mo
- Broadcast campaigns (25K messages)
- Basic automation
- Monthly reporting

PROFESSIONAL: $6,500/mo ⭐ MOST POPULAR
- Broadcast campaigns (50K messages)
- Advanced automation
- Weekly reporting
- Priority support

ENTERPRISE: $12,000/mo
- Unlimited campaigns
- Full customization
- Dedicated team
- Custom integrations
```

**Psychology:**
- Most people choose the middle option
- Makes $6.5K seem reasonable vs. $12K
- $4K makes $6.5K feel like "good value"

### 4. Never Discount (Initially)

**Instead of discounting:**
- Add more value
- Extend services
- Increase message allowance
- Add bonus features

**If you MUST discount:**
- Get something in return (longer contract, case study, referral)
- Make it temporary (3 months at discount, then full price)
- Never discount without justification

### 5. Raise Prices Every 6-12 Months

**When to raise prices:**
- After 6 months of successful delivery
- When you're at 80% capacity
- When you have a proven track record
- When market rates have increased

**How to announce:**
```
"Starting [date], our monthly retainer will increase 
from $X to $Y. This reflects the additional value we're 
delivering and our updated service offerings. 

Existing clients will receive [transition period] and 
[bonus] as a thank you for your partnership."
```

## 📋 CONTRACT TERMS

### Standard Terms

```
TERM:
- Initial term: 3 months
- Month-to-month after initial term
- 30-day cancellation notice required

PAYMENT:
- Monthly in advance
- Due on 1st of each month
- Late fee: 5% after 7 days

MESSAGE COSTS:
- Included in retainer (tiered)
- Overage: $0.03-0.05/message
- Prepaid overage available

CHANGES:
- Service changes: 30-day notice
- Price changes: 60-day notice
- Contract renewal: 60-day notice

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
- Client owns campaign content
- Agency retains methodology/IP
- Case study rights (if agreed)

CONFIDENTIALITY:
- Both parties keep info confidential
- 2-year term after contract ends
```

### Addendum: Performance Guarantee

```
PERFORMANCE GUARANTEE (Optional)

If we don't deliver at least [X]% of projected results 
in the first 90 days, we will:
- Extend services at no additional cost
- Or: Refund [Y]% of fees

This guarantee applies only if:
- Client provides all requested access/data
- Client approves campaigns within [Z] business days
- Client's product/pricing is competitive
- Market conditions remain stable

Exclusions: Product issues, website downtime, 
payment processor issues, external factors beyond 
our control.
```

## 💰 CASH FLOW MANAGEMENT

### Payment Terms Best Practices

```
✅ Invoice on 1st of month
✅ Payment due on 1st (or within 7 days)
✅ Auto-charge on file for seamless payment
✅ Late fees enforced (not threatened, enforced)
✅ 30-day notice for service pause if unpaid
✅ Prepaid for new clients (first month upfront)
```

### Reserve Fund

**Goal:** 3 months of operating expenses

**Why:**
- Client churn happens
- Seasonal dips occur
- Unexpected expenses arise
- Gives you negotiating power

**Example:**
```
Monthly Operating Costs: $20,000
Reserve Fund Target: $60,000

Build this in Year 1 before expanding
```
