## 📊 Quality Standards

**Response Time:**
- Client emails: Within 24 hours
- Urgent issues: Within 4 hours
- Internal requests: Within 48 hours

**Quality Checks:**
- All deliverables reviewed by QA before delivery
- Client satisfaction score target: 8/10+
- Revision rate target: <20%

- All client communications logged in CRM
- All work documented in project management tool
