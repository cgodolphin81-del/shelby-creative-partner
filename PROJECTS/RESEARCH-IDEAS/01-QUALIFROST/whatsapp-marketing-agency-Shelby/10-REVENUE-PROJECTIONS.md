## 🎯 KEY ASSUMPTIONS

### Market Assumptions

```
WHATSAPP USER BASE:
- Global users: 2.7+ billion (growing 10-15%/year)
- Business adoption: 30-40% of enterprises using WhatsApp
- Message volume growth: 20-30%/year
- Average revenue per user (ARPU): $50-200/year

MARKET TRENDS:
- Email open rates declining (15-20% → 12-18%)
- WhatsApp open rates stable/high (85-98%)
- Privacy regulations driving first-party data focus
- Shift from paid ads to owned channels
- AI/automation adoption increasing
```

### Agency Assumptions

```
SERVICE MIX:
- Broadcast campaigns: 40% of revenue
- Chatbot automation: 30% of revenue
- Customer support: 20% of revenue
- Other services: 10% of revenue

PRICING ASSUMPTIONS:
- Average contract value (ACV): $6,500/mo
- Client lifetime: 18-24 months
- Churn rate: 3-5%/month
- Price increase: 5-8%/year

OPERATING ASSUMPTIONS:
- Gross margin: 60-70%
- Sales cycle: 30-45 days
- Close rate: 25-35%
- CAC payback: 3-6 months
```

## ⚠️ RISKS & MITIGATION

### Business Risks

```
RISK 1: Platform dependency (WhatsApp/Meta)
MITIGATION: Multi-channel strategy, diversify revenue

RISK 2: Regulatory changes
MITIGATION: Compliance-first approach, legal counsel

RISK 3: Competition intensifying
MITIGATION: Brand building, specialization, superior service

RISK 4: Client concentration (too few large clients)
MITIGATION: Diversify client base, cap any single client at 10% of revenue

RISK 5: Economic downturn
MITIGATION: Essential service positioning, flexible pricing, cash reserve

RISK 6: Key person risk
MITIGATION: Document processes, build team, cross-train
```
