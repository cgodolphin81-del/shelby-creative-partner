## Equipment Maintenance Tips

### Regular Maintenance
- Clean lenses after every shoot
- Format cards in-camera (not computer)
- Update firmware quarterly
- Check battery health monthly
- Back up footage immediately

### Storage Best Practices
- 3-2-1 Rule: 3 copies, 2 formats, 1 offsite
- Label everything clearly
- Keep firmware update logs
- Track equipment serial numbers

### Insurance
- Insure equipment over $5,000
- Keep purchase receipts
- Document serial numbers
- Consider rental insurance for gigs
