## Template 6: Webinar Opening Script

```
[PRE-WEBINAR: 5 minutes before start]
Host: "We'll be starting in just 5 minutes. 
Grab your coffee, and don't forget to drop your questions in the chat!"

[START]
Host: "Hello everyone, and welcome to [Webinar Title]!
I'm [Name], your host today."

[HOUSEKEEPING - 2 minutes]
Host: "Quick housekeeping:
• This webinar is being recorded – you'll get the replay
• Drop questions in the chat throughout
• We'll have Q&A at the end
• Resources mentioned will be in your follow-up email"

[INTRODUCE SPEAKER - 2 minutes]
Host: "Our speaker today is [Name]. [Brief bio and credentials].
[Name], thanks for joining us!"

Speaker: "Thanks for having me! Excited to dive into [topic]..."

[TRANSITION TO CONTENT]
Host: "Let's get into it. [Speaker Name], take it away!"
```

## Script Writing Best Practices

### Formatting Tips
- Use [brackets] for visual directions
- Keep voiceover lines conversational
- Time each section (read aloud at normal pace)
- Include transition notes

### Writing Tips
- Hook in first 5 seconds
- One idea per section
- Active voice over passive
- Specific over vague
- Show, don't just tell

### Revision Checklist
- [ ] Hook grabs attention immediately
- [ ] Clear problem/solution structure
- [ ] CTA is specific and actionable
- [ ] Timing matches intended length
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice
- [ ] No jargon without explanation
- [ ] Visual directions are clear
