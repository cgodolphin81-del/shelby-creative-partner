## 1. E-commerce Fashion Brand

### Client Overview

**Company:** TrendyThreads (pseudonym)
**Industry:** Fast Fashion DTC
**Products:** Women's apparel, ages 16-30
**Revenue:** $5M/year at campaign start
**Challenge:** Meta CPA increasing, needed new Gen Z channel

### Campaign Details

**Timeline:** 6 months
**Budget:** $15,000/month ad spend + $7,500/month agency fee
**Objective:** Drive profitable sales to new collection
**Target:** Women 18-28, fashion interests, lookalike audiences

### Strategy

**Phase 1 (Month 1-2): Testing**
- 5 ad sets with different audiences
- 10 creative variations
- $500/day budget
- Focus: Find winning combinations

**Phase 2 (Month 3-4): Optimization**
- Scaled winning ad sets
- Added retargeting campaigns
- Introduced Story ads
- $1,000/day budget

**Phase 3 (Month 5-6): Scaling**
- Full-funnel approach
- AR Lens for virtual try-on
- Influencer partnerships
- $1,500-2,000/day budget

### Creative Approach

**Winning Ad Formats:**
1. **UGC-Style Haul Video** (15 seconds)
   - Customer showing multiple items
   - "Get ready with me" format
   - Authentic, not polished

2. **Before/After Styling** (10 seconds)
   - Plain outfit → Styled with their pieces
   - Text overlay: "Same girl, new fit"
   - Strong CTA: "Shop the look"

3. **Flash Sale Announcement** (6 seconds)
   - Bold text: "48 HOURS ONLY"
   - Discount code prominent
   - Urgency-driven

4. **Product Showcase** (12 seconds)
   - Close-up of fabric/quality
   - Multiple angles
   - Price point shown

### Results

| Metric | Month 1-2 | Month 3-4 | Month 5-6 | Total |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Ad Spend | $30,000 | $60,000 | $90,000 | $180,000 |
| Revenue | $75,000 | $180,000 | $360,000 | $615,000 |
| ROAS | 2.5:1 | 3.0:1 | 4.0:1 | 3.4:1 |
| CPA | $45 | $38 | $32 | $36 |
| Purchases | 1,667 | 4,737 | 11,250 | 17,654 |
| New Customers | 1,420 | 3,890 | 9,180 | 14,490 |

### Key Learnings

✅ **UGC outperformed polished creative 3:1**
✅ **6-10 second ads had highest completion rates**
✅ **Retargeting drove 40% of revenue at 6:1 ROAS**
✅ **AR Lens increased conversion rate by 35%**
✅ **Friday-Sunday had 2x better performance**

### Client Quote

> "We were skeptical about Snapchat after burning money on TikTok, but this team proved the channel works when you know what you're doing. We're now allocating 30% of our total social budget to Snapchat."
> 
> — CMO, TrendyThreads

## 2. Mobile Gaming App

### Client Overview

**Company:** PixelPlay Games
**Industry:** Mobile Gaming
**Product:** Puzzle RPG (free-to-play)
**Challenge:** High user acquisition costs, needed quality users

### Campaign Details

**Timeline:** 12 months
**Budget:** $50,000-200,000/month (scaled)
**Objective:** Drive app installs with strong Day-7 retention
**Target:** Gamers 16-35, puzzle/RPG interests, iOS + Android

### Strategy

**Creative Testing (Month 1-2):**
- 20+ ad variations
- Gameplay footage vs. cinematics
- Different hooks (challenge, reward, story)
- Identified top 3 performers

**Audience Expansion (Month 3-6):**
- Scaled lookalike audiences
- Added interest-based targeting
- Tested different geos (US, UK, CA, AU)
- Implemented dayparting

**Optimization (Month 7-12):**
- Focus on quality users (retention, LTV)
- Retargeting cart-abandoners (started but didn't purchase)
- Seasonal campaigns (Halloween, Christmas, Summer)
- Creator partnerships for authentic content

### Creative Approach

**Top Performing Ads:**

1. **"Can You Beat Level 50?" Challenge**
   - Showed difficult puzzle
   - Text: "Only 2% of players beat this"
   - CTA: "Prove them wrong"

2. **Epic Boss Battle**
   - Cinematic boss fight
   - Text: "Face the ultimate challenge"
   - CTA: "Download now"

3. **Player Testimonial**
   - Real player reaction (screen recording)
   - "I've been playing for 6 months, still addicted"
   - Authentic social proof

4. **Limited Event Promotion**
   - "48-hour exclusive event"
   - Rare rewards shown
   - FOMO-driven

### Results

| Metric | Q1 | Q2 | Q3 | Q4 | Total |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|-----|-------|
| Ad Spend | $150K | $300K | $450K | $600K | $1.5M |
| Installs | 75,000 | 180,000 | 270,000 | 400,000 | 925,000 |
| CPI | $2.00 | $1.67 | $1.67 | $1.50 | $1.62 |
| Day-7 Retention | 28% | 32% | 35% | 38% | 34% |
| Day-30 Retention | 12% | 15% | 18% | 20% | 17% |
| Paying Users | 3,750 | 10,800 | 18,900 | 32,000 | 65,450 |
| Revenue | $225K | $540K | $945K | $1.6M | $3.31M |
| ROAS | 1.5:1 | 1.8:1 | 2.1:1 | 2.7:1 | 2.2:1 |

### Key Learnings

✅ **Challenge-based hooks drove 40% lower CPI**
✅ **Dayparting (4-10 PM) improved retention 15%**
✅ **iOS users had 25% higher LTV than Android**
✅ **Retargeting drove 30% of purchases at 5:1 ROAS**
✅ **Seasonal events spiked installs 3x during campaign**

### Client Quote

> "Snapchat became our #2 user acquisition channel after Google. The quality of users is actually better — higher retention and LTV. We're planning to double our Snapchat budget next year."
> 
> — Head of User Acquisition, PixelPlay Games

## 3. Beauty Brand AR Campaign

### Client Overview

**Company:** GlowCosmetics
**Industry:** Beauty/Cosmetics
**Products:** Lipstick, eyeshadow, skincare
**Challenge:** Customers couldn't try products online, high return rate

### Campaign Details

**Timeline:** 3 months (campaign) + ongoing
**Budget:** $25,000 (Lens production) + $50,000 (promotion)
**Objective:** Increase confidence in online purchases, reduce returns
**Target:** Women 18-35, beauty interests, makeup enthusiasts

### Strategy

**AR Lens Development:**
- Virtual lipstick try-on (12 shades)
- Eyeshadow palette preview
- Skincare "glow up" filter
- Share feature with friends for feedback

**Promotion Strategy:**
- Influencer seeding (25 beauty creators)
- Paid ads driving to Lens
- In-store QR codes
- Social media cross-promotion
- Email campaign to existing customers

**UGC Amplification:**
- Repost best user content
- Weekly "Look of the Week" feature
- Contest: Best look wins $500 gift card
- Hashtag: #GlowTryOn

### Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Lens Plays | 2.5M |
| Unique Users | 1.8M |
| Average Play Time | 45 seconds |
| Shares | 180,000 (10%) |
| Screenshots | 90,000 (5%) |
| Website Clicks | 250,000 |
| attributed Sales | $1.2M |
| ROAS | 16:1 |
| Return Rate Reduction | 35% |

### Viral Moment

One influencer's video using the Lens got 5M views on TikTok, driving 500K additional Lens plays organically.

### Key Learnings

✅ **AR try-on increased conversion rate 45%**
✅ **Return rate dropped 35% (customers more confident)**
✅ **UGC from Lens drove 3x more engagement than brand content**
✅ **Influencer seeding created viral cascade effect**
✅ **Lens had 6-month shelf life (continued organic usage)**

### Client Quote

> "This wasn't just a marketing campaign — it fundamentally changed how customers shop with us. The AR try-on reduced our return rate and increased customer satisfaction. We're now developing AR for all our products."
> 
> — VP of Marketing, GlowCosmetics

## 4. Food Delivery App

### Client Overview

**Company:** QuickBite Delivery
**Industry:** Food Delivery
**Market:** 15 US cities
**Challenge:** Customer acquisition in competitive market

### Campaign Details

**Timeline:** 6 months
**Budget:** $30,000/month
**Objective:** Drive first-time orders with promo code
**Target:** 18-35, foodies, urban areas, delivery app users

### Strategy

**Geo-Targeted Campaigns:**
- City-by-city rollout
- Localized creative (landmarks, local cuisine)
- Weather-triggered ads (rainy days = more orders)
- Meal-time targeting (11 AM-2 PM, 5-9 PM)

**Creative Approach:**
- Mouth-watering food close-ups
- "Hunger level" interactive ads
- First-order discount ($15 off)
- Late-night craving content

**Retargeting:**
- App downloaders who didn't order
- Past customers (win-back)
- High-value customer lookalikes

### Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Total Ad Spend | $180,000 |
| App Installs | 45,000 |
| First-Time Orders | 28,000 |
| Install-to-Order Rate | 62% |
| CPA (First Order) | $6.43 |
| Average Order Value | $32 |
| Revenue (6 months) | $896,000 |
| ROAS | 5:1 |
| 90-Day Retention | 35% |
| LTV (90 days) | $85 |

### Key Learnings

✅ **Weather-triggered ads had 2x higher CTR**
✅ **Meal-time targeting reduced CPA 30%**
✅ **Localized creative outperformed generic 40%**
✅ **Retargeting drove 25% of orders at 8:1 ROAS**
✅ **Video ads showing food prep had highest engagement**

✅ **Tease campaign built anticipation (higher launch CTR)**

## 6. Fitness App

### Client Overview

**Company:** FitTrack Pro
**Industry:** Health & Fitness
**Product:** Workout tracking + coaching app
**Challenge:** Subscriber acquisition in crowded market

### Campaign Details

**Timeline:** 6 months
**Budget:** $20,000/month
**Objective:** Drive paid subscriptions
**Target:** Fitness enthusiasts 18-40, gym-goers, home workout fans

### Strategy

**Content Strategy:**
- Transformation stories (real users)
- Quick workout tips (15-30 seconds)
- Trainer takeovers
- Challenge campaigns (#30DayFitTrack)

**Audience Approach:**
- Interest: Fitness, gyms, health food
- Lookalikes: Current subscribers
- Retargeting: Free trial users
- Exclusion: Current subscribers

**Offer:**
- 7-day free trial
- 50% off first month
- Cancel anytime

### Results

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Total Ad Spend | $120,000 |
| Trial Sign-Ups | 24,000 |
| Trial-to-Paid Rate | 38% |
| Paid Subscribers | 9,120 |
| CPA (Paid Subscriber) | $13.16 |
| Monthly Subscription | $14.99 |
| Average Subscription Length | 5.2 months |
| LTV | $78 |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | 5.9:1 |
| Total Revenue (12 months) | $709,000 |
| ROAS (12 months) | 5.9:1 |

### Key Learnings

✅ **Transformation stories had 3x higher conversion**
✅ **Trainer credibility increased trust and sign-ups**
✅ **Challenge campaign drove viral UGC**
✅ **7-day trial converted better than 30-day (urgency)**
✅ **Retention improved with onboarding email sequence**

## 7. Summary: Industry Benchmarks

### Average Performance by Industry

| Industry | Avg ROAS | Avg CPA | Avg CTR |
|----------|----------|---------|---------|
| E-commerce (Fashion) | 3-5:1 | $30-50 | 1-2% |
| E-commerce (General) | 2-4:1 | $25-60 | 0.8-1.5% |
| Mobile Apps | 1.5-3:1 | $1.50-5 (CPI) | 1-3% |
| Beauty | 3-6:1 | $20-40 | 1.5-2.5% |
| Food/Delivery | 3-5:1 | $5-15 | 1-2% |
| Entertainment | 2-4:1 | $3-8 (trial) | 2-4% |
| Fitness | 3-6:1 | $10-20 | 1-2% |
| Gaming | 1.5-3:1 | $1-4 (CPI) | 1.5-3% |

### Success Factors Across All Campaigns

✅ **Mobile-optimized creative (vertical, sound-on + sound-off)**
✅ **Strong hook in first 3 seconds**
✅ **Clear, compelling CTA**
✅ **Proper tracking and attribution**
✅ **Continuous testing and optimization**
✅ **Audience segmentation and targeting**
✅ **Retargeting campaigns**
✅ **Authentic, platform-native content**

### Common Mistakes That Kill Campaigns

❌ **Using horizontal/Instagram creative**
❌ **Weak hook (slow build)**
❌ **No clear CTA**
❌ **Poor landing page experience**
❌ **Giving up too early (need 2-4 weeks minimum)**
❌ **Not testing enough creative variations**
❌ **Ignoring retargeting**
❌ **Over-segmenting audiences**
