## Cold Outreach Emails

### Template 1: The Genuine Fan Approach
**Subject:** Love your content about [specific topic]!

```
Hi [Name],

I've been following your content for [time period] and absolutely love how you 
[specific thing you admire about their content]. Your recent post about 
[specific post/topic] really resonated with me because [genuine reason].

I'm reaching out from [Company], where we [brief company description - 1 sentence]. 
We're launching [product/campaign] and immediately thought of you because 
[specific reason they're a good fit].

We'd love to explore a partnership that feels authentic to your brand. Are you 
open to discussing potential collaborations?

No pressure at all - either way, keep up the amazing work!

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company]
[Contact Info]
```

## Tips for Using These Templates

### ✅ Do's:
- **Personalize every message** - Reference specific content
- **Keep it conversational** - Avoid overly formal language
- **Be clear about expectations** - Transparency builds trust
- **Follow up appropriately** - 2-3 times max, spaced out
- **Respect their time** - Get to the point quickly

### ❌ Don'ts:
- Don't send generic copy-paste messages
- Don't lowball or undervalue their work
- Don't be pushy or aggressive
- Don't ghost after they respond
- Don't overpromise what you can deliver

### 📊 Response Rate Optimization:
- **Best days to send:** Tuesday-Thursday
- **Best times:** 10am-2pm in their timezone
- **Subject line tips:** Keep it under 50 characters, mention their name
- **DM tips:** Send during their active hours (check when they post)
