## 💡 Key Concepts

### The Micro-Influencer Advantage

Micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) typically outperform macro-influencers because:
- **Higher engagement rates** (3-7% vs. 1-3%)
- **More authentic connections** with their audience
- **Lower cost** per engagement
- **Better targeting** of niche audiences
- **Easier to work with** (no agents, faster response)

**Recommendation:** Start with micro-influencers for your first campaigns.

### The 30-Day Campaign Cycle

```
Week 1: Strategy & Setup
Week 2: Discovery & Outreach
Week 3: Preparation
Week 4: Launch & Analysis
```

Most campaigns should follow this timeline. Adjust based on complexity.

### ROAS Expectations

| Campaign Type | Good ROAS | Great ROAS | Excellent ROAS |
|---------------|-----------|------------|----------------|
| E-commerce | 3:1 | 4:1 | 6:1+ |
| App Downloads | 2:1 | 3:1 | 5:1+ |
| Brand Awareness | N/A | N/A | N/A (measure differently) |
| B2B Leads | 3:1 | 5:1 | 8:1+ |

**Note:** First campaigns often underperform. Expect improvement by campaign 3-4.

### FTC Compliance is Non-Negotiable

All sponsored content MUST include clear disclosure:
- Use #ad or #sponsored (not vague terms like #collab)
- Place disclosure at the BEGINNING of captions
- For video: verbal AND visual disclosure
- For stories: disclosure on EACH frame

**Penalties:** Up to $50,000+ per violation. Not worth the risk.

## 📊 Success Metrics

### What to Track

**Awareness:**
- Reach
- Impressions
- Brand search volume
- Social mentions

**Engagement:**
- Likes, comments, saves, shares
- Engagement rate
- Video completion rate

**Conversion:**
- Clicks
- CTR
- Conversions
- Revenue
- ROAS

### Benchmark Expectations

| Metric | Nano (1K-10K) | Micro (10K-100K) | Mid (100K-500K) |
|--------|---------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Engagement Rate | 5-10% | 3-7% | 2-4% |
| CTR | 2-4% | 1.5-3% | 1-2% |
| Conversion Rate | 8-12% | 6-10% | 4-7% |
| Cost Per Post | $50-500 | $500-5,000 | $5,000-20,000 |
