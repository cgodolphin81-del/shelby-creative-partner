## ROI Calculator

### Simple ROI Formula

```
ROI = (Revenue - Investment) ÷ Investment × 100

Example:
Revenue: $10,000
Investment: $3,000
ROI = ($10,000 - $3,000) ÷ $3,000 × 100 = 233%
```

### ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)

```
ROAS = Revenue ÷ Investment

Example:
Revenue: $10,000
Investment: $3,000
ROAS = $10,000 ÷ $3,000 = 3.33 (or 3.33:1)
```

### Break-Even Calculator

```
Break-Even ROAS = 1 ÷ Profit Margin

Example (40% margin):
Break-Even ROAS = 1 ÷ 0.40 = 2.5

You need 2.5:1 ROAS to break even.
Anything above 2.5:1 is profit.
```
