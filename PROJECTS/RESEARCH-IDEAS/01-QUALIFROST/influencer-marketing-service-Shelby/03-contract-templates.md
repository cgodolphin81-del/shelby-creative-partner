## Simple Influencer Agreement

*Best for: One-off posts, micro-influencers, quick campaigns*

```
INFLUENCER PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] by and between:

BRAND: [Company Name], located at [Address] ("Brand")
INFLUENCER: [Name], located at [Address], @handle: [@username] ("Influencer")

1. SCOPE OF WORK
Influencer agrees to create and publish the following content:
☐ Instagram Post: [Number] posts
☐ Instagram Stories: [Number] frames, live for 24 hours
☐ TikTok/Reel: [Number] videos, [Length] seconds each
☐ YouTube: [Number] videos, [Length] minutes each
☐ Blog Post: [Number] posts, [Word count] words minimum
☐ Other: [Specify]

2. DELIVERABLES
- Content must be posted by: [Date/Time]
- Content must remain live for minimum: [Time period, e.g., 12 months]
- Content must include: [Hashtags, tags, links, discount codes]
- Draft approval required: ☐ Yes ☐ No (If yes, approval within [X] days)

3. COMPENSATION
Total Compensation: $[Amount]
Payment Terms: 
☐ 50% upfront, 50% on completion
☐ 100% on completion
☐ Net 30 days after posting
Payment Method: [Bank transfer, PayPal, etc.]

4. PRODUCTS
Brand will provide: [Product description, value]
Shipping: ☐ Brand pays ☐ Influencer pays
Influencer ☐ may keep products ☐ must return products

5. DISCLOSURE
Influencer must clearly disclose partnership per FTC guidelines:
- Use #ad, #sponsored, or "Paid partnership with [Brand]"
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous

6. RIGHTS
Brand receives: [Specify usage rights, e.g., "90 days organic social media use"]
Influencer retains: Ownership of content, right to display in portfolio

7. CANCELLATION
If Brand cancels after work begins: Influencer keeps [X]% of fee
If Influencer cancels: Must refund any upfront payment

8. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR
Influencer is an independent contractor, not an employee.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties agree to the terms above:

Brand: _________________________ Date: ___________
        [Name, Title]

Influencer: ____________________ Date: ___________
        [Name]
```

## Comprehensive Influencer Contract

*Best for: Larger campaigns, macro-influencers, long-term partnerships*

```
INFLUENCER MARKETING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Influencer Marketing Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of 
[Date] ("Effective Date") by and between:

[COMPANY NAME], a [State] [Entity Type], with principal offices at [Address] 
("Company")

and

[INFLUENCER NAME], an individual with address at [Address], 
social media handle: [@username] ("Creator")

Collectively referred to as the "Parties."

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Company desires to engage Creator to provide influencer marketing services;
WHEREAS, Creator represents that they have the ability to create and distribute 
content on social media platforms;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1: SERVICES

1.1 Scope of Services
Creator shall provide the following services ("Services"):

a) Content Creation:
   - Platform: [Instagram/TikTok/YouTube/etc.]
   - Content Type: [Posts/Stories/Reels/Videos/etc.]
   - Quantity: [Number] pieces of content
   - Specifications: [Detailed requirements]

b) Posting Schedule:
   - First post: [Date]
   - Additional posts: [Dates or frequency]
   - Content must remain live for: [Time period]

c) Engagement:
   - Respond to comments for: [Time period after posting]
   - Share to Stories: [Yes/No, how many times]

1.2 Content Approval
a) Company shall have [X] business days to review and approve content
b) Creator shall incorporate reasonable feedback
c) [X] rounds of revisions included
d) Additional revisions: $[X] per round

1.3 Performance Standards
Creator agrees to:
a) Maintain professional quality standards
b) Not delete content during campaign period without approval
c) Not edit content significantly after posting
d) Comply with all platform terms of service

ARTICLE 2: COMPENSATION

2.1 Fees
Company shall pay Creator:
- Base Fee: $[Amount]
- Performance Bonus: $[Amount] if [metric] achieved
- Additional Compensation: [Specify]

2.2 Payment Schedule
a) [X]% upon signing: $[Amount]
b) [X]% upon content approval: $[Amount]
c) [X]% upon posting: $[Amount]
d) Final payment within [X] days of completion

2.3 Expenses
Company will reimburse pre-approved expenses up to $[Amount].
Receipts required for all reimbursements.

2.4 Taxes
Creator is responsible for all taxes. Company will issue 1099 if required.

ARTICLE 3: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 Ownership
Creator retains ownership of all content created ("Content").

3.2 License Grant
Creator grants Company the following license:
- Scope: [Organic social media / Paid advertising / All media]
- Platforms: [Specify platforms]
- Territory: [Worldwide / Specific regions]
- Duration: [X months/years or Perpetual]
- Exclusivity: [Exclusive / Non-exclusive]

3.3 Moral Rights
Creator waives moral rights to the extent permitted by law.

3.4 Portfolio Use
Creator may display Content in their portfolio and marketing materials.

ARTICLE 4: DISCLOSURES & COMPLIANCE

4.1 FTC Compliance
Creator shall comply with FTC Endorsement Guidelines, including:
- Clear and conspicuous disclosure of material connection
- Use of #ad, #sponsored, or platform disclosure tools
- Disclosure in video content (verbal and visual)

4.2 Platform Compliance
Creator shall comply with all applicable platform policies.

4.3 Legal Compliance
Creator shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

ARTICLE 5: REPRESENTATIONS & WARRANTIES

5.1 Creator Representations
Creator represents and warrants that:
a) They have full right and authority to enter this Agreement
b) Content will be original and not infringe third-party rights
c) They will comply with all applicable laws
d) Follower count and engagement metrics are accurate
e) No fake followers or engagement will be used

5.2 Company Representations
Company represents and warrants that:
a) They have full right and authority to enter this Agreement
b) Products provided are safe and as described
c) They will make payments as agreed

ARTICLE 6: CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Confidential Information
Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public information received.

6.2 Exceptions
Confidentiality does not apply to information that:
a) Is publicly known
b) Was known prior to disclosure
c) Is independently developed
d) Is required to be disclosed by law

6.3 Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive for [X] years after Agreement ends.

ARTICLE 7: TERMINATION

7.1 Termination for Convenience
Company may terminate with [X] days written notice.
Creator shall be paid for work completed through termination date.

7.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately for material breach.

7.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination:
a) Company pays for completed work
b) Creator delivers all work in progress
c) License terms remain in effect for posted content

ARTICLE 8: INDEMNIFICATION

8.1 Creator Indemnification
Creator shall indemnify Company against claims arising from:
a) Creator's negligence or misconduct
b) Content infringing third-party rights
c) Creator's breach of this Agreement

8.2 Company Indemnification
Company shall indemnify Creator against claims arising from:
a) Product defects or injuries
b) Company's negligence or misconduct
c) Company's breach of this Agreement

ARTICLE 9: LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
Total liability shall not exceed the total compensation paid under this Agreement.

ARTICLE 10: GENERAL PROVISIONS

10.1 Independent Contractor
Creator is an independent contractor, not an employee.

10.2 Assignment
Neither party may assign this Agreement without written consent.

10.3 Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [State].

10.4 Dispute Resolution
Disputes shall be resolved through [Mediation/Arbitration/Court] in [Location].

10.5 Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

10.6 Amendments
Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

10.7 Severability
If any provision is invalid, the remainder shall remain in effect.

10.8 Waiver
Failure to enforce any provision is not a waiver.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the Effective Date.

COMPANY:
_____________________________
[Name]
[Title]
[Company Name]

CREATOR:
_____________________________
[Name]
[Handle/Channel]

Date: _______________
```

## Exclusivity Agreement

*Use when you need the influencer to not work with competitors*

```
EXCLUSIVITY AGREEMENT

This Exclusivity Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] by and between:

[COMPANY NAME] ("Company")
[INFLUENCER NAME] (@handle) ("Creator")

1. EXCLUSIVITY SCOPE

1.1 Category Exclusivity
During the Term, Creator agrees not to partner with, promote, or endorse any 
brand in the following category/categories:

[Specify categories, e.g., "skincare brands," "fitness supplements," "fast food"]

1.2 Specific Competitors
Creator specifically agrees not to work with:
- [Competitor 1]
- [Competitor 2]
- [Competitor 3]
- [List all relevant competitors]

1.3 Platform Scope
This exclusivity applies to:
☐ All social media platforms
☐ Specific platforms: [List]

2. TERM

Exclusivity Period: [Start Date] to [End Date]
Total Duration: [X months/years]

3. GEOGRAPHIC SCOPE

☐ Worldwide
☐ [Specific countries/regions]

4. EXCLUSIVITY FEE

In consideration for this exclusivity, Company shall pay:
☐ Included in base compensation
☐ Additional fee: $[Amount]
☐ [X]% premium on all content fees during exclusivity period

Payment Schedule: [Specify]

5. PERMITTED ACTIVITIES

Creator MAY still:
a) Work with non-competing brands
b) Post organic content about products they genuinely use
c) Appear in media not related to excluded categories
d) [Other permitted activities]

6. PRE-EXISTING PARTNERSHIPS

Creator discloses the following existing partnerships that may conflict:
[List any existing partnerships]

These partnerships are:
☐ Permitted to continue
☐ Must be terminated by [Date]
☐ Must not be renewed

7. VIOLATIONS

If Creator violates this exclusivity:
a) Company may terminate main agreement immediately
b) Creator shall refund [X]% of exclusivity fee
c) Creator shall pay damages of $[Amount] OR [X]x the value of conflicting deal
d) Company may seek injunctive relief

8. MORAL CLAUSE

Company may terminate exclusivity (and main agreement) if Creator:
a) Engages in conduct that brings Company into disrepute
b) Is convicted of a crime
c) Makes statements contrary to Company values
d) [Other specific triggers]

9. END OF EXCLUSIVITY

Upon expiration:
a) Creator is free to work with any brand
b) Company has no further exclusivity obligations
c) Existing content usage rights remain in effect

10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

[Governing law, dispute resolution, etc. - reference main agreement]

AGREED AND ACCEPTED:

Company: _____________________ Date: ___________

Creator: _____________________ Date: ___________
```

## NDA for Influencer Partnerships

*Protect confidential information shared with influencers*

```
NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT
(Influencer Partnership)

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("NDA") is entered into on [Date] by:

[COMPANY NAME] ("Disclosing Party")
[INFLUENCER NAME] ("Receiving Party")

1. PURPOSE

This NDA protects confidential information shared in connection with a 
potential or existing influencer partnership.

2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" includes:
a) Product information (upcoming launches, formulations, features)
b) Marketing strategies and campaigns
c) Sales data and metrics
d) Customer information
e) Business plans and financial information
f) Creative concepts and campaign ideas
g) Contract terms and compensation
h) Any information marked "Confidential"

3. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does not include information that:
a) Is or becomes publicly known (not through Receiving Party's breach)
b) Was known to Receiving Party before disclosure
c) Is independently developed by Receiving Party
d) Is received from a third party without restriction
e) Is required to be disclosed by law

4. OBLIGATIONS

Receiving Party agrees to:
a) Keep Confidential Information strictly confidential
b) Use Confidential Information only for the Partnership Purpose
c) Not disclose to any third party without written consent
d) Protect Confidential Information with reasonable care
e) Limit access to those who need to know

5. SOCIAL MEDIA RESTRICTIONS

Receiving Party shall NOT post about:
a) Unreleased products
b) Upcoming campaigns before launch date
c) Confidential business information
d) Contract terms or compensation
e) Behind-the-scenes content without approval

6. DURATION

Confidentiality obligations remain in effect for:
☐ [X] years from disclosure date
☐ Indefinitely for trade secrets
☐ Until information becomes public

7. PRE-EXISTING CONTENT

If Receiving Party has existing content featuring Disclosing Party's products:
[List any pre-existing content]

This content is:
☐ Permitted to remain posted
☐ Must be removed by [Date]
☐ Must be modified per attached exhibit

8. RETURN OF MATERIALS

Upon request or termination, Receiving Party shall:
a) Return all physical materials
b) Delete all digital files
c) Confirm destruction in writing

9. REMEDIES

Breach of this NDA may cause irreparable harm. Disclosing Party may seek:
a) Injunctive relief
b) Monetary damages
c) Any other available remedies

10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

10.1 Governing Law
[State] law governs this Agreement.

10.2 No License
No intellectual property license is granted.

10.3 Entire Agreement
This constitutes the entire agreement.

10.4 Amendments
Amendments must be in writing.

AGREED AND ACCEPTED:

Disclosing Party: _____________ Date: ___________
        [Name, Title]

Receiving Party: ______________ Date: ___________
        [Name, Handle]
```

## Work-for-Hire Agreement

*When you need full ownership of created content*

```
WORK FOR HIRE AGREEMENT

This Work for Hire Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] by:

[COMPANY NAME] ("Company")
[CREATOR NAME] ("Contractor")

1. ENGAGEMENT

Company engages Contractor to create the following content ("Work"):
- Description: [Detailed description]
- Specifications: [Technical requirements]
- Quantity: [Number of pieces]
- Delivery Date: [Date]

2. WORK FOR HIRE

2.1 Ownership
The Work is a "work made for hire" as defined by 17 U.S.C. § 101.

2.2 Company Ownership
Company shall be the sole and exclusive owner of all rights in the Work, 
including copyright and all intellectual property rights.

2.3 Assignment
If the Work does not qualify as work for hire, Contractor hereby assigns 
all rights to Company.

2.4 Moral Rights
Contractor waives all moral rights to the Work to the fullest extent 
permitted by law.

3. COMPENSATION

3.1 Fee
Total Fee: $[Amount]

3.2 Payment Terms
☐ 50% on signing, 50% on delivery
☐ 100% on delivery and acceptance
☐ Net 30 days after acceptance

3.3 Buyout
This fee includes full buyout of all rights in perpetuity.

4. DELIVERABLES

4.1 Format
Work shall be delivered in:
- File formats: [Specify]
- Resolution: [Specify]
- Platform specifications: [Specify]

4.2 Acceptance
Company has [X] days to review and accept or reject Work.
Rejections must include specific reasons.
Contractor has [X] days to revise rejected Work.

5. CONTRACTOR REPRESENTATIONS

Contractor represents and warrants that:
a) Work will be original and created by Contractor
```

- [ ] Listen to creator concerns
- [ ] Document all agreed changes

- [ ] Get signed copies before work begins

### ✅ After Signing:

- [ ] Share relevant sections with team
- [ ] Set calendar reminders for key dates
- [ ] Track deliverables against contract
- [ ] Keep communication records
