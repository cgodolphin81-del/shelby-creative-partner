## Manual Search Strategies

### Strategy 1: Competitor Analysis
1. Identify 5-10 competitor brands
2. Check their tagged posts on Instagram
3. Note which creators post about them repeatedly
4. Review their "Sponsored" content tags
5. Build a list of potential partners

### Strategy 2: Hashtag Deep Dive
1. Find 10-15 niche-specific hashtags
2. Sort by "Recent" not "Top" (less saturated)
3. Save posts from creators with 5K-100K followers
4. Check their engagement quality (comments, not just likes)
5. Review their content consistency

### Strategy 3: Comment Section Mining
1. Find popular posts in your niche
2. Scroll through comments
3. Identify engaged community members
4. Check if they have their own following
5. These are often micro-influencers with loyal audiences

### Strategy 4: Google Alerts
Set up alerts for:
- "[Your niche] + influencer"
- "[Your niche] + brand deal"
- "[Your niche] + sponsored"
- "[Competitor] + partnership"

### Strategy 5: Podcast & YouTube Guest Appearances
1. Search podcasts in your niche
2. Note guest names
3. Check their social following
4. Many podcast guests are influencers seeking partnerships

### Strategy 6: Event & Conference Speakers
1. Search industry conferences
2. Review speaker lists
3. Check their social presence
4. Speakers often have established authority and following

## Vetting Criteria

### Follower Count Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Avg. Engagement | Best For |
|------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | 5-10% | Local, niche, authentic |
| Micro | 10K-100K | 3-7% | High engagement, affordable |
| Mid | 100K-500K | 2-4% | Broader reach, professional |
| Macro | 500K-1M | 1-3% | Mass awareness |
| Mega | 1M+ | <2% | Brand awareness, prestige |

### Engagement Rate Calculation
```
Engagement Rate = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Followers × 100

Industry Benchmarks:
- Instagram: 1-3% (good), 3-6% (great), 6%+ (excellent)
- TikTok: 3-6% (good), 6-12% (great), 12%+ (excellent)
- YouTube: 2-4% (good), 4-8% (great)
- Twitter: 0.5-1% (good), 1-2% (great)
```

### Audience Quality Checklist

- [ ] Follower growth is steady (not sudden spikes)
- [ ] Comments are genuine (not bot-like)
- [ ] Audience demographics match your target
- [ ] Location data aligns with your market
- [ ] Engagement comes from real accounts
- [ ] No suspicious activity patterns

### Content Quality Assessment

- [ ] Consistent posting schedule
- [ ] High-quality visuals/audio
- [ ] Authentic voice and style
- [ ] Brand-safe content history
- [ ] Aligns with your brand values
- [ ] Professional communication

## Red Flags to Avoid

### 🚩 Engagement Red Flags
- Sudden follower spikes (possible bot purchase)
- Generic comments ("Great post!", "🔥🔥🔥")
- Engagement rate <1% for micro-influencers
- Likes without comments (possible bot likes)
- Followers from unrelated countries

### 🚩 Content Red Flags
- Inconsistent posting (disappears for weeks)
- Controversial or offensive past content
- Too many sponsored posts (>50% of feed)
- Poor quality content
- Copying other creators' content

### 🚩 Professional Red Flags
- Slow or unprofessional communication
- No media kit available
- Unwilling to share analytics
- History of not delivering on partnerships
- Negative reviews from other brands

### 🚩 Audience Red Flags
- Majority of followers are bots
- Audience doesn't match claimed niche
- High follower count, low story views
- Comments in multiple unrelated languages

## Organization & Tracking

### Influencer Database Template

Create a spreadsheet with these columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Name/Handle | Influencer name and @handle |
| Platform | Primary platform (IG, TikTok, YT, etc.) |
| Followers | Current follower count |
| Engagement Rate | Calculated ER% |
| Niche | Their content category |
| Location | Country/region |
| Contact Info | Email, DM, manager |
| Rate | Their typical rate (if known) |
| Status | Prospect, Contacted, Negotiating, Active, Past |
| Notes | Personal notes, observations |
| Last Contact | Date of last communication |
| Campaign History | Previous campaigns together |

**Paid Options:**

### Tagging System

Organize influencers with tags:
- `#nano` `#micro` `#mid` `#macro`
- `#instagram` `#tiktok` `#youtube`
- `#lifestyle` `#fitness` `#beauty` `#tech`
- `#usa` `#uk` `#canada`
- `#hot-lead` `#warm` `#cold`
- `#vip` `#repeat-partner`

### Regular Maintenance

- **Weekly**: Add 10-20 new prospects
- **Monthly**: Update follower counts and engagement rates
- **Quarterly**: Remove inactive influencers
- **After campaigns**: Update performance data and relationship status

## Quick Start: 7-Day Discovery Sprint

### Day 1: Define Your Ideal Influencer
- Create your influencer persona
- List must-have criteria
- List nice-to-have criteria
- Set budget parameters

### Day 2-3: Platform Deep Dive
- Spend 2 hours on Instagram discovery
- Spend 2 hours on TikTok discovery
- Save 50 potential influencers

### Day 4: Tool-Assisted Search
- Use 1-2 discovery tools
- Export lists
- Cross-reference with manual finds

### Day 5: Vetting & Scoring
- Score each influencer 1-10
- Eliminate bottom 50%
- Deep-dive on top 25

### Day 6: Contact Info Gathering
- Find emails in bios
- Check Linktree/contact pages
- Use tools like Hunter.io for email discovery

### Day 7: Organization & Prioritization
- Build your master database
- Tag and categorize
- Prioritize top 10 for outreach
