## COLLECTIONS PROCESS

### Day 1 (Invoice Sent)
- Send invoice via email
- Invoice auto-added to client portal

### Day 3 (Reminder)
- Automated payment reminder email
- "Your invoice is due in 4 days"

### Day 7 (Due Date)
- "Payment due today" email
- Check if auto-charge succeeded (if applicable)

### Day 8 (Late)
- Personal email from account manager
- Apply late fee if policy enforced
- "Payment is now 1 day late"

### Day 15 (2 Weeks Late)
- Phone call to client
- Warning about service suspension
- Formal collections letter

### Day 30 (1 Month Late)
- Services suspended
- Final notice before collections
- Consider external collections
