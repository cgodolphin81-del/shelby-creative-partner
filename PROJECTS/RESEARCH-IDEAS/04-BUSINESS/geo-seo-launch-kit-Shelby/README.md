## BEST PRACTICES

### Cold Email
- Personalize the first line (reference something specific)
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm recipient timezone
- Follow up 3-4 times (use the follow-up sequence)
- Track open rates and adjust subject lines

### LinkedIn
- Don't pitch in the connection request
- Engage with their content first when possible
- Keep DMs under 200 characters when possible
- Use voice notes for higher response rates

### Discovery Calls
- Listen 70%, talk 30%
- Ask about pain before presenting solution
- Book the follow-up before ending the call
- Send recap email within 1 hour

### Proposals
- Send within 24 hours of discovery call
- Customize the executive summary
- Include their logo if possible
- Follow up within 48 hours
