FAQ

WHAT IS GEO SEO?
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your online presence to be cited and recommended by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.

HOW IS THIS DIFFERENT FROM TRADITIONAL SEO?
Traditional SEO optimizes for Google search results. GEO optimizes for AI assistant recommendations. You need both — they complement each other.

HOW QUICKLY WILL I SEE RESULTS?
Most clients see initial visibility improvements within 2-3 weeks. Significant results (65%+ visibility) typically achieved within 60 days.

WHAT IF AI SYSTEMS CHANGE THEIR ALGORITHMS?
We continuously monitor AI system behaviors and adjust strategies accordingly. Your monthly service includes algorithm update response.

DO YOU GUARANTEE RESULTS?
We guarantee effort and methodology. Specific visibility outcomes depend on competition, industry, and starting position. Our Accelerator and Enterprise tiers include visibility targets.

WHAT DO YOU NEED FROM US?
Access to your website CMS, analytics, and key business information. We handle the rest. Most clients spend 2-3 hours total during onboarding.

CAN I CANCEL ANYTIME?
After the 3-month minimum, you can cancel with 30 days' notice. Most clients stay 6-12 months to maintain and grow their AI visibility.

DO YOU WORK WITH COMPETITORS?
We work with one client per industry per geographic market to avoid conflicts. If we're already working with a competitor in your space, we'll let you know upfront.
