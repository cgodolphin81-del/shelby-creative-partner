## DELIVERABLE OVERVIEWS

### 1. GEO Audit Template
**Format:** Google Doc (export as PDF)  
**Length:** 20-30 pages  
**Time to Complete:** 2-3 hours per client

**Sections:**
- Executive Summary (GEO Score + top findings)
- AI Visibility Analysis (7 platforms tested)
- Technical Foundation (robots.txt, llms.txt, speed)
- Content & E-E-A-T Assessment
- Structured Data Audit
- Citations & Authority Analysis
- Competitive Benchmarking
- Recommendations (prioritized)
- Implementation Roadmap

**When to Use:**
- New client onboarding
- Quarterly deep-dive audits
- Prospect audit (sales tool)

---

### 2. GEO Score Tracker
**Format:** Google Sheets  
**Components:**
- Main tracker (scores over time)
- Formula reference document
- Chart templates

**Metrics Tracked:**
- GEO Score (0-100, weighted)
- 6 Component Scores
- AI Visibility %
- Total Citations
- AI Leads

**Automation:**
- Weekly email reports (optional script)
- Auto-calculated trends
- Conditional formatting

**When to Use:**
- Weekly score updates
- Monthly report data source
- Client dashboard backend

---

### 3. Onboarding Form
**Format:** Tally or Typeform  
**Questions:** 20 questions across 5 sections
**Time to Complete:** 15 minutes

**Sections:**
1. Business Basics (5 questions)
2. Current Visibility (5 questions)
3. Competitors (3 questions)
4. Goals & Expectations (4 questions)
5. Technical & Access (3 questions)

**Automation:**
- Email notifications on submit
- Webhook to CRM (optional)
- Thank you page with calendar link

**When to Use:**
- After contract signed
- Before kickoff call

---

### 4. Looker Studio Dashboard
**Format:** Looker Studio (Google Data Studio)  
**Pages:** 3 (Overview, Details, Competitive)  
**Data Source:** Google Sheets tracker

**Visualizations:**
- Score cards (current metrics)
- GEO Score trend (line chart)
- Component scores (bar/radar)
- Citation growth (area chart)
- Leads over time (column chart)
- Competitive comparison

**Sharing:**
- Client: View only
- Your team: Edit access
- Email delivery: Weekly PDF

**When to Use:**
- Client self-serve dashboard
- Monthly review calls
- Progress transparency

---

### 5. Notion Client Portal
**Format:** Notion workspace  
**Pages:** 5 (Home, Audit, Reports, Tasks, Communication)

**Features:**
- Quick links to all resources
- Current metrics (auto-updated)
- Task board (database)
- Meeting notes log
- Resource library
- Communication history

**Databases:**
- Tasks (with status, priority, due date)
- Meetings (with recordings, notes)
- Leads (with source, value, status)

**When to Use:**
- Central client hub
- Project tracking
- Asynchronous communication

### 6. Monthly Report PDF
**Format:** Google Doc → PDF  
**Length:** 8-12 pages  
**Delivery:** Email + Notion portal

**Sections:**
1. Cover (branded)

3. GEO Score Trends (charts)
4. AI Visibility Analysis (platform breakdown)
5. Citation Growth (new citations)
6. Content Optimization (pages done)
7. Competitive Analysis (rankings)
8. Leads & ROI (revenue impact)
9. Work Completed (deliverables)
10. Recommendations & Next Steps

**Design:**
- Branded colors and logo
- Charts and visualizations
- Professional formatting

**When to Use:**
- End of each month
- Quarterly business reviews
- Prospect social proof

### 7. DocuSign Contract
**Format:** DocuSign template  
**Length:** 8 pages + 2 exhibits  
**Review:** ⚠️ Have legal counsel review

**Sections:**
1. Services (scope, package)
2. Term & Termination
3. Compensation (fees, payment)
4. Client Responsibilities
5. Performance & Expectations
6. Confidentiality
7. Intellectual Property
8. Disclaimers & Limitations
9. General Provisions
10. Data & Privacy

**Exhibits:**
- A: Statement of Work (deliverables)
- B: Pricing (fees, schedule)

**When to Use:**
- New client agreements
- Contract renewals
- Package upgrades

### 8. Invoice Template
**Format:** Stripe / QuickBooks / Google Doc  
**Terms:** Net-7  
**Late Fees:** 1.5% per month

**Sections:**
- From/Bill To
- Services table
- Payment information
- Terms & conditions
- Invoice notes (what's included)

**Payment Methods:**
- Credit card (Stripe link)
- ACH/Bank transfer
- Wire transfer
- Check

**Collections Process:**
- Day 1: Invoice sent
- Day 3: Automated reminder
- Day 7: Due today notice
- Day 8: Personal outreach
- Day 15: Suspension warning
- Day 30: Services suspended

**When to Use:**
- Monthly billing
- Setup fees
- Additional services

- [ ] Create Google Sheets tracker (copy formulas)
- [ ] Set up Looker Studio dashboard template
- [ ] Create Notion portal template
