## DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### Document Settings
- **Page Size:** US Letter (8.5" × 11")
- **Margins:** 0.75" all sides
- **Font:** Inter, Roboto, or brand font
- **Font Sizes:**
  - Title: 24pt
  - Headers: 16pt
  - Subheaders: 12pt bold
  - Body: 11pt

### Colors
- **Primary:** Your brand color
- **Secondary:** Complementary color
- **Success:** #22C55E (Green)
- **Warning:** #F59E0B (Orange)
- **Error:** #EF4444 (Red)
- **Text:** #1F2937 (Dark Gray)
- **Borders:** #E5E7EB (Light Gray)

### Branding
- **Logo:** Top of cover page + footer
- **Footer:** "© [Year] [Your Company] | Confidential"
- **Page Numbers:** Bottom right

## EXPORT SETTINGS (Google Docs → PDF)

1. File → Download → PDF
2. Check: "Document" (not "Comments")
3. Check: "Embed fonts"
4. Click "Download"
5. Rename: `[Client]-GEO-Report-[Month-Year].pdf`
