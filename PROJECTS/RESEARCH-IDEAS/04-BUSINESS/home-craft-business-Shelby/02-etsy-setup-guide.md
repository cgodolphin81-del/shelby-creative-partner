## Before You Start

### What You'll Need

- **Email address** (dedicated business email recommended)
- **Bank account** (for deposits)
- **Credit/debit card** (for Etsy fees)
- **Government ID** (for verification)
- **Tax information** (SSN or EIN in the US)
- **Product photos** (minimum 1 per listing, up to 10)
- **Shop name** (have 3-5 options ready)

### Etsy Fees (2026)

| Fee Type | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Listing fee | $0.20 per item (4-month duration) |
| Transaction fee | 6.5% of sale price (including shipping) |
| Payment processing | 3% + $0.25 per transaction (US) |
| Offsite ads | 15% (only if sale comes from their ads) |
| Etsy Plus (optional) | $10/month |

**Example**: Sell a $30 item with $5 shipping
- Listing fee: $0.20
- Transaction fee: $2.28 (6.5% of $35)
- Payment processing: $1.25 (3% of $35 + $0.25)
- **Total fees: $3.73** (about 10.7% of total)

## Creating Your Shop

### Step 1: Go to Etsy.com/sell

1. Click "Get Started" or "Sign In"
2. Choose "Register" if you don't have an account
3. Verify your email address

### Step 2: Shop Preferences

**Shop Language**: Your primary language (affects search)
**Shop Country**: Where you're located
**Shop Currency**: What customers will see (should match your bank)

### Step 3: Choose Your Shop Name

**Tips for a Great Shop Name:**
- ✅ Keep it memorable and easy to spell
- ✅ Reflect your craft or brand personality
- ✅ Check availability on social media too
- ✅ Avoid numbers and hyphens if possible
- ✅ Keep it under 20 characters
- ❌ Don't use "Etsy" in your name (against TOS)
- ❌ Avoid overly narrow names (limits growth)

**Name Ideas by Category:**
- Candles: "Wick & Willow," "Lumina Candle Co," "The Candle Nook"
- Jewelry: "Stone & Strand," "Adorn Studio," "Wildflower Jewelry"
- Prints: "Paper Petal Press," "Ink & Ivy," "The Print Atelier"

**Pro Tip**: Search Etsy and Google to ensure it's unique. Check domain availability if you plan a website later.

### Step 4: Stock Your Shop

You need at least **1 listing** to open, but aim for **10-15** to look established.

### Step 5: Set Up Billing

- Enter credit/debit card for fees
- Enter bank account for deposits
- Etsy deposits weekly (every Monday for prior week's sales)

## Shop Branding

### Shop Icon (Logo)

- **Size**: 500x500 pixels minimum
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Tips**: Simple, recognizable at small size, reflects your brand

### Shop Banner

- **Size**: 3360x840 pixels (large banner)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Tips**: Show your products, include shop name, keep text minimal

### Shop Announcement

First thing buyers see. Keep it brief and engaging:

```
✨ Welcome to [Shop Name]! ✨

Handmade [your products] made with love in [location]. 
Free shipping on orders over $50! 

New collections drop every month — follow us on Instagram 
@[handle] for sneak peeks!

Questions? Message me anytime. I respond within 24 hours. 💕
```

### About Section

Tell your story! This builds trust:

```
## Our Story

Hi, I'm [Name]! I started [Shop Name] in [year] after [your origin story].

Every piece is handmade in my [studio/kitchen/garage] in [location]. 
I use [materials/methods] to create [what makes you special].

When I'm not crafting, you'll find me [personal touch].

Thank you for supporting small business! 💕
```

### Shop Members (Optional)

Add team members if you have help. Shows professionalism.

## Creating Listings

### Listing Anatomy

Each listing includes:

1. **Photos** (up to 10)
2. **Title** (140 characters max)
3. **Category** (be specific)
4. **Description** (detailed info)
5. **Price**
6. **Quantity**
7. **SKU** (optional, for inventory tracking)
8. **Variations** (size, color, etc.)
9. **Shipping** (profiles)
10. **Tags** (13 tags max)

### Photos

- **First photo**: Clean, well-lit product shot (this is your thumbnail)
- **Photos 2-5**: Different angles, details, in-use shots
- **Photos 6-10**: Lifestyle, packaging, size comparison, process

**Minimum**: 1 photo per listing
**Recommended**: 5-10 photos per listing

### Titles

**Formula**: [Main Keyword] + [Secondary Keyword] + [Attributes] + [Use/Occasion]

**Bad**: "Pretty Candle"
**Good**: "Soy Candle Lavender Vanilla Scented Candle Natural Aromatherapy Candle Gift for Her Self Care Candle Home Decor"

**Tips:**
- Front-load important keywords
- Use all 140 characters
- Include synonyms buyers might search
- Don't keyword stuff unnaturally

### Descriptions

**Structure:**

```
## What You'll Receive
[Exact item, size, quantity]

## Details
- Material: [what it's made of]
- Size: [dimensions]
- Color: [available options]
- Care: [how to maintain]

## Processing Time
[How long to make/ship]

## Packaging
[How it will arrive]

## Perfect For
[Gift occasions, uses]

## Questions?
Message me anytime! I'm here to help.
```

**Tips:**
- Write for humans, not just SEO
- Answer questions before they're asked
- Include dimensions in multiple formats (inches and cm)
- Mention if it's handmade, vintage, or craft supplies

## SEO Optimization

Etsy SEO helps buyers find you. Here's how to optimize:

### Keywords

**Research Tools:**
- Etsy search bar (autocomplete suggestions)
- "Related searches" at bottom of search results
- eRank (free tier available)
- Marmalead (paid, but powerful)
- Look at competitor listings

**Keyword Types:**
- **Short-tail**: "candle" (broad, competitive)
- **Long-tail**: "lavender soy candle for relaxation" (specific, less competitive)
- **Buyer intent**: "gift for mom," "wedding favor"

### Tags (13 Total)

Use all 13 tags! Each can be up to 20 characters.

**Tag Strategy:**
- 3-4: Product type (soy candle, jar candle)
- 3-4: Attributes (lavender scented, natural candle)
- 3-4: Use/occasion (gift for her, self care, home decor)
- 2-3: Style/aesthetic (minimalist, farmhouse, boho)

**Multi-word tags count as one tag:**
- ✅ "gift for mom" (uses 1 tag, 3 words)
- ❌ "gift" "for" "mom" (uses 3 tags)

### Categories

Be as specific as possible:
- ❌ Home & Living → Candle
- ✅ Home & Living → Home Decor → Candles & Holders → Container Candles

### Attributes

Fill out ALL available attributes:
- Color
- Size
- Material
- Style
- Occasion
- Holiday
- Recipient

These act as bonus tags!

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Formula

```
Materials + Labor + Overhead + Fees + Profit = Price
```

**Example: Soy Candle**
- Materials (wax, wick, fragrance, jar): $4.50
- Labor (30 min @ $15/hr): $7.50
- Overhead (utilities, packaging): $2.00
- Etsy fees (~10%): $3.00
- Profit: $8.00
- **Total Price: $25.00**

### Competitive Pricing

Research similar items:
- Filter by "Bestselling" in your category
- Note price ranges
- Position yourself: budget, mid-range, or premium

### Psychological Pricing

- $24.99 feels cheaper than $25.00
- $25 feels more premium than $24.99
- Test both approaches

### Free Shipping

Etsy boosts shops offering free shipping on orders $35+.
**Strategy**: Build shipping into product price.

## Shop Policies

Set these up before launching. They protect you and inform buyers.

### Shipping Policies

```
Processing Time: 1-3 business days
Shipping Carriers: USPS, UPS
Shipping Destinations: Worldwide (or specify)
Estimated Delivery: 
  - US: 3-7 business days
  - International: 10-21 business days
```

### Return & Exchange Policy

```
I want you to love your purchase! 

Returns: Accepted within 14 days of delivery
Exchanges: Accepted within 14 days
Custom orders: Final sale (unless damaged)
Buyer pays return shipping unless item is defective

To return: Message me within 14 days of delivery. 
I'll provide return instructions.
```

### Payment Policy

```
I accept all Etsy payment methods:
- Credit/debit cards
- Etsy Gift Cards
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- PayPal (in some regions)

Payment is due at checkout. Orders begin processing 
once payment clears.
```

### Custom Order Policy

```
I love creating custom pieces! 

Process:
1. Message me with your idea
2. I'll confirm details and provide a quote
3. You'll receive a custom listing
4. 50% deposit required to begin
5. Production time: [X] weeks

Custom orders are final sale unless damaged.
```

## After Launch

### Weekly Tasks

- **List new items** (Etsy favors active shops)
- **Renew sold items** immediately
- **Check stats** (Etsy Dashboard → Stats)
- **Respond to messages** (aim for <24 hours)
- **Share on social media** (3-5x/week)

### Monthly Tasks

- **Review bestsellers** (make more of what sells)
- **Update underperformers** (new photos, better SEO)
- **Check competitor pricing**
- **Plan new collections**
- **Review finances** (profit/loss)

### Etsy Ads

**When to start**: After you have 10+ listings and some organic sales

**Budget**: Start with $1-5/day

**Tips:**
- Ads work best for listings that already convert
- Turn off ads for items with no sales after 2 weeks
- Focus budget on bestsellers
- Monitor ROAS (return on ad spend)

### Going on Vacation

Set Vacation Mode if you can't fulfill orders:
- Pauses all listings
- Stops new orders
- You can still message buyers

## 📈 Growth Tips

### Get Your First Sale

1. **Price competitively** (slightly lower at first)
2. **Share everywhere** (social media, friends, family)
3. **Consider a launch sale** (10-15% off)
4. **Join Etsy teams** (community support)
5. **Be patient** (first sale can take weeks)

### Get Reviews

- Include a thank-you note in packages
- Follow up with a message after delivery
- Never ask for positive reviews (against TOS)
- Provide excellent customer service

### Stand Out

- Unique product photography
- Exceptional packaging
- Fast shipping
- Personal touches (handwritten notes)
- Excellent communication

---

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Copying other shops** (be original)
2. **Underpricing** (you'll burn out)
3. **Poor photos** (this is #1 reason items don't sell)
4. **Ignoring SEO** (no one can find you)
5. **Not reading TOS** (know the rules)
6. **Giving up too soon** (success takes time)
7. **Not tracking finances** (know your profit)
8. **Overpromising** (be realistic about timing)
