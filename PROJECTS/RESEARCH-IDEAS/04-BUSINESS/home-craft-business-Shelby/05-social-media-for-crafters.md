## Why Social Media Matters

**Social media is NOT optional for craft businesses:**

- 81% of Instagram users research products on the platform
- Pinterest users have high purchase intent
- TikTok can make products go viral overnight
- Social proof builds trust
- Direct line to your customers
- Free marketing (time investment, not money)

**Goals:**
- Brand awareness
- Community building
- Drive traffic to your shop
- Customer service
- Market research (see what resonates)

## Instagram Strategy

### Profile Optimization

**Username:**
- Match your shop name if possible
- Keep it simple and memorable
- Avoid numbers and underscores

**Name field:**
- Include keywords (e.g., "Sarah | Handmade Candles")
- This is searchable!

**Bio:**
```
✨ Handmade soy candles in Portland
🕯️ Small batch • Natural ingredients
📦 Free shipping over $50
👇 Shop now
[linktr.ee/yourshop]
```

**Profile photo:**
- Your logo or a clean product shot
- Recognizable at small size

**Link in bio:**
- Use Linktree, Beacons, or similar
- Include: Shop, Bestsellers, New Arrivals, About

### Content Types

**Feed Posts (3-5x/week):**

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Product showcase | Clean product photo | New candle collection |
| Lifestyle | Product in use | Candle lit on cozy shelf |
| Behind-the-scenes | Making process | Pouring candles |
| User-generated | Customer photos | Repost customer's setup |
| Educational | Tips, info | "How to care for your candle" |
| Personal | You, your story | Your workspace, your story |

**Stories (Daily or near-daily):**

- Quick updates
- Polls and questions
- New product sneak peeks
- Flash sales
- Day-in-the-life
- Behind-the-scenes

**Story highlights:**
- Shop
- Reviews
- FAQ
- BTS (Behind the Scenes)
- New Arrivals
- Bestsellers

**Reels (2-4x/week):**

Reels get the most reach! Ideas:
- Process videos (making your product)
- Before/after
- Packing orders
- Trending audio with your twist
- Quick tips
- Product reveals

### Hashtag Strategy

**Use 15-30 hashtags per post**

**Mix of sizes:**
- 5-10 large (1M+ posts): #handmade #smallbusiness
- 10-15 medium (100K-1M posts): #soycandle #candlelover
- 5-10 niche (10K-100K posts): #portlandmaker #pdxsmallbusiness

**Hashtag sets:**
Create 3-5 sets of hashtags for different post types. Rotate them.

**Example set for product post:**
```
#handmadecandles #soycandles #candlemaker #smallbatchcandles 
#candlelove #homedecor #candleaddict #supportsmallbusiness 
#shopsmall #handmadewithlove #candlecommunity #makersgonnamake 
#etsyshop #candlesofinstagram #pdxmaker
```

### Engagement Strategy

**The 20-minute rule:**
- Spend 10 minutes before posting engaging with others
- Post your content
- Spend 10 minutes after posting responding to comments

**Daily engagement:**
- Respond to ALL comments on your posts
- Reply to DMs within 24 hours
- Engage with similar accounts (not direct competitors)
- Engage with your customers' accounts
- Comment meaningfully (not just emojis)

**Who to engage with:**
- Your customers
- Similar makers (supportive community!)
- Potential customers (people who follow similar shops)
- Local accounts (if you do local markets)
- Accounts using your hashtags

### Instagram Growth Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Post consistently
- Use Reels (biggest reach)
- Engage authentically
- Use all features (posts, stories, reels)
- Collaborate with others
- Share user-generated content
- Go live occasionally

❌ **Don't:**
- Buy followers
- Use engagement pods (against TOS)
- Post and ghost
- Only promote (follow 80/20 rule)
- Ignore comments and DMs
- Use banned hashtags

## Pinterest Strategy

### Why Pinterest for Crafters

- Pinterest is a **search engine**, not social media
- Pins have a long lifespan (months or years!)
- Users have high purchase intent
- Drives traffic directly to your shop
- Less time-intensive than Instagram

### Set Up for Success

**Convert to Business Account** (free):
- Access to analytics
- Can add links to pins
- Look more professional

**Profile optimization:**
- Business name: Your shop name
- Bio: Include keywords about what you make
- Website: Link to your Etsy shop
- Claim your website (Etsy shop)

**Create boards:**
- 10-20 boards minimum
- Mix of your products and related content
- Keyword-rich board titles and descriptions

**Example boards for a candle shop:**
- Handmade Soy Candles (your products)
- Candle Care Tips
- Home Decor Ideas
- Cozy Home Inspiration
- Gift Ideas for Her
- Self Care Sunday
- [Your Shop Name] Bestsellers

### Pin Strategy

**Types of Pins:**

| Type | Description | Frequency |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| Product pins | Direct link to listings | 3-5x/week |
| Idea pins | Multi-page, like stories | 1-2x/week |
| Video pins | Short videos | 1-2x/week |
| Blog/guide pins | Link to helpful content | 1-2x/week |

**Pin Design:**

- **Size**: 1000 x 1500 pixels (2:3 ratio)
- **Vertical performs best**
- **Clear, readable text overlay**
- **Your branding** (logo, colors, fonts)
- **High-quality images**

**Tools:**
- Canva (free templates)
- Pinterest's built-in creator

**Text overlay examples:**
- "5 Ways to Make Your Candles Last Longer"
- "The Perfect Gift for Candle Lovers"
- "New Lavender Collection Just Dropped"

### Pinterest SEO

**Keywords are everything!**

**In pin titles:**
- ❌ "Pretty Candle"
- ✅ "Lavender Soy Candle | Natural Aromatherapy Candle | Self Care Gift"

**In pin descriptions:**
- 2-4 sentences
- Include keywords naturally
- Call to action
- Hashtags (5-10)

**Example:**
```
Light up your self-care routine with our handmade lavender soy candle. 
Made with natural ingredients and essential oils for a calming 
aromatherapy experience. Perfect gift for her or treat yourself! 
Shop now at [shop link].

#soycandle #lavendercandle #selfcare #aromatherapy #handmadecandles
```

**In board titles and descriptions:**
- Use keywords people search for
- Write helpful descriptions

### Pinterest Schedule

**Consistency matters more than volume:**

- **Minimum**: 3-5 pins per week
- **Ideal**: 10-15 pins per week
- **Use Tailwind** (scheduling tool) to batch create

**Tailwind:**
- $10-15/month
- Schedule pins in advance
- Join "communities" for more reach
- Analytics included

### Pinterest Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Pin consistently
- Create fresh pins for same products (different images/text)
- Use keywords everywhere
- Link to relevant pages
- Create idea pins (get more reach)
- Join group boards and communities

❌ **Don't:**
- Only pin your own content (80/20 rule)
- Use low-quality images
- Ignore SEO
- Give up too soon (takes 3-6 months to build)

## TikTok Strategy

### Why TikTok for Crafters

- **Viral potential** (one video can change your business)
- **Authenticity wins** (less polished than Instagram)
- **Behind-the-scenes performs well**
- **Younger demographic** (but all ages are on TikTok now)
- **TikTok Shop** (direct selling, growing feature)

### Profile Setup

**Username:** Match your shop/Instagram
**Bio:** Clear, personality-driven, link to shop
**Profile photo:** You or your logo

### Content That Works

**Top performing content for crafters:**

| Content Type | Description | Example |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| Process videos | Making your product | Pouring candles, assembling jewelry |
| Packing orders | ASMR-style packing | "Pack an order with me" |
| Day in the life | Your routine as a maker | "Day in my small business" |
| Storytime | Your journey | "How I started my business" |
| Tips/education | Teach something | "3 candle care mistakes" |
| Trends | Put your spin on trends | Use trending audio |
| Before/after | Transformation | Messy desk → organized studio |
| Customer reactions | Unboxing, reviews | "Customer opened their order" |
| Fails/bloopers | Keep it real | "When the wax spills everywhere" |

### TikTok Format

**Video length:**
- 15-60 seconds is sweet spot
- Can go up to 10 minutes, but shorter = better retention

**Hook (first 3 seconds):**
- Grab attention immediately
- Text overlay with hook
- Visual interest

**Examples:**
- "POV: You ordered from a small business"
- "The candle making process is so satisfying"
- "I quit my job to make candles full-time"
- "Things I wish I knew before starting a craft business"

### Hashtags

**Use 3-5 hashtags:**
- 1-2 broad: #smallbusiness #handmade
- 1-2 niche: #candlemaker #soycandles
- 1 trending (if relevant)

### Posting Schedule

- **Minimum**: 3x/week
- **Ideal**: 1x/day
- **Best times**: 6-9am, 12-2pm, 7-10pm (test for your audience)

### Engagement

- Reply to comments (boosts algorithm)
- Duet and stitch other videos
- Follow and engage with similar creators
- Use trending audio when relevant

### TikTok Tips

✅ **Do:**
- Be authentic and real
- Show your face (builds connection)
- Jump on trends quickly
- Post consistently
- Engage with comments
- Use captions/text overlay

❌ **Don't:**
- Over-produce (raw is better)
- Ignore comments
- Only post promotional content
- Give up after a few videos (consistency is key)

### Batch Content Creation

**Save time by batching:**

**Photo day (2-3 hours/month):**
- Photograph 10-20 products
- Take lifestyle shots
- Capture BTS content
- Create multiple angles

**Video day (2-3 hours/month):**
- Film 10-15 short videos
- Different angles/processes

- Create 10-15 pins in Canva
- Schedule in Tailwind

- Write all captions for the week

## Growing Your Audience

### Organic Growth Strategies

**1. Consistency**
- Post regularly
- Show up even when you don't feel like it
- Quality over quantity, but quantity matters too

**2. Engagement**
- Reply to every comment
- DM customers personally
- Engage with similar accounts
- Join creator communities

**3. Collaboration**
- Partner with complementary brands
- Guest posts/takeovers
- Giveaways together
- Cross-promote

**4. User-Generated Content**
- Encourage customers to share
- Repost customer photos (with permission)
- Create a branded hashtag
- Feature customers regularly

**5. Hashtags**
- Use relevant hashtags
- Mix of sizes
- Create your own branded hashtag
- Research what works in your niche

**6. Trends**
- Jump on relevant trends quickly
- Put your unique spin on them
- Use trending audio
- Don't force it if it doesn't fit

### Paid Growth (Optional)

**Instagram Ads:**
- Start with $5-10/day
- Boost best-performing posts
- Target interests related to your niche
- Drive to shop or specific product

**Pinterest Ads:**
- Promote best-performing pins
- $10-20/day to start
- Target keywords and interests
- Great for evergreen traffic

**TikTok Ads:**
- More complex, better for established brands
- Start with Spark Ads (boost organic posts)
- $20-50/day minimum

**When to consider paid:**
- You have consistent organic sales
- You know your customer lifetime value
- You have budget to test
- Your photos/content are high quality

## Converting Followers to Customers

### The Funnel

```
AWARENESS → INTEREST → DESIRE → ACTION
(Social)   (Engage)   (Want)   (Buy)
```

### Strategies

**1. Clear Call-to-Action**
- "Shop now" in bio
- "Link in bio" in captions
- "Tap to shop" on product tags
- Limited-time offers

**2. Make It Easy**
- Link directly to products (not just homepage)
- Use Linktree with clear categories
- Tag products in Instagram posts
- Pinterest pins link directly to listings

**3. Create Urgency**
- Limited editions
- Flash sales (24-48 hours)
- Low stock notifications
- Seasonal collections

**4. Social Proof**
- Share reviews
- Repost customer photos
- Share sales milestones
- "X people bought this week"

**5. Exclusive Offers**
- "Instagram followers get 10% off"
- Early access to new collections
- Behind-the-scenes for followers only
- Giveaways for followers

**6. Email List**
- Social media can disappear (algorithm changes)
- Build your email list
- Offer lead magnet (freebie, discount)
- Nurture with valuable content

### Content That Converts

**High-converting content types:**

| Content | Why It Converts |
|---------|-----------------|
| Product demos | Shows value in action |
| Customer reviews | Social proof |
| Before/after | Clear transformation |
| Limited-time offers | Urgency |
| Behind-the-scenes | Builds trust |
| Educational content | Positions you as expert |
| Personal stories | Emotional connection |

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram

- Reels > Posts for reach
- Stories for engagement with existing followers
- Use all features (posts, stories, reels, guides)
- Go live for Q&A or BTS
- Collaborate with others (collab posts)
- Tag products if eligible

### Pinterest

- Consistency > volume
- Fresh pins perform best (new images)
- Keywords are everything
- Idea pins get more reach
- Link to specific products, not just homepage
- Pin at optimal times (evenings, weekends)

### TikTok

- Authenticity > polish
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending audio
- Reply to comments with videos
- Post when your audience is active
- Don't delete "flop" videos (they can blow up later)

## Common Mistakes

### ❌ Buying Followers
**Why it's bad:** Fake followers don't engage, hurts your reach, looks suspicious

### ❌ Posting and Ghosting
**Why it's bad:** Social media is SOCIAL. Engage or don't bother.

### ❌ Only Promoting
**Why it's bad:** Follow the 80/20 rule. 80% value/entertainment, 20% promotion.

### ❌ Ignoring Analytics
**Why it's bad:** You won't know what's working. Review and adjust.

### ❌ Comparing Your Start to Someone Else's Peak
**Why it's bad:** Discouraging and unrealistic. Focus on your growth.

### ❌ Giving Up Too Soon
**Why it's bad:** Growth takes 3-6 months minimum. Stay consistent.

### ❌ Not Having a Strategy
**Why it's bad:** Random posting = random results. Plan your content.
