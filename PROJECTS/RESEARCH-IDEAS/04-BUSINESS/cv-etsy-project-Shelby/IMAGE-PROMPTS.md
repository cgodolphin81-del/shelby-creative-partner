## USAGE GUIDELINES

### Image Specifications

| Platform | Dimensions | Format | Max File Size |
|----------|------------|--------|---------------|
| Instagram Post | 1080x1080 (1:1) | JPG/PNG | 30MB |
| Instagram Story | 1080x1920 (9:16) | JPG/PNG | 30MB |
| Pinterest Pin | 1000x1500 (2:3) | JPG/PNG | 20MB |
| Facebook Post | 1200x630 (1.91:1) | JPG/PNG | 30MB |
| LinkedIn Post | 1200x627 (1.91:1) | JPG/PNG | 30MB |
| Email Header | 600x200 (3:1) | JPG/PNG | 1MB |
| Website Banner | 1920x600 (3.2:1) | JPG/WEBP | 500KB |

### Color Palette Recommendations
- **Primary:** Navy Blue (#1E3A5F)
- **Secondary:** Gold/Accent (#D4AF37)
- **Neutral:** White (#FFFFFF), Light Gray (#F5F5F5)
- **Text:** Dark Gray (#333333)

### Style Guidelines
- Clean and professional (avoid cluttered designs)
- Consistent branding across all images
- High contrast for readability
- Mobile-optimized (most traffic is mobile)
- Include brand logo/watermark on product images

## PROMPT CUSTOMIZATION TIPS

1. **Replace bracketed text** with your specifics (industry, initials, etc.)
2. **Adjust aspect ratios** (--ar) based on platform needs
3. **Modify colors** to match your brand palette
4. **Test variations** - generate 3-4 versions of each and pick the best
5. **Upscale** final selections for print-quality images
6. **Add text overlays** in Canva after generating base images
