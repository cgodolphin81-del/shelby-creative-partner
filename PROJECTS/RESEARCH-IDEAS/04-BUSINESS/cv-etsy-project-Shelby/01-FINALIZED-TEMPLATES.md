## ATS Compliance Checklist (All Templates)

✅ Standard section headings used throughout
✅ Common, ATS-readable fonts only (no script or decorative fonts)
✅ Clear font size hierarchy (Name 24-32pt, Headings 13-16pt, Body 10-12pt)
✅ Standard bullet points (• or -)
✅ Single-column layout (no multi-column designs)
✅ Standard margins (0.5" - 1")
✅ Black text on white/light background
✅ No graphics, icons, or images
✅ No tables or text boxes
✅ No headers/footers for critical information
✅ No photos
✅ No infographics or charts
✅ Subtle accent colors only (for headings and lines)

## Quality Assurance Tests

Before launch, each template must pass:

1. **Copy-Paste Test:** Copy PDF content → paste into plain text editor → structure remains clear
2. **Font Test:** Verify all fonts are ATS-compatible (no custom fonts)
3. **Color Test:** Ensure accent colors are subtle and professional
4. **Margin Test:** Verify 0.5" - 1" margins on all sides
5. **Section Test:** All standard sections present and clearly labeled
6. **Export Test:** Both PDF and DOCX export successfully
7. **Size Test:** Both A4 and US Letter versions created
8. **Readability Test:** Clear visual hierarchy at 100% zoom
