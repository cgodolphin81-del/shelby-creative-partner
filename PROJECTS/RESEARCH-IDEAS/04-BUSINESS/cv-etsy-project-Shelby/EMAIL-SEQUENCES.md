## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Timing
- Welcome sequence: Days 0, 2, 5
- Abandoned cart: 1 hour, 24 hours
- Post-purchase: Immediately
- Re-engagement: 60 days inactive
- Promotional: 1-2x/month max
- Newsletter: Monthly

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly (1 max)
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Personalize when possible
- A/B test different approaches

### Design Tips
- Mobile-responsive (60%+ read on mobile)
- Clear CTA buttons
- Minimal images (avoid spam filters)
- Plain text version included
- Unsubscribe link visible

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 20%+)
- Click rate (target: 3%+)
- Conversion rate (target: 2%+)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)
