## 💡 MESSAGE BEST PRACTICES

### Do's:
✓ Respond within 2-4 hours (Etsy tracks this!)
✓ Use customer's name
✓ Be warm and friendly
✓ Include emojis (but not too many)
✓ Offer genuine help
✓ Follow up if issue isn't resolved

### Don'ts:
✗ Don't be robotic or corporate
✗ Don't ignore messages (hurts shop ranking)
✗ Don't argue with customers
✗ Don't send too many promotional messages
✗ Don't share personal contact info (keep on Etsy)
