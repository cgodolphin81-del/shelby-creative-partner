## Shop Policies (Digital Downloads)

### Returns & Exchanges
```
Due to the digital nature of this product, I cannot offer returns or exchanges. 
However, if you experience any technical issues with your download, please 
contact me and I'll happily help resolve the problem or provide a replacement file.
```

### Digital Download Terms
```
What You're Buying:
- Instant digital download (no physical product will be shipped)
- Editable template files (PDF and DOCX formats)
- Personal use license

What You Can Do:
- Edit the template for your personal use
- Use the template to create your CV/resume
- Print unlimited copies for your personal job applications

What You Cannot Do:
- Resell or redistribute the template files
- Share the files with others
- Claim the template design as your own
- Use for commercial purposes (creating CVs for clients)

License: Personal Use Only
```

### Processing Time
```
This is an instant digital download. Your files will be available immediately 
after purchase. You'll also receive an email from Etsy with download instructions.
```

### Payment
```
I accept all payment methods available through Etsy, including credit cards, 
debit cards, PayPal, and Etsy Gift Cards. Payment is processed securely by Etsy.
```

### Privacy
```
Your privacy is important. I only use your information to process your order 
and send your digital files. I do not share your information with third parties 
or use it for marketing purposes.
```
