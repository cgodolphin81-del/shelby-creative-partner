# Marketing Plan - CV Templates Etsy Business

## 30-60-90 Day Strategy

### Overview
Launch CV/resume template business on Etsy with focused marketing across social media, email, and content channels.

---

## Days 1-30: LAUNCH PHASE

### Goals
- [ ] Set up Etsy shop
- [ ] List 20 CV templates
- [ ] Create 50 preview images
- [ ] Make first 5 sales
- [ ] Get first 5 reviews

### Week 1: Setup
**Days 1-3:**
- Create Etsy account
- Set up shop profile and banner
- Configure payment and shipping
- Write shop policies

**Days 4-7:**
- Create preview images for first 10 templates
- Write product descriptions
- Set up SEO (titles, tags, categories)
- List first 10 products

### Week 2: Launch
**Days 8-10:**
- List remaining 10 templates
- Create social media accounts (Instagram, Pinterest, LinkedIn)
- Design shop banner and logo

**Days 11-14:**
- Announce launch on personal networks
- Post first 5 social media posts
- Send launch email to contacts
- Run Etsy launch sale (20% off)

### Week 3-4: Initial Traction
**Daily Tasks:**
- Post 1x on Instagram/Pinterest
- Engage with 10 potential customers
- Respond to all inquiries within 2 hours
- Monitor Etsy stats

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Add 2-3 new templates
- Optimize underperforming listings
- Request reviews from buyers

### Key Metrics (Days 1-30)
- Shop visits: 500+
- Listings: 20+
- Sales: 5+
- Reviews: 5+
- Revenue: $100-300

---

## Days 31-60: GROWTH PHASE

### Goals
- [ ] 50 total sales
- [ ] 25+ reviews (4.8+ stars)
- [ ] 50+ listings
- [ ] $1,000+ revenue
- [ ] Email list of 100+ subscribers

### Week 5-6: Content Marketing
- Start Pinterest pinning (10 pins/day)
- Create YouTube shorts showing templates
- Write blog posts on resume tips
- Guest post on career blogs

### Week 7-8: Paid Advertising
- Test Etsy Ads ($5-10/day)
- Run Instagram/Facebook ads
- Retargeting campaigns
- Influencer collaborations

### Key Metrics (Days 31-60)
- Shop visits: 2,000+
- Listings: 50+
- Sales: 50+
- Reviews: 25+
- Revenue: $1,000+

---

## Days 61-90: SCALE PHASE

### Goals
- [ ] 200 total sales
- [ ] 100+ reviews
- [ ] 100+ listings
- [ ] $5,000+ revenue
- [ ] Email list of 500+ subscribers

### Week 9-10: Product Expansion
- Add cover letter templates
- Add thank you note templates
- Create bundle packages
- Launch custom design service

### Week 11-12: Automation & Systems
- Automate social media posting
- Create email sequences
- Build template customization system
- Hire virtual assistant for customer service

### Key Metrics (Days 61-90)
- Shop visits: 10,000+
- Listings: 100+
- Sales: 200+
- Reviews: 100+
- Revenue: $5,000+

---

## Marketing Channels

### 1. Etsy Organic (Primary)
- SEO optimization
- Quality photos
- Competitive pricing
- Excellent customer service

### 2. Pinterest (High Priority)
- Create 10 pins per template
- Join group boards
- Rich pins enabled
- Pin daily

### 3. Instagram (Medium Priority)
- Before/after transformations
- Resume tips carousel posts
- Stories with templates
- Reels showing customization

### 4. Email Marketing (Medium Priority)
- Lead magnet (free resume template)
- Weekly newsletter
- Product launch emails
- Abandoned cart sequences

### 5. LinkedIn (Low Priority)
- Resume tips posts
- Career advice content
- Link to Etsy shop

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low initial sales | Run launch sale, ask friends/family |
| Negative reviews | Excellent customer service, quick refunds |
| Copycats | Unique designs, build brand loyalty |
| Algorithm changes | Diversify traffic sources |
| Burnout | Automate, outsource, batch work |
