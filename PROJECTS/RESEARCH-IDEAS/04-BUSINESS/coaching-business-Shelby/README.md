## 📚 How to Use This Package

### For New Coaches:
**Follow the phases in order.** Don't skip ahead. Foundation first, then offers, then marketing. Most people fail because they reverse this order.

**Timeline:** 8 weeks to launch, 6-12 months to $100K

### For Existing Coaches (<$100K/year):
**Start with Phase 2 (Offers & Pricing).** You likely have the basics. Optimize your offers, raise prices, then systematize sales.

**Timeline:** 4 weeks to optimize, 3-6 months to 2-3x revenue

### For Established Coaches ($100K-500K/year):
**Focus on Phase 4 (Delivery) and Scaling.** You're probably maxed on time. Build group programs, hire team, create systems.

**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks to systematize, 12-18 months to $1M

### For High-Level Coaches ($500K+/year):
**Jump to Scaling System and Revenue Projections.** You need certifications, courses, team, and strategic partnerships.

**Timeline:** 6-12 months to $2M+, 18-36 months to $5M+

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Niche Mistakes:
- Choosing too broad ("I help everyone")
- Picking a niche without money
- Changing niche every 3 months
- Not validating before committing

### ❌ Offer Mistakes:
- Only one offer (no ladder)
- Pricing too low (attracts wrong clients)
- No payment plans (limits access)
- Over-complicating the offer

### ❌ Sales Mistakes:
- No discovery call script
- Not following up (most sales are 5th+ touch)
- Discounting too easily
- Not asking for the sale

### ❌ Delivery Mistakes:
- No onboarding system
- Not tracking progress
- Over-delivering (burnout)
- Not collecting testimonials

### ❌ Marketing Mistakes:
- Trying all channels at once
- Inconsistent content
- No clear call-to-action
- Not measuring ROI

### ❌ Scaling Mistakes:
- Hiring too early
- Not documenting processes
- Losing quality control
- Scaling before product-market fit
