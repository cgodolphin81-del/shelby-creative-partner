# CASH FLOW MANAGEMENT

## The 5-Account System

```
ACCOUNT 1: OPERATING ACCOUNT
• All revenue deposited here
• Pay all expenses from here
• Target balance: 2-3 months expenses

ACCOUNT 2: OWNER PAY
• Transfer salary to personal account
• Frequency: Bi-weekly or monthly
• Amount: Set percentage of revenue (30-50%)

ACCOUNT 3: TAX ACCOUNT
• Transfer tax money immediately
• Percentage: 25-30% of revenue
• Use for: Quarterly estimated taxes

ACCOUNT 4: PROFIT ACCOUNT
• Transfer profit share
• Percentage: 10-20% of revenue
• Use for: Bonuses, investments, distributions

ACCOUNT 5: GROWTH ACCOUNT
• Reinvest in business
• Percentage: 10-20% of revenue
• Use for: Hiring, marketing, product development
```

---

## Cash Flow Rules:

```
RULE 1: Pay Yourself First
• Set owner pay percentage
• Transfer automatically
• Don't skip even in slow months

RULE 2: Save for Taxes
• Transfer immediately on revenue
• Don't touch this money
• Pay quarterly estimates on time

RULE 3: Maintain Reserves
• 3-6 months expenses in operating account
• Don't overspend in good months
• Prepare for seasonality

RULE 4: Reinvest Strategically
• Use growth account for strategic investments
• ROI-focused spending
• Track return on every investment

RULE 5: Monitor Weekly
• Review cash position weekly
• Track accounts receivable
• Forecast 90 days out
```
