# SIGNATURE FRAMEWORK CREATOR

## Why You Need a Signature Framework

A signature framework:
- Makes your coaching feel like a PROVEN SYSTEM (not just advice)
- Justifies premium pricing (proprietary = valuable)
- Creates marketing hooks (easy to explain and remember)
- Builds authority (you're THE expert on YOUR method)
- Enables scaling (others can eventually deliver it)

---

## The Framework Creation Process

### Phase 1: Map Your Client's Journey

**Document the transformation from A → B:**

```
STARTING STATE (Point A):
- What's their current situation?
- What pain are they feeling?
- What have they tried that failed?
- What beliefs are holding them back?

ENDING STATE (Point B):
- What does success look like?
- How will they feel?
- What specific results will they have?
- What new beliefs will they hold?
```

**Example (Career Coach):**
```
Point A: Stuck in $120K role, undervalued, applying everywhere with no response
Point B: $250K+ offer from dream company, multiple options, confident negotiator
```

---

### Phase 2: Identify the Key Stages

Most transformations have 4-6 stages. Name each stage:

**Example Framework: "The 5-Pillar Profit System"**

```
PILLAR 1: FOUNDATION
- Audit current situation
- Identify limiting beliefs
- Set clear outcomes

PILLAR 2: STRATEGY
- Market positioning
- Offer design
- Pricing architecture

PILLAR 3: LAUNCH
- Audience building
- Content creation
- Sales conversations

PILLAR 4: SCALE
- Team building
- Systems automation
- Revenue optimization

PILLAR 5: LEGACY
- Thought leadership
- Passive income
- Exit planning
```

---

### Phase 3: Name Your Framework

**Naming Formulas:**

1. **Number + Outcome + System**
   - "The 7-Figure Launch System"
   - "The 90-Day Transformation Method"

2. **Acronym (spell out your method)**
   - "R.I.S.E. Method" (Research, Implement, Scale, Expand)
   - "C.O.R.E. Framework" (Clarity, Optimization, Revenue, Expansion)

3. **Metaphor-Based**
   - "The Revenue Rocket"
   - "The Success Scaffold"
   - "The Breakthrough Bridge"

4. **Your Name + Method**
   - "The [Your Name] Accelerator"
   - "[Your Name]'s Profit Protocol"

5. **Unique Mechanism**
   - "The Neuro-Sales System"
   - "The Quantum Leap Method"
   - "The Reverse-Engineered Success Framework"

**Your Framework Name:** _________________________________

### Phase 4: Package the Framework

Create visual assets:

```
□ Framework Diagram (PDF + PNG)
   - Show the stages visually
   - Use in all marketing materials

□ Framework One-Pager
   - Explain each stage
   - Include for prospects

□ Framework Video (5-10 min)
   - Walk through the methodology
   - Use in sales calls

□ Framework Checklist
   - Self-assessment tool
   - Lead magnet potential
```

## Framework Validation Test

Before launching, ask 5 ideal clients:

1. "Does this framework make sense to you?"
2. "Which stage feels most valuable?"
3. "What's unclear or confusing?"
4. "Would this justify a premium price?"
5. "What would make this irresistible?"

**Iterate based on feedback, then finalize.**
