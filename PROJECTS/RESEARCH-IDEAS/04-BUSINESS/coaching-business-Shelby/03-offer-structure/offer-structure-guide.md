# OFFER STRUCTURE BLUEPRINT

## The Offer Ladder Strategy

Build multiple offers at different price points to maximize lifetime value:

```
FREE → $97 → $997 → $5,000 → $25,000 → $50,000+
(Lead   (Mini   (Group  (1:1    (VIP    (Mastermind/
 Magnet) Course) Program) Coach) Day)   Retainer)
```

Each offer feeds the next. Not everyone starts at the top.

## OFFER 1: 1:1 COACHING PACKAGE

### Structure:
```
PROGRAM NAME: [Your Signature Program]
DURATION: 12 weeks (3 months)
INVESTMENT: $7,500 - $15,000
PAYMENT: Pay in full or 3 monthly payments

INCLUDES:
✓ 12 weekly 60-minute 1:1 coaching calls (Zoom)
✓ Unlimited Voxer/email support (24-48hr response)
✓ Custom action plans after each session
✓ [Signature Framework] implementation
✓ Resource library access
✓ Session recordings
✓ Bonus: [relevant bonus - e.g., resume review, brand audit]

GUARANTEE:
[Specific outcome] or [remedy - e.g., extra sessions free, partial refund]
```

### Ideal For:
- High-touch clients who want personalized attention
- Complex situations requiring customization
- Clients who value direct access to you

### Capacity:
- 5-10 clients maximum at a time
- Protects your time and energy
- Maintains exclusivity

```
- Ongoing strategic partnership needs
- Values long-term relationship
```

- 3-5 retainer clients maximum

- Protect this capacity fiercely

## OFFER COMPARISON MATRIX

| Offer Type | Price Range | Time Investment | Scalability | Best For |
|------------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|----------|
| 1:1 Coaching | $7.5K-$15K | High (12+ hrs/client) | Low | Deep transformation |
| Group Program | $2.5K-$5K | Medium (10 hrs/cohort) | Medium | Community + structure |
| Cohort Course | $1K-$2.5K | Low (pre-recorded) | High | Scale + lead gen |
| VIP Day | $7.5K-$15K | High (1 day/client) | Low | Fast results |
| Retainer | $5K-$10K/mo | Medium (4 hrs/client/mo) | Low | Ongoing partnership |

```
Bundle Price: $25,000

```

### The "Pathway" Bundle:
```
Start: Cohort Course ($1,500)
↓
Upgrade: Group Program (apply course fee, +$2,000)
↓
Upgrade: 1:1 Coaching (apply group fee, +$8,000)

Client feels they're getting credit, you increase LTV.
```
