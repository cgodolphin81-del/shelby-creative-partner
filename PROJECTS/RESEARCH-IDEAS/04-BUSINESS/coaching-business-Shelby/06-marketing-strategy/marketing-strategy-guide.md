## High-Converting Content Formulas

### The "Problem-Agitate-Solution" Post:
```
Most [audience] struggle with [problem].

They try [common solution], but it doesn't work because 
[reason].

Then they try [another solution], same result.

Here's the truth: [counterintuitive insight].

The real solution? [Your approach].

This is exactly what we do in [Program Name]. 

[Specific result] in [timeframe] without [frustration].

DM me "[KEYWORD]" if you want to learn more.
```

### The "Case Study" Post:
```
[Client Name] came to me [timeframe] ago feeling [emotion].

They were [specific struggle 1], [struggle 2], and [struggle 3].

We worked together using [your framework].

[Timeframe] later:
✓ [Result 1 with numbers]
✓ [Result 2 with numbers]
✓ [Result 3 with numbers]

But the best part? [Emotional transformation].

This is why I do what I do.

Want similar results? Link in bio / DM me.
```

### The "Contrarian Take" Post:
```
Unpopular opinion: [Controversial statement in your niche].

Everyone says [common advice], but that's actually wrong.

Here's why: [Your reasoning].

What works instead: [Your approach].

I've seen this work for [X] clients.

Agree or disagree? Let me know below. 👇
```

### The "Behind the Scenes" Post:
```
What I actually do all day as a [your role]:

6 AM: [Morning routine]
8 AM: [Client work]
12 PM: [Lunch/break]
2 PM: [Content creation]
4 PM: [Business development]
6 PM: [Wind down]

The reality? It's not as glamorous as Instagram makes it 
look.

But I wouldn't trade it for anything.

What does your day look like?
```

# WEBINAR STRATEGY

## The High-Converting Webinar Formula

### Webinar Structure (60 minutes):

```
MINUTES 0-5: WELCOME & WARM-UP
• Welcome everyone
• Housekeeping (Q&A box, recordings)
• Your story (brief, relevant)
• Poll: "Where are you struggling most?"

MINUTES 5-20: THE PROBLEM
• Deep dive into their challenge
• Why it's worse than they think
• Cost of inaction
• Why conventional solutions fail

MINUTES 20-35: THE SOLUTION
• Introduce your framework
• Walk through each step
• Show proof it works (case studies)
• Address common objections

MINUTES 35-50: THE OFFER
• Present your program
• What's included
• Bonuses (limited-time)
• Investment and payment options
• Guarantee

MINUTES 50-60: Q&A + CLOSE
• Answer questions live
• Restate offer and urgency
• Clear call-to-action
• Thank everyone
```

# PARTNERSHIP STRATEGY

## Types of Partnerships

### 1. Referral Partners
```
Who: Complementary service providers
Example: Business coach ↔ Accountant, Lawyer, Web Designer

Structure:
• 10-20% referral fee for closed business
• Or reciprocal referrals (no fee)
• Regular check-ins to stay top-of-mind
```

### 2. Joint Venture Partners
```
Who: Similar audience, non-competing offers
Example: Career coach ↔ Resume writer, LinkedIn consultant

Structure:
• Co-create a webinar or workshop
• Split revenue 50/50
• Share each other's email lists
• Cross-promote on social media
```

### 3. Affiliate Partners
```
Who: Influencers, content creators in your niche
Example: Industry podcasters, newsletter writers, YouTubers

Structure:
• 20-30% commission on sales they drive
• Provide unique tracking link
• Give them promotional materials
• Pay monthly via PayPal/Stripe
```

### 4. Strategic Partners
```
Who: Companies that serve your ideal client
Example: HR platforms, SaaS tools, professional associations

Structure:
• Exclusive discount for their members
• Revenue share or flat fee
• Co-branded content
• Speaking opportunities at their events
```

# PAID ADS STRATEGY

## Platform Recommendations by Budget

### Under $500/month:
- **LinkedIn:** Best for B2B, high-ticket ($5K+)
- **Facebook/Instagram:** Best for B2C, visual niches

### $500-$2,000/month:
- **Google Search:** Intent-based, high conversion
- **YouTube:** Great for longer-form content

### $2,000+/month:
- All of the above + retargeting campaigns
- Consider podcast sponsorships

# REFERRAL SYSTEM

## The Referral Request Framework

### Timing: Ask when they're happiest
- After a big win
- Mid-program when they're seeing results
- At program completion
- During a positive check-in

### The Ask Script:
```
"[Name], I'm so thrilled about your progress with 
[specific result]. 

I'm curious—do you know anyone else who's struggling 
with [their challenge] who might benefit from working 
together like this?

I'm only taking on [X] new clients this month, and I'd 
love to work with people who are as committed as you 
are.

If you think of anyone, I'd really appreciate an intro."
```

---

## Referral Incentive Options

### Option 1: Commission
```
"Refer someone who becomes a client, and I'll send you 
$500 as a thank you."
```

### Option 2: Credit
```
"Refer someone who becomes a client, and you'll receive 
$1,000 credit toward [future program/extension]."
```

### Option 3: Donation
```
"Refer someone who becomes a client, and I'll donate 
$500 to [charity of their choice] in your name."
```

### Option 4: Gift
```
"Refer someone who becomes a client, and I'll send you 
a [luxury gift - e.g., spa day, premium tech, experience]."
```
