## Opportunity #1: AI Workflow Automation System

### 1) Asset Type
**System/Code** - Enterprise-grade AI agent workflow automation framework including:
- Pre-built agent templates (customer service, data processing, research, content generation)
- Workflow orchestration engine
- Integration connectors (Slack, Notion, Airtable, Google Workspace, Salesforce)
- Analytics dashboard for agent performance tracking

### 2) Target Licensee (Who Pays)
- **Primary:** Mid-market companies (100-1000 employees) in professional services, e-commerce, SaaS
- **Secondary:** Enterprise corporations seeking to deploy AI agents without building from scratch
- **Specific verticals:** Marketing agencies, consulting firms, customer support operations, financial services

### 3) Licensing Model
**Hybrid Subscription + Usage-Based**
- Base annual license fee for platform access
- Usage tiers based on number of active agents and API calls
- Optional white-label add-on for agencies reselling to their clients

### 4) Pricing
| Tier | Annual License | Usage Included | Overage Rate |
|------|---------------|----------------|--------------|
| Starter | $15,000/year | 5 agents, 50K calls/month | $0.10/call |
| Professional | $45,000/year | 20 agents, 250K calls/month | $0.08/call |
| Enterprise | $85,000+/year | Unlimited agents, 1M+ calls/month | Custom |
| White-Label Add-on | +$25,000/year | Resell rights to end clients | Revenue share: 15% |

**Potential Revenue:** $50K-100K per enterprise client annually

### 5) Legal Requirements
- **License Agreement:** Define scope of use, restrictions on reverse engineering, redistribution limits
- **SLA:** 99.5% uptime guarantee for enterprise tier
- **Data Protection:** GDPR/CCPA compliance, data processing agreement (DPA)
- **IP Ownership:** Licensor retains all IP; licensee gets non-exclusive, non-transferable rights
- **Indemnification:** Mutual indemnification for IP infringement claims
- **Term:** 1-3 year initial term with auto-renewal
- **Audit Rights:** Licensor may audit usage to verify compliance

### 6) Distribution Channel
- **Direct Sales:** Enterprise outreach via LinkedIn, industry conferences
- **Marketplace:** List on AI/automation marketplaces (Zapier Partner, Make.com, AWS Marketplace)
- **Partnerships:** System integrators, digital transformation consultancies
- **Content Marketing:** Case studies, webinars demonstrating ROI
