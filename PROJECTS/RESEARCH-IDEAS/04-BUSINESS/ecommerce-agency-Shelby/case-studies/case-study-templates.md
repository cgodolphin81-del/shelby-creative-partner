## Case Study Framework

### The Story Arc

```
1. THE HERO (Client)
   - Who they are
   - What they do
   - Where they started

2. THE CHALLENGE (Problem)
   - What was broken
   - What was at stake
   - Why they needed help

3. THE GUIDE (Your Agency)
   - How you came in
   - Your approach
   - Why you were the right fit

4. THE PLAN (Solution)
   - What you did
   - How you did it
   - Timeline

5. THE TRANSFORMATION (Results)
   - Before vs. After
   - Key metrics
   - Business impact

6. THE PROOF (Testimonials)
   - Client quotes
   - Specific praise
   - Credibility markers
```

**The Diagnosis**
```
We conducted a comprehensive audit and found 4 core issues:

1. CREATIVE FATIGUE
   • Same 3 ads running for 6+ months
   • CTR declined from 2.1% to 0.8%
   • Frequency at 4.2 (way too high)

2. ACCOUNT STRUCTURE
   • 50+ ad sets (way too fragmented)
   • Budget spread too thin
   • No clear testing framework

3. TRACKING ISSUES
   • Pixel not firing on all events
   • CAPI not implemented
   • iOS14 impact not accounted for

4. AUDIENCE STRATEGY
   • Over-reliance on lookalikes
   • Interest audiences exhausted
   • No broad testing
```

**The Solution**
```
WE IMPLEMENTED A 3-PHASE APPROACH:

PHASE 1: CREATIVE OVERHAUL (Weeks 1-2)

Produced 20 new ad creatives:
• 10 UGC-style videos (founder stories, testimonials)
• 5 product demo videos
• 5 lifestyle images

Creative Framework:
• Hook (0-3 seconds): Pattern interrupt
• Problem (3-10 seconds): Relatable pain point
• Solution (10-20 seconds): Product demonstration
• Social Proof (20-25 seconds): Reviews, results
• CTA (25-30 seconds): Clear next step

PHASE 2: ACCOUNT RESTRUCTURE (Week 3)

Consolidated from 50+ ad sets to 12:
• 3 CBO campaigns (TOF, MOF, BOF)
• 4 ad sets per campaign (broad + 3 lookalikes)
• 3-5 ads per ad set

New Campaign Structure:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ TOF (80% budget)                        │
│ ├─ Broad (25-55, all genders)          │
│ ├─ LAL 1% Purchasers                   │

│ ├─ Engagers (30 days)                  │
│ ├─ Video Viewers (50%, 30 days)        │
│ ├─ Website Visitors (30 days)          │
│ └─ Email List (excluding purchasers)   │
├─────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ BOF (5% budget)                         │
│ ├─ ATC (14 days)                       │
│ ├─ IC (14 days)                        │
│ └─ View Content (7 days)               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘

PHASE 3: TESTING SYSTEM (Ongoing)

Weekly Creative Testing:
• Launch 5 new ads every Monday
• Kill losers after $200 spend or 3 days
• Scale winners (increase budget 20% every 48 hours)
• Iterate on winning concepts

Testing Framework:
• Test 1 variable at a time (hook, format, CTA)
• Maintain 70/20/10 rule (70% proven, 20% iterations, 10% wildcards)
• Document all learnings in creative database
```

```
   • Form field confusion on account creation

   • 5 customer interviews
   • 100 post-purchase surveys

5. TECHNICAL AUDIT
   • Page speed: 5.2 seconds (target: <3 seconds)
   • Mobile usability issues (Google Search Console)
   • Broken links on 3 collection pages
```

## Testimonial Best Practices

### What Makes a Great Testimonial

**DO**:
✅ Be specific (mention actual results)
✅ Include the problem they had before
✅ Describe what working with you was like
✅ Mention the results achieved
✅ Include their title and company
✅ Use a photo (increases credibility)
✅ Link to their LinkedIn (verifiable)

**DON'T**:
❌ Be vague ("They're great!")
❌ Use generic language ("Highly recommend")
❌ Make unrealistic claims ("10x in a week!")
❌ Be too short (1-2 sentences)
❌ Be anonymous (no name/company)

### Testimonial Framework

```
[PROBLEM]
"Before working with [Agency], we were [specific problem]..."

[SOLUTION]
"[Agency] came in and [specific actions they took]..."

[RESULTS]
"In [timeframe], we achieved [specific results]..."

[EXPERIENCE]
"The team is [specific qualities]. They're [differentiator]..."

[RECOMMENDATION]
"If you're [ideal client profile], work with [Agency]..."
```

### Testimonial Collection Process

**Timing**: Ask after a big win (3-6 months into partnership)

**Method**: Video call (record with permission) or written

**Questions to Ask**:
1. What was your biggest challenge before working with us?
2. What almost stopped you from hiring us?
3. What was your experience working with our team like?
4. What results have you achieved?
5. What would you tell someone considering working with us?
6. What's different about your business now vs. before?

**Follow-Up**: Send transcript for approval, request photo and LinkedIn
