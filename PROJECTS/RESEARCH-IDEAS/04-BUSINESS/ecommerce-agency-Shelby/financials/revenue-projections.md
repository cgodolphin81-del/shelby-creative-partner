2. **Software Optimization**: Regular tool audits, negotiate enterprise deals
3. **Remote-First**: Minimize office costs until necessary
4. **Automation**: Invest in tools that reduce manual work

## Unit Economics

### Client Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Year 1 Client Profile**:
- **Avg. MRR**: $6,700
- **Avg. Lifespan**: 18 months (8% monthly churn)
- **Gross Margin**: 55%
- **LTV**: $6,700 × 18 × 0.55 = **$66,330**

**Year 2 Client Profile**:
- **Avg. MRR**: $8,300
- **Avg. Lifespan**: 24 months (6% monthly churn)
- **Gross Margin**: 60%
- **LTV**: $8,300 × 24 × 0.60 = **$119,520**

**Year 3 Client Profile**:
- **Avg. MRR**: $9,500
- **Avg. Lifespan**: 30 months (5% monthly churn)
- **Gross Margin**: 65%
- **LTV**: $9,500 × 30 × 0.65 = **$185,250**

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Year 1**:
- **Marketing Spend**: $120,000
- **New Clients**: 28
- **CAC**: $120,000 / 28 = **$4,286**
- **LTV:CAC**: 15.5:1 ✅

**Year 2**:
- **Marketing Spend**: $350,000
- **New Clients**: 50
- **CAC**: $350,000 / 50 = **$7,000**
- **LTV:CAC**: 17.1:1 ✅

**Year 3**:
- **Marketing Spend**: $640,000
- **New Clients**: 71
- **CAC**: $640,000 / 71 = **$9,014**
- **LTV:CAC**: 20.5:1 ✅

### Payback Period

**Formula**: CAC / (MRR × Gross Margin)

**Year 1**: $4,286 / ($6,700 × 0.55) = **1.2 months** ✅
**Year 2**: $7,000 / ($8,300 × 0.60) = **1.4 months** ✅
**Year 3**: $9,014 / ($9,500 × 0.65) = **1.5 months** ✅

*Target: < 6 months. All years well below target.*

## Exit Scenarios

### Valuation Methodologies

**1. Revenue Multiple** (Most Common for Agencies)
- Typical range: 1.0-3.0x revenue
- Depends on: Growth rate, margins, client concentration, recurring revenue

**2. EBITDA Multiple**
- Typical range: 4.0-8.0x EBITDA
- Depends on: Margin quality, growth, scalability

**3. SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings)**
- Typical range: 2.0-4.0x SDE
- For smaller agencies (< $5M revenue)

### Valuation Scenarios (Year 3 Exit)

**Conservative** (1.5x Revenue):
- Revenue: $8.0M
- Valuation: $12M
- EBITDA Margin: 29%

**Base Case** (2.0x Revenue):
- Revenue: $8.0M
- Valuation: $16M
- EBITDA Margin: 35%

**Optimistic** (3.0x Revenue):
- Revenue: $8.0M
- Valuation: $24M
- EBITDA Margin: 40%

### Factors That Increase Valuation

✅ **Recurring Revenue** (>80% retainers)
✅ **Low Churn** (<5% monthly)
✅ **High Margins** (>30% EBITDA)
✅ **Diversified Clients** (no client >10% of revenue)
✅ **Strong Team** (management layer in place)
✅ **Systems & Processes** (documented, scalable)
✅ **Growth Rate** (>50% YoY)
✅ **Niche Expertise** (recognized leader in category)

### Factors That Decrease Valuation

❌ **Project-Heavy** (<50% recurring)
❌ **High Churn** (>10% monthly)
❌ **Low Margins** (<15% EBITDA)
❌ **Client Concentration** (>20% from one client)
❌ **Founder Dependency** (founder does everything)
❌ **No Systems** (chaotic operations)
❌ **Declining Revenue** (negative growth)
❌ **Commodity Services** (no differentiation)

**Funding Options**:
1. **Bootstrapped**: Start lean, reinvest profits
2. **Friends & Family**: $100K-500K
3. **Small Business Loan**: SBA loan, $100K-500K
4. **Angel Investor**: $500K-1M for 10-20%
5. **Revenue-Based Financing**: 5-10% of monthly revenue until 1.5-2.0x repaid

### Recommended Approach: **Bootstrapped + Conservative Growth**

**Why**:
- Agencies are cash-flow positive quickly
- No need to dilute equity
- Maintain full control
- Grow sustainably, not recklessly

**How**:
- Start with $100K-150K (personal savings or small loan)
- Focus on profitability from Month 1
- Reinvest 50% of profits into growth
- Hire only when revenue supports it
- Avoid expensive office, perks, etc. until profitable

### Critical Success Factors

**1. Client Acquisition** (Most Important)
- Consistent pipeline generation
- 2-4 new clients/month minimum
- Diversified channels (content, outbound, partnerships, referrals)

**2. Client Retention**
- Deliver results (ROAS, revenue growth)
- Proactive communication
- Regular strategy reviews
- Address issues quickly

**3. Pricing Power**
- Start competitive, increase with results
- 10-15% annual price increases
- Move upmarket over time

**4. Team Building**
- Hire A-players
- Invest in training
- Create growth paths
- Build culture

**5. Systems & Processes**
- Document everything
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Use right tools
- Continuous improvement

### Recommendations

**Year 1: Survive & Prove**
- Focus on getting to $100K/month run rate
- Build 5-10 case studies
- Establish processes
- Stay cash-flow positive

**Year 2: Systematize & Scale**
- Build management layer
- Specialize service offerings
- Increase prices
- Invest in brand

**Year 3: Optimize & Exit (or Continue)**
- Maximize margins
- Build enterprise capabilities
- Decide: Continue growing or sell
- If sell: Prepare for due diligence

---

**This model is achievable** with consistent execution, focus on client results, and disciplined financial management.

**Key takeaway**: Build a profitable business from Day 1. Don't chase growth at the expense of margins. Sustainable growth > hype.
