## Shopify

### Overview
**Market Share**: 29% of e-commerce platforms (10M+ stores)
**Founded**: 2006
**Headquarters**: Ottawa, Canada

### Pricing Plans
| Plan | Monthly Cost | Transaction Fee | Best For |
|------|-------------|-----------------|----------|
| Basic | $39/month | 2.9% + 30¢ + 2% | New stores |
| Shopify | $105/month | 2.6% + 30¢ + 1% | Growing stores |
| Advanced | $399/month | 2.4% + 30¢ + 0.5% | Scaling stores |
| Plus | $2,300/month | Custom (0.15%) | Enterprise |

*Note: Transaction fees waived with Shopify Payments*

### Pros ✅
- **Easiest to use**: Intuitive interface, minimal learning curve
- **All-in-one**: Hosting, security, updates included
- **App ecosystem**: 8,000+ apps for any functionality
- **Reliable**: 99.99% uptime, handles traffic spikes
- **Great support**: 24/7 support via chat, email, phone
- **Built-in features**: Abandoned cart, discount codes, gift cards
- **Mobile optimized**: All themes mobile-responsive
- **SEO friendly**: Clean URLs, auto-sitemap, meta editing
- **Multi-channel**: Sell on Amazon, Instagram, TikTok, POS
- **Fast checkout**: Shop Pay (1-click checkout)

### Cons ❌
- **Monthly costs**: Apps add up quickly ($100-500/month typical)
- **Limited customization**: Liquid theme language required for deep changes
- **Transaction fees**: 0.5-2% if not using Shopify Payments
- **Content limitations**: Not ideal for content-heavy sites
- **Migration complexity**: Difficult to move away from Shopify
- **Blog functionality**: Basic compared to WordPress

### Best For
- DTC brands launching and scaling
- Brands focused on speed to market
- Non-technical founders
- Brands wanting to focus on marketing, not tech
- Multi-channel sellers

### Our Experience
**We recommend Shopify for 80% of our clients.** It's the best balance of ease, features, and scalability. We've built 200+ Shopify stores with average launch time of 3-4 weeks.

## WooCommerce

### Overview
**Market Share**: 23% of e-commerce platforms (5M+ stores)
**Founded**: 2011
**Headquarters**: Open source (Automattic)

### Pricing (Typical)
| Component | Cost | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| WordPress | Free | Open source |
| WooCommerce | Free | Core plugin |
| Hosting | $15-100/month | Varies by traffic |
| Theme | $0-200 | One-time or annual |
| Extensions | $0-500/month | Payment, shipping, etc. |
| Developer | $50-200/hour | For custom work |
| **Total (Entry)** | $50-150/month | Self-managed |
| **Total (Scale)** | $200-1,000/month | Agency managed |

### Pros ✅
- **Complete control**: Full access to code and database
- **Unlimited customization**: WordPress plugin ecosystem
- **No transaction fees**: Keep 100% of revenue
- **Content + commerce**: Best-in-class blogging
- **SEO**: WordPress is SEO powerhouse
- **Cost effective**: Lower fees at scale
- **Open source**: No vendor lock-in
- **Large talent pool**: Easy to find developers

### Cons ❌
- **Technical complexity**: Requires WordPress knowledge
- **Maintenance burden**: Updates, security, backups on you
- **Hosting responsibility**: Site speed depends on your hosting
- **Security risks**: Vulnerable if not maintained
- **Plugin conflicts**: Extensions can break each other
- **Scalability challenges**: Needs optimization at scale
- **No unified support**: Multiple vendors for different issues
- **Slower launches**: 6-12 weeks typical vs. 3-4 for Shopify

### Best For
- Content-heavy sites (blogs, media)
- Brands with in-house technical teams
- Custom functionality needs
- Budget-conscious brands at scale
- WordPress existing users

### Our Experience
**We recommend WooCommerce for 15% of clients**, typically those with existing WordPress sites or specific customization needs. Requires more ongoing maintenance but offers more control.

## Platform Decision Framework

### Choose Shopify If:
- ✅ You want to launch quickly (under 4 weeks)
- ✅ You're a DTC brand focused on marketing
- ✅ You don't have in-house developers
- ✅ You want reliable, hassle-free hosting
- ✅ You plan to scale to 7-8 figures
- ✅ Multi-channel selling is important

### Choose WooCommerce If:
- ✅ You have existing WordPress site
- ✅ You have in-house technical team
- ✅ You need heavy customization
- ✅ Content marketing is core strategy
- ✅ You want to minimize platform fees at scale
- ✅ You're comfortable with maintenance

### Choose BigCommerce If:
- ✅ You have 10K+ products
- ✅ You do significant B2B/wholesale
- ✅ You need native multi-currency
- ✅ You want more checkout customization
- ✅ You're already in their ecosystem

## Migration Guide

### Migrating TO Shopify
**Timeline**: 4-8 weeks
**Cost**: $10,000-50,000

**Process**:
1. Audit current store (products, customers, orders)
2. Set up Shopify store with theme
3. Migrate products (CSV or app like Cart2Cart)
4. Migrate customers (with consent)
5. Set up 301 redirects (critical for SEO)
6. Test thoroughly before launch
7. Launch and monitor

**Tools**: Cart2Cart, LitExtension, Excelify

### Migrating FROM Shopify
**Consider carefully** - Shopify is usually the destination, not origin. Common reasons:
- Cost concerns at very high scale ($10M+)
- Need for specific customization
- B2B requirements
