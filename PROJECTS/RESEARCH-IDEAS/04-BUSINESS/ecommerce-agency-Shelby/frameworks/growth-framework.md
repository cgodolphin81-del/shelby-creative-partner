## Phase 2: CONVERSION

### Goal: Maximize percentage of visitors who purchase

### Conversion Funnel Benchmarks

```
10,000 Visitors
     │
     ▼  (2-3% add to cart)
    250 Add to Cart
     │
     ▼  (60-70% proceed)
    160 Reach Checkout
     │
     ▼  (60-70% complete)
    100 Purchases

Overall CVR: 1.0% (typical) → Target: 2.5-3.5%
```

### Conversion Rate by Traffic Source
| Source | Typical CVR | Optimization Potential |
|--------|-------------|------------------------|
| Email | 3-5% | High (segmentation) |
| Organic Search | 2-4% | Medium (landing pages) |
| Paid Search | 2-4% | Medium (keyword intent) |
| Social Paid | 1-3% | High (creative + LP) |
| Direct | 2-4% | Medium (UX improvements) |
| Referral | 2-5% | Low (already qualified) |

### CRO Levers (Priority Order)

#### 1. Page Speed (Impact: 10-20% CVR lift)
- Target: < 3 second load time
- Tools: Google PageSpeed Insights, GTMetrix
- Quick wins: Image optimization, lazy loading, CDN

#### 2. Mobile Optimization (Impact: 15-30% CVR lift)
- 60-80% of traffic is mobile
- Thumb-friendly design
- Simplified mobile checkout
- Mobile page speed critical

#### 3. Product Page Optimization (Impact: 10-25% CVR lift)
**Essential Elements**:
- High-quality images (5-10 per product)
- Video content (demonstration, lifestyle)
- Clear value proposition above fold
- Social proof (reviews, UGC)
- Urgency/scarcity (if authentic)
- Clear shipping/return info
- Prominent, compelling CTA
- FAQ section
- Related products

#### 4. Checkout Optimization (Impact: 20-35% cart recovery)
**Reduce Friction**:
- Guest checkout option
- Auto-fill addresses
- Multiple payment options (Shop Pay, PayPal, Apple Pay)
- Progress indicator
- Trust badges
- Clear shipping costs upfront
- Exit-intent offers

#### 5. Trust & Social Proof (Impact: 10-20% CVR lift)
- Product reviews (with photos)
- Trust badges (secure checkout, guarantees)
- Media mentions
- User-generated content
- Live chat support
- Clear return policy

#### 6. Offer Optimization (Impact: 15-40% CVR lift)
- Free shipping threshold
- Bundle discounts
- First-time buyer offers
- Payment plans (Afterpay, Klarna)
- Limited-time promotions
- Gift with purchase

## Phase 3: RETENTION

### Goal: Maximize repeat purchase rate and customer lifetime

### Retention Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Goal |
|--------|---------|-----------|------|
| Repeat Purchase Rate | Repeat Customers / Total Customers | 20-40% | 35%+ |
| Purchase Frequency | Total Orders / Total Customers | 2-4/year | 4+/year |
| Time Between Orders | Days between purchases | 60-90 days | < 60 days |
| Customer Lifespan | Months as active customer | 12-24 months | 24+ months |
| Churn Rate | Lost Customers / Total Customers | 5-10%/month | < 5%/month |

### Retention Levers

#### 1. Email Marketing (Impact: 20-40% of revenue)
**Essential Flows**:
| Flow | Emails | Trigger | Expected Revenue |
|------|--------|---------|------------------|
| Welcome Series | 5-7 | New subscriber | 15-25% of email rev |
| Abandoned Cart | 3-5 | Cart abandoned | 10-15% of email rev |
| Browse Abandonment | 2-3 | Viewed, no cart | 5-10% of email rev |
| Post-Purchase | 4-6 | After purchase | 10-20% of email rev |
| Win-Back | 3-5 | 60-90 days inactive | 5-10% of email rev |
| VIP/Loyalty | Ongoing | High LTV | 20-30% of email rev |

#### 2. SMS Marketing (Impact: 5-15% of revenue)
**Best Practices**:
- Get explicit consent
- Send 2-4 messages per week max
- Use for: Flash sales, cart abandonment, shipping updates
- Typical CTR: 15-25% (vs. 2-5% email)
- Typical CVR: 5-10%

#### 3. Loyalty Program (Impact: 20-30% increase in LTV)
**Program Structure**:
```
Tier 1: Member (0-999 points)
- Earn 1 point per $1
- Birthday reward
- Early access to sales

Tier 2: VIP (1,000-4,999 points)
- Earn 1.5 points per $1
- Free shipping
- Exclusive products

Tier 3: Elite (5,000+ points)
- Earn 2 points per $1
- Free expedited shipping
- Personal shopper
- Exclusive events
```

**Redemption Options**:
- $10 off per 100 points
- Free products
- Exclusive experiences
- Charitable donations

#### 4. Subscription Model (Impact: 3-5x LTV)
**Best For**: Consumables, replenishable products
**Models**:
- Subscribe & Save (10-20% discount)
- Curated box (monthly/quarterly)
- Membership (exclusive access + perks)

**Metrics to Track**:
- Subscription conversion rate: 5-15% of customers
- Churn rate: < 10% monthly
- LTV: 3-5x one-time buyers

#### 5. Customer Experience (Impact: 10-20% retention lift)
**Touchpoints to Optimize**:
- Unboxing experience
- Follow-up emails
- Customer support (response time < 2 hours)
- Easy returns (pre-paid labels)
- Personalization (product recommendations)
- Community building (social, events)

## Phase 4: LTV OPTIMIZATION

### Goal: Maximize total value per customer

### LTV Fundamentals

**LTV Formula**:
```
LTV = (AOV × Purchase Frequency × Gross Margin) × Customer Lifespan

Example:
AOV: $75
Purchase Frequency: 3/year
Gross Margin: 60%
Customer Lifespan: 2 years

LTV = ($75 × 3 × 0.60) × 2 = $270
```

### LTV Benchmarks by Category

| Category | Avg AOV | Purchase Freq | Avg LTV |
|----------|---------|---------------|---------|
| Beauty | $45-75 | 4-6/year | $400-800 |
| Fashion | $80-150 | 3-5/year | $500-1,200 |
| Home Goods | $100-200 | 2-4/year | $400-1,000 |
| Food/Beverage | $40-80 | 6-12/year | $500-1,500 |
| Health/Wellness | $50-100 | 4-8/year | $400-1,200 |
| Electronics | $200-500 | 1-2/year | $400-1,000 |
| Pet | $50-100 | 4-8/year | $500-1,200 |
| Baby/Kids | $60-120 | 4-6/year | $500-1,000 |

### LTV:CAC Ratio

| Ratio | Status | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| < 1:1 | Unsustainable | Pause acquisition, fix unit economics |
| 1:1 to 2:1 | Concerning | Optimize CAC or improve LTV |
| 3:1 | Healthy | Scale acquisition |
| 4:1+ | Excellent | Aggressive scaling, consider raising prices |

### LTV Optimization Levers

#### 1. Increase AOV (Average Order Value)
**Tactics**:
| Tactic | Implementation | Expected Lift |
|--------|----------------|---------------|
| Free shipping threshold | Set at 20-30% above current AOV | +10-20% |
| Product bundles | Curated bundles at 15-20% discount | +15-30% |
| Upsells (cart) | Related products in cart | +5-15% |
| Upsells (post-purchase) | One-click offers after checkout | +10-20% |
| Cross-sells | "Complete the look" recommendations | +10-20% |
| Payment plans | Afterpay, Klarna, Affirm | +20-40% |
| Quantity breaks | "Buy 2, get 10% off" | +15-25% |

#### 2. Increase Purchase Frequency
**Tactics**:
| Tactic | Implementation | Expected Impact |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| Replenishment reminders | Email/SMS at predicted reorder time | +20-40% |
| Subscription program | Auto-ship with discount | 3-5x frequency |
| New product launches | Exclusive access for customers | +10-20% |
| Seasonal campaigns | Timed promotions | +15-25% |
| Loyalty multipliers | Bonus points periods | +10-20% |
| Win-back campaigns | Targeted offers to lapsed | 5-15% reactivation |

#### 3. Extend Customer Lifespan
**Tactics**:
| Tactic | Implementation | Expected Impact |
|--------|----------------|-----------------|
| Engagement campaigns | Regular valuable content | +20-40% lifespan |
| VIP program | Exclusive benefits | +30-50% lifespan |
| Community building | Social groups, events | +25-40% lifespan |
| Personalization | Tailored recommendations | +15-30% lifespan |
| Surprise & delight | Unexpected gifts/notes | +10-20% lifespan |

#### 4. Improve Margins
**Tactics**:
- Negotiate better supplier rates
- Optimize shipping costs
- Reduce return rate (better product info, sizing)
- Increase prices strategically (5-10% often has minimal churn)
- Reduce discounting (focus on value, not price)

### LTV Cohort Analysis

**Track by Acquisition Month**:
```
Cohort: January 2024 Customers
- Month 0: 1,000 customers, $75 AOV, $75,000 revenue
- Month 1: 350 repeat (35%), $80 AOV, $28,000 revenue
- Month 2: 200 repeat (20%), $85 AOV, $17,000 revenue
- Month 3: 150 repeat (15%), $85 AOV, $12,750 revenue
- ...
- 12-month LTV: $185,000 / 1,000 = $185 per customer
```

**Action**: Compare cohorts to identify improvements or degradation in LTV over time.

## Summary: The Growth Equation

```
Revenue = Traffic × CVR × AOV × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan

Focus Areas:
1. Traffic → Acquisition (Paid, Organic, Referral)
2. CVR → CRO (UX, Copy, Trust, Speed)
3. AOV → Merchandising (Bundles, Upsells, Thresholds)
4. Frequency → Retention (Email, SMS, Loyalty)
5. Lifespan → Experience (Product, Support, Community)

Target LTV:CAC Ratio: 3:1 minimum, 4:1+ ideal
```
