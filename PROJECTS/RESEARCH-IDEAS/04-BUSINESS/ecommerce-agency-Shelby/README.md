## 💰 Pricing Strategy

### Three Pricing Models

**1. Project-Based**
- One-time builds (Shopify setup, email flow setup)
- Good for: Quick cash, proving value
- Typical: $5K-50K per project

**2. Retainer**
- Ongoing management (ads, email, CRO)
- Good for: Predictable revenue, deep partnerships
- Typical: $5K-50K/month

**3. Performance-Based**
- Revenue share, ROAS bonuses
- Good for: Confident delivery, aligned incentives
- Typical: Base + 10-20% of incremental revenue

*See `pricing/pricing-models.md` for complete pricing guide*

### Pricing Principles

1. **Value-based, not hour-based** - Price on outcomes, not time
2. **Start premium** - Cheap clients are the worst clients
3. **Raise prices annually** - 10-15% per year minimum
4. **Fire bad clients** - Replace with better-fit clients at higher prices

## 📈 Growth Framework

### The Growth Flywheel

```
        ACQUISITION
      (New Customers)
            │
            ▼
        CONVERSION
     (Optimize CRO)
            │
            ▼
        RETENTION
   (Repeat Purchase)
            │
            ▼
           LTV
  (Maximize Value)
            │
            └──────────┐
                       │
                       ▼
                  REFERRALS
                (Advocacy Loop)
                       │
                       └──────────┐
                                  │
                                  ▼
                        (Back to Acquisition)
```

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad Spend | 3.0-5.0 |
| CAC | Ad Spend / New Customers | < LTV/3 |
| LTV:CAC | Lifetime Value / CAC | 3:1+ |
| Email Revenue % | Email Revenue / Total Revenue | 25-40% |
| Conversion Rate | Orders / Sessions | 2.5-3.5% |
| Repeat Rate | Repeat Customers / Total | 35%+ |
| Churn Rate | Lost Clients / Total | < 8%/month |

## 📝 Case Studies

### Why Case Studies Matter

1. **Social Proof** - Results speak louder than claims
2. **Sales Tool** - Close deals faster with proof
3. **SEO** - Rank for "[service] + case study"
4. **Content** - Repurpose into blog posts, social, emails

### Case Study Formula

```
Great Case Study = Specific Client + Clear Problem + 
                   Your Solution + Measurable Results + 
                   Authentic Testimonial
```

### Minimum Viable Case Study

- **Headline**: "How We [Result] for [Client]"
- **Problem**: 2-3 sentences on their challenge
- **Solution**: What you did (bullet points)
- **Results**: Before/after metrics (specific numbers)
- **Testimonial**: 1 paragraph from client
- **CTA**: "Want similar results? [Contact us]"

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Strategy Mistakes

1. **No niche** - "We help everyone" = "We help no one"
2. **Underpricing** - Cheap clients are the most demanding
3. **No positioning** - Commodity = price competition
4. **Chasing shiny objects** - Master 1-2 channels first

### Delivery Mistakes

1. **Overpromising** - Set realistic expectations
2. **No reporting** - Clients need to see progress
3. **Reactive communication** - Be proactive
4. **No documentation** - Create SOPs as you go

### Business Mistakes

1. **No pipeline** - Always be prospecting
2. **Client concentration** - No client >10% of revenue
3. **Key person risk** - Document everything
4. **No cash buffer** - 3-6 months operating expenses
