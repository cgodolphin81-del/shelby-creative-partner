## PROMPT ENGINEERING TIPS

### For Better Results:

1. **Be Specific About Product Type**
   - "Platform sneaker" not just "shoe"
   - "Stiletto heel" not just "heel"

2. **Describe Textures Clearly**
   - "Velvet texture" vs "soft"
   - "Iridescent scale pattern" vs "shiny"

3. **Specify Lighting**
   - "Studio lighting" for clean product shots
   - "Dramatic lighting" for mood
   - "Natural lighting" for lifestyle feel

4. **Use Art References**
   - "Renaissance painting style"
   - "Vintage poster art"
   - "Art Nouveau influence"

5. **Layer Details**
   - Start with base design
   - Add secondary elements
   - Include accent details
   - Specify color palette
   - Define mood/aesthetic

### Common Issues & Fixes:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Design too busy | Add "minimalist" or reduce element count |
| Colors muddy | Specify exact color names/hex codes |
| Not shoe-shaped | Add "realistic shoe silhouette" |
| Too generic | Add more unique specific details |
| Wrong angle | Specify "side profile" or "top-down view" |

3. **Refine** — Upscale and adjust in Photoshop/Canva
4. **Mockup** — Apply to shoe templates
5. **Review** — Check print quality and placement
6. **Upload** — Add to POD platform
7. **Photograph** — Order sample and shoot lifestyle photos
