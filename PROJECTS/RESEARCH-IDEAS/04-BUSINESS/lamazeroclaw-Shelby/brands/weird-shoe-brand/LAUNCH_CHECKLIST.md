## 🚨 COMMON PITFALLS TO AVOID

1. **Not ordering samples** → Always test quality first
2. **Underpricing** → Factor in ALL costs (POD, shipping, ads, returns)
3. **Launching with too few products** → Minimum 20 for credibility
4. **Ignoring email list** → Start building Day 1
5. **Perfectionism paralysis** → Launch at 80%, improve after
6. **Not testing checkout** → Buy from yourself multiple times
7. **Underestimating customer service** → Have templates ready
8. **Giving up too soon** → First month is just the beginning
9. **Not tracking metrics** → What gets measured gets improved
10. **Trying to do everything alone** → Outsource what you can
