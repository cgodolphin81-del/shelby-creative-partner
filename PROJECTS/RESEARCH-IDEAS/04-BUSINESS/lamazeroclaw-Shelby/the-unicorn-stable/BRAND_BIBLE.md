## 5. PRICING STRATEGY

### Cost Breakdown (Example: Premium Sneaker)

| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| POD Base Cost | $45 |
| Custom Design | $0 (in-house AI) |
| Packaging | $3 |
| Shipping (averaged) | $8 |
| **Total Cost** | **$56** |

### Retail Pricing Tiers

| Product Type | Retail Price | Margin |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| Basic Sneakers | $85-95 | 50-60% |
| Premium Sneakers | $110-130 | 55-65% |
| Platform Heels | $120-150 | 60-70% |
| Ankle Boots | $130-160 | 60-70% |
| Statement Boots | $150-200 | 65-75% |
| Accessories (bags, socks) | $35-65 | 50-60% |

### Comparison: Irregular Choice vs. Unicorn Stable

| Metric | Irregular Choice | Unicorn Stable |
|--------|-----------------|----------------|
| Avg Sneaker Price | $200-350 | $85-130 |
| Avg Heel Price | $300-500 | $120-180 |
| Avg Boot Price | $350-600 | $130-200 |
| Positioning | Luxury boutique | Accessible fantasy |
| Target | High-end fashion | Mass-premium |

**Competitive Advantage:** 40-60% lower prices while maintaining unique, statement designs

### Psychological Pricing Tactics

- **Charm Pricing:** $89, $119, $149 (not $90, $120, $150)
- **Tiered Pricing:** Good ($89) / Better ($119) / Best ($149)
- **Bundle Discounts:** "Complete the Look" — shoe + bag = 15% off
- **Free Shipping Threshold:** "Free shipping over $100" (encourages 2-item orders)
- **Limited Edition Premium:** +20-30% for limited drops
