## 🎯 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TACTICS

### LinkedIn
- Post at 8 AM EST (commute time)
- Use document posts for carousels
- Engage with comments within 1 hour
- Tag relevant people (with permission)

### Twitter/X
- Thread format performs best
- Post at 8 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM EST
- Reply to trending AI topics
- Use GIFs/screenshots in replies

### Instagram
- Reels > Carousels > Static posts
- Stories daily (polls, Q&A, countdowns)
- Use 10-15 hashtags per post
- Engage with followers' stories

### TikTok
- 15-30 second videos perform best
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds (when relevant)
- Post at 12 PM and 7 PM EST

### Email
- Subject lines: Urgency + benefit
- Keep body copy under 200 words
- One CTA per email
- Segment by interest (if possible)
