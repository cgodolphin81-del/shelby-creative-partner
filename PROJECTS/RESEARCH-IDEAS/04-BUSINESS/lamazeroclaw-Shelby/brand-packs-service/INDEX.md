## 💡 Quick Tips

### Color Selection
- Use 60-30-10 rule (primary-secondary-accent)
- Test for accessibility (contrast ratio 4.5:1)
- Consider your industry norms
- Think about psychology of colors

### Typography
- Limit to 2-3 fonts maximum
- Ensure readability at all sizes
- Check font licenses for commercial use
- Test on multiple devices

### Logo Usage
- Always use vector formats when possible
- Maintain clear space around logo
- Don't recolor unless license allows
- Test at various sizes

### Social Media
- Post consistently
- Engage with your audience
- Use platform-specific best practices
- Track and optimize performance
