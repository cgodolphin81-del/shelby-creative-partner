## 📌 Pinterest Marketing

### Why Pinterest?
- Visual platform (perfect for design products)
- Long content lifespan (pins last months/years)
- High purchase intent users
- Drives qualified traffic

### Profile Setup

**Business Account:**
1. Convert to business account (free)
2. Claim website
3. Enable rich pins
4. Add profile:
   - Name: "Brand Packs Service"
   - Bio: "Professional brand identity packs for entrepreneurs. 200+ color palettes, 100+ logo templates, social media kits. Instant download. 🎨"
   - Profile image: 165x165px logo
   - Cover: 800x400px banner

**Board Strategy (15-20 boards):**
1. Brand Identity Inspiration
2. Logo Design Ideas
3. Color Palette Inspiration
4. [Industry] Branding (each industry)
5. Social Media Templates
6. Business Branding Tips
7. Entrepreneur Resources
8. Small Business Marketing
9. Canva Templates
10. Typography Inspiration
11. Brand Guidelines Examples
12. Startup Branding
13. Rebranding Ideas
14. DIY Branding
15. Design Trends 2026

### Pin Strategy

**Pin Types:**
1. **Product Pins:** Direct product promotion
2. **Idea Pins:** Multi-page tutorials
3. **Standard Pins:** Blog posts, tips
4. **Video Pins:** Quick tutorials

**Pin Design Specs:**
- Standard: 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio)
- Square: 1000x1000px
- Story: 1080x1920px
- Video: 1080x1920px (15 sec min)

**Pin Templates (Create 50+ variations):**

1. **Product Showcase:**
   - Hero image of brand pack
   - Text: "[Industry] Brand Pack"
   - Subtext: "100+ Logo Templates"
   - CTA: "Shop Now"

2. **Tip Graphics:**
   - Title: "5 Branding Mistakes to Avoid"
   - Numbered list
   - Brand colors
   - Link to blog/product

3. **Before/After:**
   - Left: "Before" (amateur design)
   - Right: "After" (professional pack)
   - Text: "Transform Your Brand"

4. **Color Palette Pins:**
   - Display 5 colors
   - Show hex codes
   - Title: "[Mood] Color Palette"
   - Link to product

5. **Quote Graphics:**
   - Inspiring business quote
   - Brand fonts
   - Subtle branding
   - Link to relevant product

### Posting Schedule

**Frequency:**
- Minimum: 5 pins/day
- Optimal: 15-25 pins/day
- Mix: 80% valuable content, 20% promotional

**Best Times:**
- Weekdays: 8-11 PM
- Weekends: 2-4 PM, 8-11 PM
- Use Pinterest Analytics to optimize

**Tools:**
- Tailwind (scheduling, tribes)
- Canva (pin creation)
- Pinterest native scheduler

### Pinterest SEO

**Keyword Research:**
- Use Pinterest search bar
- Check related searches
- Use tools: Pinterest Trends, Keyword Tool

**Optimization:**
- Titles: Include keywords naturally
- Descriptions: 2-3 sentences with keywords
- Alt text: Describe image
- Board descriptions: Keyword-rich
- Profile: Include main keywords

**Top Keywords:**
- brand identity
- logo design
- branding kit
- business branding
- brand guidelines
- logo templates
- color palette
- brand design
- small business branding
- DIY branding

### Pinterest Ads (Optional)

**Campaign Types:**
1. **Awareness:** Brand visibility
2. **Consideration:** Traffic, engagement
3. **Conversion:** Sales

**Budget:**
- Start: $10-20/day
- Scale: Based on ROAS

**Targeting:**
- Interests: Entrepreneurship, Small Business, Design
- Keywords: Branding, Logo, Business
- Audiences: Website visitors, engagers
