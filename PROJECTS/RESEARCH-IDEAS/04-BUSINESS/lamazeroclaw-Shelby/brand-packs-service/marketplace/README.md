## 🎯 Gumroad Setup

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to gumroad.com
2. Click "Start Selling"
3. Sign up with email or social
4. Complete profile:
   - Profile picture
   - Bio (2-3 sentences)
   - Social links
   - Payment info (Stripe/PayPal)

### Step 2: Configure Store
**Store Settings:**
- Store name: "Brand Packs Service"
- URL: gumroad.com/brandpacksservice
- Currency: USD (primary)
- Tax settings: Enable VAT if EU customers
