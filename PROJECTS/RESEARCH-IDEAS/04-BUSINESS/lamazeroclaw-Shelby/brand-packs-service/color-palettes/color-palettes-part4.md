## 🎨 Usage Tips

1. **60-30-10 Rule**: Use primary (60%), secondary (30%), accent (10%)
2. **Accessibility**: Ensure sufficient contrast for text
3. **Consistency**: Use same palette across all brand materials
4. **Testing**: Test colors in different lighting conditions
