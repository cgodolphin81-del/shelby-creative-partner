# 💰 Finance & Investment Brand Pack

Complete brand identity package for financial advisors, investment firms, accountants, and fintech companies.

---

## 📋 Brand Overview

**Target Audience:** Investors, businesses, individuals seeking financial guidance
**Brand Personality:** Trustworthy, professional, knowledgeable, secure
**Primary Use Cases:** Website, presentations, reports, social media, client materials
