## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Gym/Brand name] empowers people to become their strongest selves.
We provide [services] in a supportive, results-driven environment.

Mission: Help everyone achieve their fitness goals
Vision: Create the most supportive fitness community
Values: Excellence, Community, Integrity, Results
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 1 inch width (print), 100px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of logo
- **Don'ts:** Don't stretch, recolor, add effects

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Main brand (60%)
- **Secondary:** Supporting (30%)
- **Accent:** CTAs, highlights (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Oswald Bold, 48px
- **H2:** Oswald, 36px
- **H3:** Montserrat SemiBold, 24px
- **Body:** Open Sans, 16px
- **Caption:** Open Sans Light, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Motivating, supportive, direct
- **Language:** Action-oriented, inclusive, positive
- **Avoid:** Body shaming, unrealistic promises, negativity

### 6. Imagery Style
- Diverse body types
- Action shots
- Authentic moments
- High energy
- Consistent editing
