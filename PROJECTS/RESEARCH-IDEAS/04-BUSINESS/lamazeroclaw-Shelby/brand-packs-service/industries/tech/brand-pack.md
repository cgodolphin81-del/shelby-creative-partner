## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Company name] is building [product] to help [target audience] 
achieve [outcome]. We believe in [core belief].

Mission: [Your mission]
Vision: [Your vision]
Values: Innovation, Transparency, Customer-First, Excellence
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 0.5 inch width (print), 60px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of logo mark
- **Don'ts:** Don't recolor, stretch, add effects

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Main brand (60%)
- **Secondary:** Supporting (30%)
- **Accent:** CTAs, highlights (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Inter Bold, 48px
- **H2:** Inter SemiBold, 36px
- **H3:** Inter Medium, 24px
- **Body:** Roboto, 16px
- **Caption:** Roboto Light, 14px
- **Code:** JetBrains Mono, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Confident, clear, helpful
- **Language:** Simple, direct, jargon-free
- **Avoid:** Hype, overpromising, complexity

### 6. Imagery Style
- Clean, modern aesthetic
- Diverse representation
- Product-focused
- Authentic moments
- Consistent filters
