## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Brand name] empowers everyone to feel confident and beautiful.
We create [products/services] that [unique benefit].

Mission: Empower through beauty
Vision: Make quality beauty accessible to all
Values: Quality, Inclusivity, Sustainability, Innovation
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 0.5 inch width (print), 60px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of logo element
- **Don'ts:** Don't recolor, stretch, add effects

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Main brand elements (60%)
- **Secondary:** Supporting elements (30%)
- **Accent:** CTAs, highlights (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Playfair Display Bold, 48px
- **H2:** Playfair Display, 36px
- **H3:** Montserrat SemiBold, 24px
- **Body:** Lato, 16px
- **Caption:** Lato Light, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Empowering, friendly, expert
- **Language:** Inclusive, clear, inspiring
- **Avoid:** Unrealistic promises, exclusive language

### 6. Imagery Style
- Diverse models/skin tones
- Natural lighting
- Clean backgrounds
- Consistent filters
- Authentic moments
