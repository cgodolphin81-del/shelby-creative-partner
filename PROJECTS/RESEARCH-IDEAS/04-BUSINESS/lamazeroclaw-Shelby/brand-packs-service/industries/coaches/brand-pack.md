## 📄 Brand Guidelines Template

### 1. Brand Story
```
[Your coaching business name] exists to help [target audience] 
achieve [desired outcome] through [your unique approach].

Mission: [Your mission statement]
Vision: [Your vision for clients]
Values: [3-5 core values]
```

### 2. Logo Usage
- **Minimum Size:** 1 inch width (print), 100px (digital)
- **Clear Space:** Equal to height of logo on all sides
- **Don'ts:** Don't stretch, rotate, recolor, or add effects

### 3. Color Application
- **Primary:** Use for main brand elements (60%)
- **Secondary:** Use for supporting elements (30%)
- **Accent:** Use sparingly for CTAs and highlights (10%)

### 4. Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** Playfair Display Bold, 48px
- **H2:** Playfair Display, 36px
- **H3:** Montserrat SemiBold, 24px
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro, 16px
- **Caption:** Source Sans Pro Light, 14px

### 5. Voice & Tone
- **Tone:** Encouraging, professional, authentic
- **Language:** Clear, actionable, empathetic
- **Avoid:** Jargon, overly salesy language, negativity

### 6. Image Style
- Authentic, natural lighting
- Diverse representation
- Professional but approachable
- Consistent filters/editing
