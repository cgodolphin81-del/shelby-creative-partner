## ⚠️ RISKS & CONSIDERATIONS

### Legal/Ethical
- **Obituary/Grief services:** Handle with extreme sensitivity
- **Pet/Interior AI:** Don't make medical/structural claims
- **Time Capsule:** Legal entity for long-term trust
- **Breakup Writer:** Add mental health resource links

### Technical
- **API costs:** Monitor usage, implement rate limits
- **Scalability:** Start serverless, scale as needed
- **Data privacy:** Be transparent, especially with sensitive topics

### Market
- **AI fatigue:** Focus on outcomes, not the AI itself
- **Competition:** Differentiate on niche + experience, not features
- **Platform risk:** Don't build entirely on one API (have backups)
