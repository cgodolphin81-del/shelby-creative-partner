# Video Types Guide

Complete guide to professional video types for client services.

---

## 1. Explainer Videos

**Purpose:** Simplify complex concepts, products, or services in 60-90 seconds.

**Best For:**
- SaaS product launches
- Service explanations
- Process demonstrations
- Educational content

**Key Elements:**
- Hook in first 5 seconds
- Problem statement (10-15 sec)
- Solution introduction (20-30 sec)
- How it works (30-40 sec)
- Call-to-action (10-15 sec)

**Production Notes:**
- Motion graphics heavy
- Voiceover essential
- Background music required
- Brand colors throughout

**Typical Budget:** $3,000 - $8,000

---

## 2. Testimonial Videos

**Purpose:** Build trust through authentic customer stories.

**Best For:**
- Social proof
- Case studies
- Brand credibility
- Sales enablement

**Key Elements:**
- Customer introduction (5 sec)
- Challenge they faced (15 sec)
- Solution found (20 sec)
- Results achieved (20 sec)
- Recommendation (10 sec)

**Production Notes:**
- Natural lighting preferred
- Lav mic essential
- B-roll of customer using product
- Lower thirds for names/titles

**Typical Budget:** $2,000 - $6,000

---

## 3. Product Videos

**Purpose:** Showcase features, benefits, and use cases.

**Best For:**
- E-commerce
- Product launches
- Feature announcements
- Demo reels

**Key Elements:**
- Product reveal (5 sec)
- Key features (30-40 sec)
- Benefits demonstration (30 sec)
- Use cases (20 sec)
- Purchase CTA (10 sec)

**Production Notes:**
- Clean backgrounds
- Multiple angles
- Close-up shots
- Screen recordings for digital products

**Typical Budget:** $4,000 - $12,000

---

## 4. Social Media Videos

**Purpose:** Drive engagement and shares on social platforms.

**Best For:**
- Brand awareness
- Viral content
- Community building
- Campaign promotion

**Platform Specifications:**

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Duration | Max Size |
|----------|--------------|----------|----------|
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 15-90 sec | 4GB |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 15-180 sec | 287.6MB |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 15-60 sec | 100MB |
| LinkedIn | 1:1 or 16:9 | 30 sec - 10 min | 5GB |
| Facebook | 1:1 or 16:9 | 1-120 min | 4GB |
| Twitter/X | 16:9 | 2 min 20 sec | 512MB |

**Production Notes:**
- Captions mandatory (85% watch without sound)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Vertical format priority
- Trending audio consideration

**Typical Budget:** $1,500 - $5,000 per video

---

## 5. Course/Educational Videos

**Purpose:** Teach skills or knowledge systematically.

**Best For:**
- Online courses
- Training programs
- Workshops
- Webinars (recorded)

**Key Elements:**
- Learning objectives (10 sec)
- Content delivery (3-15 min per module)
- Examples/demonstrations
- Summary/key takeaways
- Next steps

**Production Notes:**
- Consistent framing
- Good audio critical
- Screen recording common
- Chapter markers helpful
- Downloadable resources

**Typical Budget:** $5,000 - $25,000 (full course)

---

## 6. Webinar Videos

**Purpose:** Live or recorded educational presentations with Q&A.

**Best For:**
- Lead generation
- Thought leadership
- Product demos
- Training sessions

**Key Elements:**
- Introduction/housekeeping (2-3 min)
- Presenter intro (1-2 min)
- Main content (30-45 min)
- Q&A session (15-20 min)
- CTA/close (3-5 min)

**Production Notes:**
- Multi-camera setup recommended
- Slides integration
- Live chat moderation
- Recording backup essential
- Follow-up email with replay

**Typical Budget:** $3,000 - $10,000

---

## 7. Brand Story Videos

**Purpose:** Connect emotionally with audience through company narrative.

**Best For:**
- About pages
- Investor pitches
- Brand campaigns
- Company culture

**Key Elements:**
- Origin story
- Mission/vision
- Team highlights
- Customer impact
- Future aspirations

**Production Notes:**
- Cinematic quality
- Emotional music
- Authentic interviews
- B-roll of workplace
- 2-4 minutes ideal length

**Typical Budget:** $8,000 - $30,000

---

## 8. Event Videos

**Purpose:** Capture and repurpose live events.

**Best For:**
- Conferences
- Product launches
- Company meetings
- Trade shows

**Deliverables:**
- Full event recording
- Highlight reel (2-3 min)
- Speaker sessions (individual)
- Social clips (15-30 sec each)
- Photo gallery

**Production Notes:**
- Multiple camera angles
- Audio mixing critical
- Lighting for venue
- Live streaming option
- Quick turnaround for highlights

**Typical Budget:** $5,000 - $50,000+

---

## 9. Animated Videos

**Purpose:** Visual storytelling through animation.

**Styles:**
- 2D Animation
- 3D Animation
- Motion Graphics
- Whiteboard
- Stop Motion

**Best For:**
- Abstract concepts
- Character-driven stories
- Brand mascots
- Explainer content

**Production Timeline:** 4-8 weeks typical

**Typical Budget:** $5,000 - $40,000

---

## 10. Interview/Podcast Videos

**Purpose:** Long-form conversation content.

**Best For:**
- Podcasts
- Expert interviews
- Panel discussions
- AMA sessions

**Production Notes:**
- Multi-cam (2-3 angles)
- Lav mics each person
- Consistent lighting
- Background branding
- Chapter markers

**Typical Budget:** $2,000 - $8,000 per episode
