
## Contingency Planning

### Common Delays & Solutions

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Client feedback delay | High | Medium | Build buffer days, set clear deadlines |
| Weather issues (outdoor) | Medium | High | Have indoor backup location |
| Talent cancellation | Low | High | Have backup talent on standby |
| Equipment failure | Low | High | Backup equipment on set |
| Revision creep | Medium | Medium | Clear scope, change order process |
| Illness | Low | High | Cross-trained team members |

### Buffer Days

```
Built-in buffers:
• Day 6 (Saturday Week 1) – Pre-prod catch-up
• Day 13 (Saturday Week 2) – Production catch-up
• Day 20 (Saturday Week 3) – Post-prod catch-up
• Day 27-28 (Weekend Week 4) – Delivery buffer

Total buffer: 4-5 days available
```

### Escalation Path

```
Issue arises → Team lead assesses → Producer notified → 
Client informed (if impacts timeline) → Solution implemented → 
Documentation updated
```
