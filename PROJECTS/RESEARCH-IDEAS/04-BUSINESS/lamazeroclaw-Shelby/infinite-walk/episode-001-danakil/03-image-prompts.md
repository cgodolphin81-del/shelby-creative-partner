## STYLE NOTES FOR ALL PROMPTS

**Color Palette Priorities:**
- Neon yellows and acid greens (sulfur)
- Rust oranges and deep reds (iron oxide)
- Pure whites (salt)
- Deep blacks (lava rock)
- Glowing orange-red (lava)
- Harsh blue sky (day) / deep black with stars (night)

**Mood Keywords:**
- Otherworldly
- Hostile beauty
- Geological power
- Human insignificance
- Extreme
- Authentic
- Unforgiving
- Sacred

**Technical Notes:**
- Favor natural lighting (harsh sun for day, lava glow for night)
- Avoid over-saturation — the colors are real, don't make them cartoonish
- Include scale references where possible (human figures, gear)
- Heat haze is a key atmospheric element
- Steam and gas vents add dynamism
