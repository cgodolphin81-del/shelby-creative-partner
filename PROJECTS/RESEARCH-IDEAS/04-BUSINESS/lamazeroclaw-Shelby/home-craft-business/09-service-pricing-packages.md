## Why Package Services

**Benefits of packaging:**

- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Easier to sell (not hourly)
- ✅ Predictable income
- ✅ Scope is defined (prevents scope creep)
- ✅ Clients know what to expect
- ✅ Easier to market
- ✅ Can productize delivery

**vs. Hourly Consulting:**

| Hourly | Packaged |
|--------|----------|
| Client watches the clock | Client focuses on value |
| Income capped by hours | Income based on value |
| Scope can expand | Scope is defined |
| Harder to sell | Easier to sell |
| Commodity pricing | Value-based pricing |

**2. Content Marketing**

**4. Paid Advertising**
- Instagram/Facebook ads
- Pinterest ads
- Google ads (for specific services)

**5. Speaking & Teaching**
- Workshops
- Webinars
- Conference speaking
- Online courses

### Sales Process

**Step 1: Discovery Call (30 min, free)**
- Learn about their business
- Understand their goals
- Identify challenges
- Determine fit
- Explain your packages

**Step 2: Proposal (within 24 hours)**
- Customized to their needs
- Clear investment
- Timeline
- What's included
- Testimonials

**Step 3: Follow-Up**
- Check in after 2-3 days
- Answer questions
- Address concerns
- Close the sale

**Step 4: Onboarding**
- Contract signed
- Invoice paid
- Welcome packet sent
- First call scheduled

### Sales Scripts

**Discovery Call Opening:**
```
"Thanks for hopping on a call with me! I'm excited to 
learn more about your business. 

To make the best use of our time, I'd love to hear:
- What got you interested in working together?
- Where is your business right now?
- Where do you want it to be in 6-12 months?
- What's your biggest challenge right now?

Let's start there!"
```

**Package Recommendation:**
```
"Based on what you've shared, I think the [Package Name] 
would be the best fit for you. Here's why...

[Explain why it matches their situation]

The investment is $X, and based on what you've told me 
about [their goal], you should see ROI within [timeframe].

Does that feel like the right fit for where you are?"
```

**Handling Price Objections:**
```
"I understand that's a significant investment. Let me ask:

What would it mean for your business if you could 
[achieve their goal]?

What's the cost of NOT making this change?

Many of my clients were hesitant about the investment 
too, but they found that [specific result] made it 
worthwhile.

Would a payment plan make this more manageable?"
```

## Delivery Systems

### Onboarding Process

**When client signs up:**

1. **Send welcome email** (automated)
   - Thank you
   - What to expect
   - Next steps

2. **Send contract** (HelloSign, DocuSign)
   - Scope of work
   - Payment terms
   - Policies

3. **Send invoice** (Stripe, PayPal, Wave)
   - First payment
   - Payment plan options

4. **Send welcome packet** (PDF or Notion)
   - Your story
   - How we'll work together
   - Communication expectations
   - Resources to review before first call

5. **Schedule first call** (Calendly)
   - Send calendar link
   - Include Zoom link
   - Add prep questions

6. **Prep for first call**
   - Review their application/intake form
   - Review their business (website, social, shop)
   - Prepare notes and questions

### Call Structure

**60-Minute Call:**
- 5 min: Check-in, personal
- 10 min: Review action items from last call
- 35 min: Deep work on agenda items
- 10 min: New action items, wrap-up

**90-Minute Call:**
- 5 min: Check-in
- 15 min: Review
- 55 min: Deep work
- 15 min: Action items, wrap-up

### Between-Call Support

**Email:**
- Respond within 48 hours (business days)
- Clear, actionable advice
- Link to resources when relevant

**Voxer (if included):**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Voice messages welcome
- Quick questions and wins

**Shared Documents:**
- Google Docs for collaborative work
- Comment and suggest mode
- Version history

## Client Management

### Intake Form

**Questions to ask before starting:**

```
BUSINESS INFORMATION
- Business name
- Website/social/shop links
- How long have you been in business?
- Current monthly revenue?
- What do you sell?

GOALS
- What are your top 3 business goals for the next 6 months?
- What does success look like for you?
- What's your biggest frustration right now?

WORKING TOGETHER
- Why do you want to work with me specifically?
- What have you tried before?
- What support do you need most?
- How do you prefer to communicate?

LOGISTICS
- What's your budget for this investment?
- What's your timeline?
- Any upcoming deadlines or events?
```

### Contracts

**Key sections:**
- Scope of work (what's included)
- Payment terms
- Cancellation policy
- Confidentiality
- Intellectual property
- Liability limitation
- Termination clause

**Use a template from:**
- HelloSign
- LawDepot
- Local attorney (best option)

### Policies

**Cancellation:**
- 24-hour notice required for rescheduling
- Missed calls without notice = forfeited
- 2+ missed calls = contract termination

**Refunds:**
- No refunds after first session (Starter)
- No refunds after strategy intensive (Scale)
- Pro-rated for unforeseen circumstances

**Communication:**
- Email response: 48 hours
- Voxer response: 24 hours
- Business hours: Mon-Fri, 9am-5pm [your timezone]

**Homework:**
- Action items are expected to be completed
- Progress affects results
- I'm here to support, not do the work for you

### Getting Results

**Keys to client success:**

1. **Clear expectations** from the start
2. **Actionable assignments** after each call
3. **Accountability** (review last week's actions)
4. **Celebrating wins** (acknowledge progress)
5. **Course correction** (adjust if something's not working)
6. **Resource provision** (give them tools to succeed)

### Collecting Testimonials

**Ask after they see results:**

```
Subject: Quick favor? 🙏

Hi [Name],

Would you be willing to share a few words about your 
experience working together? It would mean the world

Just reply with 2-3 sentences about:
- What it was like working with me
- What results you achieved

[Your name]
```

**Follow up:**
- Ask for before/after numbers if possible
- Get permission to use their name/photo
- Add to your website and marketing materials

## Scaling Your Services

### When You're Booked Solid

**Options:**

1. **Raise prices** (easiest, immediate)
2. **Group coaching** (serve more people at lower price point)
3. **Hire associate coaches** (train others to deliver your method)
4. **Create course** (passive income, serve unlimited people)
5. **Waitlist** (create demand, maintain quality)

### Group Coaching Model

**Structure:**
- 6-10 people per cohort
- Same content as 1:1, delivered in group
- 2x/month group calls
- Private community (Facebook, Slack)
- Lower price point ($500-1,500 vs. $2,000-5,000)

**Benefits:**
- Serve more people
- Community aspect
- Higher profit margin
- Less 1:1 time

**Drawbacks:**
- Less personalized
- Scheduling challenges
- Group dynamics to manage

### Course Creation

**When to create:**
- You've delivered 1:1 many times
- Content is consistent
- Demand exceeds your capacity
- Want passive income

**Pricing:**
- Self-study: $200-500
- With group support: $500-1,500
- With 1:1 add-on: $1,000-3,000
