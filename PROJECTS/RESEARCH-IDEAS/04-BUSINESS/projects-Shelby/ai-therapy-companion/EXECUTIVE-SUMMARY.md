# MindfulAI - Executive Summary

**Investment Opportunity: AI-Powered Mental Health Platform**

---

## The Problem

**Mental Health Crisis:**
- 1 in 5 adults experience mental illness annually
- 60% receive no treatment (barriers: cost, access, stigma)
- Average wait time for therapy: 3-6 weeks
- Therapy cost: $100-250/session (often not covered)
- Crisis lines: Overwhelmed, understaffed

**Gap in Market:**
- Existing apps: Either chatbots without depth OR expensive therapy
- No seamless continuum from self-care to professional help
- AI technology ready, but no clinically-validated implementation

## Business Model

**Three-Tier Subscription:**

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| Free | $0 | Everyone | 5 conversations/day, basic tools, crisis resources |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Regular users | Unlimited AI, full library, analytics, offline |
| Therapy Connect | $49.99/mo | Need human support | Everything + 2 therapy sessions/mo, messaging |

**Unit Economics:**
```
Pro Tier:
- LTV: $180 (18-month retention)

- LTV:CAC: 16:1 ✓
```
