# Shelby VR Art Gallery - Launch Plan & First 90 Days

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** March 2026  
**Audience:** Operations Team, Marketing, Leadership  

---

## LAUNCH OVERVIEW

### Launch Philosophy

**"Soft launch to learn, hard launch to earn."**

We'll launch in phases, learning from real users before full public launch. This minimizes risk, builds word-of-mouth, and ensures quality.

### Launch Phases

```
Phase 0: Internal Testing (Week 1-2)
    └── Team + close collaborators only
    
Phase 1: Friends & Family (Week 3-4)
    └── 50-100 trusted users, feedback focus
    
Phase 2: Beta Launch (Week 5-8)
    └── 500 users, invite-only, iterate based on data
    
Phase 3: Public Launch (Week 9+)
    └── Full marketing, open registration
```

## PHASE 0: INTERNAL TESTING (Weeks 1-2)

### Goals
- Identify critical bugs
- Test core user flows
- Validate technical infrastructure
- Refine onboarding experience

### Participants
- Development team (8 people)
- Shelby + creative team (3 people)
- Trusted advisors (5 people)
- **Total: ~16 users**

### Testing Focus

**Week 1: Technical Stability**
- [ ] Can users create accounts?
- [ ] Does VR navigation work smoothly?
- [ ] Are artworks loading correctly?
- [ ] Is multiplayer functional (20 users)?
- [ ] Do payments process correctly?
- [ ] Is analytics tracking properly?

**Week 2: User Experience**
- [ ] Is onboarding intuitive?
- [ ] Can users find artworks easily?
- [ ] Are UI elements readable in VR?
- [ ] Is audio at appropriate levels?
- [ ] Are there any motion sickness triggers?
- [ ] How long do users stay engaged?

### Daily Schedule

```
9:00 AM  - Standup, review overnight issues
9:30 AM  - Focused testing session 1
11:30 AM - Bug triage + fixes
2:00 PM  - Focused testing session 2
4:00 PM  - Bug triage + fixes
5:30 PM  - Daily report + plan for tomorrow
```

### Success Criteria
- Zero critical bugs (crashes, payment failures)
- <5 minor bugs per day
- 80%+ task completion rate
- Average session length >15 minutes
- Team confidence in stability

## PHASE 1: FRIENDS & FAMILY (Weeks 3-4)

### Goals
- Test with real (but forgiving) users
- Gather qualitative feedback
- Refine onboarding flow
- Build initial testimonials

### Participants
- Friends of team (30 people)
- Artist community contacts (20 people)
- VR enthusiast friends (20 people)
- Potential early collectors (10 people)
- **Total: ~80 users**

### Recruitment
- Personal invitations from team
- Private Discord channel for feedback
- NDA required (embargo until public launch)
- Incentive: Founding Member badge + lifetime 20% discount

### Feedback Collection

**In-Experience:**
- Quick emoji feedback after each session
- Optional voice memo feedback
- Bug report button (always accessible)

**Post-Session Survey (email):**
```
1. How would you rate your overall experience? (1-5)
2. What was the most memorable moment?
3. What was confusing or frustrating?
4. How likely are you to recommend this? (0-10)
5. What one thing should we improve?
6. Would you pay for this experience? (Yes/No/Maybe)
7. What would you tell a friend about it?
```

**Video Calls (optional, 15 min):**
- 10 users selected for deeper interviews
- Screen share their experience
- Ask about first impressions, confusion points
- Discuss pricing, value perception

### Week 3 Focus: Onboarding
- First-time user experience
- Account creation flow
- Tutorial effectiveness
- Initial "wow" moment

### Week 4 Focus: Engagement
- Do users return after first visit?
- Which features are most used?
- Social features adoption
- Willingness to pay

### Success Criteria
- 70%+ complete onboarding
- 50%+ return for second visit
- Average session length >20 minutes
- NPS score >40
- 80%+ would recommend to friend

## RISK MITIGATION

### Launch Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Server overload | Medium | High | Auto-scaling, load testing, CDN |
| Payment failures | Low | High | Multiple payment methods, manual fallback |
| Critical bug | Medium | High | Rollback capability, hotfix pipeline |
| Negative press | Low | Medium | Rapid response, transparency, fixes |
| Low conversion | Medium | Medium | A/B testing, pricing flexibility |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Team burnout | High | Medium | Reasonable hours, on-call rotation, time off |
| Cash flow gap | Medium | High | 6-month runway, conservative projections |
| Key person dependency | Medium | Medium | Documentation, cross-training |
| Content pipeline delay | Medium | Low | Buffer exhibitions, flexible schedule |
| Platform policy changes | Low | Medium | Multi-platform strategy, compliance monitoring |
