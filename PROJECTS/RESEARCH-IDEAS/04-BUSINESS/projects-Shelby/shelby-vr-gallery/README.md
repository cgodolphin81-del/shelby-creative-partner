## 🎨 Project Overview

**The Infinite Gallery** is an immersive virtual reality art gallery experience designed to showcase Shelby's generative art, photography, and AI creations while providing a platform for other digital artists to exhibit and sell their work.

This is not just a virtual replica of a physical gallery—it's an **impossible architecture** that could only exist in VR, where spaces shift, art evolves in real-time, and visitors from around the world can experience art together in ways never before possible.

## 🔧 Technical Highlights

### Platform Strategy
- **Primary:** WebXR (browser-based, no download)
- **Native:** Quest 2/3, SteamVR, PSVR2, Apple Vision Pro
- **Mobile:** iOS/Android companion app

### Performance Targets
| Platform | Resolution | FPS | Poly Count |
|----------|------------|-----|------------|
| Quest 2 | 1832×1920/eye | 72 | <100K |
| Quest 3 | 2064×2208/eye | 90 | <150K |
| PC VR | 2880×3024/eye | 90 | <500K |
| WebXR | Adaptive | 72 | <80K |

### Infrastructure
- **Cloud:** AWS (or Cloudflare for cost optimization)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL + Redis
- **Storage:** S3/R2 + Cloudflare CDN
- **Multiplayer:** Photon Fusion (200 concurrent per instance)
- **Auth:** Firebase Authentication
- **Payments:** Stripe (global)

## ⚠️ Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **WebXR Performance:** Progressive enhancement, fallback modes
- **Multiplayer Scaling:** Load testing, instance sharding
- **Platform Dependency:** Multi-platform strategy (not Meta-only)

### Business Risks
- **Low Adoption:** Aggressive marketing, free tier, partnerships
- **Artist Acquisition:** Competitive revenue share (70%), support services
- **Funding Gaps:** Diversified revenue, grant applications, conservative burn

### Operational Risks
- **Team Burnout:** Reasonable hours, on-call rotation, time off
- **Content Moderation:** AI + human review, clear policies
- **Cash Flow:** 6-month runway minimum, milestone-based fundraising
