# UNDERSERVED NICHE MARKETS RESEARCH REPORT
**50+ High-Value Niche Opportunities with Willingness to Pay**

*Research completed: March 2026*
*Focus: B2B niches, professional services, regulated industries, hobby enthusiasts with money*

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This report identifies 52 underserved niche markets with documented high willingness to pay, specific pain points from forums/Reddit, current inadequate solutions, and monetization recommendations. Niches are categorized by sector with entry difficulty and competition analysis.

## CATEGORY 1: TRADES & FIELD SERVICES (B2B)

### 1. HVAC Contractors
- **Market Size:** $31.5B US market (2025), ~125,000 businesses
- **Top 3 Pain Points (Reddit/forums):**
  - "Dispatching is a nightmare—techs calling me every 10 minutes asking where to go next"
  - "Customers don't pay on time and chasing invoices takes hours I don't have"
  - "Can't track which jobs are profitable vs. which are losing me money"
- **Currently Using:** ServiceTitan (too expensive for small ops), Housecall Pro (lacks HVAC-specific features), spreadsheets + paper
- **Why It Sucks:** Enterprise software costs $300-500/month minimum; generic tools don't handle refrigerant tracking, maintenance agreements, or seasonal scheduling
- **Willingness to Pay:** Already spending $150-400/month on software; proven spend on lead gen ($500-2000/month)
- **Competition Level:** Medium (dominated by expensive enterprise options)
- **Best Monetization:** SaaS ($99-199/month), affiliate for equipment suppliers
- **Entry Difficulty:** Medium (need industry knowledge, compliance features)

- **Competition Level:** Low (high barrier to entry due to regulations)
- **Best Monetization:** SaaS + compliance services, affiliate for pharmacy supplies
- **Entry Difficulty:** Hard (requires HIPAA compliance, pharmacy law knowledge, integrations)
