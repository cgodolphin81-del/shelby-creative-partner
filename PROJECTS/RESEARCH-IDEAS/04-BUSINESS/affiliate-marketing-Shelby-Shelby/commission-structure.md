## ❓ FAQ

### **Q: Can I combine volume bonuses with launch week?**
A: No. Launch week (50%) is a separate bonus that applies to the first 7 days only. Volume bonuses start after launch week ends.

### **Q: Do recurring commissions count toward volume bonuses?**
A: No. Volume bonuses are based on initial sales only, not renewals.

### **Q: What if a customer refunds?**
A: If someone requests a refund within our 30-day window, that commission is reversed from your account. This is standard across the industry.

### **Q: Can I negotiate a higher commission rate?**
A: For affiliates with 10,000+ engaged followers or proven affiliate success, we offer custom rates up to 60%. Mention it in your application or DM me directly.

### **Q: Do you offer CPA (cost per acquisition) deals?**
A: Not currently. We believe in percentage-based commissions because we're committed to our product's success — if you win, we win.

### **Q: What's your cookie duration?**
A: 90 days. If someone clicks your link and buys within 90 days, you get credited. This is above industry average (most programs are 30-day).

### **Q: Can I promote during competitor launches?**
A: Yes, but with discretion. We respect our affiliates and don't want you to be undercut. Just communicate honestly with your audience.
