## ✅ Best Practices for Promotion

### Do's

✅ **Promote authentically** - Only recommend if you genuinely use it
✅ **Share real results** - Your actual numbers build trust
✅ **Use multiple channels** - Email + social + video = maximum reach
✅ **Create urgency** - Mention the 50% discount ending
✅ **Answer questions** - Be responsive to comments/DMs
✅ **Test different approaches** - A/B test email subject lines, post formats
✅ **Track what works** - Double down on high-performing content
✅ **Stay consistent** - Promote 2-3x/week (not spammy, but regular)

### Don'ts

❌ **Don't overpromise** - Avoid "make $10K in a day" type claims
❌ **Don't spam** - No mass DMs or blast emails
❌ **Don't hide the affiliate relationship** - Always disclose
❌ **Don't use paid ads to our brand** - Organic only (no Google/FB ads to "AI Content Empire")
❌ **Don't post on coupon sites** - We don't want our product devalued
❌ **Don't make false claims** - Stick to what you've experienced
❌ **Don't forget to disclose** - "I may earn a commission" is required

### Frequency Guidelines

| Platform | Recommended Frequency |
|----------|----------------------|
| Email | 1-2x/week (max) |
| Twitter/X | 2-3x/week |
| LinkedIn | 2-3x/week |
| Instagram | 3-4x/week (mix of content) |
| TikTok/Reels | 2-3x/week |
| YouTube | 1-2x/month |

**Key:** Quality over quantity. It's better to post once with great copy than spam daily.

### What Works Best

Based on data from our 500+ affiliates:

**Top Performing Channels:**

| Rank | Channel | Avg. Earnings/Month |

| 1 | Email List | $3,200 |
| 2 | YouTube | $2,400 |

**Top Performing Content Types:**

1. **Product review videos** - Highest conversion (5-8%)
2. **Email to engaged list** - Highest CTR (4-7%)
3. **Twitter threads** - Good reach, decent conversion (2-4%)
4. **Instagram carousels** - Moderate performance (1-3%)
5. **Case studies** - High trust, good conversion (3-5%)

### How to Optimize

**For Email:**
- Subject line is everything - test 3-4 variations
- Send when your audience is most active (check your analytics)
- Personalize when possible (use merge tags)
- Include social proof (testimonials, numbers)
- Clear CTA button (not just a link)

**For Social Media:**
- Lead with value, not the pitch
- Use stories/cases from your experience
- Include screenshots of your results
- Post when your audience is online
- Engage with every comment

**For Video:**
- First 10 seconds are critical - hook them immediately
- Show the product in action (screenshare)
- Share your actual results (numbers build trust)
- Include clear CTA at the end
- Pin your affiliate link in comments/description

A: Forever! Once you're an affiliate, you can promote AI Content Empire as long as the product exists. Recurring commissions mean you can earn from the same referral for years.

### **Q: What if someone clicks my link but buys later?**
A: Your 90-day cookie means you get credited for any purchase within 90 days of the click. This is above industry average (most programs are 30-day).

### **Q: Can I promote on multiple platforms?**
A: Yes! In fact, we encourage it. Use email, social, YouTube, podcasts, blogs — whatever works for your audience.

### **Q: Do I need to disclose the affiliate relationship?**
A: Yes. Always include a clear disclosure like "I may earn a commission if you buy through my link" or "#ad" on social posts. It's the law and builds trust.

A: No worries! Not every audience is the right fit, and that's okay. Focus on the creators/entrepreneurs in your audience who are most likely to benefit. Also, we provide performance coaching to help optimize your approach.

### **Q: What if I have a question or issue?**

A: Yes, but with restrictions:
