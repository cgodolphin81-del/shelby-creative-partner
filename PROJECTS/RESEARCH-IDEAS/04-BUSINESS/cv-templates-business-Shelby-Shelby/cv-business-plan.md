# CV Template Business Plan

## 💰 Revenue Opportunities

### **1. Etsy (RECOMMENDED - Start Here!)**
**Why:** Massive existing audience, low barrier to entry, perfect for digital downloads

**Setup:**
- Create Etsy shop: "CharmaineCVTemplates" or similar
- List templates as instant digital downloads
- Price range: $5-15 per template, $25-50 for bundles

**Bundle Ideas:**
- **Executive Bundle** (10 templates) - $45
- **Industry-Specific Bundle** (15 templates) - $55
- **Creative Bundle** (12 templates) - $40
- **MEGA Bundle** (ALL templates) - $97-147
- **Entry-Level Bundle** (5 simple templates) - $19

**Pros:**
- Built-in traffic (millions of job seekers)
- Easy setup (1-2 hours)
- Passive income after setup
- Reviews build credibility

**Cons:**
- Etsy fees (~6.5% + payment processing)
- Competition (but also proves demand!)

---

### **2. Your Own Store (Phase 2)**
**Platforms:**
- **Sellfy** - Great for digital products, automated delivery
- **Payhip** - Free tier available, good for beginners
- **Gumroad** - Simple, creator-friendly

**Pros:**
- Keep 100% of profits (minus payment fees ~3%)
- Full control over branding
- Build email list for repeat sales

**Cons:**
- Need to drive your own traffic
- More setup work

---

### **3. Free Downloads (Lead Magnet)**
**Strategy:** Offer 2-3 basic templates for FREE to:
- Build email list
- Drive traffic to paid products
- Build trust and reviews

**Where to share:**
- Your website/blog
- LinkedIn articles
- Pinterest (huge for templates!)
- Reddit (r/resumes, r/jobsearch)

---

### **4. Additional Revenue Streams**

**A. CV Writing Service**
- Offer personalized CV editing using your templates
- Charge: £50-150 per CV
- Upsell to template buyers

**B. Template Customization**
- Customize templates for clients
- Charge: £25-50 per customization

**C. Career Resources Bundle**
- Add: Cover letter templates, thank you notes, interview guides
- Sell as premium package

**D. Subscription Model**
- Monthly access to ALL templates + updates
- £9.99/month or £49/year

## 📊 Pricing Strategy

| Product Type | Price Range | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Single Template | $5-12 | Basic templates lower, executive higher |
| Small Bundle (3-5) | $15-25 | 20-30% discount |
| Medium Bundle (6-10) | $30-50 | 35-45% discount |
| Large Bundle (11-20) | $55-85 | 50-60% discount |
| MEGA Bundle (ALL) | $97-147 | Best value, 70%+ discount |
| Commercial License | +$50-100 | For career coaches/recruiters |
