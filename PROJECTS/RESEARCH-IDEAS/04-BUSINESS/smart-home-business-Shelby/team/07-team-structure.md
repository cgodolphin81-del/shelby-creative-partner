### Commission Structures

#### Sales Commission Options

**Option 1: Percentage of Gross Profit**
- Commission: 10-20% of gross profit
- Example: $50K project, 40% GP = $20K GP, 15% = $3K commission
- Pros: Aligns with profitability
- Cons: More complex to calculate

**Option 2: Percentage of Revenue**
- Commission: 3-7% of project value
- Example: $50K project × 5% = $2.5K commission
- Pros: Simple, transparent
- Cons: Doesn't account for margins

**Option 3: Tiered Commission**
- 0-500K: 3%
- 500K-1M: 4%
- 1M+: 5%
- Pros: Rewards high performers
- Cons: Can create competition

**Option 4: Base + Bonus**
- Lower base salary
- Quarterly/annual bonuses for targets
- Pros: Predictable costs
- Cons: Less immediate motivation

## 8. Retention Strategies

### Why Employees Leave
1. Better compensation elsewhere
2. Lack of growth opportunities
3. Poor management
4. Burnout/overwork
5. Lack of recognition
6. Culture misalignment

### How to Keep Great Employees

1. **Pay Competitively** - Regular market reviews
2. **Provide Growth** - Clear career paths
3. **Recognize Contributions** - Regular appreciation
4. **Invest in Development** - Training and certifications
5. **Create Culture** - Make it a great place to work
6. **Listen to Feedback** - Act on employee input
7. **Offer Flexibility** - Work-life balance
8. **Share Success** - Bonuses, profit sharing

### Exit Interviews
- Conduct for all departing employees
- Understand reasons for leaving
- Identify patterns
- Implement improvements
- Maintain relationships (boomerang employees)
