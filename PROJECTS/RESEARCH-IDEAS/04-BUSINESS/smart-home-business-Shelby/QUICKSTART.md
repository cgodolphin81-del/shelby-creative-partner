# Quick Start Checklist

## Launch Your Smart Home Integration Business in 90 Days

## Pre-Launch (Days 1-30)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] **Business Registration**
  - [ ] Choose business name
  - [ ] Register LLC/Corporation
  - [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS
  - [ ] Register for state taxes
  - [ ] Open business bank account

- [ ] **Legal & Insurance**
  - [ ] Consult with business attorney
  - [ ] Draft standard contracts
  - [ ] Obtain business license
  - [ ] Get insurance quotes (liability, workers comp, etc.)
  - [ ] Purchase insurance policies

- [ ] **Financial Setup**
  - [ ] Set up accounting system (QuickBooks, Xero)
  - [ ] Establish chart of accounts
  - [ ] Set up merchant services for payments
  - [ ] Apply for business credit card
  - [ ] Secure funding (if needed)

---

### Week 2: Infrastructure

- [ ] **Technology Stack**

- [ ] **Vehicles**

- [ ] **Initial Inventory**
  - [ ] Order demo equipment
  - [ ] Stock common items (bulbs, switches, plugs)
  - [ ] Order tools and test equipment
  - [ ] Set up inventory system
  - [ ] Create equipment storage system

## Common First-90-Day Challenges

### Challenge: Not Enough Leads
**Solutions:**
- Increase marketing spend temporarily
- Activate personal network
- Offer promotional pricing for first clients
- Partner with real estate agents on new sales
- Run targeted Facebook/Google ads

### Challenge: Cash Flow Tight
**Solutions:**
- Require larger deposits (50%+)
- Offer financing options
- Negotiate better payment terms with vendors
- Factor invoices if needed
- Reduce inventory purchases

### Challenge: Team Overwhelmed
**Solutions:**
- Hire additional technician
- Subcontract overflow work
- Prioritize high-margin projects
- Improve scheduling efficiency
- Document processes to speed training

### Challenge: Quality Issues
**Solutions:**
- Slow down and focus on quality
- Implement stricter QC checks
- Additional training for team
- Bring in experienced consultant
- Review and update procedures
