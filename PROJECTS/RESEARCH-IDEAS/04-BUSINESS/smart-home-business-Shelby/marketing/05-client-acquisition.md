## 1. Target Client Profiles

### Primary Residential Segments

#### A. Luxury Homeowners
**Demographics:**
- Age: 40-65
- Household Income: $500K+
- Home Value: $2M+
- Location: High-end neighborhoods, gated communities

**Psychographics:**
- Value convenience and time-saving
- Appreciate quality over price
- Tech-comfortable but not enthusiasts
- Want seamless, invisible technology
- Expect white-glove service

**Needs:**
- Whole-home automation
- Premium security
- Entertainment systems
- Energy management
- Concierge-level support

**Acquisition Strategy:**
- Architect/builder referrals
- Luxury real estate partnerships
- High-end home shows
- Private club memberships
- Direct mail to target neighborhoods

**Average Project Value:** $75,000-200,000+

---

#### B. Tech-Forward Professionals

- Home Value: $750K-2M
- Location: Urban/suburban tech hubs

**Psychographics:**
- Early adopters
- Research-driven buyers
- Value functionality and integration
- Comfortable with apps and voice control
- Want latest technology

**Needs:**
- Integrated smart home ecosystem
- Home office technology
- Streaming/entertainment
- Security and cameras
- Energy efficiency

**Acquisition Strategy:**
- Digital marketing (Google, social)
- Tech blog partnerships
- Smart home showrooms
- Referral programs
- Content marketing (YouTube, blog)

**Average Project Value:** $25,000-75,000

---

#### C. Growing Families

## 2. Acquisition Channels

### Digital Marketing

#### Website & SEO
**Investment:** $3,000-8,000/month

**Key Activities:**
- Professional website with portfolio
- Local SEO optimization
- Service page for each offering
- Project gallery with case studies
- Client testimonials
- Blog with helpful content
- Lead capture forms

**Target Keywords:**
- "smart home installer [city]"
- "home automation [city]"
- "security camera installation [city]"
- "home theater [city]"
- "smart home companies near me"

**Expected Results:**
- 500-2,000 monthly visitors (6 months)
- 20-50 qualified leads/month
- Cost per lead: $60-150

---

#### Google Ads (PPC)
**Investment:** $2,000-10,000/month

**Campaign Structure:**
- Smart home installation (broad)
- Security cameras (specific)
- Home theater (specific)
- Home automation (broad)
- Brand campaigns (competitor names)

**Target Metrics:**
- Click-through rate: 3-5%
- Cost per click: $5-15
- Conversion rate: 5-10%
- Cost per lead: $100-300
- Close rate: 25-35%

**Expected Results:**
- 30-80 leads/month
- 8-25 projects/month
- ROI: 3:1 to 5:1

---

### Referral Programs

#### Client Referral Program
**Structure:**
- $500-1,000 referral credit for client
- $500-1,000 credit for referred friend
- No limit on number of referrals
- Credit applies to service or maintenance

**Promotion:**
- Mentioned at project completion
- Email campaign to past clients
- Included in client welcome packet
- Social media reminders

**Expected Results:**
- 20-30% of new business
- Highest quality leads
- Lowest acquisition cost
- 60-70% close rate

## 3. Sales Process

### Lead Qualification (BANT Framework)

**Budget:**
- Do they have budget for our services?
- What is their expected investment range?
- Are they pre-approved for financing?

**Authority:**
- Are they the decision maker?
- Who else is involved in the decision?
- What is their decision timeline?

**Need:**
- What problems are they trying to solve?
- What are their must-haves vs. nice-to-haves?
- How did they hear about us?

**Timeline:**
- When do they want to start?
- What is driving the timeline?
- Are they planning around other renovations?

---

### Sales Pipeline Stages

| Stage | Description | Conversion Rate |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1. Lead | Initial inquiry received | 100% |
| 2. Contacted | Initial phone call completed | 80% |
| 3. Qualified | BANT qualification complete | 60% |
| 4. Consultation | On-site or virtual consultation | 50% |
| 5. Proposal | Proposal submitted | 40% |
| 6. Negotiation | Addressing concerns, revisions | 35% |
| 7. Closed Won | Contract signed, deposit received | 25-30% |

## 6. Customer Retention & Lifetime Value

### Retention Strategies

1. **Exceptional Installation Experience**
   - Exceed expectations
   - Clean, professional work
   - Thorough training

2. **Proactive Communication**
   - Regular check-ins
   - System health reports
   - Update notifications

3. **Maintenance Programs**
   - Service plans
   - Priority support
   - Regular maintenance visits

4. **Continued Engagement**
   - Newsletter with tips
   - New product announcements
   - Upgrade opportunities

5. **Loyalty Rewards**
   - Referral bonuses
   - Repeat customer discounts
   - Anniversary acknowledgments

### Lifetime Value Calculation

```
Average Project Value: $35,000
Maintenance Contract: $3,600/year (avg 5 years)
Upgrade Projects: $10,000 (avg 2 over lifetime)
Referrals Generated: 2 (avg $35,000 each)

Total Lifetime Value: $35,000 + $18,000 + $10,000 + $70,000 = $133,000
```
