## Product Procurement Strategy

### Trade Pricing Partnerships
- Establish dealer accounts with major brands
- Target 20-40% discount off MSRP
- Maintain relationships with local distributors

### Recommended Distributors
1. **ADI Global** - Security, access control
2. **Snap One** (formerly Wirepath) - Residential integration
3. **Ingram Micro** - Broad electronics
4. **Graybar** - Commercial electrical
5. **Local electrical suppliers** - Wiring, panels, conduit

### Inventory Strategy
- Keep common items in stock (bulbs, plugs, sensors)
- Order specialty items per project
- Maintain demo equipment for showroom
