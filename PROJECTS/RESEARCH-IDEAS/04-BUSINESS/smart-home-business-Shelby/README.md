## Success Factors

### Critical Success Factors
1. **Quality Work** - Exceptional installations drive referrals
2. **Client Communication** - Keep clients informed throughout
3. **Vendor Relationships** - Good terms and support from suppliers
4. **Team Culture** - Hire well, train well, retain well
5. **Financial Discipline** - Manage cash flow, margins, and growth
6. **Continuous Learning** - Technology evolves rapidly
7. **Partnership Development** - Leverage others' networks
8. **Service Excellence** - Support drives recurring revenue

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
1. **Underpricing** - Know your costs and price profitably
2. **Overpromising** - Set realistic expectations
3. **Poor Documentation** - Document everything
4. **Ignoring Cash Flow** - Monitor receivables closely
5. **Skipping Training** - Invest in team development
6. **Going It Alone** - Build partnerships early
7. **Neglecting Support** - Recurring revenue is valuable
8. **Resisting Change** - Adapt to new technology
