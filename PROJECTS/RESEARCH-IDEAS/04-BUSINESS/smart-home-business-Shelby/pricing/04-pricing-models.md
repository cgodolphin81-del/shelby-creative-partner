## 1. Consultation Services Pricing

### Initial Consultation

| Service Type | Price | Duration | Includes |
|--------------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Virtual Consultation** | $150-300 | 1-2 hours | Video call, basic assessment, written summary |
| **On-Site Consultation** | $200-500 | 2-4 hours | Property visit, detailed assessment, preliminary recommendations |
| **Advanced System Audit** | $400-750 | 3-5 hours | Existing system evaluation, optimization plan, security audit |

### Consultation Fee Structure
- **Flat Rate:** Most consultations (preferred for transparency)
- **Hourly:** $150-250/hour for complex or extended consultations
- **Applied to Project:** 100% of consultation fee credited toward installation if booked within 30 days

### Consultation Deliverables
```

├── Everything in Premium
├── System diagnostics report
├── Security vulnerability assessment
├── Optimization roadmap
├── Upgrade cost estimates
└── Detailed written report (15-25 pages)
```

## 5. Pricing Strategy Guidelines

### Markup Structure

| Cost Type | Typical Markup |
|-----------|----------------|
| Equipment (standard) | 30-40% |
| Equipment (specialty) | 25-35% |
| Labor (installation) | 50-75% |
| Labor (programming) | 75-100% |
| Project management | 10-15% of project |
| Warranty extension | 100-200% |

### Discount Guidelines

| Scenario | Discount |
|----------|----------|
| Package deal (multiple systems) | 10-20% |
| Referral from past client | 5% |
| Repeat customer | 5-10% |
| Off-season booking | 5-10% |
| Large project ($100K+) | 5-15% |
| Builder/developer partnership | 10-20% |
| **Maximum discount** | **25%** |

### Payment Terms

#### Residential Projects
- **Under $10,000:** 50% deposit, 50% on completion
- **$10,000-50,000:** 40% deposit, 40% mid-point, 20% completion
- **$50,000+:** 30% deposit, 25% mid-point, 25% pre-commissioning, 20% completion

#### Commercial Projects
- **Under $50,000:** 50% deposit, 50% on completion
- **$50,000-200,000:** 30% deposit, progress payments, 10% retainage
- **$200,000+:** 20% deposit, monthly progress, 10% retainage

### Financing Options
- Partner with financing companies (Greensky, Synchrony)
- Offer 0% APR for 12-24 months (qualified buyers)
- Standard rates: 9.99-19.99% APR for 36-84 months

## 8. Price Adjustment Schedule

### Annual Review
- Review all pricing annually (January)
- Adjust for inflation (3-5% typical)
- Review equipment costs with suppliers
- Adjust labor rates based on market

### Triggers for Price Adjustment
- Supplier price increases >10%
- Labor cost increases
- Market rate changes
- New service offerings
- Changes in scope or complexity
