# Revenue Projections & Financial Model

## 3-Year Financial Forecast for Smart Home Integration Business

---

## 1. Executive Summary

### Business Model Overview

**Revenue Streams:**
1. **Installation Projects** (60-70% of revenue)
   - Residential smart home installations
   - Commercial installations
   - System upgrades and expansions

2. **Service & Support** (15-25% of revenue)
   - Support plan subscriptions
   - À la carte service calls
   - Maintenance contracts

3. **Products & Equipment** (10-15% of revenue)
   - Equipment markup
   - Retail sales
   - Accessory sales

4. **Consulting & Design** (5-10% of revenue)
   - Consultation fees
   - Design services
   - System audits
