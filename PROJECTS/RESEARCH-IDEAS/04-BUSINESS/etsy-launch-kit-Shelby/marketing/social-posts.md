## POST 5: VALUE TIP (No Hard Sell)

**Platform:** LinkedIn / Twitter / Instagram
**Format:** Text or carousel with tips
**Visual:** Simple graphic with tips list

```
5 resume mistakes I saw as a hiring manager (that cost people interviews):

1. ❌ Walls of text
   ✅ Use bullet points. Recruiters scan, they don't read.

2. ❌ Vague responsibilities ("Managed team")
   ✅ Quantify impact ("Led 8-person team, increased output 34%")

3. ❌ Creative formatting that breaks ATS
   ✅ Keep it clean. No tables, text boxes, or graphics.

4. ❌ One-size-fits-all approach
   ✅ Tailor keywords to each job description.

5. ❌ Hiding your best stuff at the bottom
   ✅ Lead with your strongest achievements. Top third is prime real estate.

BONUS: Save your final version as PDF. But apply with .docx for ATS.
```

## 📱 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### LinkedIn
- Post during business hours (8-10 AM or 12-2 PM, Tue-Thu best)
- Use professional tone but show personality
- Engage with comments (algorithm loves this)
- Tag relevant people/companies when appropriate
- Use 3-5 hashtags max

### Instagram
- Post when your audience is active (check Insights)
- Use all 30 hashtags (mix of big and niche)
- Stories for behind-the-scenes, urgency, quick tips
- Reels for tips, transformations, quick value
- Engage with similar accounts in your niche

### Twitter/X
- Post more frequently (2-5x/day)
- Use threads for longer content
- Engage in job search/career conversations
- Use 1-2 hashtags max
- Retweet relevant content in your niche

### Facebook
- Post to your personal profile + business page
- Share to relevant groups (job search, career, industry-specific)
- Use images (posts with images get 2.3x more engagement)
- Go live for Q&A sessions
- Engage with comments quickly

### TikTok / Reels
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Keep it under 60 seconds (ideally 15-30)
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Text overlay for key points
- Call-to-action at end
