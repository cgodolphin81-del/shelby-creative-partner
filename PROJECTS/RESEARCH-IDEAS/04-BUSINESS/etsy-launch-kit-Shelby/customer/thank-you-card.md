## HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES

### For Digital Delivery (Recommended)
1. Copy Option 1, 2, or 3 into a document
2. Add your branding (name, shop name, logo)
3. Save as PDF
4. Include in your Etsy "Thank Buyer" message OR
5. Send as a follow-up message after purchase

### For Physical Cards (If You Ship Anything)
1. Use Option 5 specs in Canva
2. Design front/inside/back
3. Order prints (Vistaprint, Moo, etc.)
4. Include with any physical items you ship

### For Email Follow-Up
1. Use Option 4 as your template
2. Set up an Etsy automation (or manual follow-up)
3. Send 1-2 hours after purchase
4. Personalize with customer name if possible

---

## BEST PRACTICES

✓ **Send within 24 hours** of purchase
✓ **Include download instructions** (not everyone knows Etsy)
✓ **Offer real support** (not just "contact us")
✓ **Add a discount code** (encourages repeat business + reviews)
✓ **Keep it warm but professional** (you're a small business, not a corporation)
✓ **Ask for reviews** (but only after offering value)

---

## PRO TIP: AUTOMATE THIS

Set up an Etsy "Message to Buyers" that sends automatically:

1. Go to Shop Manager → Settings → Info & Appearance
2. Scroll to "Message to Buyers"
3. Paste your thank you message
4. Customize with your details
5. Save

Now every buyer gets this automatically. You can still send personal follow-ups for extra touch.
