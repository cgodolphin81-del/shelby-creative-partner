  all text (Ctrl+A / Cmd+A) and re-applying the font from the toolbar.

→ If margins are off: Go to Layout → Margins and set to "Normal" 
  (1 inch on all sides).

Try these and let me know what happens! If it's still acting up, I can 
send you an alternative file format or hop on a quick screenshare.

## 🎯 CUSTOMER SERVICE BEST PRACTICES

### Response Times
- **Aim for:** Under 4 hours during business days
- **Maximum:** 24 hours
- **Weekends:** Set expectations (auto-reply if needed)

### Tone Guidelines
- **Warm but professional** (you're a small business, not a corporation)
- **Empathetic** (job searching is stressful)
- **Solution-focused** (don't just acknowledge—help)
- **Human** (use their name, sign your name, be real)

### When Things Go Wrong
1. **Acknowledge first:** "I'm sorry you're experiencing this"
2. **Take responsibility:** Even if it's not your fault, own the solution
3. **Offer options:** Give them 2-3 paths forward
4. **Follow up:** Don't let it go silent

### Turning Negatives into Positives
- Respond to negative reviews publicly (calmly, helpfully)
- Message the buyer privately to resolve
- Offer genuine solutions (not just refunds)
- Most unhappy customers become loyal if you handle it well

### Boundaries to Set
- **Scope creep:** Be clear about what's included vs. extra services
- **Urgency:** You're not on-call 24/7 (set expectations)
- **Respect:** Don't tolerate abusive messages (Etsy has your back)

## 📊 TRACKING & IMPROVEMENT

### Metrics to Watch
- Average response time
- Customer satisfaction (reviews, repeat purchases)
- Common issues (identify patterns, create FAQ)
- Refund requests (track reasons)

### Monthly Review Questions
- What questions did I answer most often? → Create FAQ/listing updates
- What caused frustration? → Fix the root cause
- What delighted customers? → Do more of that
- What can I automate? → Set up templates, auto-messages
