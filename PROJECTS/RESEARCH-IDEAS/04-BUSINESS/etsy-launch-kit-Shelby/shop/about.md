### MY STORY

Five years ago, I was a hiring manager at a Fortune 500 company. Every week, I'd review 200+ resumes for open positions. Here's what I learned:

**Most qualified candidates never made it to interview.**

Not because they weren't talented. Not because they lacked experience. But because their resumes didn't communicate their value clearly—or worse, got filtered out by ATS systems before a human ever saw them.

I watched incredible people get overlooked because of formatting mistakes, poor structure, or outdated designs. It frustrated me. These weren't bad candidates—they just had bad resumes.
