# Etsy Shop Policies

---

## 📦 DIGITAL ITEM POLICIES

### What You're Buying
All items in this shop are **digital downloads**. You will receive editable template files (Word .docx format) that you can customize on your computer. No physical product will be shipped.

---

## ⚡ DELIVERY & DOWNLOAD

### Instant Access
- Files are available for **immediate download** after purchase
- Check your Etsy "Purchases" page or email for download links
- Downloads are available indefinitely—re-download anytime

### How to Access Your Files
1. Go to Etsy.com → Your Account → Purchases
2. Find your order and click "Download Files"
3. Save to your computer or cloud storage
4. Open in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or Apple Pages

### Technical Requirements
- Microsoft Word 2016+ (recommended)
- Google Docs (free, upload the .docx file)
- Apple Pages (import the .docx file)
- Basic computer skills for editing text

## 🔄 RETURNS & EXCHANGES

### Digital Item Policy
Due to the nature of digital products, **I cannot accept returns or offer refunds** once files have been downloaded. This is standard for all digital Etsy shops.

### BUT—Here's My Promise:
If you have **any issues** with your template—can't open it, can't edit it, need help customizing—**message me immediately**. I will:
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Provide alternative file formats if needed
- Help you customize the template
- Ensure you get full value from your purchase

**I want you successful, not stuck.**

### Before You Purchase
- Review the listing photos and description carefully
- Check that the template style matches your needs
- Confirm you have software to edit .docx files
- Message me with any questions before buying
