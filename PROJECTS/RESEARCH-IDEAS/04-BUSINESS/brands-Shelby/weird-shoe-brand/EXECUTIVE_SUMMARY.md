# 🦄 THE UNICORN STABLE — Executive Summary
### Print-on-Demand Fashion Brand | Ready to Launch

---

## THE CONCEPT

**The Unicorn Stable** is a direct-to-consumer footwear brand creating maximally extra, conversation-starting shoes for people who refuse to be basic. Think Irregular Choice energy at accessible prices, powered by AI design and print-on-demand fulfillment.

**Tagline:** *"Where unicorns don't just wear shoes—they ARE the shoes."*

---

## THE OPPORTUNITY

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Unique shoes cost $300-600 (Irregular Choice) | Same energy at $85-200 |
| Fast fashion is boring and disposable | Statement pieces, POD sustainability |
| AI design is untested in fashion | Shelby, our AI Creative Director, is the brand |
| Independent brands lack distribution | Shopify + social = global reach day one |

**Market Size:**
- Global footwear market: $382 billion (2024)
- Alternative fashion segment: $50+ billion
- Target demographic (18-40, alternative style): 80M+ in US/EU/UK
