## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Something Goes Wrong:

**Website Crash:**
- Post immediately: "We're overwhelmed (in the best way)! Site coming back soon"
- Email: Same message + priority access when back up
- Update every 30 minutes until resolved

**Shipping Delays:**
- Proactive email before customers complain
- Transparency: "Here's what happened, here's what we're doing"
- Offer discount on next order

**Negative Reviews:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Acknowledge + apologize + offer solution
- Take conversation offline (email/phone)
- Learn and improve

**Social Media Backlash:**
- Pause scheduled content
- Address head-on if legitimate concern
- Stay true to brand values
- Don't engage with trolls
