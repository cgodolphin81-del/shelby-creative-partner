## FUNDING STRATEGY

### Bootstrap (Recommended)

**Pros:**
- Full ownership
- No debt
- Forced profitability
- Full control

**Cons:**
- Slower growth
- Limited resources
- Personal financial risk

**Capital Needed:** $10,000-15,000
**Source:** Personal savings, friends/family

### Small Business Loan

**Pros:**
- Faster growth possible
- Keep ownership
- Build business credit

**Cons:**
- Debt obligation
- Interest costs
- Personal guarantee often required

**Amount:** $50,000-100,000
**Use:** Inventory, marketing, hiring

### Angel Investor

**Pros:**
- Significant capital
- Mentorship
- Connections

**Cons:**
- Equity dilution (10-25%)
- Loss of some control
- Investor expectations

**Amount:** $250,000-500,000
**Use:** Rapid scaling, team building, inventory

### Recommendation: **BOOTSTRAP**

Given the POD model's low capital requirements and strong margins, bootstrapping is the optimal path. Reinvest profits for growth.
