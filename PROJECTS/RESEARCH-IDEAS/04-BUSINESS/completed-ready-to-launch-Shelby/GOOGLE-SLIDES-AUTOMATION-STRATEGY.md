- Extract quotes, stats, visuals

STEP 2: Apply Template
- Choose template based on product type
- Apply brand colors, fonts, logo
- Auto-format text for readability

STEP 3: Generate Slides
- Title slide

- CTA slide (Gumroad link, email capture)
