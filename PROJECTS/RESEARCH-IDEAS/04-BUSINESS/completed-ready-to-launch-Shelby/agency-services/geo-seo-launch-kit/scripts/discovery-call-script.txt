DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT - Word for Word
======================================

Duration: 20-30 minutes
Goal: Qualify fit, demonstrate value, book proposal review

---

OPENING (2 minutes)
-------------------

"Hey {{FirstName}}, thanks for making time today. How's your day going?"

[Listen, build rapport]

"Perfect. So here's what I'm thinking for our time today:

I want to understand your current situation with search visibility and AI, show you what I found about {{Company}}'s AI presence, and if it makes sense, talk about how we could help.

Does that sound good?

Great. Let me start by learning a bit more about where things stand..."

---

DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (8-10 minutes)
----------------------------------

CURRENT STATE:

"Tell me about how {{Company}} currently handles search visibility and online presence."

[Listen actively, take notes]

"Got it. And how's that been working for you? Any gaps you've noticed?"

"Interesting. How do most of your customers find you today?"

[Probe for: Organic search, referrals, paid ads, direct, etc.]

"Makes sense. And what's your sense of how many people are using AI tools like ChatGPT or Claude to research companies like yours?"

[Awareness check]

PAIN POINTS:

"Have you noticed any competitors showing up in AI recommendations that maybe shouldn't be? Or noticed {{Company}} missing when you'd expect to see it?"

[Specific pain identification]

"How much do you think that's costing you in terms of missed opportunities?"

[Quantify the pain]

"On a scale of 1-10, how much of a priority is fixing AI visibility for you right now?"

[Qualify urgency]

DECISION PROCESS:

"If we could solve this, who else would be involved in that decision?"

[Identify stakeholders]

"And what would your timeline ideally look like for getting this addressed?"

[Timeline qualification]

---

PRESENTATION (8-10 minutes)
---------------------------

"Thanks, this is really helpful. Let me share what I found about {{Company}}'s AI visibility..."

[Share screen - AI Visibility Audit]

"Here's what happens when I ask ChatGPT: '{{SampleQuery}}'"

[Show results - competitors appear, {{Company}} doesn't]

"And here's the same query with Claude..."

[Show pattern]

"So the pattern is clear: {{Competitor1}} and {{Competitor2}} are getting recommended consistently. {{Company}} isn't showing up."

[Pause for reaction]

"Here's why this matters:

→ AI search is growing 40% year over year
→ By 2027, Gartner predicts 75% of B2B buyers will use AI for vendor research
→ Once AI 'learns' a brand, it consistently recommends them
→ The gap is widening — early movers are locking in visibility

The good news: This is fixable. And it's not about changing your product or service — it's about making sure AI systems recognize and cite you properly."

[Transition to solution]

"Here's how we help..."

[Present GEO SEO approach - see proposal template for details]

"{{SimilarClient}} had the same challenge. After working with us:
→ 73% AI visibility in 30 days
→ 2x more qualified leads from AI traffic
→ Now recommended as a top option in their space"

---

QUALIFICATION & NEXT STEPS (5 minutes)
--------------------------------------

"Based on what we've discussed, does this feel like something that could help {{Company}}?"

[Listen for buy-in]

"Great. Here's what I'd suggest:

I'll put together a proposal with:
→ Specific recommendations for {{Company}}
→ Timeline and milestones
→ Investment options
→ Expected outcomes

We can review it together on a follow-up call. Does {{Day}} at {{Time}} work? Or feel free to book directly: {{CalendarLink}}"

[Book the follow-up]

"Perfect. I'll get that over to you by {{Date}}. Any questions before we wrap?"

[Handle objections]

"Excellent. Thanks again for your time, {{FirstName}}. Looking forward to continuing the conversation."

---

COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES
-----------------------------

"WE'RE FOCUSED ON TRADITIONAL SEO RIGHT NOW"
→ "Totally get that. Traditional SEO is still important. GEO complements it — they work together. Think of GEO as optimizing for AI assistants specifically, while SEO optimizes for Google. You need both now."

"THIS SOUNDS EXPENSIVE"
→ "I understand budget is a consideration. Let me ask: What's the cost of NOT doing this? If AI search is growing 40% YoY and you're invisible, what's that worth in missed opportunities? Our clients typically see ROI within 60-90 days."

"WE NEED TO THINK ABOUT IT"
→ "Of course. What specifically do you need to think through? [Listen] Got it. Let me send over some additional info on that. When would be a good time to reconnect?"

"SEND ME INFO FIRST"
→ "Happy to. To make sure I send the right info, what's most important for you to see? [Listen] Perfect, I'll include that in the proposal. Can we schedule a quick review call for {{Day}}?"

END OF SCRIPT
