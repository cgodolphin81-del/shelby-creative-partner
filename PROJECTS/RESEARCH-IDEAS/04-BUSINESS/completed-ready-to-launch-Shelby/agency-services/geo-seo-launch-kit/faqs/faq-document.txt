GENERAL QUESTIONS
-----------------

Q: WHAT IS GEO SEO?
A: GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your online presence to be cited and recommended by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, and Gemini. Unlike traditional SEO which optimizes for Google search results, GEO optimizes for AI-generated recommendations.

---

Q: WHY DOES AI VISIBILITY MATTER?
A: AI search is growing 40% year-over-year. More people are asking AI assistants for recommendations instead of Googling. If AI doesn't recommend your business, you're missing out on a rapidly growing channel of qualified leads. By 2027, Gartner predicts 75% of B2B buyers will use AI for vendor research.

---

Q: HOW IS GEO SEO DIFFERENT FROM TRADITIONAL SEO?
A: Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in Google's search results. GEO SEO focuses on being cited by AI assistants. They're complementary — you need both. Think of it this way:
• SEO: Optimizing for search engine algorithms
• GEO: Optimizing for AI language models and recommendation systems

---

Q: DO I NEED BOTH SEO AND GEO?
A: Yes. Traditional SEO drives organic search traffic. GEO drives AI-referred traffic. They work together. Many GEO optimizations (structured data, authoritative content, citations) also benefit traditional SEO.

---

Q: WHICH AI SYSTEMS DO YOU OPTIMIZE FOR?
A: We optimize for the major AI assistants:
• ChatGPT (OpenAI)
• Claude (Anthropic)
• Perplexity
• Google AI Overviews
• Gemini (Google)
• Bing Chat / Copilot (Microsoft)

We track your visibility across all of these and prioritize based on where your audience is most active.
