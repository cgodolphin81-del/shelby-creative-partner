# Team Building - Organizational Structure & Roles

## Overview

Building a successful virtual events agency requires a diverse team with expertise in production, technology, client management, and creative services. This document outlines organizational structures, role definitions, and hiring strategies.

---

## Organizational Structure

### Phase 1: Startup (1-10 employees)

**Structure:**
```
Founder/CEO
├── Producer/Project Manager (1-2)
├── Technical Director (1)
├── Sales/Account Manager (1)
└── Freelance Network (as needed)
    ├── Camera Operators
    ├── Audio Engineers
    ├── Graphics Designers
    └── Additional Producers
```

**Characteristics:**
- Lean team, high flexibility
- Heavy reliance on freelancers for production
- Founder handles multiple roles
- Focus on establishing processes and reputation

**Revenue Capacity:** $500K - $2M annually

---

### Phase 2: Growth (10-25 employees)

**Structure:**
```
CEO
├── Head of Production
│   ├── Senior Producer (2)
│   ├── Technical Director (2)
│   ├── Audio Engineer (1)
│   └── Production Coordinator (1)
├── Head of Client Services
│   ├── Account Manager (2)
│   └── Client Success Manager (1)
├── Head of Sales & Marketing
│   ├── Sales Manager (1)
│   └── Marketing Manager (1)
├── Creative Director
│   └── Graphic Designer (1)
└── Operations

    └── Admin/Finance (1)
```

**Characteristics:**
- Core team for consistent delivery
- Specialized roles emerging
- Reduced freelancer dependency
- Formal processes and training

**Revenue Capacity:** $2M - $10M annually

## Freelance Network

### Key Freelance Roles

| Role | Rate Range | When to Use |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Producer | $500-$1,500/day | Peak periods, specialized events |
| Technical Director | $600-$1,500/day | Multiple concurrent events |
| Audio Engineer | $500-$1,200/day | Large events, complex audio |
| Camera Operator | $400-$800/day | Multi-camera productions |
| Graphics Operator | $500-$1,000/day | Live graphics during events |
| Graphic Designer | $75-$150/hour | Project-based design work |
| Video Editor | $60-$125/hour | Post-production editing |
| Speaker Coach | $100-$300/hour | Executive coaching |

### Freelance Management

**Best Practices:**
- Maintain roster of 10-20 reliable freelancers
- Regular communication and check-ins
- Provide training on company standards
- Fair compensation and timely payment
- Build long-term relationships
- Have backups for key roles

**Onboarding:**
- NDA and contractor agreement
- Company standards training
- Shadow experienced team members
- Start with smaller events
- Regular feedback and evaluation

## Hiring Strategy

### Hiring Priorities by Stage

**Startup (0-10 employees):**
1. Producer (first hire after founder)
2. Technical Director
3. Account Manager / Sales
4. Production Coordinator

**Growth (10-25 employees):**
1. Additional Producers (2-3)
2. Sales Manager
3. Marketing Manager
4. Creative Director
5. Operations Manager

**Scale (25-50+ employees):**
1. Department heads (VP level)
2. Specialized technical roles
3. Account management team
4. Sales team expansion
5. Support roles (HR, Finance, IT)

### Interview Process

**Standard Process (Individual Contributor):**
1. Phone screen (30 min) - Recruiter/Hiring Manager
2. Skills assessment (take-home or live)
3. Interview 1 (60 min) - Hiring Manager
4. Interview 2 (60 min) - Team members
5. Final interview (30 min) - Department Head
6. Reference checks
7. Offer

**Executive Process:**
1. Phone screen (30 min) - Recruiter
2. Interview 1 (60 min) - Hiring Executive
3. Interview 2 (60 min) - Peer executives
4. Interview 3 (60 min) - CEO
5. Case study/presentation
6. Reference checks (3-5 references)
7. Offer

---

### Onboarding Program

**Week 1: Orientation**
- Company overview and culture
- Team introductions
- Tools and systems training
- Role-specific training
- Shadow team members

**Week 2-3: Training**
- Deep dive on processes
- Product/platform training
- Client interaction training
- Begin handling real work (supervised)

**Week 4: Independence**
- Take on first independent project
- Regular check-ins with manager
- 30-day review

**Month 2-3: Ramp-Up**
- Full responsibility for role
- 60-day and 90-day reviews
- Ongoing development planning

## Performance Management

### Review Cycle

- **30/60/90 Day Reviews:** For new hires
- **Quarterly Check-ins:** Informal progress discussions
- **Annual Reviews:** Formal performance review, compensation adjustment
- **Continuous Feedback:** Ongoing manager-employee conversations

### Performance Metrics by Role

**Production Team:**
- Event success rate (no major issues)
- Client satisfaction scores
- On-time delivery
- Team collaboration

**Client Services:**
- Client retention rate
- NPS scores
- Response time
- Upsell/expansion revenue

**Sales:**
- Revenue quota attainment
- Pipeline generation
- Close rate
- Average deal size

**Marketing:**
- Lead generation
- Cost per lead
- Content performance
- Brand awareness metrics
