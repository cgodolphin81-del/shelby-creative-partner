# Pricing Models - Virtual Events Agency

## Overview

This document outlines comprehensive pricing strategies for virtual and hybrid event services. Pricing should reflect value delivered, complexity of production, and market positioning while remaining competitive and profitable.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Value-Based Pricing
We price based on the **value delivered to the client**, not just hours worked. Consider:
- Event importance (annual conference vs. monthly webinar)
- Business impact (lead generation, brand awareness, employee engagement)
- Audience size and reach
- Production quality expectations
- Strategic partnership potential

### Pricing Principles
1. **Transparent** - Clear breakdown of what's included
2. **Flexible** - Modular pricing allows customization
3. **Scalable** - Pricing grows with event complexity
4. **Competitive** - Market-aware but not race-to-bottom
5. **Profitable** - Minimum 40% gross margin on all projects

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms

- **Deposit:** 50% upon contract signing
- **Second Payment:** 25% at 2 weeks before event
- **Final Payment:** 25% within 15 days post-event

### Large Projects ($100,000+)

- **Deposit:** 40% upon contract signing
- **Second Payment:** 30% at 4 weeks before event
- **Third Payment:** 20% at 1 week before event
- **Final Payment:** 10% within 15 days post-event

### Retainer Terms

- **Billing:** Monthly, in advance
- **Payment Due:** Net 15
- **Contract:** Minimum term required (6 or 12 months)
- **Cancellation:** 30-day notice, early termination fee applies

### Late Payment Policy

- **Grace Period:** 15 days
- **Late Fee:** 1.5% per month on outstanding balance
- **Service Suspension:** Events can be delayed for non-payment
- **Collections:** Accounts over 60 days sent to collections

- **Off-Peak:** 10-15% for events in slow periods

### Never Discount

- Rush fees (under 2 weeks)
- Overage charges
- Third-party pass-through costs
- Already discounted retainers

## Pricing Calculator (Quick Reference)

### Base Pricing Formula

```
Total Price = Base Service Fee + Platform Costs + Production Level + Add-Ons

Where:
- Base Service Fee: $5,000 - $25,000 (strategy, management)
- Platform Costs: $500 - $50,000 (depending on platform and attendees)
- Production Level: $5,000 - $100,000+ (basic to broadcast)
- Add-Ons: Variable (speaker coaching, graphics, engagement, etc.)
```
