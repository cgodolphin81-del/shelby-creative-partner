# Event Types - Virtual & Hybrid Event Formats

## Overview

Understanding different event formats is critical for proper planning, platform selection, and pricing. Each event type has unique requirements, success metrics, and production considerations.

---

## 1. Conferences

### Description
Multi-session, multi-track events typically spanning 1-5 days with keynote speakers, breakout sessions, and networking opportunities.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 1-5 days |
| Attendees | 500 - 50,000+ |
| Sessions | 10 - 200+ sessions |
| Tracks | 1 - 10+ parallel tracks |
| Budget Range | $50,000 - $500,000+ |

### Key Requirements
- Multiple concurrent stages/rooms
- Session scheduling and calendar
- Speaker management system
- Networking capabilities
- Virtual exhibitor/sponsor booths
- On-demand content library
- Comprehensive analytics

### Success Metrics
- Registration conversion rate
- Session attendance rates
- Average attendance duration
- Networking connections made
- Sponsor/exhibitor satisfaction
- Post-event NPS score

### Production Complexity
**High** - Requires extensive planning, multiple technical teams, and robust platform capabilities.

### Recommended Platforms
- RingCentral Events (Hopin)
- Airmeet (for mid-size)
- Custom enterprise solutions

### Typical Timeline
- **12+ weeks out:** Platform selection, speaker recruitment
- **8 weeks out:** Registration launch, marketing campaign
- **4 weeks out:** Speaker rehearsals, content collection
- **2 weeks out:** Full technical rehearsal
- **Event week:** Live production
- **Post-event:** Analytics, on-demand access

## 2. Webinars

### Description
Single-session educational or promotional presentations, typically 30-90 minutes with presentation and Q&A.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 30 - 90 minutes |
| Attendees | 50 - 5,000 |
| Speakers | 1 - 3 presenters |
| Budget Range | $2,000 - $25,000 |

### Key Requirements
- Reliable streaming platform
- Registration and reminder emails
- Presentation slide integration
- Q&A management
- Polling capabilities
- Recording and replay
- Lead capture and CRM integration

### Success Metrics
- Registration rate
- Attendance rate (typically 30-50% of registrants)
- Average watch time
- Poll participation rate
- Q&A engagement
- Lead quality/conversion

### Production Complexity
**Low to Medium** - Straightforward production, can be managed with minimal crew.

### Recommended Platforms
- Zoom Webinars
- ON24
- Vimeo Enterprise
- Platform-native webinar tools

### Typical Timeline
- **4-6 weeks out:** Topic selection, speaker confirmation
- **3 weeks out:** Registration launch, promotion
- **1 week out:** Technical rehearsal, slide review
- **Event day:** Live production
- **Post-event:** Replay distribution, follow-up

---

## 3. Product Launches

### Description
High-production unveilings of new products, features, or services designed to generate excitement and media coverage.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 30 - 120 minutes |
| Attendees | 500 - 50,000+ |
| Format | Keynote + demos + Q&A |
| Budget Range | $50,000 - $500,000+ |

### Key Requirements
- Broadcast-quality production
- Pre-recorded segments (polished)
- Live demonstration capability
- Multi-platform streaming (YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Media/press access
- Social media integration
- Embargo management (if needed)

### Success Metrics
- Live viewership
- Social media mentions/impressions
- Media coverage (earned media value)
- Product sign-ups/pre-orders
- Brand sentiment

### Production Complexity
**Very High** - Requires professional broadcast production, often with pre-produced content and live elements.

### Recommended Platforms
- Custom streaming solution
- Vimeo Enterprise + custom front-end
- YouTube Live (for public launches)
- Multi-stream distribution

### Typical Timeline
- **12-16 weeks out:** Creative concept, production planning
- **8 weeks out:** Content production begins
- **4 weeks out:** Rehearsals, technical prep
- **2 weeks out:** Full dress rehearsal
- **Event day:** Live broadcast
- **Post-event:** Content distribution, analytics

## 4. Internal Meetings & Town Halls

### Description
Company-wide communications, all-hands meetings, leadership updates, and internal training sessions.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 30 - 120 minutes |
| Attendees | 50 - 50,000 (employees) |
| Frequency | Weekly, monthly, or quarterly |
| Budget Range | $5,000 - $50,000 per event |

### Key Requirements
- Secure, internal-only access
- SSO integration
- Q&A (often anonymous)
- Polling for feedback
- Recording for absent employees
- Multi-language support (for global companies)
- Accessibility compliance

### Success Metrics
- Employee attendance rate
- Engagement (questions, poll responses)
- Post-event survey scores
- Message comprehension

### Production Complexity
**Low to Medium** - Regular cadence allows for streamlined processes.

### Recommended Platforms
- Zoom Events
- Microsoft Teams Live
- Vimeo Enterprise (for polished productions)

### Typical Timeline
- **Recurring events:** Established workflow
- **Special events:** 2-4 weeks planning
- **Ongoing:** Continuous improvement

## 5. Hybrid Events

### Description
Events that combine in-person and virtual experiences, allowing attendees to participate either on-site or remotely.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 1 - 5 days |
| In-Person Attendees | 100 - 10,000+ |
| Virtual Attendees | 500 - 50,000+ |
| Budget Range | $100,000 - $1,000,000+ |

### Key Requirements
- **Venue Technology:**
  - Professional AV setup
  - High-bandwidth internet (dedicated)
  - Multiple camera positions
  - On-site streaming encoder
  - Confidence monitors
  
- **Virtual Integration:**
  - Two-way interaction (remote Q&A, polling)
  - Virtual attendee visibility to in-person audience
  - Equal experience parity
  - Hybrid networking opportunities
  
- **Production:**
  - On-site technical crew
  - Remote production team
  - Backup systems (internet, power)
  - Rehearsal with both audiences

### Success Metrics
- In-person attendance
- Virtual registration and attendance
- Engagement parity between audiences
- Overall satisfaction scores
- Cost savings vs. fully in-person

### Production Complexity
**Very High** - Requires coordination of on-site and remote teams, redundant systems, and careful attention to experience parity.

### Recommended Platforms
- RingCentral Events (strong hybrid features)
- Custom solutions
- Airmeet (for mid-size)

### Typical Timeline
- **16+ weeks out:** Venue selection, technology audit
- **12 weeks out:** Platform selection, production planning
- **8 weeks out:** Registration launch, hybrid experience design
- **4 weeks out:** Technical planning, crew hiring
- **2 weeks out:** Full hybrid rehearsal
- **Event week:** On-site setup, live production
- **Post-event:** Analytics, content distribution

## 6. Training & Certification Events

### Description
Educational programs designed to teach skills, provide certification, or deliver continuing education credits.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 2 hours - 5 days |
| Attendees | 20 - 500 per session |
| Format | Interactive workshops, assessments |
| Budget Range | $10,000 - $100,000 |

### Key Requirements
- Breakout rooms for small groups
- Interactive exercises and activities
- Assessment/quizzing capabilities
- Attendance tracking for credits
- Certificate generation
- On-demand access for review
- Learning management system (LMS) integration

### Success Metrics
- Completion rates
- Assessment scores
- Certification pass rates
- Learner satisfaction
- Skill application (follow-up)

### Production Complexity
**Medium** - Focus on interactivity and learning outcomes over production value.

### Recommended Platforms
- Zoom (breakout rooms)
- Airmeet
- Specialized learning platforms (Intellum, etc.)

---

## 7. Networking Events & Social Gatherings

### Description
Events focused primarily on connection and relationship-building rather than content delivery.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 60 - 180 minutes |
| Attendees | 50 - 1,000 |
| Format | Informal, facilitated interaction |
| Budget Range | $5,000 - $50,000 |

### Key Requirements
- Spatial networking or 1:1 meeting scheduling
- Conversation starters/icebreakers
- Facilitated activities
- Casual atmosphere
- Multiple "rooms" or spaces
- Music/entertainment (optional)

### Success Metrics
- Connections made per attendee
- Meeting duration
- Return attendance
- Satisfaction scores
- Follow-up actions

### Production Complexity
**Low to Medium** - Less technical complexity, more focus on facilitation and experience design.

### Recommended Platforms
- Gather.town (spatial networking)
- RingCentral Events (roundtables)
- Airmeet (social lounge)
- Remo

---

## 8. Trade Shows & Exhibitions

### Description
Virtual or hybrid events focused on showcasing products and services with exhibitor booths and attendee exploration.

### Typical Specifications
| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 1 - 5 days |
| Attendees | 1,000 - 50,000+ |
| Exhibitors | 50 - 500+ booths |
| Budget Range | $100,000 - $500,000+ |

### Key Requirements
- Virtual exhibitor booths
- Product demo capabilities
- Lead capture for exhibitors
- Scheduled meetings
- Live product presentations
- Sponsorship activations
- Analytics for exhibitors

### Success Metrics
- Booth visits
- Leads captured per exhibitor
- Meeting bookings
- Exhibitor satisfaction/ROI
- Attendee engagement

### Production Complexity
**High** - Managing multiple exhibitors, ensuring booth quality, and driving traffic requires significant coordination.

### Recommended Platforms
- RingCentral Events
- Airmeet
- Specialized trade show platforms

## Event Selection Guide for Clients

### Questions to Determine Event Type

1. **What is the primary goal?**
   - Education → Webinar, Training
   - Networking → Networking Event, Conference
   - Product Announcement → Product Launch
   - Lead Generation → Webinar, Trade Show
   - Community Building → Conference, Networking

2. **Who is the audience?**
   - Internal employees → Town Hall, Training
   - Customers/Prospects → Webinar, Product Launch
   - Industry professionals → Conference, Trade Show
   - Mixed → Hybrid Event

3. **What is the budget?**
   - Under $10K → Webinar, Internal Meeting
   - $10K-$50K → Webinar, Training, Networking
   - $50K-$200K → Conference, Product Launch
   - $200K+ → Large Conference, Hybrid, Custom

4. **What is the timeline?**
   - Less than 4 weeks → Webinar, Internal Meeting
   - 4-8 weeks → Training, Networking
   - 8-12 weeks → Conference, Product Launch
   - 12+ weeks → Hybrid, Large Conference, Custom
