# 👥 Team Building - Scale Beyond Yourself

**You can't scale alone. Build a team that delivers excellence.**

---

## 📊 Team Structure by Stage

### Stage 1: Solo (0-3 clients, $0-$10K/month)
**You do everything:**
- Sales
- Strategy
- Project management
- Quality control

**Outsource:**
- Editing (freelance per video)
- Thumbnails (freelance per thumbnail)

**Team:** 1 (you) + 2-3 freelancers

---

### Stage 2: Small Team (3-8 clients, $10K-$40K/month)
**You focus on:**
- Sales
- Strategy
- Client relationships

**Hire:**
- Project Manager (part-time → full-time)
- Editor(s) (1-2 full-time)
- Thumbnail Designer (part-time)
- Script Writer (freelance)

**Team:** 1 (you) + 4-6 people

---

### Stage 3: Growing Agency (8-15 clients, $40K-$100K/month)
**You focus on:**
- Vision
- Key client relationships
- Team building

**Hire:**
- Account Manager(s) (1-2)
- Senior Editor (lead)
- Editors (3-5)
- Thumbnail Designer(s) (2)
- Script Writers (2-3)
- VA (1)

**Team:** 1 (you) + 10-15 people

---

### Stage 4: Scaled Agency (15-25 clients, $100K-$250K/month)
**You focus on:**
- Strategy
- Partnerships
- Company culture

**Hire:**
- Operations Manager
- Account Managers (3-5)
- Creative Director
- Senior Editors (3-5)
- Editors (8-12)
- Design Team Lead
- Designers (3-5)
- Writing Team Lead
- Writers (5-8)
- VAs (2-3)
- Recruiter (part-time)

**Team:** 1 (you) + 25-40 people

## 🎬 Video Editors

### Editor Skill Levels

| Level | Rate | Speed | Quality | Best For |
|-------|------|-------|---------|----------|
| **Junior** | $15-25/hr | Slow | Basic | Simple cuts, B-roll |
| **Mid** | $25-45/hr | Medium | Good | Most client work |
| **Senior** | $45-75/hr | Fast | Excellent | Hero content, complex |
| **Expert** | $75-150/hr | Fast | Exceptional | High-end productions |

---

### Editor Job Description Template

```
YouTube Video Editor Needed

We're a YouTube growth agency looking for a skilled editor to join our team.

Requirements:
- 2+ years editing YouTube content (portfolio required)
- Proficient in Premiere Pro or DaVinci Resolve
- Understanding of retention editing (pattern interrupts, pacing)
- Ability to match style/tone of different creators
- Fast turnaround (3-5 days per video)
- Good communication skills
- Available [X] hours/week

Nice to have:
- After Effects skills (motion graphics)
- Color grading experience
- Sound design experience
- Thumbnail design skills

Compensation:
- $X/hour OR $X/video
- Consistent work ([X] videos/week)
- Performance bonuses

To apply:
1. Share your portfolio (YouTube links)
2. Tell us your favorite YouTuber's editing style and why
3. Complete a paid test edit

Start date: [Date]
```

---

### Editor Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | What to Look For |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Portfolio quality | 30% | Clean cuts, good pacing, engaging |
| Technical skills | 20% | Software proficiency, audio, color |
| Speed | 20% | Can they meet deadlines? |
| Communication | 15% | Responsive, asks good questions |
| Price | 15% | Within budget |

---

### Editor Test Assignment

**Give candidates:**
- 30 minutes of raw footage
- Reference video (style to match)
- Script/outline
- 48-hour deadline

**Evaluate:**
- Pacing and flow
- Storytelling ability
- Technical quality
- Attention to brief
- Communication during process

**Pay for test:** $50-150 (shows you value their time)

---

### Editor Management

**Tools needed:**
- Frame.io (video review/feedback)
- Google Drive/Dropbox (file sharing)
- Slack (communication)
- Asana/ClickUp (task management)

**Workflow:**
```
Raw footage uploaded
        ↓
Editor notified (Asana task)
        ↓
First cut complete (3-5 days)
        ↓
Client/PM review (Frame.io comments)
        ↓
Revisions (1-2 days)
        ↓
Final export & upload
```

**Quality control:**
- PM reviews before client sees
- Checklist for every video
- Feedback loop with editor
- Monthly performance review

### Writer Job Description Template

```
YouTube Script Writer Needed

We're seeking a script writer who understands YouTube retention and pacing.

Requirements:
- Portfolio of YouTube scripts or video content
- Understanding of hook writing (first 30 seconds)
- Ability to write conversationally
- Research skills (fact-checking)
- Meet deadlines consistently
- Open to feedback/revisions

Nice to have:
- Experience in [your niche]
- SEO knowledge
- On-camera experience (understands delivery)

Compensation:
- $X per script (based on length)
- Volume: [X] scripts/week
- Bonus for high-performing videos

To apply:
1. Share writing samples
2. Write a 100-word hook for this topic: [topic]
3. Tell us your favorite educational YouTuber and why
```

---

### Script Structure Template (Give to Writers)

```markdown
# Video Script: [Title]

## Target Length: [X minutes]
## Target Word Count: [X words] (150 words/minute average)

---

### HOOK (0:00-0:30)
- Grab attention immediately
- State the outcome
- Create curiosity
- [30-50 words]

### INTRO (0:30-1:00)
- Who you are (brief)
- Why listen to you
- What they'll learn
- [50-75 words]

### MAIN CONTENT (1:00-X:00)
- Point 1: [Key idea]
- Point 2: [Key idea]
- Point 3: [Key idea]
- Examples/stories for each
- [X words based on length]

### SUMMARY (X:00-X+1:00)
- Recap key points
- Reinforce main takeaway
- [75-100 words]

### CTA (X+1:00-end)
- Subscribe ask
- Related video
- External link (if applicable)
- [50-75 words]
```

## 📋 Virtual Assistants (VAs)

### VA Responsibilities

**Admin tasks:**
- Email management
- Calendar scheduling
- Data entry
- Research

**Content tasks:**
- Upload videos (with SEO)
- Add end screens/cards
- Create chapters

- Scheduling calls
- Sending reports

---

### VA Job Description Template

```
Virtual Assistant for YouTube Agency

We need a detail-oriented VA to support our growing agency.

Requirements:
- Excellent English (written and verbal)
- Reliable internet connection
- Available during [your timezone] hours
- Experience with Google Suite
- Fast learner
- Proactive communicator

Nice to have:
- YouTube upload experience
- Basic design skills (Canva)
- Social media experience
- Previous agency experience

Compensation:
- $X/hour (based on experience/location)
- [X] hours/week to start
- Potential for growth

To apply:
1. Tell us about yourself
2. Share your availability (timezone + hours)
3. Complete a small test task
```

---

### VA Test Tasks

**Task 1: Upload a video**
- Provide video file, title, description, tags
- Ask them to upload to YouTube (unlisted)
- Add chapters, end screens, cards
- Time limit: 30 minutes

**Task 2: Research task**
- "Find 10 potential clients in [niche] with contact info"
- Provide format for results
- Time limit: 1 hour

**Task 3: Email response**
- Provide a client email with questions
- Ask them to draft a response
- Evaluate: tone, accuracy, professionalism

**Pay for test:** $15-30 total

## 💰 Compensation Structures

### Hourly vs Per-Project vs Salary

| Structure | Best For | Pros | Cons |
|-----------|----------|------|------|
| **Hourly** | VAs, ongoing work | Fair, flexible | Can incentivize slowness |
| **Per-Project** | Editors, designers | Clear cost, fast | Quality can vary |
| **Salary** | Full-time key roles | Loyalty, availability | Fixed cost regardless of workload |
| **Retainer** | Freelancers | Priority access, consistent | Minimum commitment |

---

### Recommended Structures by Role

| Role | Structure | Rate Range |
|------|-----------|------------|
| **Editor** | Per video | $150-600/video |
| **Thumbnail Designer** | Per thumbnail | $30-100/thumb |
| **Script Writer** | Per script | $100-500/script |
| **VA** | Hourly | $4-15/hour |
| **Project Manager** | Salary | $3K-6K/month |
| **Account Manager** | Salary + commission | $4K-8K/month |

---

### Performance Bonuses

**Editor bonuses:**
- On-time delivery: +5%
- No revision needed: +5%
- Video hits 100K+ views: +$100

**Designer bonuses:**
- CTR above 10%: +$20/thumbnail
- CTR above 15%: +$50/thumbnail

**Writer bonuses:**
- Video hits 100K+ views: +$100
- Retention above 60%: +$50

**Team bonuses:**
- Client renews after 3 months: $500 team bonus
- Client refers new business: 10% of first month to team

## 📋 Management Systems

### Onboarding Checklist (New Hire)

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Contract signed
- [ ] Tools access granted
- [ ] Brand guidelines reviewed
- [ ] Training videos watched
- [ ] Test project completed
- [ ] 1:1 intro call with manager

**Week 2:**
- [ ] First real project assigned
- [ ] Feedback session
- [ ] Questions answered
- [ ] Workflow confirmed

**Week 3-4:**
- [ ] Regular workload established
- [ ] Performance check-in
- [ ] Any adjustments made
- [ ] 30-day review scheduled

---

### Quality Control Checklist

**For every deliverable:**

**Video Edit:**
- [ ] Hook is strong (first 30 seconds)
- [ ] No dead air/pauses
- [ ] Audio levels consistent
- [ ] Color is corrected
- [ ] Graphics/text are correct
- [ ] End screens added
- [ ] Exported in correct format

**Thumbnail:**
- [ ] Text is readable at small size
- [ ] Face shows clear emotion (if used)
- [ ] High contrast
- [ ] On-brand
- [ ] No spelling errors
- [ ] Correct dimensions (1280x720)

**Script:**
- [ ] Hook is compelling
- [ ] Clear structure
- [ ] Conversational tone
- [ ] SEO keywords included
- [ ] Fact-checked
- [ ] Within word count

---

### Weekly Team Check-in Agenda

**30-minute team call:**

1. **Wins (5 min)**
   - What went well this week?
   - Celebrate client results

2. **Challenges (10 min)**
   - What's blocking you?
   - Where do you need help?

3. **Priorities (10 min)**
   - What's most important this week?
   - Any urgent deadlines?

4. **Announcements (5 min)**
   - New clients
   - Process changes
   - Team updates

## 🔄 Scaling the Team

### When to Hire

| Role | Trigger |
|------|---------|
| **First Editor** | When you have 2+ clients or 4+ videos/month |
| **Second Editor** | When first editor is at 80% capacity |
| **Thumbnail Designer** | When you have 4+ thumbnails/week needed |
| **Script Writer** | When clients want full-service or you hate writing |
| **VA** | When you're spending 10+ hours/week on admin |
| **Project Manager** | When you have 5+ clients or 3+ team members |
| **Account Manager** | When you have 10+ clients |

---

### Capacity Planning

**Editor capacity:**
- Junior: 2-3 videos/week (8-12 min each)
- Mid: 4-5 videos/week
- Senior: 6-8 videos/week

**Designer capacity:**
- 10-15 thumbnails/week (with revisions)

**Writer capacity:**
- Junior: 3-4 scripts/week
- Mid: 5-7 scripts/week
- Senior: 8-10 scripts/week

**VA capacity:**
- 20-30 hours/week of admin tasks
- Can handle uploads for 10-15 videos/week

---

### Replacement Planning

**Always be recruiting:**
- Keep a "maybe later" list of candidates
- Test new freelancers even when not hiring
- Cross-train team members
- Document all processes (SOPs)

**Why:**
- People leave unexpectedly
- You need backup for vacations
- Gives you leverage in negotiations
- Ready to scale when opportunity comes

- Write 10 hooks for same topic
- Transcribe and analyze top videos
- Rewrite boring scripts to be engaging
