**Content is the product. Strategy is the differentiator.**

---

## 🎬 Video Format Hierarchy

### Tier 1: Hero Content (20% of uploads)
**Purpose:** Viral potential, subscriber growth, brand building

**Characteristics:**
- High production value
- Broad appeal within niche
- 15-25 minutes
- Heavy research/editing
- Published monthly or bi-weekly

**Examples:**
- "The Complete Guide to [Topic]"
- "I Tried [X] for 30 Days - Here's What Happened"
- "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong"
- "How I Made $X in [Timeframe]"
- Documentary-style deep dives

**Expected performance:**
- 2-5x average views
- 40-60% new viewers
- High share rate

---

### Tier 2: Hub Content (50% of uploads)
**Purpose:** Consistent growth, SEO traffic, audience retention

**Characteristics:**
- Medium production value
- Specific topics/searchable
- 8-15 minutes
- Weekly or bi-weekly
- Follows proven formulas

**Examples:**
- "How to [Specific Task]"
- "[X] vs [Y] - Which Is Better?"
- "My [Process/System/Framework]"
- "The Truth About [Topic]"
- Case studies and breakdowns

**Expected performance:**
- 1-2x average views
- 50-70% search traffic
- Steady, predictable growth

---

### Tier 3: Help Content (30% of uploads)
**Purpose:** Community building, engagement, quick wins

**Characteristics:**
- Lower production value
- Quick turnaround
- 3-8 minutes or Shorts
- Can be frequent
- Responds to audience

**Examples:**
- Q&A videos
- Quick tips
- Reaction/commentary
- Behind-the-scenes
- Updates/announcements
- YouTube Shorts

**Expected performance:**
- Baseline views
- High engagement rate
- Builds loyalty

## 🎣 Viral Hook Formulas

### The "I Did X So You Don't Have To" Hook
**Structure:** Extreme effort + clear benefit
**Examples:**
- "I Applied to 100 Jobs in 24 Hours"
- "I Spent $10,000 on Facebook Ads - Here's What I Learned"
- "I Tried Every AI Tool for 30 Days"

**Why it works:** Vicarious experience, saves viewer time

---

### The "Common Belief Is Wrong" Hook
**Structure:** Challenge + proof
**Examples:**
- "Why Passive Income Is a Lie"
- "Stop Setting Goals (Do This Instead)"
- "The Productivity Advice That's Ruining Your Life"

**Why it works:** Controversy + curiosity

---

### The "Secret/Hidden Truth" Hook
**Structure:** Exclusive knowledge + benefit
**Examples:**
- "The Secret Strategy Top 1% Use"
- "What [Industry] Doesn't Want You to Know"
- "The Hidden Feature That Changes Everything"

**Why it works:** FOMO, insider knowledge

---

### The "Number + Outcome" Hook
**Structure:** Specific number + desirable result
**Examples:**
- "7 Habits That Made Me $100K"
- "3 Mistakes Costing You $10,000"
- "5 Tools I Use Every Day"

**Why it works:** Specificity = credibility

---

### The "Before/After Transformation" Hook
**Structure:** Starting point + ending point
**Examples:**
- "From $0 to $50K in 6 Months"
- "How I Went From 0 to 100K Subscribers"
- "This One Change Doubled My Revenue"

**Why it works:** Proof of possibility

---

### The "Question That Hurts" Hook
**Structure:** Painful question + solution tease
**Examples:**
- "Why Are You Still Broke?"
- "Is Your Business Actually Profitable?"
- "Are You Wasting Your 20s?"

**Why it works:** Emotional trigger + curiosity

## 🔄 Content Repurposing System

### One Long-Form → 10+ Pieces

**Source:** 10-minute YouTube video

**Creates:**
1. Full YouTube video (original)
2. 3-5 YouTube Shorts (key moments)
3. 1 Instagram Reel
4. 1 TikTok
5. 1 LinkedIn post (key insight)
6. 1 Twitter thread (main points)
7. 1 Instagram carousel (steps/tips)
8. 1 Blog post (transcript edited)
9. 1 Email newsletter (summary)
10. 3-5 Quote graphics (pull quotes)

**Time saved:** 80% vs creating from scratch

---

### Repurposing Workflow

```
Long-form video published
        ↓
Auto-transcript generated (Rev/Descript)
        ↓
Editor identifies 5-10 clips (CapCut/Premiere)
        ↓
Shorts formatted (9:16, captions)
        ↓
Social team creates posts (Canva templates)
        ↓
Writer creates blog/email (from transcript)
        ↓
Schedule all content (Buffer/Later)
```

**Turnaround:** 24-48 hours from video publish

## 📊 Content Performance Tracking

### Metrics That Matter

| Metric | Good | Great | Target |
|--------|------|-------|--------|
| CTR | 5-7% | 8-12% | 10%+ |
| AVD (Avg View Duration) | 35-45% | 50-60% | 55%+ |
| Audience Retention @ 30s | 70%+ | 80%+ | 85%+ |
| Subscriber conversion | 0.5-1% | 1-2% | 1.5%+ |
| Session time | 5-8 min | 10-15 min | 12+ min |
| Shares per video | 50-100 | 200-500 | 300+ |

---

### Content Audit Framework

**Monthly review questions:**

1. **Top 3 performing videos:**
   - What did they have in common?
   - Can we replicate the formula?

2. **Bottom 3 performing videos:**
   - What went wrong?
   - Should we pivot or improve?

3. **Best CTR video:**
   - What worked in thumbnail/title?
   - Apply to future videos

4. **Best retention video:**
   - What kept people watching?
   - Hook? Pacing? Story?

5. **Most subscribers gained:**
   - What made people subscribe?
   - Strong CTA? Unique value?
