## 📺 YouTube AdSense

### Requirements to Qualify

| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| Subscribers | 1,000+ |
| Watch time | 4,000 hours (last 12 months) |
| Shorts views | 10M views (last 90 days) - alternative |
| Policy compliance | No strikes, follow guidelines |
| Location | Available country |
| AdSense account | Created and approved |

---

### Maximizing AdSense Revenue

**1. Video Length Strategy**
- 8+ minutes = mid-roll ads available
- Sweet spot: 10-15 minutes
- Don't sacrifice retention for length

**2. Ad Placement**
- Let YouTube auto-place initially
- Manual placement after 1,000+ views
- Place mid-rolls at natural breaks
- Avoid placing during key moments

**3. Audience Geography**
- US/UK/CA/AU viewers = 5-10x higher RPM
- Create content for Western audiences
- Post during their peak hours

**4. Seasonal Optimization**
- Q4 (Oct-Dec) = 2-3x higher RPM
- Increase upload frequency in Q4
- Create "gift guide" content

---

### AdSense Revenue Calculator

```
Monthly Views × RPM / 1,000 = Monthly AdSense Revenue

Example:
500,000 views × $12 RPM / 1,000 = $6,000/month
```

**Realistic expectations:**
- 10K subs: $100-$500/month
- 100K subs: $1,000-$5,000/month
- 1M subs: $10,000-$50,000/month

## 🤝 Sponsorships

### Sponsorship Rate Card

**Standard industry rates:**

| Subscribers | Rate per 10K Views | Typical Deal |
|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| 1K-10K | $100-$300 | $500-$2,000 |
| 10K-50K | $200-$500 | $2,000-$10,000 |
| 50K-100K | $300-$800 | $5,000-$20,000 |
| 100K-500K | $500-$1,500 | $10,000-$50,000 |
| 500K-1M | $1,000-$3,000 | $25,000-$100,000 |
| 1M+ | $2,000-$5,000+ | $50,000-$250,000+ |

**Alternative pricing:**
- CPM model: $20-$50 per 1,000 expected views
- Flat rate: Based on average views, not subs
- Performance: Base + bonus for conversions

---

### Types of Sponsor Integrations

| Type | Description | Rate Multiplier |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Pre-roll (30-60s)** | Ad before content | 1.0x |
| **Mid-roll (60-90s)** | Integrated in content | 1.2x |
| **Dedicated video** | Entire video about sponsor | 2.0-3.0x |
| **Multi-video deal** | 3-5 video package | 0.8x per video |
| **Long-term partnership** | 6-12 month deal | 0.7x per video |
| **Affiliate + flat** | Lower flat + commission | 0.6x + commission |

---

### Finding Sponsors

**Inbound (they come to you):**
- List email in channel banner
- "Business inquiries" email visible
- Media kit on website
- Active on LinkedIn/Twitter

**Outbound (you pitch them):**

**Target companies:**
- Tools you already use
- Competitors of your sponsors
- Companies advertising on similar channels
- Products your audience asks about

### Sponsor Pitch Template

```
Subject: Partnership opportunity with [Your Channel]

Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Your Channel] ([link]). We help [audience] 
achieve [outcome] and have grown to [X] subscribers with [Y] average 
views per video.

I've been using [Their Product] for [timeframe] and it's genuinely 
helped us [specific result]. I think our audience would love it too.

I'd love to discuss a potential partnership. Here's what I'm thinking:

- [Integration type: pre-roll, mid-roll, dedicated]
- [Deliverables: video, shorts, social posts]
- [Timeline: when you'd publish]
- [Investment: your rate or ask for theirs]

Our audience is [demographics] and they're actively looking for 
solutions like [Their Product].

Would you be open to a quick call to explore this?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Channel link]
[Media kit link]
```

---

### Media Kit Essentials

**Include:**

1. **Channel overview**
   - Subscriber count
   - Average views (last 30 days)
   - Total views
   - Growth rate

2. **Audience demographics**
   - Age ranges
   - Gender split
   - Top countries
   - Interests

3. **Content examples**
   - Top 3 performing videos
   - Recent brand integrations (if any)
   - Content calendar

4. **Sponsorship packages**
   - Pre-roll: $X
   - Mid-roll: $X
   - Dedicated: $X
   - Package deals

5. **Testimonials** (if you have them)
6. **Contact information**

**Format:** PDF, 5-10 pages, professional design

---

### Sponsorship Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Only promote products you believe in
- Disclose sponsorship clearly (#ad, "sponsored by")
- Integrate naturally into content
- Deliver on time, every time
- Provide performance report after
- Build long-term relationships

❌ **Don't:**
- Promote sketchy products (crypto scams, etc.)
- Hide the sponsorship
- Make it feel like an ad
- Miss deadlines
- Overpromise on results
- Burn bridges with poor performance

## 🎁 Channel Memberships & Super Features

### YouTube Channel Memberships

**Requirements:**
- 1,000+ subscribers
- YouTube Partner Program member
- 18+ years old
- Not made for kids

**Pricing tiers (you set):**
- Tier 1: $0.99-$9.99/month
- Tier 2: $4.99-$24.99/month
- Tier 3: $9.99-$49.99/month

**YouTube's cut:** 30% (you keep 70%)

---

### Membership Perks Ideas

| Tier | Perks |
|------|-------|
| **Basic ($4.99)** | Badge, emoji, members-only posts |
| **Standard ($9.99)** | Above + early access, members-only videos |
| **Premium ($24.99)** | Above + monthly Q&A, Discord access |
| **VIP ($49.99)** | Above + monthly call, personalized content |

---

### Super Chat & Super Thanks

**Super Chat:**
- During live streams
- Viewers pay to highlight messages
- Range: $1-$500
- You keep 70%

**Super Thanks:**
- On regular videos
- Viewers tip $2-$50
- You keep 70%

**Strategy:**
- Go live consistently (weekly/bi-weekly)
- Acknowledge Super Chats by name
- Create Super Chat-worthy moments
- Thank tippers publicly

## 📊 Revenue Diversification Strategy

### By Channel Stage

**0-10K subscribers:**
- Focus: AdSense (once eligible)
- Secondary: Affiliates
- Goal: $500-$2,000/month

**10K-100K subscribers:**
- Focus: Sponsors + Affiliates
- Secondary: AdSense
- Start: Digital products
- Goal: $5K-$20K/month

**100K-500K subscribers:**
- Focus: Sponsors + Products
- Secondary: Affiliates + AdSense
- Add: Services, memberships
- Goal: $20K-$100K/month

**500K+ subscribers:**
- Focus: Products + Sponsors
- Secondary: All streams
- Add: Equity deals, acquisitions
- Goal: $100K-$500K+/month

---

### Revenue Mix Targets

| Revenue Stream | Target % |
|----------------|----------|
| Sponsors | 40-50% |
| Products | 25-35% |
| Affiliates | 10-20% |
| AdSense | 5-15% |
| Other | 5-10% |

**Why diversify:**
- Sponsors can disappear overnight
- Algorithm changes affect AdSense
- Products = control + margin
- Affiliates = passive income
