## Common Pitfalls & Solutions

### Pitfall 1: Underpricing
**Problem:** Charging $1K-2K/month, working constantly
**Solution:** Raise prices, fire low-paying clients, target better clients

### Pitfall 2: Overpromising
**Problem:** Guaranteeing viral results, setting unrealistic expectations
**Solution:** Under-promise, over-deliver, set realistic timelines

### Pitfall 3: Doing Everything
**Problem:** Still editing videos at $50K/month
**Solution:** Hire before you're desperate, delegate systematically

### Pitfall 4: No Niche Focus
**Problem:** Serving everyone, standing out to no one
**Solution:** Pick a niche, become the expert, charge premium

### Pitfall 5: Ignoring Sales
**Problem:** Relying on referrals, no outbound system
**Solution:** Daily outreach, consistent pipeline building

### Pitfall 6: Poor Client Selection
**Problem:** Toxic clients, constant revisions, late payments
**Solution:** Better qualifying, fire bad clients, raise minimums

### Pitfall 7: No Systems
**Problem:** Chaos, mistakes, burnout
**Solution:** Document everything, automate, use tools
