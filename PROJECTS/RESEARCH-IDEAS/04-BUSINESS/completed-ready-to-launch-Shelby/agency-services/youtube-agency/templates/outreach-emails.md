# 📧 Outreach Email Templates

**Proven templates for client acquisition**

---

## Template 1: Value-First Approach

**Best for:** Cold outreach to creators

**Subject:** Quick idea for {{channel_name}}

```
Hey {{first_name}},

I've been watching {{channel_name}} for a while - really love your 
content on {{specific_topic}}. Your video on "{{specific_video}}" 
was particularly helpful.

I noticed one thing that might be leaving views on the table: your 
thumbnails aren't matching the quality of your content. Your CTR is 
probably around 4-5% when it could be 8-10% with some optimization.

We help creators like you double their CTR and increase watch time 
through strategic thumbnail design and SEO optimization. Recent results:
- {{client_1}}: 4.2% → 9.1% CTR in 30 days
- {{client_2}}: 15K → 47K avg views in 60 days

No pitch - just wanted to share the observation. If you're ever 
interested in exploring this, I'd love to chat.

Either way, keep up the great work!

Best,
{{your_name}}
{{your_company}}
{{case_study_link}}
```

**Follow-up 1 (3 days later):**

```
Hey {{first_name}},

Just wanted to make sure you saw my note about {{channel_name}}. 

I know you're probably busy creating content, so I'll keep this brief.

We've got one spot open for {{month}} and I think {{channel_name}} 
would be a great fit for our program.

Worth a 15-minute chat to explore?

Best,
{{your_name}}
```

**Follow-up 2 (5 days later):**

```
{{first_name}},

Last try on this - don't want to be a pest.

If YouTube growth is on your radar this year, I'd love to help.

If not, no worries at all. Either way, I'm a fan of the channel.

Best,
{{your_name}}
```

## Template 10: "Break-up" Email

**Best for:** Leads that have gone silent (last attempt)

**Subject:** Should I close your file?

```
Hey {{first_name}},

I've reached out {{number}} times about helping with {{goal}}, 
but haven't heard back.

I'm assuming either:
1. The timing's not right
2. You've gone in a different direction
3. My emails are landing in spam

Either way, I'll stop reaching out. But if YouTube growth becomes 
a priority down the road, you know where to find me.

Wish you all the best with {{company/channel}}!

Best,
{{your_name}}

P.S. - Here's that {{resource}} I mentioned: {{link}}
```

**Note:** This email often gets responses from people who were 
just busy or forgot.

## Outreach Best Practices

### Personalization Checklist
- [ ] Use their first name
- [ ] Mention specific video/content
- [ ] Reference their niche/industry
- [ ] Include relevant case study
- [ ] Keep under 150 words
- [ ] One clear CTA

### Follow-up Sequence

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| 0 | Initial email |
| 3 | Follow-up 1 |
| 8 | Follow-up 2 |
| 15 | Value add (article/resource) |
| 22 | Break-up email |

### Daily Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| New outreaches | 20-50 |
| Follow-ups | 30-60 |
| Calls booked | 2-5/week |
| Clients closed | 1-2/month |

---

*Remember: Most people say yes on follow-up 3-5, not the first email. Persist!*
