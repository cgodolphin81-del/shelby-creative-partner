# CLIENT COACHING AGREEMENT

## Template

```
================================================================================
                    COACHING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into as of [Date] by and between:

COACH: [Your Name/Company Name], with principal address at [Your Address]
("Coach")

CLIENT: [Client Name], with principal address at [Client Address]
("Client")

Collectively referred to as the "Parties."

================================================================================

1. SERVICES
-----------

1.1 Coach agrees to provide the following coaching services (the "Services"):

    □ [Number] coaching sessions of [Duration] each
    □ [Email/Voxer] support with [Response Time] response
    □ Access to [Resources/Portal/Materials]
    □ [Additional services]

1.2 Services will be delivered via [Zoom/Phone/In-person] at times mutually 
    agreed upon by the Parties.

1.3 The coaching relationship will commence on [Start Date] and continue for 
    [Duration] unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. INVESTMENT & PAYMENT
-----------------------

2.1 Client agrees to pay Coach a total of $[Amount] for the Services.

2.2 Payment Schedule:
    □ Pay in Full: $[Amount] due upon signing
    □ Payment Plan: [Number] monthly payments of $[Amount], 
      beginning on [Date] and continuing on the [Day] of each month

2.3 All payments are non-refundable except as expressly stated in this 
    Agreement.

2.4 Late payments will incur a fee of $[Amount] or [Percentage]%, whichever 
    is greater, after [Number] days past due.

2.5 If payment is more than [Number] days late, Coach reserves the right to 
    suspend Services until payment is received.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES
--------------------------

3.1 Client agrees to:
    (a) Attend all scheduled sessions on time
    (b) Provide 24-hour notice for cancellations or rescheduling
    (c) Complete agreed-upon action items between sessions
    (d) Be open and honest in all communications
    (e) Take full responsibility for their decisions and actions

3.2 Missed sessions without 24-hour notice will be forfeited and cannot be 
    rescheduled.

3.3 Client understands that coaching is not therapy, counseling, or mental 
    health treatment. If Client requires such services, Client agrees to 
    seek a qualified mental health professional.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. COACH RESPONSIBILITIES
-------------------------

4.1 Coach agrees to:
    (a) Provide Services in a professional and timely manner
    (b) Maintain confidentiality as outlined in Section 5
    (c) Act in the best interest of the Client
    (d) Provide resources and support as described

4.2 Coach is not responsible for Client's actions, decisions, or results. 
    Client acknowledges that results vary and depend on many factors 
    including Client's effort, commitment, and external circumstances.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
------------------

5.1 Coach agrees to keep all information shared by Client confidential, 
    except as required by law or with Client's explicit written permission.

5.2 Coach may discuss Client's case in supervision or consultation with 
    other professionals, but will not reveal Client's identity.

5.3 Client acknowledges that communications via email or third-party 
    platforms (e.g., Voxer, WhatsApp) are not guaranteed to be secure.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. NO GUARANTEE OF RESULTS
--------------------------

6.1 Client acknowledges that coaching is a collaborative process and that 
    results cannot be guaranteed.

6.2 Any testimonials, case studies, or examples of results provided by 
    Coach are illustrative only and do not guarantee similar results for 
    Client.

6.3 Client's success depends on many factors including but not limited to:
    • Client's commitment and effort
    • Client's implementation of strategies
    • External market conditions
    • Client's unique circumstances

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
------------------------

7.1 All materials, frameworks, methodologies, and content provided by Coach 
    remain the intellectual property of Coach.

7.2 Client is granted a limited, non-transferable license to use materials 
    for personal use only during the coaching engagement.

7.3 Client may not reproduce, distribute, sell, or create derivative works 
    from Coach's materials without explicit written permission.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. TESTIMONIALS & CASE STUDIES
------------------------------

8.1 Coach may request Client's permission to share their success story as 
    a testimonial or case study.

8.2 Client's participation in testimonials is completely voluntary and 
    may be declined without any impact on Services.

8.3 If Client agrees to provide a testimonial, Coach may use Client's 
    name, photo, and results in marketing materials.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

9. TERMINATION
--------------

9.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement with [Number] days written 
    notice.

9.2 If Client terminates without cause:
    • Client is responsible for all payments through the termination date
    • No refunds will be provided for unused sessions
    • Payment plan obligations remain in effect

9.3 If Coach terminates without cause:
    • Coach will provide prorated refund for unused sessions
    • Coach will provide referrals to other qualified coaches if appropriate

9.4 Either Party may terminate immediately if the other Party breaches 
    any material term of this Agreement.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
---------------------------

10.1 Client agrees that Coach's total liability under this Agreement shall 
     not exceed the total amount paid by Client to Coach.

10.2 In no event shall Coach be liable for any indirect, incidental, 
     special, or consequential damages.

10.3 Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Coach from any claims, 
     damages, or expenses arising from Client's use of the Services.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
----------------------

11.1 In the event of a dispute, the Parties agree to first attempt good-
     faith negotiation to resolve the matter.

11.2 If negotiation fails, the Parties agree to mediation before pursuing 
     any legal action.

11.3 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of [Your State/Country].

11.4 Any legal action shall be brought in the courts of [Your Jurisdiction].

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT
--------------------

12.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties 
     and supersedes all prior agreements or understandings.

12.2 Any modifications to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by 
     both Parties.

12.3 If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid, the 
     remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

================================================================================

SIGNATURES

COACH:

Signature: _________________________________

Print Name: ________________________________

Date: ______________________________________

CLIENT:

Signature: _________________________________

Print Name: ________________________________

Date: ______________________________________

================================================================================
```

your personal information.

    • Name and contact information (email, phone)

    • Communications with us

# REFUND POLICY

## Template Options

### Option 1: No Refunds (Common for Coaching)
```
================================================================================
                         REFUND POLICY
================================================================================

All payments for coaching services are non-refundable.

By enrolling in any program or service, you acknowledge that:

• You are making a commitment to your own growth and transformation
• Coaching requires active participation and implementation
• Results depend on your effort, not just the information provided
• The value is in the transformation, not just the sessions

If you are experiencing challenges in the program, we encourage you to 
communicate openly with your coach so we can support you in getting the 
most value from your investment.

In exceptional circumstances (medical emergency, death in family, etc.), 
we may consider case-by-case exceptions. Please contact us at [Email] to 
discuss your situation.

================================================================================
```

### Option 2: Satisfaction Guarantee (7-14 Days)
```
================================================================================
                    SATISFACTION GUARANTEE
================================================================================

We stand behind the quality of our services. If you're not satisfied for 
any reason, you may request a refund within the first [7/14] days of your 
program, provided you have:

✓ Attended your kickoff call
✓ Completed all onboarding requirements
✓ Attended at least [X] coaching sessions
✓ Completed all assigned action items

To request a refund, email [Email] with your reason for cancellation. 
Refunds will be processed within [5-10] business days.

After the [7/14]-day guarantee period, all sales are final.

================================================================================
```
