# SALES SYSTEM COMPLETE KIT

## The Sales Funnel Overview

```
LEAD GENERATION
     ↓
APPLICATION FORM (qualify before call)
     ↓
DISCOVERY CALL (30-45 min)
     ↓
PROPOSAL SENT (within 24 hours)
     ↓
FOLLOW-UP SEQUENCE (5-7 touches)
     ↓
CLOSED CLIENT or NURTURE LONG-TERM
```

# DISCOVERY CALL SCRIPT

## Pre-Call Preparation

**Send this email 24 hours before:**

```
Subject: Preparing for our call tomorrow

Hi [Name],

Looking forward to our conversation tomorrow at [time].

To make the most of our time together, I'd love for you to 
think about these 3 questions:

1. What's the #1 challenge you're facing right now?
2. What have you already tried to solve it?
3. If we could achieve one breakthrough in the next 90 days, 
   what would have the biggest impact?

No need to send answers in advance—just come ready to discuss.

Talk soon,
[Your Name]
```

## Discovery Call Framework (45 minutes)

### MINUTES 0-5: RAPPORT & AGENDA

```
YOU: "Hey [Name], great to meet you! How's your day going?"

[Small talk - 2-3 minutes max]

YOU: "So here's how I'd like to use our time today:

First, I want to understand where you are, where you want to go, 
and what's been getting in the way. 

Then, if it makes sense, I'll show you how I might be able to help. 

And if we're not a fit, I'll be honest about that too and maybe 
point you in a better direction.

Does that sound good?"

[Wait for yes]
```

---

### MINUTES 5-20: DISCOVERY QUESTIONS

**Current Situation:**
```
"Tell me about where you are right now with [their goal/challenge]."

"What's working? What's not working?"

"How long has this been going on?"
```

**Desired Outcome:**
```
"If we're sitting here 90 days from now and this has been a 
home run success, what does that look like?"

"What would that be worth to you—financially, emotionally, 
in terms of time freedom?"

"Why is this important to you personally?"
```

**Past Attempts:**
```
"What have you already tried to solve this?"

"What worked? What didn't?"

"What was missing from those experiences?"
```

**Obstacles:**
```
"What do you think has been holding you back?"

"If you don't solve this in the next 6 months, what happens?"

"What's the cost of staying stuck?"
```

**Decision Factors:**
```
"Who else is involved in this decision?"

"What would need to be true for you to feel confident moving forward?"

"Have you worked with a coach/consultant before? What was that like?"
```

---

### MINUTES 20-30: PATTERN INTERRUPT & POSITIONING

**The Pattern Interrupt:**
```
YOU: "Can I be honest with you for a second?"

[Pause - they'll say yes]

YOU: "I've worked with [X number] of people in your situation, 
and I've noticed something. 

Most people think the problem is [what they think]. 

But in my experience, the real issue is usually [deeper insight 
based on their answers].

Does that resonate?"
```

**Position Your Solution:**
```
YOU: "Here's what I've found works. 

[Explain your Signature Framework in 2-3 minutes]

This is exactly what we'd do together. We'd [key steps] so you 
can [specific outcome] without [their frustration].

I've seen this work for people in your situation [brief case 
study with numbers if possible]."
```

---

### MINUTES 30-40: INVESTMENT & OBJECTION HANDLING

**Present the Investment:**
```
YOU: "So here's how this works.

I work with clients in a few different ways. Based on what you've 
shared, I think [specific package] would be the best fit.

This includes [3-4 key components] and we'd work together for 
[duration] to get you to [outcome].

The investment is $[X,XXX]. 

Most clients pay in [payment structure], and we can get you 
started within [timeframe].

How does that land with you?"
```

**SILENCE. Let them respond first.**

---

### COMMON OBJECTIONS & RESPONSES

**"I need to think about it"**
```
"Of course, this is a big decision. Can I ask—what specifically 
do you need to think through?

[Listen]

Got it. And if we could address [their concern], would there be 
anything else holding you back?"
```

**"It's more than I expected"**
```
"I understand. Can I ask—compared to what? 

[Listen]

Here's what I'd offer: this program is $[X,XXX]. You've told me 
this problem is costing you $[X,XXX] per year [or: would be worth 
$X,XXX to solve]. 

So the real question is: can you afford NOT to solve this?

That said, I do have [payment plan option] if that helps."
```

**"I need to talk to my [spouse/partner/business partner]"**
```
"Absolutely, this should be a shared decision. 

What do you think they'll say?

[Listen]

Would it be helpful if I joined a quick call with both of you to 
answer any questions? I can make time [suggest 2 time options]."
```

**"I've been burned by coaches before"**
```
"I'm sorry to hear that. Can you tell me what happened?

[Listen and validate]

I hear you. Here's what I do differently: [specific differentiator].

And I offer [guarantee] because I'm confident in the results.

Would it help if we [specific risk reversal]?"
```

---

### MINUTES 40-45: CLOSE OR NEXT STEPS

**If They're Ready:**
```
YOU: "Sounds like this could be a great fit. Here's what happens next:

1. I'll send over the agreement and invoice
2. You'll sign and submit payment
3. We'll schedule your kickoff call within 48 hours
4. You'll get your welcome packet with everything you need

I can send that over today. Does [email address] still work best?

Great. Any questions before I send that over?"
```

**If They Need Time:**
```
YOU: "I appreciate you being thoughtful about this. 

Here's what I'd suggest: I'll send over the proposal with 
everything we discussed, including the investment and what's 
included. 

Take your time to review it. I'll follow up on [specific day] 
to see what questions come up.

Does [day/time] work for a quick check-in call?"

[Get commitment to specific follow-up time]
```

[Your Name]

## CLOSE RATE OPTIMIZATION

### Metrics to Track:
```
• Calls booked → Calls held (target: 80%+)
• Calls held → Proposals sent (target: 100%)
• Proposals sent → Closed (target: 40-60%)
• Overall: Inquiries → Closed (target: 20-30%)
```

### Improvement Tactics:

1. **Better Qualification**
   - Use application form before calls
   - Disqualify early if not a fit
   - Focus on ready buyers

2. **Call Quality**
   - Record and review your calls
   - Practice objection handling
   - Follow the script (then make it your own)

3. **Follow-Up Discipline**
   - Send proposals within 24 hours
   - Follow up 5-7 times minimum
   - Most sales happen on touch 5+

4. **Social Proof**
   - Add case studies to proposals
   - Video testimonials on website
   - Specific numbers, not vague claims

5. **Risk Reversal**
   - Strong guarantees
   - Payment plans
   - Trial periods (if feasible)
