# 90-DAY ACTION PLAN

## WEEKS 1-2: FOUNDATION

### Week 1: Niche & Positioning
```
□ DAY 1-2: Complete Niche Selection Matrix
   • Score 3 potential niches
   • Choose winner
   • Validate with 5 potential clients

□ DAY 3-4: Research & Competitor Analysis
   • Identify 5 competitors
   • Analyze their offers and pricing
   • Find gaps in the market

□ DAY 5-7: Craft Your Positioning
   • Write your "Only Statement"

   • Create positioning document

✓ Niche selected and validated
✓ "Only Statement" written
✓ Competitor analysis complete
✓ Ideal client avatar defined
```

## WHAT TO DO IF...

### "I'm not getting discovery calls booked"
```
→ Increase outreach volume (2x your current effort)
→ Improve your outreach message (more personalized)
→ Add social proof to your messaging
→ Try a different channel (LinkedIn vs. email vs. Instagram)
→ Run a webinar or workshop to generate interest
```

### "I'm getting calls but not closing"
```
→ Review your discovery call recordings
→ Are you asking enough questions?
→ Are you presenting the solution clearly?
→ Are you asking for the sale?
→ Practice with a role-play partner
→ Consider lowering price point temporarily for case studies
```

### "I'm closing but can't deliver"
```
→ You're overpromising—adjust your offer
→ Create better systems and SOPs
→ Hire a VA or contractor for support
→ Reduce capacity until systems are solid
→ Raise prices to match your delivery capacity
```

### "I'm burnt out"
```
→ Review your capacity—are you overcommitted?
→ Raise prices to work with fewer clients
→ Systematize repetitive tasks
→ Hire help (VA, contractor)
→ Take a week off to recharge
→ Remember: rest is productive
```
