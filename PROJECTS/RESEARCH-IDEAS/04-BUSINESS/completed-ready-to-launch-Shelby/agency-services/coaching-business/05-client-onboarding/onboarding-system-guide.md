# CLIENT ONBOARDING SYSTEM

## The Onboarding Experience Framework

First impressions set the tone for the entire engagement. A professional, warm onboarding process:
- Builds confidence in their decision
- Reduces buyer's remorse
- Sets clear expectations
- Creates momentum from day one

# KICKOFF CALL AGENDA

## 60-Minute Structure

### MINUTES 0-10: WELCOME & CONNECTION
```
• Welcome and celebrate their decision
• Personal check-in (how are they feeling?)
• Review the agenda for the call
• Confirm their goals and motivations
```

### MINUTES 10-25: DEEP DIVE DISCOVERY
```
• Review intake form responses together
• Ask clarifying questions
• Identify any additional challenges
• Uncover any hidden objections or concerns
• Confirm desired outcomes (be specific)
```

### MINUTES 25-40: ROADMAP DESIGN
```
• Walk through the program phases
• Customize based on their situation
• Identify quick wins for early momentum
• Set milestones and checkpoints
• Discuss potential obstacles and solutions
```

### MINUTES 40-50: LOGISTICS & SYSTEMS
```
• Confirm communication preferences
• Set up Voxer/WhatsApp connection
• Schedule all sessions (or explain booking process)
• Review portal access and resources
• Answer any logistical questions
```

### MINUTES 50-60: COMMITMENT & CLOSE
```
• Ask: "What's one thing you're committed to before 
   our next session?"
• Confirm their first action item
• Express confidence in their success
• End on an energized, positive note
```
