## Design Specifications

### Platform Requirements

#### Instagram Feed
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1) or 1080 x 1350 px (4:5)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Max File Size**: 30 MB
- **Notes**: Keep key content in center 80% (avoid UI overlap)

#### Instagram Stories
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or MP4 (for video)
- **Max File Size**: 30 MB
- **Notes**: Leave 250px top and bottom safe zones

#### Instagram Reels Cover
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Notes**: Design with video overlay in mind

#### LinkedIn Post
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 627 px (1.91:1)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Max File Size**: 5 MB
- **Notes**: Professional aesthetic preferred

#### LinkedIn Document Post
- **Dimensions**: 1080 x 1350 px per page (4:5)
- **Format**: PDF (multi-page)
- **Max Pages**: 10
- **Notes**: Great for carousels/guides

#### Twitter/X Post
- **Dimensions**: 1200 x 675 px (16:9) or 1080 x 1080 px (1:1)
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- **Max File Size**: 5 MB (15 MB for Premium)
- **Notes**: Bold text for mobile scrolling
