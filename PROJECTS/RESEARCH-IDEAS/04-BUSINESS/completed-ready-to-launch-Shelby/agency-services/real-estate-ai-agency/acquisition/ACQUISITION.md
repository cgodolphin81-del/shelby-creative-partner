## 🏢 Segment 1: Brokerages

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Characteristics:**
- 20-200 agents
- $50M+ annual sales volume
- Tech-forward leadership (already using CRM, marketing automation)
- Located in competitive markets (top 50 MSAs)
- Growing or stable (not declining)
- Owner/broker is decision-maker

**Pain Points:**
- Agent recruitment and retention
- Inconsistent lead follow-up
- Training overhead for new agents
- Competition from iBuyers and tech brokerages
- Marketing ROI uncertainty

---

### Acquisition Channels

#### Channel 1: Direct Outreach (Highest ROI)

**Tactics:**
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator campaigns
- Personalized email sequences
- Cold calling (warm-up via social)
- Direct mail (high-value prospects)

**Cold Call Script:**

```
Opening:
"Hi {First Name}, this is {Your Name} with {Company}. I know I'm calling 
out of the blue - do you have 37 seconds for me to tell you why I'm 
calling, and then you can decide if we keep talking?"

[Pause - this disarms them]

Pitch:
"We work with brokerages like {theirs} to implement AI tools that help 
agents respond to leads faster and close more deals. {Similar Brokerage} 
just rolled this out and saw a 34% increase in conversions.

I'm not asking for a commitment - just 15 minutes to show you what we're 
doing and see if it makes sense to explore further.

Do you have your calendar handy?"

Objection Handling:

"Send me info."
→ "Happy to. But honestly, our stuff is pretty visual - hard to get the 
impact over email. What's your schedule like Tuesday or Wednesday for a 
quick 15-minute demo?"

"We're happy with our current tools."
→ "That's great - most of our clients were too before they switched. 
What I'd suggest is a quick comparison - 15 minutes, no pressure. If 
what you have is working better, I'll be the first to tell you to stick 
with it. Fair enough?"

"Not interested."
→ "Totally understand. Can I ask - is it timing, or is AI just not a 
priority right now?" [Listen - this tells you if it's a 'not now' or 
'never']
```

**Targets/Week:** 50 brokerages
**Expected Response Rate:** 8-12%
**Expected Demo Rate:** 3-5%
**Expected Close Rate:** 20-30% of demos

## 👤 Segment 2: Individual Agents

### Ideal Customer Profile

**Characteristics:**
- Top 10% producers (20+ transactions/year)
- $500K+ GCI
- Tech-comfortable
- Active on social media
- Invest in coaching/training
- Team leaders (leverage multiplier)

**Pain Points:**
- Lead follow-up consistency
- Time management
- Staying top-of-mind with past clients
- Competition from younger, tech-savvy agents
- Scaling beyond personal capacity

---

### Acquisition Channels

#### Channel 1: Agent Teams

**Strategy:** Target team leaders who can roll out to entire team

**Approach:**
- LinkedIn outreach to team leaders
- Team productivity calculator (lead magnet)
- Group demo sessions
- Tiered pricing for teams

**Messaging:**
```
"Most team leaders lose 20-30% of leads to slow follow-up. Our AI 
system ensures every lead gets instant response, 24/7, while your 
agents focus on closing. Team of 8? That's an extra $200-400K in 
GCI annually."
```

#### Channel 2: Social Proof & Influencers

## 📈 Sales Process

### Stage 1: Prospecting (Days 1-7)
- Identify and research target
- Multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, phone)
- Goal: Book discovery call

### Stage 2: Discovery (Days 8-14)
- 30-45 minute call
- Understand pain points, current tools, decision process
- Qualify: Budget, authority, need, timeline (BANT)
- Goal: Advance to demo

### Stage 3: Demo (Days 15-21)
- 45-60 minute personalized demo
- Show relevant use cases
- Address objections
- Goal: Proposal request

### Stage 4: Proposal (Days 22-28)
- Custom proposal with pricing
- ROI projection
- Implementation timeline
- Goal: Verbal commitment

### Stage 5: Negotiation (Days 29-35)
- Address final concerns
- Negotiate terms (if needed)
- Legal review
- Goal: Signed contract

### Stage 6: Close & Onboard (Days 36-42)
- Contract execution
- Payment processed
- Kickoff scheduled
- Goal: Successful implementation start

## 📞 Cold Calling Tips

1. **Pattern Interrupt:** Acknowledge you're interrupting
2. **Permission-Based:** "Do you have 37 seconds?"
3. **Specific & Brief:** Get to the point quickly
4. **Social Proof:** Name-drop similar companies
5. **Clear CTA:** Calendar invite, not "let me know"
6. **Handle Objections:** Have responses ready
7. **Follow-Up:** 80% of sales require 5+ contacts
