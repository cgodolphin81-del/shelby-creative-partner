## рҹ‘” Key Role Descriptions

### Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)

**Reports To:** CEO
**Compensation:** $200K-300K base + $150K-300K bonus + equity

**Responsibilities:**
- Own all revenue targets (bookings, ARR, retention)
- Build and scale sales organization
- Define sales strategy and methodology
- Hire, train, and manage sales leadership
- Establish sales processes and playbooks
- Own pricing strategy (with CEO)
- Forecast accuracy
- Board reporting on revenue metrics

**Requirements:**
- 10+ years B2B SaaS sales experience
- 5+ years in sales leadership (VP+)
- Track record scaling from $1M to $10M+ ARR
- Real estate or proptech experience (preferred)
- Strong recruiter and people developer

---

### Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

**Reports To:** CEO
**Compensation:** $220K-350K base + $100K-200K bonus + significant equity

**Responsibilities:**
- Product vision and roadmap
- Engineering team leadership
- Technical architecture decisions
- AI/ML strategy
- Security and compliance oversight
- Technology partnerships
- Hiring and developing engineering talent
- Technical due diligence (M&A, partnerships)

**Requirements:**
- 10+ years software engineering
- 5+ years engineering leadership
- Experience with AI/ML systems
- SaaS platform experience
- Security-minded (SOC 2, etc.)
- Real estate domain knowledge (plus)

---

### VP of Engineering

**Reports To:** CTO
**Compensation:** $180K-250K base + $50K-100K bonus + equity

**Responsibilities:**
- Manage engineering managers and ICs
- Sprint planning and delivery
- Code quality and best practices
- Technical hiring
- Infrastructure and DevOps
- Release management

**Requirements:**
- 8+ years engineering
- 3+ years engineering management
- Experience scaling engineering teams
- Full-stack + cloud infrastructure

---

### ML/AI Engineering Lead

**Reports To:** CTO or VP Engineering
**Compensation:** $200K-280K base + $50K-100K bonus + equity

**Responsibilities:**
- ML model development and deployment
- Model training and optimization
- Data pipeline architecture
- MLOps infrastructure
- Research and experimentation
- Mentor ML engineers

**Requirements:**
- MS/PhD in CS, Statistics, or related
- 5+ years ML engineering
- Experience with production ML systems
- Real estate data experience (plus)

---

### VP of Customer Success

**Reports To:** COO or CEO
**Compensation:** $160K-220K base + $40K-80K bonus + equity

**Responsibilities:**
- Own retention and expansion metrics
- Build CS team and processes
- Implementation methodology
- Customer health scoring
- Renewal management
- Upsell/cross-sell coordination
- Voice of customer to product

**Requirements:**
- 8+ years customer success
- 3+ years CS leadership
- SaaS experience
- Real estate or services background (plus)

---

### Account Executive (AE)

**Reports To:** Sales Manager or CRO
**Compensation:** $70K-100K base + $80K-150K OTE + equity

**Responsibilities:**
- Full-cycle sales (discovery to close)
- Pipeline management
- Forecasting
- Contract negotiation
- Handoff to implementation

**Requirements:**
- 3+ years B2B sales
- Track record of quota attainment
- Real estate experience (plus)
- CRM proficiency (Salesforce, HubSpot)

**Quota Expectations:**
- Year 1: $400K-600K ARR
- Year 2+: $600K-1M ARR
- Ramp: 50% month 1, 75% month 2, 100% month 3

---

### Sales Development Representative (SDR)

**Reports To:** Sales Manager
**Compensation:** $50K-65K base + $30K-50K OTE + equity

- Close
