## 📈 Performance-Based Pricing

### Option 1: Commission Share

**Structure:**
- Reduced base retainer (40-60% of standard)
- 10-25% of first-year commission on closed deals attributed to our system

**Tracking:**
- CRM integration for attribution
- Closed-loop reporting
- Monthly reconciliation

**Best For:** High-performing agents confident in conversion rates

**Example:**
```
Standard Growth Bundle: $5,000/mo
Performance Model: $2,500/mo + 15% commission

Agent closes $2M in sales (avg 2.5% commission = $50,000)
Our share: 15% × $50,000 = $7,500
Total monthly equivalent: $2,500 + $7,500 = $10,000

Client wins if they close >$1.7M annually
We win if they close more with our system
```

---

### Option 2: Gain-Share

**Structure:**
- Standard retainer
- 20-30% of measurable cost savings or revenue increase

**Metrics:**
- Reduced days on market (vs. market average)
- Increased lead conversion rate
- Reduced marketing cost per acquisition
- Reduced vacancy rates (property management)

**Best For:** Data-driven organizations with clear baselines

**Example:**
```
Property management client
Baseline vacancy: 8% annually
With our system: 5% annually
Additional NOI: $150,000
Our share: 25% × $150,000 = $37,500/year
```

---

### Option 3: Equity/Revenue Share

**Structure:**
- Minimal or no cash retainer
- 1-5% equity in client company OR
- 2-10% of gross revenue (ongoing)

**Best For:** 
- Startups with limited cash
- Strategic partnerships
- Co-development opportunities

**Terms:**
- Minimum 3-year commitment
- Vesting schedule (if equity)
- Buyout options defined upfront

## 🎯 Pricing Guidelines for Sales Team

### Discounting Authority

| Role | Maximum Discount | Approval Required |
|------|-----------------|-------------------|
| AE | 10% | None |
| Senior AE | 20% | None |
| Sales Manager | 30% | None |
| VP Sales | 40% | None |
| CRO/CEO | 50%+ | Board notification |

### Discounting Principles

1. **Trade concessions:** Never discount without getting something (longer term, case study, testimonial, etc.)
2. **Value-based:** Tie price to measurable outcomes
3. **Competitive:** Match on features, not just price
4. **Transparent:** Clear rationale for any discount

### Common Objections & Responses

**"Your pricing is higher than competitors."**
→ "Our clients see 2-3x ROI within 6 months. What's the cost of going with a cheaper option that doesn't deliver results?"

**"We need to start smaller."**
→ "Let's start with a 90-day pilot on one service. If we hit agreed KPIs, we expand. Fair?"

**"Can we do month-to-month?"**
→ "Yes, at 20% premium. Most clients find the 12-month term pays for itself in month 5."

## 📝 Contract Terms

### Standard Contract Provisions

**Term:**
- Initial term: 12-24 months (based on package)
- Auto-renewal: 12 months (either party can opt out with 90 days notice)

**Termination:**
- For cause: Immediate (material breach, non-payment)
- For convenience: 90 days written notice (early termination fee applies)

**Early Termination Fee:**
- Months 1-6: 100% of remaining contract value
- Months 7-12: 50% of remaining contract value
- Months 13+: 25% of remaining contract value

**Service Credits:**
- Uptime <99.9%: 5% credit per 0.1% below threshold
- Critical bug >24 hrs: 10% credit per day
- Max credit: 50% of monthly fee

**Data Ownership:**
- Client owns all their data
- We retain anonymized, aggregated data for model improvement
- Data export available at any time (standard formats)
