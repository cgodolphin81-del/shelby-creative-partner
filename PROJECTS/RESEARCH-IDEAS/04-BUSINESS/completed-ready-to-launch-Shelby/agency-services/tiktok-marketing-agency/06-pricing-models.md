## 1. Pricing Model Overview

### Common Pricing Models

**1. Monthly Retainer**
- Fixed fee for defined services
- Predictable revenue for agency
- Preferred by clients for budgeting
- Best for: Ongoing management, content creation

**2. Percentage of Ad Spend**
- 10-20% of media budget
- Scales with client growth
- Aligns incentives
- Best for: Paid media management

**3. Performance-Based**
- Base fee + commission on results
- Higher risk, higher reward
- Builds trust
- Best for: Conversion-focused campaigns

**4. Project-Based**
- One-time fee for specific deliverables
- Clear scope, fixed price
- Best for: Audits, strategy, one-off campaigns

**5. Hourly/Daily Rate**
- Time-based billing
- Best for: Consulting, training, ad-hoc work

### Recommended Model Mix

**Early Stage (Months 1-12):**
- 60% Retainer
- 30% Project-based
- 10% Performance

**Growth Stage (Months 13-24):**
- 70% Retainer
- 20% Ad spend %
- 10% Performance

**Mature Stage (Months 25+):**
- 80% Retainer
- 15% Ad spend %
- 5% Performance

## 3. Percentage of Ad Spend

### Standard Rates

**Tiered Structure:**

**$0-10K/month ad spend:**
- 15% management fee
- Minimum: $1,000/month

**$10K-50K/month ad spend:**
- 12% management fee
- Minimum: $2,000/month

**$50K-100K/month ad spend:**
- 10% management fee
- Minimum: $5,000/month

**$100K-500K/month ad spend:**
- 8% management fee
- Minimum: $8,000/month

**$500K+/month ad spend:**
- 6% management fee
- Minimum: $25,000/month

### Why Percentage Works

**For Agencies:**
- Scales with client success
- Aligns incentives
- Revenue grows as client scales
- No need to renegotiate frequently

**For Clients:**
- Transparent pricing
- Pay for value
- Scales with their budget
- No surprise fees

### Contract Terms

**Minimum Commitment:**
- 3 months minimum
- 6 months preferred
- 12 months for enterprise

**Price Protection:**
- Fee percentage locked for contract term
- Ad spend increases don't change %
- Only new contracts reset terms

**Minimum Fees:**
- Ensures profitability on small accounts
- Covers base overhead
- Protects against ad spend cuts

### Example Calculations

**Client A: $5K/month ad spend**
- Management fee: 15% = $750/month
- But minimum is $1,000
- Actual fee: $1,000/month

**Client B: $25K/month ad spend**
- Management fee: 12% = $3,000/month
- Actual fee: $3,000/month

**Client C: $200K/month ad spend**
- Management fee: 8% = $16,000/month
- Actual fee: $16,000/month

## 4. Performance-Based Pricing

### Models

**1. Base + Commission**
- Reduced base retainer
- Commission on attributed revenue
- Commission rates: 5-15% of attributed sales

**Example:**
- Base: $3,000/month (instead of $5,000)
- Commission: 10% of TikTok-attributed revenue
- If TikTok drives $50K in sales: $3K + $5K = $8,000 total

**2. Pure Performance**
- No base fee
- Higher commission (15-25%)
- High risk, high reward
- Best for: Confident agencies, proven models

**3. CPA (Cost Per Action)**
- Fixed fee per conversion
- Examples: $20 per lead, $50 per purchase
- Requires robust tracking
- Best for: Lead gen, e-commerce

**4. ROAS-Based**
- Base fee + bonus for hitting ROAS targets
- Example: 10% ROAS = 100% bonus
- 15% ROAS = 150% bonus
- Aligns with client profitability

### Implementation Requirements

**Tracking Must-Haves:**
- TikTok Pixel installed
- Conversion tracking configured
- Unique discount codes
- Affiliate links
- UTM parameters
- Attribution model agreed upon

**Contract Clarity:**
- Definition of "conversion"
- Attribution window (7-day click, 1-day view)
- Exclusions (refunds, fraud)
- Reporting frequency
- Verification process

### Risk Management

**For Agencies:**
- Minimum guarantee (e.g., $3K/month regardless of performance)
- Cap on commission (protects against outliers)
- Clear attribution rules
- 3-month minimum

**For Clients:**
- Performance guarantees
- KPI milestones
- Exit clauses if targets not met
- Transparent reporting

### When to Use Performance Pricing

**Good Fit:**
- Established tracking infrastructure
- Clear conversion goals
- Confident in capabilities
- Strong relationship with client

**Not Recommended:**
- New accounts with no data
- Poor tracking setup
- Long sales cycles
- Multiple touchpoints (hard to attribute)

## 5. Hybrid Models

### Recommended Hybrid Structures

**Model 1: Retainer + Ad Spend %**
- Base retainer for services
- Plus % of ad spend
- Example: $5K retainer + 10% of ad spend

**Model 2: Base + Performance Bonus**
- Standard retainer
- Bonus for hitting KPIs
- Example: $5K base + $2K bonus for ROAS > 4x

**Model 3: Tiered Retainer**
- Base package
- Add-on modules
- Example: $5K base + $2K for influencer management + $1K for ads

**Model 4: Project + Retainer**
- One-time project fee
- Ongoing retainer
- Example: $10K strategy project + $5K/month retainer

### Hybrid Example: Full-Service Package

**Structure:**
- Content Creation: $4,000/month
- Community Management: $1,500/month
- Ad Management: 12% of $30K spend = $3,600
- Influencer Management: $2,000/month
- **Total: $11,100/month**

**Benefits:**
- Transparent pricing
- Client sees value breakdown
- Easy to adjust scope
- Scalable

## 7. Client Tier Strategy

### Client Segmentation

**Tier 1: Dream Clients**
- Budget: $10K-20K+/month
- Examples: Well-funded startups, established brands
- Strategy: White-glove service, custom solutions
- Target: 5-10 clients

**Tier 2: Growth Clients**
- Budget: $5K-10K/month
- Examples: Scaling e-commerce, SaaS companies
- Strategy: Standard packages, efficient delivery
- Target: 15-20 clients

**Tier 3: Starter Clients**
- Budget: $2K-5K/month
- Examples: Early-stage brands, local businesses
- Strategy: Streamlined delivery, automation
- Target: 20-30 clients

**Tier 4: Project Clients**
- Budget: One-time $1K-10K
- Examples: Audit, strategy, training
- Strategy: Standardized offerings
- Target: Ongoing, variable

### Ideal Client Mix (Year 2+)

**Revenue Distribution:**
- Tier 1: 50% of revenue (5-8 clients)
- Tier 2: 35% of revenue (10-15 clients)
- Tier 3: 15% of revenue (15-20 clients)

**Why This Mix:**
- Tier 1 provides stability and high margins
- Tier 2 provides volume and predictability
- Tier 3 provides pipeline for upselling
- Diversification reduces risk

### Upsell Strategy

**From Starter to Growth:**
- At 3 months, review performance
- Show ROI and growth potential
- Present upgraded package
- Offer migration discount (1 month free)

**From Growth to Scale:**
- At 6 months, present case for investment
- Show competitive advantage of more spend
- Offer dedicated team
- Present enterprise features

**Add-On Services:**
- Influencer management: +$2K-5K/month
- Additional content: +$1K-3K/month
- Extended community management: +$1K-2K/month
- Cross-platform expansion: +$2K-5K/month

## 8. Contract Terms

### Standard Terms

**Contract Length:**
- Minimum: 3 months
- Standard: 6 months
- Preferred: 12 months
- Enterprise: 24 months

**Auto-Renewal:**
- Contracts auto-renew unless 60-day notice given
- Prevents churn
- Encourages long-term thinking

**Price Increases:**
- Annual increase: 5-10%
- Noted in contract
- Applied on anniversary

**Payment Terms:**
- Due on receipt or Net 15
- Late fee: 1.5% per month
- Auto-bill on card on file

**Termination:**
- With cause: Immediate
- Without cause: 60-day notice + final payment
- Kill fee: 50% of remaining contract value

### Scope Changes

**Change Order Process:**
- Written request required
- Impact on timeline/budget outlined
- Approved before work begins
- $150/hour for unplanned work

**Out-of-Scope Work:**
- Billable at $150-250/hour
- Pre-approval required
- Tracked and invoiced monthly

### Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

**Response Times:**
- Email: 24 hours (business days)
- Slack (enterprise): 4 hours
- Emergency: 1 hour

**Delivery Times:**
- Content revisions: 48 hours
- Reports: 3rd of month (monthly)
- Strategy sessions: Within 48 hours of request

**Performance Guarantees:**
- Follower growth: Not guaranteed (algorithm controlled)
- Engagement rate: Minimum 3% target
- Ad ROAS: Not guaranteed (market dependent)

## Pricing Psychology & Sales

### Anchoring

**Always Present 3 Tiers:**
- Good: $5K (anchor low)
- Better: $10K (target)
- Best: $20K (anchor high)

**Why It Works:**
- Middle option feels reasonable
- High option makes middle seem cheaper
- Low option feels limiting

### Value Framing

**Don't Say:**
- "This package costs $5,000/month"

**Do Say:**
- "For $5,000/month, you get [list value]"
- "That's less than $170/day for [benefits]"
- "Most clients see $50K+ in first 90 days"

### ROI Calculations

**For E-commerce Clients:**
```
Investment: $10,000/month
Expected Results:
- Additional revenue: $100,000/month
- Profit (50% margin): $50,000
- Net gain: $40,000/month
- ROI: 400%
```

**For SaaS Clients:**
```
Investment: $10,000/month
Expected Results:
- 50 qualified leads
- 10 customers (20% conversion)
- $100 MRR per customer
- $1,000 MRR = $12K ARR per customer
- Total ARR: $120,000
- Net gain: $108,000/year
```

### Objection Handling

**"It's too expensive":**
- "I understand budget is a consideration"
- "What's the ROI you need to see?"
- "Let's start with a smaller scope"
- "What would make this a no-brainer?"

**"We need to think about it":**
- "What specific concerns do you have?"
- "Is it budget or timing?"
- "Can I provide any additional info?"
- "When would be a good time to reconnect?"

**"Can you match their price?":**
- "I'd love to understand what they're including"
- "Our approach is different - here's why"
- "We can adjust scope to fit budget"
- "Let's focus on outcomes, not just price"

## Final Pricing Recommendations

### For New Agencies (Year 1)

**Start Conservative:**
- Underprice slightly to get case studies
- Raise prices every 3-5 clients
- Focus on value, not features

**Package Structure:**
- 1 starter package ($2K-3K)
- 1 growth package ($5K-7K)
- 1 custom (enterprise)

**Contract Terms:**
- 3-month minimum
- Net 15 payment terms
- Clear scope definition

### For Growing Agencies (Year 2+)

**Raise Prices:**
- 10-20% increase for new clients
- Grandfather existing clients
- Introduce higher tiers

**Add Premium Services:**
- Performance bonuses
- Priority support
- Dedicated teams

**Optimize Client Mix:**
- Fire bottom 10% of clients
- Upsell top performers
- Focus on ideal clients

### For Mature Agencies (Year 3+)

**Premium Pricing:**
- Charge for expertise and results
- Minimum $10K for most clients
- Enterprise: $20K-50K+

**Specialization:**
- Industry-specific packages
- Service-specific pricing
- Custom enterprise solutions

**Value-Based Pricing:**
- Price on outcomes, not hours
- Performance-based components
- Revenue share options

**Remember:** Pricing is not just about covering costs—it's about communicating value, attracting the right clients, and building a sustainable business. Charge what you're worth, deliver exceptional results, and raise your prices as you prove your value.
