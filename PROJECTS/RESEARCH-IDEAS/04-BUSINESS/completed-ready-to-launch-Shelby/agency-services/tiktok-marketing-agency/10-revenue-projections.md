## 1. Revenue Model Overview

### Revenue Streams

**1. Monthly Retainers (70-80% of revenue)**
- Starter packages ($2K-5K/month)
- Growth packages ($5K-10K/month)
- Scale packages ($10K-20K/month)
- Enterprise packages ($20K+/month)

**2. Ad Spend Management Fees (10-15% of revenue)**
- 10-15% of client ad spend
- Scales with client success
- Recurring revenue

**3. One-Time Projects (5-10% of revenue)**
- Strategy audits ($1K-10K)
- Campaign setup ($2K-20K)
- Training workshops ($3K-10K)
- Content production (project-based)

**4. Performance Bonuses (5% of revenue)**
- ROAS bonuses
- Revenue share
- KPI achievement bonuses

### Revenue Mix by Year

**Year 1:**
- Retainers: 60%
- Ad spend fees: 25%
- One-time projects: 10%
- Performance bonuses: 5%

**Year 2:**
- Retainers: 75%
- Ad spend fees: 15%
- One-time projects: 7%
- Performance bonuses: 3%

**Year 3:**
- Retainers: 80%
- Ad spend fees: 12%
- One-time projects: 5%
- Performance bonuses: 3%

### Key Profitability Levers

**1. Increase Average Contract Value:**
- Current: $5,500 → $7,000 → $9,000
- Target by Year 5: $12,000+
- Impact: +20-30% revenue without adding clients

**2. Improve Delivery Efficiency:**
- Current: 6-8 clients per employee
- Target by Year 3: 10-12 clients per employee
- Impact: +30-40% profit margin

**3. Reduce Churn:**
- Current: 10% → 8% → 5%
- Target by Year 3: <3%
- Impact: +15-20% revenue (retention is cheaper than acquisition)

**4. Optimize Client Mix:**
- Current: Mix of starter, growth, scale
- Target: 70% scale/enterprise, 30% growth
- Impact: +25-35% average contract value

## 7. Cash Flow Management

### Cash Flow Challenges

**Year 1 Challenges:**
- Negative cash flow first 6-8 months
- Client payments (Net 15-30) vs. payroll (weekly/bi-weekly)
- Equipment and software upfront costs
- Marketing spend before revenue

**Solutions:**
- Maintain 3-6 months operating cash reserve
- Require 50% upfront on projects
- Net 15 payment terms (not Net 30-60)
- Line of credit for cash flow gaps
- Invoice factoring (if needed)

## 10. Risk Mitigation

### Key Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**Risk 1: Client Concentration**
- **Risk:** Too much revenue from few clients
- **Mitigation:**
  - No single client > 10% of revenue
  - Diversify across industries
  - Build pipeline constantly
  - Year 1: Max 2 clients @ $5K = $10K (17% of $594K)
  - Year 3: Max 2 clients @ $20K = $40K (0.6% of $6.3M)

**Risk 2: Platform Dependency**
- **Risk:** TikTok algorithm changes or platform decline
- **Mitigation:**
  - Diversify to other platforms (Instagram, YouTube, etc.)
  - Build transferable skills
  - Stay ahead of trends
  - 20% of revenue from non-TikTok services by Year 3

**Risk 3: Economic Downturn**
- **Risk:** Clients cut marketing budgets
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on ROI-driven services
  - Build cash reserves (6+ months)
  - Flexible pricing models
  - Diversify client sizes (enterprise more stable)

**Risk 4: Talent Retention**
- **Risk:** Key team members leave
- **Mitigation:**
  - Competitive compensation
  - Equity/profit sharing
  - Professional development
  - Strong culture
  - Cross-train team members

**Risk 5: Cash Flow Issues**
- **Risk:** Late payments, uneven revenue
- **Mitigation:**
  - 50% upfront on projects
  - Net 15 payment terms
  - Line of credit
  - 3-6 months cash reserve
  - Active accounts receivable management

**Risk 6: Competition**
- **Risk:** Market saturation, price competition
- **Mitigation:**
  - Specialize in specific niches
  - Build strong case studies
  - Focus on results, not price
  - Develop proprietary methodologies
  - Build brand and reputation
