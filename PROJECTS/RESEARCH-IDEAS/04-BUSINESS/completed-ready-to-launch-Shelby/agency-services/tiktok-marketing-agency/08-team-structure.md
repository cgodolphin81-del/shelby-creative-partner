## 1. Organizational Structure

### Agency Structure Options

**Option 1: Functional Structure (Recommended)**
```
CEO/Founder
├── Head of Operations
│   ├── Account Managers (3-5)
│   └── Operations Coordinator
├── Head of Creative
│   ├── Content Creators (4-6)
│   ├── Video Editors (4-6)
│   └── Graphic Designer
├── Head of Paid Media
│   ├── Ad Buyers (3-4)
│   └── Media Analyst
├── Head of Influencer Relations
│   ├── Influencer Managers (2-3)
│   └── Outreach Specialist
└── Head of Strategy
    ├── Strategists (2-3)
    └── Data Analyst
```

**Option 2: Client-Based Structure**
```
CEO/Founder
├── Account Directors (2-3)
│   ├── Account Managers (2 per director)
│   ├── Creators (assigned to accounts)
│   ├── Editors (assigned to accounts)
│   └── Ad Buyers (assigned to accounts)
└── Support Functions
    ├── Operations
    ├── Strategy
    └── HR/Finance
```

**Option 3: Hybrid (Best for 10-30 people)**
```
CEO/Founder
├── Operations Director
│   ├── Account Management Team
│   └── Operations Team
├── Creative Director
│   ├── Content Creation Team
│   └── Post-Production Team
├── Media Director
│   └── Paid Media Team
├── Influencer Director
│   └── Influencer Team
└── Strategy Director
    └── Strategy & Analytics Team
```

### Recommended Structure (Year 2-3)

**Executive:**
- CEO/Founder (strategy, partnerships, major accounts)
- COO (operations, processes, hiring)

**Client-Facing:**
- Head of Account Management (3-5 Account Managers)
- Head of Strategy (2-3 Strategists)

**Creative:**
- Head of Creative (1)
- Content Creators (4-6)
- Video Editors (4-6)
- Graphic Designer (1)

**Paid Media:**
- Head of Paid Media (1)
- Ad Buyers (3-4)

**Influencer:**
- Head of Influencer Relations (1)
- Influencer Managers (2-3)

**Support:**
- Operations Manager (1)
- Data Analyst (1)
- Marketing Manager (1)
- HR/Finance (1 part-time or outsourced)

**Total: 25-35 people**

## 2. Key Roles & Responsibilities

### Account Manager

**Role:** Primary client contact, relationship management

**Responsibilities:**
- Weekly/monthly client check-ins
- Project coordination across teams
- Scope management
- Billing and invoicing coordination
- Client satisfaction monitoring
- Upsell identification
- Issue resolution

**Skills Needed:**
- Excellent communication
- Project management
- Problem-solving
- Client service mindset
- TikTok platform knowledge
- Basic analytics understanding

**KPIs:**
- Client satisfaction score (NPS)
- Client retention rate
- Account growth (upsells)
- Response time (<4 hours)
- Project on-time delivery

**Reporting To:** Head of Account Management

**Salary Range:** $50K-80K + bonus

## 6. Operational Workflows

### Client Onboarding Workflow

**Step 1: Contract Signed (Day 1)**
- Send welcome email
- Send onboarding questionnaire
- Add to CRM
- Create project in PM tool
- Schedule kickoff call

**Step 2: Access & Assets (Days 2-3)**
- Request account access
- Request brand assets
- Review brand guidelines
- Set up tracking (pixel, UTM)
- Grant team access

**Step 3: Discovery (Days 4-7)**
- Conduct strategy sessions
- Audit existing presence
- Competitor analysis
- Goal confirmation
- Present initial strategy

**Step 4: Planning (Days 8-14)**
- Develop content calendar
- Plan first campaigns
- Influencer research
- Create production schedule
- Approval from client

**Step 5: Launch Prep (Days 15-21)**
- Begin content production
- Set up ad campaigns
- Outreach to influencers
- Test all tracking
- Final approvals

**Step 6: Go Live (Day 21+)**
- Publish first content
- Launch campaigns
- Begin reporting
- Schedule first check-in

**Total Onboarding Time: 21-30 days**

---

### Content Production Workflow

**Step 1: Ideation (Weekly)**
- Trend research
- Client strategy review
- Brainstorming session
- Calendar planning
- Approval

**Step 2: Scripting (2-3 days before filming)**
- Write scripts
- Create shot lists
- Prepare props/locations
- Client review (if needed)

**Step 3: Filming (1-2 days)**
- Set up equipment
- Record content
- B-roll capture
- Audio check
- Backup footage

**Step 4: Editing (2-3 days)**
- Review footage
- Rough cut
- Client feedback
- Revisions
- Final export

**Step 5: Scheduling & Publishing**
- Schedule posts
- Write captions
- Select hashtags
- Schedule stories
- Publish

**Step 6: Engagement & Reporting**
- Monitor first 4 hours
- Respond to comments
- Track performance
- Weekly reporting

**Total Production Time: 7-14 days per content batch**

---

### Ad Campaign Workflow

**Step 1: Strategy (1-2 days)**
- Review campaign goals
- Define target audience
- Set budget
- Choose ad formats
- KPI definition

**Step 2: Setup (1 day)**
- Create campaign structure
- Set up ad groups
- Configure tracking
- Upload creatives
- Set bidding

**Step 3: Launch (Day 3)**
- Activate campaigns
- Monitor first 24 hours
- Check tracking
- Verify delivery
- Initial optimization

**Step 4: Optimization (Ongoing)**
- Daily monitoring
- Creative refresh (weekly)
- Audience testing
- Bid adjustments
- Budget reallocation

**Step 5: Reporting (Weekly)**
- Performance analysis
- Insights generation
- Recommendations
- Client presentation
- Strategy adjustment

**Total Campaign Setup: 2-3 days**
**Optimization Cycle: Daily monitoring, weekly major changes**

---

### Influencer Campaign Workflow

**Step 1: Strategy & Brief (1-2 days)**
- Define campaign goals
- Create brief
- Set budget
- Define deliverables
- Timeline

**Step 2: Discovery & Vetting (3-5 days)**
- Research influencers
- Analyze audiences
- Check past collabs
- Risk assessment
- Create shortlist

**Step 3: Outreach (5-7 days)**
- Send outreach messages
- Follow-ups
- Negotiate terms
- Confirm availability
- Select influencers

**Step 4: Contract & Onboarding (3-5 days)**
- Send contracts
- Collect invoices
- Provide brief
- Ship products (if applicable)
- Confirm timeline

**Step 5: Content Creation (7-14 days)**
- Influencer creates content
- Submit for review
- Provide feedback
- Request revisions
- Final approval

**Step 6: Publishing & Tracking (Ongoing)**
- Schedule posts
- Monitor launch
- Track performance
- Engage with comments
- Compile results

**Total Campaign Time: 30-45 days**

## 9. Scaling Strategy

### Scaling Triggers

**Hire When:**
- Team consistently working 50+ hour weeks
- Client satisfaction dropping
- You're the bottleneck on decisions
- Revenue per employee < $150K
- Processes documented and repeatable

**Don't Hire When:**
- Only temporary spike in workload
- Can automate instead
- Role not clearly defined
- Cash flow uncertain
- Processes not documented

### Scaling Milestones

**$1M ARR (Year 2):**
- 12-15 people
- $70K-85K revenue per employee
- 2-3 day-week process
- Standardized onboarding

**$3M ARR (Year 3):**
- 25-30 people
- $100K-120K revenue per employee
- Department heads in place
- Advanced automation

**$5M+ ARR (Year 4+):**
- 40-50 people
- $125K+ revenue per employee
- Specialized teams
- Enterprise-grade processes

### Delegation Framework

**Delegate When:**
- Task is repetitive
- Someone else can do 80% as well
- You're spending >5 hours/week
- It's not your unique value add

**Keep for Yourself:**
- Major client relationships
- Strategic decisions
- Key partnerships
- Hiring senior team
- Culture building

### Documentation Standards

**Document Everything:**
- SOPs for all recurring tasks
- Client onboarding checklists
- Creative brief templates
- Reporting templates
- Tool tutorials
- Meeting agendas and notes

**Where to Document:**
- Notion (main wiki)
- Google Docs (templates)
- Loom (video tutorials)
- PM tool (project-specific)
