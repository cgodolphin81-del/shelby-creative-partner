## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Legal & Compliance
- This guide provides general information, not legal advice
- Consult with attorney for contracts and legal matters
- Ensure compliance with FTC guidelines for disclosures
- Follow TikTok's advertising policies
- Understand data privacy requirements (GDPR, CCPA)

### Platform Changes
- TikTok's algorithm and features change frequently
- Stay updated on platform news
- Adapt strategies as needed
- Don't rely on tactics that may become obsolete

### Realistic Expectations
- Year 1 will be challenging - expect learning curve
- Not every client will be a success - know when to let go
- Results take time - don't expect overnight success
- Competition is increasing - differentiate through quality

### Ethical Considerations
- Be transparent with clients about what's possible
- Don't promise guaranteed results (algorithm controlled)
- Maintain honest relationships with influencers
- Follow all platform policies and guidelines
