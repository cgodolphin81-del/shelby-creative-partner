## 🛠️ Essential Tools

### WhatsApp BSP (Choose One)
- **WATI** - Best for beginners ($49-499/mo)
- **Twilio** - Best for developers (custom pricing)
- **Interakt** - Best for India (₹2,500-25,000/mo)
- **360dialog** - Best for EU (€0.0038-0.0072/msg)

### Supporting Tools
- **Chatbot Builder** - Landbot, Dialogflow, or WATI built-in
- **CRM** - HubSpot, Salesforce, or Zoho
- **Automation** - Zapier or Make
- **Analytics** - Google Analytics + Looker Studio
- **Project Management** - ClickUp, Asana, or Monday.com

## ⚠️ Critical Success Factors

### Must-Haves
1. **Compliance First** - Follow opt-in rules strictly
2. **Quality Over Quantity** - Better to have fewer, happy clients
3. **Prove ROI Early** - Show results in first 30 days
4. **Document Everything** - Processes, playbooks, learnings
5. **Stay Compliant** - Platform rules can change overnight

### Avoid These Mistakes
1. ❌ Buying phone/email lists
2. ❌ Sending without clear opt-in
3. ❌ Over-messaging (max 1-2 broadcasts/week)
4. ❌ Underpricing your services
5. ❌ Skipping compliance checks

## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

### Track Monthly
- **MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- **Client Count** (Total, New, Churned)
- **CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **LTV** (Lifetime Value)
- **Churn Rate** (Target: <3%/month)
- **Gross Margin** (Target: 60-70%)
- **Net Margin** (Target: 40-60%)

### Client Success Metrics
- **Open Rate** (Target: 85-98%)
- **CTR** (Target: 20-45%)
- **Conversion Rate** (Target: 8-15%)
- **Opt-Out Rate** (Target: <2%)
- **ROI** (Target: 5-15x)
