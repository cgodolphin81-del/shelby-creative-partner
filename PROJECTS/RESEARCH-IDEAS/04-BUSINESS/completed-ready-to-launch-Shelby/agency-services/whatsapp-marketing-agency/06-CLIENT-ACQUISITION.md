# Client Acquisition - Target Verticals & Sales Playbooks

## 🎯 Overview

This guide covers how to find, pitch, and close WhatsApp marketing clients in the most profitable verticals.

## 📊 TARGET VERTICALS

### Tier 1: Best Verticals (Start Here)

#### 1. E-commerce / D2C Brands

**Why They're Perfect:**
- High transaction volume
- Clear ROI (abandoned cart recovery alone = 10-25% revenue lift)
- Already understand digital marketing
- Budget available ($3K-15K/mo typical)
- Urgent pain points (cart abandonment, repeat purchases)

**Ideal Client Profile:**
```
✓ Revenue: $50K-500K/month
✓ Order volume: 500-5,000 orders/month
✓ Platform: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento
✓ AOV: $50+
✓ Repeat purchase rate: 20%+
✓ Currently using email marketing (Klaviyo, etc.)
✓ Mobile traffic: 60%+
```

**Key Pain Points:**
- Email open rates declining (15-20%)
- High cart abandonment (70%+)
- Low repeat purchase rate
- Rising ad costs (Facebook, Google)
- Need for direct customer channel

**Decision Makers:**
- Founder/CEO (small companies)
- Head of Marketing / CMO
- E-commerce Manager
- Growth Manager

**Sales Cycle:** 2-4 weeks  
**Average Contract:** $5K-10K/mo  
**Close Rate:** 25-35%

{{your_name}}

## 💼 SALES PROCESS

### Stage 1: Discovery Call (30 minutes)

**Goal:** Understand their business, identify pain points, qualify fit

**Agenda:**
```
0-5 min:   Rapport building, agenda setting
5-15 min:  Current situation (questions below)
15-25 min: Pain points & goals
25-30 min: Next steps
```

**Discovery Questions:**

```
CURRENT SITUATION:
□ Tell me about your business and ideal customer
□ What marketing channels are you currently using?
□ How's email marketing performing? (open rates, CTR)
□ What's your monthly revenue / order volume?
□ What's your average order value?
□ What's your cart abandonment rate (if e-commerce)?
□ How do you currently communicate with customers?

PAIN POINTS:
□ What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?
□ Where are you losing potential customers?
□ What happens when someone abandons their cart?
□ How do you handle customer support inquiries?
□ What's your repeat purchase rate?
□ What have you tried to solve these problems?

GOALS:
□ What would success look like in 6 months?
□ What revenue impact would WhatsApp marketing need to have?
□ What's your timeline for implementation?
□ Who else is involved in this decision?

BUDGET & AUTHORITY:
□ Have you worked with agencies before? What was your budget?
□ What's your budget range for this initiative?
□ Besides yourself, who needs to approve this?
□ What's your decision-making process?
```

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
```
□ BUDGET: Can they afford $3K-10K/mo?
□ AUTHORITY: Are you talking to the decision maker?
□ NEED: Do they have clear pain points we can solve?
□ TIMING: Are they ready to move in next 30-60 days?
```

**Red Flags (Disqualify Early):**
```
✗ Budget under $2K/mo (not a fit for our model)
✗ No clear pain points ("just exploring")
✗ No decision-making authority
✗ Timeline is 6+ months out
✗ Unwilling to share basic metrics
✗ Previous bad agency experience (trust issues)
✗ Looking for "guaranteed results" (unrealistic)
```

---

### Stage 2: Proposal & Presentation (45-60 minutes)

**Goal:** Present custom solution, show ROI, close the deal

**Presentation Structure:**
```
1. RECAP (5 min)
   - Summarize their situation
   - Confirm pain points
   - Restate goals

2. SOLUTION OVERVIEW (10 min)
   - Recommended services
   - Implementation timeline
   - Team structure

3. CASE STUDIES (10 min)
   - Similar client results
   - Specific metrics
   - Testimonials

4. INVESTMENT & ROI (10 min)
   - Pricing breakdown
   - Projected ROI
   - Payment terms

5. NEXT STEPS (5 min)
   - Contract review
   - Onboarding process
   - Start date
```

**ROI Projection Example:**

```
CURRENT STATE:
Monthly Revenue: $200,000
Cart Abandonment Rate: 72%
Abandoned Carts/Month: 450
Recovery Rate (email only): 8%
Recovered Revenue: $18,000

WITH WHATSAPP:
New Recovery Rate: 18% (email + WhatsApp)
New Recovered Revenue: $40,500
INCREMENTAL REVENUE: $22,500/month

Additional Revenue Streams:
- Post-purchase sequences: +$8,000/month
- VIP broadcasts: +$12,000/month
- Win-back campaigns: +$5,000/month

TOTAL INCREMENTAL REVENUE: $47,500/month

INVESTMENT:
Our Fee: $6,500/month
WhatsApp Costs: ~$2,000/month
TOTAL INVESTMENT: $8,500/month

ROI:
($47,500 - $8,500) / $8,500 = 459% ROI
Payback Period: < 1 week
```

**Objection Handling:**

```
OBJECTION: "It's too expensive"
RESPONSE: "I understand budget is a consideration. Let's 
look at the ROI - you're investing $8,500 to generate 
$47,500 in incremental revenue. That's a 459% return. 
Even if we only achieve half of that, you're still 2x'ing 
your investment. Does that change the equation?"

OBJECTION: "We need to think about it"
RESPONSE: "Absolutely, this is an important decision. 
What specifically do you need to think through? Is it 
the investment, the timeline, or something about the 
solution itself? [Listen] What would need to be true 
for this to be a clear yes?"

OBJECTION: "We've been burned by agencies before"
RESPONSE: "I completely understand. Can you share what 
happened? [Listen] Here's how we're different: 
[specific differentiators]. Plus, we work month-to-month 
after the initial 3 months. If we're not delivering, 
you can leave. We earn your business every month."

OBJECTION: "Let me talk to my partner/team"
RESPONSE: "That makes complete sense. Would it be helpful 
to schedule a follow-up call where I can answer their 
questions directly? Or would you prefer I send over a 
summary they can review?"

OBJECTION: "Can you guarantee results?"
RESPONSE: "I can't guarantee specific results because 
your business is unique. But I can tell you: we've 
worked with [X] similar brands, and the average result 
is [Y]. We're confident because we've done this before. 
Plus, our contract is month-to-month after 3 months. 
If we don't deliver, you're not locked in."
```

## 🏆 CLOSING TIPS

### Do's:
✅ Ask for the close directly ("Ready to get started?")
✅ Use assumptive language ("When we launch..." not "If...")
✅ Create urgency (limited onboarding slots, etc.)
✅ Get commitment on next steps before ending call
✅ Follow up within 24 hours

### Don'ts:
❌ Don't oversell or overpromise
❌ Don't badmouth competitors
❌ Don't discount without getting something in return
❌ Don't chase indefinitely (know when to move on)
❌ Don't skip the discovery call (always qualify first)
