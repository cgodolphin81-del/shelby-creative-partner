## ⚠️ Critical Success Factors

1. **Compliance is non-negotiable** — One violation can ban your API access
2. **Opt-in quality > quantity** — Permission-based lists convert 5-10x better
3. **Vertical specialization** — Deep expertise in 2-3 industries beats generalist approach
4. **Automation + human touch** — Chatbots handle 80%, humans handle escalations
5. **Measurable ROI** — Track everything, prove value monthly
