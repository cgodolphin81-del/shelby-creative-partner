# Brand Discovery Exercises

## Exercise 1: Brand Archetype Mapping

**Instructions:** Choose the archetype that best resonates with your brand.

### The 12 Brand Archetypes:

**1. The Hero**  
*Motto:* "Where there's a will, there's a way"  
*Examples:* Nike, BMW, FedEx  
*Traits:* Courageous, determined, inspiring

**2. The Outlaw**  
*Motto:* "Rules are made to be broken"  
*Examples:* Harley-Davidson, Virgin, Diesel  
*Traits:* Rebellious, revolutionary, disruptive

**3. The Sage**  
*Motto:* "The truth will set you free"  
*Examples:* Google, BBC, TED  
*Traits:* Wise, knowledgeable, trustworthy

**4. The Innocent**  
*Motto:* "Free to be you and me"  
*Examples:* Dove, McDonald's, Coca-Cola  
*Traits:* Pure, optimistic, simple

**5. The Explorer**  
*Motto:* "Don't fence me in"  
*Examples:* Jeep, Patagonia, The North Face  
*Traits:* Adventurous, ambitious, independent

**6. The Creator**  
*Motto:* "If you can imagine it, it can be done"  
*Examples:* Apple, LEGO, Adobe  
*Traits:* Innovative, imaginative, artistic

**7. The Ruler**  
*Motto:* "Power isn't everything, it's the only thing"  
*Examples:* Mercedes-Benz, American Express, Rolex  
*Traits:* Authoritative, prestigious, controlling

**8. The Magician**  
*Motto:* "I make things happen"  
*Examples:* Disney, Dyson, Red Bull  
*Traits:* Visionary, transformative, charismatic

**9. The Regular Guy/Gal**  
*Motto:* "All men and women are created equal"  
*Examples:* IKEA, Target, eBay  
*Traits:* Down-to-earth, relatable, authentic

**10. The Lover**  
*Motto:* "You're the only one"  
*Examples:* Chanel, Victoria's Secret, Alfa Romeo  
*Traits:* Passionate, sensual, intimate

**11. The Caregiver**  
*Motto:* "Love your neighbor as yourself"  
*Examples:* Johnson & Johnson, Pampers, UNICEF  
*Traits:* Nurturing, protective, generous

**12. The Jester**  
*Motto:* "If I can't dance, I don't want to be part of your revolution"  
*Examples:* Old Spice, M&M's, Skittles  
*Traits:* Playful, humorous, spontaneous
