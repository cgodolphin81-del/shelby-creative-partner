| H3-H4 | 1.4 | 0 |
| Body | 1.6 | 0 |
| Small | 1.5 | 0.01em |

---

### Typography Best Practices

**DO:**
✓ Use font hierarchy consistently
✓ Maintain readable line lengths (50-75 characters)
✓ Use appropriate font weights for emphasis
✓ Ensure sufficient contrast

**DON'T:**
✗ Use more than 2-3 fonts per design
✗ Use ALL CAPS for long text
✗ Stretch or distort fonts
✗ Use light fonts on dark backgrounds (or vice versa)
