# Revenue Projections & Financial Model

## 3-Year Financial Planning for Discord Community Agency

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

### Assumptions & Methodology

This financial model is based on:
- Conservative client acquisition rates
- Realistic churn rates (10-15%/month)
- Standard pricing tiers
- Gradual team scaling
- Operating expenses based on industry standards

**Key Assumptions:**
- Average client lifetime: 18-24 months
- Average contract value: $5,000/mo
- Gross margin: 65-75%
- Customer acquisition cost: $3,000-5,000

### Revenue Assumptions

**Conservative:**
- 50% of projections
- 20% higher churn
- 15% lower pricing
- 30% slower acquisition

**Base Case (as modeled):**
- Realistic market conditions
- Steady execution
- Moderate competition

**Optimistic:**
- 150% of projections

### Key Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Client churn >20%/mo | Medium | High | Improve onboarding, value delivery |
| Price compression | Medium | Medium | Differentiate, add premium services |
| Talent shortage | High | High | Build training pipeline, remote hiring |
| Platform risk (Discord) | Low | High | Diversify platforms, own customer data |
| Economic downturn | Medium | High | Focus on essential services, retainers |
