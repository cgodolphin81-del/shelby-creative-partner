# Pricing Models

## Strategic Pricing for Discord Community Management

---

## 💡 Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based, Not Hour-Based:** Price based on outcomes and value delivered, not hours worked.

**Tiered Approach:** Three clear tiers make decision-making easy for clients.

**Setup Fees:** Always charge setup fees to cover initial intensive work.

**Retainers:** Monthly recurring revenue provides stability and allows proactive work.

**Performance Bonuses:** Align incentives with client success.

## 💰 Revenue Share Models

### Premium Memberships
**Structure:** 20-30% of subscription revenue  
**Example:** 
- Client charges $9.99/month for premium access
- 500 premium members = $4,995/month
- Agency share (25%): $1,249/month
- **In addition to base retainer**

### Sponsorship Deals
**Structure:** 15-25% commission on deals  
**Example:**
- $10,000 sponsorship deal negotiated
- Agency commission (20%): $2,000
- **One-time or recurring based on deal structure**

### Affiliate Revenue
**Structure:** 10-20% of affiliate income generated  
**Track via:** Custom links, discount codes, attribution

## 💳 Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- **Invoicing:** Monthly in advance
- **Payment Due:** Net 7 or Net 15
- **Setup Fees:** 50% upfront, 50% on completion
- **Late Fees:** 1.5%/month on overdue balances

### Annual Prepay Discount
- **10% discount** for annual prepayment
- Improves cash flow
- Locks in clients

### Payment Methods
- Bank transfer (ACH/wire)
- Credit card (Stripe, 3% fee)
- Crypto (USDC, for Web3 clients)
- PayPal (for smaller clients)

## 📋 Pricing Psychology Tips

### 1. Anchor High
Start with Enterprise pricing in conversations. Makes Growth tier seem reasonable.

### 2. Three-Tier Rule
People naturally choose the middle option. Make Growth tier your target.

### 3. Show ROI
Always frame pricing against value:
- "$5,000/month = $167/day = less than one bad hire"
- "If we add 100 customers, you've paid for yourself 10x"

### 4. Setup Fee Justification
- "This covers 40+ hours of intensive setup work"
- "One-time investment for long-term infrastructure"

### 5. Contract Length Incentives
- 3 months: Standard pricing
- 6 months: 5% discount
- 12 months: 10% discount

## 🔄 Price Increase Strategy

### When to Raise Prices
1. **Consistently at capacity** (>80% team utilization)
2. **Delivering exceptional results** (case studies to prove it)
3. **Inflation/cost increases** (annual adjustment)
4. **Adding significant value** (new services, better tech)

### How to Raise Prices
1. **Grandfather existing clients** (6-12 months at current rate)
2. **Give 60-day notice** minimum
3. **Explain the value** being added
4. **Offer annual lock-in** at current rate

### Annual Increase
- Standard: 5-10% year-over-year
- Inflation adjustment: 3-5%
- Value-based: 10-20% (with proven ROI)
