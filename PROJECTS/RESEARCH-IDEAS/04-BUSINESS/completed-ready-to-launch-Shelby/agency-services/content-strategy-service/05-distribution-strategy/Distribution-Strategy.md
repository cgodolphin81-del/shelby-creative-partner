### 4.3 Social Engagement Rules

**Daily Tasks:**
- [ ] Respond to all comments (within 24 hours)
- [ ] Engage with 10-20 relevant posts in your niche
- [ ] Share 2-3 pieces of curated content
- [ ] Monitor brand mentions
- [ ] Check DMs/messages

**Weekly Tasks:**
- [ ] Review analytics
- [ ] Plan next week's content
- [ ] Engage with top followers
- [ ] Identify trending topics

### 4.4 Online Communities

| Community Type | Examples | Approach | Frequency |
|----------------|----------|----------|-----------|
| **Reddit** | Relevant subreddits | Value-first, no spam | 3-5x/week |
| **Quora** | Topic questions | Detailed, helpful answers | 5-10x/week |
| **Facebook Groups** | Industry groups | Engage, don't pitch | Daily |
| **LinkedIn Groups** | Professional groups | Share insights | 3-5x/week |
| **Slack/Discord** | Industry communities | Be helpful, build relationships | Daily |

**Community Engagement Rules:**
```
✓ DO:
  - Read community rules first
  - Provide genuine value
  - Answer questions thoroughly
  - Build relationships before promoting
  - Disclose affiliations when relevant

✗ DON'T:
  - Drop links without context
  - Copy-paste the same response
  - Pitch immediately after joining
  - Argue or be defensive
  - Spam multiple communities at once
```

## 6. Distribution Workflow

### 6.1 Content Distribution Checklist

**For Every Piece of Content:**

```
OWNED CHANNELS
□ Website/Blog published
□ Email newsletter included
□ Email nurture sequence updated
□ Community posted

SHARED CHANNELS
□ LinkedIn post created
□ Twitter thread/posts created
□ Instagram post/stories created
□ Facebook post created
□ Relevant communities shared (where allowed)

EARNED CHANNELS
□ Pitched to relevant journalists (if newsworthy)
□ Shared with influencers who might amplify
□ Submitted to relevant aggregators

PAID CHANNELS
□ Boosted (if budget allows)
□ Retargeting audience updated
□ A/B test creative planned
```

### 6.2 Distribution Timeline

| Time | Action | Channels |
|------|--------|----------|
| **Publish Day** | Initial launch | All owned + social |
| **Day 2** | Follow-up post | Social (different angle) |
| **Day 3** | Email newsletter | Email |
| **Day 5** | Community share | Reddit, groups, forums |
| **Day 7** | Paid boost (if applicable) | Paid social/search |
| **Week 2** | Influencer outreach | Earned |
| **Week 3** | Repurpose & re-share | Social (new format) |
| **Month 2** | Update & re-promote | Blog, email, social |
