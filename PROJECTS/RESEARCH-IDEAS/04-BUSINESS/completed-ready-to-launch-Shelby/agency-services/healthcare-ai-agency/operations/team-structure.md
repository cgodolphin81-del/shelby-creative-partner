# Team Structure

## Overview

HealthAI Consulting Group builds multidisciplinary teams combining healthcare expertise, AI/ML capabilities, compliance knowledge, and implementation excellence. This document outlines our organizational structure, roles, and hiring plans.

---

## Organizational Design Principles

1. **T-Shaped Professionals:** Deep expertise in one area, broad knowledge across domains
2. **Healthcare + AI Hybrid:** Every team member understands both healthcare and AI
3. **Compliance Embedded:** Compliance expertise integrated into all teams
4. **Client-Aligned:** Teams organized around client success
5. **Scalable:** Structure supports 3x growth without reorganization

---

## Leadership Team (C-Suite)

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
**Reports To:** Board of Directors
**Direct Reports:** All C-Suite

**Responsibilities:**
- Overall company strategy and vision
- Board relations and reporting
- Investor relations
- Key client relationships
- Company culture and values
- M&A strategy

**Qualifications:**
- 15+ years healthcare/technology experience
- 5+ years in executive leadership

## Culture & Values

### Core Values
1. **Patients First:** Every decision considers patient impact
2. **Compliance Non-Negotiable:** HIPAA compliance is table stakes
3. **Outcomes Over Outputs:** Measure success by client results
4. **Radical Transparency:** Honest communication always
5. **Continuous Learning:** Healthcare and AI evolve rapidly
6. **Team Over Individual:** Collective success
7. **Work-Life Harmony:** Sustainable high performance

### Culture Initiatives
- Weekly all-hands meetings
- Monthly learning sessions
- Quarterly offsites
- Annual company retreat
- Professional development budget ($5K/employee/year)
- Healthcare benefits (premium)
- Equity participation
- Flexible work arrangements
