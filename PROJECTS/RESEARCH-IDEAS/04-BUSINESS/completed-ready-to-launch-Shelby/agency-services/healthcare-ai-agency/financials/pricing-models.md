# Pricing Models

## Overview

HealthAI Consulting Group offers flexible pricing models designed to align with client needs, budget cycles, and value realization. All pricing is indicative and customized based on specific requirements.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Value-Based:** Pricing reflects business value delivered, not just hours worked
2. **Transparent:** Clear breakdown of costs with no hidden fees
3. **Flexible:** Multiple models to fit different procurement preferences
4. **Aligned:** Our success tied to client success
5. **Compliance-Inclusive:** HIPAA compliance costs built into all pricing

## Pricing Models

### Model 1: Fixed Price (Most Common)

**Best For:** Well-defined projects with clear requirements

**Structure:**
- Fixed total price based on scope
- Payment milestones tied to deliverables
- Change order process for scope changes

**Payment Schedule:**
| Milestone | Percentage | Trigger |
|-----------|------------|---------|
| Contract Execution | 20% | Signed agreement |
| Design Complete | 25% | Gate review approval |
| Build Complete | 25% | UAT ready |
| Go-Live | 20% | Production deployment |
| Project Close | 10% | 30 days post go-live |

**Pros:**
- Budget certainty for client
- Clear deliverables
- Simple procurement

**Cons:**
- Less flexible for changes
- Requires detailed upfront scoping
- Change orders can be slow

---

### Model 2: Time & Materials

**Best For:** Exploratory projects, ongoing optimization

**Structure:**
- Hourly/daily rates by role
- Not-to-exceed cap optional
- Weekly time tracking and reporting

**Rates (2024):**
| Role | Hourly Rate | Daily Rate |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Partner/Principal | $450 | $3,600 |
| Senior Consultant | $325 | $2,600 |
| Consultant | $250 | $2,000 |
| AI Engineer | $300 | $2,400 |
| Data Scientist | $300 | $2,400 |
| Integration Specialist | $275 | $2,200 |
| Project Manager | $275 | $2,200 |
| Change Manager | $250 | $2,000 |
| Trainer | $200 | $1,600 |

**Pros:**
- Maximum flexibility
- Fast to start
- Easy to adjust priorities

**Cons:**
- Budget uncertainty
- Requires active client management
- Can be more expensive if not managed

---

### Model 3: Hybrid (Fixed + T&M)

**Best For:** Projects with defined core + uncertain elements

**Structure:**
- Fixed price for core deliverables
- T&M for exploration/optimization
- Cap on T&M portion

**Example:**
- Core implementation: $200,000 fixed
- Optimization phase: $150/hour, $50,000 cap
- Total not-to-exceed: $250,000

**Pros:**
- Balance of certainty and flexibility
- Encourages efficiency on core
- Allows for discovery

**Cons:**
- More complex contracting
- Requires clear delineation
- Cap management needed

---

### Model 4: Value-Based / Risk-Share

**Best For:** High-confidence use cases with measurable outcomes

**Structure:**
- Lower upfront fee
- Performance-based bonuses
- Shared savings model

**Example (Billing AI):**
- Base fee: $150,000 (covers costs)
- Performance bonus: 20% of first-year collections increase
- Shared savings: 10% of ongoing annual improvements

**Example Structure:**
| Metric | Target | Bonus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Denial reduction | 30% | $50,000 |
| Collection increase | 15% | $75,000 |
| Days in A/R reduction | 20% | $25,000 |
| **Total potential** | | **$150,000** |

**Pros:**
- Aligned incentives
- Lower client risk
- Potential for higher revenue

**Cons:**
- Requires baseline agreement
- Complex measurement
- Longer payment timeline
- Higher risk for us

## ROI Calculator Inputs

### Cost Savings
- Labor reduction (FTE × fully loaded cost)
- Error reduction (error rate × cost per error)
- Supply reduction (if applicable)
- Facility cost reduction (if applicable)

### Revenue Enhancement
- Increased collections (% increase × baseline)
- Reduced denials (denial rate reduction × $/claim)
- Increased capacity (visits × margin/visit)
- Improved coding (HCC capture × reimbursement)

### Quality Improvements
- Readmission reduction (penalty avoidance)
- LOS reduction (beds freed × margin/bed-day)
- Quality bonus achievement (value-based care)
- Patient satisfaction (HCAHPS impact)

### Intangible Benefits
- Physician satisfaction (retention value)
- Patient experience (loyalty, referrals)
- Competitive differentiation
- Innovation reputation
