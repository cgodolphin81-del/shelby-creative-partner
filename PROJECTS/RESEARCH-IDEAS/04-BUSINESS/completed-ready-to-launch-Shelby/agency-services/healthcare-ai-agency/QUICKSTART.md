## Critical Success Factors

### 1. HIPAA Compliance is Non-Negotiable
- Invest in compliance from day one
- Execute BAAs with all clients
- Train all employees annually
- Document everything
- When in doubt, ask your CCO

### 2. Healthcare Expertise Matters
- Hire people who understand healthcare workflows
- Clinical validation is essential
- Physician champions are critical
- Understand the difference between IT and clinical IT

### 3. Prove ROI
- Healthcare is margin-constrained
- Document baseline metrics
- Measure outcomes rigorously
- Share success stories
- Build ROI into every proposal

### 4. Long Sales Cycles Require Patience
- 6-12 months is normal for health systems
- Multiple stakeholders involved
- Budget cycles matter
- Build pipeline accordingly
- Nurture relationships

### 5. Implementation Excellence
- Use proven methodology
- Staff projects appropriately
- Communicate constantly
- Manage change actively
- Don't cut corners on training

## Common Questions

### Q: How much capital do I need to start?
**A:** Minimum $500K for lean start, $2-3M recommended for proper launch. See [financials/revenue-projections.md](./financials/revenue-projections.md) for detailed models.

### Q: Do I need clinical staff on day one?
**A:** Yes, at least part-time CMO and clinical informaticist. Clinical credibility is essential.

### Q: What certifications do I need?
**A:** HIPAA compliance is mandatory. SOC 2 Type II and HITRUST recommended by Year 2. Individual certifications (CHC, CISSP, etc.) for key staff.

### Q: How do I price my services?
**A:** See [financials/pricing-models.md](./financials/pricing-models.md). Typical engagements: $150K-$500K. Managed services: $10K-$50K/month.

### Q: What's the biggest mistake new healthcare AI consultants make?
**A:** Underestimating compliance requirements and sales cycle length. Budget accordingly.

### Q: Can I start as a solo consultant?
**A:** Yes, but you'll need to partner for clinical expertise, compliance, and delivery capacity. Consider the "virtual firm" model initially.

### Q: How do I compete with Big 4 consulting firms?
**A:** Specialization, agility, and expertise. You can't out-spend them, but you can out-specialize them. Focus on healthcare AI specifically.
