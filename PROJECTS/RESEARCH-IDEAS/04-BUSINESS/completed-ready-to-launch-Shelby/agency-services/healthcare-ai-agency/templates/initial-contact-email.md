## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines That Work
- Personalized with organization name
- Reference mutual connections
- Mention specific challenges
- Include specific results/metrics
- Keep under 50 characters when possible

### Content Guidelines
- **Keep it brief** - Under 200 words when possible
- **Lead with value** - What's in it for them
- **Be specific** - Use real numbers and examples
- **One clear CTA** - One ask, not multiple
- **Mobile-friendly** - Short paragraphs, scannable

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 1: Initial email
- Day 4: First follow-up
- Day 8: Second follow-up with new value
- Day 15: Third follow-up
- Day 30: Breakup email
- Then: Quarterly nurture

### Compliance Notes
- Include physical address (CAN-SPAM)
- Include unsubscribe option
- Don't make false claims
- Respect Do Not Contact requests
- Document all communications in CRM
