# 01 - Niche Selection Guide
## 20 Best Local Niches for Rank & Rent

Choosing the right niche is the foundation of your lead gen business. This guide breaks down the 20 most profitable local service niches with realistic lead values and competition analysis.

---

## 🎯 Selection Criteria

When evaluating niches, consider these factors:

| Factor | Ideal Range | Why It Matters |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Avg. Job Value** | $500+ | Higher value = more willing to pay for leads |
| **Lead Value** | $50-$500 | What businesses will pay per lead |
| **Search Volume** | 1K-50K/mo | Enough demand to generate leads |
| **Competition** | Low-Medium | Easier to rank quickly |
| **Urgency** | High | Emergency services convert faster |
| **Recurring Need** | Medium+ | Repeat customers = stable demand |

## 📍 Geographic Considerations

### Ideal City Characteristics

- **Population**: 100K-500K (sweet spot)
- **Growth**: Growing suburbs > declining cities
- **Income**: Median household income $60K+
- **Climate**: Consider seasonal niches
- **Competition**: Check Google Page 1 before committing

### City Size vs. Niche Selection

| City Size | Best Niches | Expected Rental |

## 🔍 Niche Validation Checklist

Before committing to a niche/city combo:

- [ ] Google "[niche] [city]" - check competition level
- [ ] Use Google Keyword Planner - verify search volume
- [ ] Check Google Business Profile results - how many reviews do they have?
- [ ] Call 2-3 businesses - ask about pricing and availability
- [ ] Check if businesses are running Google Ads (indicates profitability)
- [ ] Verify there are at least 10-20 potential rental clients in the area
- [ ] Ensure niche isn't dominated by national chains
