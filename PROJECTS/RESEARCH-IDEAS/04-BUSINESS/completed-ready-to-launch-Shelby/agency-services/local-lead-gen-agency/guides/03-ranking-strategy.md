## 🎯 Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

### On-Page SEO Deep Dive

#### Keyword Research

**Tools:**
- Google Keyword Planner (free)
- Ahrefs/SEMrush (paid)
- Ubersuggest (freemium)
- AnswerThePublic (free)

**Keyword Types to Target:**

| Type | Example | Intent | Priority |
|------|---------|--------|----------|
| **Money Keywords** | "plumber denver", "roofing company phoenix" | High | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Service Keywords** | "drain cleaning", "roof repair" | High | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Emergency Keywords** | "emergency plumber", "24 hour roofing" | Very High | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Long-Tail** | "best plumber for water heater installation" | Medium | ⭐⭐ |
| **Near Me** | "plumber near me" | High | ⭐⭐ |

**Keyword Mapping:**

```
Homepage → [Main Service] + [City]
Service Page 1 → [Service 1] + [City]
Service Page 2 → [Service 2] + [City]
City Page 1 → [Main Service] + [City 2]
City Page 2 → [Main Service] + [City 3]
```

#### On-Page Optimization Checklist

**Title Tags:**
```
✅ Include primary keyword
✅ Include city name
✅ 55-60 characters
✅ Brand name at end (optional)

Example: "Emergency Plumber Denver | 24/7 Service | Denver Plumbing Pros"
```

**Meta Descriptions:**
```
✅ Include primary + secondary keyword
✅ Include call-to-action
✅ 150-160 characters
✅ Include phone number

Example: "Need an emergency plumber in Denver? We're available 24/7 for 
all plumbing repairs. Licensed & insured. Call (303) 555-0123 for fast service!"
```

**Header Structure:**
```
H1: Primary keyword + city (1 per page)
H2: Main sections (3-5 per page)
H3: Subsections under H2s
H4: Further breakdown (if needed)

Example:
H1: Emergency Plumbing Services in Denver
H2: Common Plumbing Problems We Solve
H3: Burst Pipes
H3: Clogged Drains
H2: Our Plumbing Process
H2: Service Areas in Denver Metro
```

**Content Optimization:**

| Element | Best Practice |
|---------|---------------|
| Keyword Density | 1-2% (natural usage) |
| Word Count | 1,000-2,000 (homepage), 800-1,500 (service pages) |
| Internal Links | 3-5 per page |
| External Links | 1-3 to authority sites |
| Images | 3-5 per page, optimized |
| Videos | 1 per page (if available) |
| Readability | Grade 8-10 level |

**URL Structure:**
```
✅ Clean and descriptive
✅ Include keyword
✅ Use hyphens, not underscores
✅ No special characters

Good: denverplumbingpros.com/emergency-plumbing-denver
Bad: denverplumbingpros.com/page-id=123
```

## 📍 Phase 2: Citations (Week 2-4)

### What Are Citations?

Citations = Online mentions of your business NAP (Name, Address, Phone)

**Why They Matter:**
- Google uses them to verify business legitimacy
- Consistent citations = higher rankings
- Drive referral traffic
- Build trust signals

### Citation Types

| Type | Examples | Priority |
|------|----------|----------|
| **Major Aggregators** | Foursquare, Acxiom, Localeze, Infogroup | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Data Axle** | DataAxle (formerly Infogroup) | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| **Industry-Specific** | HomeAdvisor, Angie's List, Thumbtack | ⭐⭐ |
| **Local Directories** | Chamber of Commerce, local business associations | ⭐⭐ |
| **General Directories** | Yelp, YellowPages, Bing Places | ⭐⭐⭐ |

### Citation Building Process

**Manual Method (Free but time-consuming):**

1. Create a spreadsheet with all citation sources
2. Create a professional email (e.g., listings@yourbrand.com)
3. Prepare your NAP info in a document
4. Submit to each site manually
5. Track submission dates and status
6. Follow up on pending listings

**Automated Tools (Paid but efficient):**

| Tool | Price | Citations | Best For |
|------|-------|-----------|----------|
| **BrightLocal** | $29-79/mo | 50-100+ | Agencies |
| **Yext** | $300+/yr | 100+ | Enterprise |
| **Moz Local** | $149/yr | 40+ | Small business |
| **Whitespark** | $20-80/mo | Custom | Manual + automated |
| **Synup** | $30-100/mo | 100+ | Multi-location |

**Our Recommendation**: BrightLocal for most operators. Best value and reporting.

### Citation Consistency Rules

```
✅ EXACT same business name everywhere
✅ EXACT same address (or service area)
✅ EXACT same phone number
✅ Same website URL
✅ Same business categories
✅ Consistent business hours
```

**Common Mistakes:**
- "Denver Plumbing" vs "Denver Plumbing Co." vs "Denver Plumbing Company"
- "123 Main St" vs "123 Main Street" vs "123 Main St."
- "(303) 555-0123" vs "303-555-0123" vs "3035550123"

**Pick ONE format and stick to it!**

## 🔗 Phase 3: Backlinks (Week 4-12+)

### Backlink Types & Value

| Type | Difficulty | Value | Priority |
|------|------------|-------|----------|
| **Editorial** (news mentions) | Hard | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High |
| **Guest Posts** | Medium | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High |
| **Resource Pages** | Medium | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High |
| **Local Partnerships** | Easy-Medium | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High |
| **Sponsorships** | Easy | ⭐⭐⭐ | Medium |
| **Directory Links** | Easy | ⭐⭐ | Low |
| **Profile Links** | Easy | ⭐ | Low |

### Link Building Strategies

#### 1. Local Partnerships (Easiest)

**Targets:**
- Local charities
- Little League teams
- School PTAs
- Chamber of Commerce
- Local business associations

**Approach:**
```
"Hi [Name],

I'm [Your Name] with [Company], a local [service] company serving [City]. 
We're looking to get more involved in the community and would love to 
explore partnership opportunities with [Organization].

We'd be happy to [sponsor an event/donate services/etc.] in exchange for 
a mention on your partners/sponsors page.

Would you be open to discussing this?

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

#### 2. Guest Posting

**Find Opportunities:**
```
Google Search Operators:

"[niche] + "write for us"
"[niche] + "guest post"
"[niche] + "submit article"
"[niche] + "contribute"
"intext:"write for us" + [city]
```

**Pitch Template:**
```
Subject: Guest Post Idea for [Site Name]

Hi [Editor Name],

I've been following [Site Name] for a while and love your content on 
[specific topic]. I noticed you haven't covered [topic idea] yet.

I'd like to contribute a guest post: "[Proposed Title]"

Outline:
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

This would provide value to your readers looking for [benefit].

I'm a [credibility statement] and have written for [other sites].

Interested?

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

#### 3. Resource Pages

**Find Resource Pages:**
```
"[niche] + "resources"
"[niche] + "helpful links"
"[niche] + "recommended"
"[city] + "resources"
inurl:resources + [niche]
```

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Resource for your [niche] page

Hi [Name],

I came across your [niche] resources page while researching [topic]. 
Great list!

I noticed you include [similar resource]. I wanted to share another 
helpful resource that might be valuable for your visitors:

[Your Site] - [Brief description of what makes it valuable]

It covers [specific topics] and includes [unique feature].

If you think it would be a good fit, I'd be honored to be included.

Either way, keep up the great work with [Site Name]!

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

#### 4. HARO (Help A Reporter Out)

**Process:**
1. Sign up at helpareporter.com (now Connectively)
2. Get daily emails with reporter queries
3. Respond quickly with valuable insights
4. Get mentioned (and linked) in articles

**Response Template:**
```
Query: [Query topic]

Expert: [Your Name]
Title: [Your Title]
Company: [Your Company]
Bio: [2-3 sentence bio]

Response:

[Direct answer to the query - 2-3 paragraphs]

Key points:
• [Point 1]
• [Point 2]
• [Point 3]

Additional insight: [Unique perspective or data point]

Happy to provide additional quotes or clarification.

[Your Name]
[Phone]
[Email]"
```

#### 5. Unlinked Mentions

**Find Unlinked Mentions:**
```
"[your brand name]" -yoursite.com
"[your site name]" -site:yoursite.com
```

**Outreach:**
```
Subject: Quick favor - link mention on [Site Name]

Hi [Name],

Thanks for mentioning [Your Brand] in your article about [topic]!

I noticed the mention isn't linked. Would you mind adding a quick link 
to our site? It would help readers find us easily.

Link: https://yoursite.com

Thanks for considering!

Best,
[Your Name]"
```

## 📝 Phase 4: Content Strategy (Ongoing)

### Content Types That Rank

#### 1. Service Pages (Priority 1)
- Main service pages
- Individual service sub-pages
- City-specific service pages

#### 2. Location Pages (Priority 2)
- "[Service] in [City]"
- "[Service] in [Neighborhood]"
- Service area pages

#### 3. Blog Content (Priority 3)

**High-Value Blog Topics:**

| Topic Type | Examples | Purpose |
|------------|----------|---------|
| **How-To** | "How to Fix a Leaky Faucet", "How to Prepare Your Roof for Winter" | Authority building |
| **Cost Guides** | "How Much Does [Service] Cost in [City]?", "[Service] Cost Breakdown 2026" | High-intent traffic |
| **Problem/Solution** | "Signs You Need a New [Service]", "What to Do When [Problem]" | Capture early-funnel |
| **Comparison** | "[Option A] vs [Option B]", "DIY vs Professional [Service]" | Decision-stage traffic |
| **Local Content** | "[City] Building Codes for [Service]", "Best [Service] Companies in [City]" | Local relevance |
| **Seasonal** | "Winter [Service] Tips", "Summer [Service] Preparation" | Timely relevance |

### Content Calendar Template

| Week | Blog Post | Service Page | Location Page |
|------|-----------|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | Cost guide | Main service | City 1 |
| 2 | How-to guide | Sub-service | City 2 |
| 3 | Problem/solution | - | City 3 |
| 4 | Comparison | - | - |

**Minimum**: 2 blog posts/month, 1 service page/month, 2 location pages/quarter

### Content Optimization Checklist

For every piece of content:

- [ ] Target keyword identified
- [ ] Keyword in title (H1)
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] Keyword in 2-3 H2s
- [ ] Keyword in URL slug
- [ ] Meta title optimized
- [ ] Meta description written
- [ ] Internal links added (3-5)
- [ ] External links added (1-3)
- [ ] Images optimized (alt text)
- [ ] Readability checked
- [ ] Word count appropriate (800-2,000)
- [ ] CTA included
- [ ] Schema markup added (if applicable)

## 📈 Phase 5: Tracking & Optimization

### Rank Tracking

**Tools:**
- BrightLocal ($29/mo)
- Whitespark ($20/mo)
- SERPWatcher (Mangools, $29/mo)
- AccuRanker ($109/mo)

**Keywords to Track:**
- 5-10 money keywords per site
- Mix of head terms and long-tail
- Include "near me" variations
- Track competitor rankings

**Tracking Frequency:**
- Weeks 1-4: Weekly
- Months 2-3: Bi-weekly
- Month 4+: Monthly

### Performance Metrics

| Metric | Target | Tool |
|--------|--------|------|
| **Organic Traffic** | Growing MoM | Google Analytics |
| **Keyword Rankings** | Top 3 for money keywords | Rank tracker |
| **GBP Impressions** | Growing MoM | GBP Dashboard |
| **GBP Actions** | Growing MoM | GBP Dashboard |
| **Click-Through Rate** | 3%+ | Search Console |
| **Bounce Rate** | <50% | Google Analytics |
| **Time on Page** | 2+ minutes | Google Analytics |
| **Conversion Rate** | 5-15% | Analytics/CRM |

### Monthly Optimization Routine

**Week 1: Review & Analyze**
- Check rank changes
- Review analytics
- Identify winners/losers
- Note competitor movements

**Week 2: Content Updates**
- Update underperforming pages
- Add fresh content
- Refresh old blog posts
- Add internal links

**Week 3: Link Building**
- Pursue new link opportunities
- Follow up on pending links
- Create linkable assets
- HARO responses

**Week 4: Technical Audit**
- Check site speed
- Fix broken links
- Update plugins
- Review schema markup

## 🚀 Advanced Tactics

### 1. Review Generation

**Why Reviews Matter:**
- Direct ranking factor for GBP
- Increases click-through rates
- Builds trust with customers
- Provides social proof

**How to Get Reviews:**

**For Lead Gen Sites:**
- Include review request in follow-up emails
- Add review links to thank-you pages
- Text customers after service
- Offer small incentives (where legal)

**Review Request Template:**
```
Subject: How did we do?

Hi [Name],

Thanks for choosing [Company Name]! We hope you're happy with our service.

Would you mind taking 30 seconds to leave us a review? It helps us 
continue serving [City] and helps others find quality [service].

[Review Link]

Thank you!
[Your Name]"
```

### 2. Local Link Networks

Build relationships with complementary local businesses:

**Ideal Partners:**
- Non-competing services
- Same target audience
- Similar business values
- Active online presence

**Example Partnerships:**
- Plumber ↔ HVAC ↔ Electrician
- Roofer ↔ Gutter Company ↔ Siding Company
- Realtor ↔ Mortgage Broker ↔ Home Inspector

**Partnership Ideas:**
- Cross-referrals
- Guest blog swaps
- Co-hosted events
- Bundle offers
- Shared content

### 3. Content Upgrades

Turn top-performing content into lead magnets:

**Examples:**
- "Ultimate [Service] Cost Guide" (PDF)
- "[Service] Checklist" (downloadable)
- "Before You Call a [Professional] Guide"
- Seasonal maintenance checklists

**Promotion:**
- Gate with email opt-in
- Promote on social media
- Include in email signature
- Share in relevant groups

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Keyword Stuffing** - Write naturally, not for bots
❌ **Buying Links** - Risk of Google penalty
❌ **Ignoring Mobile** - 60%+ of local searches are mobile
❌ **Inconsistent NAP** - Kills citation value
❌ **Set & Forget** - SEO requires ongoing work
❌ **Ignoring GBP** - It's 25% of local rankings
❌ **No Tracking** - You can't improve what you don't measure
❌ **Expecting Overnight Results** - SEO takes 3-6 months minimum
