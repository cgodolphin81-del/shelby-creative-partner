# Launch Playbook
## Pre-Launch, Launch Week & Post-Launch Sequences

---

## The Launch Mindset

**A launch is not a week. It's a campaign.**

Successful launches are won before cart opens. Your launch week is the harvest, not the planting.

**Timeline Overview:**
```
Week -4 to -1: Pre-Launch (building anticipation)
Week 0: Launch Week (cart open, sales push)
Week +1 to +4: Post-Launch (onboarding, momentum)
```

---

## Part 1: Pre-Launch (21 Days Before Cart Opens)

### The Goal
Build anticipation, warm up your audience, and create a "must-have" feeling before anyone can buy.

### The Strategy
**Phase 1: Awareness (Days 21-15)**
- Tease something big is coming
- Share behind-the-scenes
- Build curiosity

**Phase 2: Education (Days 14-8)**
- Teach valuable content related to course
- Show the problem your course solves
- Position yourself as the guide

**Phase 3: Anticipation (Days 7-1)**
- Reveal what's coming
- Share transformation stories
- Open waitlist/VIP early access

### Pre-Launch Social Media Strategy

**Platforms to Focus On:**
- Pick 1-2 where your audience lives
- Don't spread yourself thin

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Value/education
- 30% Behind-the-scenes
- 20% Social proof
- 10% Direct promotion

**Posting Frequency:**
- Instagram: 1 feed post/day, 3-5 stories/day
- Twitter/X: 3-5 tweets/day
- LinkedIn: 1 post/day
- TikTok/Reels: 3-5/week
- YouTube: 1-2 videos/week

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Reply to every comment
- Ask questions in posts
- Use polls and interactive stickers
- Go live 1-2x during pre-launch

---

### Pre-Launch Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Email open rate | 25%+ | Audience is engaged |
| Email CTR | 3%+ | Content is compelling |
| Waitlist signups | 100+ per 1K list | Strong interest |
| Social engagement | 3%+ | Content resonates |
| Reply rate | 1%+ | Deep connection |

**If metrics are low:**
- Adjust messaging
- Increase value content
- Survey your list about interests

## Part 2: Launch Week (Cart Open)

### The Goal
Convert interest into sales. Create urgency without being sleazy.

### The Strategy
**Day 1: Cart Open (Big Energy)**
- Announce cart is open
- Share your excitement
- Clear call-to-action

**Day 2: Social Proof**
- Share testimonials
- Show who's joining
- Build FOMO

**Day 3: Logic/Reason Why**
- Deep dive into curriculum
- Explain your methodology
- Address "is this right for me?"

**Day 4: Objection Handling**
- FAQ email
- Address common concerns
- Remove barriers

**Day 5: Bonus Deadline**
- Remind about bonus deadline
- Create urgency
- "Last chance for X"

**Day 6: Weekend Check-in**
- Lighter touch
- Personal note
- Answer questions

**Day 7: Cart Closing (Final Push)**
- Morning: "Last day" email
- Afternoon: "Hours left" email
- Evening: "Final hours" email

| Metric | Good | Great |

## Part 3: Post-Launch (Weeks 1-4 After Close)

### The Goal
Deliver an amazing experience, set students up for success, and plant seeds for the next launch.

### The Strategy
**Week 1: Onboarding & Quick Wins**
- Welcome sequence
- Get them started immediately
- Celebrate early wins

**Week 2: Momentum Building**
- Check-in email
- Highlight active students
- Address common questions

**Week 3: Deep Engagement**
- Share student wins
- Host live Q&A
- Encourage community participation

**Week 4: Transformation & Testimonials**
- Collect feedback
- Gather testimonials
- Tease what's next

---

### Post-Launch Engagement Tactics

**1. Welcome Call (Week 1)**
- 45-60 minute Zoom call
- Welcome everyone
- Walk through the platform
- Q&A
- Record and add to course

**2. Weekly Office Hours**
- Standing time for questions
- Drop-in format
- Build rapport with students

**3. Community Challenges**
- Weekly implementation challenges
- Prizes for participation
- Creates momentum

**4. Student Spotlights**
- Feature one student per week
- Share their story/progress
- Motivates others

**5. Progress Tracking**
- Celebrate module completions
- Public recognition (with permission)
- Milestone certificates

---

### Collecting Testimonials

**When to Ask:**
- After they complete a module
- After they get a result
- Mid-course (week 3-4)
- End of course

**What to Ask:**
1. What was your situation before the course?
2. What results have you achieved?
3. What was your favorite part?
4. Would you recommend this? Why?
5. What would you tell someone considering this course?

**Format Options:**
- Written testimonial (email/form)
- Video testimonial (Loom, Zoom)
- Audio testimonial (voice message)
- Before/after screenshots

**Incentives:**
- Entry into prize drawing
- Bonus content
- Feature on your social media
- Discount on next course

---

### Setting Up the Next Launch

**Immediately After Launch:**
1. Document what worked
2. Document what didn't work
3. Collect all testimonials
4. Gather case studies
5. Note common questions/objections

**2 Weeks After:**
1. Survey students about experience
2. Identify areas for improvement
3. Update course content as needed
4. Plan next launch date

**4 Weeks After:**
1. Start pre-launch for next cohort
2. Reach out to waitlist from this launch
3. Create new bonuses/offers
4. Update sales page with new testimonials

## Final Launch Tips

### ✅ DO:
- Start building anticipation early
- Provide value throughout pre-launch
- Be authentic and personal
- Create genuine urgency (real deadlines)
- Follow up with non-buyers (kindly)
- Deliver an amazing experience post-launch
- Collect testimonials immediately
- Document everything for next launch

### ❌ DON'T:
- Wait until launch week to engage
- Be salesy without providing value
- Fake urgency (fake countdowns, lying about deadlines)
- Ghost after the sale
- Overpromise and underdeliver
- Ignore questions or concerns
- Skip the post-mortem
- Launch without testing everything
