## How to Use These Templates

### 1. Customize for Your Agency
- Add your branding
- Adjust for your services
- Reflect your pricing
- Match your voice

### 2. Keep Them Updated
- Review quarterly
- Incorporate learnings
- Update based on what works
- Version control

### 3. Train Your Team
- Template walkthroughs
- Examples of good usage
- Quality standards
- Regular refreshers

### 4. Track Performance
- Which templates convert best?
- What gets results?
- A/B test variations
- Iterate based on data

---

## Template Best Practices

1. **Personalization is key** - Never send generic templates
2. **Test and iterate** - Track what works
3. **Keep them current** - Update regularly
4. **Quality over quantity** - Better to send fewer, better templates
5. **Brand consistency** - All templates should feel like you
6. **Mobile-friendly** - Many will read on phones
7. **Clear CTAs** - Every template should have a purpose
8. **Value-first** - Give before asking
