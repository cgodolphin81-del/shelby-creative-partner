# SEO Agency Quick Start Guide

Your roadmap to launching and scaling a profitable SEO agency.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)

### Day 1-3: Positioning

**Choose Your Niche:**
- [ ] Industry vertical (e.g., e-commerce, SaaS, legal, healthcare)
- [ ] Business size (e.g., $1M-$10M revenue)
- [ ] Service focus (e.g., technical SEO, content, local)

**Why Niche Down:**
- Easier to stand out
- Higher prices possible
- More efficient delivery
- Better case studies
- Clearer messaging

**Example Niches:**
- E-commerce SEO for DTC brands ($5M-$50M revenue)
- Local SEO for multi-location healthcare
- Technical SEO for SaaS companies
- Content SEO for B2B tech

---

### Day 4-7: Service Packages

**Define Your Offerings:**

**Starter Package ($3K-$5K/mo):**
- Technical SEO monitoring
- 4 articles/month
- 5 links/month
- Local SEO
- Monthly reporting

**Growth Package ($7.5K-$15K/mo):**
- Full technical SEO
- 8 articles/month
- 10 links/month
- Content strategy
- Bi-weekly calls

**Professional Package ($15K-$30K/mo):**
- Dedicated specialist
- 12-16 articles/month
- 15-20 links/month
- Weekly calls
- Custom reporting

**Document Everything:**
- [ ] Service descriptions
- [ ] Deliverables list
- [ ] Pricing
- [ ] Terms & conditions

---

### Day 8-10: Tools & Systems

**Essential Tools:**
- [ ] Ahrefs or SEMrush ($99-$119/mo)
- [ ] Screaming Frog (£149/year)
- [ ] Google Workspace ($6/user/mo)
- [ ] Slack (free to start)
- [ ] Project management (Asana/ClickUp, $10/user/mo)
- [ ] Reporting tool (Looker Studio - free, or AgencyAnalytics $39/mo)

**Total Monthly Cost:** ~$300-$500

**Set Up:**
- [ ] Agency website (simple is fine)
- [ ] Case study page (even if hypothetical initially)
- [ ] Service page
- [ ] Contact form
- [ ] Calendly for booking calls

---

### Day 11-14: Sales Materials

**Create:**

- [ ] LinkedIn outreach templates
- [ ] Case study template

**Key Messages:**

## Phase 2: First Clients (Week 3-8)

### Week 3-4: Outreach Launch

**Daily Activities:**
- Send 20-50 cold emails/day
- Send 10-20 LinkedIn connection requests/day
- Follow up with previous prospects
- Post on LinkedIn 3x/week

**Weekly Goals:**
- 100-250 emails sent
- 50-100 LinkedIn connections
- 5-10 calls booked
- 1-2 proposals sent

**Targets:**
- Response rate: 5-15%
- Call booking rate: 2-5%
- Close rate: 20-40% of proposals

---

### Week 5-6: Sales Calls & Proposals

**Call Structure (30-45 min):**
1. Discovery (15 min) - Learn about their business
2. Insights (10 min) - Share what you're seeing
3. Solution (10 min) - How you can help
4. Next steps (5 min) - Proposal or audit

**Proposal Follow-Up:**
- Send within 24 hours of call
- Follow up Day 2, Day 5, Day 10
- Address objections directly
- Offer to hop on another call

**Goal:** Close 1-2 clients in this period

---

### Week 7-8: Onboarding & Delivery

**Onboard First Clients:**
- [ ] Welcome email
- [ ] Contract signed
- [ ] Payment collected
- [ ] Access obtained
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled
- [ ] Audit started

**Deliver Quick Wins:**
- Fix critical technical issues
- Optimize top pages
- Show progress in first 30 days
- Communicate constantly

**Goal:** Happy clients who will renew and refer

## Phase 3: Systematize (Month 3-4)

### Document Processes

**Create SOPs For:**
- [ ] SEO audits
- [ ] Keyword research
- [ ] Content briefs
- [ ] Link building outreach
- [ ] Monthly reporting
- [ ] Client communication
- [ ] Onboarding
- [ ] Offboarding

**Why:** Enables hiring and scaling

---

### Build Case Studies

**Document Everything:**
- Before/after metrics
- Specific tactics used
- Timeline
- Client testimonial

**Format:**
- Challenge
- Solution
- Results (with data)
- Quote from client

**Goal:** 3-5 strong case studies by Month 4

---

### Refine Offerings

**Based on Learnings:**
- What services are most profitable?
- What clients are best to work with?
- What can be productized?
- What should be priced higher?

**Adjust:**
- Service packages
- Pricing
- Target client profile
- Messaging

---

## Phase 4: Scale (Month 5-12)

### Hire First Team Member

**First Hire Options:**
- SEO Specialist (if you're sales-focused)
- Sales/BD (if you're delivery-focused)
- Content Writer (if content is bottleneck)
- Link Builder (if links are bottleneck)

**When to Hire:**
- 80%+ capacity for 4+ weeks
- Turning away work
- Quality suffering
- Revenue supports it ($10K+/mo)

---

### Increase Prices

**For New Clients:**
- Raise prices 20-50%
- Based on results and demand
- Test market tolerance

**For Existing Clients:**
- Annual increases (10-20%)
- When expanding scope
- When delivering exceptional results

---

### Expand Lead Generation

**Add Channels:**
- [ ] Partnerships (agencies, consultants)
- [ ] Speaking engagements
- [ ] Content marketing
- [ ] Paid ads (if unit economics work)
- [ ] Referral program

**Goal:** Diversify beyond cold outreach

---

### Build Management Layer

**When You Hit $50K-$100K/mo:**
- Hire account managers
- Hire team leads
- You focus on strategy + sales
- Implement regular reporting

## Revenue Milestones

### $10K/month (Months 1-3)
- 2-4 clients @ $3K-$5K
- You + maybe 1 contractor
- Focus: Prove model, get results

### $30K/month (Months 4-6)
- 5-8 clients @ $4K-$6K avg
- You + 2-3 team members
- Focus: Systematize, build case studies

### $100K/month (Months 7-12)
- 15-20 clients @ $5K-$7K avg
- 10-15 person team
- Focus: Scale, optimize margins

### $300K+/month (Year 2+)
- 30-50+ clients
- 30+ person team
- Focus: Market leadership, acquisitions

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Taking Any Client
**Instead:** Qualify hard, fire bad clients

### ❌ Underpricing
**Instead:** Price for value, raise regularly

### ❌ Overpromising
**Instead:** Under-promise, over-deliver

### ❌ No Systems
**Instead:** Document everything from Day 1

### ❌ Ignoring Cash Flow
**Instead:** Require upfront payment, monitor closely

### ❌ Trying to Do Everything
**Instead:** Focus on 1-2 services, master them

### ❌ No Niche
**Instead:** Specialize, become the expert

### ❌ Stopping Outreach
**Instead:** Always be prospecting, even when full

---

## Success Principles

1. **Results matter most** - Deliver or don't survive
2. **Communication is key** - Over-communicate with clients
3. **Systems enable scale** - Document everything
4. **Niche = profit** - Specialists charge more
5. **Sales is a skill** - Get better or hire someone who is
6. **Team is everything** - Hire well, treat well
7. **Cash is king** - Manage it carefully
8. **Never stop learning** - SEO changes constantly
