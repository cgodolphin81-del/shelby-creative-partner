# Keyword Research Framework

Systematic approach to finding, analyzing, and prioritizing keywords that drive revenue.

---

## Framework Overview

```
Discovery → Analysis → Intent Mapping → Prioritization → Tracking
```

---

## Phase 1: Keyword Discovery

### Seed Keywords

**Sources:**
- [ ] Client brainstorm (10-20 terms)
- [ ] Website navigation
- [ ] Product/service pages
- [ ] Customer interviews
- [ ] Sales team input
- [ ] Support tickets/FAQs
- [ ] Industry terminology

**Example (E-commerce Fitness):**
- Home gym equipment
- Treadmills
- Dumbbells
- Workout benches
- Fitness accessories

---

### Competitor Keywords

**Process:**
1. Identify 3-5 main competitors
2. Use Ahrefs/SEMrush to extract their top keywords
3. Filter by:
   - KD < 50 (initially)
   - Volume > 100
   - Relevant to client

**Tools:**
- Ahrefs: Site Explorer → Organic Keywords
- SEMrush: Domain Overview → Organic Research
- SpyFu: Competitor Keywords

---

### SERP Analysis

**For Each Seed Keyword:**
1. Google the term (incognito)
2. Analyze top 10 results:
   - Content type (blog, product, category)
   - Search intent (informational, commercial, transactional)
   - SERP features (featured snippet, PAA, videos)
3. Note content gaps

**Tools:**
- Manual Google searches
- AlsoAsked.com (PAA questions)
- AnswerThePublic (question keywords)

---

### Keyword Expansion

**Methods:**

#### 1. Modifier Expansion
Add modifiers to seed keywords:

| Modifier Type | Examples |
|--------------|----------|
| Best | best treadmills, best dumbbells |
| Cheap | cheap home gym, affordable equipment |
| Reviews | treadmill reviews, bench reviews |
| Vs | treadmill vs elliptical |
| For | treadmills for small spaces |
| How | how to choose dumbbells |
| Buy | buy treadmill online |
| Near me | gym equipment near me |

#### 2. Question Keywords
- Who, What, When, Where, Why, How
- "Can you...", "Is it worth...", "Does..."

**Tools:**
- AnswerThePublic
- AlsoAsked
- Keyword Tool.io

#### 3. Related Searches
- Google "related searches" at bottom of SERP
- Ahrefs "Keyword Ideas"
- SEMrush "Keyword Magic Tool"

---

### Keyword Difficulty Scoring

**Ahrefs KD Scale:**
- 0-10: Easy (new sites can rank)
- 11-30: Moderate (some authority needed)
- 31-50: Hard (established sites)
- 51-70: Very Hard (strong domains)
- 71-100: Extremely Hard (top publishers)

**Adjust for:**
- Domain authority of ranking pages
- Content quality of top results
- Your client's current authority
- Budget for link building

---

### Search Intent Classification

**4 Intent Types:**

#### 1. Informational (I)
User wants to learn something.
- "how to lose weight"
- "what is keto diet"
- "benefits of strength training"

**Content Types:** Blog posts, guides, tutorials, videos

---

#### 2. Commercial Investigation (C)
User comparing options before purchase.
- "best treadmills 2024"
- "peloton vs nordictrack"
- "home gym equipment reviews"

**Content Types:** Reviews, comparisons, best-of lists, buyer's guides

---

#### 3. Transactional (T)
User ready to buy.
- "buy treadmill"
- "dumbbells for sale"
- "gym equipment discount"

**Content Types:** Product pages, category pages, pricing pages

---

#### 4. Navigational (N)
User looking for specific site/page.
- "nike running shoes"
- "planet fitness hours"
- "my fitness pal login"

**Content Types:** Brand pages, login pages, location pages

## Phase 3: Opportunity Identification

### Opportunity Score Formula

```
Opportunity Score = (Volume × Intent Value) / (KD + 1)

Intent Value Multipliers:
- Transactional: 3x
- Commercial: 2x
- Informational: 1x
- Navigational: 0.5x
```

**Example:**
- "best treadmills": (12,000 × 2) / (45 + 1) = 521
- "treadmill benefits": (2,000 × 1) / (18 + 1) = 105

**Prioritize high scores with achievable KD.**

---

### Quick Win Identification

**Criteria:**
- KD < 30
- Volume > 500
- Intent = Commercial or Transactional
- Current ranking: Page 2 (positions 11-20)

**Action:** Optimize existing content or create new content

---

### Content Gap Opportunities

**Process:**
1. Export competitor keywords (top 1000)
2. Export client keywords (top 1000)
3. Identify keywords competitors rank for, client doesn't
4. Filter by:
   - KD < 40
   - Volume > 200
   - Relevant intent

**Output:** Prioritized content creation list

---

### Long-Tail Opportunities

**Characteristics:**
- 4+ words
- Lower volume (50-500)
- Lower KD (0-20)
- Higher conversion rate
- Easier to rank

**Strategy:**
- Target 50-100 long-tail keywords
- Create comprehensive content
- Build topical authority
- Scale to medium-tail over time

## Phase 4: Keyword Prioritization

### Priority Matrix

| Priority | Criteria | Action |
|----------|----------|--------|
| P0 (Critical) | High volume, low KD, transactional | Create/optimize immediately |
| P1 (High) | Medium volume, low-medium KD, commercial | Next 30 days |
| P2 (Medium) | Low volume, low KD, any intent | Next 60-90 days |
| P3 (Low) | Any volume, high KD | Future/backlog |

---

### 90-Day Keyword Roadmap

**Month 1: Foundation**
- 10-15 P0 keywords
- Focus on quick wins
- Optimize existing pages

**Month 2: Expansion**
- 15-20 P1 keywords
- Create new content
- Begin link building

**Month 3: Scale**
- 20-25 P2 keywords
- Cluster content
- Authority building

## Phase 5: Topic Clusters

### Cluster Structure

```
Pillar Page (broad topic)
├── Cluster Content 1 (subtopic)
├── Cluster Content 2 (subtopic)
├── Cluster Content 3 (subtopic)
└── Cluster Content 4 (subtopic)
```

**Example: "Home Gym" Cluster**

**Pillar:** Ultimate Guide to Building a Home Gym
- Target: "home gym" (18,000 vol, KD 52)

**Clusters:**
- Best Home Gym Equipment (commercial)
- Home Gym Ideas for Small Spaces (informational)
- How to Build a Home Gym on a Budget (informational)
- Home Gym Floor Options (commercial)
- Home Gym Essentials Checklist (informational)

**Internal Linking:**
- All clusters link to pillar
- Pillar links to all clusters
- Clusters interlink where relevant

## Advanced Techniques

### 1. SERP Feature Targeting
- Featured Snippets: Answer questions directly, use lists/tables
- People Also Ask: Include related questions in content
- Image Pack: Optimize images with descriptive alt text
- Video: Create video content for video carousels

### 2. Seasonal Keywords
- Identify seasonal trends (Google Trends)
- Plan content 2-3 months ahead
- Update seasonal content annually

### 3. Local Keywords
- Add location modifiers
- Target "near me" queries
- Optimize for Google Business Profile

### 4. Voice Search
- Focus on question keywords
- Natural language (longer phrases)
- Featured snippet optimization

## Best Practices

1. **Start with intent** - Volume means nothing without the right intent
2. **Think clusters, not keywords** - Build topical authority
3. **Prioritize achievable wins** - Don't target KD 80+ as a new site
4. **Update regularly** - Keyword research is ongoing
5. **Track conversions** - Rankings don't pay bills, conversions do
6. **Avoid cannibalization** - One primary keyword per page
7. **Long-tail first** - Easier wins, build momentum
