## 1. Customer Support

### Overview
Handle 60-80% of support calls automatically, 24/7.

### Common Use Cases

#### FAQ & Information
- Business hours and locations
- Account balance inquiries
- Order status checks
- Policy information
- Product information

**Metrics:**
- Automation rate: 70-85%
- CSAT: 4.2/5
- Cost per call: $0.50 vs $5.00 (human)

#### Technical Support (Tier 1)
- Password resets
- Basic troubleshooting
- Account unlocks
- Service activation
- Connection issues

**Metrics:**
- Automation rate: 50-70%
- First-call resolution: 65%
- Escalation rate: 30-40%

#### Returns & Exchanges
- Return eligibility check
- Return initiation
- Exchange processing
- Refund status
- Return label generation

**Metrics:**
- Automation rate: 60-75%
- Processing time: 2 min vs 8 min
- Customer effort score: 1.8/5

### Sample Conversation Flow

```
Customer: "I need to return something I ordered"

Bot: "I can help with that. Can I get your order number?"

Customer: "It's ORD-12345"

Bot: "Thanks. I see your order for [product] delivered on [date]. 
      What would you like to return?"

Customer: "The blue widget"

Bot: "Got it. This item is eligible for return within 30 days. 
      Would you like a refund or exchange?"

Customer: "Refund please"

Bot: "Perfect. I'll email you a return label. 
      Refund will process within 5-7 business days after we receive it. 
      Anything else I can help with?"
```

| Cost per call | $5.00 | $2.00 | -60% |
| Monthly cost | $50,000 | $20,000 | -$30,000 |
| Annual savings | - | - | **$360,000** |

## Use Case Selection Framework

### Prioritization Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | Questions |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| Volume | 25% | How many calls/month? |
| Complexity | 20% | How complex are conversations? |
| ROI | 25% | What's the cost savings/revenue impact? |
| Feasibility | 20% | How easy to implement? |
| Strategic Value | 10% | Does this align with business goals? |

### Scoring Matrix

| Use Case | Volume | Complexity | ROI | Feasibility | Strategic | Total |
|----------|--------|------------|-----|-------------|-----------|-------|
| FAQ Support | 5 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 3 | 22 |
| Appointment Scheduling | 4 | 3 | 5 | 5 | 4 | 21 |
| Lead Qualification | 3 | 3 | 5 | 4 | 5 | 20 |
| Collections | 4 | 2 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 17 |

**Start with highest scores first.**
