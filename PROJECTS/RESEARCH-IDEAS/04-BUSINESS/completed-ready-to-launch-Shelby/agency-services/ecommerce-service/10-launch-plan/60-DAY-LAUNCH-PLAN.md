# 60-Day Launch Plan

## Complete Roadmap from Zero to Launch

---

## Overview

This 60-day plan takes you from concept to a fully operational, revenue-generating e-commerce store. Follow this day-by-day roadmap to launch efficiently and effectively.

### Phase Breakdown

```
PHASE 1: Foundation (Days 1-14)
├── Product research & validation
├── Business setup
└── Supplier sourcing

PHASE 2: Build (Days 15-35)
├── Store creation
├── Product pages
└── Marketing infrastructure

PHASE 3: Pre-Launch (Days 36-45)
├── Testing
├── Content creation
└── Soft launch prep

PHASE 4: Launch (Days 46-60)
├── Campaign launch
├── Optimization
└── Scale planning
```

---

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-14)

### Week 1: Product Research & Validation

#### Day 1-2: Market Research

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Identify 3-5 potential product niches
- [ ] Research each niche on Google Trends
- [ ] Analyze competitor stores (minimum 5 per niche)
- [ ] Check Facebook Ad Library for active ads
- [ ] Review TikTok for trending products
- [ ] Document findings in research spreadsheet

**Deliverable:** Niche comparison document with top 3 opportunities

**Time:** 6-8 hours

#### Day 3-4: Product Selection

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Apply product criteria checklist to each option
- [ ] Score each product (1-10) on:
  - Profit margin potential
  - Market demand
  - Competition level
  - Wow factor
  - Shipping feasibility
- [ ] Select winning product (score 22+/30)
- [ ] Identify 3-5 backup products

**Deliverable:** Selected product with validation data

**Time:** 4-6 hours

#### Day 5-7: Supplier Sourcing

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Search AliExpress/Alibaba for suppliers
- [ ] Contact 5-10 suppliers with inquiry template
- [ ] Compare pricing, MOQ, shipping times
- [ ] Order samples from top 2-3 suppliers
- [ ] Begin business registration process

**Deliverable:** Supplier shortlist + samples ordered

**Time:** 4-6 hours

## Common Roadblocks & Solutions

### Roadblock: Products not validating
**Solution:** 
- Revisit product research
- Test different angles/creatives
- Consider pivot to backup product
- Get customer feedback

### Roadblock: High CPC, low CTR
**Solution:**
- Refresh creative (new hooks)
- Test different audiences
- Improve ad relevance
- Check frequency (audience fatigue)

### Roadblock: Traffic but no sales
**Solution:**
- Audit product pages
- Check pricing vs. competitors
- Add more social proof
- Improve offer
- Check checkout friction

### Roadblock: Low email signup rate
**Solution:**
- Improve popup design
- Better incentive (15% vs 10%)
- Add exit-intent
- Multiple signup locations

### Roadblock: Ad account restrictions
**Solution:**
- Review Facebook policies
- Appeal if appropriate
- Create new ad account if needed
- Diversify to Google/TikTok
