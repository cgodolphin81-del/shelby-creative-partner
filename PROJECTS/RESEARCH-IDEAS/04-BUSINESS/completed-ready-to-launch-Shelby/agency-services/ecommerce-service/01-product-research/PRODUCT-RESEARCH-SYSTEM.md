# Product Research System

## Overview
A systematic approach to finding winning products, validating demand, and sourcing reliable suppliers for e-commerce success.

---

## Phase 1: Finding Winning Products

### Product Criteria Checklist

**✅ Must-Have Criteria:**
- [ ] Price point: $25-$150 (sweet spot for impulse + profit)
- [ ] Problem-solving or emotionally compelling
- [ ] Not easily found in local stores
- [ ] Lightweight & durable (low shipping costs, low damage rate)
- [ ] 3x minimum markup potential
- [ ] Year-round demand (not purely seasonal)
- [ ] No major brand dominance

**🎯 Ideal Characteristics:**
- Wow factor (makes people stop scrolling)
- Demonstrable (looks great in video)
- Passionate niche audience
- Upgrade/innovation of existing product
- Multiple purchase potential or consumable

### Research Methods

#### 1. Social Media Mining

**TikTok:**
- Search: #tiktokmademebuyit, #amazonfinds, #musthave
- Filter: Last 30 days, Most Liked
- Look for: 100K+ views, recent uploads, engaged comments
- Tools: TikTok Creative Center (trending products)

**Facebook/Instagram:**
- Facebook Ad Library (ads.facebook.com)
- Search competitor brands in your niche
- Note: Ad creative running 30+ days = winning product
- Instagram Explore page in target niches

**Pinterest:**
- Pinterest Trends (trends.pinterest.com)
- Search niche keywords, note rising pins
- Check "Related searches" for product ideas

#### 2. Marketplace Analysis

**Amazon:**
- Best Sellers page (amazon.com/bestsellers)
- Movers & Shakers (biggest gainers in 24h)
- New Releases in target categories
- Check reviews: 500+ reviews, 4.0+ stars, recent activity

**Etsy:**
- Bestselling items in categories
- Note: Handmade angle can inspire product variations
- Check "Best sellers" badges

**AliExpress:**
- Orders column (1000+ orders = validated)
- Check store rating (95%+ positive)
- Note shipping times and methods

#### 3. Competitor Intelligence

**Tools:**
- MyIP.MS (find Shopify stores by IP)
- BuiltWith (technology detection)
- SimilarWeb (traffic estimates)
- Facebook Ad Library (see all active ads)

**What to Analyze:**
- Product selection and pricing
- Ad creative and messaging
- Customer reviews (find pain points to solve)
- Store design and UX
- Email signup incentives

### Product Validation Framework

#### Demand Validation (Score 1-10)

| Metric | Target | Score |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Google Trends (12mo) | Stable or rising | /2 |
| Monthly searches (Keyword Tool) | 10K+ | /2 |
| Amazon reviews on similar products | 500+ total | /2 |
| Active Facebook ads | 10+ advertisers | /2 |
| Social media mentions | Growing | /2 |

**Minimum score: 7/10 to proceed**

#### Competition Analysis

**Green Flags:**
- Multiple competitors (proves market exists)
- Poor quality ads/websites (opportunity to outshine)
- Gaps in product offerings
- Weak customer service (reviews complain)

**Red Flags:**
- One dominant brand (>50% market share)
- Saturated market (50+ direct competitors)
- Price wars (margins destroyed)
- Major retailers dominate (Walmart, Target, Amazon)

## Phase 2: Supplier Sourcing

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Best For | MOQ | Shipping | Quality Control |
|----------|----------|-----|----------|-----------------|
| AliExpress | Testing, low MOQ | 1 unit | 10-30 days | Variable |
| Alibaba | Bulk orders, custom | 50-500 | 15-45 days | Negotiable |
| CJ Dropshipping | Automation, faster | 1 unit | 7-15 days | Good |
| Spocket | US/EU suppliers | 1 unit | 2-7 days | High |
| SaleHoo | Vetted suppliers | Varies | Varies | Verified |

### Supplier Vetting Checklist

**Initial Screening:**
- [ ] Store age: 2+ years preferred
- [ ] Positive feedback: 95%+
- [ ] Response time: <24 hours
- [ ] Product reviews: Read recent ones
- [ ] Shipping methods: Trackable options available

**Communication Test:**
Send this message template:
```
Hi, I'm interested in [Product Name] for my store.

Questions:
1. What's the processing time for orders?
2. Do you offer [customization option]?
3. Can you ship to [countries]?
4. What's your return/defect policy?
5. Do you provide product images/videos?

Looking forward to working together!
```

**Red Flags:**
- Slow or no response (48+ hours)
- Vague answers
- Unwilling to provide samples
- Poor English causing miscommunication
- No photos of actual product

### Sample Order Process

1. **Order samples** from 2-3 top suppliers
2. **Test thoroughly:**
   - Build quality
   - Functionality
   - Packaging
   - Shipping time
   - Instructions/manuals

3. **Compare and select:**
   - Best quality/price ratio
   - Most reliable communication
   - Fastest consistent shipping

### Negotiation Tips

**For Better Pricing:**
- Mention you're scaling (even if not yet)
- Ask for tiered pricing (100, 500, 1000 units)
- Offer to pay via Alibaba Trade Assurance
- Commit to exclusivity in your region
- Bundle multiple products from same supplier

**For Better Terms:**
- Request faster processing (24-48h vs 5-7 days)
- Ask for custom packaging options
- Negotiate defect replacement policy
- Request product photography rights

**Sample Negotiation Message:**
```
Thanks for the quote. I'm planning to scale this product 
and would like to discuss partnership terms.

If I can get:
- Price: $X.XX at 100+ units
- Processing: 48 hours
- Custom packaging option

I'm ready to place an order this week and scale to 
500+ units monthly within 60 days.

Can we make this work?
```

## Phase 3: Product Validation Before Launch

### Pre-Launch Validation Checklist

**Market Validation:**
- [ ] Google Trends shows stable/interest
- [ ] 3+ competitors actively advertising
- [ ] Social proof exists (reviews, mentions)
- [ ] Clear target audience identified
- [ ] Problem/pain point is real and urgent

**Financial Validation:**
- [ ] Landed cost calculated (product + shipping + duties)
- [ ] Minimum 3x markup achievable
- [ ] Break-even ROAS calculated
- [ ] Cash flow for inventory planned
- [ ] Profit margin after ads: 20%+

**Operational Validation:**
- [ ] Supplier reliability confirmed
- [ ] Sample quality approved
- [ ] Shipping times acceptable
- [ ] Return/refund process defined
- [ ] Customer service FAQs prepared

### Test Launch Strategy

**Week 1-2: Soft Launch**
- Budget: $20-50/day ads
- Goal: Validate CTR and add-to-cart rate
- Metrics to watch:
  - CTR: 1%+ (Facebook)
  - CPC: <$2.00
  - Add to cart: 5%+ of visitors
  - Initiate checkout: 3%+ of visitors

**Week 3-4: Scale Decision**
- If metrics meet targets → Scale budget 2x weekly
- If metrics below targets → Optimize or kill product
- Kill criteria: No sales after $300 ad spend
