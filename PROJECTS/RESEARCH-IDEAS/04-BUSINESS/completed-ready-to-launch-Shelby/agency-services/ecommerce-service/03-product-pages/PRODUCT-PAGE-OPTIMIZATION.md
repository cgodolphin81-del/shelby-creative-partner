## 1. Product Images & Video

### Image Requirements

**Minimum Standards:**
- Resolution: 2048 x 2048 pixels (allows zoom)
- Format: JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
- File size: <200KB each (use compression)
- Background: White or lifestyle context
- Consistency: Same style across all products

**Must-Have Shots (5-10 images):**
1. **Hero shot** - Clean product on white
2. **Lifestyle shot** - Product in use
3. **Scale shot** - Product next to common object
4. **Detail shots** - Close-ups of key features
5. **Packaging shot** - What arrives in mail
6. **Variation shots** - All colors/options
7. **Before/after** - If applicable
8. **Infographic** - Features labeled
9. **Video** - 15-30 seconds showing product

### Image Best Practices

**DO:**
- Show product from multiple angles
- Include lifestyle context
- Use zoom functionality
- Optimize for mobile (vertical images work well)
- Add alt text for SEO

**DON'T:**
- Use supplier stock photos only
- Show watermarks
- Have inconsistent backgrounds
- Make images too large (slow loading)
- Hide product limitations

### Video Guidelines

**Product Video Formula (15-30 seconds):**
```
0-3s:   Hook - Show problem or wow moment
3-10s:  Demo - Product solving problem
10-20s: Features - Key benefits shown
20-30s: CTA - Clear call to action
```

**Video Types That Convert:**
- Unboxing experience
- Product demonstration
- Customer testimonials
- Behind-the-scenes
- How-to/tutorial
- Before/after transformation

**Tools:**
- Canva (simple edits)
- CapCut (mobile editing)
- InVideo (templates)
- Loom (screen recording)

## 2. Product Titles

### Title Formula

```
[Brand] + [Product Name] + [Key Feature] + [Size/Color/Variant]

Example: "LumiGlow LED Desk Lamp - Dimmable, USB Rechargeable, Black"
```

### Title Best Practices

**DO:**
- Put important keywords first
- Include key differentiator
- Keep under 70 characters (SEO)
- Be specific about what it is
- Include size/color if relevant

**DON'T:**
- Use all caps
- Stuff with keywords
- Be vague ("Amazing Product")
- Use special characters (|, ★, etc.)
- Exceed 100 characters

### Title Examples

❌ **Bad:** "Amazing Wireless Earbuds - Best Quality!"
✅ **Good:** "SoundPro Wireless Earbuds - Noise Cancelling, 30Hr Battery"

❌ **Bad:** "Kitchen Gadget ★★HOT DEAL★★"
✅ **Good:** "VeggieChop Pro - 12-Blade Vegetable Chopper with Container"

## 3. Pricing Strategy

### Price Psychology

**Charm Pricing:**
- $47 instead of $50
- $97 instead of $100
- Works because left-digit effect

**Compare-at Price:**
- Show "Was $99, Now $67"
- Must be genuine (don't fake)
- Creates perceived value

**Tier Pricing:**
```
Basic: $47
Pro: $67 (BEST VALUE) ← Highlight this
Premium: $97
```

### Price Anchoring

**Techniques:**
- Show higher price first (strikethrough)
- Display "Value: $X" next to price
- Compare to competitor pricing
- Show cost-per-use breakdown

**Example:**
```
Regular Price: $129
Today: $67 (48% OFF)

That's just $0.67 per day for the next 100 days!
```

### Free Shipping Threshold

**Strategy:**
- Set threshold 20-30% above average order value
- Display progress bar in cart
- Message: "Only $X away from free shipping!"

**Example:**
- Average order: $55
- Free shipping threshold: $75
- Result: Customers add $20 more

## 4. Product Descriptions

### The Problem-Agitate-Solution Framework

**Structure:**
```
PROBLEM: Identify the pain point
AGITATE: Make it emotional, describe the frustration
SOLUTION: Present your product as the answer
PROOF: Back it up with evidence
CTA: Tell them what to do next
```

### Example (Fitness Resistance Bands)

**❌ Boring Feature List:**
- Made of natural latex
- 5 resistance levels
- Includes carry bag
- 12-month warranty

**✅ Compelling Description:**

*"Tired of expensive gym memberships and crowded workout spaces?*

*You want to get in shape, but finding time to hit the gym feels impossible. Between work, family, and life, who has 2 hours to spare?*

*Meet FitBands Pro - your complete gym in a pouch. These professional-grade resistance bands deliver a full-body workout anywhere, anytime. Clip them to a door, take them to the park, or use them in your hotel room.*

*✓ 5 resistance levels (10-50 lbs) - grow with your strength
✓ Natural latex - won't snap or lose elasticity
✓ Complete workout guide included - 50+ exercises
✓ Lifetime warranty - we stand behind our product*

*Join 50,000+ customers who've transformed their fitness routine. Your best body is one click away."*

### Description Best Practices

**DO:**
- Write for your ideal customer
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Include bullet points for scanning
- Add sensory language
- Address objections preemptively
- Include a clear CTA

**DON'T:**
- Copy supplier descriptions
- Use jargon or technical terms
- Write walls of text
- Make claims you can't prove
- Forget mobile formatting

### Feature → Benefit Translation

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| "5000mAh battery" | "Charge your phone 3x before needing a recharge" |
| "Waterproof IPX7" | "Take it in the shower without worry" |
| "Memory foam" | "Wake up without neck pain" |
| "Bluetooth 5.0" | "Connect instantly, no lag" |

## 5. Reviews & Social Proof

### Review Collection Strategy

**Timing:**
- Request review 7-14 days after delivery
- Send 2-3 reminder emails max
- Offer incentive (10% off next order)

**Tools:**
- Loox (photo reviews, $10/month)
- Judge.me (free plan available)
- Yotpo (enterprise)
- Stamped.io (good mid-tier)

### Review Display Best Practices

**On Product Page:**
- Show aggregate rating prominently
- Display review count
- Include photos in reviews
- Allow filtering (stars, with photos, recent)
- Respond to negative reviews publicly

**Review Request Email Template:**
```
Subject: How's your [Product] working out?

Hey [Name],

Your [Product] should have arrived by now! 

We'd love to hear how it's working for you. 
Take 30 seconds to leave a review and get 10% off 
your next order.

[Leave a Review Button]

Thanks for supporting our small business!

- The [Brand] Team

P.S. Photo reviews get an extra 5% off! 📸
```

### Handling Negative Reviews

**Response Framework:**
1. Acknowledge the issue
2. Apologize sincerely
3. Explain what happened (briefly)
4. Offer solution
5. Take conversation offline

**Example Response:**
```
"Hi Sarah, thank you for your feedback and I'm truly 
sorry your experience didn't meet expectations. This 
isn't the standard we hold ourselves to. 

We'd love to make this right - please email us at 
support@brand.com with your order number and we'll 
send a replacement immediately.

- The [Brand] Team"
```

## 6. Urgency & Scarcity (Ethical Use)

### Types of Urgency

**Genuine Urgency:**
- "Only 3 left in stock" (must be true)
- "Sale ends tonight at midnight"
- "Pre-order ships in 2 weeks"
- "Limited edition - won't restock"

**Soft Urgency:**
- "15 people viewing this product"
- "5 people have this in their cart"
- "Bestseller - 500+ sold this month"
- "Trending in your area"

### Implementation Tips

**DO:**
- Be honest (don't fake scarcity)
- Use sparingly (urgency fatigue is real)
- Combine with social proof
- Make it believable

**DON'T:**
- Reset countdown timers on refresh
- Show fake stock levels
- Use aggressive popups
- Create false deadlines

### Tools for Urgency
- **FOMO** - Sales notifications ($19/month)
- **Proof** - Social proof notifications ($29/month)
- **Urgency Bear** - Countdown timers (Shopify app)
- **Sales Pop** - Free basic notifications

## 8. Call-to-Action Optimization

### Add to Cart Button Best Practices

**Design:**
- High contrast color (stands out)
- Large enough for mobile (44px minimum)
- Clear, action-oriented text
- Above the fold on mobile

**Button Text Options:**
- "Add to Cart" (standard, clear)
- "Add to Bag" (fashion/lifestyle)
- "Buy Now" (impulse products)
- "Get Yours Today" (urgency)
- "Claim Your Discount" (promotion)

### Button Placement

**Desktop:**
- Right column (sticky as user scrolls)
- Repeat at bottom of description

**Mobile:**
- Below price and reviews
- Sticky bottom bar option
- Repeat after description

### Reducing Friction

**Quick Win Options:**
- Guest checkout enabled
- Multiple payment options (PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Auto-fill shipping where possible
- Clear shipping costs upfront
- No account required for purchase

## 9. Mobile Optimization

### Mobile-First Checklist

- [ ] Images load quickly (compressed)
- [ ] Text is readable without zooming
- [ ] Buttons are thumb-friendly (44px+)
- [ ] Add to cart visible without scrolling
- [ ] No horizontal scrolling
- [ ] Forms are easy to complete
- [ ] Checkout is streamlined
- [ ] Page loads in <3 seconds

### Mobile-Specific Tips

**Layout:**
- Single column design
- Larger tap targets
- Simplified navigation
- Accordion for long content
- Sticky add-to-cart button

**Performance:**
- Compress all images
- Minimize apps/scripts
- Use lazy loading
- Enable browser caching
- Use CDN

## 10. A/B Testing Framework

### What to Test (Priority Order)

**High Impact:**
1. Product images (lifestyle vs white background)
2. Headline/title
3. Price point
4. Add to cart button color/text
5. Product description length

**Medium Impact:**
6. Review placement
7. Urgency elements
8. Trust badges
9. Video presence
10. Guarantee messaging

### Testing Tools

- **Shopify:** Built-in A/B testing (Plus), third-party apps
- **Google Optimize:** Free (being sunset, use alternatives)
- **VWO:** Paid, comprehensive
- **Optimizely:** Enterprise
- **Nelio A/B Testing:** WordPress/WooCommerce

### Test Duration

**Minimum Guidelines:**
- 2 weeks minimum
- 100+ conversions per variant
- Full business cycle (include weekends)
- Statistically significant (95%+ confidence)
