### Interview Process

#### Technical Roles (Developers, Auditors)
```
Round 1: Screening (30 min)
- Background review
- Motivation assessment
- Culture fit

Round 2: Technical Assessment (take-home)
- Code review exercise
- Smart contract task
- Security analysis

Round 3: Technical Interview (60 min)
- Code review discussion
- System design
- Problem-solving

Round 4: Team Fit (45 min)
- Meet the team
- Collaboration style
- Values alignment

Round 5: Final (30 min)
- Executive conversation
- Offer discussion
- Questions
```

#### Business Roles (Sales, Marketing, PM)
```
Round 1: Screening (30 min)
- Background review
- Role understanding
- Motivation

Round 2: Case Study (take-home)
- Relevant scenario
- Strategy presentation
- Analysis

- Marketing: Strategy discussion
- PM: Scenario handling

Round 4: Team Fit (45 min)
- Cross-functional meetings
- Culture assessment

- Executive conversation
- Offer discussion
```

## Compensation Philosophy

### Principles
1. **Market Competitive:** Top 25% of market rates
2. **Equity Ownership:** All employees receive equity
3. **Performance-Based:** Clear bonus/commission structures
4. **Transparent:** Clear leveling and progression
5. **Benefits:** Comprehensive health, wellness, learning

### Equity Grants

| Level | Equity Range | Vesting |
|-------|--------------|---------|
| Junior | 0.05% - 0.2% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |
| Mid | 0.2% - 0.5% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |
| Senior | 0.5% - 1.0% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |
| Lead/Principal | 1.0% - 2.0% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |
| VP/Director | 2.0% - 5.0% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |
| C-Level | 5.0% - 15% | 4 years, 1-year cliff |

- Equity grants
- Performance bonuses
- Home office stipend

**Learning & Development:**

**Perks:**

## Culture & Values

### Core Values

1. **Security First:** Never compromise on security
2. **Client Success:** Our success is measured by client success
3. **Continuous Learning:** Stay ahead of the curve
4. **Transparency:** Open communication, honest feedback
5. **Ownership:** Take responsibility, drive results
6. **Collaboration:** Better together than alone

### Working Principles

- **Async-First:** Default to written communication
- **Meeting-Light:** Meetings only when necessary
- **Documentation:** If it's not documented, it doesn't exist
- **Feedback:** Direct, kind, actionable
- **Work-Life Balance:** Sustainable pace, respect boundaries
