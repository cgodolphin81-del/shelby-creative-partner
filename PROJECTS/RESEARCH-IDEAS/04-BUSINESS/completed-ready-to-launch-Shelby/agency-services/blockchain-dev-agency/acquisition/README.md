### 2. Outbound Sales (25% of leads)

#### Target Account List
Build a list of 100-200 high-value prospects:
- Recently funded Web3 startups (Crunchbase)
- Enterprises with blockchain initiatives (LinkedIn)
- Protocols planning upgrades (Governance forums)
- Gaming studios entering Web3 (Industry reports)

#### Outreach Sequence
```
Day 1: Personalized email
  Subject: [Specific observation about their project]
  - Reference recent news/funding
  - Specific value proposition
  - Clear CTA (15-min call)

Day 3: LinkedIn connection + note
  - Reference email
  - Share relevant content
  - No hard sell

Day 7: Follow-up email
  - Share case study
  - Address potential objections
  - Alternative CTA (send deck)

Day 14: Breakup email
  - Respectful close
  - Leave door open
  - Share one final resource
```

#### Email Templates

**Template 1: Funded Startup**

or working with external partners?

If external, I'd love to share how we've helped teams like yours ship faster 
with security-first development.

Worth a 15-min chat this week?

Best,
[Name]

1. **Security-First:** Multiple audits standard, not optional
2. **Full-Service:** Strategy → Development → Audit → Support
3. **Speed:** 30-50% faster than traditional firms
4. **Transparency:** Clear pricing, regular updates
5. **Partnership:** Long-term support, not just delivery
