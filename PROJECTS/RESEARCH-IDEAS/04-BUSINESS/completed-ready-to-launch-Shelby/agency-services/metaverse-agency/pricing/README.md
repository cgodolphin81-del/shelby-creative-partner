# Pricing Models

## Service Pricing Strategy & Rate Cards

---

## Pricing Philosophy

**Value-Based > Cost-Plus**

Price based on the value delivered to the client, not just hours worked. A $200K virtual showroom that generates $2M in sales is a bargain.

**Tiered Options**

Always present 3 options (Good/Better/Best) to anchor pricing and guide decisions.

**Transparent + Flexible**

Clear rate cards with room for custom scopes and enterprise negotiations.

## 3. Revenue Share Models

### When to Use Revenue Share

✅ Client has limited upfront budget  
✅ High confidence in monetization  
✅ Long-term partnership desired  
✅ Performance-based alignment  

### Revenue Share Structures

#### Model 1: Reduced Fee + Revenue Share
```
Upfront: 50% of standard rate
Revenue Share: 10-20% of gross revenue
Duration: 12-24 months
Cap: 2-3x upfront fee
```

#### Model 2: No Upfront + Higher Share
```
Upfront: $0 (or minimal)
Revenue Share: 25-40% of gross revenue
Duration: 24-36 months
Cap: 4-5x standard fee
```

#### Model 3: Hybrid Model
```
Upfront: 25% of standard rate
Monthly Minimum: $5K-10K
Revenue Share: 15-25% above threshold
Duration: 24 months
```

### Revenue Share Example

```
Project: Virtual Concert Venue
Standard Fee: $200K

Revenue Share Deal:
├── Upfront: $100K (50%)
├── Revenue Share: 15% of ticket sales
├── Monthly Minimum: $5K (after launch)
└── Cap: $400K total (2x standard)

Scenario - Successful Launch:
├── Year 1 Ticket Revenue: $500K
├── Our Share (15%): $75K
├── Monthly Minimums: $60K
├── Total Year 1: $100K + $75K + $60K = $235K
└── Exceeds standard fee by 17.5%
```

## 6. Pricing Psychology & Tactics

### Anchoring Strategy

```
Present 3 options to guide decision:

Option A: $75K   (Anchor - makes B look reasonable)
Option B: $150K  (Target - most clients choose this)
Option C: $300K  (Premium - for enterprise clients)
```

### Decoy Pricing

```
Basic:    $50K   (2 features)
Standard: $100K  (5 features) ← Most popular
Premium:  $125K  (6 features) ← Makes Standard look better
```

### Bundle Discount

```
Individual Services:
├── Virtual World: $150K
├── Avatar System: $50K
├── Event Launch: $75K
└── Total: $275K

Bundle Price: $225K (18% savings)
```

### Payment Terms

| Term | Discount | Use Case |
|------|----------|---------|
| **Net 30** | 0% | Standard |
| **Net 15** | 2% | Encourage faster payment |
| **50% Upfront** | 3% | Improve cash flow |
| **Full Upfront** | 5% | Maximum discount |
| **Crypto Payment** | 5-10% | For Web3 projects |

### Price Increase Strategy

```
Annual Increases:
├── Year 1: Base rates
├── Year 2: +10% (inflation + demand)
├── Year 3: +15% (proven track record)
└── Year 4+: Market rates

Grandfather existing clients at 50% of increase
```

## 9. Pricing FAQ

**Q: Why is metaverse development so expensive?**

A: It requires specialized skills (3D art, game dev, blockchain), custom tooling, and extensive testing. Think of it as building a video game + website + app combined.

**Q: Can we start small and scale?**

A: Absolutely! We recommend starting with an MVP ($50K-100K) and expanding based on results.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?**

A: Yes, 15-25% discount for qualified nonprofits and educational institutions.

**Q: What if our budget is lower than your minimum?**

A: We can discuss reduced scope, phased approach, or refer you to trusted partners.

**Q: Can we pay in crypto?**

A: Yes! We accept USDC, USDT, ETH, and MANA. 5% discount for crypto payments.

**Q: What's not included in project pricing?**

A: Platform fees, land purchases, third-party licenses, ongoing hosting, and major scope changes.

**Q: Do you offer financing?**

A: For projects $100K+, we can structure payments over 6-12 months with approved credit.

## 10. Price Adjustment Triggers

### When to Increase Prices

- [ ] Consistently booked 3+ months out
- [ ] Winning 70%+ of proposals
- [ ] Added significant capabilities
- [ ] Market rates have increased
- [ ] Inflation >5% annually
- [ ] Premium positioning achieved

### When to Offer Discounts

- [ ] Strategic portfolio piece
- [ ] Referral from existing client
- [ ] Multi-project commitment
- [ ] Off-peak timing
- [ ] Nonprofit/education
- [ ] Payment upfront

### Price Review Cadence

| Review | Frequency | Focus |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Rate Card** | Quarterly | Market alignment |
| **Proposal Win Rate** | Monthly | Pricing effectiveness |
| **Margin Analysis** | Monthly | Profitability |
| **Competitor Check** | Quarterly | Positioning |
| **Client Feedback** | Ongoing | Value perception |
