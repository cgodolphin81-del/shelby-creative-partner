## 1. Enterprise Brands

### Ideal Client Profile

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Company Size** | $100M+ revenue |
| **Industry** | Consumer goods, automotive, fashion, tech |
| **Decision Maker** | CMO, VP Marketing, Innovation Director |
| **Budget** | $100K-500K per project |
| **Pain Points** | Reaching Gen Z, digital transformation, competition |
| **Motivation** | Brand relevance, customer engagement, innovation |

### Outreach Strategy

#### Warm Introductions (Highest Conversion)
```
Channels:
├── Existing client referrals
├── Agency partnerships (digital, PR, ad agencies)
├── Investor/VC introductions
├── Board member connections
└── Industry event networking

Conversion Rate: 30-50%
```

#### Direct Outreach
```
Approach:
├── LinkedIn outreach to marketing leaders
├── Personalized email campaigns
├── Thought leadership content
├── Case study sharing
└── Event invitations

Conversion Rate: 5-15%
```

#### Inbound Marketing
```
Tactics:
├── SEO-optimized content
├── Webinar series
├── Industry reports
├── Speaking engagements
└── PR and media coverage

Conversion Rate: 10-20%
```

```
Week 1-2:    Initial Contact → Discovery Call
Week 3-4:    Proposal → Presentation
Week 5-8:    Evaluation → Legal Review
Week 9-10:   Negotiation → Close
Week 11+:    Kickoff

Total: 10-12 weeks average

**For CMOs:**
> "Drive measurable engagement with immersive brand experiences that Gen Z actually cares about."

**For Innovation Directors:**
> "Stay ahead of the curve with production-ready metaverse solutions, not just experiments."

**For CFOs:**
> "Virtual experiences cost 60% less than physical events with 10x reach and full analytics."

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Lead Qualification

**BANT Framework:**
```
Budget:    Do they have $50K+ available?
Authority: Are we talking to the decision maker?
Need:      Do they have a clear use case?
Timeline:  Are they looking to start in 90 days?

Score: 4/4 = Hot lead, 3/4 = Warm, <3 = Nurture
```

### Stage 2: Discovery Call

**Agenda (30-45 minutes):**
```
1. Introductions (5 min)
2. Their goals and challenges (15 min)
3. Our relevant experience (10 min)
4. Next steps (5 min)
5. Q&A (10 min)
```

**Key Questions:**
- What's driving your interest in the metaverse?
- What does success look like for this project?
- What's your timeline and budget range?
- Who else is involved in the decision?
- What concerns or questions do you have?

### Stage 3: Proposal

**Proposal Structure:**
```
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Understanding of Goals (1-2 pages)
3. Proposed Solution (3-5 pages)
4. Investment & Timeline (1-2 pages)
5. Case Studies (2-3 pages)
6. Team & Credentials (1-2 pages)
7. Terms & Next Steps (1 page)

Total: 10-15 pages
```

### Stage 4: Presentation

**Presentation Flow:**
```
1. Problem restatement (2 min)
2. Solution overview (5 min)
3. Visual demo/mockups (10 min)
4. Investment discussion (5 min)
5. Case studies (5 min)
6. Q&A (15 min)
7. Close/next steps (3 min)

Total: 45 minutes
```

### Stage 5: Negotiation

**Common Objections & Responses:**

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Let's discuss scope adjustments or phased approach" |
| "Need to think about it" | "What specific concerns can I address?" |
| "Competitor is cheaper" | "Let's compare scope and quality, not just price" |
| "Not sure about ROI" | "Let's build a custom ROI model for your use case" |
| "Timing isn't right" | "When would be better? Let's stay in touch" |

### Stage 6: Close

**Closing Techniques:**
```
✓ Assumptive Close: "When should we schedule kickoff?"
✓ Summary Close: "Based on what we discussed, this solution..."
✓ Urgency Close: "We have capacity starting next month..."
✓ Question Close: "What would it take to move forward today?"
```

## CRM & Pipeline Management

### Pipeline Stages

```
Lead → Qualified → Discovery → Proposal → Negotiation → Closed Won/Lost

Conversion Rates (Industry Average):
├── Lead to Qualified: 50%
├── Qualified to Discovery: 60%
├── Discovery to Proposal: 70%
├── Proposal to Negotiation: 50%
├── Negotiation to Close: 70%
└── Overall: ~7%
```

### Pipeline Targets

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| **Monthly New Leads** | 50+ |
| **Qualified Opportunities** | 25+ |
| **Active Proposals** | 10+ |
| **Close Rate** | 25-35% |
| **Average Deal Size** | $100K-200K |
| **Monthly Revenue Goal** | $500K+ |

### CRM Requirements

```
Must Track:
├── Contact information
├── Company details
├── Deal stage
├── Deal value
├── Expected close date
├── Last activity
├── Next action
├── Communication history

└── Win/loss reasons

Recommended Tools:
