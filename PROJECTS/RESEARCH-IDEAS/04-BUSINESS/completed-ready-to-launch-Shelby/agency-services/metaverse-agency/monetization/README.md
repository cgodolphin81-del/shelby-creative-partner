## 1. Virtual Goods Sales

### Overview
Sell digital items, wearables, and accessories within virtual environments.

### Item Categories

| Category | Examples | Price Range | Margin |
|----------|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Avatar Wearables** | Clothing, accessories, skins | $5-500 | 70-90% |
| **Virtual Furniture** | Decor, fixtures, art | $10-1,000 | 60-80% |
| **Vehicles** | Cars, mounts, transport | $50-5,000 | 50-70% |
| **Tools/Weapons** | Gaming items, utilities | $5-200 | 70-85% |
| **Collectibles** | Limited editions, art | $50-50,000+ | 80-95% |
| **Consumables** | Power-ups, effects | $1-20 | 90-95% |

### Pricing Strategies

**Cost-Plus Pricing**
```
Production Cost + Desired Margin = Sale Price
Example: $50 (art) + $50 (dev) + $100 (margin) = $200
```

**Value-Based Pricing**
```
Price based on perceived value to customer
Example: Exclusive branded item = $500 (regardless of cost)
```

**Tiered Pricing**
```
Common: $5-20    (60% of sales)
Rare: $50-200    (30% of sales)
Legendary: $500+ (10% of sales)
```

### Revenue Potential

| Scenario | Items/Month | Avg Price | Monthly Revenue |
|----------|-------------|-----------|-----------------|
| **Small** | 100 | $25 | $2,500 |
| **Medium** | 500 | $50 | $25,000 |
| **Large** | 2,000 | $75 | $150,000 |
| **Enterprise** | 10,000+ | $100 | $1M+ |

### Best Practices
- Release items in collections/drops
- Create scarcity (limited editions)
- Enable social display (show off purchases)
- Cross-platform compatibility increases value
- Regular new releases drive repeat purchases

## 2. NFT Integration & Sales

### Overview
Leverage blockchain technology for true digital ownership and secondary market royalties.

### NFT Types

| Type | Use Case | Price Range | Royalty % |
|------|----------|-------------|-----------|
| **Avatar NFTs** | PFP, identity | 0.1-10 ETH | 5-10% |
| **Wearables** | Clothing, accessories | 0.05-5 ETH | 5-10% |
| **Virtual Land** | Parcels, estates | 1-100 ETH | 2-5% |
| **Art/Collectibles** | Display pieces | 0.1-50 ETH | 10-15% |
| **Membership** | Access tokens | 0.5-20 ETH | 0-5% |
| **Gaming Items** | Weapons, tools | 0.01-2 ETH | 5-10% |

### Revenue Streams

**Primary Sales**
```
Collection Size × Floor Price = Primary Revenue
Example: 10,000 NFTs × 0.1 ETH = 1,000 ETH (~$2M at $2K/ETH)
```

**Secondary Royalties**
```
Secondary Volume × Royalty % = Ongoing Revenue
Example: $10M volume × 7.5% = $750K ongoing
```

**Utility Revenue**
```
NFT holders get exclusive access → Higher engagement → More spending
```

### Launch Strategy

| Phase | Activity | Timeline |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Pre-Launch** | Community building, whitelist | 4-8 weeks |
| **Mint** | Public sale, distribution | 1-7 days |
| **Post-Mint** | Utility rollout, partnerships | Ongoing |
| **Secondary** | Marketplace support, royalties | Ongoing |

### Platform Comparison

| Platform | Mint Cost | Royalty Support | Audience |
|----------|-----------|-----------------|----------|
| **OpenSea** | Low | ✅ Yes | General |
| **Rarible** | Low | ✅ Yes | Art-focused |
| **Magic Eden** | Low | ✅ Yes | Solana/NFT |
| **Decentraland** | Medium | ✅ Yes | Metaverse |

### Legal Considerations
- Securities law compliance (varies by jurisdiction)

| Format | Description | CPM Range |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| **Billboards** | Static/dynamic displays | $5-20 |
| **Video Screens** | Video ad loops | $15-40 |
| **Branded Items** | Product placement | $10-30 |
| **Sponsored Zones** | Themed areas | $20-50 |
| **Avatar Ads** | Wearable branding | $10-25 |
| **Audio Ads** | Spatial audio spots | $8-20 |

### Programmatic vs. Direct

| Approach | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Direct Sales** | Higher rates, brand control | Sales effort required |
| **Programmatic** | Automated, scalable | Lower rates, less control |
| **Hybrid** | Best of both | More complex |

### Ad Inventory Calculation

```
Monthly Impressions = DAU × Ads Per Session × 30

Example:
10,000 DAU × 5 ads/session × 30 days = 1.5M impressions

At $15 CPM: 1.5M / 1,000 × $15 = $22,500/month
```

## Metrics & KPIs

### Financial Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **MRR** | Monthly recurring revenue | Growth MoM |
| **ARR** | MRR × 12 | $1M+ Year 2 |
| **ARPU** | Revenue / Active Users | $20-50/month |
| **ARPPU** | Revenue / Paying Users | $50-200/month |
| **LTV** | ARPU × Gross Margin × Avg Lifespan | 3x CAC |
| **CAC** | Sales & Marketing / New Customers | <LTV/3 |
| **Payback** | CAC / Monthly Gross Margin | <12 months |

### Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **DAU/MAU** | Daily / Monthly Active Users | 20%+ |
| **Session Length** | Total Time / Sessions | 15+ minutes |
| **Retention D1** | Day 1 returning users | 40%+ |
| **Retention D7** | Day 7 returning users | 20%+ |
| **Conversion** | Paying / Total Users | 2-5% |

*Monetization should always balance revenue generation with user experience. Over-monetization kills engagement; under-monetization kills businesses.*
