# PROJECT KICKOFF CHECKLIST

## Pre-Kickoff (Before Meeting)

### Internal Preparation
- [ ] Contract signed and filed
- [ ] Initial payment received
- [ ] Project created in PM system (Jira/Asana)
- [ ] Team assigned and briefed
- [ ] Internal kickoff completed
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities defined
- [ ] Resource availability confirmed

### Client Setup
- [ ] Kickoff meeting scheduled (60-90 min)
- [ ] Calendar invites sent to all stakeholders
- [ ] Meeting agenda shared in advance
- [ ] Video conference link created
- [ ] Recording enabled (with permission)

### Access & Tools
- [ ] Client added to Slack/communication channel
- [ ] Shared drive/folder created
- [ ] Project management access granted
- [ ] Design tool access (Figma, etc.)
- [ ] Version control access (if applicable)

---

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda (60-90 minutes)

### 1. Introductions (10 min)
- [ ] Team introductions (both sides)
- [ ] Roles and responsibilities
- [ ] Communication preferences
- [ ] Working hours and time zones

### 2. Project Overview (15 min)
- [ ] Review project goals
- [ ] Success criteria
- [ ] Scope confirmation
- [ ] Timeline review
- [ ] Budget confirmation

### 3. Process & Communication (15 min)
- [ ] Development methodology (Agile/Waterfall)
- [ ] Meeting cadence (daily, weekly, etc.)
- [ ] Status reporting format
- [ ] Decision-making process
- [ ] Escalation path for issues
- [ ] Change order process

### 4. Technical Discussion (15 min)
- [ ] Platform confirmation
- [ ] Technical requirements
- [ ] Integration points
- [ ] Security requirements
- [ ] Performance expectations

### 5. Timeline & Milestones (10 min)
- [ ] Key dates review
- [ ] Client dependencies
- [ ] Review/approval process
- [ ] Launch criteria

### 6. Q&A and Next Steps (10 min)
- [ ] Open questions
- [ ] Action items assigned
- [ ] Next meeting scheduled
- [ ] Immediate next steps

---

### Documentation
- [ ] Meeting notes distributed

- [ ] Templates applied

- [ ] Thank you email sent
- [ ] Any open questions answered

## Communication Plan

### Regular Meetings
| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Attendees |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| **Daily Standup** | Daily | 15 min | Core team |
| **Client Check-in** | Weekly | 30 min | PMs + leads |
| **Steering Committee** | Monthly | 60 min | Executives |
| **Sprint Review** | Every 2 weeks | 60 min | All stakeholders |

### Communication Channels
| Purpose | Channel | Response Time |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| **Urgent Issues** | Phone/Slack | <1 hour |
| **Day-to-Day** | Slack | <4 hours |
| **Formal Updates** | Email | <24 hours |
| **Documents** | Shared Drive | As needed |
| **Decisions** | Email (written) | <48 hours |

## Success Criteria Checklist

### Project Success
- [ ] Delivered on time
- [ ] Delivered within budget
- [ ] Meets all requirements
- [ ] Client satisfaction >4.5/5
- [ ] Team satisfaction >4/5

### Business Success
- [ ] Profitable (margin >50%)
- [ ] Reference-able client
- [ ] Case study opportunity
- [ ] Retainer conversion potential
- [ ] Referral potential

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

### Early Warning Signs
- [ ] Client slow to respond
- [ ] Unclear requirements
- [ ] Scope discussions outside process
- [ ] Team capacity issues
- [ ] Technical blockers
- [ ] Budget concerns raised
- [ ] Timeline pressure

### Action if Red Flags Appear
1. Document the issue
2. Assess impact
3. Discuss with internal team
4. Communicate with client
5. Develop mitigation plan
6. Update risk register
7. Monitor closely

## Client Onboarding Survey

*Send this to client before or after kickoff*

**About Your Team:**
1. Who are the key decision-makers?
2. Who will be our day-to-day contact?
3. Who needs to review/approve deliverables?
4. What's the best way to reach each person?

**About the Project:**
5. What does success look like?
6. What are your biggest concerns?
7. Are there any hard deadlines?
8. Are there any constraints we should know about?

**About Communication:**
9. What's your preferred communication style?
10. How often do you want updates?
11. What format works best for you?
12. Any communication pet peeves?

**About Process:**
13. Have you worked with agencies before?
14. What worked well in past projects?
15. What didn't work well?
16. Any specific processes we should follow?
