## 💡 Pro Tips

### Topic Selection
- Choose pain over aspiration (higher attendance)
- Be specific with outcomes
- Include timeline in title
- Address objections in subtitle

### Slide Design
- Less text, more visuals
- One idea per slide
- Use brand colors consistently
- Test on mobile

### Script Delivery
- Write like you speak
- Practice out loud 5+ times
- Record and watch yourself
- Time each section

### Registration Pages
- 2-3 form fields max
- Show social proof above fold
- Add urgency (spots, deadline)
- Mobile-optimize everything

### Email Sequences
- Subject lines matter most
- One CTA per email
- Mobile-preview before sending
- Test send times

### Follow-Up
- Most sales happen after webinar
- Personal outreach converts 3x better
- Handle objections proactively
- Create genuine urgency

### Analytics
- Track everything from Day 1
- Focus on Revenue Per Registrant
- A/B test one element at a time
- Document all learnings
