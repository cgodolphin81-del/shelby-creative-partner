# Registration Page Templates: High-Converting Copy & Design

## Overview

Your registration page is the gateway to your webinar. These templates are proven to convert 25-45% of visitors into registrants.

---

## 📊 REGISTRATION PAGE ANATOMY

Every high-converting registration page includes:

1. **Headline** (grabs attention, promises outcome)
2. **Subheadline** (clarifies, adds specificity)
3. **Bullet Points** (what they'll learn)
4. **Host Credibility** (why listen to you)
5. **Date/Time** (with timezone)
6. **Registration Form** (minimal fields)
7. **Call-to-Action Button** (clear, action-oriented)
8. **Social Proof** (testimonials, logos, numbers)
9. **Urgency/Scarcity** (why register now)
10. **FAQ** (address objections)
11. **Privacy Assurance** (no spam promise)

## 🎨 DESIGN BEST PRACTICES

### Layout Principles
- **Above the fold**: Headline, subhead, form visible without scrolling
- **White space**: Don't cram—let content breathe
- **Visual hierarchy**: Guide the eye with size, color, placement
- **Mobile-first**: 50%+ will register on mobile

### Color Psychology
- **Blue**: Trust, professional (B2B, finance)
- **Green**: Growth, health, money
- **Orange/Red**: Urgency, action (use sparingly)
- **Purple**: Premium, transformation
- **Black/White**: Minimalist, elegant

### Typography
- **Headlines**: 36-48px, bold
- **Subheads**: 20-24px, medium weight
- **Body**: 16-18px, readable font
- **Buttons**: 18-20px, bold, all caps optional

### Images
- **Host photo**: Professional, friendly, high-quality
- **Background**: Clean, not distracting
- **Graphics**: Simple icons, not clipart
- **Logos**: High-res, consistent sizing

### Buttons
- **Size**: Large enough to tap on mobile (44px minimum)
- **Color**: Contrasting from page background
- **Text**: Action-oriented ("Save My Spot" vs. "Submit")
- **Placement**: Above fold, repeated below

## 📊 CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Form Optimization
- **Fields**: 2-3 fields max (name, email, optional phone)
- **Labels**: Above fields, not placeholder text
- **Privacy**: "We never share your info" near form
- **Autofill**: Enable for faster completion

### Urgency Elements
- **Spots remaining**: "Only X spots left"
- **Countdown**: Timer until webinar starts
- **Deadline**: "Registration closes [date]"
- **Live counter**: "X people registered in last 24 hours"

### Trust Signals
- **Testimonials**: Real names, photos, results
- **Media logos**: "As seen in"
- **Numbers**: "10,000+ students", "500+ webinars"
- **Security badges**: SSL, privacy icons

### A/B Test These Elements
1. Headline (outcome-focused vs. curiosity)
2. Button color (red vs. green vs. orange)
3. Button text ("Register" vs. "Save My Spot" vs. "Yes!")
4. Form length (2 fields vs. 4 fields)
5. Host photo (professional vs. casual)
6. Social proof placement (above vs. below form)
7. Urgency (timer vs. spots remaining vs. none)

## 🔗 POST-REGISTRATION FLOW

### Immediate (On Submit)
```
1. Show thank you page with:
   - Confirmation message
   - Add to calendar buttons (Google, Outlook, iCal)
```
