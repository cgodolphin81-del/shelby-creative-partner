# 30-Day Launch Plan: Complete Webinar Production Timeline

## Overview

This day-by-day plan walks you through producing and launching a high-converting webinar in 30 days. Follow this exact timeline for best results.

---

## 📅 PHASE OVERVIEW

```
WEEK 1 (Days 1-7):    FOUNDATION
                      Strategy, topic, offer, platform

WEEK 2 (Days 8-14):   CONTENT CREATION
                      Slides, script, registration page

WEEK 3 (Days 15-21):  PROMOTION SETUP
                      Emails, ads, tech, automation

WEEK 4 (Days 22-28):  PROMOTION & REHEARSAL
                      Drive registrations, rehearse

DAY 29:               WEBINAR DAY
                      Go live!

DAY 30+:              FOLLOW-UP
                      Convert attendees to buyers
```

---

## 🗓️ WEEK 1: FOUNDATION

### DAY 1: Strategy & Topic Selection

**Morning (2 hours)**
- [ ] Define your target audience
  - Who are they?
  - What's their biggest pain point?
  - What outcome do they want?
- [ ] Brainstorm 5-10 webinar topics
- [ ] Score each topic using the 4-Point Scorecard:
  - Urgency (1-10)
  - Specificity (1-10)
  - Credibility (1-10)
  - Offer Alignment (1-10)
- [ ] Select winning topic

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Define your offer
  - What are you selling?
  - Price point?
  - Transformation promised?
- [ ] Set webinar goals
  - Registration target: [number]
  - Attendance target: [percentage]%
  - Conversion target: [percentage]%
  - Revenue target: $[amount]

**Deliverable**: Webinar Strategy Document

---

### DAY 2: Platform & Tech Selection

**Morning (1 hour)**
- [ ] Choose webinar platform

**Afternoon (1 hour)**

## 🚨 CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### If Registrations Are Low (Day 25)

**Options**:
1. Increase ad budget
2. Send additional email to list
3. Partner promotion (ask others to share)
4. Extend registration deadline (if possible)
5. Add bonus for early registrants

### If Tech Fails (Day 29)

**Backup Plan**:
1. Have backup internet (phone hotspot)
2. Have backup device (laptop + tablet)
3. Have co-host who can take over
4. Have recorded intro ready
5. Have platform support number handy

### If Attendance Is Low

**Options**:
1. Still deliver (often convert better)
2. Offer 1:1 calls to attendees
3. Extend replay availability
4. Run another webinar sooner

### If Sales Are Low

**Options**:
1. Extend offer deadline
2. Add additional bonus
3. Personal outreach to attendees
4. Offer payment plan
5. Downsell to lower-ticket offer
