# 🚀 Quick Start Guide

## Launch Your First Webinar in 7 Days

Don't have 30 days? This accelerated plan gets you live in one week.

---

## ⚡ 7-DAY CRASH PLAN

### DAY 1: Foundation (4 hours)

**Morning (2 hours)**
- [ ] Choose your topic using Deliverable 1
  - Pick from the 27 proven topics OR
  - Use the 4-Point Scorecard to validate your idea
- [ ] Define your offer
  - What are you selling?
  - At what price?
  - What's the transformation?

**Afternoon (2 hours)**
- [ ] Choose your platform (Deliverable 4)
  - **Quickest**: Zoom (familiar, reliable)
  - **Best for sales**: WebinarJam (built-in features)
  - **Budget**: StreamYard Free
- [ ] Set up your account
- [ ] Schedule your webinar (7 days from now)
  - Best times: Tuesday-Thursday, 12pm or 8pm ET

**Deliverable**: Topic + Offer + Platform selected

---

### DAY 2: Content Creation (5 hours)

**Morning (3 hours)**
- [ ] Build your slide deck using Deliverable 2
  - Use the 10-slide template
  - Don't overdesign—content over perfection
  - 45-60 minutes of content total
- [ ] Export as PDF backup

**Afternoon (2 hours)**
- [ ] Write your script using Deliverable 3
  - Hook: Use Template 1 or 2
  - Story: Use the Hero's Journey template
  - Framework: 3-5 steps maximum
  - Offer: Use Value Stack template
  - Close: Use Direct Close template
- [ ] Time yourself reading it

**Deliverable**: Slides + Script complete

---

### DAY 3: Registration Page (3 hours)

**Morning (2 hours)**
- [ ] Build registration page
  - Use Deliverable 5, Template 1 (Transformation)
  - Headline: "How to [Outcome] in [Time] Without [Obstacle]"
  - 4-6 bullet points of what they'll learn
  - Your bio with 3 credibility points
  - Simple form: Name + Email only
- [ ] Set up thank you page
  - Confirmation message
  - Add to calendar buttons

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Test on mobile
- [ ] Test on desktop
- [ ] Test form submission
- [ ] Verify confirmation email

**Deliverable**: Live registration page

---

### DAY 4: Email Setup (2 hours)

**Morning (1 hour)**
- [ ] Write 5 essential emails (Deliverable 6)
  - Email 1: Invitation (send today)
  - Email 2: 1-Day Reminder (schedule)
  - Email 3: 3-Hour Reminder (schedule)
  - Email 4: Starting Soon (schedule)
  - Email 5: Thank You + Replay (schedule)

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Set up automation in your email platform
- [ ] Test all emails (send to yourself)
- [ ] Check all links work

**Deliverable**: Email sequence ready

---

### DAY 5: Promotion Launch (2 hours)

**Morning (1 hour)**
- [ ] Send Email 1 to your list
- [ ] Post on all social channels
  - LinkedIn
  - Facebook
  - Instagram
  - Twitter (if relevant)
- [ ] Message 10 people personally

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Set up simple Facebook ad ($10-20/day)
  - Image: Your headshot or slide
  - Copy: Headline from registration page
  - Target: Your audience
  - Budget: $50-100 total
- [ ] Launch ad

**Deliverable**: Promotion started

---

### DAY 6: Rehearsal & Tech Check (2 hours)

**Morning (1 hour)**
- [ ] Full rehearsal
  - Read script out loud
  - Advance slides
  - Time each section
  - Adjust as needed

**Afternoon (1 hour)**
- [ ] Tech check
  - Internet speed test (need 10+ Mbps upload)
  - Audio test
  - Video test
  - Screen share test
  - Join webinar room, test everything
- [ ] Prepare your space
  - Clean background
  - Good lighting
  - Water nearby
  - Phone on silent

**Deliverable**: Ready to go live

---

### DAY 7: WEBINAR DAY! 🎯

**Morning (30 minutes)**
- [ ] Light run-through (just the hook and offer)
- [ ] Check tech one more time
- [ ] Eat something light

**1 Hour Before**
- [ ] Join webinar room
- [ ] Test audio/video
- [ ] Upload slides
- [ ] Start recording
- [ ] Welcome early arrivals

**WEBINAR TIME**
- [ ] Deliver your content
- [ ] Engage with chat
- [ ] Make your offer
- [ ] Stay for Q&A

**After**
- [ ] Send Email 5 (Thank You + Replay)
- [ ] Celebrate!
- [ ] Rest

## 🚨 COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

### Content Mistakes
- ❌ Too much content (stick to 3-5 key points)
- ❌ No clear offer (always have something to sell)
- ❌ Reading slides verbatim (slides support, not replace you)
- ❌ No engagement (use chat, polls, questions)

### Tech Mistakes
- ❌ Testing tech day-of (test day before)
- ❌ No backup internet (have hotspot ready)
- ❌ Bad audio (use external mic if possible)
- ❌ Not recording (always record!)

### Promotion Mistakes
- ❌ Only one email (send 3-5 minimum)
- ❌ No social posts (post 3-5 times)
- ❌ No personal invites (DM 10-20 people)
- ❌ Waiting until last minute (start 5-7 days out)

### Follow-Up Mistakes
- ❌ No replay email (send within 1 hour)
- ❌ No sales follow-up (most sales happen after)
- ❌ Giving up too soon (follow up 3-5 times)

## 🎤 DELIVERY TIPS

### Before You Start
- Smile (even if nervous)
- Thank people for coming
- Set expectations (duration, Q&A at end)
- Ask for engagement ("Type YES if you can hear me")

### During Presentation
- Speak slower than normal
- Pause between key points
- Reference chat by name
- Stay positive and energetic

### During Offer
- Be confident (you believe in this)
- State price clearly
- Explain payment options
- Remind about guarantee
- Give clear CTA

### During Q&A
- Repeat questions before answering
- Keep answers concise
- Bridge back to offer when relevant
- Thank people for questions
