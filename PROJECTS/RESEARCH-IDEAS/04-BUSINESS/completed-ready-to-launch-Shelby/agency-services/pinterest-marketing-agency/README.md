## Why Pinterest Marketing?

- **Long content lifespan**: Pins work for months/years vs. hours on other platforms
- **High purchase intent**: 83% of Pinners make purchases based on platform content
- **Organic reach**: Unlike Instagram/Facebook, Pinterest still offers significant organic reach
- **Traffic driver**: Pinterest drives more referral traffic than Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube combined
- **E-commerce ready**: Built-in shopping features and product pins
