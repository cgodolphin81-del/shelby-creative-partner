## Description Length Guidelines

**Short (50-100 characters):**
- Quick tips
- Simple product pins
- Minimalist aesthetic

**Medium (100-300 characters):**
- Most pins (optimal)
- How-to tutorials
- List posts

**Long (300-500 characters):**
- Detailed guides
- Story-driven content
- Comprehensive resources

**Pinterest allows up to 500 characters in descriptions. Use them wisely!**

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Front-load keywords** - Put important keywords in the first sentence
2. **Write for humans first** - Pinterest's algorithm is smart enough to understand natural language
3. **Include a CTA** - Tell people what to do next
4. **Use emojis sparingly** - 1-3 relevant emojis can increase engagement
5. **Test and track** - See which descriptions drive the most clicks and saves
6. **Update old pins** - Refresh descriptions on underperforming pins
7. **Match the image** - Ensure description aligns with what's shown
8. **Be specific** - Vague descriptions get vague results
