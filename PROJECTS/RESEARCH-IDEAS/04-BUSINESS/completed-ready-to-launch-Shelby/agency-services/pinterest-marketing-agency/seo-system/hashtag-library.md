## Best Practices

### How Many Hashtags?
- **Minimum:** 3 hashtags
- **Optimal:** 5-8 hashtags
- **Maximum:** 20 hashtags (but diminishing returns after 8)

### Hashtag Mix Formula
```
2 Broad hashtags (1M+ pins)
3 Medium hashtags (100K-1M pins)
3 Niche hashtags (10K-100K pins)
```

### Placement
- Add hashtags at the END of your pin description
- Separate from main copy with line breaks
- No spaces in hashtags (#likeThis not #Like This)

### Research Tips
1. Check what top pins in your niche use
2. Click on hashtags to see related tags
3. Note which hashtags drive the most engagement
4. Update seasonally
5. Test and track performance

### Avoid
- Irrelevant popular hashtags (#love, #happy on business pins)
- Banned or restricted hashtags
- Too many hashtags (looks spammy)
- Repeating exact same hashtags on every pin
