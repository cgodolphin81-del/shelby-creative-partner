# E-commerce Email Calendar
## 365 Days of Send Ideas

---

## Executive Summary

This calendar provides a full year of email marketing ideas for e-commerce businesses. Mix automated flows with strategic campaigns to maximize revenue while avoiding list fatigue.

**Key Principle:** Balance promotional emails with value-added content. Follow the 80/20 rule (80% value, 20% promotion).

## 1. EMAIL FREQUENCY GUIDELINES

### Recommended Send Frequency

| Segment | Emails/Week | Emails/Month |
|---------|-------------|--------------|
| Highly Engaged | 3-5 | 12-20 |
| Moderately Engaged | 2-3 | 8-12 |
| Low Engagement | 1-2 | 4-8 |
| New Subscribers | 3-4 (first month) | 12-16 |
| VIP Customers | 4-5 | 16-20 |

### Email Type Balance

| Type | % of Sends | Purpose |
|------|------------|---------|
| Promotional | 40% | Drive sales |
| Educational | 25% | Build trust |
| Product-focused | 20% | Showcase items |
| Community/Social | 10% | Build connection |
| Survey/Feedback | 5% | Gather insights |

### Flow vs. Campaign Ratio

**Ideal Mix:**
- 60% Automated Flows (set and forget)
- 40% Manual Campaigns (strategic sends)

**Weekly Breakdown:**
- Flows: Running continuously (welcome, cart, etc.)
- Campaigns: 2-3 per week maximum
