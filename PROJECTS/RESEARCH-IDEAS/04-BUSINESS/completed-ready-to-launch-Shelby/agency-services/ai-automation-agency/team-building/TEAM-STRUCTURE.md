## Phase-Based Team Growth

### Phase 1: Solo Founder (Revenue: $0-10K/month)
```
┌─────────────────┐
│   FOUNDER       │
│   (You)         │
├─────────────────┤
│ • Sales         │
│ • Delivery      │
│ • Operations    │
│ • Everything    │
└─────────────────┘
```

**Focus:** Prove the model, get first clients, systematize delivery

**Key Hires:** None yet. Use freelancers for overflow work.

**Milestone to Next Phase:** $10K/month recurring, 3+ active clients

## Hiring Guidelines

### When to Hire

**First Hire (Delivery Support):**
- When: You're turning down work or quality is slipping
- Role: Freelance or junior engineer
- Why: Free up your time for sales

**Second Hire (Sales):**
- When: You have more leads than you can handle
- Role: SDR or AE
- Why: Scale lead generation and closing

**Third Hire (Delivery Lead):**
- When: You have 3+ engineers and can't manage them all
- Role: Delivery director or senior engineer
- Why: Ensure quality and client satisfaction

**Fourth Hire (Ops):**
- When: Administrative work is taking 20%+ of your time
- Role: Ops manager or virtual assistant
- Why: Focus on growth, not admin

---

### Hiring Process

**Step 1: Job Description**
- Clear role and responsibilities
- Required skills and experience
- Nice-to-have qualifications
- Compensation range
- Company overview

**Step 2: Sourcing**
- LinkedIn
- Indeed/Glassdoor
- Industry-specific job boards
- Referrals
- Freelance platforms (for trial)

**Step 3: Screening**
- Resume review
- Phone screen (30 min)
- Skills assessment
- Reference checks

**Step 4: Interviews**
- Technical interview (for delivery roles)
- Culture fit interview
- Final interview with founder/executive

**Step 5: Offer**
- Competitive compensation
- Clear expectations
- Probationary period (90 days)
- Onboarding plan

---

### Interview Questions by Role

**Automation Engineer:**
1. Walk me through a complex automation you've built.
2. How do you handle API rate limits and errors?
3. What's your approach to debugging a failing workflow?
4. How do you ensure security in integrations?
5. [Practical test]: Build a simple workflow in [tool]

**Account Executive:**
1. Walk me through your sales process.
2. Tell me about a deal you lost and what you learned.
3. How do you handle price objections?
4. Role-play: Sell me on a discovery call.
5. What's your track record (quota attainment)?

**Client Success Manager:**
1. How do you build relationships with clients?
2. Tell me about a time you turned around an at-risk client.
3. How do you identify upsell opportunities?
4. How do you handle an unhappy client?
5. What metrics do you track for client health?

## Remote vs. In-Office

### Our Recommendation: Remote-First

**Pros:**
- Access to global talent
- Lower overhead
- Flexibility for team
- Scalability

**Cons:**
- Culture building requires intentionality
- Communication challenges
- Timezone coordination

**Best Practices:**
- Core overlap hours (4 hours minimum)
- Regular video check-ins
- Annual in-person gatherings
- Clear documentation
- Async-first communication

## Culture & Values

### Core Values (Example)

1. **Client Success First:** We win when our clients win.
2. **Bias for Action:** Move fast, learn faster.
3. **Radical Transparency:** Honest communication, always.
4. **Continuous Learning:** Stay curious, stay growing.
5. **Quality Matters:** Good enough is rarely good enough.

### Culture Building

**Regular Activities:**
- Weekly all-hands meeting
- Monthly team building (virtual or in-person)
- Quarterly offsites (if remote)
- Annual company retreat
- Recognition program (peer-to-peer and leadership)

**Communication:**
- Slack for day-to-day
- Email for formal communication
- Loom for async updates
- Notion for documentation
- Weekly 1:1s for all team members
