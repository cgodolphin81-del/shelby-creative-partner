# Client Acquisition Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines proven strategies for consistently generating qualified leads and converting them into paying clients.

---

## Target Market Definition

### Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

**Company Characteristics:**
- **Size:** 10-200 employees
- **Revenue:** $500K - $50M annually
- **Industry:** Professional services, e-commerce, SaaS, healthcare admin, financial services
- **Tech Stack:** Uses modern tools (Slack, HubSpot, Google Workspace, etc.)
- **Location:** US, Canada, UK, Australia (English-speaking, similar time zones)

**Decision Maker Profile:**
- **Title:** CEO, COO, Operations Manager, IT Director
- **Age:** 30-55
- **Mindset:** Tech-curious, efficiency-focused, ROI-driven
- **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by manual processes, losing money to inefficiency, competitors automating

**Budget:**
- Minimum project: $5K
- Sweet spot: $15K-30K
- Can afford: $1K-5K/mo retainers

## Channel 1: LinkedIn Outreach

### Strategy Overview

**Goal:** 10-20 discovery calls per month
**Investment:** 5-10 hours/week + $0-500/mo (Sales Navigator)

### Profile Optimization

**Headline:**
```
Helping [Industry] Companies Automate [Process] | AI Automation Expert | [Specific Result]
Example: "Helping E-commerce Companies Automate Customer Service | AI Automation Expert | Save 40+ Hours/Week"
```

**About Section:**
```
I help [target market] solve [specific problem] through AI automation.

Most [target role] I talk to are struggling with:
❌ [Pain point 1]
❌ [Pain point 2]
❌ [Pain point 3]

We've helped companies like [Client 1], [Client 2], and [Client 3]:
✅ [Result 1]
✅ [Result 2]
✅ [Result 3]

If you're dealing with [problem], let's chat.
📧 [Email]
📅 [Calendly Link]
```

**Featured Section:**
- Case study PDF
- Link to calendar
- Testimonial video
- Relevant content

### Connection Strategy

**Daily Targets:**
- Send: 20-30 connection requests
- Accept: 10-20 (50%+ acceptance rate)
- Convert to conversation: 3-5
- Book calls: 1-2 per week

**Who to Connect With:**
1. CEOs/Founders at target companies
2. Operations Managers
3. IT Directors
4. People who engage with AI/automation content
5. Attendees of relevant events

### Connection Request Templates

**Template 1 (Personalized):**
```
Hey [Name]—saw your post about [topic] and really agreed with 
[specific point]. We help companies like [Their Company] automate 
[process]. Would love to connect and share what's working!

[Your Name]
```

**Template 2 (Common Ground):**
```
Hi [Name]—noticed we're both in [industry/group]. I help 
[industry] companies save 20+ hours/week through AI automation. 
Would love to connect and learn more about what you're building 
at [Company]!

[Your Name]
```

**Template 3 (Direct Value):**
```
Hey [Name]—I help [industry] companies automate [specific process] 
and typically save them 30-40 hours/week. Thought this might be 
relevant given your role at [Company]. Worth connecting?

[Your Name]
```

### Follow-Up Sequence

**After Connection (Day 1):**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]!

Quick question—how is your team currently handling [relevant process]? 
We've been helping companies like [similar company] automate this 
and save [specific result].

Worth a quick 15-minute chat to see if there's a fit?
```

**If No Response (Day 4):**
```
Hey [Name]—just floating this to the top of your inbox.

If [pain point] isn't a priority right now, no worries. But if 
it is, happy to show you what's possible in a quick call.

Here's my calendar: [Link]
```

**If Still No Response (Day 10):**
```
[Name]—I'll stop bugging you after this! 😄

Last try: If you ever want to explore automation for [process], 
here's a case study that might be helpful: [Link]

Either way, keep crushing it!
```

### Content Strategy (To Support Outreach)

**Post 2-3x per week:**
- Case studies with specific results
- Before/after comparisons
- AI automation tips
- Industry insights
- Behind-the-scenes of projects

**Example Post:**
```

Before:
❌ 200+ tickets/week
❌ 48-hour response time
❌ 2 full-time staff

Result: $60K/year saved + happier customers.

AI isn't replacing people—it's freeing them to do better work.

Want to see if this could work for your business? DM me.
```

## Channel 2: Content Marketing

### Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Establish authority, generate inbound leads
**Investment:** 5-10 hours/week
**Timeline:** 3-6 months to see significant results

### Content Pillars

1. **Educational:** "How to automate X"
2. **Case Studies:** "How we helped Client Y achieve Z"
3. **Industry Insights:** "The state of AI in [industry]"
4. **Thought Leadership:** "Why most AI projects fail"
5. **Behind the Scenes:** "How we build AI agents"

### Content Distribution

| Platform | Frequency | Content Type | Goal |
|----------|-----------|--------------|------|
| LinkedIn | 3x/week | Posts, articles | Lead gen, authority |
| Twitter/X | Daily | Threads, tips | Authority, network |
| Blog | 2x/month | Long-form guides | SEO, authority |
| YouTube | 2x/month | Tutorials, case studies | Authority, SEO |
| Newsletter | Weekly | Curated insights | Nurturing |

### Lead Magnet Ideas

**Create 1-2 to capture emails:**

1. **"AI Automation Readiness Quiz"**
   - 10 questions
   - Score + recommendations
   - Capture email for results

2. **"5 Processes You Should Automate Today" (PDF)**
   - Specific examples
   - ROI calculations
   - Implementation checklist

3. **"AI Automation ROI Calculator" (Spreadsheet)**
   - Input your numbers
   - See potential savings
   - Capture email to download

4. **"Case Study: How [Client] Saved $100K with AI" (PDF)**
   - Detailed breakdown
   - Specific tactics
   - Results

### SEO Strategy

**Target Keywords:**
- "AI automation for [industry]"
- "Automate [process]"
- "AI agent for [use case]"
- "Business process automation"
- "[Industry] automation solutions"

**Content Plan:**
- 10 pillar articles (2,000+ words)
- 20 supporting articles (1,000+ words)
- Internal linking strategy
- Backlink outreach

## Channel 4: Partnerships

### Strategy Overview

**Goal:** 5-10 qualified referrals/month
**Investment:** 5 hours/week relationship building

### Partner Types

**1. Complementary Service Providers:**
- Web development agencies
- Marketing agencies
- Business consultants
- CRM implementers
- IT managed services

**Value Exchange:**
- They refer automation projects to you
- You refer web/marketing work to them
- Revenue share: 10-20% of first project

**2. Technology Partners:**
- Zapier/Make partners
- CRM platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce)
- AI platforms (OpenAI, Anthropic partners)

**Value Exchange:**
- You implement their tools
- They refer clients needing automation
- Co-marketing opportunities

**3. Industry Associations:**
- Chamber of Commerce
- Industry-specific associations
- Business networking groups

**Value Exchange:**
- Speaking opportunities
- Member discounts
- Newsletter features

### Partnership Outreach Template

```
Subject: Partnership opportunity?

Hey [Name],

I've been following [Their Company] for a while—love what 
you're doing with [specific observation].

I run [Your Agency], where we help [target market] automate 
[pain point] using AI. We've delivered 50+ projects, typically 
saving clients 30+ hours/week.

I notice we serve similar clients but offer different services. 
I think there could be a great partnership opportunity:

- You refer automation projects to us (we pay 15% referral fee)
- We refer [their service] projects to you
- Co-create content/webinars for our audiences

Worth a 15-minute chat to explore?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Website]
```

## Channel 5: Referrals

### Strategy Overview

**Goal:** 30%+ of new business from referrals
**Investment:** Ongoing relationship management

### When to Ask for Referrals

**Best Times:**
- After successful project delivery
- When client gives positive feedback
- During retainer renewal
- After achieving a big win together

### Referral Request Script

**In Person/Call:**
```
"I'm so glad we could help with [result]! 

We're looking to work with a few more companies like yours 
this quarter. Do you know anyone in your network who might be 
struggling with [similar problem]?"

[If they hesitate]

"No pressure at all! Even an introduction would be huge. 
We're specifically looking for [ideal client profile]."
```

**Via Email:**
```
Subject: Quick favor?

Hey [Name],

Hope you're doing well!

We're looking to work with a few more [industry] companies 
this quarter, and I immediately thought of your network.

Do you know anyone who's struggling with [specific problem]? 
We've had great results helping companies like [similar client] 
achieve [specific result].

Even a quick intro would mean a lot!

And of course, we have a referral program—happy to send 
[10-15%] of the first project your way as a thank you.

Let me know!

[Your Name]
```

### Referral Program

**Structure:**
- 10% of first project (up to $5K)
- OR $1,000 flat fee for projects under $10K
- Paid when client pays their deposit

**Promotion:**
- Mention in proposal
- Include in onboarding packet
- Remind at project close
- Email to past clients quarterly
