## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)
**Focus:** Get operational and land first clients

**Key Files:**
1. `QUICK-START.md` - Follow the 30-day plan
2. `services/SERVICE-OFFERINGS.md` - Define what you sell
3. `pricing/PRICING-MODELS.md` - Set your prices
4. `templates/proposal-template.md` - Prepare to sell
5. `client-acquisition/outreach-templates.md` - Start outreach

**Goal:** Close 1-3 clients, $10K-50K revenue

---

### Phase 2: Delivery Excellence (Weeks 5-12)
**Focus:** Deliver amazing results and build case studies

**Key Files:**
1. `discovery/DISCOVERY-PROCESS.md` - Master discovery
2. `build-process/BUILD-PROCESS.md` - Deliver quality
3. `project-management/PM-FRAMEWORK.md` - Manage projects
4. `post-launch/SUPPORT-MODEL.md` - Support clients

**Goal:** 5-10 happy clients, testimonials, case studies

---

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 4-12)
**Focus:** Systematize and grow

**Key Files:**
1. `client-acquisition/ACQUISITION-STRATEGY.md` - Scale pipeline
2. `team-building/TEAM-STRUCTURE.md` - Build team
3. `team-building/hiring-guides.md` - Hire well
4. `financials/REVENUE-PROJECTIONS.md` - Plan growth

**Goal:** $50K-150K/month, 10-20 person team

---

### Phase 4: Market Leadership (Year 2-3)
**Focus:** Dominate your niche

**Key Files:**
1. All sections - Optimize everything
2. `financials/REVENUE-PROJECTIONS.md` - Plan exit or expansion

**Goal:** $10M-50M revenue, market leader
