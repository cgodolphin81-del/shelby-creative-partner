## 3. Finding Influencers

### Discovery Methods

**1. Snapchat Search**
- Search relevant keywords
- Browse Discover section
- Check "For You" recommendations
- Look at who your followers follow

**2. Influencer Platforms**
- **AspireIQ:** Full-service platform
- **Upfluence:** Database + management
- **Creator.co:** Discovery + campaigns
- **Klear:** Analytics + discovery
- **Heepsy:** Search + verification

**3. Manual Research**
- Instagram/TikTok creators (cross-platform)
- YouTube creators with Snapchat presence
- Google searches ("top Snapchat influencers [niche]")
- Industry reports and lists

**4. Competitor Analysis**
- Who are competitors working with?
- What campaigns performed well?
- Gap analysis (who aren't they working with?)

**5. Hashtag Research**
- Search relevant hashtags
- Look at top posts
- Identify consistent creators

### Vetting Criteria

**Audience Quality:**
- Follower count (authentic growth)
- Engagement rate (3%+ minimum)
- Comment quality (real vs. bot)
- Audience demographics match target

**Content Quality:**
- Visual quality
- Consistency
- Brand alignment
- Authenticity

**Professionalism:**
- Response time
- Past brand work
- References available
- Contract compliance history

**Red Flags:**
❌ Sudden follower spikes
❌ Low engagement relative to followers
❌ Generic/bot-like comments
❌ Inconsistent posting
❌ Controversial content
❌ Poor brand fit
❌ Unprofessional communication

## 4. Outreach & Negotiation

### Initial Outreach

**Email Template:**
```
Subject: Partnership Opportunity with [Brand Name]

Hey [Influencer Name],

I'm [Your Name] from [Brand Name]. I've been following 
your Snapchat for a while and love your content, especially 
[specific example].

We're launching [campaign/product] and think you'd be a 
perfect fit. Our audience overlaps with yours ([demographic]), 
and we think your followers would genuinely love [product].

Would you be open to discussing a partnership? We're thinking:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- Timeline: [dates]
- Budget: [range or "competitive compensation"]

Let me know if you're interested! Happy to hop on a call.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Contact Info]
```

**DM Approach (for smaller influencers):**
```
Hey [Name]! Love your content 🔥 We're [Brand] and think 
you'd be perfect for a collab. Interested in chatting about 
a partnership? DM us or email [address]!
```

### Negotiation Points

**Compensation Structure:**
- Flat fee per post/story
- Package deal (multiple posts)
- Performance bonuses (CPA, ROAS)
- Product + payment
- Affiliate commission
- Long-term ambassador rate

**Deliverables:**
- Number of Snaps
- Story sequences
- Spotlight videos
- Lens usage
- Cross-platform posts
- Usage rights
- Exclusivity period

**Timeline:**
- Posting dates
- Content approval deadlines
- Campaign duration
- Exclusivity window

### Rate Negotiation Tips

**Do:**
- Research their typical rates
- Start below your max budget
- Offer long-term deals (discount)
- Include performance bonuses
- Provide creative freedom
- Be clear about expectations

**Don't:**
- Lowball significantly
- Demand excessive revisions
- Micromanage content
- Expect free work for "exposure"
- Rush the negotiation

### Contract Essentials

**Must Include:**
1. Deliverables (exact specifications)
2. Timeline (posting dates, deadlines)
3. Compensation (amount, payment terms)
4. Usage rights (where, how long)
5. Exclusivity (category, duration)
6. Approval process (if any)
7. Disclosure requirements (FTC compliance)
8. Termination clause
9. Force majeure
10. Signatures

## 5. Campaign Types

### Sponsored Snaps

**Format:** 1-10 Snaps in story sequence

**Best For:**
- Product launches
- Announcements
- Time-sensitive promotions
- Quick awareness

**Pricing:** $500-10,000+ depending on influencer tier

**Example Brief:**
```
Campaign: Summer Collection Launch
Deliverables: 5-Snap story sequence
Key Messages:
- New collection available now
- 20% off first order
- Free shipping
Timeline: Post between June 1-5
CTA: Swipe up to shop
Hashtag: #BrandSummer
```

### Takeovers

**Format:** Influencer controls brand account for day

**Best For:**
- Behind-the-scenes access
- Event coverage
- Fresh perspective
- Audience cross-pollination

**Pricing:** $2,000-20,000/day

**Guidelines:**
- Provide content guardrails
- Pre-approve sensitive content
- Have backup content ready
- Promote takeover in advance
- Monitor in real-time

### Lens Campaigns

**Format:** Influencer promotes branded AR Lens

**Best For:**
- Viral campaigns
- User-generated content
- Brand engagement
- Product try-on

**Pricing:** $3,000-15,000 + Lens production

**Strategy:**
1. Create custom branded Lens
2. Seed to 10-50 influencers
3. Each creates content using Lens
4. Encourage followers to use/share
5. Amplify top UGC

### Challenge Campaigns

**Format:** Influencer starts challenge, followers participate

**Best For:**
- Viral potential
- UGC generation
- Brand awareness
- Community building

**Pricing:** $5,000-50,000+ (depending on scale)

**Example:**
```
#BrandDanceChallenge
- Create simple dance routine
- Influencer posts first
- Followers duet/remix
- Best entries featured
- Prize for winners
```

### Ambassador Programs

**Format:** Long-term partnership (3-12 months)

**Best For:**
- Consistent presence
- Authentic advocacy
- Multiple campaigns
- Brand alignment

**Pricing:** $5,000-50,000/month (depending on tier)

**Structure:**
- X posts per month
- Exclusive discount code
- First access to products
- Input on product development
- Event appearances
- Content usage rights

## 6. Campaign Management

### Pre-Campaign

**1. Brief Development**
```
Campaign Brief Template:
- Campaign objective
- Target audience
- Key messages (3 max)
- Deliverables (exact specs)
- Timeline/dates
- Budget
- Usage rights needed
- Disclosure requirements
- Brand guidelines
- Do's and Don'ts
- Contact information
```

**2. Content Approval Process**
- Decide: Pre-approval required or guidelines only?
- If approval: Set timeline (48-72 hours)
- Provide clear feedback
- Limit revision rounds (2 max)

**3. Tracking Setup**
- Unique discount codes
- UTM parameters for links
- Dedicated landing pages
- Pixel events for conversions
- Hashtag tracking

### During Campaign

**Monitoring:**
- Verify posts go live on time
- Check content quality
- Monitor comments/sentiment
- Track initial performance
- Be available for questions

**Support:**
- Provide assets promptly
- Answer questions quickly
- Share performance updates
- Amplify on brand channels

### Post-Campaign

**Reporting:**
- Collect screenshots/analytics
- Calculate ROI
- Document learnings
- Share results with influencer

**Relationship Maintenance:**
- Thank influencer
- Share performance highlights
- Discuss future opportunities
- Request testimonial (if successful)
- Add to preferred partner list

## 7. Pricing Guide

### Rate Cards by Tier

**Nano Influencers (1K-10K):**
- Single Snap: $50-200
- Story (5 Snaps): $200-500
- Takeover: $500-1,000
- Monthly ambassador: $1,000-3,000

**Micro Influencers (10K-100K):**
- Single Snap: $200-1,000
- Story (5 Snaps): $500-2,500
- Takeover: $1,000-5,000
- Monthly ambassador: $3,000-8,000

**Mid-Tier (100K-500K):**
- Single Snap: $1,000-5,000
- Story (5 Snaps): $2,500-10,000
- Takeover: $5,000-15,000
- Monthly ambassador: $8,000-20,000

**Macro (500K-1M):**
- Single Snap: $5,000-15,000
- Story (5 Snaps): $10,000-25,000
- Takeover: $15,000-40,000
- Monthly ambassador: $20,000-50,000

**Snap Stars (1M+):**
- Single Snap: $15,000-50,000+
- Story (5 Snaps): $25,000-100,000+
- Takeover: $40,000-150,000+
- Monthly ambassador: $50,000-200,000+

### Pricing Factors

**Increase Rates:**
- High engagement rate (5%+)
- Niche audience (hard to reach)
- Exclusive rights required
- Rush timeline
- Usage rights (brand ads)
- Long-term commitment
- Cross-platform requirements

**Decrease Rates:**
- Long-term partnership
- Multiple deliverables
- Creative freedom
- Product-only compensation (for some)
- Performance-based component

### Payment Terms

**Standard:**
- 50% upfront, 50% on completion
- Net 30 payment terms
- Invoice required
- W-9 for US creators

**Alternatives:**
- 100% upfront (for small amounts)
- Net 15 (preferred by creators)
- Monthly retainer (ambassadors)
- Performance bonuses (post-campaign)

## 8. Measuring Success

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Reach** | Unique viewers | Varies by tier |
| **Impressions** | Total views | 1.5-3x reach |
| **Engagement Rate** | (Reactions + Replies + Shares) / Reach × 100 | 3-8% |
| **Completion Rate** | % who watch full story | 40-70% |
| **Swipe-Up Rate** | Swipe-ups / Impressions × 100 | 1-5% |
| **Conversion Rate** | Conversions / Clicks × 100 | 2-10% |
| **CPA** | Total spend / Conversions | Varies by industry |
| **ROAS** | Revenue / Influencer spend | 3:1+ |

### Tracking Methods

**1. Discount Codes**
- Unique code per influencer
- Track in e-commerce platform
- Attribute sales directly

**2. UTM Parameters**
```
Example: 
?utm_source=snapchat&utm_medium=influencer&utm_campaign=summer2024&utm_content=influencername
```

**3. Dedicated Landing Pages**
- influencer.brand.com
- Track all traffic/conversions
- Custom experience

**4. Pixel Events**
- Track conversions from influencer traffic
- Attribute to specific posts
- Build retargeting audiences

**5. Surveys**
- Post-purchase: "How did you hear about us?"
- Brand lift: Awareness before/after

## 9. FTC Compliance

### Disclosure Requirements

**Required When:**
- Paid partnership
- Free product received
- Affiliate relationship
- Any material connection

**Acceptable Disclosures:**
- "#ad" or "#sponsored" in Snap
- "Paid partnership with [Brand]" label
- Clear verbal disclosure in video
- Text overlay: "Thanks to [Brand]"

**Not Acceptable:**
- Hidden in hashtag string
- Vague terms like "#collab"
- Only in bio (not in Snap itself)
- Buried in caption

### Best Practices

- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous
- Place at beginning of content
- Use platform's branded content tool when available
- Provide guidelines to influencers
- Review before posting
- Keep records of disclosures

## 10. Scaling Influencer Programs

### Phase 1: Testing (Month 1-2)
- 5-10 nano/micro influencers
- Single campaign type
- Manual management
- Budget: $5,000-20,000

### Phase 2: Optimization (Month 3-6)
- 10-30 influencers (mix of tiers)
- Multiple campaign types
- Spreadsheet tracking
- Budget: $20,000-75,000

### Phase 3: Scaling (Month 6-12)
- 30-100+ influencers
- Ambassador program
- Influencer platform
- Dedicated manager
- Budget: $75,000-250,000

### Phase 4: Enterprise (Year 2+)
- 100-500+ influencers
- Multi-market campaigns
- Full influencer team
- Advanced analytics
- Budget: $250,000-1M+

## 11. Common Mistakes

### Strategy Mistakes
❌ Only chasing follower count
❌ No clear campaign objectives
❌ One-off campaigns (no relationship building)
❌ Ignoring micro-influencers
❌ No tracking/attribution

### Execution Mistakes
❌ Overly restrictive creative control
❌ Poor communication
❌ Late payments
❌ Unclear briefs
❌ No usage rights negotiation

### Measurement Mistakes
❌ Only tracking vanity metrics
❌ No baseline comparison
❌ Ignoring long-term value
❌ Not calculating true ROI
❌ No post-campaign analysis
