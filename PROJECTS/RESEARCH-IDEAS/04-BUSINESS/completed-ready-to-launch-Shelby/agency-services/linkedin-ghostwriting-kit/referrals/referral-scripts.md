# REFERRAL SCRIPTS

---

## HOW TO USE THESE SCRIPTS

**When to ask for referrals:**
- After a major win (viral post, big deal closed)
- At 90-day milestone (happy client, seeing results)
- When they compliment your work ("This is amazing!")
- When they mention knowing someone who needs help
- At the end of a successful engagement

**Best practices:**
- Make it specific (who you're looking for)
- Make it easy (provide a forwardable email)
- Offer reciprocity (you'll refer business their way too)
- Never make it awkward (give them an easy out)
- Incentivize if appropriate (discount, gift, etc.)

**The golden rule:** Ask for referrals when your client is happiest with you.

## INCENTIVE IDEAS FOR REFERRALS

**Monetary:**
- $500-1,000 cash per signed client
- 10-20% of first month's fee
- $500 credit toward their next month

**Service-Based:**
- Free month of service per referral
- Bonus posts (5 extra posts per referral)
- Free add-on service (profile overhaul, video script, etc.)

**Gifts:**
- Nice bottle of wine/whiskey
- Amazon/restaurant gift card ($100-200)
- Personalized gift based on their interests

**Reciprocity:**
- Active referrals their way
- Public shoutouts on your platform
- Featured spot in your newsletter

**Charitable:**
- Donation to charity of their choice ($200-500)
- Sponsor something in their name

## WHAT TO DO WHEN YOU GET A REFERRAL

1. **Thank the referrer immediately** (within 24 hours)
2. **Update them on progress** ("Just had a great call with Jane!")
3. **Deliver incentive promptly** (when deal closes, or as promised)
4. **Send a handwritten thank-you note** (goes a long way)
5. **Keep them in the loop** ("Jane signed! You're the best.")
6. **Ask again later** (happy referrers will refer multiple times)

---

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

❌ **Don't:** Ask too early (before they've seen results)
❌ **Don't:** Make it awkward or guilt-trippy
❌ **Don't:** Promise incentives you can't deliver
❌ **Don't:** Spam their network without permission
❌ **Don't:** Forget to thank them (or deliver the incentive)
❌ **Don't:** Ask repeatedly if they say no

✅ **Do:** Make it easy and specific
✅ **Do:** Time it right (when they're happiest)
✅ **Do:** Follow through on promises
✅ **Do:** Respect their network and relationships
✅ **Do:** Show genuine gratitude
✅ **Do:** Keep it low-pressure

---

## THE MINDSET SHIFT

**Don't think:** "I'm asking for a favor."
**Do think:** "I'm offering an opportunity to help someone they care about."

Your clients WANT to help you if:
- They believe in what you do
- You make it easy
- You respect their relationships
- You deliver amazing results

Referrals aren't begging. They're the natural result of doing great work and asking at the right time.
