# Team Structure

## Overview

This document outlines the organizational structure, roles, responsibilities, and hiring roadmap for building a full-service Twitch marketing agency. We cover team composition at each growth stage and how roles evolve as the agency scales.

---

## Stage 1: Solo Founder (Months 1-6)

### Structure

```
Founder/CEO
├── Talent Management
├── Sponsorship Sales
├── Operations
└── Analytics & Optimization
```

### Founder Responsibilities

**Time Allocation**:
- 40%: Streamer management (current clients)
- 30%: Sponsorship prospecting and sales
- 15%: Operations and administration
- 10%: Analytics and optimization
- 5%: Business development

### Key Activities

**Daily**:
- Client communication and check-ins
- Sponsorship outreach (10-15 emails)
- Campaign monitoring and support
- Content calendar review

**Weekly**:
- Strategy calls with streamers
- Sponsorship meetings (2-4)
- Performance reporting
- Business development

**Monthly**:
- Financial review
- Client retention check-ins
- Marketing and content creation
- Goal setting and review

### Revenue Capacity

**Solo Founder Can Manage**:
- 3-5 streamer clients (Growth Package)
- 2-3 brand clients (Basic Retainer)
- $50K-100K monthly revenue

**Limitations**:
- Time constraints on outreach
- Limited capacity for complex campaigns
- No backup for client emergencies
- Scalability challenges

## Stage 2: Early Team (Months 6-18)

### Structure

```
Founder/CEO
├── Talent Manager (Hire #1)
├── Sponsorship Coordinator (Hire #2)
└── Operations (Founder still handles)
```

### Role 1: Talent Manager

**When to Hire**: 3+ active streamer clients, 20+ hours/week client work

**Responsibilities**:
- Day-to-day streamer communication
- Schedule and content oversight
- Community management guidance
- Basic crisis response
- Performance tracking
- Client onboarding

**Required Skills**:
- Strong communication
- Twitch platform knowledge
- Community management experience
- Problem-solving abilities
- Project management

**Compensation**:
- Base: $45,000-60,000/year
- Bonus: 10-15% of client retention
- Total: $55,000-75,000/year

**Time Allocation**:
- 50%: Client management
- 20%: Campaign coordination
- 15%: Reporting and analytics
- 15%: Internal tasks

---

### Role 2: Sponsorship Coordinator

**When to Hire**: 50+ prospecting emails/week, 3+ active campaigns

**Responsibilities**:
- Initial brand outreach
- Meeting scheduling
- Basic proposal creation
- Contract administration
- Campaign logistics
- Reporting compilation

**Required Skills**:
- Sales communication
- CRM proficiency
- Basic negotiation
- Organization
- Writing ability

**Compensation**:
- Base: $40,000-55,000/year
- Commission: 5% of closed deals
- Total: $50,000-75,000/year

**Time Allocation**:
- 60%: Prospecting and outreach
- 20%: Meeting coordination
- 10%: Proposal creation
- 10%: Reporting

---

### Founder Focus (With Team)

**Time Allocation**:
- 30%: Strategic sponsorships (enterprise deals)
- 25%: Business development
- 20%: Team management
- 15%: Key client relationships

### Revenue Capacity

**With 2 Hires**:

## Hiring Roadmap

### Phase 1: Solo (Months 1-6)

**Goal**: Prove model, build client base

**Hiring**: None
**Focus**: Founder does everything
**Revenue Target**: $50K-100K/month

---

### Phase 2: Foundation (Months 6-12)

**Goal**: Systematize, scale client base

**Hiring**:
- Month 6: Talent Manager
- Month 9: Sponsorship Coordinator

**Focus**: Delegate operational work
**Revenue Target**: $150K-250K/month

---

### Phase 3: Growth (Months 12-24)

**Goal**: Build leadership team, expand services

**Hiring**:
- Month 12: Operations Manager
- Month 15: Creative Director
- Month 18: VP of Talent
- Month 21: VP of Partnerships
- Month 24: Campaign Managers (2-3)

**Focus**: Build management layer
**Revenue Target**: $400K-700K/month

---

### Phase 4: Scale (Months 24-36)

**Goal**: Optimize operations, enter new markets

**Hiring**:
- Month 27: Analytics Director
- Month 30: Video Editors (3-5)
- Month 33: Graphic Designers (2-3)
- Month 36: COO

**Focus**: Professionalize and optimize
**Revenue Target**: $1M+/month

---

### Phase 5: Maturity (Months 36+)

**Goal**: Market leadership, diversification

**Hiring**:
- Business Development
- Additional specialized roles
- Regional expansion teams

**Focus**: Innovation and market expansion
**Revenue Target**: $2M-5M+/month

## Compensation Philosophy

### Base Salary Benchmarks

| Role | Year 1-2 | Year 3-5 | Year 5+ |
|------|----------|----------|---------|
| Talent Manager | $45-60K | $55-75K | $65-90K |
| Account Executive | $40-55K | $50-70K | $60-85K |
| Operations Manager | $55-70K | $65-85K | $75-100K |
| Creative Director | $65-85K | $80-110K | $95-130K |
| VP Level | $90-120K | $120-160K | $150-200K+ |
| C-Level | $140-180K | $180-250K | $250-400K+ |

### Bonus Structure

**Individual Bonuses**:
- Talent Managers: 10-15% based on client retention
- Account Executives: 20-30% based on quota
- Operations: 10-15% based on efficiency metrics
- Creative: 10-15% based on quality and volume

**Company Bonuses**:
- All employees: 5-15% based on company performance
- Paid out annually
- Profit-sharing component

### Equity/Ownership

**Tier 1 (Founders)**: 70-90% total
**Tier 2 (VP/C-Level)**: 2-8% each
**Tier 3 (Senior Management)**: 0.5-2% each
**Tier 4 (Key Individual Contributors)**: 0.1-0.5% each

## Remote vs. In-Person

### Recommended Approach

**Year 1-2**: Hybrid (2-3 days in office)
- Build culture
- Facilitate learning
- Enable collaboration

**Year 3+**: Fully remote with quarterly meetups
- Scale globally
- Reduce overhead
- Maintain connection through events

### Remote Work Best Practices

**Communication**:
- Daily standups (async or live)
- Weekly all-hands meetings
- Bi-weekly 1:1s with managers
- Monthly town halls

**Tools**:
- Slack for communication
- Notion for documentation
- Asana/Trello for project management
- Zoom for meetings
- Google Workspace for collaboration

## Performance Management

### Key Performance Indicators

**Talent Managers**:
- Client retention rate (target: 85%+)
- Client satisfaction score (target: 8.5/10+)
- Revenue per client growth (target: 15% YoY)
- Response time (target: <4 hours)

**Account Executives**:
- Quota attainment (target: 100%+)
- Pipeline velocity
- Conversion rates
- Average deal size

**Operations**:
- Campaign on-time delivery (target: 95%+)
- Quality score (target: 9/10+)
- Process efficiency
- Client issue resolution time

**Creative Team**:
- Output volume
- Quality score
- Client satisfaction
- Turnaround time

## Culture & Values

### Core Values

1. **Authenticity**: Be genuine in all interactions
2. **Excellence**: Deliver high-quality work consistently
3. **Integrity**: Do the right thing, even when no one is watching
4. **Growth**: Continuous learning and improvement
5. **Community**: Support each other and our clients

### Hiring Principles

**Cultural Fit > Technical Skills**:
- Train skills, can't train character
- Look for growth mindset
- Values alignment is critical
- Team chemistry matters

**Diversity & Inclusion**:
- Actively seek diverse candidates
- Inclusive hiring practices
- Pay equity audits
- Supportive environment for all

## Summary: Team Building Checklist

### Year 1
- [ ] Founder handles all roles
- [ ] Document all processes
- [ ] Build client base to 5-10
- [ ] Establish revenue to $50K+/month

### Year 2
- [ ] Hire Talent Manager
- [ ] Hire Sponsorship Coordinator
- [ ] Delegate operational work
- [ ] Establish revenue to $200K+/month

### Year 3
- [ ] Hire VP-level leadership
- [ ] Build management layer
- [ ] Expand creative team
- [ ] Establish revenue to $500K+/month

### Year 4+
- [ ] Professionalize operations
- [ ] Expand to new markets
- [ ] Build specialized teams
- [ ] Establish revenue to $1M+/month

**Key Principle**: Hire for attitude and train for skill. Build a team that aligns with your values and can grow with the company. Don't scale headcount faster than revenue can support.
