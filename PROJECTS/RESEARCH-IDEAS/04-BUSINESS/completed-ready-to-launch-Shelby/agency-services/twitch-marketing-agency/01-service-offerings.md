# Service Offerings

## Core Service Categories

Our agency provides comprehensive services across five primary domains. Each service can be offered à la carte or bundled into tiered packages.

---

## 1. Streamer Management

### Full-Service Talent Management
**Description**: End-to-end career management for streamers looking to go full-time or scale their existing presence.

**Includes**:
- Career strategy and goal setting
- Contract negotiation and legal review
- Brand partnership vetting and approval
- Schedule optimization and workload management
- Crisis management and reputation protection
- Financial planning guidance (referrals to specialists)
- Mental health and burnout prevention support

**Deliverables**:
- Quarterly career roadmap
- Monthly performance reviews
- Contract review within 48 hours
- 24/7 emergency support for urgent matters

**Ideal Client**: Streamers with 200+ average viewers ready to professionalize

**Time Commitment**: 15-20 hours/month per client

---

### Business Operations Management
**Description**: Handle the business side so streamers can focus on content creation.

**Includes**:
- LLC/corporation setup assistance
- Tax documentation organization
- Expense tracking and bookkeeping coordination
- Invoice management for sponsorships
- Payment processing setup (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
- Insurance recommendations (liability, equipment, health)

**Deliverables**:
- Monthly financial summary
- Quarterly tax preparation package
- Organized digital filing system

**Ideal Client**: Any streamer earning $3K+/month from streaming

**Time Commitment**: 8-12 hours/month per client

---

## 2. Sponsorship Management

### Brand Partnership Brokerage
**Description**: Secure and manage sponsorship deals between streamers and brands.

**Includes**:
- Brand prospecting and outreach
- Media kit creation and maintenance
- Rate card development and negotiation
- Contract negotiation (exclusivity, deliverables, usage rights)
- Campaign execution oversight
- Performance reporting to brands
- Payment collection and distribution

**Deliverables**:
- Minimum 2 qualified brand leads/month
- Negotiated contracts with favorable terms
- Post-campaign performance reports
- Timely payment processing (net-15 or better)

**Commission Structure**: 20-30% of sponsorship value

**Ideal Client**: Streamers with 100+ average viewers and professional presentation

---

### Sponsorship Activation Management
**Description**: Ensure sponsored content delivers maximum value for both streamer and brand.

**Includes**:
- Creative brief development with brand
- Content integration planning (natural, not forced)
- Script review and approval coordination
- Live stream coordination and monitoring
- Social media amplification planning
- Usage rights management for brand assets
- ROI measurement and reporting

**Deliverables**:
- Campaign playbook for each activation
- Real-time support during sponsored streams
- Comprehensive post-campaign report (7 days post-completion)
- Case study development (with permission)

**Fee Structure**: 15% of sponsorship value OR flat fee ($500-2K per campaign)

## 3. Channel Growth

### Analytics & Optimization
**Description**: Data-driven channel optimization using Twitch analytics and third-party tools.

**Includes**:
- Comprehensive channel audit (baseline metrics)
- Viewer retention analysis
- Peak performance time identification
- Category and tag optimization
- Title and thumbnail A/B testing
- Follower conversion optimization
- Competitor benchmarking

**Deliverables**:
- Initial audit report (20+ pages)
- Monthly analytics dashboard
- Bi-weekly optimization recommendations
- Quarterly strategy adjustment meetings

**Tools Used**: Twitch Dashboard, StreamsCharts, SullyGnome, SocialBlade

**Ideal Client**: Streamers serious about growth with 50+ average viewers

**Time Commitment**: 6-8 hours/month per client

**Deliverables**:

- Growth campaign reports
