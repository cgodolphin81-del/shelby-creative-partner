## 1. Brand Sponsorships

### 1.1 Partnership Types

**Sponsored Posts:**
- Single post with brand message
- Most common and straightforward
- Rate: $100-$100,000+ per post

**Campaign Partnerships:**
- Multi-post campaign (3-10 posts)
- Cross-platform activation
- Rate: $5,000-$500,000+ per campaign

**Brand Ambassador:**
- Long-term exclusive partnership (3-12 months)
- Regular content featuring brand
- Rate: $10,000-$1,000,000+ per year

**Product Launch:**
- Exclusive reveal/promotion
- High engagement events
- Rate: $5,000-$100,000+ per launch

**Affiliate Partnerships:**
- Commission-based (5-30%)
- Performance incentives
- Ongoing passive income

**Rate Calculation Formula:**
```
Base Rate = (Followers × Engagement Rate × $0.01-0.10)

Example:
100K followers × 4% engagement × $0.05 = $200 base
Adjust for: platform, content type, exclusivity, usage rights

Final Rate = Base × Multipliers
- Instagram: 1.5-2.0x
- TikTok: 1.0-1.5x
- Reels/Video: 1.5-2.0x
- Exclusive: 1.5-3.0x
- Usage Rights: 1.5-5.0x
```

## 2. Subscription Revenue

### 2.1 Platform Options

**OnlyFans:**
- Commission: 20%
- Best for: Premium/exclusive content
- Avg creator earnings: $150-$500/mo
- Top creators: $10,000-$100,000+/mo

**Patreon:**
- Commission: 5-12%
- Best for: Tiered membership, community
- Avg creator earnings: $500-$5,000/mo
- Top creators: $50,000-$500,000+/mo

**Fanhouse:**
- Commission: 20%
- Best for: Casual fan interaction
- Growing platform for influencers

**Discord (with Nitro):**
- Commission: 0% (plus payment processing)
- Best for: Community building
- Requires active management

**Custom Membership Site:**
- Commission: 2.9% + $0.30 (Stripe)
- Best for: Full control, branding
- Requires technical setup

---

### 2.4 Growth & Retention

**Acquisition Tactics:**
1. Tease exclusive content on free platforms
2. Limited-time launch discounts
3. Free trial periods (7 days)
4. Bundle deals (3 months for price of 2)
5. Referral bonuses

**Retention Tactics:**
1. Consistent posting schedule
2. Regular engagement with subscribers
3. Loyalty rewards (badges, recognition)
4. Exclusive subscriber events
5. Regular content quality audits
6. Win-back campaigns for churned subs

**Churn Reduction:**
- Target: <15% monthly churn
- Identify at-risk subscribers (decreased engagement)
- Send personalized check-in messages
- Offer retention discounts
- Survey churned subscribers for feedback

### 3.2 Fulfillment Options

**Print-on-Demand (POD):**

| Service | Products | Quality | Margin |
|---------|----------|---------|--------|
| **Printful** | Apparel, home | High | 30-50% |
| **Printify** | Wide variety | Good | 40-60% |
| **Gooten** | Diverse catalog | Good | 35-55% |
| **Teespring** | Simple, integrated | Medium | 20-40% |

**Pros:**
- No inventory risk
- Automatic fulfillment
- Global shipping
- Easy integration

**Cons:**
- Lower margins
- Less quality control
- Longer shipping times
- Limited customization

**Self-Fulfillment:**

**Pros:**
- Higher margins
- Quality control
- Custom packaging
- Faster shipping

**Cons:**
- Inventory investment
- Storage costs
- Fulfillment labor
- Shipping logistics

**Hybrid Approach:**
- POD for test products
- Self-fulfill for bestsellers
- Bulk order popular items
- Maintain quality standards

---

### 3.3 Merch Strategy

**Launch Strategy:**
1. **Tease** (2 weeks before): Sneak peeks, countdown
2. **Pre-order** (1 week): Early bird discount
3. **Launch**: Full collection release
4. **Sustain**: Ongoing promotion, restocks

**Pricing Formula:**
```
Retail Price = (Product Cost + Shipping + Platform Fee) × 2-3

Example T-Shirt:
- Product cost: $12
- Shipping: $5
- Platform fee: $3
- Total cost: $20
- Retail price: $40-60
- Profit per unit: $20-40
```

**Marketing Tactics:**
- Feature merch in content
- Limited edition drops
- Bundle deals
- Fan discount codes
- Giveaways and contests
- Influencer unboxings

## 4. Affiliate Marketing

### 4.1 Program Types

**Amazon Associates:**
- Commission: 1-10% (varies by category)
- Best for: General product recommendations
- Cookie duration: 24 hours

**ShareASale/CJ Affiliate:**
- Thousands of brands
- Commission: 5-30%
- Reliable tracking

**Brand Direct Programs:**
- Higher commissions (15-40%)
- Custom deals possible
- Direct relationship

**SaaS/Apps:**
- Recurring commissions (20-40% monthly)
- High lifetime value
- Examples: Notion, Canva, hosting

---

### 4.2 Affiliate Strategy

**Best Practices:**
1. Only promote products you genuinely recommend
2. Disclose affiliate relationships clearly
3. Create valuable content around products
4. Use trackable links
5. Monitor performance and optimize

**Content Integration:**
- "Get Ready With Me" featuring products
- "My Favorites" roundups
- Tutorial/how-to content
- Review and comparison posts
- Gift guides

**Disclosure Requirements:**
```
Clear and conspicuous disclosure required:
- "Affiliate link" or "Ad"
- #ad or #affiliate in social posts
- Disclosure before affiliate links
- Follow FTC guidelines
```

## 7. Revenue Optimization

### 7.1 Pricing Psychology

**Effective Tactics:**
- Charm pricing ($4.99 vs $5.00)
- Tiered pricing (good/better/best)
- Anchoring (show high price first)
- Scarcity (limited time/quantity)
- Social proof ("1,000+ fans joined")
- Bundle discounts (save 20%)

### 7.2 A/B Testing

**Test Variables:**
- Price points
- Tier names and benefits
- Content types
- Posting times
- Call-to-action language
- Offer framing

**Testing Framework:**
1. Hypothesis
2. Test design
3. Run test (2-4 weeks)
4. Analyze results
5. Implement winner
6. Iterate
