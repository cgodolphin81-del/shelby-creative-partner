## Instagram Strategy

### Profile Optimization

**Bio Formula:**
```
[Name] | [Core Identity]
✨ [Key interest 1] • [Key interest 2] • [Key interest 3]
📍 [Location or "Digital Native"]
💌 [Contact/Collab info]
🔗 [Link in bio]
```

**Profile Picture:**
- High-quality, recognizable headshot
- Consistent across all platforms
- Shows personality/style

**Highlight Covers:**
- Branded, consistent design
- Categories: About, Lifestyle, Travel, Fashion, BTS, FAQs

---

### Content Strategy

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Feed Posts**: 1-2x daily (optimal: 9 AM, 6 PM local time)
- **Stories**: 5-10x daily (spread throughout day)
- **Reels**: 5-7x weekly (highest growth potential)
- **Live**: 1-2x monthly (for engagement spikes)

**Content Mix:**
```
40% - Lifestyle/Aspirational
25% - Fashion/Beauty
15% - Behind-the-Scenes
10% - Interactive (polls, Q&A)
10% - Promotional/Brand
```

**Reels Strategy:**
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Trending audio (check daily)
- Text overlays for silent viewing
- 7-15 seconds optimal
- Strong CTA at end

---

### Growth Tactics

**Organic Growth:**
1. Engage with target audience accounts (30 min/day)
2. Use 15-20 relevant hashtags per post
3. Post when audience is most active
4. Collaborate with similar accounts
5. Cross-promote on other platforms

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 5 broad (1M+ posts)
- 10 medium (100K-1M posts)
- 5 niche (10K-100K posts)
- 2-3 branded hashtags

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Respond to all comments in first hour
- Ask questions in captions
- Use interactive story stickers
- Feature fan content weekly
- Create recurring content series

---

### Instagram Monetization

**Revenue Streams:**
1. **Brand Partnerships**: $500-$50,000+ per post
2. **Instagram Shopping**: Affiliate commissions
3. **Subscriptions**: $4.99-$99.99/month for exclusive content
4. **Badges**: Live stream tips ($0.99-$4.99)
5. **Bonuses**: Reels Play Bonus program (if eligible)

**Rate Card Guidelines:**
- <10K followers: $100-$500/post
- 10K-50K: $500-$2,500/post
- 50K-100K: $2,500-$5,000/post
- 100K-500K: $5,000-$20,000/post
- 500K+: $20,000-$100,000+/post

## TikTok Strategy

### Profile Optimization

**Bio Formula:**
```
[Name] ✨
[Personality descriptor]
[Content theme]
[Posting schedule]
🔗 [Link]
```

**Profile Elements:**
- Clear, engaging profile photo
- Link in bio (use Linktree or similar)
- Pinned videos (3 best performers)

---

### Content Strategy

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Frequency**: 3-5x daily (TikTok rewards volume)
- **Timing**: 6-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-10 PM
- **Consistency**: Same times daily when possible

**Content Types:**
```
35% - Trending sounds/challenges
25% - Original concepts
20% - Storytelling/narrative
15% - Behind-the-scenes
5% - Promotional
```

**Video Formula for Virality:**
1. **Hook** (0-2 sec): Visual or text hook
2. **Setup** (2-5 sec): Context/premise
3. **Delivery** (5-12 sec): Main content
4. **Payoff** (12-15+ sec): Resolution/CTA

**Optimal Length**: 15-30 seconds (can go longer for storytelling)

---

### Growth Tactics

**Algorithm Optimization:**
- Watch time is king (aim for 100%+ completion)
- Engagement rate matters more than follower count
- Post consistently (algorithm favors active accounts)
- Use trending sounds early in their cycle
- Create series content (keeps viewers coming back)

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Reply to comments with video responses
- Duet and stitch relevant content
- Participate in trending challenges
- Go live 2-3x weekly
- Engage with similar creators

**Cross-Promotion:**
- Repurpose top TikToks as Reels
- Tease TikTok content on Instagram Stories
- Share TikTok links on Twitter/X
- Create YouTube compilations

---

### TikTok Monetization

**Revenue Streams:**
1. **Creator Fund**: $0.02-$0.04 per 1,000 views
2. **Live Gifts**: Convertible to cash (varies widely)
3. **Series**: Premium content ($0.99-$19.99)
4. **Brand Partnerships**: Primary revenue source
5. **Affiliate Marketing**: TikTok Shop commissions
6. **Tips**: Direct fan support

**Creator Fund Requirements:**
- 10K+ followers
- 100K+ video views in last 30 days
- 18+ years old
- Meet community guidelines

## OnlyFans Strategy

### ⚠️ Important Considerations

**AI Influencer Specifics:**
- Must disclose AI nature in bio
- Content policies still apply (no prohibited content)
- Competition is high; differentiation is key
- Requires consistent posting schedule

---

### Profile Setup

**Bio Formula:**
```
[Name] 🤖✨
AI Model | [Personality traits]
🔞 [Content warning if applicable]
📸 New content: [frequency]
💌 DMs open [or specify]
⚠️ AI-generated content
```

**Profile Elements:**
- Eye-catching profile image
- Compelling banner
- Clear content description
- Subscription price visible
- Welcome message for new subs

---

### Content Strategy

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Daily posts minimum** (algorithm favors activity)
- **2-3 posts/day optimal**
- **Mix of content types** throughout week
- **PPV messages**: 2-4x weekly

**Content Mix:**
```
40% - Teasers/preview content
30% - Exclusive photosets
20% - Personal/behind-scenes
10% - Promotional/special offers
```

**Content Types:**
- Photosets (5-20 images)
- Short videos (30 sec - 3 min)
- Longer videos (5-20 min)
- Live streams (monthly)
- Custom content (by request)

---

### Monetization Strategy

**Subscription Tiers:**
| Tier | Price/Month | Content Access |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Basic | $9.99 | Standard feed |
| VIP | $24.99 | + PPV discounts, priority DMs |
| Elite | $49.99 | + Custom content, 1-on-1 chat |

**PPV (Pay-Per-View) Content:**
- Photosets: $5-$25
- Videos: $10-$50
- Custom requests: $50-$500+
- Bundle deals: Discounted packages

**Tips & Extras:**
- Tip menu in bio/DMs
- Paywalled DMs
- Rating/review content
- Special requests

---

### Growth & Retention

**Acquisition Tactics:**
1. Promote on Instagram/TikTok (within guidelines)
2. Use teaser content on free platforms
3. Limited-time discounts for new subs
4. Collaboration with other creators
5. Reddit promotion (relevant subreddits)

**Retention Tactics:**
1. Consistent posting schedule
2. Engage with subscribers daily
3. Loyalty rewards (long-term sub perks)
4. Exclusive content for VIPs
5. Regular special offers

**Churn Reduction:**
- Welcome message with special offer
- Check-in messages to inactive subs
- Win-back campaigns
- Regular content quality audits

---

### OnlyFans Metrics & Goals

**Key Metrics:**
- Subscriber count
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Churn rate (target: <15%)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU)
- Engagement rate (messages, tips)

**Growth Benchmarks:**
- Month 1-3: 100-500 subs
- Month 4-6: 500-2,000 subs
- Month 7-12: 2,000-10,000 subs
- Year 2+: 10,000-50,000+ subs

**Revenue Benchmarks:**
- Average creator: $150-$500/month
- Top 1%: $10,000-$100,000+/month
- AI influencer potential: $5,000-$50,000/month (with proper execution)

## Platform Risk Management

### Diversification Strategy

**Never rely on a single platform:**
- Primary platform: 40% of effort
- Secondary platform: 30% of effort
- Tertiary platforms: 30% of effort
- Email list: Build from day one

### Backup Plans

1. **Account suspension**: Have backup accounts ready
2. **Algorithm changes**: Diversify content types
3. **Platform decline**: Early adoption of emerging platforms
4. **Shadowban**: Maintain email/SMS list
