## Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

### Pitfall 1: Underpricing

**Problem:** Charging too little, can't deliver quality

**Solution:** 
- Start at minimum $8K-12K/month
- Raise prices every 2-3 clients
- Fire unprofitable clients

### Pitfall 2: Overpromising

**Problem:** Guaranteeing results you can't deliver

**Solution:**
- Promise effort, not outcomes
- Under-promise, over-deliver
- Set realistic expectations in audit

### Pitfall 3: Taking Wrong Clients

**Problem:** Working with brands that aren't ready

**Solution:**
- Stick to ICP ($50K+/month revenue)
- Disqualify quickly if red flags
- Have referral partners for smaller brands

### Pitfall 4: Delivery Bottlenecks

**Problem:** You're the bottleneck for everything

**Solution:**
- Document everything (SOPs)
- Hire before you're desperate
- Delegate, don't abdicate

### Pitfall 5: Inconsistent Prospecting

**Problem:** Feast or famine pipeline

**Solution:**
- Daily non-negotiable outreach
- Multiple channels (email, LinkedIn, content)
- Build referral engine

## Final Advice

**1. Start before you're ready.**  
You'll learn more from one real client than 100 hours of planning.

**2. Focus on results, not perfection.**  
Good enough executed beats perfect planned.

**3. Raise prices faster than feels comfortable.**  
You're probably undercharging.

**4. Fire bad clients quickly.**  
They drain energy and prevent you serving great clients.

**5. Build for the long term.**  
This is a marathon, not a sprint. Sustainable > fast.

**6. Take care of yourself.**  
Burnout helps no one. Set boundaries, take breaks, stay healthy.
