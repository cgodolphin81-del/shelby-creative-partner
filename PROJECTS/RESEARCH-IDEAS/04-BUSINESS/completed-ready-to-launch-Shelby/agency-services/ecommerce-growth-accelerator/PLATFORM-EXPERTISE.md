## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Shopify-First Strategy (Recommended)
1. Build brand on Shopify (higher margins, customer ownership)
2. Use Amazon for discovery (lower margins, high intent)
3. Drive Amazon customers to Shopify (insert cards, packaging)
4. Retarget Amazon browsers on Shopify (pixel matching)

### WooCommerce Strategy
1. Only if existing WordPress investment
2. Plan Shopify migration at $200K+/month
3. Focus on content + commerce integration
4. Invest heavily in performance optimization

### Amazon-First Strategy
1. Validate product on Amazon
2. Build email list (packaging, inserts)
3. Launch DTC at $50K+/month Amazon revenue
4. Maintain both channels strategically
