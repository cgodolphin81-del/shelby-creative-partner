**For prospecting DTC brands**

## Template 1: Cold Outreach (Observation-Based)

**Subject:** Quick question about [Brand Name]

```
Hi [First Name],

I've been following [Brand Name] for a while - love what you're doing 
with [specific observation: product, mission, marketing, etc.].

I noticed you're running ads on [Meta/Google/TikTok] and wanted to 
share something interesting: we recently helped [Similar Brand] scale 
from $[X]K to $[Y]K/month by optimizing their [specific tactic].

Not sure if this is relevant for you, but if you're looking to scale 
past your current revenue, I'd love to share what worked.

Worth a quick call?

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Your Agency]
[Link to case study]
```

**When to use:** Initial outreach to brands you've researched  
**Personalization level:** High (needs specific observation)  
**Expected response rate:** 5-15%

## Subject Line Swipes

**Observation-Based:**
- "Quick question about [Brand]"
- "Noticed something about [Brand]"
- "[Brand] + [opportunity]"
- "Thought of [Brand] when I saw this"

**Curiosity:**
- "You might want to see this"
- "Interesting finding about [category]"
- "Quick observation"
- "Something you should know"

**Social Proof:**
- "[Similar Brand] just did this"
- "How [Brand] scaled to $[X]K"
- "[Result] in 90 days"
- "Case study: [Brand]"

**Direct:**
- "Scaling [Brand] past $[X]K"
- "Growth idea for [Brand]"
- "[X] ideas for [Brand]"
- "Partnership opportunity"

**Question:**
- "Quick question"
- "Are you focused on [goal] this quarter?"
- "Worth a conversation?"
- "Open to ideas?"

## Best Practices

### Do's:
✅ Keep it short (under 100 words for cold)  
✅ Personalize the first line  
✅ One clear CTA  
✅ Mobile-friendly formatting  
✅ Send from real person (not info@)  
✅ Follow up 3-5 times  
✅ Test different angles  

### Don'ts:
❌ Generic templates with no personalization  
❌ Long paragraphs  
❌ Multiple CTAs  
❌ Attachments (use links)  
❌ Salesy language ("revolutionary", "game-changing")  
❌ Give up after one email  
❌ Fake urgency ("limited time!")
