## Hiring Strategy

### Hiring Principles

1. **Hire slow, fire fast** - Take time on hires, act quickly on misfires
2. **Culture add, not culture fit** - Seek diverse perspectives
3. **Skills can be taught, attitude can't** - Prioritize mindset
4. **Reference checks are mandatory** - Always check 2-3 references
5. **Trial periods work** - 30-90 day trials reduce risk

### Hiring Process

```
1. Job Posting (3-5 days)
   ↓
2. Application Review (2-3 days)
   ↓
3. Phone Screen (30 min)
   ↓
4. Skills Assessment
   ↓
5. Team Interview (60 min)
   ↓
6. Final Interview (Founder/Leadership)
   ↓
7. Reference Checks
   ↓
8. Offer
   ↓
9. Onboarding (30 days)
```

### Interview Questions

**General:**
- "Tell me about a time you [relevant situation]"
- "What's your biggest professional accomplishment?"
- "What are you looking for in your next role?"
- "What's your experience with [specific skill]?"
- "How do you handle [common challenge]?"

**Role-Specific (Media Buyer):**
- "Walk me through your campaign structure for a DTC brand"
- "How do you approach creative testing?"
- "What's your process when ROAS drops?"
- "What's the largest ad spend you've managed?"
- "Show me a case study of your best work"

**Culture:**
- "What type of work environment do you thrive in?"
- "How do you prefer to receive feedback?"
- "What's something you've taught yourself recently?"
- "What do you do outside of work?"

### Compensation Philosophy

**Guiding Principles:**
- Pay at or above market rate
- Include performance incentives
- Offer equity to key hires
- Review compensation annually
- Transparent about ranges

**Equity Guidelines:**
| Role Level | Equity Range |
|------------|--------------|
| C-Suite | 2-10% |
| VP/Director | 1-3% |
| Manager | 0.5-1.5% |
| Senior IC | 0.25-0.75% |
| IC | 0.1-0.25% |

## Management & Culture

### Management Cadence

| Meeting | Frequency | Duration | Attendees |
|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Daily Standup | Daily | 15 min | Team |
| 1:1 | Weekly | 30 min | Manager + Direct Report |
| Team Meeting | Weekly | 60 min | Department |
| All Hands | Bi-weekly | 60 min | Company |
| Leadership | Weekly | 60 min | Executives |
| Board/Advisory | Monthly | 90 min | Leaders + Advisors |

### Performance Reviews

**Frequency:** Quarterly (informal), Annual (formal)

**Review Framework:**
1. Goals achievement
2. Core competencies
3. Values alignment
4. Growth & development
5. Compensation discussion

**Rating Scale:**
- 5: Exceptional (exceeds all expectations)
- 4: Strong (exceeds some expectations)
- 3: Solid (meets expectations)
- 2: Developing (below expectations)
- 1: Unsatisfactory (significantly below)

### Culture Values

**Example Values:**
1. **Client success first** - We win when they win
2. **Radical transparency** - Honest, direct, kind
3. **Bias for action** - Move fast, learn faster
4. **Ownership mentality** - Think like an owner
5. **Continuous improvement** - Always getting better
6. **Team over ego** - We > me

## Remote vs. In-Office

### Remote-First (Recommended)

**Pros:**
- Access to global talent
- Lower overhead
- Flexibility for team
- Scalable

**Cons:**
- Culture building harder
- Communication challenges
- Time zone complexity
- Less spontaneous collaboration

**Best Practices:**
- Over-communicate
- Document everything
- Regular video calls
- Annual in-person gatherings
- Async-first culture

### Hybrid Model

**Structure:**
- Core hours for overlap (e.g., 10am-3pm EST)
- In-person quarterly offsites
- Optional co-working stipends
- Home office budget ($500-1K)
