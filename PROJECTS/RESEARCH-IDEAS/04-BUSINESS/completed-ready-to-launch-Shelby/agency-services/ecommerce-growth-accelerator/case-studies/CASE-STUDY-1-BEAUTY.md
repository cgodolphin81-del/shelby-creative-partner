## The Challenge

### Brand Background

Glow Naturals is a clean beauty brand founded in 2022, offering plant-based skincare products for sensitive skin. Their hero products include a vitamin C serum, hyaluronic acid moisturizer, and gentle cleanser. Target audience: women 25-45, health-conscious, willing to pay premium for clean ingredients.

### The Problem

Glow Naturals came to us after hitting a growth plateau. They had:

- **Stuck at $75K-90K/month** for 6 consecutive months
- **Declining Meta ROAS** from 3.5x to 2.1x over 4 months
- **No email strategy** beyond basic campaigns
- **High CAC** at $65 (industry avg: $45)
- **Low repeat rate** at 18% (industry avg: 30%)
- **Creative fatigue** - running same ads for 3+ months

### Goals

1. Scale to $300K+/month within 6 months
2. Achieve 3.0x+ blended ROAS
3. Grow email to 25%+ of revenue
4. Increase LTV to $250+
5. Build sustainable, profitable growth system

## Our Approach

### Phase 1: Discovery & Audit (Weeks 1-2)

**Key Findings:**

**Paid Media Issues:**
- Campaign structure was fragmented (47 active campaigns)
- No creative testing framework
- Audiences were too narrow (interest stacking)
- Retargeting was overlapping and inefficient
- No TikTok presence despite demo fit

**Email/SMS Gaps:**
- Only 2 flows active (welcome, abandoned cart)
- No segmentation strategy
- Sending 1 campaign/week (too low)
- SMS not implemented
- List growth rate: 2%/month

**Website/CRO:**
- Conversion rate: 1.4% (industry: 2.2%)
- No post-purchase upsell
- Product pages lacked social proof
- No subscription option
- Page speed: 62/100 (mobile)

**Retention:**
- No loyalty program
- No post-purchase engagement
- Replenishment timing not optimized
- No VIP tier

### Phase 2: Strategy Development (Weeks 2-3)

**Core Strategies:**

1. **Paid Media: Simplify & Scale**
   - Consolidate to 5 core campaigns (prospecting, retargeting, remarketing)
   - Implement creative testing framework (5 new creatives/week)
   - Expand to TikTok and Google Performance Max
   - Broad targeting with creative segmentation

2. **Email/SMS: Build the Machine**
   - Implement 8 core flows
   - Segment by behavior, RFM, product preference
   - Increase to 3-4 campaigns/week
   - Launch SMS for flash sales and cart recovery

3. **CRO: Remove Friction**
   - Optimize product pages (reviews, UGC, FAQs)
   - Add post-purchase upsell funnel
   - Launch subscription program (15% discount)
   - Improve page speed to 80+

4. **Retention: Maximize LTV**
   - Launch loyalty program (Smile.io)
   - Implement replenishment flows
   - Create VIP tier with exclusive perks
   - Post-purchase education series

## What Made the Difference

### 1. Creative Testing System

**The Problem:** Running same 3 creatives for months, severe fatigue.

**Our Solution:**
- Built creative brief template
- Hired 3 UGC creators
- Testing framework: 5 new creatives/week
- Rapid iteration (kill losers in 48 hours)

**Results:**
- Found 8 winning creatives in 6 months
- CTR increased 100%
- CPM decreased 36%
- Creative became their competitive advantage

### 2. Email Flow Architecture

**The Problem:** Only 2 flows, leaving $50K+/month on table.

**Our Solution:**
- Implemented 8 core flows:
  - Welcome (7 emails)
  - Abandoned Cart (4 emails + 2 SMS)
  - Browse Abandonment (3 emails)
  - Post-Purchase (8 emails)
  - Review Request (2 emails)
  - Replenishment (3 emails)
  - VIP (4 emails)
  - Winback (4 emails)

**Results:**
- Flow revenue: $8K/mo → $65K/mo
- Email revenue %: 12% → 31%
- List growth: 2%/mo → 11%/mo

### 3. Subscription Program

**The Problem:** No recurring revenue, unpredictable cash flow.

**Our Solution:**
- Launched Recharge integration
- 15% discount for subscribers
- Flexible frequency (30, 45, 60 days)
- Subscription-exclusive products

**Results:**
- 18% of orders are subscriptions
- $58K/month recurring revenue
- Subscriber LTV: $425 (vs. $268 one-time)
- Churn: 8%/month (industry: 12%)

## Key Takeaways

### For Similar Beauty Brands:

1. **Creative is the new targeting** - Broad audiences + great creative outperforms narrow targeting every time.

2. **Email is your profit center** - At 31% of revenue with 80%+ margins, email drives disproportionate profit.

3. **Subscriptions create predictability** - Recurring revenue makes planning, hiring, and investing so much easier.

4. **Retention is cheaper than acquisition** - Every dollar spent on LTV optimization returns 3-5x vs. acquisition spend.

### What We'd Do Again:

- Creative testing framework (non-negotiable)
- Flow-first email strategy
- Subscription program from day 1
- TikTok for prospecting

### What We'd Do Differently:

- Launch SMS earlier (waited until month 3)
- Push for loyalty program sooner
- Test influencer partnerships earlier
