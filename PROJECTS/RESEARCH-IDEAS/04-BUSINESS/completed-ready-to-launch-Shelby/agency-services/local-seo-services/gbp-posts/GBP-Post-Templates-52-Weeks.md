## 📊 GBP POST BEST PRACTICES

### Image Requirements:
- **Size:** 720 x 720 pixels minimum (1080 x 1080 recommended)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square)
- **No text overlay** on more than 20% of image

### Content Guidelines:
- **Character Limit:** 1,500 characters
- **Call-to-Action:** Always include one
- **Phone Number:** Include in most posts
- **Links:** Add when relevant (website, booking, etc.)
- **Hashtags:** Use 3-5 local hashtags

### Posting Tips:
- ✅ Post consistently (2-3x per week)
- ✅ Use high-quality, relevant images
- ✅ Include clear calls-to-action
- ✅ Respond to comments within 24 hours
- ✅ Track which posts perform best
- ✅ Update expired offers promptly
- ❌ Don't post identical content repeatedly
- ❌ Don't use stock photos when possible
- ❌ Don't forget to add location context

### Performance Metrics to Track:
- Post views
- Photo views
- Call clicks
- Direction requests
- Website clicks
- Engagement (comments)
