## ⏰ TIMING SEQUENCE FRAMEWORK

### Optimal Request Timing by Industry

| Industry | Best Time to Ask | Method |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| Medical/Dental | 2 hours after appointment | SMS + Email |
| Home Services | 24 hours after job completion | Email + SMS |
| Restaurants | Same day (2 hours after visit) | SMS |
| Retail | 24 hours after purchase | Email |
| Auto Repair | 4 hours after pickup | SMS |
| Real Estate | 48 hours after closing | Email + Phone |
| Legal | 1 week after case resolution | Email |
| Beauty/Salon | 2 hours after appointment | SMS |
| Fitness/Gym | After 5th visit | Email + In-app |
| Professional Services | 1 week after project | Email |

### Multi-Touch Sequence (Recommended)

```
TOUCH 1: Immediate (0-4 hours post-service)
→ SMS: Quick, casual ask with direct link

TOUCH 2: Follow-up (24-48 hours)
→ Email: More detailed, personalized message

TOUCH 3: Final (7 days)
→ Email: "Last chance" with incentive (if compliant)

TOUCH 4: Recovery (14 days - non-responders only)
→ Phone call or in-person ask
```

## ⚠️ COMPLIANCE & BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ✅ Ask ALL customers (don't cherry-pick)
- ✅ Make it easy (direct links, QR codes)
- ✅ Train staff to ask in-person
- ✅ Respond to EVERY review
- ✅ Follow platform guidelines
- ✅ Keep SMS under 160 characters
- ✅ Include opt-out in SMS

### DON'T:
- ❌ Offer incentives for positive reviews (against Google/Yelp TOS)
- ❌ Write reviews for customers
- ❌ Filter customers based on expected rating
- ❌ Use review gating (negative → private, positive → public)
- ❌ Spam or send excessive requests
- ❌ Include personal/health info in templates

### Platform-Specific Rules

**Google:**
- No incentives for reviews
- No review gating
- Must ask all customers equally

**Yelp:**
- Don't ask for reviews at point of sale
- No incentives
- Let customers find you organically (preferred)

**Facebook:**
- Can offer incentives if disclosed
- Must not require positive sentiment

**HIPAA (Medical):**
- No PHI in communications
- Secure patient data
- Include privacy disclaimer
