## 1. Content Marketing

### Strategy Overview
Establish thought leadership through valuable, educational content that demonstrates expertise and attracts inbound leads.

### Content Types

#### Blog Posts (2-4 per month)
**Topics:**
- Security best practices
- Compliance guides
- Threat analysis
- Case studies (anonymized)
- Industry trends
- How-to guides

**SEO Optimization:**
- Target keywords: "security audit," "SOC 2 compliance," "penetration testing"
- Long-tail keywords: "SOC 2 Type 2 requirements for SaaS"
- Local SEO: "cybersecurity consultant [city]"

**Distribution:**
- Company blog
- LinkedIn articles
- Medium publications
- Industry forums

#### White Papers & E-books (1 per quarter)
**Topics:**
- "Complete Guide to SOC 2 Compliance"
- "2026 State of Cybersecurity"
- "Penetration Testing ROI Calculator"
- "CISO's Guide to Risk Management"

**Gating Strategy:**
- Require email for download
- Follow-up email sequence
- Sales notification for high-value downloads

#### Webinars (1-2 per month)
**Format:**
- 45-minute presentation + 15-minute Q&A
- Co-hosted with partners when possible
- Recorded for on-demand viewing

**Topics:**
- Compliance deep-dives
- Threat briefings
- Tool demonstrations
- Customer success stories

**Promotion:**
- Email marketing
- Social media
- Partner co-marketing
- Paid promotion (LinkedIn)

#### Video Content (1-2 per week)
**Platforms:**
- YouTube (long-form)
- LinkedIn (short-form)
- TikTok/Instagram Reels (awareness)

**Content:**
- Security tips (60 seconds)
- Tool tutorials (5-10 minutes)
- Expert interviews (20-30 minutes)
- Conference talks (repurposed)

## 2. Free Security Assessments

### Strategy Overview
Offer complimentary mini-assessments to demonstrate value and start conversations with prospects.

### Assessment Types

#### Quick Security Scan (Free)
**What's Included:**
- External vulnerability scan
- Basic security posture review
- 5-page summary report
- 30-minute consultation

**Time Investment:** 2-3 hours
**Lead Quality:** Medium
**Conversion Rate:** 15-25%

**Process:**
1. Prospect signs up online
2. Provide IP addresses/domains
3. Run automated scans
4. Generate report
5. Schedule consultation call
6. Present findings + recommendations
7. Propose full engagement

#### Compliance Gap Analysis (Free)
**What's Included:**
- Framework selection guidance
- High-level gap assessment
- Prioritized roadmap
- 1-hour consultation

**Time Investment:** 3-4 hours
**Lead Quality:** High
**Conversion Rate:** 30-40%

**Target:**
- Companies seeking SOC 2
- Healthcare organizations (HIPAA)
- Payment processors (PCI-DSS)
- EU-facing businesses (GDPR)

#### Executive Security Briefing (Free)
**What's Included:**
- 60-minute presentation
- Threat landscape overview
- Industry-specific risks
- Q&A session

**Time Investment:** 2-3 hours (including prep)
**Lead Quality:** Very High
**Conversion Rate:** 40-50%

**Target:**
- C-suite executives
- Board members
- Companies post-breach
- Organizations seeking vCISO

### Assessment-to-Engagement Funnel

```
Free Assessment Request
        │
        ▼
┌───────────────────┐
│   Qualification   │  → Disqualify if not a fit
│   Call (30 min)   │
└─────────┬─────────┘
          │
          ▼
┌───────────────────┐
│   Assessment      │  → Deliver value, build trust
│   Delivery        │
└─────────┬─────────┘
          │
          ▼
┌───────────────────┐
│   Findings        │  → Present gaps, discuss impact
│   Presentation    │
└─────────┬─────────┘
          │
          ▼
┌───────────────────┐
│   Proposal        │  → Recommend solutions
│   Discussion      │
└─────────┬─────────┘
          │
          ▼
     Closed Deal
```

### Best Practices
- **Set expectations:** Be clear about what's included
- **Deliver value:** Provide actionable insights, not just problems
- **Don't oversell:** Let the findings speak for themselves
- **Follow up promptly:** Send proposal within 48 hours
- **Track everything:** Monitor conversion rates by assessment type

## 3. Partnership Development

### Strategy Overview
Build strategic partnerships that generate referrals and expand market reach.

### Partner Types

**Examples:**
- SIEM vendors (Splunk, Sentinel, Sumo Logic)
- Cloud providers (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- Security tool vendors (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto, Okta)

**Partnership Benefits:**

**Approach:**
1. Identify relevant vendors
2. Reach out to partner teams
3. Complete partner onboarding
4. Attend partner training
5. Co-create marketing content
6. Establish referral process

#### Channel Partners
**Examples:**
- Managed Service Providers (MSPs)
- IT consulting firms
- Accounting firms (for SOC 2)
- Law firms (for privacy/compliance)
- Insurance brokers (for cyber insurance)

| Model | Description | Revenue Share |

## 5. Outbound Prospecting

### Strategy Overview
Proactive outreach to targeted prospects with personalized, value-driven messaging.

### Target Account Selection

#### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Company Characteristics:**
- Industry: SaaS, Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce
- Size: 50-500 employees
- Revenue: $10M - $500M
- Funding: Series A+ (for startups)
- Geography: North America, Europe
- Technology: Cloud-native, modern stack

**Trigger Events:**
- Recent funding round
- New CISO/CTO hired
- Compliance deadline approaching
- Recent breach in industry
- New product launch
- M&A activity

### Outbound Channels

#### Email Outreach
**Best Practices:**
- Personalized subject lines
- Short, value-focused messages
- Clear call-to-action
- Mobile-friendly formatting
- A/B testing

**Email Sequence Example:**

```
Email 1 (Day 1): Introduction + Value Proposition
Email 2 (Day 3): Relevant Case Study
Email 3 (Day 7): Industry Insight + Offer
Email 4 (Day 14): Breakup Email
```

**Sample Email:**

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s security posture

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Company] recently [trigger event - funding, hiring, etc.]. 
Congratulations!

Many companies at your stage face similar security challenges:
- Preparing for SOC 2 compliance for enterprise customers
- Scaling security practices with rapid growth
- Managing third-party risk

We recently helped [Similar Company] achieve SOC 2 Type II in 12 weeks, 
unlocking $2M in enterprise deals.

Would you be open to a brief 15-minute call to discuss your security 
priorities for this year?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### LinkedIn Outreach
**Best Practices:**
- Optimize profile first
- Personalize connection requests
- Engage with prospect content
- Share valuable insights
- Don't sell immediately

**Connection Request:**
```
Hi [Name], I've been following [Company]'s growth and noticed 
your work in [area]. Would love to connect and exchange insights 
on [relevant topic].
```

**Follow-up (after connection):**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]. I noticed [observation about 
their company/role]. We've been helping similar companies with 
[relevant challenge]. Would you be open to a brief conversation?
```

#### Phone Outreach
**Best Practices:**
- Research before calling
- Have clear objective
- Respect their time
- Focus on their challenges
- Ask for next step

**Opening Script:**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] with CyberGuard. I know I'm 
calling out of the blue - do you have 30 seconds for me to tell 
you why I'm calling, and then you can decide if we continue?"
```

### Outreach Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Email open rate | 25-35% |
| Email reply rate | 5-10% |
| Connection acceptance rate | 30-50% |
| Call connection rate | 15-25% |
| Meeting booking rate | 3-5% of total outreach |
| Opportunity conversion | 20-30% of meetings |

## 6. Referral Program

### Strategy Overview
Leverage satisfied clients and professional network for warm introductions.

### Client Referral Program

#### Program Structure
**Incentives:**
- 10% referral fee on first-year revenue
- $5,000 bonus for enterprise deals ($100K+)
- Gift cards for smaller referrals
- Charitable donation option

**Eligibility:**
- Current clients in good standing
- Closed deals only (no upfront payment)
- No self-referrals

#### Referral Process
1. Client identifies prospect
2. Client makes introduction (email/LinkedIn)
3. You follow up with prospect
4. Deal progresses through sales cycle
5. Referral fee paid upon contract signing

#### Asking for Referrals
**Timing:**
- After successful project completion
- After positive feedback/testimonial
- During quarterly business reviews
- When client achieves milestone

**Script:**
```
"We're thrilled with how the [project] turned out. We're 
currently working with companies like [their company] in 
[their industry]. Do you know any other leaders who might 
be facing similar security challenges?"
```

### Professional Network Referrals

#### Sources
- Former colleagues
- Industry contacts
- Conference connections
- Alumni networks
- Board advisors

#### Nurturing Strategy
- Regular check-ins (quarterly)
- Share relevant insights
- Make introductions for them
- Invite to events
- Send holiday gifts

## 8. Sales Process

### Lead Qualification

#### BANT Framework
- **Budget:** Do they have budget for security services?
- **Authority:** Are we talking to the decision-maker?
- **Need:** Do they have a clear security need?
- **Timeline:** What's their implementation timeline?

#### Qualification Questions
```
1. What security challenges are you currently facing?
2. Have you done security assessments before?
3. What compliance requirements do you have?
4. What's your security budget for this year?
5. Who else is involved in the decision?
6. What's your timeline for getting started?
7. What happens if you don't address this?
```

### Sales Stages

| Stage | Description | Probability |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Lead | Initial contact made | 10% |
| Qualified | BANT confirmed | 25% |
| Discovery | Needs assessment complete | 40% |
| Proposal | Proposal submitted | 60% |
| Negotiation | Terms being discussed | 75% |
| Closed Won | Contract signed | 100% |

### Proposal Best Practices
- Customized to prospect's needs
- Clear problem statement
- Detailed solution description
- Timeline and milestones
- Investment breakdown
- Case studies/references
- Clear next steps
- Valid for 30 days
