# Team Structure

## Organizational Design for Cybersecurity Consulting Firm

---

## Overview

Building the right team is critical to delivering high-quality cybersecurity services. This document outlines the organizational structure, roles, responsibilities, and hiring guidelines for scaling a cybersecurity consulting practice.

### Growth Phases

```
Phase 1: Foundation (0-10 employees)
┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│  Founder/CEO                        │
│  ├─ Senior Security Consultant (2)  │
│  └─ Operations Manager              │
└─────────────────────────────────────┘

Phase 2: Growth (10-25 employees)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  CEO                                        │
│  ├─ VP of Services                          │
│  │   ├─ Security Consultants (5)            │
│  │   └─ Penetration Testers (3)             │
│  ├─ VP of Sales & Marketing                 │
│  │   ├─ Account Executives (2)              │
│  │   └─ Marketing Manager (1)               │
│  └─ Operations                              │
│      ├─ Project Managers (2)                │
│      └─ Admin/HR (1)                        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Phase 3: Scale (25-50 employees)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  CEO                                                    │
│  ├─ Chief Security Officer                              │
│  │   ├─ Audit Practice Lead                             │
│  │   │   └─ Security Auditors (5)                       │
│  │   ├─ Penetration Testing Lead                        │
│  │   │   └─ Penetration Testers (6)                     │
│  │   └─ Compliance Practice Lead                        │
│  │       └─ Compliance Consultants (4)                  │
│  ├─ Chief Revenue Officer                               │
│  │   ├─ Enterprise Sales (3)                            │
│  │   ├─ SMB Sales (2)                                   │
│  │   └─ Marketing Team (4)                              │
│  ├─ Chief Operating Officer                             │
│  │   ├─ Project Management Office (4)                   │
│  │   ├─ Client Success (3)                              │
│  │   └─ Operations/HR/Finance (4)                       │
│  └─ vCISO/Advisory Practice                             │
│      └─ vCISOs/Senior Advisors (3)                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Phase 4: Enterprise (50+ employees)
┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  CEO                                                          │
│  ├─ CTO                                                       │
│  ├─ CISO (Practice)                                           │
│  ├─ CRO                                                       │
│  ├─ COO                                                       │
│  ├─ CFO                                                       │
│  └─ Regional VPs (if multi-location)                          │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Leadership Team

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

**Responsibilities:**
- Company vision and strategy
- Fundraising and investor relations
- Key client relationships
- Executive team leadership
- Company culture
- M&A activities

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years in cybersecurity or consulting
- Previous leadership experience
- Strong industry network
- Business development expertise

**Compensation:**
- Base: $200,000 - $300,000
- Equity: 20-40% (founder)
- Bonus: 50-100% of base

---

### Chief Security Officer (CSO) / Practice Lead

**Responsibilities:**
- Technical service delivery oversight
- Quality assurance
- Methodology development
- Technical hiring
- Research and development
- Client escalations

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years in security consulting
- Multiple certifications (CISSP, OSCP, CISM)
- Published research or speaking experience
- Team management experience

**Compensation:**
- Base: $180,000 - $250,000
- Equity: 2-5%
- Bonus: 30-50% of base

---

### Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)

**Responsibilities:**
- Revenue strategy and targets
- Sales team leadership
- Pipeline management
- Pricing strategy
- Partner relationships
- Marketing alignment

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years in B2B sales
- cybersecurity industry experience
- Track record of building sales teams
- Strong negotiation skills

**Compensation:**
- Base: $150,000 - $200,000
- Commission: 10-15% of quota
- Equity: 1-3%
- OTE: $300,000 - $500,000

---

### Chief Operating Officer (COO)

**Responsibilities:**
- Operations management
- Project delivery oversight
- Resource allocation
- Process optimization
- HR and talent management
- Finance oversight

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years in operations/consulting
- Professional services experience
- Strong analytical skills
- People management expertise

**Compensation:**
- Base: $160,000 - $220,000
- Equity: 1-3%
- Bonus: 30-50% of base

## Technical Roles

**Level: Junior**
- 1-3 years experience

**Level: Senior**
- 4-7 years experience
- Salary: $100,000 - $140,000

**Level: Principal**
- 8+ years experience
- Salary: $150,000 - $200,000

**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct security assessments
- Perform vulnerability analysis
- Write technical reports
- Client communication
- Remediation guidance

**Required Skills:**

## Hiring Guidelines

```
1. Job Posting (1 week)
   │
   ▼
2. Resume Screening (1 week)
   │
   ▼
3. Phone Screen (30 min)
   │
   ▼
4. Technical Assessment
   │
   ▼
5. Technical Interview (1-2 hours)
   │
   ▼
6. Cultural Fit Interview (1 hour)
   │
   ▼
7. Reference Checks
   │
   ▼
8. Offer Extended
   │
   ▼
```

### Compensation Philosophy

**Market Positioning:** 60th-75th percentile
**Philosophy:** Pay for performance, reward growth

**Components:**
- Base salary (60-80% of OTE for sales)
- Performance bonus (10-30%)
- Equity (0.1-5% based on level)
- Benefits (health, dental, vision, 401k)
- Professional development budget ($3,000-5,000/year)
- Conference attendance

### Benefits Package

| Benefit | Details |
