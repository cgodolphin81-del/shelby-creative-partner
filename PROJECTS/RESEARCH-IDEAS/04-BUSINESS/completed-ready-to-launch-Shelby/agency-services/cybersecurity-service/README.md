## Company Overview

**CyberGuard** is a full-service cybersecurity consulting firm providing enterprise-grade security solutions to businesses of all sizes. We specialize in risk assessment, compliance, penetration testing, and managed security services.

### Mission Statement
To empower organizations with the security posture, compliance frameworks, and ongoing protection needed to thrive in an increasingly hostile digital landscape.

### Core Values
- **Integrity**: Uncompromising ethics in all engagements
- **Excellence**: Industry-leading methodologies and deliverables
- **Partnership**: Long-term client relationships, not one-off transactions
- **Innovation**: Staying ahead of emerging threats and technologies
