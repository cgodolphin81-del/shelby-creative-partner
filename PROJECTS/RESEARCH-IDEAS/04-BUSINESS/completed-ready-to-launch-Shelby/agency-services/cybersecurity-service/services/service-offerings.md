## Engagement Models

### **Project-Based**
- Fixed scope, fixed price
- Defined deliverables
- Typical duration: 2-12 weeks
- Best for: Audits, assessments, implementations

### **Retainer-Based**
- Monthly recurring engagement
- Flexible scope within agreed hours
- Priority scheduling
- Best for: Ongoing advisory, compliance support

### **Managed Services**
- Fully outsourced security function
- 24/7 monitoring and response
- Predictable monthly cost
- Best for: Organizations without security teams
