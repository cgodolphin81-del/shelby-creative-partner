## Sales Playbook

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Best Initial Targets:**
- SaaS companies (50-200 employees)
- Recently funded (Series A/B)
- Selling to enterprise customers
- No dedicated security team
- Compliance deadlines approaching

**Where to Find Them:**
- LinkedIn (CTO, VP Engineering, CEO)
- Crunchbase (recent funding)
- Industry events and meetups
- Referrals from existing network
- Partner channels (MSPs, accountants)

### Sales Process

```
1. Prospecting → 2. Discovery → 3. Proposal → 4. Close → 5. Deliver
   (1-2 weeks)    (1 call)       (2-3 days)   (1-2 weeks)  (Ongoing)
```

**Conversion Targets:**
- Lead to Discovery: 30%
- Discovery to Proposal: 50%
- Proposal to Close: 40%
- **Overall: 6%** (industry standard)

### Daily Sales Activities

| Activity | Target | Time |
|----------|--------|------|
| Emails sent | 20 | 1 hour |
| Calls made | 10 | 1 hour |
| LinkedIn connections | 10 | 30 min |
| Follow-ups | 15 | 1 hour |
| Discovery calls | 2-3 | 2 hours |
| **Total** | | **5-6 hours** |

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Sales & Marketing
- ❌ Waiting for inbound leads
- ❌ Underpricing to win deals
- ❌ Not following up consistently
- ❌ Overpromising on delivery
- ✅ **Do:** Consistent outbound, value-based pricing, persistent follow-up

### Delivery
- ❌ Taking on too much too soon
- ❌ Not documenting processes
- ❌ Skipping quality reviews
- ❌ Poor communication
- ✅ **Do:** Start small, document everything, QA everything, over-communicate

### Operations
- ❌ Mixing personal and business finances
- ❌ Not tracking time and expenses
- ❌ Skipping contracts
- ❌ Ignoring cash flow
- ✅ **Do:** Separate accounts, track everything, always use contracts, monitor weekly

### Hiring
- ❌ Hiring too early
- ❌ Hiring for cheap instead of quality
- ❌ Not defining roles clearly
- ❌ Skipping reference checks
- ✅ **Do:** Wait until overwhelmed, pay for quality, clear JDs, thorough vetting
