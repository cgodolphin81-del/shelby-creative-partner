## Executive Summary

### Market Opportunity

**Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $58.2B by 2030
**Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $12.4B (SMB review management)
**Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $124M (Year 3 target: 1% of SAM)

### Go-to-Market Objectives (Year 1)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Customers | 2,500 |
| MRR | $350,000 |
| ARR | $4.2M |
| Churn Rate | <5% monthly |
| CAC Payback | <12 months |
| NPS | 60+ |

### Core Strategy

ReviewFlow Pro will pursue a **product-led growth (PLG)** strategy with sales-assisted conversion for enterprise segments. The approach combines:

1. **Self-serve acquisition** for Starter plan (low-touch)
2. **Inside sales** for Professional plan (medium-touch)
3. **Field sales** for Enterprise plan (high-touch)
4. **Partner channel** for agency/white-label (leveraged)

## Target Markets

### Primary Segments

#### Segment 1: Local Businesses (Starter Plan)

**Profile:**
- Single location businesses
- 1-50 employees
- $500K - $5M annual revenue
- Owner-operated or small management team

**Verticals:**
- Restaurants & cafes
- Retail stores
- Medical/dental practices
- Auto services
- Home services (plumbers, electricians, HVAC)
- Beauty & wellness salons
- Fitness studios

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time for review management
- Don't know how to respond to reviews
- Losing customers to competitors with better reviews
- No system for requesting reviews

**Buying Triggers:**
- Recent negative review impact
- Competitor outperforming on reviews
- New marketing initiative
- Change in ownership/management

**Acquisition Channels:**
- Google Ads (search)
- Facebook/Instagram ads
- Local business associations
- Chamber of Commerce partnerships
- Referral program

---

#### Segment 2: Multi-Location Businesses (Professional Plan)

**Profile:**
- 2-50 locations
- 50-500 employees
- $5M - $50M annual revenue
- Dedicated marketing/customer experience team

**Verticals:**
- Restaurant chains
- Retail chains
- Healthcare groups
- Auto dealer groups
- Hospitality groups
- Education (private schools, tutoring centers)
- Professional services (law firms, accounting)

**Pain Points:**
- Inconsistent review management across locations

**Buying Triggers:**

- Leadership change (new CMO/COO)

**Acquisition Channels:**
- LinkedIn advertising
- Industry conferences
- Content marketing (SEO)
- Partner referrals (CRM, POS vendors)
- Inside sales outreach

### Sales Process by Segment

#### Self-Serve (Starter Plan)

**Process:**
1. Website visit
2. Free trial signup (no credit card)
3. Product-led onboarding (email sequence)
4. In-app upgrade prompts
5. Self-serve checkout

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Frictionless signup
- Interactive product tour
- Template library access
- Time-limited trial bonuses
- Exit-intent offers

**Target Conversion Rate:** 15% trial-to-paid

---

#### Inside Sales (Professional Plan)

**Process:**
1. Website visit or inbound lead
2. Trial signup or demo request
3. SDR qualification call (15 min)
4. AE demo (30 min)
5. Trial with AE support
6. Close

**Sales Cycle:** 14-30 days

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- Budget: $200+/month available
- Authority: Decision maker or influencer
- Need: Multi-location or advanced features needed
- Timeline: Looking to solve within 90 days

**Target Conversion Rate:** 25% demo-to-close

---

#### Enterprise Sales (Enterprise Plan)

**Process:**
1. Inbound lead or outbound prospecting
2. Discovery call (45 min)
3. Executive briefing (optional)
4. Custom demo (60 min)
5. Security/compliance review
6. Pilot/POC (30 days)
7. Contract negotiation
8. Close

**Sales Cycle:** 60-180 days

**Stakeholders:**
- Economic buyer (CFO, CEO)
- Technical buyer (CTO, IT Director)
- User buyer (CMO, CXO)
- Champion (Marketing/CX Manager)

**Target Conversion Rate:** 20% opportunity-to-close

---

#### Agency Sales (White-Label)

**Process:**
1. Partner inquiry
2. Partnership qualification
3. Partner onboarding
4. Joint business planning
5. Co-selling support
6. Ongoing enablement

**Sales Cycle:** 30-60 days

**Partner Qualification:**
- Active client base (10+ clients)
- Relevant service offerings
- Growth trajectory
- Cultural fit

**Target:** 50 agency partners in Year 1
