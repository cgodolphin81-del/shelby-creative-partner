💝 About This Project

Why I Built This

The internet can be a heavy place sometimes. Between the ads, the tracking, the arguments, and the constant pressure to buy things... it's easy to forget that the web can also be a source of genuine joy and connection.

I built the Infinite Compliment Machine as an antidote to all that. No ads. No tracking. No monetization. Just a simple tool that reminds you that you matter, you're valued, and you deserve to hear something wonderful today.

Because sometimes, that's all we need.

How It Works

The app uses a database of 1000+ compliments, carefully crafted to be genuine, meaningful, and personalized. When you enter your name, mood, and interests, the algorithm selects a compliment that fits your current state.

Everything happens in your browser - nothing is sent to a server, nothing is stored permanently. Your privacy matters, even in a compliment machine.

The Tech

Built with vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks!), pure CSS animations, and a whole lot of love. The entire site is less than 100KB, loads in under a second, and works on any device.

Sometimes the best solutions are the simplest ones.
