# FormFlow Pricing

## Pricing Philosophy

Transparent, fair pricing with a generous free tier. No hidden fees, no gotchas. Upgrade when you need more, not because you're forced to.

## Billing Details

### Payment Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Check (Enterprise only)

### Billing Cycle
- Monthly: Billed every 30 days
- Annual: Billed once per year
- Proration: Automatic when upgrading
- Refunds: Pro-rated for downgrades

### Taxes
- VAT included for EU customers
- Sales tax for US customers (where applicable)
- GST for Australian customers
- Tax invoices available

### Cancellation
- Cancel anytime
- No cancellation fees
- Access until end of billing period
- Data export available
- 30-day data retention after cancellation
