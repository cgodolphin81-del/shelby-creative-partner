## Transaction Fee Economics

### How Transaction Fees Work

```
Example: $497 Course Sale on Growth Plan

Gross Sale:           $497.00
Platform Fee (3%):    -$14.91
Payment Processing:   -$14.71 (Stripe: 2.9% + $0.30)
─────────────────────────────────
Net to Creator:       $467.38

Creator keeps 94% of sale price
```

## Pricing FAQ

### Q: Can I change plans anytime?
**A:** Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### Q: What happens if I exceed my video storage limit?
**A:** You'll be notified at 80% and 100% capacity. You can purchase extra storage ($10/100 GB) or upgrade your plan. Courses remain accessible; you just can't upload new videos until you free up space or upgrade.

### Q: Are transaction fees charged on memberships?
**A:** No! Membership recurring revenue has 0% transaction fees on all plans. Transaction fees only apply to one-time course sales.

### Q: Do you charge for students?
**A:** Never! All plans include unlimited students. This is a core principle—your success shouldn't be limited by arbitrary caps.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** All major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfer (ACH) for annual plans. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.

### Q: Is there a setup fee?
**A:** No setup fees for standard plans. Enterprise plans may include implementation fees for custom integrations and mobile apps.

### Q: Can I pause my subscription?
**A:** Yes, you can pause your subscription for up to 3 months. Your courses remain accessible to existing students, but you can't accept new enrollments during the pause.
