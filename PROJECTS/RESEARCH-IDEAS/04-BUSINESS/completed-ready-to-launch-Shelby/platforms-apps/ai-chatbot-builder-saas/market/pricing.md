## Usage Calculation

### What Counts as a Conversation?

A **conversation** is a continuous exchange between a user and your bot, ending when:
- 30 minutes of inactivity pass
- User closes the chat
- Issue is resolved
- Handoff to human occurs

**Multiple messages within a conversation = 1 conversation**

### Examples

**Example 1**: Customer asks about pricing, bot responds, customer asks follow-up, bot answers, customer says thanks and leaves.
- **Result**: 1 conversation (8 messages exchanged)

**Example 2**: Customer asks about shipping, gets answer, leaves. Returns 2 hours later asking about returns.
- **Result**: 2 conversations (separate sessions)

**Example 3**: Customer starts chat, bot greets, customer doesn't respond for 35 minutes.
- **Result**: 1 conversation (counted when started)

## Pricing Psychology

### Why This Structure Works

1. **Low Entry Point** ($29): Reduces friction for SMBs to try
2. **Clear Upgrade Path**: Each tier has obvious value jump
3. **Usage-Aligned**: Customers pay for value received
4. **Overage Protection**: Reasonable overage rates prevent bill shock
5. **Annual Incentive**: 20% discount improves cash flow + retention

### Anchor Pricing

Enterprise tier ($299) makes Business ($199) feel reasonable, which makes Pro ($99) feel like a steal for growing companies.

## Upgrade/Downgrade Policy

### Upgrades
- **Immediate**: Features available right away
- **Prorated**: Pay difference for remainder of billing period
- **Example**: Upgrade from Starter to Pro on day 15 of month → pay $35 (half of $70 difference)

### Downgrades
- **Next Period**: Takes effect at next billing cycle
- **Usage Check**: If over new plan limits, must reduce usage or wait
- **Example**: Downgrade from Pro to Starter → effective next month, must stay under 1K conversations

### Cancellation
- **Anytime**: Cancel online or via support
- **Access Until End**: Keep using until billing period ends
- **Data Export**: Download all data before cancellation
- **Reactivation**: Within 90 days, restore with all data

## Pricing FAQ

**Q: What happens if I exceed my conversation limit?**  
A: You'll be charged the overage rate ($0.02-0.05 per conversation depending on plan). You'll receive email alerts at 80% and 100% of your limit.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**  
A: Yes! Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

**Q: Do conversations reset monthly?**  
A: Yes, your conversation count resets on your billing date each month.

**Q: What if I'm on annual and need to upgrade?**  
A: We'll prorate the difference and charge immediately. Your billing date stays the same.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**  
A: No setup fees on any plan. Enterprise custom deployments may have professional services fees.

**Q: Can I pay quarterly?**  
A: Currently monthly and annual only. Quarterly billing coming Q3 2026.

**Q: What currencies do you accept?**  
A: USD primary. EUR, GBP, CAD, AUD supported with automatic conversion.
