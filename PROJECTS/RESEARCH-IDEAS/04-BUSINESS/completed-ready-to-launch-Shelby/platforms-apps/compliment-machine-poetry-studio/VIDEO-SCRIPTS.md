## 🎬 Production Notes

### Visual Style (Both Products):
- **Intimate, close-up shots** of real emotions
- **Phone screens** showing the products in action
- **Before/After** emotional states (not just features)
- **User testimonials** as text overlays
- **Minimal, clean UI** showcase

### Music:
- **Compliment Machine:** Warm, hopeful piano building to uplift
- **Poetry Studio:** Emotional, contemplative strings/piano

- "60 SECONDS TO FEEL SEEN" (Compliment Machine)
- "FREE RIGHT NOW" (Compliment Machine)
- "WORDS THAT HEAL" (Poetry Studio)
