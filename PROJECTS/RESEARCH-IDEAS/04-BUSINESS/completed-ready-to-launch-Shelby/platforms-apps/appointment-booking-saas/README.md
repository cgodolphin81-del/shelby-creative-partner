# BookFlow - Appointment Booking SaaS Platform

## Executive Summary

**BookFlow** is a modern appointment booking and scheduling SaaS platform designed to compete with Calendly, Acuity, and Cal.com. Built for consultants, agencies, healthcare providers, and educational institutions, BookFlow combines powerful scheduling automation with seamless calendar synchronization, payment processing, and team management capabilities.

### Vision
To become the most developer-friendly and customizable appointment booking platform, offering enterprise-grade features at SMB-friendly prices.

### Mission
Eliminate scheduling friction for professionals and teams worldwide through intelligent automation, beautiful booking experiences, and seamless integrations.

## Product Overview

### Core Value Proposition

1. **50% Faster Setup** - Pre-built templates and one-click integrations
2. **40% Cost Savings** - Competitive pricing with more features included
3. **Developer-First** - Comprehensive API and webhook ecosystem
4. **White-Label Ready** - Full branding customization at all tiers
5. **AI-Powered** - Smart scheduling suggestions and automated follow-ups

### Key Differentiators

| Feature | BookFlow | Calendly | Cal.com |
|---------|----------|----------|---------|
| Starting Price | Free | Free | Free |
| Pro Tier | $8/mo | $12/mo | $10/mo |
| Team Tier | $20/mo | $20/mo | $18/mo |
| API Access | All tiers | Enterprise | All tiers |
| White-Label | Pro+ | Enterprise | Pro+ |
| Payment Integration | All paid | Paid add-on | Included |
| Custom Branding | All tiers | Limited | All tiers |
| AI Scheduling | Included | Add-on | Limited |
