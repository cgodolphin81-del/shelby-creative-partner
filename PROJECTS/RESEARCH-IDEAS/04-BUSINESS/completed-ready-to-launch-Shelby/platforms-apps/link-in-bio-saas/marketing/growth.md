## 📈 Growth Loops

### Loop 1: Viral Watermarks (Primary)

```
User creates free page
        │
        ▼
Page includes "Powered by LinkFlow"
        │
        ▼
Visitors see watermark on creator's page
        │
        ▼
Curious visitors click watermark
        │
        ▼
Land on LinkFlow signup page
        │
        ▼
New user creates their own page
        │
        └──────────────┘
```

**Metrics:**
- Watermark CTR: Target 3-5%
- Conversion Rate: Target 20-30%
- Viral Coefficient (k): Target 0.3-0.5

**Optimization:**
- A/B test watermark design
- Test placement (bottom vs. floating)
- Test messaging ("Create your own" vs. "Get your link")
- Add urgency ("Free for limited time")

### Loop 2: Template Marketplace

```
Designer creates template
        │
        ▼
Template listed in marketplace
        │
        ▼
Users browse and purchase templates
        │
        ▼
Designer earns 70% revenue
        │
        ▼
Designer promotes their templates
        │
        ▼
New users discover LinkFlow
        │
        └──────────────┘
```

**Metrics:**
- Templates launched: 500 Year 1
- Template purchases: 2,000/month
- Designer acquisition: 50 active designers

### Loop 3: Product Virality

```
Creator sells product via LinkFlow
        │
        ▼
Customer receives product + receipt
        │
        ▼
Receipt includes "Sell with LinkFlow"
        │
        ▼
Customer (often also a creator) signs up
        │
        ▼
New creator starts selling
        │
        └──────────────┘
```

**Metrics:**
- Products sold: 10,000 Year 1
- Customer-to-creator conversion: 5%
- Viral coefficient: 0.1-0.2

### Loop 4: Content SEO

```
Publish SEO content
        │
        ▼
Rank for creator keywords
        │
        ▼
Organic traffic to blog
        │
        ▼
Blog CTAs to signup
        │
        ▼
New users from organic
        │
        └──────────────┘
```

**Metrics:**

## 🎯 Growth Experiments

### Experiment Backlog (Priority Order)

| Experiment | Hypothesis | Impact | Effort | Priority |
|------------|------------|--------|--------|----------|
| Watermark A/B Test | Better CTA = 2x CTR | High | Low | P0 |
| Onboarding Flow | Guided setup = 20% higher activation | High | Medium | P0 |
| Pricing Page | Annual default = 30% more annual | Medium | Low | P1 |
| Referral Program | Give/Get = 15% referral rate | High | Medium | P1 |
| Exit-Intent Popup | Offer = 10% email capture | Medium | Low | P2 |
| Template Marketplace | 500 templates = 10% revenue | High | High | P1 |
| Affiliate Program | 30% commission = 100 partners | High | Medium | P1 |
| Mobile App | App users = 2x engagement | High | High | P2 |

### Testing Framework

```
1. Hypothesis: Clear statement of expected outcome
2. Metric: Primary success metric
3. Baseline: Current performance
4. Target: Goal for improvement
5. Duration: Test length (min. 2 weeks)
6. Sample Size: Minimum users for significance
7. Results: Win/Lose/Inconclusive
8. Learnings: Document regardless of outcome
```

## 📈 Retention Strategy

### Activation Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Page Created | 80% | Within 24 hours of signup |
| First Link Added | 70% | Within 24 hours |
| Page Published | 60% | Within 7 days |
| First Share | 50% | Within 7 days |
| First Click | 40% | Within 14 days |
| First Sale/Tip | 15% | Within 30 days |

### Retention Tactics

#### Day 1-7: Onboarding
- Welcome email series (5 emails)
- In-app onboarding checklist
- Template suggestions
- Quick win: "Add your first link"

#### Day 8-30: Activation
- "Share your page" nudge
- Analytics summary (first week)
- Product setup guide
- Success story emails

#### Day 31-90: Monetization
- "Make your first sale" guide
- Tip jar setup tutorial
- Email capture best practices
- Case studies of earning creators

#### Day 91+: Retention
- Monthly analytics reports
- New feature announcements
- Creator spotlights
- Re-engagement campaigns (at-risk users)

### Churn Reduction

| Churn Reason | Solution | Expected Impact |
|--------------|----------|-----------------|
| No value realized | Better onboarding | -30% churn |
| Price sensitivity | Annual discount | -20% churn |
| Missing features | Roadmap transparency | -15% churn |
| Technical issues | Better support | -25% churn |
| Outgrew platform | Enterprise tier | -10% churn |
