## 🎁 Persona-Specific Onboarding

### Influencer Onboarding Flow
1. **Sign up:** Instagram/TikTok login option
2. **Import:** Auto-pull social profiles
3. **Template:** "Influencer" template pre-selected
4. **Quick Links:** Pre-populate with common links (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube)
5. **Monetization Tip:** "Add a Tip Jar to let followers support you"
6. **First Win:** Share your LinkFlow page on Instagram Stories

### Musician Onboarding Flow
1. **Sign up:** Email or Spotify login
2. **Import:** Auto-find artist profiles on streaming platforms
3. **Template:** "Musician" template pre-selected
4. **Quick Links:** Pre-populate Spotify, Apple Music, Bandcamp
5. **Monetization Tip:** "Sell exclusive tracks directly to fans"
6. **First Win:** Add your latest release

### Digital Creator Onboarding Flow
1. **Sign up:** Email with business context
2. **Import:** Connect YouTube, podcast platforms
3. **Template:** "Coach/Creator" template pre-selected
4. **Quick Links:** Pre-populate with course/coaching links
5. **Monetization Tip:** "Create a digital product in 5 minutes"
6. **First Win:** Launch your first product

### Small Business Onboarding Flow
1. **Sign up:** Simple email signup
2. **Import:** Connect Instagram, Facebook
3. **Template:** "Business" template pre-selected
4. **Quick Links:** Pre-populate with website, contact, location
5. **Monetization Tip:** "Capture customer emails for promotions"
6. **First Win:** Add your bestselling product

## 🚫 Anti-Personas (Who We're NOT For)

### 1. Casual Users
- Just want to share a few links
- No monetization goals
- Won't pay for features
- **Alternative:** Free Linktree, Carrd

### 2. Enterprise E-commerce
- Need full online store
- Complex inventory management
- Need advanced shipping, taxes
- **Alternative:** Shopify, WooCommerce

### 3. Pure Bloggers
- Primary goal is content, not commerce
- Need CMS, not link page
- **Alternative:** Substack, WordPress

### 4. Non-Monetizing Hobbyists
- No intention to earn money
- Happy with free tools
- **Alternative:** Free tier of any platform
