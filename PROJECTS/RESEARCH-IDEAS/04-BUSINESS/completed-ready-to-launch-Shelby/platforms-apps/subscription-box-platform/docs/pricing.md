# Pricing Tiers

## Overview

BoxFlow offers flexible pricing designed to scale with your business. Our pricing combines a base SaaS fee with a small percentage of Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) to align our success with yours.

## FAQ

**Q: What counts as GMV?**
A: GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) is the total value of all subscription boxes shipped in a month, before fees, discounts, or returns.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my subscriber limit?**
A: You'll be charged the overage rate ($0.10-$0.20/sub) or automatically upgraded to the next plan.

**Q: Are there setup fees?**
A: No setup fees for standard plans. Enterprise custom integrations may have one-time development fees.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for startups?**
A: Yes! Contact us for startup pricing if you're pre-Series A.
