## Sales Process

### Self-Serve (Starter/Growth)

**Flow:**
1. Website visit
2. Free trial signup (14 days)
3. Onboarding email sequence
4. Product-led growth
5. Conversion to paid
6. Expansion/upgrade

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Trial signup rate: 5% of visitors
- Trial-to-paid: 25%
- Time to convert: 7-14 days
- CAC: $200-400

---

### Sales-Assisted (Professional)

**Flow:**
1. Demo request
2. Discovery call (30 min)
3. Custom demo (45 min)
4. Proposal sent
5. Negotiation
6. Close

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Demo request rate: 2% of visitors
- Demo-to-close: 30%
- Sales cycle: 2-4 weeks
- CAC: $1,000-2,000

---

### Enterprise Sales

**Flow:**
1. Inbound/outbound lead
2. Discovery call
3. Technical deep-dive
4. Security review
5. Executive alignment
6. Contract negotiation
7. Legal review
8. Close

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Lead-to-opportunity: 40%
- Opportunity-to-close: 25%
- Sales cycle: 3-6 months
- CAC: $10,000-20,000

## Key Metrics & KPIs

### Acquisition Metrics

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** Target < $1,500
- **CAC by Channel:** Track per channel
- **Conversion Rate:** Visitor → Trial → Paid
- **Sales Cycle Length:** Days from lead to close
- **Pipeline Velocity:** Deal value × win rate ÷ cycle length

### Efficiency Metrics

- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** Target 3:1 or higher
- **CAC Payback Period:** Target < 12 months
- **Magic Number:** (Quarterly revenue growth × 4) ÷ sales & marketing spend
- **Rule of 40:** Growth rate + profit margin > 40%
