# Case Study: GlowBox Beauty

## Company Overview

**Company:** GlowBox Beauty
**Founded:** 2023
**Location:** Los Angeles, CA
**Vertical:** Beauty & Cosmetics
**BoxFlow Customer Since:** January 2024

---

## The Challenge

GlowBox Beauty launched with a simple idea: deliver curated premium beauty samples to subscribers monthly. However, within 6 months, they hit major operational walls:

**Problems:**
- Manual subscription management in spreadsheets
- Payment failures causing 15% involuntary churn
- No customer portal for subscription management
- Fulfillment errors at 12% rate
- No visibility into inventory or forecasting
- Customer support overwhelmed (48+ hour response times)

**Impact:**
- Churn rate: 8% monthly (96% annual)
- Customer satisfaction: 2.8/5
- Operational costs: 35% of revenue
- Growth stalled at 800 subscribers

---

## The Solution

GlowBox migrated to BoxFlow in January 2024, implementing:

### 1. Subscription Management
- Automated recurring billing with Stripe
- Dunning management for failed payments
- Self-service customer portal
- Pause, skip, and modify functionality

### 2. Inventory & Fulfillment
- Real-time inventory tracking
- Integration with ShipBob (3PL partner)
- Automated pick lists and packing slips
- Barcode scanning for accuracy

### 3. Customer Experience
- Branded customer portal
- Preference quiz for personalization
- Automated shipping notifications
- In-app support chat

### 4. Analytics & Insights
- Churn prediction dashboard
- Cohort retention analysis
- Product performance tracking
- Customer lifetime value modeling

## Lessons Learned

### What Worked Well
- **Phased rollout:** Testing with 10% of customers first caught issues early
- **Customer communication:** Transparent migration emails reduced confusion
- **Team training:** Investing in team onboarding paid dividends
- **Data migration:** Clean data before migration prevented problems

### Challenges Overcome
- **Initial resistance:** Some team members resistant to change → addressed with training
- **Custom field mapping:** Legacy data didn't match BoxFlow schema → custom migration script
- **3PL integration:** ShipBob API had quirks → BoxFlow support helped resolve
