## BEST PRACTICES

### Do:
- ✓ Keep it mysterious but not frustrating
- ✓ Provide enough hint to build curiosity
- ✓ Make the CTA clear (subscribe, turn on notifications)
- ✓ Tease across multiple platforms
- ✓ Follow through on promises (launch on time!)

### Don't:
- ✗ Reveal too much (ruins surprise)
- ✗ Be too vague (no one engages)
- ✗ Forget to include launch date
- ✗ Tease multiple times per day (spammy)
- ✗ Delay Episode 057 after building anticipation
