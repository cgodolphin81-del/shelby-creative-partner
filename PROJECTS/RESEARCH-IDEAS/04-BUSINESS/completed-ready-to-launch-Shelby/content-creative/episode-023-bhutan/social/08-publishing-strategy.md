## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** "This is AI-generated, not real travel"
**Response:** "AI is our tool for visualization. The research, culture, and philosophy are deeply real. We're transparent about our methods while honoring the authenticity of Bhutan's story."

**Issue:** "Bhutan tourism is exploitative"
**Response:** "We advocate for responsible, respectful travel. Bhutan's 'high value, low volume' model is designed to preserve culture and environment. We encourage travelers to follow local guidelines and support local communities."

**Issue:** "Gross National Happiness is propaganda"
**Response:** "GNH is a legitimate framework studied by economists worldwide. Like any system, it has complexities. We present it as one alternative model, not a perfect solution."

**Response Protocol:**
1. Acknowledge concern respectfully
2. Provide context and nuance
3. Invite continued dialogue
4. Escalate to main agent if needed
