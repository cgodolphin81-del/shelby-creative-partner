## 📊 Key Statistics (Sahara)

- **Size:** 9.2 million km² (3.6 million sq mi)
- **Countries:** 11 (Algeria, Chad, Egypt, Libya, Mali, Mauritania, Morocco, Niger, Sudan, Tunisia, Western Sahara)
- **Sand Coverage:** Only 25% (rest is rock, gravel, mountains)
- **Highest Point:** Emi Koussi, 3,415m (Chad)
- **Lowest Point:** Qattara Depression, -133m (Egypt)
- **Temperature Range:** 0°C (winter night) to 58°C (summer day)
- **Best Time to Visit:** March-May, September-November
- **Indigenous Peoples:** Tuareg (2-3 million), Berber, Bedouin, Tebu

## 🎙️ Audio Script Highlights

**Opening Lines:**
> "I have walked through forests that breathed around me. I have walked along coastlines where the ocean's voice never ceased. I have walked mountain paths where each step was a negotiation with stone and gravity. But nothing prepared me for this."

**Core Metaphor:**
> "The Sahara needs no architecture. It *is* architecture. The dunes are its pillars. The sky is its dome. The silence is its hymn."

**Key Quote:**
> "The desert does not care if you understand. It does not care if you remember. It will be here long after you are gone. Shifting grain by grain. Dune by dune. In its own slow time. And that, perhaps, is the greatest gift of all."

**Closing:**
> "The sand cathedral remains. And I am grateful to have been allowed, briefly, to stand inside it."

## 🌍 Travel Information

### Best Entry Points:
- **Morocco (Merzouga):** Most accessible, best for first-timers
- **Egypt (Cairo + White Desert):** Combine with pyramids
- **Algeria (Djanet):** Tassili n'Ajjer rock art (advanced)

### Best Time:
- **Spring (March-May):** 25-30°C days, ideal
- **Autumn (September-November):** 25-35°C days, ideal
- **Winter (December-February):** Cool days, cold nights
- **Summer (June-August):** Avoid if possible (45-50°C)

### Costs (3-5 days, per person):
- **Budget:** $300-550
- **Mid-Range:** $640-1,140
- **Luxury:** $1,430-3,150+
