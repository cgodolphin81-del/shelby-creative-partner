## TEXTURES

### Sand Textures

**Fine Sand (Erg Chebbi):**
- Smooth, flowing lines
- Wind-rippled surfaces
- Soft gradients
- Minimal harsh edges
- Reference: "silk draped over dunes"

**Coarse Sand/Gravel:**
- Visible individual grains
- Mixed with small stones
- Rougher texture
- More contrast
- Reference: "crushed stone and sand"

**Wind Ripples:**
- Parallel wave patterns
- Consistent spacing (2-5cm)
- Soft shadows in troughs
- Directional (shows wind direction)
- Reference: "corduroy fabric, organic"

### Rock Textures

**Sandstone (Tassili n'Ajjer):**
- Layered sedimentary bands
- Weathered, eroded surfaces
- Warm red-orange tones
- Pitted and pocked
- Reference: "ancient, water-worn"

**Volcanic Rock (Ahaggar/Aïr):**
- Dark gray to black
- Rough, jagged surfaces
- Crystalline structures visible
- Sharp edges
- Reference: " cooled lava, brutal"

**Limestone (White Desert, Egypt):**
- Chalky white surfaces
- Smooth, wind-polished
- Surreal, sculptural forms
- Soft edges
- Reference: "chalk sculptures, alien"

### Cultural Textures

**Tagelmust (Indigo Fabric):**
- Woven cotton texture
- Faded, worn areas
- Indigo staining visible
- Soft, draped folds
- Reference: "lived-in, sacred"

**Mud Brick (Kasbah):**
- Hand-formed, irregular
- Cracked, weathered surfaces
- Straw/organic matter visible
- Earth tones (ochre, sienna)
- Reference: "ancient, hand-built"

**Leather (Tuareg Crafts):**
- Tooled, embossed patterns
- Worn smooth in places
- Rich brown tones
- Geometric designs
- Reference: "generations of use"

**Woven Textiles:**
- Berber rug patterns
- Geometric, tribal designs
- Natural dyes (muted tones)
- Visible weave structure
- Reference: "handwoven, ancestral"

## LIGHTING

### Time of Day Lighting

**Golden Hour (Sunrise/Sunset):**
- **Direction:** Low, raking light
- **Color Temperature:** 2800-3500K (warm)
- **Shadows:** Long, soft-edged
- **Contrast:** High (bright highlights, deep shadows)
- **Mood:** Warm, hopeful, contemplative
- **Best For:** Dune photography, portraits, emotional scenes

**Blue Hour (Twilight):**
- **Direction:** Diffuse, no direct sun
- **Color Temperature:** 8000-12000K (cool blue)
- **Shadows:** Soft, minimal
- **Contrast:** Low (even lighting)
- **Mood:** Mysterious, peaceful, transitional
- **Best For:** Camp scenes, silhouettes, introspective moments

**Midday:**
- **Direction:** Overhead, harsh
- **Color Temperature:** 5500K (neutral)
- **Shadows:** Short, hard-edged
- **Contrast:** Very high
- **Mood:** Harsh, unforgiving, intense
- **Best For:** Showing heat, emptiness, challenge

**Night (Moonlit):**
- **Direction:** Depends on moon position
- **Color Temperature:** 4000K (moon) + star field
- **Shadows:** Soft, blue-tinted
- **Contrast:** Medium (moonlit areas vs. deep shadow)
- **Mood:** Mystical, vast, cosmic
- **Best For:** Stargazing, campfire scenes, contemplation

**Night (Campfire):**
- **Direction:** From fire outward
- **Color Temperature:** 2000K (very warm)
- **Shadows:** Flickering, dynamic
- **Contrast:** High (fire glow vs. darkness)
- **Mood:** Intimate, ancient, communal
- **Best For:** Human moments, storytelling, warmth

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds (Desert Adaptation)

**Horizon Placement:**
- **Sky Emphasis:** Horizon on bottom third (emphasizes vastness of sky)
- **Land Emphasis:** Horizon on top third (emphasizes dune patterns)
- **Balance:** Horizon centered (for symmetry, minimalism)

**Subject Placement:**
- **Tiny Human:** Place at intersection point (emphasizes scale)
- **Dune Crest:** Follow leading lines to vanishing point
- **Oasis:** Center frame (as focal point in emptiness)

### Leading Lines

**Dune Crests:**
- Use sharp dune edges to lead eye through frame
- S-curves create flow
- Diagonal lines create tension/movement

**Footprints:**
- Trail from foreground into distance
- Creates narrative (journey)
- Disappearing over crest = mystery

**Caravan/Camel Lines:**
- Single file = rhythm, repetition
- Diagonal across frame = movement
- Silhouetted = iconic

### Framing

**Natural Frames:**
- Dune hollow (amphitheater effect)
- Rock arch (Tassili formations)
- Palm fronds (oasis framing)

**Negative Space:**
- Leave 60-80% of frame as sky or sand
- Emphasizes isolation, vastness
- Subject becomes small, meaningful

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Cinematic, film-like quality
- Warm color grading (golden hour bias)

## SPECIFIC VISUAL REFERENCES

### Dune Shapes

**Barchan Dunes:**
- Crescent-shaped
- Horns point downwind
- Isolated, migrating
- Reference: "classic dune shape"

**Linear Dunes:**
- Long, parallel ridges
- Can stretch for kilometers
- Wind from multiple directions
- Reference: "sand waves, organized"

**Star Dunes:**
- Pyramid-shaped
- Multiple arms
- Wind from all directions
- Reference: "mountain of sand"

### Sky References

**Milky Way Core:**
- Thick, luminous band
- Visible dust lanes (dark areas)
- Pink/red nebulae hints
- Reference: "long exposure astrophotography"

**Sunset Clouds:**
- Wispy cirrus (high altitude)
- Catch last light
- Orange/pink/purple
- Reference: "painted sky"

**Clear Desert Sky:**
- Deep, saturated blue
- No haze (low humidity)
- Stars visible even in twilight
- Reference: "pristine, high altitude"

## MOOD BOARD COLLAGE NOTES

### Create These Combinations:

**1. Vastness + Smallness**
- Wide dune landscape (80% of frame)
- Tiny human silhouette (20% or less)
- Emphasizes scale, solitude

**2. Ancient + Present**
- Rock art detail (left side)
- Modern traveler (right side)
- Same sky above both
- Emphasizes continuity

**3. Fire + Stars**
- Campfire glow (bottom third)
- Milky Way (top two-thirds)
- Human figure between
- Emphasizes connection (earth to cosmos)

**4. Emptiness + Life**
- Vast sand (background)
- Single acacia tree (mid-ground)
- Tuareg guide (foreground)
- Emphasizes resilience

**5. Motion + Stillness**
- Wind-blown sand (motion blur)
- Static rock formation (sharp)
- Human figure (still, observing)
- Emphasizes permanence vs. change

## COLOR GRADING PRESETS

### Lightroom/Photoshop Direction

**Base Adjustments:**
- Warmth: +10 to +20 (golden hour bias)
- Tint: Slight magenta (desert glow)
- Exposure: Slightly underexpose (preserve highlights)
- Contrast: +15 (drama)
- Highlights: -20 (recover sky detail)
- Shadows: +10 (lift dune detail)
- Whites: -10 (soft highlights)
- Blacks: -5 (deep but not crushed)

**Color Adjustments:**
- Orange saturation: +10 (sand warmth)
- Yellow saturation: +5 (sunlight)
- Blue saturation: +15 (sky depth)
- Blue luminance: -10 (deeper sky)
- Purple saturation: +10 (twilight)

**Effects:**
- Clarity: +10 (texture)
- Dehaze: +5 (atmospheric clarity)
- Vignette: -10 (subtle, focus center)
- Grain: +5 (film feel, optional)
