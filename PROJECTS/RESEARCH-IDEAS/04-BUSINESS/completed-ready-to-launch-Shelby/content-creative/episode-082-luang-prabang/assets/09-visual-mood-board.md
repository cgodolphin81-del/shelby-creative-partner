## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headlines)
**Playfair Display** (Serif, elegant, traditional)
- Use: Episode titles, quotes, headers
- Weights: Regular, Bold, Italic
- Feeling: Timeless, sacred, editorial

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Lato** or **Open Sans** (Sans-serif, clean, readable)
- Use: Body text, captions, descriptions
- Weights: Light, Regular, Bold
- Feeling: Modern, approachable, clear

### Accent Font (Special Elements)
**Cinzel** or **Cormorant Garamond** (Decorative, ceremonial)
- Use: Episode number, special quotes, title treatments
- Weights: Regular, Bold
- Feeling: Ceremonial, ancient, important

## IMAGE STYLES

### Photography Aesthetic

**Overall Mood:**
- Warm, golden hour lighting
- Soft shadows, gentle contrast
- Slightly desaturated greens
- Enhanced gold and orange tones
- Film-like grain (subtle)
- Contemplative, not touristy

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds for landscapes
- Leading lines (temple steps, river paths)
- Negative space for quotes
- Close-ups on details (hands, fabric, carvings)
- Wide shots for scale (temples, waterfalls)

**Editing Style:**
- Warm color temperature (5500-6500K)
- Slight fade in shadows
- Soft highlights
- Gentle clarity (not over-sharpened)
- Vignette for focus (subtle)

---

### Specific Shot Types

**Tak Bat Ceremony:**
- Silhouette shots against dawn sky
- Distant, respectful framing
- Focus on robes, bowls, hands
- Motion blur for walking monks
- Violet and orange color grade

**Temples:**
- Golden hour exterior shots
- Detail shots of gold leaf, carvings
- Interior with incense and candles
- Symmetrical compositions
- Warm, glowing light

**Kuang Si Falls:**
- Wide shots showing tiers
- Underwater shots (if possible)
- Action shots (jumping, swimming)
- Turquoise color enhancement
- Lush green framing

**Daily Life:**
- Candid market scenes
- Food preparation
- Textile weaving
- Riverside activities
- Natural, unposed moments

**Shelby (Host):**
- Contemplative poses
- Back to camera (viewing scenes)
- Respectful distance from ceremonies
- Natural light portraits
- Authentic, not staged

## VIDEO MOTION GRAPHICS

### Opening Sequence (5-10 seconds)

**Visuals:**
1. Fade in: Dawn sky gradient
2. Silhouette of temple spires
3. Gold leaf particles floating
4. Episode title appears (Playfair Display)
5. Subtle glow effect

**Audio:**
- Temple bell (single strike)
- Gentle ambient pad
- River flowing underneath

**Animation:**
- Slow, contemplative pace
- Smooth fades and dissolves
- No quick cuts
- Meditative rhythm

---

### Lower Thirds

**Style:**
- Semi-transparent dark overlay
- Gold accent line
- White text (Lato Regular)
- Fade in/out (0.5 seconds)

**Content:**
- Location names
- Key quotes
- Episode number

---

### Transitions

**Cross-Dissolve:**
- Standard transition between scenes
- Duration: 1-2 seconds
- Feeling: Gentle, flowing

**Gold Particle Fade:**
- For sacred/temple moments
- Gold particles float across screen
- Duration: 1.5 seconds

**Water Ripple:**
- For Kuang Si/waterfall scenes
- Ripple effect over image
- Duration: 1 second

**Light Leak:**
- For dawn/sunset transitions
- Warm orange/yellow light
- Duration: 1-2 seconds

## BRAND CONSISTENCY

### Logo Usage

**Primary Logo:**
- Placement: Bottom right or center bottom
- Size: Visible but not dominant
- Color: White or gold (depending on background)

**Watermark:**
- Opacity: 30-50%
- Placement: Corner
- Use: Photos, video content

---

### Voice & Tone

**Visual Tone:**
- Contemplative, not frantic
- Sacred, not touristy
- Authentic, not staged
- Warm, not cold
- Respectful, not exploitative

**Color Consistency:**
- Always use defined palette
- No neon or oversaturated colors
- Maintain warm overall tone
- Gold and saffron as signatures

---

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

- Text on backgrounds: 4.5:1 minimum ratio
- Avoid saffron on gold (low contrast)
- Use dark overlays under light text
- Test with color blindness simulators

### Alt Text

- Describe all images for screen readers
- Include location, subject, mood
- Example: "Monk in saffron robe walking at dawn in Luang Prabang, Laos"

### Captions

- All video content: Closed captions
- Accurate transcription
- Speaker identification
- Sound effect descriptions
