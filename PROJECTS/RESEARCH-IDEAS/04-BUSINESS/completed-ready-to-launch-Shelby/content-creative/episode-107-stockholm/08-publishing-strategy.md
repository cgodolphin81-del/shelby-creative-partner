## Pre-Launch Phase (7 Days Before)

### Day -7: Tease Campaign Begins

**Instagram Stories:**
- Post 1-2 teaser stories per day
- Content: Stockholm photos, short quotes, "Something's coming..."
- Use countdown sticker for launch day

**Twitter/X:**
- Thread teaser: "I've been walking through a city that befriends the darkness..."
- 3-4 tweets over the week
- Engage with Stockholm/travel accounts

**Email List:**
- Send "Coming Soon" email (light touch)
- Subject: "Next week: A city of islands and light 🇸🇪"
- Include one evocative image, no links yet

### Day -5: Behind-the-Scenes Content

**Instagram Feed:**
- BTS photo (recording setup, Stockholm location, research notes)
- Caption: "Recording Episode 107 in Stockholm. This one... this one is special."

**LinkedIn:**
- Post about research process
- "What I learned studying Swedish culture for 40+ hours..."
- Position as thoughtful, well-researched content

**TikTok/Reels:**
- 15-second teaser clip
- Best visual moment + hook
- "Full episode drops [DATE]"

### Day -3: Quote Graphics

**Instagram/Pinterest:**
- 3-5 quote graphics from the episode
- "You do not conquer the dark. You fill it with warmth."
- "Lagom: Just the right amount."
- "The light always returns. Always."

**Twitter:**
- Share one quote per day
- Simple text or image

### Day -2: Countdown Intensifies

**All Platforms:**
- "2 days until Episode 107"
- Share episode artwork
- Reminder of launch time

**Email List:**
- Send "Tomorrow" email
- Subject: "Tomorrow: Stockholm 🇸🇪"
- Include full episode description, links ready

### Day -1: Final Tease

**Instagram Stories:**
- Countdown sticker (24 hours)
- Share what you're most excited about
- Q&A sticker: "Ask me about Stockholm!"

**Twitter:**
- "Tomorrow. 9 AM ET. Episode 107: The Northern Light."
- Pin this tweet

**All Platforms:**
- Consistent messaging: "Tomorrow"
- Build anticipation without overdoing it

## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Spotify

**Optimization:**
- Compelling episode description (first 200 characters critical)
- Chapter markers for key sections:
  - 0:00 Opening
  - 1:30 Gamla Stan
  - 5:00 Vasa Museum
  - 8:00 Archipelago
  - 11:30 Lagom & Fika
  - 14:30 Light & Darkness
  - 17:30 Closing

**Spotify-Specific:**
- Submit for Spotify editorial consideration (2+ weeks before launch)
- Use Spotify for Podcasters analytics
- Create audiogram clips for social

**Playlists:**
- Pitch to travel podcast playlists
- Create your own "Nordic Journeys" playlist
- Add Episode 107 + similar episodes

### Apple Podcasts

**Optimization:**
- Keywords in title/description: Stockholm, Sweden, Travel, Nordic
- Encourage ratings/reviews in episode
- Respond to all reviews (shows engagement)

**Charts:**
- Launch timing matters (Tuesday-Thursday mornings)
- Concentrate listens in first 48 hours for chart impact
- Email list is crucial for initial spike

### YouTube

**If Audio-Only:**
- Static image with waveform animation
- Full show notes in description
- Chapters/timestamps

**If Video Version:**
- B-roll of Stockholm locations
- Subtitles/captions (critical for accessibility + SEO)
- End screen linking to subscribe + previous episode

**SEO:**
- Title: "Stockholm, Sweden: The Northern Light | Episode 107"
- Description: Full show notes + links
- Tags: Stockholm, Sweden, Travel Podcast, Nordic, Scandinavia, Gamla Stan, Vasa Museum

### Instagram

**Feed:**
- 1-2 posts in launch week
- High-quality visuals (Stockholm is photogenic)
- Long-form captions perform well for travel content

**Stories:**
- Daily during launch week
- Use all interactive stickers (polls, Q&A, quizzes)
- Link sticker to episode

**Reels:**
- 2-3 reels in launch week
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Trending audio + Stockholm visuals

**Engagement:**
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Engage with travel/Stockholm accounts
- Use location tags (Stockholm, Gamla Stan, etc.)

### TikTok

**Content:**
- 3-5 videos in launch week
- Mix of: hooks, quotes, BTS, travel tips
- 15-60 seconds optimal

**Strategy:**
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Text overlays (many watch without sound)
- Use trending sounds when relevant
- Hashtags: #Stockholm #Sweden #Travel #Podcast #TravelTok

### LinkedIn

**Positioning:**
- Professional angle: leadership lessons, work-life balance, lagom philosophy
- Not "travel podcast" but "wisdom from around the world"

**Content:**
- Long-form posts perform best
- Personal + professional blend
- Engage with comments thoughtfully

**Timing:**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM ET
- Avoid Monday mornings (inbox overload)
- Avoid Fridays (wind-down mode)

### Facebook

**Organic:**
- Share to personal profile
- Relevant groups (travel, podcasts, Sweden enthusiasts)
- Follow group rules (some don't allow self-promo)

**Paid (Optional):**
- $50-100 test budget
- Target: 30-55, travel interests, podcast listeners
- Video ads perform better than static

### Email

**List Segmentation:**
- Send to full list
- Segment engaged listeners for follow-up
- Non-openers get different subject line on resend

**Metrics to Track:**
- Open rate (aim for 25%+)
- Click rate (aim for 3%+)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)

**Follow-Up:**
- Resend to non-openers after 3 days
- Different subject line
- Same content

## Risk Mitigation

### What If...

**Low initial engagement?**
- Double down on email list
- Engage more personally with early commenters
- Consider paid boost after 48 hours

**Technical issues?**
- Have backup hosting ready
- Test all links before launch
- Communicate transparently if problems arise

**Negative feedback?**
- Respond graciously
- Don't argue publicly
- Learn and improve for next episode

**Stockholm tourism concerns?**
- Acknowledge overtourism if raised
- Emphasize respectful, slow travel
- Share sustainable travel tips from guide
