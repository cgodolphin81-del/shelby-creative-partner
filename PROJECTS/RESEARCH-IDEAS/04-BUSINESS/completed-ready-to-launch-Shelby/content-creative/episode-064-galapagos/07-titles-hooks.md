*Why it works:*
- Evokes mystery and discovery
- Suggests something lost waiting to be found
- Hints at both geographic and historical isolation
- Emotional resonance (forgotten = needs remembering)

## HOOKS (Opening Lines)

### For Podcast/Audio

**Hook 1 (Mystery):**
"Nine hundred kilometers from anywhere, time moves differently..."

**Hook 2 (Direct):**
"There are places on Earth where the rules are rewritten. The Galapagos Islands are such a place."

**Hook 3 (Question):**
"What happens when life is left alone for three million years? The Galapagos show us."

**Hook 4 (Personal):**
"I just met a tortoise that was born before the American Civil War. Here's what it taught me."

**Hook 5 (Darwin):**
"Charles Darwin spent five weeks here and changed how we understand life itself. This is that place."

**Hook 6 (Dramatic):**
"By 1970, the Galapagos were dying. Today, they're recovering. This is how we changed the story."

**Hook 7 (Sensory):**
"Black lava. Blue water. Green life. White sky. And creatures that exist nowhere else on Earth."

**Hook 8 (Intrigue):**
"The only place where penguins live on the equator. Where lizards choose the sea. Where tortoises outlive nations."
