## EPISODE 065 SUGGESTION

**If continuing South American theme:**

**Title:** "Where the Earth Touches the Sky"
**Location:** Salar de Uyuni, Bolivia / Andean Highlands
**Themes:** 
- Human resilience at altitude
- Ancient cultures and modern life
- Salt flats as natural wonder
- Llamas, alpacas, vicuña
- Indigenous wisdom
- The thin air of the high Andes

**Contrast to Galapagos:**
- Islands → Mountains
- Ocean → Salt flats
- Wildlife isolation → Human adaptation
- Evolution → Culture
- Sea level → 4,000+ meters

This creates a natural narrative arc: from natural evolution to human resilience, from isolation to community, from ocean to sky.
