## 📝 CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Something Goes Wrong

**Scenario 1: Factual Error Discovered**
- Acknowledge quickly
- Correct transparently
- Thank the person who caught it
- Update content with note

**Scenario 2: Negative Feedback**
- Listen first, respond second
- Distinguish between constructive criticism and trolling
- Engage with the former, ignore the latter
- Learn and improve

**Scenario 3: Conservation Backlash**
- If criticized for "promoting tourism to fragile place"
- Acknowledge the concern
- Clarify intent: awareness, not exploitation
- Emphasize responsible travel guidelines
- Donate portion of any revenue to conservation

**Principle:** Transparency + Accountability + Action
