## 🪝 Episode Hooks (Opening Lines)

### Primary Hook (Recommended)
**"Have you ever breathed air so alive, it felt like the forest itself was sharing its breath with you?"**

*Why it works:*
- Immediately sensory and immersive
- Creates curiosity (how can air be "alive"?)
- Establishes the intimate, nature-connected tone
- Personal ("you") draws listener in
- Poetic without being pretentious
