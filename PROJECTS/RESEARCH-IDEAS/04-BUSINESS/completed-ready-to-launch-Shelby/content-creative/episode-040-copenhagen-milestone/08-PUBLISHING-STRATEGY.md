## 🚨 CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue: Technical problems (episode doesn't publish)**
- Response: Immediate social post acknowledging issue
- Fix: Work with podcast host
- Follow-up: Thank you for patience, now live

**Issue: Negative feedback/criticism**
- Response: Acknowledge, don't defend
- Action: Learn, improve next episode
- If trolling: Don't engage, delete if necessary

**Issue: Low initial engagement**
- Response: Don't panic, momentum builds
- Action: Increase personal outreach
- Reminder: Quality > vanity metrics

**Issue: Copyright claims (music, images)**
- Prevention: Use licensed/royalty-free only
- Response: Remove/replace immediately
- Learn: Double-check all assets pre-launch
