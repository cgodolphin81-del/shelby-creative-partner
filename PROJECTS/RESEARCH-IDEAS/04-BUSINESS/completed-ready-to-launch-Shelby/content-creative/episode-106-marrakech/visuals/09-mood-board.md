## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Font:** Montserrat or similar geometric sans-serif  
**Weights:** Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600)  
**Use:** Episode titles, main headings, social media graphics  
**Why:** Clean, modern, readable, works well with Arabic-inspired content

### Secondary Font (Body Text)
**Font:** Source Sans Pro or similar humanist sans-serif  
**Weights:** Regular (400), Light (300)  
**Use:** Body text, captions, descriptions  
**Why:** Highly readable, friendly, versatile

### Accent Font (Special Occasions)
**Font:** Playfair Display or similar serif  
**Weights:** Regular (400), Italic (400)  
**Use:** Quotes, poetic text, special callouts  
**Why:** Elegant, contemplative, pairs well with sans-serif

### Arabic-Inspired Decorative (Use Sparingly)
**Font:** Noto Naskh Arabic or similar (for decorative elements only)  
**Use:** Occasional decorative text, cultural authenticity  
**Why:** Adds cultural context, use respectfully and minimally

## Visual Elements & Textures

### Patterns

**Zellige Geometric Patterns**
- Islamic geometric tilework
- Use as: Backgrounds, borders, overlays
- Colors: Majorelle Blue, Tile Green, Sunset Gold, Atlas White
- Opacity: 10-30% for subtle texture

**Moroccan Arch Shapes**
- Horseshoe arches, keyhole arches
- Use as: Frame elements, dividers, graphic accents
- Style: Clean lines, not overly ornate

## Photography Style Guide

### Color Treatment

**Warm, Rich, Authentic**
- Slightly warm white balance (emphasize reds and golds)
- Rich, saturated colors (especially reds, blues, oranges)
- Avoid over-filtering; keep colors true to life
- Golden hour shots prioritized

### Composition

**Medina/Souk:**
- Tight frames, layered depth
- Leading lines through alleyways
- Candid moments, authentic interactions
- Mix of wide (context) and detail (textures) shots

**Jardin Majorelle:**
- Clean, symmetrical compositions
- Bold color contrasts (blue vs. green)
- Architectural lines, geometric patterns
- Peaceful, contemplative mood

**Riads:**
- Symmetry and balance
- Light streaming from above
- Courtyard focal points
- Intimate, peaceful atmosphere

**Atlas Mountains:**
- Wide, epic landscapes
- Human scale (small figures in vast landscape)
- Layered depth (foreground, mid, background)
- Natural, documentary style

**Jemaa el-Fnaa:**
- Dynamic, energetic compositions
- Low light, warm ambient glow
- Motion and life
- Candid, documentary approach

## Mood & Atmosphere

### Words to Evoke

- **Warm** (reds, golds, oranges)
- **Ancient** (textures, patina, aged materials)
- **Vibrant** (spice colors, souk energy)
- **Peaceful** (riad courtyards, gardens, Atlas)
- **Mysterious** (night scenes, medina alleys, shadows)
- **Authentic** (candid moments, real interactions)
- **Sacred** (call to prayer, mosques, spiritual moments)
- **Artistic** (Majorelle Blue, YSL legacy, craftsmanship)

### Words to Avoid

- Overly touristy/clichéd
- Exoticizing or othering language
- Stereotypical "Arabian Nights" tropes
- Overly filtered or artificial aesthetics

---

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Contrast
- Ensure text has sufficient contrast against backgrounds (WCAG AA minimum)
- Avoid red/green combinations for critical information
- Provide alt text for all images

### Readability
- Large, clear fonts for main text
- Avoid text over busy backgrounds
- Use overlays/boxes behind text when needed

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Represent Morocco authentically, not stereotypically
- Respect religious and cultural sites
- Avoid orientalist or exoticizing imagery
- Include diverse representations (locals, travelers, various ages)
