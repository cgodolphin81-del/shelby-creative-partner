## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram
**Goal:** Visual storytelling, community building

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Stunning photography (churches, pilgrims)
- 30% Behind-the-scenes (Shelby exploring, research)
- 20% Quote cards (poetic essay excerpts)
- 10% Engagement (questions, polls)

**Posting Schedule:**
- Feed: 1x/day for first week, then 3x/week
- Stories: Daily for first week, then 3-5x/week
- Reels: 2-3x/week (60-sec highlights)

**Hashtag Strategy:**
```
Primary (5): #Lalibela #Ethiopia #TheInfiniteWalk #SacredPlaces #UNESCO
Secondary (10): #TravelPodcast #Africa #Pilgrimage #History #Faith #Wanderlust #TravelStories #AncientArchitecture #SpiritualTravel #Podcast
```

**Engagement Tactics:**
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Ask questions in captions
- Use poll stickers in Stories
- Share user-generated content (reposts)

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Cultural appropriation concerns
**Response:** "This story is told with deep respect for Ethiopian Orthodox tradition. I consulted multiple sources and aimed to honor the faith and history of Lalibela. If you have concerns, I'd love to hear them."

**Issue:** Factual inaccuracies pointed out
**Response:** "Thank you for catching this. I've updated the show notes with the correct information. Accuracy matters."

**Issue:** Negative reviews
**Response:** (Internal only) Note feedback, improve future episodes. Do not engage publicly unless constructive.

**Issue:** Low engagement
**Response:** Double down on organic engagement. Reach out to micro-influencers. Consider small paid boost.
