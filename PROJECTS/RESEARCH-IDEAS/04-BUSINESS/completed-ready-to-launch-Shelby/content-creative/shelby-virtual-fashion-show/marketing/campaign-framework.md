### Visual Identity (Tease Phase)

**Design Guidelines:**
```
Color Palette:
  - Primary: Deep charcoal (#2D2D2D)
  - Accent: Muted gold (#C9A961)
  - Background: Pure black (#000000)
  - Text: White (#FFFFFF)

Typography:
  - Headlines: Custom serif (mysterious, elegant)
  - Body: Clean sans-serif (modern, readable)

Imagery Style:
  - High contrast
  - Partial reveals (never full garment)
  - Focus on texture and light
  - Abstract compositions

Motion Graphics:
  - Slow, deliberate reveals
  - Light leaks and lens flares
  - Film grain texture
  - Minimal transitions
```
