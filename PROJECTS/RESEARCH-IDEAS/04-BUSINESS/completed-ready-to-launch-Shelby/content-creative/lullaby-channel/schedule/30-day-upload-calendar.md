## Daily Shorts Strategy

### Shorts Content Types
1. **Highlight Clips** (15-30 sec of best lullaby moments)
2. **Parent Tips** (quick sleep advice with music bed)
3. **Behind-the-Scenes** (AI creation process)
4. **Before/After** (fussy baby → sleeping baby)
5. **Lyric Snippets** (animated lyrics from storybook lullabies)

### Shorts Posting Schedule
- **Time:** 12:00 PM EST (lunch break scrolling)
- **Frequency:** Daily (minimum), 2-3x/day ideal
- **Hashtags:** #lullaby #babytips #sleeptraining #newmom #parentinghacks

### Shorts Optimization
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Text overlay (many watch without sound)
- Call-to-action: "Full version on our channel!"
- Link to full video in comments
