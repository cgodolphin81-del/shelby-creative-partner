## Technical Setup

### Email Service Provider Options

#### Option A: ConvertKit (Recommended)
- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers
- **Paid:** $15/month (unlimited subscribers)
- **Pros:** Creator-focused, easy automation, great deliverability
- **Best For:** Content creators, digital products

#### Option B: MailerLite
- **Free Plan:** Up to 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month
- **Paid:** $10/month (unlimited)
- **Pros:** Affordable, good features, easy to use
- **Best For:** Budget-conscious starters

#### Option C: Flodesk
- **Free Trial:** 30 days
- **Paid:** $38/month (unlimited everything)
- **Pros:** Beautiful templates, flat pricing
- **Best For:** Aesthetic-focused brands

### Setup Process (ConvertKit Example)

#### Step 1: Create Account
1. Go to convertkit.com
2. Sign up for free account
3. Complete profile settings

#### Step 2: Create Landing Page
1. Go to Landing Pages → Create New
2. Choose template (simple opt-in)
3. Customize:
   - **Headline:** "Get Your Free Lullaby!"
   - **Subheadline:** "Download 'Moonbeam Dreams' - our most popular sleep song for babies"
   - **Image:** Beautiful lullaby cover art
   - **Form Fields:** Name, Email
   - **Button Text:** "Send Me the Lullaby!"
   - **Privacy Note:** "We never share your email. Unsubscribe anytime."

#### Step 3: Create Email Sequence
1. Go to Sequences → Create New
2. Name: "Welcome Sequence"
3. Add emails (see templates below)
4. Set delays between emails

#### Step 4: Connect Landing Page to Sequence
1. In Landing Page settings
2. Set "After signup" → "Add to sequence"
3. Select "Welcome Sequence"

#### Step 5: Add Lead Magnet Delivery
1. In first email of sequence
2. Attach MP3 file OR
3. Add download link (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.)

### Email #3: Value - Sleep Tips (Day 4)

```
Subject: 3 mistakes parents make at bedtime (and how to fix them)

Hi {{ first_name }},

Let's talk about bedtime mistakes. 

I've made ALL of them. And I bet you have too. No judgment! 

Here are the 3 most common ones I see:

❌ MISTAKE #1: Starting the routine too late

When babies get overtired, their bodies produce cortisol and 
adrenaline. This makes it HARDER to fall asleep, not easier.

✅ FIX: Watch for sleep cues (eye rubbing, ear pulling, fussiness) 
and start the routine BEFORE baby is overtired.

---

❌ MISTAKE #2: Too much stimulation before bed

Bath time is great. But if it turns into splashing and laughing, 
your baby is getting energized, not calm.

✅ FIX: Keep pre-bed activities calm and quiet. Dim the lights, 
speak softly, move slowly.

---

❌ MISTAKE #3: Inconsistent bedtime

Bedtime at 7 PM one night and 9 PM the next confuses your baby's 
internal clock.

✅ FIX: Pick a bedtime and stick to it (within 30 minutes) every 
single night. Even on weekends!

---

BONUS TIP: Use the same lullaby every night. 

Consistency = sleep cues. When your baby hears that specific 
song, their body will start to wind down automatically.

That's why "Moonbeam Dreams" is designed to be played nightly. 
The familiarity is comforting and signals "it's time to sleep."

---
```

## Compliance & Best Practices

### Legal Requirements
- **CAN-SPAM Act (US):** Include physical address, unsubscribe link
- **GDPR (EU):** Explicit consent, data protection
- **CASL (Canada):** Express or implied consent required

### Best Practices
✅ Always use double opt-in (confirms email is valid)
✅ Include unsubscribe link in every email
✅ Never buy email lists
✅ Honor unsubscribe requests immediately
✅ Keep subscriber data secure
✅ Be transparent about email frequency

### List Hygiene
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Re-engage inactive subscribers (90+ days no opens)
• Send "We miss you" email
• Offer incentive to stay
• Remove if no response
- Clean list every 6 months
