## Risk Factors & Mitigation

### Potential Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| YouTube algorithm changes | Medium | High | Diversify to email + other platforms |
| AI music copyright issues | Low | High | Stay updated on regulations, own your prompts |
| Market saturation | Medium | Medium | Focus on quality + unique branding |
| Burnout | High | High | Automate, outsource, set boundaries |
| Platform dependency | High | High | Build owned assets (email, website) |

### Mitigation Strategy
1. **Diversify revenue streams** (don't rely on YouTube alone)
2. **Build email list** (owned audience)
3. **Create evergreen content** (continues earning for years)
4. **Automate where possible** (email sequences, social scheduling)
5. **Reinvest profits** (build buffer for slow months)
