## Template #1: "Classic Moon & Stars"

**Best For:** Traditional lullabies, general sleep music
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Duration:** 3-5 minutes

### Visual Concept
- **Background:** Deep navy blue gradient (#1A1A2E to #2D2D44)
- **Main Element:** Large soft-glowing crescent moon (upper right)
- **Animation:** Twinkling stars (subtle, slow), gentle moon glow pulse
- **Foreground:** Silhouette of sleeping baby (bottom left, subtle)
- **Text:** Song title in white with soft glow

### Technical Specs
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (16:9)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps
- **Animation Style:** Subtle loop (no jarring movements)
- **Color Grade:** Cool tones, soft contrast

### Thumbnail Design
```
Layout:
- Background: Same navy gradient
- Moon: Large, prominent, right side
- Text: "Moonbeam Dreams" in large white font (top center)
- Subtitle: "Gentle Lullaby for Babies" (smaller, below)
- Badge: "3 HR VERSION" in corner (if applicable)
- Branding: Small moon logo in bottom right

Colors:
- Background: #1A1A2E (navy)
- Moon: #F4E7C3 (soft yellow with glow)
- Text: #FFFFFF (white with drop shadow)
- Accent: #6B5B95 (purple stars)

Fonts:
- Title: Nunito Bold, 72pt
- Subtitle: Open Sans, 36pt
```

### Creation Tools
- **Free:** Canva, DaVinci Resolve
- **Paid:** After Effects, Premiere Pro
- **Stock Elements:** Pixabay (stars, moon), Pexels (baby silhouettes)

### Animation Notes
- Stars twinkle on 3-5 second intervals (randomized)
- Moon glow pulses every 8 seconds (subtle, 10% opacity change)
- No camera movement (static shot)
- Loop seamlessly for extended versions

## Thumbnail Best Practices

### Design Rules
1. **Text Size:** Minimum 60pt for main title (readable on mobile)
2. **Contrast:** High contrast between text and background
3. **Faces:** Human/baby faces increase CTR (when appropriate)
4. **Colors:** Use brand colors consistently
5. **Branding:** Small logo in corner (consistent placement)

### A/B Testing
- Test different thumbnail styles
- Track CTR in YouTube Analytics
- Iterate based on performance
- Keep winners as templates

### Thumbnail Checklist
- [ ] Text readable at small size
- [ ] High contrast
- [ ] Brand colors used
- [ ] Logo in consistent position
- [ ] No clutter (simple, clear message)
- [ ] Emotion conveyed (peaceful, sleepy)
- [ ] Accurate to video content
- [ ] No clickbait (maintain trust)
