## Etsy Shop Setup

### Step 1: Create Your Etsy Account

1. Go to **etsy.com/sell**
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Choose account type: **Individual** (start) or **Business** (if LLC)
4. Complete profile:
   - Shop name: **MoonbeamLullabies** (or similar)
   - Shop language: English
   - Shop currency: USD (or your local currency)

## Marketing Your Etsy Shop

### Etsy SEO Optimization

#### Keywords to Use
- Primary: custom lullaby, personalized lullaby, baby lullaby
- Secondary: baby shower gift, newborn gift, personalized baby gift
- Long-tail: custom baby song with name, AI generated lullaby

#### Listing Optimization Tips
1. **Use all 13 tags** on every listing
2. **Repeat keywords** in title, description, and tags
3. **Long titles** (use all 140 characters)
4. **First 40 characters** are most important (show in search)
5. **Update seasonally** (add "Christmas gift" in Q4, etc.)

### Etsy Ads
- **Start small:** $2-5/day
- **Target:** Best-performing listing
- **Monitor:** Cost per click, conversion rate
- **Optimize:** Pause underperforming keywords

### External Marketing

#### Pinterest
- Create pins for each lullaby package
- Link directly to Etsy listing
- Use keywords in pin descriptions
- Join group boards (baby, parenting, gifts)

#### Instagram
- Share customer testimonials (with permission)
- Behind-the-scenes of creation process
- Baby sleep tips (drive traffic to shop)
- Use hashtags: #customlullaby #babyshowergift

#### TikTok
- Show the creation process
- Share customer reactions
- Sleep tips + product mention
- Trending sounds with your twist

## Scaling the Business

### When to Raise Prices
- **Consistent 5+ orders/week** → Raise by $5
- **Waitlist forming** → Raise by $5-10
- **Premium becoming most popular** → Adjust tier pricing

### Adding New Products
- **Sibling bundle:** 2 lullabies for $60 (save $10)
- **Grandparent gift:** Shorter, simpler lullaby ($15)
- **Pet lullaby:** For furry family members ($20)
- **Anniversary lullaby:** For couples ($35)

### Outsourcing
- **Lyric writing:** Hire freelance songwriter ($10-20/song)
- **Cover art:** Hire designer on Fiverr ($5-15/artwork)
- **Customer service:** VA for messages ($5-10/hour)

### Etsy Fees (Calculate into Pricing)
- **Listing fee:** $0.20 per item (4 months or until sold)
- **Transaction fee:** 6.5% of sale price
- **Payment processing:** 3% + $0.25 (US)
- **Offsite ads:** 15% (only if sale comes from Etsy ads)

**Example: $35 Standard Package**
- Sale price: $35.00

- Payment processing (3% + $0.25): -$1.30
