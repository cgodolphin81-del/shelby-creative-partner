## SEO Strategy Overview

### YouTube Algorithm Factors
1. **Watch Time** (most important) - Longer videos = more watch time
2. **CTR (Click-Through Rate)** - Compelling thumbnails and titles
3. **Engagement** - Likes, comments, shares, subscriptions
4. **Session Time** - Keeping viewers on YouTube (playlists help)
5. **Relevance** - Keywords in title, description, tags

### Keyword Research Insights
**High-Volume Keywords:**
- "lullaby" (500K+ monthly searches)
- "baby sleep music" (200K+ monthly searches)
- "bedtime songs" (100K+ monthly searches)
- "sleep music for babies" (150K+ monthly searches)
- "calming music for infants" (50K+ monthly searches)

**Long-Tail Keywords (Lower Competition):**
- "lullaby to make baby sleep fast"
- "gentle music for baby bedtime"
- "soothing songs for newborns"
- "peaceful lullaby for sleeping"
- "calm baby music for night time"
