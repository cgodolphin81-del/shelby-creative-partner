## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

**Historical Inaccuracy:**
- Have sources ready to share
- Acknowledge corrections gracefully
- Update show notes if needed

**Travel Advice Outdated:**
- Note "prices as of March 2026"
- Encourage listeners to verify
- Update blog post as needed

**Negative Feedback:**
- Respond professionally
- Don't argue in comments
- Take valid criticism to heart

**Technical Issues:**
- Have backup audio file ready
- Test all links before posting
- Monitor for broken links first 24 hours
