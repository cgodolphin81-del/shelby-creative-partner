## USAGE NOTES

**Aspect Ratios:**
- `--ar 16:9` - YouTube thumbnails, blog headers, presentations
- `--ar 4:3` - Instagram landscape, web content
- `--ar 3:2` - Standard photography, prints
- `--ar 4:5` - Instagram portrait, mobile
- `--ar 1:1` - Square format, social media
- `--ar 3:4` - Vertical compositions

**Style Notes:**
- All prompts optimized for Midjourney v6
- Adjust `--v` parameter for your preferred version
- Add `--stylize 250` for more artistic interpretation
- Add `--quality 2` for highest quality renders

**Ethical Considerations:**
- When depicting indigenous people, ensure respectful representation
- Consider working with indigenous artists for authentic cultural imagery
- Use these as inspiration, not replacement for real photography
- Support indigenous communities when using their cultural imagery
