## Technical Specifications

### Image Ratios
- **16:9** - Primary format for social media, YouTube thumbnails, blog headers
- **9:16** - Vertical format for Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels
- **4:5** - Instagram feed posts, Pinterest pins
- **21:9** - Panoramic landscapes, website banners

### Style Notes
- **Photorealistic** - Aim for National Geographic quality
- **Cinematic** - Dramatic lighting, epic scale
- **Atmospheric** - Mist, clouds, mood
- **Natural Light** - Golden hour preferred
- **Human Element** - Include people for scale and story

### AI Generation Tips
- Use negative prompts: "blurry, low quality, distorted, unrealistic colors"
- Specify "photorealistic, 8K, ultra-detailed"
- Add "National Geographic style" for quality
- Include aspect ratio in every prompt
- For consistent character (Shelby), use same description each time

### Usage Recommendations
- **Social Media:** Prompts 1, 16, 26, 44, 45 (most dramatic)
- **Blog Headers:** Prompts 4, 13, 36, 40 (wide panoramic)
- **Quote Cards:** Prompts 2, 5, 15, 48 (simpler compositions)
- **Video Thumbnails:** Prompts 1, 16, 26, 44 (high contrast, clear subject)
- **Email Headers:** Prompts 7, 11, 46, 53 (emotional, atmospheric)

## Prompt Engineering Notes

### Key Elements to Include
1. **Subject:** What is the main focus
2. **Environment:** Setting and context
3. **Lighting:** Time of day, quality of light
4. **Mood:** Emotional tone
5. **Style:** Photography type, artistic approach
6. **Technical:** Resolution, aspect ratio, quality

### Common Modifiers
- **Time:** sunrise, sunset, golden hour, blue hour, night, dawn, dusk
- **Weather:** mist, fog, rain, clear, storm, clouds
- **Season:** spring, summer, autumn, winter, fall colors, snow
- **Quality:** photorealistic, 8K, ultra-detailed, cinematic, National Geographic
- **Mood:** peaceful, dramatic, mystical, epic, serene, awe-inspiring
