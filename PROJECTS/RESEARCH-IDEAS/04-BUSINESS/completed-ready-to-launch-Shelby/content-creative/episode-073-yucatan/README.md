## Quick Overview

Episode 073 takes listeners on a journey through the Yucatan Peninsula, the heartland of the ancient Maya civilization. This episode explores:

- 🌽 The Maya creation story: humans fashioned from white and yellow corn
- 🐍 Chichen Itza's equinox serpent shadow phenomenon
- 💙 The 6,000+ cenotes: sacred sinkholes and doorways to the underworld
- ⭕ Maya philosophy: time as a circle, not a line
- 🌿 The jungle that remembers and reclaims
- 🌌 Ancient astronomy and the stars that watch us

**Central Theme:** We are all children of corn. We all return to earth. We all live in cycles we barely understand. The circle turns.
