## Crisis Management

### If Technical Issues Arise
**Audio quality problems:**
- Acknowledge quickly: "Aware of audio issues on Episode 073. Re-uploading now. Thank you for patience."
- Fix within 24 hours maximum
- Offer apology and explanation

**Platform distribution delays:**
- Communicate: "Episode 073 is live on [platforms]. Spotify/Apple should have it within 24 hours."
- Provide alternative listen link (website direct)
- Follow up when resolved

### If Negative Feedback Occurs
**Cultural sensitivity concerns:**
- Listen carefully and validate: "Thank you for sharing this perspective. I hear you."
- If valid criticism: Acknowledge, learn, commit to doing better
- If misunderstanding: Clarify gently, don't argue
- Take conversation to DM if needed

**Factual errors:**
- Acknowledge: "Thank you for catching this. You're right, [correction]."
- Update show notes if necessary
- Express gratitude for community helping improve accuracy

### If Low Engagement
**Don't:**
- Panic or delete posts
- Compare to other creators' launches
- Over-post out of desperation

**Do:**
- Engage more authentically with existing audience
- Reach out to engaged listeners personally
- Consider paid promotion boost
- Learn and adjust for next launch
- Remember: evergreen content finds audience over time
