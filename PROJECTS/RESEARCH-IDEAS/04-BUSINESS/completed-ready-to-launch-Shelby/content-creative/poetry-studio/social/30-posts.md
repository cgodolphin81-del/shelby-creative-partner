- Use all 30 hashtags (mix of popular + niche)
- Post at 11am-1pm and 7pm-9pm local time

- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Post at 6am-10am and 7pm-11pm
- Engage with comments in first hour

- Vertical pins (2:3 ratio) perform best
- Add text overlay for context
- Link directly to relevant landing page
- Pins have 3-month+ lifespan (evergreen!)
