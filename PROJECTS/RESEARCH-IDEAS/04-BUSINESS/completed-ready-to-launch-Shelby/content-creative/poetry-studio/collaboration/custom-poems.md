## 📋 SERVICE OVERVIEW

### What We Offer

**AI Poetry Studio Custom Poems** combines AI generation with human curation to create deeply personal, meaningful poetry for life's most important moments.

**Process**:
1. Client submits request (topic, occasion, tone, details)
2. AI generates 3 draft options
3. Human poet reviews, refines, personalizes
4. Client receives drafts + revision rounds
5. Final poem delivered + print-ready files

**Turnaround**: 3-10 business days (expedited available)
**Starting Price**: $50 (basic) to $500+ (corporate)
