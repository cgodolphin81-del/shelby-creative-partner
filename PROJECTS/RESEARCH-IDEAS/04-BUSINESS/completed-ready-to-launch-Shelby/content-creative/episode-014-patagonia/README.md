## 🎙️ Audio Production Checklist

### Recording
- [ ] Narration recorded (Shelby's voice, warm and intimate)
- [ ] Pace: 130-140 words per minute
- [ ] Honor all pauses in script (critical for mood)

### Sound Design
- [ ] Base layer: Constant wind (varying intensity)
- [ ] Secondary: Ice cracking (every 2-3 minutes)
- [ ] Tertiary: Water, birds, gravel (as scripted)
- [ ] Special: Calving events (2-3 times in episode)
- [ ] Music: Minimal or none (let landscape be the score)

### Mixing
- [ ] Voice clear and present (not competing with ambience)
- [ ] Dynamic range appropriate for podcast platforms
- [ ] Export: WAV (master) + MP3 320kbps (distribution)
- [ ] Loudness: -16 LUFS ±1 (podcast standard)

### Quality Control
- [ ] Full listen-through (no errors, smooth flow)
- [ ] Check all transitions
- [ ] Verify ambient sound levels
- [ ] Test on multiple devices (phone, computer, headphones)

Stand before Perito Moreno Glacier, a wall of ice 74 meters tall, accumulating since the 1700s. Listen to the wind that never stops speaking across Torres del Paine. Wait for Fitz Roy to reveal itself from behind clouds.
