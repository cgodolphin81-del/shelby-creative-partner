## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Playfair Display** (Serif)
- Elegant, classic, editorial
- Use for: Titles, quotes, episode names
- Weights: Regular, Bold, Black
- **Why:** Evokes history, literature, Romanticism

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Lato** or **Open Sans** (Sans-serif)
- Clean, readable, modern
- Use for: Body text, captions, descriptions
- Weights: Light, Regular, Bold
- **Why:** Balances ornate headings, highly readable

### Accent Font (Special Use)
**Cinzel** or **Trajan Pro** (Display)
- Classical, inscribed, monumental
- Use for: Quotes, special graphics, Cabo da Roca inscription
- Weights: Regular, Bold
- **Why:** Evokes ancient inscriptions, stone carvings

### Font Pairings

**Option 1 (Classic):**
- Headings: Playfair Display Bold
- Body: Lato Regular
- Accent: Cinzel Regular

**Option 2 (Modern):**
- Headings: Playfair Display Black
- Body: Open Sans Regular
- Accent: Playfair Display Italic

**Option 3 (Minimal):**
- Headings: Cinzel Bold
- Body: Lato Light
- Accent: Lato Bold

### Composition Guidelines

**Rule of Thirds:**
- Place horizons on upper or lower third
- Position key elements at intersection points
- Use for: Landscape shots, palace views

**Leading Lines:**
- Paths, walls, staircases
- Draw eye into the image
- Use for: Initiation Well, palace approaches, castle ramparts

**Framing:**
- Use trees, arches, windows as frames
- Create depth and context
- Use for: Palace reveals, garden scenes

**Symmetry:**
- Initiation Well (top-down)
- Palace facades
- Garden designs
- Use for: Architectural shots, pattern emphasis

**Scale:**
- Include human figures for scale
- Show vastness of landscapes
- Use for: Palace exteriors, cliff views, castle walls

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

- **Text on images:** Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- **Use overlays:** Semi-transparent boxes behind text
- **Avoid:** Yellow text on light backgrounds, grey on grey

### Alt Text

- Describe images for screen readers
- Include location, key elements, mood
- **Example:** "Pena Palace colorful yellow and red facade against green forest hills in Sintra, Portugal"

### Readable Fonts

- Minimum 14px for body text
- Minimum 24px for headings
- Avoid overly decorative fonts for body text
