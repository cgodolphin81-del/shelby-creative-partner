- Fresh powder (soft, uniform)

### Aurora Textures
- Flowing ribbons
- Diffused glow
- Vertical rays
- Corona patterns

**Usage:** Overlays, backgrounds, magical moments

### Rock & Mountain Textures
- Rough, jagged surfaces

- Sharp edges

**Usage:** Contrast, grounding, structure

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds
- Place horizon on lower third (emphasize sky/aurora)
- Place subject on intersection points
- Create balance between ice and sky

---

### Leading Lines
- Glacier crevasses leading to subject
- Fjord lines drawing eye inward
- Aurora rays pointing to focal point
- Mountain ridges creating flow

---

### Negative Space
- Embrace vastness of Arctic
- Don't fear empty space
- Let subjects breathe
- Minimalism reflects Arctic essence

---

### Scale & Perspective
- Show human smallness against landscape
- Use wide shots for context
- Close-ups for intimacy (ice crystals, wildlife)
- Aerial views for patterns

## LIGHTING GUIDELINES

### Natural Light Scenarios

**Polar Night:**
- Low light, blue tones
- Artificial lights create warmth
- Aurora as primary light source
- Long exposures for photography

**Midnight Sun:**
- Golden hour that never ends
- Soft, diffused light
- Long shadows even at "night"
- Warm tones

**Twilight (Shoulder Seasons):**
- Blue hour extended
- Soft, even lighting
- Perfect for photography
- Moody atmosphere

---

### Artificial Lighting

**Warm (2700-3000K):**
- Indoor scenes
- Town lights
- Cozy moments
- Contrast with cold exterior

**Cool (5000-6500K):**
- Ice caves
- Glacier interiors
- Clinical, clean feel
- Match natural Arctic light

## FILE FORMATS & SPECS

### Images
- **Format:** PNG (transparency), JPEG (photos)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web
- **Color Space:** sRGB (web), CMYK (print)
- **Sizes:**
  - Instagram Feed: 1080x1080 (1:1) or 1080x1350 (4:5)
  - Instagram Stories: 1080x1920 (9:16)
  - Twitter: 1200x675 (16:9)
  - Facebook: 1200x630 (1.91:1)
  - Pinterest: 1000x1500 (2:3)
  - Podcast Cover: 3000x3000 (1:1)
  - YouTube Thumbnail: 1280x720 (16:9)

---

### Video
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum, 4K if available
- **Frame Rate:** 24fps (cinematic), 30fps (standard)
- **Aspect Ratios:** 16:9 (YouTube), 9:16 (TikTok/Reels), 1:1 (feed)

---

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- Ensure text readable on backgrounds
- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
- Test with color blindness simulators
- Provide alt text for all images

---

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe image content clearly
- Include relevant context
- Mention colors when important
- Keep concise but informative

**Example:**
"A massive glacier face towers over a turquoise fjord in Svalbard, Norway. Deep blue ice contrasts with white snow caps. Overcast Arctic sky."
