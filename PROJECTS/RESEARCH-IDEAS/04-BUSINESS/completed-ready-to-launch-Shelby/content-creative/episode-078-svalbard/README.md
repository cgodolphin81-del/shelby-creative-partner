## THEMES & MOTIFS

### Central Themes
- **Edges:** The edge of the habitable, edges as invitations
- **Ice:** Scripture, deep time, patience, humility
- **Darkness:** Polar night, learning to love the dark, fullness of darkness
- **Smallness:** Human scale vs. vastness, smallness connects
- **Guest Status:** We are visitors, nature holds the cards
- **Impermanence:** Ice melts, tracks erased, nothing lasts

### Recurring Motifs
- **Scripture/Writing:** Glaciers write, ice as text, verses and stanzas
- **Cathedral/Sacred:** Ice Cathedral, awe, reverence, holy (but not religious)
- **Breathing:** Earth breathing (aurora), ice breathing, lungs crystallizing
- **Tracks/Footprints:** Bear tracks, erased by snow, impermanence
- **Seeds:** Seed Vault, backup plans, hope, uncertainty
- **Light/Dark:** Polar night vs. midnight sun, both extremes
