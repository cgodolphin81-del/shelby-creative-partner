**Headline:** 70 Episodes. Here's What I've Learned About Consistency.

**Body:**
Seventy episodes of the Infinite Walk.

That's roughly one episode every week for a year and a half. Through busy seasons, quiet moments, travel, and life.

What's kept it going?

1. **Start small.** Episode 1 was just me and a microphone. Not 55 AI images and a full travel guide.

2. **Show up consistently.** Even when inspiration lagged. Even when no one seemed to be listening.

3. **Let it evolve.** The podcast I'm making now isn't the podcast I started. That's growth, not failure.

4. **Find your why.** For me: capturing moments of stillness in a world that never stops moving.

5. **Celebrate milestones.** Episode 70 in the Eternal City? That's not an accident. That's intention.
