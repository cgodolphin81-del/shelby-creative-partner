## 🍝 TRAVEL GUIDE HIGHLIGHTS

### Skip-the-Line Strategies
- Colosseum: Book underground tour, enter via Forum
- Vatican: First entry (9 AM) or Wednesday afternoon
- St. Peter's: Side doors, climb dome early
- Borghese: Timed entry, book weeks ahead

### Best Times to Visit
- **Season:** September-October (fall) or April-May (spring)
- **Time of Day:** Early morning (7-9 AM) or late afternoon (4-6 PM)
- **Avoid:** Midday crowds, August heat, free Sunday at Vatican

### Budget Breakdown (Per Day)
- **Budget:** €60-100
- **Mid-Range:** €150-250
- **Luxury:** €400-800+

### Must-Try Food
- Cacio e Pepe
- Carbonara
- Pizza al Taglio
- Supplì
- Gelato (always)
