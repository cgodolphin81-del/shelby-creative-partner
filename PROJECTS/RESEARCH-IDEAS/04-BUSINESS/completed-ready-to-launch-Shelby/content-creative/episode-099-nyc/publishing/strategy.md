## 🚨 CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

**Technical Problems:**
- Episode doesn't upload correctly
- Audio quality issues
- Platform distribution delays

**Response:**
- Have backup files ready
- Test on all platforms before launch
- Communicate quickly if issues arise

**Negative Feedback:**
- Criticism of episode content
- Comparisons to other NYC content
- Milestone expectations not met

**Response:**
- Acknowledge valid points
- Don't argue with trolls
- Focus on positive engagement
- Learn for future episodes

**Timing Conflicts:**
- Major news overshadows launch
- Competing events
- Platform outages

**Response:**
- Have flexible launch window
- Monitor news cycle
- Be prepared to delay if necessary
