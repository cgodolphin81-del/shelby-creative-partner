## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

#### Mood & Atmosphere
- **Lighting**: Golden hour, blue hour, overcast (embracing London grey)
- **Composition**: Wide establishing shots, intimate details, human scale
- **Treatment**: Slightly desaturated, cool tones, cinematic
- **Avoid**: Overly bright, saturated, touristy postcard style

#### Key Subjects
1. **Big Ben**: Multiple angles, times of day, with/without people
2. **Thames**: Reflections, bridges, boats, riverside walks
3. **Tower of London**: Stones, ravens, Crown Jewels (where permitted)
4. **British Museum**: Architecture, artifacts, contemplative visitors
5. **Royal Parks**: Trees, paths, benches, peaceful moments
6. **Shelby Character**: Integrated naturally, not forced

#### Editing Style
- **Contrast**: Medium to high
- **Saturation**: Slightly reduced (-10 to -20)
- **Temperature**: Cool to neutral (embrace grey)
- **Shadows**: Lifted slightly for detail
- **Highlights**: Controlled, not blown out
- **Grain**: Subtle film grain for texture (optional)

## LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

### Composition

#### Rule of Thirds
- Place key elements at intersection points
- Especially for social media graphics

#### Breathing Room
- Generous margins and padding
- Let content breathe (like parks in London)

#### Hierarchy
1. **Primary**: Episode number (50), title
2. **Secondary**: Key message, date
3. **Tertiary**: Details, links

### Aspect Ratios

#### Social Media
- **Instagram Post**: 1:1 (1080x1080)
- **Instagram Story/Reels**: 9:16 (1080x1920)
- **Facebook Post**: 1.91:1 (1200x630)
- **Twitter/X Post**: 16:9 (1200x675) or 1:1
- **Pinterest Pin**: 2:3 (1000x1500)
- **YouTube Thumbnail**: 16:9 (1280x720)

#### Podcast
- **Cover Art**: 1:1 (3000x3000 minimum)
- **Social Share**: 16:9 (1920x1080)

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Text on Background**: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text**: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Test**: Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Color Blindness Considerations
- Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
- Use patterns, labels, or icons as backup
- Test with color blindness simulators

### Readability
- Font size minimum 16px for body text
- Headlines 24px+ for social media
- Avoid light text on light backgrounds

## FILE FORMATS & SPECIFICATIONS

### Images
- **Format**: PNG for graphics with text, JPEG for photos
- **Resolution**: 72 DPI for web, 300 DPI for print
- **Color Space**: sRGB for web, CMYK for print
- **Compression**: Optimized for web (TinyPNG, etc.)

### Videos
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum, 4K if possible
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps standard, 60fps for smooth motion
- **Aspect Ratios**: Match platform (see above)

### Source Files
- **Design**: Figma, Canva, or Adobe Creative Cloud
- **Organization**: Layered, labeled, versioned
- **Backup**: Cloud storage + local backup
