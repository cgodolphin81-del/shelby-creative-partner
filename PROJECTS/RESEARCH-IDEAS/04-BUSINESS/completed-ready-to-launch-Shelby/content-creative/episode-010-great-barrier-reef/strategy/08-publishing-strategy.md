## TIMING CONSIDERATIONS

### Best Times to Publish

**Great Barrier Reef Seasonality:**
- **June-October:** Peak season, most interest
- **November:** Coral spawning (unique angle)
- **December-February:** Summer, Australian audiences traveling
- **March-May:** Shoulder season, good for planning content

**News Hooks:**
- World Oceans Day (June 8)
- Earth Day (April 22)
- Climate summits (COP meetings)
- New bleaching reports
- Conservation milestones

### Avoid

- Major competing news events
- Holiday weekends (lower engagement)
- Conflicting with other major launches

## CRISIS COMMUNICATION

### If Criticized For:

**"Flying to Australia increases carbon footprint"**
- Acknowledge the irony
- Explain purpose: bearing witness, raising awareness
- Note carbon offset purchases
- Emphasize action over perfection

**"Tourism damages the reef"**
- Distinguish responsible vs. irresponsible tourism
- Highlight eco-certified operators
- Note economic importance (64,000 jobs)
- Emphasize education and conservation funding

**"This is just doom and gloom"**
- Acknowledge the balance is hard
- Highlight hope and action
- Point to recovery stories
- Focus on "not too late" message

### Response Guidelines
- Respond thoughtfully, not defensively
- Acknowledge valid criticism
- Provide context when needed
- Don't engage with bad-faith actors
- Take conversations private when appropriate
