### r/travel Post
**Title**: [Guide] Everything You Need to Know About Salar de Uyuni, Bolivia

**Body**:
Just released Episode 068 of my travel podcast about Salar de Uyuni, and I wanted to share the travel guide we compiled for anyone planning to visit.

**Quick Facts:**
- World's largest salt flat (10,582 km²)
- Elevation: 3,656m (acclimatize first!)
- Best mirror effect: Jan-Mar
- Best dry season (salt patterns): Jun-Oct
- Gateway town: Uyuni

**Key Tips:**
1. Spend 2-3 days in La Paz first to acclimatize
2. Bring serious sun protection (SPF 50+, polarized sunglasses)
3. Cash is king—ATMs are limited
4. Book salt hotels in advance for unique experience
5. Be flexible with weather—mirror effect requires calm conditions

**Budget:**
- Day tour: $30-60 USD
- 3-day extended tour: $150-300 USD
- Accommodation: $15-150 USD/night depending on style
