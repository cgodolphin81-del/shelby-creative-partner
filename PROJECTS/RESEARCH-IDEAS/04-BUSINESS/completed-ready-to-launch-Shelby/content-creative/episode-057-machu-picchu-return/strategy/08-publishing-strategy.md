## 🎯 PLATFORM STRATEGY

### Podcast Platforms (Primary)

**Spotify:**
- Submit 2 weeks before launch
- Ensure episode artwork is optimized (3000x3000px)
- Add episode description with keywords
- Create Spotify-specific clip for sharing

**Apple Podcasts:**
- Submit 2 weeks before launch
- Ensure RSS feed is updated
- Add chapter markers if possible
- Encourage reviews in episode

**Google Podcasts:**
- Auto-syncs from RSS
- Verify episode appears correctly
- Add transcript for SEO

**Other Platforms:**
- Pocket Casts, Overcast, Castbox, etc.
- All sync from RSS feed
- Verify appearance on launch day

**Strategy:**
- Primary discovery happens here
- Optimize title/description for search
- Encourage saves and shares
- Request reviews (especially Apple)

---

### YouTube (Secondary but Important)

**Video Format:**
- Static image + audio (episode artwork)

- Title: "I Returned to Machu Picchu. Here's What Changed."

- Tags: Machu Picchu, Peru, travel podcast, Infinite Walk, etc.
- Chapters: Add timestamps for key sections

**Community Tab:**
- Day -3: Tease post
- Day 0: Launch announcement
- Day +7: Engagement post

**YouTube Shorts:**
- 3-5 shorts from episode content
