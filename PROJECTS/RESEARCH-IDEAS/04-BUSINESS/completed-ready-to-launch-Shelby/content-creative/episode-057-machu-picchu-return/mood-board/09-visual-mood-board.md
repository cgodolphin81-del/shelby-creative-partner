## 🖼️ IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) preferred
- Soft, diffused light for contemplative moments
- Dramatic backlighting for silhouettes
- Avoid harsh midday sun

**Composition:**
- Wide shots for epic scale
- Close-ups for intimate details
- Rule of thirds for landscapes
- Negative space for text overlay

**Subjects:**
- Ancient stone textures
- Mist and clouds in motion
- Llamas (calm, dignified)
- Terraces and agricultural features
- Temple architecture
- Walker silhouettes (small in frame)

**Avoid:**
- Crowded tourist shots
- Selfies at iconic viewpoints
- Over-saturated, HDR look
- Generic stock photography

### Color Grading

**Overall Look:**
- Slightly desaturated greens
- Warm highlights (gold/orange)
- Cool shadows (blue/grey)
- Soft contrast (not harsh)
- Film-like quality (not digital-sharp)

**LUT/Presets:**
- Kodak Portra 400 inspired
- Fuji Pro 400H alternative
- Custom: "Machu Picchu Return" (create from palette)

## 🎬 VIDEO STYLE

### Visual Treatment

**Pacing:**
- Slow, contemplative cuts
- Hold shots longer than typical
- Allow silence to breathe
- Match narration rhythm

**Transitions:**
- Slow fades (1-2 seconds)
- Mist/cloud wipes
- Match cuts (stone to stone)
- Avoid flashy transitions

**Effects:**
- Subtle film grain
- Soft vignettes
- Gentle light leaks (golden)
- Mist overlays

### Motion Graphics

**Episode Titles:**
- Fade in from mist
- Stone texture reveal
- Gold accent animation
- Slow, deliberate movement

**Lower Thirds:**
- Minimal, clean
- Stone grey background
- Gold accent line
- Montserrat Regular

**Quote Graphics:**
- Playfair Display Italic
- Cloud white background
- Subtle stone texture
- Gold accent underline

## 📊 BRAND CONSISTENCY

### Do's

✅ Use the four color families consistently  
✅ Maintain slow, contemplative visual pacing  
✅ Prioritize authentic imagery over stock  
✅ Allow negative space and breathing room  
✅ Match visual tone to episode mood (reverent, contemplative)  

### Don'ts

❌ Use bright, saturated colors outside palette  
❌ Rush cuts or flashy transitions  
❌ Use generic travel stock photography  
❌ Overcrowd designs with text/elements  
❌ Break from contemplative, reverent tone

## 🎯 MOOD BOARD SUMMARY

### Visual Keywords

- **Ancient:** Stone, weathered, enduring
- **Ethereal:** Mist, clouds, soft light
- **Warm:** Gold, sunrise, gratitude
- **Contemplative:** Slow, spacious, reverent
- **Living:** Green, vegetation, llamas
- **Brief:** Human scale against mountain scale

### Emotional Journey

1. **Opening:** Mist, mystery, revelation (Cloud Whites + Sunrise Golds)
2. **Middle:** Stone, endurance, relationship (Stone Greys + Mountain Greens)
3. **Close:** Gratitude, warmth, integration (Sunrise Golds + Cloud Whites)

### Overall Aesthetic

> "Ancient stones in morning mist. Golden light on enduring rock. The brief human presence against the mountain that remembers. Contemplative, reverent, warm."
