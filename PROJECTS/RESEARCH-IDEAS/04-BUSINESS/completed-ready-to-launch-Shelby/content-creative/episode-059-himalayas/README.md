**Purpose:** Practical travel information for listeners

**Contents:**

- Kathmandu arrival and preparation
- EBC trek overview (12-14 day itinerary)
- Permits required (Sagarmatha National Park, TIMS, costs)
- Costs breakdown (budget/mid-range/luxury)
- Best times to trek (pre-monsoon, post-monsoon, winter)
- Altitude sickness (symptoms, prevention, treatment)
- Essential gear list (what to bring, what to rent)
- Fitness requirements and preparation
- Tea houses and food on trail
- Packing tips and weight limits
- Insurance and emergency evacuation
- Sustainable trekking practices
- Pro tips from experienced trekkers

**Use for:** Show notes, blog post, listener resources, email content

**Key Info:**
- Permits: ~$50 USD total (Sagarmatha + TIMS)
- Trek cost: $1,200-2,500 (guided), $800-1,500 (independent)
- Best seasons: March-May, September-November
- Max altitude: 5,364m (17,598ft) at Base Camp
- Typical duration: 12-14 days round trip from Lukla
