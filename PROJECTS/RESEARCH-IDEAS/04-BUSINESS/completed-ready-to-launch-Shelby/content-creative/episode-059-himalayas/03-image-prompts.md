## 🎨 STYLE NOTES

### Color Palette
- **Snow White:** #F8F8FF - Purity, altitude, silence
- **Prayer Flag Blue:** #4A90E2 - Sky, spirituality, peace
- **Prayer Flag Red:** #D0021B - Life, energy, courage
- **Sky Blue:** #87CEEB - Vastness, clarity, freedom
- **Rock Grey:** #696969 - Permanence, challenge, earth
- **Ice Blue:** #A5F2F3 - Glacier ice, cold, clarity
- **Monastery Red:** #8B0000 - Spiritual, traditional
- **Golden Hour:** #FFD700 - Sunrise/sunset warmth

### Mood Keywords
- Awe-inspiring
- Humbling
- Spiritual
- Remote
- Challenging
- Serene
- Epic
- Contemplative
- Pristine
- Ancient

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 3000x3000px minimum (episode artwork)
- **Aspect Ratios:** 
  - 1:1 (Instagram, podcast artwork)
  - 16:9 (YouTube, presentations)
  - 9:16 (Stories, Reels, TikTok)
  - 4:5 (Instagram feed)
- **Style:** Hyperrealistic, cinematic, National Geographic aesthetic
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, dramatic shadows, high contrast
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, leading lines, scale emphasis

### AI Tool Recommendations
- **Midjourney v6:** Best for cinematic, atmospheric shots
- **DALL-E 3:** Best for specific text elements, precise compositions
- **Stable Diffusion XL:** Best for control, custom models
- **Leonardo.ai:** Best for consistent character generation (Shelby)

### Prompt Tips
- Add "photorealistic, 8K, National Geographic photography" for realistic shots
- Add "cinematic lighting, dramatic shadows" for mood
- Add "wide angle, epic landscape" for vast scenes
- Add "close-up, macro, detail" for intimate shots
- Add "golden hour, sunrise, sunset" for best light
- Add "minimalist, clean composition" for modern aesthetic
- Use negative prompts: "no people, no text, no watermark" when needed
