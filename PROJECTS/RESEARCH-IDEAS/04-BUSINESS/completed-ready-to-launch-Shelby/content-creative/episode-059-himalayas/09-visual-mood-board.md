---

### Accessibility

**Text Contrast:**
- White text on: Rock Grey, Deep Night, Monastery Red ✅
- Black text on: Snow White, Sky Blue, Ice Blue, Golden Hour ✅
- Avoid: White on Sky Blue, Yellow on White

**Color Blindness Considerations:**
- Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
- Use patterns, icons, or text labels
- Test with color blindness simulators

**WCAG Compliance:**
- Minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1 for normal text
- Minimum contrast ratio: 3:1 for large text
- Use online contrast checkers

## 📐 IMAGE COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds
- Place horizon on upper or lower third (not center)
- Place subjects at intersection points
- Leave negative space in direction of movement/gaze

### Leading Lines
- Use trails, ridges, prayer flag lines
- Lead eye through image
- Create depth and movement

### Scale
- Include human figure for scale when possible
- Emphasize vastness of mountains
- Small human, massive landscape

### Layers
- Foreground: Prayer flags, rocks, trekker
- Midground: Villages, valleys
- Background: Peaks, sky
- Create depth

### Negative Space
- Don't fear empty sky or snow
- Use for text overlay
- Emphasize isolation, vastness

## 🎬 VIDEO/ANIMATION STYLE

### Motion Principles
- **Slow and deliberate:** Match episode pacing
- **Minimal transitions:** Fade, dissolve (no flashy effects)
- **Natural movement:** Wind in flags, clouds moving, subtle zooms
- **Hold on moments:** Let powerful images breathe

### Animation Style
- **Text reveals:** Simple fade or typewriter
- **Color transitions:** Smooth gradients
- **Icon animation:** Subtle scale or opacity
- **Avoid:** Bouncy, energetic, corporate motion

### Music/Sound
- **Style:** Ambient, Tibetan influences
- **Instruments:** Singing bowls, soft pads, minimal percussion
- **Volume:** Below narration
- **Use:** Transitions, emotional moments

## ✍️ TYPOGRAPHY GUIDANCE

### Primary Fonts

**Headings: Montserrat Bold**
- Use: Titles, headers, emphasis
- Weights: 700 (Bold), 800 (Extra Bold)
- Style: All caps for impact, or Title Case
- Tracking: Slightly increased for all caps

**Body: Open Sans Regular**
- Use: Body text, captions, descriptions
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi Bold)
- Style: Sentence case
- Line height: 1.5-1.6 for readability

**Accent (Optional): Himalaya or Similar**
- Use: Special quotes, cultural elements
- Style: Use sparingly
- Note: Ensure readability

---

### Typography Hierarchy

**H1 (Episode Title):**
- Font: Montserrat Bold
- Size: 48-72px (digital), 36-48pt (print)
- Case: All Caps or Title Case
- Color: Rock Grey or Deep Night on light, Snow White on dark

**H2 (Section Headers):**
- Font: Montserrat Bold
- Size: 32-40px (digital), 24-32pt (print)
- Case: Title Case
- Color: Rock Grey or Prayer Flag Blue

**H3 (Subheaders):**
- Font: Montserrat Semi Bold
- Size: 24-28px (digital), 18-22pt (print)
- Case: Title Case
- Color: Rock Grey

**Body Text:**
- Font: Open Sans Regular
- Size: 16-18px (digital), 12-14pt (print)
- Case: Sentence case
- Color: Rock Grey or Deep Night

**Caption/Small:**
- Font: Open Sans Regular
- Size: 12-14px (digital), 10-12pt (print)
- Case: Sentence case
- Color: Rock Grey (lighter opacity)

---

### Text on Images

**Best Practices:**
- High contrast between text and background
- Use text shadow or overlay for readability
- Keep text minimal (especially on social)
- Test at thumbnail size

**Overlay Options:**
- Dark gradient overlay (bottom to top)
- Solid color block behind text
- Text shadow (subtle, 1-2px)
- Blur background behind text area

**Avoid:**
- Thin fonts on busy backgrounds
- Low contrast combinations
- Too much text on single image
- All caps for long text

## ✅ DO'S AND DON'TS

### DO ✅

- Use high-contrast combinations for readability
- Include human scale in landscape shots
- Honor cultural elements respectfully
- Test all visuals at thumbnail size
- Maintain consistency across platforms
- Use negative space effectively
- Let powerful images breathe (don't over-design)
- Prioritize authenticity over perfection

### DON'T ❌

- Use conquest language in visuals (no "conquering peaks")
- Appropriate cultural symbols without context
- Overcrowd images with text
- Use low-resolution or pixelated images
- Ignore accessibility (contrast, color blindness)
- Overuse filters or effects
- Misrepresent the experience (be honest)
- Use stock photos that feel inauthentic
