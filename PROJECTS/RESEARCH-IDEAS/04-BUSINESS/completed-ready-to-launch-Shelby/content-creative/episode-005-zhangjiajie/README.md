### 09 — Visual Mood Board
**Use for:** Design consistency, image selection, brand guidelines

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Mist Gray (#C8CDD1), Stone Gray (#6B7280), Moss Green (#5A7D5E)
- Secondary: Golden Hour (#D4A574), Dawn Pink (#E8C4C4), Deep Forest (#2D4A3E)
- Accent: Glass Blue (#A8C5D9), Autumn Rust (#A05238)

**Typography:**
- Primary: Cormorant Garamond (titles, quotes)
- Secondary: Inter (body, captions)
- Accent: Cinzel (dramatic titles)

**Visual Philosophy:**
> "The void is as important as the form."

**Key Principles:**
- Vertical dominance (emphasize height)
- Layered depth (3+ distinct layers)
- Negative space (40-60% of frame)
- Human scale (small figures, vast landscape)

**Do's:**
- Embrace negative space
- Keep colors muted and natural
- Let mist be a character
- Use vertical compositions

**Don'ts:**
- Oversaturate greens
- Use harsh contrast
- Fill every inch of frame
- Add too many text elements
