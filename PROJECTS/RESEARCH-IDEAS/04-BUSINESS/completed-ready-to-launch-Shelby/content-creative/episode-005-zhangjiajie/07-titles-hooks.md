## HOOK FORMULAS (Mix & Match)

### The "Before/After" Hook
> "Before [EVENT], this place was [STATE]. After, it became [NEW STATE]. I went to find what remained."

**Example:**
> "Before Avatar, Zhangjiajie was sacred. After, it became a destination. I went to find what remained."

### The "Number Contrast" Hook
> "[LARGE NUMBER] of [THING]. [SMALL NUMBER] of [THING]. The ratio is [ADJECTIVE]. And yet."

**Example:**
> "Three hundred million years of geology. Four days of my life. The ratio is absurd. And yet."

### The "Everyone/No One" Hook
> "Everyone knows [THING]. Almost no one knows [SECRET]."

**Example:**
> "Everyone knows Avatar. Almost no one knows where Pandora came from."

### The "I Did [X] So You Don't Have To" Hook
> "I [ACTION] so you don't have to. Here's what I learned."

**Example:**
> "I walked 50 kilometers through Zhangjiajie's pillar forest so you don't have to. Here's what I learned."

### The "Question → Answer" Hook
> "[PROVOCATIVE QUESTION]? [SURPRISING ANSWER]."

**Example:**
> "Can stone float? In Zhangjiajie, it does."

### The "They Say / I Say" Hook
> "They say [COMMON BELIEF]. I say [COUNTERPOINT]."

**Example:**
> "They say Zhangjiajie is about the views. I say it's about the silence between them."
