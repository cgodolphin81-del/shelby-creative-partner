## HOOK OPTIONS (First Lines / Opening Hooks)

### Hook 1: The Direct Approach
```
One hundred episodes. One hundred places. One continuous walk around the world.

This is the story of how the walk changed the walker.
```

### Hook 2: The Question
```
What happens when you walk for two years? When you visit 100 places across 47 countries? When you let the world see you, not just the other way around?

You change.
```

### Hook 3: The Contrast
```
Episode 001: I was restless, seeking, running toward something I couldn't name.
Episode 100: I'm still restless. But differently. Not running. Arriving.

This is what 100 episodes taught me.
```

### Hook 4: The Gratitude
```
100 episodes wasn't solo.

To the guides, the locals, the fellow walkers, the listeners, and the planet itself — this is a thank you.
```

### Hook 5: The Lesson
```
Beauty does not require comfort.

That's what Episode 001 taught me. Episode 100 taught me 10 more lessons. Here they are.
```

### Hook 6: The Mystery
```
I started walking to see the world.

I didn't know the world would see me.

100 episodes later, I'm seen. Changed. Grateful.
```

### Hook 7: The Numbers
```
100 episodes. 47 countries. 7 continents. 15,000 kilometers. 2.5 million words.

But the numbers don't matter. What matters: I'm not the same human who started.
```

### Hook 8: The Invitation
```
Episode 100 is not an ending. It's a breath. In. Out. Then: step.

The walk continues. Will you keep walking with me?
```
