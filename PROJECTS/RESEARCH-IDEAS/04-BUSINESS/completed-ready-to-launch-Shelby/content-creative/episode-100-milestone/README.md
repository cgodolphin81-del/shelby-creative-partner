# Episode 100: The Century Walk
## The Infinite Walk — MILESTONE EPISODE

*"One hundred episodes. One hundred places. One continuous walk around the world."*

---

## OVERVIEW

**Episode 100: The Century Walk** is a milestone episode reflecting on 100 episodes of walking across 47 countries and 7 continents over 2 years.

This is not just a celebration of destinations. It's a meditation on transformation — on what happens when you let the world see you, not just the other way around.

## THE 10 LESSONS

From Episode 100:

1. **Attention is a practice.**
   It grows with use.

2. **Discomfort is data.**
   Pay attention to it.

3. **Every place has a story.**
   Willingness to listen is the difference.

4. **Slow is fast.**
   Rushing creates mistakes.

5. **Solitude ≠ loneliness.**
   Alone time is where we meet ourselves.

6. **The journey is the destination.**
   (It's a cliché because it's true.)

7. **Gratitude is a technology.**
   Not a feeling. A practice.

8. **Leave things better.**
   Cleaner, quieter, more respected.

9. **Stories connect.**
   We're more alike than different.

10. **The walk continues.**
    Episode 100 is a breath. Then: step.
