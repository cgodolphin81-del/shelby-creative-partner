## HOOKS BY PLATFORM

### PODCAST INTRO HOOKS (First 30 seconds)

**Hook 1: The Impossible City**
"Amsterdam does not sit upon the earth. It floats. This is not metaphor. This is engineering made poetry. Beneath every cobblestone, every canal house, every bicycle... there is water. Always water. Welcome to Episode 104: The Floating City."

**Hook 2: The Number**
"Thirteen thousand, six hundred and fifty-nine. That's how many wooden piles hold up the Royal Palace in Amsterdam. Submerged in darkness for seven centuries. Bearing weight they were never meant to carry. This is a city built on impossibility. And it works."

**Hook 3: The Choice**
"In the 1960s, Amsterdam was drowning in cars. Children were dying—five hundred per year. The city had a choice: become like every other city... or choose something different. They chose bicycles. This is the story of that choice."

**Hook 4: The Quote**
"'In spite of everything, I still believe that people are really good at heart.' Anne Frank was fifteen when she wrote that. She would die in Bergen-Belsen, weeks before liberation. Her words survived. This is Amsterdam: a city that remembers."

**Hook 5: The Paradox**
"The Netherlands should not exist. Half the country is below sea level. The other half floods regularly. And yet: one of the world's wealthiest, most stable nations. This is the story of stubborn optimism. This is Amsterdam."

**RECOMMENDED:** Hook 1 - It's the most poetic and sets the tone perfectly.
