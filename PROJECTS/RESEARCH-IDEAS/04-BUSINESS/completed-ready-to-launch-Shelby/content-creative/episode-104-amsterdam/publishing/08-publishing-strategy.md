## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Apple Podcasts
- **Optimization:** Complete show notes, episode artwork, keywords
- **Reviews:** Ask listeners to rate/review (critical for algorithm)
- **Strategy:** "If you enjoyed this episode, please leave a review. It helps more people discover the show."

### Spotify
- **Optimization:** Use Spotify for Podcasters analytics
- **Features:** Add video if possible (Spotify Video)
- **Playlists:** Pitch to Spotify editorial playlists (if eligible)

### YouTube
- **Format:** Audio with static image or simple visualizer
- **SEO:** Title, description, tags optimized for "Amsterdam podcast"
- **Cards/End Screen:** Link to subscribe, previous episodes
- **Community:** Use Community tab for updates

### Instagram
- **Feed:** 3-5 posts in first week, then 1-2/week
- **Stories:** Daily for first 3 days, then 3-4/week
- **Reels:** 2-3 in first week, then 1/week
- **Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 2 hours

### TikTok
- **Frequency:** 1 video/day for first 5 days
- **Content:** Mix of hooks, BTS, travel tips
- **Trending:** Use trending audio if relevant
- **Engagement:** Reply to comments with video responses

### LinkedIn
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts in first week, then 1/week
- **Content:** Professional angles (urban planning, leadership, culture)
- **Engagement:** Respond to all comments thoughtfully
- **Networking:** Tag relevant companies/orgs (Amsterdam Tourism, etc.)

### Email
- **Frequency:** 1 launch email, 1 follow-up, 1 final reminder
- **Segmentation:** engaged subscribers vs. full list
- **Personalization:** Use first names, reference past engagement
- **CTA:** One clear action per email

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Technical Issues:
- **Problem:** Episode not showing on platforms
- **Solution:** Contact podcast host immediately, post delay notice on social

### If Negative Feedback:
- **Problem:** Criticism of content/approach
- **Solution:** Acknowledge, don't argue, learn for next episode

### If Low Engagement:
- **Problem:** Lower than expected downloads/engagement
- **Solution:** Don't panic. One episode doesn't define the show. Analyze, adjust, move forward.

### If Controversy:
- **Problem:** Sensitive topic (Anne Frank, colonialism) generates pushback
- **Solution:** Stand by thoughtful work, engage respectfully, clarify intent if needed
