## Key Statistics (Quick Reference)

### K2 Mountain
- **Height:** 8,611m (28,251 ft)
- **Rank:** 2nd highest in world
- **Fatality Rate:** ~25% (1 in 4)
- **First Ascent:** 1954 (Italian expedition)
- **Total Summits:** ~800+
- **Local Name:** Chhogori ("King of Mountains")
- **Nickname:** The Savage Mountain

### The Trek
- **Duration:** 18-22 days round trip
- **Trekking Days:** 12-15 days
- **Distance:** 160-186 km
- **Max Elevation:** 5,150m (K2 Base Camp)
- **Concordia:** 4,690m
- **Glacier:** Baltoro (63km long)
- **Difficulty:** Challenging/Strenuous

### Costs (2025-2026)
- **Trek Package:** $2,200-4,000 USD
- **Total Trip:** $3,500-6,000 USD (with flights)
- **Permits:** $300-500 USD (usually included)
- **Insurance:** $200-500 USD
- **Tips:** $150-300 USD

### Best Time to Go
- **Season:** June to September
- **Optimal:** July to Early August
- **Avoid:** October to May
