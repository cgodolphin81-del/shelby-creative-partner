**Mission:** To visit and witness Earth's most extreme and transformative landscapes. Not tourism. Not adventure for adventure's sake. Attention. Witness. Truth-telling.

**Episode 001:** Danakil Depression, Ethiopia
- Youngest land on Earth
- 125m below sea level
- Hottest place humans regularly walk
