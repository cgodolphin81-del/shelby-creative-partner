## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TACTICS

### Instagram
- **Best Times:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM (test for your audience)
- **Stories:** Use polls, questions, countdown stickers
- **Reels:** 15-30 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds
- **Hashtags:** Mix of popular (#Travel, #Podcast) and niche (#RapaNui, #Moai)

### Twitter
- **Thread Strategy:** First tweet must hook, last tweet must have CTA
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment in first 2 hours
- **Retweets:** Share listener reactions, tag them
- **Timing:** Tweet 3-5 times on launch day (not all at once)

### LinkedIn
- **Professional Angle:** Focus on leadership, resilience, legacy lessons
- **Long-form:** 1,000+ word posts perform well
- **Tagging:** Tag relevant companies/organizations ( respectfully)
- **Comments:** Respond thoughtfully, not generically

### TikTok / Reels
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds critical
- **Text Overlay:** Key phrases on screen
- **Audio:** Use trending audio if it fits (or original narration)
- **CTA:** "Link in bio" or "Listen now"

### Reddit
- **Authenticity:** Be genuine, not promotional
- **Engagement:** Answer every question, participate in discussion
- **Timing:** Post when subreddits are most active (varies)
- **Follow Rules:** Some subs have strict self-promo rules

### Email
- **Subject Line:** A/B test if possible
- **Preview Text:** Use it! Second hook
- **Personal Touch:** Write like you're writing to a friend
- **CTA:** One clear action (listen now)

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If Launch Day Issues:
- **Technical problems:** Have backup hosting ready, communicate transparently
- **Low initial engagement:** Activate personal network, ask friends to share
- **Negative feedback:** Respond graciously, learn, don't argue

### If Something Goes Viral:
- **Server capacity:** Ensure website can handle traffic
- **Media inquiries:** Have press kit ready
- **Follow-up content:** Be ready to capitalize on attention

### If Engagement is Lower Than Expected:
- **Re-evaluate timing:** Try different day/time
- **Adjust creative:** Test different hooks/images
- **Paid boost:** Small budget to amplify best content
- **Learn:** Document what didn't work for next episode
