## AUTHOR'S NOTE

This episode represents the midpoint of The Infinite Walk's first season. Antarctica is the pivot point—the moment the journey turns from external exploration to internal integration.

The contrast with Zhangjiajie (Episode 005) is intentional:
- **Warmth → Cold**
- **Life → Endurance**
- **Vertical → Horizontal**
- **Human presence → Human absence**
- **Floating → Silence**

Episode 007 (Arctic Circle) will complete the polar trilogy, bringing light back after darkness, magic after science, wonder after endurance.

The walk continues.
