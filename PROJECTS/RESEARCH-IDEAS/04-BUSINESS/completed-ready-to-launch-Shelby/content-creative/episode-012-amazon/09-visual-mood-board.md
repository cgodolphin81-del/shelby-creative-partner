## VISUAL THEMES

### Theme 1: Density
**Visual Representation:**
- Layered canopy shots
- Multiple planes of depth
- Overlapping foliage
- No empty space in frame

**Example Images:**
- Canopy tower view (green to horizon)
- Close-up of epiphyte-covered branch
- Understory with multiple layers visible
- Aerial of endless forest

**Mood:** Overwhelming, alive, interconnected

---

### Theme 2: Water Duality
**Visual Representation:**
- Split compositions (two waters)
- Reflections on dark water
- Contrast between black and brown rivers
- Water surface as mirror

**Example Images:**
- Meeting of Waters aerial
- Canoe on Rio Negro at dawn
- Pink dolphin emerging
- Giant water lilies

**Mood:** Mysterious, dual, flowing

---

### Theme 3: Light & Shadow
**Visual Representation:**
- God rays through canopy
- Dappled sunlight on forest floor
- Silhouettes against bright sky
- Night scenes with minimal light

**Example Images:**
- Sunbeams through canopy
- Jaguar emerging from shadows
- Night dock with stars
- Firelight on faces

**Mood:** Dramatic, sacred, intimate

---

### Theme 4: Life Everywhere
**Visual Representation:**
- Wildlife in natural habitat

**Example Images:**

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Style
- **National Geographic quality** — professional, detailed
- **Natural lighting** — golden hour preferred
- **Wide dynamic range** — capture shadows and highlights
- **Shallow depth of field** — for wildlife portraits
- **Deep depth of field** — for landscapes

### AI Image Generation Style
- **Midjourney v6** — primary tool
- **Photorealistic** — not illustrative
- **Cinematic lighting** — dramatic but natural
- **High detail** — 8K resolution prompts
- **Consistent color grading** — emerald greens, warm browns

### Composition Guidelines
- **Rule of thirds** — for most shots
- **Leading lines** — rivers, trails, branches
- **Framing** — use foliage as natural frames
- **Scale** — include human/animal for size reference
- **Layers** — foreground, middle, background

## VIDEO STYLE (YouTube/Social)

### Visual Treatment
- **Slow pans** on still images (Ken Burns effect)
- **Subtle zooms** — not distracting
- **Cross-dissolves** — smooth transitions
- **Fade to black** — between sections
- **Text overlays** — minimal, elegant

### Color Grading
- **Warm tones** — golden hour feel
- **Enhanced greens** — emerald, not neon
- **Deep blacks** — especially for night scenes
- **Soft highlights** — no blown-out skies
- **Consistent LUT** — apply to all footage

### Motion Graphics
- **Minimal** — let imagery speak
- **Lower thirds** — for names, locations
- **Chapter titles** — elegant serif font
- **Subtle animations** — fade in/out
- **No flashy effects** — maintain documentary feel
