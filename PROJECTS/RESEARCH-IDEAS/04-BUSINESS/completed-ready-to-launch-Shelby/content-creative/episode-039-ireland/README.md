## 🎙️ EPISODE OVERVIEW

### Title Options
1. **"The Celtic Edge: Ireland"** *(Recommended)*
2. "Episode 039: The Celtic Edge - Walking the Edge of Europe"
3. "Ireland: Where the Land Ends and the Atlantic Begins"
4. "The Celtic Edge: 300 Million Years of Stone and Story"

### Logline
Where the land ends and the Atlantic begins. Where ancient stones remember what we've forgotten. Where rain is not inconvenience—it's baptism. Episode 039 takes you to Ireland.

### Episode Description
In this episode of The Infinite Walk, we journey to Ireland:

- 🇮🇪 **The Cliffs of Moher:** 700 feet of vertical drama, 300 million years of geological history
- 🇮🇪 **The Ring of Kerry:** 179 kilometers of purple mountains, mirror lakes, and rainbow villages
- 🇮🇪 **Ancient castles and Neolithic tombs:** Newgrange (older than the pyramids), Rock of Cashel, Ross Castle
- 🇮🇪 **Celtic culture:** Mythology, symbols, the concept of *dúchas* (inheritance of place)
- 🇮🇪 **Pub culture:** Guinness, trad music, the warmth of Irish hospitality
- 🇮🇪 **The poetry of rain:** Why Ireland's weather is not inconvenience, but cleansing

This is not a travel guide. This is a love letter to a land that changes you.

### Key Themes
- **Edge:** The Cliffs as metaphor for life's edges, boundaries, transformations
- **Ancient:** 300 million years of stone, 5,000 years of human history
- **Dúchas:** Irish concept of inheritance of place, bond between person and landscape
- **Rain as Baptism:** Weather as cleansing, renewal, acceptance
- **Living Culture:** Ancient stones + living pubs, history + present

### Step 2: Recording

- [ ] Test microphone levels (-6dB peak)
- [ ] Record narration using `scripts/04-audio-narration-20min.md`
- [ ] Follow timing cues (20 minutes total with music/pauses)

### Step 3: Post-Production
- [ ] Edit narration (remove breaths, mistakes, long pauses)
- [ ] Add music (traditional Irish: fiddle, tin whistle, bodhrán)
- [ ] Add sound effects (wind, waves, pub ambience, rain)
- [ ] Normalize to -16 LUFS (podcast standard)
- [ ] Export as MP3 (128kbps minimum, 192kbps recommended)

### Step 4: Quality Check
- [ ] Listen to full episode (check for errors)
- [ ] Verify music levels (background, not overpowering)
- [ ] Check intro/outro clarity
- [ ] Test on multiple devices (phone, computer, car)
