Episode 040. The milestone. The marker. The moment.

### Mid-Episode (Reflection)
```
Thirty-nine episodes ago, I began walking.

I've walked cliffs and cities. Deserts and forests. Ancient stones and modern streets. I've walked alone, and I've walked with you—through your headphones, through your screens, through your messages and reviews and conversations.

Each episode, a step. Each step, a story. Each story, a thread in something larger.

Forty threads. Forty steps. Forty walks.

[Pause]

And tonight... this walk. This place. This moment.

It matters.
```

### Closing (Looking Forward)
```
Forty episodes complete.

But this is not the end. It's a marker. A milestone. A moment to breathe, to reflect, to remember.

The walk continues.

Episode 041. Episode 050. Episode 100. However far this path goes.

Thank you for walking with me. For listening. For being part of this.

[Pause]

The Infinite Walk continues.

And I'll see you... on the next walk.
```
