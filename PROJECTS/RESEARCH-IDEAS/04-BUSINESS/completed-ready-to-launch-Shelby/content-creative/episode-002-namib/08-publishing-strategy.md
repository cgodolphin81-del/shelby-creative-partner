## PLATFORM STRATEGY

### Primary Platforms (Focus Energy Here)

**1. Instagram**
- **Audience:** Visual travelers, photographers, mindful explorers
- **Content Type:** High-quality images, carousels, Reels, Stories
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 feed post/day for 5 days, daily Stories during launch week
- **Goal:** Build visual brand, engage travel community

**2. TikTok**
- **Audience:** Younger travelers, curiosity-driven, short-form consumers
- **Content Type:** 15-45 second videos, trending audio, hooks
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 video/day for 5 days
- **Goal:** Viral potential, series awareness

**3. YouTube**
- **Audience:** Long-form content consumers, travel documentary fans
- **Content Type:** 10-15 min video (essay + images + narration)
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 video (with potential for shorts from same content)
- **Goal:** Evergreen content, search traffic, monetization

**4. Newsletter (Substack/Beehiiv)**
- **Audience:** Dedicated followers, deep readers
- **Content Type:** Full essay + behind-the-scenes + next episode tease
- **Posting Frequency:** 1 issue (launch week)
- **Goal:** Direct relationship, loyal audience, potential revenue

---

### Secondary Platforms (Repurpose Content)

**5. Medium**
- Cross-post essay (if not exclusive elsewhere)
- Tag: Travel, Philosophy, Nature, Writing

**6. Pinterest**
- Create pins from images + quotes
- Link back to blog/website

**7. Twitter/X**
- Thread format: "10 things I learned in the Namib Desert"

**8. LinkedIn**

## ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

### First 2 Hours (Critical Window)
- Respond to EVERY comment (even just emoji)
- Like all comments
- Share best comments to Stories
- This signals algorithm that content is engaging

### First 24 Hours
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours
- Ask follow-up questions to deepen conversation
- DM engaged followers (thank them, build relationship)
- Share to Stories 2-3 times throughout day

### Ongoing
- Respond to comments for 7 days minimum
- Engage with similar accounts (travel, photography, philosophy)
- Join conversations in your niche
- Don't just broadcast — converse

### Community Building
- Feature follower content (with credit)
- Create UGC opportunities ("Show me your desert photos")
- Run polls and questions in Stories
- Build email list (newsletter signups)

**Personal Network:**
- Share with friends, colleagues, existing audience

- Comment on similar accounts (genuinely, not spammy)

## ANALYTICS & OPTIMIZATION

### Metrics to Track

**Instagram:**
- Reach, Impressions
- Engagement Rate (likes + comments + saves / reach)
- Saves (high-value metric)
- Shares
- Profile visits
- Link clicks (if link in bio)

**TikTok:**
- Views
- Watch time / completion rate
- Likes, comments, shares
- Follower growth
- Traffic to profile

**YouTube:**
- Views (first 24 hours, first 7 days)
- Watch time (average view duration)
- Click-through rate (thumbnail/title)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Subscriber growth

**Newsletter:**
- Open rate (target: 35-50%+)
- Click-through rate
- Unsubscribe rate
- Replies/engagement

**Website/Blog:**
- Page views
- Time on page
- Bounce rate
- Traffic sources

### Weekly Review Questions

1. Which post performed best? Why?
2. Which hook/title resonated most?
3. What time got best engagement?
4. Which platform drove most traffic?
5. What did people comment about most?
6. What questions did they ask?
7. What should I do more of? Less of?

### Optimization Actions

**If engagement is low:**
- Test different hooks
- Post at different times
- Improve visual quality
- Ask more questions in captions

**If reach is low:**
- Use better hashtags
- Engage more with community
- Consider paid promotion
- Collaborate with others

**If watch time is low (video):**
- Improve first 3 seconds (hook)
- Tighten editing
- Add captions/text overlays
- Shorten overall length
