## 📷 PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

### Lighting
- **Golden hour only** for ghats (sunrise/sunset)
- **Warm color temperature** (3200-4500K)
- **High contrast** for fire imagery
- **Soft, diffused** for spiritual moments
- **Natural light** preferred over artificial

### Composition
- **Rule of thirds** for landscapes
- **Leading lines** (ghat steps, river flow)
- **Silhouettes** for anonymity/respect
- **Wide shots** for context
- **Close-ups** for emotion (hands, flames, flowers)

### Treatment
- **Warm tones** enhanced
- **Shadows** lifted slightly
- **Highlights** protected
- **Vibrance** +10-15
- **Clarity** +5-10 (not overdone)
- **Grain** subtle for film look

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Oversaturated colors
- ❌ Heavy HDR
- ❌ Cold color temperatures
- ❌ Harsh flash
- ❌ Over-sharpening
- ❌ Inappropriate filters

## 🎬 VIDEO STYLE

### Color Grading
- **Warm tones** pushed (+10-15)
- **Blues** slightly desaturated (-5)
- **Oranges** enhanced (+10)
- **Shadows** cool tint (slight blue)
- **Highlights** warm tint (slight orange)
- **Film grain** subtle (5-10%)

### Transitions
- **Slow fades** (2-3 seconds)
- **Cross-dissolves** for contemplative moments
- **Hard cuts** for dramatic emphasis
- **Match cuts** (flame to sun, water to sky)
- **No flashy transitions**

### Motion
- **Slow, deliberate** pacing
- **Minimal camera movement**
- **Static shots** allowed to breathe
- **Time-lapse** for sunrise/crowds
- **Slow motion** for fire/water (50-60%)

### Text Animation
- **Fade in/out** only
- **No flashy effects**
- **Hold time**: 5 seconds minimum
- **Position**: Lower third or centered
- **Font**: Source Sans Pro with subtle drop shadow

## 🎯 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### 1. Respect First
- Never trivialize sacred imagery
- Maintain dignity in all visuals
- When in doubt, simplify
- Cultural sensitivity over aesthetics

### 2. Warmth Over Coolth
- Favor warm color temperatures
- Evoke fire, dawn, gold
- Cool blues only for water/depth
- Avoid sterile, cold looks

### 3. Simplicity Over Complexity
- Minimal text on images
- Let imagery breathe
- White space is your friend
- One focal point per composition

### 4. Consistency Across Platforms
- Same color palette everywhere
- Same fonts everywhere
- Same logo placement
- Recognizable at a glance

### 5. Accessibility
- High contrast for readability
- Alt text on all images
- Captions on all videos
- Color-blind friendly combinations

## 📋 EXPORT SETTINGS

### Web Images
- **Format**: JPEG (photos), PNG (graphics with transparency)
- **Quality**: 80-85% (balance quality/size)
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **Resolution**: 72 PPI
- **Max Size**: 500KB per image

### Print Images
- **Format**: TIFF or high-quality JPEG
- **Quality**: 100%
- **Color Space**: CMYK (for print) or RGB (for digital printing)
- **Resolution**: 300 PPI
- **Bleed**: 3mm if applicable

### Video
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps (cinematic) or 30fps (social)
- **Bitrate**: 10-15 Mbps
- **Audio**: AAC, 48kHz, 320kbps

### Social Media Specific
- **Instagram**: JPEG, sRGB, 1080px on shortest side
- **Twitter**: PNG for graphics, JPEG for photos
- **LinkedIn**: PNG for graphics (crisp text)
- **Pinterest**: PNG, 2:3 aspect ratio
- **YouTube**: PNG for thumbnails, 1280x720 minimum
