## Episode Overview

Episode 054 explores the Canyon Kingdom—Grand Canyon, Zion, Bryce Canyon, and Arches National Parks. This is not just a travel episode; it's a journey through deep time, exploring what it means to be human in a landscape that's a thousand times older than human civilization.

**Key Themes:**
- ⏰ **Deep Time**: Geologic time vs. human time
- 🌊 **The Patience of Water**: How rivers cut through stone
- 😭 **The Sublime**: When beauty meets terror
- 🪶 **Native Time**: Hopi, Havasupai, Navajo perspectives
- 🎨 **Erosion as Art**: Destruction is also creation

**Locations:**
- 🏜️ Grand Canyon National Park, Arizona
- 🏔️ Zion National Park, Utah
- 🪨 Bryce Canyon National Park, Utah
- 🌉 Arches National Park, Utah

✅ Used correct tribal names (Havasupai, Hualapai, Hopi, Navajo, Paiute)
