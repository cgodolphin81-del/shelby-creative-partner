- 16:9 = YouTube, blog headers, presentations
- 3:2 = Instagram landscape, standard photography
- 4:3 = Instagram portrait, traditional format
- 9:16 = Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels
- 1:1 = Instagram square, podcast covers

**Style Consistency**:
- Use "photorealistic" for landscape shots
- Use "Pixar-style 3D render" for Shelby character
- Maintain warm color palette: reds, oranges, golds

- Golden hour lighting preferred for drama
