### 2. The Science Post
```
Here's what's actually happening when you see the northern lights:

☀️ Particles from the sun (solar wind) travel 93 million miles through space
🌍 Earth's magnetic field catches them and funnels them toward the poles
⚡ They collide with oxygen and nitrogen atoms 80-640 km up
💚 Green = oxygen at lower altitudes
❤️ Red = oxygen at very high altitudes (rare!)
💜 Purple/blue = nitrogen molecules

The result? The greatest light show on Earth.

And it performs in absolute silence.
```
