## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** - Standard video/YouTube, landscape photography
- **4:3** - Traditional photography, Instagram landscape
- **9:16** - Vertical video, Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels

### Style Parameters
- `--style raw` - More photographic, less artistic interpretation
- `--v 6` - Use Midjourney version 6 for best quality
- Add `--ar` for aspect ratio as shown in each prompt

### Tips for Best Results
1. **Iterate**: Run each prompt 2-4 times for variations
2. **Adjust**: Modify colors, lighting, or composition based on results
3. **Upscale**: Use upscaling for final images
4. **Consistency**: Keep style consistent across episode images
5. **Mood**: Match the poetic, contemplative tone of the essay

### Color Palette Keywords
- Electric blue
- Sunset orange
- Coral pink
- Turquoise
- Deep navy blue
- Vibrant purple
- Golden yellow
- Emerald green
