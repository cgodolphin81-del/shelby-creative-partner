## Visual Themes

### Theme 1: Above the Clouds
**Mood:** Ethereal, transcendent, peaceful  
**Key Elements:**
- Clouds below viewpoint
- Mountain peaks emerging from cloud sea
- Soft, diffused light
- White and light blue dominance
- Sense of altitude and space

**Image Examples:**
- Jungfraujoch Sphinx Observatory above cloud layer
- Aerial view of peaks as islands in cloud ocean
- Sunrise breaking through cloud cover
- Silhouette of peaks against bright sky

**Design Application:**
- Light, airy layouts
- Gradient backgrounds (white to light blue)
- Minimalist typography
- Lots of negative space

---

**Design Application:**

- Blur effects for motion

## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Montserrat** or **Poppins**
- Clean, modern, geometric
- Works well for travel/lifestyle
- Excellent readability
- Available in multiple weights

**Usage:**
- Episode titles
- Section headers
- Social media graphics
- Thumbnails

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Open Sans** or **Lato**
- Clean, neutral, highly readable
- Works well for long-form content
- Professional but approachable

**Usage:**
- Blog posts
- Travel guide body text
- Email content
- Captions

### Accent Font (Special)
**Playfair Display** or **Cormorant Garamond**
- Elegant serif
- For poetic/essay content
- Adds sophistication

**Usage:**
- Poetic essay excerpts
- Quote graphics
- Special announcements
- Episode title treatments

## Image Style Guide

### Photography Style
- **Natural light preferred** (golden hour, blue hour)
- **Wide landscapes** for epic scale
- **Detail shots** for intimacy (chocolate, flowers, textures)
- **Human element** for scale and relatability
- **Authentic moments** over overly posed shots

### Color Grading
- **Cool tones** for mountain/snow scenes (blues, whites)
- **Warm tones** for villages/interiors (browns, golds)
- **High contrast** for dramatic peaks
- **Soft/muted** for peaceful valley scenes
- **Vibrant** for wildflowers and summer meadows

### Composition
- **Rule of thirds** for landscapes
- **Leading lines** (railway tracks, paths, rivers)
- **Framing** (arches, windows, doorways)
- **Scale references** (people, trains, buildings)
- **Negative space** for text overlay

- **Slow zoom** on mountain peaks
- **Pan across** panoramic views

### Music/Sound
- **Ambient, ethereal** pads for mountain scenes
- **Gentle piano** for reflective moments
- **Natural sounds** (wind, water, train, bells)
- **Build to crescendo** for dramatic reveals

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Contrast
- Ensure text has sufficient contrast against backgrounds
- Use WCAG 2.1 AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text)
- Provide alt text for all images
- Don't rely solely on color to convey information

### Readability
- Minimum font size: 16px for body text
- Clear, legible fonts
- Adequate line spacing (1.5x)
- Avoid text over busy images (use overlays)

### Inclusive Design
- Diverse representation in human imagery
- Accessible travel information (mobility considerations)
- Clear, simple language
- Multiple format options (audio, text, visual)

## Quick Reference

### Do's ✅
- Use natural, authentic imagery
- Maintain consistent branding
- Ensure text readability
- Include human element for scale
- Use high-resolution images (300 DPI for print, 72 DPI for web)
- Test designs on mobile devices
- Keep file sizes optimized for web

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't overuse filters/effects
- Don't clutter designs with too much text
- Don't use low-resolution images
- Don't ignore brand consistency
- Don't forget accessibility
- Don't use copyrighted images without permission
- Don't sacrifice readability for style
