## 🙏 Cultural Sensitivity Notes

When creating and publishing content about Bhutan:

1. **Respect Sacred Sites:** Never use disrespectful imagery of temples, monks, or religious practices
2. **Avoid Exoticizing:** Present Bhutan as a living culture, not a museum piece or fantasy Shangri-La
3. **Acknowledge Complexity:** GNH is real and revolutionary but not perfect; avoid romanticizing poverty
4. **Tourism Impact:** Promote responsible, sustainable travel practices; explain SDF benefits
5. **Modern Bhutan:** Show balance of tradition and modernity, not just "ancient kingdom"
6. **Local Voices:** When possible, amplify Bhutanese perspectives and voices
7. **Accuracy:** Fact-check all claims about Bhutan, Buddhism, GNH
8. **Sacred Symbols:** Use Buddhist symbols respectfully, understand their meaning

## 🌟 What Makes This Episode Different

Episode 071 is not just a travel documentary. It is an invitation to reconsider:

- **How we measure progress** (GDP vs. GNH)
- **How we relate to the sacred** (separation vs. integration)
- **How we understand pilgrimage** (destination vs. transformation)
- **How we practice spirituality** (clutching vs. releasing)
- **How we think about happiness** (product vs. practice)

This episode asks the listener to reflect on their own life, their own measures, their own path.

It is contemplative. It is reverent. It is an invitation.
