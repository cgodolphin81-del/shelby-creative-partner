## 📝 Prompt Usage Guidelines

### Best Practices
1. **Respect Sacred Imagery:** Never generate disrespectful or frivolous images of religious sites
2. **Cultural Accuracy:** Verify traditional dress, architecture, and symbols are accurate
3. **Avoid Stereotypes:** Show modern Bhutan alongside traditional, not just "ancient kingdom"
4. **Shelby Consistency:** Maintain consistent appearance across all Shelby prompts
5. **Quality Over Quantity:** Use higher iteration counts for key images

### Model Recommendations
- **Midjourney v6:** Best for landscapes, atmospheric shots, artistic compositions
- **DALL-E 3:** Best for accurate text, specific architectural details
- **Stable Diffusion XL:** Best for custom Shelby character consistency

### Aspect Ratio Guide
- **16:9:** YouTube video, website headers, presentations
- **1:1:** Instagram feed, Facebook posts
- **3:4:** Pinterest, portrait social posts
- **9:16:** Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels
