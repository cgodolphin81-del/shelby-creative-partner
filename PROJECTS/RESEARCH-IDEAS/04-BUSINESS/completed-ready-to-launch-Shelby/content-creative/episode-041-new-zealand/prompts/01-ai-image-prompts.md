## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **Landscape (16:9)**: Best for scenic shots, fiords, mountains
- **Portrait (9:16)**: Best for waterfalls, cliffs, vertical compositions
- **Square (1:1)**: Best for social media, wildlife portraits
- **Ultrawide (21:9)**: Best for panoramic vistas, aerial shots

### Style Keywords
- **Photorealistic**: For documentary-style images
- **Cinematic**: For dramatic, moody compositions
- **Fine Art**: For abstract, minimalist approaches
- **National Geographic**: For nature/wildlife photography
- **Travel Photography**: For inspirational, aspirational images

### Color Palettes
- **Fiord Blues**: Deep blues, blue-greens, black water
- **Mountain Greens**: Forest greens, alpine greens, moss
- **Lake Turquoises**: Glacial turquoise, milky blue-green
- **Sky Whites**: Cloud whites, snow whites, mist
- **Golden Hour**: Warm oranges, pinks, golds

### Best For
- **Social Media**: 16, 17, 19, 43-50 (Shelby shots)
- **Blog Headers**: 1, 2, 16, 29, 38
- **Print/Posters**: 4, 18, 29, 59, 60
- **Presentations**: 8, 15, 28, 38, 42
- **Merchandise**: 1, 18, 29, 59 (iconic images)
