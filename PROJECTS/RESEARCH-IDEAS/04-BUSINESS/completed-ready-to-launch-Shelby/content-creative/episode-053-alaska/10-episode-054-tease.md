## CULTURAL SENSITIVITY NOTES

### Aboriginal Culture Representation

**Do:**
- Acknowledge Traditional Owners of land you discuss
- Use "Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander" when appropriate
- Respect sacred sites (some should not be photographed/discussed)
- Credit Indigenous sources and voices
- Understand this is living culture, not historical artifact

**Don't:**
- Appropriate sacred symbols or stories
- Speak for Indigenous Australians
- Treat culture as monolithic (hundreds of distinct nations)
- Perpetuate stereotypes
- Use outdated or offensive terminology

**Resources:**
- Reconciliation Australia (reconciliation.org.au)
- Local Indigenous tourism organizations
- Aboriginal-led tour operators
- Indigenous media outlets (NITV, Koori Mail)
