## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram
**Frequency:** Daily for Week 1, then 3-4x/week
**Format Mix:**
- 40% Feed posts (high-quality images)
- 40% Stories (behind-the-scenes, engagement)
- 20% Reels (short video clips)

**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 6-8 PM (audience-dependent)
**Hashtags:** 10-15 per post (mix of popular and niche)
**Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 2 hours

**Content Calendar:**
- Mon: Episode highlight
- Tue: Travel tip from guide
- Wed: Wildlife/nature focus
- Thu: Quote graphic
- Fri: Behind-the-scenes
- Sat: User engagement question
- Sun: Reflection/personal

---

### TikTok / Reels
**Frequency:** Daily for Week 1, then 4-5x/week
**Video Length:** 15-30 seconds (hook fast)
**Style:** Authentic, not over-produced

**Content Types:**
- Hook-driven narration clips
- Fact-based content (glaciers, aurora, wildlife)
- Behind-the-scenes
- Travel tips

**Best Times:** 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM
**Sounds:** Use trending audio when relevant
**Hashtags:** 3-5 targeted hashtags

---

### Twitter / X
**Frequency:** 3-5 tweets per day (Week 1), then 1-2/day
**Format:** Mix of text, images, threads

**Content Types:**
- Pull quotes from episode
- Facts about Alaska
- Thread on specific topics (glaciers, aurora, wildlife)
- Engagement questions
- Retweets of Alaska/travel content

**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-8 PM
**Engagement:** Reply to mentions within 1 hour

---

### Facebook
**Frequency:** Daily for Week 1, then 3-4x/week
**Format:** Longer posts, images, links

**Content Types:**
- Episode announcements
- Travel guide highlights
- Discussion questions
- Photo albums (Alaska images)

**Best Times:** 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM
**Groups:** Share to relevant travel/podcast groups (follow rules)

---

### LinkedIn
**Frequency:** 2-3 posts per week
**Format:** Professional angle, longer-form

**Content Types:**
- Leadership/perspective lessons from Alaska
- Professional reflection post
- Travel guide for business travelers

**Best Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM (weekday mornings)
**Tone:** Thoughtful, professional, not salesy

---

### YouTube
**Frequency:** Episode upload + 2-3 Shorts
**Format:** Audiogram or static image with audio

**Content Types:**
- Full episode (audio + static/animated image)
- Shorts: 30-60 second clips from episode
- Behind-the-scenes

**Best Times:** 2-4 PM (weekday), 10 AM-12 PM (weekend)
**SEO:** Optimize title, description, tags (see titles doc)

---

### Email Newsletter
**Frequency:** Launch day + 1 follow-up
**Format:** Personal, conversational

**Email 1 (Launch Day):**
- Subject: "Episode 53: The Last Frontier"
- Personal intro about why Alaska matters
- Listen link
- What's included (travel guide, prompts, etc.)
- Tease next episode

**Email 2 (Day 5-7):**
- Subject: "Did you catch Episode 53?"
- For those who missed it
- Share any listener feedback
- Reminder that travel guide is free resource

**Best Send Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM-12 PM

## COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

### Responding to Comments
**Guidelines:**
- Respond within 2 hours when possible
- Be genuine, not robotic
- Ask follow-up questions
- Thank people for sharing
- Handle criticism gracefully

**Common Comments & Responses:**
- "Beautiful episode!" → "Thank you! Alaska is such a special place. Have you ever been?"
- "Adding to my bucket list!" → "Do it! The travel guide has everything you need to plan. Link in bio."
- "This made me cry." → "That means a lot. Wilderness has a way of getting inside us. Thank you for listening."

### User-Generated Content
**Encourage:**
- Listeners sharing their Alaska photos
- Reviews on podcast platforms
- Tags when they listen
- Stories about their own "last frontier" experiences

**Repost Strategy:**
- Always ask permission
- Credit the creator
- Add your own reflection
- Share to Stories and occasionally Feed

## ANALYTICS & OPTIMIZATION

### Key Metrics to Track

**Podcast Platforms:**
- Downloads (first 7 days, 30 days, 90 days)
- Completion rate (how many listen to the end)
- Geographic data (where are listeners?)
- Platform breakdown (Spotify, Apple, etc.)

**Social Media:**
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate on links
- Follower growth
- Best performing content types

**Email:**
- Open rate
- Click-through rate
- Unsubscribe rate
- Reply rate (engagement)

**Website (if applicable):**
- Page views on episode page
- Time on page
- Download clicks
- Travel guide downloads

### Optimization Strategy

**Week 1 Review:**
- Which platform drove most engagement?
- Which hook resonated most?
- What time of day performed best?
- Which content type got most saves/shares?

**Adjustments:**
- Double down on what worked
- Reduce or revise what didn't
- Test new hooks on underperforming platforms
- Reallocate ad budget to best channels

**Month 1 Review:**
- Total downloads vs. previous episodes
- Social media growth
- Email list growth
- Listener feedback themes

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

**Negative Feedback:**
- Respond gracefully, don't argue
- Acknowledge valid criticism
- Take conversation private if needed
- Learn and improve

**Factual Errors:**
- Correct promptly and transparently
- Update episode notes if needed
- Thank person who caught it
- Don't make excuses

**Technical Issues:**
- Have backup hosting ready
- Communicate delays honestly
- Offer makegood (bonus content, etc.)

**Controversial Topics:**
- Alaska has complex indigenous history
- Climate change is sensitive
- Approach with respect and accuracy
- Listen to criticism from affected communities
