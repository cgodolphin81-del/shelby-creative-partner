## RPM Rates by Niche

**RPM (Revenue Per Mille)** = Earnings per 1,000 pageviews. This is the most important metric for display ad revenue.

### Key Insights

**Highest RPM Niches:**
1. **Insurance** - Highest advertiser competition, high customer lifetime value
2. **Finance/Investing** - High-value conversions (brokerage accounts, loans, credit cards)
3. **Legal** - Extremely high cost-per-acquisition for law firms
4. **Business/Marketing Software** - B2B purchases, recurring revenue products

**Why RPM Varies:**
- **Advertiser competition** - More advertisers bidding = higher rates
- **Customer value** - Higher purchase values = advertisers pay more
- **Purchase intent** - Commercial keywords outperform informational
- **Geography** - Tier 1 country traffic worth 5-10x Tier 3 traffic

## Ad Network Comparison

### Google AdSense

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Minimum Traffic** | None |
| **Approval Difficulty** | Easy |
| **Revenue Share** | 68% to publisher |
| **Payment Threshold** | $100 |
| **Payment Terms** | Net 30 |
| **Typical RPM** | $2-15 |
| **Best For** | New sites, testing, backup monetization |

**Pros:**
- Instant approval for most sites
- No traffic requirements
- Reliable payments
- Easy integration

**Cons:**
- Lowest RPM rates
- Limited optimization
- Basic reporting

- Net 65 payment terms (cash flow consideration)

## Market Trends 2025

### What's Working

✅ **Topical Authority** - Google rewards comprehensive topic coverage over isolated articles
✅ **E-E-A-T** - Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness signals matter more than ever
✅ **AI Overviews Optimization** - Content structured for AI citation gains visibility
✅ **Video Integration** - Sites with embedded video see 20-30% higher engagement
✅ **Newsletter Building** - Email lists provide owned audience, reducing algorithm dependency

### What's Not Working

❌ **Thin Affiliate Sites** - Pure product roundup sites struggle post-2024 updates
❌ **AI-Only Content** - Unedited AI content gets filtered; human review essential
❌ **Keyword Stuffing** - Natural language and user intent prioritized
❌ **Exact Match Domains** - Brand building matters more than keyword domains
❌ **Link Schemes** | Paid links, PBNs, excessive exchanges risk penalties

### Emerging Opportunities

🔥 **Micro-Communities** - Niche forums, Discord servers, Facebook groups as traffic sources
🔥 **Original Data/Research** - Proprietary studies earn links and media coverage
🔥 **Expert Networks** - Contributing quotes to HARO, Qwoted, Help a B2B Writer
🔥 **Multi-Format Content** - Articles + videos + podcasts + downloadable resources
🔥 **Local + National Hybrid** - Local service pages with national informational content

## Action Steps

1. **Choose your niche** based on profitability matrix and your expertise
2. **Validate demand** using keyword research tools (Ahrefs, SEMrush, or free alternatives)
3. **Analyze competitors** - What's ranking? What content gaps exist?
4. **Calculate potential** - Estimate traffic × RPM + affiliate conversions
5. **Commit to 12+ months** - Authority sites take time to mature
