### Portfolio Optimization Strategy

- Top 20% of sites generate 80% of revenue
- Focus resources on top performers
- Double down on what works

#### Step 2: Fix Mid-Performers
- Audit content quality
- Improve monetization
- Add internal linking
- Build backlinks

#### Step 3: Kill or Pivot Losers
- After 6 months, if < $500/mo:
  - Option A: Pivot content strategy
  - Option B: Merge with stronger site
  - Option C: Let expire

## 🚨 Risk Management

### Common Scaling Risks

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|------------|
| Google algorithm update | High | Diversify traffic, focus on quality |
| Affiliate program changes | Medium | Multiple programs per site |
| Content quality issues | Medium | Strong editing process |
| Team management challenges | Medium | Clear SOPs, regular check-ins |
| Cash flow problems | Medium | Maintain 6-month runway |
| Burnout | High | Delegate, automate, hire |
