## 📊 Affiliate Placement Strategy

### High-Converting Placements

```
1. Product Reviews (Highest CTR)
   - In-depth review with pros/cons
   - Comparison tables
   - "Best of" roundups
   - CTA: "Check Price on [Retailer]"

2. Top Lists (High CTR)
   - "10 Best [Products] for [Use Case]"
   - "X vs Y Comparison"
   - "Top 5 [Products] Under $100"
   - CTA: "See Full List & Prices"

3. Tutorial Integration (Medium CTR)
   - "How to [Achieve Goal] with [Product]"
   - Step-by-step guides
   - CTA: "Get [Product] to Follow Along"

4. Resource Pages (Medium CTR)
   - "Essential Tools for [Goal]"
   - "My Setup & Gear"
   - CTA: "Get These Tools"

5. Contextual Links (Low CTR, Passive)
   - Natural mentions in content
   - Product name hyperlinked
   - CTA: Minimal
```

### CTA Button Best Practices

```markdown
## Effective CTAs

**Primary CTA:**
- Color: Contrasting (orange, green, blue)
- Text: Action-oriented
  - ✓ "Check Current Price"
  - ✓ "Get 20% Off Today"
  - ✓ "See Availability"
  - ✗ "Click Here"
- Placement: Above fold, after key points, in conclusion

**Secondary CTA:**
- Text: "Compare Prices" or "Read Review"
- Style: Outline button
- Placement: Next to primary CTA

**In-content CTA:**
- Text: Natural link within content
- Style: Highlighted text
- Placement: After relevant section
```

## 🎁 Sponsored Content

### Pricing Guide

| Pageviews/Mo | CPM Rate | Per Post |
|--------------|----------|----------|
| 10K-50K | $20-40 | $200-500 |
| 50K-100K | $40-70 | $500-1,500 |
| 100K-500K | $70-120 | $1,500-5,000 |
| 500K-1M | $120-200 | $5,000-15,000 |
| 1M+ | $200-400 | $15,000-50,000 |

### Sponsorship Types

1. **Sponsored Article** ($500-5,000)
   - Full article about product/service
   - Disclosure required
   - 1-2 links allowed

2. **Product Review** ($300-3,000)
   - Honest review of sponsored product
   - Must include pros/cons
   - 2-3 links allowed

3. **Banner Ad** ($200-2,000/month)
   - 300x250 or 728x90 placement
   - 1-month minimum
   - Performance tracking

4. **Newsletter Sponsorship** ($500-5,000)
   - Dedicated email to subscribers
   - 1-2 links + logo
   - 1-time or recurring

## ⚠️ Compliance & Disclosure

### Required Disclosures

```html
<!-- Amazon Associates Disclosure -->
<div class="affiliate-disclosure">
  <p>As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases.</p>
</div>

<!-- General Affiliate Disclosure -->
<div class="affiliate-disclosure">
  <p>This post contains affiliate links. If you use these links 
  to buy something we may earn a commission. Thanks!</p>
</div>

<!-- Sponsored Content Disclosure -->
<div class="sponsored-disclosure">
  <p><strong>Sponsored:</strong> This article was written in 
  partnership with [Company Name].</p>
</div>
```

### FTC Compliance

1. **Clear and conspicuous** disclosure
2. **Before** the affiliate link
3. **Easy to understand** language
4. **Visible** on every page with affiliate links
