### Post 2: Thought Leadership - ATS Article (March 26)

```
🤖 Why 75% of CVs Never Reach Human Eyes (And How to Fix Yours)

Most job applications are rejected by ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems) before a recruiter ever sees them.

Here's what I learned creating 15 ATS-optimized CV templates:

1. FORMAT MATTERS MORE THAN YOU THINK
Single-column layouts pass ATS 3x more often than creative multi-column designs.

2. FONTS AREN'T JUST AESTHETIC
Arial, Calibri, and Times New Roman aren't boring - they're ATS-safe.

3. HEADINGS ARE KEY
"Work Experience" ✅ vs. "Where I've Been" ❌
ATS looks for standard section names.

4. NO GRAPHICS (sorry, designers)
Icons, charts, and images get scrambled by ATS. Keep it text-based.

5. KEYWORDS WIN
Mirror the language from the job description. If they say "project management," don't write "led initiatives."

The good news? You don't need to be a designer to create an ATS-friendly CV. That's literally why I built my template shop! 

What's your biggest CV struggle? Let me know in the comments! 👇

#ATS #ResumeWriting #JobSearch #CareerAdvice #HRTech #Recruiting #JobSeeker #CareerTips #ProfessionalDevelopment
```
