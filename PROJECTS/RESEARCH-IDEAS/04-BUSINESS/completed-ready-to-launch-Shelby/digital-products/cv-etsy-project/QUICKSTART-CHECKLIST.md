## 🆘 If You Get Stuck

**Template Design Issues:**
- Re-read `TEMPLATE-SPECS.md`
- Search Canva templates for "resume" as starting point
- Keep it simple - clean is better than fancy for ATS

**Etsy Setup Issues:**
- Check Etsy Seller Handbook
- Search YouTube: "how to set up Etsy shop 2026"
- Message me for help with copy/writing

**Mockup Issues:**
- Use Canva's pre-made mockup templates
- Keep it simple: template preview + text overlay
- Consistency > perfection

**Motivation Issues:**
- Remember: Done is better than perfect
- You can always improve listings later
- First sale is the hardest - it gets easier
- Visualize your first 5-star review! ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
