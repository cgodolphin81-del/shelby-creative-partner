## 🚨 Critical Reminders

### ATS Compliance (MOST IMPORTANT)
Your templates MUST be ATS-friendly or customers will request refunds. Test each template:
1. Copy content from PDF
2. Paste into plain text editor (Notepad)
3. Verify structure is maintained (no jumbled text)
4. If it fails, redesign without complex formatting

### File Delivery
- Etsy handles digital delivery automatically
- Upload files to each listing (PDF + DOCX for each size)
- Total: 4 files per template × 15 templates = 60 files
- Organize files in labeled folders before uploading

### Customer Service
- Respond to messages within 24 hours (ideally faster)
- Be helpful and friendly
- If someone has technical issues, offer to help troubleshoot
- Good reviews are critical for new shops
