## 🎨 CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

### For Personal Branding:
1. Use your headshot consistently
2. Match colors to your personal brand
3. Keep fonts consistent
4. Add your website/handle to templates

### For Companies:
1. Use brand colors and logo
2. Create template variations for different content types
3. Ensure all team members use consistent styling
4. Include company website on banners
