## 💡 Pricing Psychology

### Anchor Pricing
- Show "Regular Price: $97" crossed out
- Display "Today: $47" prominently
- Creates perceived value and urgency

### Charm Pricing
- $47 instead of $50
- $27 instead of $30
- Odd numbers feel like better deals

### Tier Strategy
- 3 tiers guide buyers to middle option
- Most people choose PRO (your target)
- PREMIUM makes PRO look reasonable

## 💳 Payment Options

### Gumroad (Recommended for Start)
- **Fee:** 10% + $0.30 per sale
- **Payout:** Instant to debit card, or weekly to bank
- **Pros:** Easy setup, built-in audience, handles VAT
- **Cons:** Higher fees than some alternatives

### Stripe + Lemon Squeezy
- **Fee:** ~5-8% total
- **Payout:** Weekly
- **Pros:** Lower fees, professional checkout
- **Cons:** More setup required

### Self-Hosted (WooCommerce)
- **Fee:** ~3% (Stripe fees only)
- **Payout:** 2-day rolling
- **Pros:** Lowest fees, full control
- **Cons:** Most setup, handle VAT yourself

**Recommendation:** Start with Gumroad, migrate when hitting $5k/month.
