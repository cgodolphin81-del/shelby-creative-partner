# FREELANCER'S AI TOOLKIT - PRICING & MARKETING SUMMARY

## PRICING STRATEGY

### Tier Structure
- **Basic ($67):** 20 tool guides, 5 workflows, templates, prompts, email support
- **Premium ($97):** Basic + Notion OS, videos (90 min), bonuses 1-5, priority support, 6mo updates
- **Agency ($197):** Premium + team license (5 users), white-label, client templates, 1yr updates, onboarding call

### Pricing Psychology
- Anchor at $297 (crossed out)
- Show "Best Value" on Premium
- Agency appears as professional investment

### Revenue Projections (Conservative)
- Month 1: 150 sales × $80 avg = $12,000
- Month 3: 400 sales × $80 avg = $32,000
- Month 6: 700 sales × $80 avg = $56,000
- Year 1: 3,000 sales × $80 avg = $240,000
