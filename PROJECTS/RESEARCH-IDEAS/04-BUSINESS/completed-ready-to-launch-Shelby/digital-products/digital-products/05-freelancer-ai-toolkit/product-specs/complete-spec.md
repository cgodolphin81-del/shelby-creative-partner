## WORKFLOWS INCLUDED

### Workflow 1: Client Onboarding System
**Tools:** Notion AI, Zapier, Grammarly, Tome
**Time Saved:** 3-5 hours per client
**Steps:**
1. Intake form → Notion database (Zapier)
2. Welcome email draft (ChatGPT)
3. Proposal/pitch deck (Tome AI)
4. Contract review (Grammarly)
5. Onboarding doc (Notion AI)

### Workflow 2: Content Creation System
**Tools:** ChatGPT, Midjourney, Canva, Descript
**Time Saved:** 8-12 hours per week
**Steps:**
1. Topic research (ChatGPT)
2. Outline generation (ChatGPT)
3. First draft (ChatGPT/Claude)
4. Images/graphics (Midjourney + Canva)
5. Video version (Descript)
6. Social snippets (ChatGPT + Canva)

### Workflow 3: Outreach & Sales System
**Tools:** Lavender, ChatGPT, Crystal, Zapier
**Time Saved:** 5-8 hours per week
**Steps:**
1. Prospect list building
2. Personalization research (ChatGPT)
3. Email drafting (Lavender + ChatGPT)
4. Tone optimization (Crystal)
5. Follow-up sequences (Lavender)
6. Meeting booking (Zapier → Calendar)

### Workflow 4: Delivery & Quality System
**Tools:** Grammarly, Notion AI, Fireflies, Zapier
**Time Saved:** 4-6 hours per project
**Steps:**
1. Client meeting transcription (Fireflies)
2. Action items extraction (Notion AI)
3. Draft creation (ChatGPT)
4. Quality check (Grammarly + human review)
5. Delivery package (Notion)
6. Feedback collection (Zapier)

### Workflow 5: Admin & Operations System
**Tools:** Notion, Zapier, Reclaim, Fireflies
**Time Saved:** 3-5 hours per week
**Steps:**
1. Time tracking (automatic)
2. Invoice generation (Zapier)
3. Meeting scheduling (Reclaim)
4. Meeting notes (Fireflies)
5. Weekly review (Notion AI)
6. Planning (Reclaim + Notion)
