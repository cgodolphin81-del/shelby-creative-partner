## 📐 GLOBAL SETTINGS

### Canvas Sizes:
- **Instagram Posts:** 1080 x 1080 px (square)
- **Instagram Stories:** 1080 x 1920 px (vertical)
- **Instagram Portrait:** 1080 x 1350 px (4:5 ratio)

### Brand Colors (Customizable):
```
Primary: #2563EB (Royal Blue)
Secondary: #7C3AED (Purple)
Accent: #F59E0B (Amber)
Success: #10B981 (Emerald)
Error: #EF4444 (Red)
Neutral Light: #F9FAFB
Neutral Dark: #1F2937
White: #FFFFFF
Black: #000000
```

### Font Pairings:
```
Heading Font: Montserrat Bold (or Poppins Bold)
Subheading: Montserrat SemiBold
Body: Montserrat Regular
Accent: Montserrat Light Italic

Alternative Pairing:
Heading: Playfair Display Bold
Body: Lato Regular
```

### Grid System:
- **Margins:** 60px on all sides
- **Gutter:** 20px between elements
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text 100px from edges

## 🎨 HOW TO USE THESE TEMPLATES IN CANVA

### Step 1: Set Up Brand Kit
1. Go to Brand Hub in Canva
2. Add your brand colors (replace the defaults)
3. Upload your logo
4. Set your brand fonts

### Step 2: Create Template
1. Click "Create a design" → Custom size (1080 x 1080)
2. Follow the specifications above
3. Save as template (click "Share" → "Template link")

### Step 3: Customize for Each Use
1. Open template
2. Replace text with your content
3. Swap images as needed
4. Adjust colors if needed
5. Download or share

### Step 4: Organize in Folders
Create folders in Canva:
- 📁 Instagram Posts
  - 📁 Quotes
  - 📁 Promotional
  - 📁 Educational
  - 📁 BTS
  - 📁 Engagement
  - 📁 Testimonials
  - 📁 Seasonal
- 📁 Instagram Stories
