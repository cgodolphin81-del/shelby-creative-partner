# AI PROMPT LIBRARY - MARKETING PLAN

## Target Audience

### Primary: Content Creators & Marketers
- Age: 25-45
- Platforms: Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram
- Pain points: Content volume, quality, time
- Budget: $50-200 for tools/resources

### Secondary: Entrepreneurs & Freelancers
- Age: 28-50
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube
- Pain points: Wearing many hats, scaling output
- Budget: $100-500 for business tools

### Tertiary: Developers & Technical Users
- Age: 22-40
- Platforms: Twitter, GitHub, Reddit
- Pain points: Coding speed, documentation
- Budget: $50-300 for productivity tools

## Marketing Channels

## Launch Strategy

### Pre-Launch (2 weeks before)

**Week -2:**
- Tease on social media
- Build waitlist (optional)
- Recruit affiliates
- Prepare all assets

**Week -1:**
- Daily teasers
- Share behind-the-scenes
- "Coming soon" email
- Affiliate briefings

### Launch Week

**Day 1 (Launch Day):**
- Email list: Launch announcement
- Social: All platforms
- Paid ads: Turn on
- Affiliates: Send launch kit
- Goal: 50 sales Day 1

**Day 2-3:**
- Share early testimonials
- Post demo videos
- Engage with comments
- Retargeting ads

**Day 4-5:**
- Case study post
- Live Q&A (Twitter Spaces/LinkedIn Live)
- Urgency: "Price increases soon"

**Day 6-7:**
- Launch recap
- Thank you post
- Final urgency push
- Goal: 200 sales Week 1

### Post-Launch (Ongoing)

**Week 2-4:**
- Continue content marketing
- Collect testimonials
- Optimize ads
- Affiliate outreach

**Month 2-3:**
- Add new prompts (free update)
- Announce to existing customers
- Re-launch to list
- Expand ad spend

**Ongoing:**
- Weekly content
- Monthly promotions
- Quarterly updates
- Annual price increases

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Conversion
**Mitigation:**
- A/B test landing page
- Improve social proof
- Adjust pricing
- Strengthen guarantee

### Risk: High Refunds
**Mitigation:**
- Improve product quality
- Set clearer expectations
- Add more onboarding
- Enhance support

### Risk: Ad Account Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Diversify channels
- Build organic presence
- Focus on email list
- Leverage affiliates

### Risk: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Continuous updates
- Build community
- Focus on quality
- Develop moat (brand, testimonials)
