# Excel Budget Template - Complete Digital Product

## 📊 Product Overview

Professional Excel budget template for personal finance management. Track income, expenses, savings, and achieve financial goals.

## 📦 What's Included

### Excel Workbook Features
- **Dashboard** - Visual overview with charts
- **Income Tracker** - All income sources
- **Expense Tracker** - Categorized expenses
- **Monthly Budget** - Budget vs actual comparison
- **Annual Summary** - Year-at-a-glance
- **Savings Goals** - Track progress to goals
- **Debt Payoff** - Debt snowball/avalanche calculator
- **Net Worth** - Asset and liability tracker

### Built-in Formulas
- Automatic categorization
- Month-over-month comparisons
- Spending trend analysis
- Budget variance calculations
- Savings rate calculations
- Debt payoff projections

### Visual Elements
- Interactive charts (pie, bar, line)
- Conditional formatting (over/under budget)
- Progress bars for goals
- Color-coded categories
