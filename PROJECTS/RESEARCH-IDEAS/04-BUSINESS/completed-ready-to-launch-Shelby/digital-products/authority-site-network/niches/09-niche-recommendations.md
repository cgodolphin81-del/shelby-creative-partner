## 📊 Niche Selection Criteria

**What Makes a Profitable Niche in 2026:**

1. **High Buyer Intent** - People ready to spend money
2. **Recurring Commissions** - Monthly/annual subscriptions
3. **High Ticket Prices** - $100+ average order value
4. **Growing Market** - Expanding, not shrinking
5. **Evergreen Demand** - Not a fad
6. **Multiple Affiliate Programs** - Diversification options
7. **Content Scalability** - Hundreds of article ideas
8. **AI-Resistant** - Requires human expertise/experience
