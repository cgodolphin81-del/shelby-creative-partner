## 📋 Essential Shop Policies

### 1. Payment Policy

**Template:**
```
PAYMENT POLICY

We accept all payment methods available through Etsy:
✓ Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
✓ PayPal
✓ Apple Pay
✓ Google Pay
✓ Etsy Gift Cards
✓ Klarna (Buy Now, Pay Later)

All payments are processed securely through Etsy's 
payment system. Your financial information is never 
stored on our servers.

For custom orders, a 50% deposit is required before 
work begins. The remaining balance is due before 
delivery of final files.
```

### 2. Processing Time Policy

**Template:**
```
PROCESSING TIME

INSTANT DOWNLOADS:
Most digital products are available for immediate

an email with download links within minutes.

Custom designs require 3-5 business days for creation.
```

### 3. Delivery & Download Policy

**Template:**
```
DELIVERY & DOWNLOADS

HOW YOU RECEIVE YOUR FILES:
1. After purchase, you'll receive an email from Etsy 
   with download links
2. Files are also available in your Etsy account:
   "Account" → "Purchases and Reviews"
3. You can download files up to 5 times from your 
   Etsy account

FILE TYPES YOU MAY RECEIVE:
- PDF files (for printables)
- ZIP files (containing multiple files)
- SVG files (for cutting machines)
- JPG/PNG images
- Template links (Canva, Notion, etc.)

DOWNLOAD INSTRUCTIONS:
✓ Click the download button in your email or Etsy 
  account
✓ Save files to your computer/cloud storage
✓ For printables: Download in highest quality (300 DPI)
✓ For templates: Click "Use Template" link

IMPORTANT: We recommend saving files immediately and 
backing them up. Etsy only stores download links for 
14 days after purchase.
```

### 4. Refund & Return Policy

**Template:**
```
REFUND & RETURN POLICY

Due to the digital nature of our products, we cannot 
accept returns or offer refunds once files have been 
downloaded. This is standard practice for digital 
products on Etsy.

WE WILL REFUND IF:
✓ The file is corrupted or won't open
✓ You received the wrong item
✓ There's a technical error preventing use
✓ The product doesn't match the description

WE CANNOT REFUND IF:
✓ You changed your mind after downloading
✓ You didn't read the product description carefully
✓ You don't have the right software to open files
✓ You're unhappy with the design after downloading

HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND:
Message us within 7 days of purchase with:
1. Your order number
2. Description of the issue
3. Screenshots if applicable

We'll resolve your issue within 24-48 hours!
```

### 5. Cancellation Policy

**Template:**
```
CANCELLATION POLICY

INSTANT DOWNLOADS:
Cancellations can only be accepted within 2 hours of 
purchase and only if the file has NOT been downloaded. 
Once downloaded, the sale is final.

CUSTOM ORDERS:
Custom orders can be cancelled before the first draft 
is sent. If work has begun, a 50% cancellation fee 
applies to cover time invested.

HOW TO CANCEL:
Message us immediately at [your email or Etsy message] 
with your order number and reason for cancellation.
```

## 📝 FAQ Template

**Copy and paste this into your shop FAQ section:**

```
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I download my files?
A: After purchase, you'll receive an email with 
   download links. Files are also in your Etsy 
   account under "Purchases and Reviews."

Q: What if I can't download my files?
A: First, check your spam folder for the email. 
   If still missing, message us with your order 
   number and we'll resend immediately!

Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Due to the digital nature of products, refunds 
   are only given for technical issues or wrong 
   files. Please read descriptions carefully before 
   purchasing.

Q: Can I resell your designs?
A: Standard purchases are for personal use only. 
   Commercial licenses are available - message us!

Q: What software do I need?
A: Most files work with free software! We include 
   instructions with every purchase. PDFs need 
   Adobe Reader (free), SVGs need Cricut/Silhouette 
   (free trials available).

Q: Do you offer custom orders?
A: Yes! Message us with your vision. Custom orders 
   require a 50% deposit and take 5-7 days.

Q: How do I contact you?
A: Best way is through Etsy Messages! We respond 
   within 24 hours. For urgent matters, email: 
   [your email]
```
