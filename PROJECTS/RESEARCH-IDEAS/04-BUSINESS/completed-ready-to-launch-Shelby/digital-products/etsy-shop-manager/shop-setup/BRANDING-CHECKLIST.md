# 🎨 Shop Branding Checklist

Complete this checklist before launching your Etsy shop.

---

## ✅ Shop Identity

### Shop Name
- [ ] Name is memorable and brandable
- [ ] Name reflects your niche/products
- [ ] Name is easy to spell and pronounce
- [ ] Name is available on social media platforms
- [ ] Name doesn't infringe on trademarks
- [ ] Name is under 20 characters (displays better)

**Name Ideas Worksheet:**
```
Primary: _______________________
Backup 1: ______________________
Backup 2: ______________________
Backup 3: ______________________
```

### Shop Logo
- [ ] Logo is 500x500px minimum (Etsy requirement)
- [ ] Logo is clear at small sizes (thumbnail)
- [ ] Logo reflects your brand personality
- [ ] Logo works in color AND black/white
- [ ] File format: PNG with transparent background

### Shop Banner
- [ ] Banner is 3360x840px (recommended size)
- [ ] Banner displays your best products
- [ ] Banner includes shop name/tagline
- [ ] Banner is on-brand (colors, fonts, style)
- [ ] Mobile version looks good (test on phone)

### Color Palette
- [ ] Primary color: _______ (hex: _____)
- [ ] Secondary color: _______ (hex: _____)
- [ ] Accent color: _______ (hex: _____)
- [ ] Background color: _______ (hex: _____)
- [ ] Text color: _______ (hex: _____)

**Tip:** Use 2-3 colors max for cohesive branding.

### Typography
- [ ] Primary font (headings): ________________
- [ ] Secondary font (body): ________________

## ✅ Shop Story & About Section

### About Section Checklist
- [ ] Shop owner photo (friendly, professional)
- [ ] Shop story (why you started)
- [ ] What makes your products unique
- [ ] Your process/how products are made

- [ ] Call-to-action (follow, favorite, contact)

## ✅ Shop Policies

### Required Policies
- [ ] Payment methods accepted
- [ ] Processing time (digital: instant-24hrs)
- [ ] Delivery method (digital download)
- [ ] Returns/exchanges policy
- [ ] Cancellation policy
- [ ] Custom orders policy

**Digital Product Policy Template:**
```markdown
PAYMENT
We accept all Etsy payment methods including credit cards, 
PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

PROCESSING TIME
Digital products are available for instant download after 
payment confirmation. Some custom items may take 24-48 hours.

DELIVERY
All items are digital downloads. You'll receive an email from 
Etsy with download links, and items are also available in your 
Etsy account under "Purchases and Reviews."

RETURNS & EXCHANGES
Due to the digital nature of our products, we cannot accept 
returns or offer refunds once the file has been downloaded. 
However, if you have any issues with your file, please message 
us and we'll happily help resolve it!

CANCELLATIONS
Please message us within 2 hours of purchase if you need to 
cancel. After files are downloaded, cancellations cannot be 
accepted.

CUSTOM ORDERS
We love creating custom designs! Message us before ordering 
to discuss your vision, timeline, and pricing.
```

### FAQ Section
- [ ] How do I download my files?
- [ ] What if I have trouble downloading?
- [ ] Can I get a refund?
- [ ] Can I resell your designs?
- [ ] Do you offer custom orders?
- [ ] How do I contact you?

## ✅ Social Proof Setup

### Before Launch
- [ ] Create 3-5 mock reviews (from friends/family who purchase)
- [ ] Prepare response templates for reviews
- [ ] Set up review request system (follow-up messages)

### Review Strategy
- [ ] Include thank-you note in downloads
- [ ] Send follow-up message 3-5 days after purchase
- [ ] Respond to ALL reviews (positive and negative)
- [ ] Address any issues publicly and professionally
