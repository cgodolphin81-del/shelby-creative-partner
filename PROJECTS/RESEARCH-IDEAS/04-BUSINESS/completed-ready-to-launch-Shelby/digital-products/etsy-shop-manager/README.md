A comprehensive system for building, managing, and scaling a successful Etsy shop selling digital products.

## 💡 How to Use This System

1. **Follow the order** - Start with shop setup, work through sequentially
2. **Customize everything** - Adapt templates to your niche and brand
3. **Track your progress** - Use the checklists in each folder
4. **Review analytics monthly** - Optimize based on data
5. **Scale intentionally** - Don't expand until current shop is profitable

## 🔑 Success Metrics

- **Conversion Rate:** Target 2-5%
- **Average Order Value:** $15-50 (digital products)
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** 3+ purchases
- **Review Rating:** 4.8+ stars
- **Monthly Revenue Goals:**
  - Month 1: $100-500
  - Month 3: $500-2,000
  - Month 6: $2,000-10,000
  - Year 1: $10,000-50,000+
