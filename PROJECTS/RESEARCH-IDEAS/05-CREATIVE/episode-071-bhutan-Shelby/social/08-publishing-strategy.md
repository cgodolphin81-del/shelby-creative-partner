## 🚨 Crisis Management

### Potential Issues

**1. Cultural Sensitivity Concerns**
- *Risk:* Accusations of cultural appropriation or exoticizing
- *Response:* Acknowledge, clarify intent, amplify Bhutanese voices
- *Prevention:* Cultural sensitivity notes in all content

**2. Factual Inaccuracies**
- *Risk:* Wrong information about Bhutan, GNH, Buddhism
- *Response:* Correct publicly, thank person who pointed out
- *Prevention:* Fact-check all claims before publishing

**3. Tourism Impact Concerns**
- *Risk:* "You're encouraging overtourism"
- *Response:* Emphasize responsible travel, SDF benefits, limits
- *Prevention:* Include sustainable travel messaging

**4. Technical Issues**
- *Risk:* Video won't play, audio problems, broken links
- *Response:* Fix immediately, communicate transparently
- *Prevention:* Test everything before launch

### Response Protocol

1. **Acknowledge within 2 hours**
2. **Assess validity of concern**
3. **Craft thoughtful response (not defensive)**
4. **Take action if needed (edit, correct, remove)**
5. **Document learnings for future episodes**
