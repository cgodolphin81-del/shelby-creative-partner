## Usage Notes

**Best Practices:**
- Use `--v 6` for Midjourney v6 (latest, most photorealistic)
- Add `--style raw` for less artistic interpretation, more photographic
- Use `--stylize 700-800` for artistic/abstract prompts
- For DALL-E 3: Remove Midjourney parameters, keep descriptive text
- For Stable Diffusion: Add negative prompts (blurry, distorted, low quality)

**Aspect Ratios:**
- `--ar 16:9` = YouTube, landscape, presentations
- `--ar 9:16` = Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels, vertical content
- `--ar 1:1` = Instagram posts, thumbnails, social squares
- `--ar 3:4` = Pinterest, portrait orientation
- `--ar 4:3` = Traditional photography, some social formats

**Color Palette Integration:**
- Desert gold: #D4A843
- Steel grey: #4A5568
- Oasis green: #38A169
- Midnight blue: #1A365D
- Mirror silver: #C0C0C0
