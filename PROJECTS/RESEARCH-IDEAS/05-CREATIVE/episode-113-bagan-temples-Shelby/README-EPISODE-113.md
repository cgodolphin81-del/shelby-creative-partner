### Key Themes

1. **Temples Rising from Dust**
   - Physical: 2,200 temples emerging from plain
   - Metaphorical: Civilization, faith, devotion made visible
   - **Use in:** Opening hooks, visual content

2. **Devotion in Brick**
   - Craft, precision, work that outlasts the worker
   - The question: What are you building?
   - **Use in:** LinkedIn content, professional audiences

3. **Sunrise Over Plains**
   - Daily rebirth, light revealing pattern
   - The moment when chaos becomes order
   - **Use in:** Visual content, Instagram, sunrise posts

4. **Silence and Prayer**
   - Silence as practiced (not absence)
   - Respect, reverence, collective experience
   - **Use in:** Contemplative posts, meditation audiences

5. **Myanmar's Troubled Beauty**
   - Acknowledging complexity
   - Beauty exists alongside suffering
   - Both are true
   - **Use in:** Thoughtful discussions, ethical travel content

## ETHICAL GUIDELINES

### Political Sensitivity

This episode addresses Myanmar's political situation honestly but does not prescribe action. Key principles:

1. **Present facts, not opinions:** "Bagan is beautiful. Myanmar is suffering. Both are true."
2. **Acknowledge complexity:** No simple answers to "Should you visit?"
3. **Respect local voices:** Center Burmese perspectives where possible
4. **Avoid exploitation:** Do not use suffering as a marketing hook
5. **Provide resources:** Link to humanitarian organizations in show notes

### Cultural Respect

1. **Pronunciation:** Verify all Burmese place names
2. **Dress code:** Model respectful temple attire in all visuals
3. **Photography:** Never show worshippers without permission (use silhouettes, distance)
4. **Monks:** Respectful representation, no exploitation
5. **Sacred spaces:** Treat all temples as active places of worship

### Environmental Responsibility

1. **Leave no trace:** Model responsible tourism in all content
2. **Do not encourage:** Climbing on restricted structures, trespassing
3. **Promote:** Local businesses, sustainable practices
4. **Acknowledge:** Environmental impact of travel (especially balloons)
