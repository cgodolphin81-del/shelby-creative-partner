1. **Ocean:** Constant, rhythmic waves (Indian Ocean)
2. **Train:** Kandy-Ella train (famous scenic route)
3. **Wildlife:** Tropical bird (Sri Lanka has 430+ species)
4. **Transition:** Temple bell → ocean → train → silence

**Music:**
- Minimal or none
- Let natural soundscape carry the emotion
- If music: soft strings or piano, very sparse

**Timing:**
- Total: 30-45 seconds
- Pacing: Slow, deliberate
- Silence is your friend — use it

## TIMING STRATEGY

### When to Release Tease

**Option 1: Immediate (T+1 from Episode 113)**
- **Pros:** Capitalizes on Episode 113 momentum
- **Cons:** Might overshadow Episode 113 launch
- **Best if:** Episode 113 is performing very well

**Option 2: Mid-Cycle (T+4 from Episode 113)**
- **Pros:** Maintains momentum, doesn't compete with launch
- **Cons:** Slight gap in content
- **Best if:** Standard release schedule

**Option 3: Pre-Launch Episode 114 (T-3 from Episode 114)**
- **Pros:** Builds specific anticipation for Ep 114
- **Cons:** Longer gap between episodes
- **Best if:** Building to a specific release date

**Recommended:** Option 2 — Release tease at T+4 (four days after Episode 113 launch). This maintains engagement without overshadowing the current episode.
