# Episode 082 Tease: Next Destination

## The Tease

**From the Pink City... to the City of Lakes.**

We've walked through Jaipur's pink sandstone streets. We've watched sunrise over Hawa Mahal's 953 windows. We've marveled at the astronomer-king's legacy written in stone.

But India has more stories to tell.

Next time on the Infinite Walk, we journey to a place where water mirrors palaces. Where every ripple tells a tale of royalty. Where the moon rises over lakes dotted with islands of marble and memory.

A city built not on one color, but on many. Not on one vision, but on centuries of dreams layered like petals.

The Pink City welcomed us. The City of Lakes will captivate us.

**Episode 082: Udaipur, India.**

Coming soon to the Infinite Walk.
