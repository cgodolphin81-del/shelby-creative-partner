## Image Style Guide

### Photography Style

**Golden Hour Priority**
- Shoot/Select: Sunrise and sunset images
- Temperature: Warm (5500-6500K)
- Mood: Nostalgic, dreamy, inviting

**High Dynamic Range**
- Capture: Both shadows and highlights
- Purpose: Show intricate architectural details
- Style: Natural, not over-processed

**Human Element**
- Include: People in frames (where appropriate)
- Purpose: Scale, life, cultural context
- Style: Candid, respectful, authentic

**Architectural Details**
- Focus: Intricate carvings, patterns, textures
- Purpose: Show craftsmanship
- Style: Sharp, detailed, reverent

---

### AI Image Generation Guidelines

**Prompt Structure:**
```
[Subject], [Location/Setting], [Lighting], [Mood], [Style], [Technical]
```

**Example:**
```
"Amber Fort at sunrise, pink sandstone glowing, Maota Lake reflection, 
golden hour lighting, serene atmosphere, travel photography style, 8K"
```

**Consistent Elements:**
- Warm color grading
- High detail (8K quality)
- Natural lighting preferred
- Cultural authenticity
- Respectful representation

**Avoid:**
- Over-saturation
- Stereotypical imagery
- Inaccurate architectural details
- Disrespectful cultural portrayal

## Composition Guidelines

### Rule of Thirds
- Place key elements at intersection points
- Use for: Landscape shots, architectural photos
- Exception: Symmetrical compositions (palace gates)

### Leading Lines
- Use: Streets, corridors, palace halls
- Purpose: Draw viewer into scene
- Example: Pink street leading to Hawa Mahal

### Framing
- Use: Arches, windows, doorways
- Purpose: Create depth, focus attention
- Example: Shooting through jharokha lattice

### Symmetry
- Use: Palace courtyards, Jantar Mantar instruments
- Purpose: Show precision, balance
- Example: Pritam Niwas Chowk four gates

### Scale
- Include: Human figures for monument scale
- Purpose: Show grandeur
- Example: Person at base of Samrat Yantra

## Accessibility Guidelines

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum:** WCAG AA standard (4.5:1 for normal text)
- **Check:** All text on colored backgrounds
- **Tool:** WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- **All Images:** Descriptive alt text
- **Format:** "[Subject] at [location], [key details]"
- **Example:** "Hawa Mahal pink sandstone façade at sunrise with 953 lattice windows"

### Captions
- **All Video:** Closed captions required
- **Style:** White text with black outline
- **Position:** Bottom third, not obscuring key visuals

### Screen Reader
- **Documents:** Proper heading hierarchy
- **Links:** Descriptive link text
- **Icons:** Text labels or aria-labels
