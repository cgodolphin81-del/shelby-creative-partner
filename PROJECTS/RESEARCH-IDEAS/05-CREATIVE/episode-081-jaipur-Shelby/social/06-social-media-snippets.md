### Post 4: Pink Streets
**Image:** Narrow pink street with rickshaw

**Caption:**
Why pink? 🤔

In 1876, the Prince of Wales was visiting Jaipur. Maharaja Ram Singh wanted to welcome him properly. Flowers fade. Feasts end. But color? Color endures.

So he painted the entire city pink—the Rajasthani color of hospitality.

Nearly 150 years later, the city is STILL pink. The municipal corporation enforces it. Building owners maintain it. It's not nostalgia. It's identity.

The Pink City doesn't just welcome you. It receives you. 👑
