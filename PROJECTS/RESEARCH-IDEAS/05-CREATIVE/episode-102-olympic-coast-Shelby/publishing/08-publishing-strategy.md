## LAUNCH DAY (Day 0)

### Optimal Launch Time
**Recommended:** Tuesday or Wednesday, 6:00 AM PST
- Catches East Coast morning commute (9 AM EST)
- West Coast listeners can download for day
- Mid-week performs better than Monday (busy) or Friday (weekend mode)

### Launch Day Timeline

**6:00 AM PST - Episode Goes Live**

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Episode promotion
- 30% Behind-the-scenes
- 20% Educational (tide pools, geology, Indigenous history)
- 10% Personal/Community

**Best Times to Post:**
- Weekdays: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM PST
- Weekends: 9-11 AM, 6-8 PM PST

**Stories Strategy:**
- Daily during Week 1
- Use polls, questions, countdown stickers
- Share listener DMs (with permission)
- Behind-the-scenes snippets

**Reels Strategy:**
- 3-5 Reels in first week
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Text overlay for sound-off viewing
- CTA: "Listen now" or "Link in bio"

---

### TikTok

**Content Strategy:**
- Post 1x/day for first week
- Then 3-4x/week
- Mix of: episode clips, BTS, educational, personal

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 3-5 broad: #podcast #nature #travel
- 3-5 niche: #olympicnationalpark #seastacks #tidepool
- 2-3 branded: #theinfinitewalk #coastaledge

**Engagement:**
- Reply to comments with video responses
- Duet/stitch relevant content
- Jump on trending sounds (when relevant)

---

### YouTube

**Strategy:**
- Upload full episode audio with static image or simple visualizer
- Create 2-3 short clips as YouTube Shorts
- Post community post on launch day
- Add to relevant playlists

**SEO:**
- Title: "The Coastal Edge: Olympic Coast Walking Meditation | Episode 102"
- Description: Full show notes with timestamps
- Tags: olympic coast, walking meditation, nature podcast, washington, sea stacks
- Thumbnail: High-contrast, readable text

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Technical Issues:
- Communicate immediately via Stories/email
- Provide alternative listening links
- Apologize, fix, follow up

### If Negative Feedback:
- Respond graciously (publicly if public)
- Take constructive criticism seriously
- Don't argue, don't delete (unless abusive)
- Learn and improve

### If Low Engagement:
- Don't panic (algorithms vary)
- Double down on engagement (respond to everything)
- Try different content formats
- Ask audience what they want
