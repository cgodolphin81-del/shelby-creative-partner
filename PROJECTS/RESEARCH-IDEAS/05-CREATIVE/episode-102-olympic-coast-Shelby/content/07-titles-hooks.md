## HOOKS BY PLATFORM

### PODCAST INTRO HOOKS (15-30 seconds)

**Hook 1: The Philosophical Open**
```
There is a place where the continent gives up. Not in surrender. Not in defeat. But in a kind of sacred exhaustion. This is Episode 102: The Coastal Edge.
```

**Hook 2: The Geographic Wonder**
```
Seventy-three miles of rugged Washington coastline. Towering sea stacks. Ancient tide pools. Ten thousand years of Indigenous history. Welcome to the Olympic Coast. Welcome to the edge.
```

**Hook 3: The Personal Invitation**
```
I walked for three days along the Olympic Coast. Thirty-three miles of tide-dependent hiking, of scrambling over headlands, of camping on beaches as the sun set over the Pacific. What I found there... changed how I think about edges. About time. About being temporary.
```

**Hook 4: The Paradox**
```
Salt preserves and destroys. Edges end and begin. Rock stands and falls. The Olympic Coast is a place of paradoxes. And it has something to teach us about what it means to become.
```

**Hook 5: The Question**
```
Why do we come to the edge? To the place where land meets sea, where certainty ends and mystery begins? I went to find out. This is what the coast taught me.
```
