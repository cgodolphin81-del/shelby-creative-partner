## VISUAL STYLES

### Photography Style

**Mood:** Moody, atmospheric, contemplative
**Lighting:** Natural, soft, often overcast or golden hour
**Composition:** Wide landscapes, intimate details, human scale
**Editing:** Slightly desaturated, cool tones, subtle vignette

**Key Characteristics:**
- Embrace fog and mist (not flaws, features)
- Show scale (tiny human, vast landscape)
- Capture motion (waves, clouds, flowing water)
- Include texture (driftwood, rocks, sand patterns)
- Find stillness within movement

---

### Graphic Design Style

**Principles:**
1. **Minimal:** Let the photography breathe
2. **Layered:** Use gradients and overlays subtly
3. **Textured:** Add subtle grain or texture for depth
4. **Organic:** Avoid harsh lines, embrace flow
5. **Contemplative:** Give space for reflection

**Layout Guidelines:**
- Generous white (or mist) space
- Text positioned for readability over images
- Consistent margin/padding across platforms
- Hierarchy: Image → Title → Subtitle → CTA

---

### Video Style

**Color Grading:**
- Cool tones (blues, teals)
- Slightly desaturated
- Soft contrast
- Subtle film grain

**Motion:**
- Slow, contemplative pacing
- Smooth transitions (fades, gentle zooms)
- Natural sound emphasized
- Minimal text animation

**Aspect Ratios:**
- 16:9 (YouTube, standard video)
- 9:16 (Stories, Reels, TikTok)
- 1:1 (Instagram feed videos)
- 4:5 (Instagram portrait)

## IMAGE PRESETS / FILTERS

### Lightroom Preset: "Coastal Edge"

**Basic Adjustments:**
- Exposure: -0.3 to -0.7
- Contrast: +10 to +15
- Highlights: -30 to -50
- Shadows: +20 to +30
- Whites: -10 to -20
- Blacks: +10 to +15

**Color Adjustments:**
- Temp: -5 to -10 (cooler)
- Tint: 0 to +5 (slight magenta)
- Vibrance: -10 to -15
- Saturation: -5 to -10

**HSL Adjustments:**
- Blues: Hue -5, Sat -10, Lum -15
- Greens: Hue +10, Sat -20, Lum -10
- Yellows: Sat -30, Lum +10 (desaturate sand)
- Oranges: Sat -20 (skin tones natural)

**Effects:**
- Clarity: +5 to +10
- Dehaze: -5 to -10 (add mist)
- Vignette: -10 to -15

---

### VSCO Filter Recommendations

**Primary:** A6 or HB2 (moody, desaturated)
**Alternative:** C1 or M5 (cool, cinematic)
**Adjustments:** Reduce saturation, add grain, cool temperature

## ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- All text must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)
- Use contrast checker tools before publishing
- Provide alt text for all images

### Alt Text Examples:
- "Dramatic sea stack rising from Pacific Ocean at sunset, Olympic Coast Washington"
- "Colorful tide pool with orange sea stars and green anemones"
- "Shelby sitting on driftwood log looking out at ocean, golden hour"

### Considerations:
- Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning
- Use patterns/textures in addition to color
- Ensure text is large enough to read on mobile

---

## BRAND CONSISTENCY CHECKLIST

### Every Visual Should Have:
- [ ] Consistent color palette (Ocean Blues, Seafoam Greens, Driftwood Browns, Sky Greys)
- [ ] Readable typography (max 2-3 fonts)
- [ ] The Infinite Walk branding (subtle, not overwhelming)
- [ ] Episode number (where relevant)
- [ ] Appropriate aspect ratio for platform
- [ ] Alt text for accessibility
- [ ] Mobile-optimized (check on phone before posting)

### Before Publishing, Ask:
1. Does this feel like Episode 102?
2. Is the text readable?
3. Does it work without sound (for video)?
4. Is it accessible?
5. Does it serve the story?
