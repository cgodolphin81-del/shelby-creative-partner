## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

**1. Factual Errors**
- **Prevention**: Triple-check all facts (dates, numbers, names)
- **Response**: Immediate correction, public acknowledgment, updated show notes

**2. Cultural Sensitivity**
- **Prevention**: Research puffin hunting controversy, present balanced view
- **Response**: Listen, learn, apologize if needed, educate

**3. Technical Issues**
- **Prevention**: Test audio, RSS feed, links before launch
- **Response**: Quick fix, communicate delay if necessary

**4. Negative Feedback**
- **Prevention**: Set expectations (poetic, contemplative style)
- **Response**: Acknowledge, thank for feedback, move forward

### Response Protocol

**Tier 1 (Minor):**
- Social media manager responds within 24 hours
- Document for future reference

**Tier 2 (Moderate):**
- Team discussion within 12 hours
- Public response within 24 hours
- Action plan implemented

**Tier 3 (Major):**
- Immediate team meeting
- Public statement within 6 hours
- Corrective action taken
- Follow-up communication
