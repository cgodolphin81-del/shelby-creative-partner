## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headlines)

**Font Family**: Montserrat / Helvetica Neue / Arial (fallback)
**Weights**: Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600)
**Usage**: Episode titles, social media headers, major headlines
**Style**: Clean, modern, readable, Nordic feel

**Example:**
```
THE FIRE ISLAND
Episode 061
```

---

### Secondary Font (Body Text)

**Font Family**: Open Sans / Lato / Georgia (fallback)
**Weights**: Regular (400), Medium (500)
**Usage**: Body text, captions, descriptions
**Style**: Readable, warm, approachable

**Example:**
```
Say it with me: I am coming back to the fire.
Not running from it. Coming back.
```

---

### Accent Font (Quotes & Emphasis)

**Font Family**: Playfair Display / Georgia / Times New Roman (fallback)
**Weights**: Italic (400 Italic)
**Usage**: Pull quotes, poetic lines, emphasis
**Style**: Elegant, contemplative, literary

**Example:**
```
*"Nothing is ever the same after fire.*
*But life continues. Not despite the fire.*
*Because of it."*
```

---

### Monospace Font (Technical/Data)

**Font Family**: Roboto Mono / Courier New / monospace (fallback)
**Weights**: Regular (400)
**Usage**: Dates, statistics, technical information
**Style**: Precise, factual, documentary

**Example:**
```
January 23, 1973 • 1:55 AM
5,300 evacuated • 400 homes buried
```

- **Composition**: Wide, dramatic, rule of thirds

- **Colors**: Muted, natural, volcanic palette

- **Subject**: Puffins (primary), other seabirds

**Historical/Documentary:**
- **Style**: Black and white or desaturated
- **Subject**: 1973 eruption photos, museum exhibits

- **Mood**: Solemn, educational, reverent

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum**: WCAG AA (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- **Preferred**: WCAG AAA (7:1 for normal text, 4.5:1 for large text)
- **Test**: All combinations before publishing

### Text Size
- **Minimum**: 14px (body text), 18px (preferred)
- **Headlines**: 24px minimum, scalable
- **Mobile**: Ensure readability on small screens

### Alt Text
- **All Images**: Descriptive alt text
- **Decorative**: Empty alt="" if purely decorative
- **Infographics**: Full description in caption or longdesc

### Motion Sensitivity
- **Animations**: Provide pause/stop option
- **Auto-play**: Avoid or provide controls
- **Vestibular**: Avoid rapid flashing, spinning
