## ICE FORMATIONS (1-8)

### 01. Glacier Blue Interior
```
Photorealistic, ultra-detailed. Close-up cross-section of ancient glacier ice, showing deep compressed blue ice layers. Light filtering through translucent ice, creating ethereal blue glow. Visible annual layers, dark bands representing summer deposits, light bands representing winter. Ice texture is smooth yet complex, with tiny air bubbles trapped inside. Shot with natural lighting, no artificial enhancement. Color palette: deep glacier blue (#A8C5D9), ice white (#F8F9FA), subtle gray shadows. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: National Geographic photography, hyperrealistic, 8K.
```

### 02. Iceberg Cathedral
```
Photorealistic, epic scale. Massive iceberg floating in calm Antarctic waters, sculpted by wind and water into cathedral-like arches and spires. Iceberg is 100+ meters tall, white snow-covered top, brilliant blue ice exposed on underwater portion visible through crystal-clear water. Perfect mirror reflection in glass-still water. Overcast sky, soft diffused lighting. Distant dark mountains on horizon. Color palette: ice white, glacier blue, abyss black (#1A202C), steel gray water. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: Ansel Adams landscape photography, dramatic, majestic.
```

### 03. Ice Cave Interior
```
Photorealistic, intimate scale. Inside a glacial ice cave, walls of compressed blue ice glowing with transmitted light. Smooth, undulating ice walls with visible flow patterns. Light source from cave entrance creates gradient from bright white to deep blue. Ice floor is wet, reflective. Small icicles hanging from ceiling. Atmosphere is cold, sacred, cathedral-like. Color palette: electric blue, cyan, white, deep navy shadows. Aspect ratio 4:5. Style: Fine art photography, ethereal, otherworldly.
```

### 04. Calving Glacier Face
```
Photorealistic, action shot. Glacier face at the moment of calving—large chunk of ice breaking off and crashing into water below. Overcast sky, dramatic lighting. Color palette: glacier blue, white foam spray, dark gray water, slate sky. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: Adventure photography, dramatic, powerful.

### 05. Ice Fractures
```
Photorealistic, macro detail. Extreme close-up of ice surface showing intricate fracture patterns. Cracks radiating outward like lightning, filled with deep blue shadows. Ice surface is textured with wind-carved patterns, sastrugi. Some areas translucent, others opaque white. Shot in harsh Antarctic sunlight, creating high contrast shadows. Color palette: white, ice blue, deep blue-black in cracks. Aspect ratio 1:1. Style: Abstract nature photography, textural, geometric.
```

### 06. Frozen Waves

Photorealistic, seascape. Antarctic shoreline where waves have frozen mid-motion, creating sculptural ice formations along the coast. Semi-circular ice waves, frozen spray patterns, layered ice buildup. Dark volcanic rock visible beneath ice. Ocean beyond is dark, choppy. Overcast sky, cold lighting. Color palette: ice white, translucent blue, black rock, steel gray water. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: Coastal landscape photography, dramatic, frozen moment.

### 07. Subglacial Lake Conceptual

Photorealistic, conceptual. Cross-section view showing subglacial lake beneath Antarctic ice sheet. Top layer: 2km thick ice sheet with visible layers. Middle: dark subglacial lake (Lake Vostok style) with hints of microbial life glowing faintly. Bottom: ancient bedrock. Lighting is mysterious, with faint bioluminescent glow from below. Scientific illustration meets photorealism. Color palette: ice blue, deep navy, faint green bioluminescence, dark rock. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: Scientific visualization, cinematic, mysterious.

### 08. Aurora on Ice
```
Photorealistic, nightscape. Aurora australis (Southern Lights) dancing over Antarctic ice shelf. Green and purple aurora curtains filling the sky, stars visible through gaps. Ice shelf below is white with blue shadows, stretching to horizon. Complete darkness except for aurora and starlight. Long exposure effect, aurora slightly blurred from movement. Color palette: aurora green (#68D391), purple, deep navy sky, white ice. Aspect ratio 16:9. Style: Astrophotography, magical, serene.

## PROMPT USAGE NOTES

### Recommended AI Tools
- **Midjourney v6+:** Best for photorealistic landscapes, wildlife
- **DALL-E 3:** Best for conceptual shots, text integration
- **Stable Diffusion XL:** Best for customization, control
- **Adobe Firefly:** Best for commercial-safe images

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9:** Standard video, presentations, web
- **4:5:** Instagram portrait, vertical content
- **21:9:** Cinematic, dramatic landscapes
- **1:1:** Social media squares, infographics

### Style Keywords to Mix In
- `National Geographic photography`
- `BBC Earth documentary`
- `Ansel Adams landscape`
- `Cinematic lighting`
- `Hyperrealistic, 8K`
- `Natural lighting only`
- `No artificial enhancement`
