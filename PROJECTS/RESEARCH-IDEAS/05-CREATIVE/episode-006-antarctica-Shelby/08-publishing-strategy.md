## RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Risks

**1. Technical Issues**
- **Risk:** Audio files corrupt, links broken
- **Mitigation:** Test all links 24 hours before, have backup hosting

**2. Low Engagement**
- **Risk:** Launch falls flat, low downloads
- **Mitigation:** Activate email list, engage personally, extend promo period

**3. Negative Feedback**
- **Risk:** Criticism of AI imagery, poetic style
- **Mitigation:** Acknowledge feedback, stay true to vision, engage constructively

**4. Platform Algorithm Changes**
- **Risk:** Social reach drops unexpectedly
- **Mitigation:** Diversify platforms, build email list, don't rely on single channel

**5. Timing Conflicts**
- **Risk:** Major news event overshadows launch
- **Mitigation:** Monitor news, be prepared to delay if necessary
