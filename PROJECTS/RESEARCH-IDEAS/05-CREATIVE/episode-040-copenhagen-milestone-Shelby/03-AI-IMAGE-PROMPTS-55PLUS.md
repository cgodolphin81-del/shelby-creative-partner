## 🎯 USAGE NOTES

**Recommended AI Tools:**
- Midjourney v6+ for photorealistic scenes
- DALL-E 3 for accurate text and specific compositions
- Stable Diffusion XL for custom fine-tuning
- Runway/Pika for video generation from prompts

**Style Parameters:**
- Use `--style raw` in Midjourney for more photographic results
- Aspect ratios: 16:9 for video thumbnails, 3:2 for blog posts, 4:3 for social
- Add `--v 6.0` or current version for best quality

**Customization Tips:**
- Adjust lighting (golden hour, blue hour, overcast) for mood
- Add/remove people based on preference
- Modify season (winter hygge vs. summer vibrancy)
- Swap specific landmarks as needed
