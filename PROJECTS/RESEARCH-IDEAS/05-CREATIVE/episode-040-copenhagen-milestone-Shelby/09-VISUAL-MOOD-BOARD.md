## 🖼️ VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Photography Style

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour preferred (warm, soft light)
- Blue hour for evening/mood shots
- Avoid harsh midday sun
- Embrace overcast Scandinavian light (soft, diffused)

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines (canals, bike lanes, streets)
- Reflections (water, windows)
- Human element (cyclists, café patrons, street life)
- Architectural details (colorful facades, windows, doors)

**Mood:**
- Warm and inviting (hygge)
- Vibrant but not oversaturated
- Authentic moments over posed shots
- Embrace imperfection (weathered wood, cobblestones)

---

### Graphic Design Style

**Typography:**

**Primary Font: Modern Scandinavian**
- Options: Montserrat, Proxima Nova, Avenir
- Style: Clean, geometric, readable
- Usage: Headlines, titles, main text

**Secondary Font: Warm Humanist**
- Options: Merriweather, Lora, Source Serif Pro
- Style: Approachable, readable, classic
- Usage: Body text, quotes, longer content

**Accent Font: Display (Milestone)**
- Options: Playfair Display, Abril Fatface
- Style: Elegant, celebratory
- Usage: "40 EPISODES" milestone text, special graphics

**Typography Hierarchy:**
```
H1 (Episode Title): 48-64px, Bold, Midnight Blue
H2 (Section): 32-40px, Semibold, Harbor Blue
H3 (Subsection): 24-28px, Medium, Nyhavn Blue
Body: 16-18px, Regular, Warm Wood or Midnight Blue
Caption: 14px, Light, Cozy Gray
```

### Photo Editing Preset

**Lightroom/Photoshop Settings:**

**Exposure:**
- Exposure: 0 to +0.3
- Contrast: +10 to +15
- Highlights: -20 to -30
- Shadows: +20 to +30
- Whites: +10
- Blacks: -5

**Color:**
- Temp: +5 to +10 (warmer)
- Tint: 0 to +5 (slight magenta)
- Vibrance: +10
- Saturation: -5 to 0 (slight desaturation)

**Color Grading:**
- Shadows: Slight blue (#3D5A73, 5-10%)
- Midtones: Warm gold (#F5E6A3, 5-10%)
- Highlights: Warm yellow (#F4B942, 5-10%)

**Effects:**
- Clarity: +5 to +10
- Dehaze: 0 to +5
- Vignette: -10 to -15 (subtle)
- Grain: +5 (for film feel)
