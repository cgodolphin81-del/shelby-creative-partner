## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Cinzel** or **Cormorant Garamond**
- Style: Classical, carved stone aesthetic
- Weights: Regular, Semi-Bold, Bold
- Usage: Episode titles, section headers, quote cards
- Rationale: Evokes ancient inscriptions, Nabatean carvings

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Lato** or **Source Sans Pro**
- Style: Clean, readable, modern
- Weights: Regular, Light, Bold
- Usage: Body text, descriptions, social media captions
- Rationale: Balances classical headings with modern readability

### Accent Font (Special Elements)
**Playfair Display** or **Libre Baskerville**
- Style: Elegant, editorial
- Weights: Regular, Italic, Bold
- Usage: Pull quotes, episode descriptions, email subject lines
- Rationale: Adds sophistication to philosophical content

## TEXTURE LIBRARY

### Stone Textures
**Usage:** Backgrounds, overlays, visual depth

1. **Sandstone Grain**
   - Fine-grained, rose-colored
   - Subtle weathering patterns
   - Use at 10-20% opacity for backgrounds

2. **Carved Tool Marks**
   - Visible chisel marks
   - Directional lines (horizontal/vertical)
   - Use for section dividers, borders

3. **Water Erosion Patterns**
   - Flowing, organic lines
   - Darker streaks on lighter stone
   - Use for transitions, special sections

4. **Weathered Patina**
   - Age-darkened surfaces
   - Uneven coloration
   - Use for vintage/aged effects

### Desert Textures
**Usage:** Backgrounds, atmospheric elements

1. **Desert Sand**
   - Fine, wind-swept patterns
   - Gold to orange gradient
   - Use for warm backgrounds

2. **Canyon Stratification**
   - Layered rock formations
   - Horizontal bands of color
   - Use for section backgrounds

3. **Cracked Earth**
   - Dry, ancient ground
   - Geometric crack patterns
   - Use sparingly for texture

### Light Textures
**Usage:** Overlays, mood enhancement

1. **Dawn Light Rays**
   - Soft, angled beams
   - Warm golden color
   - Use at 15-25% opacity

2. **Dust Particles**
   - Floating specks in light
   - Atmospheric depth
   - Use for mystical/magical moments

3. **Shadow Gradients**
   - Soft to hard transitions
   - Directional (canyon walls)
   - Use for depth, framing

## IMAGERY STYLES

### Photography Style
**Mood:** Contemplative, atmospheric, timeless

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (dawn/dusk) preferred
- Soft, directional light
- Avoid harsh midday sun
- Embrace shadows for depth

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds for landscapes
- Leading lines (Siq canyon)
- Human element for scale (small figures)
- Wide shots for context, close-ups for detail

**Color Treatment:**
- Warm tones emphasized
- Slightly desaturated shadows
- Rich rose-reds and golds
- Natural, not oversaturated

### AI-Generated Imagery
**Style:** Photorealistic with atmospheric enhancement

**Key Elements:**
- Accurate architectural details
- Proper lighting for time of day
- Human figures for scale (when appropriate)
- Atmospheric effects (dust, light rays)

**Avoid:**
- Overly stylized/cartoonish looks
- Inaccurate architectural details
- Anachronistic elements
- Unrealistic colors

### Illustration Style (if used)
**Approach:** Minimalist line art with watercolor washes

**Techniques:**
- Fine line work for architectural details
- Loose watercolor for sky/landscape
- Limited color palette (rose, gold, blue)
- White space for breathing room

## BRANDING ELEMENTS

### Logo Placement
**Podcast Logo:**
- Bottom corner (all platforms)
- Consistent size (not dominant)
- Semi-transparent if over imagery

**Episode Number:**
- Subtle inclusion (066)
- Consistent placement
- Same font as series branding

### Watermarking
**Approach:** Minimal, non-intrusive
- Series name in corner
- Episode title on promotional images
- Website URL on travel guide PDF

### Consistency Rules
1. **Always use** rose-red palette as primary
2. **Never use** cool blues as dominant color (accent only)
3. **Maintain** warm color temperature across all platforms
4. **Include** human element in ~1/3 of visuals (for scale, relatability)
5. **Keep** text overlays minimal and readable

## ACCESSIBILITY CONSIDERATIONS

### Color Contrast
- **Text on backgrounds:** Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- **Large text (headings):** Minimum 3:1 contrast ratio
- **Tools:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- **All images:** Descriptive alt text
- **Include:** Subject, action, context, mood
- **Example:** "The Treasury at Petra, Jordan, glowing pink at dawn with empty courtyard and narrow Siq canyon framing the view"

### Readable Typography
- **Minimum size:** 16px for body text
- **Line height:** 1.5x font size
- **Avoid:** All caps for long text, overly decorative fonts for body

### Multiple Formats
- **Images:** Provide text alternatives
- **Videos:** Include captions/subtitles
- **Audio:** Provide transcripts
