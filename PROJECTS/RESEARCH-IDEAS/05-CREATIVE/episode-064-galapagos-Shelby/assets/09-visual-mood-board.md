## TYPOGRAPHY

### PRIMARY FONT (Headings)
**Recommendation:** Playfair Display or similar serif
- Elegant, timeless, editorial
- Evokes natural history documentation
- Works well for "Darwin" aesthetic

**Weights:**
- Regular (400): Subheadings
- Semi-Bold (600): Main titles
- Bold (700): Episode titles, impact

---

### SECONDARY FONT (Body)
**Recommendation:** Lato, Open Sans, or similar sans-serif
- Clean, readable, modern
- Accessible across platforms
- Pairs well with serif headings

**Weights:**
- Regular (400): Body text
- Medium (500): Emphasis, captions
- Bold (700): Pull quotes

---

### ACCENT FONT (Special Use)
**Recommendation:** Courier New or similar monospace
- Scientific documentation feel
- Darwin's journal aesthetic
- Data, coordinates, dates

**Use sparingly for:**
- Location coordinates
- Species names (scientific)
- Dates, historical references

---

## IMAGE STYLES

### WILDLIFE PORTRAITS
**Style:** National Geographic documentary
- Sharp focus on subject
- Natural lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Environmental context visible
- Eye contact when possible

**Treatment:**
- Slight vignette
- Warm color grade (golden hour)
- High contrast for black/white subjects
- Preserve natural colors (especially booby blue!)

---

### LANDSCAPE SHOTS
**Style:** Cinematic, epic scale
- Wide angle for vastness
- Dramatic sky emphasis
- Include scale reference (person, animal)
- Capture isolation

**Treatment:**
- High dynamic range
- Emphasize black lava/blue water contrast
- Sunset/sunrise preferred
- Mist/fog for atmosphere

---

### CONSERVATION IMAGES
**Style:** Hopeful, human element
- Include conservationists in action
- Baby animals (tortoise hatchlings)
- Before/after when available
- Authentic, not staged

**Treatment:**
- Warmer tones (hope)
- Softer contrast
- Focus on faces (human and animal)
- Natural, documentary feel

---

### ABSTRACT/TEXTURE
**Style:** Artistic, detail-focused
- Lava textures
- Tortoise shell patterns
- Water movement
- Salt crystals (iguana sneeze!)

**Treatment:**
- High contrast
- Macro photography aesthetic
- Can be desaturated for backgrounds
- Use as overlays, textures

## ACCESSIBILITY NOTES

### Color Contrast
- All text must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)
- White text on dark backgrounds: Always safe
- Booby Blue on white: Add dark outline for readability
- Never use red/green alone to convey meaning

### Alt Text
- All images need descriptive alt text
- Include species names when relevant
- Describe action, not just appearance
- Example: "Giant tortoise walking through misty highland forest, Santa Cruz Island"

### Motion
- Avoid rapid flashing (photosensitive epilepsy)
- Subtle animations only
- Provide pause/stop controls for video
