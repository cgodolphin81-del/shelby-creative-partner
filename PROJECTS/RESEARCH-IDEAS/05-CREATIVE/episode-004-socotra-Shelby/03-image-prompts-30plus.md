## Usage Notes

**Aspect Ratios:**
- `--ar 16:9` — Standard video/widescreen
- `--ar 21:9` — Cinematic panoramic
- `--ar 4:5` — Instagram portrait
- `--ar 3:4` — Standard portrait
- `--ar 1:1` — Square format

**Style Parameters:**
- `--style raw` — Midjourney photorealistic
- Add `photorealistic, 8K, National Geographic` for documentary style
- Add `ethereal, mystical, fine art` for artistic interpretations

**Best Practices:**
- Use prompts as-is for DALL-E 3
- Add `--v 6` for Midjourney v6
- Adjust lighting descriptors for time of day
- Modify clothing/details for Shelby consistency
