## GENERATION TIPS

### Best Practices
1. **Generate 4 variations** per prompt for selection
2. **Upscale best results** to maximum resolution
3. **Maintain consistent style** across all episode imagery
4. **Create both formats**: landscape (16:9) for video/thumbnails, square (1:1) for social
5. **Organize by category** for easy access during production

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Always depict temples and religious practices respectfully
- Avoid inappropriate poses near Buddha images
- Show tea pickers with dignity, not as exotic subjects
- Handle civil war-related imagery with extreme care (not included in prompts)
- Honor local customs in all depictions

### Recommended Settings
- **Midjourney v6:** Best for photorealistic landscapes and cultural scenes
- **DALL-E 3:** Best for conceptual imagery and text integration
- **Stable Diffusion:** Best for customization and specific style control

### File Naming Convention
```
EP114_[CATEGORY]_[NUMBER]_[FORMAT].jpg
Example: EP114_SIGIRIYA_001_16x9.jpg
```
