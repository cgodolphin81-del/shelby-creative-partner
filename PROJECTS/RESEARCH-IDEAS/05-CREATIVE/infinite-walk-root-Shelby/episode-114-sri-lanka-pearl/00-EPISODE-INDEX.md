- **Word Count:** ~2,600 words
- **Estimated Duration:** 20 minutes
- **Pace:** Measured, contemplative, warm
- **Chapters:** 8 main sections + opening/closing

### Chapter Breakdown
| Chapter | Title | Duration | Word Count |
|---------|-------|----------|------------|
| Opening | The Pearl Awakens | 0:45 | ~120 |
| 1 | The Lion Rock | 2:30 | ~350 |
| 2 | Kingdoms of Gold | 2:30 | ~350 |
| 3 | Tea and Mist | 2:30 | ~350 |

| 5 | Ocean's Edge | 2:30 | ~350 |
| 6 | Scars and Healing | 2:30 | ~350 |
| 7 | Elephants and Whales | 2:15 | ~300 |
| 8 | Spice and Salt | 2:00 | ~280 |
| Closing | The Pearl Remembers | 1:45 | ~250 |

### Sound Design Notes
- **Opening:** Gentle ocean waves, distant temple bell, morning birds
- **Sigiriya:** Wind through ancient stone, subtle drone
- **Tea country:** Light rain, rustling leaves, distant train whistle
- **Temple:** Chanting (soft), bells, incense atmosphere
- **Ocean:** Waves, seabirds, underwater ambience
- **Wildlife:** Jungle sounds, elephant calls, whale songs
- **Closing:** Gentle waves fading, single bell, silence

### Voice Direction
- **Tone:** Warm, respectful, contemplative, intimate
- **Pace:** Slow and measured, with reverence for sacred topics
- **Pauses:** Critical for emotional and cultural moments
- **Emphasis:** Handle civil war section with particular care
