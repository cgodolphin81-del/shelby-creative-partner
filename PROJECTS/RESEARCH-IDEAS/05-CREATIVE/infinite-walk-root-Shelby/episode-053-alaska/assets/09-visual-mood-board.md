## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Clean Sans-Serif
**Recommended:** Inter, Helvetica Neue, or SF Pro
- **Headlines:** Bold, 32-48px
- **Subheadings:** Semibold, 20-24px
- **Body:** Regular, 16-18px
- **Captions:** Regular, 14px

**Why:** Clean, modern, readable at all sizes, works well with nature imagery

### Secondary Font: Serif (for quotes)
**Recommended:** Georgia, Playfair Display, or Merriweather
- **Pull Quotes:** Italic, 24-32px
- **Poetic Text:** Regular, 18-20px

**Why:** Adds warmth and gravitas to contemplative content

### Font Pairing Examples

**Headline:**
```
Font: Inter Bold
Size: 42px
Color: Deep Ice #1A5F7A
Letter spacing: -0.5px
```

**Body Text:**
```
Font: Inter Regular
Size: 18px
Color: Midnight Blue #0F172A (light bg)
Line height: 1.6
Max width: 65 characters
```

**Pull Quote:**
```
Font: Playfair Display Italic
Size: 28px
Color: Glacier Blue #4A9FB5
Left border: 3px solid Aurora Green
Padding: 16px left
```

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Natural, authentic, not over-saturated
- Emphasis on scale and vastness
- Human elements small in frame (shows scale)
- Golden hour and blue hour preferred
- Minimal post-processing

**Key Subjects:**

1. **Glaciers**
   - Close-up: Ice texture, blue color, crevasses
   - Wide: Full glacier face, scale with person/kayak
   - Detail: Meltwater, ice caves, bubbles
   - Mood: Ancient, powerful, ephemeral

2. **Aurora**
   - Landscape: Aurora over mountains/lakes
   - Portrait: Person silhouetted beneath lights
   - Detail: Aurora texture, color variations
   - Mood: Magical, holy, overwhelming

3. **Wildlife**
   - Bears: In natural habitat, respectful distance
   - Moose: Wetland settings, willow thickets
   - Birds: Eagles, puffins in flight or perched
   - Mood: Wild, untamed, free

4. **Landscapes**
   - Denali: Full mountain (clear day rare, special)
   - Tundra: Vast, empty, autumn colors
   - Coast: Turnagain Arm, tidewater glaciers
   - Mood: Vast, indifferent, beautiful

5. **Human Elements**
   - Dog sledding: Action shots, team running
   - Cabins: Cozy, warm light, snowy exterior
   - Hikers: Small figures in vast landscape
   - Mood: Adventure, respect, smallness

### Image Treatment

**Filters/Adjustments:**
- Slightly cool temperature (emphasize ice)
- Moderate contrast (not HDR)
- Preserve shadow detail
- Natural saturation (not oversaturated)
- Subtle vignette for focus

**Do Not:**
- Oversaturate (especially aurora)
- Heavy HDR processing
- Artificial sharpening
- Obvious filters
- Watermarks on primary images

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

**Minimum Ratios (WCAG AA):**
- Normal text: 4.5:1
- Large text: 3:1
- UI elements: 3:1

**Tested Combinations:**
- ✅ Deep Ice on Ice White (7.2:1)
- ✅ Ice White on Midnight Blue (15.8:1)
- ✅ Aurora Green on Midnight Blue (5.1:1)
- ⚠️ Glacier Blue on Ice White (2.8:1) - Use for large text only

### Alt Text Guidelines

**For Images:**
- Describe the image content
- Include context (why it matters)
- Keep concise (125 characters ideal)
- Don't start with "Image of"

**Examples:**
- ✅ "Aurora borealis dances over Wonder Lake with Denali reflected in still water"
- ✅ "Grizzly bear stands in river catching salmon, water splashing around legs"
- ✅ "Sled dog team runs across snowy tundra, musher on sled behind"

### Font Sizes

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Body text: 16px
- Captions: 14px
- Buttons: 16px
- Headlines: 24px+

**Scalability:**
- Use relative units (rem, em)
- Test at 200% zoom
- Ensure layout doesn't break
