## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Treatment

- **Treatment:** Desaturated, slight sepia or cool tone

- **Feeling:** Archival, documentary, respectful

- **Treatment:** Natural colors, slight warmth

## LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

### Composition

#### Rule of Thirds
- Place key elements on third lines/intersections
- Especially for landscape photography
- Creates dynamic, engaging compositions

#### Negative Space
- Don't fear empty space
- Let images breathe
- Especially important for heavy topics (Holocaust, Wall)

#### Layering
- Overlay text on images with gradient masks
- Use textures at low opacity for depth
- Create visual hierarchy through layering

### Spacing & Margins
- **Generous margins:** 10% minimum on all sides
- **Line spacing:** 1.5-1.6 for body text
- **Paragraph spacing:** 1.5x line height
- **Feeling:** Breathable, not cramped

### Visual Hierarchy
1. **Primary:** Episode title, main image
2. **Secondary:** Section headers, key quotes
3. **Tertiary:** Body text, captions, details

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### Instagram Feed
- **Size:** 1080x1080px (square) or 1080x1350px (portrait)
- **Grid:** Plan 9-grid layout for cohesive profile
- **Text:** Minimal text on images (use captions instead)
- **Colors:** High contrast for small-screen visibility

### Instagram Stories
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (9:16)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text within center 80% (avoid edges)
- **Interactive:** Use polls, questions, link stickers
- **Branding:** Subtle logo/handle in corner

### TikTok/Reels
- **Size:** 1080x1920px (9:16 vertical)
- **Text:** Large, bold, high contrast
- **Captions:** Burned-in, easy to read without sound
- **Hook:** First 3 seconds critical

### YouTube Thumbnail
- **Size:** 1280x720px (16:9)
- **Text:** 3-5 words max, large and bold
- **Face:** If using Shelby, expressive, well-lit
- **Contrast:** High contrast for small preview

### YouTube Video
- **Size:** 1920x1080px (16:9)
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text within 90% center area
- **Chapters:** Visual chapter markers with consistent style
- **End Screen:** Consistent branding, subscribe CTA

### Podcast Cover Art
- **Size:** 3000x3000px (1:1 square)
- **Text:** Episode number + title, readable at small sizes
- **Branding:** Consistent with Infinite Walk series
- **Colors:** High contrast, bold

### Twitter/X
- **Post Images:** 1200x675px (16:9) or 1080x1080px (square)
- **Thread Headers:** Consistent branding for each tweet
- **Text on Image:** Minimal, Twitter compresses heavily
- **Alt Text:** Always add for accessibility

### LinkedIn
- **Post Images:** 1200x627px (1.91:1)
- **Article Header:** 1920x1080px
- **Style:** Professional, clean, less flashy than Instagram
- **Text:** Can be longer, more informational

### Pinterest
- **Pin Size:** 1000x1500px (2:3 vertical)
- **Text:** Clear, benefit-driven headlines
- **Branding:** Logo/URL at bottom
- **Colors:** Bright, eye-catching (perform better)

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum Ratio:** 4.5:1 for normal text (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text:** 3:1 minimum
- **Check:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker
- **Avoid:** Gold text on white (fails contrast)

### Alt Text
- **All Images:** Descriptive alt text
- **Decorative:** Mark as decorative if purely aesthetic
- **Charts/Graphs:** Describe data and trends
- **Example:** "Brandenburg Gate at sunrise with golden light on columns"

### Font Sizes
- **Minimum:** 14px for body text
- **Headings:** Hierarchical, clear progression
- **Mobile:** Test on small screens
- **Zoom:** Content should work at 200% zoom

### Motion & Animation
- **Reduce Motion:** Respect user preferences
- **Auto-play:** Avoid or provide pause
- **Flashing:** No content flashes more than 3 times/second
- **Subtle:** Prefer subtle transitions over dramatic

## DESIGN DON'TS

❌ Don't use more than 3 fonts in one design
❌ Don't oversaturate colors (keep it authentic to Berlin)
❌ Don't use Comic Sans, Papyrus, or overly casual fonts
❌ Don't place text over busy areas of images
❌ Don't ignore mobile responsiveness
❌ Don't use low-resolution images (pixelation)
❌ Don't make text too small to read
❌ Don't use pure black (#000000) - use Ash Black (#1A1A1A)
❌ Don't create designs that fail accessibility standards
❌ Don't use Nazi imagery or symbols (illegal in Germany, offensive)
