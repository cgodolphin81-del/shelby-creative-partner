## VISUAL GUIDELINES FOR TEASE

### Keep It Mysterious
- Use silhouettes, not clear images
- Shadow and light, not full reveal
- Abstract representations
- Close-up details, not wide shots

### Maintain Brand Consistency
- Same fonts as Episode 108
- Consistent color palette (can introduce new destination colors subtly)
- Infinite Walk logo visible
- Episode numbering clear

### Create Visual Connection
- If Episode 108 used phoenix imagery, show transformation to new symbol
- Use similar composition/style for recognition
- Bridge the visual gap between episodes

## THINGS TO AVOID

❌ Don't reveal too much (kill the mystery)
❌ Don't tease too early (audience forgets)
❌ Don't over-promise (deliver on the hype)
❌ Don't ignore comments/engagement on tease
❌ Don't use low-quality visuals (maintain standards)
❌ Don't tease if Episode 109 isn't ready (delays kill momentum)
❌ Don't make it all about you (focus on the journey, the destination)
