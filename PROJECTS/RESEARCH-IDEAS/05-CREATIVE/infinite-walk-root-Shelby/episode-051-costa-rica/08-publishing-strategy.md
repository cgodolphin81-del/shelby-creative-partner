## ⚠️ Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Inaccurate travel information (prices changed, location closed)
**Response:** Update content promptly, acknowledge in Stories/email, thank listeners for corrections

**Issue:** Cultural sensitivity concerns
**Response:** Listen, learn, apologize if warranted, commit to doing better, consult local voices

**Issue:** Negative reviews/criticism
**Response:** Respond thoughtfully (not defensively), thank for feedback, use for improvement

**Issue:** Technical issues (episode not publishing, broken links)
**Response:** Fix immediately, communicate transparently, offer alternative access
