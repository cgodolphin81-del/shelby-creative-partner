## 📐 Design Specifications

### Episode Artwork

**Dimensions:**
- Square: 3000x3000px (minimum 1400x1400px)
- Format: PNG or JPEG
- Color mode: RGB

**Composition:**
- Central focal point (toucan, volcano, or sloth)
- Text: "The Emerald Isle" (prominent)
- Subtitle: "Episode 051" (smaller)
- Show branding: "The Infinite Walk" (consistent placement)

**Style:**
- High contrast for thumbnail visibility
- Readable at small sizes (mobile)
- Consistent with previous episode artwork
- Emerald green dominant

**Text Overlay:**
- Font: Clean, modern sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Proxima Nova)
- Color: White or light cream with subtle drop shadow
- Placement: Top or bottom third (avoid center)

---

### Social Media Templates

**Instagram Post (Square)**
- Size: 1080x1080px
- Consistent border or frame style
- Episode branding in corner
- White space for caption readability

**Instagram Post (Portrait)**
- Size: 1080x1350px (4:5 ratio)
- Better for wildlife vertical shots
- Same branding consistency

**Instagram Stories**
- Size: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- Safe zone: Keep text within center 1080x1420px
- Interactive elements (polls, questions) in accessible areas

**TikTok / Reels**
- Size: 1080x1920px (9:16 ratio)
- Text overlays: Large, bold, readable without sound
- Branding: Subtle watermark in corner

**Twitter/X**
- Size: 1200x675px (16:9 ratio)
- Also works: 1080x1080px (square)
- Text on image: Minimal (Twitter compresses)

**LinkedIn**
- Size: 1200x627px (1.91:1 ratio)
- Professional, clean design
- Text overlay acceptable (longer captions)

**Pinterest**
- Size: 1000x1500px (2:3 ratio optimal)
- Vertical performs best
- Text overlay: Clear, keyword-rich

**YouTube Thumbnail**
- Size: 1280x720px (16:9 ratio)
- High contrast, readable at small sizes
- Minimal text (3-5 words max)
- Emotionally compelling image

## 🎨 Typography

### Primary Fonts

**Headlines:**
- Montserrat Bold
- Proxima Nova Bold
- Futura Bold
- Gotham Bold

**Body Text:**
- Montserrat Regular
- Proxima Nova Regular
- Open Sans
- Lato

**Accent/Display:**
- Playfair Display (for elegant quotes)
- Pacifico (for "Pura Vida" text)
- Handwritten style (for personal touches)

### Font Usage Guidelines

**Hierarchy:**
- H1 (Episode Title): 48-72px, Bold
- H2 (Section Headers): 32-40px, Bold
- H3 (Subheaders): 24-28px, Semi-Bold
- Body: 16-18px, Regular
- Caption: 14px, Regular or Light

**Color:**
- Primary text: #1A1A1A (near black)
- Secondary text: #4A4A4A (dark gray)
- Accent text: #1B4D3E (emerald green) or #FF6B35 (toucan orange)
- Background text: #FFFFFF (white) on dark backgrounds

**Spacing:**
- Line height: 1.5x font size
- Letter spacing: Normal to slightly increased for headlines
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5x line height

## ♿ Accessibility Guidelines

### Visual Accessibility

**Color Contrast:**
- Text on background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- Large text: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

**Alt Text:**
- All images: Descriptive alt text
- Include species names when relevant
- Describe action/emotion, not just subject
- Example: "Keel-billed toucan perched on mossy branch, rainbow-colored beak prominent against emerald rainforest background"

**Font Size:**
- Minimum 16px for body text
- Scalable (allow user zoom)
- Avoid text embedded in images when possible

**Color Blindness:**
- Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
- Use patterns, labels, or icons as well
- Test with color blindness simulators
