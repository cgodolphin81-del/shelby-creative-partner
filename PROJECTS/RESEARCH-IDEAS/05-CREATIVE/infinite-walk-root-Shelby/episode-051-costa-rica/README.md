## 🎵 Audio Production Notes

### Music Suggestions

**Opening:**
- Gentle acoustic guitar
- Tropical ambiance (subtle)
- Distant bird calls (natural, not overwhelming)

**Chapter Transitions:**
- Subtle musical shifts
- Maintain consistent mood
- Avoid jarring changes

**Closing:**
- Warm, hopeful resolution
- Swell to conclusion
- Gentle fade

### Sound Effects

**Use Sparingly:**
- Howler monkey call (Chapter 5)
- Volcanic steam vent (Chapter 3)
- Rainforest ambiance (background, low volume)
- Ocean waves (Chapter 7, closing)

**Guidelines:**
- Natural, authentic recordings preferred
- Never overpower narration
- Fade in/out smoothly
- Match emotional tone of section

### Narration Pacing

**Target:** 20 minutes total

| Section | Duration | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| Introduction | 1:30 | Warm, inviting |
| Chapter 1: Arrival | 2:30 | Build wonder |
| Chapter 2: Canopy | 3:00 | Slow, contemplative |
| Chapter 3: Volcanic | 3:00 | Awe, reverence |
| Chapter 4: Slow Ones | 3:00 | Gentle, wise |
| Chapter 5: Loud Ones | 2:30 | Energetic, primal |
| Chapter 6: Pura Vida | 2:30 | Warm, philosophical |
| Conclusion | 2:00 | Peaceful, resolved |
| Episode 052 Tease | 0:30 | Mysterious, building |

**Pause Guidelines:**
- Honor all written pauses
- 2-3 seconds between chapters
- 5 seconds at major transitions
- Let sound effects breathe
