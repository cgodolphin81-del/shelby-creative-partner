## COLOR USAGE GUIDELINES

### DO:
- Use Ice Blues as primary brand colors for this episode
- Pair Dark Granite with Glacial White for maximum readability
- Use Pale Cyan and Cloud White for backgrounds
- Add Turquoise sparingly for water-related content
- Use Sunrise Pink only for special/emotional moments

### DON'T:
- Use saturated colors (no bright reds, greens, yellows)
- Mix too many colors in one composition (max 3-4)
- Use warm colors as primary (this is a cool episode)
- Create harsh contrasts except for specific emphasis
- Use pure black (#000000) - use Dark Granite instead

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Titles & Headlines
**Font:** Montserrat (or similar geometric sans-serif)
**Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600)
**Usage:** Episode titles, social media text, headers

**Example:**
```
ICE CATHEDRAL
Montserrat Semi-Bold, All Caps, Tracking +50
Color: #1B4D7F (Deep Glacier Blue) or #FFFFFF (on dark)
```

### Secondary Font: Body Text
**Font:** Source Sans Pro (or similar clean sans-serif)
**Weights:** Regular (400), Light (300)
**Usage:** Descriptions, captions, show notes, long-form text

**Example:**
```
Episode 014: Where ice remembers everything
Source Sans Pro Light, Sentence case
Color: #2C2C2C (Dark Granite) or #E8F4F8 (on dark)
```

### Accent Font: Special Elements
**Font:** Playfair Display (or similar elegant serif)
**Weights:** Regular (400), Italic (400)
**Usage:** Quotes, poetic text, emotional moments

**Example:**
```
"Permanence is fragile."
Playfair Display Italic
Color: #4A7BA7 (Mid-Tone Ice)
```

### Font Sizes (Digital)
- **Main Title:** 48-72px (social), 36-48px (web)
- **Subtitle:** 24-32px
- **Body:** 16-18px
- **Caption:** 14px
- **Minimum:** 12px (never go smaller)

### Font Spacing
- **Line Height:** 1.5-1.6 for body, 1.2-1.3 for titles
- **Letter Spacing:** +25 to +100 for all-caps titles
- **Paragraph Spacing:** 1.5x line height

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Mood:**
- Cinematic and epic
- High contrast (but not harsh)
- Cool color temperature
- Dramatic lighting (golden hour, blue hour)
- Sense of scale (tiny human, vast landscape)

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds (but break it for impact)
- Leading lines (glaciers, paths, ridges)
- Negative space (sky, ice, water)
- Depth layers (foreground, mid, background)
- Human element for scale (when applicable)

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) - warm accents
- Blue hour (dawn/dusk) - cool tones dominate
- Overcast - soft, even, moody
- Storm light - dramatic, high contrast
- Avoid: Harsh midday sun (flat, uninteresting)

**Post-Processing:**
- Increase contrast moderately
- Cool color temperature (shift toward blue)
- Enhance blues and cyans selectively
- Reduce saturation on warm colors
- Add subtle vignette for focus
- Maintain natural look (no heavy filters)

---

### AI Image Generation Style Guide

**For All Prompts, Add:**
```
Photorealistic, cinematic lighting, National Geographic photography style, 
high resolution 8K, dramatic contrast, color graded, cool color temperature
```

**Avoid:**
- Cartoon or illustrated styles
- Overly saturated colors
- Warm color palettes
- Busy, cluttered compositions
- Artificial-looking elements

**Preferred Aspect Ratios:**
- **16:9** - YouTube, blog headers, presentations
- **4:5** - Instagram feed posts
- **9:16** - Stories, Reels, TikTok, Shorts
- **1:1** - Podcast artwork, some social posts

## VIDEO MOTION GRAPHICS

### For TikTok/Reels/Shorts

**Text Animation:**
- Fade in (0.5s)
- Slight scale up (100% → 105%)
- Hold (2-3s)
- Fade out (0.5s)

**Transitions:**
- Fade to white (ice theme)
- Slide (horizontal, like wind)
- Zoom (into ice/landscape detail)

**Effects:**
- Subtle particle overlay (snow/ice crystals)
- Light leaks (cool tones only)
- Film grain (subtle, for texture)

**Duration:**
- Hook: 0-3 seconds (critical)
- Main content: 3-15 seconds
- CTA: 15-20 seconds (if applicable)

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC ADAPTATIONS

### Instagram
- **Aesthetic:** Polished, cohesive grid
- **Colors:** Stay within palette strictly
- **Filters:** None or minimal (maintain consistency)
- **Stories:** More casual, behind-the-scenes ok

### TikTok
- **Aesthetic:** Raw, authentic, immediate
- **Colors:** Can be more saturated for impact
- **Text:** Large, bold, quick to read
- **Style:** Less polished, more real

### YouTube
- **Aesthetic:** Professional, high production
- **Colors:** Consistent with brand
- **Thumbnails:** Maximum contrast, large text
- **End Screens:** Include palette and branding

### Podcast Platforms
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, legible at small sizes
- **Colors:** High contrast essential
- **Text:** Minimal (often not visible anyway)
- **Focus:** Strong central image

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum Ratio:** 4.5:1 for normal text (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text:** 3:1 minimum
- **Test:** Use contrast checker tools
- **Avoid:** Light text on light backgrounds

### Alt Text for Images
- Describe the image content
- Include colors when relevant
- Mention text overlay if present
- Example: "Perito Moreno Glacier face, deep blue ice with white striations, turquoise water in foreground. Text overlay: ICE CATHEDRAL in white bold letters."

### Readable Text
- Minimum 12px for body text
- Avoid thin fonts at small sizes
- Ensure adequate spacing
- Don't rely on color alone for meaning
