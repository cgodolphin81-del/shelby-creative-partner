## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- `--ar 16:9` - Standard widescreen, ideal for YouTube, presentations
- `--ar 3:2` - Classic photography ratio, good for prints
- `--ar 4:3` - Traditional photo ratio, social media friendly
- `--ar 21:9` - Ultra-widescreen cinematic, epic landscapes
- `--ar 1:1` - Square format, Instagram posts

### Style Parameters
- `--v 6` - Midjourney version 6 for best quality
- `--style raw` - More photographic, less stylized
- `--s 250` - Stylize parameter for artistic interpretation

### Tips for Best Results
1. Run prompts in batches by category for consistency
2. Adjust lighting descriptors based on desired mood
3. Use `--seed` parameter for consistent character appearance (Shelby)
4. For architectural shots, prioritize detail keywords
5. For emotional shots, emphasize atmosphere and mood
6. Consider creating variations of favorite prompts with different times of day

### Recommended Generation Order
1. Start with Category 1 (City Palace) - establish main location
2. Category 2 (Lake Pichola) - establish water element
3. Category 3-5 (Other locations) - expand visual world
4. Category 6 (Shelby) - add human element
5. Category 7-8 (Culture & Cinematic) - add depth and variety
