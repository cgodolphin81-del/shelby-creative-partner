## Engagement Tactics

### Review Campaign
**Goal:** Get 50+ reviews in first week

**Tactics:**
1. Ask in episode (end of show)
2. Email subscribers with direct links
3. Social media posts: "Leave a review"
4. Offer incentive (Q&A, bonus content)
5. Share reviews on social (social proof)

**Scripts:**
- *"If this episode resonated with you, please leave a review on Apple Podcasts. It helps more people find the show."*
- *"Reviews are the lifeblood of independent podcasts. If you have 30 seconds, I'd be so grateful."*

### Share Campaign
**Goal:** Maximize organic shares

**Tactics:**
1. Create shareable quote graphics
2. Make it easy to share (click-to-tweet)
3. Ask specific questions ("Tag someone who...")
4. Share listener stories
5. Create FOMO ("Everyone's talking about this episode")

### Community Engagement
**Goal:** Build lasting community

**Tactics:**
1. Respond to EVERY comment (first 48 hours)
2. Ask questions in captions
3. Create polls and surveys
4. Feature listener content
5. Host live sessions
6. Create Facebook Group discussion

## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Technical problems (episode not live)
**Response:** 
- Communicate immediately on social
- Provide alternative listen link
- Apologize, fix, re-launch

**Issue:** Negative feedback/criticism
**Response:**
- Acknowledge respectfully
- Don't argue publicly
- Take constructive feedback privately
- Learn and improve

**Issue:** Low engagement
**Response:**
- Don't panic (normal for some episodes)
- Double down on best-performing platform
- Adjust messaging
- Engage more personally

**Issue:** Copyright claims (music, images)
**Response:**
- Remove/replace immediately
- Use royalty-free going forward
- Credit all sources
