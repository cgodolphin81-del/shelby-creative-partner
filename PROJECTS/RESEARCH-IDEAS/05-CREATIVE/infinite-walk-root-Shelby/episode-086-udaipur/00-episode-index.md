## Key Statistics

### Udaipur Facts

- **Founded:** 1559 by Maharana Udai Singh II
- **City Palace Construction:** 400 years (1559-1900s)
- **Rulers Who Contributed:** 42
- **Generations:** 22
- **Lake Pichola Created:** 1387 (639 years old)
- **Jag Mandir Built:** 1551-1652 (101 years)
- **Jagdish Temple Completed:** 1651 (375 years of worship)
- **Jagdish Temple Height:** 79 feet
- **Monsoon Palace Built:** 1884
- **Prince Khurram's Refuge:** 1623-1624 (Shah Jahan)
- **James Bond Film:** Octopussy (1983) - filmed at Jag Mandir & Monsoon Palace
