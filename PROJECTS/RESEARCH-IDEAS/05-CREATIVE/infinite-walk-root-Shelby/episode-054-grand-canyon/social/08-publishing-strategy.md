## COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

### Response Guidelines
- **First 24 hours**: Respond to ALL comments/messages
- **T+1 to T+7**: Respond to all comments within 12 hours
- **T+7 onward**: Respond within 24-48 hours

### Tone
- Warm, genuine, grateful
- Acknowledge all feedback (positive and constructive)
- Never argue with negative feedback (thank them, move on)
- Amplify listener stories and reactions

### User-Generated Content
- Encourage: "Share your canyon photos with #InfiniteWalk"
- Repost: Listener photos, reactions, reviews (with permission)
- Feature: "Listener of the Week" in Stories or newsletter

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Technical Issues
- Audio quality problems: Acknowledge, re-upload if necessary, apologize
- Website down: Use Linktree or direct podcast platform links
- Social media outage: Cross-post on alternative platforms

### If Negative Feedback
- Listen: Acknowledge the feedback
- Thank: "Thanks for sharing this perspective"
- Learn: Note for future episodes
- Don't: Argue, delete (unless abusive), or ignore

### If Cultural Sensitivity Issues
- Listen to Native voices first
- Acknowledge mistakes quickly and sincerely
- Commit to doing better
- Donate to relevant Native organizations (if appropriate)
- Update content with corrections
