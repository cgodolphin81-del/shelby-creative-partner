## Contingency Plans

### If Launch Day Issues Occur

**Problem:** Podcast platforms delay approval
**Solution:** 
- Push back social media by 2-4 hours
- Email subscribers with "coming soon" message
- Post teaser content instead of launch content

**Problem:** Technical issues with audio
**Solution:**
- Have backup audio file ready
- Test on multiple devices before launch
- Have edited "clean" version available

**Problem:** Low initial engagement
**Solution:**
- Increase paid promotion budget
- Reach out to travel influencers for shares
- Create additional engaging content (polls, Q&A)
