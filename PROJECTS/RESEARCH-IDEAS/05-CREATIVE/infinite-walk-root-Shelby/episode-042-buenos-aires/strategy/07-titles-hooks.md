### Episode Description (Short)
```
A city built by exiles. A dance of melancholy. In Episode 042, we walk the colorful streets of La Boca, dance until dawn in the milongas, and discover what it means to belong somewhere you've never been home. 20 minutes. Buenos Aires, Argentina.
```

### Episode Description (Long)
```
You arrive in Buenos Aires carrying someone else's longing.

The plane descends through clouds that have crossed oceans, and below you sprawls a city that refuses to be one thing. It is European and South American. Elegant and worn. Alive with a restlessness that hums through the pavement and into your bones.

They call this the Paris of South America. But Paris never learned to tango. Paris never knew what it meant to build a neighborhood from the leftover paint of ships.

In this 20-minute audio documentary:

→ The colorful streets of La Boca, painted by necessity
→ Tango: melancholy made physical, born in brothels and bars
→ Recoleta Cemetery: 6,400 mausoleums, a city of memory
→ The parrilla ritual: fire, patience, and the best steak of your life
→ Late nights in the milongas, where strangers dance until 4 AM
→ What it means to belong to a city built by exiles

This is not a travel guide. This is a love letter to a city that teaches you that passion is not a liability. That melancholy is not a disease to be cured but a condition to be inhabited. That you can be elegant and broken at the same time.

📍 Buenos Aires, Argentina
⏱️ 20 minutes
🎵 Music: Traditional tango, bandoneon

The Infinite Walk is an audio documentary series about cities, culture, and the human experience. New episodes every week.
```
