## 📊 COLOR USAGE GUIDE

### Percentage Breakdown

| Color | Usage % | Primary Application |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| Ancient Stone | 35% | Backgrounds, base imagery |
| Vatican Gold | 20% | Accents, highlights, CTAs |
| Tiber Greens | 15% | Natural elements, balance |
| Sunset Oranges | 20% | Warmth, emotion, key visuals |
| Night Blues | 10% | Text, contrast, night scenes |

### Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Use Ancient Stone as primary background
- Layer Vatican Gold over Ancient Stone for warmth
- Use Tiber Greens to balance warm palette
- Apply Sunset Oranges for emotional moments
- Use Night Blues for text and readability

**DON'T:**
- Use pure black (#000000) - too harsh
- Use pure white (#FFFFFF) - too stark
- Overuse Vatican Gold (becomes garish)
- Mix too many accent colors in one composition
- Ignore the 60-30-10 rule (60% base, 30% secondary, 10% accent)

## 📐 TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Typeface: Headlines
**Font:** Playfair Display
**Weights:** Regular, SemiBold, Bold
**Usage:** Episode titles, major headings, quote graphics

**Why:**
- Elegant serif with historical resonance
- Evokes classical Roman inscriptions
- Highly readable at large sizes
- Pairs beautifully with sans-serif body

**Example:**
```
THE ETERNAL CITY RETURN
Rome, Italy | Episode 070
```

---

### Secondary Typeface: Body Text
**Font:** Montserrat
**Weights:** Light, Regular, Medium
**Usage:** Show notes, social captions, descriptions

**Why:**
- Clean, modern sans-serif
- Excellent readability at small sizes
- Complements Playfair Display
- Versatile across platforms

**Example:**
```
There are places you visit once. And there are 
places that visit you. Rome is the second kind.
```

---

### Accent Typeface: Numbers/Episode
**Font:** Futura Bold or Gotham Bold
**Weights:** Bold
**Usage:** Episode numbers, dates, statistics

**Why:**
- Geometric, modern contrast to serif
- Strong visual presence
- Clear at any size
- Milestone emphasis

**Example:**
```
EPISODE 070
70 EPISODES | 70 CITIES
```

## 🖼️ IMAGE STYLING GUIDE

### Photo Treatment

**For All Episode Imagery:**

1. **Warm Color Grade:**
   - Slight increase in temperature (+5-10)
   - Reduce highlights (-10-15)
   - Increase shadows (+10-20)
   - Warm tone in midtones

2. **Contrast:**
   - Moderate contrast (+10-15)
   - Preserve detail in shadows and highlights
   - Avoid crushed blacks

3. **Vignette:**
   - Subtle vignette (-5 to -10)
   - Draws eye to center
   - Cinematic feel

4. **Grain:**
   - Optional subtle film grain (5-10%)
   - Adds texture and warmth
   - Evokes timeless quality

### AI Image Generation Style Notes

**For All Prompts:**
- Include "photorealistic" for documentary feel
- Add "golden hour" or "blue hour" for specific lighting
- Use "cinematic" for dramatic compositions
- Specify lens type (35mm, 50mm, 85mm) for consistency
- Include emotional descriptors (contemplative, peaceful, awe)

**Example Prompt Structure:**
```
[Subject], [location/context], [lighting], [mood/emotion], 
photorealistic, [lens type], [composition notes]
```

## 🎯 ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- All text must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Test with contrast checkers before publishing
- Provide alt text for all images

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe the image content and context
- Include location and mood
- Example: "Colosseum at golden hour with warm orange light illuminating ancient travertine stone arches, Rome, Italy"

### Text Overlays
- Keep text minimal on images
- Provide full text in captions/descriptions
- Use high contrast for readability
