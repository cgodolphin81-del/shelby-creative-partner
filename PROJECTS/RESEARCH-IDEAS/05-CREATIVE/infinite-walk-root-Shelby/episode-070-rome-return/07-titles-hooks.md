## 🪝 HOOK LINES (Opening Lines)

### Primary Hook (Recommended)
**"There are places you visit once. And there are places that visit you. Rome is the second kind."**

*Why it works:*
- Immediately establishes the theme of return
- Poetic without being pretentious
- Creates curiosity (what does "places that visit you" mean?)
- Sets contemplative tone
- Memorable and quotable

**For Mystery/Curiosity:**
