## 📝 Usage Notes

**Recommended AI Tools:**
- Midjourney v6+ for artistic interpretations
- DALL-E 3 for accurate landmark representation
- Stable Diffusion XL for custom styling
- Adobe Firefly for commercial-safe images

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Instagram Posts: 1:1 or 4:5
- Instagram Stories/Reels: 9:16
- YouTube Thumbnails: 16:9
- Blog Headers: 16:9 or 3:2
- Pinterest: 2:3

**Style Modifiers to Add:**
- `--ar 16:9` (aspect ratio for Midjourney)
- `--v 6.0` (version for Midjourney)
- `--style raw` (photorealistic for Midjourney)
- "photorealistic, 8K, professional photography" (for DALL-E)

**Best Practices:**
- Generate multiple variations per prompt
- Use negative prompts to avoid crowds when needed
- Consider time of day for consistent lighting
- Maintain color palette consistency across episode
