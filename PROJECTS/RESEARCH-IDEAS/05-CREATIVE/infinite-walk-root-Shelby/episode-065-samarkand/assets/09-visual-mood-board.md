## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headlines)
**Recommendation:** Playfair Display or similar serif
**Why:** Elegant, historical, readable
**Weights:** Bold (700) for headlines, Regular (400) for subheads
**Color:** Lapis Lazuli or Cream (depending on background)

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Recommendation:** Lato, Open Sans, or similar sans-serif
**Why:** Clean, modern, highly readable
**Weights:** Regular (400) for body, Medium (500) for emphasis
**Color:** Weathered Stone or Celestial Indigo

### Accent Font (Special Use)
**Recommendation:** A display font with Middle Eastern/Central Asian influence
**Examples:** 
- Noto Naskh Arabic (for authenticity)
- Cinzel (for classical feel)
- Cormorant (for elegance)
**Usage:** Quotes, special graphics, episode titles

### Arabic/Persian Script (Optional, for authenticity)
**Font:** Noto Nastaliq Urdu or similar
**Usage:** 
- Decorative elements only
- With proper translation/context
- Respectful, accurate use
**Note:** Consult with native speakers for accuracy

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Guidelines

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) preferred
- Warm, directional light
- Avoid harsh midday sun
- Embrace shadows for drama

**Composition:**
- Symmetrical (Islamic architecture)
- Leading lines (pathways, arches)
- Human scale (include people for perspective)
- Detail shots (tilework, textures)

**Color Grading:**
- Warm tones emphasized
- Blues rich but natural
- Slightly desaturated shadows
- Film-like quality (subtle grain)

**Subjects:**
- Architecture (Registan, Gur-e-Amir, Shah-i-Zinda)
- People (candid, respectful, with permission)
- Details (tiles, calligraphy, textures)
- Landscapes (desert, city, oasis)

---

### AI Image Generation Style Guide

**For Midjourney/DALL-E/Stable Diffusion:**

**Prompt Structure:**
```
[Subject], [Style], [Lighting], [Color Palette], [Composition], [Technical]
```

**Example:**
```
Registan Square at sunrise, architectural photography, golden hour lighting, 
lapis blue and turquoise tiles, symmetrical composition, 8K, photorealistic
```

**Style Keywords:**
- Photorealistic, cinematic, documentary
- Travel photography, architectural photography
- Warm tones, rich colors
- High detail, 8K, professional

**Avoid:**
- Over-saturated, unnatural colors
- Modern anachronisms (cars, modern signs in historical shots)
- Disrespectful depictions of religious sites
- Stereotypical or exoticized portrayals

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

**WCAG AA Compliance:**
- Normal text: 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio
- Large text: 3:1 minimum contrast ratio
- UI elements: 3:1 minimum contrast ratio

**Tested Combinations:**
✅ Lapis text on Cream background (passes)
✅ Cream text on Lapis background (passes)
✅ Weathered Stone on Cream (passes)
❌ Turquoise on Desert Sand (fails - avoid)

**Tools:**
- WebAIM Contrast Checker
- Color Oracle (color blindness simulator)
- Stark (design plugin)

### Alt Text

**For All Images:**
- Describe subject, action, context
- Include relevant text in image
- Keep concise but informative
- Example: "Registan Square at sunrise, three madrasas with turquoise tile facades glowing in golden light"
