## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Technical problems (episode not publishing)
**Response:**
1. Immediate: Post on all platforms "Experiencing technical difficulties, episode will be live within [TIMEFRAME]"
2. Fix issue with platform support
3. Once live: "We're live! Thank you for your patience" + extend promotion by 24 hours

**Issue:** Negative feedback/criticism
**Response:**
1. Assess: Is it constructive or trolling?
2. Constructive: Thank them, acknowledge, consider feedback
3. Trolling: Don't engage, delete if abusive
4. Never argue publicly

**Issue:** Cultural sensitivity concerns
**Response:**
1. Listen carefully to concerns
2. Research and verify
3. If valid: Public acknowledgment, apology, commit to learning
4. If misunderstanding: Gentle education with sources
5. Always center affected voices

**Issue:** Low initial engagement
**Response:**
1. Don't panic (algorithm takes 24-48 hours)
2. Increase engagement efforts (reply to everything)
3. Consider paid boost ($20-50 to test)
4. Reach out to collaborators for shares
5. Focus on evergreen promotion (this episode has long tail)
