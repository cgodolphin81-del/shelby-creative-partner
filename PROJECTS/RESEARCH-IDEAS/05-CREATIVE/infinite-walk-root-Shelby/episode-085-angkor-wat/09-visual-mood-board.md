### Color Usage Guidelines

#### Do's
✅ Use Temple Grey as primary text color (not pure black)
✅ Pair Sunrise Orange with Deep Shadow for contrast
✅ Use Jungle Green for nature/Ta Prohm content
✅ Use Sky Blue for backgrounds and peaceful imagery
✅ Layer Sandstone Beige as warm neutral base
✅ Use Golden Hour sparingly for special moments

#### Don'ts
❌ Don't use pure black (#000000) - too harsh
❌ Don't use all primary colors in one design
❌ Don't use Lotus Pink for text (low contrast)
❌ Don't overuse Golden Hour (loses impact)
❌ Don't mix warm and cool tones without intention

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Display/Headlines

#### **Cinzel** (Google Fonts)
**Style:** Elegant, classical, inscription-like
**Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
**Usage:** Episode titles, major headlines, quote cards

**Why:** Evokes ancient inscriptions, classical architecture, timeless elegance

**Example:**
```
THE GOLDEN CITY
Cinzel Bold, 48pt, Temple Grey #6B6B6B
```

**Alternatives:**
- Trajan Pro (paid, similar classical feel)
- Cormorant Garamond (free, more elegant)
- Playfair Display (free, more modern)

---

### Secondary Font: Body Text

#### **Lora** (Google Fonts)
**Style:** Contemporary serif with roots in calligraphy
**Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)
**Usage:** Body text, show notes, articles, descriptions

**Why:** Highly readable, warm, literary feel, excellent for long-form

**Example:**
```
Before the sun remembers its own name...
Lora Regular, 16pt, Deep Shadow #2C2C2C
Line height: 1.6
```

**Alternatives:**
- Merriweather (free, similar readability)
- Georgia (system font, reliable)
- EB Garamond (free, more classical)

---

### Tertiary Font: UI/Accents

#### **Montserrat** (Google Fonts)
**Style:** Modern, geometric sans-serif
**Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)
**Usage:** Navigation, buttons, captions, social media text, timestamps

**Why:** Clean, modern contrast to serif fonts, highly legible at small sizes

**Example:**
```
EPISODE 085
Montserrat Semi-Bold, 12pt, Sunrise Orange #FF8C42
Letter spacing: 2px
```

**Alternatives:**
- Source Sans Pro (free, similar)
- Open Sans (free, more neutral)
- Proxima Nova (paid, premium feel)

## VISUAL STYLE

### Photography Direction

#### Mood & Atmosphere
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, soft diffused light, dramatic shadows
- **Composition:** Symmetry (Angkor Wat), framing (Ta Prohm roots), faces (Bayon)
- **Color Treatment:** Warm tones, slightly desaturated, film-like quality
- **Emotion:** Awe, reverence, mystery, tranquility

#### Key Shots
1. **Sunrise Reflection:** Angkor Wat in still water, orange/purple sky
2. **Bayon Faces:** Close-ups, eye-level, soft light
3. **Tree Roots:** Ta Prohm, jungle embracing stone
4. **Silhouettes:** Traveler against temple, sunrise/sunset
5. **Details:** Apsara carvings, textures, weathered stone
6. **Scale:** Wide shots showing temple magnitude
7. **Life:** Monks, locals, daily worship (respectful)

#### Editing Style
- **Exposure:** Slightly underexposed for mood
- **Contrast:** Medium-high for drama
- **Highlights:** Recovered (don't blow out skies)
- **Shadows:** Lifted slightly (show detail)
- **Temperature:** Warm (5500-6500K)
- **Tint:** Slightly toward magenta
- **Vibrance:** +10 (not saturation)
- **Clarity:** +15 (enhance stone texture)
- **Vignette:** Subtle (-10)

---

- **Style:** Minimalist line art, watercolor washes

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Text on Background:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text:** Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Test:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Font Sizes
- **Minimum Body:** 16px (web), 14px (mobile)
- **Minimum UI:** 12px
- **Scalable:** Use relative units (rem, em)

### Alt Text
- All images: Descriptive alt text
- Decorative images: Empty alt=""
- Complex images: Long description available

### Video
- **Captions:** Required for all video content
- **Audio Descriptions:** For key visual moments
- **Transcripts:** Available for all episodes

## BRAND CONSISTENCY

### Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo:** Full lockup (text + icon)
- **Secondary Logo:** Icon only (social profiles)
- **Minimum Size:** 24px height
- **Clear Space:** 1x logo height on all sides
- **Background:** Light or dark (not busy)

### Voice & Tone (Visual)
- **Reverent:** Not irreverent or flippant
- **Contemplative:** Not rushed or chaotic
- **Warm:** Not cold or corporate
- **Authentic:** Not stock or generic
- **Timeless:** Not trendy or dated

### Do's and Don'ts

#### Do ✅
- Use the color palette consistently
- Maintain typography hierarchy
- Leave white space (breathing room)
- Use high-quality images only
- Credit photographers/sources
- Test on mobile devices
- Check accessibility

#### Don't ❌
- Use off-palette colors
- Mix too many fonts
- Crowd designs with elements
- Use low-resolution images
- Stretch or distort logo
- Ignore mobile experience
- Forget accessibility
