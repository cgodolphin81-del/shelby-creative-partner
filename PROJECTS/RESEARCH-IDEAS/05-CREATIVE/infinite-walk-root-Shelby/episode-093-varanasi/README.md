## 🚨 Cultural Sensitivity Reminders

### Critical Guidelines
1. **No photography at cremation ghats** - Emphasize this in all travel content
2. **Respect temple boundaries** - Non-Hindus cannot enter Kashi Vishwanath sanctum
3. **Witness, don't spectate** - Frame as pilgrimage, not tourism
4. **Ask before photographing people** - Especially sadhus (expect to pay)
5. **Dress modestly** - Shoulders and knees covered
6. **No leather in temples** - Belts, bags, wallets must be removed

### In This Episode Package
- Travel guide leads with cultural sensitivity section
- Social media includes PSA on respectful travel
- AI prompts avoid explicit cremation imagery
- Script emphasizes witness role, not expert
- All content frames Varanasi as sacred, not touristy
