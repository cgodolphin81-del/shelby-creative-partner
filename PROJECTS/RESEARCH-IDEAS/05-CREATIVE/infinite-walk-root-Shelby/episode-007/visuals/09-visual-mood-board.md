## VISUAL THEMES

### 1. Vastness & Scale
**Concept**: Tiny human, enormous sky
**Visuals**:
- Wide shots with small human silhouette
- Low horizon line (1/3 ground, 2/3 sky)
- Aurora filling entire frame
- Sense of being overwhelmed by nature

**Example Composition**:
```
[SKY - 2/3 of frame: Aurora dancing]
-------------------------------------
[GROUND - 1/3: Tiny silhouette, snow]
```

**Emotion**: Humility, awe, insignificance (in a good way)

---

### 2. Intimacy & Wonder
**Concept**: Personal, emotional moment
**Visuals**:
- Close-up of face with aurora reflection in eyes
- Tears, breath crystallizing
- Hands reaching up
- Profile silhouette against lights

**Example Composition**:
```
[Close-up: Face illuminated by green glow]
[Soft focus aurora in background]
```

**Emotion**: Vulnerability, transformation, deep feeling

---

### 3. Movement & Dance
**Concept**: Aurora as living entity
**Visuals**:
- Long exposure showing motion
- Ribbons flowing across frame
- Time-lapse sequences
- Multiple layers of light

**Visual Techniques**:
- 15-30 second exposures
- Star trails + aurora composite
- Burst sequences showing progression

**Emotion**: Energy, life, dynamism

---

### 4. Silence & Stillness
**Concept**: The quiet of the Arctic night
**Visuals**:
- Calm, frozen lake reflections
- Still trees, no wind
- Minimalist compositions
- Negative space

**Example Composition**:
```
[Large areas of dark sky]
[Single aurora ribbon]
[Perfect reflection below]
```

**Emotion**: Peace, meditation, sacred space

---

### 5. Science Meets Magic
**Concept**: Both/and, not either/or
**Visuals**:
- Split compositions (data + beauty)
- Infographic overlays on photos
- Before/after: expectation vs. reality
- Equipment + aurora (camera on tripod)

**Emotion**: Understanding, integration, wisdom

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headlines)
**Montserrat** or **Playfair Display**
- Clean, modern, readable
- Bold weights for impact
- All caps for short headlines

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Lato** or **Source Sans Pro**
- Clean, friendly, highly readable
- Regular and light weights
- Good for long-form text

### Accent Font (Quotes, Special)
**Cormorant Garamond** or **Cinzel**
- Elegant, slightly mystical
- Use sparingly for impact
- Perfect for pull quotes

### Font Pairing Examples
```
HEADLINE: Montserrat Bold, 48px, All Caps
Body: Lato Regular, 18px, Sentence case

HEADLINE: Playfair Display Bold, 52px
Body: Source Sans Pro, 17px

QUOTE: Cormorant Garamond Italic, 28px
Attribution: Montserrat Regular, 14px
```

### Color Usage for Text
- **White text**: On dark aurora/sky backgrounds
- **Black text**: On snow/light backgrounds
- **Green accent**: Match aurora green (#00FF7F)
- **Purple accent**: Match aurora purple (#9B59B6)

## IMAGE STYLES

### Photography Style
- **Long exposure**: 10-30 seconds for smooth aurora
- **High ISO**: 1600-3200 for light sensitivity
- **Wide angle**: 14-24mm for vastness
- **RAW format**: Maximum editing flexibility
- **Natural colors**: Minimal saturation boosting

### Color Grading
- **Shadows**: Cool blue tones
- **Highlights**: Preserve aurora greens naturally
- **Contrast**: Moderate to high
- **Vibrance**: Slight boost to aurora colors
- **Grain**: Minimal, keep it clean

### Composition Rules
- **Rule of thirds**: Aurora on upper third line
- **Leading lines**: Paths, shorelines pointing to aurora
- **Framing**: Trees, caves, windows framing sky
- **Symmetry**: Reflections in water/ice
- **Negative space**: Let the sky breathe

## VIDEO STYLE

### Color Grade
- **Teal & Orange**: Cool shadows, warm highlights
- **Aurora Enhancement**: Slight saturation boost on greens/purples
- **Skin Tones**: Natural, warm (contrast with cool environment)
- **Blacks**: Crushed slightly for drama
- **Overall**: Cinematic, moody, but not oversaturated

### Camera Movement
- **Slow pans**: Across landscape
- **Static shots**: Let aurora move, not camera
- **Handheld**: Minimal, only for intimate moments
- **Drone**: Sweeping landscape reveals (if available)
- **Time-lapse**: Essential for aurora movement

### Editing Pace
- **Slow**: Match the meditative quality
- **Longer holds**: Let viewers absorb the beauty
- **Transitions**: Smooth fades, not quick cuts
- **Music**: Ambient, building to emotional peaks
- **Silence**: Use it. The aurora is silent.

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDES

### Instagram Feed
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:5 (vertical) or 1:1 (square)
- **Grid Planning**: Alternate between dark/light
- **Consistency**: Similar color grade across posts
- **Text Overlay**: Minimal, readable on mobile

### Instagram Stories
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 vertical
- **Text**: Large, high contrast
- **Interactive**: Polls, questions, sliders
- **Link**: Always use link sticker

### Instagram Reels / TikTok
- **Aspect Ratio**: 9:16 vertical
- **Safe Zones**: Keep text away from edges
- **Captions**: Always include on-screen text
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds must grab attention

### YouTube
- **Thumbnail**: 1280x720 minimum, 16:9
- **Text**: Large, high contrast, 3-5 words max
- **Face**: Include human element when possible
- **Branding**: Consistent style across episodes

### Pinterest
- **Aspect Ratio**: 2:3 vertical (1000x1500px ideal)
- **Text Overlay**: Clear, benefit-driven
- **Branding**: Logo/website on all pins
- **Colors**: Bright, eye-catching (aurora performs well)

### Blog/Website
- **Featured Image**: 1200x630px (social sharing)
- **In-Post Images**: Max width 1200px
- **Lazy Loading**: For performance
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive for accessibility

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- Ensure text passes WCAG AA standards
- Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text
- 3:1 for large text (18px+ or 14px bold)

### Alt Text
- Describe images for screen readers
- Include aurora colors, location, emotion
- Example: "Green and purple aurora borealis dancing over snow-covered mountains in Tromsø, Norway"

### Captions
- Always include for video content
- Accurate timing
- Describe important sounds (wind, etc.)
