## USAGE NOTES

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** - YouTube, blog headers, presentations
- **3:4** - Instagram portrait, mobile wallpapers
- **4:5** - Instagram feed, detailed shots
- **1:1** - Instagram square, podcast covers
- **9:16** - Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels
- **3:1** - Twitter/Facebook headers

### Style Parameters
- `--style raw` - More photorealistic, less artistic interpretation
- `--q 2` - Higher quality rendering
- Adjust based on your AI image generator

### Color Grading Notes
- Keep warm tones for wood/temple shots
- Use soft, diffused lighting for zen/bamboo
- Golden hour for torii gates and pavilions
- Muted, contemplative palette for rock gardens

### Consistency Tips
- Use similar lighting across episode images
- Maintain color palette (wood browns, sakura pinks, temple reds, moss greens)
- Ensure Shelby's appearance is consistent across all prompts featuring the character
- Balance iconic landmarks with intimate details
