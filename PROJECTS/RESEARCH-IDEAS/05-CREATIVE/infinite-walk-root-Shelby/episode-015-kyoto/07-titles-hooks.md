# Titles & Hooks - Episode 015: Kyoto

**Options for episode titles, taglines, and marketing hooks**

---

## PRIMARY TITLE

### **The Space Between**

**Why this works:**
- Evocative and mysterious
- References multiple layers: space between gates, breaths, thoughts, people, ancient/modern
- Contrasts with Episode 014's concrete "Ice Cathedral"
- Invites curiosity
- Works across cultures
- Short, memorable, punchy

**Subtitle:** Kyoto, Japan

**Full Title:** The Space Between: Kyoto, Japan

---

## ALTERNATIVE TITLES

### Option 1: Wood Cathedral
**Pros:** Direct parallel to "Ice Cathedral," clear contrast
**Cons:** Less poetic, more literal
**Use if:** Want explicit Episode 014 connection

### Option 2: Silence in Crowds
**Pros:** Clear theme, intriguing paradox
**Cons:** Less mysterious, more explanatory
**Use if:** Want theme front-and-center

### Option 3: 2,000 Temples
**Pros:** Concrete, impressive number, intriguing
**Cons:** Doesn't capture theme, just fact
**Use if:** Want curiosity-driven title

### Option 4: The Practice
**Pros:** Short, meaningful, contrasts with "found" silence
**Cons:** Vague without context
**Use if:** Minimalist approach

### Option 5: Kyoto
**Pros:** Simple, clear, confident
**Cons:** No intrigue, just place name
**Use if:** Brand is strong enough to carry it

### Option 6: Gates of Inari
**Pros:** Specific, visual, intriguing
**Cons:** Too narrow (only one location)
**Use if:** Focusing episode on Fushimi Inari

### Option 7: Wabi-Sabi
**Pros:** Unique, philosophical, memorable
**Cons:** Unfamiliar to Western audiences
**Use if:** Want to educate/introduce concept

### Option 8: From Ice to Wood
**Pros:** Clear contrast with Ep 014, poetic
**Cons:** Long, explanatory
**Use if:** Explicitly marketing as contrast

### Option 9: The Wooden Temple
**Pros:** Concrete, contrasts with ice, warm
**Cons:** A bit plain
**Use if:** Want simplicity

### Option 10: Presence
**Pros:** Single word, powerful, theme
**Cons:** Abstract, overused in mindfulness space
**Use if:** Want minimalist philosophical angle
