### Post 2: Behind the Production
```
Creating Episode 015: The Space Between taught me as much as researching it.

Kyoto is a city of contrasts:
- 1.5M people, yet profound stillness
- 2,000 temples, yet modern city
- 1,200 years of history, yet completely alive

The challenge: how do you capture the space between in audio?

The answer: silence. Not absence of sound, but presence of attention.

This episode uses silence differently than Episode 014 (Patagonia). Where ice demanded awe, wood invites presence. Where wind spoke constantly, bells speak intentionally.

Production notes:
- 20-minute target (consistent with Ep 014)
- Minimal music (let silence work)
- Temple bells as transitions
- Intimate vocal delivery

The result: an episode about presence, designed to be experienced with presence.

Listen: [LINK]
```
