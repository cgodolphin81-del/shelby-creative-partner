## 🎵 Music & Sound Design Notes

### Episode 112 Theme Differences

| Element | Episode 111 (Delta) | Episode 112 (Bay) |
|---------|---------------------|-------------------|
| **Primary Instrument** | Đàn tranh (zither) | Đàn bầu (monochord) |
| **Ambience** | River, close water | Bay, open water, wind |
| **Tempo** | Flowing, moderate | Slower, more expansive |
| **Mood** | Intimate, contemplative | Mysterious, epic |
| **Cultural Elements** | Floating market sounds | Boat bells, distant gulls |
| **Color Palette** | Warm browns, golds | Cool greens, greys |

### Tease Music Cues

**For all tease versions:**
- Use Episode 112 theme preview (not Episode 111)
- 5-second music bed minimum
- End on unresolved note (creates anticipation)
- Fade out, don't cut abruptly
