## COLOR PALETTE

### Primary Colors
**Celebration Gold:** `#D4AF37`
- Use for: Milestone graphics, headlines, accents
- Evokes: Achievement, warmth, premium quality

**Deep Black:** `#0A0A0A`
- Use for: Backgrounds, text, grounding elements
- Evokes: Sophistication, depth, the unknown

**Pure White:** `#FFFFFF`
- Use for: Text on dark, highlights, breathing room
- Evokes: Clarity, new beginnings

### Secondary Colors
**Journey Purple:** `#4B0082` (Indigo)
- Use for: Gradients, overlays, depth
- Evokes: Wisdom, transformation, mystery

**Sunrise Orange:** `#FF6B35`
- Use for: Accents, CTAs, energy
- Evokes: Dawn, new beginnings, warmth

**Earth Brown:** `#8B4513`
- Use for: Natural elements, grounding
- Evokes: Ground, paths, stability

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Headline Fonts
**Primary:** Playfair Display (Serif)
- Use for: Episode titles, major headlines
- Weights: Regular (400), Bold (700)
- Evokes: Elegance, timelessness, literary quality

**Alternative:** Cormorant Garamond
- More classical, slightly more ornate
- Good for: Quote graphics, special moments

### Body Fonts
**Primary:** Inter (Sans-Serif)
- Use for: Body text, captions, UI
- Weights: Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (500)
- Evokes: Clarity, modernity, readability

**Alternative:** Source Sans Pro
- Slightly warmer, more human
- Good for: Longer-form content

### Accent Fonts
**Script:** Great Vibes or similar
- Use for: "100" numerals, celebration text
- Sparingly: Only for special moments
- Evokes: Handwritten, personal, celebratory

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Treatment
**Overall Aesthetic:** Cinematic, warm, slightly desaturated

**Specific Treatments:**

1. **Milestone Montage Images:**
   - Warm gold overlay (10-15% opacity)
   - Slight vignette
   - High contrast
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:5

2. **Before/After Transformation:**
   - Episode 001: Cooler tones, more contrast
   - Episode 100: Warmer tones, softer
   - Split-screen or side-by-side

3. **Quote Graphics:**
   - Dark background (black or deep purple)
   - Gold text for key phrases
   - White text for supporting text
   - Subtle texture overlay (paper, grain)

4. **Location Collages:**
   - Grid layout (10 iconic locations)
   - Consistent filter across all
   - Gold border or accent
   - Episode numbers overlaid

### AI Image Generation Style Guide
**For Midjourney/DALL-E:**

```
Style keywords to include:
- "cinematic lighting"
- "golden hour" or "warm tones"
- "celebration atmosphere"
- "milestone moment"
- "journey montage"
- "epic scale"
- "emotional resonance"
- "National Geographic style"
- "award-winning photography"

Style keywords to avoid:
- "cartoon"
- "illustration" (unless specifically wanted)
- "oversaturated"
- "HDR"
- "neon" (except for Danakil-specific images)
```

**Example Prompt for Milestone Graphic:**
```
Epic milestone celebration image for 100th episode of travel journey. Golden confetti falling over world map. Silhouette of walker on path stretching to horizon. Warm golden hour lighting. Cinematic, inspirational, National Geographic style. Text space at top. 16:9 aspect ratio.
```

## ACCESSIBILITY NOTES

### Color Contrast:
- Ensure all text meets WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text)
- Gold on white may not have sufficient contrast — use gold on dark backgrounds
- Provide alt text for all images

### Alt Text Examples:
```
"Gold '100' numeral on black background with journey montage below"
"Before and after comparison: Episode 001 desert landscape vs Episode 100 mountain sunrise"
"Quote graphic: 'Beauty does not require comfort' in gold text on dark gradient background"
```

### Video Accessibility:
- Include captions on all video content
- Provide transcripts for audio narration
- Avoid rapid flashing or strobing effects
