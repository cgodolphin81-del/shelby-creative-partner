## 📊 KEY FACTS (For Show Notes & Promotion)

**Quick Reference:**
- **Founded:** 753 BCE (traditional date)
- **Roman Empire Peak:** 117 CE (under Trajan)
- **Colosseum Built:** 70-80 CE
- **Pantheon Built:** 113-125 CE (Hadrian)
- **Vatican City:** Independent since 1929 (Lateran Treaty)
- **Population:** ~2.8 million (city), ~4.3 million (metro)
- **Colosseum Capacity:** 50,000-80,000 spectators
- **Pantheon Dome:** Largest unreinforced concrete dome (still, 2,000 years later)
- **Best Time to Visit:** April-May, September-October
- **Skip-the-Line:** Essential for Colosseum, Vatican Museums

## 🏛️ CULTURAL & HISTORICAL SENSITIVITY

When creating and publishing content about Rome:

1. **Respect Sacred Spaces:** Vatican City is an active religious site—dress codes and behavior matter
2. **Ancient Sites:** These are archaeological treasures, not just photo ops—treat with reverence
3. **Living City:** Rome is not a museum; 2.8 million people live here—show modern life too
4. **Historical Accuracy:** Verify facts about Roman Empire, dates, emperors
5. **Overtourism:** Acknowledge the impact of tourism and promote responsible travel
6. **Layers of History:** Show Rome as accumulated history, not just "ancient ruins"
