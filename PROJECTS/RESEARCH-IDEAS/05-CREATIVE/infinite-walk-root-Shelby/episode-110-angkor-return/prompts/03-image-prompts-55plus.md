## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** - YouTube thumbnails, desktop wallpapers, blog headers
- **4:5** - Instagram posts, Pinterest, vertical content
- **3:2** - Traditional photography, prints
- **1:1** - Instagram squares, profile images, details
- **21:9** - Cinematic banners, ultra-wide displays

### Style Recommendations
- Use `--style raw` for Midjourney photorealistic results
- Adjust `--ar` based on platform needs
- For DALL-E 3: Remove parameters, describe aspect ratio in prompt
- For Stable Diffusion: Add negative prompts for quality control

### Color Palette Keywords
- Temple greys: weathered stone, ancient granite, moss-covered
- Jungle greens: emerald canopy, lush vegetation, tropical foliage
- Sunrise oranges: golden hour, amber light, dawn glow
- Bayon blues: twilight sky, indigo hour, deep azure
- Earth tones: terracotta, ochre, burnt sienna

### Emotional Keywords
- Contemplative, mysterious, peaceful, awe-inspiring, spiritual
- Ancient, timeless, weathered, enduring, resilient
- Ethereal, dreamlike, atmospheric, moody, cinematic
