## Image Styles

### Photography Style

**Mirror Effect Shots:**
- Symmetrical compositions
- Person or object for scale
- Low angle (camera at water level)
- Wide-angle lens (16-24mm equivalent)
- Polarizing filter to reduce glare
- Time: Sunrise or sunset for best colors
- Mood: Surreal, infinite, dreamlike

**Hexagon Pattern Shots:**
- Close-up macro for texture
- Low angle for endless field effect
- Side lighting to emphasize shadows
- Time: Morning or late afternoon
- Mood: Geometric, ordered, meditative

**Incahuasi Island:**
- Wide shots showing isolation
- Telephoto for cactus detail
- Include sky for scale
- Time: Golden hour for warm light
- Mood: Ancient, resilient, majestic

**Flamingo Shots:**
- Eye-level with birds
- Shallow depth of field for portraits
- Wide for flocks
- Time: Early morning (most active)
- Mood: Delicate, vibrant, alive

**Night Sky:**
- Long exposure (15-30 seconds)
- Wide aperture (f/2.8 or lower)
- High ISO (1600-3200)
- Tripod essential
- Include foreground interest (cacti, person, salt patterns)
- Mood: Cosmic, infinite, humbling

## Composition Guidelines

### Rule of Thirds
- Place horizon on upper or lower third (not center, unless mirror symmetry)
- Position subjects at intersection points
- Create balance between sky and salt

### Leading Lines
- Use salt cracks to lead eye
- Horizon line as divider
- Cactus rows as natural guides
- Path or tracks for journey narrative

### Symmetry
- Critical for mirror shots
- Perfect horizontal symmetry
- Center composition acceptable (encouraged) for reflections

### Scale
- Always include human element for scale when possible
- Show vastness through tiny figures
- Contrast: small human vs. infinite landscape

### Negative Space
- Embrace emptiness
- Salt flat as negative space
- Sky as negative space
- Let images breathe

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast
- All text must meet WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 ratio minimum)
- White text on dark backgrounds or vice versa
- Avoid red/green combinations (color blindness)

### Alt Text
- All images need descriptive alt text
- Include: Subject, action, context, emotion
- Example: "Person standing on Salar de Uyuni salt flat during wet season, perfect mirror reflection of blue sky and white clouds visible in shallow water, creating surreal effect of walking on sky"

### Captions
- All video content needs captions
- YouTube auto-captions should be reviewed/corrected
- Social media videos: Burn in captions or use platform tools
