### Caption 6 — Practical Tip
**Image:** Someone climbing a dune at sunrise

PSA: If you're visiting Sossusvlei, WAKE UP EARLY.

Park gates open at sunrise (5:30-6:30am depending on season). Be there when they open. Better yet, stay inside the park and enter before public opening.

Why?
✓ Best light (shadows define the dunes)
✓ Coolest temperatures (critical in summer)
✓ Fewest crowds (Dune 45 gets PACKED)
✓ Animals more active
✓ Entire day ahead for other activities

By 11am, the light is harsh and the heat is brutal. The desert's mercy is brief.

Worth every second of lost sleep.

🏜️ Save this for your Namibia trip!
