## ⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

1. **Don't** post everything on Day 1 (space it out)
2. **Don't** ignore comments in first 24 hours (algorithm boost)
3. **Don't** use same caption everywhere (platform-optimize)
4. **Don't** forget to actually LISTEN to your own episode before publishing
5. **Don't** skip the email list (your most engaged audience)
6. **Don't** expect viral success (play the long game)
7. **Don't** forget to track what works (analytics matter)
