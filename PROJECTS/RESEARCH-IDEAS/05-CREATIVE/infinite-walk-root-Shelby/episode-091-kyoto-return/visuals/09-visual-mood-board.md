## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Noto Serif JP** or **Playfair Display**
- Elegant, traditional feel
- Works well with Japanese aesthetic
- Free (Google Fonts)

```
Usage:
- Episode titles
- Section headers
- Quote graphics
- Size: 36-72px for social, 24-36px for web
```

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Noto Sans JP** or **Lato**
- Clean, readable
- Modern but not stark
- Free (Google Fonts)

```
Usage:
- Body text
- Captions
- Social media descriptions
- Size: 14-18px for web, 16-24px for social
```

### Accent Font (Special Use)
**Japanese Brush Style** (for occasional Japanese characters)
- Examples: Kanji Brush, Shojumaru
- Use sparingly for Japanese words (桜，禅，etc.)

```
Usage:
- Japanese terms (mono no aware, shokunin, etc.)
- Decorative elements only
- Not for body text
```

## Visual Themes & Motifs

### 1. Torii Gate Frames
**Concept:** Use torii gate shapes as frames for images or text

**Applications:**
- Instagram post borders
- Video transitions
- Section dividers in graphics
- Website headers

**Execution:**
- Simple vermilion rectangle with opening
- Can be stylized or realistic
- Works as subtle watermark or bold frame

---

### 2. Cherry Blossom Overlays
**Concept:** Soft petal overlays for dreamy, ephemeral feel

**Applications:**
- Background textures
- Transition effects in video
- Photo overlays
- Email headers

**Execution:**
- PNG transparent petal images
- Opacity: 20-40%
- Scatter pattern, not uniform
- Colors: Sakura pink, pale pink, white

---

### 3. Bamboo Vertical Lines
**Concept:** Vertical bamboo stalk patterns as design elements

**Applications:**
- Background patterns
- Sidebars in graphics
- Story highlight covers
- YouTube end screens

**Execution:**
- Simple vertical lines in bamboo green
- Varying thickness (like natural bamboo)
- Spaced evenly or organically
- Can be subtle texture or bold statement

---

### 4. Temple Roof Silhouettes
**Concept:** Iconic curved Japanese roof lines

**Applications:**
- Logo variations
- Section dividers
- Watermark
- Merchandise designs

**Execution:**
- Simple black or dark grey silhouette
- Recognizable even at small sizes
- Can pair with full moon or sun

---

### 5. Enso Circle (Zen Circle)
**Concept:** Imperfect hand-drawn circle representing enlightenment

**Applications:**
- Episode number backgrounds
- Quote graphic frames
- Logo element
- Meditation/breathing exercise visuals

**Execution:**
- Brush stroke style, not perfect circle
- Black or dark grey
- Can be open (incomplete) or closed
- Represents: Imperfection, completeness, the void

---

### 6. Water Reflections
**Concept:** Mirror-like reflections (Kinkaku-ji pond, canal)

**Applications:**
- Split-image compositions
- Transition effects
- Metaphor for reflection/introspection
- Social media carousels

**Execution:**
- Flip image vertically
- Add slight blur to reflection
- Reduce opacity of reflection (70-80%)
- Add subtle ripple effect if animated

## Image Treatment Guidelines

### For AI-Generated Images

**Consistency Settings:**
- Use same model version throughout (e.g., Midjourney v6)
- Maintain consistent aspect ratios per platform
- Keep color grading similar across all images

**Color Grading:**
- Slightly desaturated (not oversaturated)
- Warm tones for temple/golden hour shots
- Cool tones for bamboo/misty shots
- Maintain natural colors (avoid heavy filters)

**Style Keywords to Include:**
- "cinematic lighting"
- "travel photography"
- "natural colors"
- "atmospheric"
- "serene"
- "meditative"

---

### For Photography

**Editing Style:**
- Natural, not over-processed
- Slight warmth for temple shots
- Enhanced greens for bamboo
- Soft highlights for cherry blossoms
- Maintain shadow detail

**Presets/Filters:**
- Create custom preset for episode consistency
- Apply to all social media images
- Include in brand kit for team/freelancers

## Platform-Specific Visual Guidelines

### Instagram Feed (1080x1080px or 1080x1350px)
- **Primary colors:** Vermilion, bamboo green
- **Text:** Minimal, use images to tell story
- **Consistency:** Every 3rd post should be quote graphic for variety
- **Grid planning:** Alternate between temples, nature, quotes

### Instagram Stories (1080x1920px)
- **Templates:** Create 5-7 reusable templates
- **Colors:** Full palette, more playful
- **Interactive:** Use polls, questions, countdowns
- **Behind-the-scenes:** Show script, recording process

### YouTube Thumbnail (1280x720px)
- **Text:** 3-5 words max, large and bold
- **Colors:** High contrast (vermillion on grey, gold on black)
- **Image:** Single focal point (temple, face, gates)
- **Branding:** Consistent episode number placement

### Pinterest Pins (1000x1500px)
- **Text:** Descriptive titles, easy to read
- **Colors:** Warm, inviting (pinks, golds, greens)
- **Layout:** Vertical, text at top or bottom
- **Multiple pins:** Create 5-10 variations per topic

### LinkedIn Post Images (1200x627px)
- **Style:** Professional, clean
- **Colors:** Muted palette (greys, greens, deep reds)
- **Text:** Thoughtful quotes, statistics
- **Tone:** Educational, insightful

### Facebook Post Images (1200x630px)
- **Style:** Warm, community-focused
- **Colors:** Full palette
- **Text:** Questions, engaging hooks
- **Format:** Single image or carousel
