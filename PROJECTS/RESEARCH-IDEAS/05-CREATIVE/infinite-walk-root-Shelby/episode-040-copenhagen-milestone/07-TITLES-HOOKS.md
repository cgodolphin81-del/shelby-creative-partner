### Hook Option 4 — Stat Hook
"Forty-nine percent. That's the percentage of all trips to work or education in Copenhagen made by bicycle. Not ten percent. Not twenty. Forty-nine. Nearly half. This isn't an accident. It's a choice. A city choosing people over cars. Clean air over convenience. Community over speed. And it's just one reason Copenhagen is the perfect city for Episode 40 of The Infinite Walk."

### Hook Option 5 — Hygge Hook
"The Danes have a word: *hygge*. HOO-ga. You might hear it translated as 'coziness,' but that's like calling the ocean 'wet.' Hygge is not a thing—it's a way of being. It's the candlelit dinner where no one checks their phone. The wool socks and the warm bread. The knowledge that, for this moment, everything is enough. Denmark is consistently ranked among the world's happiest countries. Maybe it's not a coincidence. This is Episode 40. This is Copenhagen."

## 🎙️ PODCAST DESCRIPTION (Show Notes)

### Full Description

**EPISODE 40: THE NORTHERN CAPITAL — COPENHAGEN, DENMARK**

Forty episodes. Forty cities. One milestone moment.

For this special 40th episode of The Infinite Walk, we're in Copenhagen: a city that has mastered the art of living well.

In this 25-minute milestone episode:

🌈 **NYHAVN'S COLORFUL HARBOR** — Where Hans Christian Andersen lived and wrote fairy tales that would travel the world

🎡 **TIVOLI GARDENS** — The 1843 amusement park that inspired Walt Disney and still makes people happy 180 years later

🧜 **THE LITTLE MERMAID** — Copenhagen's iconic statue, a symbol of resilience through over a century of vandalism and restoration

🕯️ **HYGGE AS PHILOSOPHY** — Why the Danish concept of hygge is more than "coziness"—it's a rebellion against the cult of more

🚴 **CYCLING CULTURE** — How Copenhagen became the world's best cycling city (49% of trips to work are by bike!)

💭 **40 EPISODES OF WALKING** — Reflections on what I've learned from Marrakech to Kyoto, Miami to Edinburgh, Petra to Peru
