## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Recommended:** Playfair Display or similar serif
- **Why:** Elegant, historic, echoes Dutch Golden Age
- **Usage:** Episode titles, section headers, quote graphics
- **Weight:** Regular and Bold
- **Color:** Canal Deep Blue (#1B3A4B) or Brick (#8B3A2F)

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Recommended:** Lato, Open Sans, or similar sans-serif
- **Why:** Clean, modern, readable, contrasts with headings
- **Usage:** Body text, captions, social media
- **Weight:** Regular and Light
- **Color:** Dark Gray (#4A4A4A)

### Accent Font (Special Elements)
**Recommended:** Handwritten or script (sparingly)
- **Why:** Personal, human touch for quotes
- **Usage:** Anne Frank quotes, personal notes
- **Color:** Brick Red or Tulip Purple

### Font Pairing Examples
```
HEADLINE: Playfair Display Bold, 48px, #1B3A4B
Subhead: Playfair Display Regular, 24px, #8B3A2F
Body: Lato Regular, 16px, #4A4A4A
Quote: Lato Light Italic, 18px, #6B4C9A
```

## IMAGERY STYLES

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Cinematic and atmospheric
- Natural lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Mix of wide establishing shots and intimate details
- Authentic, not overly touristy
- Human presence without being staged

**Composition:**
- Leading lines (canals, bike paths, bridges)
- Reflections in water
- Framing through arches and windows
- Layered depth (foreground bikes, midground canal, background houses)
- Rule of thirds for landscapes

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset): Warm, romantic
- Blue hour (dawn/dusk): Moody, contemplative
- Overcast: Soft, even, true colors
- Night: Warm artificial lights against cool dark

**Processing:**
- Slightly desaturated blues
- Warm highlights, cool shadows
- Subtle film grain for texture
- Avoid over-saturation (keep authentic)
- Consistent color grade across all images

## LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

### Grid System
- **12-column grid** for web/digital
- **Flexible margins** for mobile responsiveness
- **Consistent spacing:** 8px base unit (8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64px)

### Hierarchy
1. **Episode Title:** Largest, most prominent
2. **Section Headers:** Clear visual breaks
3. **Quotes:** Distinct styling, indented
4. **Body Text:** Readable, comfortable line length
5. **Captions:** Smaller, lighter

### White Space
- **Generous margins:** Let content breathe
- **Paragraph spacing:** 1.5x line height
- **Image padding:** Space around photos
- **Don't fear empty space:** It's not wasted

### Image Placement
- **Full-width:** Hero images, establishing shots
- **Half-width:** Supporting images with text
- **Grid:** Multiple images, gallery style
- **Overlay:** Text on image (ensure readability with overlay)

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDES

### Instagram Feed
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 (1080x1350px) or 1:1 (1080x1080px)
- **Grid Planning:** Alternate between quotes, photos, carousels
- **Color Consistency:** Use preset/filter for uniform look
- **Text on Images:** Minimal, high contrast

### Instagram Stories
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (1080x1920px)
- **Safe Zones:** Keep text away from edges (250px top/bottom)
- **Interactive Elements:** Polls, sliders, questions
- **Branding:** Subtle logo or handle in corner

### TikTok / Reels
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 vertical
- **Safe Zones:** Avoid bottom 20% (UI overlays)
- **Text Timing:** On screen long enough to read twice
- **Color Grading:** Consistent with overall palette

### YouTube Thumbnail
- **Size:** 1280x720px (16:9)
- **Text:** Large, bold, minimal words (3-5 max)
- **Face:** Include Shelby if possible (connection)
- **Contrast:** High contrast for small preview

### Pinterest Pins
- **Size:** 1000x1500px (2:3 vertical)
- **Text Overlay:** Clear, readable title
- **Branding:** Website/handle at bottom
- **Colors:** Bright, eye-catching (tulip colors work well)

### LinkedIn
- **Image Size:** 1200x627px (1.91:1)
- **Professional Tone:** Cleaner, less playful
- **Text:** Can be longer, more informative
- **Colors:** Muted palette (blues, grays, brick)

### Email
- **Width:** 600-640px (standard email width)
- **Images:** Optimize for fast loading (<200KB)
- **Alt Text:** Describe all images
- **Mobile:** Test on mobile (50%+ opens)

## ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- **Text on Background:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text:** Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Test Tools:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- **Describe:** What's in the image, why it matters
- **Be Specific:** "Canal house with step gable" not "building"
- **Context:** How image relates to content
- **Length:** 125 characters or less ideally

### Font Sizes
- **Body Text:** Minimum 16px
- **Mobile:** Consider 18px for better readability
- **Scalable:** Allow user zoom without breaking layout

### Motion
- **Avoid:** Auto-playing video with sound
- **Provide:** Pause controls for any animation
- **Consider:** Reduced motion preference in CSS
