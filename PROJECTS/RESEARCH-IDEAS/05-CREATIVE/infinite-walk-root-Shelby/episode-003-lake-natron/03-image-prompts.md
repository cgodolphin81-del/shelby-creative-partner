## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- `--ar 16:9` — Standard landscape (web, presentations)
- `--ar 4:5` — Portrait (Instagram feed, mobile)
- `--ar 1:1` — Square (Instagram, social media)
- `--ar 3:2` — Classic photography ratio
- `--ar 21:9` — Ultra-wide (panoramas, headers)

### Style Parameters (Midjourney)
- `--v 6` — Use Midjourney version 6
- `--style raw` — More photographic, less artistic interpretation
- `--s 250` — Stylize parameter (adjust for more/less artistic)

### Quality Settings
- Request "8K resolution" or "ultra-detailed" for maximum quality
- Use "photorealistic" for documentary-style images
- Use "digital art" or "painterly" for artistic interpretations

### Ethical Considerations
- These prompts are for generating *representations* of Lake Natron
- Always credit as "AI-generated imagery inspired by Lake Natron, Tanzania"
- Do not present AI images as actual photographs
- Consider donating to flamingo conservation if using commercially
