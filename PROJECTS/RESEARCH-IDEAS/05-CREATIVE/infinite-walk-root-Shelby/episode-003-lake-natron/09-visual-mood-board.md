## 📐 COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds
- Place horizon on upper or lower third (not center)
- Flamingo flocks: Fill the frame or use negative space (no middle ground)
- Single subjects: Off-center, looking into the frame

### Leading Lines
- Shoreline leading to flamingos
- Salt cracks creating geometric patterns
- Road to the lake (journey narrative)
- Volcano slope leading eye upward

### Scale & Perspective
- Show human smallness against vast landscape
- Aerial shots for scale of flamingo flocks
- Macro for salt crystals, textures
- Eye-level for intimate flamingo portraits

### Negative Space
- Use salt flats as breathing room
- Don't overcrowd frames
- Let the red water dominate when appropriate
- Embrace minimalism for impact

## 🎬 VISUAL TREATMENTS

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- National Geographic meets fine art
- Documentary authenticity with artistic sensibility
- Natural light only (no flash on wildlife)
- High dynamic range (capture extremes)

**Editing Approach:**
- Enhance natural colors (don't oversaturate)
- Maintain authenticity (this place is unreal enough)
- Warm tones for emotional content
- Cool tones for scientific/educational content
- Consistent grading across all images

**Do:**
- Boost reds/oranges slightly (they're the story)
- Maintain shadow detail
- Keep skin tones natural (when Shelby is present)
- Use subtle vignettes for focus

**Don't:**
- Oversaturate to the point of artificiality
- Over-sharpen (looks amateur)
- Heavy HDR (looks dated)
- Filters that obscure the truth of the place

---

---

### Graphic Design Elements

**Typography:**

**Headlines:**
- Clean, modern sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat, Proxima Nova)
- Bold weight for impact
- White or black depending on background
- All caps for short titles

**Body Text:**
- Readable serif or sans-serif (e.g., Merriweather, Lato)
- Comfortable line height
- High contrast with background
- Maximum 65 characters per line

**Accent Text:**
- Italic for quotes, poetic moments
- Small caps for section headers
- Color accent (pink or red) for emphasis

**Layout Principles:**
- Generous white space
- Images breathe (don't crowd)
- Consistent margins
- Mobile-first design

## 📱 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC VISUAL GUIDES

### Instagram Feed

**Dimensions:**
- Square: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1)
- Portrait: 1080 x 1350 px (4:5) — **recommended** (takes more screen space)
- Landscape: 1080 x 566 px (1.91:1)

**Best Practices:**
- Portrait orientation for maximum impact
- Consistent filter/preset across feed
- Text overlay minimal (or none)
- First image in carousel is the hook

**Grid Aesthetic:**
- Alternate between red-dominant and pink-dominant
- Mix of wide shots and details
- Every 3rd post: human element or text-based

---

### Instagram Stories

**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)

**Best Practices:**
- Text in "safe zone" (not covered by UI)
- Use polls, questions, link stickers
- Behind-the-scenes performs well
- Subtitle any spoken content

**Templates:**
- Quote over landscape
- "Did you know?" fact cards
- Before/after (research → final)
- Countdown stickers for launches

---

### TikTok / Reels

**Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 px (9:16)

**Best Practices:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds (visually striking)
- Text overlay throughout (many watch muted)
- Vertical video only
- End with CTA (link in bio, follow, etc.)

**Visual Rhythm:**
- Cut every 2-4 seconds (maintain attention)
- Mix of wide, medium, close-up
- Movement within frames (panning, zooming)
- Match cuts to music beats

---

### Blog/Website

**Featured Image:** 1200 x 630 px (1.91:1) — also works for social sharing

**In-Article Images:**
- Width: 1200 px (or full-width for impact)
- Height: Variable (maintain aspect ratio)
- Format: WebP for performance, JPEG fallback
- Compression: 70-80% quality (balance quality/speed)

**Layout:**
- Image at top (hero)
- Images breaking up text every 300-500 words
- Captions on all images (accessibility + context)
- Alt text for SEO and accessibility

---

### Email Newsletter

**Header Image:** 600-800 px wide
**Inline Images:** 600 px wide (max)
**File Size:** Under 200 KB each (fast loading)

**Best Practices:**
- One hero image at top
- Use images to break up text
- Alt text essential (many block images)
- Test on mobile (where most will read)

---

### YouTube (If Applicable)

**Thumbnail:** 1280 x 720 px (16:9)
**Minimum:** 640 x 360 px

**Best Practices:**
- High contrast (visible at small sizes)

### Color Grading Reference

**Look:** Warm, slightly desaturated except for reds/pinks

**Lightroom/Photoshop Starting Points:**
- Temperature: +5 to +10 (warm)
- Tint: Slight magenta (+2 to +5)
- Vibrance: +10 (boosts muted colors, protects skin tones)
- Saturation: 0 or -5 (don't oversaturate)
- Reds: +10 saturation, +5 luminance
- Oranges: +5 saturation
- Shadows: Slight blue tint (split toning)
- Highlights: Slight warm tint (split toning)

**Presets:**
- Create custom preset based on best image
- Apply consistently across all photos
- Adjust per-image as needed
