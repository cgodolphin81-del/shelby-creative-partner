## 📝 GENERATION TIPS

3. **Create multiple formats** - 16:9 for video, 1:1 for social, 9:16 for stories
4. **Upscale favorites** - Use AI upscaling for print quality
5. **Organize by category** - Match folder structure for easy access

### Recommended Settings
- **Midjourney v6:** `--style raw --q 2` for photorealistic
- **DALL-E 3:** Use full prompt, add "photorealistic" or "illustration"
- **Stable Diffusion:** Adjust CFG 7-9, steps 30-50

### Color Consistency
- Use mist greys, mountain greens, river blues, sunset golds
- Avoid oversaturated colors
- Maintain atmospheric, contemplative mood
