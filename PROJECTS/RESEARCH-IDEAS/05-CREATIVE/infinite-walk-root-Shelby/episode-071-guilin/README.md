## 📍 EPISODE OVERVIEW

**Episode Number:** 071  
**Title:** The Dragon's Cradle  
**Location:** Guilin & Yangshuo, Guangxi, China  
**Duration:** ~20 minutes  
**Theme:** Presence, mythology, cultural heritage, the soul of China  
**Release Date:** March 24, 2026  

**Logline:** There is a place where the earth dreamed in stone. Journey to Guilin and Yangshuo—where karst mountains are sleeping dragons, rivers remember, and rice terraces are prayers carved over 650 years.

**Key Elements:**
- Karst mountain landscapes (200-300 million years old)
- Cormorant fishermen (1,300-year tradition, fewer than 100 remain)
- Longji Rice Terraces (650 years, built entirely by hand)
- Li River (featured on Chinese 20 yuan note)
- Chinese mythology (mountains as dragons)
- Ink painting tradition (1,000 years of artistic inspiration)
