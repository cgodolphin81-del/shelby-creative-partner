### Podcast/Audio (5 hooks)

**Hook 1 (Mystery/Discovery):**
"Three hundred and eighty million years ago, this was ocean floor. Sand settled into layers, patient as prayer. Then the earth remembered it could move. What was seabed became skyward. What was horizontal learned to be vertical. Welcome to Zhangjiajie. Welcome to the Jade Empire."

**Hook 2 (Avatar Connection):**
"James Cameron came here in 2008. He looked up at pillars that pierced clouds and asked: 'How does stone float?' He went home and built Pandora. But here's the truth: He wasn't creating fantasy. He was remembering reality."

**Hook 3 (Philosophical):**
"Why do we come? We come from cities of glass and steel. From lives measured in meetings and emails. From flat worlds where the tallest thing is a skyscraper we built ourselves. We come and we look up. And something changes."

**Hook 4 (Glass Bridge/Fear):**
"Four hundred and thirty meters long. Three hundred meters high. Ninety-nine panels of glass. Your feet walk on air. Some people crawl. Some close their eyes. Some laugh, terrified and delighted. This is not a bridge. This is a mirror."

**Hook 5 (Final Truth):**
"We are all visitors here. The macaques are visitors. The trees are visitors. The mist is a visitor. Even the stone is a visitor—briefly arranged, soon to be rearranged. Nothing owns Zhangjiajie. Not even the pillars. Zhangjiajie belongs to time. And time belongs to no one."

### What to Test

**Title Tests:**
- "The Jade Empire" vs. "Floating Mountains"

- Measure: Open rates, click-through rates, views

**Hook Tests:**
- Avatar-focused hook vs. Philosophy-focused hook

- Measure: Engagement rate, saves, shares

**Visual Tests:**
- Avatar Mountain image vs. Glass Bridge POV
- Test on: Instagram posts, Facebook ads
- Measure: Engagement, clicks to link

**CTA Tests:**
- "Listen now" vs. "Start your journey"
- Test on: Email buttons, social media links
- Measure: Click-through rate

**Platform-Specific Tests:**

**Instagram:**
- Test carousel (multiple images) vs. single image
- Test long caption vs. short caption
- Test hashtags in caption vs. first comment

**Twitter/X:**
- Test thread length (5 tweets vs. 7 tweets)
- Test with image vs. without
- Test posting time (morning vs. evening)

**Facebook:**
- Test video vs. image post
- Test long-form post vs. short with link
- Test posting day (weekday vs. weekend)

**LinkedIn:**
- Test professional angle vs. personal story
- Test article attachment vs. link only
- Test posting time (work hours vs. evening)

**Email:**
- Test subject lines (curiosity vs. direct)
- Test send time (morning vs. afternoon)
- Test plain text vs. HTML design

### How to Test

1. **Split audience:** Divide your audience into two groups
2. **Test one variable:** Change only one element at a time
3. **Run long enough:** At least 24-48 hours for meaningful data
4. **Measure:** Track relevant metrics (opens, clicks, engagement)
5. **Implement winner:** Use the winning version for broader audience
6. **Document:** Keep record of what worked for future reference

### Tools for Testing

- **Instagram:** Native Insights
- **Twitter:** Twitter Analytics
- **Facebook:** Facebook Insights
- **LinkedIn:** LinkedIn Analytics
- **Email:** Mailchimp, ConvertKit, or your ESP's A/B testing feature
- **YouTube:** YouTube Studio Analytics
