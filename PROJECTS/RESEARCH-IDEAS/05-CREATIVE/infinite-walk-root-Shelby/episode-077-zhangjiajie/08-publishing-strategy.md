## Crisis Management

### Scenario 1: Technical Issues (Episode Doesn't Launch)

**Action Plan:**
1. Contact podcast host immediately
2. Post on social media: "Experiencing technical difficulties. Episode 077 will be live soon. Thank you for patience!"
3. Email list: Same message
4. Once resolved: "We're live! Thank you for your patience. The mountain remembers."
5. Consider extending launch promotions by 1-2 days

---

### Scenario 2: Negative Feedback/Reviews

**Action Plan:**
1. Don't respond defensively
2. If constructive: "Thank you for the feedback. I appreciate you taking the time to share your thoughts."
3. If trollish: Don't engage. Delete if on your own platforms.
4. Focus on positive feedback
5. Remember: Not every episode resonates with every listener (and that's okay)

---

### Scenario 3: Low Engagement/Downloads

**Action Plan:**
1. Don't panic (launch velocity varies)
2. Review what worked/didn't work
3. Double down on best-performing platforms
4. Consider paid promotion ($50-100 test)
5. Reach out to partners for cross-promotion
6. Focus on evergreen content (this episode has long shelf life)
7. Remember: Episode 076 may have outperformed, but Episode 077 builds the foundation for 078

---

### Scenario 4: Controversy (Cultural Sensitivity, etc.)

**Action Plan:**
1. Listen first. Understand the concern.
2. If valid: Acknowledge, apologize, commit to doing better
3. If misunderstanding: Clarify respectfully, provide context
4. Don't be defensive
5. Consult with cultural experts if needed
6. Learn and apply to future episodes
