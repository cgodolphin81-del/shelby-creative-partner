1. "Have you ever traveled an ancient trade route? What was it like?"
2. "What's the highest altitude you've traveled to? How did you handle it?"
3. "Tea or coffee? How do you take it?"
4. "What's a forgotten piece of history you think more people should know about?"
5. "If you could walk any ancient path in the world, which would you choose?"
6. "What does 'Shangri-La' mean to you? Paradise? Escape? Something else?"
7. "Have you ever visited China? Where? What surprised you?"
8. "What's the most meaningful souvenir you've brought back from a trip?"
9. "Do you prefer famous tourist destinations or off-the-beaten-path places? Why?"
10. "What's a trade or craft that's disappeared in your lifetime that you wish hadn't?"
