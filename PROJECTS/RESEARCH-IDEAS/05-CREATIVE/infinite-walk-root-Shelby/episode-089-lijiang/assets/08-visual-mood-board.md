**Episode Title:**
- Font: Noto Serif SC Bold

- Font: Noto Serif SC Medium

- Font: Noto Sans SC Regular

**Captions/Quotes:**
- Font: Noto Sans SC Light Italic

**Chinese Characters:**
- Font: Ma Shan Zheng or Noto Serif SC

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Style

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Cinematic and atmospheric
- High dynamic range (capture shadows and highlights)
- Slightly desaturated (except prayer flag colors)
- Emphasis on scale and human smallness vs. nature's grandeur

**Composition:**
- Rule of thirds for landscapes
- Leading lines (paths, canals, mountain ridges)
- Foreground interest (prayer flags, tea cups, cobblestones)
- Human elements for scale (small figures in vast landscapes)
- Symmetry for architecture (temples, old town squares)

**Lighting:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) for warmth
- Blue hour (twilight) for mystery
- Harsh midday sun for altitude/drama
- Soft overcast for intimate cultural moments
- Avoid: Flat lighting, harsh flash

**Color Grading:**
- Cool shadows (blues, greys)
- Warm highlights (gold, amber)
- Slightly muted saturation overall
- Prayer flag colors: Punchy and vibrant
- Skin tones: Natural, warm

### AI Image Generation Guidelines

**Midjourney Parameters:**
```
--ar 16:9 (landscapes)
--ar 4:5 (portraits, social)
--ar 1:1 (details, squares)
--v 6 (or current version)
--style raw (for photorealistic)
--q 2 (higher quality)
```

**Prompt Structure:**
1. Subject (what)
2. Location/setting (where)
3. Lighting/time (when)
4. Mood/atmosphere (feeling)
5. Style/medium (how)
6. Technical specs (aspect ratio, quality)

**Example:**
```
Aerial view of Lijiang Ancient Town at dawn, UNESCO World Heritage site, traditional Naxi architecture with grey tiled roofs, intricate canal system reflecting pink and orange sunrise sky, cobblestone streets empty in early morning light, mist rising from rooftops, Jade Dragon Snow Mountain visible in distant background, cinematic lighting, photorealistic, 16:9 aspect ratio --ar 16:9 --v 6 --style raw
```

**Avoid in AI Images:**
- Overly saturated colors (except prayer flags)
- Modern elements (cars, power lines, signs)
- Inconsistent architecture
- Too many people (unless market scene)
- Generic "Asian" stereotypes
- Text within images (hard to read, doesn't translate)

## LAYOUT PRINCIPLES

### White Space
- Generous margins (let images breathe)
- Clear hierarchy (title → subtitle → body)
- Don't overcrowd (episodes about vastness and silence)
- Let key images dominate

### Grid System
- 12-column grid for web
- Consistent margins across platforms
- Align text and images to grid
- Break grid intentionally for impact (full-bleed images)

### Image/Text Ratio
- Social posts: 70% image, 30% text
- Blog posts: 40% image, 60% text
- Email: 50/50 balance
- Never let text overwhelm imagery

### Hierarchy
1. **Hero Image:** Dominant, full-width or large
2. **Episode Title:** Clear, prominent
3. **Key Quote/Teaser:** Italic, centered
4. **Body Content:** Readable, scannable
5. **CTA:** Clear, contrasting color

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- Text on background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- Large text: Minimum 3:1 ratio
- Test with: WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- All images: Descriptive alt text
- Be specific: "Prayer flags on mountain pass" not "flags"
- Include context: Why this image matters
- Don't start with "image of" (redundant)

### Font Size
- Minimum: 16px for body text
- Headings: Scalable (relative units)
- Email: 14px minimum

### Screen Readers
- Semantic HTML (headings, lists, etc.)
- Meaningful link text (not "click here")
- Logical reading order
- ARIA labels where needed

## DO'S AND DON'TS

### DO
✅ Use the color palette consistently
✅ Prioritize high-quality imagery
✅ Leave generous white space
✅ Include human elements for scale
✅ Respect cultural symbols and practices
✅ Test accessibility (contrast, alt text)
✅ Maintain brand consistency across platforms
✅ Let key images dominate layouts
✅ Use typography hierarchy clearly
✅ Optimize images for each platform

### DON'T
❌ Over-saturate colors (except prayer flags)
❌ Use low-resolution or pixelated images
❌ Crowd layouts with too much text
❌ Include modern anachronisms in historical imagery
❌ Appropriate sacred symbols without context
❌ Ignore accessibility guidelines
❌ Deviate wildly from brand guidelines
❌ Let text overwhelm imagery
❌ Use inconsistent fonts or sizes
❌ Upload unoptimized images (slow load times)
