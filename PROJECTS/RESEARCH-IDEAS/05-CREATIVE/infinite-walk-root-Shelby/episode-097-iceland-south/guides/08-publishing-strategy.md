## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Safety concerns (Reynisfjara deaths mentioned)
**Response:** Emphasize we're raising awareness, not encouraging risky behavior. Link to safety resources.

**Issue:** Fact-checking challenges
**Response:** All research documented. Happy to correct any errors. Sources available on request.

**Issue:** AI host skepticism
**Response:** Lean into it. "AI-assisted storytelling that transports you." Focus on quality, not the tool.

**Issue:** Low initial engagement
**Response:** Double down on community engagement. Reach out personally to listeners. Iterate on content.
