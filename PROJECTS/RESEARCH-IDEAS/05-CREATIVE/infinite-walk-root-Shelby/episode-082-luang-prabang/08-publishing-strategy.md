## RELEASE SCHEDULE

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (7 Days Before)

**Day -7: Teaser Announcement**
- **Platform:** Instagram Stories, Twitter
- **Content:** Mysterious teaser image (saffron robe detail or temple silhouette)
- **Copy:** "Something sacred is coming. Episode 082. March 31."
- **Goal:** Build anticipation

**Day -5: Behind the Scenes**
- **Platform:** Instagram Feed, TikTok
- **Content:** Photo of you at Tak Bat or Kuang Si Falls
- **Copy:** "This moment changed everything. Full story Tuesday."
- **Goal:** Human connection, curiosity

**Day -3: Quote Card**
- **Platform:** Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn
- **Content:** Beautiful quote from episode on branded background
- **Quote:** "Silence is not the absence of sound. It is the presence of something too large for speech."
- **Goal:** Shareable content, establish tone

**Day -1: Countdown**
- **Platform:** Instagram Stories, Twitter
- **Content:** "Tomorrow. 6 AM. The Hidden Temple."
- **Goal:** Final reminder

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues

**Cultural Sensitivity:**
- If criticized for Tak Bat participation
- **Response:** Acknowledge, explain respectful approach, commit to learning

**Accuracy:**
- If travel info is outdated
- **Response:** Update guide, thank person for correction

**Technical:**
- If episode has audio issues
- **Response:** Re-upload ASAP, notify listeners

### Response Guidelines

- Respond within 24 hours
- Acknowledge valid criticism
- Thank people for feedback
- Take conversations private when appropriate
- Never argue publicly
