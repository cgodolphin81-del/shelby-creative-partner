## VISUAL STYLE NOTES

**Color Palette:**
- Emerald greens (hills, grass, moss)
- Stone grays (cliffs, castles, ancient stones)
- Sky blues (Atlantic Ocean, clear skies)
- Heather purples (blooming hillsides)
- Warm golds (pub interiors, sunset light)
- Deep blacks (Guinness, storm clouds)

**Mood Keywords:**
- Ancient, mystical, atmospheric, dramatic
- windswept, wild, romantic, melancholic
- cozy, warm, welcoming, authentic

**Composition Tips:**
- Use wide angles for landscapes (show scale)
- Include human element for perspective
- Capture weather as character (rain, mist, storms)
- Golden hour and blue hour for drama
- Long exposures for water and clouds
