### Hook Option 1: Sensory Immersion
```
Close your eyes. Feel the wind. Salt on your lips. The sound of waves crashing against stone—three hundred million years of stone. You are standing at the Cliffs of Moher, where Ireland ends and the Atlantic begins.
```

### Hook Option 2: Provocative Question
```
What if a place could change you? Not just your photos, not just your stories—but something deeper. Your internal geography. Your soul. Ireland does that. This is why.
```

```
Three hundred million years. That's how old the Cliffs of Moher are. Your ancestors were single-celled organisms when these stones began their vigil. Stand here, and the rational mind surrenders.
```
