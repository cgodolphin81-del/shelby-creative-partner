## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Technical Issues
- Have backup hosting ready
- Communicate delays transparently
- Offer exclusive content as apology if significant delay

### If Negative Feedback
- Respond with grace and curiosity
- Don't argue publicly
- Take legitimate criticism to heart
- Ignore trolls

### If Low Engagement
- Don't panic—some episodes take time to find audience
- Double down on organic engagement
- Consider paid boost if content is strong
- Learn and apply to next episode
