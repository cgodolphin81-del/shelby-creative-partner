## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Headlines
**Font**: Playfair Display (serif) or Cinzel
**Style**: Elegant, classical, timeless
**Weights**: Regular, Bold
**Usage**: Episode titles, main headlines, quote graphics

**Why**: Evokes historical gravitas, pairs beautifully with Islamic architecture aesthetics

---

### Secondary Font: Body Text
**Font**: Lato or Open Sans (sans-serif)
**Style**: Clean, readable, modern
**Weights**: Light, Regular, Medium
**Usage**: Body copy, captions, descriptions

**Why**: High readability, balances ornate headlines, works at all sizes

---

### Accent Font: Decorative
**Font**: Great Vibes or similar script (sparingly)
**Style**: Flowing, calligraphic
**Usage**: Pull quotes, special emphasis only

**Why**: Evokes Arabic calligraphy tradition without appropriating

---

### Font Pairing Examples

**Headline**: Playfair Display Bold, 48px, #1E3A8A
**Subhead**: Playfair Display Regular, 24px, #5D4037
**Body**: Lato Regular, 16px, #5D4037
**Caption**: Lato Light, 14px, #5D4037

## TEXTURE & PATTERN DIRECTION

### Islamic Geometric Patterns

**Sources**:
- Registan tilework photos
- Traditional Uzbek patterns
- 8-point and 12-point stars
- Interlocking polygons

**Usage**:
- Subtle backgrounds (10-20% opacity)
- Border decorations
- Social media template frames
- Website section dividers

**Style Notes**:
- Keep patterns simple, not overwhelming
- Use as texture, not focal point
- Maintain cultural respect—these are sacred geometries

---

### Weathered/aged Textures

**Sources**:
- Old brick photography
- Weathered wood grain
- Cracked plaster
- Sand texture overlays

**Usage**:
- Background layers
- Vintage feel for historical content
- Depth in flat designs

**Style Notes**:
- Subtle application (5-15% opacity)
- Don't make text unreadable
- Combine with clean elements for balance

---

### Light & Shadow

**Sources**:
- Archway shadows
- Lattice work (mashrabiya) patterns
- Dappled light through courtyards
- Sunset/sunrise gradients

**Usage**:
- Depth in compositions
- Mood setting
- Transition effects

**Style Notes**:
- Soft edges for contemplative feel
- Hard edges for dramatic moments
- Match time of day to content mood

---

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Color Treatment**:
- Warm tones enhanced (golds, oranges)
- Blues pushed toward lapis/turquoise
- Slight desaturation of greens
- Maintain natural skin tones

**Contrast**:
- Moderate to high contrast
- Deep shadows for drama
- Bright highlights for divine light effect

**Composition**:
- Symmetry when possible (Islamic architecture)
- Leading lines (archways, corridors)
- Human element for scale
- Detail shots (tilework, carvings)

---

### AI Image Generation Style

**Prompt Modifiers**:
```
"cinematic, dramatic lighting, photorealistic, 8K, professional photography"
"warm golden hour light, blue hour, atmospheric"
"intricate detail, sharp focus, depth of field"
"epic scale, vast landscapes, sense of wonder"
```

**Avoid**:
- Overly saturated, unnatural colors
- Modern elements (cars, signs, etc.)
- Crowded tourist scenes
- Poor quality, blurry images

## VIDEO & MOTION

### Color Grading

**Look**: Warm, cinematic, slightly desaturated
**LUT Direction**: Kodak 2383 print emulation or similar
**Blacks**: Slightly lifted (not crushed)
**Highlights**: Warm, golden
**Midtones**: Rich, full

---

### Motion Style

**Pace**: Slow, contemplative
**Transitions**: Smooth fades, subtle zooms
**Text Animation**: Gentle fade-ins, no bouncy effects
**Music**: Ambient, Middle Eastern influences, minimal percussion

**Frame Rate**: 24fps (cinematic)
**Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 (main), 9:16 (social), 1:1 (Instagram)

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

**Minimum Ratios** (WCAG AA):
- Normal text: 4.5:1
- Large text: 3:1
- UI elements: 3:1

**Tested Combinations**:
- ✅ Lapis on White: 8.2:1 (Pass)
- ✅ Deep Brown on White: 7.8:1 (Pass)
- ✅ White on Lapis: 8.2:1 (Pass)
- ✅ White on Desert Orange: 4.6:1 (Pass)
- ⚠️ Turquoise on White: 2.1:1 (Fail - use for large text only)
- ⚠️ Sand Gold on White: 1.8:1 (Fail - use for backgrounds only)

---

### Alt Text Guidelines

**For Images**:
- Describe the scene objectively
- Include key architectural elements
- Mention colors when relevant
- Note emotional quality if appropriate

**Example**:
"Three madrasas with brilliant blue domes frame the Registan Square in Samarkand. Intricate turquoise tilework covers the facades. Golden sunrise light illuminates the courtyard. No people visible."

## CULTURAL SENSITIVITY

### Do's
✅ Research patterns before using them
✅ Understand symbolic meanings
✅ Credit sources when using traditional designs
✅ Use colors in culturally appropriate ways
✅ Consult with cultural experts when possible

### Don'ts
❌ Don't use sacred symbols as decoration
❌ Don't mix patterns from different cultures randomly
❌ Don't claim traditional designs as original
❌ Don't use religious text decoratively
❌ Don't appropriate without understanding
