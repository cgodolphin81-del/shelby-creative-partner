## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Instagram

**Feed Posts:** 5-7 during launch week
**Stories:** Daily during launch week, 3-4x/week after
**Reels:** 2-3 during launch week
**Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 2 hours
**Hashtags:** 15-20 per post (mix of popular and niche)
**Best Times:** 8-9 AM, 12 PM, 7-8 PM (test for your audience)

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Episode promotion
- 30% Location beauty/travel inspiration
- 20% Behind-the-scenes/process
- 10% Community engagement (polls, questions)

### Twitter/X

**Threads:** 1-2 per week (launch week: daily)
**Single Tweets:** 2-3 per day during launch
**Engagement:** Reply to all mentions, engage with travel Twitter
**Hashtags:** 2-3 per tweet max
**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM

**Strategy:**
- Use threads for storytelling
- Quote-tweet travel content with insights
- Engage with Portugal/travel accounts
- Participate in relevant hashtags (#TravelTuesday, etc.)

### Facebook

**Feed Posts:** 1 per day during launch week
**Stories:** 3-5 per week
**Groups:** Share in 3-5 travel groups (follow rules)
**Engagement:** Respond to all comments
**Best Times:** 1-3 PM, 7-9 PM

**Strategy:**
- Longer-form captions perform well
- Share to personal profile + page
- Use Facebook Events for launch
- Consider Facebook Ads ($20-50 for reach)

### LinkedIn

**Posts:** 2-3 during launch week
**Articles:** 1 long-form article (essay adaptation)
**Engagement:** Professional angle, respond thoughtfully
**Best Times:** 7-9 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM (weekdays)

**Strategy:**

- Connect to broader themes (Romanticism vs. efficiency)
- Engage with culture/travel/creative professionals
- Use LinkedIn Newsletter if available

### TikTok

**Videos:** 3-5 during launch week

## Analytics & Measurement

### Key Metrics to Track

**Launch Week:**
- Total listens (first 7 days)
- Completion rate (% who listen to end)
- Social engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Email open rate + click rate
- Website traffic (episode page)
- New subscribers/followers

**Ongoing (30 Days):**
- Total listens (cumulative)
- Average listen duration
- Social growth
- Email list growth
- Search rankings (for "Sintra podcast," etc.)

### Tools

- **Podcast Host Analytics:** Listen counts, completion rates
- **Google Analytics:** Website traffic, behavior
- **Social Analytics:** Native platform insights
- **Email Platform:** Open rates, click rates, conversions
- **Link Tracking:** Bitly, UTM parameters

### Success Benchmarks

**Strong Launch:**
- 500+ listens in first week
- 60%+ completion rate
- 5%+ engagement rate on social
- 25%+ email open rate
- 10+ new subscribers/followers per day

**Adjust If:**
- Completion rate < 40% (episode too long? pacing issues?)
- Social engagement < 2% (content not resonating?)
- Email open rate < 15% (subject line issues?)

## Contingency Plans

### If Launch Is Quiet

1. **Re-engage email list** with different subject line
2. **Boost top-performing post** with paid promotion
3. **Reach out to 5-10 more influencers/bloggers**
4. **Create additional content** (blog post, video)
5. **Engage more actively** in travel communities

### If Technical Issues

1. **Have backup hosting** ready (alternative podcast platform)
2. **Test all links** before launch
3. **Prepare status update** template for social/email
4. **Offer alternative listen method** (direct file, YouTube)

### If Negative Feedback

1. **Respond graciously** to constructive criticism
2. **Don't engage** with trolls
3. **Learn and iterate** for future episodes
4. **Focus on positive feedback** and loyal listeners
