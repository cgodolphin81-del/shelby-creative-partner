## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues:

**Factual Errors:**
- Correct immediately with transparency
- Update show notes
- Acknowledge in next episode if significant

**Negative Reviews:**
- Respond professionally (if responding at all)
- Don't argue
- Learn from valid criticism

**Conservation Controversy:**
- Some may argue tourism harms Galapagos
- Acknowledge complexity
- Emphasize responsible tourism message
- Link to conservation organizations

**Technical Issues:**
- Audio problems: Re-upload if severe
- Platform issues: Communicate via social media
- Website down: Have backup (Linktree, etc.)
