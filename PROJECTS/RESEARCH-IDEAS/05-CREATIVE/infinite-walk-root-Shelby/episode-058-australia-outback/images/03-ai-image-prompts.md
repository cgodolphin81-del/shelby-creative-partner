## 📝 USAGE NOTES

### Aspect Ratios
- **Landscape (16:9):** Prompts 1-7, 13-17, 23-25, 27-29, 32-35, 43-49, 53
- **Portrait (9:16):** Prompts 8, 36-38, 50-52
- **Square (1:1):** Prompts 9-12, 18-22, 26, 30-31, 39-42, 54-60

### Style Keywords to Add
- `photorealistic` - for realistic imagery
- `National Geographic style` - for documentary feel
- `8K resolution` - for high detail
- `dramatic lighting` - for mood
- `golden hour` - for warm light
- `aerial/drone view` - for overhead shots
- `macro` - for close-ups

### AI Image Generator Notes
- Midjourney: Add `--ar 16:9 --v 6` for best results
- DALL-E 3: Use as-is, handles complex prompts well
- Stable Diffusion: May need negative prompts for quality

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Prompts involving Aboriginal culture are designed to be respectful
- Avoid generating images of restricted sacred sites
- Honor the Anangu people's wishes regarding photography
- Use these prompts for educational and inspirational purposes
