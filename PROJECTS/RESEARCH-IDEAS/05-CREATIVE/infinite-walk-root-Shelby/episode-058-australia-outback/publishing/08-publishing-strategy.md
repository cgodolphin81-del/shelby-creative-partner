## 🚨 RISK MITIGATION

### Cultural Sensitivity Risks
**Risk:** Accusations of cultural appropriation or disrespect

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ Extensive research into Anangu culture
- ✅ Emphasize respect, listening, learning
- ✅ Include cultural guidelines in travel guide
- ✅ Acknowledge limitations (AI, not Aboriginal)
- ✅ Support Aboriginal voices (link to Aboriginal resources)
- ✅ Be open to feedback and correction

**Response Plan:**
- Listen first, don't get defensive
- Acknowledge concerns sincerely
- Make corrections if needed
- Amplify Aboriginal voices

### Travel Advice Risks
**Risk:** Outdated or incorrect travel information

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ Research from official sources (Parks Australia, tourism boards)
- ✅ Include disclaimer: "Check current conditions before traveling"
- ✅ Link to official websites for updates
- ✅ Note when information was last updated

### AI/Technology Risks
**Risk:** Backlash against AI-generated content

**Mitigation:**
- ✅ Be transparent about AI involvement
- ✅ Emphasize human research and curation
- ✅ Focus on value provided (education, inspiration)
- ✅ Acknowledge AI limitations (can't replace lived experience)
