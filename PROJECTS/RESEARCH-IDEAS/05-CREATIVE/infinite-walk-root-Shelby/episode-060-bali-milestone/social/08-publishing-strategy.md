## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram

**Feed Posts**: 5-7 posts over 2 weeks
- Milestone announcement
- Then & Now carousel
- Rice terrace aesthetic
- Temple gratitude
- Quote graphics (3-4)
- Listener reactions
- One week reflection

**Stories**: Daily for first week, then 3-4x/week
- Behind-the-scenes
- Polls: "Where should we go for Episode 61?"
- Q&A: "Ask me anything about 60 episodes"
- Share listener DMs (with permission)
- Countdown stickers (pre-launch)
- Link stickers (all posts)

**Reels**: 2-3 reels
- 60-second milestone montage
- Quote + aesthetic video
- Personal reflection video

**Engagement**: Respond to ALL comments within 2 hours for first 3 days

---

### Twitter/X

**Launch Day**: 6-tweet thread + 3-4 additional tweets
**Week 1**: 2-3 tweets/day (quotes, engagement, retweets)
**Week 2**: 1-2 tweets/day (transition to Ep 061)

**Strategy**:
- Use thread for long-form storytelling
- Quote tweet listener reactions
- Engage with podcast community hashtags
- Reply to every mention for first 48 hours

**Hashtags**: #InfiniteWalk #Episode060 #Podcast #Bali #60Episodes

---

### LinkedIn

**Launch Day**: 1 long-form post (professional angle)
**Week 1**: 2-3 posts (lessons learned, consistency, growth)

## METRICS TO TRACK

### Download/Listen Metrics
- Total downloads (first 7 days, first 30 days)
- Comparison to Episode 050 (previous milestone)
- Comparison to average episode (baseline)
- Platform breakdown (Apple, Spotify, etc.)
- Completion rate (do people listen to all 25 minutes?)

### Engagement Metrics
- Social media: Likes, comments, shares, saves
- Email: Open rate, click rate, reply rate, forwards
- Reviews: New reviews on Apple Podcasts, Spotify
- DMs/Messages: Volume and sentiment

### Growth Metrics
- New subscribers/followers (all platforms)
- Email list growth
- Website traffic (if applicable)
- Search volume for "Infinite Walk podcast"

### Qualitative Metrics
- Listener stories shared
- Depth of comments/messages
- Press mentions
- Collaboration requests

**Success Benchmarks** (adjust based on current show size):
- 2-3x normal download numbers for milestone
- 50+ new reviews across platforms
- 100+ social media engagements
- 10+ personal listener messages
- 5-10% email list growth
