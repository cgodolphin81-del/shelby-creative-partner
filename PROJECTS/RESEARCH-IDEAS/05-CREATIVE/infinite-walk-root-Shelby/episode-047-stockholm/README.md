## 🎙️ EPISODE OVERVIEW

### Title Options
1. **"The Viking Capital: Stockholm, Sweden"** *(Recommended)*
2. "Episode 047: The Viking Capital - Walking the Edge of Light"
3. "Stockholm: Where Water Meets Stone"
4. "The Nordic Way: Stockholm and the Balance of Opposites"
5. "Water and Stone: A Stockholm Walk"

### Logline
Where the archipelago meets the city. Where tradition meets innovation. Where light battles darkness for half the year. Episode 047 takes you to Stockholm, Sweden.

### Episode Description
In this episode of The Infinite Walk, we journey to Stockholm, Sweden:

- 🇸🇪 **Gamla Stan:** 700-year-old cobblestone streets, colorful merchant houses, the Royal Palace
- 🇸🇪 **The Vasa Museum:** A 17th-century warship that sank on its maiden voyage, preserved for 333 years
- 🇸🇪 **Stockholm Archipelago:** 30,000+ islands stretching into the Baltic Sea
- 🇸🇪 **Nobel Prize:** How Alfred Nobel rewrote his legacy after reading his own obituary
- 🇸🇪 **Swedish Design:** "Democracy through design" – IKEA, Orrefors, Acne Studios
- 🇸🇪 **Fika Culture:** The Swedish coffee ritual that's revolutionary in a busy world
- 🇸🇪 **Light & Darkness:** Living at 59° north – 18.5 hours of daylight in June, 6 hours in December
- 🇸🇪 **The Nordic Way:** Balance in a world of extremes

This is not a travel guide. This is a love letter to a city that understands tension.

Water and stone. Light and darkness. Innovation and tradition.

The Nordic way.

### Key Themes
- **Water and Stone:** The archipelago, Gamla Stan, geological and cultural foundations
- **Ambition and Gravity:** The Vasa's story of failure becoming teaching
- **Recognition and Responsibility:** Nobel Prize, legacy, Alfred Nobel's redemption
- **Pause and Productivity:** Fika culture, the Nordic approach to work-life balance
- **Light and Darkness:** Living at 59° north, seasonal extremes, resilience
- **The Nordic Way:** Balance, lagom, individualism and collectivism held in tension

### Target Audience
- Travel enthusiasts (25-55, culturally curious)
- Podcast listeners (weekly consumers, 20-60 min episodes)
- Culture seekers (history, design, philosophy)
- Nordic/Scandinavian interest (existing fanbase)
- Personal development (leadership, balance, mindfulness)

## 📝 PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Pre-Production
- [ ] Read `research/01-location-research.md` for context and accuracy
- [ ] Review `scripts/02-poetic-essay.md` for narrative flow and themes
- [ ] Study `scripts/04-audio-narration-20min.md` for recording (timing, music cues, pauses)
- [ ] Generate images using `prompts/03-image-prompts-55plus.md` (67 prompts across 7 categories)
- [ ] Create episode art using `assets/09-visual-mood-board.md` (color palettes, typography, templates)

### Step 2: Recording
- [ ] Set up recording environment (quiet, dry room, minimal reverb)
- [ ] Test microphone levels (peak at -6dB, average around -18dB)
- [ ] Record narration using `scripts/04-audio-narration-20min.md`
- [ ] Follow timing cues exactly (20 minutes total with music/pauses)
- [ ] Record multiple takes of key sections (opening, Vasa story, fika, closing)
- [ ] Capture room tone (30 seconds of silence for editing)

### Step 3: Post-Production
- [ ] Edit narration (remove breaths, mistakes, long pauses, mouth clicks)
- [ ] Add music (Scandinavian ambient, traditional Swedish folk, modern minimalist)
  - Intro/Outro: Ólafur Arnalds, Nils Frahm, or similar
  - Gamla Stan: Medieval/period strings, subtle
  - Vasa: Dramatic orchestral, building tension
  - Nobel: Elegant, ceremonial
  - Design/Fika: Light, modern, minimalist
  - Light/Darkness: Ethereal, atmospheric contrast
  - Nordic Way: Balanced, folk influences
- [ ] Add sound effects (water, wind, cobblestones, café ambience, ferry, candles)
- [ ] Music ducking: -18dB under voice, -6dB during pauses
- [ ] Normalize to -16 LUFS (podcast standard)
- [ ] EQ: Warmth around 200Hz, clarity around 3kHz
- [ ] Compression: Gentle, 2:1 ratio, preserve dynamics
- [ ] Export as MP3 (192kbps minimum) and WAV (archival)

### Step 4: Quality Check
- [ ] Listen to full episode (check for errors, flow, pacing)
- [ ] Verify music levels (background, not overpowering narration)
- [ ] Check intro/outro clarity (episode number, title, host name)
- [ ] Test on multiple devices (headphones, car speakers, phone speakers)
- [ ] Verify file metadata (ID3 tags: title, artist, episode number, artwork)

### Step 5: Artwork & Assets
- [ ] Episode artwork (3000x3000px, JPG/PNG)
  - Use `assets/09-visual-mood-board.md` for color palette and design
  - Include: "Episode 047", "The Viking Capital", "Stockholm, Sweden", show logo
- [ ] Social media graphics (Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, Pinterest)
- [ ] YouTube thumbnail (1280x720px, high contrast, minimal text)
- [ ] AI-generated images from prompts (select 10-15 best for social/media)
