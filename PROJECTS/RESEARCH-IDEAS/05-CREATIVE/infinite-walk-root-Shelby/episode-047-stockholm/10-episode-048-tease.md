## ⚠️ TEASE BEST PRACTICES

### Do's
✅ Be mysterious but not frustratingly vague  
✅ Engage with every guess (builds community)  
✅ Drop hints progressively (don't reveal everything at once)  
✅ Make it fun, not a test  
✅ Follow through on prizes/promises  
✅ Keep timeline consistent (don't delay reveal)  

### Don'ts
❌ Reveal too early (kills anticipation)  
❌ Make hints impossible to guess  
❌ Ignore guesses (disengages community)  
❌ Change destination mid-tease (confusing)  
❌ Over-promise and under-deliver  
❌ Forget to thank participants
