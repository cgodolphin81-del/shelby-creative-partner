## 🍜 FOOD HIGHLIGHTS

### Hanoi Specialties
- **Pho Bo** - Beef noodle soup (breakfast staple)
- **Bun Cha** - Grilled pork with noodles (Obama's favorite)
- **Banh Mi** - Vietnamese sandwich
- **Ca Phe Trung** - Egg coffee (Giang Cafe, since 1946)
- **Cha Ca** - Turmeric fish with dill

### Hoi An Specialties
- **Cao Lau** - Thick noodles with pork (Hoi An signature)
- **Banh Bao Banh Vac** - White Rose dumplings
- **Com Ga** - Chicken rice (turmeric-yellow)
- **Mi Quang** - Turmeric noodles

### National Favorites
- **Goi Cuon** - Fresh spring rolls
- **Pho Ga** - Chicken pho
- **Bun Bo Hue** - Spicy beef noodle soup
- **Banh Xeo** - Crispy pancake
- **Che** - Sweet dessert soup
