### Long Description (200 words)
**Episode 052: The Rising Sun - Tokyo, Japan**

The first light finds the temple before it finds the city.

At 5:47 AM, while Shibuya still dreams in neon, the incense at Senso-ji burns low and blue. An elderly woman in a faded kimono bows three times. She doesn't pray for much anymore—just health for her grandson, peace for her husband's memory, enough strength to climb the stairs another year.

The Kannon hears her. The Kannon has heard millions like her since 645 AD.

This episode explores eight truths of Tokyo through immersive audio storytelling:

1. Everything ends, and everything begins again
2. You can be alone in a crowd of millions
3. Nature is always watching
4. Technology reveals what we were hungry for
5. Mastery is a form of love
6. Silence is the presence of something worth listening to
7. The future is a practice, not a destination
8. The city does not belong to you, but you belong to it

Journey from dawn at Senso-ji to the scramble at Shibuya. From Mount Fuji's silent watch to Akihabara's electric dreams. From the ritual of the sushi chef to the ephemeral beauty of cherry blossoms.

Twenty minutes. Best experienced with headphones, on a long walk, late at night, or while watching the sunrise.

Tokyo is waiting. Not for tourists, not for pilgrims, but for witnesses.

Be one.
