## 🎯 PLACEMENT STRATEGY

### In Episode 052 Audio

**Option A: Cold Open Tease (Beginning)**
- Add 15-second tease before episode starts
- Builds anticipation from the start
- Risk: Might confuse first-time listeners

**Option B: Mid-Roll Tease (Middle)**
- Add 10-second tease at 15-minute mark
- Natural break point
- Risk: Interrupts flow

**Option C: Hot Close Tease (End) - RECOMMENDED**
- Add 30-second tease after episode ends
- Natural transition
- Listeners already engaged
- Standard podcast practice

**Implementation:**
- Add after credits/outro
- Keep music bed consistent
- Use same voice tone
- 30 seconds max
