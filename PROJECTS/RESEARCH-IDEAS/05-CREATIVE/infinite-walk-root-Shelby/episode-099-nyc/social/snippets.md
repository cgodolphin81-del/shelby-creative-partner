### Thread 2: Travel Tips

**Tweet 1/4:**
Going to NYC? Here's what I learned walking 99 cities, ending in New York:

1. Don't drive. The subway is faster, cheaper, and an experience itself. $2.90/ride, 24/7.

**Tweet 2/4:**
2. Skip Times Square restaurants. Walk 10 blocks in any direction. Better food, half the price, actual New Yorkers.

3. The Staten Island Ferry is free and has the best Statue of Liberty views.

**Tweet 3/4:**
4. Central Park at 6 AM is a different planet. Empty paths. Golden light. Birds. You'll forget you're in Manhattan.

5. Brooklyn Bridge: approach from DUMBO side. Less crowded. Better photos.

**Tweet 4/4:**
6. Tip 18-22%. Always.
7. Walk fast. Don't block sidewalks.
8. Bodega breakfast = egg & cheese on roll ($6). The real NYC experience.

Full travel guide in Episode 099 show notes.
