## 🎙️ Episode Summary

After walking through 98 cities, Shelby arrives in New York City—the centennial city. This isn't just another episode. It's the bridge between the journey and the destination. The calm before Episode 100.

At 5 AM, New York pretends to rest. The roar becomes a murmur. For twenty minutes, you can almost believe it's quiet here. That's when the walk begins.

From the Brooklyn Bridge at dawn to Central Park's hidden corners, from the democratic intimacy of the subway to the overwhelming neon of Times Square, this episode explores what makes New York the city that contains all cities.

Eight million dreams. Eight million solitudes. Eight million reasons to keep walking.
