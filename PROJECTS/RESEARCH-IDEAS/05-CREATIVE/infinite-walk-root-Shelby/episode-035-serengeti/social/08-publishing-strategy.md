## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Instagram

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Beautiful imagery (landscapes, wildlife)
- 30% Educational (facts, infographics)
- 20% Behind-the-scenes (Shelby, research process)
- 10% Engagement (polls, questions, user content)

**Posting Schedule:**
- Feed: Once daily for first week, then 3x/week
- Stories: Daily for first week, then 3-5x/week
- Reels: 3-5 during launch week, then 2x/week

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- 5-7 broad (#Serengeti, #Wildlife, #Africa)
- 5-7 niche (#GreatMigration, #SafariLife, #TanzaniaTravel)
- 3-5 branded (#InfiniteWalk, #ShelbyWalks)

**Engagement:**
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours
- Engage with safari/travel accounts
- Use Stories polls and questions daily

---

### Twitter/X

## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues

**1. Cultural Sensitivity (Maasai Representation)**
- Ensure respectful, accurate portrayal
- Acknowledge modern challenges
- Avoid romanticizing or stereotyping
- If criticized: Listen, learn, apologize, educate

**Response:**
> "Thank you for this feedback. My intention was to honor Maasai culture and their relationship with the land. I hear your concerns about [specific issue]. I'm committed to learning and doing better. If you'd like to share more, I'm listening."

**2. Wildlife Ethics (Safari Tourism)**
- Acknowledge complexity
- Emphasize responsible tourism
- Highlight conservation efforts
- Avoid glorifying harmful practices

**Response:**
> "Great question. Safari tourism is complex. I focused on ethical operators who support conservation and local communities. It's not perfect, but done right, it can fund protection of these ecosystems. I should have been clearer about this — thanks for raising it."

**3. Factual Accuracy**
- Double-check all stats and claims
- Cite sources in show notes
- If error found: Acknowledge, correct, thank person who caught it

**Response:**
> "You're absolutely right — I misstated [fact]. The correct information is [correction]. Thank you for catching this. I've updated the show notes. Accuracy matters, and I appreciate you holding me to that standard."
