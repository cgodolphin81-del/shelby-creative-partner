## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Headlines

#### Playfair Display
**Style:** Serif, elegant, timeless  
**Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)  
**Usage:** Episode titles, major headlines, quote cards

**Example:**
```
The Plain of Temples
Playfair Display Bold, 48pt
```

**Why:** Evokes history, craftsmanship, elegance. Pairs well with ancient subject matter.

---

### Secondary Font: Body Text

#### Lato
**Style:** Sans-serif, clean, readable  
**Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Medium (700)  
**Usage:** Body copy, social captions, descriptions

**Example:**
```
2,200 temples. 800 years. One sunrise.
Lato Regular, 16pt
```

**Why:** Modern contrast to historical subject. Highly readable across platforms.

---

### Accent Font: Quotes & Special

#### Cormorant Garamond
**Style:** Serif, delicate, literary  
**Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600)  
**Usage:** Pull quotes, poetic text, special moments

**Example:**
```
"You arrive in dust."
Cormorant Garamond Light Italic, 24pt
```

**Why:** Adds literary quality, perfect for poetic essay excerpts.

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

#### Mood: Contemplative & Authentic
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, dawn, dusk (avoid harsh midday)
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, leading lines, negative space
- **Subjects:** Temples, details, people (respectful, candid)
- **Avoid:** Over-saturated, touristy clichés, staged shots

---

#### Key Shot Types

**1. The Reveal (Wide Landscape)**
- Aerial or elevated view of temple plain
- Sunrise/sunset lighting
- Mist or atmospheric haze
- Balloons (if available)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape), 4:5 (portrait for social)

**2. The Detail (Texture & Craft)**
- Close-up of brickwork
- Hand touching ancient stone
- Fresco details
- Weathered surfaces
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square), 4:5 (portrait)

**3. The Human Element (Respectful)**
- Monk silhouette (backlit, anonymous)
- Devotee praying (from distance, respectful)
- Local life (candid, not staged)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:4 (portrait), 1:1 (square)

**4. The Perspective (Unique Angle)**
- Looking up at temple spire
- Through temple doorway/window
- Reflection in water
- Shadow play
- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:5 (portrait), 16:9 (landscape)

---

### Color Grading

#### Preset Direction: "Bagan Warmth"
- **Temperature:** +10 (warm)
- **Tint:** +5 (slight magenta)
- **Exposure:** -0.3 (slightly underexposed for mood)
- **Contrast:** +15 (enhance depth)
- **Highlights:** -20 (protect sky detail)
- **Shadows:** +10 (lift for detail)
- **Whites:** -10 (soft highlight roll-off)
- **Blacks:** +5 (slightly lifted for filmic look)
- **Vibrance:** +10 (subtle color enhancement)
- **Saturation:** -5 (muted, not oversaturated)

#### Split Toning
- **Highlights:** Warm gold (#D4AF37), Saturation 15
- **Shadows:** Cool blue (#4682B4), Saturation 10

## ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum Ratio:** 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- **Temple Gold on Earth Red:** 3.8:1 (use for large text only)
- **White on Earth Red:** 5.2:1 (accessible)
- **Temple Gold on Deep Night Blue:** 8.1:1 (highly accessible)

### Alt Text Guidelines
- **Describe:** Subject, action, context, mood
- **Example:** "Aerial view of Bagan temple plain at sunrise, hundreds of ancient stupas emerging from morning mist, hot air balloons in distance"
- **Avoid:** "Image of" or "Photo of" (redundant)

### Font Sizes (Minimum)
- **Social Media:** 16pt body, 24pt headlines
- **Presentations:** 24pt body, 36pt headlines
- **Print:** 12pt body, 18pt headlines
