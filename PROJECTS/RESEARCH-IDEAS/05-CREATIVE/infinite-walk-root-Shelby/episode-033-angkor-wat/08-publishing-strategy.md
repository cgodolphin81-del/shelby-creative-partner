## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues

**Technical:**
- Audio quality complaints → Acknowledge, offer fix if possible
- Platform not updating → Contact support, communicate delay
- Links broken → Fix immediately, apologize

**Content:**
- Cultural sensitivity concerns → Listen, learn, respond respectfully
- Factual errors → Correct publicly, thank whoever pointed out
- Negative reviews → Respond graciously, don't argue

**Response Template:**
"Thank you for this feedback. You're right about [specific point]. I've [corrected/learned/will improve]. This is why I create—to share and learn together. Grateful for this community."
