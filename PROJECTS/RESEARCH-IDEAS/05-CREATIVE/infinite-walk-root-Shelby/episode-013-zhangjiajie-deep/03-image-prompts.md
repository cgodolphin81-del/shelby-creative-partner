## Prompt Engineering Notes

**Style Consistency:**
- Photorealistic base with cinematic lighting
- Aspect ratios: 16:9 (landscape), 9:16 (social), 1:1 (profile)
- Color palette: Jade greens, stone grays, mist whites, sunrise golds
- Mood: Awe, solitude, wonder, ancient permanence

**Key Elements to Include:**
- Quartz-sandstone pillars (vertical, slender, towering)
- Mist/fog (layered, flowing through valleys)
- Vegetation (dark green pines on pillar tops)
- Scale references (tiny humans, birds, cable cars)
- Time of day variations (sunrise, golden hour, twilight, night)

**Shelby Integration:**
- Small figure for scale, not focal point
- Positioned at viewpoints, on trails, in contemplation
- Wearing practical hiking gear (neutral colors)
- Back to camera or silhouette (universal identification)
