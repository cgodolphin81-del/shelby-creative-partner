### Caption 6: The Climb
```
800 stone steps.

That's what stands between you and the Monastery.

The path is steep. Unforgiving. The sun is high. Your legs burn. Your lungs ache.

Petra stretches below—stone and shadow, the Siq a thin scar, the Treasury a white gleam. Beyond, the desert rolls away. Endless. Indifferent.

You sit on the edge. Legs dangling over nothing. Alone.

And you finally understand: the climb was the point all along.
```
