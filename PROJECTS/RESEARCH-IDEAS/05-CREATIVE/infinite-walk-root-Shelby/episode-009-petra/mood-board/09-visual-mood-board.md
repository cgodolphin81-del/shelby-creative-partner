## 📐 Texture Library

### Stone Textures

**Weathered Sandstone**
- Deep horizontal striations
- Pitted surface from 2,000 years of erosion
- Color variations: rose, orange, purple bands
- Use for: Backgrounds, overlays, title cards

**Carved Details**
- Sharp chisel marks (visible in close-ups)
- Corinthian column fluting
- Floral frieze patterns
- Use for: Detail shots, transitions, texture overlays

**Smooth Polished Stone**
- Areas protected from elements
- Subtle sheen in sunlight
- Warmer tones than weathered areas
- Use for: Contrast with rough textures

### Desert Textures

**Desert Sand**
- Fine, wind-swept grains
- Subtle ripples from wind patterns
- Warm gold to cool shadow gradients
- Use for: Backgrounds, transitions

**Rocky Scree**
- Loose stones and gravel
- Sharp edges, varied sizes
- Mix of rose, gray, and brown tones
- Use for: Ground textures, hiking scenes

**Desert Pavement**
- Flat, compacted surface
- Darker stones on top (desert varnish)
- Cracked, dry appearance
- Use for: Wide landscape shots

### Natural Textures

**Thorn Bush**
- Sparse, hardy desert vegetation
- Gray-green leaves, thorny branches
- Resilient, surviving in harsh conditions
- Use for: Foreground interest, scale reference

**Wildflowers (Spring)**
- Small, delicate blooms
- Purple, white, yellow after rare rains
- Brief, precious, unexpected
- Use for: Hope, renewal, contrast

**Sky Gradients**
- Dawn: Pink → Orange → Blue
- Midday: Deep, clear blue
- Dusk: Orange → Purple → Deep blue
- Night: Black with brilliant stars
- Use for: Backgrounds, mood setting

### Human Textures

**Worn Hiking Boots**
- Dust-covered, scuffed
- Evidence of the journey
- Grounded, practical
- Use for: Personal connection, scale

**Weathered Hands**
- Touching ancient stone
- Skin texture + stone texture contrast
- Human connection to history
- Use for: Emotional moments, intimacy

**Traditional Textiles**
- Bedouin keffiyeh patterns
- Woven textures, geometric designs
- Black, white, red traditional colors
- Use for: Cultural context, human element

## 💡 Lighting Moods

### Dawn (5:30 AM - 7:30 AM)
**Characteristics:**
- Soft, diffused light
- Pink and lavender sky
- Long, gentle shadows
- Stone glows from within
- Cool shadows, warm highlights

**Emotional Tone:**
- Hope
- Revelation
- Quiet awe
- New beginnings

**Best For:**
- Treasury reveal
- Siq entrance
- Emotional openings
- Contemplative moments

**Color Grade:**
- Lift shadows slightly (cool)
- Warm highlights (pink/orange)
- Soft contrast
- Slight haze/glow effect

---

### Morning Golden Hour (7:30 AM - 9:30 AM)
**Characteristics:**
- Warm, directional light
- Defined shadows
- Stone at its most vibrant
- Clear, crisp air
- High color saturation

**Emotional Tone:**
- Energy
- Clarity
- Discovery
- Adventure

**Best For:**
- Main exploration sequences
- Architectural details
- Hiking scenes
- Active moments

**Color Grade:**
- Warm white balance (5500-6000K)
- Boost orange/red channels
- Moderate contrast
- Sharp, clear image

---

### Midday (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM)
**Characteristics:**
- Harsh, overhead light
- Deep, short shadows
- High contrast
- Colors can appear washed
- Heat shimmer visible

**Emotional Tone:**
- Challenge
- Intensity

- Showing difficulty of climb

- Realistic travel content

- Neutral white balance (5200K)
- Reduce highlights (prevent blowout)
- Lift shadows (reveal detail)
- Slight desaturation (heat effect)

## 🖼️ Composition Guidelines

### Signature Shots

**The Reveal**
- Frame: Wide shot from inside Siq
- Subject: Treasury emerging from canyon
- Composition: Rule of thirds, Treasury at right third
- Foreground: Dark Siq walls (frame within frame)
- Human element: Small figure (Shelby) in lower left
- Emotion: Awe, discovery

**The Carver's View**
- Frame: Close-up on carved details
- Subject: Corinthian columns, urn, friezes
- Composition: Fill frame with detail
- Light: Angled morning light (emphasize texture)
- Human element: Hand touching stone (optional)
- Emotion: Connection across time

**The Climb**
- Frame: Low angle looking up
- Subject: Trail to Monastery, stone steps
- Composition: Leading lines upward
- Light: Morning or late afternoon
- Human element: Figure climbing (small in frame)
- Emotion: Challenge, determination

**The Summit**
- Frame: Wide panoramic
- Subject: View from Monastery
- Composition: Horizon on lower third
- Light: Late afternoon (long shadows)
- Human element: Figure sitting at edge
- Emotion: Achievement, perspective

**The Solitude**
- Frame: Medium shot
- Subject: Alone with the stone
- Composition: Subject small in vast landscape
- Light: Golden hour
- Human element: Central but small
- Emotion: Contemplation, insignificance, peace

## 🎬 Visual Transitions

### Cut Transitions
- **Match Cut:** Similar shapes (urn → sun, column → tree)
- **Whip Pan:** Quick horizontal movement (Siq walls)
- **Fade to Black:** Between major sections
- **Cross Dissolve:** Time passing, light changing

### Effect Transitions
- **Light Leak:** Warm orange/pink overlay (dawn feeling)
- **Film Grain:** Subtle texture (timeless, analog feel)
- **Dust Particles:** Floating in light beams (Siq interior)
- **Heat Shimmer:** Midday desert scenes

### Color Transitions
- **Dawn to Day:** Pink → Gold → Blue (time passing)
- **Day to Dusk:** Blue → Orange → Purple (journey ending)
- **Memory Flash:** Desaturated → Saturated (past → present)

- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (vertical)
