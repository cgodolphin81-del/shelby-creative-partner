## ANALYTICS TO TRACK

### Week 1 (T-0 to T+7):
- **Total listens/plays** (all platforms)
- **Completion rate** (how many finished the episode)
- **Social engagement** (likes, comments, shares)
- **Email open rate** (launch email)
- **YouTube:** Views, watch time, CTR on thumbnail

### Week 2 (T+8 to T+15):
- **Total listens** (cumulative)
- **Organic vs. promoted** traffic
- **New subscribers/followers** gained
- **Reviews/ratings** (podcast platforms)
- **Top referral sources** (where listeners came from)

### Success Metrics (Adjust for Your Scale):
- **Small show (<1K listens/episode):** 20% engagement rate, 5+ reviews
- **Medium show (1K-10K):** 15% engagement, 20+ reviews
- **Large show (10K+):** 10% engagement, 100+ reviews

## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If Launch Day Issues Occur:
- **Audio problems:** Have backup file ready, communicate transparently
- **Platform outage:** Post on alternative platforms, update when resolved
- **Low initial engagement:** Boost with paid promotion (if budget allows)

### If Episode Underperforms:
- **Diagnose:** Was it the topic? The promotion? The timing?
- **Adjust:** Double down on what worked, cut what didn't
- **Don't panic:** One episode does not define the show
