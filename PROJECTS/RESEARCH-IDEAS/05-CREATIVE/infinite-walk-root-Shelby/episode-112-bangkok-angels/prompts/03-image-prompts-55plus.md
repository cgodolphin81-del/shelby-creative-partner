## USAGE NOTES

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** — YouTube thumbnails, video frames, landscape photos
- **4:5** — Instagram portrait posts, vertical content
- **1:1** — Instagram square posts, profile images, icons

### Style Parameters
- `--v 6` — Midjourney version 6 for best quality
- Add `--stylize 250` for more artistic interpretation
- Add `--chaos 20` for more variety in results

### Best Practices
1. Generate multiple variations of each prompt
2. Mix realistic and artistic interpretations
3. Use for thumbnails, social posts, episode artwork
4. Consider consistency in color grading across images
5. Match visual style to episode tone (contemplative, immersive)
