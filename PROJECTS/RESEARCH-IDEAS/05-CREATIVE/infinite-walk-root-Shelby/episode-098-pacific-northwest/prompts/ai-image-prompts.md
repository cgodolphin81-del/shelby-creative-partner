## USAGE NOTES

**Recommended Aspect Ratios:**
- Landscape (16:9): Prompts 1-10, 51-60 (landscapes)
- Portrait (4:5 or 9:16): Prompts 11-20, 41-50 (trees, Shelby)
- Square (1:1): Prompts 21-30, 31-40 (details, wildlife)
- Flexible: Prompts 61-65 (conceptual)

**Color Palette for Consistency:**
- Forest Greens: #2D5016, #3A5F0B
- Moss Greens: #4A5D23, #6B7B3F
- Tree Browns: #3E2723, #5D4037
- Mist Greys: #90A4AE, #B0BEC5
- Accent Golds: #FFD54F (for light beams)

**Style Consistency:**
- Photorealistic for landscapes and wildlife
- Slightly painterly for atmospheric shots
- Macro detail for moss and ferns
- Cinematic composition throughout
- Natural lighting preferred (golden hour, overcast soft light)
