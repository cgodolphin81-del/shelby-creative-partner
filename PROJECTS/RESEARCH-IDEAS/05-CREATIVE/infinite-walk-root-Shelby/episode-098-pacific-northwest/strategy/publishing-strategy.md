## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram

**Primary Format:** Mix of feed posts, Reels, Stories
**Posting Frequency:** Daily for Week 1, then 3-4x/week
**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 7-9 PM (test for your audience)

**Strategy:**
- Use all features (feed, Reels, Stories, Guides)
- Leverage location tags (Hoh Rainforest, Olympic National Park)
- Engage with PNW/nature community
- Use Story highlights to archive episode content

**Hashtags:**
- 5-10 per post (mix of popular and niche)
- Rotate to avoid shadowban
- Create branded hashtag: #TheInfiniteWalk

---

### TikTok

**Primary Format:** Short-form video (15-60 seconds)
**Posting Frequency:** Daily for Week 1, then 4-5x/week
**Best Times:** 6-9 PM (when people are scrolling)

**Strategy:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending audio when relevant
- Text overlays for silent viewing
- CTA in caption and video

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (facts, ecology)
- 30% Emotional (quotes, reflections)
- 20% Personal (your experience)
- 10% Promotional (direct CTAs)

---

### Twitter/X

**Primary Format:** Threads + individual tweets
**Posting Frequency:** 2-3x/day for Week 1, then 1x/day
**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-7 PM

**Strategy:**
- Lead with main thread on launch day
- Engage with nature/PNW Twitter community
- Quote tweet relevant news (conservation, parks)
- Use thread reader for long-form

**Engagement:**
- Reply to all comments (first 48 hours critical)
- Quote tweet positive responses
- Tag relevant accounts (parks, conservation orgs)

---

## COMMUNITY MANAGEMENT

### Response Guidelines:

**Timing:**
- Respond to all comments within 24 hours (first 48 hours critical)
- Check messages 2-3x/day during launch week
- Set up notifications for mentions

**Tone:**
- Warm and genuine
- Not corporate or robotic
- Admit when you don't know something
- Thank people for sharing their experiences

**Handling Negative Comments:**
- Don't delete unless spam/hate speech
- Respond with curiosity, not defensiveness
- Take conversations to DM if needed
- Remember: you represent the show

### Encouraging User-Generated Content:

**Ask:**
- "Have you visited the Hoh? Share your photos!"
- "What did the forest teach you?"
- "Tag someone who needs this today"

**Repost:**
- Share listener photos (with credit)
- Feature listener stories in Stories
- Create highlight reel of responses

**Incentivize:**
- Contest: "Best Hoh photo wins [prize]"
- Feature: "Listener of the week"
- Community: Create hashtag for listeners

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues:

**Inaccurate Information:**
- Response: "Thank you for catching that. I'll update the materials."
- Prevention: Fact-check thoroughly before publishing

**Cultural Insensitivity:**
- Response: Listen, apologize, learn, do better
- Prevention: Research Indigenous history, consult sources

**Environmental Impact Concerns:**
- Response: Acknowledge, share Leave No Trace principles
- Prevention: Emphasize responsible visitation in content

**Technical Issues:**
- Response: "Working on it! Thanks for patience."
- Prevention: Test all links before launch
