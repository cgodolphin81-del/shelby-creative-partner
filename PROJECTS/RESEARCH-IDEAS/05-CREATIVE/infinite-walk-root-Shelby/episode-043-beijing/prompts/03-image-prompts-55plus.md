## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** — Standard video/YouTube, cinematic landscapes
- **3:2** — Traditional photography, versatile
- **4:5** — Instagram portrait, vertical emphasis
- **21:9** — Ultra-widescreen cinematic

### Style Parameters
- **--v 6** — Midjourney version 6 for photorealism
- **--style raw** — More literal interpretation, less artistic license
- **--ar** — Aspect ratio as specified

### Best Practices
1. Run prompts in batches by category for consistency
2. Adjust lighting descriptors based on desired mood
3. Add/remove human figures based on needs
4. For Shelby prompts, ensure consistent robot design
5. Test variations of key scenes (different times of day, weather)
