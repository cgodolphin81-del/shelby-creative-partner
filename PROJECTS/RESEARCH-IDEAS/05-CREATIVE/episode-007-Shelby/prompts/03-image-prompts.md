## PROMPT ENGINEERING TIPS

### For Midjourney:
- Add `--v 6` for latest version
- Use `--style raw` for photorealistic results
- Adjust `--ar` for aspect ratio
- Add `--q 2` for higher quality (uses more GPU)
- Use `--s 250` for moderate stylization

### For DALL-E 3:
- Be descriptive and specific
- Include style references (National Geographic, etc.)
- Mention lighting conditions
- Specify mood and atmosphere

### For Stable Diffusion:
- Use negative prompts: "blurry, low quality, distorted"
- Add model-specific keywords
- Adjust CFG scale (7-9 recommended)
- Use appropriate checkpoints for landscapes
