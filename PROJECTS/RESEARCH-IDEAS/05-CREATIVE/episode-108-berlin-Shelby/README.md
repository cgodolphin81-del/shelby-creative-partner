## 🎯 KEY THEMES

### Core Metaphor
**The Phoenix** - Rising from ashes, not despite the fire but because of it.

### Three Acts of Berlin's Story
1. **Destruction** (1945 - WWII bombing, 60% of city destroyed)
2. **Division** (1961-1989 - The Wall, 28 years of separation)
3. **Rebirth** (1989-present - Reunification, cultural renaissance)

### Supporting Themes
- Memory without bitterness
- Forgiveness as choice, not obligation
- Honesty about the past as redemption
- Art as transformation (Wall → East Side Gallery)
- Music as catharsis (techno as rebirth sound)
- Scars as testimony, not shame
- Germany's complicated soul
