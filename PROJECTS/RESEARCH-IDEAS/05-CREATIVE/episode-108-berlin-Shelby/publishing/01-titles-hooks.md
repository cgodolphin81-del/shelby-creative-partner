## A/B TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS

### Test These Variables:
1. **Emotional vs. Factual Hooks**
   - Emotional: "I cried at the Wall"
   - Factual: "28 years of division, 1 night of freedom"

2. **Mystery vs. Clarity**
   - Mystery: "This city's secret"
   - Clarity: "Berlin's complete story"

3. **Short vs. Long Titles**
   - Short: "The Phoenix City"
   - Long: "The Phoenix City: Berlin's Story of Destruction and Rebirth"

4. **Image Choices**
   - Brandenburg Gate (recognizable)
   - East Side Gallery (colorful)
   - Holocaust Memorial (somber)
   - Techno club (unexpected)

### Metrics to Track:
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Completion rate (how many finish the episode)
- Shares per platform
- Comments/engagement
- Save rate (Instagram, TikTok)
