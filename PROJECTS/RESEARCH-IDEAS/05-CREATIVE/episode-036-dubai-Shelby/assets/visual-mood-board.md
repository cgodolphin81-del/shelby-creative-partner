## IMAGERY STYLES

### Photography Style

**Modern Architecture:**
- Clean lines, geometric compositions
- Blue hour and golden hour lighting
- Wide-angle perspectives
- Minimalist framing
- High contrast between steel and sky

**Desert Landscapes:**
- Warm, golden tones
- Emphasis on textures and patterns
- Silhouettes at sunrise/sunset
- Vast, open compositions
- Sense of scale and isolation

**Cultural/Heritage:**
- Warm, intimate lighting
- Close-up details (textures, crafts)
- Candid moments in souks
- Rich, saturated colors
- Human-centered framing

**Luxury/Lifestyle:**
- Bright, airy lighting
- Clean, uncluttered compositions
- Aspirational but authentic
- Focus on experiences, not just objects
- Warm color grading

---

### Image Treatment

**Color Grading:**
- Warm highlights (golden hour feel)
- Cool shadows (steel and sky)
- Slightly increased saturation
- Moderate contrast
- Subtle vignette for focus

**Filters/Presets:**
- Lightroom: "Golden Hour" or "Modern Architectural"
- VSCO: A6, HB2, or KU8
- Custom: Warm tones +3, Cool shadows -2, Clarity +5

**Do's:**
✅ Natural skin tones
✅ Preserve architectural details
✅ Maintain sky gradient integrity
✅ Keep gold/metallic elements rich
✅ Balance warm and cool tones

**Don'ts:**
❌ Over-saturated skies
❌ Crushed blacks
❌ Unrealistic HDR effects
❌ Heavy filters that obscure detail
❌ Inconsistent color temperature

## ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- **Text on background:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- **Large text:** Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Check tools:** WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- Describe images clearly and concisely
- Include key visual elements
- Mention colors when relevant
- Example: "Burj Khalifa towering into orange and purple sunset sky, Dubai Fountain mid-show with blue and gold water arcs"

### Readable Fonts
- Minimum 14px for body text
- Avoid light font weights on busy backgrounds
- Use text shadows/outlines for legibility
