# Marketing Plan - WorldForge AI

**30-60-90 Day Go-to-Market Strategy**

---

## Product Positioning

**What:** AI-powered world-building platform  
**Who:** Writers, game developers, worldbuilders, creatives  
**Why:** Create rich, consistent fictional worlds faster

---

## 30-Day Plan (Pre-Launch)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- [ ] Finalize product positioning
- [ ] Create landing page
- [ ] Set up email capture
- [ ] Prepare social media profiles

### Week 3-4: Beta Program
- [ ] Recruit 20-50 beta testers
- [ ] Collect feedback
- [ ] Fix critical issues
- [ ] Gather testimonials

### Goals for 30 Days:
- Landing page live
- 100+ email subscribers
- Beta feedback incorporated

---

## 60-Day Plan (Launch)

### Week 5-6: Soft Launch
- [ ] Launch to email list
- [ ] Post in writing/game dev communities
- [ ] Offer early-bird pricing
- [ ] Collect case studies

### Week 7-8: Content Marketing
- [ ] Publish world-building guides
- [ ] Create tutorial videos
- [ ] Share user worlds (with permission)
- [ ] Guest post on relevant blogs

### Goals for 60 Days:
- 200+ users
- 10+ paying customers
- 5+ testimonials/case studies

---

## 90-Day Plan (Scale)

### Week 9-10: Paid Acquisition
- [ ] Test Facebook/Reddit ads

- [ ] Run launch promotions
- [ ] Optimize conversion funnel

### Week 11-12: Product Expansion
- [ ] Add premium features

## Target Audiences

1. **Fantasy/Sci-Fi Writers** - World-building for novels
2. **TTRPG Players** - D&D, Pathfinder campaign worlds
3. **Game Developers** - Indie game world design
4. **Educators** - Creative writing teachers

---

## Pricing Strategy

- **Free Tier:** Basic world-building (limited)
- **Pro:** $15-29/month (full features)
- **Team:** $49-99/month (collaboration)
