## USAGE NOTES

### Aspect Ratios
- `--ar 16:9` : Landscape, cinematic, YouTube thumbnails
- `--ar 3:2` : Standard photography, blog posts
- `--ar 1:1` : Square, Instagram posts, profile images
- `--ar 21:9` : Ultra-wide, cinematic banners

### Best Practices
1. Generate multiple variations of each prompt
2. Mix and match elements (e.g., flamingos + mirror effect)
3. Adjust seasons based on desired effect (wet vs dry)
4. Use "Shelby" prompts for branded episode content
5. Combine atmospheric styles for unique artistic interpretations

### Color Palette Keywords
- Salt whites: `pure white`, `crystalline white`, `bright white`
- Sky blues: `azure`, `cerulean`, `deep blue`, `twilight blue`
- Flamingo pinks: `coral pink`, `salmon`, `rose`, `magenta`
- Sunset golds: `golden hour`, `amber`, `orange glow`, `warm light`
