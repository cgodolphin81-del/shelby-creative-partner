## 📅 Overview

### Campaign Duration
**Total:** 21 days (7 pre + 1 launch + 13 post)

### Primary Goals
1. **Maximize Episode 111 listens** (target: 2x average episode performance)
2. **Grow subscriber base** (target: +15% new subscribers during campaign)
3. **Build anticipation for Episode 112** (Ha Long Bay tease)
4. **Establish Vietnam/Mekong Delta as signature content**

### Key Platforms
- **Primary:** Podcast platforms (Apple, Spotify, Google)
- **Secondary:** Instagram, YouTube, TikTok/Reels
- **Tertiary:** Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Facebook, Pinterest
- **Owned:** Email list, website

### Content Pillars
1. **Visual Beauty** — Floating markets, rice paddies, sampan silhouettes
2. **Cultural Depth** — Vietnam's layers, food history, war memory
3. **Philosophical Themes** — Rivers as memory, flow vs. permanence, resilience
4. **Practical Value** — Travel tips, destination inspiration

## ⚠️ Contingency Plans

### If Launch Day Issues Occur

| Issue | Response |
|-------|----------|
| **Podcast platform delay** | Post "Coming in [X] hours" on social, email list |
| **Low initial engagement** | Boost top-performing post ($20-50), engage more actively |
| **Technical issues (website down)** | Direct all traffic to podcast platforms, fix ASAP |
| **Negative feedback** | Acknowledge respectfully, don't argue, learn |
| **Low email open rate** | Send follow-up with different subject line (24 hrs later) |

### If Momentum Drops (Days +3 to +7)

**Tactics:**
1. Share unexpected listener testimonial
2. Post "behind the scenes" content (research process)
3. Go live on Instagram for Q&A
4. Collaborate with travel influencer for reshare
5. Run small giveaway (Vietnam travel guide PDF, etc.)
