### What to Test

### Testing Methodology

1. **Split audiences evenly** (50/50) for first 48 hours
2. **Track primary metric** (clicks, opens, engagement)
3. **Declare winner** after statistical significance (min. 1,000 impressions per variant)
4. **Apply winner** to remaining distribution
5. **Document learnings** for future episodes
