## Hook Variations

### Opening Hook (Audio/Video Teaser)

**Hook 1 - Sensory:**
"There are cities built for function. Cities built for glory. And then... there are cities built for *feeling.* Cities that bypass the intellect and speak directly to the senses. Marrakech is such a city."

**Hook 2 - Direct:**
"They call it the Red City. Al Hamra. And the name is not poetic exaggeration. It is literal truth. At sunset, the entire city catches fire. Not metaphorically. *Literally.*"

**Hook 3 - Mysterious:**
"You get lost in the Marrakech medina. This is not a bug. This is a feature. Six hundred hectares. Two thousand four hundred alleyways. GPS gives up. You navigate with intuition and the kindness of strangers."

**Hook 4 - Emotional:**
"Marrakech is not a city you visit. It's a city that *visits you*—lingering in memory long after departure, in the scent of cumin, the glow of red stone at sunset, the echo of the muezzin's call."

**Hook 5 - Contrast:**
"From the outside: blank walls. A single door. No windows. A fortress. From the inside: a courtyard. A fountain. Orange trees. Light pouring from above. This is the riad. This is Marrakech."
