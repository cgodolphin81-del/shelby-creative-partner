## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Negative feedback about cultural representation
**Response:** Acknowledge, listen, clarify intent, commit to doing better

**Issue:** Technical issues (episode not live, broken links)
**Response:** Quick acknowledgment, fix ASAP, update all platforms

**Issue:** Low engagement
**Response:** Boost organic efforts, engage more actively, consider paid promotion

**Issue:** Controversy about Morocco travel (political concerns)
**Response:** Acknowledge complexity, focus on cultural appreciation, provide balanced perspective
