## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headers, Titles)
**Font:** Playfair Display (serif)
**Weights:** 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)
**Usage:** Episode titles, quote cards, headers

**Why:** Elegant, timeless, evokes history and gravitas

**Example:**
```
THE NEW JERUSALEM
Playfair Display Bold, 48px
Color: #FFFFFF or #2C3E50
```

---

### Secondary Font (Body, Captions)
**Font:** Lato or Montserrat (sans-serif)
**Weights:** 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)
**Usage:** Body text, social captions, descriptions

**Why:** Clean, readable, modern contrast to historic subject

**Example:**
```
Episode 080: The New Jerusalem
Lato Regular, 16px
Color: #2C3E50 or #FFFFFF
```

---

### Accent Font (Quotes, Poetry)
**Font:** Cormorant Garamond (serif, italic)
**Weights:** 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold)
**Usage:** Poetic excerpts, pull quotes

**Why:** Elegant, literary, emotional resonance

**Example:**
```
"The stone was carved by angels, they say."
Cormorant Garamond Italic, 24px
Color: #D4AF37 or #C1440E
```

## VISUAL STYLES

### Photography Treatment

#### Church Exteriors
- **Warmth:** +15 (enhance red stone)
- **Contrast:** +10 (dramatic shadows)
- **Clarity:** +20 (stone texture)
- **Vignette:** Slight (focus on church)
- **Saturation:** +5 (bring out red tones)

#### Church Interiors
- **Exposure:** -10 (preserve candlelight mood)
- **Shadows:** +20 (reveal detail)
- **Highlights:** -30 (protect candle flames)
- **Temperature:** +10 (warm candlelight)
- **Grain:** +5 (film-like texture)

#### Pilgrims & Priests
- **Skin Tones:** Natural (no heavy editing)
- **White Balance:** Accurate (honor white robes)
- **Contrast:** Moderate (preserve detail in white)
- **Vignette:** Minimal (keep focus on faces)

#### Landscapes
- **Sky:** Enhance blues (Highland Sky)
- **Earth:** Warm reds and browns
- **Clarity:** +15 (texture in highlands)
- **Dehaze:** +10 (clear highland air)

## DO's AND DON'Ts

### DO ✅
- Use warm tones to honor the red stone
- Preserve the dignity of pilgrims and priests in photos
- Keep text readable on all backgrounds (use overlays)
- Maintain consistency across platforms
- Use gold accents sparingly (for sacred elements)
- Honor the spiritual nature of the subject
- Test thumbnails at small sizes (mobile)

### DON'T ❌
- Over-saturate (keep it natural)
- Use cool blues as primary colors (wrong mood)
- Place text over busy areas of images
- Use more than 2-3 fonts per design
- Make it look like a travel ad (this is storytelling)
- Exploit religious imagery disrespectfully
- Ignore accessibility (contrast ratios)

---

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Text on backgrounds:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- **Large text (18px+):** Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Primary combinations:**
  - White (#FFFFFF) on Lalibela Red (#C1440E): ✅ 5.2:1
  - White on Deep Abyssinian (#2C3E50): ✅ 12.6:1
  - Ancient Gold (#D4AF37) on Deep Abyssinian: ✅ 8.1:1

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe the image content
- Include context (church name, location)
- Mention emotional tone if relevant
- Example: "Aerial view of Biete Ghiorgis, the cross-shaped rock-hewn church in Lalibela, Ethiopia, surrounded by red volcanic stone"

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC ADAPTATIONS

### Instagram
- **Format:** 4:5 (feed), 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
- **Colors:** Vibrant (scroll-stopping)
- **Text:** Minimal (caption carries detail)

### Facebook
- **Format:** 1:1 or 1.91:1
- **Colors:** Slightly muted (older demographic)
- **Text:** Can be more detailed

### LinkedIn
- **Format:** 1:1 or 1.91:1
- **Colors:** Professional (more blues, less red)
- **Text:** Thoughtful, longer-form

### Pinterest
- **Format:** 2:3 (vertical)
- **Colors:** Warm, inviting
- **Text:** Clear, benefit-driven

### YouTube
- **Format:** 16:9 (thumbnail)
- **Colors:** High contrast (small preview)
- **Text:** Large, readable at mobile size

### TikTok
- **Format:** 9:16 (vertical video)
- **Colors:** Bold, eye-catching
- **Text:** Large, quick to read
