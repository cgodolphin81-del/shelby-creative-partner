## 🎯 KEY FACTS (For Quick Reference)

### Lake Natron Essentials

- **Location:** Northern Tanzania, Great Rift Valley
- **pH Level:** 10.5-12 (comparable to ammonia)
- **Temperature:** Up to 60°C (140°F)
- **Flamingo Population:** 2.5 million lesser flamingos
- **Global Significance:** 75% of world's lesser flamingos breed here
- **Color Source:** Cyanobacteria (red pigments)
- **Best Time to Visit:** June-October (dry season)
- **Distance from Arusha:** 214 km (5-6 hours)

### Episode Essentials

- **Title:** "Pink on Alkaline"
- **Word Count:** ~2,100 words
- **Audio Duration:** ~15 minutes
- **Tone:** Contemplative, reverent, urgent (conservation)
- **Key Themes:** Resilience, adaptation, fragility, belonging
- **Conservation Angle:** Protect flamingo breeding ground

- Follow pacing/emotion cues in script
