## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Episode is Delayed
- **Communicate Early**: Tell audience as soon as possible
- **Explain Why**: Transparency builds trust
- **New Date**: Commit to specific new date
- **Bonus Content**: Share BTS or teaser to maintain interest

### If Technical Issues
- **Have Backup**: Downloaded episode ready on multiple devices
- **Quick Communication**: "We're experiencing technical difficulties"
- **Timeline**: "Back up within X hours"
- **Make Good**: Bonus content or early access to next episode

### If Negative Feedback
- **Respond Gracefully**: Thank them for feedback
- **Don't Argue**: Not worth the energy on milestone episode
- **Learn**: Take constructive criticism seriously
- **Move Forward**: Focus on positive responses
