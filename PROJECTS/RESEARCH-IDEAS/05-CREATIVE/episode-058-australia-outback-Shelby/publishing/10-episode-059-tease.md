## ⚠️ TEASE DON'TS

### Don't:
- ❌ Reveal too much too soon (kill the mystery)
- ❌ Make promises you can't keep (overhype)
- ❌ Ignore wrong guesses (engage with all)
- ❌ Tease for too long (1 week max)
- ❌ Forget to follow through (deliver on anticipation)
- ❌ Be vague without purpose (mystery should feel intentional)
- ❌ Spoil major surprises (keep some reveals for the episode)

### Do:
- ✅ Build genuine curiosity
- ✅ Engage with all guesses and comments
- ✅ Make the audience feel part of the journey
- ✅ Deliver on the promise (Episode 059 should be worth the wait)
- ✅ Connect Episode 058 and 059 thematically
- ✅ Have fun with it (enthusiasm is contagious)
