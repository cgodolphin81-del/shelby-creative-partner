## 📐 TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Headlines & Titles

**Font Family:** Montserrat (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights:** 700 (Bold), 800 (Extra Bold)
**Usage:** Episode titles, section headers, key quotes

**Why:** Modern, clean, highly readable, works well with AI-generated imagery

**Alternatives:**
- Oswald (bold, condensed)
- Playfair Display (elegant, editorial)
- Bebas Neue (tall, impactful)

---

### Secondary Font: Body Text

**Font Family:** Source Sans Pro (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights:** 400 (Regular), 600 (Semi-Bold)
**Usage:** Body copy, captions, descriptions

**Why:** Clean, readable at all sizes, pairs well with Montserrat

**Alternatives:**
- Open Sans (friendly, versatile)
- Lato (professional, warm)
- Roboto (modern, neutral)

---

### Accent Font: Quotes & Special Text

**Font Family:** Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights:** 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold)
**Usage:** Pull quotes, poetic text, special moments

**Why:** Elegant, literary, adds sophistication to poetic content

**Alternatives:**
- Playfair Display (classic, editorial)
- Merriweather (readable serif)
- Lora (contemporary serif)

## 🖼️ IMAGE STYLES

### Photography Style

**Mood:** Cinematic, warm, contemplative
**Lighting:** Golden hour, dramatic shadows, high contrast
**Composition:** Rule of thirds, leading lines, sense of scale
**Color Grade:** Warm tones, enhanced reds/oranges, deep shadows

**Key Characteristics:**
- Wide landscapes showing vastness
- Small human (or Shelby) figures for scale
- Silhouettes at sunrise/sunset
- Textural details (rock, sand, salt)
- Aerial perspectives for pink lakes

---

### AI Image Generation Style

**Prompt Keywords:**
- `photorealistic` - for realistic imagery
- `National Geographic style` - documentary feel
- `cinematic lighting` - dramatic, professional
- `golden hour` - warm, magical light
- `8K resolution` - high detail
- `wide angle` - epic landscapes
- `aerial view` - drone perspectives
- `contemplative mood` - thoughtful, peaceful

**Avoid:**
- Over-saturated, unnatural colors
- Cartoonish or anime styles
- Cluttered compositions
- Harsh midday lighting
- Tourist-crowded scenes

## 🎬 ANIMATION/MOTION GUIDELINES

### Transitions
- **Fade:** Slow, contemplative (1-2 seconds)
- **Slide:** Gentle, from natural direction (wind, journey)
- **Zoom:** Slow push-in for emotional moments
- **Dissolve:** Cross-dissolve for time transitions

### Text Animation
- **Fade In:** Letters or words, slow (0.5-1 second)
- **Typewriter:** For quotes, poetic effect
- **Scale:** Gentle pulse for emphasis
- **Avoid:** Bouncy, energetic, corporate animations

### Color Transitions
- **Sunrise:** Dark to light, cool to warm
- **Sunset:** Warm to dark, orange to deep red
- **Time Lapse:** Show color changes on Uluru

### Music/Sync
- **Beat Matching:** Subtle, not distracting
- **Emotional Peaks:** Match visual reveals to music swells
- **Silence:** Use pauses effectively (like in audio script)

## 🌈 ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast
- **Text on Background:** Minimum 4.5:1 ratio (WCAG AA)
- **Large Text:** Minimum 3:1 ratio
- **Check:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker

### Alt Text
- **Describe:** What's in the image, not just "image of Uluru"
- **Context:** Include why it matters to the story
- **Example:** "Uluru monolith glowing deep red at sunset against purple sky, with small silhouettes of visitors at viewing area in foreground"

### Text Size
- **Minimum:** 16pt for body text on web
- **Social Media:** Ensure readable on mobile without zooming
- **Headlines:** Large enough to read in feed without clicking

### Motion Sensitivity
- **Avoid:** Flashing, rapid animations
- **Provide:** Option to reduce motion (if interactive)
- **Consider:** Some users may be sensitive to certain animations
