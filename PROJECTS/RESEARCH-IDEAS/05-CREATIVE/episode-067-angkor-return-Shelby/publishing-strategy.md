## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGIES

### Instagram
**Primary Content:** High-quality images, carousels, Reels
**Posting Frequency:** 1 feed post/day for first 5 days, then 3-4/week
**Stories:** Daily during launch week
**Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 2 hours
**Hashtags:** 10-15 per post (mix of popular and niche)
**Best Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM (test and adjust)

### TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
**Primary Content:** 15-60 second videos with strong hooks
**Posting Frequency:** 1-2/day during launch week, then 3-5/week
**Strategy:** Use trending audio when relevant, strong text overlays
**Engagement:** Reply to comments with video responses when possible
**Best Times:** 6-9 PM (varies by audience)

### Twitter/X
**Primary Content:** Thread on launch day, quotes and engagement thereafter
**Posting Frequency:** 3-5 tweets/day during launch week
**Strategy:** Pin launch thread, engage with travel/mindfulness community
**Engagement:** Reply to mentions, retweet listener shares
**Best Times:** 8-10 AM, 7-9 PM

### LinkedIn
**Primary Content:** Leadership/philosophy angle, professional reflection
**Posting Frequency:** 1-2 posts/week
**Strategy:** Long-form post with professional insights
**Engagement:** Respond to comments, share in relevant groups
**Best Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM (weekday mornings best)

### Facebook
**Primary Content:** Episode announcement, community engagement
**Posting Frequency:** 3-4 posts/week
**Strategy:** Share to relevant groups (travel, podcasts, mindfulness)
**Engagement:** Respond to comments, ask questions
**Best Times:** 1-4 PM (weekends can be strong)

### YouTube
**Primary Content:** Full episode video (audio + visuals), Shorts
**Posting Frequency:** 1 full video/week, 3-5 Shorts/week
**Strategy:** SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, tags
**Engagement:** Reply to all comments, pin top comment
**Best Times:** 2-4 PM weekdays, 9-11 AM weekends

### Pinterest
**Primary Content:** Quote graphics, travel guide pins, episode artwork
**Posting Frequency:** 5-10 pins/week (can schedule in advance)
**Strategy:** Rich pins, keyword-optimized descriptions
**Engagement:** Join group boards, create themed boards
**Best Times:** 8-11 PM (Pinterest is evening platform)

### Email Newsletter
**Primary Content:** Episode announcement, exclusive content, travel guide
**Sending:** Launch day + follow-up to new subscribers
**Strategy:** Personal tone, clear CTA, mobile-optimized
**Engagement:** Track open rates, click rates, reply to responses
**Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 AM or 12-2 PM

## ENGAGEMENT GUIDELINES

### Response Time
- **First 24 hours:** Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- **Days 2-7:** Respond within 12 hours
- **Ongoing:** Respond within 24 hours

### Tone
- Warm, authentic, grateful
- Acknowledge every piece of feedback
- Don't argue with negative comments (thank and move on)
- Amplify listener stories and experiences

### User-Generated Content
- Repost listener stories/photos (with permission)
- Share reviews on social media
- Feature listener comments in newsletters
- Create community highlight posts

### Handling Negative Feedback
- Thank the person for feedback
- Don't be defensive
- If valid criticism, acknowledge and learn
- If trolling, don't engage (or brief, polite response)
- Never delete negative comments (unless spam/hate speech)

## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Episode Underperforms
- Don't panic—one episode doesn't define the show
- Analyze: Was it the topic, the promotion, the timing?
- Survey listeners: What resonated? What didn't?
- Adjust strategy for next episode
- Remember: Some episodes have long tails (discoverable over time)

### If Technical Issues
- Have backup download link ready
- Communicate transparently with audience
- Fix ASAP, then announce resolution
- Offer bonus content as apology if significant issue

### If Negative Feedback
- Assess if valid criticism or trolling
- Valid: Acknowledge, thank, learn
- Trolling: Don't feed, or brief polite response
- Never argue publicly
- Take legitimate lessons, discard noise
