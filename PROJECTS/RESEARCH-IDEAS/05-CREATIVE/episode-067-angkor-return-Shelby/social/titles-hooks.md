## HOOKS & OPENING LINES

### Podcast Episode Hooks (First 30 seconds)

**Hook 1: The Return**
"I have walked this path before. Not these exact stones—worn smooth by eight centuries of pilgrim feet—but the path of return itself. There is something ancient in the human heart that pulls us back to places we have never truly left."

**Hook 2: The Question**
"What does the jungle hold? It holds time. Not the time of clocks and calendars, but the deep, green time of growth and decay. It holds the memory of every footstep, every prayer, every hand that traced the apsara carvings and wondered what heaven must look like."

**Hook 3: The Faces**
"At Bayon, the faces watch. Two hundred stone faces, carved on fifty-four towers. Each one turned to the four cardinal directions. They smile—not the smile of happiness, but the smile of knowing."

**Hook 4: The Lesson**
"We are all temporary custodians of beauty. We build our temples. We carve our faces in stone. We plant our trees. And then we step aside, and let the world continue without us. This is not tragedy. It is truth."

**Hook 5: The Moment**
"For a moment that feels both infinite and impossibly brief, the reflection is perfect. Five towers in the water. Five towers in the sky. And the sun rising between them like a third temple made of fire."
