## RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Risks & Solutions

**Risk 1: Low Initial Engagement**
- **Solution:** Lean into organic engagement, respond to every comment, collaborate with micro-influencers
- **Backup:** Paid promotion boost ($100-200)

**Risk 2: Technical Issues (Audio/Video)**
- **Solution:** Quality check before launch, have backup files ready
- **Backup:** Quick re-edit and re-upload within 24 hours

**Risk 3: Negative Feedback**
- **Solution:** Respond professionally, learn from valid criticism, ignore trolls
- **Backup:** Community guidelines, moderation tools

**Risk 4: Conservation Message Ignored**
- **Solution:** Make CTA clear and easy, partner with established orgs
- **Backup:** Focus on awareness metrics, not just donations

**Risk 5: Platform Algorithm Changes**
- **Solution:** Diversify across platforms, build email list
- **Backup:** Double down on owned channels (website, email)
