## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headlines)
**Playfair Display** (Google Fonts - Free)
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800, 900
- Style: Elegant serif, high contrast
- Usage: Episode titles, headlines, quotes
- Why: Evokes classical French elegance, editorial feel

**Alternatives:**
- Bodoni (similar high-contrast serif)
- Didot (classic French fashion magazine feel)
- Cinzel (more ornate, Roman influence)

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Lato** (Google Fonts - Free)
- Weights: 100, 300, 400, 700, 900
- Style: Clean, modern sans-serif
- Usage: Body text, captions, social media
- Why: Highly readable, pairs well with Playfair

**Alternatives:**
- Montserrat (slightly more geometric)
- Open Sans (neutral, versatile)
- Source Sans Pro (clean, professional)

### Accent Font (Ornate)
**Cinzel Decorative** (Google Fonts - Free)
- Weights: 400, 700, 900
- Style: Ornate, classical Roman influence
- Usage: Special quotes, episode numbers, decorative elements
- Why: Evokes Versailles baroque aesthetic

**Usage Example:**
```
EPISODE XLIX
[Playfair Display Bold, 72pt, Sun King Gold]

The Sun King's City
[Playfair Display Italic, 48pt, Palace Gold]

Versailles & Paris, France
[Lato Regular, 24pt, Parisian Fog]
```

## VISUAL ELEMENTS

### Patterns & Textures

**Baroque Ornament**
- Style: Intricate scrollwork, acanthus leaves
- Usage: Borders, corner decorations, dividers
- Color: Sun King Gold on Midnight Paris

- Style: Subtle gradient overlays, light flares

## PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

### Color Treatment

**Preset Guidelines:**
- **Warmth:** +10-15 (golden hour feel)
- **Contrast:** +5-10 (dramatic but not harsh)
- **Highlights:** -10-20 (preserve detail in bright areas)
- **Shadows:** +10-15 (lift for dreamy feel)
- **Saturation:** +5-10 (enhance golds and blues)
- **Vibrance:** +10-15 (selective color pop)
- **Clarity:** +5-10 (architectural detail)
- **Vignette:** -5 to -10 (subtle, draws focus)

### Composition

**Versailles:**
- Symmetrical compositions (reflects formal gardens)
- Wide shots to capture scale
- Leading lines (hallways, garden paths)
- Golden hour lighting preferred
- Include human figures for scale

**Paris Streets:**
- Street photography aesthetic
- Candid moments at cafés
- Architectural details (doorways, balconies)
- Cobblestone textures
- Morning or late afternoon light

**Eiffel Tower:**
- Multiple angles (close-up ironwork, full tower)
- Include foreground interest (trees, people, Seine)
- Sunset and night sparkle shots essential
- Avoid cliché postcard angles when possible

**Montmartre:**
- Winding cobblestone streets
- Artist easels and paintings
- Sacré-Cœur from below and above
- Village atmosphere
- Soft, diffused light

### Subject Treatment

**Shelby in Scene:**
- Natural, candid poses
- Not overly posed or staged
- Engaging with environment (looking at art, walking, at café)
- Wardrobe: Elegant casual, neutral tones with one pop of color
- Lighting: Natural, golden hour preferred
- Emotion: Wonder, curiosity, contemplation

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- All text meets WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 minimum)
- Gold text on dark backgrounds only
- White text on dark overlays for readability
- Avoid gold on light backgrounds

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe images fully: "Hall of Mirrors at Palace of Versailles, golden afternoon light reflecting off 357 mirrors"
- Include emotion: "Shelby standing on steps of Sacré-Cœur, contemplative expression, Paris skyline in background"
- Be specific: "Close-up of fresh croissant on white plate, flaky golden layers visible"

### Considerations
- Don't rely on color alone to convey information
- Use patterns + color for charts/graphs
- Provide text alternatives for all visual content
