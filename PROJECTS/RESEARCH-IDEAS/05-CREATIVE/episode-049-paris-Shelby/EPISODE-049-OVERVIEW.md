## 🎯 Key Episode Themes

1. **Beauty Has a Cost** - Versailles built on worker sacrifice, art collected through questionable means
2. **Legacy & Memory** - What outlasts us, what we leave behind
3. **Contradiction** - Paris as both monument and living city, eyesore and miracle
4. **Light** - The quality of Parisian light, literal and metaphorical
5. **Small Beauties** - Croissants, cafés, books by the Seine
6. **Vision vs. Empathy** - Louis XIV's legacy, leadership lessons
