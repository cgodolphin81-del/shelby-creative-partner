## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Playfair Display (Serif)
**Usage:** Headlines, titles, poetic text
**Weights:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)
**Style:** Elegant, literary, timeless
**Why:** Evokes the poetic nature of the episode, feels established and trustworthy

```
Example Headline:
Playfair Display Bold, 48px
"The Ancient River"
Letter spacing: -0.5px
Line height: 1.2
Color: #FFFFFF or #1B4332
```

### Secondary Font: Lato (Sans-Serif)
**Usage:** Body text, captions, UI elements
**Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Bold (700)
**Style:** Clean, readable, modern
**Why:** Excellent readability, pairs well with Playfair, works across platforms

```
Example Body:
Lato Regular, 16px
"Episode 055 takes us to the heart of the Amazon..."
Letter spacing: 0px
Line height: 1.6
Color: #343A40 or #FFFFFF
```

**Usage:** Numbers, statistics, calls-to-action

**Why:** Makes statistics pop, strong CTAs, contemporary feel

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Mood:**
- Lush and vibrant (not desaturated)
- Natural lighting (golden hour preferred)
- Sense of scale (human figures for perspective)
- Respectful indigenous representation
- Wildlife in natural habitat (not staged)

**Composition:**
- Wide landscapes for context
- Close-ups for emotional connection
- Aerial shots for scale
- Symmetry (Meeting of Waters)
- Rule of thirds for wildlife

**Avoid:**
- Overly filtered/processed images
- Cliché "savage" indigenous portrayals
- Staged wildlife interactions
- Destruction imagery without hope/solution
- Poverty tourism aesthetics

### AI Image Generation Guidelines

**Quality Settings:**
- Midjourney v6 or equivalent
- Aspect ratio varies by platform (see prompts)
- Stylize: 200-300 (natural, not over-processed)
- Quality: 2 (highest)

**Consistent Elements:**
- Color grading toward greens and earth tones
- Natural lighting (avoid harsh midday sun)
- Atmospheric depth (mist, layers)
- Human scale references when appropriate

**Subjects to Generate:**
- Meeting of Waters (aerial)
- Jaguar in natural habitat
- Scarlet macaws in flight
- Canopy from below
- Indigenous community (respectful, with consent considerations)
- Shelby as explorer (various scenes)
- River dolphin surfacing
- Flooded forest
- Sunset over river

## ANIMATION & MOTION

### Motion Principles

**Pace:** Slow and deliberate (like the Amazon itself)
**Easing:** Ease-in-out (natural, organic)
**Duration:** 300-600ms for UI, 3-5s for backgrounds

### Animated Elements

**Leaf Fall:**
- Subtle leaf silhouettes falling
- Duration: 8-12s loop
- Opacity: 20-30%
- Use: Website hero, video intro

**Water Ripple:**
- Concentric circles expanding
- Duration: 2-3s per ripple
- Opacity: 10-15%
- Use: Section transitions, loading states

**Fade Transitions:**
- Slow crossfades between images
- Duration: 1-2s
- Use: Slideshows, carousels

**Text Reveal:**
- Letters fade in sequentially
- Duration: 100ms per letter
- Easing: Ease-out
- Use: Titles, quotes

### Video Intro (10 seconds)

```
0:00-0:02: Black screen, jungle sounds fade in
0:02-0:04: Mist/fog reveals jungle green gradient
0:04-0:06: "THE ANCIENT RIVER" fades in (letter by letter)
0:06-0:08: Jaguar eye close-up fades in and out
0:08-0:10: "EPISODE 055" + podcast logo
0:10: Fade to episode content

Music: Indigenous flute motif, subtle
Sound: River sounds, bird calls underneath
```

### Social Media Motion

**Instagram Story Animation:**
- Text slides in from bottom
- Images fade with slight scale (95% to 100%)
- Stickers pop with bounce effect

**TikTok/Reels:**
- Quick cuts (0.5-1s each)
- Text pops on beat
- Zoom transitions between scenes

**YouTube Intro:**
- 5-second max
- Podcast logo animation
- Episode title reveal
- Music sting

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast

**Minimum Ratios (WCAG AA):**
- Normal text: 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Large text (18px+): 3:1 contrast ratio
- UI elements: 3:1 contrast ratio

**Tested Combinations:**
- ✅ #FFFFFF on #1B4332 (12.5:1) - Pass
- ✅ #FFFFFF on #023E8A (8.2:1) - Pass
- ✅ #343A40 on #FFFFFF (12.5:1) - Pass
- ✅ #D90429 on #FFFFFF (4.8:1) - Pass
- ⚠️ #52B788 on #FFFFFF (2.1:1) - Fail (use on dark only)
- ✅ #52B788 on #1B4332 (5.8:1) - Pass

### Alt Text Guidelines

**For AI Images:**
- Describe subject, action, setting
- Include emotional tone when relevant
- Mention if AI-generated
- Example: "AI-generated image of a jaguar walking through Amazon rainforest understory, golden light filtering through canopy, sense of power and grace"

**For Infographics:**
- Describe data being shown
- Include key statistics in text
- Example: "Infographic showing 17% of Amazon rainforest already lost, with timeline to 20% tipping point"

### Font Sizes

**Minimum Sizes:**
- Body text: 16px (web), 14px (mobile)
- Headlines: 24px minimum
- Captions: 12px minimum (never smaller)

### Keyboard Navigation

- All interactive elements focusable
- Visible focus states (2px outline, #52B788)
- Logical tab order
- Skip-to-content link
