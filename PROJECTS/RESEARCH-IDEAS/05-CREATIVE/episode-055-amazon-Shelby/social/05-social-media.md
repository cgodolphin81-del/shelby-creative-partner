**Headline:** Responsible Tourism in the Amazon: A Guide for Travel Professionals

**Post:**
The Amazon is one of the world's most extraordinary destinations—and one of the most vulnerable.

For travel professionals, the question is: How do we share this wonder without contributing to its destruction?

Key principles from Episode 055:

✓ Choose operators with IBAMA certification
✓ Employ local guides (not imported)
✓ Keep groups small (under 12)
✓ Visit indigenous communities ethically (with consent, fair compensation)
✓ Educate travelers on environmental impact
✓ Support conservation organizations

Responsible tourism is not a niche. It's the only viable future.

The Amazon does not need more visitors. It needs better ones.
