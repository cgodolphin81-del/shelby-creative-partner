## ⚠️ RISK MITIGATION

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Low initial downloads
- **Solution:** Activate email list, personal outreach to super-fans, cross-promotion

**Issue:** Negative feedback (historical accuracy, cultural sensitivity)
- **Solution:** Acknowledge, correct if needed, learn publicly

**Issue:** Technical issues (audio quality, platform errors)
- **Solution:** Have backup audio file, test upload 48 hours early, have re-upload plan

**Issue:** Low engagement on social
- **Solution:** Increase personal engagement (reply to all comments), adjust posting times, try different content formats

**Issue:** Algorithm changes (platform reach drops)
- **Solution:** Diversify platforms, build email list (owned audience), focus on community over reach

### Crisis Communication

**If historical/cultural inaccuracies are flagged:**
1. Acknowledge quickly (within 24 hours)
2. Thank the person for correction
3. Correct in show notes if needed
4. Learn and improve for future episodes
5. Be transparent about changes

**If technical issues prevent launch:**
1. Communicate delay immediately (email, social)
2. Provide new timeline
3. Offer bonus content as apology (behind-the-scenes, extended cuts)
4. Follow through on new timeline
