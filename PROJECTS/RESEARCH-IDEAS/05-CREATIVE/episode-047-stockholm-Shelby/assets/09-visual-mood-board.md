## 🎨 PRIMARY COLOR PALETTE

### Gamla Stan Colors (Warm, Historic)
These colors represent the colorful buildings of Stockholm's Old Town—the heart of the city.

| Color | Hex | RGB | Use Case |
|-------|-----|-----|----------|
| **Ochre Yellow** | `#E8B947` | 232, 185, 71 | Primary accent, buttons, highlights |
| **Terracotta Red** | `#C15B3F` | 193, 91, 63 | Secondary accent, CTAs, emphasis |
| **Burnt Orange** | `#D97A3F` | 217, 122, 63 | Tertiary accent, warm backgrounds |
| **Deep Green** | `#2D4A3E` | 45, 74, 62 | Shutters, contrast, text (light backgrounds) |
| **Warm White** | `#F5F1E8` | 245, 241, 232 | Backgrounds, text (dark backgrounds) |

**Usage Notes:**
- Gamla Stan colors work best for warm, inviting content
- Pair ochre with deep green for classic Swedish contrast
- Use terracotta for urgency (CTAs, sale items)
- These colors evoke history, tradition, warmth

## 📐 TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font: Modern Sans-Serif
**Font:** Inter / Helvetica Neue / Arial

**Usage:**
- **Headlines:** Bold, 32-48px (web), 24-36pt (print)
- **Subheadings:** Semi-bold, 24-32px (web), 18-24pt (print)
- **Body:** Regular, 16-18px (web), 11-12pt (print)
- **Captions:** Regular, 14px (web), 10pt (print)

**Why:** Clean, readable, Scandinavian aesthetic, universally available

### Secondary Font: Humanist Sans
**Font:** Proxima Nova / Montserrat / Open Sans

**Usage:**
- **Pull Quotes:** Bold, italic, 20-24px
- **Call-to-Actions:** Bold, all caps, 16-18px
- **Buttons:** Semi-bold, 16px, letter-spacing +1px

**Why:** Friendly, approachable, modern, pairs well with primary

### Accent Font: Serif (Optional)
**Font:** Playfair Display / Merriweather / Georgia

**Usage:**
- **Episode Titles:** Italic, 36-48px
- **Poetic Quotes:** Italic, 20-24px
- **Special Moments:** When you want to evoke history, tradition

**Why:** Adds elegance, contrasts with sans-serif, evokes Gamla Stan's history

Water and stone. Light and darkness. Innovation and tradition.

## 🖼️ IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Guidelines

**1. Light Quality**
- **Golden Hour:** Soft, warm, directional (Gamla Stan, archipelago)
- **Blue Hour:** Cool, moody, atmospheric (winter, evening)
- **Overcast:** Even, diffuse, flattering (portraits, details)
- **Avoid:** Harsh midday sun, heavy shadows, over-saturation

**2. Composition**
- **Rule of Thirds:** Classic, balanced
- **Leading Lines:** Cobblestone streets, archipelago horizons
- **Framing:** Doorways, windows, arches (Gamla Stan alleys)
- **Negative Space:** Scandinavian minimalism, breathing room
- **Scale:** Include people for Vasa ship, archipelago vastness

**3. Subject Matter**
- **Architecture:** Colorful Gamla Stan buildings, Royal Palace, City Hall
- **Water:** Archipelago, harbor, reflections, ferry wakes
- **Details:** Cobblestones, candle flames, fika pastries, Nobel medals
- **People:** Candid moments, fika conversations, archipelago explorers
- **Nature:** Pine trees, birch bark, seasonal changes (snow, blossoms)

**4. Color Grading**
- **Warm:** Slightly warm temperature for Gamla Stan, fika
- **Cool:** Cooler temperature for archipelago, winter
- **Contrast:** Moderate (not too flat, not too punchy)
- **Saturation:** Natural, slightly muted (Scandinavian aesthetic)
- **Shadows:** Lifted slightly (avoid crushing blacks)

## 🎯 ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum Ratio:** 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- **Tools:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker, Stark plugin
- **Avoid:** Red/green combinations (color blindness)

### Alt Text
- **Description:** Describe what's in the image, not just "Stockholm"
- **Example:** "Colorful 17th-century buildings in Gamla Stan Stockholm at golden hour, cobblestone street, warm evening light"
- **Length:** 125 characters max (screen reader friendly)

### Captions
- **Video:** Always include closed captions
- **Accuracy:** Review auto-captions for errors
- **Formatting:** Indicate speakers, sound effects ([music], [waves])

### Screen Reader Friendly
- **Hashtags:** Use CamelCase (#TheInfiniteWalk not #theinfinitewalk)
- **Emoji:** Use sparingly, don't rely on emoji for meaning
- **Links:** Descriptive link text ("Listen to Episode 047" not "Click here")
