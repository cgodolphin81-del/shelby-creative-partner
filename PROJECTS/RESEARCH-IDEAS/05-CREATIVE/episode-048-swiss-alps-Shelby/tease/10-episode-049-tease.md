## Actual Episode 049 Destination

**RECOMMENDATION: Marrakech, Morocco**

**Why Marrakech:**
- Perfect contrast to Swiss Alps (precision vs. chaos, snow vs. desert, quiet vs. vibrant)
- Rich sensory experience (spices, colors, sounds)
- Strong visual identity (orange, reds, golds)
- Accessible destination (popular, well-documented)
- Great content potential (markets, gardens, architecture, food)

**Episode 049 Title Ideas:**
- "The Marrakech Dream"
- "Orange City: A Marrakech Journey"
- "Episode 049: Marrakech - The Red City"
- "From Alps to Souks: The Marrakech Episode"

**Key Themes:**
- Sensory overload (in the best way)
- Ancient meets modern
- Color and chaos as beauty
- Hospitality and warmth
- Desert gateway

**If not Marrakech, alternatives:**
- Dubai, UAE (modern desert city)
- Petra, Jordan (ancient wonder)
- Cairo, Egypt (pyramids, ancient civilization)
- Tunis, Tunisia (Carthage, Sahara gateway)
