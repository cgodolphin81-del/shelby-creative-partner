## Risk Management

### Potential Issues & Mitigation

**Weather/Seasonal Concerns:**
- Issue: Episode features summer, but launching in winter
- Mitigation: Emphasize year-round appeal, winter activities

**Cost Sensitivity:**
- Issue: Switzerland is expensive, may deter some travelers
- Mitigation: Include budget tips, emphasize Swiss Pass value

**Competition:**
- Issue: Many Swiss Alps travel guides exist
- Mitigation: Differentiate with poetic/storytelling approach, audio experience

**Algorithm Changes:**
- Issue: Platform algorithm shifts reduce reach
- Mitigation: Diversify across platforms, build email list

**Negative Feedback:**
- Issue: Criticism of content or recommendations
- Mitigation: Respond graciously, update content if valid
