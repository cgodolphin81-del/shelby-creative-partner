# Marketing Plan - Combination Innovations

**30-60-90 Day Strategy**  
**Status:** Research Reference Material

---

## Product Positioning

**What:** Innovation research catalog and combination product ideas  
**Who:** Entrepreneurs, product developers, innovation teams  
**Why:** Save months of research, get actionable innovation concepts

---

## 30-Day Plan (Foundation)

### Week 1-2: Package the Research
- [ ] Create polished PDF version of catalog
- [ ] Design cover and section dividers
- [ ] Write executive summary
- [ ] Create sample chapter (free lead magnet)

### Week 3-4: Set Up Distribution
- [ ] Choose platform (Gumroad, Etsy, or direct)
- [ ] Set up product listing
- [ ] Create sales page copy
- [ ] Price positioning ($27-47 for full catalog)

### Goals for 30 Days:
- Product packaged and ready to sell
- Distribution channel set up
- First 10 beta readers identified

---

## 60-Day Plan (Launch)

### Week 5-6: Soft Launch
- [ ] Share with email list (if any)
- [ ] Post in entrepreneur communities
- [ ] Offer early-bird pricing (50% off)
- [ ] Collect testimonials from beta readers

### Week 7-8: Content Marketing
- [ ] Write 3 blog posts about combination innovation
- [ ] Create 5 social media posts with examples
- [ ] Record 1-2 short videos explaining concepts
- [ ] Guest post on innovation/entrepreneur blogs

### Goals for 60 Days:
- 20-50 sales
- 5+ testimonials collected
- Content marketing engine started

---

## 90-Day Plan (Scale)

### Week 9-10: Expand Reach
- [ ] Run targeted ads (Facebook/LinkedIn)
- [ ] Partner with innovation podcasts
- [ ] Create affiliate program (30% commission)
- [ ] Pitch to business publications

### Week 11-12: Product Extensions
- [ ] Consider premium tier (consulting add-on)
- [ ] Create workshop/course version
- [ ] Develop industry-specific versions
- [ ] Plan Version 2.0 with new examples

### Goals for 90 Days:
- 100+ total sales
- Multiple revenue streams identified
- Clear path to scale
