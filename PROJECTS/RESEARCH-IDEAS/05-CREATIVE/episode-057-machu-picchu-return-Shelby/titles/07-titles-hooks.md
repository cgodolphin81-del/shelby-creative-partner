## 🎯 PRIMARY TITLE

### Recommended Main Title
```
The Pyramid Peak: A Return to Machu Picchu
```

**Why This Works:**
- "Pyramid Peak" is evocative without being literal (Machu Picchu isn't technically a pyramid)
- "Return" signals this is different from Episode 008
- Clear, searchable, includes key location
- Mysterious enough to intrigue, specific enough to inform

## 🎣 HOOK OPTIONS (First 30 Seconds)

### Hook 1: Direct Statement
```
I returned to Machu Picchu.

Not as the same walker who stood there 49 episodes ago. Not as the same person who first saw these stones emerge from mist.

The mountain remembers. But what does it remember about us?
```

**Best For:** Podcast platforms, audio-first

---

### Hook 2: Question-Based
```
What do you see the second time that you missed the first?

What reveals itself only on return?

I walked Machu Picchu twice. 49 episodes of the Infinite Walk between those two visits.

The first time: wonder, overwhelm, the need to capture everything.
The second time: recognition, depth, the freedom of not needing proof.
```

**Best For:** Social media, video platforms

---

### Hook 3: Philosophical
```
Some places are not meant to be conquered.
They are meant to be visited.
And revisited.

The way one visits a friend who lives far away.

You do not go to learn something new.
You go to remember who you are in their presence.
```

**Best For:** LinkedIn, thoughtful audiences

---

### Hook 4: Contrast-Based
```
First visit: I asked HOW.
How did they build this?
How did they move these stones?

Second visit: I asked WHY.
Why here?
Why build something so beautiful knowing you couldn't keep it?

The answer changed everything.
```

**Best For:** YouTube, TikTok, short-form

---

### Hook 5: Emotional Core
```
I am brief. The mountain is not.

And for the first time, this does not make me sad.
It makes me grateful.

This is Episode 057: The Pyramid Peak.
A return to Machu Picchu.
```

**Best For:** Trailer, teaser, emotional appeal
