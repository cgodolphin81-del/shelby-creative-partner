## 🎙️ AUDIO PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice/TTS Settings
- **Voice:** Warm, reflective, mature (Shelby's established voice)
- **Speed:** 0.85-0.9x normal (contemplative pacing)
- **Pitch:** Slightly lower than conversational
- **Emphasis:** Marked italics should be stressed

### Sound Design
- **Opening:** Bus engine, birds, wind (5 seconds before voice)
- **Transitions:** Light Andean flute between sections (3-6 seconds each)
- **Ambient:** Subtle mountain wind throughout (very low mix)
- **Closing:** Wind, bird call, fade (8 seconds)

### Music Notes
- Keep music minimal and supportive
- Andean flute or pan pipes for cultural authenticity
- Avoid melodrama—let the words carry emotion
- Music should breathe with the pauses
