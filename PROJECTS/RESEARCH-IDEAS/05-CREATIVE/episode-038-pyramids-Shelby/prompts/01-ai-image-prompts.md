## Usage Notes

- **Aspect Ratios**: Most prompts work with 16:9 (landscape), 4:5 (portrait for social), or 1:1 (square)
- **Style Keywords**: Add "photorealistic, 8K, ultra-detailed" for photos or "digital painting, concept art" for illustrations
- **Mood Adjustments**: Modify time of day, weather, and season for variety
- **Character Consistency**: For Shelby images, maintain consistent appearance across prompts
- **Color Palette**: Reference the mood board—sand golds, limestone whites, Nile blues, sunset oranges
