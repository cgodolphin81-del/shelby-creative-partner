**Visual elements:**

- Slight golden tint

- Gentle contrast

- Temple bell close-up

## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Titles)
**Font:** Playfair Display (serif, elegant)  
**Weights:** Regular, Bold  
**Use:** Episode titles, main headings  
**Color:** Gold (#D4AF37) or White  
**Treatment:** Slight letter-spacing, all caps for impact

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Font:** Lato or Montserrat (sans-serif, clean)  
**Weights:** Regular, Medium, Bold  
**Use:** Body text, captions, descriptions  
**Color:** Dark gray (#333333) or White (on dark backgrounds)  
**Treatment:** Clean, readable, modern

### Accent Font (Special Moments)
**Font:** Great Vibes or similar script  
**Weights:** Regular  
**Use:** Pull quotes, special moments, poetic text  
**Color:** Gold or Saffron  
**Treatment:** Use sparingly, for emotional impact

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Style
- **Authentic over staged:** Candid moments, real life
- **Golden hour preferred:** Warm, soft light
- **High contrast for street scenes:** Neon, night, energy
- **Soft focus for temples:** Reverent, peaceful
- **Wide angles for landscapes:** Capture scale of city
- **Close-ups for details:** Hands, food, textures

### Composition Guidelines
- **Rule of thirds:** Standard, reliable
- **Leading lines:** Roads, rivers, train tracks
- **Framing:** Temple doorways, windows, arches
- **Layering:** Foreground (street), midground (river), background (skyline)
- **Negative space:** For text overlays, breathing room

### Avoid
- Overly touristy clichés (unless subverted)
- Poverty porn (respectful documentation only)
- Stereotypical "exotic" framing
- Over-saturated, unrealistic colors
- Cluttered compositions (unless chaos is the point)

## ACCESSIBILITY

### Color Contrast
- **Minimum ratio:** 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text
- **Check:** Use WebAIM Contrast Checker
- **Avoid:** Gold text on white (poor contrast)

### Alt Text
- **All images:** Descriptive alt text
- **Videos:** Captions/subtitles
- **Graphics:** Text descriptions

### Color Blindness
- **Avoid:** Red/green combinations as only differentiator
- **Test:** Use color blindness simulator
- **Ensure:** Information not conveyed by color alone
