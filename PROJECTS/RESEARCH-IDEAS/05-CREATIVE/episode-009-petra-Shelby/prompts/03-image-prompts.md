## 🎯 Usage Notes

### Best Practices
- **Aspect Ratios:** Adjust based on platform (16:9 for YouTube, 1:1 for Instagram feed, 9:16 for TikTok/Reels)
- **Style Consistency:** Use similar lighting and color grading across all images for cohesive episode aesthetic
- **Shelby's Appearance:** Keep consistent (shoulder-length hair, practical hiking clothes, neutral earth tones, no distinctive jewelry)
- **Lighting:** Golden hour (dawn/dusk) provides the most dramatic, warm tones that match Petra's rose-red stone

### Prompt Customization
- Replace "Shelby" with specific appearance details as needed
- Adjust time of day for different moods (dawn = hope/reveal, midday = harsh reality, dusk = reflection)
- Modify weather conditions for variety (clear = standard, overcast = moody, rare rain = unique)
- Add or remove people based on desired atmosphere (solitude vs. shared experience)

### Technical Notes
- For Midjourney: Add `--ar 16:9 --v 6` or similar parameters
- For DALL-E 3: Prompts work as-is, specify aspect ratio in settings
- For Stable Diffusion: May need negative prompts for unwanted elements
- Consider upscaling for print-quality images
