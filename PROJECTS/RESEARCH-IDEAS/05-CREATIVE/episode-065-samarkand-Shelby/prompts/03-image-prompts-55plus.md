## Usage Notes

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Landscape (16:9): Scenic views, Registan wide shots, desert landscapes
- Portrait (9:16): Social media stories, vertical video thumbnails
- Square (1:1): Instagram feed posts, profile images
- Cinematic (21:9): Video thumbnails, dramatic scenes

**Style Variations:**
- Photorealistic: For travel guide imagery
- Cinematic: For video thumbnails and dramatic scenes
- Artistic/Painterly: For social media content
- Documentary: For authentic cultural moments

**Color Grading:**
- Warm golden hour tones
- Emphasize lapis blues and turquoise
- Rich terracotta and sand colors
- Deep shadows for drama
- Vibrant but natural saturation
