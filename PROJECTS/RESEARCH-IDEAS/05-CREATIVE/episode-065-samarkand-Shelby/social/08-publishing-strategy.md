## CONTINGENCY PLANS

### If Launch Day Issues Occur:

**Technical Problems:**
- Have backup audio file ready
- Test all links before launch
- Prepare "coming soon" placeholder posts

**Low Engagement:**
- Increase personal outreach (DMs, emails)
- Boost best-performing post ($20-50)
- Engage more actively in comments/communities

**Negative Feedback:**
- Respond professionally and promptly
- Address legitimate concerns
- Don't engage with trolls

### If Story Goes Viral:

**Prepare For:**
- Increased website traffic (ensure hosting can handle)
- More comments/messages (set aside time to respond)
- Press inquiries (have media kit ready)
- New followers (welcome them, guide to best content)
