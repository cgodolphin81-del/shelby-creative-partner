## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Headline Font
**Font**: Playfair Display (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights**: Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600)
**Usage**: Episode titles, section headers, "60" numerals
**Why**: Elegant, milestone-appropriate, editorial feel, pairs well with serif body

**Alternative**: Cormorant Garamond (more ornate, slightly more sacred feel)

---

### Secondary/Subhead Font
**Font**: Montserrat (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights**: Medium (500), Semi-Bold (600)
**Usage**: Subtitles, location names, secondary information
**Why**: Clean, modern, readable, contrasts well with Playfair

**Alternative**: Lato (slightly softer, more approachable)

---

### Body Text Font
**Font**: Lato (Google Fonts - Free)
**Weights**: Regular (400), Light (300)
**Usage**: Long-form text, essays, descriptions
**Why**: Highly readable, warm, friendly, works at small sizes

**Alternative**: Source Sans Pro (slightly more professional)

---

### Accent/Script Font (Sparingly)
**Font**: Dancing Script or Great Vibes (Google Fonts - Free)
**Usage**: "Thank You", "Gratitude", special milestone text
**Why**: Handwritten feel, personal, emotional
**Caution**: Use VERY sparingly (max 1-2 words at a time)

---

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

#### Landscape Shots
**Style**: Wide, cinematic, golden hour
**Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines (rice terrace layers)
**Mood**: Serene, majestic, awe-inspiring
**Color Grade**: Warm tones, enhanced greens and golds, slight vignette

**Key Shots**:
- Tegalalang sunrise (emerald green + gold light)
- Uluwatu sunset (orange sky + ocean blue)
- Campuhan Ridge dawn (mist + soft light)

---

#### Temple/Architecture
**Style**: Symmetrical, reverent, detailed
**Composition**: Centered, straight lines, respect for sacred geometry
**Mood**: Sacred, ancient, peaceful
**Color Grade**: Warm stone tones, gold highlights, soft shadows

**Key Shots**:
- Split gates (candi bentar) - symmetrical
- Temple towers (meru) - vertical composition

## COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds
**Apply to**: All landscape photography, character placement
**Why**: Classic, balanced, professional
**Exception**: Symmetrical temple shots (center is intentional)

---

### Leading Lines
**Apply to**: Rice terrace shots, pathways, water flow
**Why**: Draws eye through image, creates depth
**Examples**: Terrace layers, temple steps, river paths

---

### Negative Space
**Apply to**: Quote graphics, title cards, social posts
**Why**: Lets text breathe, feels premium, mindful
**Amount**: 40-60% negative space for text overlays

---

### Layering/Depth
**Apply to**: All compositions
**Why**: Creates dimension, mirrors "layers of experience" theme
**How**: Foreground element + midground subject + background context

## ACCESSIBILITY CHECKLIST

### Color Contrast
- [ ] All text meets WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large)
- [ ] Temple Gold on Rice Green: Check contrast (may need adjustment)
- [ ] Sunset Orange on Ocean Blue: Verify readability
- [ ] Provide alt text for all images

### Color Blindness Considerations
- [ ] Don't rely solely on color to convey meaning
- [ ] Use patterns + color for charts/graphics
- [ ] Test with color blindness simulator (Coblis, Color Oracle)

### Text Readability
- [ ] Minimum font size: 16px for body, 24px for headings
- [ ] Sufficient line height (1.5x for body text)
- [ ] Avoid text over busy images (use overlays)
