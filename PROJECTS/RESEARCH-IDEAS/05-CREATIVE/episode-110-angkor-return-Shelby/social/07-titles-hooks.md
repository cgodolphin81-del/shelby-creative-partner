## Press/Media Pitch Angles

### For Travel Podcasts/Publications
**Angle:** "Beyond the Tourist Trail: A Meditative Return to Angkor Wat"
**Hook:** Not a travel guide, but a meditation on what happens when you return to a place with new eyes. Perfect for travelers seeking depth over destinations.

### For Mindfulness/Spirituality Outlets
**Angle:** "What Ancient Temples Teach About Impermanence"
**Hook:** 900-year-old stones and 500-year-old jungle offer unexpected wisdom on letting go, building anyway, and finding peace in change.

### For Philosophy/Intellectual Media
**Angle:** "The Paradox of Permanent Impermanence at Angkor"
**Hook:** How Cambodia's temples embody the Buddhist truth of anicca (impermanence) while standing as monuments to human endurance.

### For Personal Growth/Leadership
**Angle:** "Building Anyway: Cambodia's Lesson in Resilience"
**Hook:** A nation that endured genocide chose to carve beauty into stone. What leaders can learn from Angkor's builders about meaning over permanence.

## Final Title Recommendation

**Primary:** **The Floating Temples**
**Subtitle:** *Angkor Wat Return, Cambodia*
**Tagline:** *A meditation on return, impermanence, and Cambodia's soul*

**Why this works:**
- "Floating" evokes the misty dawn reflection (visual hook)
- "Temples" is clear and searchable
- Subtitle provides context and SEO
- Tagline sets expectations for tone and depth
- Mysterious without being clickbaity
- Honors the contemplative nature of the episode
