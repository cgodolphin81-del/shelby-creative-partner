## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Spotify
- **Optimize:** Episode title, description, show notes
- **Features:** Submit for Spotify editorial consideration
- **Strategy:** High completion rate > initial clicks (algorithm favors retention)
- **Action:** Encourage listeners to follow, not just play

### Apple Podcasts
- **Optimize:** Keywords in title and description
- **Features:** Submit for "New & Noteworthy"
- **Strategy:** Reviews matter—ask satisfied listeners to rate
- **Action:** Include review request in show notes and email

### YouTube
- **Optimize:** Title, description, tags, chapters, end screens
- **Features:** Community tab post, pinned comment with links
- **Strategy:** Watch time > views (favor longer, engaged listens)
- **Action:** Create custom thumbnail with sunrise + text overlay

### Instagram
- **Optimize:** Hashtags (mix of large and niche), alt text on images
- **Features:** Use all formats (feed, stories, reels, guides)
- **Strategy:** Saves and shares > likes (algorithm priority)
- **Action:** Create "Angkor" highlight for permanent access

### TikTok
- **Optimize:** Hook in first 3 seconds, captions, trending sounds
- **Features:** Stitch/duet capability, text overlays
- **Strategy:** Completion rate and re-watches matter most
- **Action:** Post 3 videos in launch week, then 1-2/week evergreen

### LinkedIn
- **Optimize:** Professional angle (leadership, resilience, wisdom)
- **Features:** Article format for long-form, newsletter integration
- **Strategy:** Comments and meaningful discussion > reactions
- **Action:** Tag relevant thought leaders (mindfully, not spammy)

### Pinterest
- **Optimize:** Vertical images, keyword-rich descriptions
- **Features:** Rich pins, board organization
- **Strategy:** Long shelf-life (pins work for months/years)
- **Action:** Create dedicated "The Infinite Walk" board

### Email
- **Optimize:** Subject line, preview text, mobile-friendly design
- **Features:** Segmentation (engaged vs. new subscribers)
- **Strategy:** Click-through rate and replies (engagement signals)
- **Action:** Send launch + follow-up, then include in weekly digest

## Risk Management

### Potential Issues & Mitigation

**Issue:** Low initial engagement
**Mitigation:** 
- Activate personal network (ask friends to listen/share)
- Engage heavily in first 2 hours (algorithm boost)
- Consider small paid promotion boost

**Issue:** Negative feedback/criticism
**Mitigation:**
- Respond with grace and openness
- Don't argue—thank and learn
- Moderate spam/hate, but allow constructive criticism

**Issue:** Technical problems (audio quality, distribution)
**Mitigation:**
- Test all platforms before launch
- Have backup distribution method ready
- Communicate transparently if issues arise

**Issue:** Cultural sensitivity concerns
**Mitigation:**
- Episode already approaches Cambodia with respect
- Acknowledge own perspective (outsider looking in)
- Invite Cambodian voices to respond/share
