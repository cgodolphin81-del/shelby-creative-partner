## Photography Style Guide

### Composition Rules

**Rule of Thirds:**
- Place horizon on lower third (emphasize sky)
- Place palace on left or right third (not center)
- Leave negative space for text overlay

**Leading Lines:**
- Use palace corridors as leading lines
- Lake shorelines guide eye to palace
- Steps leading to temple

**Framing:**
- Use arches and windows as frames
- Jharokhas (balconies) frame views
- Doorways frame subjects

**Symmetry:**
- Palace reflections (perfect symmetry)
- Temple architecture (vertical symmetry)
- Garden layouts (geometric symmetry)

---

### Lighting Guidelines

**Golden Hour (Best):**
- Shoot 1 hour after sunrise or before sunset
- Warm, soft light on white marble
- Long, dramatic shadows
- Orange/pink skies

**Blue Hour (Second Best):**
- 20-30 minutes after sunset
- Deep blue sky
- Palace lights beginning to glow
- Peaceful, serene mood

**Midday (Avoid if possible):**
- Harsh shadows
- Washed-out colors
- If necessary: use shade, find contrast

**Monsoon (Dramatic):**
- Cloudy, diffused light
- Saturated colors
- Moody atmosphere
- Embrace the drama

---

### Subject Guidelines

**Palaces:**
- Capture full facade (establish scale)
- Detail shots (carvings, windows, balconies)
- Interior courtyards
- Views FROM palace (perspective)

**Lakes:**
- Calm water for reflections
- Boats (traditional wooden)
- Shoreline with ghats
- Sunrise/sunset on water

**Temples:**
- Full exterior (spire prominent)
- Carved details (pillars, walls)
- Steps leading up
- Respectful interior shots (if allowed)

**People:**
- Silhouettes (more universal)
- Back to camera (viewing scene)
- Local artisans (with permission)
- Avoid tourist crowds

## Graphic Design Elements

### Patterns & Textures

**Jali (Lattice) Pattern:**
```
Usage: Backgrounds, overlays, dividers
Style: Intricate geometric patterns
Color: Palace White on Lake Blue (or vice versa)
Opacity: 10-20% for backgrounds
```

**Marble Texture:**
```
Usage: Backgrounds for text
Style: White marble with subtle veining
Color: Palace White with grey veins
Source: High-res marble photos or generated
```

**Water Ripple:**
```
Usage: Overlays, transitions
Style: Concentric circles, subtle
Color: Lake Blue at 10-15% opacity
Use: To suggest reflection, movement
```

**Henna/Mehndi Motifs:**
```
Usage: Decorative corners, borders
Style: Traditional Rajasthani patterns
Color: Ruby Red or Marigold Orange
Use: Cultural authenticity
```

**Warm Look (Daytime):**
- Temperature: +10
- Tint: Slight magenta
- Highlights: Warm (golden)
- Shadows: Slight purple
- Saturation: +5

**Cool Look (Evening):**
- Temperature: -5
- Tint: Neutral

**Dramatic Look (Monsoon):**

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram

**Feed Posts:**
- Size: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
- Colors: Vibrant, high contrast
- Text: Minimal (Instagram is visual)
- Hashtags: 15-20 relevant tags

**Stories:**
- Size: 1080x1920
- Colors: Bold, eye-catching
- Text: Large, readable
- Interactive: Polls, questions, quizzes

**Reels:**
- Size: 1080x1920
- Duration: 15-60 seconds
- Hook: First 3 seconds critical
- Text: On-screen for silent viewing

### YouTube

**Thumbnails:**
- Size: 1280x720 (16:9)
- Text: Large, bold, 3-5 words max
- Colors: High contrast, vibrant
- Face: Include if possible (connection)

**Video:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Color: Rec. 709 standard
- Audio: -14 LUFS (podcast standard)

### Pinterest

**Pin Images:**
- Size: 1000x1500 (2:3 ratio)
- Text: Clear, readable title
- Colors: Warm, inviting
- Branding: Subtle logo/watermark

### LinkedIn

**Post Images:**
- Size: 1200x627 (landscape)
- Text: Professional, clear
- Colors: More muted, professional
- Content: Data, insights, quotes

### TikTok

**Video:**
- Size: 1080x1920 (vertical)
- Duration: 15-60 seconds
- Text: Large, bold, on-screen
- Sound: Trending audio when relevant

## Accessibility Guidelines

### Color Contrast
- Text on background: Minimum 4.5:1 ratio
- Large text (18px+): Minimum 3:1 ratio
- Use contrast checker tools

### Alt Text
- Describe all images
- Include context, not just visual
- Example: "City Palace reflecting in Lake Pichola at sunset, white marble glowing gold"

### Captions
- All videos: Include captions
- Podcast: Provide transcript
- Social: Use camelCase for hashtags (#CityOfLakes not #cityoflakes)
