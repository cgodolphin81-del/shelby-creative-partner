## TIMING STRATEGY

### When to Start Teasing:
- **Day 1-3 after Episode 100:** Let the milestone breathe. Don't rush past it.
- **Day 4-5:** First subtle tease (Stories, Twitter)
- **Day 7:** Email tease to subscribers
- **Day 10:** Main tease (all platforms)
- **Day 14:** Episode 101 launch

### Why This Timing:
- Honors Episode 100 (doesn't immediately pivot)
- Builds anticipation naturally
- Gives time to process Episode 100 feedback
- Maintains momentum without rushing

## MYSTERY VS. CLARITY BALANCE

### What to Reveal:
- ✓ Episode 101 is coming
- ✓ The walk continues
- ✓ Something is shifting (depth over width)
- ✓ Community input is welcome
- ✓ Timing (general timeframe)

### What to Keep Mystery:
- ✗ Specific location (if decided)
- ✗ Exact release date (until closer)
- ✗ Full format changes (tease only)
- ✗ All collaborations (surprise some)

### Why Mystery Matters:
- Creates anticipation
- Allows flexibility (plans change)
- Makes announcement more impactful
- Keeps creative freedom

## LONG-TERM VISION (Episodes 101-200)

### Themes for Next Century:
1. **Depth Over Width:** Fewer places, more time in each
2. **Returns:** Going back to transformative locations
3. **Community:** Featuring listener stories, collaborative episodes
4. **Practice:** Not just visiting, but practicing what was learned
5. **Teaching:** Guides, frameworks, helping others travel with intention
6. **Integration:** What happens after the walk? How to bring it home
7. **Collaboration:** Working with guides, locals, other creators
8. **Impact:** Giving back, conservation, supporting places visited
9. **Evolution:** How the format itself evolves
10. **Legacy:** What 200 episodes means
