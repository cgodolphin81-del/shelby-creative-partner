## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Instagram
**Posting Schedule:**
- Main post: Launch day + 2 follow-ups
- Stories: Daily for first week, then 2-3x/week
- Reels: 3-5 during launch week

**Best Times:**
- Posts: 11 AM - 1 PM, 7-9 PM
- Stories: Throughout day
- Reels: 6-9 PM

**Hashtags:** Use 15-20 per post (mix of popular and niche)

**Engagement:**
- Reply to all comments within 2 hours
- Engage with similar accounts daily
- Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes)

---

### Twitter/X
**Posting Schedule:**
- Thread: Launch day (pinned for 24h)
- Individual tweets: 2-3/day for first week
- Engage with replies throughout

**Best Times:**
- 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-9 PM (your timezone)

**Strategy:**
- Thread performs best—lead with that

- Engage with travel/podcast communities
- Use 2-3 hashtags max per tweet

## Contingency Plans

### If Launch Day Has Technical Issues
1. Communicate immediately on social media
2. Provide alternative listening options
3. Offer bonus content as apology
4. Reschedule promotional posts if needed

### If Episode Underperforms
1. Don't panic—one episode doesn't define the show
2. Analyze: title, thumbnail, description, timing
3. A/B test different approaches for next episode
4. Double down on paid promotion (if budget allows)
5. Engage more deeply with existing audience

### If Episode Overperforms
1. Have bonus content ready (blog post, video)
2. Prepare for increased engagement
3. Consider paid promotion to capitalize
4. Document what worked for future episodes
5. Thank your audience publicly
