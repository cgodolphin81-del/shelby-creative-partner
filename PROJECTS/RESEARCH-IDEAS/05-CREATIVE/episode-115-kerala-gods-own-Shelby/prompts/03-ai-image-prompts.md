## Technical Notes

**Recommended Models:**
- Midjourney v6 for photorealistic imagery
- DALL-E 3 for conceptual/composite images
- Stable Diffusion XL for fine-tuned control

**Aspect Ratios:**
- 16:9 for video thumbnails, YouTube, cinematic shots
- 3:2 for Instagram, print, standard photography
- 4:3 for Instagram portrait, detailed close-ups

**Style Keywords:**
- Photorealistic, cinematic, National Geographic style
- Warm color grading for golden hour shots
- Cool tones for monsoon/misty scenes
- High contrast for Kathakali performances

**Consistency Tips:**
- Use same seed values for related images
- Maintain consistent color palette across episode
- Reference "Kerala" and "South India" for geographic accuracy
- Specify traditional elements for cultural authenticity
