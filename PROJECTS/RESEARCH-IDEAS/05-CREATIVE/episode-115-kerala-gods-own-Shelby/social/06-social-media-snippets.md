```
Research: 40+ hours across 15 sources
```

### Post 3: Cultural Sensitivity (T+7 Days)
```
A note on cultural representation in travel content.

When creating Episode 115 (Kerala), I asked myself:

→ Am I exoticizing or honoring?
→ Am I extracting or contributing?
→ Am I the hero of this story, or a witness?

Travel content has a colonial hangover. The "discoverer" narrative. The "untouched paradise" framing. The local people as backdrop.

I chose differently:

✓ Kerala doesn't need discovery. It has 5,000 years of visitors.
✓ The locals are not scenery. They are the story.
✓ I am not the hero. I am the student.

Result: A documentary that respects its subject.

To fellow creators: Your audience can feel the difference between extraction and collaboration.

Choose collaboration.
```
