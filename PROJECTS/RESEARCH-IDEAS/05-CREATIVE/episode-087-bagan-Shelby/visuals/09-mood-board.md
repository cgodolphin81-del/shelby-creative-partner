## Photography Style

### Lighting

**Golden Hour (Primary)**
- Time: 5:30-7:00 AM, 5:00-6:30 PM
- Quality: Warm, soft, directional
- Mood: Magical, spiritual, awe-inspiring
- Use: Hero images, emotional moments

**Blue Hour (Secondary)**
- Time: Just before sunrise, just after sunset
- Quality: Cool, soft, even
- Mood: Calm, contemplative, mysterious
- Use: Evening content, river scenes

**Midday (Avoid or Modify)**
- Time: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Quality: Harsh, high contrast
- Mood: Challenging for photography
- Use: Interior shots, shaded areas only

### Composition

**Rule of Thirds**
- Place temples at intersection points
- Horizon on upper or lower third
- Create balance and interest

**Leading Lines**
- Temple paths, river banks, balloon trails
- Guide viewer through image
- Create depth and journey

**Framing**
- Use temple arches, windows, doorways
- Frame subjects within architecture
- Add context and depth

**Scale**
- Include human figures for scale
- Show vastness of temple plain
- Create awe and perspective

**Symmetry**
- Ananda Temple (perfectly symmetrical)
- Reflection shots (water, ponds)
- Create balance and harmony

### Color Grading

**Warm Grade (Sunrise/Sunset)**
- Temperature: +10 to +20 (warmer)
- Tint: Slight magenta (+5)
- Highlights: Warm orange
- Shadows: Slight teal (complement)
- Vibrance: +10 to +20
- Use: Hero images, emotional content

**Cool Grade (River/Evening)**
- Temperature: -5 to -10 (cooler)
- Tint: Slight green
- Highlights: Cool blue
- Shadows: Deep purple
- Vibrance: +5 to +10
- Use: Evening content, calm moments

**Neutral Grade (General)**
- Temperature: 0 (neutral)
- Tint: 0 (neutral)
- Highlights: Neutral white
- Shadows: Neutral gray
- Vibrance: +10
- Use: Travel guide, informational content

## Video Style

### Pacing

**Opening (0:00-1:30)**
- Pace: Slow, contemplative
- Cuts: Every 5-8 seconds
- Mood: Mysterious, inviting
- Music: Soft ambient with temple bells

**Main Content (1:30-19:00)**
- Pace: Moderate, storytelling
- Cuts: Every 3-5 seconds
- Mood: Engaging, informative
- Music: Varies by chapter

**Closing (19:00-20:00)**
- Pace: Slow, reflective
- Cuts: Every 6-10 seconds
- Mood: Contemplative, inspiring
- Music: Swelling, emotional

### Transitions

**Cross-Dissolve**
- Use: General transitions
- Duration: 0.5-1 second
- Mood: Smooth, natural

**Fade to Black**
- Use: Chapter transitions
- Duration: 0.5-1 second
- Mood: Definitive, pause

**Light Leak**
- Use: Sunrise/sunset transitions
- Duration: 0.3-0.5 second
- Mood: Warm, magical

**Whip Pan**
- Use: Adventure content (balloons)
- Duration: 0.2-0.3 second
- Mood: Dynamic, energetic

### Effects

**Slow Motion**
- Use: Sunrise moments, balloon launch
- Speed: 50-60% of normal
- Mood: Magical, emphasized

**Time-Lapse**
- Use: Sunrise/sunset, clouds, shadows
- Duration: 5-10 seconds in final
- Mood: Epic, passage of time

**Zoom (Ken Burns)**
- Use: Still images
- Speed: Slow, subtle
- Mood: Cinematic, engaging

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

**Text on Backgrounds:**
- Minimum ratio: 4.5:1 (WCAG AA)
- Preferred ratio: 7:1 (WCAG AAA)
- Test: Use contrast checker tools

**Examples:**
- ✅ White text on Temple Red (passes)
- ✅ Charcoal text on Off-White (passes)
- ❌ Sunrise Orange text on White (fails - too light)
- ✅ Charcoal text on Sandstone Beige (passes)

### Alt Text

**For Images:**
- Describe subject, action, context
- Include colors when relevant
- Keep concise but informative
- Example: "Sunrise over Bagan temple plain with hot air balloons, golden light illuminating 2,000+ ancient Buddhist temples in Myanmar"

### Captions

**For Videos:**
- Burned-in captions for social media
- SRT files for YouTube
- Accurate transcription
- Include sound effects when relevant
