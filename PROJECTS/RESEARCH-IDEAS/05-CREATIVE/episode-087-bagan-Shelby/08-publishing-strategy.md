## Crisis Management

### Potential Issues

**Political Sensitivity:**
- Myanmar has had political instability since 2021
- Some may question traveling to Myanmar

**Response:**
- Acknowledge situation in travel guide
- Emphasize: Bagan generally stable, less affected than cities
- Focus on: Supporting local economy through responsible tourism
- Provide: Resources for checking current advisories

**Cultural Sensitivity:**
- Questions about photographing monks, temples

**Response:**
- Clear etiquette guidelines in travel guide
- Emphasize: Ask permission, dress respectfully
- Model: Respectful behavior in content

**Negative Comments:**
- Critical comments about destination, content, etc.

**Response:**
- Respond professionally, thank for feedback
- Don't engage with trolls
- Delete spam/hate speech
- Take constructive criticism seriously
