### Post 3: Historical Fact
**Image:** Ananda Temple

**Text:**
🏛️ DID YOU KNOW?

Ananda Temple was built in 1105 by King Kyanzittha. It's considered the most beautiful temple in Bagan, with four standing Buddha statues (each 9.5 meters tall) facing the four cardinal directions.

The architecture shows Mon (Indian) influence, with almost Greek-style arches. This was a kingdom connected to the wider Buddhist world.

Walk the circumambulation path today, and you're following the same route pilgrims have taken for 900+ years.

Some things transcend time.
