## Episode Descriptions

### Short Description (50-75 words)
**Bagan, Myanmar is home to the largest concentration of Buddhist temples in the world. Over 2,000 ancient structures spread across a 41-square-kilometer plain. At sunrise, these temples catch fire with golden light. Episode 087 explores the history, the faith, and the legacy of the Fire Temple. 20 minutes of audio storytelling.**

### Medium Description (100-150 words)
**Between the 9th and 13th centuries, the Pagan Kingdom built over 10,000 Buddhist monuments. Today, 2,000+ temples remain—scattered across a plain beside the Irrawaddy River. This is Bagan, Myanmar: a landscape where faith became geography, where devotion was erected brick by brick. Episode 087 takes you to the Fire Temple at dawn, exploring the history of the temple-builders, the experience of sunrise over ancient stones, and the question: what does it mean to build for eternity? Includes complete travel guide, 70+ AI image prompts, and social media kit.**

### Long Description (200+ words)
**Have you ever stood in a place where faith became geography? Where devotion wasn't whispered, but erected—brick by brick, prayer by prayer—until the landscape itself became a cathedral?

Welcome to Bagan, Myanmar.

Between the 9th and 13th centuries, the kings and people of the Pagan Kingdom constructed over 10,000 Buddhist monuments. Not one great temple. Not a single cathedral. Ten thousand acts of devotion, scattered across 41 square kilometers.

Today, approximately 2,000 temples remain. They survived Mongol invasions, centuries of abandonment, earthquakes, and political turmoil. They are not relics. They are living spaces of worship, where pilgrims still circle with flowers and incense, where monks still walk barefoot on morning alms rounds.

Episode 087 of the Infinite Walk takes you to Bagan at dawn. We'll climb temple terraces in the dark, watch the sun ignite 2,000 spires, and ask: what does it mean to build for futures you will never see?

This episode includes:
🎙️ 20-minute audio narration
📍 Complete location research (history, temples, ballooning, Buddhist heritage)
📝 Poetic essay on temples as prayers
🎨 70+ AI image prompts (temples, balloons, Irrawaddy, Shelby exploring)
📚 Full travel guide (getting there, costs, best times, culture, current situation)
📱 Social media snippets (Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, TikTok, Pinterest)
🎨 Visual mood board (temple reds, sunrise oranges, sky blues, balloon golds)

This is the Fire Temple. This is Bagan. This is devotion made visible.**
