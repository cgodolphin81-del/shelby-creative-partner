**Created with love for:** Charmaine's Mother

## 🎯 PROJECT SUMMARY

This is a complete vintage radio drama collection inspired by South Africa's beloved **Springbok Radio** (1950-1985). Created as a personal gift for Charmaine's mother, who has fond memories of listening to Springbok Radio in her youth.

**What's Included:**
- ✅ 10 original radio drama scripts (1950s-60s South African setting)
- ✅ Complete AI voice setup guide (ElevenLabs)
- ✅ Vintage sound effects library and sourcing guide
- ✅ Recording and production guide (editing, mixing, mastering)
- ✅ Private playlist setup instructions (Spotify, YouTube, etc.)
- ✅ Public archive options (if desired)
- ✅ Memorial/heritage documentation guide
- ✅ Technical setup and hosting guide
- ✅ Love letter scripts for Charmaine to record (intro/outro)

**Status:** ✅ **COMPLETE** - All 10 deliverables finished

## 🚀 QUICK START GUIDE

### For Charmaine - Next Steps:

#### Phase 1: Pre-Production (Week 1)
1. **Read all scripts** - Get familiar with the stories
2. **Review voice setup guide** - Decide: AI voices or human actors?
3. **Set up ElevenLabs account** - If using AI voices
4. **Test voice configurations** - Make sure they sound right
5. **Review sound effects guide** - Bookmark resources

#### Phase 2: Production (Weeks 2-6)
1. **Record voices** - Either generate AI or record human actors
2. **Source sound effects** - Download from BBC Sound Effects, Freesound
3. **Edit episodes** - Follow production guide (Audacity or Reaper)
4. **Add music** - Royalty-free vintage-style music
5. **Master with vintage EQ** - Slapback delay, period-accurate sound

#### Phase 3: Personal Touch (Week 7)
1. **Record intro/outro** - Use love letter scripts
2. **Edit into collection** - Add as bookends
3. **Create cover art** - Vintage radio image
4. **Final quality check** - Listen to entire collection

#### Phase 4: Delivery (Week 8)
1. **Choose delivery method** - Spotify + USB drive recommended
2. **Upload and organize** - Follow playlist setup guide
3. **Create gift presentation** - Box, card, instructions
4. **Present to Mom** - Record her reaction if possible!

#### Phase 5: Preservation (Ongoing)
1. **Document the project** - Follow heritage documentation guide
2. **Share with family** - Private links to relatives
3. **Archive permanently** - Internet Archive or Preservation Society
4. **Maintain backups** - 3-2-1 rule (3 copies, 2 media types, 1 offsite)

## 🛠 TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### Minimum Setup:
- Computer (any modern laptop/desktop)
- USB microphone (Blue Yeti or similar, ~R1,500)
- Headphones (any closed-back)
- Audacity (free software)
- ElevenLabs account ($22/month)
- Quiet recording space

### Ideal Setup:
- XLR microphone (Shure SM7B or Rode PodMic)
- Audio interface (Focusrite Scarlett 2i2)
- Professional headphones (Audio-Technica ATH-M50x)
- Reaper or Adobe Audition
- Acoustic treatment (blankets, foam panels)
- Dedicated recording space

### Budget Estimate:
```
Minimum:    R2,000-3,000 (mic, software free, DIY)
Average:    R5,000-8,000 (decent gear, ElevenLabs, music)
Professional: R15,000-25,000 (pro gear, voice actors, original music)
```
