**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE  
**Type:** Personal Creative Project (Gift)  
**Created:** March 2024

---

## Overview

A vintage radio drama collection inspired by South Africa's beloved Springbok Radio (1950-1985). Created as a personal gift for Charmaine's mother, who has fond memories of listening to Springbok Radio in her youth.

## What's Included

- **10 Original Radio Drama Scripts** - 15 minutes each, ~155 minutes total
- **Complete Production Guide** - AI voice setup, sound effects, recording
- **Historical Research** - Springbok Radio history and cultural context
- **Private Distribution Setup** - Spotify, YouTube, or private archive options
