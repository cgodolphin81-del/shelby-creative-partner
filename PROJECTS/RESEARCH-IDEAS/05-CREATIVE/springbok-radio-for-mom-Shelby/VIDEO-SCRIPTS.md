## Video 3: Behind the Scenes - Production (3-4 minutes)

**Title:** "How I Made a Vintage Radio Drama Collection"  
**Platform:** YouTube (optional public), family  
**Style:** Educational, authentic, process-driven

---

**[0:00-0:20] INTRO**
*(Charmaine on camera, warm setting)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"Hi, I'm Charmaine. And I just completed a passion project: 10 vintage radio dramas for my mom. Today, I'll show you how I made them."

---

**[0:20-0:50] PHASE 1: RESEARCH**
*(Show research documents, Springbok Radio history)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"First, I researched Springbok Radio. 17,000 programs aired between 1950-1985. I studied the style, the dialogue, the sound design. This wasn't just about writing scripts. It was about capturing an era."

---

**[0:50-1:30] PHASE 2: SCRIPTWRITING**
*(Show scripts, writing process)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"10 episodes. Each 15 minutes. I set them all in 1950s-60s South Africa. Cape Town. Durban. Johannesburg. Family dramas. Gentle mysteries. Romance. The genres Springbok Radio was known for."

---

**[1:30-2:10] PHASE 3: VOICES**
*(Show ElevenLabs or voice setup)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"For voices, I used AI. ElevenLabs. I could create multiple characters, each with period-appropriate accents. South African English. Some Afrikaans influence. The technology is wild."

---

**[2:10-2:40] PHASE 4: SOUND EFFECTS**
*(Show SFX library, Foley work)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"Sound effects matter. Door creaks. Train whistles. Tea being poured. I sourced vintage SFX libraries and recreated some sounds myself. Authenticity is everything."

---

**[2:40-3:10] PHASE 5: ASSEMBLY**
*(Show audio editing software, final mix)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"Assembly is where it comes together. Dialogue. Effects. Music. Mixing. Mastering. Each episode took 4-6 hours to produce. 10 episodes. That's... a lot of hours."

---

**[3:10-3:30] THE RESULT**
*(Show final product, mom's reaction if available)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"My mom's reaction made it all worth it. 'This takes me back 60 years.' That's why we create. For moments like that."

---

**[3:30-3:45] OUTRO**
*(Warm closing)*

**CHARMAINE:**
"If you're thinking of creating something for someone you love... do it. They'll remember it forever. Thanks for watching."
