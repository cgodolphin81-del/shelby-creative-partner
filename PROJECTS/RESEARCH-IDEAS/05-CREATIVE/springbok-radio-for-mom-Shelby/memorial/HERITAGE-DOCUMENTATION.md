# Memorial & Heritage Documentation
## Preserving This Gift for Future Generations

**Created for:** Springbok Radio for Mom  
**Goal:** Ensure this collection endures as family heritage  
**Vision:** A living legacy that outlives us all

---

## WHY DOCUMENT THIS?

### This Project Is More Than Entertainment:

✅ **Family History:** Captures stories, values, and love for future generations  
✅ **Cultural Heritage:** Preserves the spirit of South African radio's golden age  
✅ **Personal Legacy:** Your creative gift to your mother  
✅ **Historical Record:** Documents what mattered to your family in 2024  
✅ **Inspiration:** Might inspire grandchildren/great-grandchildren to create

### The Alternative (If Not Documented):
❌ Files get lost when devices fail  
❌ Context is lost ("Who made this? Why?")  
❌ Future generations don't know the story  
❌ Mom's reaction and memories aren't preserved  
❌ The love behind it fades with time

**Documentation ensures this gift lives on.**

## PRESERVATION STRATEGY

### The 3-2-1 Rule:

**3 copies** of everything  
**2 different media types** (digital + physical)  
**1 copy offsite** (different location)

### Implementation:

#### Digital Copies:
```
Copy 1: Your computer (primary)
Copy 2: External hard drive (backup)
Copy 3: Cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox)
Copy 4: Internet Archive (preservation)
```

#### Physical Copies:
```
Copy 1: Mom's home (USB + documentation)
Copy 2: Your home (complete archive)
Copy 3: Trusted family member (additional backup)
```

### File Formats for Longevity:

**Audio:**
- ✅ WAV (archival, uncompressed)
- ✅ MP3 320kbps (compatible, smaller)
- ❌ Proprietary formats (might not be readable in future)

**Documents:**
- ✅ PDF/A (archival PDF standard)
- ✅ Plain text (.txt)
- ✅ PDF (widely compatible)

**Photos:**
- ✅ JPEG (universal)
- ✅ TIFF (archival, larger)
- ✅ PNG (lossless)

**Video:**
- ✅ MP4 (H.264 codec)
- ✅ MOV (compatible)

### Metadata to Include:

**For Every File:**
```
Filename: SpringbokRadio_Ep01_LetterFromCapeTown_2024.wav

ID3 Tags / Metadata:
- Title: Episode 1: The Letter from Cape Town
```
