## Content Calendar Best Practices

**Posting Schedule:**
- Instagram: 1-2 posts/day + Stories
- Twitter: 3-5 tweets/day
- TikTok: 3-4 videos/week
- LinkedIn: 3 posts/week
- YouTube: 2 videos/week
- Discord: Daily engagement

**Engagement Strategy:**
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- Repost user creations daily
- Host weekly community challenges
- Feature top creators weekly

**Metrics to Track:**
- Engagement rate (target: 5%+)
- Click-through rate (target: 3%+)
- Conversion rate (target: 2%+)
- Follower growth (target: 10%/month)
