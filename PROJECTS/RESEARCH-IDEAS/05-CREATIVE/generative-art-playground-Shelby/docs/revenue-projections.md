# Revenue Projections - Generative Art Playground
## 3-Year Financial Model

---

## Executive Summary

**Business Model:** Freemium SaaS with multiple revenue streams
- Subscription tiers (Free/Pro/Enterprise)
- Print-on-demand commissions (15-30%)
- NFT marketplace royalties (2.5%)
- API access for enterprise

**Total Addressable Market:** $2.3B (AI art tools + NFT creation + POD)
**Target:** 1% market share by Year 3

---

## Revenue Streams Breakdown

### 1. Subscription Revenue

| Tier | Price | Target Segment |
|------|-------|----------------|
| Free | $0 | Hobbyists, testers |
| Pro | $9.99/mo | Serious creators, artists |
| Enterprise | $49.99/mo | Studios, agencies, teams |

**Conversion Assumptions:**
- Free → Pro: 5% conversion rate
- Pro → Enterprise: 2% conversion rate
- Monthly churn: 8% (industry standard for creative tools)

### 2. Print-on-Demand Commission

- Average order value: $45
- Platform commission: 20% (average)
- Profit per order: $9
- Attach rate: 15% of Pro users monthly

### 3. NFT Royalties

- Average NFT price: 0.3 ETH (~$600)
- Platform royalty: 2.5% on secondary sales
- Average royalty per NFT: $15
- Mint rate: 30% of Pro users monthly

## Risk Factors

1. **Market Risk:** AI art market becomes saturated
   - Mitigation: Focus on unique features (NFT + POD integration)

2. **Technology Risk:** Stable Diffusion API costs increase
   - Mitigation: Build relationships with multiple providers

3. **Regulatory Risk:** NFT/crypto regulations change
   - Mitigation: Diversify revenue (subscriptions + POD = 80%+)

4. **Competition Risk:** Large players enter market
   - Mitigation: Build strong community, focus on UX

**ArtPlayground is positioned for strong, profitable growth:**
