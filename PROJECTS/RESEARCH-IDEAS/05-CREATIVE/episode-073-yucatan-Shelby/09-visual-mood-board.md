## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Cinzel** or **Trajan Pro**
- Classical, carved stone feeling
- Evokes ancient inscriptions
- Use for: Titles, headers, quote cards

**Alternatives:**
- Playfair Display (elegant, editorial)
- Cormorant Garamond (classic, refined)
- Libre Baskerville (readable, traditional)

---

### Secondary Font (Body Text)
**Lato** or **Source Sans Pro**
- Clean, readable, modern
- Good contrast with classical headings
- Use for: Body text, captions, descriptions

**Alternatives:**
- Open Sans (friendly, versatile)
- Merriweather (readable serif option)
- Montserrat (geometric, clean)

---

### Accent Font (Special Use)
**Maya-inspired decorative font** (sparingly)
- Use for: Episode numbers, special graphics
- Don't overuse—can feel gimmicky
- Consider custom Maya glyph-inspired elements

**Alternatives:**
- Uncial-style fonts (ancient manuscript feel)
- Hand-lettered titles for personal touch

## Imagery Style

### Photography Aesthetic

**Overall Look:**
- Cinematic and atmospheric
- Rich, saturated colors (but not oversaturated)
- Warm tones for jungle/ruins
- Cool tones for cenotes/water
- Golden hour lighting preferred
- High contrast between light and shadow

**Composition:**
- Wide landscapes for scale
- Intimate details for texture
- Human element for connection (Shelby exploring)
- Symmetry for cenotes and pyramids
- Leading lines (paths, stairs, light beams)

**Processing:**
- Slight film grain for texture
- Warm shadows, cool highlights (or vice versa)
- Vignette for focus
- Clarity on textures (stone, leaves, water)
- Soft glow on light sources

## Accessibility Considerations

### Color Contrast
- Ensure all text has 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- Test color combinations for color blindness
- Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning

### Alt Text
- Write descriptive alt text for all images
- Include context and mood, not just literal description
- Example: "El Castillo pyramid at Chichen Itza bathed in golden sunrise light, jungle mist rising from the base, creating a mysterious and ancient atmosphere"

### Captions
- All video content should have captions
- Use clear, readable font
- High contrast caption background
- Accurate transcription of audio

### Screen Reader Friendly
- Use semantic headings in documents
- Descriptive link text (not "click here")
- Logical reading order in graphics
