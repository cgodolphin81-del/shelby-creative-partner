---

Hey [Name],

Lullabies help. But they're just one piece of the puzzle.

Here are 5 science-backed tips for better baby sleep:

**1. Consistent bedtime routine** 🕐
Same time, same steps, every night. Baby brains love predictability.

**2. Dark, cool room** 🌡️
68-72°F. Blackout curtains. Darkness = melatonin.

**3. White noise or lullabies** 🎵
Mimics the womb. Masks household noise. We've got you covered here!

**4. No screens before bed** 📱
Blue light disrupts sleep hormones. Keep it analog.

**5. Consistent wake time** ☀️
Yes, even on weekends. Regulates internal clock.
