# 📱 Social Media Promotion Plan - 30 Posts

Complete 30-day social media content calendar for Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest.

---

## Platform Strategy Overview

### Instagram (@moonbeamlullabies)
- **Focus:** Aesthetic visuals, parent community, behind-the-scenes
- **Posting:** 1 feed post/day + 3-5 Stories/day
- **Content Mix:** 40% educational, 30% promotional, 20% community, 10% personal
- **Hashtags:** 15-20 per post (mix of sizes)

### TikTok (@moonbeamlullabies)
- **Focus:** Quick tips, relatable parent content, trending sounds
- **Posting:** 1-2 videos/day
- **Content Mix:** 50% tips, 30% trending, 20% promotional
- **Style:** Authentic, raw, relatable

### Pinterest (Moonbeam Lullabies)
- **Focus:** Evergreen content, sleep tips, nursery inspiration
- **Posting:** 5-10 pins/day (mix of new and evergreen)
- **Content Mix:** 60% educational, 30% promotional, 10% inspirational
- **Style:** Beautiful graphics, actionable tips

## Content Creation Tips

### Batch Creation
- **Once per week:** Create all graphics in Canva
- **Once per week:** Film 5-7 TikToks in one session
- **Once per month:** Plan entire month's content calendar

### Tools
- **Canva:** Graphics, Pinterest pins, Instagram posts
- **CapCut:** Video editing for TikTok/Reels
- **Later/Buffer:** Scheduling posts
- **Planoly:** Instagram planning

### Best Posting Times
- **Instagram:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM (local time)
- **TikTok:** 12 PM, 5 PM, 9 PM (local time)
- **Pinterest:** 8 PM - 4 AM (users plan ahead)

---

*Consistency is key! Post regularly, engage with your community, and adjust based on what performs best.*
