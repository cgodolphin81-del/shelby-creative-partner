**Usage Notes:**
- Maintain consistent color palette: soft lavenders, pale blues, creams, gentle purples
- All thumbnails should be readable at small sizes (mobile)
- Use high contrast for text on thumbnails
- Keep brand mascot (sleeping moon) consistent across all graphics
- Optimize for mobile-first viewing (most parents on phones)

**Tools Recommended:**
- Canva Pro (templates and brand kit)
- Midjourney/DALL-E 3 (custom illustrations)
- Photoshop (advanced editing)
- Figma (brand guidelines)
