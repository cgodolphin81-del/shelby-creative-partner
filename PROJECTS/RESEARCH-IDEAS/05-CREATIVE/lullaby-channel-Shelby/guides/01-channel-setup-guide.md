#### Logo Concepts

2. **Cloud Bed**: Baby sleeping on a cloud with stars above
3. **Musical Moon**: Moon with musical notes flowing from it

- **Headings**: Nunito or Quicksand (rounded, friendly)
- **Body**: Open Sans or Lato (clean, readable)
- **Accent**: Pacifico or Dancing Script (for special text)

### Channel Art Specifications

#### YouTube Banner
- **Size**: 2560 x 1440 px (safe zone: 1546 x 423 px center)
- **Elements**: Channel name, upload schedule, social handles
- **Style**: Soft gradient (purple to blue), stars, moon, sleeping baby silhouette

#### Profile Picture
- **Size**: 800 x 800 px (displays as circle)
- **Design**: Simple moon icon or stylized "M" with stars

#### Video Thumbnail Template
- **Size**: 1280 x 720 px
- **Elements**: Soft background, title text, moon/star icons, consistent branding
- **Font**: Large, readable text (minimum 60pt)
