## How to Launch This Channel

### Phase 1: Channel Setup (1-2 days)

**Step 1: Create YouTube Channel**
1. Go to YouTube.com
2. Sign in with Google account
3. Click profile icon → "Create a channel"
4. Name: "Moonbeam Lullabies"
5. Handle: @MoonbeamLullabies (or similar)
6. Verify account (phone verification)

**Step 2: Branding**
1. **Profile Picture:** Use prompt #3 from IMAGE-PROMPTS.md
2. **Channel Banner:** Use prompt #2, 2560x1440 pixels
3. **Channel Description:** Copy from SEO guide
4. **Links:** Add website, social media, email

**Step 3: Channel Settings**
1. Enable channel keywords (lullaby, baby sleep, sleep music, etc.)
2. Set upload defaults (privacy: public, category: Music)
3. Enable custom thumbnails (requires phone verification)
4. Set up channel trailer slot

---

### Phase 2: Content Production (1-2 weeks)

**Step 1: Generate Lullabies**
1. Sign up for Suno AI (suno.ai) or Udio (udio.com)
2. Use prompts from `lullabies/10-sample-lullaby-prompts.md`
3. Generate each lullaby (may take 5-10 attempts per song)
4. Download high-quality audio files

**Step 2: Create Videos**
1. Choose video template from `templates/05-video-templates.md`
2. Use Canva or similar for visuals
3. Combine audio + visuals in editing software
4. Export as MP4, 1080p minimum

**Recommended Editing Tools:**
- **Free:** DaVinci Resolve, CapCut, iMovie
- **Paid:** Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro

**Step 3: Create Thumbnails**
1. Use prompts from IMAGE-PROMPTS.md (thumbnails section)
2. Create in Canva (1280x720 pixels)
3. Ensure text is readable at small sizes
4. Save as PNG or JPG

**Step 4: Write Metadata**
1. Use SEO guide for titles, descriptions, tags
2. Copy-paste optimized descriptions
3. Add to appropriate playlists
4. Set category: Music

## Monetization Setup

### 1. YouTube Partner Program (YPP)
**Requirements:**
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 watch hours (past 12 months)
- Follow all policies

**Application:**
1. Go to YouTube Studio → Monetization
2. Apply when eligible
3. Set up AdSense account
4. Enable ads on videos

**Timeline:** 3-6 months typically

---

### 2. Custom Lullaby Service
**Setup:**
1. Create order form (Google Forms, Typeform, or website)
2. Set pricing: $50-150 per lullaby
3. Create payment link (Stripe, PayPal)
4. Set up delivery system (email with download link)

1. Customer fills out form (baby name, message, style preferences)
2. You create lullaby using AI tools (2-3 hours work)
3. Mix and master (1 hour)
4. Deliver via email with lyric card
5. Follow up for testimonial

## Tips for Success

### Content Quality:
1. **Audio quality is everything** - Parents will tolerate simple visuals, but audio must be soothing and professional
2. **Test on real babies** - Get feedback from parent friends before publishing
3. **Length matters** - 8-hour versions perform best for overnight sleep
4. **Consistency wins** - Upload schedule matters more than perfection

### SEO & Discovery:
1. **Research keywords** - Use TubeBuddy or VidIQ for keyword research
2. **Optimize first 48 hours** - YouTube tests new videos heavily in first 2 days
3. **Playlists are key** - Organize content into age-specific and theme playlists
4. **Engagement signals** - Respond to every comment in first 2 hours

### Community Building:
1. **Be present** - Respond to comments daily
2. **Ask for feedback** - Parents love sharing their experience
3. **Share wins** - Celebrate milestones with your community
4. **Collaborate** - Partner with parenting influencers and bloggers

### Monetization:
1. **Diversify early** - Don't wait for YPP, start custom lullabies immediately
2. **Email list** - Capture emails from day 1 (link in description)
3. **Test pricing** - Custom lullaby pricing: start at $50, increase as demand grows
4. **Track everything** - Know your numbers: CPM, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost

## Common Questions

**Q: Is AI music copyright-safe?**  
A: Suno and Udio provide commercial rights for paid plans. Read their terms. You own what you create on paid tiers.

**Q: How long until I make money?**  
A: Realistically 3-6 months for ad revenue. Custom lullabies can generate income immediately.

**Q: Do I need music experience?**  
A: No. The AI handles composition. Your job is curation, prompting, and post-production.

**Q: What if babies don't respond?**  
A: Every baby is different. Offer refunds on custom lullabies if no results. Collect feedback and iterate.

**Q: Can I expand beyond YouTube?**  
A: Absolutely! Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music are all options. Consider after YouTube is established.
