## Overview

A complete YouTube channel launch package for **Moonbeam Lullabies** - an AI-powered lullaby channel with multiple revenue streams.

## What's Included

- **Channel Setup Guide** - Branding, art, description, tags
- **10 Sample Lullaby Prompts** - AI music generation (Suno, Udio)
- **Video Templates** - 5 video style templates with thumbnails
- **30-Day Upload Calendar** - Day-by-day posting schedule
- **SEO Optimization Guide** - Titles, descriptions, tags, playlists
- **Monetization Strategy** - Multiple revenue streams
- **Custom Orders System** - Personalized lullaby service
- **Email Templates** - Customer communication
- **Revenue Projections** - Financial modeling

1. **YouTube Ad Revenue** - Monetized channel
2. **Sponsorships** - Baby/parenting brands
3. **Custom Lullabies** - Personalized songs ($50-150 each)
4. **Digital Products** - Lullaby albums, sleep guides
5. **Patreon/Membership** - Exclusive content
6. **Licensing** - Music licensing for apps/media
