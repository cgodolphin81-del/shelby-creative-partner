# Marketing Plan - Moonbeam Lullabies

**30-60-90 Day YouTube Channel Launch Strategy**

---

## Product Positioning

**What:** AI-powered lullaby YouTube channel  
**Who:** Parents with babies/toddlers (ages 0-4)  
**Why:** Gentle, effective sleep music for modern parents  
**Unique Angle:** Personalized custom lullabies available

---

## 30-Day Plan (Launch & Foundation)

### Week 1: Channel Setup
- [ ] Create YouTube channel "Moonbeam Lullabies"
- [ ] Design channel art (banner, logo, profile pic)
- [ ] Write channel description with SEO keywords
- [ ] Set up channel trailer (use best lullaby)
- [ ] Create 5 playlists by theme/age

### Week 2-3: Content Production
- [ ] Generate 10 lullabies using AI prompts
- [ ] Create videos using 5 templates (2 videos per template)
- [ ] Design thumbnails for all videos
- [ ] Write SEO-optimized titles and descriptions
- [ ] Add tags and categorize into playlists

### Week 4: Launch
- [ ] Upload first 10 videos (schedule releases)
- [ ] Create channel trailer
- [ ] Post community introduction
- [ ] Share with personal network
- [ ] Post in parenting Facebook groups (where allowed)

### Goals for 30 Days:
- 10 videos uploaded
- Channel fully branded
- 100 subscribers
- 1,000 total views

---

## 60-Day Plan (Growth & Engagement)

### Week 5-6: Consistency
- [ ] Upload 2-3 videos per week (follow calendar)
- [ ] Post 2-3 Shorts per week (lullaby snippets)
- [ ] Respond to all comments
- [ ] Create 2 community posts per week
- [ ] Analyze top-performing content

### Week 7-8: Promotion
- [ ] Reach out to parenting bloggers for features
- [ ] Guest post on parenting websites
- [ ] Collaborate with parenting influencers
- [ ] Run YouTube ads ($50-100 test budget)
- [ ] Launch custom lullaby service

### Goals for 60 Days:
- 30 total videos
- 500 subscribers
- 10,000 total views
- First custom lullaby orders

---

## 90-Day Plan (Monetization & Scale)

### Week 9-10: Monetization
- [ ] Apply for YouTube Partner Program (if eligible)
- [ ] Set up Patreon for exclusive content
- [ ] Create digital products (lullaby albums)
- [ ] Reach out to baby product brands for sponsorships
- [ ] Optimize custom lullaby ordering process

### Week 11-12: Optimization
- [ ] Double down on top-performing content types
- [ ] A/B test thumbnails and titles
- [ ] Create series/sequels to popular videos
- [ ] Plan content calendar for next quarter
- [ ] Consider expanding to Spotify/Apple Music

### Goals for 90 Days:
- 50 total videos
- 1,000 subscribers
- 50,000 total views
- $500-1,000 monthly revenue (combined streams)

## Content Strategy

### Video Types:
1. **Classic Lullabies** - Traditional songs (Twinkle Twinkle, etc.)
2. **Original Compositions** - Unique AI-generated lullabies
3. **Themed Collections** - Bedtime, nap time, bath time
4. **Age-Specific** - Newborn, 6mo+, toddler
5. **Duration Variations** - 10 min, 30 min, 1 hour, 8 hours

### Upload Schedule:
- **Long-form videos:** 2-3 per week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- **Shorts:** 3-4 per week (Tue, Thu, Sat, Sun)
- **Community posts:** 2 per week
