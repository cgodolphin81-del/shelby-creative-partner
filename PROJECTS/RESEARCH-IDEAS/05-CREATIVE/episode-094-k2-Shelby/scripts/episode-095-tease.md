## Key Themes to Highlight

### Contrast with Episode 094
| K2 (Ep 094) | Ancient Forest (Ep 095) |
|-------------|------------------------|
| Highest peaks | Deepest forests |
| Ice and stone | Moss and wood |
| Pakistan | Pacific Northwest |
| Altitude | Depth |
| Dangerous | Peaceful (but powerful) |
| Sparse life | Abundant life |
| Blue/grey palette | Green palette |
| 8,611m up | 2,000+ years old |
| Human challenge | Natural endurance |

### Emotional Arc
- Episode 094: Awe through smallness, danger, altitude
- Episode 095: Awe through age, permanence, life

### Unifying Themes
- Both are about things that outlast us
- Both are about places that don't need humans
- Both are about humility before nature
- Both are about what remains
