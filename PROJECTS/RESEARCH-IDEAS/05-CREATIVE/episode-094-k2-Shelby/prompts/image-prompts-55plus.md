## Technical Notes for All Prompts

**Style Keywords to Mix In:**
- Photorealistic, cinematic, National Geographic style
- Ansel Adams influence for black and white
- Fine art landscape photography
- Documentary candid moments
- Epic adventure cinematography

**Lighting Conditions:**
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
- Blue hour (twilight)
- Harsh midday for contrast
- Stormy dramatic lighting
- Night/astrophotography

**Compositions:**
- Wide angle for landscapes
- Close-up for details
- Portrait orientation for vertical peaks
- Panoramic for vistas
- Rule of thirds for balance

**Mood Keywords:**
- Awe-inspiring, humbling, epic, intimate
- Dangerous, beautiful, serene, powerful
- Contemplative, adventurous, spiritual, raw

**Aspect Ratios:**
- 16:9 for cinematic landscapes
- 4:5 for social media portraits
- 1:1 for profile images
- 3:2 for traditional photography
- 9:16 for stories/reels
