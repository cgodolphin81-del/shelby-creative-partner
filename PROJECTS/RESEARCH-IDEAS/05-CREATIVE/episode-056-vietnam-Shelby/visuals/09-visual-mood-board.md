## TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Font (Headings)

**Playfair Display**
- Style: Serif, elegant, editorial
- Weights: Regular, Bold, Black
- Use: Episode titles, section headers, quotes
- Why: Evokes tradition, storytelling, sophistication

**Alternatives:**
- Merriweather (free, similar feel)
- Cormorant Garamond (free, more elegant)
- EB Garamond (free, classic)

**Usage:**
```
Episode Titles: Playfair Display Black, 48-72px
Section Headers: Playfair Display Bold, 32-40px
Quotes: Playfair Display Italic, 24-28px
```

---

### Secondary Font (Body)

**Lato**
- Style: Sans-serif, clean, readable
- Weights: Light, Regular, Bold
- Use: Body text, captions, descriptions
- Why: Modern, accessible, works at all sizes

**Alternatives:**
- Open Sans (free, very readable)
- Montserrat (free, slightly more geometric)
- Source Sans Pro (free, professional)

**Usage:**
```
Body Text: Lato Regular, 16-18px
Captions: Lato Light, 14px
Emphasis: Lato Bold, 16-18px
```

---

### Accent Font (Special Use)

**Dancing Script** or **Great Vibes**
- Style: Script, handwritten, flowing
- Use: Pull quotes, special moments, Vietnamese phrases
- Why: Adds humanity, warmth, personal touch

**Usage:**
```
Vietnamese Phrases: Dancing Script Regular, 20-24px
Pull Quotes: Dancing Script Bold, 28-32px
Signatures: Dancing Script Regular, 18-20px
```

---

### Vietnamese Language Support

**Important:** Ensure all fonts support Vietnamese diacritics (á, à, ả, ã, ạ, ă, ắ, etc.)

**Recommended:**
- Playfair Display ✓ (supports Vietnamese)
- Lato ✓ (supports Vietnamese)
- Noto Serif/Sans ✓ (Google Fonts, excellent Vietnamese support)

## IMAGERY STYLE

### Photography Direction

**Overall Aesthetic:**
- Warm, inviting, slightly desaturated
- Film-inspired (Kodak Portra 400 vibe)
- Natural light preferred
- Golden hour = ideal

**Ha Long Bay:**
- Aerial shots emphasize scale
- Water should be emerald/turquoise
- Mist adds mystery
- Include human element (junk boat, kayak) for scale

**Hoi An:**
- Evening/night shots for lanterns
- Long exposures for light trails
- Reflections in water
- Yellow walls + green shutters = iconic

**Hanoi:**
- Street-level perspective
- Capture motion (motorbikes, vendors)
- Morning light in Old Quarter
- Include food (pho, banh mi, egg coffee)

---

### Image Treatment

**Filters/Presets:**
- Warm temperature (+5 to +10)
- Slightly desaturated (-5 to -10)
- Lift shadows slightly (+10)
- Soft contrast (not harsh)
- Subtle vignette (-5)

**Avoid:**
- Oversaturated colors
- Heavy HDR
- Cold/blue tones (unless intentional for contrast)
- Heavy filters that obscure detail

---

### Composition Guidelines

**Rule of Thirds:**
- Place horizon on lower third (Ha Long Bay)
- Place subject on intersection points
- Leave negative space for text overlay

**Leading Lines:**
- Streets of Hanoi (motorbike trails)
- River Thu Bon (Hoi An)
- Karst formations (Ha Long Bay)

**Framing:**
- Doorways and windows (Hoi An shop houses)
- Cave openings (Ha Long Bay)
- Lantern clusters (frame subject)

**Scale:**
- Include people in landscape shots
- Show vastness of Ha Long Bay
- Show narrowness of Hanoi streets

## ACCESSIBILITY GUIDELINES

### Color Contrast

**Minimum Contrast Ratios (WCAG AA):**
- Normal text: 4.5:1
- Large text: 3:1
- UI elements: 3:1

**Tested Combinations:**
- ✓ Limestone Gray on Rice Paper White (7.2:1)
- ✓ White on Ha Long Green (5.8:1)
- ✓ White on Street Red (4.9:1)
- ✗ Lantern Gold on White (2.1:1) - Don't use for text

**Fix Low Contrast:**
- Darken Lantern Gold for text use
- Add text shadow or outline
- Use on darker backgrounds only

---

### Alt Text Guidelines

**Images:**
- Describe content, not just "image of"
- Include colors, mood, key elements
- Keep under 125 characters when possible

**Examples:**
- ✓ "Sunrise over Ha Long Bay, limestone karsts rising from emerald water, traditional junk boat in foreground"
- ✗ "Beautiful Vietnam landscape"

**Decorative Images:**
- Use empty alt="" for purely decorative images
- Screen readers will skip them

---

### Motion & Animation

**Guidelines:**
- Avoid rapid flashing (seizure risk)
- Provide pause/stop for animations
- Keep animations subtle and slow
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion (CSS)

**Safe Animations:**
- Slow fades
- Gentle slides
- Subtle scale changes

**Avoid:**
- Rapid blinking
- Spinning elements
- Parallax scrolling (can cause nausea)
