## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **16:9** — Standard landscape, YouTube thumbnails, presentations
- **21:9** — Cinematic widescreen, dramatic panoramas
- **4:3** — Traditional photography, Instagram landscape
- **3:4** — Instagram portrait, mobile-first
- **9:16** — Stories, TikTok, Reels, vertical video
- **1:1** — Square, Instagram feed, profile images

### Style Parameters (Midjourney)
- `--style raw` — More photorealistic, less artistic interpretation
- `--stylize 500-750` — Higher values = more artistic freedom
- `--q 2` — Higher quality, longer render time

### Best Practices
1. **Iterate** — Generate multiple variations of promising prompts
2. **Combine** — Mix elements from different prompts for unique results
3. **Customize** — Adjust Shelby's appearance to match your brand
4. **Seasonal** — Create sets for different seasons/weather
5. **Consistency** — Use similar parameters across a series for cohesive look
