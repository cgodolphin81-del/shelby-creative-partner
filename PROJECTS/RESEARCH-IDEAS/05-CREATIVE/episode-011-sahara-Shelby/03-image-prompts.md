## Usage Notes

### Aspect Ratios
- **Landscape (16:9):** Best for vast dune scenes, horizons, night skies
- **Portrait (4:5 or 9:16):** Best for human subjects, vertical dune faces, social media
- **Square (1:1):** Best for details, textures, intimate scenes
- **Ultra-wide (21:9):** Best for epic landscapes, immersive scenes

### Style Modifiers
Add these to prompts for specific aesthetics:
- `National Geographic photography style` — documentary, authentic
- `Fine art landscape photography` — artistic, contemplative
- `Cinematic color grading` — dramatic, film-like
- `Minimalist composition` — clean, simple
- `Award-winning travel photography` — professional, polished
- `Documentary photography` — authentic, unposed
- `Fine art photography` — artistic, gallery-worthy

### Technical Specs for Best Results
- **Resolution:** Request 8K or highest available
- **Detail:** Specify "hyper-detailed" or "intricate detail"
- **Lighting:** Always specify time of day and lighting conditions
- **Mood:** Include emotional descriptors (contemplative, awe-inspiring, peaceful)
- **Composition:** Mention framing preferences (wide-angle, telephoto, macro)
