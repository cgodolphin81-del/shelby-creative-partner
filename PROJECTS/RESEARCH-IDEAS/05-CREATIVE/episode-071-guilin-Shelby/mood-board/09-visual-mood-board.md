## 📐 TYPOGRAPHY

### Primary Typeface Pairing

#### Headings: **Playfair Display**
- **Style:** Elegant serif with high contrast
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Italic (400), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** Episode titles, chapter headings, quote cards
- **Why:** Evokes tradition, elegance, Chinese calligraphy influence

#### Body: **Lato** or **Open Sans**
- **Style:** Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Weights:** Light (300), Regular (400), Bold (700)
- **Usage:** Body text, captions, social media
- **Why:** Modern readability, complements Playfair

### Alternative Pairings

#### Option 2: Traditional Feel
- **Headings:** Cormorant Garamond
- **Body:** Source Sans Pro
- **Vibe:** More literary, book-like

#### Option 3: Modern Clean
- **Headings:** Montserrat
- **Body:** Lato
- **Vibe:** Contemporary, minimal

## 🖼️ TEXTURE & PATTERN LIBRARY

### Primary Textures

#### 1. Rice Paper Texture
- **Description:** Subtle fibrous texture of traditional Chinese paper
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, overlays, quote cards
- **Opacity:** 10-30% overlay
- **Feeling:** Traditional, artistic, soft

#### 2. Watercolor Bleed
- **Description:** Soft edges where watercolor meets paper
- **Usage:** Transitions, artistic elements
- **Style:** Organic, flowing
- **Feeling:** Artistic, contemplative

#### 3. Ink Wash Gradient
- **Description:** Gradation from black to transparent
- **Usage:** Overlays, depth, mood
- **Direction:** Top to bottom or radial
- **Feeling:** Mysterious, traditional

#### 4. Limestone Texture
- **Description:** Subtle karst rock surface
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, title cards
- **Opacity:** 5-15%
- **Feeling:** Ancient, permanent, grounded

- **Usage:** Transitions, Li River scenes

## 📸 IMAGERY STYLES

### Photography Style Guide

#### Landscape Photography
- **Composition:** Wide, epic scale
- **Lighting:** Golden hour, misty morning
- **Mood:** Contemplative, majestic
- **Editing:** Natural, slightly muted, emphasize mist
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (video), 4:5 (Instagram), 3:2 (general)

#### Portrait/People Photography
- **Style:** Documentary, candid
- **Lighting:** Natural, soft
- **Focus:** Eyes, hands, activities
- **Mood:** Respectful, intimate
- **Editing:** Warm tones, film-like

#### Detail Shots
- **Subjects:** Textures, hands, tools, food
- **Composition:** Close-up, intimate
- **Lighting:** Side lighting for texture
- **Mood:** Tactile, authentic
- **Editing:** Sharp, detailed

### AI Image Generation Style

#### Photorealistic
- **Prompt Keywords:** "photorealistic, National Geographic style, cinematic"
- **Settings:** Midjourney --style raw --q 2
- **Use:** Hero images, travel guide, website

#### Artistic/Interpretive
- **Prompt Keywords:** "ink wash painting, watercolor, Chinese art style"
- **Settings:** Midjourney --stylize 500-750
- **Use:** Quote cards, social media, artistic posts

#### Minimalist
- **Prompt Keywords:** "minimalist, negative space, simple composition"
- **Settings:** Midjourney --stylize 250
- **Use:** Quote cards, clean social posts

### Image Treatment

#### Consistent Grading
- **Exposure:** Slightly underexposed (moody)
- **Contrast:** Moderate (not harsh)
- **Highlights:** Recovered (preserve detail)
- **Shadows:** Lifted slightly (not crushed)
- **Saturation:** Muted (not oversaturated)
- **Temperature:** Slightly cool (misty feel)

#### Vignette
- **Amount:** Subtle (10-20%)
- **Purpose:** Focus attention, cinematic feel
- **Style:** Soft, gradual

#### Grain (Optional)
- **Amount:** Minimal (5-10%)
- **Purpose:** Film-like quality
- **Use:** Selective, not on all images

## 🎭 COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Rule of Thirds
- **Application:** Place key elements on third lines
- **Horizon:** On upper or lower third, never center
- **Subjects:** At intersection points

### Leading Lines
- **Examples:** River, paths, terrace edges
- **Purpose:** Guide eye through image
- **Direction:** Toward main subject

### Framing
- **Natural Frames:** Mountain arches, tree branches
- **Purpose:** Focus attention, add depth
- **Examples:** Moon Hill arch, cave openings

### Negative Space
- **Importance:** Critical for this episode's aesthetic
- **Usage:** Mist, sky, water as negative space
- **Balance:** 40-60% negative space in contemplative images

### Layering
- **Foreground:** Bamboo, boats, people
- **Midground:** River, fields, villages
- **Background:** Karst peaks, sky
- **Purpose:** Create depth, scale

## 💡 LIGHTING STYLES

### Golden Hour
- **Time:** Sunrise (5:30-7:00 AM), Sunset (6:00-7:30 PM)
- **Quality:** Warm, soft, directional
- **Color:** Sunset Gold, warm oranges
- **Mood:** Hopeful, magical, warm
- **Best For:** Hero landscapes, portraits

### Blue Hour
- **Time:** 20-30 minutes after sunset
- **Quality:** Cool, even, soft
- **Color:** Deep Azure, River Blue
- **Mood:** Contemplative, mysterious, calm
- **Best For:** City lights, water reflections

### Misty Morning
- **Time:** Dawn to mid-morning
- **Quality:** Diffused, soft, ethereal
- **Color:** Mist Grey, muted tones
- **Mood:** Mysterious, contemplative, dreamlike
- **Best For:** Karst peaks, atmospheric shots

### Overcast
- **Time:** Any time on cloudy days
- **Quality:** Even, no harsh shadows
- **Color:** Muted, natural
- **Mood:** Calm, consistent
- **Best For:** Rice terraces, villages, details

### Night/Lantern
- **Time:** Evening, after dark
- **Quality:** Warm point sources, dark shadows
- **Color:** Lantern Orange against deep shadows
- **Mood:** Intimate, traditional, magical
- **Best For:** Cormorant fishing, West Street
