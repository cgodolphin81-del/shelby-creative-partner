# Episode 072 Tease: Reflection & Return
## From the Dragon's Cradle to...

## 🌅 THE TEASE

There is a pattern in walking.

You go out. You explore. You discover landscapes that change you. And then... you return.

Not because the journey is over. But because some places are not meant to be visited once. They are meant to be revisited. Reunderstood. Relived.

Guilin taught me about presence. About being small in the presence of something vast. About landscapes that shape civilizations.

But there is another lesson waiting.

The lesson of return.

What happens when you go back to a place that changed you? Are you the same? Is it the same? Or does the magic live not in the place itself, but in the space between who you were and who you've become?

Episode 072 explores this question.

We return to a place we've walked before. A landscape that appeared in our earliest episodes. A destination that called to us then... and calls again now.

Because the Infinite Walk is not a straight line. It is a spiral. We circle back. We deepen. We understand more.

The Dragon's Cradle held us. Now, another place waits.

Same mountains. Different eyes.

Same river. New reflections.

Episode 072. Coming soon.
