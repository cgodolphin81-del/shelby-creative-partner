## 🎭 TONE GUIDELINES

**Voice:**
- Warm but not saccharine
- World-weary but hopeful
- Intimate but not confessional
- Observant but not preachy

**Avoid:**
- Clichés ("I'm lovin' it", "The Big Apple")
- Overused phrases ("city that never sleeps" - use sparingly)
- Tourist-speak ("Must-see!", "Hidden gem!")
- Pretentiousness (keep it grounded)

**Embrace:**
- Specific details (the man with the bodega breakfast)
- Sensory language (sounds, smells, textures)
- Emotional honesty (loneliness, wonder, exhaustion)
- The paradox (crowded but alone, huge but intimate)
