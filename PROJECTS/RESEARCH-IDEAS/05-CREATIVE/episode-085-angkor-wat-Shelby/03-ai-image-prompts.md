## PROMPT USAGE NOTES

### Aspect Ratios
- `--ar 16:9` - Widescreen, cinematic, landscapes
- `--ar 3:2` - Standard photo ratio, versatile
- `--ar 4:3` - Classic photography, social media
- `--ar 4:5` - Portrait orientation, Instagram
- `--ar 1:1` - Square, social media posts
- `--ar 21:9` - Ultra-widescreen, cinematic

### Style Keywords
- Photorealistic, cinematic, documentary
- Fine art, atmospheric, moody
- Travel photography, National Geographic style
- Architectural photography, cultural heritage

### Lighting
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
- Blue hour (pre-dawn/twilight)
- Dappled light (jungle canopy)
- Dramatic side lighting
- Soft diffused light

### Tips for Best Results
1. Adjust prompt details for specific AI model
2. Combine elements from multiple prompts
3. Add specific camera/lens details for realism
4. Experiment with different aspect ratios
5. Use negative prompts to remove unwanted elements
6. Iterate and refine based on initial results
