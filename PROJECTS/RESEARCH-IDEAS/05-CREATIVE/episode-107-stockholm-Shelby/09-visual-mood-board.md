## Lighting

### Natural Light

**Golden Hour (Summer)**
- 8-10 PM, extended golden light
- Warm, slanting rays
- Long shadows
- Buildings glow gold

**Blue Hour (Winter)**
- 2-4 PM, twilight blue
- Cool, atmospheric
- Candles become visible
- Magical, intimate

**Midnight Sun (June-July)**
- 11 PM-2 AM, perpetual twilight
- Lavender-pink sky
- No true darkness
- Dreamlike quality

**Winter Dawn**
- 8-9 AM, slow sunrise
- Pale pink and blue
- Low angle light
- Crisp, cold clarity

### Artificial Light

**Candlelight**
- Warm, flickering glow
- Multiple candles together
- Intimate, cozy
- Essential for winter atmosphere

**Pendant Lamps**
- Scandinavian design (PH lamps, etc.)
- Warm white (2700-3000K)
- Soft, diffused light
- Minimalist fixtures

**Street Lamps**
- Warm yellow (not LED cold white)
- Pools of light on cobblestones
- Atmospheric, nostalgic
- Creates path through darkness

**Museum Lighting**
- Dramatic spotlights
- High contrast
- Vasa ship: dim ambient, focused beams
- Creates reverence and awe

## Typography

### Primary Typeface (Headers/Display)

**Option A: Serif (Traditional, Elegant)**
- **Playfair Display** (Google Fonts, free)
- Classic, editorial feel
- High contrast, elegant
- Works well for: Titles, quotes, pull quotes

**Option B: Sans-Serif (Modern, Clean)**
- **Inter** (Google Fonts, free)
- Clean, readable, modern
- Scandinavian design aesthetic
- Works well for: Headers, UI, clean layouts

### Secondary Typeface (Body)

**Option A: Clean Sans**
- **Source Sans Pro** (Google Fonts, free)
- Highly readable
- Neutral, professional
- Works well for: Body text, show notes

**Option B: Warm Sans**
- **Lato** (Google Fonts, free)
- Slightly rounded, friendly
- Approachable but professional
- Works well for: Social media, captions

### Usage Guidelines

**Headlines:**
- Playfair Display Bold or Inter Bold
- Large, confident
- Generous letter spacing

**Body Text:**
- Source Sans Pro Regular or Lato Regular
- Comfortable reading size (16px+ digital)
- Good line height (1.5-1.6)

**Quotes:**
- Playfair Display Italic
- Larger than body text
- Ample white space around

**Captions/Meta:**
- Inter or Source Sans Pro Light
- Smaller, subtle
- Lower contrast

## Photography Style

### Composition

**Minimalism**
- Clean lines, simple compositions
- Negative space is your friend
- One focal point, not many
- Scandinavian design principle

**Symmetry & Balance**
- Centered compositions
- Reflections in water
- Architectural symmetry
- Calm, ordered feeling

**Leading Lines**
- Cobblestone streets
- Archipelago horizons
- Museum galleries
- Draw viewer into scene

**Framing**
- Doorways and arches
- Windows looking out
- Natural frames (trees, rocks)
- Creates depth and context

### Color Treatment

**Natural, Not Oversaturated**
- True-to-life colors
- Slight warmth in shadows
- Preserve highlights
- No heavy filters

**Cool Shadows, Warm Highlights**
- Shadows: Slight blue/cyan tint
- Highlights: Warm golden tone
- Creates depth and dimension
- Nordic light quality

**Consistent Color Grade**
- Develop preset/LUT for series
- Apply consistently across all images
- Slightly desaturated greens
- Warm yellows/oranges, cool blues

### Subject Matter

**Architecture**
- Gamla Stan buildings (colorful facades)

- Details: doorways, windows, textures
- Both wide shots and intimate details

**Landscape**
- Archipelago aerials
- Individual islands/skerries
- Water and sky
- Seasonal variations

**People**
- Candid, not posed
- Fika moments (hands, coffee, pastries)
- Walking shots (from behind, in motion)
- Silhouettes against light

**Objects**
- Candles (lit, glowing)
- Coffee cups, pastries

## Accessibility

### Color Contrast

**WCAG AA Standards:**
- Normal text: 4.5:1 contrast ratio minimum
- Large text: 3:1 contrast ratio minimum
- UI elements: 3:1 contrast ratio minimum

**Testing:**
- Use WebAIM Contrast Checker
- Test with color blindness simulators
- Ensure readability in various lighting

### Alt Text

**Images:**
- Descriptive, not decorative
- Include key visual elements
- Mention mood/atmosphere when relevant
- Keep concise but informative

**Example:**
"Cobblestone street in Gamla Stan, Stockholm. Colorful 17th-century buildings in ochre yellow and burnt orange line the narrow alley. Early morning light creates long shadows. No people visible."

### Video Captions

**Requirements:**
- Accurate, synchronized captions
- Include sound effects and music cues
- Speaker identification (if multiple)
- Platform-native captions (not burned-in only)

- Consistent color palette

- Unified voice and tone

**Adapt For:**
- Platform-specific formats (square, vertical, horizontal)
- Audience expectations (LinkedIn more professional, Instagram more visual)
