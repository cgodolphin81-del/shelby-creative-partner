## Typography

### Primary Font (Headings)
**Font:** Montserrat or similar geometric sans-serif
**Weights:** Bold (700), Semi-Bold (600)
**Usage:** Episode titles, headers, quotes

**Why:** Clean, modern, readable, works well with ancient/modern contrast

---

### Secondary Font (Body)
**Font:** Merriweather or similar serif
**Weights:** Regular (400), Italic (400 italic)
**Usage:** Body text, descriptions, longer quotes

**Why:** Readable, classic, adds warmth and humanity

---

### Accent Font (Special Use)
**Font:** Playfair Display or similar elegant serif
**Weights:** Bold (700), Italic (700 italic)
**Usage:** Pull quotes, special graphics, episode numbers

**Why:** Elegant, distinctive, adds sophistication

## Composition Guidelines

### Rule of Thirds
- Place key elements (Machu Picchu, Huayna Picchu) at intersection points
- Horizon lines on upper or lower third (not center)
- Creates dynamic, balanced compositions

### Leading Lines
- Use Inca paths, terraces, mountain ridges as leading lines
- Draw eye toward focal point
- Creates depth and journey feeling

### Framing
- Use Temple of Three Windows as natural frame
- Frame views with stone doorways
- Creates depth and context

### Scale
- Include human figures for scale (optional)
- Shows vastness of landscape
- Creates relatability

## Image Treatment

### Filters/Presets

**Option 1: Warm & Golden (Recommended)**
- Increase warmth (+5-10)
- Slight vignette (-10)
- Increase clarity (+10)
- Warm highlights, cool shadows
- Mimics golden hour

**Option 2: Moody & Mysterious**
- Decrease exposure (-5)
- Increase contrast (+15)
- Cool tones (blue tint in shadows)
- Heavy vignette (-20)
- Desaturate greens slightly

**Option 3: Natural & Clean**
- Minimal editing
- True-to-life colors
- Slight clarity boost (+5)
- Natural contrast
- Best for travel guide content

```
--ar 4:5 (Instagram portrait)
--ar 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
--v 6 (latest version)
```

**Example:**

## Accessibility

### Alt Text Guidelines
- Describe key visual elements
- Include context (location, time of day)
- Keep under 125 characters when possible
- Example: "Machu Picchu ruins at sunrise with golden light on stone temples and Huayna Picchu peak in background"

### Color Contrast
- Ensure text meets WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 ratio minimum)
- Use contrast checker tools
- Avoid text on busy backgrounds
- Add text shadows or overlays if needed

### Screen Reader Friendly
- Don't rely on color alone to convey meaning
- Use descriptive filenames
- Include captions on social media when relevant
