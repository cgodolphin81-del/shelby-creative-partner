## Quick Overview

Episode 077 takes listeners on a journey to Zhangjiajie, China—the real-life inspiration for Avatar's floating mountains. This episode explores:

- 🏔️ The Avatar mountains: 3,000+ quartz-sandstone pillars rising from mist
- 🌉 The Glass Bridge: 300 meters above a canyon, facing fear on transparent glass
- ⛰️ Heaven's Gate: 999 steps to a natural arch 131 meters high
- 🌫️ The mist that reveals more than it hides
- 🐒 What the macaques know that we've forgotten
- 💚 The Jade Empire: 380 million years of nature's artistry

**Central Theme:** We are all visitors here. The mountain remembers.
