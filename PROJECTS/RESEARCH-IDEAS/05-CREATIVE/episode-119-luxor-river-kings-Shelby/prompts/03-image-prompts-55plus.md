## 🎨 STYLE REFERENCE NOTES

### Color Palette (Consistent Across All Images)
- **Lapis Blue:** #1E3A8A, #2563EB
- **Gold Leaf:** #D4AF37, #F4D03F
- **Sandstone:** #D2B48C, #C4A777
- **Nile Green:** #2F5233, #4A7C59
- **Papyrus Beige:** #F5E6D3, #E8D5B5

### Lighting Moods
- **Dawn:** Cool blues warming to gold
- **Golden Hour:** Warm amber, long shadows
- **Midday:** Harsh, high contrast
- **Twilight:** Deep blues, artificial warmth
- **Night:** Mystery, selective illumination

### Composition Tips
- Use leading lines (columns, corridors, river)
- Frame within frames (doorways, arches)
- Include human scale for monumentality
- Leave negative space for text overlays
- Consider rule of thirds for social media
