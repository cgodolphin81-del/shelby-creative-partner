### Podcast Platforms (Apple, Spotify, etc.)

**Main Description:**
```
What if you could walk on the sky?

At Salar de Uyuni in Bolivia, the earth becomes a mirror. A thin layer of water transforms 10,000 square kilometers of salt flat into the world's largest reflection. Up is down. Sky is earth. Infinite meets infinite.

In Episode 096 of The Infinite Walk, "The Crystal Desert," we journey to one of Earth's most surreal landscapes. We explore the mirror effect that defies perception, the perfect hexagons of the dry season, the ancient cacti of Incahuasi Island, and the flamingos of Laguna Colorada.

Beneath the salt lies 21 million tons of lithium—the largest deposit on Earth. Enough to power a civilization. But extraction threatens the very ecosystems that make this place miraculous.

This is more than a travel story. It's a meditation on reflection, truth, and what it means to stand at the edge of understanding.

"The mirror does not deceive. It reveals."

Duration: 20 minutes
Narration: Shelby
```
