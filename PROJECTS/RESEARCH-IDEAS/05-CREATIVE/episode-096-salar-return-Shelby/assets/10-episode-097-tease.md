## Location Rationale (Why Japan?)

### Thematic Connection to Episode 096:

**Bolivia (Episode 096):**
- Vast, open, external infinity
- Mirror that shows everything
- Bright, white, overwhelming
- Natural wonder
- External reflection

**Japan (Episode 097):**
- Contained, internal infinity
- Zen garden that shows nothing (emptiness)
- Subtle, nuanced, quiet
- Cultural/philosophical depth
- Internal reflection

**The Journey:**
- From external to internal
- From vast to intimate
- From bright to shaded
- From showing everything to showing nothing
- Both are meditations on perception and truth

**Narrative Arc:**
```
Episode 096: "Look at everything the mirror reveals."
Episode 097: "Look at what the empty garden reveals."
```

### Working Title: "The Quiet Between"

### Key Themes:
- Stillness and silence
- Impermanence (mono no aware)
- The beauty of endings
- Tradition vs. modernity
- Internal vs. external reflection

### Key Locations:
- Kyoto temples (Kinkaku-ji, Ryoan-ji, Fushimi Inari)
- Arashiyama bamboo grove

- Miyajima's floating torii

- Tea ceremony (mindfulness, ritual)
- Zen gardens (emptiness, meditation)
- Cherry blossoms (impermanence, beauty)
- Torii gates (thresholds, transitions)
