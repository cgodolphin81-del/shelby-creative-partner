## 📝 Usage Notes

**Aspect Ratios:**
- Social media posts: 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (portrait)
- Stories/Reels: 9:16 (vertical)
- YouTube thumbnails: 16:9 (horizontal)
- Blog headers: 3:1 or 16:9

**Style Keywords to Add:**
- `National Geographic photography`
- `BBC Earth documentary style`
- `Travel + Leisure magazine`
- `Cinematic lighting`
- `8K resolution, ultra-detailed`
- `Natural lighting, golden hour`
- `Shallow depth of field, bokeh`

**Negative Prompts (what to avoid):**
- No people in wildlife shots (unless specified)
- No plastic or trash visible
- No power lines or modern infrastructure (unless urban scene)
- No unrealistic colors (keep natural)
- No blurry or low-quality elements

**Model Recommendations:**
- Midjourney v6+ for photorealistic wildlife
- DALL-E 3 for conceptual/illustrative
- Stable Diffusion XL for control and customization
