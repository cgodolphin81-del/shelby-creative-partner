### Imagery Suggestions

**Tease Images (Don't Reveal Too Much):**

✅ **DO Use:**
- Silhouettes (cactus, rock formations)
- Extreme close-ups (texture of stone, cactus flower)
- Abstract colors (red/orange gradients)
- Shadows and mystery
- Water in unexpected desert places

❌ **DON'T Use:**
- Iconic, immediately recognizable landmarks
- Clear location names in images
- Obvious state/country identifiers
- Overused stock desert photos

**Specific Ideas:**
- Cactus flower blooming (macro, species unclear)
- Slot canyon walls (abstract, colorful)
- Desert spring with turquoise water
- Petroglyphs (ancient art, location ambiguous)
- Sunset over silhouetted rock formations
