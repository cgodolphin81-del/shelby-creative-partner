## PROMPT USAGE NOTES

**Aspect Ratios:**
- `--ar 16:9` - Standard landscape (YouTube, presentations)
- `--ar 3:4` - Portrait (Instagram, mobile)
- `--ar 4:5` - Vertical portrait
- `--ar 1:1` - Square (social media)

**Style Modifiers:**
- `--v 6` - Midjourney v6 for photorealism
- `--style raw` - Less artistic interpretation, more photographic
- `--s 250` - Stylization level (default 100, higher = more artistic)

**Tips:**
- Adjust lighting (golden hour, overcast, night) for mood
- Add season indicators (summer wildflowers, winter snow)
- Specify location for authenticity (Denali, Kenai, etc.)
- Use for episode thumbnails, social posts, blog images
