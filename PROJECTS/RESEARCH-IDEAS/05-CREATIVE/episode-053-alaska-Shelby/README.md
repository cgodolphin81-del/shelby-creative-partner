## 🎯 KEY THEMES

### Primary Themes
1. **Human Smallness** - The humility that comes from encountering true wilderness
2. **Deep Time** - Glaciers, ancient landscapes, geological history
3. **Refusal** - Wilderness that cannot and will not be tamed
4. **Witness** - The responsibility to see and remember
5. **Temporary Presence** - We are visitors, not owners

### Secondary Themes
- Indigenous wisdom and continuous culture
- Climate change and glacier retreat
- The ancient human-dog bond
- Aurora as holy encounter
- Wildlife ethics and respect

## 🎙️ PRODUCTION NOTES

### Recording
- **Voice:** Warm, contemplative, measured pace
- **Environment:** Quiet space, minimal reverb
- **Format:** WAV or high-quality MP3
- **Normalization:** -16 LUFS (podcast standard)

### Sound Design
- **Bed:** Subtle wind/tundra ambience throughout
- **Effects:** Ice cracking, glacier calving, wildlife (sparingly)
- **Music:** Ambient, cinematic, builds and recedes
- **Pauses:** Critical for atmosphere (2-4 seconds at key moments)

### Music Suggestions
- Opening: Sparse ice/strings
- Section 1: Building tension
- Section 2: Soft contemplative
- Section 3: Cold crystalline
- Section 4: Ethereal shimmering
- Section 5: Rhythmic warm
- Section 6: Return to theme
- Closing: Fade out
