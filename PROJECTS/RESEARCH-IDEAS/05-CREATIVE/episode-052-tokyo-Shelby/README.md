- Emergency info
- Pro tips

**Use for:** Show notes, blog post, listener resources, email content

**Key Info:**
- JR Pass 7-day: ¥50,000 (~$330 USD)
- Daily budget: $55-80 (budget), $130-200 (mid-range), $330+ (luxury)
- Best times: Spring (cherry blossoms), Autumn (foliage)
- Avoid: Golden Week (late April/early May), Obon (mid-August)
