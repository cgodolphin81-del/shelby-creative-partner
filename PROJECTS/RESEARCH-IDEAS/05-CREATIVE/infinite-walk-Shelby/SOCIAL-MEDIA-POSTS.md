## 🎨 Visual Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep blues, soft grays, warm earth tones
- **Accent:** Golden hour oranges, sunrise pinks
- **Mood:** Calm, contemplative, inviting

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Quotes:** Elegant serif or handwritten
- **Body:** Readable, accessible

### Image Style
- AI-generated with consistent aesthetic
- Moody, atmospheric, cinematic
- Avoid overly bright or saturated looks
