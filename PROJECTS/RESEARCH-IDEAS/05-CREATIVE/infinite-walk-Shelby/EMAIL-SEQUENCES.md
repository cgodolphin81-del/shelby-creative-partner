## 🎯 Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly (1 max)
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Personalize when possible

### Send Times
- **Welcome sequence:** Automated (immediate, D3, D7, D14, D21)
- **Weekly episodes:** Tuesday 7am, Friday 7am (subscriber timezone)
- **Monthly newsletter:** First of month, 10am

### Segmentation Ideas
- New subscribers (0-30 days)
- Active listeners (opened last 3 emails)
- Inactive (no opens in 60+ days)
- Superfans (replied, reviewed, or shared)
