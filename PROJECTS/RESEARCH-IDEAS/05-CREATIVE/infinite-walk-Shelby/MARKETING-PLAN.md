# The Infinite Walk - Marketing Plan 📣

## 30-60-90 Day Podcast Launch Strategy

**Project:** The Infinite Walk (120 Episodes)  
**Launch Date:** TBD  
**Marketing Lead:** Shelby & Charmaine

---

## 📅 Phase 1: Days 1-30 (Foundation & Pre-Launch)

### Week 1-2: Infrastructure Setup
- [ ] **Podcast Hosting:** Choose and configure hosting platform (Libsyn, Podbean, or Anchor)
- [ ] **RSS Feed:** Generate and validate RSS feed
- [ ] **Podcast Directories:** Submit to:
  - [ ] Apple Podcasts
  - [ ] Spotify
  - [ ] Google Podcasts
  - [ ] Amazon Music
  - [ ] Stitcher
  - [ ] iHeartRadio
- [ ] **Website:** Launch dedicated Infinite Walk website
  - [ ] Domain registration
  - [ ] Hosting setup
  - [ ] Episode landing pages
  - [ ] Email capture form
- [ ] **Social Media:** Claim handles across all platforms
  - [ ] Instagram: @TheInfiniteWalk
  - [ ] Twitter/X: @InfiniteWalkPod
  - [ ] TikTok: @InfiniteWalk
  - [ ] Pinterest: The Infinite Walk
  - [ ] YouTube: The Infinite Walk

### Week 3-4: Content Preparation
- [ ] **Record First 10 Episodes:** Batch record episodes 1-10
- [ ] **Generate Images:** Create AI visuals for episodes 1-10 (550+ images)
- [ ] **Create Trailers:** Produce 3 trailer variations (30s, 60s, 90s)
- [ ] **Design Assets:** Create cover art, social templates, email headers
- [ ] **Email List:** Set up email service provider (ConvertKit, Mailchimp)
- [ ] **Lead Magnet:** Create free downloadable (e.g., "10 Walks to Start Your Journey")

### Week 5-6: Pre-Launch Buzz
- [ ] **Teaser Campaign:** Begin social media teasers
  - Daily countdown posts
  - Behind-the-scenes content
  - Location reveals
- [ ] **Email Sequence:** Send to early subscribers
  - Announcement email
  - Story behind the project
  - Exclusive first listen offer
- [ ] **Press Outreach:** Contact podcast blogs and travel publications
- [ ] **Influencer Outreach:** Identify and contact 20-30 micro-influencers

---

## 📅 Phase 2: Days 31-60 (Launch & Momentum)

### Week 7-8: LAUNCH WEEK
- [ ] **Episode 1 Release:** Publish first episode across all platforms
- [ ] **Launch Day Push:**
  - Social media blitz (all platforms)
  - Email to full list
  - Ask friends/family to subscribe and review
- [ ] **Live Event:** Host Instagram Live or Twitter Spaces Q&A
- [ ] **Paid Ads:** Begin targeted social media ads ($10-20/day)
  - Instagram Stories
  - Facebook feeds
  - Pinterest promoted pins

### Week 9-10: Build Momentum
- [ ] **Release Schedule:** Publish 2 episodes per week (Tuesdays & Fridays)
- [ ] **Cross-Promotion:** Appear on 3-5 other podcasts as guest
- [ ] **User-Generated Content:** Encourage listeners to share their walks
  - Create hashtag: #MyInfiniteWalk
  - Feature listener stories
- [ ] **Reviews Campaign:** Ask listeners for Apple Podcast reviews
- [ ] **Content Marketing:**
  - Write 2 blog posts about the project
  - Guest post on travel/meditation blogs
  - LinkedIn articles about the creative process

### Week 11-12: Sustain & Optimize
- [ ] **Analyze Metrics:** Review download numbers, listener retention, demographics
- [ ] **Optimize:** Adjust release times, episode length, topics based on data
- [ ] **Community Building:** Launch Facebook Group or Discord server
- [ ] **Merchandise:** Design and launch simple merch (stickers, prints)
- [ ] **Partnership Outreach:** Contact meditation apps for potential licensing

---

## 📅 Phase 3: Days 61-90 (Growth & Monetization)

### Week 13-14: Expand Reach
- [ ] **YouTube Launch:** Begin posting video versions with AI imagery
- [ ] **TikTok Strategy:** Post daily short-form content (quotes, visuals, behind-scenes)
- [ ] **Pinterest Strategy:** Create 100+ pins linking to episodes
- [ ] **SEO Optimization:** Optimize website and show notes for search
- [ ] **Email Growth:** Run lead generation ads to grow email list

### Week 15-16: Monetization
- [ ] **Sponsorship Pitch Deck:** Create media kit with listener demographics
- [ ] **Sponsor Outreach:** Contact 10-15 potential sponsors (travel, wellness, tech)
- [ ] **Patreon Launch:** Offer bonus content for supporters
  - Early access to episodes
  - Extended cuts
  - Monthly live walks
- [ ] **Affiliate Partnerships:** Join affiliate programs for travel gear, books, apps

### Week 17-18: Scale & Plan Season 2
- [ ] **Listener Survey:** Gather feedback on favorite episodes, desired topics
- [ ] **Season 2 Planning:** Outline next 50-100 episodes
- [ ] **Live Events:** Plan virtual or in-person walking events
- [ ] **Book Proposal:** Consider compiling essays into a book
- [ ] **Course Creation:** Develop "Create Your Own Contemplative Walk" course

## 🚨 Crisis Management

### If Launch is Slow
- Increase ad spend temporarily
- Reach out to personal network more aggressively
- Consider delaying full launch to build more pre-launch buzz
- Partner with established podcast for cross-promotion

### If Negative Feedback
- Respond graciously and professionally
- Use feedback to improve future episodes
- Don't engage with trolls
- Focus on positive reviews and listener love
