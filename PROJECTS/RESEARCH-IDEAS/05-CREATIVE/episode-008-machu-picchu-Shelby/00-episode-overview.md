## 📊 KEY FACTS (For Show Notes & Promotion)

**Quick Reference:**
- **Built:** ~1450 CE by Pachacutec Inca
- **Rediscovered:** 1911 by Hiram Bingham
- **Hidden From:** Spanish conquistadors (400+ years)
- **Elevation:** 2,453m (8,048 ft)
- **Daily Capacity:** 4,500–5,600 visitors
- **Ticket Cost:** $50–75 USD
- **Booking:** 3–6 months ahead (peak season)
- **Best Time:** April–October (dry season)
- **UNESCO:** 1983
- **Seven Wonders:** 2007

## 🎧 AUDIO PRODUCTION NOTES

### Voice Direction
- Warm, contemplative, unhurried
- Allow silence to breathe — don't rush pauses
- Emphasize contrasts: ancient/modern, brief/eternal, stone/mist
- Natural pacing — like telling a story to a friend

### Sound Design
- **Opening:** Mountain wind, distant birds, soft Andean instrumentation
- **Transitions:** Subtle music shifts to match emotional beats
- **Ambient:** Occasional bird calls, wind, distant water
- **Closing:** Music swells gently, then fades

### Pacing
- **Total Runtime:** ~15:00 minutes
- **Word Count:** ~2,100 words
- **Speaking Pace:** ~140 words/minute (contemplative)
- **Pauses:** Marked in script — honor them
