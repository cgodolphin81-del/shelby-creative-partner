## 📐 DESIGN GUIDELINES

### Typography

**Primary Font (Headings):**
```
Montserrat Bold
or
Playfair Display
```
- Use for: Titles, episode numbers, major headings
- Weight: 600-700
- Tracking: Slightly expanded (+10-20)

**Secondary Font (Body):**
```
Open Sans
or
Lato Regular
```
- Use for: Descriptions, body text, captions
- Weight: 400
- Line height: 1.5-1.6

**Accent Font (Special Moments):**
```
Cormorant Garamond Italic
```
- Use for: Quotes, poetic text, episode titles
- Weight: 400-500
- Style: Italic for emphasis

---

### Layout Principles

**1. Breathable Space**
- Generous margins (minimum 10% of canvas)
- Let images breathe
- White space = luxury = contemplation

**2. Hierarchy**
- Episode number small and subtle
- Title prominent
- Supporting text secondary
- Never compete with imagery

**3. Consistency**
- Same font pairings across all assets
- Consistent color usage
- Recognizable branding

**4. Contrast**
- Light text on dark (or vice versa)
- Ensure readability at small sizes
- Test on mobile devices

## 🎬 VIDEO/ANIMATION STYLE

### Motion Principles

**Pace:** Slow, contemplative, deliberate
**Transitions:** Soft fades, gentle pans, slow zooms
**Duration:** 15 minutes total (matches audio)

### Movement Patterns

1. **The Reveal**
   - Start: Mist-covered
   - Action: Slow fade/clear
   - End: Full vista revealed
   - Duration: 3-5 seconds

2. **The Pan**
   - Start: Detail (stone, llama, window)
   - Action: Slow horizontal or vertical pan
   - End: Wider context
   - Duration: 5-8 seconds

3. **The Zoom**
   - Start: Wide landscape
   - Action: Slow push in
   - End: Intimate detail
   - Duration: 8-12 seconds

4. **The Stillness**
   - Start: Full frame image
   - Action: Subtle parallax or none
   - End: Same, let it breathe
   - Duration: 10-15 seconds

---

### Color Grading

**Overall Look:**
- Desaturated greens (misty, not vibrant)
- Cool shadows (blue-gray)
- Warm highlights (golden hour)
- Soft contrast (not harsh)
- Slight vignette (focus center)

**LUT Recommendations:**
- Kodak 2383 Print (filmic)
- Fujifilm Classic Chrome (muted)
- Custom: Reduce saturation 15%, increase shadows blue

## 📊 ACCESSIBILITY CHECK

### Contrast Ratios
Ensure all text meets WCAG AA standards:
- Normal text: 4.5:1 minimum
- Large text: 3:1 minimum
- UI elements: 3:1 minimum

**Tools:**
- WebAIM Contrast Checker
- Color Oracle (color blindness simulator)
- Stark (Figma/Sketch plugin)

### Alt Text
All images should have descriptive alt text:
- "Machu Picchu ruins emerging from morning mist"
- "Close-up of Inca stone masonry with perfect joints"
- "Llama standing on agricultural terraces at Machu Picchu"
