## 🌟 BONUS: PETRA RESEARCH SNEAK PEEK

**For your own reference as you build Episode 009:**

**Key Petra Facts:**
- Built by the Nabataeans (4th century BCE – 1st century CE)
- Rediscovered by Western world: 1812 (Johann Ludwig Burckhardt)
- UNESCO World Heritage Site: 1985
- New Seven Wonder: 2007
- Location: Ma'an Governorate, Jordan
- Best time to visit: March-May, September-November
- The Treasury (Al-Khazneh): 40m wide, 30m tall
- The Monastery (Ad Deir): Similar size, higher elevation
- The Siq: 1.2 km narrow canyon entrance
- Colors: Rose-red, pink, orange, gold, brown (sandstone)
- Water engineering: Sophisticated dam and channel system

**Episode 009 Angle:**
- Contrast with Machu Picchu (carved vs. built, desert vs. cloud forest)
- The Nabataeans: Traders, artists, mysterious civilization
- Petra by candlelight (night experience)
- The journey through the Siq (anticipation, reveal)
- Modern Bedouin communities (living history)
