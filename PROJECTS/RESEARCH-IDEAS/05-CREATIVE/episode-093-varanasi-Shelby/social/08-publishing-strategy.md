## 🚨 CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue: Cultural Appropriation Concerns**
- Response: Acknowledge, listen, clarify intent
- Action: Emphasize witness role, not expert
- Message: "I am a guest, not a guide. I share my experience, not prescriptions."

**Issue: Cremation Photography Backlash**
- Response: Clarify no photos were taken at cremation ghats
- Action: Reiterate cultural sensitivity guidelines
- Message: "I did not photograph ceremonies. I witnessed with respect."

**Issue: Factual Inaccuracy Claims**
- Response: Verify, correct if wrong, thank contributor
- Action: Update materials if needed
- Message: "Thank you for this correction. I've updated the guide."

**Issue: Negative Reviews**
- Response: Thank for feedback, don't argue
- Action: Learn, improve future episodes
- Message: "I appreciate you listening and sharing your perspective."

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### General
1. **Authenticity over perfection** - Real resonates more than polished
2. **Engagement > Broadcasting** - Reply to every comment (early)
3. **Consistency matters** - Show up daily, even when tired
4. **Listen to your audience** - They'll tell you what they want
5. **Document everything** - Learnings inform Episode 094

### Platform-Specific
1. **Instagram**: Stories drive more engagement than feed
2. **Twitter**: Threads outperform single tweets 3:1
3. **LinkedIn**: Long-form posts (1,000+ chars) perform best
4. **TikTok**: First 3 seconds determine everything
5. **Email**: Subject line is 80% of success

### Timing
1. **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
2. **Best times**: 10 AM - 12 PM (varies by audience)
3. **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends (for B2B)
4. **Test**: Your audience is unique—experiment!

### Content
1. **Video first**: Platforms prioritize video
2. **Carousel posts**: Higher engagement on Instagram/LinkedIn
3. **Quotes**: Highly shareable, low effort
4. **Behind-the-scenes**: Builds connection
5. **User-generated content**: Amplify listener stories
