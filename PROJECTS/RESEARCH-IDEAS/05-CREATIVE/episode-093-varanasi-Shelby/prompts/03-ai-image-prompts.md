## USAGE NOTES

### Recommended Models:
- **Midjourney v6**: Best for photorealistic and artistic shots
- **DALL-E 3**: Good for symbolic and conceptual images
- **Stable Diffusion XL**: Fine-tune for specific cultural accuracy

### Aspect Ratios:
- **16:9**: YouTube thumbnails, video backgrounds, banners
- **3:2**: Blog featured images, standard photography
- **4:3**: Social media, presentations
- **1:1**: Instagram posts, profile images, album art

### Cultural Sensitivity:
- Always maintain respectful distance from cremation imagery
- Never depict faces of mourners or deceased
- Honor temple restrictions (non-Hindus cannot enter certain areas)
- Use symbolic representation for sacred ceremonies
- Consult cultural advisors before publication

### Color Grading:
- Warm tones for dawn/dusk scenes (3200-4500K)
- Cool tones for river/water shots (5500-6500K)
- High contrast for fire imagery
- Soft, ethereal look for spiritual/symbolic images
