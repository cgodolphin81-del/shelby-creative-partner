## Style Notes for All Prompts

### Color Palette
- **Tango scenes:** Deep reds, blacks, gold, warm amber lighting
- **La Boca:** Vibrant blues, yellows, reds, greens (saturated and bold)
- **Recoleta:** Elegant neutrals, stone grays, wrought iron black, tree greens
- **Cafes:** Warm wood tones, vintage brass, soft golden lighting
- **Night scenes:** Deep blacks, neon accents, street light oranges
- **Cemetery:** Stone grays, weathered whites, dramatic shadows

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Passionate and melancholic (tango essence)
- Elegant but worn (city character)
- Warm and intimate (cafe culture)
- Vibrant and chaotic (La Boca)
- Solemn and beautiful (cemetery)
- Cinematic and atmospheric (night scenes)

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 8K where applicable
- **Style:** Photorealistic unless vintage/film specified
- **Lighting:** Dramatic, warm, atmospheric
- **Composition:** Cinematic, thoughtful framing
- **Depth:** Shallow depth of field for portraits, wide for landscapes
