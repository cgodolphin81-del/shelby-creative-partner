## 📊 KEY FACTS & STATISTICS

### For Social Media & Promotion

- **Size:** 2,300 km long, 344,400 km² area
- **Reefs:** 2,900 individual reefs
- **Islands:** 900 islands
- **Species:** 1,500+ fish, 400+ coral, 6 turtle species
- **Age:** Current structure began 8,000 years ago
- **Jobs:** 64,000+ supported by reef tourism
- **Economic Value:** $6.4 billion AUD annually
- **Visitors:** 2+ million annually
- **Bleaching 2024:** 70%+ of reef affected
- **Mass Bleaching Events:** 1998, 2002, 2016, 2017, 2020, 2022, 2024
