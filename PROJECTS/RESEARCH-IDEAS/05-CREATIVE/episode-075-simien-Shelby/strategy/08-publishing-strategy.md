## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Potential Issues & Responses

**Issue:** Episode delayed
**Response:** Honest communication. "Taking extra time to make this one special. Worth the wait."

**Issue:** Negative feedback
**Response:** Acknowledge, don't argue. "Thanks for sharing your perspective. I appreciate you listening."

**Issue:** Technical problems (audio quality, etc.)
**Response:** Own it. "Learning and improving with each episode. Thanks for bearing with me."

**Issue:** Cultural sensitivity concerns
**Response:** Listen, learn, apologize if needed. "I'm committed to telling these stories respectfully. Thank you for the feedback."

**Issue:** Low initial engagement
**Response:** Stay consistent. Keep promoting. Quality finds its audience.
