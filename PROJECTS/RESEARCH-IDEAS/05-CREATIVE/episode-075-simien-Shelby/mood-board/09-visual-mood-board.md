## TYPOGRAPHY

### Headline Fonts
**Primary:** Playfair Display (serif, elegant, editorial)
- Use for: Episode titles, major headings
- Weight: Bold, Semi-bold
- Color: Rock grey (#4A4A4A) or Church gold (#B8860B)

**Alternative:** Montserrat (sans-serif, modern, clean)
- Use for: Modern applications, social media graphics
- Weight: Bold, Extra-bold
- Color: Sky blue (#1E3A5F) or Rock grey (#4A4A4A)

### Body Fonts
**Primary:** Lato (sans-serif, readable, friendly)
- Use for: Body text, captions, descriptions
- Weight: Regular, Light
- Color: Dark grey (#4A4A4A)

**Alternative:** Merriweather (serif, readable, traditional)
- Use for: Long-form content, essays
- Weight: Regular
- Color: Dark grey (#3D3D3D)

### Accent Fonts
**Handwritten Style:** Caveat or Dancing Script
- Use for: Personal notes, quotes, emphasis
- Color: Gelada red (#C41E3A) or Church gold (#DAA520)

## PHOTOGRAPHY STYLE

### Composition
- **Rule of thirds:** Standard, balanced
- **Leading lines:** Trails, escarpment edges, ridges
- **Framing:** Rock formations framing views
- **Scale:** Human figures for size reference
- **Negative space:** Vast sky, empty valleys

### Lighting
- **Golden hour:** Sunrise/sunset (warm, dramatic)
- **Blue hour:** Dawn/dusk (cool, atmospheric)
- **Harsh midday:** High contrast, dramatic shadows
- **Overcast:** Soft, even light (wildlife portraits)
- **Backlit:** Silhouettes, rim lighting

### Processing
- **Natural colors:** True to life, slight enhancement
- **Contrast:** Moderate to high (drama)
- **Saturation:** Slightly muted (authentic feel)
- **Shadows:** Lifted slightly (detail retention)
- **Highlights:** Controlled (prevent blowout)
- **Vignette:** Subtle (focus attention)

---

## VIDEO/MOTION STYLE

### Pacing
- **Slow, deliberate:** Match the contemplative tone
- **Long holds:** Let viewers absorb landscapes
- **Slow zooms:** Create intimacy with subjects
- **Time-lapse:** Clouds, stars, light changes

### Transitions
- **Cross-dissolve:** Smooth, gentle
- **Fade to black:** Section breaks
- **Match cut:** Similar shapes/motions
- **Whip pan:** Energy, movement (sparingly)

### Music/Sound
- **Atmospheric pads:** Ethereal, spacious
- **Traditional Ethiopian:** Subtle, respectful
- **Natural sound:** Wind, birds, gelada calls
- **Silence:** Powerful, used intentionally

## ACCESSIBILITY CONSIDERATIONS

### Color Contrast
- Text on images: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Avoid red/green combinations (color blindness)
- Use patterns + colors for information

### Alt Text
- Describe images for screen readers
- Include: Subject, action, setting, mood
- Example: "Gelada baboon grazing on highland grass at dawn, Simien Mountains, Ethiopia"

### Captions
- All video content: Closed captions
- Social videos: Burned-in captions for sound-off viewing
