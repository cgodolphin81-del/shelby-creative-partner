## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Issue:** Audio quality problems
- **Solution:** Re-record sections, use noise reduction tools (iZotope RX, Audacity)

**Issue:** Low initial downloads
- **Solution:** Activate email list, engage in communities, consider paid promotion

**Issue:** Copyright claims on music
- **Solution:** Use royalty-free music, keep licenses documented

**Issue:** Negative feedback on AI images
- **Solution:** Be transparent about AI use, focus on storytelling over visuals

**Issue:** Platform algorithm changes
- **Solution:** Diversify distribution (podcast + YouTube + blog + email)
