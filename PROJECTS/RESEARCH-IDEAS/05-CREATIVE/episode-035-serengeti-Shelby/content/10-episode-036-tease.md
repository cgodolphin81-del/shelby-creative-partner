## Key Themes for Episode 036

### 1. Ambition vs. Nature
- What does it mean to conquer the desert?
- Is this human ingenuity or hubris?
- The cost of building the impossible

### 2. Transformation
- From fishing villages to global hub
- Oil wealth to tourism/tech economy
- 50 years of radical change

### 3. Engineering Wonder
- Tallest building (Burj Khalifa)
- Artificial islands (Palm, World)
- Indoor ski slope in desert
- Automated metro, driverless trains

### 4. Cultural Complexity
- Expatriate population (85%+)
- Traditional Emirati culture preserved
- East meets West
- Modernity and tradition in tension

### 5. Sustainability Questions
- Water in the desert (desalination)
- Energy consumption
- Green building initiatives
- The future of desert cities

### 6. Human Stories
- The workers who built it
- The entrepreneurs who flocked to it
- The locals navigating change
- The tourists experiencing it
