## 🎯 Key Themes

### Primary Themes
1. **Water as Foundation** - Amsterdam floats on wooden piles; adaptation over resistance
2. **Bicycles as Freedom** - Equality by design; practical autonomy
3. **Tolerance and Memory** - Anne Frank's legacy; tolerance as daily practice
4. **Dutch Soul** - Stubborn optimism; building anyway

### Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Canal blues (#1B3A4B), Brick reds (#8B3A2F), Bike orange (#FF6B35), Tulip colors
- **Mood:** Atmospheric, contemplative, authentic, cinematic
- **Style:** Natural lighting, mix of wide and intimate, not overly touristy

### Tone & Voice
- Poetic but accessible
- Contemplative, not preachy
- Warm, human, genuine
- Respectful of history and culture
