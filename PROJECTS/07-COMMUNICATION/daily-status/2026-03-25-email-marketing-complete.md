## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Client Acquisition
- **Risk:** Difficulty finding first clients
- **Mitigation:** Start with warm network, offer pilot pricing, leverage LinkedIn

### Risk 2: Deliverability Issues
- **Risk:** Emails going to spam
- **Mitigation:** Follow deliverability checklist, warm up domains, monitor sender reputation

### Risk 3: Client Churn
- **Risk:** Clients cancel after 1-2 months
- **Mitigation:** Show clear ROI in reports, proactive communication, deliver results quickly

### Risk 4: Scope Creep
- **Risk:** Clients request unlimited revisions/additions
- **Mitigation:** Clear contracts, defined deliverables, change order process
