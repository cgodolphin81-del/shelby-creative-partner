## 💰 Business Models

### Model A: Build & Rent (Passive Income)
**What:** Build websites, rank them, rent to local businesses  
**Investment:** $100-500 per site + 40 hours build time  
**Revenue:** $500-3,000/month per site  
**Timeline:** 3-6 months to first revenue (SEO ranking)  
**Scale:** 25-50 sites = $37,500-75,000/month  

**Pros:**
- ✅ Passive income after initial build
- ✅ You own the assets
- ✅ Recurring revenue
- ✅ Exit potential (25-40x monthly revenue)
- ✅ Location independent

**Cons:**
- ❌ 3-6 months before first revenue
- ❌ SEO learning curve
- ❌ Requires patience

### Model B: Done-For-You Service (Active Income)
**What:** Build lead gen sites for clients  
**Investment:** Time + expertise  
**Revenue:** $5,000-7,000 one-time + $1,000-1,500/month management  
**Timeline:** 1-2 months to first revenue  
**Scale:** 10 clients = $50,000-70,000/month  

**Pros:**
- ✅ Faster revenue (immediate)
- ✅ Higher per-client revenue
- ✅ No SEO risk (client owns site)
- ✅ Scalable with team

**Cons:**
- ❌ Active work (not passive)
- ❌ Client management required
- ❌ No asset ownership

### Model C: Hybrid (Recommended)
**What:** Build 2-3 sites for yourself + 3-5 for clients  
**Revenue:** $3,000-6,000/month passive + $15,000-25,000/month active  
**Timeline:** Immediate active income + 3-6 months passive  
**Best of Both Worlds**

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: SEO Takes Longer Than Expected
- **Risk:** Site doesn't rank in 3-6 months
- **Mitigation:** Follow proven guides, build quality content, be patient, consider paid ads initially

### Risk 2: Can't Find Rental Partners
- **Risk:** Site ranks but no businesses want to rent
- **Mitigation:** Start outreach before site fully ranks, show projected lead volume, offer trial period

### Risk 3: Google Algorithm Updates
- **Risk:** Rankings drop after algorithm update
- **Mitigation:** Build real assets (quality content, legitimate business), avoid black-hat tactics

### Risk 4: Rental Partner Churn
- **Risk:** Business stops paying or switches partners
- **Mitigation:** Have backup partners lined up, own the asset (can redirect leads), contract terms
