## 🎯 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### **LinkedIn:**
- Post during work hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
- Professional tone
- Longer posts perform well
- Use carousels for tips

### **Instagram:**
- Post when your audience is active (use Insights)
- Use all features: Feed, Stories, Reels
- Hashtags: 15-30 per post
- Engage with comments within 1 hour

### **Facebook:**
- Post in job search groups (if allowed)
- Share to your personal profile
- Use Facebook Marketplace for digital products
- Go Live for Q&A sessions

### **Twitter/X:**
- Post 2-3x per day
- Use trending hashtags
- Engage with job search community
- Thread format for tips

### **TikTok:**
- Post 1x per day (minimum 3x/week)
- Use trending sounds
- Quick tips (30-60 seconds)
- Behind the scenes content
