### Post 2: Value-Add Article Style
**Text:**
3 CV Mistakes I See Even Senior Professionals Make 🚫

1. **The Wall of Text**
Recruiters spend 6-7 seconds on your CV. Dense paragraphs = instant reject. Use bullet points, white space, clear sections.

2. **Responsibilities Over Achievements**
"Managed a team" vs. "Led 12-person team, increased productivity 35%"
One tells what you did. The other tells how WELL you did it.

3. **One-Size-Fits-All**
Sending the same CV to every job? That's why you're not getting callbacks. Tailor for each role.

These are exactly the issues I solved when designing my CV templates. Because great experience deserves great presentation.
