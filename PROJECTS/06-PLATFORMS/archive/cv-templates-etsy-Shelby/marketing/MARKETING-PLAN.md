## 📅 PHASE 1: Days 1-30 (Launch & Awareness)

### Week 1: Launch Week
**Focus:** Maximum visibility, first sales, reviews

**Daily Actions:**
- **Day 1:** Launch all 15 listings, share on all social channels
- **Day 2:** Engage with comments, respond to inquiries
- **Day 3:** Pinterest pin blast (20+ pins)
- **Day 4:** LinkedIn article: "CV Mistakes to Avoid in 2026"
- **Day 5:** TikTok/Reels: Template transformation video
- **Day 6:** Email sequence #1 to warm leads
- **Day 7:** Review analytics, adjust pricing if needed

**Metrics to Track:**
- Views per listing
- Click-through rate
- Conversion rate
- First 5 reviews

### Week 2-4: Building Momentum
**Focus:** Content marketing, SEO optimization, social proof

**Content Calendar:**
- **3x/week:** Instagram posts (before/after transformations)
- **Daily:** Pinterest pins (schedule in batches)
- **2x/week:** LinkedIn posts (career advice + soft sell)
- **2x/week:** TikTok/Reels (quick tips, templates)
- **Weekly:** Email newsletter (career tips + featured templates)

**Tactics:**
- Run Etsy Ads ($5-10/day budget)
- Offer limited-time bundle discount
- Request reviews from first buyers
- Create Pinterest boards for each template category
- Join LinkedIn career groups, provide value

## 📅 PHASE 2: Days 31-60 (Growth & Optimization)

### Focus Areas:
1. **Double Down on Winners**
   - Identify top 3 performing listings
   - Create variations (color schemes, industries)
   - Increase ad spend on winners

2. **Expand Product Line**
   - Add industry-specific bundles (Tech, Healthcare, Finance)
   - Create matching thank-you card templates
   - Add LinkedIn profile optimization guide

3. **Build Authority**
   - Guest post on career blogs
   - Partner with career coaches (affiliate program)
   - Create free lead magnet (CV checklist)

4. **Optimize Conversions**
   - A/B test listing images
   - Refine product descriptions
   - Add video previews to listings
   - Improve FAQ sections

### Marketing Activities:
- **Influencer Outreach:** Partner with 5-10 career influencers
- **Email Marketing:** Segment list, send targeted offers
- **Social Proof:** Feature customer success stories
- **SEO:** Optimize all listings based on search data

## 📅 PHASE 3: Days 61-90 (Scale & Automate)

### Focus Areas:
1. **Scale What Works**
   - Increase Etsy Ads budget to $20-30/day

4. **Brand Building**

## 🚨 Contingency Plans

### If Sales Are Slow (First 2 Weeks):
1. Reduce prices by 10-15% temporarily
2. Increase Etsy Ads budget
3. Create urgency (limited-time offer)
4. Reach out to network for first sales
5. Add more listing photos/videos

### If Reviews Are Lacking:
1. Follow up with buyers personally
2. Offer discount on next purchase for review
3. Include review request in download files
4. Make reviewing easy (direct links)

### If Traffic Is Low:
1. Optimize SEO tags and titles
2. Increase Pinterest activity
3. Run external promotions
4. Partner with influencers
5. Create viral-worthy content
