# 📱 Social Media Content Calendar - 30 Days

TikTok & Instagram content strategy for Mystic AI launch.

---

## Content Pillars

1. **Educational** (40%): Tarot meanings, spread tutorials
2. **Engagement** (30%): Pick-a-card, daily draws, questions
3. **Promotional** (20%): App features, testimonials, offers
4. **Behind-the-Scenes** (10%): Building the app, team, process

---

## Posting Schedule

- **TikTok**: 1-2x daily (morning 8am, evening 7pm)
- **Instagram Reels**: 1x daily (evening 6pm)
- **Instagram Stories**: 3-5x daily (engagement)

## Engagement Tactics

1. **Reply to ALL comments** (first 60 minutes critical)
2. **Ask questions in captions** to drive comments
3. **Use polls in Stories** for engagement
4. **Go LIVE weekly** for community building
5. **Collaborate with micro-influencers** (10K-100K followers)
6. **Create branded hashtag**: #MysticAIReadings

---

## Metrics to Track

- **Views**: Target 10K+ per video
- **Engagement Rate**: Target 5%+ (likes + comments / views)
- **Follower Growth**: Target 1K+ per week
- **Link Clicks**: Track via bit.ly or UTM
- **Conversion**: Downloads from social (target 10% of clicks)

---

## Content Creation Tips

1. **Hook in first 3 seconds** or they scroll
2. **Use captions** (80% watch without sound)
3. **Vertical video only** (9:16 aspect ratio)
4. **Post consistently** (algorithm rewards consistency)
5. **Engage for 30 min after posting** (boosts reach)
6. **Repurpose top content** (remix, update, re-post)
