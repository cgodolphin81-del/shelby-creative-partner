## Art Direction Notes

### Consistency Rules
1. **Card Borders**: 20px gold foil border with suit symbol in corners
2. **Number Placement**: Top-left and bottom-right (rotated)
3. **Title Font**: "Cinzel" or similar serif, gold foil effect
4. **Background**: Subtle gradient matching suit element
5. **Central Image**: Full-bleed illustration, 80% of card height

### Technical Specs
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920px (mobile portrait, 9:16 aspect)
- **Format**: PNG with transparency
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **File Naming**: `{suit}_{rank}.png` (e.g., `wands_ace.png`, `cups_queen.png`)
