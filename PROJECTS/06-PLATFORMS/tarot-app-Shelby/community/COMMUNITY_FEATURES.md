## 6. Community Guidelines

### Code of Conduct
```
1. Be respectful and kind
2. No medical, legal, or financial advice
3. No hate speech or discrimination
4. Respect different spiritual paths
5. No spam or self-promotion
6. Protect your privacy (yours and others')
```

### Reporting & Moderation
- Report button on all user content
- 24-hour response time
- Three-strike system for violations
- Appeal process available

### Content Warnings
- Readings may contain sensitive topics
- Users can filter certain topics (death, conflict, etc.)
- Mental health resources linked in app

## Technical Requirements

### Backend
- **Real-time**: WebSocket for live readings
- **Notifications**: Firebase Cloud Messaging / APNS
- **Content Moderation**: AI filtering + human review
- **Analytics**: Mixpanel or Amplitude for engagement tracking

### Frontend
- **Feed**: Infinite scroll, pull-to-refresh
- **Comments**: Nested threads, real-time updates
- **Sharing**: Native share sheets, custom card generation
- **Calendar**: Moon phase calendar with reminders

### Privacy & Security
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Anonymity**: Optional anonymous participation
- **Data Export**: GDPR compliance, download your data
- **Age Gate**: 18+ for community features
