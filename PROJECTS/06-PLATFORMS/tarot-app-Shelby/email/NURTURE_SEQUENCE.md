## Email Best Practices

### Design
- Mobile-responsive (60%+ open on mobile)
- Plain text option available
- Brand colors (purple, gold, white)
- Clear, single CTA per email

### Timing
- Send times: Tuesday-Thursday 10am or 2pm (user's timezone)
- Avoid weekends (lower engagement)
- Space emails to avoid fatigue

### Metrics to Track
- **Open Rate**: Target 35%+ (spirituality niche performs well)
- **Click Rate**: Target 5%+
- **Conversion**: Target 8-12% trial → paid
- **Unsubscribe**: Keep under 2% per email

### A/B Tests
- Subject lines (emoji vs. no emoji)
- Send times (morning vs. afternoon)
- CTA copy ("Start Trial" vs. "Unlock Premium")
- Email length (short vs. long form)
