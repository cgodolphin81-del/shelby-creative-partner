## Technical Requirements

### Performance
- Page load < 3 seconds
- Mobile-optimized (60%+ traffic)
- Lazy load images
- CDN for assets

### SEO
- Title: "AI Tarot Readings | Mystic AI - Free 3-Card Reading"
- Meta: "Get personalized AI-powered tarot readings. Free 3-card spread. Celtic Cross, daily draws, journal tracking. Start your spiritual journey."
- Open Graph tags for social sharing
- Schema markup for app

### Analytics
- Google Analytics 4
- Facebook Pixel
- Conversion tracking on signup
- Heat maps (Hotjar)

### A/B Tests
- Hero CTA: "Get Free Reading" vs "Try Tarot Free"
- Pricing: $7.99 vs $9.99
- Trial: 7-day vs 14-day

1. **Primary**: Email signup / App download
2. **Secondary**: Free reading completion
3. **Tertiary**: Social share
