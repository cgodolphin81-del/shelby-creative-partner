## Key Metrics & Assumptions

### User Acquisition
- **Organic growth:** 30% (SEO, social sharing, viral memes)
- **Paid acquisition:** 40% (social ads, influencer partnerships)
- **Referrals:** 20% (user invitations, challenge participation)
- **Partnerships:** 10% (brand collaborations, integrations)

### Conversion Rates
- **Free → Pro:** 5% (industry standard for freemium)
- **Pro → API:** 2% (power users, developers)
- **Trial → Paid (Brands):** 40% (14-day free trial)

### Churn Rates
- **Pro subscriptions:** 8% monthly (96% annual retention)
- **API subscriptions:** 5% monthly (95% annual retention)
- **Brand contracts:** 10% annual (90% retention)

## Risk Factors

1. **Competition:** Imgflip, Canva, Kapwing entering meme space
   - *Mitigation:* Focus on AI features, viral prediction, community

2. **Platform Risk:** Social media API changes
   - *Mitigation:* Diversify integrations, build direct relationships

3. **Content Moderation:** NSFW, copyright issues
   - *Mitigation:* AI moderation, DMCA compliance, clear ToS

4. **AI Costs:** OpenAI pricing changes
   - *Mitigation:* Multiple providers, caching, optimization
