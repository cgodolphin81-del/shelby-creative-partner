## 🔧 Platform Features Summary

### Core Features

✅ **Manuscript Upload System**
- Support for DOCX, PDF, EPUB, TXT, HTML
- Automatic chapter detection (95% accuracy)
- Content validation and proofing
- SSML markup support
- Pronunciation guide creation

✅ **AI Voice Library**
- 50+ professional narrator voices
- Genre-specific voice matching
- Emotional range (14+ emotions)
- Custom voice cloning available
- Multi-voice productions

✅ **Audio Editing System**
- Pacing controls (0.5x - 2.0x)
- Word and phrase emphasis
- Emotion markers and blending
- Audio quality tools (EQ, compression, noise reduction)
- Real-time preview

✅ **Quality Control Workflow**
- AI-powered proofing (pronunciation, misreads, pacing)
- Technical compliance checks (loudness, peaks, format)
- Human review process
- Correction and revision system
- Platform compliance certification

✅ **Distribution System**
- Direct to Audible/ACX, Apple, Spotify, Google Play
- Aggregator integration (Findaway)
- Library distribution (OverDrive, Hoopla)
- International distribution (150+ countries)
- Metadata and ISBN management

✅ **Author Onboarding**
- Guided 4-phase onboarding journey
- Voice selection assistance
- Manuscript preparation support
- Launch day checklist
- Post-launch support
