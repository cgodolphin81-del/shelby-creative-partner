## Niche Selection Framework

### The SPICE Test
Evaluate each niche against these criteria:

**S - Size:** Is the audience large enough? (10M+ monthly listeners ideal)
**P - Passion:** Are listeners highly engaged? (check Reddit, Facebook groups)
**I - Income:** Do they spend money? (disposable income, B2B budgets)
**C - Competition:** How saturated is the space? (fewer = easier entry)
**E - Expertise:** Do you have unique knowledge/angle?

## Action Steps

1. **Score each niche** using the SPICE test (1-5 scale)
2. **Validate demand** with Google Trends, Reddit communities, Amazon books
3. **Check competition** - search Apple Podcasts, Spotify for existing shows
4. **Interview potential listeners** - 5-10 people in target audience
5. **Test content** - create 3 pilot episodes before committing
