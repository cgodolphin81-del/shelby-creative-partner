## 🎬 TRAILER EPISODE GUIDE

### Purpose
- Introduce your show to potential listeners
- Generate pre-launch interest
- Submit to platforms before full episodes ready
- Set expectations for content

### Trailer Structure (2-3 minutes)

```
0:00-0:15  - Hook (most compelling moment from future episodes)
0:15-0:30  - Show introduction (name, host, concept)
0:30-1:00  - What listeners will get (benefits, topics)
1:00-1:30  - Why you're qualified (credibility)
1:30-2:00  - Launch date + where to subscribe
2:00-2:30  - Call to action (subscribe, share, visit website)
```

### Trailer Script Template

```
[HOOK - 15 seconds]
"The moment I realized everything I knew about [topic] was wrong..."

[INTRO - 15 seconds]
"Welcome to [SHOW NAME], the podcast that [unique value proposition].
I'm your host, [NAME], and I'm here to [what you'll do for listeners]."

[WHAT TO EXPECT - 30 seconds]
"Every [frequency], we'll explore [topics]. You'll discover [benefit 1],
[benefit 2], and [benefit 3]. Whether you're [audience description],
this show is for you."

[CREDIBILITY - 30 seconds]
"I've spent [X years] [relevant experience]. I've [achievement 1],
[achievement 2], and now I'm bringing everything I've learned to you."

[LAUNCH INFO - 30 seconds]
"[SHOW NAME] launches [DATE]. Subscribe now on Apple Podcasts, Spotify,
or wherever you listen. The first episode drops [DATE] and you won't
want to miss it."

[CALL TO ACTION - 30 seconds]
"Hit subscribe right now. Share this trailer with someone who needs to
hear this. Visit [WEBSITE] to join our community. I'll see you on
[LAUNCH DATE]."
```

### Trailer Production Tips

1. **Make it professional** - This is first impression
2. **Include music** - Sets tone for show
3. **Show your personality** - Let listeners connect
4. **Be specific** - What exactly will they get?
5. **Clear CTA** - Tell them exactly what to do

## 📺 FIRST 10 EPISODES STRATEGY

### Why 10 Episodes?

- **Binge potential** - Listeners can dive deep
- **Algorithm boost** - Platforms favor active shows
- **Momentum** - Consistent content builds habit
- **Buffer** - Life happens, you need backup

### Episode Planning Template

| Episode | Title | Type | Guest | Key Topics | CTA |
|---------|-------|------|-------|------------|-----|
| 1 | [Launch Episode] | Solo | N/A | Origin story, mission | Subscribe |
| 2 | [Foundational Topic] | Solo/Interview | Optional | Core concept #1 | Website |
| 3 | [Guest Expert] | Interview | Expert | Deep dive topic | Guest promo |
| 4 | [How-To Guide] | Solo | N/A | Step-by-step guide | Resource |
| 5 | [Case Study] | Solo/Interview | Practitioner | Real example | Consultation |
| 6 | [Controversial Take] | Solo | N/A | Industry myth-busting | Discussion |
| 7 | [Top 10 List] | Solo | N/A | Best practices | Share |
| 8 | [Interview] | Interview | Influencer | Their story | Follow guest |
| 9 | [Q&A Episode] | Solo | N/A | Listener questions | Submit Qs |
| 10 | [Milestone Reflection] | Solo | N/A | Lessons learned | Review |

### Content Strategy for First 10

**Episodes 1-3: Foundation**
- Establish credibility
- Define show's purpose
- Set content expectations
- Build initial trust

**Episodes 4-7: Value Delivery**
- Deep, actionable content
- Show expertise
- Provide quick wins
- Encourage sharing

**Episodes 8-10: Community Building**
- Feature guests/audience
- Encourage interaction
- Build momentum
- Set up future content

### Episode Length Guidelines

| Format | Ideal Length | Why |
|--------|--------------|-----|
| Daily news | 10-20 min | Quick consumption |
| Solo educational | 20-40 min | Attention span |
| Interview | 45-60 min | Depth + engagement |
| Narrative | 30-50 min | Story pacing |
| Roundtable | 60-90 min | Multiple perspectives |

## 🚀 LAUNCH WEEK PROMOTION PLAN

### Launch Day (Day 0)

**Morning (6-9 AM)**
- [ ] Release episodes 1-3 simultaneously
- [ ] Send email to launch list
- [ ] Post on all social channels
- [ ] Update all social bios with show link
- [ ] Personal DMs to close network

**Midday (12-2 PM)**
- [ ] Share on LinkedIn (professional angle)
- [ ] Post Instagram Stories (behind-the-scenes)
- [ ] Share in relevant Facebook groups
- [ ] Post in relevant subreddits (follow rules)
- [ ] Update email signature

**Evening (6-8 PM)**
- [ ] Go live on Instagram/Facebook
- [ ] Thank early supporters publicly
- [ ] Share first reviews/testimonials
- [ ] Engage with all comments
- [ ] Prepare Day 2 content

### Day 1-3: Momentum Building

**Daily Tasks:**
- [ ] Release one new episode (episodes 4-6)
- [ ] Create 3-5 social posts per episode
- [ ] Share audiograms (video clips with waveform)
- [ ] Engage with every comment/message
- [ ] Request reviews from listeners

**Content Types:**
- Quote graphics from episodes
- Behind-the-scenes photos
- Episode highlight clips (30-60 sec)
- Listener testimonials
- "Why I started this" posts

### Day 4-7: Expansion

**Outreach:**
- [ ] Contact podcast newsletters for features
- [ ] Reach out to industry blogs
- [ ] Pitch to local media
- [ ] Contact influencers in your niche
- [ ] Ask guests to share their episodes

**Paid Promotion (Optional Budget: $200-500)**
- [ ] Facebook/Instagram ads ($100-200)
- [ ] Podcast ad swap ($0-100)
- [ ] Influencer partnerships ($0-200)
- [ ] Reddit ads ($50-100)

## 🔄 POST-LAUNCH MOMENTUM

### Weeks 2-4: Maintain Momentum

- Continue releasing on schedule (non-negotiable)
- Engage with every listener comment
- Request reviews in each episode
- Share listener feedback/testimonials
- Pitch for podcast "Best of" lists
- Network with other podcasters

### Month 2-3: Optimization

- Analyze top-performing episodes
- Double down on what works
- Adjust format based on feedback
- Explore guest opportunities
- Consider paid promotion
- Build email list actively

### Month 4-6: Growth

- Pursue sponsorships (if metrics support)
- Cross-promote with similar shows
- Explore additional platforms (YouTube, TikTok)
- Consider live events
- Develop premium offerings
- Plan season 2 / special series

## ⚠️ COMMON LAUNCH MISTAKES

### ❌ Don't Do This

1. **Launch with only 1-2 episodes** - No binge potential
2. **Inconsistent schedule** - Kills algorithm momentum
3. **No call-to-action** - Listeners won't know what to do
4. **Ignore reviews** - Engagement signals matter
5. **No social promotion** - Build outside podcast apps
6. **Perfect paralysis** - Launch before you feel "ready"
7. **No email list** - Own your audience
8. **Give up too soon** - Most shows quit before month 6

### ✅ Do This Instead

1. **Launch with 3-5 episodes** minimum
2. **Commit to consistent schedule** (weekly minimum)
3. **Clear CTAs** in every episode
4. **Respond to every review** (first 50+)
5. **Multi-platform promotion** strategy
6. **Launch at 80% ready** - iterate publicly
7. **Build email list** from day 1
8. **Commit to 6 months** minimum before evaluating
