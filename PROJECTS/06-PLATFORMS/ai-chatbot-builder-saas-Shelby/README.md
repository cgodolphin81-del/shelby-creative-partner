# ChatFlow AI - No-Code AI Chatbot Builder SaaS

**Build intelligent customer conversation bots in minutes, not months.**

ChatFlow AI is a comprehensive no-code platform that enables SMBs to create, train, and deploy AI-powered chatbots across multiple channels—without writing a single line of code. Think Intercom or Drift, but accessible, affordable, and AI-first.

---

## 🎯 Vision

Democratize AI customer engagement for small and medium businesses by providing enterprise-grade chatbot capabilities at SMB-friendly prices.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Sign Up** → Create your account at chatflow.ai
2. **Train Your Bot** → Upload docs, add URLs, or create Q&A pairs
3. **Customize Flow** → Use visual builder to design conversation paths
4. **Deploy** → Add one line of code or connect your channels
5. **Monitor** → Track performance in real-time analytics
