# Landing Page Specification

## Overview

SoulSpace landing page converts visitors into registered users through emotional connection, clear value proposition, and trust signals.

---

## Page Goals

**Primary:** Drive app downloads and sign-ups
**Secondary:** Build email list, establish brand trust
**Tertiary:** SEO ranking for "AI emotional support", "mental health companion"

**Target Conversion Rate:** 8-12% (visitor → sign-up)

## Technical Requirements

### Performance
```
- Page load: < 3 seconds
- Time to interactive: < 4 seconds
- Lighthouse score: 90+ (all categories)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Progressive Web App (PWA) capable
```

### SEO
```
Target Keywords:
- "AI emotional support"
- "mental health chatbot"
- "AI therapist alternative"
- "loneliness support app"
- "anxiety support app"

Meta Tags:
- Title: "SoulSpace - Your AI Emotional Companion, Always There"
- Description: "24/7 emotional support with AI companions who listen, understand, and care. Start free. No judgment. No wait."
- OG Image: Warm, inviting hero illustration
```

### Analytics
```
Tracking:
- Google Analytics 4
- Facebook Pixel
- Google Tag Manager
- Hotjar (heatmaps)

Events to Track:
- CTA clicks
- Scroll depth
- Video plays
- Companion selection clicks
- Form submissions
- Download button clicks
```

### A/B Testing
```
Test Variants:
1. Hero headline (emotional vs. functional)
2. CTA color (coral vs. lavender)
3. Social proof placement (above vs. below fold)
4. Pricing display (monthly vs. annual default)
5. Testimonial count (3 vs. 6)

Tool: Optimizely or Google Optimize
```

---

## Copy Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- ✅ Warm, empathetic, human
- ✅ Simple language (8th grade reading level)
- ✅ Specific benefits over features
- ✅ Inclusive, diverse representation
- ✅ Honest about AI limitations

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Clinical or medical language
- ❌ Overpromise ("cure anxiety")
- ❌ Fear-mongering
- ❌ Jargon ("LLM-powered", "NLP")
- ❌ Dismissive of therapy
