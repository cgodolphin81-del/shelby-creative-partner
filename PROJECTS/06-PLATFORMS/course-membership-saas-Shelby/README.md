# CourseFlow Pro - Online Course & Membership Platform SaaS

**A complete Teachable/Kajabi alternative for creators, coaches, and educators**

---

## 🎯 Overview

CourseFlow Pro is a comprehensive SaaS platform enabling creators to build, market, and sell online courses and membership communities. Designed as a cost-effective alternative to Teachable and Kajabi, it combines powerful course creation tools with robust membership management, community features, and marketing automation.

### Vision
Democratize online education by providing enterprise-grade course and membership tools at accessible price points for creators at all levels.

### Mission
Empower 100,000+ creators to build sustainable online businesses through intuitive technology, fair pricing, and exceptional support.

## 🚀 Quick Start

### For Creators
1. Sign up for a 14-day free trial
2. Build your course using the drag-and-drop builder
3. Set up payment processing (Stripe integration)
4. Launch and start selling

### For Students
1. Browse courses or access via creator link
2. Purchase or subscribe
3. Learn at your own pace with progress tracking
4. Engage with community and earn certificates

## 📈 Business Model

### Revenue Streams
1. **Subscription Revenue** (70%): Monthly/annual SaaS fees
2. **Transaction Fees** (20%): Percentage of course sales
3. **Add-ons** (7%): Custom domains, premium themes, priority support
4. **Enterprise** (3%): Custom deployments, SLAs, training

### Unit Economics (Target)
- **CAC**: $450 (blended across channels)
- **LTV**: $4,200 (30-month avg retention @ $140/mo ARPU)
- **LTV:CAC**: 9.3:1
- **Gross Margin**: 82%
- **Net Revenue Retention**: 115%
