## 8️⃣ FAQ SECTION

**Q: What types of IP do you license?**  
A: Software, patents, trademarks, content, characters, methodologies, courses, books, music, designs. Most IP types.

**Q: How much can I expect in royalties?**  
A: Varies widely. 5-15% of licensee revenue is typical. Could be $10K/year or $1M+/year depending on IP.

**Q: How long do licenses last?**  
A: Typically 3-10 years. Can renew. Some are perpetual with ongoing royalties.

**Q: Do I keep ownership of my IP?**  
A: Yes! Licensing ≠ selling. You retain ownership. Licensee gets usage rights only.

**Q: What if licensee doesn't pay?**  
A: We monitor compliance, audit sales, enforce payments. Legal recourse if needed.

**Q: Can I license to multiple companies?**  
A: Yes! Non-exclusive licenses allow multiple licensees. More revenue streams.

**Q: What industries do you work with?**  
A: All industries. Tech, media, consumer goods, education, healthcare, entertainment.
