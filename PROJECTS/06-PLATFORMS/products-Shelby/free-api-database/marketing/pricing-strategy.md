## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Strategy

**Display Order:** Lifetime → Pro → Free

1. **Lifetime ($99)** shown first
   - Makes Pro seem reasonable
   - Establishes value anchor
   - Captures high-intent buyers

2. **Pro ($29)** highlighted as "Most Popular"
   - Appears as the sensible choice
   - Best value perception
   - Default option for most

3. **Free ($0)** as entry point
   - No barrier to entry
   - Builds trust
   - Captures price-sensitive users

### Price Points Rationale

**Why $29 for Pro?**
- Under $30 psychological barrier
- Impulse buy territory
- Less than 1 hour of freelance rate
- Cheaper than most courses
- Easy expense approval for employees

**Why $99 for Lifetime?**
- Under $100 psychological barrier
- 3.4x Pro price (not 4x or 5x)
- Clear value: "Never pay again"
- Premium without being exclusive
- Room for future price increase to $149

**Why Free tier?**
- Removes all friction
- Builds email list
- Creates reciprocity
- Demonstrates value
- Reduces refund requests

## Upsell Strategy

### Post-Purchase Upsell Flow

**For Free Users:**
1. **Day 1:** Welcome email with 20 APIs
2. **Day 3:** Value email (highlight popular APIs)
3. **Day 5:** Case study (how someone used the database)
4. **Day 7:** Limited-time offer ($5 off Pro)
5. **Day 14:** Last chance reminder
6. **Day 30:** Final offer + bonus

**For Pro Buyers:**
1. **Immediately after purchase:** Thank you + Lifetime upgrade offer ($70 upgrade)
2. **Day 7:** Success tips + community invite
3. **Day 30:** Update notification + request testimonial
4. **Day 60:** Advanced use cases + affiliate program invite
5. **Day 90:** New features + referral program

### Upsell Triggers

- **Browse Pro page 2+ times** → Show comparison popup
- **Download free tier** → Email sequence starts
- **Add Pro to cart but abandon** → Abandoned cart email (24 hours)
- **Purchase Pro** → Immediate Lifetime upsell (one-time offer)
- **Open 5+ emails** → High-intent, send special offer

## Discount Strategy

### When to Discount

**DO Discount:**
- Product Hunt launch (20% off, 5 days)
- Black Friday / Cyber Monday (30% off, 1 week)
- New Year (25% off, 2 weeks)
- Milestone celebrations (1000 customers, etc.)
- Bundle partnerships

**DON'T Discount:**
- Regular weeks (devalues product)
- First month (test full price)
- After price increase announcements
- For individual requests (sets bad precedent)

### Discount Tactics

1. **Percentage Off:** "20% Off Everything"
2. **Dollar Amount:** "$10 Off Pro Tier"
3. **Bonus Addition:** "Free 1:1 Onboarding Call"
4. **Extended Terms:** "60-Day Refund Window"
5. **Bundle Deal:** "Database + Course for $X"

### Coupon Codes

- **LAUNCH20** — 20% off (launch week)
- **BLACKFRIDAY** — 30% off (Black Friday)
- **NEWYEAR25** — 25% off (January)
- **COMMUNITY** — 15% off (for Discord/Reddit members)
- **AFFILIATE10** — 10% off (affiliate-specific)

## Price Increase Strategy

### When to Increase Prices

**Signals it's time:**
- Conversion rate >25% (too low)
- Customer feedback: "This should cost more"
- Consistent sell-out of bonuses
- 3+ months of positive testimonials
- Added significant value (50+ new APIs)

**How to increase:**
1. **Announce 2 weeks in advance**
2. **Grandfather existing customers**
3. **Offer "lock in current price" window**
4. **Add value with the increase**

## Refund Policy

### Policy Terms

**30-Day Money-Back Guarantee**

- No questions asked
- Full refund within 30 days of purchase
- Keep the database (we don't revoke access)
- One refund per customer (prevent abuse)

### Why This Works

- Reduces purchase anxiety
- Increases conversion rate
- Builds trust
- Actual refund rate typically <5%
- Positive word-of-mouth

### Refund Process

1. Customer emails support
2. Acknowledge within 24 hours
3. Process refund within 48 hours
4. Send confirmation email
5. Optional: Ask for feedback (why they refunded)

### Refund Recovery

For refund requests, try:
1. **Understand the issue** — What didn't meet expectations?
2. **Offer help** — Can we solve their problem?
3. **Partial refund** — Offer 50% if they keep access
4. **Credit** — Offer credit for future products
5. **Full refund** — If nothing works, refund gracefully
