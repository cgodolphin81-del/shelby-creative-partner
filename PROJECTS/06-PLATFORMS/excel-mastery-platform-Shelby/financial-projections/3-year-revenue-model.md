### 3. Membership Subscriptions

**Pricing**: $29/month ($348/year)

| Year | Avg Members | Monthly MRR | Annual Revenue |
|------|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| Year 1 | 200 | $5,800 | $60,000 |
| Year 2 | 750 | $21,750 | $240,000 |
| Year 3 | 1,800 | $52,200 | $600,000 |

**Member Acquisition**:
- Month 1-3: 20 members/month
- Month 4-6: 40 members/month
- Month 7-12: 60 members/month
- Year 2: 80 members/month average
- Year 3: 100 members/month average

**Churn Rate**: 5% monthly (industry standard)

**LTV Calculation**:
- Average membership duration: 8 months
- LTV: $29 × 8 = $232
- CAC target: < $70 (3:1 LTV:CAC ratio)
