# 💌 Customer Service Guide

Great customer service = 5-star reviews = more sales.

---

## 📊 Why Customer Service Matters

**Statistics:**
- 91% of customers will return after a positive service experience
- 1 bad review can cost you 30+ potential customers
- Responding to reviews increases trust by 45%
- Fast response time = higher conversion rate

## ⏰ Response Time Standards

### Etsy Response Expectations

| Time Frame | Standard | Impact |
|------------|----------|--------|
| < 4 hours | Excellent | Boosts shop rating |
| 4-12 hours | Good | Maintains standards |
| 12-24 hours | Acceptable | Meets minimum |
| 24-48 hours | Slow | May affect rating |
| 48+ hours | Poor | Risk of negative review |

### Response Time Goals

**Ideal:**
- Messages: Under 6 hours
- Review responses: Under 24 hours
- Refund requests: Under 12 hours
- Custom orders: Under 4 hours

### 2. Download Issue Response

**Template:**
```
Hi [CUSTOMER NAME]! 👋

I'm so sorry you're having trouble downloading your files! 
Let me help you right away.

TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS:

1. CHECK SPAM/JUNK FOLDER
   Sometimes Etsy emails get filtered. Search for "Etsy 
   Order Confirmation" or "Download Receipt."

2. TRY A DIFFERENT DEVICE
   Sometimes downloading from phone vs. computer helps.

3. CLEAR YOUR CACHE
   Browser settings → Clear browsing data → Cached images 
   and files → Try again.

4. USE THE ETSY APP
   Download the Etsy app and try downloading from there.

IF STILL NOT WORKING:
Please let me know:
- What device/browser you're using
- Any error message you see
- When you made the purchase

I'll get this resolved for you within 24 hours! 

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

## 📞 Handling Difficult Situations

### Dispute Resolution Flowchart

```
Customer complains
        ↓
Acknowledge & empathize
        ↓
Investigate the issue
        ↓
Offer solution:
├─ Refund (if valid)
├─ Replacement (if possible)
├─ Store credit
└─ Helpful guidance
        ↓
Follow up after resolution
        ↓
Document for future reference
```

### Escalation Triggers

**Escalate to Etsy if:**
- Customer opens case
- Threat of chargeback
- Harassing behavior
- Policy violations

**Document everything:**
- Save all message transcripts
- Note dates/times of interactions
- Record solutions offered
- Track patterns

## ⭐ Review Management

### Review Response Strategy

**5-Star Reviews:**
- Thank them personally
- Mention something specific
- Invite them back
- Respond within 24 hours

**4-Star Reviews:**
- Thank them
- Ask what could be better
- Show you care about improvement
- Respond within 24 hours

**1-3 Star Reviews:**
- Acknowledge their concern
- Apologize sincerely
- Offer solution privately
- Don't argue publicly
- Respond within 12 hours

### Review Response Examples

**5-Star:**
```
"Amazing! Love these SVGs!" 
→ "So happy you love them! They're some of my favorites 
   too! Thanks for the 5 stars! 💕"
```

**4-Star:**
```
"Good quality, wish there were more colors."
→ "Thank you! I'll take your feedback into account for 
   future designs. More colors coming soon! ✨"
```

**3-Star:**
```
"File was corrupted, had to wait for replacement."
→ "So sorry about the corrupted file! I've reviewed my 
   upload process to prevent this. Thanks for your patience!
```

**1-Star:**
```
"Waste of money, didn't work."
→ "I'm sorry this didn't work for you. I've sent you a 
   private message with a full refund and help. Your 
   satisfaction matters!"
```

## 📱 Communication Best Practices

### Tone & Style

**Do:**
- ✓ Use friendly, warm language
- ✓ Include 1-2 emojis (not too many)
- ✓ Write in short paragraphs
- ✓ Use bullet points for clarity
- ✓ Sign off with your name

**Don't:**
- ✗ Use corporate/robotic language
- ✗ Overuse emojis (😂😂😂)
- ✗ Write walls of text
- ✗ Use all caps (looks like shouting)
- ✗ Leave messages unsigned

### Response Timing

**Best times to respond:**
- Morning (9-11 AM): Fresh start
- Afternoon (2-4 PM): After lunch
- Evening (7-9 PM): After work

**Avoid:**
- Late night (11 PM - 6 AM)
- During peak work hours if possible
- Weekends (if you take time off)

## 🔧 Automation & Efficiency

### Auto-Responses (Etsy Settings)

**Set up in Etsy Shop Manager:**
1. Go to Shop Settings → Info
2. Add "Automatic Message"
3. Write template for common inquiries
4. Enable for specific situations

### Saved Replies (Etsy Messages)

**Create shortcuts for:**
- Download instructions
- File format questions
- Custom order inquiries
- Refund requests
- Commercial license info

**How to create:**
1. Etsy Messages → Settings
2. "Saved Replies"
3. Create template
4. Use shortcut key in messages

## ⚠️ Red Flags & Warning Signs
