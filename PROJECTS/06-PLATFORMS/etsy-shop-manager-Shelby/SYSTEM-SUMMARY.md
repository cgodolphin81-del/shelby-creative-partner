## 💡 Key Success Factors

Based on the case studies and guides:

1. **Quality over quantity** - 30 excellent listings > 100 mediocre
2. **SEO from day one** - Research keywords before listing
3. **Excellent customer service** - Fast responses = good reviews
4. **Pinterest marketing** - Best traffic source for digital products
5. **Email list building** - Start early, automate later
6. **Bundle strategy** - Increase average order value
7. **Continuous optimization** - A/B test everything
8. **Strategic pricing** - Market research + psychology
9. **Automation early** - Save time from the start
10. **Learn from others** - Study successful shops
