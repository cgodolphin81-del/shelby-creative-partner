# 📅 30-Day Launch Calendar
## Shopify Store Launch Timeline & Checklist

---

## 🎯 OVERVIEW

This 30-day calendar guides you through launching your Shopify store from start to finish. Each day has specific tasks to keep you on track for a successful launch.

**Timeline:** 30 Days  
**Phases:** Planning → Build → Test → Launch → Post-Launch  
**Success Metric:** Live, functional store ready for customers

---

## 🚨 CONTINGENCY PLANNING

### Common Launch Issues & Solutions

**Issue: Site goes down**
- Solution: Contact Shopify support immediately
- Backup: Have status.shopify.com bookmarked

**Issue: Payment processing fails**
- Solution: Check payment gateway status
- Backup: Have manual payment option ready

**Issue: High cart abandonment**
- Solution: Review checkout flow, add trust signals
- Backup: Launch abandoned cart emails immediately

**Issue: Site too slow**
- Solution: Enable CDN, optimize images, disable heavy apps
- Backup: Have speed-optimized backup theme ready

**Issue: Tracking not working**
- Solution: Verify pixel installation, use debugging tools
- Backup: Manual tracking until fixed
