# 📧 Email Marketing Flows
## Klaviyo Automation Setup for E-Commerce

---

## 🎯 FLOW OVERVIEW

### Essential Flows Every Store Needs

| Flow | Trigger | Emails | Expected Revenue |
|------|---------|--------|------------------|
| Welcome Series | New subscriber | 3-5 emails | 20-30% of email revenue |
| Abandoned Cart | Cart abandoned | 3-4 emails | 10-15% recovery rate |
| Browse Abandonment | Viewed product, no ATC | 2-3 emails | 3-5% conversion |
| Post-Purchase | Order placed | 3-5 emails | Increases LTV 25%+ |
| Winback | No purchase 60-90 days | 2-3 emails | 5-10% reactivation |
| VIP/ Loyalty | High-value customers | Ongoing | 40%+ of revenue |

## 👋 WELCOME SERIES FLOW

### Flow Setup
**Trigger:** Subscribed to list (newsletter, popup, checkout)  
**Filter:** Has not placed order (for pure welcome) OR anyone (for onboarding)  
**Timing:** Start immediately after signup

---

## 🎨 EMAIL DESIGN BEST PRACTICES

### Design Guidelines
- [ ] **Mobile-first design** (60%+ emails opened on mobile)
- [ ] **Single column layout** (max 600px width)
- [ ] **Clear hierarchy** (headline, body, CTA)
- [ ] **Brand colors** consistent
- [ ] **Readable fonts** (14px+ body, 22px+ headlines)
- [ ] **Alt text** on all images
- [ ] **Plain text version** included
- [ ] **Unsubscribe link** visible

### CTA Best Practices
- [ ] **Above the fold** (visible without scrolling)
- [ ] **Contrasting color** from background
- [ ] **Clear action text** ("Shop Now" not "Click Here")
- [ ] **Button size** 44x44px minimum (mobile)
- [ ] **White space** around button
- [ ] **Repeated CTAs** in longer emails

### Image Guidelines
- [ ] **Optimized file size** (under 200KB)
- [ ] **WebP or JPG** format
- [ ] **Alt text** for accessibility
- [ ] **Text in images minimized** (deliverability)
- [ ] **Hero image** 600px wide
- [ ] **Product images** clear and high-quality

## 📈 FLOW OPTIMIZATION TIPS

### Testing Ideas
1. **Subject lines** (emoji vs. no emoji, question vs. statement)
2. **Send times** (morning vs. afternoon vs. evening)
3. **Incentive amounts** (10% vs. 15% vs. free shipping)
4. **Email frequency** (more vs. fewer emails in flow)
5. **Content length** (short vs. long form)
6. **CTA placement** (top vs. middle vs. repeated)
7. **Personalization** (first name vs. no name)
8. **Images** (lifestyle vs. product-only)

### Segmentation Opportunities
- **High-value customers:** VIP flow with exclusive offers
- **Product-specific:** Different flows for different product categories
- **Geographic:** Different timing/offers by region
- **Engagement:** Active vs. inactive subscribers
- **Purchase history:** First-time vs. repeat customers
