## ⚠️ APP INSTALLATION BEST PRACTICES

### Before Installing
1. **Read recent reviews** (last 3 months)
2. **Check developer response rate** to support issues
3. **Test on staging** or duplicate theme first
4. **Review pricing tiers** and understand limits
5. **Check compatibility** with your theme and other apps

### After Installing
1. **Configure all settings** before going live
2. **Test on mobile and desktop**
3. **Monitor site speed** impact
4. **Set up analytics** to track ROI
5. **Document what the app does** for your team

### App Maintenance
- **Monthly:** Review app usage and ROI
- **Quarterly:** Audit for unused apps
- **Annually:** Re-evaluate entire stack for better options

### Red Flags 🚩
- No reviews or very few reviews
- Developer doesn't respond to support tickets
- App hasn't been updated in 6+ months
- Significant speed impact (>1 second)
- Poor documentation
- Hidden fees or unclear pricing
