# FormFlow - Executive Summary

## Company Overview

**FormFlow** is a next-generation form builder SaaS platform designed to compete with Typeform, JotForm, and Google Forms. We combine beautiful design, powerful features, and developer-friendly tools at a fraction of the cost.

---

## The Problem

Existing form builders force users to choose between:

1. **Simple but limited** (Google Forms) - Free but basic, no branding, no payments
2. **Beautiful but expensive** (Typeform) - Great UX but 10x pricing at scale
3. **Powerful but complex** (JotForm) - Every feature but overwhelming UI
4. **Cheap but dated** (Wufoo, others) - Legacy platforms, slow innovation

---

## The Solution

FormFlow delivers:

✅ **Beautiful, conversion-optimized forms** - Modern design that matches your brand
✅ **Powerful features** - Logic jumps, payments, file uploads, multi-page forms
✅ **Developer-first** - Best-in-class API, webhooks, and integrations
✅ **Fair pricing** - 10x more generous free tier, transparent pricing
✅ **Fast performance** - Forms load 3x faster than competitors
