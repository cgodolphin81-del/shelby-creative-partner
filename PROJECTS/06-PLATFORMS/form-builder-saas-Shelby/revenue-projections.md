# FormFlow Revenue Projections

## Executive Summary

FormFlow targets $1M ARR in Year 1, $5M ARR in Year 2, and $15M ARR in Year 3 through a combination of self-serve freemium conversion and enterprise sales.

---

## Key Assumptions

### Market Assumptions
- Form builder market growing at 12% CAGR
- Increasing demand for online forms post-pandemic
- Shift from paper to digital forms continuing
- SMB digital transformation accelerating

### Product Assumptions
- Free → Paid conversion: 5-8%
- Monthly churn: 3-5% (SMB), 1-2% (Enterprise)
- Average selling price (ASP): $35/month (blended)
- Enterprise deal size: $5,000-50,000 ARR

### Go-to-Market Assumptions
- CAC payback period: < 12 months
- LTV:CAC ratio: > 3:1
- Organic traffic: 40% of signups by Year 2
- Viral coefficient: 0.3-0.5

### Unit Economics

**Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):**
```
Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): $35/month
Gross Margin: 85%
Monthly Churn: 4%
Lifetime: 1 / 0.04 = 25 months

LTV = $35 × 85% × 25 = $744
```

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):**
```
Year 1: $200 (paid heavy)
Year 2: $150 (organic growing)
Year 3: $100 (viral + organic)

Target LTV:CAC = 3:1
Target CAC = $744 / 3 = $248
```

**CAC Payback Period:**
```
Month 1 Revenue: $35
Gross Margin: 85%
Gross Profit: $30

Payback = $200 / $30 = 6.7 months
```

## Path to Profitability

### Breakeven Analysis

**Fixed Costs:** $500K/month (Year 3)
**Variable Costs:** 15% of revenue
**Average Revenue Per User:** $35/month

**Breakeven Calculation:**
```
Breakeven Users = Fixed Costs / (ARPU - Variable Cost per User)
Breakeven Users = $500,000 / ($35 - $5.25)
Breakeven Users = $500,000 / $29.75
Breakeven Users = 16,806 paid users

At $35/month ARPU:
Breakeven MRR = $588,210
Breakeven ARR = $7,058,520
```

**Timeline:** Q2-Q3 Year 3
