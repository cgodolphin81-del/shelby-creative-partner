# BookFlow - Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

**"Freemium with purpose"** - Our free tier is genuinely useful, not a crippled demo. We convert users through value, not frustration.

### Core Principles

1. **Free tier converts** - Users should experience core value before paying
2. **Clear upgrade triggers** - Users know exactly why they need to upgrade
3. **Annual discount** - 20% off for annual commitment (2 months free)
4. **No hidden fees** - All features clearly listed, no surprise charges
5. **Transparent payment fees** - Standard payment processor fees only

## Refund & Cancellation Policy

### Refund Policy

- **Monthly plans:** Prorated refund within 30 days
- **Annual plans:** Prorated refund within 60 days
- **Enterprise:** Per contract terms

### Cancellation

- Cancel anytime from dashboard
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Data exported automatically on cancellation
- 90-day data retention (recoverable)

### Downgrade Policy

- Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle
- Features remain until period ends
- Data preserved, just inaccessible on lower tier

---

## Price Increase Policy

**Commitment to existing customers:**

- Grandfather existing customers for 12 months
- 60-day notice before any price changes
- No price increases for annual plans during contract
- Transparent communication about value added
