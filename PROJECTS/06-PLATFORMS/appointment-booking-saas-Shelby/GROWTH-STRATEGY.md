# BookFlow - Growth Strategy

## Growth Philosophy

**"Product-led growth with human touch"** - Let the product sell itself, but be there when users need help.

### North Star Metric

**Weekly Active Bookers (WAB)** - Number of unique users who receive at least one booking per week

This metric captures:
- ✅ Active usage (not just signups)
- ✅ Value delivery (bookings = value)
- ✅ Retention signal (weekly = habit)
- ✅ Revenue correlation (active users convert)

## 1. Freemium Growth Engine

### Free Tier Strategy

**Goal:** Convert 5-8% of free users to paid within 90 days

#### Free Tier Limits (Designed for Conversion)

| Limit | Purpose | Conversion Trigger |
|-------|---------|-------------------|
| 50 bookings/month | Enough to experience value | Hit limit during growth |
| 3 event types | Basic flexibility | Need more variety |
| 2 calendars | Core functionality | Need more integrations |
| No payments | Major feature gate | Want to monetize |
| BookFlow branding | Professional image | Want custom brand |

#### Activation Funnel

```
Signup → Create Event Type → Connect Calendar → Share Link → First Booking
   │           │                  │                │              │
  100%       75%                60%              45%            35%
                                              (Target: 50%)
```

#### Activation Tactics

1. **Onboarding Checklist**
   ```
   ☐ Create your first event type (5 min)
   ☐ Connect your calendar (2 min)
   ☐ Customize your booking page (3 min)
   ☐ Share your link (1 min)
   
   🎉 Complete all steps → Get 14 days of Pro free
   ```

2. **Progressive Disclosure**
   - Show features gradually
   - Unlock advanced features through usage
   - Celebrate milestones

3. **Time-Based Triggers**
   - Day 1: Welcome email + setup guide
   - Day 3: "Have you created your first event?"
   - Day 7: "Here's how others are using BookFlow"
   - Day 14: "Upgrade to unlock payments"
   - Day 30: "You've had X bookings! Ready for Pro?"

4. **Usage-Based Triggers**
   - At 40 bookings: "You're close to your limit!"
   - At payment attempt: "Upgrade to accept payments"
   - At team invite: "Upgrade for team scheduling"

## 4. Paid Acquisition

**Platforms:** Google Ads, Bing Ads

**Target Keywords:**
- "Calendly alternative" (high intent)
- "appointment scheduling software" (medium intent)

**Budget:** $5,000-10,000/month
**Target CAC:** < $50

**Platforms:** LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram

**LinkedIn:**
- Target: Agency owners, consultants, coaches
- Format: Sponsored content, InMail
- Message: Professional scheduling solution

- Target: Coaches, therapists, tutors
- Format: Carousel ads, video ads
- Message: "Get your evenings back"

**Budget:** $10,000-20,000/month

**Budget:** 20% of total ad spend
**Target ROAS:** 4:1

## 7. Product-Led Growth Tactics

### In-App Messaging

#### Upgrade Prompts

| Trigger | Message | CTA |
|---------|---------|-----|
| 45/50 bookings used | "You're almost at your limit! Upgrade to Pro for unlimited bookings." | "View Plans" |
| Payment attempt on Free | "Accept payments with Stripe! Upgrade to Starter to get paid." | "Unlock Payments" |
| Team invite on Free | "Add your team! Starter includes 5 team members." | "Start Team Plan" |
| Custom domain attempt | "Use your own domain! Available on Pro plan." | "Upgrade to Pro" |

#### Success Moments

| Moment | Celebration |
|--------|-------------|
| First booking | "🎉 Your first booking! Share your link to get more." |
| 10 bookings | "10 bookings! You're on a roll. Need more features?" |
| Payment received | "💰 Payment received! You've earned $X this month." |
| Team member joins | "Your team is growing! 4 spots left on Starter." |

### Feature Gating Strategy

```
Free Features (Experience Value)
├── Create booking pages
├── Connect calendars
├── Email notifications
└── Basic analytics

↓ Upgrade Trigger (Payment, Team, Brand)

Starter Features (Monetize)
├── Accept payments
├── SMS notifications
├── Remove branding
└── 5 team members

↓ Upgrade Trigger (Scale, API, Custom)

Pro Features (Scale)
├── Unlimited everything
├── API access
├── Custom domain
└── 20 team members

↓ Upgrade Trigger (Enterprise Needs)

Enterprise Features (Enterprise)
├── SSO/SAML
├── HIPAA compliance
├── Dedicated support
└── Custom contracts
```

### Aha Moment Optimization

**Primary Aha Moment:** First successful booking

**Time to Aha Goal:** < 24 hours from signup

**Optimization Tactics:**
1. Simplify event type creation (reduce from 5 fields to 3)
2. Pre-fill calendar connection (detect Google account)
3. One-click booking page customization
4. Instant shareable link after setup
5. Demo booking option (let users experience as guest)

### Experiment Framework

