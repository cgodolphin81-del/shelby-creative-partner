## 📊 Pricing Strategy

### Penetration Pricing (Months 1-6)
- 20% below market average
- Focus on acquisition over margin
- Build portfolio and testimonials

### Value-Based Pricing (Months 7-12)
- Increase prices 15-25%
- Tier by value delivered, not just time
- Introduce premium tiers

### Enterprise Pricing (Ongoing)
- Custom quotes based on scope
- Annual contracts (10-20% discount)
- Value-based (ROI justification)

### Price Elasticity Testing
- A/B test pricing on website
- Monitor conversion rates
- Survey lost deals on price sensitivity
