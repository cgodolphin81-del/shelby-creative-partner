## 🎨 Animation Styles Reference

### 2D Animation
- **Best for:** Explainer videos, educational content, cartoons
- **Price:** $500-$15,000 per minute
- **Timeline:** 1-3 weeks typical
- **Tools:** Toon Boom Harmony, Adobe Animate

### 3D Animation
- **Best for:** Product visualization, architectural, character films
- **Price:** $2,000-$50,000+ per minute
- **Timeline:** 2-6 weeks typical
- **Tools:** Blender, Maya, Cinema 4D

### Motion Graphics
- **Best for:** Brand videos, titles, social content, presentations
- **Price:** $300-$5,000 per minute
- **Timeline:** 3 days - 2 weeks
- **Tools:** After Effects, Cinema 4D

### Stop-Motion Style
- **Best for:** Quirky brand content, children's content, artistic projects
- **Price:** $800-$8,000 per minute (digital)
- **Timeline:** 1-3 weeks
- **Note:** Physical stop-motion available at $5,000-$30,000/minute

### AI-Generated
- **Best for:** Rapid prototyping, abstract visuals, backgrounds
- **Price:** $200-$3,000 per minute (AI-heavy)
- **Timeline:** 24 hours - 1 week
- **Tools:** Runway, Pika, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion

### Anime Style
- **Best for:** Story content, gaming, youth-focused brands
- **Price:** $1,000-$20,000 per minute
- **Timeline:** 2-4 weeks
- **Tools:** Clip Studio Paint EX, Toon Boom
