## 🚨 Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Product
- ❌ Building too many features before launch
- ❌ Perfectionism over shipping
- ❌ Ignoring client feedback
- ✅ **Do:** Launch MVP, iterate based on feedback

### Team
- ❌ Hiring too fast
- ❌ Not documenting processes
- ❌ Poor role definition
- ✅ **Do:** Hire slowly, document everything, clarify roles

### Finance
- ❌ Underpricing services
- ❌ Not requiring deposits
- ❌ Ignoring cash flow
- ✅ **Do:** Price for value, 50% upfront, monitor cash weekly

### Marketing
- ❌ Trying all channels at once
- ❌ Inconsistent content
- ❌ Not tracking ROI
- ✅ **Do:** Focus on 2-3 channels, be consistent, measure everything

### Operations
- ❌ No quality control process
- ❌ Overpromising timelines
- ❌ Poor communication
- ✅ **Do:** QC checkpoints, realistic timelines, over-communicate
