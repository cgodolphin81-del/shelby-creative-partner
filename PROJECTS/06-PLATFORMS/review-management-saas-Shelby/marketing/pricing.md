## FAQ

### General Pricing Questions

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No, there are no setup fees for any plan. Enterprise custom integrations may have one-time development costs.

**Q: Can I change plans later?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes are prorated.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards, ACH bank transfers, and wire transfers. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice (Net 30).

**Q: Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?**
A: Yes, registered 501(c)(3) organizations receive 20% off all plans. Contact support@reviewflowpro.com.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my review request limit?**
A: You'll be notified at 80% and 100% of your limit. You can purchase additional requests at $0.10 each, or upgrade your plan.

**Q: Is there a long-term contract?**
A: Monthly plans are month-to-month. Annual plans require 12-month commitment. Enterprise contracts are typically 1-3 years.

### Trial & Refund Questions

**Q: How does the free trial work?**
A: All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new customers. After 30 days, cancellations are effective at the end of the billing period.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: You can export all your data at any time. After cancellation, data is retained for 90 days, then permanently deleted.

### Enterprise Questions

**Q: Can you customize the platform for our needs?**
A: Yes, Enterprise plans include custom development hours. Contact us to discuss requirements.

**Q: Do you offer on-premise deployment?**
A: No, ReviewFlow Pro is cloud-only. However, we offer data residency options in US, EU, and APAC regions.

**Q: Can we get a dedicated instance?**
A: Yes, dedicated instances are available for Enterprise customers. Contact enterprise@reviewflowpro.com.
