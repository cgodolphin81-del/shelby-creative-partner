# Service Overview

## What is BoxFlow?

BoxFlow is an end-to-end subscription box management platform that handles everything from customer acquisition to fulfillment. We enable businesses to launch and scale subscription box services without building complex infrastructure from scratch.

---

## Core Services

### 1. Curated Box Management

**Box Builder Studio**
- Drag-and-drop box configuration
- Product catalog management
- Seasonal box planning
- A/B testing for box contents
- Customer preference tracking

**Curation Tools**
- AI-powered product recommendations
- Trend analysis and forecasting
- Supplier integration dashboard
- Quality control workflows
- Sample management system

### 2. Recurring Billing Engine

**Subscription Management**
- Flexible billing cycles (weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Proration handling for mid-cycle changes
- Dunning management for failed payments
- Upgrade/downgrade workflows
- Pause and skip functionality

**Payment Processing**
- Multi-currency support (50+ currencies)
- Local payment methods (SEPA, iDEAL, Alipay, etc.)
- Tax calculation and compliance (VAT, GST, sales tax)
- PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure
- Fraud detection and prevention

### 3. Inventory Management

**Real-Time Tracking**
- Multi-warehouse support
- Automatic reorder points
- Batch and expiry tracking
- Serial number management
- Stock allocation rules

**Forecasting**
- Demand prediction using ML
- Seasonal trend analysis
- Supplier lead time optimization
- Safety stock recommendations
- Inventory valuation reports
