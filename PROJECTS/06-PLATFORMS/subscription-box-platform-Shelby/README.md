# BoxFlow - Subscription Box Management SaaS Platform

## Executive Summary

BoxFlow is a comprehensive SaaS platform designed to power subscription box businesses of all sizes. From curated monthly boxes to recurring billing and fulfillment management, BoxFlow provides the infrastructure needed to launch, scale, and optimize subscription commerce.

**Mission:** Enable creators, brands, and entrepreneurs to build thriving subscription box businesses without the operational complexity.

**Vision:** Become the operating system for the $65B+ subscription box industry by 2030.
