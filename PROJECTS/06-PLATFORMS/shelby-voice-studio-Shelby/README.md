## Overview

Shelby Voice Studio is a comprehensive AI voice cloning and narration service platform that enables content creators, businesses, and developers to access high-quality synthetic voices for various applications. The platform combines ethical voice cloning practices with a curated library of 50+ AI voices and custom voice creation capabilities.

**Mission:** Provide accessible, ethical, and professional-grade voice synthesis services while maintaining strict consent and disclosure standards.

## ⚖️ Ethical Commitments

Shelby Voice Studio is committed to ethical AI voice practices:

1. **Consent First** — All voice cloning requires explicit written consent
2. **Transparent Disclosure** — AI-generated audio includes metadata disclosure
3. **Deepfake Prevention** — Watermarking, verification, usage restrictions
4. **Fair Compensation** — Voice owners receive fair payment for their work
5. **Human Oversight** — All custom clones require manual review
6. **Privacy Protection** — Voice data encrypted and securely stored
7. **Right to Revoke** — Voice owners can revoke consent (non-exclusive)
8. **Prohibited Uses** — No political misinformation, fraud, or impersonation
