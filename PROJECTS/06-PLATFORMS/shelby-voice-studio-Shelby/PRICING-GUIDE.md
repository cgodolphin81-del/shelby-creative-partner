## 6. Payment Terms

### 6.1 Standard Terms

- **Projects under $500:** Full payment upfront
- **Projects $500-2,500:** 50% deposit, 50% on delivery
- **Projects over $2,500:** 50% deposit, 25% on sample, 25% on delivery
- **Subscriptions:** Monthly or annual prepay
- **Enterprise:** Net 15 or Net 30 (approved accounts)

### 6.2 Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Card (Stripe) — No fee
- PayPal — 3% fee
- Bank Transfer (ACH) — No fee for US, $25 international
- Wire Transfer — $25 fee
- Check — Accepted for established clients

### 6.3 Refund Policy

- **Deposits:** Non-refundable (covers reserved time)
- **Projects:** Refundable if not started
- **Subscriptions:** Pro-rated refund within first 14 days
- **Custom Clones:** Non-refundable after recording begins

## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the result?

**A:** We include revision rounds with every project. If we can't meet your expectations after revisions, we offer a partial refund based on work completed.

### Q: Can I upgrade my subscription mid-month?

**A:** Yes! Upgrades are pro-rated. You'll pay the difference for the remainder of the billing cycle.

### Q: Do you offer payment plans for large projects?

**A:** Yes, for projects over $5,000, we can arrange milestone-based payments. Contact us for details.

### Q: What's your cancellation policy?

**A:** Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime. Projects can be cancelled before work begins for a full refund (minus processing fees).

### Q: Are there any hidden fees?

**A:** No hidden fees. All pricing is transparent. Payment processing fees (PayPal) are disclosed upfront.

### Q: Do you offer invoices for business expenses?

**A:** Yes, all payments include a detailed invoice suitable for business expense tracking.

### Q: Can I get a custom quote?

**A:** Absolutely! For projects not covered here, contact us at hello@shelbyvoicestudio.com for a custom quote.
