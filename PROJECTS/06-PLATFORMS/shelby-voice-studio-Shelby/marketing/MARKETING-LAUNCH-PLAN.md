## 1. Brand Positioning

### 1.1 Brand Statement

**Shelby Voice Studio** is the ethical AI voice platform for creators who care about quality and consent. We combine professional-grade voice synthesis with transparent practices, giving content creators access to 50+ premium voices while protecting voice owners' rights.

### 1.2 Unique Value Propositions

| Competitor | Their Position | Our Differentiation |
|------------|---------------|---------------------|
| ElevenLabs | Technology-first, developer-focused | Ethics-first, creator-focused |
| Murf.ai | Business/e-learning focus | Full-service + self-serve |
| PlayHT | Volume/price focus | Quality + curation focus |
| Fiverr/Upwork | Human voiceover marketplace | AI speed + human quality control |

## 5. Customer Acquisition Tactics

### 5.1 Free Tools (Lead Magnets)

**Voice Sample Generator:**
- Free 100-character sample with any voice
- No account required
- Watermarked output
- Email capture for full version

**Audiobook Cost Calculator:**
- Interactive calculator showing traditional vs. AI costs
- Collects email for detailed report
- Shareable results

**Script Timer:**
- Free tool to estimate script duration
- Useful for video creators, podcasters
- Branding + upsell opportunity

---

### 5.2 Referral Program

**Structure:**
- Referrer: 15% commission on first project, 5% on subscription (Year 1)
- Referee: 20% off first project
- No limit on referrals
- Payout: PayPal, credit, or check

**Tracking:**
- Unique referral links
- Dashboard for tracking referrals
- Automated payouts at $50 threshold

**Goal:** 20% of new customers from referrals by Month 12

---

### 5.3 Affiliate Program

**Commission Structure:**
- 20% commission on first purchase
- 10% recurring on subscriptions (6 months)
- Cookie duration: 90 days

**Target Affiliates:**
- Writing/publishing bloggers
- YouTube creators (video production niche)
- Podcasters with relevant audiences
- Course creators in marketing/content space

**Affiliate Tools:**
- Banners, email swipes, social posts
- Dedicated affiliate dashboard
- Monthly newsletter with tips + promotions

---

### 5.4 Promotions & Campaigns

**Launch Promotion:**
- 25% off first 3 months (subscriptions)
- Free custom voice sample (projects)
- Limited to first 100 customers

**Seasonal Campaigns:**
- **Q2:** "Spring Into Audiobooks" — Author focus
- **Q3:** "Back to School" — E-learning focus
- **Q4:** "Holiday Content Rush" — Video/podcast focus

**Flash Sales:**
- 48-hour sales around major events (Black Friday, Product Hunt anniversaries)
- 30-40% discounts
- Email + social promotion
