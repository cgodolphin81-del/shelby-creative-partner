## 🎯 TIER 1: FREE TIER

**Price**: $0/month

**Purpose**: Lead generation, product sampling, email capture

**Includes**:
- ✅ 5 poems per day
- ✅ All styles (haiku, sonnet, free verse, rap)
- ✅ Basic text download (PNG, TXT)
- ✅ Standard quality
- ✅ Personal use only
- ❌ No voice narration
- ❌ No commercial license
- ❌ No prints discount
- ❌ Watermarked downloads

**Limits**:
- 5 poems/day (resets at midnight UTC)
- 150 poems/month max
- Standard queue (may have wait time during peak)

**Conversion Goal**: 5-8% to Premium

**Price**: $9.99/month or $99/year (save 17%)

**Purpose**: Core revenue driver, best value for enthusiasts

**Includes**:
- ✅ Unlimited poems (no daily limits)
- ✅ All styles + exclusive Premium styles
- ✅ Voice narration (3 per day, any voice)
- ✅ High-quality downloads (no watermark)
- ✅ Instagram-ready cards (Canva templates)
- ✅ 10% discount on all prints
- ✅ Priority generation (faster queue)
- ✅ Save favorite poems to library
- ✅ Personal use + social sharing
- ❌ No commercial license
- ❌ No API access

**Voice Narration Limits**:
- 3 narrations per day
- 60 seconds max per poem
- All voice profiles available
- Standard MP3 quality

**Conversion Goal**: Retention 70%+ month-to-month

## 📈 PRICE PSYCHOLOGY TACTICS

### Anchoring

Show Pro plan first ($29.99) to make Premium ($9.99) feel like a bargain.

### Decoy Pricing

```
Basic: $0/mo    ← Free (good value)
Premium: $9.99/mo  ← TARGET (best value highlighted)
Pro: $29.99/mo  ← Anchor (makes Premium look reasonable)
```

### Urgency

- "Limited spots at founding member price"
- "Offer expires in 24 hours"
- "Only 3 custom poem slots left this week"

### Social Proof

- "Join 2,847 poets this month"
- "⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.9/5 from 500+ reviews"
- "Featured in [Publications]"

## 🔄 REFUND POLICY

### Subscriptions

- 30-day money-back guarantee (no questions asked)
- Cancel anytime (no cancellation fees)
- Prorated refunds for annual plans
- Downgrade anytime (effective next billing cycle)

### Prints & Physical Goods

- 30-day returns for damaged/wrong items
- Customer pays return shipping (unless our error)
- Refund or replacement (customer's choice)
- No returns on customized items (unless defective)

### Custom Poems

- 100% satisfaction guarantee
- Unlimited revisions until happy
- Full refund if we can't meet expectations
- No refunds after final delivery + approval
