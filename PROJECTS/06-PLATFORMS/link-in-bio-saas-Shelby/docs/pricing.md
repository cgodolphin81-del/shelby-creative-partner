## 🎯 Pricing Psychology & Positioning

### Strategic Price Points

#### Free Tier: $0
**Purpose:** Maximum acquisition, viral growth
- No credit card required
- Full-featured enough to be useful
- Transaction fee monetizes successful creators
- Watermark drives viral acquisition

**Psychology:** "Nothing to lose" removes all friction

#### Starter Tier: $5/month
**Purpose:** Entry-level paid, "no-brainer" upgrade
- Less than one coffee per week
- Cheaper than one transaction fee for most creators
- Positioned as "remove fees" upgrade
- Annual option: $48 (4 months free)

**Psychology:** Price is so low, ROI is immediate

#### Pro Tier: $15/month
**Purpose:** Professional creators, power users
- 3x Starter but positioned as 10x value
- Still under $20 "business expense" mental threshold
- Custom domain alone justifies price for professionals
- Annual option: $144 (save $36)

**Psychology:** Premium but accessible, "serious creator" tier

#### Enterprise: Custom (Starting $99/month)
**Purpose:** Agencies, brands, management companies
- Custom pricing based on needs
- Minimum $99/month positioning
- Volume discounts for multiple accounts
- White-label options available

**Psychology:** Exclusive, tailored, premium service

## 🔄 Upgrade Triggers & Paths

### Natural Upgrade Moments

#### Free → Starter Triggers
1. **First Sale:** 5% fee = immediate ROI calculation
2. **Branding Concern:** "Remove LinkFlow logo"
3. **Email Capture:** Need to build audience
4. **Product Limit:** Hit 3 product cap
5. **Storage Limit:** Hit 500MB cap
6. **Analytics Need:** Want more than 7 days

#### Starter → Pro Triggers
1. **Custom Domain:** Professional branding
2. **Advanced Analytics:** Need unlimited history
3. **A/B Testing:** Optimizing conversions
4. **Team Collaboration:** Need multiple users
5. **API Access:** Custom integrations
6. **Product Growth:** Need more than 25 products

#### Pro → Enterprise Triggers
1. **Agency Needs:** Managing multiple clients
2. **White-Label:** Removing LinkFlow branding entirely
3. **Team Size:** More than 5 team members
4. **Security Requirements:** SSO, compliance
5. **Support Needs:** Dedicated help
6. **Volume:** Managing 100+ creator pages

### Upgrade Nudges (In-App)

```javascript
// Example: Transaction fee nudge
if (user.tier === 'free' && user.monthlySales > 20) {
  showNudge({
    title: "You've made $20 in sales! 🎉",
    message: "Upgrade to Starter to keep 100% of your earnings. " +
             "You've already paid $1 in fees — Starter pays for itself.",
    cta: "Upgrade to Starter ($5/mo)",
    dismissible: true
  });
}

// Example: Product limit nudge
if (user.tier === 'starter' && user.products >= 23) {
  showNudge({
    title: "Running out of product slots?",
    message: "You have 23/25 products. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited products.",
    cta: "Upgrade to Pro ($15/mo)",
    dismissible: true
  });
}
```

## 💳 Payment & Billing Details

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Bank Transfer (Enterprise only)

### Billing Cycle Options
- **Monthly:** Charged every 30 days
- **Annual:** Charged once per year (20% discount)
- **Enterprise:** Monthly or annual, custom terms

### Refund Policy
- **Subscription Fees:** 30-day money-back guarantee
- **Transaction Fees:** Non-refundable (already paid to creator)
- **Marketplace Purchases:** 14-day refund if template doesn't work

### Proration Policy
- **Upgrades:** Immediate, prorated credit applied
- **Downgrades:** Effective at next billing cycle
- **Cancellations:** Access until end of billing period

### Dunning Management
```
Day 0: Payment fails → Email notification
Day 3: Retry payment → Email + in-app notification
Day 7: Retry payment → Email (urgent)
Day 10: Retry payment → Final email
Day 14: Account suspended → Access restricted
Day 30: Account archived → Data retained 90 days
```

## 📋 Pricing FAQ

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes! Upgrade immediately (prorated), downgrade at next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens if I cancel?**
A: You keep access until the end of your billing period. Your page stays live on Free tier.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: Never. No hidden fees, no setup costs.

**Q: Do you charge for page views?**
A: No! Unlimited page views on all tiers.

**Q: What's included in the transaction fee?**
A: Payment processing, payout handling, fraud protection, customer support for transactions.

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual?**
A: Yes! Contact support or use the billing portal.

**Q: Do you offer educational discounts?**
A: Yes! 50% off for verified students and educators.

**Q: What if I exceed my email limits?**
A: We'll notify you at 80%. You can upgrade or purchase additional sends ($5 per 10,000).
