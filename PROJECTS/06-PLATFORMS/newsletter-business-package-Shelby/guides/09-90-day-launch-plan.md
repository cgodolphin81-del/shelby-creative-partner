# 90-Day Launch Plan
## Day-by-Day Guide from Zero to Paid

---

## Overview

This plan takes you from **0 subscribers to first paid customers** in 90 days.

**Assumptions:**
- You're starting from scratch
- You can commit 10-15 hours/week
- You have expertise in a specific niche
- Budget: $500-2,000 for tools/ads

**Expected Outcomes:**
- 1,000-3,000 subscribers
- 50-150 paid subscribers
- $500-3,000 MRR
- Validated business model

---

## PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1-14)

### Day 1: Niche & Positioning
**Time:** 3 hours

**Tasks:**
- [ ] Review niche selection guide
- [ ] Pick your niche (use scoring framework)
- [ ] Define target audience persona
- [ ] Write value proposition (1 sentence)
- [ ] Choose newsletter name
- [ ] Check domain availability

**Deliverable:** Niche + positioning document

**Template:**
```
NICHE: [Your niche]
AUDIENCE: [Who they are, what they want]
VALUE PROP: "I help [audience] achieve [outcome] through [method]"
NAME: [Newsletter name]
DOMAIN: [YourNewsletter.com]
```

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

### Must Do's
1. **Publish consistently** - Never miss a scheduled issue
2. **Engage with readers** - Reply to every email
3. **Promote every issue** - Don't just send, share
4. **Track metrics** - Know your numbers weekly
5. **Iterate quickly** - Double down on what works

### Avoid These Mistakes
1. ❌ Waiting for "perfect" before launching
2. ❌ Ignoring reader feedback
3. ❌ Monetizing too early (before 1K subs)
4. ❌ Inconsistent publishing
5. ❌ Not promoting your work
