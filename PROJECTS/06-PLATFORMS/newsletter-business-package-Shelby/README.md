## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Waiting for perfect** - Launch before you're ready
2. **Monetizing too late** - Start at 1K subs, not 10K
3. **Inconsistent publishing** - Consistency beats quality early on
4. **Ignoring analytics** - Track everything from day 1
5. **No niche focus** - Specific beats general every time
6. **Buying ads too early** - Organic first, paid after PMF
7. **Not building in public** - Share your journey
8. **Ignoring reader feedback** - They tell you what they want
