# Design Briefs - Visual Direction

**Research Date:** March 2026
**Purpose:** Establish cohesive visual identity across all 250 products

---

## Brand Identity Overview

### Brand Voice
- **Primary:** Warm, relatable, honest
- **Secondary:** Humorous, heartfelt, specific
- **Avoid:** Generic, overly cutesy, preachy

### Visual Personality
- **Style:** Modern with vintage touches
- **Colors:** Warm neutrals + pops of color
- **Typography:** Mix of script and sans-serif
- **Imagery:** Real pets, not stock photos

## Typography Guidelines

### Primary Fonts
1. **Headline/Display:**
   - **Montserrat Bold** - Modern, clean, versatile
   - **Playfair Display** - Elegant, for memorial products
   - **Bebas Neue** - Bold statements, athletic vibe

2. **Body/Script:**
   - **Pacifico** - Friendly, casual script
   - **Dancing Script** - Elegant, heartfelt
   - **Quicksand** - Clean, readable sans-serif

3. **Accent/Novelty:**
   - **Permanent Marker** - Hand-drawn feel
   - **Caveat** - Natural handwriting
   - **Righteous** - Modern, tech products

### Font Pairing Examples
- **T-Shirts:** Montserrat Bold + Pacifico
- **Mugs:** Bebas Neue + Quicksand
- **Memorial:** Playfair Display + Dancing Script
- **Digital Products:** Montserrat + Quicksand

## Design Styles by Product Category

### T-Shirts (50 designs)

- Single color or two-color max
- Center chest or left chest placement
- **Example:** "Dog Mom: Like a Regular Mom But Cooler" in Montserrat Bold

- Simple line art + slogan
- Breed-specific silhouettes
- Soft color palettes
- **Example:** Frenchie outline + "Professional Snort Interpreter"

#### Style 3: Vintage/Retro (10 designs)
- Distressed textures
- Retro color schemes (muted tones)
- Badge/emblem layouts

## Quality Standards

### Print Products:
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Color Mode:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital
- **Bleed:** 0.125" for items with edge-to-edge print
- **File Formats:** PNG (transparent), SVG (vectors), PDF (print-ready)

### Digital Products:
- **Resolution:** 72 DPI (screen), 300 DPI (printable)
- **File Sizes:** Optimized for download (<10MB per product)
- **Formats:** PDF, PNG, Canva template links

### Consistency Checks:
- [ ] All designs use approved color palette
- [ ] Typography follows guidelines
- [ ] Brand voice is consistent
- [ ] No generic stock imagery
- [ ] Breed-specific designs are accurate

## Design Workflow

1. **Concept:** Sketch + slogan pairing
2. **Draft:** Digital mockup in brand colors
3. **Review:** Check against brand guidelines
4. **Refine:** Adjust based on feedback
5. **Finalize:** Export in all required formats
6. **Mockup:** Create product preview images
7. **Launch:** Upload to sales platforms
