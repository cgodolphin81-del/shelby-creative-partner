# Pricing Strategy - Competitive & Profitable

**Research Date:** March 2026
**Goal:** Balance competitiveness with healthy margins across all 250 products

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles
1. **Value-Based, Not Cost-Plus:** Price based on perceived value, not just production cost
2. **Tiered Options:** Good/Better/Best for key product categories
3. **Psychological Pricing:** Use $X.97, $X.95 endings strategically
4. **Bundle Discounts:** Encourage higher AOV (average order value)
5. **Premium Positioning:** Mid-to-high range, justify with quality

## Price Testing Strategy

### A/B Tests to Run:
1. **$27.97 vs. $29.97** on t-shirts (psychological pricing)
2. **Bundle discount: 20% vs. 25%** (find sweet spot)
3. **Free shipping threshold: $50 vs. $75** (increase AOV)
4. **Digital product: $9.97 vs. $12.97** (value perception)

### Metrics to Track:
- Conversion rate by price point
- Average order value (AOV)
- Cart abandonment rate
- Refund/return rate
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
