## Usage Guidelines

### For T-Shirts:
- Keep under 8 words for front designs
- Longer slogans work on back or inside collar
- Pair with simple graphics for maximum impact

### For Mugs:
- 4-6 words ideal for visibility
- Wrap-around text for longer slogans
- Consider inside-rim text for surprises

### For Stickers:
- Short, punchy phrases (2-5 words)
- Bold, readable fonts
- Works well in packs with variety

### For Social Media:
- Relatable, shareable content
- Pair with pet photos/videos
- Use as caption starters

### For Memorial Products:
- Softer, more heartfelt tone
- Consider script fonts
- Pair with paw prints, hearts, rainbows
