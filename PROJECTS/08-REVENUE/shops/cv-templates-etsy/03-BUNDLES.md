## Listing Strategy

1. **List individual templates first** ($4.99 each)
2. **Add bundles as upsells** in each listing description
3. **Use Etsy's variation feature** for bundles or create separate bundle listings
4. **Cross-link** between individual and bundle listings

### Recommended Approach:
- Create 10 individual listings
- Create 3 bundle listings
- In each individual listing, mention: "Want more options? Check out our Professional Pack (10 templates) or MEGA Pack (320+ templates) for better value!"
