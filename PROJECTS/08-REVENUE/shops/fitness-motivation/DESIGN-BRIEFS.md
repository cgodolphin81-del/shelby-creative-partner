# DESIGN BRIEFS - Visual Direction

## 🎨 BRAND VISUAL IDENTITY

**"Progress over perfection. Showing up over showing off. Real humans, not fitness influencers."**

| Value | Visual Expression |
|-------|-------------------|

## 📋 DESIGN BRIEF #1: "REAL HUMAN" COLLECTION

### Overview
The flagship collection that defines our brand. Designs that speak to everyday people trying their best.

### Target Audience
- Beginners intimidated by fitness culture
- People who've had bad experiences with "intense" fitness brands
- Anyone who wants humor over intimidation

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Warm Sage | #87A878 | Primary brand color |
| Soft Coral | #F88379 | Accent, CTAs |
| Cream | #F5F0EB | Backgrounds |
| Charcoal | #3D3D3D | Text |
| Muted Yellow | #E8C547 | Highlights |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** "Recoleta" or "Fraunces" (soft serif, friendly)
- **Body:** "Inter" or "DM Sans" (clean, readable)
- **Accents:** Handwritten script for emphasis

### Imagery Style
- Real people, not models
- Diverse body types, ages, abilities
- Candid moments, not posed
- "During" shots, not before/after
- Natural lighting, minimal retouching

### Example Applications
- T-shirt: "I Showed Up" in soft sage on cream
- Social post: Real person mid-workout, slightly sweaty, smiling
- Website: Hero image of diverse group in a home living room workout

### Do's
✅ Show real effort (sweat, flushed faces)  
✅ Include various body types in same frame  
✅ Use natural, achievable settings  
✅ Celebrate small moments  

### Don'ts
❌ Heavily retouched skin  
❌ Only "fit" bodies  
❌ Gym-only settings  
❌ Aggressive poses

## 🎨 PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

### Instagram/TikTok
- **Format:** Vertical (9:16) for Reels/TikTok, Square (1:1) for feed
- **Style:** Bold text overlays, hook in first 3 seconds
- **Colors:** High contrast for small screens
- **CTA:** Clear, single action per post

### Etsy/Shop Listings
- **Format:** Multiple angles, lifestyle + product shots
- **Style:** Clean backgrounds, consistent lighting
- **Colors:** True-to-life color accuracy
- **CTA:** Add to cart, favorite, share

### Digital Products (PDFs)
- **Format:** US Letter (8.5x11) + A4
- **Style:** Print-friendly (minimal ink coverage)
- **Colors:** Work in both color and B&W
- **CTA:** Clear instructions, next steps

### Website/Blog
- **Format:** Responsive, mobile-first
- **Style:** Clean, scannable, plenty of white space
- **Colors:** On-brand, accessible contrast ratios
- **CTA:** Contextual, not aggressive

## 📐 DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

### T-Shirts & Apparel
- **Print Area:** 12" x 16" max (front), 4" x 4" (sleeve)
- **File Format:** PNG with transparent background, 300 DPI
- **Color Mode:** CMYK for print
- **Bleed:** 0.125" on all sides

### Mugs
- **Print Area:** 8.5" x 3" (wraparound)
- **File Format:** PNG or SVG, 300 DPI
- **Safe Zone:** Keep text 0.25" from edges

### Posters/Prints
- **Sizes:** 11x14, 16x20, 18x24 inches
- **File Format:** PDF (print-ready), PNG (digital)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Color:** CMYK for print, RGB for digital

### Stickers
- **Sizes:** 1" to 6" (varies by design)
- **File Format:** SVG or PNG with transparent background
- **Bleed:** 0.125" for die-cut
- **Material:** Vinyl (waterproof) or paper (indoor)

### Digital Products
- **Page Size:** 8.5" x 11" (US Letter) + A4
- **File Format:** PDF (interactive), PNG (printables)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for printables
- **Fonts:** Embedded or outlined

## ✅ QUALITY CHECKLIST

Before any design goes to production:

- [ ] Aligns with brand values (approachable, funny, inclusive)
- [ ] Uses approved color palette
- [ ] Typography is legible at intended size
- [ ] No ableist or body-shaming language
- [ ] Tested on diverse mockups (various body types)
- [ ] Works in color and black & white (for printables)
- [ ] File specs meet production requirements
- [ ] Spell-checked and grammar-checked
- [ ] Unique angle (not generic fitness cliché)
- [ ] Would make target audience feel seen, not judged
