## 🎯 COMPETITIVE GAPS IDENTIFIED

### What Competitors Do WELL:
1. ✅ Strong visual branding
2. ✅ Consistent social media presence
3. ✅ Clear product photography
4. ✅ Fast shipping (Etsy top sellers)
5. ✅ Community building (Instagram influencers)

### What Competitors Do POORLY:
1. ❌ **Intimidating messaging** - "No excuses", "Beast mode only"
2. ❌ **Limited size inclusivity** - Most stop at 2XL or 3XL
3. ❌ **Generic designs** - Same slogans across multiple shops
4. ❌ **High price points** - Average $45-65 for basic items
5. ❌ **Lack of beginner focus** - Most content assumes fitness knowledge
6. ❌ **All-or-nothing mentality** - No recognition of small wins
