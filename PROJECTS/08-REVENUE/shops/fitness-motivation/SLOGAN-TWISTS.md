## 🎨 DESIGN RECOMMENDATIONS BY SLOGAN TYPE

### Funny/Relatable:
- **Fonts:** Casual, handwritten, slightly imperfect
- **Colors:** Bright, playful (coral, mint, yellow)
- **Graphics:** Simple icons, doodles

### Honest Struggles:
- **Fonts:** Clean sans-serif, honest and direct
- **Colors:** Muted tones (sage, dusty rose, slate)
- **Graphics:** Minimal, let text shine

### Beginner Support:
- **Fonts:** Friendly rounded sans-serif
- **Colors:** Welcoming (soft blue, warm gray, cream)
- **Graphics:** Encouraging symbols (stars, checkmarks)

### Parent Reality:
- **Fonts:** Mix of bold and casual
- **Colors:** Real-life tones (coffee brown, tired gray, hope yellow)
- **Graphics:** Parent + fitness combo icons
