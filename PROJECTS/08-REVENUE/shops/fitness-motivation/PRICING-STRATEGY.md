## 💰 PRICING PHILOSOPHY

### Core Principles
1. **Accessible but not cheap** - Quality deserves fair pricing
2. **Undercut influencer merch** - 30-40% less than celebrity brands
3. **Value-based tiers** - Good, Better, Best options
4. **Psychological pricing** - .99 for impulse, .00 for premium
5. **Bundle discounts** - Encourage larger cart sizes

### Competitive Positioning

| Tier | Competitor Price | Our Price | Savings |
|------|-----------------|-----------|---------|
| **Budget** | Etsy: $15-25 | $12-20 | 20% less |
| **Mid-Range** | Influencer: $45-65 | $25-40 | 40% less |
| **Premium** | Lululemon: $98-128 | $50-75 | 45% less |

## 📊 PRICE PSYCHOLOGY TACTICS

### Charm Pricing
- $24.99 instead of $25 (left-digit effect)
- $17 instead of $20 (feels significantly less)
- $97 instead of $100 (premium but not quite triple digits)

### Anchoring
- Show "Regular: $35, Now: $24.99"
- Place expensive item first to make others seem reasonable
- Use "Most Popular" badge on target product

### Decoy Effect
| Option | Price | Strategy |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Basic | $17 | Entry point |
| Pro | $24 | **Target** (best value) |
| Ultimate | $47 | Makes Pro look reasonable |

### Scarcity & Urgency
- "Only 3 left in stock"
- "Sale ends in 24 hours"
- "Limited edition design"
- Use sparingly and honestly

### Free Shipping Threshold
- Free shipping on orders $50+
- Average order value: $38
- **Goal:** Encourage adding one more item

### Bundle Pricing
- Individual: $17 + $24 + $19 = $60
- Bundle: $47 (22% savings)
- **Result:** Higher AOV, customer feels they won

## 🚨 PRICING RED FLAGS

### Warning Signs
- ❌ Consistently losing to competitors on price
- ❌ High traffic, low conversion (price objection)
- ❌ High refund rate (value mismatch)
- ❌ Can't maintain healthy margins
- ❌ Race to the bottom pressure

### Solutions
- ✅ Emphasize unique value, not price
- ✅ Improve product quality/differentiation
- ✅ Better communicate benefits
- ✅ Target different customer segment
- ✅ Bundle to increase perceived value
