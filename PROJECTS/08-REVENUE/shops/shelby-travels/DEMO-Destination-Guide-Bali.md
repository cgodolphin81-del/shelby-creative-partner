## ⚠️ Important Tips

### Do's:
- ✅ Respect temples (wear sarong)
- ✅ Bargain at markets (start at 50%)
- ✅ Drink bottled water
- ✅ Use sunscreen
- ✅ Carry cash (many places don't take cards)

### Don'ts:
- ❌ Touch people's heads (sacred)
- ❌ Point with index finger (use thumb)
- ❌ Show public affection (conservative culture)
- ❌ Drink tap water
- ❌ Ride scooter without helmet/permit

### Emergency Numbers:
- Police: 110
- Ambulance: 118
- Tourist Police: +62 361 754599
