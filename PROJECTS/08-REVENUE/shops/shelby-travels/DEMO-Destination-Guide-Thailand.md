## 🎒 What to Pack for Thailand

### Essential Gear

**Clothing:**
- Lightweight, breathable fabrics
- Modest clothing for temples (shoulders, knees)
- Swimwear (2-3 sets)
- Sandals + walking shoes
- Light jacket (AC can be cold)
- Rain jacket (May-October is rainy season)

**Tech:**
- Universal Adapter - Thailand uses Type A/B/C plugs
- Power Bank 20000mAh - Essential for long days
- Waterproof phone case - Beach and boat days
- Camera - Thailand is incredibly photogenic

**Health:**
- Mosquito repellent (dengue risk)
- Sunscreen (expensive locally)
- Basic meds (stomach meds essential)
- Hand sanitizer

## 🌟 Best Time to Visit

### Cool Season (November-February) ⭐ BEST
- **Pros:** Dry, pleasant weather (25-30°C)
- **Cons:** Peak tourist season, higher prices
- **Best for:** Everything - ideal conditions

### Hot Season (March-May)
- **Pros:** Warm weather, fewer crowds
- **Cons:** Very hot (35-40°C), humid
- **Best for:** Beaches, islands, water activities

### Rainy Season (June-October)
- **Pros:** Fewer tourists, lush green, lower prices
- **Cons:** Heavy rain, some islands closed
- **Best for:** Northern Thailand, cultural sites
