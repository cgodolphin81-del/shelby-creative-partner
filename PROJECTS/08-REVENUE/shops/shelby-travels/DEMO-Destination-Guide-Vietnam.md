**[✈️ Search Flights to Vietnam](https://www.booking.com/flights?aid=YOUR_BOOKING_ID)**

**Pro Tips:**
- Book 2-3 months ahead for best prices
- Consider multi-city: fly into Hanoi, out of Ho Chi Minh
- Budget carriers: VietJet Air, Bamboo Airways, AirAsia
- Peak season: November-April (dry season)

### Visa Requirements

**Visa on Arrival (VoA):**
- **Cost:** ~$25 USD (single entry, 1 month)
- **Duration:** 1-3 months available
- **Process:** Apply online, pick up at airport
- **Required:** Approval letter, photos, cash

**E-Visa (RECOMMENDED):**
- **Cost:** $25 USD
- **Duration:** Up to 90 days
- **Process:** Apply online 7+ days before travel
- **Processing:** 3-5 business days
- **Valid for:** All entry points

**Check your status:** [Vietnam Immigration](https://evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn/)

## 🚗 Getting Around

### Trains
- **Reunification Express:** Hanoi to Ho Chi Minh City (30-33 hours)
- **Cost:** $25-100 depending on class
- **Comfort:** AC, sleeper berths available
- **Book:** [Direct via Vietnam Railways](https://www.vnrail.com/) or GetYourGuide

### Buses
- **Sleeper buses:** Hanoi to HCMC (30-35 hours)
- **Cost:** $20-40
- **Companies:** Sinh Cafe, The Sinh Tourist, Futa Bus
- **Comfort:** Reclining seats, some with beds

### Motorbike
- **Cost:** $5-10/day
- **Requirements:** International Driving Permit
- **Pros:** Freedom, cheap, authentic
- **Cons:** Dangerous traffic, police checkpoints

### Grab/Gojek
- **Cost:** $2-10 per ride
- **Pros:** Cheap, app-based, reliable
- **Cons:** Can't go everywhere (some areas blocked)

## ⚠️ Important Tips

### Do's:
- ✅ Bargain at markets (start at 50%)
- ✅ Drink bottled water
- ✅ Use Grab/Gojek for transport
- ✅ Wear comfortable shoes
- ✅ Carry small bills

### Don'ts:
- ❌ Don't drink tap water
- ❌ Don't wear revealing clothes at temples
- ❌ Don't argue with motorbike drivers
- ❌ Don't carry large amounts of cash
- ❌ Don't walk alone at night in unfamiliar areas

- Police: 113
- Ambulance: 115
