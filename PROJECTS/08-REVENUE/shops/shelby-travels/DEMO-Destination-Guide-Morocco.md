## 🎒 What to Pack for Morocco

### Essential Gear

**Clothing:**
- Modest clothing (shoulders, knees covered)
- Lightweight, breathable fabrics
- Warm layers for desert nights (can be cold!)
- Comfortable walking shoes
- Scarf (for sun, dust, mosque visits)
- Light rain jacket (November-March)

**Tech:**
- Universal Adapter - Morocco uses Type C/E plugs
- Power Bank 20000mAh - Essential for long days
- Camera - Morocco is incredibly photogenic
- Headlamp (for desert camps, riads)

**Health:**
- Sunscreen (strong sun)
- Lip balm with SPF
- Hand sanitizer
- Basic meds (stomach meds essential)
- Insect repellent

## 🚗 Getting Around

### Trains (ONCF)
- **Routes:** Casablanca ↔ Rabat ↔ Fes ↔ Marrakech ↔ Tangier
- **Cost:** $15-40 depending on distance
- **Comfort:** 1st and 2nd class, AC, comfortable
- **Book:** [ONCF Website](https://www.oncf-voyages.ma/) or GetYourGuide

### Buses (CTM, Supratours)
- **Routes:** Nationwide coverage
- **Cost:** $10-30 depending on distance
- **Comfort:** AC, reclining seats, reliable
- **Companies:** CTM (best), Supratours

### Grand Taxis
- **What:** Shared taxis (Mercedes sedans)
- **Cost:** $5-20 per person
- **Routes:** Short distances, intercity
- **Tip:** Negotiate price before getting in

### Car Rental
- **Cost:** $30-80/day

## ⚠️ Important Tips

### Do's:
- ✅ Dress modestly (especially women)
- ✅ Bargain at souks (expected!)
- ✅ Drink bottled water
- ✅ Learn basic Arabic/French phrases
- ✅ Ask before photographing people

### Don'ts:
- ❌ Don't drink tap water
- ❌ Don't eat with left hand (considered unclean)
- ❌ Don't public displays of affection
- ❌ Don't visit mosques as non-Muslims (except Hassan II)
- ❌ Don't accept unsolicited help/guides

- Police: 19
- Ambulance: 15

## 🌟 Best Time to Visit

### Spring (March-May) ⭐ BEST
- **Pros:** Pleasant weather, wildflowers, fewer crowds
- **Cons:** Some rain in north
- **Best for:** Everything - ideal conditions

### Summer (June-August)
- **Pros:** Long days, desert is hot but dry
- **Cons:** Very hot (40°C+ in Marrakech/desert)
- **Best for:** Coastal towns, mountains

### Fall (September-November) ⭐ BEST
- **Pros:** Pleasant weather, harvest season
- **Cons:** Busy in October
- **Best for:** Everything - ideal conditions

### Winter (December-February)
- **Pros:** Few tourists, cheap prices, snow in Atlas
- **Cons:** Cold nights (especially desert), some rain
- **Best for:** Desert (warm days), cities
