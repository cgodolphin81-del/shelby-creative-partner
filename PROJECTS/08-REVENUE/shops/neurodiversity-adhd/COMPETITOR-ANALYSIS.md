## KEY FINDINGS

### What's Selling Best:
1. **Digital Planners** - $8-25, high margins, instant delivery
2. **Relatable T-Shirts** - $22-35, viral potential
3. **Sticker Packs** - $5-15, impulse buys, high volume
4. **Mugs with Humor** - $18-25, gift favorite
5. **Affirmation Prints** - $12-30, home/office decor

### Common Slogans (OVERUSED):
- "ADHD but make it fashion"
- "Neurospicy"
- "Different Not Less"
- "Actually Autistic"
- "ADHD Awareness"
- "It's Okay to Be Weird"

### Customer Complaints (Opportunities):
- "Too clinical/serious"
- "Same slogans everywhere"
- "Nothing for late-diagnosed adults"
- "No AuDHD-specific products"
- "Prices too high"
- "Generic designs"

### Viral Content Patterns (TikTok/Instagram):
- **Executive dysfunction humor** - 1M+ views
- **"Things I hyperfixated on this week"** - 2M+ views
- **Sensory overload relatability** - 800K+ views
- **Late diagnosis stories** - 1.5M+ views
- **"ADHD tax" (losing things)** - 3M+ views

## OUR DIFFERENTIATION STRATEGY

### Tone & Voice:
| Competitors | Us |
|-------------|-----|
| Clinical, serious | Funny, supportive friend |
| One-size-fits-all | Sub-niche specific |
| Generic motivation | Real talk ("Some days you shower, that's a win") |
| Awareness-focused | Life-improvement focused |
| Perfect productivity | Guilt-free "tried my best" |

### Product Angles:
- **Not:** "ADHD Daily Planner"
- **But:** "The 'I Tried My Best' Planner - Guilt-Free Productivity for Bad Brain Days"

- **Not:** "Neurodivergent T-Shirt"
- **But:** "My Brain is a Browser with 47 Tabs Open (And 3 Are Frozen)"

- **Not:** "Motivational Stickers"
- **But:** "Realistic Affirmations for Neurospicy Humans"

### Sub-Niches We're Targeting:
1. **AuDHD** (Autism + ADHD overlap)
2. **Late-diagnosed women** (30s-50s)
3. **ADHD parents** (managing kids + own ADHD)
4. **ADHD students** (high school/college)
5. **ADHD professionals** (workplace struggles)
6. **Sensory-sensitive individuals**
7. **Rejection Sensitive Dysphoria (RSD)**
8. **Autistic burnout recovery**
