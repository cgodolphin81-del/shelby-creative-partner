## USAGE GUIDELINES

### Do:
- ✅ Mix and match slogans across product types
- ✅ Adapt wording slightly for specific audiences
- ✅ Test which resonates best with target sub-niches
- ✅ Keep tone funny + supportive, never mean-spirited

### Don't:
- ❌ Use ableist language ("crazy," "insane," "lame")
- ❌ Perpetuate harmful stereotypes
- ❌ Mock or minimize neurodivergent experiences
- ❌ Copy competitor slogans exactly
