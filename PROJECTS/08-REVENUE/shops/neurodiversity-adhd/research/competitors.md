## MARKET GAPS ANALYSIS

### What Competitors Are Missing:

1. **Humor-Forward Brand** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Most competitors are serious/clinical
   - Opportunity: Funny, relatable, self-deprecating humor
   - Example: "ADHD Tax Paid Here" mug, "Object Permanence? Never Met Her" tee

2. **Integrated System** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Products are fragmented (planner here, course there, merch elsewhere)
   - Opportunity: Cohesive ecosystem (digital + physical + content)
   - Example: Buy planner, get access to video tutorials + community

3. **Community-Centric** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Most are transactional (buy product, leave)
   - Opportunity: Build community (Discord, challenges, accountability)
   - Example: 30-day planner challenge with community support

4. **Affordable Entry Points** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Many products are $50-500+
   - Opportunity: $10-30 products for broader accessibility
   - Example: $12 daily planner vs. $200 course

5. **AuDHD/Intersectional Focus** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Most focus on ADHD only or autism only
   - Opportunity: Products for AuDHD, ADHD+anxiety, ADHD+women
   - Example: "AuDHD Life" workbook, "ADHD Women" collection

6. **Content + Product Integration** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Content creators don't always have products
   - Product sellers don't always create content
   - Opportunity: Each product has supporting content, each article has product tie-in

7. **Modern Aesthetic** ⭐⭐⭐
   - Many designs are generic, clipart-y, or overly clinical
   - Opportunity: Trendy, Instagram-worthy, colorful but not childish
   - Example: Aesthetic Notion templates, trendy affirmation posters

8. **Body Doubling Integration** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
   - Huge need, few products address this
   - Opportunity: Virtual body doubling, accountability features in planners
   - Example: Planner with built-in accountability partner matching

## THREATS TO MONITOR

1. **Market Saturation**
   - More creators entering ADHD niche
   - Mitigation: Differentiate through humor, community, quality

2. **Platform Changes**
   - Etsy algorithm changes, Instagram reach decline
   - Mitigation: Build email list, own the customer relationship

3. **Copycats**
   - Successful products will be copied
   - Mitigation: Brand loyalty, community, constant innovation

4. **ADHD Community Skepticism**
   - Community can detect grifters
   - Mitigation: Authenticity, lived experience, give before asking

5. **Economic Downturn**
   - Discretionary spending decreases
   - Mitigation: Affordable products, payment plans, value demonstration
