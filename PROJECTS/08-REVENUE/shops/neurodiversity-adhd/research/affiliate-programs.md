## AFFILIATE STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

### Best Practices:

1. **Disclose Transparently**
   - Always use #ad, #affiliate, or clear disclosure
   - ADHD community values authenticity
   - FTC requirement

2. **Only Promote What You Use**
   - Test products before recommending
   - Share honest reviews (pros AND cons)
   - Build trust over quick commissions

3. **Create Value-First Content**
   - Review articles, comparisons, tutorials
   - Not just "buy this" but "here's how to use it"
   - Solve problems, not just sell

4. **Diversify Affiliate Income**
   - Don't rely on one program
   - Mix of recurring + one-time
   - Various price points

5. **Track Performance**
   - Monitor which links convert
   - Double down on winners
   - Cut underperformers

6. **Negotiate Better Rates**
   - Once you have traffic, ask for higher commissions
   - Many programs have tiered structures
   - Worth asking once you're driving sales
